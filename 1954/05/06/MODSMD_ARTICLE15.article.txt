# Salgo Directs Purcell Opera
## 
### 
The Stanford University concert
presentation of Purcell's opera
"Dido and Aeneas" will be given
at Cubberley Aliditorium Saturday
at 8:15 p.m. and Sunday at 4 p.m.
It will be directed by Sandor' Salgo,
associate professor of music and di
rector of the Stanford Symphony
Orchestra and of the San Jose Sym
phony.
Hugo Melchione, graduate student
in the Speech and Drama Depart
ment, will be stage director.
The choral work is being done by
the Stanford Chorale under the di
rection of Professor Harold Schmidt.
Putnam Aldrich, of the Stanford
Music Department, will play the
basso part on the harpsichord.
Tickets for both performances are
50 cents. They may be purchased
at The Knoll or at the Peninsula
Box Office in Palo Alto.
