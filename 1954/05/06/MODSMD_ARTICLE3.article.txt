protested the sale of "McCarthy for
Fuehrer" buttons on the campus.
Joe Pool and Mike Hudson, who
said they were representing a group
of friends loyal to Sen. Joseph I?.
McCarthy, registered a complaint
with Robert. P. HufT, assistant coun
selor for men, against what they
called "soliciting without a license

Bill Metcalf. sophomore, who is
