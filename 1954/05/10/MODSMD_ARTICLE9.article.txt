# Shakespeare Will Be Series Topic
## 
### 
Dr. John C. Adams, authority on
Shakespeare and president of Hof
stra College, will speak on "Shake
speare's Stage and Stagecraft" to
morrow night at 7:30 in Memorial
Auditorium in the Tuesday Evening
Series.
A faculty member of Cornell and
Syracuse universities, Adams has
spoken on Shakespeare at. many
American universities and colleges
and at the Shakespeare Symposium
at Stratford-on-Avon in England.
