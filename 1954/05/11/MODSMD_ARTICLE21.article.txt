# Distaffers to Hear Business Officers
## 
### 
Stanford's business officers will
explain and illustrate business office
activities for Distaff Club when it
meets at 7:45 tomorrow night in
Encina Hall.
Explaining the functions of the
office will be Business Manager Alf
E. Brandin and his assistants, Er
nest S. Erwin, S. F. Post, and Dwight
B. Adams. Philip G. Duffy will also
discuss his activities as purchasing
agent for the University.
