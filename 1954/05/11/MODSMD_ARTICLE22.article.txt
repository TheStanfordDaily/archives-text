# Interclub Speaking Contest Is Tonight
## 
### 
Four finalists will compete tonight
in the Interclub Speaking Contest
sponsored by the ASSU Speech As
sociation.
The contest, which will begin at
7, will be held in the Interclub
lounge. The speeches will be of the
after-dinner variety.
Competing are: Jim Flanagan, El
Capitan; Ilal Hansen, Los Arcos; Joe
Long. Breakers; and Jim Mitchell,
El Campo.
