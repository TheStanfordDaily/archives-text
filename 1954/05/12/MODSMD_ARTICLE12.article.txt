# Lozabnick, Bing Attend Conference
## 
### 
Stanford student body president,
Don Lozabnick and president-elect
Pete Bing will attend an annual
meeting of the Pacific Student Pres
idents' Association, to be held today
through Saturday in San Francisco.
The association, Lozabnick report
ed, provides an opportunity for the
new and old leaders to meet and ex
change ideas which will lead to the
solution of common problems.
Student presidents from the. 10
Far Western states, British Colum
bia, and possibly Alaska and Mexico
will represent over 60 colleges at the
conference.
