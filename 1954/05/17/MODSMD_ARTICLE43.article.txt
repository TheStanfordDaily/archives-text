# Sims Gains Finals Of Golf Tourney
## 
### 
Bob Sims gained the finals of the
All-University Golf Championships
with a one-up victory over former
Stanford star Keith Beekman in a
match played last week.
Sims, San Francisco senior, led
the qualifying round with a one
uncler 70, trailed by PCC Southern
Division champ Warren Dailey. who
carded a 71.
Among the 32 who qualified for
the semi-finals were former Card
greats Jerry Smith, Dick McElyea,
and Beekman, who are now in
Graduate School here at Stanford.
The finals will be held sometime
before Friday.
