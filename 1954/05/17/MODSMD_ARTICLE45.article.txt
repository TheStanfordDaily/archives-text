# Activity Tryouts Scheduled
## 
### 
Interviews for summer quarter
ASSU officers will be held today
and tomorrow from 3 to 5 p.m. in
the ASSU office.
Students planning to be here sum
mer quarter may try out for the of
fices of student body president and
vice - president, and the following
Excom representatives: two under
graduate men, one undergraduate
woman, one graduate student, and
one student from the Education
Council.
» * *
Applications are now being ac
cepted in the ASSU office for Lake
Lag lifeguards. Interviews start to
day and continue through Friday

from 3 to 5 p.m. Applications must
be made before interviews.
• ♦ *
The Foreign Scholarship Commit
tee will hold interviews from 2 to 5
p.m. today and tomorrow to select
committee members for next year.
Applicants may sign up for the
interviews at the ASSU office.

Clubhouse. Larry Tarlow will 'do
the interviewing. There is a sign-up
