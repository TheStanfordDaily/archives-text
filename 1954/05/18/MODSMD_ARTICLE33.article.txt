# Indian Racketmen Bounce San Jose For 6-3 Count
## 
### 
Coach Bob Renker let his four
top men have a day off, and the
rest of the squad showed up sur
prisingly well yesterday afternoon
as they spiked San Jose State, 6-3.
In the match, which was held on
the San Jose courts, Stanford lost
two singles battles and only one
doubles contest. In a previous meet
ing this season the Cardinal first
string blanked the Spartans, 9-0.
Dave Duff gave a fine performance
in cutting down State's top man, Don
Straub, in three sets 6-2, 3-6, 6-3.
Duff then teamed up with Bob Nor
ton to route Straub and Corny Reese,
6-3, 2-6, 6-3, in doubles.
Complete results:
Singles
Dave Duff (S) d. Don Straub, 6-2,
3-6,6-3.

6-2, 6-3.

6-4, 6-3.

Reese, 6-2, 6-3.

6-2, 6-2.

6-4.
