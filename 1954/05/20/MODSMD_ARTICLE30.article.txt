# Spring Show Staff Meets for Critique
## 
### 
The east and crews of the recent
Ram's Head production of ''Any
thing Goes" met yesterday afternoon
with Hubert Heflfner, executive head
of the Speech and Drama Depart
ment, and other stuff members to
discuss the show.
All aspects of writing, directing,
and technical production were re
viewed and criticized. Such critiques
will be held after all future shows.
