# Lag Firelight Fling Scheduled Tonight
## 
### 
Lagunita Court will present "Fire
light Fling," an all - campus open
house, tonight after the Spring Sing.
Music for dancing will be provided
by a combo featuring Ted Lasagne.
Kean Anderson and John Boswell
will furnish piano and vocal enter
tainment.
There will be a bonfire in the
court, and free ice cream cones and
coflee will be served.
The open house will last until 1
a.m.
