# Pre-reg Opens This Monday
## 
### 
Preregistration of Upper Division
and Graduate students for the au
tumn quarter begins Monday and
continues through Tuesday. The
Registrar's Office yesterday an
nounced the following procedure for
these students:
1. Obtain an official study list card
from your major school or depart
ment and fill out your program in
ink with the approval and signature
of your major professor.
2. ff your study list includes any
courses requiring signing class lists
the signing may be done at the Reg
istrar's Office, Room 111-A, between
8 a.m. and 12 noon, and 1 and 5 p.m.
on Monday and Tuesday. Your name
on the class list assures you of ad
mission to the class, provided you
are present at the first class meet
3. Turn in your official study list
card, in person, to the check-out
clerk at the information desk, Reg
istrar's Office, before 5 p.m. Tues
day.
Lower Division students are urged
to see their advisers on Wednesday,
Thursday, or Friday. Pre-registra
tion for these students will begin
next Thursday and continue until
Saturday.
