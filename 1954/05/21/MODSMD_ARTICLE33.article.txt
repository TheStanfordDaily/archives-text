SENIOR SCROLL—Jane Wood, Senior Class Scroll and Plaque
chairman, shows Juana Rathbun, designer of the Scroll, where
to sign her name among those of other members of the Class
of 1954. The Scroll will be kept in the ASSU Office until the
end of the quarter and all seniors may sign it before that time.
It will be buried under the '54 Plaque on Inner Quad along with
other class records and mementos. —Daily photo by Ken Green
