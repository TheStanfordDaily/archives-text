# Gypsies Whirl At Theta Chi
## 
### 
The Gypsy Dance, the last all
campus costume dance of the year,
will be- held tomorrow night at the
Theta Chi House from 9 p.m. to 1
a.m. There will be no admission
charge.
As a special feature, Don Marteen,
Southern California television per
sonality, will entertain with his gui
tar, songs, and sleight -of - hand
tricks. Marteen appeared at the
dance two years ago.
The fortune-telling booth will be
run for charity, and all proceeds will
be turned over to the Con Home. A!
Schaaf and his sextet will furnish
the music for the evening,
Tom Miller is chairman of the
dance.
