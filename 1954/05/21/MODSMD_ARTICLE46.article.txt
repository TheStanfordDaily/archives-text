# Alumni Plan Barbecue
## 
### 
The Alumni Sons and Daughters
will hold their annual barbecue this
Sunday, May 23, at Angell Field.
The barbecue will begin at 12:30
p.m.
Deadline for reservations is 4 p.m.
today. They may be made at the of
fice of the Alumni Secretary, Jack
Shoup, in Bowman Alumni House.
Second- and third - generation
Stanford Students may attend the
barbecue as guests. Living group
presidents are asked to report the
number they know are attending.
