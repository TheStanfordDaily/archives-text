# Cowell to Give Series Speech
## 
### 
"Autobiography of a Composer"
will be the title of a talk by Henry
Cowell, noted American composer
and author, at the Tuesday Evening
Series tonight at 7:30 in Memorial
Auditorium.
According to critic Virgil Thom
son, Coweil's '"experiments, begun
three decades ago, in har
mony, and instrumental sonorities,
today are the bible of the young."
Cowell, a native of Menlo Park,
was a child prodigy violinist. He
received his musical education at
the University of California and the
Institute of Applied Music in New
York.
