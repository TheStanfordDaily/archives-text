# KRAMER IN PALY TONIGHT
## 
### 
Jack Kramer's professional tennis
troupe will stage a capsule tourna
ment tonight at 8 in the Palo Alto
High School gymansium under the
auspices of the Palo Alto tennis and
sports clubs.
Participants will be Pancho Gon
zales, Pancho Segura, Frank Sedg
man, and Bobby Riggs. Kramer,
himself, will make one doubles ap
pearance.
General admission tickets are
$2.50. They may be obtained from
all Paly sporting goods stores, Roos
Brothers, and from Stanford Coach
Bob Renker.
