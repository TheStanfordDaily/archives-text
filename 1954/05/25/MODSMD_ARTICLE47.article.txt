# Ray Young Voted 'Most Valuable'
## 
### 
Ray Young was selected by his
teammates as Stanford's ''Most Val
uable Player" of the year in a base
ball meeting last week. Young, who
also garnered the CIBA MVP award,
will receive a watch.
Warren Goodrich copped the Kee
ble Camera Shop award given to the
leading Stanford batter and base
runner. Goodrich received a desk
pen set.
Senior first, sacker A1 Talboy also
was honored by his mates as for the
second year in a row he came off
with the "Busher of the Year"
award. Talboy was the recipient of
a brown bottle bat.
