# Physicist to Speak To Independents
## 
### 
The Independent caucus of the
Political Union will meet, today at
4:30 p.m. in Bowman Alumni House
to hear Dr. Paul Kirkpatrick of the
Physics Depart m e n t speak on
"Physics and Politics, 1954."
This is the third and last in a
•-eries of talks sponsored by the In
dependent group to further political
education on the campus.
Following the talk, the Independ
ents will hold an election of officers
for next year. Refreshments will be
served.
