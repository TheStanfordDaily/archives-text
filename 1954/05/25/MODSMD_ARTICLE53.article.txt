# Band Concert Set For Sunday Night
## 
### 
The Stanford University Band, di
rected by Julius Shuchat, will give
a concert Sunday at 8:15 p.m. in
Memorial Auditorium under the
auspices of the Department of Mu
sic. There will be no admission
charge.
The major work on the program
will be the Symphony No. 1 for band
by Ernest Williams.
D. Clark Lindsay, student con
ductor of the band, will lead the
group iti a performance of "Ecos
saise" and "Polonaise" by Beetho
ven, and Jon Elkus, graduate stu
dent in music, will conduct his own
arrangement of the "Prelude and
March of the Synod" from the op
era "Henry VIII" by Saint-Sacns.
