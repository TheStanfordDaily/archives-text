# Upper Division Pre-Reg for Fall To Close Today
## 
### 
Today is the last day for upper di
vision prc-registration for autumn
quarter. Students in this division
must have their official study list
cards, with their major professor's
signature, turned in to the check
out clerk at the information desk,
Registrar's office, Room 111-A, be
fore 5 p.m.
Class lists may he signed between
8 a.m. and 12 noon or 1 and 5 p.m.
today.
Tomorrow lower division pre-reg
istration starts with conferences
with advisers. According to the Reg
istrar's office this extra day has been
added to provide students and ad
visers more time to plan class sched
ules.
The Registrar's offices will be open
for lower division signing between
8 a.m. and 12 noon on Thursday and
Friday and Saturday and between 1
and 5 p.m. on Thursday and Friday.
Those not receiving permits arc to
go to the lower division office, Room
ll-D. Deadline for pre-rcgistration
is noon Saturday.
