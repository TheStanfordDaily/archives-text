# Choir, Orchestra To Give Concert
## 
### 
The Stanford Symphony Orches
tra, Chorus, and Memorial Church
Choir will present a concert Friday
at 8:15 p.m. in Memorial Audito
rium.
The concert will be conducted by
Sandor Salgo. regular conductor of
the Stanford* Symphony and the San
Jose Symphony. Professor Harold
C. Schmidt is the director of the
two Choral groups.
