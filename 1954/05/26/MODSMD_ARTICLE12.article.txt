# 2 Seniors Win UN Internships
## 
### 
Norm Sweet and Barbara Tuerk
have been awarded this year's in
ternships to the United Nations by
the Stanford-United Nations Intern
ship Selection Committee.
Sweet and Miss Tuerk, both grad
uating seniors, will spend eight
weeks in New York with the U.N.
secretariat this summer.
Sweet is former president of the
Institute of International Relations,
and an active member of the Stan
ford Area Young Democrats. Miss
Tuerk is the present staff editor of
The Daily and active in the Political
Union.
