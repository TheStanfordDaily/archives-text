# Pre-Med Group Meets Tonight
## 
###

medical and pre-nursing fraternity,
will conclude a busy year at its final
meeting at 7:30 tonight in Bowman
Alumni House.

the admissions committee of the
University of California School of
Medicine, will speak to the group
on "What the University of Califor
nia Expects of a Pre-Med." In ad
dition, officers for the coming year
will be elected.

ma has been given the opportunity
to watch an operation in the Lane
Hospital amphitheater at 9 a.m. Sat
urday. The first nine members to
call John McFee at DA 3-8631 after
