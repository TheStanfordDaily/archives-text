# Seniors Urged To Join Alums Now
## 
### 
Graduating seniors who are plan
ning to join the Stanford Alumni
Association are urged by the Asso
ciation to join before graduation. If
they do so, they may purchase a
life membership at the special stu
dent rate of $75 rather than the
usual $90.
Students joining before .June 15
will be eligible for higher positions
on the football ticket distribution
lists.
