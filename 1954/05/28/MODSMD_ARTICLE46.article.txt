# Class to Present Spanish One-Acts
## 
### 
The Stanford Spanish Dramatics
class will present three one-act plays
at 8 p.m. tomorrow in Cubberley
Auditorium.
Professor Isabel M. Schovill, who
instructs the class, will be the di
rector.
