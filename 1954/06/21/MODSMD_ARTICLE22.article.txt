# Tryouts Today For Shaw Play
## 
### 
The Stanford Players will hold ,
tryouts for G. B. Shaw's ''You Can
Never Tell" tonight and Thursday
at 7:30 p.m. in the Little Theater.
Four female and six male roles
are open.
The play will be performed July
28-31 and Aug. 4-7. This will be the
Players' only production of the
quarter. F. Cowles Strickland, as
sociate professor of speech and
drama, will direct.
The Little Theater is in the north
west corner of Memorial Audito- !
rium.
