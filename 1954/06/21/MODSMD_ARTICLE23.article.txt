# Open for Meals
## 
### 
Encina Commons, the Cellar, and
the Union Cafeteria will be open
for summer meals.
The Cellar will be open Monday
through Thursday from 7:30 a.m.
until 10 p.m., and Friday and Sat
urday from 7:30 a.m. to 8 p.m.
Hours at the cafeteria will be 12 to
12:45 p.m. and 6 to 6:30 p.m. daily
except Saturday.
Meal tickets for Encina Commons
may be purchased at Stern and
Crothers offices.
