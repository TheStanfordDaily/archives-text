# Cosmo Club Plans Meeting Today at 4
## 
### 
The Cosmo Club, which provides
foreign and American students an
opportunity to exchange views over
cups of coffee, will hold its first get
together of the quarter this after
noon between 4 and 6 in Bowman
Alumni House on Santa Teresa
Street.
Bob Jessen, who is in charge of
the gathering, said, "The Cosmo
Club has been very popular in the
past and the idea has been copied
by other schools, because the Amer
icans and the foreign students have
so much to contribute to each oth
er's knowledge of world conditions."
The club is sponsored by the In
ternational Club of the Stanford In
stitute for International Relations.
