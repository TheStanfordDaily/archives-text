# Men May Still Enroll for Gym
## 
### 
Enrollment is still open for men's
physical education courses in swim
ming, tennis, and golf, according to
C. Myron Sprague, associate direc
tor of physical education.
Elementary tennis is available at
10 a.m. and 2:15 and 4:15 p.m. on
Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays.
An advanced tennis course is given
at 3:15 p.m.
Golf is offered at 11 a.m. and 1
p.m. on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
Elementary swimming is offered at
4:15 p.m. daily, and elementary div
ing is given at 4:15 p.m. on Mon
days, Tuesdays, and Thursdays. In
termediate swimming and advanced
swimming and diving courses are
available throughout the day, and a
life-saving course is offered at 4:15
p.m. on Mondays, Wednesdays, and
Fridays.
