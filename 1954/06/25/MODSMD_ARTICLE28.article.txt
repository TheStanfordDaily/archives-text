# Hours, Regulations Announced For Encina, Women's Pools
## 
### 
Summer quarter hours have been
announced for the Women's Pool
and the Encina Pool.
The Women's Pool will be open
Saturdays from 2 to 5 p.m. Regis
tered women may bring one guest
(husband, mother, or friend or sis
ter of college age). Men registered
for summer quarter may enter only
is i guest or with their wives. Male
members of the faculty and the stall
may be admitted with their wives.
Women members of the faculty
and the staff may be admitted and
may bring their husbands.
On Sunday the Women's Pool will
be open from 3 to 5 p.m. with the
.same rules for admittance. From
1:1") to 2:45 p.m. the pool will be
open to children between the ages
of four and 16 of registered students,
facultv, and staff members, if ae

• companied by an adult
Swimmers must provide suit> and
towels. Women must wear caps.
The women's dressing room will not
be opened Saturday.
The Encina Pool will be open
Monday through Friday from 10
a.m. to 5:30 p.m., Saturday from 10
a.m. to 2 p.m., and Sunday from 1
to 4 p.m.
The pool will be open to sons of
faculty members, if accompanied by
their fathers, on weekdays from
noon to 2 p.m. and on Sundays from
1 to 4 p.m.
