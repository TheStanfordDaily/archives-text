# Intermezzo Series Begins Thursday
## 
### 
"God Needs Men" will be the first
presentation in the Intermezzo sum
mer series of motion pictures Thurs
day at 4 p.m. in Cubberley Audito
rium.
''God Needs Men" is a French film
with English subtitles based on the
QuefTelec novel, "Island of Sinners."
The picture, which has won many
European awards, has become a
subject of considerable controversy
here and abroad.
The series is sponsored by Memo
rial Church, the library, and aca
demic departments of the Univer
sity. The films are open to students,
faculty, and staff members without
charge.
