# Administrative Positions Filled
## 
### 
Two appointments to University
posts were announced recently by
President J. E. Wallace Sterling.
Richard F. O'Brien was appointed
associate general secretary and
James R. Johnson will take over
the position of assistant director of
the Stanford Placement Service.
O'Brien, who has been with the
Placement Service since 1948, will
serve with General Secretary David
S. Jacobson in arranging and regu
lating the University's fund-raising
program.
Johnson will take over O'Brien's
former employment duties, which lie
largely in the field of industrial
placement of graduating students.
