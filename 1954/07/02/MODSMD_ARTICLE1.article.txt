# Guest Appearance
## 
### 

TOURING CHORISTERS—Conductor G. Wallace Woodworth
leads the Harvard Glee Club, the Radcliffe Choral Society, and
several special guests, including Stanford Choir Director Harold
C. Schmidt (fourth from right in first row) in "Cum sancto spiritu"
by Bach. The singers, who are on a transcontinental tour, sang
in Memorial Auditorium Tuesday night.
—Daily photo by Herb Smith
