# GRANT TO MED SCHOOL
## 
### 
A grant of $7,500 for cancer re
search has been awarded to Stanford
School of Medicine by the Ladies'
Auxiliary to the California Veterans
of Foreign Wars, it was announced
recently by Dean Windsor C. Cut
ting.
The grant is half of a $15,000 prize
awarded to the California auxiliaries
by VFW national headquarters for
raising the greatest amount of money
per member in an annual national
cancer fund drive. Over 400 Califor
nia auxiliaries participated.
