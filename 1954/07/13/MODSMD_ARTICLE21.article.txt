# Nationals Picked In All-Star Tilt
## 
### 
The National League, with Phila
delphia's Robin Roberts starting on
the mound, is favored to win its fifth
straight All-Star game in Cleveland's
Municipal Stadium today.
Manager Casey Stengel of the
American League has nominated
Whitey Ford of his own New York
Yanks to face the senior loop stars
in the 21st annual classic.
Neither Stengel nor Brooklyn's
Wait Alston, manager of the Nation
als. indicated who would follow the
starters.
According to the All-Star game
rules, each pitcher can work only
three innings.
The game will be televised in the
Bay Area on KRON at 10:15 a.m.
