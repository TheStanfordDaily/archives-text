# 'Golden Boy' to Be Film Society Show
## 
### 
'"Golden Boy," adapted from Clif
ford Odet's play, will be shown by
the Stanford Film Society Thursday
night at 8 in Cubberley Auditorium.
The movie stars William Holden
and Barbara Stanwyck.
Admission is by membership in
the society only. The cost is 51.65.
