# Dr. Bartky to Speak At Education Lunch
## 
### 
Dr. A. John Bartky, former dean
of the School of Education, will be
the speaker at Tuesday's Phi Delta
Kappa luncheon.
Lunch will be served at noon at
Encina Commons. Tickets may be
purchased for $1 at the Phi Delta
Kappa office. They will be on sale
until noon Mondav.
