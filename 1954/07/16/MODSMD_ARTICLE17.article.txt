# Stern Coffee Hour
## 
### 
Stern Hall will host the campus at
a coffee hour after the Sunday
Flicks from 9:30 to 11 p.m. There
will be dancing in the court and
refreshments will be served.
Students may come date or stag,
and dress is informal.
