# Keller to Speak At PDK Luncheon
## 
### 
Franklin J. Keller, principal of
the Metropolitan Vocational High
School in New York City, will be
the speaker at Tuesday's Phi Delta
Kappa luncheon.
The subject of his talk will be
"Phi Delta Kappa as a Mediator of
Our Culture."
Lunch will be served at noon at
Encina Commons. Tickets may be
purchased at the PDK office in Cub
berley for Si before noon Monday.
