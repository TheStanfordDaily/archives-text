# Med School Given $26,860 Fund Grant
## 
### 
Stanford's Medical School was re
cently awarded a grant of $26,860.92
by the National Fund for Medical
Education, which also made grants
to three other California medical
schools.
The College of Medical Evangel
ists, University of California, Uni
versity of Southern California, and
Stanford split $110,061.74 between
them.
The National Fund also gave
grants to 70 other four-year medical
schools and six two-year basic sci
ence schools. A total of $2,176,904.71
was given.
The Fund grants, which are unre
stricted, are primarily used by the
schools to fill teaching vacancies, to
create new faculty posts, and to ini
tiate courses in areas of recent sci
entific advances.
