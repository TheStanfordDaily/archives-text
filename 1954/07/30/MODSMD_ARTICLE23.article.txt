# Warner's Condition 'Slightly Improved'
## 
### 
Condition of Glenn S. (Pop)
Warner, retired football and
coaching great, was reported as
"slightly improved" yesterday at
Palo Alto Hospital.
Warner re-entered the hospital
last week after an earlier opera
tion for a throat tumor. Doctors
had described his condition at that
time as "serious, but not critical."
Warner, 83, coached at Stanford
from 192-1 to 1932. and invented
the wing- back system of football.
