# History Fraternity Plans Friday Picnic
## 
### 
Phi Alpha Theta, history honor
society, is having a picnic for the
graduate student and faculty mem
bers ol the History Department and
their families Friday at 5 p.m.
The picnic will be held at Flood
P. Ik, located on Bayshore Highway
just north of Palo Alto. Coffee will
be provided.
