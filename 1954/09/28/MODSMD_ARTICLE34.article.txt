# '55 Quad Initiates New Sales Method
## 
### 
A new Quad sales method was
started yesterday to improve stu
dent convenience and sales effi
ciency. The system will afford a
positive estimate for printing, and in
hope of increased sales, the price of
the 1955 Quad has been lowered to
$6.50 without name on cover and
$6.75 with name on cover.
