# 49ers SIGN DUNCAN
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Sept. 27 —(UP)
—Maury Duncan, former San Fran
cisco State quarterback, today was
signed by the San Francisco Forty-
Niners as reserve signal-caller after
second-string quarterback Arnold
Galiffa was found to be injured in
yesterday's win over the Washing
ton Redskins.
