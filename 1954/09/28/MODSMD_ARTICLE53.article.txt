# Mural Managers To Meet Thursday
## 
### 
Intramural Director Elwyn Bugge
announced yesterday that there will
be a meeting of intramural mana
gers in Room 47 of the BAC Thurs
day at 4:30 p.m. All organizations
which expect to participate in the
mural program should send a rep
resentative, Bugge said.
Bowling will be returned to the
mural sports program this fall at the
Village alleys. Each four-man team
must pay a $10 deposit. The cost will
be 10 cents a line. Pinboys will be
provided.
Sign-up lists for fall sports will bo
posted today.
