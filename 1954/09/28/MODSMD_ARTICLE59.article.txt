# Students May Buy Football Tickets
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the Indians
six home football games and the
UCLA game in Los Angeles and the
Big Game at Cal will be sold to
morrow, Thursday, and Friday.
The tickets will be available at
booths in the Pavilion between 8:30
a.m. and 4 p.m.
Prices are $1 for the Illinois and
Navy games, $1.75 for the UCLA
and USC contests, and $2 for the
Big Game. There will be no charge
for the Washington, Washington
State, and San Jose State contests
but admit tickets must be secured in
advance.
Student body cards must be pre
sented to purchase rooters' tickets.
No student tickets will be sold at
the Stadium on the days of the
games.
