# Daily Looking For New Staffers
## 
### 
New freshmen and other stu
dents interested in working on The
Daily are again urged to stop by
the Shack any day this week and
sign up with Pat King, editor;
Walt Williams, managing editor;
or Ruth Waters, staff editor.
A meeting for all tryoutees has
been scheduled for Friday after
noon at At this meeting Daily
policy, style, and the tryout pro
gram will he explained by Editorial
Board members.
Openings are available for re
porters, feature writers, and pho
tographers. Sportswriters are also
needed, and may sign up with Don
Webster, sports editor.
