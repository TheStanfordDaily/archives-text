# Agnes Moorehead To Star Locally
## 
### 
Agnes Moorehead will star in a
show produced by Paul Gregory
and directed by Charles Laughton,
tonight at 8:30 in Roosevelt Jr. High
School auditorium, San Jose.
The star of radio, screen, and
theater will present a program of
works from the literary men of our
time such as Ring Lardner, Marcel
Proust, and Rupert Brooke, as well
as selections from Shakespeare, de
Maupassant, and readings from the
Bible.
Tickets may be obtained at the
Hotel Vendome, IGI West Santa
Clara Street, in San Jose, CYpress
2-G624, or at the door this evening.
Popular prices prevail.
