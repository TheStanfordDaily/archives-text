# Library Hours Set
## 
### 
The Stanford Library announced
recently a change in opening hours
for the fall quarter. Monday through
Friday, the Library will open at 7:45
a.m. and will close at 10 p.m.
Saturday hours will be 7:45 a.m. to
4 p.m., and Sunday hours will be 1
to 5 p.m. The Reserve Book Room
will remain open until 10 p.m. on
both Saturday and Sunday.
