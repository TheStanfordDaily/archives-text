# Encina to Host Campus at Rally
## 
### 
Encina's front lawn will be the
scene of the first football rally of
the year tonight at 7:30 in prepara
tion for tomorrow's game with Illi
nois.
There will be yells, band music,
and a bevy of beautiful pom-pom
girls, according to rally chairman
Curt Pendergrass. Head yell leader
Ed Mahoney and his assistants will
handle the yells, while the pom-pom
girls cavort to the strains of the
Stanford band.
Head varsity football coach Chuck
Taylor is scheduled to put in an ap
pearance as is varsity captain Win
Wedge.
The rally will last about half an
hour.
