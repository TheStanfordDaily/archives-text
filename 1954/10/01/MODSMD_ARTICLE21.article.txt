# Wesley Foundation To Hold Banquet
## 
### 
The Wesley Foundation will hold
a banquet this Sunday at 6 p.m. for
all Methodist preference students.
Dr. Melvin E. Wheatley will speak
on "The 'You' in the University"
with Dean H. Donald Winbigler as
master of ceremonies.
There is no charge for the dinner
and program and all Methodist pref
erence students are invited to attend.
