# Medical Test Set for Nov,1
## 
### 
The Medical College Admission
Test, sponsored by the Association
of American Medical Colleges, will
be given on Nov. 1. Application
blanks and copies of the Bulletin of
Information may be obtained from
the Medical Admissions Office. Rooiji
112 D.
Applications for the test must be
received at the Princeton office of
the Educational Testing Service on
or before Oct. 18. Only applicants
for admission to classes beginning
the study of medicine in the fall of
1955 who did not take the test in
May should take it at this time.
Students wishing to apply to the
Stanford School of Medicine are
urged to submit applications as soon
as possible. Application blanks and
information may be obtained at
Room 112 D.
No applications will be considered
until the results of the Medical Col
lege Admission Test are on file in
the Admissions Office. Applications
which are complete on Nov. 15 will
be given consideration. The final
closing date is Dec. 15.
