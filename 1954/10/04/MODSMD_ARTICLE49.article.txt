# Rome School Gives Art, Study Grants
## 
### 
The American Academy in Rome
is again offering a limited number
of fellowships for mature students
and artists capable of doing inde
pendent work.
Applications and submissions of
work, in the form prescribed, must
be received at the Academy's New
York office before Jan. 1, 1955. Re
quests for details should be ad
dressed to the Executive Secretary,
American Academy in Rome, 101
Park Avenue, New York 17, N.Y.
