# Friday Is Deadline For Reg Cards, Room Payment
## 
### 
Student registration study cards
and class cards may be returned to
the Registrar's Office throughout this
week. Deadline for botli is 5 p.m.
Friday.
Registrar Harvey Hall has empa
sized that students should file the
cards as soon as possible, to avoid
last minute lines and possible fines.
A fine of $2 will be charged for
cards filed next week; $10 will be
charged thereafter.
The same Friday deadline applies
for payment, of room arid board bills.
Payment will be accepted at the
Cashier's office. Building 1, Inner
Quad between 8 a.m. and 5 p.m. Vil
lage residents may submit payment
at the Village Cashier's office be
tween the hours of 9:30 a.m. and
12:30 p.m. or 1:30 to 3:30 p.m.
Checks for room and board bills
may be mailed to the Cashier's of
fice. If postmarked before Oct. 8
(Friday), no fine will be charged.
