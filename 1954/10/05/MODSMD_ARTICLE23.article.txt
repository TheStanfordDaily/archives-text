# Soph Excom Tryouts Begin
## 
### 
Interviews for sophomore class ex
com will be conducted today and
tomorrow by Dave Jenks, sopho
more president, and Pat Schulze,
secretary, on the third floor of the
women's clubhouse.
All sophomores who want to try
out for sophomore excom may pick
up an application blank at the ASSU
office. Interviews are scheduled from
1 to 3 p.m. and 4 to 6 p.m. today, and
4:45 to 6 p.m. tomorrow.
Sophomore class officers are look
ing for students with ideas and spe
cial abilities in journalism, art, sec
retarial work, entertainment and
publicity. They emphasize that ev
eryone who applies will be given a
job in aiding class activities.
