# GOP Rally-Dance Planned for Friday
## 
### 
The Young Republicans of the
Stanford Area will hold a rally
dance at 8 p.m. on Friday at Bay
Meadows in honor of President Ei
senhower's birthday.
Governor Goodwin J. Knight, Don
Eastvold, Attorney General of Wash
ington, and Congressman J. Arthur
Younger of San Mateo County will
address the group. Operatic star
Dun Kirk and John Noel's four
piece combo will entertain.
Tickets to the rally cost $1, al
though members will be admitted
free.
Freshmen will receive invitations
by mail and may mail their remit
tances. Other students may contact
Jack Pike, Alpha Delt, or Bob
Sprinkle, Stern.
