# Pre-med Students To Hold Meeting
## 
### 
The fall quarter meeting of
Sigma Pni Sigma, Stanford pre
medical and pre-nursing honorary
fraternity, will be held at 7:30 p.m.
Wedne-day in Koom 53, Cubberley
Auditorium. This will be a business
meeting to discuss a possible party
and plans for the quarter.
This year Sigma Phi Sigma will
again have a busy program which
will center around speaker meetings
about once a month, student-faculty
panel discussions on subjects of in
terest to pre - meds, and hospital
tours, according to Jerry Severin,
president.
Organization dues are $1. Refresh
ments will be served.
