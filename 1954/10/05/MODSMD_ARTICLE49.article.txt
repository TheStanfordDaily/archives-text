# Rules Announced In Poetry Contest
## 
### 
Stanford students are invited to
submit original verse for possible
publication in the Twelfth Annual
Anthology of College Poetry.
Manuscripts must be typed or
written in ink on one side of a sheet.
The student's home address, name of
college, and college address must
appear on each manuscript. Closing
date for submission of entries is
Nov. 5, and manuscripts should be
sent to Dennis Hartman. Secretary,
National Poetry Association, 3210
Selby Avenue, Los Angeles 34.
