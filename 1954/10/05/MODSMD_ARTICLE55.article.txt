# 'Theatre Matinee' Series to Resume
## 
### 
The Department of Speech and
Drama will resume its weekly "The
atre Matinee" tomorrow at 4:15 p.m.
in the Little Theater.
Tomorrow's program will be a
movie, "Volpone," which is in
French with English subtitles.
"Theatre Matinee" is a weekly
program of plays, lectures, readings,
and demonstrations. It is presented
every Wednesday at 4:15, and there
is no admission charge.
