# 'King's Men' To Sing Here
## 
### 
"The King's Men," famed quartet
of the Fibber McGee and Molly
program, will sing at Stanford Oct.
22 in a student-sponsored concert to
finance a scholarship for a student
from the Free University of Berlin
The quartet will appear in Memo
rial Hall at 8:30 p.m. Tickets, priced
at $2.50, $2. $1.50, and $1, are on
sale at the Concert Series box office
at the Stanford Union from 1 to 5
p.m., Mondays though Fridays, and
at the Peninsula Box Office, 315
University Avenue, Palo Alto.
Similar scholarships were ar
ranged by the Berlin Affiliation
Committee of the lIR last year and
for the current year.
