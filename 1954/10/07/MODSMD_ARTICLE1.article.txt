# Street Scene

MUSIC ON THE ROW—The AWS will hold a street dance and
open house Saturday right after the Navy game. Shown here
holding a poster advertising the dance and open house are,
left to right, Melissa Woodring, Sally Cherry, Mary Ann Gold
man, and Helen Fischer. The traditional open house will in
clude music by Spike Lynch and his band, and Dave Guard
entertaining on his guitar. Refreshments, provided by the Un
ion Units, Storey and Lathrop, will be served on the lawns in
front of Lathrop and Storey. —Daily photo by Logan Pazdral
