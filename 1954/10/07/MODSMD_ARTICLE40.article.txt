# Sports Briefs
## 
### 
(Compiled from the United Press)

In the world of sports yesterday,
Charley Norkus scored a seventh -
round technical knockout over ex
professional grid star Charley Pow
ell to put the brakes on the latter's
fabulous rise in the nation's heavy
weight ranks.
Meanwhile, Vic Seixas and Tony
Trabert lived up to their role as
favorites as they paced a field of
seeded stars through the second
round of the Pan American Tennis
Championships in Mexico City.
The San Francisco Forty-Niners
received good news when it was
learned that their mighty rookie
tackle of 1952, Bob Toneff was being
released from the Army and would
join the club on Sunday.
