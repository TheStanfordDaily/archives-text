# FRQSH PICTURE SIGN-UP
## 
### 
All frosh from Encina and the
Village are asked to sign up for
Quad and Frosh Book pictures now.
Appointments may be secured by
signing on the Encina lobby bulletin
board.
Encina frosh pictures will be taken
on Monday and Wednesday, Oct. 11
and 13. Village frosh pictures will
be taken next Tuesday and Thurs
day. Coats and ties are required.
