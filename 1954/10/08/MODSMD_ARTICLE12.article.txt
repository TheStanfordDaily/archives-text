# 'Don't Drive to Game' Advises Chief Davis
## 
### 
"Walk, don't drive to the game."
This is an appeal made by Chief
Goi'don Davis to all students who
plan to attend the Stanford-Navy
game Saturday.
"You can walk home a lot
quicker, and you won't he caught
in the crush of cars," Davis said.
"And if you don't drive, you will
help relieve traffic congestion."
Davis also urges students to
walk to classes to avoid the park
ing problem and "eliminate need
lessly driven miles."
