# KUCHEL TO SPEAK
## 
### 
U.S. Senator Thomas Kuchel will
speak at a non-partisan luncheon
Monday noon at Rickey's.
Tickets for the luncheon are avail
able from Jack Pike at the Alpha
Delta Phi house.
A car caravan to welcome Senator
Kuchel has been arranged, and will
leave the Stanford Post OHice at
11:30 Monday morning.
