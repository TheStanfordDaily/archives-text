# Today: Study Cards, Room, Board Bills Due
## 
###

arc due at the Registrar's Office
at 5 p.m. today. A card should he
turned in for every approved
course which the student is taking
for credit.
Students returning cards after
the deadline will he assessed late
fees of $2 for the first week and
Sift thereafter.

student room and hoard hills is
today at 5 p.m. Bills may be paid
at the CashicFs Office, Inner
Quad, which will he open between
K a.m. and 5 p.m. Payment by
Village residents may be made at
the Village Cashier's Office be
tween 9:30 a.m. and 12:30 p.m. or
from 1:30 to 3:30 p.m.
