# Gaieties to End Tryouts Tonight
## 
### 
The final session of Big Game Gai
eties tryouts will take place tonight
from 7 to 10 in the basement of
Memorial Auditorium, according to
Ram's Head Gaieties director Milton
Cohen.
Tryoutees will be asked to act,
sing, and dance, and are requested
to bring their own music. However,
an accompanist will be available.
The dance director also asks that
women tryoutees wear full skirts.
Besides the cast auditions, tryouts
will be held for students interested
in working on props, lighting, shift
crews, set construction, makeup,
sound, and costumes.
The Daily will publish a list of
the new Gaieties cast in Monday's
paper.
