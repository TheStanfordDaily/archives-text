# Newman Club Fall Activities to Begin
## 
### 
The Stanford Newman Club, un
der the direction of President Ralph
Moricone, will begin fall quarter
activities this Sunday with a Com
munion breakfast at 9:15 a.m. in the
Union dining room.
Father Robert Giguiere, assistant
at St. Ann's parish, will give an in
spirational talk.
Tickets will be on sale at the door,
priced at $1.35 for Newman Club
members, $1.50 for non-members.
Father John Tierney, chaplain of
St. Ann's, has announced that Mass
times for the school year will be
8 a.m., 9 a.m., 11 a.m., and 12:15 p.m.
Rides leave the Post Office 15 min
utes before Mass begins.
