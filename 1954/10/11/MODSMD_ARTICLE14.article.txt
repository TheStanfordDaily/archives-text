# Cinerama Cancels Stanford Showing
## 
### 
ASSU Vice - President Dianne
Goldman and Concert Series Mana
ger John Tirrell announced yester
day that the exclusive Stanford
showing of "This Is Cinerama,"
which was listed on the social cal
endar for Oct. 29, has been canceled.
Due to new policy, Cinerama is
not allowed to offer special rates to
university students and is not able
to present the originally scheduled
performance.
Other events for the date of Oct.
20 will remain as listed on Social
Calendar.
