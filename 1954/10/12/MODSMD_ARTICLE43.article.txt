# Free X-Ray Unit To Visit Stanford
## 
### 
Tlie Santa Clara mobile X-ray
unit will be on campus tomorrow for
free X-rays of all Stanford faculty
and students.
The unit will be located on the
south side of the Bookstore and will
be open from 10 a.m. to 'noon and
from 1 to 1:30 p.m.
All persons over 15 years of age
are eligible to be X-rayed by the
unit.
