# STUDENT RATES
## Symphony Tickets Sell At Nitery 
### 
Special rate tickets for the
Thursday evening student se
ries of concerts by the San Fran
cisco Symphony Orchestra go
on sale at the Nitery today from
12:30 to 4:30 p.m. Sales will
continue tomorrow and Thurs
day afternoons
Prices for season tickets to all 18
concerts have been set at $9, first
three rows of the orchestra; $17, bal
cony; and $22.50, remainder of the
orchestra, balcony circle, dress cir
cle, and grand tier. This is a saving
of 50 per cent over regular box office
prices.
This season will be the first for the
San Francisco Symphony's perma
nent conductor, Enrique Jorda, who
was the conductor of the Cape Town
Symphony Orchestra in South Af
rica before coming here.
The student series will open on
Thursday evening, Nov. 11. World
famous guitarist Andres Segovia
will be the first guest soloist of the
season on Nov. 25. He will be fol
lowed by Grant Johannesen and
Gina Bachauer, pianists, on Dec. 2
and Dec. 16, respectively.
PERFORMERS LISTED
Performers during the winter will
feature Joseph Szigeti on Jan. 13,
Clifford Curzon on Jan. 20, Leon
Fleisher on Feb. 3, Zino Frances
catti on Feb. 17, Rudolph Firkusny
on Feb. 24, Dame Myra Hess on
March 10, and Alexander Brailow
sky on MaYch 17. Lucine Amara,
Yehudi Menuhin, and Donna Turner
Smith will be guest soloists during
the early weeks of spring quarter.
Guest conductors this year will be
Bruno Walter, Thomas Scherman,
and Earl Murray.
