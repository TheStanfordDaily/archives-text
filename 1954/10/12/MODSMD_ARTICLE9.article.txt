# Stanford to Host Recreation Course
## 
### 
A new type of intensive study pro
gram for professional workers in the
recreation field will be offered at
Stanford, it was announced today.
Called the In-Service Training In
stitute on Administration of Recre
ation and Park Services, the session
will be sponsored jointly by the Uni
versity and the State of California
Recreation Commission. It will be
held Nov. 28 through Dec. 4.
Courses will be taught by a com
bined faculty of university instruc
tors and experienced professional
public recreation administrators.
Application to attend may be made
to Professor Nixon at the School of
Education, Stanford University.
