# Stevenson Will Speak At San Francisco Rally
## 
### 
Adlai E. Stevenson, in his only
Northern California appearance, will
speak at a Democratic rally at the
Civic Auditorium in San Francisco
Saturday.
The rally will begin at 8 p.m.
Tickets are $1. Rides will leave the
post officc at 6:15 and 6:30 p.m.
Contact Ruth Wolpe at Union
Residence, DA 3-9043, for tickets.
