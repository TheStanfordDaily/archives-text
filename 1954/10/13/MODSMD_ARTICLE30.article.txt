# Comedy Play Scheduled For Theater Matinee
## 
### 
"A Sunny Day," a one-act com
edy by the Quintero brothers, will
be featured at this afternoon's The
ater Matinee in the Little Theater at
4:15.
The cast includes Dolly Ashley,
Asher Wilson, Nancy McDonald,
Marianne Richardson, Douglas Cook,
and Ben Etheridge. Asher Wilson is
the director.
Admission is free to the public.
