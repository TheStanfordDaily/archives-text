# Alumni to Host Post-Game Party
## 
### 
The Stanford Junior Alumni of
Southern California will give a
cocktail party Saturday immediately
following the Stanford-UCLA foot
ball game, in the Renaissance Koom
of the Biltmore Hotel in downtown
Los Angeles.
All Stanford alumni and friends
are invited, with a special invitation
to all Stanford students. ID's will
be checked at the door.
There will be no admission charge.
The party will end at 10 p.m.
