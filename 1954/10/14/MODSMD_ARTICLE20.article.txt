# Poli Union GOP's Discuss Fall Plans
## 
### 
Publicity, committee organization,
and finances were discussed last
night, by the Republican caucus of
the Political Union in its first meet
ing of the quarter.
Dues for the caucus were debated
by the meeting. A motion setting
them at 50 cents for the 1954 fall
quarter only was passed.
Sign-ups for the four caucus com
mittees, finance, publicity, research,
and press, were conducted.
