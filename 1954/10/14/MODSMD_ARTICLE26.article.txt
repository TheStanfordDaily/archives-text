# 15th Century Art Is Lecture Topic
## 
### 
Genevieve Thibault, Countess of
Chamibure, will lecture on "The
Concert in 15th Century Art" to
morrow at 4:30 p.m. in Cubberley
Auditorium.
The talk, sponsored by the Music
Department, will be illustrated with
slides and recordings. It will be open
to the public without charge.
