# Rev. Miller Will Speak Sunday
## 
### 
The Rev. Alexander Miller, lec
turer in religion at Stanford, will be
the preacher in Memorial Church
Sunday at 11 a.m. He will speak on
"True Doubt and False Certainty."
David H. Romeis will assist in the
service of worship.
Miller is the author of several
books, including "The Christian Sig
nificance of Karl Marx" and "Chris
tianity and My Job."
Professor Harold C. Schmidt will
lead the Memorial Church Choir in
two anthems, and University Organ
ist Herbert B. Nanney will play for
the service.
Immediately following the service
a coffee hour will be held in Bow
man Alumni House. It is sponsored
by the YWCA and open to all.
