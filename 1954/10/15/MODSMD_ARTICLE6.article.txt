# Greyhound Bus Strike Averted
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 14 (UP) —
A threatened bus drivers' strike
which would have tied up Pacific
Greyhound operations in seven
Western states was averted early to
day with a last-minute agreement
between company aud AFL union
negotiators.
The tentative agreement, was an
nounced at 12:04 a.m.—three min
utes after the deadline for a walk
out by some 3,000 drivers and sta
tion operators in California. Oregon,
Arizona, Utah, New Mexico, Nevada,
and part of Texas.
