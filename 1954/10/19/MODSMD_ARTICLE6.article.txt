# Auden Speaks Here Tonight
## 
### 
W. H. Auden, one of the world's
foremost contemporary poets, will
speak tonight at 7:30 in Memorial
Auditorium. The subject of his talk
will be "The Hero in Modern Po
etry."
Auden will illustrate the lecture
with readings from his poems, many
of which are concerned with man as
a social creature. Auden has con-,
cerned himself with interpreting
man's political and social problems
in psychological terms.
Professor Yvor Winters, poet and
member of the Stanford English De
partment, will introduce the speaker.
The lecture is open to the public
free of charge, and is given under
the auspices of the Committee on
Public Exercises.
