# Bible Study Class To Begin Monday
## 
### 
Jacque Beaumont, director of the
Federation of Protestant Students in
Paris, will conduct a Bible study
group for Stanford students begin
ning Monday. Sponsored by the
Presbyterian Church of the United
States, he is currently directing a
study group at the University of
California.
An open house will be held in the
"Y" lounge on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse at 4 p.m. Mon
day.
The study group will thereafter
meet each Monday from 12 to 1 p.m.
in the "Y" lounge. Students plan
ning to attend should contact the
"Y" office this afternoon or Monday
morning. Lunch will be served for
30 cents.
