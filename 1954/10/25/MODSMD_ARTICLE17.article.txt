# Tickets Sell For Comedy
## 
### 
Tickets for the Stanford Players'
production of Thornton Wilder'.s
fantastic comedy, "The Skin of Out'
Teeth."' continue on sale today for
$1 at the Memorial Auditorium box
office from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m. The
show opens Wednesday night at 8:15
in the Little Theater. It will run
through Saturday this week and
Wednesday through Saturday next
week.
Mr. and Mrs. Antrobus will be
played by George Willcy and Pris
ciila Oliver.
Ruth Binns has the role of Sabina.
These three characters In Wilder's
fable represent Man, his Wife, and
the Eternal Female. The play is a
quick look at the history of the
world in three acts.
David Whorf and Judy Doty will
play the Antrobus childrert.
Nancy Mac Donald plays the for
tune teller who can tell the future
but cannot tell the past. The dino
saur and mammoth who get lost in
the ice age will be played by Astrid
Wilsrud and William Davis.
Director for the production is F.
Cowles Strickland.
