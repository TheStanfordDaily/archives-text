# Nixon Will Speak This Friday Night
## 
### 
Vice-President Richard Nixon will
give a major policy address Friday at
the San Mateo High School audi
torium at 8 p.m.
The speech, given under the
auspices of the "Younger for Con
gress" group of San Mateo County
will be televised coast to coast on
Saturday night.
The Young Republicans of Iho
Stanford Area are forming a rides
caravan in the Cubherlcy parking
lot at 7 p.m. Admission to the rally
is free.
