# YWCA to Discuss Oppenheimer Case
## 
###

Own Ethics or Those of His Employ
er: The Oppenheimer Case" will be
today's topic at the "Y" Public Af
fairs Luncheon at noon on the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
Tom Bartlett of the Political Sci
ence Department will moderate the
discussion.
