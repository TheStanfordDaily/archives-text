# Roble Names 2 Spokesmen
## 
### 
Holly Roth and Lee Clinch were
chosen ASSU representative and
representative to AWS respectively
from Roble in run-off elections held
last Tuesday.
Nearly all Roble girls turned out
to vote, according to Miss Margaret
Trainor, Roble director.
As ASSU representative, Miss
Roth, who is from Milwaukee, is an
ex-officio member of ASSU and will
serve as a liaison between the girls
of Roble and the ASSU.
Miss Clinch is from Dallas, Texas.
She will attend AWS meetings and
keep Roble . informed about AWS
functions.
