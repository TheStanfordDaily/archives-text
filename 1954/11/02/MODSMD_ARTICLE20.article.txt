# Biz School Alumni Will Hold Dinner
## 
### 
The annual Business School Stu
dent-Alumni Dinner will be held to
morrow night at Rickey's. H. Taylor
Perry, graduate of the Biz School
Class of '29 and now vice-president
of the Bank of America, will be
guest speaker.
Tickets are on sale at the School's
library counter for $2.50 and in
clude dinner, tax, and tip. Students
are urged to bring their wives.
