# NSA Will Appoint Travel Director
## 
### 
The regional office of the National
Students Association has announced
that a regional travel director will
be selected from one of the NSA
member schools.
Supplied with information from
the regional office, he would assist in
co-ordinating intercollegiate travel
programs and present NSA travel
opportunities to his and other
schools.
The regional travel chairman may
be offered an expense-paid job as
tour leader or NSA representative
during the summer, 1955. travel pro
gram in Europe.
Students interested in applying
for this position may contact A 1
Ivey at 312-B, Stanford Village.
