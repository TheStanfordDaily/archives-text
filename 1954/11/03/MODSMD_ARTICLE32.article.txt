# Sterling Hall Officers
## 
### 
New officers have been elected
for Sterling Hall, graduate women's
residence. The new officers are Bet
ty Bigelow, president; Beverly Ray,
vice-president; Armistre Ametjlan,
secretary; Gay Dirstine, treasurer;
Jean Embach, social chairman;
Elaine Smith, publicity; and Harriet
Kirby, WAA chairman.
