# Eating Clubs Plan Mixer-Rally Dance
## 
### 
The arrangements have been made
and the date set for the all-campus
stag or drag Rally Dance, to be held
Friday. The open mixer is sponsored
by the Stanford eating clubs, and
will directly follow the homecoming
rally in Frost Amphitheater.
According to Bill Allen, Rally
Dance chairman, the dance will be
held in the eating club lounge from
9 p.m. to 1 a.m. Music will be pro
vided by Spike Lynch and the Star
dusters. Refreshments will be served.
No admission will be charged.
