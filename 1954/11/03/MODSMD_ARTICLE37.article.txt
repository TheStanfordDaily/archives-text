# Stern Hall to Hold All-Campus Dance
## 
### 
Stern Hall's annual all - campus
Homecoming Dance will be held this
Saturday night from 9 to 1. Tickets
are now on sale for $1 at the ASSU
office, in front of the Cellar, and
in the Stern Hall office.
Ram's Head will provide enter
tainment, and Wayne Walker of San
Jose will play for the dance, which
will be in the dining room of the
hall.
Chairman of the dance is Mike
Stanton.
