# Australian Scholar To Speak Over KZSU
## 
### 
David Penny, ASSU Foreign
Scholar from Australia, will be a
guest on "Foreign Focus," a radio
program presented by the Institute
of International Relations tonight
from 9 till 9:25 over KZSU.
John Florida will moderate the
program, and Helen Dewar and
Frank Smolar will serve as panel
members.
