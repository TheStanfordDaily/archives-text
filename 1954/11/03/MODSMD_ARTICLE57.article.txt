# Hymn Festival Is Slated for Nov. 14
## 
### 
Stanford Memorial Church and six
Palo Alto churches will join in a
Christian Hymn Festival Nov. 14 at
4 p.m. in the Stanford church.
Combined choirs of the churches
will present a program of Bach and
Vaughan Williams compositions. The
150 voices will be under the direc
tion of Harold C. Schmidt, associate
professor of music and director of
choral music at Stanford. The pub
lic is invited.
