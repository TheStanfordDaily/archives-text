# Stanford to Host English Teachers
## 
### 
Almost 250 members of the central
section of the California Association
of Teachers of English will confer
at Stanford Saturday.
They will register at 9 a.m. for the
half-day session in Cubberley Au
ditorium. Three talks and a sym
posium will be on the program.
• Professor William J. Iverson of
the Stanford School of Education will
speak on developmental reading.
