# Topic of New Book Is Food Problems
## 
### 
The seventh book In the Food
Research Institute series entitled
"Food, Agriculture, and World War
II" was published recently by the
Stanford Press. The title is "Food
and Agriculture in Britain, 1939-45."
R. J. Hammond, the author, ap
praises in his book the actions taken
by the government of the United
Kingdom in meeting the tremendous
problems of food supply and agri
cultural production which confront
ed it during the war.
Hammond's book is based on the
same confidential material that he
used in the preparation of the official
United Kingdom history of food pol
icy and administration during the
war.
Other Food Research Institute
publications in the series include
studies on wartime food administra
tion in India, Japan, Australia, and
Occupied Europe.
