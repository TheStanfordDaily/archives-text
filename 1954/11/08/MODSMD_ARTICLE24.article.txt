# Travelers Group To Hold Reunion
## 
### 
Members of the Experiment in
International Living will compare
the experiences they had this sum
mer in foreign countries at a re
union dinner ;it Carlton's Restaurant
today.
All former Experimenters and
those interested in traveling with
the Experiment are invited. Cars
will leave the Cellar at 6:15 p.m.
Experimenters go abroad in
groups of 10, either mixed or all
girl, under the guidance of a trained
leader. They live for a month with
foreign families in separate homes,
then travel informally through the
country with their youni' hosts.
