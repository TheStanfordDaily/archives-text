# Symphony Season Opens Thursday
## 
### 
The San Francisco Symphony Or
chestra, under permanent conductor
Enrique Jorda, opens its 43rd season
Thursday, Friday, and Saturday in
the War Memorial Opera House.
In accordance with the tradition of
past years, the opening concerts will
be entirely orchestral. The program
will include Mendelssohn's Overture
to "Fingal's Cave," Beethoven's
Symphony No. 6 ("Pastoral"), sym
phony fragments from Wagner's
"Die Meistersin'ger," and Paul Hin
demith's Ballet Music from "Noblis
slima Visione."
