# One-Act Scheduled For Matinee Show
## 
### 
A one-act play by Thornton Wil
der, "Hie Long Christmas Dinner,"
will be featured on tomorrow after
noon's Theater Matinee. The show
is at 4:15 in the Little Theater.
Theater Matinee is a weekly pro
gram presented by the Department
of Speech and Drama and is open
to the public without charge.
