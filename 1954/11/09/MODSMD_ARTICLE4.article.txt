# Mothers' Club Tea Will Be Held Today
## 
### 
The Mothers' Club annual Chrys
anthemum Tea will 'be held from 2
to 1:30 at the Stanford Museum.
Proceeds will be used to maintain
the two campus rest homes.
Entertainment, at the tea will in
clude the Stanford Chorale, directed
by Harold C. Schmidt, with harpist
Marjoric Trammel.
