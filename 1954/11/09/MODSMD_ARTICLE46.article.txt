# Movie to Feature Prehistoric Caves
## 
### 
A color film, "The Caves of Las
caux," concerning the Stone Age
paintings at Lascaux, France, will
be shown tomorrow afternoon at
4:15 in Cubberley Auditorium by
Professor Beat Gerow of the An
thropology Department. The public
is invited.
The recently discovered Lascaux
paintings belong to the Aurignacian
period, between 25,000 and 100,000
years ago.
