# Harlan Nominated To Supreme Court
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 8 (UP) -
John Marshall Harlan, a New York
federal circuit, judge and a Repub
lican, has been chosen by President
Eisenhower to succeed the late Rob
ert H. Jackson on the Supreme
Court bench, the While House an
nounced today.
Mr. Eisenhower, turning to the
courts for his second appointment
