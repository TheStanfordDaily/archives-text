# Bowman House Wing Planned
## 
### 
Work is under way on a $51,000
addition to Bowman Alumni House,
it was announced yesterday by Wil
liam H. Stark of Fresno, president of
the Stanford Alumni Association.
The 25x00-foot, wing, housing a
committee loom, alumni magazine
office, women's lounge, and a stor
age-workroom, will be completed in
approximately four months.
The new addition will be joined
to Bowman House by a glassed-in
passageway, The committee room
will be paneled in redwood and will
have sliding glass doors opening onto
the patio.
