# Consul to Discuss 'Germany's Future'
## 
### 
Dr. Eberhard Hesse, vice-consul
of the German consulate in San
Francisco, will speak on campus to
day at 4:15 p.m. on the first floor of
the Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Hesse will speak briefly on
"Germany's Future," then answer
questions the audience may have.
He will discuss effects of incorpora
tion of Germany into NATO and
possibilities of ratification of the
Paris Agreement by the German
Parliament.
This is the second in a scries of
consular visits presented by the In
stitute of International Relations.
