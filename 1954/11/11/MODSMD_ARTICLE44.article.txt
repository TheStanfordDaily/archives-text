# Grad Association To Interview Today
## 
### 
The Graduate Students' Associa
tion will hold interviews today and
tomorrow for the positions of pub
licity director and reporter to The
Daily, graduate prc-registration ori
entation chairman, and organization
co-director.
Interviews will be held today from
noon to 1:30 p.m. and tomorrow
from 4 to 5 p.m. in the Education
lounge, Cubberley Auditorium.
Students who cannot be present
at these times are asked to call Gor
don Linden, UL 1-1450, between 6
and 8 p.m. or leave a note at the
Law School office.
