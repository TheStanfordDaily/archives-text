# Dixon-Yates Contract
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 14 (UP)—
Senator Estes Kefauver (D., Tenn.)
said today the Dixon-Yates contract
will be a "running sore . . . for years
to come" and will cost President Ei
senhower bipartisan support.
