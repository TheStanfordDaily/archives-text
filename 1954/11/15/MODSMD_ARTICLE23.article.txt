# 'Cherry Orchard' Casting Continues
## 
### 
The regular weekly tryouts of the
Stanford Players this afternoon in
the Little Theater from 4:30 to 5:30
will be devoted to casting the second
act of Chekov's "The Cherry Or
chard."
The production, directed by F.
Cowles Strickland, will be presented
on the Theater Matinee program
early in winter quarter.
Tryouts are open to all Stanford
students, faculty, and staff.
