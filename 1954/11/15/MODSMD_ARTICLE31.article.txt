# Stanford Is Second In Cross-country
## 
### 
San Jose State won five out of the
first six places to capture a three
way cross-country meet at San Jose
last weekend. The victors scored 17
points to 41) for Stanford and 54 for
California. Stanford defeated Cal
24-31 in dual meet competition.
Spartan Don Hubbard set a new
record over the four-mile course.
