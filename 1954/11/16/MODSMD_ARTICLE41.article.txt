# Two Card Polo Players Have National Rating
## 
### 
Stanford poloists Bill Sampson
and Juan Reynal have been rated
at two and one goals respectively in
the 1954-55 handicaps released by
the Indoor Polo Association of
America last week.
Sampson was one of three college
players rated as high as two goals,
while only three other players be
sides Reynal were rated at a single
goal. Only seven college players
were rated.
