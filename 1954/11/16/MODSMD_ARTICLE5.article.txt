# Art Gallery Shows Varied Exhibitions
## 
### 
A show of photographs and plans
of new homes designed by 12 lead
ing American architects and a
photographic series, "The Art of
Games," will be opening items in the
Stanford Art Gallery today.
Called "New American Houses,
1952-1953," the exhibit, contains
work by Richard Neutra, Mise Van
der Rohe, Frank Lloyd Wright, and
others.
The sport display runs from pho
tographs of ancient murals to mod
em news pictures.
The exhibits will both run through
Nov. 28. The gallery, to which ad
mission is free, is open Tuesdays
through Saturdays, 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.,
and Sundays, noon to 4 p.m.
