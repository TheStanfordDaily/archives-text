# Ed School Dean To Appear on TV
## 
### 
Dr. I. James Quillen, Dean of
the School of Education, will take
part in the television show, "What's
Your Opinion?" this evening at 6
on station KPIX.
The panel discussion, in which
Dean Quillen and three others will
take part, is to be based on the ques
tion, "Is our family life disintegrat
ing?"
According to the invitational let
ter received by Dr. Quillen, the pro
gram is spontaneous and unre
hearsed. It is sponsored by the San
Francisco Junior Chamber of Com
merce. KPIX, and the Citizens' Ad
visory Board as an educational pub
lic service.
