# Alumni Plan Celebrations
## 
### 
Stanford alumni or many classes
will be guests at 11 separate reun
ions scheduled at various hotels in
San Francisco tomorrow night.
President Wallace Sterling, escorted
by the Stanford Band, plans to drop
in on each of the reunion parties
sometime during the course of the
evening.
The classes which have graduated
at five-year intervals since 1914, the
Stanford Medical School Alumni,
and the Stanford Junior Alumni As
sociation (which includes those who
have graduated within the past 10
years) are those which will get to
gether tomorrow evening. The Class
of 1929 will celebrate its twenty-fifth
anniversary. '
