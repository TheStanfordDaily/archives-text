# Frosh May Apply For Class Excom
## 
### 
Application blanks for Freshman
Class Excom may be picked up to
day, tomorrow, and Wednesday in
the ASSU lobby. Applications must
be turned in not later than 5 p.m.
Wednesday.
According to Frosh President Paul
Williams, each applicant will be
given a five-minute interview be
ginning on Monday of next week.
Filled applications must be dropped
in the box provided for them in the
ASSU lobby, Williams said.
