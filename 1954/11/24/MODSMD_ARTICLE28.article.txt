# Coeds to Apply for Shrinkage Monday
## 
### 
Women students not assigned to
one of their first three choices of
housing may apply for shrinkage on
Monday from 2 to 5 p.m. in the
Housing Office, 101-A, Inner Quad.
There are two shrinkage periods
and the student may choose which
one she wishes to apply for. The
first lasts from house drawing time
to registration day of winter quarter
and the other lasts until two weeks
after registration.
