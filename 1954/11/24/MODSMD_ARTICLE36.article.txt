# One-Act Script Contest Opens
## 
### 
Ram's Head recently announced
the opening of a contest for students
and public interested in writing
scripts for the Winter Quarter One-
Acts.
Three orginal plays are needed,
and those chosen will be presented
one night during winter quarter.
Deadline for the scripts is the first
day of winter quarter.
Scripts may be given to Alice
MiddlekaufT, One - Acts production
manager, at the Ram's Head office
in the Nitery.
* * a
Contest for the Spring Show
scripts has also opened. A prize of
$200 is offered for the best three-act
musical comedy turned in.
