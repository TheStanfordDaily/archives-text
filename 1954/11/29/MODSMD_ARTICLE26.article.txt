# Physicists to Talk At Tuesday Series
## 
### 
Professors of Physics Edward L.
Ginzton and W. K. H. Panofsky will
present an illustrated lecture on
"Linear Accelerators and Nuclear
Research" at 7:30 p.m. tomorrow in
Memorial Auditorium to conclude
the autumn Tuesday Evening Series.
The two scientists head the two
divisions of the W. W. Hansen Lab
oratories, Professor Ginzton direct
ing the Microwave Laboratory and
Professor Panofsky the High Energy
Physics Laboratory.
The Tuesday Evening Series will
be resumed Jan. 11.
