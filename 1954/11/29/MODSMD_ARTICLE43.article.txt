# Farm Speakers Win Recognition
## 
### 
Jinx Patterson and Arliss Barnes
won two first places and Steve Nye
and Dobie Langenkamp took other
honors for Stanford at the Western
States Speech Association Tourna
ment held last week in Tucson, Ari
zona.
Miss Patterson and Miss Barnes
finished first in the women's upper
division, and Miss Patterson
finished first in the impromptu event
and reached the finals in discussion.
Nye and Langenkamp finished
third in the men's lower division and
Nye reached the quarterfinals in the
extemporaneous event.
