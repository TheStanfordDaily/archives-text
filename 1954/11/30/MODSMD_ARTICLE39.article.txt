# Bud Held Nominee For Sullivan Award
## 
### 
Franklin (Bud) Held, former
Stanford track star and holder of
the world's record in the javelin
throw, is among the nine nominees
for the 1954 James E. Sullivan Me
morial Award.
The award is made annually to
the outstanding amateur athlete in
the United States, and the winner
will be announced in January.
Held set his world's record in the
javelin in 1953 with a toss of better
than 260 feet.
