# Residence Formals
## 
### 
Branner Hall will hold a formal
dance this Friday night from 9 until
1". The theme of the dance is "Candy
Cane Lane." Spike Lynch and his
Stardusters will provide the music.
Ventura Hall will hold its annual
Christmas dance Friday. The formal,
a dinner dance, will be held at the
Lyndon Hotel in Los Gatos from 9
until 1.
Howard Fredrick's Band is sched
uled to provide dance music for the
Roble formal Saturday night. The
dance will be held in the Women's
Gym from 9 until 1.
Theme for the dance is "Pink
Champagne."
