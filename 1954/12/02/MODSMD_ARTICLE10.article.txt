# PRE-REGISTRATION
## LD Student Class Lists Open Today 
### 
Lower division pre-registra
tion continues today, and stu
dents with scheduled appoint
ment slips may begin signing
class lists at the Registrar's Of
ice from 8 a.m. until noon, and
1 to 5 p.m.
Conferences with advisers will
continue until Friday. Students may
obtain a study card to be filled out
in ink, and approved by the adviser
at this time.
Courses marked with a $ in the
time schedule may be signed for
during registration appointment
time, or until Dec. 7.
Official study lists may be turned
in in person to a registration clerk
who will stamp the appointment slip
a£ evidence of registration. The
deadline for returning the study lists
is noon, Dec. 7.
