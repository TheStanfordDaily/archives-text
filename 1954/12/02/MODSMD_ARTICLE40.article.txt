# KQED to Solicit Funds on Campus
## 
### 
The fund - i-aising committee for
KQED, the Bay Area's educational
television station, announced Tues
day night that Stanford faculty
members and campus groups will be
contacted by solicitors seeking to
raise $5,000 in the Palo Alto area
for support of the station.
The fund - raising campaign will
start next Monday.
Junior memberships will be avail
able for $1, while active charter
memberships will cost $10, and
sponsor charter memberships will
cost $100. Every member will be
given one vote at the yearly Bay
Area Educational Television Asso
ciation meeting, and will receive a
periodic program guide.
