# Women's Council Elects Chairman
## 
### 
The newly elected chairman of
Women's Council is Clare Van Sickle,
a senior who lives at Lagunita.
Other members who will assume
new duties on the Council at the
beginning of winter quarter arc Col
leen Baker, vice-chairman; Ann
Franks, supply chairman; Ann Muel
ler. secretary; and Lois Padelford,
key clerk.
Miss Mueller and Miss Padelford
arc the newly elected members to
the Council this quarter. The two
outgoing members are Jean McClure
and Lynn Ferguson, chairman and
vice-chairman of Women's Council
for fall quarter.
