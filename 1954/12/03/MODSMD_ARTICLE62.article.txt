# Stanford Sports Briefs
## SOCCER ALU-STARS 
### 
Stanford soccer players Niels
Vinding, Peter Mack, Ken Woods,
and Sam Sunrez, all named to the
Northern California Soccer Associ
ation All-Star-team last week, will
play in the league's annual all-star
game in San tomorrow
against conference champion USF.
USF, which finished second in the
conference during the regular season,
won the playoff championship by
defeating season champion CCSF 2
to 1 Thursday.
