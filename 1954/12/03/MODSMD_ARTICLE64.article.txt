# RIFLE TEAM VICTORY
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity rifle team beat
the San Jose State varsity rifle team
yesterday afternoon by a score of
137G to 136 D. Sophomore Tom Mur
ray, a San Mateo lad, was high man
for Stanford with 280 points.
This win, the rifle team's fourth
straight, gave them a season record
of 4 and 1.
