# GOLDBERG NOMINATED
## 
### 
Center Jerry Goldberg is Stan
ford's nominee for the 1954 Pop
Warner Award as the most valuable
college senior football player on the
Pacific Coast. Coaches, sportswriters,
and sportscasters will vote later this
month to select the top player for
the award. The winner will receive
his award at the PALO Club's an
nual "million dollar banquet" next
month. Stanford has had two win
ners of the award in the past, tackle
Russ Pomeroy in 1950 and quarter
back Bob Garrett last year.
