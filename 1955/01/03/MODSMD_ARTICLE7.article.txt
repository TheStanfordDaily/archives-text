# DR. STONE MINERAL RITES
## 
### 
Funeral services were held Fri
day afternoon in Palo Alto for Dr.
Calvin Perry Stone, professor of
psychology, who died at his Stanford
home on Dec. 28.
A memorial service for Dr. Stone
was also held Friday afternoon.
Dr. Stone, considered en expert in
the field of abnormal psychology,
was 62 at the time of his death.
He is survived by his wife, Minnie
Ruth Stone, of Stanford, and two
sons and a daughter.
