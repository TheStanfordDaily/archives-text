# A 						Phi 0 Opens Book Exchange In Libe Today
## 
### 
Alpha Phi Omega's book ex
change will be open today, to
morrow, and Wednesday in Main
Library 106, at the rear on the
first floor, to help students buy
and sell used textbooks.
The service fraternity serves as
agent, holding used textbooks for
students wishing to sell them.
Should the books be sold,' money
received at the exchange will be
returned to the seller later in the
quarter. Students wishing to buy
used texts may do so at the ex
change.
Students leaving books with A
Phi O to be placed on sale will be
charged a handling fee of 10 cents
for each book.
Exchange hours are from 9 a.m.
to noon and from 1 to 4 p.m. each
day. The exchange will not be
open on Thursday this quarter.
