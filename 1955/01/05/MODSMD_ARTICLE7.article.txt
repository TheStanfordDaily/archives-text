# Search Continues In Hit-Run Case
## 
### 
Stanford police yesterday contin
ued their search for a hit-run driver
who crashed' into two cars near the
Phi Sigma Kappa house on Monday
night.
The driver's car was reported as
a dark blue convertible with a Cali
fornia license plate, the first two
numbers being "2V." Other clues
left at the scene of the accident were
a pair of loafers, socks, a champagne
glass, and a spare tire brace.
