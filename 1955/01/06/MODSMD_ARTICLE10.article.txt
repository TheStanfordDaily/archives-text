# A 						President Laid to Rest
## 
### 

FUNERAL RITES—The funeral procession for ex Panama President Jose Remon leaves the cathe
dral in Panama City for the cemetery escorted by an honor guard. Remon was assassinated by
unknown machine-gunners at the city's Juan Franco race track last Sunday night. Meanwhile,
Remon's First Vice-President and Foreign Minister Jose Ramon Guizado has been sworn in as
the dead executive's successor. —NEA Telefoto
