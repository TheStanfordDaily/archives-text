# Consul to Discuss 1954 French Policy
## 
### 
''French International Policy in
1954 will be the topic of discussion
today at 4:15 p.m. in Bowman Alum
ni House.
The deputy consul of the French
consulate in San Francisco, Yves
Koubaud. will give a talk, then an
swer questions which the audience
may ask of him.
All interested people arc invited
to attend. Refreshments will be
served.
