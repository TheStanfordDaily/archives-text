# Retreat Planned By Newman Club
## 
### 
The Stanford Newman Club will
begin activities for 1955 with a re
treat for women from Friday to
Sunday.
The retreat, which will begin with
a dinner Friday evening, will be
held at Vallombrosa in Menlo Park.
Women who are interested should
contact either Klancy Clark or Bev
Hensing at Manzanita or Pat Nevins
at Lagunita.
