# Rev. Minto to Give Sermon on Sunday
## 
### 
The first service of winter quarter
will 'be held at Memorial Church on
Sunday at 11 a.m. by the Rev. R. M.
Minto, who has just returned from
Scotland. The topic of the service
will be ''Past, Present, and Future."
The University Choir will be un
der the direction of Harold C.
Schmidt. Herbert B. Nanney, Uni
versity organist, will play Franck's
"Chorale in A Minor" as the pre
lude and Bach's "In Thee Is Glad
ness" as the postlude.
