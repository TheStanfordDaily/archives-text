# Rushing the House Phone
## 
### 

THE RUSHING RUSH —Fraternity and eating club men line up at the house phone in Encina
lobby after 7:30 la3t night to contact freshman rushees. The club and fraternity men are not
permitted in the living group areas of the frosh dorms except between the hours of 11:30
a.m. and 1:30 p.m., and from 5:30 to 7:30 p.m., under a new rule established by the freshman
sponsors. Daily photo by Ken Green
