# IIR Will Choose Policy Delegates
## 
### 
Interviews will be conductcd to
day to select the Stanford delega
tion to the Western College Con
ference on American Foreign Pol
icy.
The interviews will be held from
1 to 3 p.m. in the Institute of In
ternational Relations.
The Conference will be present
ed on campus Jan. 30 to Feb. 2.
The central topic is "America
Faces World Communism." Har
old E. Stassen, keynote speaker,
is but one of the authorities in
vited to lecture.
Other features of the confer
ence will be seminars, foreign stu
dent panel, and the annual debate
with USC.
