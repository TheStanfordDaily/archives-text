# ASSU Continues Study Committee Interviews Today
## 
### 
Interviews for the student com
mittee to work on a study of the
Stanford undergraduate program
will continue today from 4 to 5:30
p.m. in the office of ASSU Presi
dent Pete Bing.
Signups for interviews from 4 to
5:30 p.m. tomorrow will be taken
at the ASSU office.
The 13 students selected for the
committee will work under the
direction of President Wallace
Sterling and Professor Donald
Taylor, who are heading the Study
of Undergraduate Education.
The study, financed by a grant
from the Ford Foundation, will
include curriculum, motivation for
study at Stanford, the types of ca
reers selected by Stanford grad
uates, and the general intellectual
and social atmosphere at Stanford.
Students will be interviewed by
Pete Bing. Dianne Goldman, Rudy
Reich, and Pat King.
