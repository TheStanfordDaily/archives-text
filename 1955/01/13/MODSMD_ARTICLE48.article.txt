# One-Act Tryouts Will End Tonight
## 
### 
Tryouts will end tonight for Ram's
Head's Winter One-Acts. Tryouts
will be held from 7 to 10 p.m. in the
Little Theater.
The three plays which have been
chosen are "Two, Squared," "The
Sea Shell," and "A House in the
Country." They will be presented
Feb. 17, 18, and 19.
Alice MiddlekaufT, production co
ordinator, says that all students arc
welcome to try out regardless of
dramatic experience.
