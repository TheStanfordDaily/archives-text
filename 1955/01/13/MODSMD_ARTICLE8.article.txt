# Room and Board Payments Due
## 
### 
Tomorrow is the last day for
paying University board and
room bills without an added fee,
according to the controller's of
fice.
Payment may be made between
8 a.m. and 5 p.m. at the cash
ier's office, Building 1, Inner
Quad.
Students living at Stanford Vil
lage may make payment at the
Village cashier's office, 9:30 a.m.
to 12:30 p.m. and 1:30 to 3:30 p.m.
Checks accompanied by the
statement may be mailed to the
cashier's office but must be post
marked no later than Jan. 14.
Any student who cannot pay
now may see the Counselor for
Men or Women concerning a de
ferment.
