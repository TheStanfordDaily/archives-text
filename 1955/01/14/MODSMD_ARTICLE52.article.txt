# Sigma Phi Sigma To Meet Tuesday
## 
### 
Sigma Phi Sigma, Stanford pre
mcdical and pre - nursing society,
will hold its first meeting of the
quarter at 7:30 p.m., Tuesday, in
Bowman Alumni House.
Rollen Waterson, legal counselor
for the California Medical
tion, will talk on the modern eco
nomics of medicine. A short busi
ness session will follow. Refresh
ments will be served .
Quad pictures will also be taken
at the meeting, and all members arc
requested to wear white shirts.
