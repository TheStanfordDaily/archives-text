# Visitors to Discuss Germany's Future
## 
### 
"The Future of Germany in In
ternational Affairs" will be the topic:
discussed by five young German
diplomats at the "Y" Public Affairs
Luncheon tomorrow noon.
The discussion, to be held in the |
"Y" lounge on the third floor of the ,
Women's Clubhouse, will be limited
to those who signed up last week.
The diplomats, four men and one
woman, are traveling in the United
States under the auspices of the
Governmental Affairs Institute of
Washington, D.C. They arrived on
campus Friday and will be here
through Tuesday.
