# Last Quad Pictures
## 
### 
The Quad has announced that
living groups and senior pictures
will be taken during the last week
in January. Those who failed to
have their picture taken autumn
quarter arc strongly urged to come
in, for this is the last time that pic
tures will be taken this year. Start
ing Monday ,a photographer will be
on duty at the Quad office from 9 to
12 and 1 to 5, and will continue to
be there through Friday, Jan. 28.
