# Theater Matinee To Show Chekhov
## 
### 
The second act of Anton Che
khov's "The Cherry Orchard" will
be featured on tomorrow after
noon's Theater Matinee at 4:15 in
the Little Theater.
The drama will be performed
twice by the same cast, in realistic
and expressionistic styles, as a dem
onstration of acting and directing
techniques. Director is F. Cowles
Strickland.
Theater Matinee is open to the
public without charge.
