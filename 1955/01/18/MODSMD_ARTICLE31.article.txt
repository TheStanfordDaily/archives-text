# Biz School to Hear Guest Economist
## 
### 
Leonard E. Read, former Palo Al
tan who is now president of the
Foundation for Economic Education
of New York, is the guest today of
the Stanford Graduate School of
Business.
Conferences with Dean J. Hugh
Jackson and other professors and a
faculty luncheon are on his sched
ule.
At one time manager of the Palo
Alto Chamber of Commerce, Read
later became manager of the Los
Angeles chamber. He, was serving
as executive vice-president of the
National Industrial Conference
Board when he was appointed pres
ident of the newly formed Founda
tion for Economic Education in 1940.
