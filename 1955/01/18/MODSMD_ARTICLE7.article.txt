# Nominations Set For Coed Posts
## 
### 
Nominations begin today for
AWS and WAA offices.
The offices with their class qual
ifications are: AWS president must
be a junior at the end of winter
quarter; AWS vice-president must
be a sophomore; AWS secretary
must be a freshman; and social
co-ordinator a junior.
There are no class restrictions
concerning the WAA offices,
which are president, vice-presi
dent, recording secretary, and cor
responding secretary.
Nomination blanks are available
at the ASSU office. Fifteen names
are needed to nominate a student.
Nominations will be collected each
day from the nomination box in
front of the ASSU office.
Nominations will close on
Thursday.
