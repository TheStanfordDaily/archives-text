# Vaccination Blanks Are Due by Jan. 31
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates for all
students registering at Stanford for
the first time must be turned into
the Registrar's Office by Monday,
Jan. 31.
Certificates filed after that dead
line will be subject to a $2 fine. Reg
istration for students who fail to file
the certificates by Feb. 7 will be
subject to cancellation by the com
mittee on Public Health.
