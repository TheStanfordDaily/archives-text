# Tickets Sell for Juilliard Quartet
## 
### 
Tickets are on sale for a special
concert of the Juilliard String Quar
tet at Stanford University on Feb. 2.
The chamber music group, which
has received acclaim from critics in
Europe and America, will perform
at 8:15 p.m. in Cubbcrlcy Audito
riu m.
Their program will consist of die
Quartet in D major, K. 575, by
Mozart; Quarct No. I by Piston;
and Quartet in E minor, Opus 59,
No. 2 by Beethoven.
The Quartet was established after
World War II with the aid of the
Juilliard Musical Foundation. The
musicians are in residence at the
Juilliard School of Music in New
York as members of the ensemble
faculty.
Tickets are priced at $2, general
admission, and $1.50 for students.
Tickets are available at the Me
morial Hall box ofiice, DAvcnport
3-0(565, Monday through Friday.
12:30 to 4:30 p.m.
