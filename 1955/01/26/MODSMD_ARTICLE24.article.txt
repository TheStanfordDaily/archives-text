# Folkdancers Begin Instruction Series
## 
### 
Starting this week, the Stanford
Folkdancers group will begin a new
program of intermediate and ad
vanced folkdancing instruction. The
meetings will be held in the dance
studio of the.Women's Gym every
Wednesday evening at 8:30.
This week's program of instruc
tion will include foreign dances such
as the Schweitzer, La Mesticita, the
Oberek, and Siamsa Beirte. All in
terested students and faculty mem
bers are invited.
The Folkdancers' new official rep
resentative is Pat Hofmann. She
will replace Ann Knoles, who has
graduated.
