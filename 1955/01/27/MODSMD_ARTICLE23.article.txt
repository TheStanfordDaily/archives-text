# Tickets Will Sell For Military Ball
## 
### 
Tickets will go on sale in the
AFKOTC office in the BAC today
for the sixth annual Military Ball,
to be held Feb. 11 at San Francisco's
Sheraton-Palace Hotel.
This year's Military Ball, tradi
tionally the biggest HO social event
of the year, will feature the music
of the NBC Orfhestra.
KOTC cadets in charge of the ball
said that each of the three ROTC
detachments here at Stanford would
select a Queen to reign jointly for
the evening. Winners of the Queen
contests will be announced some
time next week.
