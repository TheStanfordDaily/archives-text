# Dr. Allen to Discuss Hemingway Characters
## 
### 
'"The Hostile Characters of Ernest
Hemingway" will be discussed today
by Charles A. Allen, assistant pro
fessor of English and general editor
of the Stanford Press, at a meeting
today of the Stanford Philological
Association.
The session, to which the public
is invited, will be held at 4:15 p.m.
in the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse.
