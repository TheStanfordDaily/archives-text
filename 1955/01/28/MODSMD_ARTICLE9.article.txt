# FROSH CARD SALES
## 
### 
Today as the final day of the
freshman class card sales and the
class excom hopes that today's sales
will push the total above the 91 per
cent mark set by last year's class.
The cards, which cost $5, entitle
the holder to attend the Frosh-Soph
Jamboree, the Cal - Stanford Frosh
Exchange, and all class assemblies
and talent shows without charge and
offers reduced rates to class dances
and reunions.
