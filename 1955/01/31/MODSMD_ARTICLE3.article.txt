# Famed Pianist Plays Tonight
## 
### 
Gonzalo Soriano, acclaimcd as the
greatest pianist to come from Spain
in 25 years, plays at 8:15 tonight in
Memorial Auditorium under the
auspices of the Stanford Concert
Series.
Soriano will play Beethoven's
"Moonlight" Sonata, Liszt's Hunga
rian Rhapsody No. 13, Bach's Toc
cata and Fugue in D Minor, three
Scarlatti sonatas, and works by
Mompou and Ravel.
A few tickets are still available
and may be obtained at the Concert
Scries office in the Nitery from 1 to
2 this afternoon. Prices are $1.25,
$1.75, $2.25, and $2.75.
