# Salgo, Aldrich Plan Recital for Sunday
## 
### 
A recital of eighteenth century
sonatas for violin and harpsichord
by Sandor Salgo and Putnam Al
drich will be given next Sunday at
4 p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium.
The program will consist of So
nata in A Major by Bach, the Sonata
(Book 3. No. 1) of Leclair, and the
Mozart Sonata in F Major, K. 37(5.
The Leclair'sonata has been recently
realized from the original manu
script by Mr. Aldrich. There is no
admission charge.
