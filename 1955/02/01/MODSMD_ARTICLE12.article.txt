# One-Acts Tickets Go on Sale Today
## 
### 

Tickets for the Pain's Head Win
ter One-Acts go on sale today at the
Memorial Auditorium box office and
Louis Jefferys' Peninsula Box Office
on University Avenue in Palo Alto.
Price is $1.
One-Acts will be presented in
the Little Theater on Feb. 17, 18, and
19.
Ram's Head also announced that
students interested in working on
the crew for One-Acts must contact
Greg Merwin at the Phi Sigma
Kappa house or Alice Middlekauff,
production co-ordinator, at the
Ram's Head office at once.
