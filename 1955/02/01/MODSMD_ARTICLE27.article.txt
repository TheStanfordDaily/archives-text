# German Theater Will Be Discussed
## 
### 
Professor William Melnitz of the
University of California at Los An
geles will speak at tomorrow after
noon's Theater Matinee on "Meinin
gen, Brahm, and Reinhardt in the
German Theater."
Melnitz is an authority on the
modern German theater, having
been associated for several seasons
with Max Reinhardt.
Theater Matinee is presented ev
ery week at 4:15 p.m. in the Little
Theater. Admission is free.
