# Immunization Shot Series to Continue
## 
### 
A series of free tetanus and ty
phoid immunizations are now being
given at the Health Service, under
the sponsorship of the Student Health
Committee.
Those who have been previously
inoculated have been advised by Dr.
George Houck, director of the
Health Service, to get booster shots.
Students planning European travel
this summer arc requested to start
their series early.
