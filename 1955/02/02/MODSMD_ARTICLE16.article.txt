# Parley to End
## 
### 
A final seminar and a banquet, to
day conclude the Western College
Conference on American Foreign
Policy, bringing to an end the four
day parley.
Following the conference theme,
"America Faces World Commu
nism," C. Easton Rothwell will speak
on '"The Price of a Free World" at
the noon banquet at Rickey's Studio
Inn. Rothwell is the director of the
Hoover Institute and Library. The
banquet is for conference delegates
and members of the Institute of In
ternational Relations, sponsors of
the convention.
The seminars will bring their dis
cussions to a close from 9 to 11:30
this morning.
