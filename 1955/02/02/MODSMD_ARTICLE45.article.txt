# Korean Consul To Address IIR
## 
### 
Korean Consul General in San
Francisco, Young Han Choo. will
speak to the members of the Insti
tute of International Relations to
morrow at 4:15 p.m. in Bowman.
Choo has been closely associated
with Syngman Rhee since 1915 when
he was a student of the Korean
president in Honolulu. He also
worked with Rhee in Washington,
D.C., in 1924 in an agency of the
Korean Provisional Government.
