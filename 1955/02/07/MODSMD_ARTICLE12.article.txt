# ‘I’ Club Seminar Sign-ups to Begin
## 
### 
Students may sign up today
through Thursday at the lIR Shack
from 4 to 6 p.m. for an "'I" Club
sponsored seminar scheduled for
the weekend of Feb. 18-20.
Those attending the seminar, to
be held at the Sequoia Seminar
camp at Ben Lomond, will discuss
the topics '"How much does our
Stanford diploma mean?" and "Why
live: does man have a purpose?"
The cost of the weekend is $2.75
a person.
