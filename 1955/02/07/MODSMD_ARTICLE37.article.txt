# IIR Will Sponsor Truckee Ski Trip
## 
### 
The Stanford Institute of Inter
national Relations is sponsoring a
ski weekend Friday and Saturday.
Reservations are still available for
the Truckee trip. Cost will be $2
per night for lodging. The group
will be leaving between 3 and 4
p.m. Friday.
Those interested in making the
trip may sign up at the lIR office.
