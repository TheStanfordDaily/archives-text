# Intermezzo Talk To Be on Writers
## 
### 
Miss Jessamyn West, novelist and
short story writer, will give today's
Intermezzo lecture in the Bonder
Room of the Main Library at 4:15.
Her topic will '"The Function of
the Writer."
Miss West's latest novel is "Cress
Delahanty." Her short stories have
appeared in the New Yorker and
Harper's Magazine.
