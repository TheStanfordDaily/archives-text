# Architect Will Be Selected
## 
### 
Interviews to select the architect
for Palo Alto Hospital and the Stan
ford Mcdical School have begun.
At suggestion of President Ster
ling, one architect will be hired for
the two projects, but under separate
contracts. The two hospitals will
probably be built on adjoining sites.
Approximately 20 architects have
applied.
Serving on the interviewing com
mittee are Alf Brandin, Stanford
business manager; Ken Cuthbertson,
assistant to Dr. Sterling; and Wind
sor Cutting, Dean of Stanford Medi
cal School.
