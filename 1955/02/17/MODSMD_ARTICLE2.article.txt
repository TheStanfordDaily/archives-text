# S.F. Symphony To Play Vivaldi
## 
### 
Violinist Zino Franeescatti will be
soloist with the San Francisco Sym
phony tonight at 8:30.
lie will play Vivaldi's Concerto in
C Major and Lalo's "Symphonic
Espagnole." for violin and orchestra.
The orchestra, conducted by
Enrique Jorda,' will play Piston's
Symphony No. 3 and Borodin's Over
ture to "Prince Igor."
Students wishing to buy or sell
tickets may sign up at the Concert
Series office in the Nitery from 1 to
5 this afternoon.
