# Charity Drive Results Given
## 
### 
The semi-final figures for results
of the Winter Quarter Charity Drive
are $3,091.60, as announced yester
day by Ertc Field, treasurer of the
Drives Committee.
This figure does not include dona
tions from a couple of fraternities,
and the proceeds from the Friday
and Saturday car wash.
Stillman House won the per cap
ita honors, with an average of $2.83
collected from each coed.
Solicitors may turn in their mon
ey to the ASSU office.
