# Canon Green To Visit Here
## 
### 
Canon Bryan Green of Birming
ham, England, will preach next Sun
day at the 11 a.m. service in Memo
rial Church. Canon Green is making
a brief stop here on his way to a
similar mission in Hawaii.
The rector of the parish church of
Birmingham, Canon Green is a
noted preacher of the Church of
England and has addressed capacity
crowds all over the world.
Canon Green was on campus one
year ago when he .spoke not only at
the Sunday services but every night
that week in Memorial Auditorium,
each time to an overflow crowd.
A Mission reunion has been plan
ned for Saturday night at 7:30 in the
Women's Clubhouse. There will also
be a coffee hour in Bowman Alumni
House following the church service
Sunday morning.
