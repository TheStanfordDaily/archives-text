# Farm Wrestlers Meet TI Sailors
## 
### 
Coach Bruce Bailey will take the
Stanford varsity wrestling team to
Treasure Island tonight to do battle
with the TI Navymen there. The
matches with the rugged sailors will
begin at 8 o'clock.
Pacing the Card grapplers will be
Don Manoukian, the Indians' unde
feated heavyweight. Manoukian has
scored 10 consecutive victories this
sason.
Filling out the Stanford line-up
are Kirk Nylan (177), Bob Beatie
(167), A 1 Laubscher (157), Stan
Hartmann (147), Larry Marsh (137),
Jerry Lowell (130), and Bob Fish
er (123).
