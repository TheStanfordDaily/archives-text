# 'Mary of Scotland' Tickets Go on Sale
## 
### 
Tickets go on sale today for the
forthcoming Stanford Players pro
duction of Maxwell Anderson's
"Mary of Scotland." They may be
obtained at the Memorial Hall box
office for $1 from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m.
The show runs March 3-5.
Robert Loper is the director.
