# Tryouts to End For Melodrama
## 
### 
Final tryouts for the Stanford
Players production of David Belas
co's "Girl of the Golden West" will
be held tonight from 7:30 to 10:30
in the Little Theater.
The show, which runs April 13-1G
and 20-23, is directed by Douglas
Cook.
"Girl of the Golden West" is a
melodrama of the early '49er days,
and features parts for 20 men and
two women.
Tryouts are open to all students,
faculty, and staff.
