# Professor Captures Engineering Honor
## 
### 
Dr. Oswald G. Villard Jr., Stanford
associate professor of electrical en
gineering, last night won the Out
standing young Bay Engineer of
1955 award.
The award was presented to the
39-year-old scientist by H. J. Brun
nier, chairman of the Bay Area En
gineers' Week committee, at an an
nual banquet of the engineering
profession.
Villard, a graduate of Stanford and
Yale, has been experimenting in up
per atmosphere radio communica
tions.
