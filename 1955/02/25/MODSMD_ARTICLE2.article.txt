# 'Soccer Ball' To Follow Cal Game Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Soccer Association
will present an all-campus dance,
the Soccer Ball, following the Cal-
Stanford basketball game tonight
from 10 to 1 in the Women's Gym.
The tempo of the basketball game
will shift to the tempo of Bill White
and his four-piece jazz combo, who
will provide a mixture of hot and
sentimental music.
Tickets for the come-as-you-are
affair will be on sale today on Engi
neering Corner for 50 cents. Tickets
also may be purchased at tfyc door.
