# Tickets on Sale For Soph Cotillion
## 
### 
Tickets will continue on sale to
day for the Sophomore Cotillion
which will be held at the San Fran
cisco Surf Club on Saturday, March
5, from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m.
The bids, which can be purchased
on the four corners of Quad and
from living group representatives,
sell for $2.75 each. Holders of soph
omore class cards, however, may
purchase bids for $2.25.
The Cotillion, which is an annual
affair, will feature continuous music
by the dance band of Ray Hackett
and the jazz band of Rebel Daily.
Dress will be semi - formal and
a free corsage will be presented to
every girl.
