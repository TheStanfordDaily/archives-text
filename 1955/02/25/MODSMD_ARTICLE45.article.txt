# FROSH SWIMMERS WIN
## 
### 
Robin Moore scored a double win
to pace the Stanford frosh swim
mers to a 48-34 win over the San
Jose State frosh in the Encina Pool
Wednesday.
Moore won the 100- and 220-yard
freestyle in :50.4 and 2:33.0.
Larry Wood was star of the losers
in the 150-yard individual medley
and the 200-yard backstroke.
