# SPRING QUARTER
## Upper Division Pre-Reg Begins 
### 
Spring quarter pre-registration for upper division and graduate
students will be today and tomorrow. The Registrar's Office has
announced the following procedure for these students:
1. Official study list cards, obtained from the major school or
department, must be filled out in ink with the approval and sig
♦ nature of the major professor.

2. If the study list, includes any
courses marked in the Time
| Schedule, the appropriate class lists
mast bo signed at the information
desk, Registrar's Ollice, Room 111-A,
between 8 a.m. and noon or be
tween 1 and 5 p.m. today or tomor
row.
3. Study list cards must be turned
in, in person, to the check-out clerk

Lower division students must
see their advisers 011 Wednesday,
Thursday, or Friday, before prc
registration, according to the Reg
istrar's Ollice.
