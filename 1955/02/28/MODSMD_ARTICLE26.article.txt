# Tryouts for Opera Chorus Start Today
## 
### 
All students und members of the
Stanford community who are inter
ested in joining the Opera Chorus
are invited to contact Mr. Harold C.
Schmidt at DA 3-6731 at the Knoll
today or tomorrow.
The opera, which will appear on
the Stanford Players' Series May 26,
27, and 28, is the first West Coast
performance of Stravinsky's "The
Rake's Progress."
The opera is under the musical di
rection of Professor Sandor Salgo,
Professor F. Cowles Strickland, stage
director, and Professor Scnmidt,
chorus director.
