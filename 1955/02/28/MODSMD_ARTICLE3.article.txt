# News Around the World
## Compiled from Dispatches of the United Press Red Korean Troops May Raid Formosa 
### 
SEOUE. Feb. 27—The Communists
are moving thousands of troops from
Korea to China, possibly in prepa
ration for an attack on the Chinese
Nationalist stronghold of Formosa,
South Korean military sources said
today.
At the same time, these sources
said, Russia and Red China are beef

ing up the native army and air force
in Communist Korea—in defiance of
the Korean truce —to replace the
transferred troops.
U.S. Army officers declined com
ment on the reports.
