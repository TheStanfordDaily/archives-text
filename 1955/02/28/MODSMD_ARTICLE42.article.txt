# Harvard Professor Will Speak Today
## 
### 
Dr. Donald H. Menzel, director of
the Harvard College Observatory,
will speak on the subject of sun
spots, magnetic storms, and the au
rora at 2 p.m. today in the lecture
room of the Electronics Research
Laboratory.
Dr. Menzel is visiting the campus
briefly' in connection with research
work pertaining to radio propaga
tion in the upper atmosphere. He
is an authority on phenomena of the
upper atmosphere and of the sun.
