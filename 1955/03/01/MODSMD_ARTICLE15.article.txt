# Hubcap Thieves Nabbed by Police
## 
### 
Two young thieves were arrested
by a Stanford policeman early Sun
day morning after confessing to the
theft of a hubcap from the car of a
Stanford student.
Officer Bert Herring made the ar
rest of Dennis Willis, 19, and Rich
ard K. Shores, 18, of San Carlos.
The two youths had stolen the hub
cap from the Ford of Carrol D.
Pcwthers, Encina.
The boys were driving a car be
longing to a soldier at Fort Old, and
police are uncertain whether or not
they arc in rightful possession of the
automobile.
Both suspects have prior records.
They are being held in custody
awaiting a hearing next Thursday.
