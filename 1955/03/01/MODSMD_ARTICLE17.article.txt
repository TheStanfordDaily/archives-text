# Upper Division and Graduate Pre-Registration Ends Today
## 
### 
Today is the last day of spring
quarter pre-registiation for up
per division and graduate stu
dents.
The following procedure applies
to upper division and graduate .stu
dents:
1. Official study list, cards, ob
tained from the major school or de
partment, must be filled out in ink
and signed by the major professor.
2. If the study list includes any
courses marked "§" in the Time
Schedule, the appropriate class lists
must be signed at the information
desk in the Registrar's Office, Room
111-A, between 8 a.iti. and noon or
between 1 and 5 p.m. today.
3. Study list cards must be turned
in, in person, to the check-out clerk
at. the information desk in the Reg
istrar's Oflice before 5 p.m. today.
Signup lists for French and Span
ish 12, 13, 27, and 28 will be available
as follows:
French — Room 245, Monday
through Friday, 0 to 11:30 a.m. and
2 to 3 p.m.
Spanish — Room 401, Monday
through Friday, 10 a.m. to noon and
2 to 4 p.m.
Two courses not listed in the Time
Schedule will also be given next
quarter. They are Business Law 11,
for 5 units MTWThF at 10, by Pro
fessor Frank and AG 75, Goethe's
Faust in English Translation, MWF
at 10, by Professor Blauth. This
course is open only to freshmen.
