# TGIF
## Senior Reunion Slated Friday 
### 
TGIF is the theme for the senior
reunion to be held this Friday from
3:30 to 6, at Mangini's Barn.
Turk Murphy from the Tin Angel
in San Francisco will he on hand
to provide jazz music for those in
attendance. The dress will be casual,
and the admission fee is 25 cents
per person.
The event is not restricted to Sen
iors only. Members of all classes
will be • admiteed.
The committee in charge of this
reunion are Sue Zigler, Harvey
I liber, and Cam Kennedy.
