# End-Quarter Libe Hours Announced
## 
### 
The Library has announced that
the Reserve Book Room and the
Reference Room will be open from
7:45 a.m. to 11 p.m. starting Monday.
These hours will continue until
Thursday, March 17. The rest of the
Library will be open from 7:45 a.m.
to 10 p.m. during this time.
Friday. March 18, the Library will
close at 5:30 p.m.
From March 19 to 28 the Library
will open at 8 a.m. and close at 4:30
p.m. Tuesday, March 29, regular
hours will be resumed.
