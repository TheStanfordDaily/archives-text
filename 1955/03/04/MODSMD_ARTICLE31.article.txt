PAJAMA GAMES—Peach Hite, Tom Decker, Margo Boothe,
and Paul Williams are in a sneak preview of the stag freshman
pajama dance which will be held for all freshman card holders
in the Woman's Gym tonight from 9 to 1. The dance will be
preceded by a grand march which will start from Encina about
8:30 a.m. and tour Branner and Roble on the way to the dance,
Prizes will be awarded for the best outfits. Spike Lynch's Star
dusters will provide the music. The freshman excom has asked
that no pajamas be worn off campus after the dance.
