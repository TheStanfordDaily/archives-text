# Debaters Go To Tournament
## 
### 
Ten members of the Stanford
Speech Association left campus yes
terday to participate in the debate
tournament at Pepperdine College.
Stanford's five teams are Jinx
Paterson and Arliss Barnes, upper
division women; Mike Hudson and
Marilyn Myers, and Eddie and Leon
Ardzrooni, upper division men;
Steve Nye and Dodie Langenkamp.
and John Falchi and Jack Hymans,
lower division men.
