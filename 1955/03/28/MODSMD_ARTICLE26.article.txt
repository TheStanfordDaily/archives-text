# Articles Written, Speeches, Trips Made by Farm Profs
## La 						Plante Visits Japan 
### 
John La Plante, art instructor and
curator of Oriental art at Stanford,
will be in Japan for the next five
months for the first portion of a
year's study of Buddhist art.
La Plante, studying on a grant
from the Ford Foundation Board of
Overseas Training and Research,
will spend the remaining seven
months in India, Indonesia, Java,
Indochina, Burma, Siam, and Af
ghanistan, returning to Stanford by
way of Eurooe.
