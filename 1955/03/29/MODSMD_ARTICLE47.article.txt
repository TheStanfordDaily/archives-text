# Stanford Teams Place in Debate
## 
### 
Each of the four Stanford debate
teams that participated in the tour
nament at Pepperdine College won
a spot on the elimination ladder.
The team of Steve Nye and Dodie
Langenkamp reached the finals in
the upper division men's classifica
tion. They lost in the final round,
thus garnering second placc.
Jinx Paterson and Arliss Barnes,
upper division women, and Bob
Brozio and Jim Mack, lower division
men, tied for third placc in their di
visions.
John Falchi and Jack Hymans
tied for fifth place in the lower di
vision men's classification.
