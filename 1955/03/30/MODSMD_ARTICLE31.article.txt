# Church Is Holding Lenten Services
## 
### 
A daily service of prayer, from
12:55 to 1:05 p.m., is being held at
Memorial Church Monday through
Friday until April 7, Maundy
Thursday, as part of the Lenten
program.
Students interested in conducting
one of these services of prayer may
get further information at the
church vestry.

A Candlelight service will be held
at 9:30 tonight in Memorial Church.
It will consist of organ meditation,
Scripture reading, and prayer.
