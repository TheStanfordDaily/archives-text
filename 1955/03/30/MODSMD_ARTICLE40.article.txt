# FARM FENCER WINS
## 
### 
John McDougall, a member of the
Elwyn Bugge - coached Stanford
fencing team, emerged from the
Northwest Fencing Tournament at
Seattle on March 2(5 and 27 as Junior
Foil Champion. The Card fencer
won out over a host of 30 entries
from Seattle, Portland, and Vancou
ver, 8.C., in the weekend tourney.
McDougall was the only entrant
from the Stanford team.
