# Duo Picked For Debate With London
## 
### 
Douglas Aitken David Jacob
son have been selected by the Stan
ford Speech Association to meet two
visiting University of London debat
ers in the annual international de
bate which will be presented Tues
day.
The debate, scheduled for 7:30 p.m.
April 15 in Memorial Auditorium,
will be a feature of the Tuesday
Evening Series. It is sponsored
jointly by the Committee on Public
Exercises and the Stanford Speech
Association.
Aitken, a graduate student, and
Jacobson, a senior, are both majors
in history. The debate topic is the
national collegiate debate question
regarding United States recognition
of the Communist government in
China.
