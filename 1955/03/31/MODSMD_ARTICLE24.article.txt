# Menuhin Will Be Symphony Soloist
## 
### 
Violinist Yehudi Menuhin will be
i guest soloist with the San Francisco
Symphony tonight at 8:30. playing
Mozart's Concerto for Violin and
Orchestra No. 5.
Menuhin, famed in his youth as a
"child prodigy," is appearing in San
Francisco on one of his many con
cert tours, during which he has
built a huge popular following
among concert audiences.
Enrique Jorda will conduct the
orchestra in Bartok's "Rhapsody No.
2." Rameau's "Dardanus," and Cop
land's Suite from "Rodeo."
Students wishing to buy or sell
tickets may sign up at the Concert
Series office in the Nitery from 1 to
5 this afternoon.
