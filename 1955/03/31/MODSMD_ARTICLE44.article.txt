# AWS Announces New Office Hours For This Quarter
## 
### 
The new Associated Women Stu
dents officers have announced the
following officc hours. There will
be an officer in the AWS office from
1 to 2 p.m. Monday through Friday.
The AWS office, located on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house, is open daily for student use.
Marilyn Peterson will be there
Mondays; Sue de Jong, Tuesdays;
Myrna Kelley, Wednesdays; Ellie
Kronfeld, Thursdays; and Harriet
Thomas, AWS president, Fridays.
