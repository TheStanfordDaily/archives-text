# Two Professors Travel to England
## 
### 
Two Stanford professors are en
route to England to' deliver invita
tional papers at the Faraday Society
meeting to be held April 4-6 in
Cambridge.
The professors are Richard A.
Ogg, chemistry, and George E. Pake,
physics. They are among the 25 ex
perts who will address the scientific
society at this annually held dis
cussion.
The speakers plan to return to
campus in mid-April.
