# Director Named To SRI Board
## 
### 
President Wallace Sterling, board
chairman of Stanford Research In
stitute, has announced that J. P.
Weyerhaeuser, Jr., of Tacoma,
Wash., has been named to the board
of directors of the institute.
In accepting the invitation of the
University trustees, Weyerhaeuser
joins 36 other business and indus
trial executives and educators in
guiding the policies of the nonprofit
applied research organization.
Weyerhaeuser is president of
Weyerhaeuser Timber Co., of Ta
coma, and has long been identified
with the economic development of
the Pacific Northwest.
