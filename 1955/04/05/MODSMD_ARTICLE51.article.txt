# '55 Announcements
## 
### 
Seniors desiring graduation an
nouncements or programs may order
them now at the Bookstore.
Programs and announcements are
to be paid for at the time of order
ing. Announcements are 15 cents
each and programs, bound in white
leather, will cost $1.50. There is no
limit to the number ordered so long
as they are paid for when ordering.
The program will include a listing
of all students graduating and their
degrees. It will also list honors and
scholarships.
