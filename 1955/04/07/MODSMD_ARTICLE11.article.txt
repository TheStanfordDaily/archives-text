# Study Lists, Bills Due Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tomorrow is the deadline for
turning in official study lists and
class cards. Next week a $2 fine
will be charged, and after next
week the fine will he $lft.
Cards may be turned in at the
Registrar's Information Office,
Room 111-A, Inner Quad.
Elmer Wagner, Associate Regis
trar, emphasized that if students
all wait until tomorrow to turn in
their cards, this Avill cause jam-ups
and delay. The office would great
ly appreciate attempts by the stu
dents to turn in their cards at
their earliest opportunity.
Tomorrow is also the last day
that board and room bills may he
paid without an added fee. Pay
ment may be made at the cashier's
office between 8 a.m. and 5 p.m.
Students who Jive at Stanford
Village may pay at the Village
cashier's office. 9:30 a.m. to 12:30
p.m. and 1:30 to 3:30 p.m.
Checks, accompanied by the
statement, mutt he postmarked no
later than tomorrow. Any student
who cannot pay now may sec the
Counselor for Men or Women
concerning a deferment.
