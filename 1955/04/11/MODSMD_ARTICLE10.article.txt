# Water Safety Course to Begin
## 
### 
Palo. Alto Area Red Cross Water
Safety Program Cor 1955 will include
courses for Senior Life Saving and
Water Safety Instructor to be given
during April and May.
The Senior Life Saving course will
be held April 11, 12, 13, 14, 18, and 19
from 7 to 10 p.m.
Preliminary training to the Water
Safety Instructor Course, required
of all those who register for the In
structor Course, will be given on
Saturday, 9 a.m. to noon; April 20, 7
to 10 p.m., and April 23, 9 a.m. to
noon. Registrants must be at least
18 years old and must hold current
Senior Life Saving Certificate.
The Water Safety Instructor
Course will be given on April 25, 27,
28, May 2, and May 4, from 7 to 10
p.m. Registrants must be 18 years
of age, must hold current, Senior
Life Saving Certificate, and have
completed the preliminary course.
All courses will be held at Ray
Lyman Wilbur Junior High School.
Registration may be made at the
Red Cross chapter house at 400
Mitchell Lane, or by telephoning DA
2-2143, or by mail.
