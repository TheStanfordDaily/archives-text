# Card Golfers Split
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity golf team split
a pair of matches over the weekend.
On Friday the Cards topped Califor
nia, 21-6, at the Richmond golf
course as Fred Brown, Phil Getchell,
and Warren Dailey all shot 72's.
Stanford bowed to the Lake Mer
ced Country Club team, 16-11, on
Saturday. Chuck Van Linge was
low for the Indians with a 76.
