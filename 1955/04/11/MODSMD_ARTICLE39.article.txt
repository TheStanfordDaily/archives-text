# '55 Announcements
## 
### 
Seniors desiring announcements
and programs may order them now
at the Bookstore.
Announcements are 15 cents each
and the white leather-bound pro
grams are $1.50. Both must be paid
for at the time the order is placed,
according to Bookstore officials.
The programs will include a list
of the graduating class.
