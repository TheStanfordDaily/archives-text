# Social Chairmen Party
## 
### 
A get-together for campus social
chairmen is planned by the Social
Co-ordinatint* Board for Saturday,
April 16, from 1:30 to 5 p.m. The
object of the party is to enable the
various social chairmen to meet each
other.
Refreshments and entertainment,
including an aquacade, are being
planned by members of the board.
