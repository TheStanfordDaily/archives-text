# Gallery to Exhibit New Modern Art
## 
### 
Over 200 original drawings by fa
mous 20th century artists will be
shown in a special exhibition at the
An Gallery beginning Sunday and
continuing through May 15.
The exhibit, '"Twentieth Century
Drawings," includes work of almost
every major artist of the era. Ma
tisse, Picasso, Renoir, Modigliani, |
1 Leger, Degas, and Toulouse-Lautrec
are among those represented.
Drawings in ink, chalk, pencil, and
mixed media illustrate contempo
rary trends expressed by both long
established artists and newcomers.
The Art Gallery will be open to
the public daily, except Mondays,
from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. during the
"Twentieth Century Drawings" ex
hibition. Sunday hours are noon to
! 4 p.m.
