# Camera Club Sets May Exhibit Date
## 
### 
The Stanford Camera Club has
announced that it will hold an ex
hibition during the latter part of
May. Everyone, including non
members, may enter photographs.
Pictures should be eight inches
square or larger, and mounted on a
suitable mounting board. Photo
graphs for the exhibit may be sub
mitted to Mr. Gorham in Room 10(5
iu the Stanford Library by May 9.
All who are interested either in
entering the exhibition or in joining
the Camera Club are invited to at
tend a meeting in the Village Audi
torium tonight at 8.
