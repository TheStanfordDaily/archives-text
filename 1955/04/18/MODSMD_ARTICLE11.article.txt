# A 						Phi O to Return Books, Money Today
## 
### 
Alpha Phi Omega's Book Ex
change in Main Library Room 106
will reopen today and tomorrow to
return money and unsold books
from its exchange the first week of
this quarter.
Hours will be 9 a.m. to noon and
1 to 4 p.m. both days; any money or
unsold books not picked up by 4
p.m. tomorrow will revert to the
service fraternity for use in its serv
ice projects, exchange chairman
Ralph Moore indicated.
