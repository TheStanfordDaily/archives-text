# News Around the World
## Compiled from Dispatches of the United Press Red China Builds New Air Offense 
### 
AUGUSTA, Ga., April 17 (UP)—
Secretary of State John Foster Dul
les revealed today that the United
States has just learned of a mighty
buildup of Red Chinese air power
on the mainland opposite Formosa.
Dulles told reporters after confer
ring with President Eisenhower that

this country has learned of the new
offensive air strength across the For
mosan Strait within the "last few
days."
