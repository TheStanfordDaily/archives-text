# Stanford Chorale To Give Concert
## 
### 
The Stanford Chorale will present
a program of madrigals, chansons,
and folk songs at 3 p.m. Sunday at
Villa Montalvo in Saratoga. The
concert is sponsored by the Mont
alvo Association, a Santa Clara
County citizens' organization for the
creative arts center.
Professor Harold Schmidt will
conduct 25 student singers. Two
songs by Kirke Mecham, assistant
choral conductor, will be featured.
The "Sonata for Oboe and Clari
net" by Leonard Ratncr, acting head
of the Music Department, will be
included on the program.
