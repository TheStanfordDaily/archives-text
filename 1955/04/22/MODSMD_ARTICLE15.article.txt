# Rogues' Ball Set Tonight on Row
## 
### 
"An atmosphere of intrigue" is
the theme for Delta Chi's annual
Rogues' Ball to be given from 9 to
1 tonight at the Delta Chi house.
In keeping with the atmosphere,
the house will be decorated with
black drapes and brilliant red
lights. The dancer's will be
dressed in the costumes of "rogues
and their dolls."
A small combo will play. Re
freshments will be served.
The ball is open to the public.
Admission is free.
