# Papoose Mermen Face Carlmont H.S.
## 
### 
Robin Moore will lead the Stan
ford frosh swimmers today against
Carlmont High School in a meet
scheduled for 4 p.m. in the Encina
Pool.
Moore, who set a new pool record
of 50.7 seconds in the 100-yard free
style on Saturday, will be out to
better this time and may break the
national freshman record of 50.6
seconds.
Tom Decker, sprint ace, will be
looking for his third straight win in
the 50-yard freestyle. Decker turned
in a 25.1 last week.
