# Chamber Ensemble To Appear Here
## 
### 
The New York Philharmonic
Chamber Ensemble will make its
first West Coast appearance in a
concert here May 3 at 8:30 p.m. in
Cubberley Auditorium.
The group of 10 string and wind
instrumentalists from the New York
Philharmonic Orchestra will play
Quartet for Flute and Strings, K.
298. by Mozart; Pastorale by Mil
haud; Divertimente by Haydn; Trio,
Opus 40, Roussel; and Octet, Opus
166, by Schubert.
Tickets for the concert, which will
be given under the auspices of the
Stanford Committee on Public Ex
ercises, are $2 general admission
and $1.50 for students. They are on
sale at Memorial Hall box office.
