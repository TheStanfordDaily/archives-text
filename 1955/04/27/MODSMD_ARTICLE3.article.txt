# Coro Foundation To Discuss Jobs
## 
### 
Job opportunities offered by the
Coro Foundation of San Francisco
will be discussed at an open Cap
and Gown meeting tonight by a
panel of Coro interns.
The meeting will be held at 7:30
p.m. in the Women's Clubhouse.
The discussion, designed especial
ly for senior women interested in
the field of public affairs, is open
to all women.
Participating in the program will
be two Stanford graduates, Diane
Catlin and Barbara Tuerk.
