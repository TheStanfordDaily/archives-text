# 'Service Wife Information' Series Continues
## 
### 
The second meeting in a series on
"What Every Service Wife Should
Know" will take place tonight at 7:30
in Room 47 of the BAC Building.
The series is being presented by the
Stanford Officers' Wives' Club to in
form wives and fiancees of students
who will be commissioned by the
three ROTC units at Stanford what
is expected of them as officers'
wives.
The program tonight will be bro
ken down into three panels, each
composed of officers' wives of each
service branch. These panels will be
general discussions answering ques
tions on the differences in the
service they represent. The meeting
will be followed by a coffee hour.
