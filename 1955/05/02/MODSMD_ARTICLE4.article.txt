# IIR Conference
## 
### 
The seventh annual conference on
the United Nations for students in
California high schools, sponsored
by the Institute of International Re
lations. concluded last Saturday
with a luncheon at the Old Planta
tion.
Approximately 120 delegates, rep
resenting 28 high schools, attended
the conference. It included speeches
and panels as well as campus tours
and social events.
