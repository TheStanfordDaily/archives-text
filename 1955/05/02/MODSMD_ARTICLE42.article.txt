# Symphony Ducat Money Unclaimed
## 
### 
Unclaimed money from the sale
of symphony tickets under the Con
cert Series ticket exchange program
must be picked up by May 13, said
Series manager John Tirrell.
Money may be claimed by presen
tation of student body card at the
Concert Series office in the Nitery
from 1 to 5 p.m. Monday through
Friday. Any remaining money will
become the property of the Concert
Series.
