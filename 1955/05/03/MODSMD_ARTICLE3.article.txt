# N.Y. Ensemble Plays Tonight
## 
### 
The New York Philharmonic
Chamber Ensemble will present
their first West Coast concert at 8:30
tonight in Cubberley Auditorium.
The Chamber Ensemble, organ
ized during the 1951-52 music sea
son. gave its first New York con
cert in February, 1952.
Thirty-seven instrumentalists are
included in the group. Ten will be
included in the Stanford concert of
contrasting classic and contempo
rary music for unusual combina
tions of instruments.
The program includes Milhaud's
"Pastorale for Oboe, Clarinet, and
Bassoon," Haydn's "Divertimento
for Quintet of Wind Instruments,"
Roussel's "Trio for Flute, Viola, and
'Cello, Opus 40," and Schubert's
"Octet for Five String and Three
Wind Instruments, Opus 166."
Tickets, priced at $2 general ad
mission and $1.50 for students, are
available at the Memorial Hall box
office from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m.
