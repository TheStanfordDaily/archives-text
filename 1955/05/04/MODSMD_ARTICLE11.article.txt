# Political Union Members to Elect Officers Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Political Union will
choose officers for the 1955-56 sea
son at a meeting in Cubberley Au
ditorium tonight at 7:15.
Immediately following the elec
tions there will be a short but im
portant business meeting, according
to Political Union president Rex
Kramer. The purpose of the meet
ing will be to vote on the amend
ments added to the new Political
Union constitution by Excom at a
recent meeting.
The Republican caucus of the Po
litical Union selected its officers for
the coming year Monday night.
Mike Hudson was chosen president;
Bob Baker, political vice-president;
and Joe Pool, executive vice-presi
dent and treasurer.
