# Record Crowd Sees Art Exhibit
## 
### 
The "Twentieth Century Draw
ings" exhibition currently at the
Stanford Art Gallery has set a new
attendance record, with 2,577 visi
tors in the first 12 days of the show.
The Art Gallery is open to the
public daily, except Mondays, from
10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Sunday hours are
noon to 4 p.m.
