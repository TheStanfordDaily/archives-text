# Nigerian to Speak At Bowman Today
## 
### 
"Self-Government in Nigeria" will
be the topic of Dr. Aliu Babatunde
Fafunwa's speech at the Bowman
Alumni House today at 4:15 p.m.
Dr. Fafunwa is assistant Nigerian
liaison officer in Washington. He has
worked with the United Nations as
an area specialist and written
several magazine articles.
The speaker received his Ph.D.
from New York University and has
taught at Bethune-Cookman College
in Daytona Beach, Florida.
The talk, sponsored by the Insti
tute of International Relations, is
open to the public.
