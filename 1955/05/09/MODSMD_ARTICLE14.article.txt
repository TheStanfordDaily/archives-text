# Medical Fraternity Will Meet Tonight
## 
### 
Sigma Phi Sigma. Stanford pro
medical and prenursing society, will
hear Dr. David L. Bassett, associate
professor of anatomy, tomorrow
night at 8 in Bowman Alumni House.
Dr. Bassett will speak on his new
stereoscopic atlas of anatomy and
will show slides to illustrate his
talk. Refreshments will be served
at the close of the meeting.
