# Friends Secretary To Speak on Draft
## 
### 
George Burcham, an Associate
Peace Secretary for the American
Friends Service Committee, will
speak tomorrow at 4 p.m. on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house.
The topic of Burcham's presenta
tion will be the evidence in support
of objections to the "National Re
serve Plan" which is presently be
fore Congress.
