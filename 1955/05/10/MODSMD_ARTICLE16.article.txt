# Concert Series Seeks Fall Assistant Manager
## 
### 
The Concert Series is interview
ing students for next fall's assistant
manager positions during the rest
of this week at the Nitery from 1 to
5 p.m.
All freshmen and sophomores who
have ushered for the Concert Series
this year are urged to try out by
next year's manager David Sears.
