# Stern Sign-ups To Be Tomorrow
## 
### 
Room sign-ups Tor Stern Hall for
autumn quarter will be held in the
Housing Office, Room 101-A, Inner
Quad, from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
Thursday.
Village and off-campus men in
terested in living in Stern may sign
up at that time. Eating club men
and men who have not completed
45 units of work or who are not in
attendance this quarter are not eli
gible for the sign-up.
Sign-ups for transfer of room de
posits from spring to autumn quar
ter will be held in the men's resi
dences Thursday.
