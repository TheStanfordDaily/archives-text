# Revival Film Series Will Show 'Greed'
## 
### 
The Revival Film Series movie
tonight at 7:30 in Cubberley Audi
torium will be "Greed," based on
Frank Norris' novel "McTeague."
The film, to be shown under the
auspices of the Committee on Pub
lic Exercises, will star Gibson Gow
land, Jean Hersholt, Zasu Pitts, and
Chester Conklin.
