# Play from Spain To Be Presented
## 
### 
A new play from Spain, written
by one of the country's leading
playwrights, will have its first U.S.
performance here Saturday.
The Spanish dramatic class at the
University will present the three
act comedy, "Juego de Nirios," by
Victor Ruiz Iriarte at 8 p.m. in Cub
berley Auditorium.
The production, in Spanish, will
be open to the public without
charge.
Professor Isabel M. Schevill of ed
ucation and Spanish director of
the play.
Members of the cast are Susan
Branard, Mrs. Phyllis Jamison,
Russell Lapham, John Latta, Len
nard Livingston, John Saunders,
Diane Shemanski, and Diana Waite.
