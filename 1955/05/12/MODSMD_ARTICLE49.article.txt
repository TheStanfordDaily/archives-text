# AWS to Discuss Scholarship Cup
## 
### 
There will be an AWS Board meet
ing this afternoon at 4:15 on the
second floor of the Women's Club
house.
Harriet Thomas, AWS President
announced that the major business
will be the awarding of the travel
ing AWS Scholarship cup. The cup
is awarded to the women's living
group with the highest grade point
average for the preceding quarter.
At present Hawthorne House has
the cup. They will give it to Miss
Adele Strogen, Assistant Counselor
for women, who will award it to
the winning house on the basis of
winter quarter's grades.
