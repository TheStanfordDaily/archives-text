# Chem Honorary Picks Members
## 
### 
Three undergraduate women and
a research associate in chemistry
were recently initiated into the local
chapter of lota Sigma Pi. national
honorary society for women in chem
istry.
Janet Katz, Union; Sally Ann Wil
liams, Oaks; and Louise Hinckley,
Lagunita, and Dr. Dorothy Fedlich,
research associate in biochemistry,
were initiated April 27.
