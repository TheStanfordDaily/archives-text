# Sigma Delta Chi Gives $100 Award
## 
### 
Dick Meister, junior journalism
major from San Francisco, was
awarded a SlOO scholarship by the
Northern California Professional
Chapter of Sigma Delta Chi, nation
al journalistic fraternity, Tuesday
night.
Winners of Sigma Delta Chi cer
tificates of distinction and promise
were Larry Jensen, Portland, and
Bob lacopi, Yuba City. Both are
also juniors majoring in journalism.
The awards were made at a dinner
meeting in San Francisco, at which
Turner Catledge, managing editor
of the New York Times, was the
featured speaker.
