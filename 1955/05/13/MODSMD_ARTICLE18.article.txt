# Area Demos Plan Party at Seacliff
## 
###

Stanford Area will hold a beach
party tomorrow from 10:15 a.m. to
(5 p.m. at Seacliff.
Rides will leave from the Cellar
at 10:15 a.m. Anyone interested in
attending may contact Ruth Wolpe
at Union by noon. The cost will
be $1.
