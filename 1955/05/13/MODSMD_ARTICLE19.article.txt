# YWCA Schedules Elections for Monday
## 
### 
YWCA OFFICE-SEEKERS- Pictured above are the women running for 1955-56 offices in the
campus YWCA. They are (back row, left to right) Jeanne Powell, president; Melissa Woodring,
vice-president; Anne Powell, treasurer; (front row) Jackie Rouse, treasurer; Loretta Leong, sec
retary; Trudy King, secretary. Ann Sussex, not pictured, is also running for vice-president. The
elections will be held Monday. Ballots will be distributed to all house presidents. Only 'Y' mem
bers are eligible to vote.
