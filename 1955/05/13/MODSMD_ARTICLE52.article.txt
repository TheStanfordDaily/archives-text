# New Plan Offered to Ed Majors
## 
### 
Stanford's Education Department
is trying out a new experiment
which will give fifth-year education
majors both their teaching expe
rience and a chance to earn money.
The new experimental plan is as
follows: Palo Alto Unified School
District has hired four fifth-year
education majors to teach in the
schools next year. The four stu
dents, Marilyn Willey, Sue McCloud.
Eleanor Genshlea, and Sally Beel,
will each preside over a classroom.
A full-time experienced teacher
has also been hired by the school
system to supervise the work of the
girls.
To prepare for next year's work,
the four girls will teach this sum
mer in the Summer Demonstration
School sponsored by the Stanford
School of Education.
