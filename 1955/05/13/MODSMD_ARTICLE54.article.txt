# Stern Formal Is Tomorrow
## 
### 
"Blue Champagne" will be the
theme of the annual Stern spring
formal to be held at the San Jose
Country Club from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m.
tomorrow.
Howard Fredric and his orchestra
will provide the music for the closed
dance. Tickets are $3 per couple
and may be procured by Stern men
from their wing social chairmen.
