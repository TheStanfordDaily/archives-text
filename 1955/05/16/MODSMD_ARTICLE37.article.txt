# 'Welfare State' Is 'Y' Luncheon Topic
## 
### 
"How Far Should We Go with the
Welfare State?" will be the topic of
the "Y" Public Affairs Luncheon to
morrow noon.
Carl Stover of the Political Sci
ence Department will moderate the
discussion, which will be held in the
"Y" lounge on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse.
