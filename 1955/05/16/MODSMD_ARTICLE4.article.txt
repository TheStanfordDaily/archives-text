# Poll to Evaluate Campus Activities
## 
### 
An activities poll questionnaire,
formulated by the Social Co-ordi
nating Board, will be passed out to
night to about 600 selected students
representing all classes and living
groups.
Students receiving forms
will be asked to fill them out, eval
uating the campus social events of
the current year, and return them
to their living group social chairmen
by Thursday.
The purpose of the activities poll
is to get an over-all consensus of
opinion about these events.
