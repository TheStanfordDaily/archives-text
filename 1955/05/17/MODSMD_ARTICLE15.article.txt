# Karl Shapiro to Speak Thursday
## 
### 
Pulitzer Prize-winning poet Karl
Shapiro will speak here on contem
porary poetry Thursday.
The talk, at 4:15 p.m. in the Ben
der Room of the Main Library, will
be open to the public without
charge.
Shapiro is editor of Poetry maga
zine and consultant to the Library
of Congress. He is the author of
"Trial of a Poet" and "Person, Place,
and Thing."
He will speak at Stanford on the
Intermezzo Series, sponsored joint
ly by the Creative Writing Center
and the Library. Professor Wallace
E. Stegner, author and director of
the Center, will introduce him.
