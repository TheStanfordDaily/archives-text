# 'Welfare Stale' to Be Topic of 'Y' Luncheon
## 
### 
"How Far Should We Go with the
Welfare State?" will be the topic of
the "Y" Public Affairs Luncheon
this noon in the "Y" lounge on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house.
Carl Stover of the Political Sci
ence Department will moderate the
discussion. Lunch will be 30 cents.
