# Mothers to Hear Air Officer Speak
## 
### 
Colonel Ralph W. Keller, profes
sor of air science and tactics, will
speak to the Stanford Mothers' Club
today at 2 p.m. in the Women's
Clubhouse.
A no-host luncheon in the Union
dining room will be held at 12 noon.
Reservations may be made with
Mrs. T. O. Dowdell, DA 3-4914.
