# Paly School Board Elections Friday
## 
### 
Permanent campus residents who
are registered voters are eligible to
cast ballots in the Palo Alto School
District election Friday.
Their polling place is the May field
elementary school on El Camino
Real. The two candidates for the
single vacancy are Mrs. Harriet S.
Emerson of Palo Alto and Dr. Be;i
Page, Stanford professor of geology.
