# 'Rake' Opera Tickets Sell
## 
### 
Tickets are on sale now for the
West Coast premiere of Igor Stra
vinsky's "The Hake's Progress." The
production, presented jointly by the
Stanford Players and the Depart
ment of Music, will play in Memo
rial Auditorium May 26-28.
Tickets are $1 and 51.50 and may
be obtained at the Memorial Hall
box office from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m. to
day.
Heading the cast as the Rake is
tenor Robert Rounseville, who cre
ated that role in the opera's 1951
world premiere in Venice. Rounse
ville is a member of the New York
City Center Opera and starred re
cently in the English movie "The
Tales of Hoffmann."
Ruth Roehr will appear in the
part of Anne Trulove. the Rake's
beloved. Miss Roehr, a graduate of
Stanford, is currently a member of
the San Francisco Opera.
