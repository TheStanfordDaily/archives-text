# Original Play Is Matinee Program
## 
### 
The Speech and Drama Depart
ment will present an original one
act play, "Ship's Table," today at
4:15 p.m. in the Little Theater, as
part of the Theater Matinee series.
The play, which was written by
Bill Burke, concerns racial dis
crimination on a ship. It features a
cast of seven and is directed by
Webster Smalley.
