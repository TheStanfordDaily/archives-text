# Effects of Atom Bomb to Be Told
## 
### 
The effects of the atom bomb on
the younger generation in Hiro
shima and Nagasaki will be dis
cussed tomorrow by Professor Wil
liam W. Greulich of the Anatomy
Department.
The talk, sponsored by the Stan
ford chapter of the Federation of
American Scientists, will be given at
noon in Room 126 of the Electronics
Research Laboratory.
Greulich directed a five - year
postwar study of the Japanese chil -
dren who survived the bombings.
The study was sponsored by the
Atomic Energy Commission.
