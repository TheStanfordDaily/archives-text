# Lagunita to Hold Open House
## 
### 
Lagunita Court will hold an open
house after Saturday night's Spring
Sing.
The affair, entitled "Song of the
Islands," will last from 10 p.m. to
1 a.m.
Entertainment, refreshments, and

music for dancing will be provided
in Lagunita Court, which will be
transformed into a Polynesian at
mosphere in keeping with the
theme.
The open house is open to Stan
ford students, faculty, and staff.
