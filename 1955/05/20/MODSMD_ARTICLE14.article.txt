# Sophs to Trek to Beach for Sunday Reunion
## 
### 
SOPHOMORE REUNION—The map above points the way to '57's last Sophomore Reunion Sun
day from 12:30 to 6 p.m. The beach, three miles south of San Gregorio, will be supplied with
volleyball and baseball equipment. John Masterson will entertain with his guitar. According
to Mack Gordon, chairman, firewood will be furnished at the beach but students should bring
their own food. A sign will bp posted on Highway 1 south of San Gregorio and information may
be obtained by calling Gordon at the Zeta Psi house.
