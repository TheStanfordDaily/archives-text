# Professor to Discuss Atom Bomb Effects
## 
### 
Professor William W. Greulich of
the Department of Anatomy will
discuss his observations of the ef
fects of atom bomb blasts on Hiro
shima and Nagasaki children at an
open meeting of the Stanford chap
ter of the Federation of American
Scientists today.
The meeting will be held in the
lecture room of the Electronics Re
search Laboratory at noon.
