# Summer ASSU
## 
### 
Interviews for summer quarter
officers will be held Wednesday,
Friday, and next Monday from 3 to
4:30 p.m. in the Legislature room.
Officers to be selected are president,
vice-president, and five members
for the Executive Committee. Of
these five, two must be women, and
one must be a graduate student.
Students may sign up for interviews
at the ASSU office.
