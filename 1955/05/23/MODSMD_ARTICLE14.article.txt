# Fall From Roof Injures KA Soph
## 
### 
William R. Lang, Kappa Alpha,
was reported "doiny well" at the
Palo Alto Hospital yesterday, fol
lowing a fall from his fraternity
house Friday afternoon.
Lang was making his way from a
window to an area used as a sun
porch by way of the room when he
lost his balance and fell approxi
mately 30 feet. He was taken to the
hospital by ambulance.
Lang, a sophomore from Salt Lake
City, suffered broken ribs and a
fractured pelvis in the fall.
