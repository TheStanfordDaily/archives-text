# Sammet Named NSA Co-ordinator
## 
### 
Jim Sammet, a junior from New
York, has been selected as National
Students' Association Co-ordinator
for next year by an interviewing
board composed of Pete Bing, Bob
Gable, Bill Tooley, A 1 Wilson.
George Ralph, and A 1 Loomis.
Sammet has served this year as
regional NSA travel director. He
has attended district meetings since
his election as travel director at the
Regional Assembly at UCLA last
November. He also attended the Re
gional Assembly held at Stanford
last month.
