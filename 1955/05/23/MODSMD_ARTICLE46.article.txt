# '55 Announcements
## 
### 
Senior Hall—Tickets for the Sen
ior Ball go on sale Wednesday at
the ASSU office and from senior
class social co-ordinating board
members in the living groups. Tick
ets are $10 apiece.
The senior ball, June 16, will be
held at the Castlewood Country
Club from 9 p.m. to 4 a.m. Break
fast will be served from 2 to 4 a.m.
Ernie Heckscher, Jack Fina, and
Stan Wilson will provide the enter
tainment. Continuous music will be
featured and a trio will play in the
patio from 10 p.m. to 1 a.m.
Senior Scroll—Printed copies of
the Senior Scroll are now on sale
at the ASSU office for 10 cents
apiece.
Senior Reunion—Chez Yvonne has
been secured for the Senior Reunion
Wednesday, June 15. The reunion
will be from 9 p.m. on.
Senior Class Day—Saturday, June
18, will be Senior Class Day with the
traditional activities of reading the
will, the history, and the poem. The
laying of the Class Plate In Inner
Quad will be followed by a Pil
grimage of Commemoration to the
Tomb of the Founders by all seniors.
Senior Ice Cream Sale — Today
from 1 to 4 p.m. at Engineering Cor
ner. Ice cream will cost 10 cents.
Baccalaureate and Commencement
Tickets — Requests for tickets may
be turned in by seniors to the Com
mittee on Public Exercises along
with the card indicating whether
the student will be marching in the
processions. Baccalaureate tickets
are not available for those not par
ticipating in the procession.
