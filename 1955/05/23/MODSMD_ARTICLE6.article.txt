# Interviews
## Cardinals 
### 
Interviews for membership in
Cardinals will continue today and
tomorrow from 3:15 to 4:30 p.m. in
the Cardinals office. Sign-up sheets
are posted in the ASSU office.
Five students will be chosen from
next year's senior class and five
from next year's junior class. The
ratio will be three boys to two girls
in each class.
Qualifications, which will be ta
ken into consideration in the selec
tion of members, according to Car
dinals president Jim Smith, are
availability of time, previous service
to the University, experience, ini
tiative, and ideas.
