# NSA Congress
## 
### 
Interviews for those interested in
attending the National Student Con
gress this summer from Aug. 21 to
31 will be held today from 1 to 6
p.m. in the Legislature room.
All returning students are eligible
to try out for the Congress, which
will be held at the University of
Minnesota in Minneapolis. Travel
ing expenses and fees at the Con
gress will be paid.
