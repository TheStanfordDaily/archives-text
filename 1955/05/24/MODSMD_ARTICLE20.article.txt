# IFC Inspectors
## 
### 
Interfraternity Council is now
taking applications for the two va
cant positions of kitchen inspector
for 1955-56.
Anyone interested in applying
may see Mrs. A. M. Riasetto, Stan
ford Public Health Nurse, Room 75,
Cubberley, from 4 to 5 p.m. daily
before next Monday. All applicants
must be fraternity men. ♦
Duties of the kitchen inspector in
volve checking each of the 24 fra
ternity house kitchens once a quar
ter. Remuneration consists of 24 |
free meals and a $25 salary.
