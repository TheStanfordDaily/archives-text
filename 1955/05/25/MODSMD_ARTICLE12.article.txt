# Interviews
## 
### 
The following organization inter
views will be held today:
Fundamental Standard and Honor
Code Commission interviews will be
held from 3:15 to 5:30 p.m. today
and tomorrow. Students may sign
for interviews in the ASSU office.
AWS interviews for summer offi
cers, standing committee chairmen,
and treasurer will be held today
from 4 to 6 p.m. and 7 to 9 p.m. in
the AWS office in the Women's
Clubhouse. Applications and inter
view sign-up sheets are available
at the ASSU office.
Political Union interviews will be
held from 2 to 5 p.m. in the Politi
cal Union office on the third floor
of the Student Union.

Athletic Board interviews in the
ASSU office, and tryouts will be
held in the Excom room from 4:30
to 6 p.m.
Summer quarter ASSU office in
terviews will be held from 3 to 4:30
p.m. in the Excom room today, Fri
day, and Monday.
