# Top Honors Go To 170 Seniors
## 'Distinction' Awards Based on High Scholastic Achievement 
### 
In recognition of high scholastic achievement, 170 seniors will
receive the bachelor's degree with "great distinction," or with
"distinction," at graduation.
The "great distinction" award is given to approximately the
top five percent of the graduating class, and the "distinction"

award to the next ten per cent.
These awards are based primarily
on the work of the last two years.
Students registered for courses in
the School of Law, for which grades
are not reported until the end of the
year, will be considered for gradua
tion honors during the summer.

receive the bachelor's degree with "great distinction," or with
"distinction," at graduation.
The "great distinction" award is given to approximately the
top five percent of the graduating class, and the "distinction"

award to the next ten per cent.
These awards are based primarily
on the work of the last two years.

the School of Law, for which grades
are not reported until the end of the
