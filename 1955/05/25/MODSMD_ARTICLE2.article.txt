# Frosh Set Beach Reunion Saturday
## 
### 
The last class of '58 get-together
of the year will be held Saturday at
Natural Bridges Beach near Santa
Cruz as the freshmen gather for a
"fun-for-all" stag beach party.
The party, scheduled to last from
noon until 6 p.m., will feature en
tertainment, contests, and food. At
2 p.m. a lunch consisting of hot dogs,
potato salad, soft drinks, and ice
cream will be served. Following
lunch, a treasure hunt and a sand
castle building contest will be held.
Tickets, costing 50 cents with a
class card and $1 without, may be
purchased from wing and corridor
representatives in Branner, Roble,
Encina, and the Village.
