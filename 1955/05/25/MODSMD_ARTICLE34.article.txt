# Block 'S' Banquet
## 
### 
Matt Armitage, senior guard
who broke his leg midway in the
1954 football season, was given the
Jim Reynolds Award for the ath
lete with the most spirit in 1954-
55, at the annual Block "S" Bar
becue Saturday night following
the football scrimmage.
The ceremony also honored
Husky Hunt and Everett Dean,
Stanford coaches who are retir
ing at the end of the current year.
Winners of leadership awards
were Paul Wiggin, football;
George Selleck, basketball; Len
Swartz, baseball; Bud Bradford,
manager, track; Jack Frost, ten
nis; Phil Getchell. golf; Larry
Heim, swimming.
