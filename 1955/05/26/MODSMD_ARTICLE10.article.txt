# More Summer Sponsors Needed
## 
### 
Due to the increase in summer
enrollment, AWS Sponsor Choosing
Committee will hold interviews for
additional women summer quarter
sponsors on Tuesday.
Applications are available today
through Monday at the ASSU office.
All applications are due at 5 p.m.
Monday. Applicants may sign up
for interviews on Tuesday at the
time they turn in application blanks.
Anyone trying out for an AWS or
ASSU position is also eligible to
try out for sponsoring, but v/ould
only be able to hold one of these
positions during summer quarter.
