# AWS Summer Tryouts to End
## 
### 
Today is the final day of tryouts
for AWS summer quarter officers,
AWS standing committee chairmen,
and AWS treasurer.
Application blanks are available
in the ASSU office and interviews
will be held from 4 to 6 p.m. and
7 to 9 p.m. in the AWS office on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house.
Standing committees are housing,
social regulations, election, and
scholarship. Chairmanships are open
to junior and senior women. With
the exception of housing committee,
applicants need not have served on
the committees previously.
Summer quarter AWS officers will
be president, vice-president, secre
tary - treasurer, and chairman of
Women's Council. The AWS treas
urer will serve through next spring
quarter.
