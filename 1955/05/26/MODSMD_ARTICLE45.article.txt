# SENIOR ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
Senior Ball—Tickets are on sale
at the ASSU office and from mem
bers of the senior class co-ordinat
ing board at $10 a couple.
Providing entertainment will be
Ernie Heckscher, Jack Fina, and
Stan Wilson.
Baccalaureate and Commencement
Tickets—Senior requests for tickets
are due at the Committee on Public
Exercises along with a card indi
cating whether the student will be
marching in the processions. No
Baccalaureate tickets will be avail
able for those not participating in
the procession.
Transcripts of Credit — Seniors
needing transcripts of their grades
immediately following graduation
are requested to make application
for them at the Registrar's Office in
order that everything may be set up
now. This way only this quarter's
grades will have to be added.
