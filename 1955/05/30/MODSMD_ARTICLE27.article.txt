# Kitchen Inspector
## 
### 
Today is the deadline for filing
application for interfraternity kitch
en inspector for 1955-56. There are
two openings for fraternity men.
Applicants are to contact Mrs. A.
M. Riasetto, Stanford Public Health
Nurse, Room 75, Cubberley, from 4
to 5 p.m. today.
Duties of the kitchen inspector in
volve checking each of the 24 fra
ternity house kitchens once a quar
ter. Remuneration consists of 24
free meals and a $25 salary.
