# Five Students Join Speech Fraternity
## 
### 
Five members of the Stanfoid
Speech Association were recently
initiated into the local chapter of
Delta Sigma Rho, national honorary
speech fraternity.
Initiates are Leon Ardzrooni, Ar
liss• Barnes, John Falchi, Dave Ja
cobson, and Glenn West.
West will be president, Miss
Barnes, secretary, for the next term.
