# Mothers' Club Gives Scholarships
## 
### 
The Mothers' Club of Stanford
University has made two scholarship
gifts to Stanford totalling $2,500, it
was announced today by Mrs. Nor
man J. Silberling, president.
A gift of $1,500 will provide two
annual full tuition scholarships. The
club also contributed $1,000 to the
Parnie Hamilton Storey Scholarship
Fund.
The late Mrs. Storey was fifth
president of the Mothers' Club. The
scholarship endowment fund was
established in her memory in 1945
by Delta Gamma alumnae of Palo
Alto and other friends.
Scholarship activities of the
Mothers' Club are in addition to its
support of the men's and women's
rest homes on the campus.
