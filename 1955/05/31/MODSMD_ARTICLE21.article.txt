# Tryouts to Start For Grad Posts
## 
### 
The graduate class will hold inter
views today for the positions of pre
reg committee chairman, social
chairman, and publicity chairman.
Interviews will be held from 3 to
5 p.m. in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse and from 7 to
9 p.m. in the Village Council room
in the Administration Building.
Students may sign up for inter
views at the ASSU office or phone
graduate class president Hugh
Smith at DA 3-9451, Ext. 19.
