# Campus Interviews
## Rally Com 
### 
Tryouts for next year's Rally
Committee will be held tomorrow
from 3 to 6 p.m. and Friday from 1
to 6 p.m. in the Rally Committee
office, according to chairman Don
Crowell.
Any interested freshmen or soph
omores are eligible to try out. Those
who wish to be interviewed must
sign up for an appointment on the
list posted outside the ASSU office.
They must also fill out an applica
tion blank, and bring it with them
to the interview.
