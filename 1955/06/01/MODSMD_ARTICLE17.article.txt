# Luncheon Slated For Tomorrow By Distaff Club
## 
### 
The first of two final luncheons
of the school year will be held by
Distaff Club tomorrow in the Stan
ford Union Dining Room.
Next Wednesday the bring-your
sandwich luncheon will take place
in the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse, where coffee will be
served by Faculty Women's Club
sponsors of Distaff Club.
On Tuesday, Distaffers will be
guests at a Fashion Show being pre
sented by the Law Wives in co
operation with the Clothes Closet.
The show will start at 8:30 p.m. in
the Stanford Women's Clubhouse.
