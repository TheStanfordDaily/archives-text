# Israel to Be Topic Of Speech Today
## 
### 
Mrs. Ruth Goldschmidt, public
affairs officer of the Israeli Consul
ate-General's office in New York
will speak on "The Life and Culture
of Israel" today at 4:15 p.m. in the
lower lounge of the Women's Club
house.
Among her other activities, Mrs.
Goldschmidt reported the 1947 Arab-
Israeli war for the Jewish News
Agency, has handled public relations
for the Weitzmann Institute, and
currently is teaching a course for
the New York City Board of Educa
tion.
