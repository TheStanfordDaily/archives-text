# NROTC Society Plans Beach Party
## 
### 
The Quarterdeck Society of Naval
ROTC mid hipmen will hold a beach
party in commemoration of the end
of the year this Saturday at Sleepy
Lagoon Beach near Davenport.
Featuring games, contests, steaks,
and other attractions, the party is
primarily a date affair. Admission
is free to middies with Quarterdeck
Society cards; those who are not
members will be charged $1.50. Ad
mission for dates is $1.25.
