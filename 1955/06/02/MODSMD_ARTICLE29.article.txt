# SIRO to Start Drive Monday
## 
### 
The annual SIRO drive to collect
clothing, books, and magazines for
overseas war or disaster stricken
areas will get under way Monday.
The drive, conducted by the Stan
ford International Relief Organiza
tion, a branch of the student Insti
tute of International Relations, will
run through June 15.
Boxes will be placed at the IIR
building and student residences on
campus and at the SIRO building in
Stanford Village.
