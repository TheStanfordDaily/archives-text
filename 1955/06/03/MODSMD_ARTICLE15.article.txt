# Fall Pre-Reg Ends at Noon Tomorrow
## 
### 
Lower division pre-registra
tion for autumn quarter will end

last day for students to see their
advisers, which they must do
before signing class lists.
The following pre-registration pro
cedure for lower division students
has been announced by the Regis
trar's Office:
1. The student must have an ad
vance conference with his adviser
during his office hours today. The
appointment time on the pre-regis
tration permit is for signing class
iists, not for seeing advisers.
2. At the time of his conference,
the student must obtain an official
study list card from his adviser and
fill out his program in ink for his
Suviser's approval and signature.
CLASS LISTS
3. If the student's study list in
cludes any courses marked with an
"S" in the autumn time schedule, he
must sign the appropriate class lists
at the information desk, Registrar's
Office, Room 111-A. between 8 a.m.
and noon today and tomorrow or be
tween 1 and 5 p.m. today.
Appointments for signing class
lists continue through tomorrow
noon. The student may sign class
lists at the time given on the permit
mailed to him or any time thereafter.
His name on the class list assures
him of admission to the class pro
vided he is present at the first class
meeting. If the student did not re
ceive a permit, he should go to the
Lower Division Office, Room 111-D.
STUDY LIST
4. The student must turn in his
official study list card, in person, to
the check-out clerk at the informa
tion desk before tomorrow noon.
