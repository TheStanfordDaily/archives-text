# Frosh Track
## 
### 
The frosh cindermen, coached by
Ray Dean in his last year at Stan
ford, chalked up a five-won, one
lost record and wound up a success
ful season by whipping favored Cal
ifornia, 71-60.
The Papoose thinclads were led
by record - setters Ray Williamson
and Maury Graves, Chuck Cobb,
Kent Hansen, Jim Hart, and Don
Richardson.
Co-captain Graves paced off a 9:34
two-mile against the Cal Bearcubs
to set a Little Big Meet record
against the two schools. Williamson
twice broke Bob Mathias' freshman
shot put record during the spring,
his best put being 49-9.
