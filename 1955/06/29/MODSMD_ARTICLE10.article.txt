# Lagunita to Host Open House on Saturday Night
## 
### 
Lagunita Court will host an ASSU
AWS-sponsored open house Satur
day night from 8:30 to 12 featuring
music and dancing, refreshments,
and a bonfire and marshmallow
roast.
The open house, to be held in La
gunita's inner court, is a no-date
affair and is open to the whole cam
pus. Music will be provided by a
four- or five-piece combo.
Dress is casual. Entertainment for
the open house will include ping
pong, cards, television, and informal
singing with piano accompaniment.
According to Kay Vinson, AWS
summer quarter vice-president, "it
is just an open house for everyone
to get acquainted and have a good
time."
