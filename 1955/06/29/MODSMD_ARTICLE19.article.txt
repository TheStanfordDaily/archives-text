# University Facilities To Close on Fourth
## 
### 
The Stanford Bookstore, Cellar,
post office, Union office, and libraries
will close their doors and bow to the
Fourth of July festivities on Mon
day.
The snack bar outside the Cellar
will be open from 7:30 a.m. to 4:30
p.m. as usual, and the Union cafete
ria in Union Residence will open
from 12 to 12:45 p.m. for lunch and
from 6 to 6:30 p.m. for dinner.
