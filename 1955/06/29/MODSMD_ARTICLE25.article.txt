# Folk, Square Dance Program to Begin
## 
### 
The Stanford summer folkdancing
program will begin tonight at a "get
acquainted" folk and square dance
from 8 to 9:30.
Mrs. Miriam Lidster of the De
partment of Physical Education for
Women will be the instructor for
the squares and mixers which will
be danced in the patio of the Wom
en's Gymnasium. It will be spon
sored by the ASSU, and is open to
all students and faculty.
On Friday there will be student
conducted folk and square dancing
from 8 to 11 p.m. in the Village Au
ditorium. Elementary and interme
diate dances will be done, under the
sponsorship of the Stanford Folk
dancers.
Similar programs will be held the
next three Wednesdays and every
Friday during the quarter.
