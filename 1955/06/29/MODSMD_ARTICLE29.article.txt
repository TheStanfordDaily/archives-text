# Men's Pool Hours
## 
### 
Open hours at the men's pool are
Monday through Saturday from 12
noon to 2 p.m. and Sunday from 1
to 4 p.m.
All registered men students, fac
ulty men and sons may use the pool.
Sons must be accompanied by their
faculty father, the Physical Educa
tion Department detailed.
Faculty men must have an athletic
card, which may be obtained for
$3.50 at the Department of Athletics.
