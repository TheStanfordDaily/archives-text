# 'Medieval Family' To Start Tuesday Evening Series
## 
### 
A noted English scholar, Henry S.
Bennett, will be the opening speaker
on the summer quarter Tuesday
Evening Series July 5 at 8 p.m. in
Memorial Hall.
His lecture on "A Medieval Eng
lish Family" will be one of six free
public programs sponsored by the
Committee on Public Exercises.
Bennett, who is a fellow of Em
manuel College, Cambridge, also
holds a readership in English litera
ture at the British university. He is
an authority on English literature
and social history of the late Middle
Ages and the author of several
books, the latest of which is "Eng
lish Books and Readers, 1475 to
1557," published in 1952. He was a
visiting professor at the University
of Chicago this spring and will be at
the Huntington Library in Pasadena
this summer.
