# Marais, Miranda Folksong Concert Scheduled Here
## 
### 
International folksong singers Ma
rais and Miranda will give a recital
at Memorial Auditorium next Fri
day, July 15, at 8:30 p.m.
Their program wiil consist of hu
morous and serious songs from a va
riety of countries, sung partly in the
original language and partly in Eng
lish.
The couple have appeared in con
certs and in television. They have
also sung on the racio and made re
cordings of their work.
MaraLs began his career by sing
ing South African folk songs for the
British Broadcasting Corporation.
His wife, Miranda Barchuch de la
Pardo, is a former piano teacher.
They both have appeared with the
Voice of America.
Tickets for the concert are on sale
at the Peninsula Box Office in Palo
Alto and at the Memorial Auditorium
box office from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m.,
Monday through Friday. Price is
$1.50. All seats are reserved.
