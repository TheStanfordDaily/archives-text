# Sunday Service
## 
### 
The Rev. Henry M. Shires of the
Episcopal Theological School, Cam
bridge, Massachusetts, will deliver
the sermon at Memorial Church this
Sunday for the 11 a.m. service of
worship.
During the service the Memorial
Church Choir will provide anthems.
The choir is under the direction of
Professor Herbert B. Nanney, Uni
versity organist.
