# Organ Recital
## 
### 
An organ recital will be presented
in Memorial Church at 4 p.m. Sun
day. Professor Herbert B. Nanney,
University organist, will play works
representing a varied program of
organ music, from the sixteenth to
the twentieth century.
Music by Cavazzoni, Bach, Men
delssohn, Schumann, Cesar Franck,
Roger, and Piston will be included
in the recital.
