# Contract Officer Appointment Made In Business Office
## 
### 
Thomas W. Ford, University of
Michigan law school graduate, has
been appointed contract administra
tive officer in the Stanford business
office, it was announced recently by
President Wallace Sterling.
Ford, who has been practicing law
for the past five years in Youngs
town, Ohio, will handle financial,
patent, and other non-scientific as
pects of University research con
tracts, according to Business Mana
ger Alf E. Brandin.
These contracts, approximating
$4,000,000 annually, are held with
private industry and such govern
ment agencies as the National Sci
ence Foundation, Atomic Energy
Commission, and the military serv
ices.
