# Cashier to Begin Summer Schedule
## 
### 
Starting Monday, when all room
ind board bills are expected to have
been paid, the Cashier's Office will
operate on a reduced schedule.
The new hours are 10 a.m. to 12:30
o.m. and 1:30 to 3 p.m. A similar
schedule was in operation summer
quarter last year. This provided ad
equate service, the Controller found.
Opportunities for cashing checks
will henceforth be limited to the
hours listed above at the Cashier's
Office, and from 10 a.m. to 12 noon
ind 2 to 4 p.m. at the Bookstore
office. Checks up to $5 are cashed
it the Bookstore counters and in the
Cellar snack bar at all open hours.
