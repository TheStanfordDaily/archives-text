# Burmese Prime Minister to Visit Stanford Today
## 
### 
Prime Minister U Nu of Burma,
now on a month-long tour of the
United States, will visit here this
afternoon.
The Burmese official and his party
will come to the campus at 3 as
guests of President Wallace Ster
ling, following a luncheon at the
Burlingame home of Brayton Wil
bur, head of the Asia Foundation.
After inspecting the campus and
viewing the Santa Clara Valley
from Hoover Tower, Mr. Nu will
meet with Stanford deans and fac
ulty members at an invitational re
ception in the Lou Henry Hoover
Room of the Library.
James Barrington, Burmese am
bassador to the United States, will
accompany Mr. Nu.
