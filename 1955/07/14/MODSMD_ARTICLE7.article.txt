# Chamber Music
## 
### 
The Chapiber Music Ensemble,
composed of students in the summer
Chamber Music Institute of the
Stanford Music Department, will
give a concert Friday, July 22, at
8:15 p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium.
Jane Hohfeld, San Francisco pianist,
will be guest artist. There will be
no admission charge.
