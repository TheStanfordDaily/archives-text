# Christie Play to Be Given in Palo Alto
## 
### 
"Ten Little Indians," a mystery
play by Agatha Christie, will be pre
sented tomorrow and Saturday at
the Palo Alto Community Theater.
Curtain time is 8:30 p.m. The the
ater is located on Middlefield Road
and Melville Avenue, Palo Alto.
This play will be the eighth an
nual production of the Teen Play
ers, a group of college and high
school students from the Bay Area.
Tickets are on sale for $1.20. They
may be reserved by telephoning the
Palo Alto Community Theater, DA
3-2300, between 1 and 5 in the after
noon and after 7:30 in the evening.
