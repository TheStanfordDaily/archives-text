# Museum Acquires Two Renaissance Paintings
## 
### 
Acquisition of two Renaissance
paintings was announced recently by
the University. The paintings are
"The Crowning of Mary" by Sebas
tian del Piombo (1485-1547) and
"The Head of an Apostle" by Jacob
Jordaens (1593-1678). They are gifts
of Mr. and Mrs. Stewart Marshall of
Palo Alto, who earlier gave two
similar canvases to Stanford.
