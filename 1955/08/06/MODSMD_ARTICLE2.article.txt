# Galvez St. Closed For Widening Job
## 
### 
Galvez Street next to Stanford
Stadium will be closed to traffic
starting today as workmen begin a
$29,000 widening job.
The street will be widened to four
lanes from El Camino Real to Ar
boretum Road, which connects Gal
vez with the Stanford Shopping
Center.
Traffic islands and street lighting
will also be installed at the Galvez-
Arboretum intersection.
During the work traffic will be
detoured from El Camino to cam
pus by way of Palm Drive.
