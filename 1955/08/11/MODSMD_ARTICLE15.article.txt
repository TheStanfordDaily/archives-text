# Alumni Directory Sells 4,000 Copies
## 
### 
Approximately 4,000 copies of the
first Stanford Alumni Directory to
be published in 25 years have been
purchased in a special pre-publica
tion sale.
The directory, listing the nearly
70,000 Stanford alumni, is a joint
publication of the University and the
Alumni Association.
Copies may be ordered from the
Alumni Association at Bowman
Alumni House.
