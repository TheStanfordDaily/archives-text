# Bookstore Hours. Extend Next Week
## 
### 
The Stanford Bookstore will be
open from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. Monday
through Thursday next week for the
convenience of students stocking up
for fall quarter. On Friday the
hours will be 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Satur
day hours will be 8 a.m. to noon.
Students are advised to fill out
checks in advance and buy books
early, according to Mrs. Helene Bau
chou, bookstore manager.
