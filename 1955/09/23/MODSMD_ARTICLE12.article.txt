# A 						Phi O to Hold Fall Book Sales
## 
### 
Alpha Phi Omega, service frater
nity, will hold its Autumn Quarter
book exchange Monday through
Thursday from 9 a.m. to noon and
from Ito 4 p.an. in the main Library.
Students may buy and sell used
textbooks through the fraternity at
their headquarters behind the main
stairway in the. library.
Norman Kibbe, chairman of the
non - profit project, said freshmen
may purchase used Western Civili
zation textbooks at the exchange.
The service fraternity is composed
of former Boy Scouts who render
services to the campus. The book
exchange is one of their quarterly
projects.
