# Stanford Bookstore Announces Hours
## 
### 
The Stanford Bookstore will be
open from S a.m. to 8 p.m. through
Thur day. Regular hours will be
resumed Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.,
and Saturdays S am., to noon.

Bauchou advises students to fill
out checks in advance and to buy
