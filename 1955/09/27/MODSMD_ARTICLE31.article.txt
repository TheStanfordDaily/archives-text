# Music Tryouts To Be Held Today
## 
### 
Tryouts will be held today for |
positions in the orchestra, chorus |
and choir, and voice divisions of the
Music Department.
Students may apply for orchestra
openings to Sandor Salgo in Room
100 at the Knoll from 2 to 4 p.m.
today. Additional interviews will be
held from 4 to 6 p.m. tomorrow, and
4 to 5 p.m. Thursday.
Several student positions are open j
in the University chorus and choir. J
Applicants may see Harold Schmidt j
in the Choir Room of Memorial
Church today from 10 a.m. to noon
and 2 to 4 p.m.
Choir manager's post, which pays j
a small salary, is open and those in- !
terested may see Schmidt during }
the hours for choir tryouts.
Piano accompanists will be inter
viewed during the choir tryouts. j
Those interested in voice tryouts j
may see Otto Schulmann in Room j
100 at the Knoll today from 8:30
a.m. to 4 p.m.
