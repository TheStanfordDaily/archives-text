# Tractor Cuts Up Lawn at Stillman
## 
### 
Lawn in front of Stillman House
was cut up Sunday night when an
unidentified prankster drove a heavy
tractor in front of the house from
the construction site of the Florence
Moore Hall.
Police said that anyone caught
moving equipment from construction
sites on campus will face discipli
nary action. A similar incident had
occurred two weeks before when a
tractor was parked in front of the
Zeta Psi house.
