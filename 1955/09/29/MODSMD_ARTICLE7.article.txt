# Lag Fair to Sell White Elephants
## 
### 
One of the main features of Satur
day night's Lagunita Farm Fair will
be the white elephant auction, pre
sided over by auctioneers H. Donald
Winbigler, dean of students, and
Elva Faye Brown, dean of women.
The fair will open at 8 p.m. and
end at midnight, with all proceeds
going to the Austrian village of St.
Johann, which Lagunita adopted just
after World War 11. Other attrac
tions include a W. C. Fields movie,
booths, games of skill, and a seance.
Overall chairman of the Fair is
Paulette Grantham.
