# Air Raid Blast To Be Sounded Today at Eleven
## 
### 
Today at 11 a.m. the Santa
Clara County Fire Control will
sound an air raid blast. The siren
will sound at the campus Fire
house at 11 a.m. There will be a
1-minute solid blast, 1 minute of
silence, then a 1-minute oscil
lating blast.
No students will be involved
in the test. This is only to test
the sirens and equipment.
