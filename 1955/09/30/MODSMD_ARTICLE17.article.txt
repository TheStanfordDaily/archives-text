# Explosion Rocks San Francisco Area
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO—A thunderous
explosion jarred San Francisco from
end to end here tonight, touching
ufi what apparently was one of the
most monumental false alarms in the
city's history.
After a solid hour of searching for
the source of the blast, police re
ported they could not find where as
much as a small firecracker had
gone off.
