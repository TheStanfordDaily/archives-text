# Life's Alternatives Is Sermon Topic
## 
### 
Professor Robert E. Fitch, from
the Pacific School of Religion, Berke
ley, will be guest preacher at Me
morial Church Sunday. His topic
will be "The Alternatives of Life."
Anthems will be provided by the
Memorial Church Choir, under the
direction of Harold C. Schmidt, at
the 11 a.m. service.
Sunday will be known as Matric
ulation Sunday, and a special Holy
Communion Service will be held at
9 a.m.
