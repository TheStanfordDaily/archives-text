# Interviews and Tryouts
## Asst. Financial Manager 
### 
Applications are available now in
the ASSU office for the position of
assistant student financial manager.
Applicants must have the completed
forms returned to the ASSU office
by noon Saturday, Oct. 8.
Any sophomore, junior or senior
student Is eligible for the job, which
carries with it a $35-a-month salary.
Bill Toolcy, student financial man
ager, will make the appointment.
As his assistant the applicant chosen
will aid in the coordination of the
work of the student treasurers and
social chairmen.
