# Cosmo Coffee Hour
## 
### 
Cosmo Club's first CofTeo Hour of
autumn quarter will be held this
afternoon from 4 to (! in Bowman
Alumni House.
Erdogan Altan, Cosmo Club's pres
ident, stressed the fact that all Stan
ford students are invited to come—
not just foreign students. The pur
pose of the Coffee Hours is to let
foreign students meet American stu
dents and vice versa.
