# Medical School Applicant Test To Be Held Oct. 31
## 
### 
The Medical College Admission
Test, sponsored by the Association
of American Medical Colleges, will
be administered on Oct. 31 for the
benefit of the 1956 Medical School
applicants who failed to take the
test when it was given in May.
Application blanks and copies of
the Bulletin of Information may be
obtained from the Medical Admis
sions Office, Room 112-D.
Applications for the test must be
received at the Princeton office of
the Educational Testing Service on
or before Oct. 17. No application
can be accepted if it is received after
this closing date.
Only applicants for admission to
classes beginning the study of medi
cine in the fall of 1956 who did not
take the test in May are to take it ,
at this time.
Students wishing to apply to the
Stanford School of Medicine are
urged to submit applications as soon
as possible. Application blanks and
information may be obtained at the
Medical Admissions Office, Room
112-D.
