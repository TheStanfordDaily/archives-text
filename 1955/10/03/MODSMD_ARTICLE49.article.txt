# Rally Committee
## 
### 
Freshman tryouts for membership
on the Rally Committee will be held
this week in the Rally Com office,
in the ASSU offices at Union.
First day of interviews, which will
be limited to seven minutes each, is
today, with hours 1 to 5 p.m. To
morrow's hours are 1 to 5 and 7 to
10 p.m.; Wednesday's, 1 to 5 p.m.
Among the Rally Corn's duties
are management of football rallies,
card stunts, ushering at games, and
publicity. The cheer leaders and
pom-pom girls are Rally Com mem
bers.
