Smart guys
and dolls ...
. . . forget bill-paying blues and bother with a Bank
of America Tenplan Checking Account. With Ten
plan you get ten checks for only $1.25. There's no
monthly service charge or minimum balance required.
Just keep enough on deposit to cover the checks you
write. The handy wallet pack helps you keep track
of your expenses. To open a Tenplan Account, visit
the Palo Alto Branch of neighbor Bank of America.
If you desire, your account may be transferred to the
new Stanford Branch to be opened at the first of the
year.
