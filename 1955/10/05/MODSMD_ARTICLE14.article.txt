# Caucus to Hear Lend-Lease Man
## 
### 
Political Union Independents will
sponsor a speech by Major George
"Racey" Jordan this Friday at 4 p.m.
in Cubberley Auditorium.
Jordan was Lend-Lease expediter
for aid to Russia during World
War 11.
In 1949-50 he testified before the
congressional Un-American Activi
ties Committee regarding the use of
the lend-lease to give the Russians
atomic secrets and materials. He is
author of "Major Jordan's Diaries."
