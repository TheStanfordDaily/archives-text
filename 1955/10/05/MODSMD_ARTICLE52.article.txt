# Chinese Paintings Are On Exhibit at Gallery
## 
### 
An exhibit on contemporary Chi
nese paintings is now being shown
at the Stanford Art Gallery. The
display was first assembled in 1948
in China for exhibition in the Metro
politan Museum of Art.
The art gallery is open Tuesday
through Saturday, 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.,
and Sunday, 12 noon to 4 p.m. It is
closed on Mondays.
