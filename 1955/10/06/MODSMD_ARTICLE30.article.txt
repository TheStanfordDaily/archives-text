# WAA Reorganizes, Gets New Name
## 
### 
The Women's Athletic Association
has reorganized its constitution and
is now known as Women's Recrea
tional Association.
Main activities planned for this
quarter, according to President Sally
Phillips, are light buffet dinners and
recreation between women students
and faculty members from all de
partments on Wednesday evenings.
The WRA will also sponsor the
intramural volleyball tournaments
among the women's living groups
this quarter. On Nov. 5 there will
be a Sports Day in San Jose, fea
turing participation in folk-dancing,
swimming, volleyball, hockey, tennis
and archery.
