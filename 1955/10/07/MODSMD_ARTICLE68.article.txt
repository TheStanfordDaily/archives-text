# Alpine Practice Climb To Instruct Beginners
## 
### 
The Stanford Alpine Club will
hold a practice climb at Hunter's
Hill this Sunday. This climb is in
tended to instruct beginners in safe
climbing practices. All new mem
bers and those interested in joining
the club are invited to attend.
Those going will meet on History
I Corner at 8 a.m. They are advised
to wear old clothes and tennis shoes,
! and to bring sack lunches.
