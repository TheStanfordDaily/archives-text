# Marshall Award Interviews Slated
## 
### 
John A. Ford. British consul in
San Francisco, will be visiting at
the law school'today to interview
law students interested in applying
for the Marshall scholarships.
Ford will lunch with members
of the law faculty and then meet
with the interested students at 1:30
in the office of the Dean of the Law
School.
Three Marshall scholarships will
be awarded in the Pacific region.
They are given for a United King
dom university for two years, with
a possible extension to three.
The scholarships amount to ap
proximately $2,500 each per year.
They are granted by the English
government as an expression of
gratitude for Marshall Plan aid.
Scholarships may be awarded to
United States citizens of both sexes.
Married students are eligible.
