# Horn Will Address Poli Union Demos
## 
### 
At the initial meeting of the Politi
cal Union Democrats tonight Dr.
Robert A. Horn will speak on the
Federal Employee Security program.
Dr. Horn, an assistant professor of
political science and an expert on
civil rights and the Constitution, will
discuss recent developments in the
program.
The Poli Union Caucus will be
held at 7:30 p.m. in Bowman Alumni
House, according to Helen Dewar,
president of the group. Refreshment,
will be served.
