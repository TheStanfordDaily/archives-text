# 'Orpheus' Opens Intermezzo Series
## 
### 
The autumn Intermezzo Series
will begin tomorrow with Jean Coc
teau's "Orpheus," a French film with
English sub - titles, in Cubberley
Auditorium at 4:15 p.m.
The series is sponsored by the
Stanford Libraries and the Ger
manic and Romanic Languages De
partment. A foreign film will be
shown every two weeks. No ad
mission is charged.
"Eroica," the story of Beethoven,
a German movie with English sub
titles. is scheduled for Oct. 27.
