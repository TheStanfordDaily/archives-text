# Poli Union Board Will Meet Today
## 
### 
Political Union executive board
will meet at 4 this afternoon in the
lounge of the ASSU building, with
Ernie Hartz presiding.
Plans will be completed for a din
ner preceding a speech to Poli Union
by Senator William F. Knowland
scheduled for Oct. 24.
