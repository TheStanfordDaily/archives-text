# Organ Recitals To Begin Sunday
## 
### 
The first in a series of four autumn
quarter organ recitals will be pre
sented Sunday at 4 p.m. in Memorial
Church.
Herbert B. Nanney, University or
ganist, will play Johann Froberger's
Toccata in D Minor; two chorale
preludes by Buxtehude; the Bach
Toccata, Adagio and Fugue in C
Major; a Capriccio by Sweelinck;
"Comest Thou, Jesus" by Bach; and
the "Grand Jeu" by Du Mage. The
program will close with the Fifth
Concerto in F Major by Handel.
The second program will be given
Friday, Oct. 21, at 8:15 p.m. by Wil
liam C. Teague in his first appear
ance on the West Coast.
