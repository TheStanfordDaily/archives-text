# Literary Magazine Seeks Secretary
## 
### 
The Stanford literary magazine,
which will be published in January,
is in urgent need of a secretary to
atsist the business manager, accord
ing to Linda Lovelace, editor.
Interested persons may see Hal
Hansen in the literary magazine of
fice, Room 335, top floor of the Main
Library, from 10 to 11 a.m. daily, or
Linda Lovelace, who is in the office
from 1 until 2 p.m. daily.
Anyone wishing to submit short
story, poetry, or article may bring
manuscripts to the literary magazine
office or give them to Wilfred Stone
in Room 53.
