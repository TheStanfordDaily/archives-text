# Quad Photos
## 
### 
Today is the last day that fresh
men can get their pictures taken for
the Frosh Book and The Quad.
Any Wilbur freshman or any other
freshman who has not yet, had his or
her picture taken may report to
Lounge No. 3 in Stern Hall from
9 a.m. to 12 noon or from 1 to 5 p.m.
Tomorrow through Friday, Wo
men's Row residents may report to
the Quad office to be photographed.
Proper dress for the pictures in
cludes coat and tie for men and a
white blouse with sleeves for the
women.
