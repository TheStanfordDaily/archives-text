# Grad Student Cited For Drunk Driving
## 
### 
Hugh L. Smith of 1001 Cowper
Street, a graduate student in me
chanical engineering, was cited for
drunk and reckless driving early
Sunday morning after striking four
parked cars on Galvez Street.
Police said that Smith was north
bound on Galvez Street at 4 a.m.
when he inexplicably crossed the
white line and smashed into the four
cars, which were diagonally parked
on the other side of the street.
