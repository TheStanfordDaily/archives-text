# Concert Tickets Are Now Available
## 
### 
Students who have ordered season
tickets for the Stanford Concert
Series may pick them up now at
the Concert Series office in the
Nitery from 1 to 5 p.m., Monday
through Friday.
First concert of the season will
present Leonard Warren, Metropoli
tan Opera Company baritone, on
Oct. 31.
Student ushers are still needed
for the Warren concert, according
to Manager Dave Sears. Ushers are
required to put in two hours' work
at the Concert Series office. They arc
admitted free to the concert.
