# New Stern Patio Addition Finished
## 
### 
Stern Hall now has a new patio,
which was under construction for
nearly two months.
This addition was made possible
by funds from the Lucie Stern Es
tate at an estimated cost of $10,400,
and is located between the lounges
of wings 2 and 3.
Men responsible for the job are
architects Eld rich T. Spencer and
William Clement Ambrose.
