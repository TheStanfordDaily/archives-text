# Folk Dancers Plan To Learn 'Hambo'
## 
### 
The Stanford Folk and Square
Dancers will hold their weekly
meeting tonight from 7:30 to 9 in
the dance studio of the Women's
Gymnasium.
The group's main activity this
week will be to learn Hambo, a
European dance which will be taught
by Mrs. Miriam Lidster, the group's
director, who has just returned from
the seventh International Folk Dan
cers Camp, at Stockton.
The program will also include re
view of Scottish and other dances.
All interested persons are invited.
