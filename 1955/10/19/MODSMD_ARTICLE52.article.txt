# UCLA Game Flicks On View Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford's third-quarter comeback
in the UCLA game may be seen to
night again when Cardinals present
their weekly football movies at 7:30
p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium. All
students are invited. There is no
charge.
Coach Joe Ruetz, who was intro
duced at last week's Homecoming
rally, will narrate tonight's film, via
loudspeakers placed in the audi
torium. Head YelJ Leader Jim Wal
ters will be present to discuss his
plans for the year, before the show
ing of the movie.
The program is expected to take
approximately 1 hour 15 minutes.
This is the second in a series of
five Wednesday evening football
movies sponsored by Cardinals under
the direction of Bill Matthews.
