# Shell Announces Fund For Science Teachers
## 
### 
To help offset the dangerous cut
back in science teaching at the sec
ondary school level, the Shell Com
panies Foundation yesterday an
nounced a $50,000 grant to the Uni
versity to establish fellowships for
high'school teachers of science and
mathematics.
The grant will finance an eight
week session for 30 teachers during
the summer of 1956. The Foundation
plans to make additional grants to
continue the program in future
years.
