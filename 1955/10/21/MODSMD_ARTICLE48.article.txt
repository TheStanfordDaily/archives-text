# Concerts Set For Quarter
## 
### 
Eight performances, all open to
the public free of charge, are on the
autumn quarter musical calendar of
the Music Department.
Two chamber music concerts are
bein<? prepared under the direction
of Professor Sandor Salgo. They will
be given Oct. 30 and Nov. 13 at 4
p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium.
Herbert B. Nanney, University or
ganist, will present recitals Nov. 20
and Dec. 11 in Memorial Church at
4 p.m.
On Nov. 29 the University Chorus,
led by Professor Harold C. Schmidt,
will appear on the Tuesday Evening
Series in Memorial Hall at 7:30 p.m.
Two special Christmas music pro
grams are scheduled for Memorial
Church. The Stanford Chorale will
sing Wednesday evening, Dec. 7, and
the Memorial Church Choir will give
its traditional Christmas program on
Sunday, Dec. 11.
The Stanford Symphony Orches
tra, directed by Salgo, will present
a concert Dec. 4 at 8:15 p.m. in
Memorial Hall. .
