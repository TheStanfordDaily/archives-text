# Farm Folk Dancers Will Meet Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Folkdancers will
continue their teaching of beginning
and intermediate folk dancing to
night at 8 in the Village Auditorium.
Dances tonight will include a Swe
dish Hambo, the Scottish, Gay Gor
dons, a basic polka and a Danish
HornfuTer, according to program
chairman Pat Hofmann.
Another dance to be featured is
the Vrtelka Czardas, which was
taught to the group during the Last
three weeks.
