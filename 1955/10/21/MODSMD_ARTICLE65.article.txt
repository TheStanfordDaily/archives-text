# Baritone Will Sing At Hadassah Benefit
## 
### 
Michael Loring, baritone singer,
will give a concert Sunday at. 8:30
p.m. in the Palo Alto High School
auditorium. The concert is being
staged by the Sequoiah Chapter of
Hadassah.
Proceeds from the concert will
support Hadassah's non - sectarian
Medical Relief program. Tickets are
priced at $1.50 and may be obtained
by calling DA 5-6926.
