# Gaieties Mail-Order Sales End Tomorrow
## 
### 
Mail-order ticket sales for Ram's
Head's "Big Game Gaieties" will end
tomorrow, business manager Don
Yates has announced.
All order blanks must be received
by Ram's Head before tomorrow
night.
As soon as mail orders are filled,
individual tickets will be sold at the
Memorial Hall box office.
Block tickets are still available
for Tuesday and Wednesday nights.
Order blanks are obtainable from
living group officers.
