# Texas Students Form Campus Club
## 
### 
The Texas Club, a group to be
formed for students from the Lone
Star state, will hold its first meeting
tomorrow at 5:15 p.m. in the Nitery.
The group will organize at that
time and discuss plans for the quar
ter, which include chartering a plane
home for Christmas vacation and
several parties.
