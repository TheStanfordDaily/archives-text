# Quad Photos
## 
### 
Quad pictures of Branner and La
gunita upper-classwomen, Roble and
Branner sponsors, and Lagunita res
ident assistants will be taken to
morrow and Thursday from 9 a.m.
day through Thursday from 9 a.m.
to 12 noon and from 1 to 5 p.m.
Union and Union Unit pictures
will be taken Friday at the same
times.
All who wish their pictures in the
Quad must have them taken on their
assigned days, Editor Tom Dant said.
Once proofs have been returned,
students have three days in which
to turn them in.
