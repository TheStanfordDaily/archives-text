# Quad Photos
## 
### 
Quad pictures of Branner and La
gunita uppcr-classwomen, Roble and
Branner sponsors, and Lagunita res
ident assistants will be taken today
from 9 a.m. to 12 noon and from 1
to 5 p.m.
Union and Union Unit pictures
will be taken tomorrow at the same
times.
Anyone who hasn't had his picture
taken when previously scheduled
may have one taken this week.
Students must bring $1.25 to the
Quad office when they appear for
the pictures, according to editor Tom
Dant.
Anyone who has paid for his 1955
Quad and did not pick it up in Jimp
may do so Thursday from 1 to 5 p.m.
in the ASSU office.
