# Theta Chi Corner Closed to Traffic
## 
### 
Stanford Police Chief Gordon
Davis announced yesterday that the
intersection of Alvarado Row and
Arguello Road will be closed start
ing this morning.
The intersection is in front of the
Theta Chi house.
Davis said that the contractor at
work on the new peripheral road
would try to have the intersection
open to one-way traffic at 8 in the
morning and 5 in the afternoon.
The rest of the day, drivers will
be forced to use Lasuen Street, or
enter the* Quad area from the Palo
Alto side.
