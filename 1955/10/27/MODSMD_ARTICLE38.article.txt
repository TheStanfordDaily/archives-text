# Tryouts Start Today For Theatre Matinee
## 
### 
Tryouts will be held today and
tomorrow for "This Way to Christ
inas," the last Wednesday Matinee
to be presented this quarter.
Seven parts, three of them for
women, are available. The position
of stage manager is also open.
Anyone interested may report to
the little theatre today from 4 to 6
p.m. or tomorrow from 2 to 4 p.m.
on the main stage in Memorial Audi
torium.
