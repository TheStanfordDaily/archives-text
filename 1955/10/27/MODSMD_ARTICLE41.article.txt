# IIR Conference Department Seek Student Workers
## 
### 
The conference department of the
Institute of International Relations
recently announced "an extensive
program" for this year.
In order to carry out its prospec
tive program, the conference depart
ment, according to chairman Walt
Hays, will need more student help.
There are a large number of jobs
open to students who are interested
in the following types of work: pro
curing main speakers; arranging
panel discussions; handling social
events; and general leg-work in
connection with publicity and com
munications.
For those interested in these ac
tivities there will be a meeting today
in the Women's Clubhouse lounge
at 4:15 p.m.
Hays said no formal tryouts or
interviews will be conducted.
