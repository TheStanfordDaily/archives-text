# Sale of SC Train Tickets Continues
## 
### 
112 Ticket sales for the rooters' train
to the USC game will continue today
and tomorrow at the reduced round
trip lure of $17.93.
Reservations can be made at the
Nitery from 3 to 5 p.m. and tickets
may be picked up the following day.
Sales will also be held through
Thursday of next week.
The train will leave Palo Alto at
7:40 'p.m. on Nov. 4, instead of 8:27
p.m. as was originally scheduled. It
will arrive in Los Angeles at 6:40
the next morning.
An extra car has been added to
accommodate 40 extra students. A
recreation car will also be provided
for dancing with continuous re
corded music throughout the trip.
