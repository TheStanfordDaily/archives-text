# Stanford Coeds To Hosl 3 Schools In 'Swimposium'
## 
### 
Stanford will host women from
San Jose State, San Francisco State
and Mills College at a "Swimposi
um" program of synchronized swim
ming instruction tomorrow at 10
a.m. at the women's pool.
The program will consist of a
demonstration of synchronized swim
ming stunts by Thora John.son of
the San Francisco Women's Athletic
Club.
