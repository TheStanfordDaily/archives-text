# Solar Energy Symposium Begins
## 
### 
The World Symposium on Ap
plied Solar Energy opens today
under the sponsorship of the Stan
ford Research Institute and the Uni
versity of Arizona.
The general session of the con
ference will include discussions on
the domestic uses of the sun's en
ergy. solar machines, food and feed
from the sun's energy, and direct
conversion of solar energy. More
than 1,000 are expected to attend.
Men will talk on heating and cool
ing with the sun's energy, solar
water heaters, architectural prob
lems and high temperature furnaces.
