# Student Injured Avoiding Deer
## 
### 
Stanford student Vincent Brown,
21, is in Palo Alto Hospital with a
possible back injury that he suffered
early Saturday morning. It resulted
from swerving his car and then
crashing to avoid a deer on Canada
road, near Woodside.
Brown's car rolled over three
times, coming to rest on its wheels.
According to the California Highway
Patrol the accident occured at 2:43
a.m.
Brown lives at the Kappa Sigma
fraternity house.
