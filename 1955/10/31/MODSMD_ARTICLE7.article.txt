# SC Train Tickets Remain on Sale
## 
### 
Because of the addition of an extra
car to the Stanford rooters' train to
the USC game, ticket sales will con
tinue through next Thursday for the
40 more available seats.
Ticket prices have been reduced
from $19.03 for round trip to $17.93
by Southern Pacific because of un
expected student interest.
There will be four passenger cars,
plus three cars for the Stanford
band. In addition, there will be a
recreation car with dancing space
for (50 couples, and a tape recorder
for continuous music.
The train will leave Palo Alto at
7:45 p.m. on Nov. 4. It will arrive in
Los Angeles at 6:40 the following
morning. There will be a choice of
trains for the return trip.
Reservations may be made at the
Nitcry from 3 to 5 p.m. through
Wednesday. On Thursday the hours
will be 9 a.m. to 1 p.m.
Tickets may be picked up the day
following purchase.
