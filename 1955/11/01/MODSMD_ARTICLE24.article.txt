# ASSU Office Has Honor Code Books
## 
### 
Extra copies of the Honor Code
booklets distributed last week by
the Fundamental Standard and Hon
or Code Commission are available
at the ASSU office, according to
Commission chairman Harry Miller.
Students who did not receive the
booklets in living groups or by mail
may pick them up at the office be
tween 8 a.m. and noon or 1 and 5
p.m. daily.
