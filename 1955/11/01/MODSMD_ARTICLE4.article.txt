# Chrysanthemum Tea Is Wednesday
## 
### 
The Chrysanthemum Tea of the
Stanford Mothers' Club will be given
tomorrow at the home of Presi
dent and Mrs. Wallace Sterling from
2 to 4:30 p.m.
Mrs. Harvey Hall is general chair
man for the benefit which raises
funds for two Mothers' Club proj
ects: the student rest homes and a
scholarship fund.
Guests will be given the opportu
nity to see the home and gardens.
Tickets are $2 and may be secured
by contacting Mrs. E. L. Tatum, 541
San Juan Road, or Mrs. William A.
Mudgett, DA 3-6923.
