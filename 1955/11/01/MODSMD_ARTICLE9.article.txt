# Final Voting Set Today, Tomorrow for 3 Offices
## 
### 
Final elections for Women's Council, Men's Council and Village
LASSU Representative will be held today and tomorrow.
Delay in final elections for these three offices was caused by elec
tion rule infractions which forced a re-run of the primary.
Polls will be open in front of the Main Library from 8:30 a.m. until
3;30 p.m. today, and from 8:30 a.m. until 1:30 p.m. tomorrow Polls
outside the Village dining area will be open from 11 a.m. until 1 p.m.
both today and tomorrow.
Candidates remaining in the contest for the two Women's Council
posts are Jo Ann Wiedmann, Jane Pyle, Carol Eymann and Carolyn
Fratessa.
Candidates vying in the Men's Council race are Richard Andrews,
Denny Anspach and Jerry Fuller.
Phil Euler and Andrew Kjos are candidates in the coming run-off
for Village Legislature Representative.
