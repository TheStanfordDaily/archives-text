# Applications Here For Draft Exam
## 
### 
Applications for the Selective Ser
vice College Qualification Test, to
be given on Nov. 17 and April 19,
may be obtained in the National
Service Office.
To be eligible to apply for the Se
lective Service tests a student must:
(1) intend to request deferment as
a student; (2) be satisfactorily pur
suing a full-time course of instruc
tion; and (3) must not previously
have taken the qualification test.
Applications for the Nov. 17 test
must be postmarked not later than
midnight Nov. 2; for the April 19
test, midnight March 5, 1956.
