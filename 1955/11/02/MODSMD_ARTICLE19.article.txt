# SC Train Ticket Purchase Urged
## 
### 
Rally Com urges that students who
signed up for the rooters' train to
the SC game buy their tickets today
or tomorrow at the ASSU office.
Tickets may be purchased today
from 3 to 5 p.m. and tomorrow from
9 a.m. to 1 p.m. They may be picked
up the following day. Round trip
fare is $17.93.
