# Ruble Coed Chosen Cow Palace Queen
## 
### 
Joan DeEds, Roble freshman, was
recently chosen as Rodeo Queen of
the Grand National Horse Show and
Livestock Exposition at the Cow
Palace in San Francisco.
Miss DeEds received the news of
her nomination during her arrival
at Stanford this fall. Following the
unexpected news, she attended in
terviews and had her picture taken
for the final judging.
Since her appointment, she has
ridden in the Grand Opening every
night and has helped in presenting
trophies to the various winners.
