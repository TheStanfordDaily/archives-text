# Tryouts Area Young GOP
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Young Republicans
of the Stanford Area Frosh Council
will be held today and tomorrow
from 4:30 to 6 p.m. in the magazine
room, downstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse.
The purpose of the organization, is
to provide closer contact between
freshman Young Republicans and
the older active members. Persons
wanting further information may see
Bob Sprinkle or Jack Pike at Wilbur
Hall, Peggy Graham at Branner, or
Phyllis Westphal at Ruble.
