# SC Train Ticket Sales End Today
## 
### 
Today is the last day that students
who signed up for the rooters' train
to the SC game may make reserva
tions and purchase tickets.
Tickets may be bought from 9 a.m.
to 1 p.m. in front of the ASSU office.
Round trip fare is $17.93 a reduced
price from the orignial $19.03. Tick
ets may be picked up the day after
they are purchased.
The train will leave Palo Alto at
7:45 p.m. tomorrow night and will
arrive in Los Angeles at 6:40 a.m.
Saturday. A choice of trains is avail
able for the return trip.
