# Stanford Scientists Attend Symposium On Solar Energy
## 
### 
More than 90 scientists from 33
countries are participating in the
World Symposium on Applied Solar
Energy in Phoenix.
The symposium is under the joint
sponsorship of the Stanford Research
Institute and the University of Ari
zona.
The 800 scientists are hearing dis
cussions on the domestic uses of the
sun's energy and direct conversion
of solar energy.
They are viewing nearly 100 solar
engineering displays from all parts
of the world. On exhibition are
working models of a 2.5 horsepower
sun-driven engine.
The conference will end Saturday.
