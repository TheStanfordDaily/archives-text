# Big Game Parties' Registration Due
## 
### 
All Big Game parties or over
nights must be registered by Tues
day noon at the ASSU office, accord
ing to ASSU Vice President Helen
Zarifes. Applications may be ob
tained at the ASSU window.
The ASSU Constitution By-laws
require two patrons for each party.
If any living group has not had its
patrons approved, it must do so by
filing applications at the ASSU office.
