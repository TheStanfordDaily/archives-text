# Quad Photos
## 
### 
Individual pictures will be taken
today for the last time of the eating
clubs from 9 a.m. to noon and 1 to 5
p.m. at the Quad office.
Members must wear coat and tie.
Those photographed must pay
$1.25 at the time the picture is taken,
according to Quad editor Tom Dant.
