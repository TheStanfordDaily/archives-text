# SRI Meeting Called Success
## 
### 
The World Symposium on
Applied Solar Energy which
ended Saturday in Phoenix
"produced several significant
developments that could bring
commercial applications," a
spokesman of the Stanford Re
search Institute has declared.
The conference was under the co
sponsorship of the Stanford Research
Institute and the University of Ari
zona.
Merritt L. Kastens, vice chairmen
of the conference that attracted hun
dreds of scientists from more than
30 countries, was particular impress
ed by a device from Israel which
reduces the amount of heat lost
through reflection.
"I think this was a breakthrough
that could revolutionize solar sci
ence," Kastens said.
He said that the conference had
accomplished its main purpose of
exchanging ideas between scientists
and showing business men what had
been done with solar energy.
