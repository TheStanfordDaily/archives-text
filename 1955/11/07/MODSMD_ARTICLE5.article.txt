# Behavioral Science Will Be Subject Of Series Speech
## 
### 
Dr. Ralph W. Tyler, director of
the Center for Advanced Study in
the Behavioral Sciences, will' be the
lecturer at the Tuesday Evening
Series program tomorrow night.
"Developments in Behavioral Sci
ences and Their Implications in Ed
ucation" will be Dr. Tyler's topic.
The program will begin at 7:30 p.m.
in Memorial Hall.
In the course of the lecture Dr.
Tyler will describe the activities of
the Center, which is located on the
Lathrop hill overlooking the Stan
ford campus.
