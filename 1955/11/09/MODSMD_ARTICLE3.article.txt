# Stanford Lawyers To Vie with USF In Moot Court
## 
### 
Student lawyers from Stanford
University and the University of San
Francisco will vie Friday at 8 p.m.
at Hastings Law School to determine
the northern regional representative
in the national moot court competi
tion.
The northern and southern repre
sentatives will meet to determine the
winner in the 15th region, which is
made up of California and Nevada
law schools. Regional winners will
meet, in national competition in New
York in December.
The Stanford team is composed of
three third-year law students, Leon
ard Maizlish, William Rinehart and
Marshall Foreman.
