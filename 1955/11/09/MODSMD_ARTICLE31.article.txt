# Intermezzo to Show 'II Trovatore' Film
## 
### 
Giuseppe Verdi's opera, "II Trova
tore," will be presented by the In
termezzo Foreign Film Series to
morrow at 4:15 p.m." in Cubberley
Auditorium.
The opera is performed by the
orchestra and chorus of the Rome
Opera House.
The film series is sponsored jointly
by the Stanford Library and the De
partment of Germanic and Romanic-
Languages.
