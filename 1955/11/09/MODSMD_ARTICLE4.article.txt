# Nursing Students Will Be Honored At Campus Tea
## 
### 
The annual AWS Nurses Tea will
honor 30 first year nurses from the
Stanford nursing school this after
noon from 4:30 to 5:30 at Bowman
Alumni House.
The purpose of the tea is to ac
quaint the first year nursing womeh
with Stanford and to give the Stan
ford women a chance to meet the
nurses, according to Mary Helen
Kelson, chairman of the program.
Sophomore pre - nursing students
will act as hostesses for the informal
tea.
All Stanford women are invited.
Quad clothes are considered appro
priate for the occasion.
The nurses have been invited to
eat dinner at the women's living
residences after the tea.
