# SF Congressman To Address Area Republicans
## 
### 
Young Republicans of the Stanford
Area will hold their November
meeting tomorrow at the home of
Mr. and Mrs. J. Cary, 361 Vine St.,
Menlo Park, from 3 to 5 p.m.
Congressman William S. Maillard
of San Francisco will speak briefly,
and an informal political poll dealing
with the coming presidential elec
tion will be conducted.
The meeting will be open to all
mqmbers of the Young Republicans
of California. Anyone needing a ride
may contact Cliff Kimber at the
Theta Chi house.
