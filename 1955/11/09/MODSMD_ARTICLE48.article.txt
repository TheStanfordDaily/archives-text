# Guatemalan Guest Of Spanish Group
## 
### 
The consul-general of Guatemala
will be featured guest at tomorrow's
meeting of Sigma Delta Pi.
The national honorary Spanish so
ciety will meet at 7 p.m. in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Consul-general Jorge H. Cruz will
show a film "Los Altos de Guate
mala," and will talk about his home
land.
Ronald Hilton, director of the In
stitute of Hispanic-American Stud
ies. will also speak about Guatemala.
The program will be conducted in
Spanish.
A short business meeting will pro
cede the program, President Len
Livingston announced.
