# Verdi Opera Film To Be Run Today
## 
### 
The Italian film version of Giu
seppe Verdi's opera, "II Trovatore,"
will be shown this afternoon at 4:15
in Cubberley Auditorium.
The opera features an English
commentary. It is performed by the
orchestra and chorus of the Rome
Opera House.
"II Trovatore" is the third in the
Intermezzo Foreign Film Scries
sponsored by the Library and the
Department of Germanic and Ro
manic Languages.
