# Ushers Are Needed For Series Concert
## 
### 
Concert Series ushers for Ruth
Daniels' Memorial Auditorium ap
pearance Nov. 21 are still needed,
according to Manager Dave Sears.
Ushers, who are admitted free to
the concert, must work in the Con
cert Series office for a few hours in
the weeks before the performance,
Sears said.
Students may sign up at the Series
office in the Nitery from 1 to 5 p.m.,
Monday through Friday.
