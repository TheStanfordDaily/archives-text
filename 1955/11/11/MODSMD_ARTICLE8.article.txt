# Over 1100 Parents To Visit Campus
## 
### 
Over 1100 parents of new under
graduates are expected to attend the
second annual Parents Day on cam
pus tomorrow.
At 9:30 a.m. President Wallace
Sterling and ASSU President George
Ralph will speak at the welcoming
assembly in Memorial Auditorium.
Administration staff members will
be introduced, followed by a faculty
panel headed by Dean of Students
H. Donald Winbigler. The lower di
vision curriculum and general edu
cational objectives of the university
will be discussed.
From 11 a.m. to noon Roble, Bran
ner, Wilbur and Stern Halls will
hold open house for parents. A box
lunch will be served at Bowman
Alumni House, where the Stanford
Band will play.
Special buses will take the parents
to the Oregon-Stanford game. An
after-game reception for the stu
dents and their parents will be held
at the home of President and Mrs.
Sterling.
