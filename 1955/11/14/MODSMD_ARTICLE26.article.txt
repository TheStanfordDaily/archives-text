# Exam Petition Deadline
## 
### 
The deadline for filing petitions
for change in the final examination
schedule is Nov. 21.
Students who have three exams
scheduled for one day or two for
the same time are eligible to peti
tion for changes. <•
Petitions are to be obtained and
filed at Mrs. Claudia S. Knowles*
office, Room 111-R of the Registrar's
Office.
