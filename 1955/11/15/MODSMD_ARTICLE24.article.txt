# Theater Matinee
## 
### 
| "Pioneers in the American Motion
| Picture Industry" is the theme for
' the Theater Matinee to be presented
this Wednesday at 4:15 p.m. in the
Little Theater.
The films to be shown include
"The Great Train Robbery," "New
York Hat" and several early Chaplin
films. The commentary will be given
by Wendell Cole.
