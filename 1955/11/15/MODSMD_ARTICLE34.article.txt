# House Drawings Begin Tomorrow
## 
### 
House drawings for Roble senior
sponsors will be held tomorrow from
9 a.m. to noon and from 12:30 to 4
p.m. in the Housing Office, Room
101-A.
General house drawings are sched
uled for Thursday from 9 a.m. to
noon and from 12:30 to 4 p.m. in the
lower lounge of the Women's Club
house. Cancellations may be made
on Friday.
Questions concerning the drawings
may be addressed to Housing Com
mittee representatives Mary Rich
ardson, Branner; Chris Tedesco, Ro
ble; Lucy Grey, Lagunita" 1 ; Nancy
Roselyn, Jordan; Gail Johannes,
Hawthorne; and Lu Anne Kirkpat
rick, Ventura.

made in the Housing Office Nov. 28,
from 2 to 5 p.m.
