# YWCA Will Show Conference Slides
## 
### 
Slides taken at last year's YWCA
YMCA regional student conference
will be shown today at 5 p.m. in the
YWCA office, 3rd floor of the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
The conference is held annually
at Asilomar on the Monterey Penin
sula and is attended by students
from colleges throughout the Pacific
Southwest.
This year's theme is "What Does
It Mean to Be?" and discussions will
center around the contemporary
campus scene and student needs.
Those interested in attending the
conference may obtain more infor
mation at the "Y" office at today's
meeting.
