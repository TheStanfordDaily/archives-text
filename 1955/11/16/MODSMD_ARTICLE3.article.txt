# Heritage Scholars Will Hold Meeting
## 
### 
Heritage Fund Scholarship holders
will meet today from 4:30 to 5:30
p.m. in Bowman Alumni House.
The Heritage Fund Scholarship
program consists primarily of funds
solicited from local merchants. It
was inaugurated by the Class of '55.
Among the speakers at the meet
ing will be James Triolo, associate
general .secretary; Frank Schwartz,
Bill Lund and Fred Ruhland, chair
man of the Heritage Fund program
for the Class of '56.
