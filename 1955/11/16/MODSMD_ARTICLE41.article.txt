# Roble Sponsors' House Drawing Scheduled Today
## 
### 
House drawing for Roble senior
sponsors will be held today from 9
a.m. to noon and from 12:30 to 4 p.m.
in the Housing Office, Room 101 A.
All Roble senior sponsors must re
port to the Housing Office whether
they are drawing or returning to
their old dormitories.
General house drawings are sched
uled for tomorrow from 9 a.m. to
noon and from 12:110 to 4 p.m. in the
lower lounge of the Women's Club
house.
All Branner sophomores must
draw tomorrow, and they will be
assigned to residences ahead of the
rest of their class.
Cancellations may be made before
4:30 p.m. Friday.
Applications for shrinkage may be
made in the Housing Office Nov. 28
from 2 to 5 p.m.
