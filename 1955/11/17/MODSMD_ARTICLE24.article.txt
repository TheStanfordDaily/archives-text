# Women May Draw For House Change
## 
### 
House drawing for those Stanford
women who wish to change their
residence will be held today from
9 a.m. to noon and from 12:30 to 4
p.m. in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse.
The following houses have, vacan
cies which may be filled by drawing.
Lagunita. 11; Ventura, 5; Union, 1;
Elm, 1; Madrono, 3; Manzanita, 2;
Hurlburt, 1; Jordan, 1; and Storey, 3.
All Branner sophomores must
draw today. They will be assigned
to residences ahead of the rest of
their class.
Cancellations may be made before
4:30 p.m. Friday.
Applications for shrinkage may be
made in the Housing Office from 2
to 5 p.m. in Nov. 28.
