# Students May Pick Up Vienna Choir Tickets
## 
### 
Students who have ordered tickets
for the February performance of the
Vienna Choir Boys, sponsored by
the lIR, may pick them up at the
Concert Series office in the Nitery,
from 1 to 5 p.m., Monday through
Friday.
