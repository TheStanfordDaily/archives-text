# IIR to Sponsor Christmas Tour
## 
### 
The Institute of International Re
lations is planning a tour for a group
of American and foreign students
during the Christmas holiday.
The tour will leave Stanford on
Dec. 23, returning on Jan. 2, and will
include Hollywood, Palm Springs,
Grand Canyon, Zion National Park
and Las Vegas.
The group will be guests of fami- I
lies and service clubs at some of the
places.
Cost of transportation will be $35.
Signup sheets are posted at the lIR
shack and on the Cellar bulletin
board.
