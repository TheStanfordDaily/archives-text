# Plans to Be Made For SIRO Drive
## 
### 
All organizations are requested to
send a representative to a meetin«
of the Stanford International Relief
Organization today at 4:15 p.m.,
chairman Bruno Morelli announced.
Dr. Kanti Vyas, headmaster of
New Era School, Bombay, India, will
speak at the meeting which will bo
held in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Vyas is currently studying
secondary education in this country.
The purpose of the meeting is to
plan the annual Fall Book and Cloth
ing Drive which will be held during
dead week.
Students are urged to donate tech
nical books and old clothing to the
drive, according to Morelli.
All donations will go to the World
University Service.
