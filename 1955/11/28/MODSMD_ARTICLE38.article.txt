# Symphony to Give Mem And Concert
## 
### 
The San Francisco Symphony will
appear in Memorial Auditorium Jan
uary 30 as a special Concert Series
feature, Series Manager Dave Sears
announced.
Regular Conductor Enrique Jorda
will direct the Symphony in its
Stanford concert.
Its appearance here will be the
orchestra's only concert on the Pe
ninsula this season, Sears said.
The San Francisco orchestra be
gins its 44th season tomorrow night
in War Memorial Opera House.
Tickets for the Stanford concert,
the program for which will be an
nounced at a later date, are avail
able at the Concert Series office in
the Nitery.
Hours of the Series are 1 to 5 p.m.
Monday through Friday.
