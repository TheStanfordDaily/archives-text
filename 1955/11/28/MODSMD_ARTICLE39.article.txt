# Students May Up For IIR Holiday Tour
## 
### 
The Institute of International Re
lations is planning a tour for foreign
and American students over the
Christmas holidays.
Among the spots the group will
; visit will be Hollywood, Palm
Springs, Grand Canyon, Zion Na
tional Park and Las Vegas.
Sign-up sheets are posted at the
lIR shack and on the Cellar bulletin
board for those who would like to
go. Transportation cost will be $35.
