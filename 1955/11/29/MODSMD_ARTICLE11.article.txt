# Unions to Be Debate Topic
## 
### 
"Are labor unions becoming too
powerful?"
Two guest speakers and two Stan
ford students will debate this con
troversial issue at a Political Union
meeting in Cubberley Auditorium
Thursday night.
Taking the negative view will be
I. B. Padway, a San Jose attorney,
and John Falchi, Stanford junior.
Debating affirmatively will be W. M.
Caldwell, president of the California
Assn. of Employers, and Mike Hud
son, a senior and chairman of the
Poli Union Republican Caucus.
It will be the second event of the
quarter sponsored by the campus
group. Starting time will be 8 p.m.
The program has been arranged
by Poli Union program chairman
Cliff Kimber.
