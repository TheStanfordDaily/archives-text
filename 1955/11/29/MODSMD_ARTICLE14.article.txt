# Quad Photos
## 
### 
Individual Quad pictures will be
taken today and tomorrow of eight
fraternities between 9 a.m. and noon
and from 1 to 5 p.m.
Groups scheduled for photographs
are: Phi Sigma Kappa, Theta Xi,
Sigma Ch'i, Delta Tau Delta, Alpha
Sigma Phi, Alpha Delta Phi, Zeta Psi
and Kappa Sigma.
Pictures will be taken at the same
times on Thursday, Friday and Mon
day of Phi Kappa Psi, Phi Delta
Theta. Sigma Mu, Beta Theta Pi, Phi
Gamma Delta, Chi Psi, Delta Upsilon
and Theta Chi.
Dress for Quad photos should in
clude a coat and tie according to
Quad officials.
