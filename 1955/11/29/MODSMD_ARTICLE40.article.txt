# Flying Club to Hear Crack UAL Pilot
## 
### 
Captain Embree Rockwell, a Unit
ed Airlines DC-7 pilot, will speak
at a meeting of the Indian Flyers to
night at 7 p.m. in the Nitery.
Captain Rockwell, who has held
many of the transcontinental DC-7
speed records, will discuss the DC-8,
United's new jet airliner.
Refreshments will be served at the
meeting, which is open to the public.
