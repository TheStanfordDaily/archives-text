# 'Bawlout' on Sale, Dressy New Cover
## 
### 
J
Dressed in a bright new cover, this
year's University Directory (Bawl
out) is now on sale at the Cashier's
Office in Encina for 50 cents a copy.
Appearing on the cover is the
Stanford motto, "Die Luft der Frei
heit weht" (The winds of freedom
blow).
For the first time, glossy paper has
been used in the Bawlout.
