# Quad Photos
## 
### 
Individual Quad pictures will be
taken today of the following living
groups between 9 a.m. and noon and
from 1 to 5 p.m.:
Phi Sigma Kappa, Theta Xi, Sigma
Chi, Delta Tau Delta, Alpha Sigma
Plii, Alpha Delta Phi, Zeta Psi and
Kappa Sigma.
Pictures will be taken at the same
times on Thursday, Friday and Mon
day of Phi Kappa Psi. Phi Delta
Theta, Sigma Nu, Beta Theta Pi,
Phi Gamma Delta, Chi Psi, Delta
Upsilon and Delta Chi.
Dress for Quad photos must in
clude a coat and tie, according to
Quad officials.
