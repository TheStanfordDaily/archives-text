# IIR to Host Watkins At Open Meeting
## 
###

lations will hold an open meeting
today at Bowman Alumni House at

Political Science, will be special
guest. All members of the lIR and
those interested in international re
lations are invited to attend.

talk by Shoiehi Ban, Japanese con
sul, in Bowman Alumni House at
4:15 p.m.
