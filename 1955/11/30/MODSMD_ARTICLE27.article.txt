# Intermezzo to Show Movie on Franz Liszt
## 
### 
"Franz Liszt," French musical bi
ography, will be shown tomorrow at
4:15 p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium.
The picture was originally released
under the title "Reves d'Amour."
"Franz Liszt" is one of the current
Intermezzo series of foreign films
sponsored by the Library and the
Department of Germanic and Ro
manic Languages.
