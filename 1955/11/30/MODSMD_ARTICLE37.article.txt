# Tarr, Wiggin, Taylor Named Outstanding
## 
### 
Stanford made a clean sweep of
the grid awards given last night by
the Northern California Football
Writers Association at a banquet at
Rickey's Studio Inn.
Fullback Bill Tarr was named
"back of the year, and tackle Paul
Wiggin was honored as "lineman of
the year." Coach Chuck Taylor was
presented the "coach of the year"
award.
Hie PALO sports club staged the
banquet.
