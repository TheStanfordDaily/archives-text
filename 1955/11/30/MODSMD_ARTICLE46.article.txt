# Ski Club Will Meet Today at Cubberley
## 
### 
The Stanford Ski Club will hold
a meeting at 7:30 tonight in Cubber
ley Auditorium.
Speaking to the group will be Pete
Johnson, owner of the winter resort
"Cisco at Tunnel Mountain."
Cisco's head ski instructor will
also speak on the "French Technique
of Parallel Skiing."
Business to be brought up at the
meeting will include the subject of
rates for Christmas skiing.
