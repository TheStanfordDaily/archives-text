# Frosh Grid Banquet Will Be Hold Tonight
## 
### 
Not to be outdone by their varsity
brothers, the freshman football team
will hold its annual team banquet
tonight. The dinner will get under
way at 6:30 p.m. at Chez Yvonne's.
Highlighting the affair will be the
presentations of awards to outstand
ing frosh players. The event i.s the
last organized function for the fresh
man team as a whole, according to
Bob Ghilotti, freshman coach.
