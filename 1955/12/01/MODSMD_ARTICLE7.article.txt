# ASSU Cabinet To Convene Tonight
## 
### 
The ASSU Cabinet will hold its
second meeting tonight at 7:30 in
the Board Room of Bowman Alumni
House, ASSU President George
Ralph said yesterday.
Jack Richardson, chairman of the
Activities Evaluation Committee, will
discuss some of the findings of his
committee. Ralph has planned no
other business.
The Cabinet, which was created
in the constitution approved last
spring quarter, has met only once
previously. At that time it was de
cided that at least one more meeting
should be held this quarter.
