# Intermezzo Shows Liszt Film Today
## 
### 
The French musical biography,
"Frunz Liszt," will be shown today
at 4:15 in Cubberley Auditorium.

music by the orchestra of the Paris
Conservatory and pianist Nikita Ma
gal ofT.
The picture will be presented by
the Library anrl the Department of
Germanic and Romanic Languages
in the current Intermezzo foreign
film series. It features English sub
