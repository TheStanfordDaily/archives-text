# AWS Will Hold January Elections
## 
### 
AWS elections will be held on
Jan. 31. AWS officers announced
yesterday.
Nominations for the offices will be
held on Jan. 17, 18 and 19. Declina
tions will be received on Jan. ,20,
primary elections will take place on
Jan. 25 and the finals on Jan. 31.
Offices to be filled are president,
vice-president, secretary and social
co-ordinator.
Women running for president and
social co-ordinator must be juniors
at the end of winter quarter. Can
didates for vice-president must be
sophomores, and candidates for sec
retary must be freshmen.
Further information about the
available positions may be obtained
from the present AWS officers.
They are president, Harriet
Thomas, Jordan; vice-president, El
lie Kronfeld, Lagunita; secretary,
Myrna Kelley, Jordan; treasurer,
Mary Helen Stillman, and
social co-ordinator, Marilyn Peter
son. Guthrie.
