# SIRO Book Drive To Begin Monday
## 
### 
The Stanford International Relief
Organization will begin its annual
Fall Book and Clothing Drive Mon
day.
Boxes will be located in all the
dormitories for students to contrib
ute items. According to Drive Chair
man Bruno Morelli, students are
urged to contribute clothing, pam
phlets, magazines, or books. Books
of a technical or scientific nature are
especially needed.
Collections from the drive, which
will end next Sunday, will be given
to the World University Service
(WUS).
