# Radio-TV Grants Available to Coeds
## 
### 
Applications are now available for
a scholarship of $500 for junior
women planning a career in radio
or television, the American Women
in Radio and Television, Inc., of New
York City announced recently.
Deadline for scholarship entries is
Jan. 1(5, according to the AWRT.
Applications may be obtained from
the organization's offices, 70 East
45th St., New York.
Applicants are required to have
recommendations from the dean of
students, two faculty members un
der whom she has studied and two
personal references.
