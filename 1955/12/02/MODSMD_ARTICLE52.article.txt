# Planetarium To Give Xmas Show
## 
### 
The Morrison Planetarium will
again present the story of the Christ
mas Star throughout the month of
December.
The planetarium show will feature
the sky as it was on the night of
Jan. 1, 7 8.C., the date of Christ's
birth.
George Bunion, Planetarium man
ager, and the staff will add to the
sky their conception of the Christ
mas star itself.
They will discuss the various theo
ries attempting to explain the star
in terms of a natural phenomena.
The question of Christ's birth date
will also be discussed.
Throughout December the Christ
mas show will be presented daily
(except Monday and Tuesday) at
3:30 and 8:15 p.m.
