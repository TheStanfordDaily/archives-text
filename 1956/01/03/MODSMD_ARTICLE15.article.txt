# Sequoia Magazine To Sponsor Dinner
## 
### 
Sequoia Magazine will sponsor a
pre - publication dinner Saturday
evening at the Eating Club lounge.
Tickets will be on sale today at the
Pnvilion for $2.25 and will sell at the
Cellar and Main Library during the
week.
Guest speaker for the dinner,
which will honor the first issue of
Stanford's new literary magazine, is
Dr. Margery Bailey who will talk on
"The Intellectual Vacuum at Stan
ford."
