# Today's Registration Expected to Top 7200
## Highest Winter Enrollment Since 1950 Should Be Reached This Quarter 
### 
Today's winter quarter registration is expected to exceed 7200,
according to Elmer C. Wagner, associate registrar.
This total would put the enrollment at its highest peak for win
ter quarter since Jan. 7, 1950, when 7514 students had registered
in the first four days of the quarter. Last year's total, after four
♦ days of registration, was GGB7.
