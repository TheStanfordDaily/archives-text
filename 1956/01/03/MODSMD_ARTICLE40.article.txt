# Wrestlers Take 4th At San Jose Meet
## 
### 
Franz Brazil won first place in the
147 lb. class to pace a three man
Stanford squad to fourth place in the
team competition at the AAU Novice
Wrestling Tournament, Dec. 3 at San
Jose.
Bob Carmody placed third at 137
lb. and John Pew at 191 lb. added a
pin to the Stanford score.
The San Jose State varsity was
first with 36 points. Sarita Clara and
the San Jose frosh were second and
third with 14 and 10 points respec
tively, while Stanford had nine.
