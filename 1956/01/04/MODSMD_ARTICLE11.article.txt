# Two Play Tryouts To Be Held Today
## 
### 
Tryouts for two Stanford Players
productions will be held tonight.
Auditions for the musical play
"The Golden Apple" will be held in
Memorial Auditorium at '4:15 p.m.
and at 9 p.m.
People who auditioned last quar
ter do not have to try again. Singers
are requested to bring their own
music for a selection in English
which they have prepared.
Tryouts for J. B. Priestley's "Dan
gerous Corner" will be held in the
Little Theater tonight from 7:30 to
10 p.m.
This is a modern melodrama which
will open on Jan. 25 for a two-week
run.
