# Van Beinum Will Conduct Orchestra
## 
### 
Eduard Van Beinum will conduct
the San Francisco Symphony to
morrow night in his first local ap
pearance.
Van Beinum is regular conductor
of Amsterdam's famed Concertge
bouw and the London Philharmonic
Orchestra.
The Dutch conductor has chosen
Bach's Third Suite, Bartok's Con
certo for Orchestra and Brahms'
Second Symphony for the Opera
House concert, which will start at
8:30 p.m.
Students wishing to buy or sell
tickets for the concert may sign up
at the Concert Series office in the
Nitery from 1 to 5 p.m. today and
tomorrow.
