# Wilderness Photos Shown at Gallery
## 
### 
A display of photographs entitled
I"The Words of the Earth," express-
I ing the influence of the wilderness
I on man, is now being shown at the
Art Gallery. The exhibit will re
main through Jan. 8.
Thirty-one photographs and ac
companying text make up the ex
hibit. Both text and photographs are
by Cedric Wright, one of the official
photographers for the Sierra Club.
Most of the photographs were taken
on Sierra Club trips.
Wright has presented what he de
scribes as "an artist's discussion of
education in relation to world peace."
The exhibit, taken from a book by
the same title which he is writing,
was prepared with the aid of Nancy
Newhall, of Eastman House in Roch
ester.
