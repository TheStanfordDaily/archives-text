# Publications Office Gets New Location
## 
### 
The News and Publications Serv
ice, formerly the Information and
Publications Oflice, has moved to its
new location on the second floor of
Building 1, Inner Quad.
Building 1 was the former site of
the Cashier's Office. It was remod
eled last quarter and two classrooms
and two seminar rooms now replace
the Cashier's Office.
The Law Oflice has taken over the
former Publications Office and the
Cashier's Oflice is now located in
theEncina Lobby.
