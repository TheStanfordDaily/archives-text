# Students Will Visit Fisherman's Wharf
## 
### 
The Host Department of the In
stitute of International Relations will
be conducting a tour to San Fran
cisco Saturday.
The tour, open to both foreign and
American students, will leave the
Cellar at 10:30 a.m. and will visit
Fisherman's Wharf, Golden Gate
Park and other places of interest.
Sign-up sheets are posted at the
lIR shack and the"I" Club bulletin
board at the Cellar. According to
Barbara Von Briesen, Host Depart
ment chairman, cars will be needed
for the trip.
