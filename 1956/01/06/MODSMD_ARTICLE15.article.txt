# Frosh Switch Row Sections As Houses Search for New Blood
## 
### 
Stanford's fraternities welcome another group of freshmen • i
other rushees tonight at open houses as the frosh groups ch.
sections of the row.
The open houses began last night with the usual hand-shr
festivities going on in 23 of the Farm's 24 houses. Only Theta > ,

found guilty of a Hell Week viola
tion, is not rushing this quarter.
Freshmen living in Arroyo, Cedro,
Junipero, Madera, and Otero will
move from the lower row which they
visited last night to the upper row.
Frosh from Rinconada, Soto, Tran
cos and Stern Hall, who visited up
per row last night, visit the houses
nearer Quad tonight.
The status changes back tomorrow
night to last night's order.
HOURS
Houses open tonight at 7:15 and
will not close until 9:4-5.
Freshmen beginning their rushing
last night went through the annual
tours of rooms, sleeping porches, and
game rooms, also taking trips to
punch bowls and scrapbooks.
Fraternity members judge every
freshman they meet, thus making
first impressions very important.
The rushees lingered in each house
for an average of about 30 minutes.
Open houses last through Tuesday
night at 9 at which time formal rush
ing begins. Five nights of parties,
luncheons and dinners given by the
23 rushing houses follow.
NO-CONTACT RULE
The no-contact rule remains until
Tuesday night when formal rush
ing starts. It docs not return until
Sunday, Jan. 15. On that day, it
applies until 5 p.m. at which time
pledges go to their respective houses
for dinners and pledge-card signing.
During formal rushing, fraternity
men will be allowed in frosh dorms
only between 11:30 a.m. and 1:30 p.m.
and between 5 and 7:30 p.m.
Also, fraternity men may not tele
phone frosh unless they know the
rushees' special call numbers.
Houses comprising the upper row
are Alpha Delta Phi. Alpha Tau
Omega, Delta Chi, Delta Kappa Ep
silon. Delta Tau Delta, Kappa Alpha,
Phi Gamma Delta, Phi Kappa Psi,
Phi Sigma Kappa, Sigma Nu, Theta
Delta Chi and Theta Xi.
Lower row fraternities are Alpha
Kappa Lambda, Alpha Sigma Phi,
Beta Theta Pi, Chi Psi, Delta Upsi
lon, Kappa Sigma, Phi Delta Theta,
Phi Kappa Sigma, Sigma Alpha Ep
silon, Sigma Chi, Theta Chi, and
Zota Psi.
He said the land was being pur
chased now while it is relatively
cheap, and because the University
has indicated it may have to put it
to other uses if purchase is delayed.
Nathan Finch, chairman of the
Board of Education, said the board
would act Monday on a resolution
authorizing the condemnation suit.
He expects the two parties to be
fairly well agreed in advance on the
sale price, however,

NO-CONTACT RULE
The no-contact rule remains until
Tuesday night when formal rush
ing starts. It docs not return until
Sunday, Jan. 15. On that day, it
applies until 5 p.m. at which time
pledges go to their respective houses
for dinners and pledge-card signing.
During formal rushing, fraternity
men will be allowed in frosh dorms
only between 11:30 a.m. and 1:30 p.m.
and between 5 and 7:30 p.m.
Also, fraternity men may not tele
phone frosh unless they know the
rushees' special call numbers.
