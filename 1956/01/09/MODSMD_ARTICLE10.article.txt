# Sterling to Open Tuesday Series
## 
### 
President Wallace Sterling will
open the winter quarter Tuesday
Evening Series lectures tomorrow
night with an informal talk on
"Stanford Today and Tomorrow."
Dr. Sterling will speak in Memo
rial Auditorium at 7:30 p.m. He will
be introduced by William C. Steere,
Dean of the Graduate Division.
Tuesday Series is sponsored by the
Committee on Public Exercises and
is open to the public without charge.
