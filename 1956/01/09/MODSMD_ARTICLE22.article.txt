# Shaw Play Opens Theatre Matinee
## 
### 
"The Man of Destiny," by George
Bernard Shaw, will be the first
Theatre Matinee production of the
winter quarter.
In the cast of the one-act play are
Ronald Bazarini, Judith Doiy, Hart \
Sprager and John Masterson. It will
be presented in /the Little Theatre]
of Memorial Auditorium Wednesday
at 4:15 p.m.
The Theatre Matinee Series is a
presentation of the Department of
Speech and Drama and is presented
weekly at the Little Theatre of Me
morial Auditorium.
The matinee series is open to the I
public without charge.
