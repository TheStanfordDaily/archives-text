# Lite Reg Fee Gets $5 Hike Tomorrow
## 
### 
Today is the last day that students
may register for a late fee of $5. To
morrow the fee will be raised to $10.
Registrar Harvey Hall requests
that students file class cards in the
Regi trar's Office at their earliest
convenience in order to avoid the
last day line.
Friday is the deadline for filing
class cards without a late filing fee.
As of yesterday at 5 p.m. 7403 stu
dents had registered for winter
quarter, exceeding last year's num
ber at this time by 340.
The ratio of men to women so far
has increased from 2.52 to 2.72.
