# Music Tickets Are on Sale
## 
### 
Tickets are still available for the
Dave Brubeck concert, the Chamber
Music concert and the Vienna Boys
Choir concert.
The Brubeck concert, titled "Jazz
at Mem Aud," will be held in Me
morial' Auditorium on Jan. 21 from
3-5 p.m. The concert, sponsored by
the class of '57, is being held to raise
money for charity. Some of the pro
ceeds will go to the Winter Charity
Drive.
Sale price on tickets is $1.50. They
may be bought at the Cellar from
10 a.m. to 3 p.m. and at the ASSU
Offifce from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.
On Jan. 27 the Fine Arts Quartet
will feature a chamber music con
cert at 8:15 in Cubberley Audito
rium. Tickets for the concert are on
sale at the Memorial Auditorium
box office from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m.
The Affiliation Department of the
lIP is sponsoring a concert by the
ViennA Boys Choir on Sunday after
noon, Feb. 19. The purpose of the
concert is to raise money for a schol
arship to the Free University of
Berlin and Keio University in Japan.
Tickets are on sale at the Concert
Series box office for $1.50 to $3.
