# Gregor Piatigorsky Tickets Sold Out
## 
### 
Tickets for Gregor Piatigorsky's
Concert Series appearance Friday
night in Memorial Auditorium have
been sold out, according to Manager
Dave Sears.
Student ushers for the concert are
still needed, he said. Ushers must
work for two hours in the Concert
Series office in the Nitery before
they are eligible.
They are then entitled to hear the
concert free of charge, Sears said.
