# Pledge Classes Fill Rapidly as Closed Rushing Continues
## 
### 
Closed rushing continues tonight
as fraternities maintain their busy
party schedules. With many houses
reporting that their pledge classes
are filling rapidly, formal ru.hing
will continue through this Saturday
night.

into effect Sunday until 5 p.m.

hdld Sunday following removal of
the no-contact rule.

in Wilbur and Stern halls from 11:30

p.m. this week.
