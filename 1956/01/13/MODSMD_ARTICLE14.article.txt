# Sequoia Enters Second Printing
## 
### 
Sequoia magazine will run off a
second printing next week "due to
unanticipated demand" for the first
edition which was published yes
terday, Editor Linda Lovelace an
nounced.
The magazine's first edition
numbered 700 copies, which were
sold out by 11 yesterday morning.
"We haven't decided how many
copies to order for the second
printing," IVliss Lovelace said. "We
will welcome all orders for reserve
copies."
The new printing of the literary
magazine will be sold either on
Thursday or Friday, she said.
