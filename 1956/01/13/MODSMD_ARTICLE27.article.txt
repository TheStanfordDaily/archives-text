# Folk Dancers Meet Tonight in Village
## 
### 
Stanford Folk Dancers will hold
their regular meeting tonight in the
Village Auditorium. Everyone is
welcome and admission is free.
The fir t session, for advanced
dancers, will begin at 7:30 p.m. The
regular teaching session for begin
ners will start at 8 p.m.
Among dances on tonight's agenda
are Russian Polka, Skaters Waltz,
Schuplattler Landler, 80.-ton Two-
Step and Krici-Krici Ticek.
