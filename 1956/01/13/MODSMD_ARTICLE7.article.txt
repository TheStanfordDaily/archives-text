## FBI Solves Million-Dollar Brink's Theft; Six Involved 
### 
BOSTON, Jan. 12 (UP) —The fantastic $1,219,000 Brink's holdup
—the biggest cash haul in history—was solved today with the
arrest of six men. All are from the greater Boston area.
Solution to the fabulous Jan. 17, 1950, robbery came five days
before the expiration of the state statute of limitations under which

they can be prosecuted on armed
robbery charges.
All gang members except one were
among the many witnesses who ap
peared before a federal grand jury
which investigated the holdup. For
five years the holdup looked like the
perfect crime.
