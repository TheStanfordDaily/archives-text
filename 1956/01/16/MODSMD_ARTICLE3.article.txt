# Ski Club Carnival Reservations Open
## 
### 
Reservations for the Ski Club's
Annual Winter Carnival may be
made today through Friday between
the hours of 11 a.m. and 4 p.m. at
the ASSU Office in the Union.
The carnival will be held at Squaw
Valley on Jan. 27, 28 and 29.
The cost for the weekend will be
$3 a night for rooms and $3 for the
banquet Saturday night. Reserva
tions must be paid for in advance.
Ski Club cards will be sold at the
same time reservations are sold, but
it is not necessary to be a member
in order to attend the carnival.
Members will be entitled to re
duced rates on the Squaw Valley
facilities.
