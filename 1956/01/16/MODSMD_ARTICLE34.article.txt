# Hushing Ends; Fraternities Announce Pledges
## 392 Pledged As New Record Set; Theta Chi at Top 
### 
Another two-week period of hec
tic rushing ended abruptly last night
as Stanford's 23 active national fra
ternities watched carefully as 392
rushees signed their pledge cards.
Theta Xi did not rush this year.
This year's total sets a new record.
It is an increase of eight over the
1953 all-time high of 382, and an in
crease ol 36 over last year's total of
356.
Theta Chi captured the largest
number of pledges with 23. Chi Psi,
who pledged the largest number last
year, came in low this year with 10.
The average pledge class this year
was a substantial 17. The last two
years have netted an average of 14.8
and 14.7 respectively.
