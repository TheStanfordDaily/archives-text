# Deans Will Speak To Mothers' Club
## 
### 
Stanford Mothers' Club will listen
to Stanford's three deans today at
2 p.m. in the Women's Clubhouse.
Speakers will be Dean of Students
H. Donald Winbigler, Dean of Wom
en Miss Elva Fay Brown and Dean
of Men William G. Craig.
A no-host luncehon will begin at
12 noon in the Stanford Union. Res
ervations may be made with Mrs.
John Ball, DA 2-0039, or Mrs. Frank
Richey, DA 2-4861.
