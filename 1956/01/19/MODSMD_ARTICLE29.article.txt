# Crosten to Address Philologists on Opera
## 
### 
Professor William L. Crosten of
the Stanford Music Department will
address the Philological Association
tomorrow at 4:15 p.m. in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
"The Idea of the Opera" will be
Professor Crosten's topic. The paper
he is presenting is a discussion of
the phrase "drama through music"
as a designation for the art of opera.
