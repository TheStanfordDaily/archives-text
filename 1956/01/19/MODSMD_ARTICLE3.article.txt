# Nominations Close Today for Four AWS Positions
## 
### 
Nominees for AWS offices will be
announced tomorrow. There are still
positions open.
Nominations will close today at 5
p.m. All petitions for these offices
must be turned in to the ASSU office
iby 5 this afternoon.
Petitions may still be obtained to
day at the office. Each petition must
be signed by 15 women and turned
in today.
Offices open for election are presi
dent, vice-president, secretary and
.social co-ordinator.
There will be a candidates' meet
ing at 5 p.m. this afternoon in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Declinations are tomorrow. Pri
mary elections are Jan. 25, and finals
Jan. 31.
