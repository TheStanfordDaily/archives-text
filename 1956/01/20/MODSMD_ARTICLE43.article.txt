# Sports Cars
## 
### 
The Stanford Sports Car Club will
hold a concourse de elegance and
tour this Sunday. The concourse de
elegance, a contest to see who can
put their car in the best shape, will
be staged in front of Bowman Alum
ni House at 11 a.m. with the tour to
follow.
Both events are open to all Stan
ford students, with ownership of a
spouts car not a prerequisite to join.
