# Margery Bailey Speaks Tomorrow
## 
### 
Dr. Margery Bailey, well known
by Stanford students for her courses
in Shakespeare, will appear as guest
lecturer on the Tuesday Series pro
gram tomorrow night at 7:30 in Me
morial Auditorium.
Dr. Bailey, who will retire from
teaching this year, has chosen as her
topic "A Testament of Teaching."
She has been at Stanford since 1941
and is a full professor in the English
department.
Shakespeare has been Dr. Bailey's
special interest for a number of years.
In addition to courses she teaches in
the subject, she has also acted and
served as a critic for the Oregon
Shakespeare Festival Association.
Dr. Bailey will be introduced by
George Frank Sensabaugh, professor
of English. The Tuesday Series is
open to the public without cost and
is sponsored by the Committee on
Public Exercises.
