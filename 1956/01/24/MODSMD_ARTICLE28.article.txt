# Grad Scholarship Grants Available
## 
### 
Scholarship awards up to $2400
are available to students interested
in beginning or continuing graduate
study at Stanford in 195(3-57, accord
ing to Mrs. C. E. Rothwell, graduate
financial awards secretary.
Applications may be obtained from
the Office of Financial Awards,
Room 11 IF, Registrar's Office.
In addition to the approximately
150 awards granted last year by the
25 major University departments,
there will be 10 awards of $2400 each
for students entering their first year
of graduate study at Stanford (with
the intention of continuing for Ph.D.
degree) in one of the behavioral sci
ences.
These awards are made possible
through a specific gift from the be
havioral science program of the Ford
Foundation.
