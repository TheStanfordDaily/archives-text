# NROTC Riflemen Hated Very High
## 
### 
Stanford's Naval ROTC rifle team,
coached by Master Sergeant Roy R.
Luke, USMC, placed third in the
William Randolph Hearst Naval
ROTC rifle competition for 1955. This
marks the second consecutive year
in which Stanford's team has been
among the top three in the nation.
Oliver R. Jones Jr., whose score
was 191, and Joseph P. N. Goodcll,
with a score of 190 out of a possible
200, received honorable mention for
individual high scores.
