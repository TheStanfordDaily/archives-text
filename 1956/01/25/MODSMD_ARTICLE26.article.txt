# A 						Phi O to Return Book Sale Cheeks
## 
### 
Checks and unsold books from the
winter quarter Alpha Phi Omega
book sale will be returned today and
tomorrow from 9 a.m. to noon and
1 to 4 p.m. in Room 10G in the Main
Library.
If the book checks are not picked
up before 4 p.m. tomorrow, they be
come property of A Phi O, according
to A Phi O spokesman Norm Kibbe.
