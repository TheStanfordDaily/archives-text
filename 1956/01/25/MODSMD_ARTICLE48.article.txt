# Folkdancers Plan Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
Two European dances will be
taught at the Stanford Folk and
Square Dancers meeting tonight
from 7:30 to 9 in the dance studio of
the Women's Gym.
Gerikina and Kastiranos Syrtos
will be the new dances done, and
the Hopak and Hambo will be re
viewed.
Next week the Square Dancers
will have a guest teacher, Anatole
Joukou.ski, and will also review
dances already learned.
