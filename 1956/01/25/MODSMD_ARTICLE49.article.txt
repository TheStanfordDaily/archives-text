# Y Marriage Series To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. Robert Dunn of the Palo Alto
Clinic will speak on "The Physical
Aspects of Marriage," the second
phase in the YWCA Marriage and
the Home Series, tonight at 7:30 in
the Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Dunn has spoken previously at
similar programs on campus. Every
one is invited to tonight's discussion,
which will be held in the lower
lounge of the clubhouse.
