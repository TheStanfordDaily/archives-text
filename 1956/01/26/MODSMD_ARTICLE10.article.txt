# Talks on Moore Hall Begin Today
## 
### 
The first ot a series of talks about
the new women's dormitory, Flor
ence Moore Hall, will be presented
by the AWS Residence Planning
Committee today at 4:30 in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse.
Each area of women's living groups
will be given the opportunity to
attend one of these talks. Today's
talk will be open to all residents of
the Lower Row.
The committee will be at Roble on
Feb. 2; Lagunita, Feb. 7; Union Units
and Ventura, Feb. 9, in the Women's
Clubhouse; Branner, Feb. 14; Upper
Row, Off-Campus and Union Resi
dence, Feb. 23, in Women's Club
house.
