# Playground Croup Will Meet Today
## 
###

YWCA playground program may at
tend a meeting today at 5 p.m. in the
"Y" office, third floor, Women's
Clubhouse.
YWCA officers will explain the
program, which is designed for the
children in the area of Sherman
Playground, Palo Alto.
YWCA officers request that those
now working on the program and
former workers attend.
