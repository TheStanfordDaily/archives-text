# Jorda to Conduct SF Symphony In Mem And Monday
## 
### 
Enrique Jorda will conduct the
San Francisco Symphony in Memo
rial Auditorium Monday night at
8:15 in its only Peninsula appear
ance of the season.
Tickets for the concert, an extra
program of the Concert Series, are
available at the Concert Series in
the Nitery from 1 to 5 p.m. until
Friday.
Violinist David Ahel will be solo
ist with the Symphony, ploying
Tschaikowsky's Concerto in D for
Violin and Orchestra.
Orchestral part of the program
will include Berlioz's "Roman Carni
val" overture, Beethoven's Sympho
ny No. 1 and Ravel's "La Valse "
Prices for the concert are $2.25,
$2, $1.75 and $1.50.
