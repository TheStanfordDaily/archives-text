# SF Symphony In Mem Aud Monday
## 
### 
The San Francisco Symphony, un
der the baton of Enrique Jorda, will
perform in Memorial Auditorium on
Monday night at 8:15.
This will be the only Peninsula ap
pearance of the orchesra this season.
The program is an extra event in
the regular Concert Series, and tick
ets are available in the Nitery from
1 to 5 p.m. today.
David Abel, violinist, will be the
soloist with the Symphony, playing
the Tschaikowsky Concerto in D for
Violin and Orchestra.
Jorda will conduct the orchestra
in Berlioz's "Roman Carnival" over
ture, Beethoven's Symphony No. 1
and Ravel's "La Valse."
Ticket prices for the concert are
$2.25, $2, $1.75 and $1.50.
