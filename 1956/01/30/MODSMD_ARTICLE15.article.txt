# Jorda to Conduct Symphony Tonight
## 
### 
The San Francisco Symphony
will appear in Memorial Audito
rium tonight at 8:15 in its only
Peninsula concert of the season.
Young Bay Area violinist David
Abel will be guest soloist with the
orchestra, playing Tchaikowsky's
Violin Concerto in D.
Abel will also he soloist with
the Symphony at its regular
Thursday night concert at ihe San
Francisco Opera House.
Enrique Jorda will conduct the
orchestra in Berlioz's "Roman
Carnival" overture, Beethoven's
Symphony No. 1 and Ravel's "La I
Valse."
Tickets for the concert, an ex- ]
tra program of Ihe Concert Se- j
ries, are available at the Concert
Series office in the Nitery from 1 !
to 5 p.m. today. Prices are $2.2.1, i
$2. $1.75 and $1.50.
