# New Study Group Will Meet Tonight
## 
### 
The study group on "Indian
Rights and American Justice" will
hold its first meeting at 8 tonight
in the YVVCA office in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. All interested stu
dents are invited to attend.
The study group is sponsored
by Memorial Church. It will con
tinue to meet on Monday nights
and will divide its time between
study sessions and meetings de
voted to guest speakers.
