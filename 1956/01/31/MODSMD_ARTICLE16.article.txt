# Quad Pledge List Deadline Is Today
## 
### 
Deadline is today for eating
clubs and fraternities to submit
pledge lists to the Quad, accord
ing to Editor Tom Dant.
Club and house representatives
may leave the lists at the Quad
office between 3 and 5 p.m. today.
Lists left at any other time may
be put under the door, Dant
added.
