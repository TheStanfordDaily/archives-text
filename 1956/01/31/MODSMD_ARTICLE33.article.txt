# Immunization Plan Begins for Students
## 
### 
The Student Health Committee
begins its yearly immunization
program today.
This program includes a series
of shots against smallpox, para
typhoid and tetanus, and a second
scries against typhus, cholera and
plague especially set up for those
traveling abroad next summer.
The immunizations are given
every Tuesday from 1 to 5 p.m.
in the nurses' office in the Health
Service.
