# Dons Still First; Face State Next
## 
### 
The nation's number one team
battles for first place in its own
conference tonight as the USF
Dons host San Jose State in Kezar
Pavilion.
Both the Dons, winners of their
last 40 games, and the Spartans
are unbeaten in California Bas
ketball Association action.
San Francisco retained its top
position by toppling California
last Saturday by a 33-24 margin.
Dayton, in spite of a loss to sixth
ranked Louisville over the week
end, clung to its number two spot
on the United Press poll.
UCLA was ranked 13th by the
UP writers while Stanford held
down the number 22 position.
Washington was the only other
PCC team to receive a vote.
