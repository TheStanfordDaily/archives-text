# Talent Show Tickets on Sale
## 
### 
Tickets go on sale today at 1:30
p.m. for the Foreign Students'
Talent Show to he held February
9, 10 and 11 at the Utile Theatre.
Members of the Institute of In
ternational Relations will sell the
tickets for $1 at the Peninsula
Box Office, Town and Country
Village, according to Business
Manager George Abel.
Tickets will also sell next week
in a booth on campus and at the
door on the nights of the perform
ance.
Rehearsals are now under way
and backstage workers are need
ed. according to Abel.
John Jamieson, assisted by Bar
bara Armstrong, directs the show.
