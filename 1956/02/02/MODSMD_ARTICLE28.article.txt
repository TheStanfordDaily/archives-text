# Freshmen Plan Stag Dance Saturday Night
## 
### 
The Class of '59 will hold its
Bohemian dance at the Pavilion
Saturday night from 9 p.m. to 1
a.m. The dance is stag.
Spike Lynch and his five-piece
orchestra will provide the music
for the dance.
Admission is by class card.
