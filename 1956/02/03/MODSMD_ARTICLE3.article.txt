# 'Dangerous Corner' Closes
## 
### 
Don Soule and Astrid Willsrud have leading roles in the Stan
lord Players' production ol' .1. B. Priestley's melodrama, "Danger
ous Corner," tonight and tomorrow night in the Little Theater.
Curtain time is 8:15 both nights. Tickets are still available at the
Memorial Auditorium box office. —Daily photo by Jack Churchill.
