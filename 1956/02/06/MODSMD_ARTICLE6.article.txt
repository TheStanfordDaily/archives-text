# Scholarships Open For Austrian Study
## 
### 
Four scholarships for graduate
study in Austria during 19">6-57
are being offered to United States
students. March 1 is the closing
date for applications.
The scholarships include travel
allowances and eight monthly
payments of about $100.
Application blanks may be se
cured from the U. S. Student De
partment of the Institute of Inter
national Education, 1 East 67th
Street, New York, N. Y.
