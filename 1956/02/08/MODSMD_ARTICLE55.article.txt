# Frosh Class Card Sale Nets $5,700
## 
### 
Ninety-one per cent of the Class
of '59 bought class cards during
the membership drive which end
ed Saturday. This gives the class
a treasury of approximately $5,-
700, according to drive chairman
Steve Tallent.
Tallent termed the results "sat
isfactory." A 98 per cent goal was
set at the start of the sale.
Cards were sold to 97 per cent
of the freshman women.
