HEARING CASES AT MOOT COURT trials last night were, left
to right, Associate Justice Jim Moore, Chief Justice John B. Hurl
but and Associate Justice Bob Redford. Moore and Redford are
third-year law students and Hurlbut is a faculty member. They
are judging the ease of Rock v. Secretary of State, presented by
John Luther. Luther is one of 24 second-year law students com
peting in the first round of the Moot Courtcontest. Simultaneously,
two other cases were being heard in other rooms. The cases were
State of Stanford v. Michaels and Davis v. State of Stanford. The
first round will continue through tomorrow night. Winners of
last night's hearings will be announced tomorrow.
