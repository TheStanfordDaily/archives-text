# Students Involved In Auto Accident
## 
### 
Two automobiles were badly
damaged Wednesday afternoon in
an accident at the corner of Gal
vez and Pine, according to Police
Chief Gordon Davis. No one was
hurt.
Involved in the crack-up were
cars driven by two students,
Thomas M. Kurihara, 714 Santa
Ynez, and Peter Bradford, Stern
674.
Kurihara, traveling north on
Galvez, caused the accident, ac
cording to Police Chief Davis, by
making an illegal left turn in
front of the southbound car of
Bradford.
