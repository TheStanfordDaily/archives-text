# Axe Society to Hold Interviews Today
## 
### 
Axe Society tryouts will be held
today, tomorrow and Wednesday
from 4 to 5:30 p.m.
Interviews will be conducted in
the Legislature and Axe Society
Excom offices. Sign-up sheets are
posted outside the ASSU office.
Axe Society is a service organi
zation concerned primarily with
helping the Alumni Association
with their on-campus functions,
according to Julie Olson, secre
tary.
Its current job is editing the
Frosh Handbook, but it also par
ticipates in charity drives and
conducts a host program for the
General Secretary's Office, ac
cording to Miss Olson.
