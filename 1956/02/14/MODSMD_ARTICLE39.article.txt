# Indian Wrestlers To Face SF State
## 
### 
Coach Wesley Ruff's wrestling
team will host San Francisco
State tonight in quest of its fifth
victory of the season against iwo
losses.
Slated to grapple against San
Francisco State are Bob Fisher at
123 pounds, Gerald Lowell at 130,
George Ong at 137, Pete Likins
at 147, Bob Carmody at 157, Alan
Laubscher at IG7, Bill Pitkin at
177 and Koger Edelson in the
heavyweight class.
Pacing the Indians this season
has been 147-pounder Pete Likins,
who is undefeated.
Likins missed the last match
but is expected to be ready to
night.
