# Margery Bailey To Lecture On 'Criminal Actor'
## 
### 
Dr. Margery Bailey of the Stan
ford English department will be
featured at Theatre Matinee pro
gram this afternoon in the Little
Theatre at 4:15. She will speak on
"The Criminal Actor."
Professor Bailey, a specialist in
18th century literature and Shake
spearean production, has been on
the Stanford faculty since 1915.
In that time she has been active
in local theater, in the Drama
tists' Alliance, which encourages
the production of original drama
with an annual contest for new
plays, and in the Ashland (Ore.)
Shakespearean Festival, at which
she has acted and taught a field
course in Shakespeare.
