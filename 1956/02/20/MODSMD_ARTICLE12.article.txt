# Williams Fraternity Admits 2 Negroes
## 
### 
WILLIAMSTOWN, Mass. (I'P)
Two Negro students around
whom centered a major contro
versy within the national society
of Sigma Phi have been initiated
into the Williams College chapter
of the fraternity.
Earlier this month the national
society of Sigma Phi condemned
the Williams chapter for pledging
Negroes and called on its 3000
members to expel the unit.
