# Union Units Dance At Mark Hopkins
## 
### 
Union Units will hold their Win
ter Formal Friday in the Room of
the Dons, Mark Hopkins Hotel,
San Francisco.
Leroy Hueslon and his five
piece orchestra will play at the
formal, which will last from 9
p.m. to 1 a.m. Tickets are $2 and
are available from members of
the ticket committee in the units.
