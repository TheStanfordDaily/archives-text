# Sun Valley Signups Will Close Today
## 
### 
Today is the last day for stu
dents to sign up for the annual
skiing week held at Sun Valley
every spring vacation, according
to Leon Nelson, Ski Club presi
dent.
Reservations are being taken in
front of the Cellar from 11 a.m. to
2 p.m. and in the Nitery from 3
to 5 p.m.
A $20 deposit is required at the
time of reservation and will be
credited to the total cost.
