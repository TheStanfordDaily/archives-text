# Shell and Oar Has Crew Workday
## 
### 
Stanford crew in conjunction
with Shell and Oar Society held
a workday on Feb. J1 at their
Redwood City boat house to make
needed repairs on the house and
equipment and to give the women
a chance to go out in the shells
for the first time.
Shell and Oar is composed of
about 30 women who aid the crew.
