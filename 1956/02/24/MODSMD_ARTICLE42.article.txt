# JV Hoopsters Meet Mare Navy, CCSF
## 
### 
George Lynn's junior varsity
basketball team hosts Mate Island
tonight and City College of San
Francisco tomorrow afternoon to
close out their intercollegiate sea
son.
The Braves face the sailors to
night at 8 and battle CCSF at 5:45
tomorrow afternoon. The Stan
ford freshman quintet edged the
Rams by a tight 63-61 margin last
week.
Expected to pace the Brave
hoopsters are jump-shooting Pete
Mansfield, Jerry Rose and John
Henderson.
