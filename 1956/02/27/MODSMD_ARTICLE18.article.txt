# Game Tickets To Go on Sale at BAG
## 
### 
Tickets for the .Stanford-Cali
fornia basketball game at Berk
eley March 112) and in Stanford
Pavilion March 10 go on sale at
the Athletic Department at 9
a.m. today.
General admission tickets for
the San Jose State game at
Stanford, March will also go
on sale.
Student admission price for
each of the California games is
50 cents. The San Jose State
game is free for students.
General admission price for
the California game at Berkeley
is $1.75. For the game at Stan
ford it is $1.50.
