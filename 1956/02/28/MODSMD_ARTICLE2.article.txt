# Gateway Singers Will Be Featured At Soph Cotillion
## 
### 
The Gateway Singers from the
hungry i, a group specializing in
new versions of folk songs, will
be the featured entertainment at
the Soph Cotillion Friday.
Thd group, which consists of
three men and a girl, sings while
accompanying themselves on gui
tar, bass and five-string banjo.
They will entertain at the Cotil
lion from 10 to 10:30 p.m.
Tickets for the dance, which
will be at the Sir Francis Drake
in San Francisco, will continue on
sale for the remainder of this
week.
They will be sold at the Post
Office and Enginering Corner for
$2.25 for sophomore class card
holders and $2.75 for other stu
dents.
Jimmy Diamond's orchestra
will play for the dance, which
will be held from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m.
Dress will be cocktail instead of
formal.
