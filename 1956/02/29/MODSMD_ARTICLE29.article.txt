# Dance Interpreter To Speak on India
## 
### 
Shndari Shridhai ani, interpret
er of Indian dances, will speak on
dances, customs and culture of
India at tonight's meeting of the
Stanford Folk Dancers in the
Women's Gymnasium.
Mrs. Shridharani, wife of Dr.
Krishnalai Shridharani, author
and Indian government official,
will dance and show Indian cos
tumes.
She is currently touring the
United States and has studied and
danced in England and Europe.
She is the founder and director of
tiif Trizeni Art Center in New
Delhi.
