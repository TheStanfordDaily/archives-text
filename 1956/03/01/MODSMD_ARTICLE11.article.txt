# Area Young GOP's To Hear Dolwig
## 
### 
California Assemblyman Dirk
Dolwig will be guest of the Stan
ford and Menlo Atherton Young
Republican groups tonight.
He will speak on current politi
cal events at a meeting at 1140
Hobart Str., Menlo Park, to be
followed by a general question
and-answer period. Refreshments
will be served.
Rides to the gathering, leaving
at 7:30 p.m., may be obtained by
calling Chris Brookhouse at Wil
bur Hall.
