# Iran Expels Soviet Attache for Spying
## 
### 
TEHRAN, Iran. March 1 (U.R)
Iran today ordered the assistant
military attache of the Soviet em
bassy expelled as a spy. He was
arrested with an Iranian air force
officer in an automobile contain
ing secret documents.
Immediately after the arrest,
Iran charged that Russia has
stepped up espionage operations
since Iran joined the pro-Western
Baghdad Pact last summer.
