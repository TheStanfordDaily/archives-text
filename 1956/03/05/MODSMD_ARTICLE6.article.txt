Students interested in discuss
ing the possible formation of a
Stanford chapter of the National
Association for the Advancement
of Colored People are invited to
attend a meeting tomorrow after
noon at 4 in the Women's Club
house.
A previous campus chapter was
active until about when lack
of interest caused it to disband.
At tomorrow's meeting some of
the possible activities which an
NAACP chapter could perform
will be discussed.
