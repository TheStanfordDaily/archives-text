# Off-Campus Work Petitions Available
## 
### 
The Student - Faculty Commit
tee meets every three weeks to
discuss the approval of petitions
of students wishing to contact
students on campus for off-cam
pus business organizations.
Any student interested is urged
to apply for a petition, according
to Marcia Kirwan, president. The
committee considers these appli
cations and enforces certain pro
tective regulations.
Prohibitions include no displays
of the merchandise on campus
and no door-to-door soliciting.
Anyone wishing to contact stu
dents fur any business organiza
tion may apply for a petition by
contacting Gladys Perry at the
student employment office. For
more information, students may
contact Marcia Kirwan.
