# Papoose Netters Score Win Over Downey High, 7-2
## 
### 
Stanford's frosh tennis team
rolled past Downey High School
of Modesto Monday by a 7-2 score.
Only Rudy Hernando, Downey
ace, managed to outplay the Pa
poose netters.
Hernando topped Stanford's
Dave Nelson in the first singles
match, 7-5, 6-4. In first doubles
play, Hernando teamed with Bob
Hilgeman to whip Nelson and
Brooks Rawlins, 10-8, 6-4.
The frosh swept the other five
singles and two doubles matches.
Other results: Rawlins def. Hil
geman, 6-4, 7-5; Dick Gould def.
Jack Griffin, 5-7, 10-8, 6-1; A 1 Gal
laway def. Ray Harader, 6-4, 6-2;
Dave Baldwin def. Greg Mercia
dis, 6-0, 6-3; and Hal Reynolds def.
Tom Muench, 6-2, 6-2.
In doubles play Carrush and
Johnson def. Griffin and Harader,
default, and Griffin and Dorman
def. Merciadis and Muench, 6-0,
6-2.
