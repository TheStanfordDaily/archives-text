# Dean Will Explain Tuition Exchange
## 
###

Winbigler will give the pros and
cons of .joining the American As
sociation of University Professors
Tuition Exchange at the Stanford
chapter's meeting today at 4:15
p.m. in Room 2. Cubberley.
The Tuition Exchange is an
agency for administering a pro
gram under which faculty sons
and daughters from any member
institution arc granted free tui
tion to other member institutions.
