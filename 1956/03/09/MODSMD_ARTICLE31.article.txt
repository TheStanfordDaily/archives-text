# Senior Programs Are Still on Sale
## 
### 
Commencement programs and
announcements will continue on
sale at the Bookstore for the re
mainder of the quarter, according
to sales chairman Mirni Donovan.
The leather - bound programs
cost $1.50 and contain the names
of all candidates for degrees, plus
a schedule of Commencement
week activities. Announcements
sell for 15 cents.
The Class Scroll will be avail
able for all seniors to sign al the
Pavilion on Reg Day and at the
Registrar's Office on days fol
lowing.
