Employment Interviews
1956 ENGINEERING GRADUATES
AND ENGINEERING STUDENTS
Prospective 1956 graduates and under-graduates
in Mining Engineering, Metallurgical Engineering,
Mechanical Engineering, Industrial Engineering, and
Geology interested in permanent or summer employ
ment with Kennecott Copper Corporation, Nevada
Mines Division, at McCill or Ruth, Nevada, may se
cure interviews with representatives of the Company
on APRIL 4 and 5, 1956.
CONTACT MR. R. C. BEVERSTOCK, ASSISTANT DIRECTOR OF
PLACEMENT FOR APPOINTMENTS WITH THE
COMPANY REPRESENTATIVES
