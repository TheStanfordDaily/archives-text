# '58 Class Reunion Scheduled Today
## 
### 
The sophomore class will hold
its spring quarter reunion this
afternoon from 3 to 6 at Mangini's
Barn.
Bob Scobey's .Dixieland Band
will play for the event; which was
originally scheduled for yester
day. Admission to the reunion is
50 cents with a class card or 7.1
cents without.
General chairman is Bill Duke,
Phil Euler is in charge of enter
tainment and Alden Danncr is
handling publicity.
