# Crew Will Launch Season Saturday
## 
### 
Stanford's crew will open its
season Saturday at 11:30 a.m. in
the Redwood City yacht harbor
when it hosts Southern Califor
nia.
In preliminary events there will
be a frosh race at 10:30 and a jun
ior varsity clash at 11 a.m.
All races will be over the 2000-
meter Olympic course.
Manning Stanford's varsity
boat will be Gordon Best, stroke;
Jim Wachter, seven; Bob Clark,
six; Alan MacPherson, five; John
Shumacher. four; Will Stinson,
three; A 1 Loskamp, two; Ron
Rose, bow; and Rowd Davis, cox
swain.
