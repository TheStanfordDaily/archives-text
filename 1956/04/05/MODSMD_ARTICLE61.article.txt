# Cosmo Club to Meet At Bowman Tomorrow
## 
### 
Cosmo Club will hold its first
meeting of the quarter tomorrow
from 3 to 6 p.m. in Bowman Alum
ni House, according to Peter Kiri
anoff, vice-president.
The club will also hold a dance,
open to the public, Friday night
from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m. at Chez
Yvonne.
