# Kabuki Concert Ushers Needed
## 
### 
Student ushers for the Kabuki
Dancers' Concert Series appear
ance Sunday night at 8:15 in Me
morial Auditorium are still need
ed, according to Manager Dave
Sears.
Ushers must work for a few
hours this week to be eligible,
Sears said. They are admitted
free to the concert.
Students may apply to the Con
cert Series office in the Nitery
from 1 to 5 p.m. today and to
morrow.
