# Employment Card Changes Advised
## 
### 
Students planning to work part
time during spring quarter have
been requested by the Student
Employment Office to reactivate
their employment cards.
Baby-sitting is included as part
time employment.
Class schedules, address and
job availability are included in
the information on the employ
ment cards.
The employment office, located
in Room 1, maintains listings of
permanent and temporary part
time jobs, odd jobs and a few full
time jobs.
