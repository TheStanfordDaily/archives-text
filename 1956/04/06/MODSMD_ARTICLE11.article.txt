# Frosh Beach Party To Be Held Sunday

### 
Freshmen will congregate at

the Class of '59's informal beach
narty. A car parade will leave Wil

dirfner, according to Mimi Zants,
chairman.
Tickets may be obtained from
freshman corridor and floor so
