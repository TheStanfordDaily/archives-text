# Senior to Attend Mock Convention
## 
### 
Stanford senior Joe Poole will
represent Wisconsin in the Repub
lican mock presidential nominat
ing convention this weekend at
the University of Southern Cali
fornia.
The Los Angeles university will
host more than 1300 delegates
from 45 California colleges in the
mock Republican and Democratic
conventions.
Minnesota's Governor Orville
Freeman and United States Sen
ator Thomas H. Kuchel will be
keynote speakers for the two con
ventions being held for the first
time for West Coast universities.
The idea for the student-run
meetings originated at Oberlin
College in Ohio, where such con
claves have been held for years.
