# Folk Dancers Start On New Instruction
## 
### 
Stanford Folk Dancers will be
gin a new program ef beginning,
intermediate and advanced in
struction tonight at 8 p.m. in the
Village Auditorium.
Among the dances to be taught
at this first meeting are Melon
dita Tango, Minskl Dinskl and
Treggnertanz. There will also be
square dancing.
Rides will leave the Bookstore
at 7:30 p.m. There is no admission
charge.
