# Stanford NAACP To Elect Officers
## 
### 
Stanford's newly-revived chap
ter of the National Association
for the Advancement of Colored
People will elect officers at its
first meeting of spring quarter
Wednesday at 8 p.m. in Room 36.
Frank Williams, NAACP west
ern regional director, will discuss
the organization's objectives and
activities.
Interested students are invited
to attend.
