# Mellinkoff Award To Be Established
## 
### 
Establishment of the Mellinkoff
Medal for public speaking will be
discussed at a dinner tomorrow
at Union Dining Hall.
The medal will be awarded an
nually to a Stanford student who
excels in public speaking. The
award is being established by Mrs.
Albeit Mellinkoff in honor of her
four children, all Stanford gradu
ates.
Her son, Abe Mellinkoff. was a
former editor of The Stanford
Daily and is now city editor of
the San Francisco Chronicle.
