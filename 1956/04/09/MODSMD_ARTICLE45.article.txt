# Junior Prom Bids Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Junior Prom bids go on sale
today from 8 a.m. to 3 p.m. at
Engineering Coiner and the Cel
lar.
Ticket sales will continue until
April 20, day of the Prom, or until
they are sold out. according to
Mary Bell and Jim Skelly, ticket
sales co-chairmen. Bids are $3.50
a couple.
Theme of this year's Prom,
which will be held at the Shera
ton-Palace in San Francisco, is
"A Garden in Gold." Jerry Gray's
band is providing the music.
