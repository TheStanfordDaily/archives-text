# Yell Leader School Begins Tomorrow
## 
### 
Jim Walters and his assistants
will conduct a training course in
yell leading from 4 to 5 p.m. to
morrow, Wednesday and Thurs
day at the Theta Chi house.
Object of the course, says Wal
ters, is to teach techniques of yell
leading and to talk over problems
with those interested in future
tryouts for the position of head
yell leader or assistant yell lead
ers.
