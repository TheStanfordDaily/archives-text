# Walters to Lead Yell Leader Course
## 
### 
A training' course in yell lead
ing will be held from 4 to 5 today,
tomorrow and Thursday at the
Theta Chi house.
Jim Walters and his assistants
will conduct the lessons.
Techniques of yell leading and
advice to those interested in tly
ing out for the position will be
given, Walters said. Assistant yell
leading posts are open also.
