# Players Will Hold Tryouts Is Tonight
## 
### 
Tryouts for the final offering
of the Stanford Players' current
season, "Love's Labour's Lost,"
will be held this evening from 7:30
to 10:30 in Room 205 of Memorial
Auditorium.
The tryouts are open, director
Robert Loper emphasized, to all
students and staff, with or with
out experience.
The Shakespearean comedy of
manners opens May 23 in the Lit
tle Theater and offers speaking
roles for 12 men and five women.
Several non-speaking parts are to
be cast as well.
