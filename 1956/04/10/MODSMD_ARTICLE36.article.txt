# Babe Zaharias Struck By New Cancer Pain
## 
### 
GALVESTON. Tex.. April 0
| (UP) — Famed woman athlete
! Babe Zaharias, fighting for her
life in a battle with cancer, has
developed a new pain in her left
I side, and her husband said tonight
| she "is not in good condition."
