# Two Movies Will Open Film Series
## 
### 
A double feature will open the
spring quarter Film Series Thurs
day night in Cubberley Audito
rium.
"A Fool There Was" and "A
Corn of Wheat" will begin at 7:30.
Admission to the eight-week se
ries is through membership in the
Stanford Film Society.
Membership cards are now on
sale at the Memorial Auditorium
box office from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m.
