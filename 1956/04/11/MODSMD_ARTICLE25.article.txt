# Foreign Student Wins Time, Inc., Sales Award
## 
### 
Alain Coriat, graduate student
from Tangier, Morocco, has won
a second prize in a competition
sponsored by Time, Inc., to select
the outstanding magazine sales
men among many college stu
dents.
Coriat, a graduate of the Uni
versity of the Hautes Etudes Com
mereiales in Paris, came to this
country in 1955. He is working
for a master's degree in business
administration.
Coriat's prize is a $250 invest
ment. in a mutual fund of his own
choosing.
