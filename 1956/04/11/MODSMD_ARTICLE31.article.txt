# Mural Spring Sports Season Begins Monday
## 
### 
Intramural softball and volley
ball will begin Monday, Intramu
ral Director Elwyn Bugge announ
ced yesterday.
League assignments will be
posted in the gym this afternoon,
Bugge said.
He also requested that softball
rosters be filed in the mural office
this week and announced that the
softball fields would be available
for practice this week.
