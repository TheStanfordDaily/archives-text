# Theme for Dance ‘French Apache’
## 
### 
"French Apache" will be the
theme of the all-campus dance to
be held tomorrow night from 112) to
i 1 at the Theta Xi house.
Tight, slit skirts or full can-can
outfits with sweaters or peasant
blouses are recommended costume
for women. Men may appear in
loose pants, "T" shirts, turtle-neck
sweaters, berets and scarfs.
