# Lewis and Clark Prof to Preach
## 
### 
Dr. John B. Harrington, profes
sor of philosophy at Lewis and
Clark College, Portland, Oregon,
will deliver the sermon at the 11
a.m. Memorial Church service on
Sunday.
Harrington's topic will be "Be
yond Ourselves into Freedom."
John Hendricks will do one of
the readings.
Harrington will take the place
of University Chaplain Robert
Minto. Minto is away this Sunday
attending the annual conference
of the National Association of Col
lege and University Chaplains at
Vassar College.
