# Modern Art to Be Shown at Gallery
## 
### 
Prize-winning artists in Stan
ford's first annual Purchase
Awards Exhibition of Contempo
rary American Painters will be
announced Sunday, according to
N. Perry Moerdyke of Palo Alto,
chairman of the Committee for
Art at Stanford.
Major trends in contemporary
art are represented in paintings
by Stuart Davis, Adolph Gottlieb,
Mark Tobey and others.
The exhibition will open to the
public on Tuesday at the Stanford
Art Gallery and will continue un
til May 20.
