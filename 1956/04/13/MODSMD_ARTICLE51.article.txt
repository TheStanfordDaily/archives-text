# Albert Says Team To Hospitalize Foe
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 12
(UP> Frankie Albert, now coach
of the Forty • niner professional
football team and ex-Stanford ace,
today promised a hospital admin
istrator to send "a couple of
George Hulas' Chicago Bears in
for treatment" the next time the
Bears are out here.
