# SF Theater Offers Students Discount
## 
### 
San Francisco's Alcazar Thea
tre has announced a ticket dis
count plan for Stanford students,
according to Concert Series Man
ager Dave Sears.
Under the plan students may
pick up discount coupons at the
Series office from 1 to 5 p.m.,
Monday through Friday, and may
exchange them for tickets at the
Alcazar box office.
Regular $3.85 tickets will be
sold for $3. $3.30 for $2.20, $2.75
for $1.65 and $2.20 for $1.10.
The discount rates are good for
any matinee or evening perform
ance except Saturday night. Sears
said.
