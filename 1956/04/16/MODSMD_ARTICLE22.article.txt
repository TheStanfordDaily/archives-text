# Theatre Matinee Tryouts to Begin
## 
### 
Tryouts for two Theatre Mati
nee programs will be held tomor
row afternoon. Readings for an
original one-act drama, "Curfew
on Jasper Street" by Chase Webb,
are scheduled for 3 to 5 p.m. in
the Green Room of Little Theatre.
From 4 to 5:30 p.m. in Room
205, Memorial Auditorium, and
again Thursday, open tryouts will
be held by director Connie Ruys
for a medieval romance, "Lance
lot of Denmark."
