# Tennis Varsity Smashes Past Santa Clara
## Keesing Leads Farm To Easy 8-1 Victory Over SC's Broncos 
### 
Santa Clara's Broncos furnished
little opposition as the Stanford
varsity tennis squad rolled on
with an easy 8-1 victory yesterday
afternoon here. Roger Keesing,
# the Indians' number one man,
dropped Ed Pugh, Santa Clara's
best, 6-1, 6-8, 6-3, in the match fea
ture.
Other singles victors for Stan
ford were Gil White, who scored
a 6-4, 6-3 win over Terry McQuee
ny; Ernie Dußray, a 6-0, 6-2 victor
over Hal Koop; Ed West, who
dropped Santa Clara's Paul Neu
mann, 2-6, 6-2, 6-3, and Gene Neb
eker, with a 6*2, 6-0 mark over
Jim Barry.
In doubles play Keesing teamed
with Bob Bowden in squashing
P u g hand McQueeny, 6-0, 6-2.
Dave Duff and Phil Hogue, an
other Stanford pair, jumped Neu
mann and Koop, 6-1, 6-2. Dußray
and Shaun Corette dropped Larry
Cesar and Barry, 5-7, 6-1, 6-2.
The lone Santa Clara score
came on Larry Cesar's 6-1, 4-6, 6-3
win over Craig Ely.
