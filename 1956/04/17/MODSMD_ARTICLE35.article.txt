# Sequoia Article To Be Reprinted
## 
### 
An article which appeared in
the first issue of Sequoia will be
reprinted in Ivory Tower, literary
magazine uf University of Minne
sota.
A wire was received yesterday
from the magazine requesting the
right to reprint "Tom Heggen
and Mr. Roberts," by Dan Cohen.
The material for the article was
gathered at University of Minne
sota, where Heggen was an under
graduate and did much of his
early writing.
Sequoia has released the article
for reprint.
Next issue of the magazine will
appear on May 2.
