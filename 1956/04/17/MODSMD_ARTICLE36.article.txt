# Frosh, Cal to Hold Exchange Saturday
## 
### 
Stanford's freshman class will
hold an exchange with the Uni
versity of California frosh Satur
day on the UC campus.
Tickets will be sold that day,
priced at $1 to class card holders
and $1.50 to others. Limited trans
portation is being provided free
to class card holders.
Sign-up sheets for buses will be
posted at Wilbur, Branner and
Roble halls, according to Reid Ro
gers, frosh oxcom member.
Plans include outdoor activities,
dinner and a dance in the evening.
Muses are scheduled to leave Stan
ford at 2 p.m. and return at 10:30
p.m.
