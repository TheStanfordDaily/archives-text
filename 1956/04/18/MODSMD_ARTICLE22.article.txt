# Tuesday Matinee To Give Comedy

An original family comedy,
"The Urbans on Ursulines," writ
ten and directed by Ronald Ba/.a
rini, will be presented on this aft
ernoon's Theatre Matinee at 4:15
in the Little Theatre.
Featured in the cast are Ann
Simmons, Virginia Fry, Marilyn
Pool, Babeaux, Hart
Sprager, Mark Simmons and
Katharine Goldberg. Ba/arini, a
graduate student in Speech and
Drama, was seen here in the Stan
lord Players' production of
"Nightmare Abbey."
Theatre Matinee is open to the
public without charge.
