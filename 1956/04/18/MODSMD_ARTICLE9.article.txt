# Thief Takes $300 From KA House
## 
### 
In a daring burglary early yes
terday morning, $300 was stolen
from 15 wallets in the Kappa Al-
pha house, according to Police
Chief Gordon Davis.
Missing from one wallet is $21
which was lying in a bureau
drawer.
Wesley Foell, a member of the
fraternity, reported the theft to
police. Foell placed the time of the
burglary as some time between 2
and 6 a.m.
