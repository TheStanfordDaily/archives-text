# Seminar to Hear Chlorophyll Talk
## 
### 
Dr. James H. C. Smith from
Carnegie Institution will speak at
a Biological Sciences Seminar this
afternoon in Room 430, Jordan
Hall. Topic of the seminar is "For
mation of Chlorophyll in Leaves
and Leaf Extracts."
The seminar will be preceded by
a tea at 4 p.m. Students, faculty
and visitors are welcome.
