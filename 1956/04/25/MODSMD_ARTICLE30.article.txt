# Sterling Defends Press on Campus
## 
### 
President Wallace Slcrling said
in the April 14 issue of "Editor
and Publisher" that "the great
principle of freedom of the press
should operate just as strongly on
campus as elsewhere."
Sterling's statement appeared in
an article discussing the pros and
cons of freedom of the press for
college newspapers.
Other college presidents to ad
vocate freedom of the campus
press included Robert G. Sproul
of the University of California.
