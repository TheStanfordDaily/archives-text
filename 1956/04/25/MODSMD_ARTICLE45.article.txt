# Two Students Win Fulbright Awards
## 
### 
Two scholarships have been
awarded to students of the Stan
ford Geography department.
Eileen Conaghan won a Ful
bright Scholarship for study in
the International Training Center
for Aerial Surveys in Holland.
Mary Carmine Has accepted a
scholarship for advanced study at
Columbia University. She plans to
work for the M.A. degree.
