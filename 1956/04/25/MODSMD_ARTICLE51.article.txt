# Area Young GOP To Meet Friday
## 
### 
The Young Republicans will
meet Friday from 3 to 6 p.m. to
"kick-off" their membership cam
paign for the coming school year.
Informal talks will be given by
Dick Dolwig, candidate for State
Senator, and Carl Britschi, run
ning for assemblyman from this
district.
The meeting will be held at the
home of Mr. and Mrs. Junius
Cary, 361 Vine St., Menlo Park.
Refreshments will be served#for
50 cents and a Young Republican
membership card.
Rides will leave Wilbur, Lagu
nita and Roble Hails at 3:20 and
4:20 p.m.
