# Summer Chinese Course to Be Given
## 
### 
Asiatic and Slavic Department
will offer a concentrated second
year Chinese class this summer.
The course is offered for 15 units.
K. Y. Hsu, instructor in Chi
nese, will teach the class every
day from 9 to 12 during the 10-
week period. Anyone interested in
this course may contact Hsu at
Asiatic and Slavic office.
