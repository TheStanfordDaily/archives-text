# Area Young GOP Elects Officers
## 
### 
With refreshments in hand,
Young Republicans of the Stan
ford Area shouted approval of a
motion to "censure the Political
Union" at an informal lawn party
last Friday.
After hearing a short talk by
Carl "Ike" Britschgi, GOP can
didate for Assemblyman, the YH's
elected new officers and then
cheered through the "censure"
motion made by Bob Sprinkel.
Officers for the coming year
will be co-chairmen Cliff Kimber
and Everett Kindig, vice-president
Tim Forden and treasurer Bob
Baker.
Kimber is a senior from Sacra
mento and Kindig is a sophomore
from Burlingame. Retiring presi
dent is Steve Nve.
