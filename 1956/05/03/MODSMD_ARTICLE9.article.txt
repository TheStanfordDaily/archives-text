# ESPRI to Have Borneo Speaker
## 
### 
ESPRT, the Educational Society
for the Promotion of Relations-In
tercultural, will have H. T. Wu, a
Fulbright scholar from Borneo, as
its guest speaker today.
His topic, "How Chinese Are the
Americans?" will discuss the in
fluence of American culture on
Chinese immigrants and their
children.
The meeting, which is open to
the public, will be held al noon in
Room 57, Education Building.
