# Newspaperman To Present Case
## 
### 
A meeting will be held at the
Palo Alto Community Center,
Room R, at S p.m. tonight for
those interested in hearing Carl
and Anne Braden of Louisville,
Ky., present their case.
Braden, a newspaperman, has
been sentenced to 15 years' im
prisonment and fined $5000 for
sedition. Six others, including his
wife Anne, have been indicted and
are awaiting trial.
