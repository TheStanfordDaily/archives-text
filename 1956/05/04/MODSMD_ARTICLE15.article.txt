## 
### 
Richard Lawson has been se

interne for the coming summer,
according to the Political Science
Department.
Lawson, a senior, will train in
secretariat affairs at UN head
quarters in New York, along with
16 other internes from all over
the world.
He is an international relations
major and president of the Insti
tute of International Relations.
