# AWS Will Study Living Problems
## 
### 
A now committee has been
formed by AWS to investigate the
problems and mechanics of han
dling group living problems
through a student group, accord
ing to Ellic Kronfield, AWS presi
dent.
The committee won't handle
cases itself but will look into the
problem and recommend what
machinery should be set up to
handle cases of residence diffi
culties.
Carol Eymann has been named
chairman of the new group. Com
mittee members are Carol de
Lima, Kay Hamilton, Carolyn
Greenwood, Bobbie Herrmann,
Mudgie Knowles, Helen Nebeker,
Pat Oling, Jane Pyle and Nancy
Wiser.
