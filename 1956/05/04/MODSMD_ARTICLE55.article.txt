# WRA Play Day To Be Tomorrow
## 
### 
Medha Yodh von Essen will
teach a master lesson in dances
from India as part of the Wom
en's Recreation Association Play
Day activities tomorrow morning.
The master lesson will last from
10 a.m. to 12 noon, according to
publicity chairman Karen
Braucht.
Mrs. von Essen is a graduate of
Stanford and a former Orchesis
member.
Other activities women may
participate in during the Play Day
include volleyball, Softball, pitch
and - putt golf tournament and
archery shoots.
Lunch will be served at 12.
