# Spring Show Tickets on Sale
## 
### 
Tickets for "Wonderful Town,"
this year's Spring Show, are being
sold on Engineering Corner from
1) a.m. to 2 p.m. at the Memorial
Hall box office from 12:30 to 4:30
p.m. and all day at the Peninsula
Box Office, Town and Country
Village.
Tickets for the show, which
opens May 0 al Memorial Hall,
sell for 00 cents, $1.25 and $1.50.
The musical, which features
dancing and jazz music by Leon
ard Bernstein, is based on the ac
tual experiences of two sisters
when they arrive in New York in
search of careers. Bobbi Tantau
And Marilyn Poppino play the
parts of the two adventurous sis
ters. Supporting them in the other
lead roles are Hart Spraeger, Fred
Cone, Gary Waynesmith and Wil
liam Kinesolvine.
