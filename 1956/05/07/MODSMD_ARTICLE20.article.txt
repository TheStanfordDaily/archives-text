# Scientist to Talk On Disarmament
## 
### 
The Stanford Chapter of the
Federation of American Scientists
will present an informal talk on
"The Disarmament Outlook" to
morrow at 12:10 p.m. in Bowman
Alumni House.
Speaker will be David L. Hill,
member of the Disarmament
Studies Committee of the Federa
tion of American Scientists.
