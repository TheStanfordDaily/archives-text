# Prospective 'Y' Advisers to Meet
## 
### 
A meeting will be held tonight
for all women interested in advis
ing YWCA high school clubs in
Palo Alto.
The meeting will be held on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house at 7:30 p.m. All women
who will be seniors or graduate
students next year are eligible for
the jobs.
High school faculty counselors
and former advisers will be pres
ent to explain their duties and to
answer questions.
Personal interviews for the jobs
will he held tomorrow and Friday
from 1:30 to 3:30 p.m. at the
Women's Clubhouse. Signup
sheets will be available at the
meeting.
