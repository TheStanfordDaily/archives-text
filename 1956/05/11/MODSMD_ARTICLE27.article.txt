# Hilton Talk on Colombia To Begin KZSU Series
## 
### 
Ronald B. Hilton, head of the
Hispanic - American Department,
will give a tape-recorded talk on
"The Tragedy of Colombia" Sun
day at 3:30 p.m. on KZSU.
This is the first in a series of
"Hilton Tape" programs schedul
ed by the campus station.
