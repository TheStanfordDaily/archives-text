# 'Look, Ma—No Hands'
## 
### 
Friday afternoon the fountain in front of the Library sprouted
wings. A mysterious contraption appeared out of nowhere and
settled over the fountain, causing questions, comment and more
questions. But the thing proved 110 threat to Steve Carr, Tom Karly
and Tad Cody who climbed atop it perhaps to play Superman.
