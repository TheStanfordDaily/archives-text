# Hughes to Speak On Tues. Series
## 
### 
"French Politics and the North
African Crisis" will be discussed
by Professor H. Stuart Hughes on
tomorrow night's Tuesday Series
program at 7:30 in Memorial Au
ditorium.
Dr. Hughes is executive head of
the Department of History at
Stanford. He recently studied in
Europe on a Guggenheim fellow
ship.
Thomas A. Bailey, history pro
fessor. will introduce the speaker.
The lecture is open to the public
free of charge.
