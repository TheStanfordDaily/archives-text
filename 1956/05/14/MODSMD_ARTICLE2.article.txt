# Scholar to Talk On Belgian Congo
## 
### 
Dr. J. A. Goris, noted scholar
and director of the Belgian In
formation Bureau for the United
States, will speak at Stanford to
morrow on the Belgian Congo.
His talk on "Social and Eco
nomic Developments in the Bel
gian Congo" is scheduled for 4:15
p.m. in Bowman Alumni House.
C. East on Rothwell, director of
the Hoover Institute and Library,
will preside.
Goris, whose offices are in New
York, has spent several years in
the Congo in his government's
service.
