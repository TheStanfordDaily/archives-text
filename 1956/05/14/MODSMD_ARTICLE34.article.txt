# Chi Psi Thinclads Roll To Intramural Victory
## Casper Wins Both Hurdles, Anchors Relay; Mazzoni Shatters Mural Shot-Put Record 
### 
Hurdler Dave Casper and distance runner Hans Morawitz sparked
Chi Psi to victory in the intramural track meet Saturday on Angell
Field.
Casper captured both hurdle races and anchored the triumphant
Chi Psi relay team. Morawitz led a three-man sweep of the mile by

the victors and finished second
in the 880.
Chi Psi tallied 52 points in the
meet to 38 for second-place Sigma
Chi. SAE finished third with 17,
El Campo scored 16, El Capitan
tallied 11 and Cedro 2 chalked up
IOMs to complete the top six
teams.
Shot-putter Tim Mazzoni of Del
ta Chi cracked the intramural
mark with a toss of 47-4.

Hurdler Dave Casper and distance runner Hans Morawitz sparked
Chi Psi to victory in the intramural track meet Saturday on Angell
Field.
Casper captured both hurdle races and anchored the triumphant
Chi Psi relay team. Morawitz led a three-man sweep of the mile by

the victors and finished second
in the 880.
Chi Psi tallied 52 points in the
meet to 38 for second-place Sigma
Chi. SAE finished third with 17,
El Campo scored 16, El Capitan
tallied 11 and Cedro 2 chalked up
IOMs to complete the top six
teams.
Shot-putter Tim Mazzoni of Del
ta Chi cracked the intramural
mark with a toss of 47-4.
