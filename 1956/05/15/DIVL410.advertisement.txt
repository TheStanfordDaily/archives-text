The Monterey Institute
of Foreign Studies
will hold a second summer session July 9 through August
17 on the famous Monterey peninsula. Members of the
teaching profession, graduates and undergraduates may
earn credit while taking intensive courses in the languages
and civilizations of France, Germany, Italy or Spain, com
pletely given in the chosen language by native instructors.
Courses will also be offered in Social Studies, including
History of Diplomacy (Fr. and Eng ), Foreign Governments,
International Trade, etc.
For catalog and full particulars write to
Box 1 522, Monterey, California, or
564 Mayfield Road, Stanford, California
