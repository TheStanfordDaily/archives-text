# '56 Notices
## 
### 
Tickets for the senior ferry
boat ride will be on sale today
and tomorrow for seniors only
and Friday for the whole cam
pus, according to senior class
president John Leland. Student
body cards will be checked. Price
for the tickets will be $5 a couple
for seniors.
The boat Harbor Queen will
cruise around San Francisco Bay
from 9 p.m. to midnight Friday,
May 25. One hundred ten tickets
are available for the trip.
This is the last week the senior
scroll will be available for signing
at the ASSU Office. Photostat
copies of the scroll will sell for
10 cents.
Ice cream sales sponsored by
the senior class will take place
periodically at the Quad corners.
