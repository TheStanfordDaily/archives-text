# Swiss Ambassador To Tour Campus
## 
### 
Swiss Minister to the United
States and Mme. Henry do Tor
rente, with their official party,
will be guests on campus today.
C. East on Rothwell, director of
the Hoover Institute and Library,
will be their host on a tour of the
libiarv and at a Union faculty
luncheon.
Albert Weitnauer. commercial
counselor of the Swiss delegation
in Washington, is accompanying
the de Torrentes on their tour of
the United States.
