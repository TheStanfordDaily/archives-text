# Glee Club to Make Two Appearances
## 
### 
The Men's Glee Club will make
two appearances this weekend.
As a special event at the Spring
Sing, the group plans to perform
non-competitively at the end while
the judges are making their de
cisions. Several numbers have
been announced, among them the
"Whiffenpoof Song," "Halls of
Ivy" and Stanford's alma mater.
On Saturday night the group
will perform before alumni re
union dinners at the Union and at
the general conference dinner at
Bowman Alumni House.
