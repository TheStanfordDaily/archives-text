# Earthquake Class To Hold Reunion
## 
### 
The golden anniversary reunion
of Stanford's "earthquake class"
of 1906 will he celebrated this
Saturday, along with 15 other
early-day classes.
The occasion will be the 21th
annual campus alumni confer
ence. A day-long program of fac
ulty lectures, discussions of Uni
versity affairs and sports events
is expected to draw over 1000
alumni and friends to campus.
The traditional joint reunion
dinner of all classes from IH!>2
through 1905 has been scheduled
for 6:15 p.m. in the Union. A. L.
Trowbridge, '05, Bakersfield, is
chairman and George Gamble, '05,
Palo Alto, vice-chairman.
