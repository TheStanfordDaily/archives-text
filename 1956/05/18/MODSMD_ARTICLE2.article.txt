# Theta Chi Slates Costume Dance
## 
### 
Saturday night Theta Chi will
hold its annual costume Gypsy
Dance from 9 to 1, featuring Al
Schaff's band and Don Martine of
Las Vegas.
There will be no admission
charge, and dancing wili be on a
skylight roof with refreshments
in the basement of the Theta Chi
house. As an added attraction,
Madame Zanzibar will be on hand
with her crystal ball.
