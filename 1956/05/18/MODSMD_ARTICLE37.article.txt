# Clergy to Address Discussion Group
## 
### 
A rabhi, a priest and a chaplain
will discuss Jewish, Catholic and
Protestant religious practices on
Sunday at 7:30 p.m. in Branner
lounge.
Discussion leaders will be Rabbi
Harris Hirschberg of Temple Beth
Jacob, Redwood City; Father
John Tierney of St. Ann's Chapel,
Palo Alto; and Assistant Chaplain
David Romeis, Memorial Church.
Before the discussion a movie,
"One God," will be shown. Memo
rial Church Committee and Bran
ner's Vespers Committee will
sponsor the program.
