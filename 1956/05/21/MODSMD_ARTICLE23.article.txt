# Soph Beach Party Tickets on Sale
## 
### 
Tickets go on sale today for the
sophomore class beach party to
be held Sunday at Natural Bridge
State Beach.
Price of tickets will be 75 cents
with a class card or $1 without.
The tickets will be sold on Quad
and in men's living groups.
The beach party will last from
11 a.m. to 5 p.m. and will include
a steak luncheon. A treasure hunt
will be held.
