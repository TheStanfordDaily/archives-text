# Universe Is Topic Of Tuesday Series
## 
### 
"Cosmology and Some of Its
Current Problems" will be dis
cussed by William Hunter McCrea,
a British scientist, at the Tuesday
Evening Series tomorrow night at
7:30 in Memorial Auditorium.
McCrea is a visiting professor
of astronomy at the University of
California. He received both the
M.A. and Ph.D. degrees at Cam
bridge University and has been a
professor of mathematics at Royal
Holloway College, University of
London, since 1944.
