# Gibbons, Foster Win Net Tourney
## 
### 
Marge Gibbons and Dick Foster
won the WRA sponsored mixed
doubles tennis tournament Friday.
They defeated Bonnie Mellvaine
and Gene Nebeker 6-3, 6-2 in the
finals.
The two couples had previously
beaten Val Hervey, and John Kroe
ger, Helen Nebeker and Syl Kline
in the semi-finals.
The matches Saturday climaxed
a tournament which had lasted
since April 21.
