# Moore Residents To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
All women who will be living in
Florence Moore Hall will meet to
night for the first lime at 7 p.m. in
their individual units.
The purpose of the meeting will
be to get acquainted with the other
residents and to nominate candi
dates for president, vice-president
and social chairman, secretary and
committe of eight members from
each group.
Meeting places and leaders of
the various residences are posted
in all women's living groups.
