# Soph Gets French Study Scholarship
## 
### 
Patricia Keene, a Ventura soph
omore majoring in French, has
been accepted for the 1956-57 Jun
ior Year in France, a year's for
eign study program administered
by Sweet Briar College.
The group of 88 students will
sail for France in September.
They will enroll in the winter
term of Sorbonne University in
Paris. While in-France they will
live with French families.
