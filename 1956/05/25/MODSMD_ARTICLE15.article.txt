# Thurber to Join SU Administration
## 
### 
Appointment of James P. Thur
ber Jr. as an associate general
secretary of Stanford University
was announced yesterday by Pres
ident Wallace Sterling.
Thurber, who has been with the
Wall Street Journal since 1953,
will serve with general secretary
David S. Jacobson. The general
secretary is in charge of the Uni
versity's fund-raising program.
Since his discharge from the
Army he has been on the Wall
Street Journal's reporting staff in
San Francisco, New York and De
troit. His last assignment with Ihe
paper was bureau manager in
Houston.
