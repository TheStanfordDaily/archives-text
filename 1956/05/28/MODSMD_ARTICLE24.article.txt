# Crew Featured In 'Sport' Article
## 
### 
The July issue of Sport Maga
zine, which will come out tomor
row, features an article by Dugal
O'Liam on "Stanford's Orphan
Crew."
"Disowned by their university,"
says Sport, "and without money
or equipment, these kids proved
there are still some amateurs left
in sports. They went out on their
own and built a winner."
The crew, coached by Lou Lind
sey, will compete next month in
the Intercollegiate Rowing Asso
ciation Regatta and the U.S.
Olympic tryouts at Lake Onon
daga, N.Y.
