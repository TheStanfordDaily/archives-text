# Steiner to Speak On Japan Tonight
## 
### 
"Japan Occupation and After"
will be the topic of a lecture to be
given by Dr. Kurt Steiner tonight
at 8 at Bowman Alumni House.
The talk is sponsored by Delta
Phi Epsilon, the foreign service
fraternity.
Dr. Steiner is the acting assist
ant professor of political science.
The discussion is open to the
public.
