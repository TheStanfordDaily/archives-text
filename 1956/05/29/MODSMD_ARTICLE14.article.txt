# Sterling Talks To Red Cross Today
## 
### 
President Wallace Sterling will
be the principal speaker at the an
nual meeting of the Palo Alto
Chapter of the American Red
Cross this afternoon.
The theme of the 40th annual
meeting is "International Under
standing." Other speakers will be
Hery Salim. Stanford Indonesian
student, and Miss Prudence Vyn,
Palo Alto High School senior and
chairman of the Junior Red Cross
Interschool Council.
The meeting, which is open to
the public, will be at I p.m. in the
garden of the Red Cross Chapter
House, 400 Mitchell Lane.
