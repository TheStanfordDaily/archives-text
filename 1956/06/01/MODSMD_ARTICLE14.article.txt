# Symphonic Hand Will Give Concert
## 
### 
The last university activity be
fore dead and finals weeks, that
of the annual Symphonic Band
Concert, will begin at 8:15 Sunday
night at Memorial Auditorium.
The band, especially selected for
instrumentation, will play a pro
gram of 11 selections, including an
original composition by a Stan
ford student.
Julius Shuchat, band instructor,
will conduct the 65-plece group in
a concert open to the public free of
charge.
