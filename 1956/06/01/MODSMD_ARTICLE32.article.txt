# Spanish Play Set In Cubberley at 8
## 
### 
The American premiere of "Ma
drugada," a Spanish play by An
tonio Buero Vallejo, will he pre
sented tonight and tomorrow eve
ning in Cubberley Auditorium.
Curtain time for both perform
ances is 8 p.m.
The Spanish drama class, under
the guidance of Professor Isabel
Schevill, is producing the play.
Following the performance to
morrow, there will be a recep
tion for the audience in the Wom
en's Clubhouse given by the Span
ish Honor Society. An exhibition
of original paintings by Katherine
Benton, Bay Area artist, will be
featured at the reception.
