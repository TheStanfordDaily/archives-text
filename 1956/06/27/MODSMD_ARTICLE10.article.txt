# Choir Practice Begins Tomorrow
## 
### 
Any students or staff members
who are interested in singing may
still join the Memorial Church
Summer Choir by attending the
rehearsal scheduled for tomorrow
at 7 p.m. Students enrolled for
the summer quarter may earn
two units of credit. Rehearsals
will be held on Tuesdays at 5 p.m.
and Thursdays at 7 p.m. The
choir will sing regularly at the
Sunday morning services at the
Church.
