# Folk Dancers Begin Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford summer folk
dancing program will begin to
night at a "get-acquainted" folk
and square dance session from
7:30 to 9.
Mrs. Miriam Lidster of the De
partment of Physical Education
for Women will be the instructor
for the squares and mixers which
will be danced in the patio of the
Women's Gymnasium. It will be
sponsored by the ASSU and is
open to all students and faculty.
On Friday there will be student
conducted folk and square danc
ing from 8 to 11 p.m. in the Vil
lage Auditorium. Elementary and
intermediate dances will be done
under the sponsorship of the
Stanford Folkdancers.
Similar programs will be held
every Wednesday and Friday dur
ing the quarter.
