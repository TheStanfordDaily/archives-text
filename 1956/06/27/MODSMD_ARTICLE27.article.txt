# Professor Brown Heads Stanford Faculty Club
## 
### 
Leland H. Brown, professor of
electrical engineering, has been
elected 1956-57 president of the
Stanford Faculty Club. He suc
ceeds Dean of Students 11. Donald
Winbigler.
Other new officers are Alfred
H. Grommon, associate professor
of education and English, vice
president; Hobart N. Young, re
search associate, Hoover Institute
and Library, secretary; Robert O.
Houghton, assistant controller,
treasurer; and Clifford F. Weigle,
professor of journalism, and Ar
thur Kroeger, professor of mar
keting, directors.
