# Campus Shop Rents Bikes
## 
### 
Tired of walking? Stanford's
Campus Bike Shop has the an
swer.
The shop has a supply of both
men's and women's bicycles, which
they will rent to any student or
faculty member for an hour, a
day, a month, or a quarter.
Prices and other information
about the bike shop are available
at the shop itself, which is located
on St. Theresa Street across from
Bowman Alumni House.
In addition To rentals, the shop
repairs bikes and bike tires and
also sells new and used bikes.
