## 
###

ber 21, the University Cashier's
Office hours will be 10 to 12:3u

of Encina Hall, and regular Uni
versity bills should be paid there.

hours are 8 a.m. to 9 p.m., Mon
day through Thursday; 8 to 5 on
Fridays; 8 to 12 on Saturdays;
and 1 to 5 on Sundays.
