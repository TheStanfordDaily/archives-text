# Bowman House Reception Line
## 
### 

Forming the receiving line at the Bowman Reception, June 29, were left to right, Elmer C. Wagner,
Associate Registrar and Executive Officer for the Summer Quarter, Frederick E. Terman, Provost and
Dean of the School of Engineering. Mrs. Winbigler, and R. Donald Winbigler, Dean of Students, who are
greeting Larry Baum, Seattle, Wash., and Kathy Roberts, Orinda, Calif., both freshmen.
