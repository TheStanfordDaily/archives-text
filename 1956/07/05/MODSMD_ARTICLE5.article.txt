# Checks, Reg Cards Deadline Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tomorrow is the last day for
paying University board and
room bills without an added fee,
according to the controller's of
fice. The deadline for turning in
study cards is also tomorrow.
Payment may be made at the
cashier's office in the lobby of
Encina Hall. Checks accompa
nied by a statement may be
mailed to the cashier's office but
must be postmarked no later
than July 6.
IBM class cards and the of
ficial study list are due at the
Registrar's Office tomorrow.
All changes in the study list
must be initialed by the adviser
before the Registrar's Office
will accept them.
A $2 fee will be charged for
cards that are turned in after
Friday.
