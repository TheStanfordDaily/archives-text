# Volleyball Games Start This Week
## 
### 
The ASSU sponsored campus
wide volleyball competition got
started this week with the first
all men match Tuesday. The
tournament will continue for four
weeks, with the men's series be
ing played on July 17, 24. and 31,
and the mixed series being played
today, July 19, 26, and August 2.
Each team includes six members.
Neil Papiano, chairman of the
tournament, has announced that
the winners will be awarded free
dinners and trophies.
