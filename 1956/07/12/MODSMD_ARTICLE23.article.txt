# 700 Pounds of Melon Consumed
## 
### 
Satuiuay's Watermelon Feed
was termed a success by chair
man Hall Seely after the 700
pounds of melon had been com
pletely devoured by the Stanford
students.
Watermelon eating was only one
of the important happenings of
the afternoon. Between eating
the students engaged in volley
ball, baseball, tennis, swimming
and general getting acquainted.
For Saturday only the swimming
pool was open to men unescorted
by the women.
