# Bishop Shires Will Deliver Sermon
## 
### 
The Right Reverend Henry H.
Shires, Suffragan Bishop of the
Diocese of California (Episcopal),
will preach the sermon at Memo
rial Church this Sunday, Au
gust 5.
The service, which will be held
at 11 a.m., will include anthems
by the Memorial Church Summer
Choir, under the direction of Mr.
Herbert B. Nanney.
Following the service there will
be a coffee hour at Bowman
Alumni House. The public is in
vited.
