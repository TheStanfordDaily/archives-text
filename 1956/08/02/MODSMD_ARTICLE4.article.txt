# Composer, Pianist Will Lecture Here
## 
### 
Dr. Felix R. Labunski, com
poser, pianist, and professor of
music at the College-Conservatory
of Music of Cincinnati, will be the
guest speaker in the Tuesday eve
ning series this week.
Dr. Labunski will present a lec
ture entitled "European Music of
this Century." He will highlight
his talk with recorded illustra
tions.
Coming to the United States in
1936, Dr. Labunski taught at New
York City College, and then went
to Cincinnati.
The lecture will begin at 8 p.m.
in Memorial Hall, and is open to
the public.
