# Professor Ryan Receives Grant
## 
### 
Lawrence V. Ryan, assistant
professor of English at Stanford
University, has been awarded a
research grant at the Henry E.
Huntington Library and Art Gal
lery in San Marino.
He will return to Stanford in
the fall and continue to teach
courses in Medieval and Renais
sance literature and in Shake
speare.
Professor Ryan has been at
Stanford since 1952 in the English
Department and received his edu
cation at College of St. Thomas
and Northwestern University.
