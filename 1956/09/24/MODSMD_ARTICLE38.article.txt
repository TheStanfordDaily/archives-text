# Keio University Offers Grant
## 
### 
Keio University, Tokyo, Japan,
is offering a tuition scholarship
to a Stanford student, for the 1957-
58 school year.
Interested students may sign up
at the Institute of International
Relations desk in the Pavilion to
day and tomorrow or contact Ron
Simpson, DA 3-8756. Required is
at least one year of Japanese and
an interest in the cultural aspects
of Japan.
Studying at Stanford this year
under the program is Masao Oda
from Keio University.
