# Folkdancers Plan Opening Meeting
## 
### 
Stanford Folk and Square Dan
cers will hold their first meeting
of the quarter tomorrow in the
dance studio of the Women's
Gym from 7:30 to 9 p.m.
Ballet slippers, tennis shoes,
paddies or gymnast shoes must be
worn, since street shoes are not
allowed.
