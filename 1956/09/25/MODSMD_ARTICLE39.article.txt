# Mural Managers Meet With Bugge
## 
### 
Campus intramural managers
will meet with Intramural Direc
tor Elwyn H. Bugge in Room 50
of the athletic office building
(BAC) Thursday at 4:15 p.m. to
discuss plans for an extensive
fall program. All organizations
on campus, and especially fresh
man groups, are urged to send a
representative to this meeting.
It is expected that this year's
program will include football,
handball, horseshoes, table tennis
and two-man volleyball. Still in
doubt is the addition of bowling,
for which facilities have not yet
been secured, to the schedule.
Interested individuals may reg
ister now for participation in this
program by signing sheets posted
on the intramural bulletin board
in the gym.
