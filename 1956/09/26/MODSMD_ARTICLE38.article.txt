# Keio Scholarship Is Now Available
## 
### 
A tuition scholarship to study
at Keio University, Tokyo, Japan,
during the school year of 1957-58,
will be awarded this year to a
Stanford University student.
Requirements for application
include at least one year of study
of the Japanese language, which
may be taken this current year
of 1956-57, and a general interest
and knowledge of the country's
people and culture.
Interested students may con
tact Ron Simpson, DA 3-8756, or
inquire at the Institute of Inter
national Relations shack.
