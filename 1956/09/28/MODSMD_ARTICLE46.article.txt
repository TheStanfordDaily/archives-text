# Gymnasium Pool Opens for Women
## 
### 
The Women's Pool will open
this Sunday and will remain open
during the quarter for all women
and their guests according to the
following schedule:
Monday, Wednesday and Fri
day, 11 a.m. to noon; and Tues
day, Thursday, Saturday and
Sunday, 1 to 4 p.m. During the
weekdays, Monday through
Thursday, the pool will also be
open from 5 to 5:40 p.m.
