# Women's Council To Hear Appeals
## 
### 
Women's Council will hold its
first hearing tonight for women
wishing to appeal social regula
tion cases. Appeals of violations
will be heard each Monday night
from 7:45 to 9:30 p.m. in the
ASSU Exeom room.
Appointments may be made at
the council office which is located
at I he top of the stairs in the old
Union during regular office
hours.
Office hours every week are:
Monday, 1 to 2 p.m., 4:30 to 5:30
p.m.; Tuesday, 2 to 3 p.m.; Wed
nesday, 1 to 2 p.m.; Thursday, 3
to 4 p.m.
Officers of Women's
Council Are Named
Members of Women's Council
for the autumn quarter are: Car
ol Eymann, chairman, Storey
House; Jane Dooge, vice- chair
man, Gavilan; Sharon Roach, sec
retary, Gavi 1 a n ; Jane Pyle,
treasurer and supplies, Gavilan;
and Judy Lehman, key clerk, Ga
vilan.
