# Satire Talk Opens Tuesday Lectures
## 
### 
Professor James Sutherland
will speak on "The Nature of Sat
ire" tonight at 7:30 in Memorial
Auditorium. This lecture will
mark the opening of the autumn
quarter Tuesday Evening Scries.
Professor Sutherland is Lord
Northcliffe Professor of Modern
English Literature at University
College, University of London,
and has been at Huntington Li
brary in Pasadena for several
months.
For over 20 years he has been
publishing works about English
literature, and is currently work
ing on one volume of the Oxford
History of English Literature.
