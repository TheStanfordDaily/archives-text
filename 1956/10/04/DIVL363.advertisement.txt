"I WON'T WEAR A THING
BUT TOWNE AND KING!"
says HECTOR LIBERACHI, pogostick champ.
SNOWBANK, ALA., April lo —
Rated the fastest man on a pogo stick
since St. Vitus. Hector circled his old
man's barn on his bouncing broomstick
in 7.3 sec. flat, a new rccord. Hector's
hobbies arc taxidermy, fiddlin' and
girls. When interviewed, he said
modestly,"'Twarn't nuthin'. 1 had a dry
track!' Hector, a 7-colo* 1 sweaterman,
says Townclla Sweater Shirts are his
four season favorite.
