# Four Jazz Notables To Spark Concert
## 
### 
The Peninsula's first modern
jazz festival will he hold in Me
morial Auditorium tomorrow eve
ning at 8.
Four jazzland notables will ap
pear in the concert to benefit the
Scholarship Fund. Jazz pianist
Earl "Fatha" Hines and his quar
tet, Cal Tjader and his quintet,
I tenor sax soloist Brew Moore and
solo pianist Vince Guaraldi will
be featured.
Tickets may bo obtained now
at the Memorial Auditorium box
office from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m., at
Hage's Stanford Music Shop, in
Palo Alto, or by writing P.O. Box
1311, Palo Alto.
Reserved seats arc $2.50 and $3;
general admission $2.
