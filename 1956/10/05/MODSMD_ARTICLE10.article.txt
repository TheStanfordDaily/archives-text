# Today Is Deadline For Class Cards
## 
### 
Today at 5 p.m. is the deadline
for filing IBM class cards at the
Registrar's Office, according to
Registrar Harvey Hall.
A service foe of $2 will be
charged for late cards next week,
and a $10 fee after that.
Students are urged to file their
cards as soon as possible to avoid
the last minute rush. Hall also
has asked that students with in
complete lists go to the Regis
trar's Office to receive aid in com
pleting their lists.
