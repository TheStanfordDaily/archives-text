# Tickets on Sale For 'Beef-b-que'
## 
### 
Today is the last day tickets
| may be purchased for the sopho
more "Beef-b-que." to be held at
Adobe Creek Lodge tomorrow
from 1 p.m. to 1 a.m.
Lists will be posted in Lagunita
and the ASSIJ office for students
who need or can provide rides.
Students without transportation
may also contact Alan Kane.
Rides will leave Lagunita from 1
to 5 p.m. on the hour.
Dress for the "Beef h que" will
be pedal-pushers for the women
and denims for the men.
