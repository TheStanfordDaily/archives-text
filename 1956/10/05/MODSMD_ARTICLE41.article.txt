# Mural Football Starts Next Week
## 
### 
Intramural Director Elwyn
Bugge announced yesterday that
intramural football is ready to
get under way.
The first games will be played
Monday at 4 and 5 p.m. Six
games will be played at each of
these times Monday through Fri
day. Saturday games will be
played at 10 and 11 a.m. starting
a week from tomorrow. The Fri
day and Saturday games will be
necessary until the program gets
organized, Bugge said.
Officials for the games are still
needed. Anyone interested in be
ing an official may call Bugge at
the BAC, or Jack Hoagland at the
AKL fraternity house.
Next week's football schedule
will be posted tomorrow after
noon.
