# Once Around the Quad
## 
### 
Alpine Club: The first practice
climb for autumn quarter will be
at Mira Loma. All persons inter
ested meet at History Corner,
10:15 a.m. Sunday.
Baha'i Club: Lunchoon, 1 p.m.
Sunday. Rides from post office
at 12:45. For information contact
John A. Miller, Stern 112.
Daily Staff Meeting: 1:30 p.m.
Sunday, Daily Shack. Attend
ance required.
English Review Club: Meeting
for all graduate English students,
6:30 p.m., home of Professor and
Mrs. Sensabaugh, 1350 Byron St.
Folk Dancers: 8 p.m., Stanford
Village Auditorium. Rides leave
from Cellar, 7:30 p.m.
Menlo Park College Group: 7
p.m. Sunday. Assistant Minister
Dr. Derr is speaker. Rides leave
post office at 6:45 p.m.
Lutheran Students: 7:15 p.m.
Sunday, Grace Lutheran Church,
3149 Waverley, Palo Alto.
Wesley Foundation: 7:30 p.m.
Sunday, First Methodist Church,
Palo Alto. Rev. Robert W. Moon
will speak on "The Art of Rebel
lion."
Westminster Fellowship: 5:30
to 7 p.m. today. Dinner in honor
of freshmen and new members.
Rides will leave major dorm lob
bies at 5:20.
YWCA Bible Study: Noon Mon
day, third floor, Women's Club
house. Lunch and discussion.
