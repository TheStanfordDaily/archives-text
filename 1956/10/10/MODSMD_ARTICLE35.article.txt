# 'Mayor' T. Mitchell Will Speak Today
## 
### 
Thomas Mitchell, television's |
"Mayor of the Town," will speak
on "Theater Reminiscences" to- j
day at 4:15 p.m. in the Little
Theatre.
Today's program will be the
year's first Theatre Matinee,
sponsored by the Department of
Speech and Drama. Mitchell's
most recent appearance on the
local stage was in the touring
company of "Death of a Sales
man." "Gone with the Wind,"
