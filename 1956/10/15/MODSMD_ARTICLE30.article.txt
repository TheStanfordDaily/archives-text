# Chicago Crushes 49ers, Rams Edged by Lions
## 
### 
The Chicago Bears exploited
hard tackling and bitter rushing
by their defensive team today for
a 31-7 win over the San Francisco
49ers before 49,714 Windy City
fans.
At the same time the Detroit
Lions piled up a 17-point lead in
the first half today but had to
stave off a desperate Los Ange
les Rams rally to keep their per
fect record intact with a 24-21 tri
umph before 50,281 fans.
