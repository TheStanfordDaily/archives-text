# 'Books as Art' Is Topic for Panel
## 
### 
"The Rook as a Work of Art"
will be discussed by four panel
ists in the Bender Room of the
Main Library at 8 tonight.
The panel will be composed of
Herbert Fahey and Mrs. John I.
Walter, book binders; Peter Fa
hey, binder and teacher, and
Thomas Carr Howe, director of
the California Palace of the Le
gion of Honor. The panelists will
explore the subject of the book
which is so finely designed, illus
trated and bound that it becomes
more an object Of art than a ve
hicle of communication.
