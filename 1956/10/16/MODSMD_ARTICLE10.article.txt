# Stanford Librarian Marie Volkov Dies
## 
### 
Mrs. Marie Volkov, senior ref
erence librarian who worked with
the art collection in the Humani
ties and Social Sciences Division
of the Stanford Library, died last
Thursday after a short illness.
Funeral services were held at
the Russian Orthodox Church,
Menlo Park, on Saturday.
Mrs. Volkov. a graduate of the
Russian University in Riga. Lat
via, took library training at Co
lumbia University. She was em
ployed at the Cooper Union Mu
seum in New York City before
coming to Stanford in 1949.
