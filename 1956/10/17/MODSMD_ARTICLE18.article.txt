# No Limit Placed On Float Expenses
## 
### 
Homecoming Committee has
announced that this year floats
will not only be judged on the
basis of execution and originality
but also on what is done with the
amount of money spent.
No limit has been set on the
amount of money which groups
can use in carrying out the float
theme "Fricassee SC."
Prizes for the floats will be
given in the categories of humor,
originality, execution, theme,
mixed upper division (over 150
participants in" making float),
mixed lower division and grand
sweepstakes.
Following tradition, there will
he a rally immediately after the
float parade next Friday at which
the winners will be announced.
The winning floats will be on
show before the game on Satur
day at the Stadium.
