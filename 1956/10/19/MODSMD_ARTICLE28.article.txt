# AWS to Sponsor Lost Articles Sale
## 
### 
Lost and unclaimed articles
will go on sale at bargain prices
Monday at the sale sponsored by
the Associated Women Students.
The sale will be from 1 to 5
p.m. Monday and Tuesday for
students and will be open to the
public Wednesday from 1 to 5
p.m. in the Bull Pen on the third
floor of Encina Hall.
Proceeds from the sale will go
toward an AWS scholarship. A
variety of clothing, furniture and
sports equipment, all left in the
dormitories at the end of spring
quarter, willl be on display.
