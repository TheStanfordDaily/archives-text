# Stanford Golfers Do Well at Redding
## 
### 
Three Stanford varsity golfera
made impressive showings at the
Redding Golf Invitational hold
last week end at Redding, Calif.
Woodgie Reich tied for fourth
place with a 71-73; Alf Burtleson
placed sixth with a 74-72; and Jim
Rowley made a 78-78.
Burtleson said that they were
at a definite disadvantage, never
having played the course before.
He felt the Stanford players had
done t ery well.
Dick Bailey of Redding won the
invitational with a 65-74.
