# Trojans Ahead In Grid Series
## 
### 
Southern California and Stan
ford first started their rivalry in
1905. USC leads in the 34 game
series having won 19 contests.
Stanford has won 13 games and
two have ended in ties.
The Cards took the '05 opener
16-0. The series was not renewed
until 1918. USC winning 25-8.
Last year the Indians beat South
ern Cal 28-20 in the Los Angeles
Memorial Coliseum.
Although doing well in the Col
iseum. Stanford can't seem to
win in its own stadium. Out of
14 games played at home, Stan
ford has only been able to win
two. In fact the Indians'have not
beaten the Trojans here since
1940.
