# '57 Class Cards Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Juniors may buy their class
cards today and tomorrow out
side the Cellar from 10 a.m. to
3 p.m.
These are the only two days
class members will be able to buy
the cards, which admit them to
all class functions at a reduced
price, according to junior class
president Wally Honeywell.
