# Essay Contest For Law Students
## 
### 
Earl D. Osborn, president of
the Institute for International
Order, has announced the open
ing of an essay contest for under
graduate law students.
The topic is "Disarmament and
the Law" and the essays may
deal with any national or inter
national problems in the field of
disarmament.
A first prize of $200 and a sec
ond prize of $100 will be awarded.
The first prize winner will be eli
gible to compete in the national
contest with winners from nine
other law schools.
