# KZSU to Present Horror Program
## 
### 
"Catwife," a bloody tale about
a wife who turns into a cat, will
be presented over KZSU tonight
at 7:30 by the Radio-Television
Announcing class.
Principals in the Halloween hor
ror show will he Pat Triplett as
Linda, the wife; Serguey Kondra
tieff as the husband, John; and
Elaine Willis as the cat.
The supporting cast includes
Sheldon Fay, Ann Kingsbury, Don
Murchison, Don Petters, Carol
Solquist and Doug Walker. Ev
elyn Dees will direct and Don
Dawson will handle sound effects.
