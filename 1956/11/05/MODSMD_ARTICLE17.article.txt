# 'Thieves' Carnival' Tickets Available
## 
### 
Tickets are available for :
"Thieves' Carnival," St a n for d
Players' second production this
season.
The comedy by Jean Anouilh
will open Thursday night in Me
morial Auditorium and will run
through Saturday night. It is di
rected by Robert Loper.
Tickets will be sold at the Me
morial Auditorium box office
from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m. today at
$1 and $1.50.
