# UCLA Thinclads Nick Cards, 20-37
## 
### 
Despite running their best team
race of the year, the Stanford
cross-country squad fell to pow
erful UCLA, 20-37, Saturday in
Los Angeles.
Doug Martin placed third he
hind the Bruins' Dick Rodriguez
and Ken Riding.
Ralph Love finished fifth for
Stanford, with Rob Monzingo, Er
nie Cunliffe and Roger Beck
rounding out the scoring by fin
ishing eighth, 10th and 11th.
Each Stanford man improved
an average of 41 seconds over his
previous race and appeared ready
to give San Jose State and Camp
Pendleton good battles in a tri
angular meet next week.
