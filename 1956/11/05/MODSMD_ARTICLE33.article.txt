# SU Students Win In Speech Meet
## 
### 
Two Stanford debating teams
tied for first place Saturday in
the annual Northern California
forensic tourney at St. Mary's
College.
Entries included teams from all
Northern California colleges. Ty
ing for first place were the Stan
ford teams of John. Hill and Fred
Hillyer and Kirk West and Eliza
beth Marshall.
In individual events, West tied
for third in the impromptu divi
sion, Don Proudfoot and Miss
Marshall finished in a third-place
tie in the extemporaneous divi
sion and Arden Shenker placed
fourth in oratory. Thirteen Stan
ford students entered.
