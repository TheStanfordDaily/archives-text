# Gaieties Will Cater To Campus Tastes
## 
### 
Tickets for Big Game Gaieties
continue on sale today through
Friday at the Memorial Audito
rium box office from 12:30 to 4:30
p.m.
Gaieties this year will be
geared to campus tastes, accord
ing to Roger Cannell, Ram's
Head spokesman. Musical num
bers planned include a "Girlie
Kick," a line of 40 girls in a take
off from the Ziegfeld Follies, and
a history of jazz number.
A mystery guest star will also
be included in the cast of 70 as
sembled by Director Walt Brown
for this year's show.
Tickets may also be purchased
at the Peninsula Box Office in
the Town and Country Village.
No tickets will be sold on Quad
this year.
Prices are $1.50, $1.25 and $1.
