# Prof to Administer Rockefeller Fund
## 
### 
Arthur F. Wright, associate
professor of history at Stanford,
will administer a $57,400 grant
from the Rockefeller Foundation
for studies in Chinese thought.
The award was made to the Far
Eastern Association.
Wright has headed the Com
mittee on Chinese Thought,
standing committee of the asso
ciation, since 1951. It has pro
duced two symposium volumes
and sponsored conferences on the
ideals of Chinese civilization.
The new program, which will
extend through 1960 under the
Rockefeller grant, will deal with
Ihe aspects of Confucianism in
China, Korea and Japan.
