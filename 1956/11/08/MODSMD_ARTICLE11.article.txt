# Hostel to Feature Election Analysis
## 
### 
A retrospective analysis of the
national elections and the differ
ent planks of «the party platforms
will he the topic of discussion at
the Cardinal sponsored Stanford
Hostel this weekend.
Guest professors will be Dr.
Cornelius Cotter, political sci
ence. and Dr. Herbert Packer,
School of Law.
Sign ups for the hostel end to
day at 3 p.m. at the ASSU office.
The hostels are held at the all
weather Jones Gulch Camp and
start at 4 p.m. Friday and end at
2 p.m. Sunday. Registration fee
is $5 and covers room, board, in
surance and transportation for
the weekend.
