# Theta Sigs Host Journalism Group
## 
### 
Stanford women in journalism
will be honored tomorrow after
noon at a reception hostessed by
Theta Sigma Phi, women's hono
rary journalism fraternity.
The reception will be held from
4 to 6 p.m. at the home of Pro
fessor and Mrs. Clifford Weigle,
756 Santa Ynez, Stanford. Rides
will leave the Daily shack at 3:50
and 4:15 p.m. Ali women inter
ested in becoming journalism
majors have been invited.
