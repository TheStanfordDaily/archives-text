# Sports Car Club Plans New Rally
## 
### 
Tomorrow Stanford Sports
Car Club will hold its second
rally of the quarter, "Navigator's
Nightmare." This is a four-hour
time-distance event, starting at 7
p.m. at Stanford Stadium and fin
ishing at a local night spot.
On Nov. 15 at 7:30 p.m. in
Room 320, Geology Corner, the
club will feature a one-hour mov
ie presented by a Porsche factory
representative.
