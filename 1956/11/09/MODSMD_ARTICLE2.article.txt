# Tradition Revived For Open House
## 
### 
The traditional all-campus open
house honoring Judge George E.
Crothers will take place again
this year, after the Oregon State
game.
In the past few years this tra
dition had fallen into disuse. To
morrow, however, it will he re
vived in Crothers Hall. The open
house will feature a band and re
freshments.
Judge Crothers, who is H6, will
take part in the event. Afterwards
he will dine with the residents of
the hall and faculty members of
the School of Law.
