# Hungary Crisis Is KZSU Topic
## 
### 
The crisis in Hungary will be
the topic of a roundtable discus
ion over KZSU at 5 p.m. today.
Presenting the case for the
Hungarian people will be Stan
ford students Mike Hudson and
Louis Harss. The position of the
United States government will be
presented by student Jim Palmer
and faculty member Joseph G.
Oswald.
Hudson is a second-year law
student and is active in the Stan
ford Political Union. Harss is the
author of a widely commented-on
letter in last Thursday's Daily.
Palmer is a first-year law student
and writes the weekly "National
Scene" column for The Daily. Os
wald is a Western Civ instructor
and is working toward his Ph.D.
degree.
