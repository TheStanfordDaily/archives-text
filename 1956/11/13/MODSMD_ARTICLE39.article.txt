# 'Twelfth Night' Tryouts Thursday
## 
### 
A second session of tryouts for
"Twelfth Night," Stanford Play
ers' next production, will be held
Thursday at 4 p.m. and 7:30 p.m.
in the Little Theatre.
The tryouts, also held on Mon
day, are open to all Stanford stu
dents, faculty and staff members.
"Twelfth Night," to be directed
by F. Cowles Strickland, will
open Jan. 31 in Memorial Audito
rium for a three-night run. Re
hearsals will begin this quarter
but will be held next quarter.
