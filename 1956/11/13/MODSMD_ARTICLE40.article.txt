# Tickets Available For Jazz Concert
## 
### 
Tickets are going quickly for
the Nov. 21 Stan Kenton concert,
according to Publicity Chairman
Margo Booth.
Tickets are available at Engi
neering Corner and the Memorial
Auditorium box office for $2 to
all students and staff.
Kenton is now "starring" at the
Macumba in the City and will
bring his entire 20-piece band and
female vocalist to the Farm for
the concert in conjunction with
Big Game Week festivities.
