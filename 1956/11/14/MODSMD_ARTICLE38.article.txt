# Ticket Sales End Friday For Symphony Series
## 
### 
Ticket sales for the 18 concerts
of the San Francisco Symphony
will end Friday. This will be the
last chance to obtain student
rates on season symphony tick
ets, said Bob Martin, chairman of
the San Francisco Symphony Fo
rum.
Student rates offer over a 50
per cent saving in all price
ranges, Martin said. Tickets are
available in the dress circle, bal
cony and orchestra. They may be
purchased from 1 to 5 p.m. at the
Concert Series office in the Ni
tery.
