# British Consul General To Speak at Bowman
## 
### 
Sir Robert S. Hadow, British
consul-general in San Francisco,
will speak tomorrow at 4:15 p.m.
on "Why France and Britain
Fought Alone."
His speech, sponsored by the
Institute of International Rela
tions, will take place in Bowman
Aluhini House. Last month Sir
Robert and Senator George Ma
lone (R-Nev.) were involved in
an exchange of words that nearly
resulted in blows.
