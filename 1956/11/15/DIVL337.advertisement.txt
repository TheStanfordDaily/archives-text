WE ARE INTERESTED
IN YOUR FUTURE
On November 16, 1956, representatives of the Amer
ican Brake Shoe Co. will be interviewing students having
industrial and mechanical engineering training and desirous
of a profitable future in a diversifed and progressive in
dustrial supply company.
The American Brake Shoe Co. is composed of 11 sepa
rate manufacturing divisions serving all types of industries.
Descriptive literature regarding American Brake Shoe
Co.'s various products is available at the Placement Office,
where interview appointments may also be made for
NOVEMBER 16
