##

tics continue today on Quad and
at Memorial Auditorium.

Engineering Corner from 11 a.m.

Auditorium box office from 12:30
to 4:30 p.m. Tickets are also on
sale at the Peninsula Box Office
in Town and Country Village.
Prices are $1.50, $1.25 and $1.
