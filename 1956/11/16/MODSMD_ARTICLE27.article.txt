# Nanney to Play In Sunday Concert
## 
### 
A concert of Baroque music
will he given in Memorial Church
Sunday at 4 p.m. Herbert Nan
ney, University organist, and
chamber ensembles under the di
rection of Sandor Salgo will be
featured.
The concert is sponsored by the
music department and is open to
the public free of charge.
