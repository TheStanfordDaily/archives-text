# ROOM 111-A
## Graduates, UD Must Complete Pre-Reg Today 
### 
Room 111-A, Registrar's Office,
continues to be the hub of campus
activity today as upper division
and graduate students complete
pre-registration for winter quar
ter.
E. C. Wagner, associate regis
trar, said that upper division and
graduate students must turn in
their official study list cards in
person to the check-out clerk at
the Information Desk, Room 111-
A, before 5 p.m. today.
Tomorrow freshmen and lower
division students will begin meet
ing with their advisers. Office
hours are listed in the winter
quarter time schedules, which are
available at the Registrar's Office.
At the time of their conference,
students will fill out their study
list cards and have them signed
by their advisers. For any courses
marked "§" in the time schedule,
students must sign class lists in
the Registrar's Office.
Women students planning to
register for physical education
have been instructed to go to the
Women's Physical Education De
partment after seeing their advis
ers. Juniors, seniors and gradu
ates will sign up today, and fresh
men and sophomores will sign up
tomorrow, Thursday, Friday and
Saturday from 8 a.m. to 12 noon
and from 1 to 5 p.m.
Freshmen and lower division
students must turn in their study
list cards before 12 noon, Dec. 8,
Wagner said.
Pre-registration in lower divi
sion French classes will be held
in Room 309 today, tomorrow,
Thursday and Friday from 8:30
a.m. to 12 noon and 1 to 4 p.m.
