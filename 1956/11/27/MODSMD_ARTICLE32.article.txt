# Brodie Honored As 'Back of Year
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 26
(UP)—Quarterback John Brodie
of Stanford, who was drafted as
expected by the San Francisco
49ers, and tackle John Nisby of
the College of the Pacific today
were named Back and Lineman
of the Year by the Northern Cali
fornia Football Writers' Associa
tion.
