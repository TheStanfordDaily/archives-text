# Gridiron Banquet Scheduled Tonight
## 
### 
Five special awards for out
standing gridiron achievement by
Stanford football players will
highlight the third annual Stan
ford Football Awards Banquet
tonight at the Fairmont Hotel in
San Francisco.
Varsity letters and sweaters
will also be given to the players.
Gifts to the letter winners will be
presented by the Stanford Club
of San Francisco.
Presidetn J. Wallace Sterling
will address the crowd. Paul
Speegle will serve as master of
ceremonies.
