# Quad Pictures
## 
### 
Quad pictures scheduled today
include Sigma Chi, Sigma Nu,
Theta Chi, Zetes, Theta Delts,
Theta Xi, Crothers and Crothers
Memorial.
Tomorrow residents of Stern,
Crothers, Crothers Memorial, eat
ing clubs and fraternities who
have not been photographed yet
will have their pictures taken. All
pictures are taken at the Quad
office.
Friday is the deadline for indi
vidual senior pictures in (he
Quad, according to Editor Gary
Willcuts.
No appointment is necessary
and seniors may have their pic
tures taken in the Quad office
daily between 9 a.m. and noon
and 1 and 5 p.m. Price is $1.25.
The Quad staff emphasizes
that senior pictures are separate
from those of living groups and
that all seniors are entitled to
have their pictures in both the
living-group and senior sections.
