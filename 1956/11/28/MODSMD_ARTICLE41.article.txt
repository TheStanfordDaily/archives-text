# Farm YR's Plan Election Meeting
## 
### 
Stanford Area Young Republi
cans will hold an election meeting
tonight at 7:30 in the Stanford
Elementary School, Mayfield and
Santa Ynez.
Preceding the election-business
meeting, Assistant Professor of
Political Science Robert A. Horn
will give an informal talk on the
national election results and Ei
senhower's "New Republican
ism."
Up for election will be the posts
of president, vice-president, cor
responding secretary, recording
secretary, treasurer and activities
coordinator.
Outgoing President Cliff Kim
ber invited all interested students
to attend. Voting will be open
only to members holding YR
1956-57 membership cards. Cards
will be sold at the door.
Rides will leave Roble, Bran
ner, Lagunita and the Post Office
at 7:20 p.m.
