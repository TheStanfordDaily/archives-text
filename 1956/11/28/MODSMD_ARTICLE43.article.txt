# KZSU Elects Robinson New Station Manager
## 
### 
Bill Robinson has been clected
KZSU station manager for the
period beginning last Monday and
ending May 12, according to out
going Station Manager Terry
Bateman.
Other new officers announced
yesterday by Bateman are Gareth
Tripp, program manager; Gordon
MacGinitie, business manager;
and Tom Smith, chief engineer.
