# Metal 'Wetting' Process Found
## 
### 
A Stanford professor and a
Stanford graduate have discov
erer! a process which may greatly
increase the efficiency of atomic
power reactors and other modern
power plants.
The process, a way for making
molten metals "wet" other metals,
was developed by Professor O.
Cutler Shepard, director of the
Division of Mineral Technology,
and Dr. Edward P. French, who
received his degree at Stanford in
1952.
