# Tichenal SJS Coach; Replaces Bob Bronzan
## 
### 
SAN JOSE, Nov. 28 (UP) Bob
Titehenal, former coach at the
University of New Mexico, today
was named head football coach of
his alma mater San Jose State
('ollege.
Titehenal succeeds Bob Bonzan.
