# Intermezzo Will Feature Chorale
## 
### 
"The Music of 15th and 16th
Century Italy," featuring the
Stanford Chorale, is the topic of
next Thursday's Intermezzo Pro
gram taking place in the Bender
Room at 3:45 p.m.
To complement this program,
the Library will display in the
Bender Room the original work
ing manuscript of Pietro Masca
gni's opera "Cavalleria Rusti
cana."
