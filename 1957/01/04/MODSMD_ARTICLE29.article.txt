# Brodie in Hawaii For Hula Bowl Tilt
## 
### 
All-American John Brodie, vot
ed the most valuable player in the
Shrine East-West game, is now in
Honolulu to play with the College
All-Stars in Sunday's Hula Bowl
game.
In the East-West game Brodie's
pass to UCLA's Pete O'Garro led
to the West's 7-0 victory over the
East.
