# Tryouts
## 
### 
KZSU
Tryouts for program directors
will be held today through Wed
nesday at the KZSU studios in
Memorial Auditorium, 4 to 6 p.m.
Experience unnecessary.
Men's Glee Club
Tryouts will be held tomorrow
and Jan. 15 at 9 p.m. in Wood
pecker Lodge or by arrangement
with Professor Rasmussen at the
Knoll.
Quad
Prospective staff members for
the 1957 Quad will meet at the
Quad office tomorrow at 5 p.m.
Openings are available for typ
ists, filers, contacters, identifiers,
cartoonists and artists.
Ram's Head
Tryouts for roles in three origi
nal one-act plays will be held in
Memorial Auditorium tomorrow,
Wednesday and Thursday from
7 to 10 p.m. People new to Ram's
Head productions are invited to
try out.
