# Graduates Offered Danforth Grants
## 
### 
Students who plan to take their
first year of graduate study in
teaching beginning in September
1957 are invited to apply for Dan
forth graduate fellowships.
The Danforth Foundation of
fers grants to both single and
married fellows. Students with
or without financial need are in
vited to apply.
Applications must be completed
by Jan. 31. Single fellows will re
ceive maximum grants of $1800
and married fellows $2400 with
additional stipend for children.
Further information may be
obtained from the Financial
Awards office.
