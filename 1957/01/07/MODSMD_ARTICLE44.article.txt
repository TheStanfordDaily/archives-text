# KZSU Schedule

### 
KZSU will resume regular
broadcasting today with several
new programs added to its daily
schedule, according to Assistant
Program Manager Judy Vollmar.
A Treasury of Music, heard for
many years on KEAII and KIBE,
will be presented Sunday through
Friday from 10 to 10:30 p.m.
The new schedule includes a
program of ballet music on Mon
days, The Concerto—Music 3 Lis
tening on Tuesdays, Collector's
Corner on Wednesdays and Melo
dies of the Masters on Thursdays.
