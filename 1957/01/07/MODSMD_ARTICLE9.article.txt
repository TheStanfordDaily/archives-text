# 'Y' Group to Plan Future Meetings
## 
### 
The "Y" Public Affairs group
will hold its first luncheon meet
ing of winter quarter tomorrow
from noon to 1 p.m. (in the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
The purpose of the meeting
will be to plan topics for the
forthcoming sessions, according
to Carolyn Leys, group chairman.
A 35-cent lunch will be served.
