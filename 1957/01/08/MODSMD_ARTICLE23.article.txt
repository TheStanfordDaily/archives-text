# Anniversary Fete To Be Held Friday
## 
### 
An anniversary banquet ob
serving the 25th year of the Ex
periment in International Living
will be held Friday at 7 p.m. at
the Sheraton-Palace Hotel in San
Francisco.
Honored guests will include Dr.
Donald B. Watt, founder of the
program, and Gordon Boyce, na
tional president. Former Experi
menters from Northern Califor
nia will also be in attendance. All
Stanford students interested in
the Experiment are also invited,
according to Karen Erickson,
University chairman.
Students may contact Miss Er
ickson for reservations at the In
stitute of International Relations
shack today between 4 and 6 p.m.
and at Lagunita between 7 and 8
p.m.
