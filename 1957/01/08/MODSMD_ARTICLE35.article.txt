# SU Musicians to Perform on Radio

the first of an occasional series
called "Music in the Colleges,"
will be presented tomorrow at
8:15 p.m. on FM radio station
KPFA.

playing violins, Elizabeth van
Worst on the viola and Harold
Hill playing the cello will per
form tho Haydn "Lark" Quartet
in D Major, Op. 64, No. 5; Stor
nelli e Ballate by Malipiero, and
the Beethoven Quartet in F Ma
jor, Op. 59, No. 1.
