# Tryouts
## 
### 
kZSU
Tryouts for program directors
will be held today through tomor
row at the KZSU studios in Me
morial Auditorium, 4 to 6 p.m.
Experience unnecessary.
Men's Glee Club
Tryouts will be held today and
Jan. 13 at 9 a.m. in Woodpecker
Lodge or by arrangement with
Professor Rasmussen at the
Knoll.
Quail
Prospective staff members for
the 1937 Quad will meet at the
Quad office today at 3 p.m. Open
ings are available for typists, fil
ers, contactors, identifiers, car
toonists and artists.
Rum's Head
Tryouts for roles in three origi
nal one-act plays will be hold in
Memorial Auditorium today, to
morrow and Thursday from 7 to
10 p.m. People new to Ram's
Head are invited to try out.
Stanford Players
Tryouts for the Stanford Play
ers production of Maugham's
"The Circle" will be held today
from 7:30 to 10 p.m. in the Little
Theatre. Tryouts are open to all.
There are 10 speaking parts.
