# Polio Inoculations To Continue Today
## 
### 
Polio shots will continue to be
Riven this quarter at the Health
Service.
The shots are given in a series
of three. Two are given one
month apart, and the third shot
seven months later. Each injec
tion costs $1.25.
Students under 21 are required
by the Health Service to bring
permission slips signed by a par
ent or guardian. Slips can be
picked up at the Health Service
and returned at the time of the
first shot.
