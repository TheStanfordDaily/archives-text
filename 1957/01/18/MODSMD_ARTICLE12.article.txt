# Queen of England Bestows Honor on President Sterling
## 
### 
Queen Elizabeth II has conferred a high honor on Stanford
President J. E. Wallace Sterling, the British consul-general in San
Francisco announced yesterday.
The English sovereign made Dr. Sterling an honorary com
mander in the Most Excellent Order of the British Empire, in rec
ognition of his "outstanding service for the cause of Anglo-Ameri
can understanding."
Dr. Sterling said he was "amazed and very complimented."
The University president was formerly connected with Marshall
Scholarships which are grants given by the British government to
American students for study in the United Kingdom.
