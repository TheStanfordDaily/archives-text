# Indian Republic Observance Set

### 
Republic Day of India will be
celebrated tomorrow at 8 p.m. at
El Tigre eating club. All inter
ested persons have been invited
to the program, which will in
clude a talk by the consul-general
of India, films about that nation
and Indian music.
It was seven years'ago. Jan. 26,
1950, that India constituted itself
into a sovereign democratic re
public "to secure to all its citi
zens justice, liberty and equality,
and to promote among them all
fraternity assuring the dignity of
the individual and the unity of
the nation."
For reasons of convenience it is
being celebrated here a week ear
lier on Jan. 19, according to a rep
resentative of the India Associa
tion.
