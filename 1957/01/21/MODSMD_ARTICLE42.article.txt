# Ski Weekend Open To All SU Students
## 
### 
Starting today, members of all
classes may buy tickets for the
sophomore-junior ski weekend at
the Reno Ski Bowl from the reg
ular ticket booths at Engineering
Corner and the Post Office.
The round-trip bus ride to Reno
Friday afternoon and back to
Stanford Sunday evening will
cost $S. The Saturday night ban
quet will lie $2.50 for class card
holders and $3.50 for others. Mo
tel accommodations are available
at $2 per night for a bed and $1
a night for sleeping bag space.
