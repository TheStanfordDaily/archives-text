# SU Bridge Players To Enter Tourney
## 
### 
Stanford will enter two under
graduates in the National Inter
collegiate Bridge Tournament.
Competition to pick the two Stan
ford players will be held in early
February, according to a Stan
ford Bridge Club spokesman.
Students may sign up for the
competition by contacting Don
Krauss, Delta Chi. Any under
graduate is eligible.
The Bridge Club meets every
Tuesday in the lower lounge.
Women's Clubhouse, at 7:15 p.m.
