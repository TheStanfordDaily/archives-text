# Panofsky to Tell Of Russian Tour
## 
### 
Dr. Wolfgang Panofsky of the
Stanford Physics Department
will tell of his recent trip to Rus
sia at the "Y" Public Affairs
luncheon this noon.
The group meets from noon to
1 p.m. on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse. Students
may bring their own lunch or
buy one for 35 cents.
