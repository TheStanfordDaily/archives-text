# Music Department Outlines Winter Quarter Concerts
## 
### 
Concerts to be presented during
winter quarter were announced
this week by the department of
music.
As a part of the Tuesday Eve
ning Series, Carl Weinrich, world
famed organist, will perform
Sunday in Memorial Church. Pro
gram lime is 8 p.m.
Chamber music concerts will be
presented on Feb. 10 and 24 in
Cubberley Auditorium at 4 p.m.
The University Chorus, under
the direction of Harold Schmidt,
will present varied music styles
Feb. 17 in Memorial Auditorium
at 4 p.m.
The Stanford Symphony, San
dor Salgo conducting, will give a
concert on the Tuesday Evening
Series Feb. 19 at 8 p.m. in Memo
rial Auditorium.
Final event will be a Concert of
Sacred Music presented by Uni
versity Organist Herbert Nanney
on March 10 at 4 p.m. in Memo
rial Church. The program, also
featuring instrumental ensem
bles, will honor Founders' Day.
