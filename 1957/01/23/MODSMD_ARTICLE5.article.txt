# Club Leases Site For New Building
## 
### 
The University has signed a
lease with the University Club of
Palo Alio for the club's seven
acre clubhouse site on Junipero
Serra Boulevard near Page Mill
Road.
Construction will begin in the
spring and the building is to be
finished this summer.
Club President Evert Young
said $150,000 will eventually be
invested in a clubhouse, swim
ming pool, tennis courts and oth
er recreational facilities. The
emphasis will be on family recre
