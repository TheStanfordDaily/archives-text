# Tryouts
## 
### 
Graduation Officers
Trvouts will be held again to
day from 4 to 5:30 p.m. in the
basement of the Women's Club
house for the four honorary sen
ior class graduation officers.
All June graduates are eligible
to try for the offices of historian,
class orator, class poet and writer
of the class will. No sign-up is
necessary.
Concert Ushers
Twenty-five girls are needed to
usher at the Gateway Singers
concert Feb. 9 at 2:30 p.m. in Me
morial Auditorium. Sign-ups are
in the Associated Women Stu
dents office.
