# Frosh Will Hold Dance Saturday
## 
### 
"Anything Goes" is the theme
for the freshman class dance Sat
urday from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m. in the
Women's Gymnasium. Admission
is free to trosh class card holders.
Freshmen may carry out the
dance theme by wearing anything
they wish, according to class
president Wally Hawley. Spike
Lynch's band, "The Stardusters,"
will play.
The dance is to be stag or drag.
