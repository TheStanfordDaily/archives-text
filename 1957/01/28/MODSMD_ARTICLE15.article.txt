# Bomb Demolishes House in Alabama
## 
### 
MONTCOMKRY, Ala , Jan. 21
(UP) A dynamite bomb, tossed
during the pre dawn darkness,
shattered a Negro's house and
damaged a service station early
today while another fizzled out at
the home of the city's top integra
lion leader.
Police said that an unidentified
Negro cab driver was slighiiy in
ju red and three othei cab com
pany employees shaken up when
a bomb exploded* between a Nc
gro residence and a combination
service station and Negro cab
stand.
