# Racial Relations Talks to Be Held
## 
### 
Wilson C. Riles, secretary of
the Pacific Southwest region of
the Fellowship of Reconciliation,
will deliver two talks about racial
relations tomorrow. Both are
open to the public.
At 4:15 p.m. he will speak to
the Stanford YYVCA in the YWCA
lounge in the Women's Club
house. His topic will be "Russia
and the Race Issue."
Tomorrow evening at 7:30 he
will address the Stanford chapter
of the National Association for
the Advancement of Colored Peo
ple in Room 90, Inner Quad, on
"New Hope in the South."
