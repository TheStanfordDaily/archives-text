# Berlin Scholarship Deadline Monday
## 
### 
The application deadline for the
Free University of Berlin scholar
ship has been extended until next
Monday, according to the Berlin
Affiliation Committee of the lIR.
Students of sophomore standing
and above are eligible to apply.
Applications may bo secured at
the lIR and at the ASSU office,
and must be turned in to the lIR
office no later than 2:30 p.m.
Monday.
