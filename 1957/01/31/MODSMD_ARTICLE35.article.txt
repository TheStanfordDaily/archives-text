# Tickets Available For IIR Cabaret
## 
### 
Students may buy tickets on
Quad and in the Peninsula Box
Office next week for the Feb. 16
International Cabaret, sponsored
by the Institute of International
Relations Host Committee.
Ticket price is $2. The Cabaret
will feature an international din
ner of Hungarian, Philippine,
Japanese, Egyptian and Austrian
foods.
Also possessing an internation
al flavor, the floor show will in
clude a South African bulladeer,
African bongo drummers, a Bali
nese dancer, a Russian hopak
dancer and a French guitarist.
The Cabaret will be held at 7
p.m. in the Methodist social hall
at Byron and Hamilton Streets,
Palo Alto.
