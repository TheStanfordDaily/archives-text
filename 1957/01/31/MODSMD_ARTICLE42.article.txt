# Tryouts
## 
### 
Tryouts for the four senior
honorary graduation officers will
be held this afternoon from 1 to
5:30 p.m. in the basement of the
Women's Clubhouse.
The offices are Class Historian,
Class Orator, Class Poet and writ
er of the Senior Will.
Those selected will present
their speeches both orally and in
written form during graduation
week. . Copies of past speeches
will be available at the inter
views.
