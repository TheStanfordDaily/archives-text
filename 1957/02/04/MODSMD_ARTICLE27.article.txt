# Hughes to Teach History at Harvard
## 
### 
History Professor H. Stuart
Hughes, who left Stanford at the
end of autumn quarter, will be
come professor of history at liar
vard University in September.
Hughes, a grandson of the late
Supreme Court Chief Justice
Charles Evans Hughes, is a fel
low at the Center for Advanced
Study in the Behavioral Sciences
where he is doing research and
writing until he goes to Harvard
in the autumn.
Hughes taught at Harvard
from 1948 to 1952 when he came
to Stanford. At Stanford he was
executive head of the History De
partment and taught courses in
modern European history.
Professor Thomas A. Bailey is
now acting executive head of the
History Department, succeeding
Hughes.
