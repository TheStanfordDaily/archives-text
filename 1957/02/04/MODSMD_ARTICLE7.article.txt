# Egypt Opposes Ike Doctrine
## 
### 
CAIRO, Feb. 3 (UP)—The
newspaper Al-Shaah said today
it had taken a poll of Egyptians
on the Eisenhower Middle East
Doctrine which showed three
persons in favor ami 68,342 op
posed.
