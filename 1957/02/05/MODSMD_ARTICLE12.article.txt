# Even Poets Can't Stand The Pelvis
## 
### 
Winning entry on Bob Bee
ley's "Littlest, Texan" KZSU "I
Hate Elvis" contest was a poem
submitted by Dick White.
Elvis, Elvis
Isn't so bad . . .
It's- that pelvis
That makes me mad.
Swinging and swaying,
Never standing still —
The whole damned thing
•lust makes nie ill.
