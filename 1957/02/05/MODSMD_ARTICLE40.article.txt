# General Studies Program Clarified
## 
### 
Under the new General Studies
program, freshmen who took
only one group activity autumn
quarter will not get unit credit
for the work according to the
General Studies Office.
Officials said students will get
credit toward the group activity
requirement, but will not get
grade-point credit averaged with
their other grades.
Freshmen having questions re
garding the group activity re
quirement may consult the Gen
eral Studies Office, Room 111-D.
