# Queen of the Military Ball
## 
### 
Crowned ami hailed as the most beautiful woman at Friday night's
military ball, Betsy Haines, a 21-year-old senior from Des Moines,
lowa, and a resident of I'aloma House, smiles happily over her
success. Alter all, she has the distinction of being the first Stanford
student to be chosen queen of the ball by Stanford units of the
Army. Navy and Air Force ROTC in several years. The annual
dance was held in the Palace Hotel in San Francisco.
