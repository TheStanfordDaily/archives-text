# LASSU to Discuss Judicial Council
## 
### 
The establishment of a subordi
nate judicial council to handle
cases only in the Graduate School
of Business will be discussed lo
night by ASSU Legislature.
Also on the agenda is a review
of the Model United Nations
budget and consideration of a by
law change to permit Legislature
officers elected in the spring to
assume office at that time instead
of the following autumn.
