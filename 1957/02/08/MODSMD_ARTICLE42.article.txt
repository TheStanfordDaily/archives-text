# JOHNSON TAKES LEAD
## 
### 
TUCSON, Ariz., Feu. 7 (UP)—
Howie Johnson of Houston, Tex.,
a strapping young man with a
bad heart, fired a six-under-par (11
today to take a one-stroke lead
in the first round of the $15,000
Tucson Open Golf Tournament,
where sub - par players were a
dime a do/en.
