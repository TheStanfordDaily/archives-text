# Overseas Shots To Be Given Free
## 
### 
Immunizations for students go
ing overseas this summer or next
year will be given every Tuesday
from 1 to 4 p.m. at the Health
Service.
Students are reminded that
they will need smallpox, typhus,
tetanus, yellow fever and typhoid
shots for Africa, Asia, Oceania,
the Philippines and the West In
dies. Panama and South America
require all the above plus chol
era. For Europe and Mexico, all
but cholera and yellow fever".
The immunizations are free, in
cluding the yellow fever shot,
which can be obtained free of
charge only at the Public Health
Hospital, 15th at Lake, San Fran
cisco.
