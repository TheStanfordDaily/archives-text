# Pub Rel to Release Entertainment File
## 
### 
The Public Relations Board has
compiled an entertainment file
which will soon be available to
individuals and groups.
The file includes information
on restaurant prices, cover
charges, group rates, entertain
ment, dancing spots and lodges in
the San Francisco, Peninsula and
East Bay areas.
