# Ski Race Winners Reported by Club
## 
### 
Three students won ski races
held Saturday by the Ski Club at
their Winter Carnival in the Reno
Ski Bowl.
Walter Breuer plaeed first in
the men's intermediate division,
with Tor Folkedal placing second
and Richard Broekway third. In
the women's intermediate division
Paulette Frankl plaeed first, with
Marylinda Wheeler second and
Elaine Mayes third.
In the men's bunny division
Paul Gilbert placed first. Bill Carr
second and Jim Brown third. Lou
ise Howe placed first in the wom
en's bunny division, with Helen
Weys placing second and Jan Ben
ninger third.
