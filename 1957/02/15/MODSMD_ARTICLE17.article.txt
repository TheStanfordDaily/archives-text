# Stanford to Host High School Day
## 
### 
Stanford wil be host to North
ern California High School Day
tomorrow. Sixty schools have
been invited and some 230 stu
dents are expected to attend.
Following registration students
will convene at Cubberley at 9:15
a m for a general assembly and
welcome by C. Howard Brooks,
assistant director of admissions;
Bob Freelen, ASSU president, and
Dotty Kidd, High School Day
chairman.
The remainder of the program
will consist of departmental
tours, lunch at freshman resi
dences and campus tours. Follow
ing the tours, students will at
tend either Winter One-Acts or
the frosh basketball game.
High School Day is sponsored
by Stanford Cardinals.
