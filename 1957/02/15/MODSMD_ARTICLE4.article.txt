# Gateway Singers Featured Tonight
## 
### 
The Gateway Singers will be
featured entertainment tonight at
the Soph Cotillion. The all-cam
pus dance will be held at the Fair
mont Hotel in San Francisco from
9to I Charley Stern's orchestra
will play for dancing. Dress is
semi-formal.
Tickets are $2.75 with class
card, $3.75 without.
