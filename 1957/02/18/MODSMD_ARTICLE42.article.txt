# World Ski Movie Shown Tonight
## 
### 
18—WORLD SKI MOVIE
The Warren Miller movie,
"Have Skis—Will Travel," will be
shown tonight at 8 in Cubberley
Auditorium by the Stanford Ski
Club.
The movie includes shots of ski
ing taken by Miller all over the
world.
Tickets are $1.25 and will be
sold at the door. The public has
been invited to attend.
