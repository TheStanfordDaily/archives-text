# Spiegelberg's Book Published
## 
### 
Professor Frederic Spiegelberg
of the Asiatic and Slavic Studies
Department has written a book
called "Living Religions of the
World," which was published by
Prentice-Hall this winter.
The book is based on Spiegel
berg's lectures on comparative re
ligion. It is a sympathetic study
of the religions men live by the
world over. Aimed at giving a
fresh insight into the main be
liefs, the book explores the his
tory, the chief doctrines and the
leading personalities of each
faith.
