# Tryouts Are Today For Little Theatre
## 
### 
Tryouts for Theatre Matinee
scenes and short plays will be
held today at 4 p.m. in the Little
Theatre in Memorial Auditorium.
These will be conducted by Stan
ford Players Workshop, an in
formal studio group for students
interested in the theater.
Four of the plays to lie present
ed later this year are "The Hun
gerers," "The Importance of Be
ing Earnest," "Helena's Husband"
and "There Are Crimes and
Crimes."
