# Debaters to Attend Stockton Meeting
## 
### 
Fifteen Stanford students will
attend the Northern California
Forensic Association speech tour
nament at Stockton College to
morrow and Friday.
Debaters competing will he
Roger Austin, Gloria Bianchi, Hal
Griffin, Tom Herlocker.
Doreen Hunter, Dan Kremer,
Carl Milligan, Lester O'Shea, Don
Proudfoot, Arden Shenker, Nancy
Snookel, Carol Thompson, James
White and Mike Willemsen.
