# Bridge Tourney Here Thursday
## 
### 
The National Intercollegiate
Bridge Tournament will be held
Thursday evening at 7:30 in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Students may contact Don
Krauss at the Delta Chi house to
sign up for the tournament. En
tries will be accepted no later
than 6 p.m. Wednesday.
Beginning tomorrow Bridge
Club will run a junior tourna
ment for persons with no dupli
cate bridge experience. This will
be held every Tuesday evenin
7:15. Instruction in iuplicate
bridge tactics will precede the
game every Tuesday at t :45.
All persons coming for the first
time may play without criarge.
