# SU Netters Advance in Tournament
## 
### 
Jack Douglas and Dave Nelson
reached the quarter-finals of the
Pebble Beach Intercollegiate Invi
tational Tennis Tournament held
last week end among UCLA, Cal,
USC and Stanford.
Douglas, first-ranked Indian net
ter and a junior from Santa Mon
ica, bowled over Gordon Davis of
Southern Cal in the first round,
6-2. 6-3. In the quarterfinals the
chunky base- liner lost to John
Cranston of UCLA in a 6-4. 1-6, 6-4
upset.
Nelson, a sophomore from Chi
cago. upended Van Grant of USC
in a 3-6, 6-2, 6-4 first-round thriller.
But lie met Davis cupper Mike
Franks in the quarter-finals and
went out, 6-3, 6-1.
Stanford's other two entries,
Gilbert White and Brooks Raw
lins. both went to the sidelines in
the first round.
White fell before Franks by a
6-3, 7,-5 count and Rawlins dropped
a 6-2, 6-0 decision to Cranston.
UCLA and USC, as expected,
dominated the tournament, with
two net men from each of the two
Los Angeles schools reaching the
semi-finals. The finals between
Alex Olrnedo ol USC and Cran
ston were postponed until April
20 by rain.
