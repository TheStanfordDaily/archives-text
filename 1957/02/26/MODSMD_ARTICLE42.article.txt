# Jr. Party Theme Is 'Charlie Brown'
## 
### 
"Charlie Brown" and "Peanuts"
will be the theme of the junior
class dinner-dance to be held Sat
urday night from 7 to 2 at Adobe
Creek Lodge.
Tickets for the "dressy-sport"
affair are now being sold in men's
living groups and at Engineering
Corner of Quad. One member of
each couple must be a member of
the junior class, committee mem
bers said. Tickets are priced at
$3 apiece for class card holders
and $3.50 for those without class
cards.
