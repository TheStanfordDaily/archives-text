# Sports Car Slides Shown Tonight
## 
### 
Slides of-sports car races at the
Nuremberg Ring in Germany will
be featured at the meeting of the
Stanford Sports Car Club tonight
at 7:30 in Room 320, Geology Cor
ner.
Mark Dees will show the slides
which will also include races at
Spa in Belgium and English hill
climbs. The meeting is open to
the public.
