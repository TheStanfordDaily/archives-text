# Freshman Netters Rip CCSF, 9-0
## 
### 
Jim Jeffries' three-set first-sin
gles victory paced the Stanford
freshman tennis team to a 9-0
sweep over City College of San
Francisco yesterday at Stanford.
Jeffries won his match from Bud
Sharf of CCSF, 6-4, 3-6, 6-3.
