# Sequoia Will Host Dr. Bailey, Campus
## 
### 
To celebrate the winter publica
tion of Sequoia, the literary mag
azine will sponsor an informal
gathering at the Bowman Alumni
House for interested students and
faculty on Monday at 8:15 p.m.
As part of the program, Dr.
Margery Bailey, guest speaker
from the English Department,
will discuss "Fiction 18 to 29."
Also during the evening W. Wieg
and, graduate student in English,
will receive his $75 award as the
winner of the Sequoia article con
test. Wiegand's article, a study
on J. D. Salinger, will be featured
in the comping issue.
After the presentation and Dr.
Bailey's talk, refreshments will be
served.
