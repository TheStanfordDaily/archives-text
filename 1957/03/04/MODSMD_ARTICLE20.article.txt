# Stanford Students To Produce Movie
## 
### 
A complete feature film with
exclusive Stanford student cast
and setting is being produced
here for Con Home Week.
Don Manoukian, Robin Moore,
Don Carswell, Phil Fehlen, Dr.
William C. Bark of the History
Department and John Masterson
star in this movie called "The
Claw."
Keeble's Town and Country
Photography Shop is donating
the film, while Tom Smith, chief
engineer at KZSU, is handling
sound.
The movie will be humorous; it
deals with a student who loves
fun too much and goes mad, caus
ing many difficulties.
Proceeds from the showings
will go to the Stanford Convales
cent Home.
