# KZSU Gets London Report Recordings
## 
### 
KZSU is receiving and broad
casting, at three-week or one
month intervals, a series of re
cordings from London on current
events, art, theater and music, ac
cording to Graham Norton, Eng
lish foreign scholar and KZSU
staff member, who arranged the
series.
Entitled "Report from London,"
the series is recorded as an inde
pendent venture in the Merlin
Studios in London by two young
television producers of Associated
Television, Ltd., both personal
friends of Norton.
