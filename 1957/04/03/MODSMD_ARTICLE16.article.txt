# Politics Workshop Held on Campus
## 
### 
A five-day workshop on "Prac
tical Politics," sponsored by the
Effective Citizens Organization,
is in progress on campus. The
workshop is dealing with all
phases of party structure and op
erations and with both local and
national aspects of politics.
The purpose is to motivate the
great reserve of young men in
the business community who can
perform a distinct service in the
cause of good government, ac
cording to the ECO.
