# Theta Xi to Hold All-Campus Dance
## 
### 
Theta Xi house will bp trans
formed into a "French cafe" for
the annual all-campus Apache
Dance, Saturday from 9 p.m. to
1 a.m.
"Apaches" will enter the house
through the "sewer" at the back
door. The basement will be deco
rated to simulate an underground
cafe.
Admission is free, and costumes
will be tight sweaters and pants
for men. tight sweaters and skirts
for women.
Music will be piped to all parts
of the house from records.
