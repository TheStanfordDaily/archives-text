# Lake Lagunita's Boathouse Rules Are Announced
## 
### 
The following Stanford Boat
house rules were announced yes
terday by Legislature:
• No one except Stanford stu
dents shall be allowed on the
boathouse deck in Lake Lagunita.
• No swimming or canoeing
when lifeguards are not on duty.
• There shall be no swimming
beyond the rafts at any time.
• Canoes and boats may not be
overloaded.
• No non-swimmers shall be al
lowed in the canoes.
• No one shall change places in
a canoe or a boat off shore.
• No running is permitted on
the boathouse deck.
• Living groups are warned to
lock all canoes to racks and keep
their paddles in the lockers pro
vided. The ASSU cannot he held
responsible for the misuse of ca
noes that are not kept locked.
° Name of the owner must be
painted on canoe.
• Valuables should not be left
in the dressing rooms or in the
boathouse.
• Powerboats of any description
are forbidden on the lake.
• Underwater breathing equip
ment is prohibited.
