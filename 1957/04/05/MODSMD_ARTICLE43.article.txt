# YR Picnic Today In Redwood City
## 
### 
The Stanford Area Young Re
publicans will hold their annual
Spring Lawn Party today from 2
to 6 p.m. at 1271 Dewey St. in
Redwood City. The public is in
vited.
Rides will leave Lagunita,
Branner, Wilbur and Stern Halls
at 2, 3 and 4 p.m. The affair is a
"bring-your-own" picnic, but re
freshments will be sold. Future
club plans will be discussed at
the party.
