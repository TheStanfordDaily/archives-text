# Draft Is Subject Of Talk Tonight
## 
### 
A discussion of the draft and
its implications will be presented
tonight at 7:15 in room 320, Ge
ology Corner. Tau Beta Pi, pro
fessional engineering fraternity,
is sponsoring the talk..
Entitled "Sad Sack or Scientist ?
It's Up to You!", the talk will deal
with present Army programs for
scientific and professional person
nel (SPP) who are subject to the
draft, some advantages of not be
ing an officer, and basic training
suggestions.
Malcolm Murray, an SPP in the
Army, will lead the discussion.
