# Friday Deadline For Room, Board
## 
### 
Friday is the last day that Uni
versity board and room bills may
be paid without an added fee, the
controller's office announced yes
terday.
Payment may be made at the
cashier's office, Encina Hall, he
tween 8 a.m. and 5 p.m. Checks,
accompanied by the statement,
may be mailed to the cashier's
office but must be postmarked no
later than April 12.
Any student who cannot pay
now may see the Dean of Men or
Dean of Women concerning a dc
ferment.
