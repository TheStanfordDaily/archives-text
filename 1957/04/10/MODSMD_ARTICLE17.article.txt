# Hostel Sign-ups Close Tomorrow
## 
### 
Signups lor this weekend's
Stanford Hostel. "The Universe:
Planned or Unplanned," will close
at 1 p.m. tomorrow In the ASSU
of fire By noon yesterday 11 of
the 20 openings had been filled.
Stanford Hostels are weekend
excursions to Jones Gulch ("amp
in the Santa Cruz Mountains.
They are "designed to allow all
students freshmen through
graduates to take advantage of
a weekend away from the pres
sure of the campus," said Judy
Vollmar, Hostel Committee sec
retary.
At the hostel students meet
faculty members on an informal
basis, and can share ideas with
other students outside the class
room atmosphere, she said.
A car caravan will leave Bow
man Alumni House at 4:15 p.m.
Friday and will return to campus
early Sunday afternoon. Students
with classes lasting until after
p.m. Friday should so indi
cate when signing up, Miss Voll
mar said.
