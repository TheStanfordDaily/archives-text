# Farm Hostel Trip Set This Weekend
## 
### 
A car caravan will leave Bow
man Alumni House today at 4:30
p.m. for this weekend's Hostel in
the Santa Cruz Mountains. The
topic for discussion will be "The
Universe: Planned or Unplan
ned?"
Those attending have been
asked to meet in front of Bow
man at 4 p.m. and are reminded
to bring warm clothing for the
evenings, sleeping bags and swim
suits.
Women may sign out to Jones
Gulch "Y" Camp, Stanford Hos
tel, according to Judy Vollman,
Hostel Committee chairman.
Professor Willis Harman will
accompany the group.
