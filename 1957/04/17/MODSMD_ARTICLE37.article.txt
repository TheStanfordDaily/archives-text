# 50 High School Teachers Invited To SU Institute
## 
### 
Fifty high school teachers of
science and mathematics from
throughout the United States
have been selected to participate
in a special institute at Stanford
during the 1957-58 academic year.
Harold M. Bacon, professor of
mathematics, will serve as direc
tor. Associate director is Paul De-
Hart Hurd, associate professor of
education.
Participants will choose their
programs from the full range of
the University's curriculum, pri
marily in the sciences, mathe
matics and education. Additional
special courses will be provided.
Funds for the institute were
granted by the National Science
Foundation.
