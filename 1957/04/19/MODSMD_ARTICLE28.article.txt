# $100 Essay Prize Offered Students
## 
### 
The Melville Jacoby Essay
Prize of $100 will he awarded for
the best essay by a Stanford un
dergraduate or graduate student
relating to a political, social or
economic subject on the Orient.
Papers should be from 2500 to
5000 words in length, typewrit
ten and contain a list of refer
ences. Quotations should be used
sparingly.
Essays may be submitted to
James E. Brinton of the Depart
ment of Communication and
Journalism before April 30.
Term papers and other class
assignments arc not eligible.
