# Crew to Sponsor Garner Concert
## 
### 
Errol Garner will perform in a
jazz concert to be held Friday,
April 2G, in Memorial Auditorium
at 4:15 p.m. The Stanford crew
is sponsoring the concert.
Tickets are $1.50 and can be
purchased from crew members
or- at Engineering Corner or the
Cellar. Proceeds will be used to
send the crew to New York for
the National Championships.
