# DRIVERS WILL RACE
## 
### 
Stanford University will be>
represented by its own sports car
racing team at the Santa Barbara
and Santa Rosa races this May.
This is possibly tho first time
that a university has entered
such an organized racing team.
This racing team, which is part
of the Stanford Sports Car Club,
receives no monetary support
from the University. The aver
age cost to each driver is approx
imately $100 per race.
