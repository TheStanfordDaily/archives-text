# Vaccination Slips Deadline April 29
## 
### 
Monday, April 29, is the last
day for filing smallpox vaccina
tion certificates by students who
entered Stanford 'this quarter.
Vaccination certificates should
be filed in the Registrar's Office.
Students not filing by April 29
will be subject, to a $2 fine and
if the certificates are not turned
in by May 6 a student's registra
tion is subject to cancellation.
