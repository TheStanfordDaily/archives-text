# Work in Europe Available to SU
## 
### 
Stanford students John Gay
and Steven Schmeider have been
nominated as the first Stanford
participants in the summer work
exchange program of the Asso
ciation Internationale des Etu
diants en Sciences Economiques
et Commerciales.
Lester O'Shea, chairman of the
AIESEC at Stanford, said that
those interested in foreign jobs
or work on the AIESEC commit
tee should contact him at the lIR
shack.
