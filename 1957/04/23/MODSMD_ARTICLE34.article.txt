# Frosh Swimmers Host Schools
## 
### 
Aiming to extend their perfect
record to seven wins and no
losses, the Stanford frosh swim
mers will take to the water
against San Rafael and Rich
mond High Schools today at 3:30
in the Stanford pools.
Tom Haynie's squad will be re
turning to action after having
last Friday's meet with Monterey
Peninsula College canceled by the
visitors at the last minute.
