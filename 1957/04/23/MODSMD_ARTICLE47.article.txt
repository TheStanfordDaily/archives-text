# Faculty Library Committee Named
## 
### 
An ad hoc faculty committee
has been appointed to advise on
the University's undergraduate
library needs.
According to President Wallace
Sterling, the committee's function
will be "to consider overall prob
lems created by the fact that the
undergraduate student body
lacks library facilities." The com
mittee will also look into further
needs arising from the General
Studies program.
The Undergraduate Library
Committee consists of chairman
Professor Virgil Pro
fessors Thomas M. Barclay and
William C. Bark, Dean Philip H.
Rhinelander, Associate Director
of Planning M. H. L. Sanders,
University Press Director Leon
E. Seltzer, Dean William G.
Steere and Director of Univer
sity Libraries R. C. Swank.
