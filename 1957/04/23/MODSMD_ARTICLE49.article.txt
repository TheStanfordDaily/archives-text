# 'I' Club to Hold Masquerade Ball
## 
### 
"I Club Masquerade Ball will
be held at Chez Y\onne on Friday
from 9 p.m. to 2 a.m. The bail
will be open to all students.
Music for the occasion will be
provided by Clayton Graham and
his orchestra. And six valuable

prizes will be given for the best
costumes.
Tickets can be purchased at the
door or at the"I" Club coffee
hour on Friday between 4 and 6
p.m. in Bowman Alumni House.
