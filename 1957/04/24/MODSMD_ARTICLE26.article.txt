# SJS Prof to Talk On 'Wild Duck'
## 
### 
Dr. James Clancy, San Jose
Stale College, professor, will talk
on Ibsen's play "The Wild Duck"
this afternoon at 4:15 in Ihe Lit
tle Theatre in Memorial Audito
rium.
Dr. Clancy is associate profes
sor of drama and a staff director
at San Jose. He is a graduate of
Stanford's drama department.
The talk will herald the open
ing of the Stanford Players' pro
duction of "The Wild Duck" to
night in Memorial Auditorium.
Theatre Matinee is open to the
public.
