# 'I' Club Sponsors Masquerade Ball
## 
### 
Stanford's International Club
will sponsor a Masquerade Ball
this Friday from 9 p.m. to 2 a.m.
at Chez Yvonne. The affair is
open to all students.
Clayton Graham and his or
chestra will provide music.
Tickets may be bought at the
door or at the"I" Club coffee
hour on Friday between 4 and (5
p.m. in Bowman Alumni House.
