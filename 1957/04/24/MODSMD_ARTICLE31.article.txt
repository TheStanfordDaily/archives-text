## 
### 
Germanic and Romanic Lan
guages Professor Kurt F. Hein
hardt will deliver a paper on
"Psychoanalysis and the Synthe
sis of Existence" tomorrow af
ternoon at 4:15 in the lower
lounge of the Women's Club
house.
Reinhardt said the paper will
deal with some of the most recent
endeavors in the fields of psy
chiatry and psychosomatic medi
cine to use the "existential" in
sights gained from an integrated
understanding of the human per
son.
