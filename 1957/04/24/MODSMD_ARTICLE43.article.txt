# Frosh Racketmen Whip San Mateo
## 
### 
In one of their closest matches
of the season, Coach Rob Ren
ker's frosh tennis learn whipped
College of San Mateo, 5-2, Mon
day on the varsity courts.
The undefeated frosh have
thoroughly dominated opponents
this year. Monday, Jim Jeffries,
Robin Hilgeman, Dick Mosk, Gil
Ranney and Jerry Dobson won
singles matches but the two
doubles teams lost.
