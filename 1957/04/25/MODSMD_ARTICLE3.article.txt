# 'The Wild Duck’ Opens at Mem Aud
## 
### 
The Henrik Ibsen tragedy "The Wild Duck" opened last night in
Memorial Auditorium with David O'lSrien, Philip Angeloft and
Ann Pine in leading roles. Director is Dr. Robert Loper, assistant
professor of drama. The play continues tonight through Saturday
and next week from Wednesday through Saturday.
—Daily photo by Neale Creamer
