# Dean of Religion To Speak Sunday
## 
###

dean of the Pacific School of Re
ligion at Berkeley, will preach
Sunday at Memorial Church on

many subjects, plans to arrive

track meet. He has written a
number of books and numerous
articles on philosophy and reli
