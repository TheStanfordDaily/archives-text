# Roth Runs Row's Car Cleaning Kick
## 
### 
Vehicles of all shapes and sizes
can be washed today in a campus
car wash to be held from 10 a.m.
to 5 p.m. at Roth House.
Proceeds from the project will
go to the Row Scholarship Fund.
Price of the wash will be $1, chair
man Mary Kay Bronson said. Cars
will be washed the way the own
ers want them to be done, she
added.
