# Sigma Chi's Select Soph Sweetheart
## 
### 
Linda Player, class of '59, was
chosen Sweetheart of Sigma Chi
for 1957 at the annual fraternity
overnight last weekend.
Other sweetheart candidates
were Belva Borquist, Anne Gor
man, Jan Graves, Jane Carmody,
Leora Gregg, Toni Turner, Susie
Goode and Carole Erickson.
