# Tickets for Prom Continue on Sale
## 
### 
The Junior Prom will be held
tomorrow night from 9 to 1 in the
Golden and Nob Hill rooms of
the Fairmont Hotel in San Fran
cisco.
Tickets for the event will con
tinue being sold on Engineering
Corner and at the Cellar from 9
a.m. to 2 p.m. today and tomor
row. Prices are $1 for students
with junior and senior class cards
and $4.50 for everyone else.
Dinner's donated by various
restaurants including L'Omelette,
Omar Khayyam's, the Blue Fox
and Amelio's will be given away
at noon today at the Cellar.
Air France Airlines is donating
champagne and decorations for
the dance.
Nelson Riddle and his orches
tra will provide the music for the
event. Records of Riddle's music
with special Prom folders will be
sold at the dance with the pro
ceeds going to the Arnaud Lea
velle Memorial Fund.
