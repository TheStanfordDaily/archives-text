# Quad Lists Editors For 1958 Yearbook
## 
### 
Ten editors have been apponted
to head the editorial staff of the
1958 Quad, according to Mike
Roemer, editor in chief.
Roomer said that Anne Ballard
will be managing editor; Edwin
Porter, darkroom manager; Nat
Turner, head photographer; Bob
Burco, copy editor; Sally Smead,
mugs; Dick Maag. candids; Nan
cy Clark, portraits; Tina Bailey,
organizations; Dave Moncrief,
sports; and Carol Belknap, pub
licity and personnel.
The editor said that the cover
contract for next year has been
signed and that other contract ne
gotiations are under way.
