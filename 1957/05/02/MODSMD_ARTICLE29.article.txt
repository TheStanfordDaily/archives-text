# SU Music Majors Chosen for Award
## 
### 
The Palo Alto chapter of Mu
Phi Epsilon, honorary music or
ganization, selected Stanford mu
sic majors Natalie Jenne and Hel
en Janzen, pianisis, and Eliza
beth Van Vorst, violinist, as re
cipients of its second annual
scholarship awards.
These artists, with Mel Carey,
pianist and winner of last year's
award, will be presented at the
annual breakfast musicale given
by Mu Phi Epsilon at the Allied
Aits Guild on May 23.
