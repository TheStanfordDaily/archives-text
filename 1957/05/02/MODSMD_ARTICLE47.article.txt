# Joukowsky to Lead Folk Dance Class
## 
### 
Anatol Joukowsky, director of
the Tuesday evening concert of
ethnic Slavic dances, will be guest
instructor at the Women's Dance
Studio in the Women's Gymna
sium this afternoon from 4:15 to
5:30.
Anyone who has had elemen
tary folk dance or its equivalent
may participate in the master les
son.
