# Upper How Holds Open House Today
## 
### 
Women's upper row residences
will hold open houses today from
1 to 5:30 p.m. for women who
wish to see these living areas be
fore drawing on May 9.
Guthrie, Roth, Jordan and Du
rand will be open to visitors and
hostesses will be waiting in the
lobbies.
The last open house will be
held Saturday morning at Lagu
nita Court.
