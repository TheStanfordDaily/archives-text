# Tickets Available To Wilbur Formal
## 
### 
Bids for the Wilbur Hall
Spring Formal will be sold today
for $2. Tickets are available from
the eight dance chairmen and all
the wing social chairmen.
The dance is to be held Satur
day night at the Surf Club in San
Francisco, from 8:30 to 12:30.
Dress for the occasion may be
either tux or a suit.
