# Delta Chi's Host All-Campus Dance
## 
### 
"Rogues' Ball." an all-campus
dance sponsored by the Delta
Chi's, will be held this Saturday
from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m. at the Delta
Chi house. Costume is "anything
you always wanted to be."
Jack Rudd and his orchestra
will provide music for the dance,
which is free to all Stanford stu
dents and their dates.
