# KZSU Holds Sale Of Extra Records
## 
### 
KZSU is holding a sale of all
records being discarded from its
record library. They will be sold
in the basement of Memorial Au
ditorium any time after 3 p.m.
this week.
Both 78 and 33 Va rpm records
are available at prices ranging
from 10 cents to $1.50.
The records include duplicate
copies and those which can no
longer be used over the radio be
cause of broadcast quality. Most
records are in good condition but
cannot be used because of the
high sensitivity of radio equip
ment.
