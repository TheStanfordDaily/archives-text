# Seniors Sponsor Ice Cream Sale
## 
### 
The senior class is sponsoring
an ice cream sale today and ev
ery Tuesday and Thursday dur
ing the month of May, with pro
ceeds going to the Heritage
Fund.
Jan Winkler, chairman of the
sales, reports that ice cream will
be sold at Engineering Corner
from I until 5 p.m. on days when
the weather is warm.
Ice cream will also be taken
in a cart to Lake Lagunita, the
volleyball courts on the Row, sun
porches and Navy and Air Force
drills.
There are three varieties of
ice cream - fudge bars, popsic
les and chocolate-covered bars.
Suggestions will be taken for oth
er varieties. The ice cream will
sell for 10 cents.
