# Students to Give Chekhov Comedy
## 
### 
"The Marriage Proposal," a fa
mous one • act farce by Anton
Chekhov, will be presented on
Theatre Matinee today at 4:15
p.m. in the Little Theatre.
The play is directed by Joseph
Regan, a student in the Depart
ment of Speech and Drama, who
directed one of Ram's Head's
Winter One - Acts.
Theatre Matinee is open to the
public without charge.
