# Museum Features Religious Exhibits
## 
### 
The stations of the Cross, sta
tues of the Madonna and Child
and other religious subjects carv
ed in ivory will be on display at
the Stanford Museum throughout
this month.
The 53 carvings, all on loan
from the collection of Mr. and
Mrs. Ralph C. Lee, date from the
12th through the 17th century and
are primarily from Italy, Spain,
Portugal and France.
The Stanford Museum is open
daily except Monday from 1 to 5
p.m. Admission charge is 25
cents except for students and
staff, who are admitted free.
