# 'Rounce and Coffin' Holds Book Show
## 
### 
The 16th annual Western Books
Exhibition will be on display at
the Stanford Library through Sat
urday.
Sponsor of the show is the
Rounce and Coffin Club of Los
Angeles, a group of printers,
booksellers and librarians inter
ested in fine printing.
Forty books were selected from
entries submitted from the 11
Western states, Hawaii and Brit
ish Columbia.
