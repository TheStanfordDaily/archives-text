# Dancers to Give Con Home Program
## 
###

Work, the Stanford Folkdancers
and the Intermediate Advanced
Class will present a program for :
the children at the Home tomor
row at 4:30 p.m.
The group will also participate
in the International Tea this Sun
day, giving two exhibitions in the
patio of the Women's Gym from
3 to 5 p.m.

nounce thaj recreational folk
dancing will be canceled tonight,
but it will be resumed next Wed
nesday at 7:30 in the Women's
Gym.
