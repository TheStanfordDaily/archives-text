# SRI Constructs Solar Furnace
## 
### 
Stanford Research Institute
has awarded a $3200 contract to
the Tibbs Construction Co! of
Palo Alto for construction of a
35-foot solar furnace tower to be
located at the Institute's head
quarters in Menlo Park.
Construction of the tower,
which will house the first solar
furnace to be built in Northern
California, will begin immediate
ly-
The furnace will be used in
experimental heating and melt
ing of specially developed high
temperature materials. It will be
in actual operation by early au
tumn.
Dr. Nevin K. Hiester, manager
of the Chemical Engineering Sec
tion at SRI, is project supervisor.
