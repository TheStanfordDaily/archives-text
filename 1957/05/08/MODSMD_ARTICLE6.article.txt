# SU Students Win Science Awards
## 
### 
The National Science Founda
tion has awarded National Sci
ence Foundation Fellowships to
four Stanford undergraduate
students. They are William J.
Buckingham, in mathematics;
Dennis Holliday, in physics; Zanc
C. Motteler, in mathematics; and
James F. Neighbor, in physics.
Buckingham and Holliday will
continue study at Princeton Uni
versity; Motteler plans to study
at the University of Minnesota;
Neighbor will study at Harvard
University.
