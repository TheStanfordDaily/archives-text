# Stanford Fencers Host California
## 
### 
Stanford's fencing team will
host California tomorrow at 1:30
p.m. in the Pavilion in a series
of duels. Admission to the event
is free.
Coach Elvvyn Bugge's team
will consist of Bob Simon, John
Gates, John Daniels, George Of
fen, Andrew Stevenson, and Gari
Andreini.
