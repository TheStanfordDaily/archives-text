# Simon, Often Pace Fencers to Win
## 
### 
With Bob Simon winning all
five of his bouts and George Often
copping four of five, Stanford's
fencing team edged California on
Saturday, 14-11.
Also competing under coach El
wyn Bugge in the Pavilion con
test were Gari Andreini, John
Daniels, John Gates and Andrew
Stevenson.
