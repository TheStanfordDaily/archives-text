# Museum Declares Wednesday Free
## 
### 
Tomorrow has been declared a
free day at the Stanford Museum
to celebrate the third anniversary
of its reopening after a complete
remodeling.
Those visiting the museum on
"Free Day" will see special ex
hibils of carved ivory, Chinese
porcelain and Spanish lusterware
as well as the many permanent
exhibits.
They will also get an advance
peek at a forthcoming exhibit,
"They Looked Like This," 157 au
thentically costumed miniatures
of historic personages. Two of
the models will be on display on
Wednesday.
The Stanford Museum is open
daily from 1 to 5 p.m. Admission
charge for the general public is
customarily 25 cents.
