# Off-Campus Men To Sign for Stern
## 
### 
Village and off-campus men in
terested in living in Stern HaJl
for autumn quarter must sign up
today between 8:30 a.m. and 4:30
p.m. Cards will be available in
the Housing Office, Room 101 A,
Inner Quad.
Eating club men and men who
have not completed 45 units of
work or who are not registered
for spring quarter are not eli
gible to sign up.
Graduate students wishing to
live in a campus dormitory for
autumn quarter may sign a wait
ing list in the Housing Office this
quarter. Sign up for transfer of
room deposits from spring and
autumn quarter will be held in
the men's residences today.
