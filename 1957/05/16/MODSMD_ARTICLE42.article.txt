# SU Alumni to Hear Med Center Plans
## 
### 
The Stanford Medical Alumni
from San Mateo and Santa Clara
Counties and their wives will be
told the plans for the Palo Alto-
Stanford Medical Center at the
Palo Alto Elks' Club tomorrow
at 7 p.m.
President Wallace Sterling,
acting Dean of the Medical
School Robert H. Alway, Univer
sity Board of Trustees Chairman
Lloyd Dinkelspiel and Stanford
Medical Center Chairman W.
Parmer Fuller 111 will speak at
the dinner.
