# Hillier, Dauer Win McKinney Contest
## 
### 
The winners of the Mary A.
Earl McKinney Essay Contest for
undergraduate engineering stu
dents have been announced by the
English Department. First prize
($100) was awarded to Frederick
S. Hillier. Second prize ($25) was
won by Arthur F. Dauer. Topic
of this year's contest was "Auto
mation and the Individual."
