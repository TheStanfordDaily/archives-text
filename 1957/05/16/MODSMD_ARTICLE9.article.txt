# Indian Writer Sets Speech in Palo Alto
## 
### 
The local National Guardian
Committee of the Community
Center is sponsoring a talk by
Kumar Goshal. editor and author
from India, tomorrow at 8 p.m.
in Room R of the Palo Alto Com
munity Center.
Goshal will speak on "The Mid
dle East — Is Peace Possible?"
The public has been invited.
