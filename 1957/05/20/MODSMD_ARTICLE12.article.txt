# TV Program to Tell Med School Views
## 
### 
Television viewers will have an
opportunity to see contrasting
views of the Stanford Medical
School, present and future, to
night from 8 to 8:30 on KQED,
Channel 9. "Kaleidoscope."
Robert H. Alway, acting Dean
of the Medical School, will dem
onstrate the present "inadequate
and antiquated'' facilities by
means of photographs.
