# Frosh Auction 'Indian'
## 
### 
Going, going, «one" cries the auctioneer at the Freshman Class
Grand Triad Friday night at Wilbur Hall. Frosh from the Univer
sity of California, San Jose State College and Stanford converged
on Wilbur for an evening which included games, dancing and an
auction of symbols of the three schools.
—Daily Photo by John Denham
