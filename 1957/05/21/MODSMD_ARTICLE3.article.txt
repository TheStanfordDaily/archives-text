# Club Postpones Swim Exhibition
## 
### 
"By the Sea," which was to be
presented by the Stanford Swim
Club, sponsored by Women's
Recreation Association, has been
postponed, according to Sybil
Hoffman, Swim Club president.
The performance has been re
scheduled for May 29 and 30.
Tickets may be obtained free of
charge from any Swim Club
member' or at the ASSU office.
