# Senior Hall Set For June 13 at Claremont Hotel
## 
### 
The Senior Ball, the only Stan
ford formal dance where cor
sages are allowed, will be held
this year in the Garden Room
and terrace of the Claremont Ho
ld in Berkeley on June 13. with
Iwo bands playing from 9 p.m.
to 4 a.m.
Frank DeVoI and his band,
Capitol recording artists, and
Wall Tollison's group, well
known Bay Area hand, will share
the music spotlight with DeVol
playing until 1 a.m. and Tollison
the last three hours.
Tickets go on sale this week
for seniors from 8 a.m. until 3
p.m. at Engineering Corner.
They cost $10 per couple and in
clude an early breakfast. Ar
rangements for women return
ing to campus will be announced
this week.
