# Two Intermezzo Programs Today
## 
### 
Two Intermezzo programs,
sponsored by the Stanford Li
hrary, will hp presented simul
taneously today.
British poetess Mrs. Francis
Cornford will talk at 4 p.m. in
the Render Room on "A Geor
gian Root." To supplement the
speech, there will be an exhibit
of hooks and portraits of the pe
riod.
The Intermezzo Film Series
will present "Sadko," a Russian
color film which received the
highest award at the Venice Film
Festival, in Cubberley Audito
rium at 4:15 p.m. R is based on
a Russian folktale with music by
Rimsky-Korsakov. The dialogue
is in Russian with English sub
titles.
