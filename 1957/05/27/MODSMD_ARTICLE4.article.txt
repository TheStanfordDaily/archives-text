# Arabian Culture Exhibited
## 
### 
Stanford Arab students hosted last Friday's International Club
meeting at liowman Alninni House, a weekly activity of the Insti
tute of International Relations. Each student represented his own
nation in native costume, entertainment and handicraft exhibits.
Authentic Arab refreshments were also served. "I" Club president
for next year is Vartan Gregorian.
—Daily Photo by Neale Creamer
