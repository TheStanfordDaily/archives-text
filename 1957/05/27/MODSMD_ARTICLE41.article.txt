# 18 Students Sought For Summer Tour
## 
### 
Eighteen students are being
sought to participate in a nine
week Japanese tour sponsored
by San Jose State College. The
tour is slated to begin July 1, ac
cording to Dr. William A. Vatch
er, professor of political science
at SJSC.
Eight semester unit credits in
social science will be offered in
conjunction with the tour. The
$1454 expense will include pay
ment for transportation, tuition
and accommodations.
