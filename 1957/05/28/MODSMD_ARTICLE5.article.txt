# Ticket Sales Open For Senior Ball
## 
### 
The Claremont Hotel In Berke
ley will be the scene of the Senior
Ball on June 13 at 9 p.m. The
Garden Room and terrace of the
hotel have been reserved, and the
orchestras of Frank DeVol and
Walt Tolleson have been engaged.
Ticket sales arc now open to all
classes, but may be bought this
week only. The sale is limited to
450 tickets, according to co-chair
men Duane Jordan and Mary Bell.
