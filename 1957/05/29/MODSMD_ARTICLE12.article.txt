# WRA Swim Show to Be Held Tonight, Tomorrow at Gym Pool
## 
### 
The Stanford Women's Swim Club will present its annual swim
show, "By the Sea," tonight and tomorrow night at 8:30 in the
Women's Gymnasium pool.
The show, sponsored by the Women's Recreation Association,
will feature a solo by Sally Phillips Angove, former national

champion in synchronized swim
ming. Mrs. Angove and football
star Paul Wiggin will present a
comedy number.
The opening number will depict
swimming in 1890, including the
traditional bathing suits of that
time.
Several members of the wom
en's diving classes will present
exhibitions in form diving. A
group of "sailors" will cavort in
the water in "Ship Ahoy!"
Further novelty numbers in
clude a presentation featuring six
Hawaiian girls and a synchroniz
ed swimming duet by Sybil Hoff
man and Diane Custer.
The program will conclude with
all members of the show swim
ming in a finale representing the
waves of the sea. The program is
open to the public free of charge.
