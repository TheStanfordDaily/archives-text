# Theatre Matinee Has Puppet Show
## 
### 
An Indonesian shadow puppet
demonstration will be held for
Theatre Matinee next Wednes
day at 4:15 p.m. in Little Theatre.
The shadow play, called "Wa
jang in Java," will be explained
and illustrated by Dr. T. Purwo
husodo, an expert in the field of
dermatology who is studying for
a year in this country on a teach
ing exchange program. The pup
pets are fantastically designed
figtifes in Hindu-mythology.
On the same program will be
a hand puppet play supervised
by Virginia Opsvig, a teacher of
creative dramatics in the Depart
ment of Speech and Drama.
The program is open to Ihe
public free of charge.
