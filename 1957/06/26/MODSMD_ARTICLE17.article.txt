# Student Policemen Jobs Available
## 
### 
Student, policeman interviews
will be held tomorrow between
7 p.m. and 10:30 p.m. by Miss
Beverly Fitch, summer financial
manager.
The interviews will be held in
the ASSU office. Beverly stresses
that appointments are necessary
for the interviews, and may be
made today or tomorrow on sign
up sheets at the ASSU office.
Student police are present at
all ASSU social functions, Flicks,
and co-recreational swimming. The
pay for the job is at the rate of
$1 per hour. Hours are arranged
with the policemen and financial
manager.
A head policeman will be ap
pointed and receive $1.25 per hour.
