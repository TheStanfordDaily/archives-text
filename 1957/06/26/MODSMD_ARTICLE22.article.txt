# Summer Library Hours Announced
## 
### 
Library hours for the .summer
quarter will be: Monday through
Thursday, 8 a.m. to 9 p.m.; Fri
day and Saturday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.
Sunday the library will be open
in the afternoon from 1 p.m. to
5 p.m.
The main reading room and the
periodical room will not be opened
until 9 a.m. Monday through Sat
urday.
The hours for the documentary
room and the engineering library
will be posted on the doors of the
rooms.
Because of the holiday on July 4
when the library will be closed all
day the library will close at 5 p.m.
on Wednesday, July 3.
