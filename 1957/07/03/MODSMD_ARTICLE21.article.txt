# Movie Discount Cards Available
## 
### 
Movie Discount Cards are on sale now! If you don't have one,
you can purchase on for one dollar in the ASSU office above "The
Cellar." These cards are honored at theaters located on the Pen
insular or in San Francisco.
Just present your card at the box office of any of the following
theaters: Stanford and Varsity in Palo Alto, Park in Menlo Park,
Fox in Redwood City, Carlos in San Carlos, San Mateo in San
Mateo, Peninsula in Burlingame, and the Fox in San Francisco.
"You save thirty-five t;ents per show; so buy a card today. It's
a dollar well spent," urges Beve Fitch, student financial manager.
