# Business School Hosts Executives
## 
### 
Fifty-eight business managers,
including five from foreign coun
tries, enrolled Sunday in the sixth
annual Executive Development
program of the Graduate School
of Business at Stanford Univer
sity.
The executives, selected by their
respective companies for their
current high rank and their abil
ity to further advance learning,
will take eight weeks of concen
trated study with the Business
School professors.
One each is coming from
France, England, and India and
two are coming from Canada. The
others will arrive from continental
United States and Hawaii.
Course work covers the various
problems and methods of busi
ness.
The student executives will be
housed in part of Stanford's new
est men's residence, Wilbur Hall.
