# Ole Folks' Fling
## 
### 
Graduate women will entertain
graduate men tonight at Lagunita
Court. The soiree will begin at
8 p.m. and run until 11 p.m.
The evening will be highlighted
by music, dancing and refresh
ments. For those who tire easily,
the girls have condescended to add
a series of sedentary parlor games
to round out the evening's enter
tainment.
