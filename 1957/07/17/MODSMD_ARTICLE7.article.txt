# DEADLINES
## 
### 
The deadline for filing vaccination slips is Monday, July 22;
failure to file by that date will result in a fine of two dollars. If
not filed by Monday, July 29, registration in the University will
subject to cancellation by the committee on public health.
Petitions for changes in examination schedules must be filed at
Room 111-R, Registrar's Office by July 29.
