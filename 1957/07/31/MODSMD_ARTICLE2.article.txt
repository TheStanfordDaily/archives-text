# Ole Pard To Host Volleyball Champs
## 
### 
"Free hamburgers and milk
shakes will be donated to the win
ners of the volleyball tournaments
by the familiar Old Pard of Pard's
Restaurant," said Dick Offut,
chairman of the tournament.
Only three games remain to be
played, eliminations being almost
completed. In the co-ed tourney,
Judy Hubbard, Susan Wheeler,
Bob Besse, and John Phelps will
set a final battle against Elsie
Holler, Claire Nemoede, Bob Rog
ers and Frank Coale, at 4:30 p.m.
tomorrow.
The two remaining games of the
men's teams were to be played yes
terday to determine the two teams
who will play in the finals.
