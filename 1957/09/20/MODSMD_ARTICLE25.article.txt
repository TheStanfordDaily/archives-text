to 12 noon and 2 to 4 p.m., and
Tuesday from 9 a.m. to 12 noon.
Tryouts will be held at the re

Rehearsal
Freshman choir rehearsal will
be held at 10:30 a.m. tomorrow at
Memorial Church. "I hope we
have a large number of men turn

this year's choir is bigger than
last year's 130-voice group."
There are opportunities for
solo work in all three organiza
