# Northern Lights Put On Big Display
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO (/P) Th"
aurora borealis put on another
show last night.
The tower at San Francisco In
ternational Airport said airline
pilots all over Northern Califor
nia reported seeing a display of
the northorn lights.
Observers on the ground at So
nora, in the foothills of the Sier
ra Nevada, said the pulsing pink
light began around 9:40 p.m.
