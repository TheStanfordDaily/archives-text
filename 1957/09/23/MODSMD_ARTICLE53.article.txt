# Graduate Student Wins Econ Prize
## 
### 
David A. Baerncopf, graduate
student in economics, has been
announced the first winner of the
Eliot Jones Award of the Western
Economic Association. His essay,
"A Note on Long Cycle Analysis,"
was selected over entries from
colleges and universities in the
western United States, Canada
and Hawaii. Baerncopf was in
vited to read his paper in Salt
Lake City August 28-30.
