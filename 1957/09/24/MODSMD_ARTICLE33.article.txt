# Stanford Cancer Research Trebled
## 
### 
The pace of cancer research at
Stanford has trebled in the past
four years, Dr. Henry S. Kaplan,
chief of the Stanford Medical
School's radiology department,
reported Sunday.
Dr. Kaplan has just ended a
two-year term as chairman of
the University's Cancer Research
Committee.
