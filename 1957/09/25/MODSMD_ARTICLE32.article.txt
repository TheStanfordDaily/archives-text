# Publisher Accepts Manuscripts Now
## 
### 
Manuscripts of short stories
or novelettes will be accepted
until Jan. 1, 1958, for a series of
volumes of short fiction by new
American writers, Charles Scrib
ner's Sons said today.
The publishers are seeking
manuscripts between 80 and 200
pages, and they should be sent
directly to Perry Knowlton,
Charles Scribner's Sons, 597 sth
Ave., New York 17, N.Y.
