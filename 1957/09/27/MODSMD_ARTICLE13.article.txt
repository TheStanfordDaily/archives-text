# Cornelia Skinner At Memorial Aud
## 
### 
Cornelia Otis Skinner, world
famous dramatic actress, will ap
pear at Memorial Auditorium to
morrow evening at 8:30, sponsor
ed by the Palo Alto-Stanford Club.
Miss Skinner, famous for her
pantomime and monodramas, in
which she peoples the stage with
imaginary characters and pro
duces drama from situations in
volving these characters, appears
for Ihe first time in several years,
according to Mrs. J. B. Ruther
ford. member of the board of di
rectors of the Palo Alto-Stanford
Club.
Tickets may be obtained at the I
box office of Memorial Auditori-1
um from 12:30 to 3:15 p.m.
