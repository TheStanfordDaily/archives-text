# 5 Frosh Penalized By Men's Council
## 
### 
Men's Council last night sus
pended five Wilbur Hall freshmen
from the University for one quar
ter and then suspended the pen
alty, in the first Fundamental
Standard case of autumn quarter.
The men were found guilty of
having possession of liquor in a
car. They had driven to a parking
lot to consume the beverage, but
were apprehended by Palo Alto
police, who turned them over to
the University for disciplinary ac
tion.
The five men also received five
Corporation Yard hours each and
were placed on probation for au
tumn quarter.
