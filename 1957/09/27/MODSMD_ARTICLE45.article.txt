# Soph Class Flans Reunion for Oct. 5
## 
### 
Round-up time for the class of
'60 will be celebrated in style Sat
urday, Oct. 5, with a barbecued
chicken dinner and an afternoon
and evening of sophomoric fun.
The annual function will be held
at Adobe Creek Lodge at 2 p.m.
Tickets will be on sale at Engi
neering Corner and the Post Of
fice at $2.75 for class card hold
ers and $3 for those without class
cards. Tickets must be purchased
by Thursday, Oct. 3.
Swimming, dancing, tennis,
baseball and the informal chick
en dinner will round out the list
of activities in the first outing of
the class of '60.
