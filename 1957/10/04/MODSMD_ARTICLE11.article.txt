# Po Prostu Banned: Polish Youths Riot
## 
### 
WARSAW (ff)—Polish riot po
lice and armed factory militiamen
attacked demonstrating students
last night three times with tear
gas. Several students were ar
rested.
The students rallied to protest
a government ban on their weekly
anti-Stalinist paper Po Prostu.
The name means "Straight On."
