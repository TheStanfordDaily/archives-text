# Children's Drama Class Announced
## 
### 
The Speech and Drama Depart
ment is organizing a class in cre
ative dramatics for children seven
years old and in the second grade.
The group will meet every Mon
day for eight weeks in Memorial
Hall from 3:15 to 4:05 p.m.. under
the instruction of Virginia Opsvig.
The fee is $5. Application blanks
for the children may be obtained
at Memorial Hail either from the
Speech and Drama office or, from
1 to 3 p.m., from Miss Opsvig.
