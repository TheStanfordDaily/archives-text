# Freshman Photos Continue at Roble
## 
### 
Individual Quad pictures of Ro
ble and off-campus freshmen will
be taken at Roble tomorrow and
Friday from 9 a.m. to noon and
from 1 to 5 p.m.
Men must wear light shirts,
dark coats and ties, and women
white sweaters or white blouses
with sleeves. Price is $1.50, cover
ing the proofs and a place in the
Quad.
