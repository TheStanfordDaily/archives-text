# SU Man Expelled, Three Suspended
## 
### 
Five Stanford men wore recent
ly tried before Men's Council on
a Fundamental Standard violation
involving drinking and rowdyism
in the stands during a football
game.
Specifically the five were charg
ed with possession of alcoholic
beverages and acting in a manner
unbecoming Stanford students.
One of the five was acquitted
of the charges. Of the remaining
four, one was permanently ex
pelled and one received two quar
ters' suspension and probation for
the remainder of the term.
One of the remaining two re
ceived a quarter's suspension and
probation for one year. The other
received one quarter's suspension
with no probation.
