# Open House Given At Storey Saturday
## 
### 
Durand and Storey houses will
sponsor an open house after the
Washington Stalo game Satur
day at Storey House from 1:30 to
6 p.m.
Leon White, Stanford guitarist
and singer who played at the
Roth open house two weeks ago,
will provide the entertainment.
Punch, cookies and doughnuts
will be served.
