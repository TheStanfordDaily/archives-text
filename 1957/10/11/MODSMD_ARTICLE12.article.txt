# Ratner to Speak At Women's Club
## 
### 
St a n for d Faculty Women's
Club will meet Monday at 2 p.m.
in the upper lounge of the Wom
en's Clubhouse. Officers will be
introduced by Mrs. H. Donald
Winbigler, president.
Dr. Leonard G. Ratner, asso
ciate professor of music, will give
a demonstration lecture entitled
"Listening to Music."
An informal luncheon will be
held in the lower lounge of the
clubhouse at 12:45. New mem
bers will be welcomed.
