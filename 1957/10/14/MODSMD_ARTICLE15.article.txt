# New WRA Pool Policy Announced
## 
### 
The Women's Recreation Asso
ciation has announced a change
in its policy regarding guest priv
ileges at the women's pool on
weekends during autumn quar
ter.
Formerly a registered woman
student could have one man guest
at the pool on Saturday and Sun
day afternoons.
Now each woman student may
have two men guests at the pool
and as many women guests as
she desires as long as they are all
college age or older. Weekend
pool hours are 1-1 p.m. on Satur
days and Sundays.
