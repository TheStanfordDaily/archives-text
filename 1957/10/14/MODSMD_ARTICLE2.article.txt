# Quad Photographs Lagunita Women
## 
### 
Lagunita women, excepting
seniors, will have their pictures
taken for the 1958 Quad today,
tomorrow and Wednesday at the
Quad office from 9 a.m. to noon
and from 1 to 5 p.m.
Women are to wear white
sweaters or blouses with sleeves.
Sign-up sheets for appointments
are posted in Lagunita but wom
en without appointments may
also report to the Quad office at
the scheduled times.
