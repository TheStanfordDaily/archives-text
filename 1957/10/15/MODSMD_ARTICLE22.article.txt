# Lag Photos Taken Today, Tomorrow
## 
### 
All students except seniors in
Lugunita will have their individ
ual pictures taken for the 1958
Stanford Quad today and tomor
row from 9 a.m. to 12 noon and
from 1 to 5 p.m. in the Quad of
fice.
All men must wear light shirts
and dark coats and ties. Women
must wear white blouses with
sleeves, or white sweaters.
The price of pictures, which
pays for the proofs and a place
in the Quad, will be $1.50.
Graduate students in Crothers,
Crothers Memorial and Ventura
will be photographed Thursday
and Friday.
