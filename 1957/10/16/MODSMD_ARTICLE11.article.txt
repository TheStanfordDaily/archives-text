# Gallery Features American Artists
## 
### 
"Fifteen American Painters,"
an exhibition of recent works by
painters who have received
awards in national competitions,
is being displayed in the Stanford
Art Gallery and will remain
through Nov. 3.
The paintings show a wide
range of styles from severe sim
plicity to extreme complexity,
and reflect the general trends of
mid-twentieth century American
painting. Leon Goldin, Howard
Warshaw, Leonard Edmonson
and John Paul Jones arc among
the better known painters repre
sented.
Etchings and engravings from
the University collections will bo
shown concurrently in the gal
lery foyer.
