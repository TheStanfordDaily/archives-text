# 'I' Center to Be Opened Thursday
## 
### 
As part of the campus observ
ance of United Nations Day,
Dean of Humanities and Sciences
Philip H. Rhinelander will dedi
cate the new Stanford Interna
tional Center, 539 Lasuen Street,
on Thursday at 4:30 p.m.
An open house will follow the
ceremony in the building, which
was formerly the residence of
University Chaplain E. M. Minto.
It will officially open for busi
ness on Monday, Nov. 4.
The center will house two
lounges, a dining room and kitch
en, reception area and offices on
the ground floor. Administrative
facilities and an apartment for a
host couple are located on the
