# Prizes on Display For Row Carnival
## 
### 
Prizes which will be given away
at the Women's Row Carnival this
Friday night following the UCLA
rally arc now on display in the
Bookstore window.
These prizes, donated by down
town Palo Alto and Stanford
Shopping Center merchants, in
clude sweaters and jewelry. Other
awards include gift orders and
dinners for-two at Peninsula and
San Frartcisco restaurants, includ
ing the Mark Hopkins and Fair
mont hotels.
Besides many skill booths, there
will be a street dance from 7:45
to Ii p.m., to the music of Lou
Terman's band. An auction will
be another special feature, plus
such entertainment as fortune
telling and horror movies.
