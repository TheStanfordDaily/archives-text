# Band, Pep Squad Open UCLA Rally
## 
### 
The UCLA rally will begin to
night at 7 in front of the Post
Office, with the arrival of the
Stanford Band.
The band will be marching
from Wilbur, leaving there about
6:45. At the same time the cheer
leaders will be at Roble and La
gunita, trying to coax their in
habitants to attend the rally.
In case of rain, the rally will
probably be held on the porch of
one of the houses on the Row,
said Tom Pike, sub-chairman on
Rally Com.
