# AWS Carnations Sold Before Game
## 
### 
The annual sale of carnations
by the Associated Women Stu
dents will be held for the first
time this year, Saturday morn
ing, before the UCLA game.
Men's living groups and clubs
have had the opportunity to
order these carnations in blocks,
and the carnations will be sold
this Saturday in all dormitories
and on campus for 25 cents each.
Profits from this sale and the
one on Big Game Day will go to
a scholarship fund for Stanford
women students.
