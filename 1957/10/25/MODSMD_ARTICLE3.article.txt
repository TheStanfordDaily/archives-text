# Quad Takes Senior Men's Color Photos
## 
### 
Colored portraits of senior fra
ternity men for the Quad will be
taken today and Monday from 1)
a.m. to noon and from 1 to 5 p.m.
at the Quad office. Fraternity
men not signed up for appoint
ments may have their color por
traits taken at any timo on these
days, the office reported.
Eating club, off-campus and in
dependent senior men may have
their pictures taken on Monday,
Tuesday and Wednesday at the
same times.
Froshbooks may be ordered at
the Quad office any day from 1
to 6 p.m.
