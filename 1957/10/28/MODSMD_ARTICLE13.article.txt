# Seniors' Portraits Taken This Week
## 
### 
Colored portraits of senior eat
ing club, off • campus and inde
pendent men for the Quad will br
taken today. Tuesday and Wed
nesday from 9 a.m. to noon and
from 1 to 5 p.m. Today is also
the last day for senior fraternity
men to have their pictures taken.
Those senior men not signed
up for appointments may have
their color pictures taken at any
time on these days according to
the Quad office.
Thursday and Friday of this
week makeup pictures for all
seniors will be taken. Any senior
who has not had his portraits
taken should report on these two
days.
Students may order Frosh
books any day from 1 to 6 p.m.
at the Quad office.
