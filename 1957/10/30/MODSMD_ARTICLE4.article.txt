# Polls Open at 10 a.m. For ASSU Primaries
## 
### 
Polls for the autumn primary elections will open today at 10
a.m. at Engineering Corner and will remain open until 4:30 p.m.
Students will be asked to present their student body cards at
the tables, where ballots will be issued. Students are then to mark
the ballots and place them in the proper ballot boxes, which will
be clearly marked. No pencils will be available at the polling
tables.
Tomorrow polls will be open at 8:30 a.m. and will close at 3:30
p.m. Totals will be announced in Friday's Daily.
