# Block Ticket Sale Ends for Gaieties
## 
### 
Sale of block tickets for Big
Game Gaieties to eligible groups
will end tomorrow. Priced at $1,
$1.25 and $1.50, tickets are sold by

Wednesday; at 0:15 (after the

Friday. Checks and money orders
may be mailed to P.O. Box 830,
