# All-Campus Mixer Planned by Sophs
## 
### 
A "campus coffee mixer" will
be sponsored this Sunday night
after the flicks and each Sunday
thereafter by the sophomore
class. The affair will be held at
the Nitery.
Free coffee will be served from
9:30 to midnight at the "stag or
drag" mixer. After the first eve
ning there will be a slight charge
for coffee and doughnuts, an
nounced soph class officers.
