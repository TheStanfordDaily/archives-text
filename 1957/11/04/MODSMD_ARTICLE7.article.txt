# Mayfield Ave. Will Close, Davis Says
## 
### 
Mayfield Avenue from College
Terrace to Frenchman's Road at
the end of the Row will be closed
"for good" beginning tomorrow,
according to police chief Gordon
Davis.
The closing i.s necessary because
of construction work on a tract
fronting Mayfield Avenue. Davis
said. He advises persons going to
and from College Terrace to use
Escondido Road, which runs past
Wilbur and Branner halls.
