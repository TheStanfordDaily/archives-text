# Funeral Services For Cook Today
## 
### 
Funeral services for Larry
Cook, who was fatally injured in
an automobile accident Saturday,
will be held at 11 a.m. at the
Fast Lawn Mausoleum Chapel in
Sacramento. The Daily incor
rectly announced yesterday that
the services would be held at 1
p.m. today.
