# Quad to Take Pics Of Moore Women
## 
### 
Sophomores and .juniors living
in Florence Moore may have
their 1958 Quad portraits taken
today and tomorrow. Hours are
from 9 a.m. to noon and from 1 to
5 p.m.
Women are requested to wear
white blouses. All pictures will
be taken at the Quad office; the
price is $1.50, to be paid when
the picture is taken.
Froshbooks may be purchased
at the Quad office any afternoon
from 4 to G, and unclaimed 195G
Quads may be purchased at the
ASSU office from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
