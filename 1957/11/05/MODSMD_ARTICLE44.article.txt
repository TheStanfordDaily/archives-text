# Little Theatre Will Hold Cast Tryouts
## 
### 
Tryouts for tho final perform
ance of t lie Wednesday matinees
will be held at 4:30 p.m. today
in the Little Theatre, Memorial
Auditorium.
Two men will be cast in each
of two plays, "Purgatory," by
William Butler Yeats, and "Liter
ate Highwayman," a Japanese
Kyogen comedy. All students are
eligible to read for parts. The
plays will be presented Dec. 4.
"Purgatory" deals with an old
man's attempts to expiate the
sins of his dead mother and re
lease her soul. "Literate High
wayman" tells the story of two
robbers who discover that honor
is meaningless when no one is
present to applaud it.
