# Series to Feature English Speaker
## 
### 
John Wisdom, professor at
Cambridge University and one of
England's foremost philosophers,
will be featured on the Tuesday
Evening Series tonight at 7:30
in Memorial Auditorium.
Subject of Wisdom's speech
will be "Paradox and Discovery."
A fellow of Trinity College,
Professor Wisdom is at present a
visiting faculty member at UC
LA. He is the author of two
books. "Philosophy and Psycho
analysis" and "Other Minds."
An additional address will be
given by Professor Wisdom to a
Philosophy Department collo
quium in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse tomorrow at
'1:15 p.m. The subject will be "A
Feature of Wittgenstein's Tech
nique."
