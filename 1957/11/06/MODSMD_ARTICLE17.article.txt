# Stanford Players To Hold Auditions
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Stanford Play
ers' production of "He Who Gets
Slapped" will be held in floom
205 today, tomorrow and Friday
at 7:30 p.m.
An additional tryout will bo
held Friday from 3 to 5 p.m. on
the main stage of Memorial Au
ditorium.
All students, faculty and staff
are eligible to try out for this
romantic story of an aristocrat
who leaves his world to join the
circus world, meets a beautiful
bareback rider and falls in love
with her.
There are parts for six women
and 10 men. Dr. Robert Dierlam,
director, would especially like to
hear from anyone who can swing
on a trapeze, do acrobats or jug
gle.
