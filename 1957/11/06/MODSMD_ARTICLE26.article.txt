# Consul to Speak On 'Germany, '57'
## 
### 
Dr. Peter Hermes, German
consul in San Francisco, will
speak on "Germany, 1957" at
Bowman Alumni House tomor
row at 4:15 p.m.
Sponsored by the lIR, Dr. Her
mes will be a dinner guest at
Florence Moore Hall following
his talk. Anyone wishing to at
tend this dinner should sign up
at the lI.R shack from -1 to 6 p.m.
today or tomorrow. The price of
the dinner will be $1 .25 per per
son for everyone except women
living in Moore.
