# Stanford Dames Set Square Dance
## 
### 
William R. Davis will call and
provide the music for the Stan
ford Dames square dance Satur
day at K p.m. at the Faculty
Men's Clubhouse on Pine Avenue.
Donations of $1 will be collect
ed at the door. Prizes will be
given at the dance and cider and
doughnuts will tie served. Stan
ford Dames and their husbands
have been invited.
Mrs. David Schink. dance chair
man, will be assisted by Mrs. Ed
win Bennett, Mrs. Ray Moody,
Mrs. John L. Sprague, Mrs. Clyde
Toumela, Mrs. Richard Walz and
Mrs. Irwin Wunderman.
