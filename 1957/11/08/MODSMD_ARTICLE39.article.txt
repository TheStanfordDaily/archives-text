# Tennis Tournament Deadline Is Monday
## 
### 
Third-round singles matches in
I he Women's Tennis Tournament
must be played and recorded by
Monday at 6 p.m. or participants
will have to be defaulted out of
the tournament. The next round
will then have to be played off
and recorded by Nov. 17 at 6 p.m.
