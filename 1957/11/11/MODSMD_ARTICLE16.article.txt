# Series Features Frank O'Connor
## 
### 
Frank O'Connor, rioted Irish
writer of short, stories whose
style and compassion have won
him many fans in the United
States, will be the next guest of
the Tuesday Evening Series to
morrow night at 7:30 in Memo
rial Auditorium.
O'Connor will answer the ques
tion he says is so often put to
him at literary teas: "Mr. O'Con
nor, why don't you write about
America?"
Wednesday at 4:15 he will read
one of his short stories in Dinkel
spiel Auditorium. This program
is also open to the public and is
under the auspices of the Stan
ford Creative Writing Center.
