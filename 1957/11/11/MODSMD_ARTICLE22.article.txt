# Tickets on Sale For Deller Trio
## 
### 
Tickets are now on sale for
the Alfred Deller Trio, scheduled
to appear on Sunday, Nov. 24, at
A p.m. in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
The trio made its first U.S. tour
in 1955 and presents music from
the Renaissance and Baroque
eras. Alfred Deller, vocalist, is
accompanied by Desmond Dupre
on the lute and viola de gamba,
and Robert Conant on the harpsi
chord.
Tickets at $2 may be obtained
from the Dinkelspiel box office,
DA 6-3691, on weekdays from
12:30 to 4:30 p.m., and at the
Peninsula Box Office, Town and
Country Village, DA 2-2122.
