# Salk Polio Shots Available Today
## 
### 
Anticipating the advent of the
polio season, the Health Service
will give Salk vaccine shots Tues
day and Thursday from 1 to 3
p.m. Since the introduction of
the Salk vaccine, the polio rate in
California has dropped by 80 per
cent.
Students wishing to have polio
shots must not receive other im
munizations (flu, smallpox, etc.>
cither two weeks before or after
the polio inoculation.
