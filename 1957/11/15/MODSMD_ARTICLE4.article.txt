# Ballet Tomorrow
## 
### 
Nora Kovach and I.vivan Itabovsky will appear with their dance
company tomorrow at 8:15 p.m. in Memorial Auditorium as the
second ASSU Concert Series program this season. Their ballet
troupe will present "Highlights from Some of 1.1k; World's Great
est Ballets." The ensemble includes Bulgarian-born Sonia Arova,
and Job Sanders, who is well known in the theater world as a per
former as well as a choreographer.
