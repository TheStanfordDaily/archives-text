# Freshmen to Get Axe Handbook
## 
### 
Axe Society will give activi
ties handbooks to all freshmen
at house meetings tonight, hand
book co-editor Colin Johnston
has announced.
The handbook includes specific
information about all Stanford
activities and the aims of Stan
ford student government. The
story of the "Immortal 21" and
the rest of the Axe tradition is
also featured in the handbook.
Students may obtain additional
copies of the handbook at the AS
SU office.
