# Upper Division Will Pre-Reg Wednesday
## 
### 
Pro-registration for winter
quarter begins Wednesday, ac
cording to Registrar Ilarvey Ilall.
Uppe»' division and graduate
students wili pre-registcr Wed
nesday and Thursday, arid fresh
man, sophomore and lower divi
sion students will sign class lists
beginning Friday and continuing
until Dec. 3.
Students pre-regislering Wed
nesday and Thursday are to ob
tain an official study list card
from their major school or de
partment and fill out their pro
gram in ink with the approval
and signature of their major pro
fessor.
For any courses marked "8" in
the winter quarter time schedule,
the students must sign the appro
priate class lists at the Informa
tion Desk, Registrar's Office,
Room 111-A, between 8 a.m. and
noon or between 1 and 5 p.m.
Wednesday and Thursday. The
name on the class list assures the
student of admission to class, if
he attends the first class meet
ing.
The official study list card
must be turned in, in person, to
the check-out clerk at the Infor
mation Desk, Registrar's Office,
Room 111-A, before 5 p.m. Thurs
day.
Hall said that part-time gradu
ate students on the honors co
operative program or other pro
grams are asked to pre-register.
