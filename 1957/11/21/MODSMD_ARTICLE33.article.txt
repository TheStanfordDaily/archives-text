# New Book Tells Of '40 'Wow' Boys
## 
### 
Stanford's historic 1910 football
team, the "Wow Boys>" and their
Amazing 1940 season are the sub
ject of a new book entitled "The
Wow Boys." The book is written
by Cyclone Covey, a former Stan
ford student.
Don Litjbendorfer, Stanford's
athletic publicity director, has
written a foreword for the book,
which is expected at the Book
store this week.
