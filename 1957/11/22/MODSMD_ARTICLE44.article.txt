# Eating Club Men Get Quad Pictures
## 
### 
Eating club sophomores and
juniors will continue having their
Quad pictures taken today.
On Monday and Tuesday, Vil
lage and off-campus sophomores
and juniors as well as sophomore
and junior women living in Un
ion and on the Row will have
their pictures taken.
No portraits for the 1958 Quad
will be taken after Tuesday, Nov.
26.
