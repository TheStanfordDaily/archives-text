# Quad Portraits To End Wednesday
## 
### 
Sophomores and juniors living
off campus, in the Union or on
the Row will have their Quad
portraits taken today through
Wednesday. Photos will be taken
i from 9 a.m. to 12 noon and from
; 1 to 5 p.m.
It was requested that men
j wear ties, coats and white shirts;
i and women, white blouses or
j sweaters with sleeves.
This week will be the last op
i portunity for sophomores and
juniors to have their photos ta
ken. No portraits will be taken
after Wednesday.
Froshbooks may be ordered at
j the Quad office any afternoon
from 4 to 6.
