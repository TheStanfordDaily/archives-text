# Language Profs Meet at Stanford
## 
### 
Dr. Frank G. Black, professor
of English at the University of
Oregon and president of the Phil
ological Association of the Pa
cific Coast, will be the principal
speaker when the group holds its
55th annual meeting at Stanford
University Friday and Saturday.
Dr. Black will discuss "The
Serpent in the Garden: Thoughts
upon the Tradition of the Novel"
before a group of 200 college
English and language professors
from six Western states ai the
annual banquet.
Professor Helmut R. Boeninger
of the Germanic and Romanic
Languages Department is chair
man of the local committee.
