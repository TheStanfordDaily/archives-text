# Design Contest Awards Offered
## 
### 
Forty-six cash awards are be
ing offered to undergraduates in
the James F. Lincoln Arc Welding
Foundation's annual mechanical
and structural design competition.
These awards are made in two
separate competitions, one for
mechanical designs and the other
for structural designs. The top
award is $12."50.
Rules are available from the
James F. Lincoln Arc Welding
Foundation, Cleveland 17, Ohio.
