# Indians Sixth Best In Harrier Meet
## 
### 
A disappointing sixth was the
best Stanford could do in the
PCC cross-country champion
ships at UCLA. The Indians
soared to 90 points as favored
Idaho won the meet with a low
of 25. Oregon was second and
UCLA third.
Doug Martin got home first for
Stanford, in 17th spot. Keith Wal
lace was 19th, Maury Graves
22nd, and Norman Lloyd 32nd.
Max Truex of USC was an easy
individual winner.
