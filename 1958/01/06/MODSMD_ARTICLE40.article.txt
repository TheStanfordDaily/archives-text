York Academy of Sciences.
They are Emmett Durrum,
M.D., associate clinical professor
of medical microbiology; Freder
ick A. Fuhrman, professor of
physiology; Robert F. Kallman,
research associate in radiology,
Stanford Medical School; and
Pierre Van Rysselberghe, lec
turer and research associate in
chemistry.
The Academy membership is
an honor extended to scholars
who have made outstanding con
