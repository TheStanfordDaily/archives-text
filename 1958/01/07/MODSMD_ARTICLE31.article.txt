# Douglas Placed On All-American List
## 
### 
Jackie Douglas, versatile Stan
ford athlete, was named as a
member of the first annual NC
AA All-American tennis squad.
Douglas, who plays quarterback
on the football team as well as
first man on the tennis squad,
was one of 14 college players se
lected from a group of some 2000
netters.
J. D. Morgan, NCTCA presi
dent and UCLA coach, spoke of
the 14 All - Americans as being
among the finest prospects in
amateur tennis.
Douglas was selected to the
team for play in his junior year
and still has one more year of eli
gibility left on the SU team.
