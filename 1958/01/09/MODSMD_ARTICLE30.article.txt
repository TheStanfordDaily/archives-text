# One-Act Tryouts Will End Tonight
## 
### 
Final tryouts for Winter One-
Acts will be held tonight al 7:30
in the Little Theatre of Memorial
Hall. All students are eligible.
Ham's Head will present the
three one-acts on the nights of
Feb. 1215.
The first play, a fantasy, was
written by Charles Barclay Gold
smith and will he directed by
Joan Hays. The second play con
cerns racial problems in lower
Manhattan, written by William
West and directed by Ed Crane.
The third one-act, written by
Timothy Welch and directed by
Dick Lamb, is an experimental
impressionistic play. Cast lists
for all three plays will be posted
tomorrow.
