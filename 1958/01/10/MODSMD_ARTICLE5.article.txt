# Basketball Tickets Available at BAC
## 
### 
Tickets for weekend basketball
games against Washington State
and Washington are still avail
able at the BAG ticket booth.
Hooters' tickets are free upon
presentation of a student body
card, and general admission tick
els are $1.50.
Tonight's game against WSC
and tomorrow's against the Hus
kies will both begin at 8, with the
freshman preliminaries set for 6.
The ticket booth will be open
from 8 a.m. until noon and from
1 to 5 p.m.
