# Ski Club Carnival Reservations End
## 
### 
Reservations will close today
for the Ski Club Carnival to be
held at Reno Jan. 17-19. They
may be made in front of the Cel
lar from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m.
Additional spaces for the Sat
urday night banquet have been
found for those who signed up.
Tickets will be available for only
those who pay the $2.50 today.
All those who have cars or
need rides and who have not al
ready signed up are asked to
leave a message at the reserva
tion table.
Final arrangements will be
made next Wednesday night at
Ski Club meeting, Cubberley
Auditorium, 7:30 p.m.
