# Registration Tops Winter Quarter '57
## 
### 
Registration topped the 8000
mark as Registrar Harvey Hall
reported 8053 students had signed
up for winter quarter as of Jan.
10.
This is an increase of 103 over
the total at the same time last
year.
Figures show 5975 men and
2078 women make up the present
figure. The Stanford ratio re
mains at 2.9 to 1.
A late registration fee of $5 will
be charged those who register
today. The fine will be $10 starl
ing tomorrow. Late registrants
may pick up their books in the
Registrar's Office, Room 111-A.
