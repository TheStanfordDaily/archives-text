# Annual 'Left-over' Sale Ends Today
## 
### 
Today, from 1 to 5 p.m., is the
last chance to pick up assorted
items at the annual "left-overs"
sale in FJncina Hall, sponsored by
the Associated Women Students.
Furniture, ski equipment, cloth
ing, lamps, suitcases and books,
left in women's dormitories last
spring quarter, will be on sale at
low prices.
Proceeds of the sale will go to
ward the AYVS Scholarship Fund,
which enables a woman exchange
student from abroad to attend
Stanford each year. Martha Has
tings is directing the event.
