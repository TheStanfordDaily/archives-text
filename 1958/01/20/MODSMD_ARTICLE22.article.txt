# Assistant Director Of SRI Resigns
## 
### 
John I. Yellott has resigned as
an assistant director of Stanford
Research Institute to devote full
time to his duties as executive
director of the Association for
Applied Solar Energy in Phoenix,
Ariz. Yellott joined the staff of
the Institute in 1956, and has
been based at SRl's Mountain
States Office in Phoenix.
The Association for Applied
Solar Energy is a non-profit pub
lic service institution originally
started with assistance from
Stanford Research Institute. Its
objectives include fostering re
search in solar energy and en
couraging the application of
solar energy.
