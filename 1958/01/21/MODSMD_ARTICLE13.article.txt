# '58 Class to Pick Honorary Officers
## 
### 
Honorary officers of the class
of '58 are now being selected by
class officers, according to Vice-
President Carol Salquist.
The officers, who will partici
pate in Class Day exercises, in
clude class orator, historian, poet
and writer of the class will.
Sign-up sheets are posted out
side the ASSU office and inter
views will be conducted this af
ternoon from 2 to 5 p.m. and to
morrow from 1 to 4 p.m. at the
Women's Clubhouse.
Contributions of the honorary
officers will be printed in the
Class Directory, and the officers'
names will be given special men
tion on the Graduation Program.
