# Frosh Continue Class Card Sale
## 
### 
Freshman class cards, good for
four years of class activities, are
being, sold in the freshman dorms
for $5, President Lee Gentry an
nounced.
Goal for the card sale drive is
$60fK), the total cost of 1200 cards.
Class cards offer cut rates on
class functions and parties, such
as the freshman party, Sopho
more Cotillion and the Junior and
Senior Proms, Gentry said.
They also finance exchanges,
the class newspaper and class
sponsored occasions such as ski
weekends.
