# Tryouts
## 
### 
AVVS GENERAL HOUSING
COMMITTEE
Tryouts will be held today, to
morrow and Thursday for the
General Housing Committee.
Sign-up sheets are in the ASSU
office.
RAM'S HEAD ASSISTANT
BUSIN ESS MANAGER
Next Tuesday tryouts will be
held for Ram's Head assistant
business manager for winter and
spring quarters, from 2 to 5 p.m.
in the Ram's Head office. Sign
up sheets are in the ASSU office
for eligible sophomores and jun
iors.
HONORARY SENIOR CLASS
OFFICERS'
Sign-up sheets are posted out
side the ASSU office for tryouts
for senior class poet, historian,
orator and writer of the class
will. Tryouts will be held today
and tomorrow from 1 to 5 p.m.
in the Women's Clubhouse, and
anyone unable to come at the
posted time should contact Carol
Salquist at Durand House.
WOMEN'S SWIM CLUB
Tryouts for members will be
held Thursday from 8 a.m. to 4
p.m. and also next Tuesday if
special requests are made. Sign
ups. with the requirements, are
posted in the Women's Gym. and
individual judging will be done
by members of Swim Club.
