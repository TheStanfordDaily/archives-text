# McAfee to Lead 'Y' Group Talk
## 
### 
YWCA luncheon discussion
group will have Malcolm McAfee,
Doctor of Sociology, to lead the
talk on "Bachelor Party," last
Sunday's Flick. The meeting is
Thursday at noon on the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
McAfee is presently director of
Westminster Foundation and was
formerly a professor at Davidson
College in North Carolina. Lunch
eon charge is 35 cents.
