# Committee Selects 63 Students For New Stanford in Stuttgart
## Spokesman Says 'Tough time’ Choosing 63 to Attend Stuttgart 
### 
Faculty members of the selection committee for the students who
will attend the first semester at "Stanford in Germany" announced
their decisions last night. The committee issued the following state
ment :
"The Committee charged with the task of having to select 03

students from a total of 197 ap
plicants really had a tough time.
"The selection was made by
taking three factors into consider
ation: (1) grades; (2) class dis
tribution, i.e. a fair share of soph
omores, juniors, and seniors; (3>
linguistic skill in German. The
final list contains the names of
33 men and 30 women.
"A number of alternates are
being notified by mail to keep
themselves ready, if one of the 63
will have to change his or her
plans. All those who could not be
listed now among the lucky 63
will be considered again for the
second term."

will have to change his or her
