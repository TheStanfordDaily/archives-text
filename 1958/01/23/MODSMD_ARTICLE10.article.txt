# Yugoslav Will Give 'East or West' Talk
## 
### 
Miodrag Mihaijlovich, Yugo
slav vice-consul in San Francisco,
will speak on "Yugoslavia—East
or West?" at the"I" Center, 539
Lasuen St., at 4:15 p.m. today.
In addition to his present post,
he has served in the State Secre
tariat of Foreign Affairs, in the
UN Secretariat and as vice-consul
in Australia.
After his address, he will eat
dinner at Iloble Hall. Anyone
wishing to dine with him should
contact Sharon Timmons at La*
gunita. Price of dinner for non
residents of Roblc is $1.25.
