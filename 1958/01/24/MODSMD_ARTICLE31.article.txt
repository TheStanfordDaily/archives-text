# Fehring to Guest On SU Spotlight
## 
### 
Dutch Fehring, Stanford varsity
baseball coach and assistant in
football last season, will appear
on "Stanford Spotlight" tomorrow
at 10:45 a.m. on KIBE.
Fehring will talk with host Bob
Murphy about the recent coaching
change here which saw Jack Cur
tice hired to replace the retiring
Chuck Taylor.
They'll also discuss the baseball
clinic scheduled for Feb. 8 here,
sponsored jointly by the Univer
sity and the San Francisco Giants.
