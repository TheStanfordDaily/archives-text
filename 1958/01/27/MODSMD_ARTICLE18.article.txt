# Ike's Brother Dies Following Illness
## 
### 
KANSAS CITY C/P)— -Arthur B.
Eisenhower, 71, older brother of
President Eisenhower, died at his
home late yesterday. He had been
ill with a heart ailment for some
time since his retirement from a
post with a Kansas City bank.
Arthur Eisenhower was vice
chairman of the board of the
Commerce Trust Company when
he retired in 1956. He later suf
fered a heart attack.
He formerly was a director of
Trans • World Airlines and the
Coleman Company of Wichita,
and a number of other corpora
tions.
