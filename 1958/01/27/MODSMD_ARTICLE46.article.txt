# Eleanor Roosevelt To Talk on Russia
## 
### 
Mrs. Eleanor Roosevelt comes
to Stanford Friday, Feb. 14, for a
lecture at Memorial Hall.
She will speak at 4:15 p.m. on
"Russia —the Country and People
as I Saw Them," an account of her
most recent visit abroad.
Tickets are on sale at Memorial
Ilall box office on a first come,
first served basis at 75 cents and
$1 each.
