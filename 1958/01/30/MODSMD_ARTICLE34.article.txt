# Pakistan Educator Visits Law School
## 
### 
The Stanford Law School is
"one of the foremost I have
found," campus visitor Dr. N. U.
A. Siddiqui said yesterday.
Dr. Siddiqui, dean of the law
faculty at Dacca University in
East Pakistan, is traveling in the
United States on a leader ex
change program sponsored by the
State Department.
During his visit Dr. Siddiqui re
newed acquaintances with Mu
hammed Ilyas and Muhammed
Morshed, administrative civil
judges from West and East Paki
stan. The two judges are study
ing international legal training
at the Law School.
