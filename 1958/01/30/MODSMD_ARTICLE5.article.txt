# Price for Quails Goes Up Friday
## 
### 
Prices of 1058 Quads ordered
after tomorrow will be $1 higher
than at present, and no names
will be on the cover, according to
the Quad office.
Froshbooks that have been or
dered but have not been picked up
by Friday will be resold, and no
refunds will lie made, according
to the Quad.
No Froshbooks have been re
served for rushing chairmen.
Cash or checks for Quads may
be mailed to Box 100 or brought
to the Quad office between 4 and
p.m.
