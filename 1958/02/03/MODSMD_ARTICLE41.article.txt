# Turk and 'Fatha' At Santa Clara U.
## 
### 
Turk Murphy, Earl "Fatha"
Mines and Barbara Dane will be
featured in a jazz concert, "Jazz
at the Ship," next Monay at the
University of Santa Clara.
The concert, sponsored by the
associated students of the univer
sity, will be held from 8 to 10:30
p.m. at "The Ship." Santa Clara
University auditorium. Tickets
will cost $1.50, according to Don
Doherty, publicity director.
