# Symphony Offers Student Soloist
## 
### 
For the first time, the Stanford
University Symphony Orchestra
will present one of its own mem
bers as soloist at its winter con
cert Friday evening at 8:30 and
Sunday afternoon at 4.
The soloist will be Gloria Vial,
the orchestra's concert master. She
will play Beethoven's "D Major
Violin Concerto," accompanied by
the 70-picce orchestra.
All tickets have been given out
for both free performances, which
will be under the direction of San
dor Salgo, professor of music.
The concert will open with Ver
di's overture, "La For/.a del Dos
tino," and close with Cesar
Franck's "Symphony in I) Minor."
