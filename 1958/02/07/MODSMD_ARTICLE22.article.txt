# Applications Due For Auditioners
## 
### 
Deadline for applications for
auditions for the 1958 San Fran
cisco Opera debut is Feb. 20, ac
cording to Kurt Herbert Adler,
general director.
The debut is a nationally
known contest sponsored by the
Merola Memorial Fund with the
assistance of the Opera Guilds of
California. Finals will take place
on Sunday evening, May 25, in
San Francisco and will be broad
cast over the Pacific network of
CBS radio.
Women applicants must be IX
years or older and men must be
over 21.
Applications are available at
the San Francisco Opera Debut
Auditions Office, Opera House,
San Francisco 2.
