# Matinee Set For Tomorrow
## 
### 
Original poems and short sto
ries by Stanford students and
teachers will be presented at the
Little Theater tomorrow at 4:15
p.m. as part of the Wednesday
Matinee program.
Tom Gunn and Wes Trimpi, in
structors in the English depart
ment, will read their own poetry.
Celia Howard, speech and drama
major, will read a short-short
story by Anita de Cosse, also a
student.
Another short-short by Charles
Courtney will also be read. Court
ney is a speech and drama major.
The program is directed by
Haig Bosmajian, a student of
rhetoric and public address.
