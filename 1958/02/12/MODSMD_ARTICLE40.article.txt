# Lagunita Dance
## 
### 
"He Loves Me, He Loves Me
Not" will be the theme of the Val
entine dance to be held Saturday
at Lagunita Court, from 8:30 p.m.
to 12:30 a.m. Music will be pro
vided by Paul Sivadery's band
and entertainment by men's and
women's singing groups.
Tickets cost $1.25 per couple,
according to Joanie Gustafson,
general chairman.
