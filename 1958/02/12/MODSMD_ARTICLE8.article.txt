# Stanford and Cal Sponsor Series
## 
### 
"The United States and the
World in Mid-Century" is the top
ic of a 10-week lecture series be
ginning next Tuesday, presented
jointly by Stanford and the Uni
versity of California.
Experts in international affairs

The program will be presented
in San Francisco and Berkeley.
Next week's lecture will bo given
at 8 p.m. Tuesday at 55 Laguna
