# Hispanic Satire
## 
### 
"Spirits on the Loose," an origi
nal play by Luis Osorio, visiting
lecturer in Hispanic American
Studies, will be presented tonight
at 8 in Cubberley Auditorium.
A satire on spiritualism, the
three-act play will be produced
and directed by its author. It will
be repeated in its Spanish version
two weeks from tonight at the
same time and place.
