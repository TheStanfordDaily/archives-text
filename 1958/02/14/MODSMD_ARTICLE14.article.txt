# 15-Unit Language Courses Offered
## 
### 
Two intensive language
courses, one in elementary Rus
sian and the other in elementary
Chinese, will be offered summer
quarter by the Department of
Asiatic and Slavic Studies.
Both courses will be given on a
15-unit basis and will have three
hours of classroom work per day,
Monday through Friday, for a
10-week period.
The courses will cover a year's
study.
Since both courses are limited
in enrollment, students interested
in taking either course are ad
vised to contact the Asiatic and
Slavic Studies Department as
soon as possible.
