# Dutch Dignitary To Visit Campus
## 
### 
Dr. J. H. van Roijen, Nether
lands ambassador to the United
States, will visit the Stanford
campus Monday.
The ambassador will be accom
panied by his wife and the Dutch
consul in San Francisco, VV. van
Tets. The group will meet with
Netherlands students at the In
ternational Center at 11 a.m..
visit the Hoover Institution from
11:45 a.m. to 12:15 p.m. and then
lunch with President and Mrs.
Sterling at the President's home.
Ambassador van Roijen will
make a major speech Wednesday
at a joint meeting of the San
Francisco and Netherlands Cham
bers of Commerce and the World
Trade Association.
