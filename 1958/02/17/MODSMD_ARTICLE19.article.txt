# Asian Schools Series Topic
## 
### 
The problems of a university in
southeastern Asia will be outlined
tomorrow night at 7:30 in Memo
rial Auditorium by a British pro
fessor who was once a Japanese
prisoner of war.
Dr. T. H. Silcock, chairman of
the economics department at the
University of Malaya. Singapore,
will base his Tuesday Series talk
on his experiences of founding
Asian educational institutions and
working with them.
Dr. Silcock has served as a UN
economics consultant on Asia and
the Far East and as fiscal adviser
to the government of Sarawak.
