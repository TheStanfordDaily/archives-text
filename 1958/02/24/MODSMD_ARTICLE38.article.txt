# Tryouts
##

OPERA AUDITIONS
Tryouts will be held today, To
morrow and Wednesday for po
sitions in the Stanford Chorale's
presentation of "The Unicorn,"
"The Gorgon" and"The Manti
core," an hour-length opera by
Gian Carlo Menotti.
There are openings on the Cho
rale for five baritones and basses.
Anyone interested is requested to
call Professor Schmidt at the
Knoll, DA 3-6731, or see him at
Dinkelspiel Auditorium today be
tween 11:30 a.m. and 1 p.m.
I'REREG
The Pre-Reg Committee will
hold tryouts at 7:30 p.m. today
in Room 12, Women's Clubhouse.
