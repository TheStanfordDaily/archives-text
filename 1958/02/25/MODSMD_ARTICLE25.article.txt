# Amadeus Quartet To Perform Here
##

will perform at Dinkelspiel Audi
torium March 2 at 4 p.m.
Tickets are on sale at the Din
kelspiel box office and the Penin
sula Box Office, Palo Alto, for $2.
The group features 'cellist Mar
tin Lovett, violinists Norbert
Brainin and Siegmund Nissel and
violist Peter Schidlof.
