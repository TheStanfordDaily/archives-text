# Chorus Tapes Will Be Played
## 
### 
A partial recording of the Feb.
14 and 16 Stanford Chorus con
certs will be played today at 12:15
in the rehearsal hall of Dinkelspiel
Auditorium.
Works to be played are the sec
ond chorus of the Berlioz "Re
quiem," contemporary selections
and chorales from "Gastoldi" and
as much of Glouck's "Allegro" as
time permits.
The program is sponsored joint
ly by the Music Department and
Sigma Alpha lota.
