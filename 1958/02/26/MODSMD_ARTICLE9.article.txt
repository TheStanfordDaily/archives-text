# Demo Caucus At Bowman
## 
### 
Robert Peckham, chairman of
the Democratic State Central
Committee, will speak to the
Democratic Caucus of the Politi
cal Union today at 4:15 in Bow
man Alumni House.
Peekham will discuss the party
organization, especially as it
functions in California.
A former Stanford undergrad
uate, Peckham was chairman of
a student political group in 1938.
He is now a Sunnyvale attorney
in addition to his work with the
San Jose Democratic Party.
