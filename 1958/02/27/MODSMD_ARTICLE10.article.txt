# Rushing Parties Slated Tonight
## 
### 
Prospective fraternity mem
bers will attend the third group
<»112 rush parties in the fraternities
tonight from 7:15 to 9:15.
Freshmen from Arroyo and Ce
!ro will visit group C: Alpha Del
'a Phi, Alpha Tau Omega, Delta
Chi, Theta Delta Chi, Phi Sigma
Kappa and Phi Kappa Psi..
Juniper and Medcra men go to
group D: Theta Chi, Phi Kappa
Sigma. Phi Gamma Delta, Theta

Nu.

will visit group A: Sigma Chi,

and Delta Upsilon.

will go to group B: Kappa Sigma,
Kappa Alpha, Alpha Kappa
Lambda, Beta Theta Pi, Chi Psi
and Delta Kappa Epsilon.
The final rushing will be on
Tuesday, March 4.
