# House Drawings
## 
### 
According to the Women's
Housing Committee, 1 his is the
last day for women to draw for
spring quarter residences. All
upperclass women are eligible to
draw, except the sponsors who
drew yesterday.
Drawing is to take place in the
Women's Clubhouse from 9 to 12
a.m. and from 1 to 4 p.m.
