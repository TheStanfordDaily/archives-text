# Former Chaplain To Speak Sunday
## 
### 
Visiting Stanford this weekend
will be Dr. D. Elton Trueblood,
former University chaplain, who
will preach next Sunday at the
11 o'clock service at Memorial
Church.
Dr. Trueblood, who served at
Stanford from 1936 to 1945, is
now professor of philosophy at
Earlham College, Richmond* Intl.
He held Ihe position of Chief of
Religious Information for the
Voice of America in Washington,
D.C., from 1954 to 1956.
A Quaker, Dr. Trueblood has
been active in relief work and in
ternational exchange activities
and is the founder and president
of the Yokefellows Movement, an
organization of Christian laymen.
