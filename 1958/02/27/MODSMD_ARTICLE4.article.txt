# Political Union Tryouts Today
## 
### 
The Stanford Political Union
has set today for tryouts for op
position speaker to Norman
Thomas, six times Socialist party
candidate for U.S. President.
Tryouts will be held this after
noon between 1 and 4 p.m. in the
Political Union office. Interested
parties will be expected to have
some information ready when in
terviewed.
Thomas will speak on a subject
relevant to Socialism at the March
Political Union meeting. Oppo
sition speakers will be allotted a
13-minute period after the main
speech for presentation of their
opposing views. A floor debate by
representatives of the caucuses
and by members of the audience
will follow.
