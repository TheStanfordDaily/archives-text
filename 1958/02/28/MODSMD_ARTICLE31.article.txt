# Lacrosse Plans To Be Discussed
## 
### 
Stanford may soon have com
petition in lacrosse if present
plans are consummated. Bill
Sterling, initiator of the move to
bring the popular Midwestern
sport here, has asked that any
one interested in the game come
to a meeting Sunday at 7 p.m. in
the Nitery. It was stressed that
knowledge of the game is not a
prerequisite.
