# Sophs Call Off Coffee Mixers
## 
### 
The Sophomore Excom has
voted to temporarily discontinue
the "coffee mixers" which have
been held in the Nitery each Sun
day evening this quarter follow
ing the flicks.
In the action taken last week
the committee said that the all
campus "coffee mixers" would
be resumed on their regular Sun
day evening 9 to midnight sched
ule at the beginning of spring
quarter.
