# Students Lead Music Tonight
## 
### 
Eight music students will share
directorship of the Stanford Sym
phony Orchestra tonight at 8:30
in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
First on the program. Elizabeth
Van Vorst will direct the overture
of Cluck's "Iphigenia in Aulis."
Michael Collins, a graduate stu
dent. will conduct the first move
ment from Mozart's piano con
certo in B flat major,
Director Sandor Salgo will next
turn over his baton to Jan Mer
win, Marjorie Trammel. Suzanne
Payne and Ben Pureell. who will
each conduct one of the four
movements of Schubert's "Unfin
ished" Symphony No. 5.
