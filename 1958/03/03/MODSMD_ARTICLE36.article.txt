# Golfers Triumph
## 
### 
Burl Finger's Stanford golfers
got off on a winning but -shaky
foot Friday as they edged the Pe
ninsula Golf and Country Club,
15-12, on Iho Stanford course.
Bill Seanor shot a five-over-par
76 to nip Peninsula's Bob Roos in
the feature match.
The Papooses also launched
their season with an 18-0 victory
over Menlo College.
