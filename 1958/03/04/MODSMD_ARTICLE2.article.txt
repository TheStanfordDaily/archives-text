# Referendum Polls Open Tomorrow
## 
### 
The polls will be open tomor
row from 8:30 a.m. until 3 p.m.
and Thursday from 8:30 a.m. to
2 p.m. at Engineering Corner for
student voting on the Judicial
Council referendum.
Text of the referendum is as
follows:

SI! bylaws, under paragraph I),
section 1, of article \ 111 of the
ASSU constitution.
Article 111, Section :>:

against liini, in writing" and in
advance.

amendments together they may
not vote in favor of one and

voted down by Legislature last
Thursday. A three fourths vote
would have been necessary to
pass the proposal.
