# Book Rebates May Be Claimed
## 
### 
Additional 5 per cent rebates on
last year's Bookstore purchases
are now about half distributed,
according to Mrs. Leon Bauchou,
Bookstore manager.
Students may pick up their re
bate from 9 a.m. to noon and 1 to
4 p.m. this week until noon Satur
day. Those unable to collect their
rebates this week may get them
later in the year.
The Bookstore is trying to get
all rebates distributed this week,
Mrs. Bauchou said.
