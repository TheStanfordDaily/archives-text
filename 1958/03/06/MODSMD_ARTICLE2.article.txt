# Two Depts. Revise Activities, Names
## 
### 
The activities and names of two
Stanford academic departments
have been revised, it was announc
ed yesterday.
The Department of Germanic
and Romanic Languages has been
renamed the Department of Mod
em European Languages.
The Department of Asiatic and
Slavic Studies is now the Depart
ment of Asian Languages, headed
by Shau Wing Chan, professor of
Chinese.
Instructors and staff for Slavic
languages and literatures have
been transferred to the jurisdic
tion of the Modern European Lan
guages department, which is head
ed by Friedrich W. Strothmann,
professor of German. Professor
Strothmann was head of the Ger
manic and Romanic Languages
depart ment.
