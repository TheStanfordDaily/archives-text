# Calabash Club Elects Officers
## 
### 
The Ma Ma Loa Calabash Club
in Honolulu, Hawaii, has issued a
charter for a chapter at Stanford
University. At the club's first
meeting, A 1 Harrington was
elected president; Dave Moxcrief,
vice-president; Woody Godbold,
treasurer; and Lissa Steere, sec
retary.
According to the charter, the
group's purpose is to help per
petuate the ancient Hawaiian cul
ture through research and social
activities.
The club is planning an annual
luau for the student body as one
of its principal projects. Part of
the proceeds from this event
will be donated to the Hawaiian
Resident 1 lome for Cerebral
Palsy Children.
