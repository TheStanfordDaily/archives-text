# Tryouts
## 
### 
SEQUOIA
Tryouts for Sequoia editorial
staff will bo held this afternoon
from 2 to 5:30 p.m. in the Se
quoia office. The staff will work
with the editorial board on page
layout, editing, criticism, art
work and other phases of pub
lishing the magazine. Interviews
will Also be held for next year's
business manager.
STANFORD PLAYERS
Tryouts for dancers interested
in either speaking or non-speak
ing roles in "A Midsummer
Night's Dream" will be held to
day from 4:30 to 5:30 p.m. in the
Little Theatre. Parts to be cast
include Titania, Queen of the
Fairies, and her retinue.
