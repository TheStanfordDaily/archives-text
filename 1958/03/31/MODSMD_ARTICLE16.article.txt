# A Phi O Opens Book Exchange
## 
### 
The Alpha Phi Omega book ex
change opens today to receive and
sell spring quarter textbooks.
The exchange is located in Tem
porary Building A, room 30, next
to the Western Civ Library. It
will be open through Wednesday,
from 9 a.m. to noon and 1 to 4
p.m.
Students are urged to bring
books they wish to sell as early as
possible, according to A Phi O
members.
A fee of 10 cents will be-charged
for each book put up for sale to
cover the cost of handling the
books and operating the exchange.
