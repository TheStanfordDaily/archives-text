# Tryouts
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Stanford Play
ers' final production of the sea
son, "A Midsummer Night's
Dream," will be held tonight and
tomorrow night from 7:3U to 1U
in the Little Theatre.
The production, which will be
directed by Robert Loper, will be
staged in the Frost Amphitheater
May 22, 23 and 24.
The play will need a large num
ber of actors, with speaking parts
for 13 men and five women, and
many more non-speaking parts.
