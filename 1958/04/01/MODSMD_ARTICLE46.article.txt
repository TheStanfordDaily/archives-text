# Tryouts
## 
### 
PRE-REG COMMITTEE
Tryouts for the Foreign Stu
dent Pre-Registration Committee
for autumn quarter will be held
tonight, tomorrow and Thursday
this week and next week. The
sign-up list for interview times is
posted by the ASSU office.
KZSU
Tryouts for KZSU announcers
will be held today and tomorrow
from 2:15 to 4:30 in the basement
of Memorial Auditorium. No ex
perience is needed.
STANFORD CHOIR
Tryouts for basses will be held
at 3:30 today in the choir room,
Memorial Church.
STANFORD PLAYERS
Tryouts for "A Midsummer
Night's Dream" will be held to
day in the Little Theatre from
7:30 to 10 p.m.
