# Snack Bar Ready
## 
### 
Wilbur Hall announced yester
day the opening of the Wilbur
"Wreck Room" in the basement
of the hall. The new snack bar
will sell fruit juice, sandwiches,
cigarettes and other items.
The Wreck Room will be open
seven days a week, from 3:30 to
4:30 p.m. and from 9:30 to 11
p.m.
