# Press to Publish Book on Slavery
## 
### 
"Marie, or "Slavery in the
United States," a document on
the status of the Negro in ante
bellum America by Gustavo de
Beaumont, will be published by
Stanford University Press in Sep
tember. The first English trans
lation of the work will be by Bar
bara Chapman.
The necessity of uncovering
and understanding the back
ground of the racial problem, its
economic, psychological and his
torical roots, is emphasized in
the book. Beaumont was the
traveling companion of Alexis de
Tocqueville.
