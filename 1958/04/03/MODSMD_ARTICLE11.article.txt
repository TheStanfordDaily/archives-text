# Center Postpones Work Day Plans
##

California sunshine, the Work
Day at the International Center
for Saturday has been canceled.
Students who signed lip to
work in the garden will be forced
to postpone their endeavor for a
time, according to Mrs. Inez Rich
ardson of the"I" Center.
