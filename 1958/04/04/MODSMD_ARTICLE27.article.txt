# Frosh Spikers Battle Weather
## 
### 
Weather odds are against it,
but Floyd Strain's frosh track
men will still try to get in the
season's second meet tomorrow
at 10 a.m. on Angell Field against
Hartnell, Menlo and Fast Contra
Costa JC's.
Smarting from the eligibility
losses of Chris Knott and Gary
Fstabrook, the frosh will bank
on distance man Keith Wallace,
440-880 runner Jeff Beaubier and
sprinter, broad-jumper and jave
linist Brooks Townsend.
Hurdler Bob Martin and weight
man Nappy McNaughton should
also help ihe frosh if there's a
meet.
