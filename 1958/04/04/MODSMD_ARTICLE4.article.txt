# Row to Hold Open Houses
## 
### 
Three more evenings of frater
nity open houses will be held be
fore the beginning of formal rush
ing this Sunday at 9 p.m.
They are scheduled for tonight
and tomorrow night from 7:15 to
9:15 and Sunday night from .7:15
to 9, according to IFC President
Gordy Maderas.
Formal rushing begins at 9
p.m. Sunday and will continue
until 5:30 Friday night, April 11.
Freshmen may be pledged any
time during this period.
While formal rusihng is in
progress fraternity members may
enter Wilbur only to pick up bids,
or to use the lobby telephone be
tween the hours of 11:30 a.m. and
1:30 p.m. and from 5 to 7:30 p.m.
The no-contact rule will apply
between 2:30 a.m. and 5:30 p.m.
Friday, April 11. After the close
of formal rushing at 5:30 p.m.
next Friday, pledges will sign
pledge cards in the fraternity
houses.
