# Workshop to Present 'Godot'
## 
### 
Joseph and Anthony Miksak,
father and son, will appear in the
Aetor's Workshop production of
"Waiting for Godot" being pre
sented tonight at 8:30 in Memo
rial Auditorium.
Joseph Miksak will portray the
tyrannical master Pozzo in Sam
uel Beckett's burlesque tragicom
edy. Anthony will be seen as the
boy who appears as an ironic
heavenly messenger.
Tickets are on sale at Memorial
Auditorium box office between
12:30 and 4:30 for the special stu
dent rate of $1.25. The play is
being presented under the aus
pices of the Committee on Public
Exercises.
