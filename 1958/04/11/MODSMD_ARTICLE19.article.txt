# Pledges Sign Cards Tonight
## 
### 
New fraternity members will
sign pledge cards today at 5:30
p.m. as the week of formal rush
ing comes to a close. Initiation
ceremones for new pledges will
be held in the houses and eating
clubs beginning tonight, accord
ing to IFC President Gordy Ma
deras.
Prospective pledges have been
attending invitational rushing
parties during the past week fol
lowing four nights of open houses
last quarter.
