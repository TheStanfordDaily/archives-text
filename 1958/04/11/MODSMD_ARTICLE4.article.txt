# IIR Benefit Concert Set
## 
### 
Irmgard Seefried, Viennese so
prano renowned for her perform
ances in opera, recitals, and ora
torios, will appear at Memorial
Auditorium April 19 at 8:30 p.m.
in a benefit concert sponsored by
the Institute of International Re
lations.
She has been a leading singer
with the Vienna State Opera ever
since she made her debut there
in 1913 as Eva in "Die Meister
singer." She is particularly cele
brated for her roles in the Mozart
operas and has appeared in every
Salzburg festival since 1946.
Proceeds for the concert will
assist the lIR in providing ex
change scholarships at Stanford
for students from the Free Uni
versity of Berlin and Kcio Univer
sity in Japan. This concert is the
sole way in which money is
raised for these scholarships.
Tickets are priced at $1.50, $2,
$2.50 and $3, and may be ob
tained at the ASSU Concert Se
ries office in llie Nitery oi by call
ing DA 3-3870.
