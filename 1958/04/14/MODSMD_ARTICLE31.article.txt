# Tryouts
## 
### 
FOREIGN STUDENT
PRE REG COM
Interviews will continue this
week to select members of next
year's Foreign Student Pre-Reg
istration Committee.
The committee returns a week
before the beginning of autumn
quarter to meet the entering for
eign students and assist them in
orientation.
Tryouts will be held at the In
ternational Center Tuesday, Wed
nesday and Thursday nights from
7 to 10. A sign-up sheet for inter
view times is posted by the ASSU
office.
FUN STAN COM
Lists are posted at the ASSU
office for tryouts for Fundamen
tal Standard and Honor Code
Commission for the coming year.
Students interested should sign
the lists Monday through Friday.
