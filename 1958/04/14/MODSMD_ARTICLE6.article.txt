# Harrison Sets U.S. Swim Mark
## 
### 
George Harrison, who just
set a national collegiate frosh
record in the 200-yard butterfly
Tuesday, came through again
Saturday to set another nation
al frosh mark. His 56.6 time
in the 100-yard butterfly bet
tered the old mark of 58.5.
Harrison is also the recent
winner of the National AAU
400-yard individual medley title.
His performance led Tom
Ilaynie's frosh swimmers to a
55-48-48 victory over highly
rated Fresno and Los Altos
high schools'. Harrison also
swam a leg on the winning 200-
yard medley relay team.
