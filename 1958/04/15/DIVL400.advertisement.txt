Do you like to
travel?
If you arc single and between 1 91/2
and 26, there's a splendid opportu
nity waiting for you. Along with its
current expansion, American Air
lines needs many more attractive
stewardesses. You must be 5 2 to
5' 8" in height, 1 30 lbs. or less in
proportion to height, with 20 50
eyesight or better without corrective
lenses. Those accepted have FREE
TRAININC, excellent salaries, lib
eral expense accounts and the privi
lege of free travel.
Even though you may not reach the
minimum age requirement, if you
arc interested in this as a career,
please feel free to discuss this with
us.
