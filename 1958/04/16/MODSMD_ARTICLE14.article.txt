# Theta Xi to Hold Apache Dance
## 
### 
The annual Theta Xi all-campus
apache dance will be held Satur
day, 9 p.m. to 1 a.m., at the Theta
Xi house. There will be dancing
in an exotic French atmosphere,
and refreshments will be served
in the cellar cabaret.
Guests will enter the cabaret
through the basement and climb
into the house through a "sewer."
Attire will be French and admis
sion is free.
