# Tryouts
## 
### 
Fundamental S I a 11 d a r d and
Honor Code Commission: Stu
dents interested in trying out for

the coming year should sign lists
posted in the ASSU office this
week.
Political Union: Program and
publicity committees are holding
tryouts for new members today
from 9 to 10 a.m. and from 2 to
3 p.m. in the Political Union of
fice in the Student Union.
Group Activity Units: Fresh
men and sophomores who wish
to receive group activity units
this quarter for work on "Call
Me Madam" should appear in
Mem Aud this evening at 7:30.
See Mr. Shrader.
