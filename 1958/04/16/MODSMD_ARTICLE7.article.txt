# Contest Offers Second Prize
## 
### 
A second prize of $35 will be
awarded the person or living
group collecting the second big
gest pile of Marlboro or Parlia
ment packs for the Con Home
Week contest.
First-place winner in the con
test will receive $75, which will
be donated to the Convalescent
Homo in his name according to
the way he wishes. The Marlboro
contest will close May 3, the final
flay of Con Home Week.
