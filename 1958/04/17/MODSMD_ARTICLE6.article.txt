# Come Weeth Me, Babee...
## 
### 
Un homme prernl une amie clans ses bras. Et puis, on dansc.
That's French for what one does at the Theta Xi all-campus Apache
Dance, Saturday night from 9 to 1 at the Theta Xi house, which

cellar cabaret). Guests will enter the house through a "sewer" from
the cabaret in the basement. Attire is French, admission free.
