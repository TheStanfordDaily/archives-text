# Cashier's Office Has New Hours
## 
### 
From now till the end of the
quarter, the Cashier's Office in
the Encina lobby will be open
from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.
The office is open from 8 a.m.
to 5 p.m. only during the first two
weeks of each quarter to assist
students in the paying of fees,
said Otis H. Sather, cashier.
