# Poets to Discuss S.F. Renaissance
## 
### 
Two San Francisco poets,
Brother Antonius and Robert
Duncan, will discuss "The San
Francisco Renaissance: Fact or
Fraud" at 4 p.m. tomorrow in the
lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse.
Mel Tappan of the English De
partment and Gangadhar Gadgil,
a graduate student from India
who recently published a book of
poems, will join the poets in an
informal panel discussion.
All students and interested per
sons are invited to take an active
part in the discussion sponsored
by Sequoia magazine.
