# S.F. Poets Speak On 'Renaissance'
## 
### 
Brother Antonius and Robert
Duncan, two San Francisco poets,
will discuss "The San Francisco
Renaissance: Fact or Fraud" at 4
p.m. today in the lower lounge of
the Women's Clubhouse.
The discussion is sponsored by
Sequoia magazine and all inter
ested persons are invited to at
tend.
