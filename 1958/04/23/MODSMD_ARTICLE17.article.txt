# Art Show Opens In Gallery Today
## 
### 
The annual student exhibition
given by the Department of Art
and Architecture opens today in
the Art Gallery and will continue
through May 11.
The show, which is under the
direction of Frank Laury, faculty
adviser, and John La Ply n to, asso
ciate director of the Art Gallery,
includes the work of both under
graduate and graduate students.
On display are charcoal and pen
and ink drawings, oil paintings,
water colors, and Ihree- dimen
sional design projects, such as
jewelry, furniture and sculpture.
The architecture projects vary
from small-scale house designs to
hotel designs and a redevelop
ment of a section of El Camino.
During the last week of the show
some of the student work will be
on sale.
