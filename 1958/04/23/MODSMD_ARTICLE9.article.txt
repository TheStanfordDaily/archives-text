# Frost to Read. Discuss Works
## 
### 
Robert Frost, well-known Amer
ican poet, will read and discuss
selected portions of his works on
Monday at 4:13 p.m. in Memorial
Auditorium.
Frost, 84, is a native of San
Francisco and a four-time winner
of the Pulitzer prize for poetry.
He has spent most of his life in
New England and has taught and
lectured at most Ivy League and
many Middle Western colleges
and universities.
Tickets are on sale at Memo
rial Auditorium box office, priced
at $1 to the public and 73 cents to
students.
