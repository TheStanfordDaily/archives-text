# Credit Evaluation Deadline Friday
## 
### 
The deadline for petitions for
evaluation of transfer credits is
Friday, according to the Regis
trar's Office. Persons who do not
file petitions by the deadline will
not have their transfer credits
evaluated before next quarter.
Forms are available at the in
formation desk of the Registrar's
Office, Room 111-A. and must be
returned there before 5 p.m. to
morrow.
