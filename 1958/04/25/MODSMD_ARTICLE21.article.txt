# Parade to Protest Continued A-Tests
## 
### 
"Stop Nuclear Tests" will bp
the theme of a parade tomorrow
in Palo Alto. Two Stanford pro
fessors, Dr. Albert Baez and Mul
ford Sibley, will be among the
four speakers.
The parade will leave Univer
sity and Cowper streets at 1:30
p.m. and will end at Cogswell
Park at the corner of Lytton and
Ramona.
The protest is sponsored by the
Peninsula Committee for the
Abolition of Nuclear Tests.
