# Vaccination Slips Due Monday
## 
### 
Students new to the University
this quarter must turn in vacci
nation slips to the credentials
window in the Admissions Office,
room 112-A, Inner Quad, by Mon
day, according to the Committee
on Public Health.
A late filing fee of $2 will he
charged if vaccination slips are
turned in after the deadline. If no
slip Is received by May 5, then
the student involved will have his
registration subject to cancella
tion by the committee.
