# Student Works Will Be Heard
## 
### 
More than 100 student musi
cians will perform the works of
32 young composers in a college
music symposium to be held at
Dinkelspiel Auditorium on Friday
and Saturday.
Composers and musicians from
eight colleges and universities
will participate in the annual
event. Schools represented will
include Mills, Brigham Young,
University of California, UCLA,
USC and Redlands.
The first program will be Fri
day at 3:30 p.m. and will include
works by Alexander Horak and
Eleanor Ogden of Stanford. The
second program will be that eve
ning at 8:30.
Three programs will be given
on Saturday, at 10:15 a.m., 3 p.m.
and 8:30 p.m. On the concluding
program are Natalie Jenne and
Robert MacKinnon of Stanford.
