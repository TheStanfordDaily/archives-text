# 'Mizzie' Today
## 
### 
Arthur Schnitzler's "Countess
Mi/./Ae," a character comedy set
in early 20th century Vienna, will
be presented at today's Wednes
day Matinee performance at dtlj
p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium.
"Countess Mizzle" will be di
rected by Catharine Means. The
cast includes Jim Freidburger,
Scudder Brown, Joan Hays and
Doris Viola. The performance is
open to the public without charge.
