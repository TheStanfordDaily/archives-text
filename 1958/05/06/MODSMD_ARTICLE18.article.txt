# Senior Awarded Guest Editorship
## 
### 
Senior Joyce Denebrink has
been chosen one of 20 guest edi
tors for Mademoiselle magazine's
annual August college issue.
She was chosen from among
750 undergraduate members of
the magazine's national college
board at colleges and universities
across the country.
Guest editors receive regular
salaries and are selected on the
basis of assignments on campus
fashion and other activities com
pleted for Mademoiselle during
the year.
