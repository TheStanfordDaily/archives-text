# Brinton to Tell Of Progress
## 
### 
Tonight's Tuesday Evening Se
ries audience will hear Crane
Brinton, Harvard historian and
political philosopher, lecture on
"The Doctrine of Progress To
day" at 7:30 tonight in Memorial
Auditorium.
Dr. Brinton, a former Rhodes
scholar, has taught at his alma
mater since 1923, when he re
ceiver! the doctorate degree.
A co-founder of the Harvard tu
torial system, the house plan and
the Society of Fellows, Dr. Brin
ton is a member of the French
Legion of Honor, American Philo
sophical Society, American His
torical Association and the Royal
Historical Society.
He has been senior fellow of
the Society of Fellows since 1942
when he was appointed chairman.
During World War 11, Brinton
was special assistant to the Of
fice of Strategic Services, Euro
pean Theater, in London and
Paris.
