# Area YD's Plan Membership Drive
## 
### 
The Stanford Area Young Dem
ocrats will hold a membership
drive meeting tomorrow at the
recreation room of the Stanford
Elementary School at 7:30 p.m.
There will be a speaker, and a
resolution concerning a nuclear
lest ban will be presented for the
club's consideration.
Paul Butler, chairman of the
Democratic National Committee,
is coming to Stanford Monday
and plans for his visit will be dis
cussed.
