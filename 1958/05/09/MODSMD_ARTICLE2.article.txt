# Dean to Entertain Students, Faculty
## 
### 
Sunday from 3 to 5 p.m. Dean
and Mrs. Philip Rhinelander will
hold an open house for all inter
ested students.
This event will be the first in a
planned bi-monthly open house
series sponsored by the Student-
Faculty Commission.
Sunday's open house will be at
610 Gerona. off Lasuen Street.
"All students who would like to
spend an enjoyable time and be
come acquainted with faculty
members and their famiiles are
more than welcome to attend all
of the open houses," commented
Jane Fetter, chairman of the com
mission.
