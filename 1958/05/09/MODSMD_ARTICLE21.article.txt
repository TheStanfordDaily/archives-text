# Fraternity Fined By IFC Decision
## 
### 
Kappa Sigma fraternity has
been assessed 400 Corporation
Yard hours by the IFC Judicial
Council for having beer in the
fraternity house during rushing
week.
These hours must be worked
off by May 31, according to IFC
President Neil Scheidell.
The beer, discovered by campus
police, was left over from a rush
ing party.
Theta Xi fraternity received
the same punishment for having
alcoholic beverages on campus in
1954.
