# 'I' Club Dance
## 
### 
The 50th anniversary of the In
ternational Club of the lIR will
be celebrated tonight by a dance
at Chez Yvonne from 9 to 2.
Special entertainment includ
ing an International Gala Dance
will be provided, and a band will
play for dancing.
Tickets may be obtained at the
"I" Club Coffee Hour at Bowman
Alumni House today at 4:15 p.m.
or at the door. Admission is 50
cents a person.
