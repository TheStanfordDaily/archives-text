# Kingston Chaplain To Speak Sunday
## 
### 
The Rev. Marshall Laverty,
chaplain at Queen's University,
Kingston, Ont., will preach the
sermon Sunday at Memorial
Church. His topic will be "What
About Your Roots?"
Laverty, who was a chaplain
in the Canadian army during the
war, is a past president of the
National Association of Univer
sity Chaplains.
