# Shortstop, CPA Appointed to Staff
## 
### 
Two new staff appointees have
been announced by President
Wallace Sterling.
Warren F. Goodrich, an NCAA
All-American shortstop while at
Stanford, will serve as assistant
to General Secretary David S. Ja
cobson in the University's fund
raising program.
He was graduated from Stan
ford in 1954, played professional
baseball for two seasons, then
served as information officer for
the Strategic Air Command.
Kenneth D. Creighton has been
appointed assistant to University
Controller Duncan I. McFadden.
He is a former San Francisco cer
tified public accountant and a
graduate of the University of
British Columbia.
