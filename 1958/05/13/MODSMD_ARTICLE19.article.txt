# Latin Dance Party
## 
### 
The dance section of the Fac
ulty Women's Club will hold a
Latin American party for faculty
members and their guests from
8:30 p.m. to midnight Friday in
the Women's Clubhouse. Alba
Osorio and Luis Enrique Osorio
will entertain with several South
American dances. Supper will be
served at 11 p.m. Charge is $1
per couple.
