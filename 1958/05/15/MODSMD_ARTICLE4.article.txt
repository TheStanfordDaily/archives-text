# Co-Rec Archery Tournament Sat.
## 
### 
An archery tournament for all
men and women in any way con
nected with the University will be
held this Saturday on the Wom
en's Gym archery field, WRA has
announced.
The tournament, from 1 to 3
p.m., will feature an American
round for men, beginning at 1:15,
and a Columbia round for wom
en, which will start at 1:30 p.m.
Persons needing University
equipment may come to the field
after 12:30 p.m. on Saturday.
For further information con
tact Mrs. Margaret Barr at the
Women's Gym or Carol Dunbar
in Roble.
