# SU Symphony Travels to Cal
## 
### 
The Stanford Symphony will
travel to Cal next Tuesday to pre
sent a program which will in
clude the world premiere of a
work by UC professor Charles
Cushing.
The new piece, "Poem for Bari
tone and Orchestra," will feature
Stanley Noonan as soloist.
Other numbers will include
works by Rosen, Haydn, Ravel
and Gabrieli.
