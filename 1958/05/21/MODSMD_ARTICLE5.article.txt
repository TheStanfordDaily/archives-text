# Reviewers to Host Literary Critic
## 
### 
Kenneth Burke, literary critic,
will discuss "Tragedy" tomorrow
at 4:15 p.m. in the Jones Room of
the Library.
The English Review Club is
sponsoring Burke's visit.
The critic teaches the theory of
language at Bennington College,
Vt., and holds a fellowship this
year with the Center for Ad
vanced Study in the Behavioral
Sciences.
