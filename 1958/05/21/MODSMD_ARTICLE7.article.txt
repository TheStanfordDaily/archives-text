# Russian Movie To Play Today
## 
### 
The Russian classic "Alexander
Nevsky" will bo shown at 7:30 to
night in Cubberley Auditorium.
Admission will be free.
'J'he movie, produced in 1938 by
Sergei Eisenstein, is based on an
invasion of Russia by the knights
of the Teutonic Order in the 13th
century. The sound track is in
Russian with English subtitles.
The two hour showing is spon
sored by Sequoia magazine and
the Department of Asiatic and
Slavic Studies.
