# Shakespeare Rained Out
## 
### 
Weekend performances of
Shakespeare's "A Midsummer
Night's Dream" have been post
poned because of rain until next
Thursday, Friday and Saturday
nights, according to Dr. Nor
man Philbrick, executive head
of the department of speech
and drama.
Tickets for this week's per
formances are good for the
same nights next week, or
money will be refunded.
Rain insurance, up to $2000,
will be collected by the Speech
and Drama Department be
cause of forced cancellation of
the play. The policy states
that a minimum of .05 inch of
rain had to fall between 11 a.m.
and 9 p.m. on the day of a per
formance before the insurance
would become effective.
