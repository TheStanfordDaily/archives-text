# Review Tuesday To Honor Cadets
## 
### 
Four outstanding cadets in
Stanford University's ROTC
training program will be honored
at the annual Presidential Review
in the football stadium Tuesday,
May 27, at 3:15 p.m. The cadets
and their units are as follows:
Army, James R. Simpson;
Navy, Paul M. Reitler; Marine
Corps, John F. MoNelly; Air
Force, Donald H. Gage. President
Sterling will inspect the massed
cadet officers and men and then
present Ihe awards.
