# Blanks Available For Rotary Grant
## 
### 
Information and applications
for Rotary Foundation Fellow
ships for graduate study abroad
are now available at the Finan
cial Awards Office, Room 111-F,
Inner Quad.
The Rotary fellowship includes
all transportation, education and
living expenses for the 1959-60
academic year in a university of
the student's choice throughout
the world.
