# Shakespeare's Runaways
## 
### 
Nancy Cole as Hermia and James Freiburger as Eysander portray
a pair of runaway lovers in the Stanford Players'' production of
Shakespeare's "A Midsummer Night's Dream." The fantasy marks
the Players' 21st season and is the first outdoor presentation ever
attempted here. Rained out last weekend, the play will be per
formed in Frost tomorrow through Saturday at 8:30 p.m. Twenty
one dancing wood nymphs will augment the 41-student cast.
