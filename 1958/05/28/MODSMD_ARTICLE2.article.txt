# Top Student Wins Watch
## 
### 
Frederick S. Hillier has been
selected as one of Ihe top men in
the 1958 graduating class in Stan
ford's School of Engineering.
Hillier, with a 3.9 grade point
average, will receive this year's
Hamilton Award, an electric wrist
watch with a "platinum-energized
motor."
The prize is given annually by
the Hamilton Watch Company of
Lancaster, Pa. It goes to the
school's senior student "who most
successfully has combined profi
ciency in his major field with
achievements in social sciences or
humanities."
