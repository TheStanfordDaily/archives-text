# Pinkham Selected
## 
### 
Pat Pinkham, Political Union
president, has been selected as
Chairman for the bi-partisan
school of politics (political train
ing program) which will bo held
in the Stanford area next Janu
ary.
The program is intended to pro
vide. training in the more practi
cal aspects of politics for interest
ed college students.
The committee at present has
six members; it will he expanded
to include 15. Those interested in
taking an active part in the pro
gram are urged to contact the
Political Union, the YR's or the
YD's.
