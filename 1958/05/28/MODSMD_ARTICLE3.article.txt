# 'Dream' to Open With Fair Skies
## 
### 
Fair skies and warm weather
are predicted for the next week,
in honor of tomorrow night's
opening performance of "Mid
summer Night's Dream" at Frost
Amphitheater.
Shakespeare's comedy, which
will be portrayed by Ihe Stanford
Players, will begin at 8:30 each
night through Saturday.
Cast'in starring roles are Betsy
Flint as Helena; James Freibur
ger as Lysander; Nancy Cole,
Hermia; and Dick Lamb, Deme
trius.
