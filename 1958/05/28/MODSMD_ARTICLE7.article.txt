# Sterling Reviews ROTC Cadets
## 
### 
The massed units of Stanford's
Army, Navy and Air Force ROTC
cadets paraded yesterday before
President J. E. Wallace Sterling
in the third annual President's
Review in Stanford Stadium.
Award ceremonies included the
presentation of outstanding cadet
awards to James R. Simpson.
Army; Paul M. Roitler, Navy;
John F. MeNolly, Marine Corps;
and Donald 11. Cage, Air Force,
by President Sterling.
