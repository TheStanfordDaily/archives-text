# Hostel Com Lists Tryouts
## 
### 
Committeeman tryouts for the
revised and expanded hostel pro
gram will he held today from
12:45 to 3:45 p.m. and Monday
from 1 to 2 p.m. and 4 to 5 p.m.
in the ASSIJ Exeom room.
The hostel program will he
doubled next year to include more
than 2.1 outings and discussions
after having the most successful
year of its short history in 19,17-
58 under chairman Dick Coffing.
Coffing will return next year to
direct the expanded plan and
committee members will arrange
discussion sites, work with pro
fessors who lead the outings and
do extensive work on the new ar
rangement for private group hos
tels.
