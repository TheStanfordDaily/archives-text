# Fall Pre-Reg To Continue
## 
### 
Autumn quarter pre - registra
tion will continue today as fresh
men, sophomores and lower divi
sion students begin signing class
lists in the Registrar's Office,
Room 111-A, Inner Quad.
Signing of class lists will con
tinue tomorrow between 8 a.m.
and noon and from 1 to 5 p.m.,
and Saturday from 8 a.m. to noon.
Monday from 8 a.m. to noon
will be the last chance to sign
class lists, and all IBM study
cards must be filed with the
check-out clerk, Room 111-A, by
noon Monday.
The pre-registration procedure
for all freshman, sophomore and
lower division students is as fol
lows:
• Consult adviser during, his
special pre-registration period of
fice hours and obtain an IBM
study card from him.
• Sign class lists in the Regis
trar's office at any time after the
time stamped on study card.
• Turn in study card to check
out clerk in the Registrar's Office.
According to the Registrar's
Office, a limited number of jun
iors will be permitted to enroll in
one Senior Colloquium in order
to gradually expand the program
to accommodate the Senior Collo
qui;# requirement for all A.B.
candidates of the class of '60 or
later.
