# Memorial Day Service Planned
## 
### 
Memorial Day will he observed
tomorrow at Memorial Church
with a brief service of remem
brance at 11 a.m. which will in
clude the laying of a wreath at
the chancel and color ceremonies
by ROTC units.
The National Anthem and pray
ers led by Chaplain Minto will
complete the service. Students
are invited to attend.
Just before the observance in
the church the flag in front of the
main library will be lowered to
half mast as a firing squad fires
a salute.
