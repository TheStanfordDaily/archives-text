# Crew Out of IRA
## 
### 
The Stanford Crew Association
has voted not to enter shells in
the IRA Regatta in Syracuse this
year. The crew will use funds
previously earmarked for the trip
to improve boathouse facilities.
Officers elected for the 1958-59
year are captain Chuck Brothers,
co-captain Rolf Peterson, secre
tary Scott Moore and treasurer
Bud McKnight.
