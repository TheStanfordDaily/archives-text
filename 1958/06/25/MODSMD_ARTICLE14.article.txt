# First Candlelight Service Tonight
## 
### 
Students of all denominations
are cordially invited to attend the
first candlelight service of the
quarter at Memorial Church. The
service will begin at 9:30 this eve
ning, and will be officiated by the
Rev. R. M. Minto, Stanford chap
lain.
These services will be held all
quarter on Wednesday evenings
under the direction of Chaplain
Minto and Assistant Chaplain
Thomas Derr.
