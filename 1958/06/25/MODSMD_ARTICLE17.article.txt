# Bowling Alley Opens on Campus
## 
### 
A bowling alley has opened on
the Stanford campus, it was an
nounced yesterday by Robert G.
Young, operations manager for
the Department of Athletics. The
two-lane alley is adjacent to the
soccer field and the ROTC area.
Bowling will cost 35 cents a
line, and the alley will bo open
daily from 4:30 to 6 p.m.
