# Church to Host Organ Recital
## 
### 
The first organ recital of the
summer quarter will be given by
Herbert Nanney, University or
ganist, on Sunday at 4 p.m. in
Stanford Memorial Church.
Mr. Nanney has planned a var
ied program ranging from the
17th to the 20th century. Num
bers of special interest will be the
Toccata, Adagio and Fugue in C
major by Bach; Hindemith's
First Sonata; and "Piece Hero
i'que" by Franck. Compositions
by Pachelbel, Purcell, Buxtehude,
Fischer and Karg-Elert will also
be performed.
