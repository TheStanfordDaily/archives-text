# Correction In Library Hours
## 
### 
A correction in the hours for the
Main Library has been announced
by R. C. Swank, University libra
ries director.
The main library will be open
until 5 p.m. on Fridays, rather
than until 6 p.m.. as previously
reported.
The library will close at 5:30
p.m. on Thursday, July 3, and will
remain closed July 4, 5, and 6.
Regular hours will resume on
Monday, July 7.
