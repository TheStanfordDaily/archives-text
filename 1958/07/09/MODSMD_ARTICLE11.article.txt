# Sibley Leads Hostel July 12
## 
### 
The first student hostel of the
summer quarter will be held this
Saturday, July 12, at Portola
State Park.
"The Formation of Values" will
be the topic of discussion and will
be led by Mulford Q. Sibley, visit
ing professor of political theory.
Dr. Sibley, a pacifist, is famous
for his work "Conscription of Con
science." He has been visiting at
Stanford during this past year
from University of Minnesota.
Sign-ups are being taken in the
ASSU office and are limited to
20 students.
