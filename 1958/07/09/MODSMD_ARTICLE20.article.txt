# Save Money At the Movies!
## 
### 
Movie discount cards, good at all
Peninsula Fox theaters, are on sale
for one dollar in the ASSU office,
located above the Cellar.
Just present your card at the
box office of any of the following
theaters: Stanford and Varsity in
Palo Alto, Park in Menlo Park,
Fox in Redwood City, Carlos in
San Carlos, San Mateo in San
Mateo, Peninsula in Burlingame,
and the Fox in San Francisco.
Cards are valid until September
24. "You save thirty-five cents per
show, so buy a card today. It's a
dollar well spent," urges Ray
York, student financial manager.
