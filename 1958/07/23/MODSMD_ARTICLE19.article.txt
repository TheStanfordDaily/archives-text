# Tuesday Musicale Features Soprano
## 
### 
Dawn McClelland, soprano, and
Suzanne Payne, pianist, will give
a joint recital at the Tuesday
Noon Musicale, July 29, at 12:15
p.m. in the Rehearsal Hall of
Dinkelspiel Auditorium. Miss Mc-
Clelland is a member of the West
Bay Opera Association, and Miss
Payne is a graduate student in
music at Stanford.
The program is presented free
of charge by the local chapter of
Sigma Alpha lota and the De
partment of Music.
