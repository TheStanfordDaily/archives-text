# Series Presents 'Oedipus Rex'
## 
### 
"Oedipus Rex," a recent show
ing at the Guild Theater and a
picture which a New York Times
review called "spectacular and
awesome," will be presented by
the Committee on Public Exer
cises at the August 5 Tuesday
Evening Series program. The
movie will begin at 8 p.m. in Me
morial Auditorium.
Directed by Tyrone Guthrie,
the Sophocles play is enacted by
the Stratford Ontario Shake
spearean Festival Players. Clifton
Faddiman has said that this is
"among the handful of the finest
movies ever made."
The event is open to the public
and there is no admission charge.
