# Food Research Receives $200,000
## 
### 
The Stanford Fond Research
Institute has received a grant of
$200,000 from the Carnegie Cor
poration of New York for contin
ued research on economic devel
opment in Africa south of the Sa
hara.
The program was begun in 1954
under a Carnegie grant of $150,-
000. Five members of the Insti-
I tute staff, Merrill K. Bennett,
Karl Brandt, William O. Jones, S.
Daniel Neumark and Bruce F.
Johnston, have been to tropical
Africa to work on the project.
Stanford has become one of
five principal centers for African
studies in the United States.
