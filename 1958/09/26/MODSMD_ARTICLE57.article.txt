# New Med School Dean Appointed
## 
### 
Dr. Lowell A. Rantz, past pro
fessor of medicine, was named
associate dean of the School of
Medicine, replacing Dr. Jay Ward
Smith, according to Dean Robert
H. Alway.
Dr. Rantz will assist Dean Al
way in the administration of med
ical affairs and professional rela
tions, and continue teaching, re
search and clinical activities. He
is known as an authority on bac
terial diseases and their treat
ment, and is a consultant to the
Veterans Administration.
