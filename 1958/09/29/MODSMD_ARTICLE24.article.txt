# Craig Assumes Additional Duties
## 
### 
Dean of men William G. Craig
will assume additional duties of
associate dean of students, presi
dent Wallace Sterling announced
Friday.
Craig will be associate to dean
of students H. Donald Winbigler
and will continue as dean of men,
Sterling said in announcing the
promotion.
Craig was. dean of students at
Washington State University and
Kansas State College before com
ing to Stanford in 1955.
He holds a master's degree in
educational psychology from the
University of Minnesota and a
doctorate in education from Har
vard Universitv.
