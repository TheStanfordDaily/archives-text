# Mural Schedule
## 
### 
Intramural Sports director El
wyn Bugge has announced that
sign-up sheets for all fall intra
mural sports will be posted on the
intramural bulletin board at the
BAC today. All units should start
organizing teams today for touch
football, bowling, two-man volley
ball, basketball free-throws, single
wall handball and cross-country.
Later in the quarter an intra
mural boxing program will be
started.
Director Bugge also said that a
meeting for all unit representa
tives has been scheduled for this
Thursday afternoon at 4:30 at the
BAC. "Each unit should elect its
intramural representative now
and not \Vait until it elects the
rest of its officers," he noted.
