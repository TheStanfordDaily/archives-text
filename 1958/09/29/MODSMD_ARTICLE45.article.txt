# Movie Discount Cards on Sale
## 
### 
Stanford women will have a
chance to save their dates some
money again this year with mov
ie discount cards.
The cards, renamed "flick
down" cards, will be on sale at the
Pavilion today and tomorrow for
$1 to students and faculty.
They entitle the purchaser to
use junior-price tickets at area
theaters until September, 1959.
