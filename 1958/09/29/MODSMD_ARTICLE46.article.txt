# Desk Blotters Replace Dividex
## 
### 
Six thousand copies of an ASSU
approved Stanford desk blotter
will be distributed to students
quarterly, according to co-editors
Al. Haimson and Paul Wanner.
The publication replaces the Divi
dex.
The 19x24 blotters all contain
sports schedules and key campus
telephone numbers in addition to
advertisements of local mer
chants. The co-editors, said three
types of blotters will be available,
respectively containing the ads of
"large quality firms, eating and
recreation spots, and personal
service firms."
