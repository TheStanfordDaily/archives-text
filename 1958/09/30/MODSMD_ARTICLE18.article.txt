# Sale Continues
## 
### 
The A Phi O's will continue
their nonprofit book exchange in
temporary building A-70, next to
the Western Civ Library, from 8
a.m. to 12 noon and from 1 to 4
p.m. today.
Persons wishing to sell used
books are requested to bring them
in as early as posible, according
to chairman John Zieber. No
books will be accepted for ex
change after Wednesday.
