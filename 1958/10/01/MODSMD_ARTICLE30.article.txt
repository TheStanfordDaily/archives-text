# A Phi O Exchange
## 
### 
The Alpha Phi Omega Book Ex
change will open again today
from 8 a.m. to 12 noon and from
1 to 4 p.m. as the Exchange
swings into its last two days.
Books needed are: Sewell, "His
tory of Western Art"; Knolls and
Snyder, "Readings in Western
Civilization": both "1.C.C.W." Vol
umes I and II; Perrin, "Writer's
Guide"; and Keast and Streeter,
"Province of Prose."
Today is the last day to sell
books, but students may buy
books both today and tomorrow.
