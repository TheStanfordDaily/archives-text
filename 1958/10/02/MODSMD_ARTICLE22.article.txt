# Stanford Art Gallery to Show Architectural Photo Exhibit
## 
### 
A photographic exhibit of the
architectural arts of Southern
California is on display in the Art
Gallery.
It is the first show of the 1958-
.19 academic year, and is a loan
from the Long Beach Museum of
Art.
Displayed are the works of 21

architectural organizations rep
resenting more than 1000 mem
bers of the American Institute of
Architects in the area.
Julius Shulman, noted archi
tectural photographer, and eight
other camera men took the pic
tures, which will he on view
