# Gallery to Kent Original Prints
## 
### 
The Stanford Art Gallery is
currently offering students the
chance to rent original colored
lithographs, wood engravings,
etchings and serigraphs at a fee
of $1.25 per quarter or $3 per
year. Choice of the prints must
be indicated on a card available
at the Gallery .before Oct. 5, after
which a drawing will determine
the final distribution.
