# TESTS CONTINUE
## 
### 
UNITED NATIONS, N.Y. (/P>—
The Soviet Union declared Tues
day that lack of East-West agi-ee
ment will compel it to continue
nuclear weapons tests until the
total reaches that of the United
States and Britain combined.
Soviet Foreign Minister Andrei
Gromyko said he wanted to em
phasize the importance Moscow
attaches to a Soviet resolution rec
ommending that all countries con
ducting tests discontinue them im
mediately and then negotiate an
appropriate agreement.
