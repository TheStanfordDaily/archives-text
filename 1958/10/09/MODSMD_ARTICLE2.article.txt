# Jazz Sextet To Perform
## 
### 
The Stanford University Music
Department's subscription scries
of concerts opens Sunday, Oct. 12,
with a 4 p.m. concert by the
Chamber Jazz Sextet.
This and three other events will
be presented in Dinkelspiel Audi
torium on the campus. Season
tickets at $7 are on sale in the
Dinkelspiel box office and at the
Peninsula Cox Office, Town and
Country Village, Palo Alto.
Leader of the Chamber Jazz
group is Allyn Ferguson of San
Jose, who plays French horn and
is a former graduate student at
Stanford.
