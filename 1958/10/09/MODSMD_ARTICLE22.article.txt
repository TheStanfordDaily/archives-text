# WRA Schedules Pool Hours
## 
### 
The Women's Recreation Asso
ciation has announced open hours
for the women's swimming pool.
Open hours will be held Mon
day through Thursday from 5:20
to 5:45; Friday from 4 to 5:45;
Saturday (away games) and Sun
day from 1 to 4. The pool will
also be open temporarily from 1
to 4 on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
Each registered woman may

bring two guests of at least col
lege age on weekends, at which
time locker facilities will not be
available.
