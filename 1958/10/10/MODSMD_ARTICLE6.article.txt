# ‘I’ Club Sponsors All-Campus Dance
## 
###

acquainted dance at the Chez
Yvonne tonight from 9 to 1.
Stanford foreign students will
be present and hi-fi records will
provide the dance music. All in
terested students are invited to
attend, and there will be no ad
mission charge.
