# Tryouts
## 
### 
AW'S Scholarship Committee:
Interviews today from 5 to G p.m.
on the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse.
Stanford Players: All students,
alumni and members of the Stan
ford family are invited to attend
the tryouts for the production of
the comedy "Venus Observed"
next Monday and Tuesday eve
nings from 7 to 11:30 p.m. on Ihe
main stage of Memorial Audito
rium. Production dales are Dec.
4, 5 and 6.
Stanford Players: Tryouts to
day and Friday, 5 6 p.m., for the
Little Theatre production of the
"Second Shepherd's Play." Show
will be produced in December.
