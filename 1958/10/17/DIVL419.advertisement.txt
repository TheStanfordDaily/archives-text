QUESTION: What on the Peninsula is growing almost as I
rapidly as Stanford's war industries? What, next
to the missile business, is there no other business
like?
ANSWER: Kepler's Books & Magazines.
—The Peninsula's only complete paperback book
store.
—In brand-new quarters, with doubled floor space,
just one-half block from its original location.
Wc hope to be here until the first missile homes on the target.
