# Hangen to Be Guest Minister
## 
### 
Dr. Emerson G. Ilangen, minis
ter of the First Congregational
Church, Long Beach, will be the
preacher at Memorial Church at
the 11 o'clock service Sunday. His
sermon topic will be "Unwilling
to Be Found."
At 4 p.m. Sunday, University
Organist Herbert B. Nanney will
present an organ concert.
