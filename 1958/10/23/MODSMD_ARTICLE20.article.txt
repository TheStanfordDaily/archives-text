# Rush Continues
## 
### 
The fall rushing program for
Stanford's eating clubs will con
tinue this evening from 7:30 to
9:30 for sophomore, junior and
senior independents and transfer
students.
Club members and hostesses
will greet the rushees and will try
to acquaint them with the eating
club system and its advantages.
Refreshments will be served at
each of the seven clubs.
