# Marksmen to Fire
## 
### 
Dales for the first two marks
manship meets of the year were
set by the Stanford Varsity Rifle
and Pistol Club Tuesday night.
Stanford rifle and pistol marks
men will journey to Santa Clara
on Nov. 13 for the first meet after
the long summer break away
from firing, soon followed by a
home meet with Davis Nov. 20.

The varsity club is independent
of the ROTC teams and member
ship is open to any Stanford stu
dent, including women, and mem
bers have access to special equip
ment to aid in accuracy not open
to the .ROTC.
Dues are $5 a quarter and $10
for the entire year.
