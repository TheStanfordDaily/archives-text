# Concert Tickets
## 
### 
Season tickets for the 1958-59
Concert Series may be picked up
by their purchasers at the Con
cert Series office in the Nitery,
Monday through Friday, from 1
to 5 p.m.
Individual performance tickets
for the Nov. 9 presentation of
"Candide" will go on sale at 1
p.m. next Monday, Oct. 27.
