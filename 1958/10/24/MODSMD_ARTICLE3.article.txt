# '49 Grad Named Adjutant General
## 
### 
Colonel Paul A. Bucha has been
named Adjustant General, Head
quarters, U.S. Army Japan, the
Army announced.
Bucha, who received a master's
degree in business administration
from Stanford in '49, assumed his
new duties on Oct. 10. The head
quarters is located at Camp
Zama, about 30 miles southwest
of Tokvo.
