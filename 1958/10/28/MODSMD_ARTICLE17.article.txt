# Axe Society Gets Ready for Rooters
## 
### 
Stanford Axe Society, the off
shoot of the "Immortal 21," is
holding tryouts today and tomor
row, according to President
Chuck White.
As custodian of the Axe, the
society was instrumental in the
recent move to display the Axe
on campus.
Future activities will include
presentation of Big Game Week's
annual Axe Day and selling pom
pons at the Big Game reunions in
the city.
The tryouts will be held at Bow
man Alumni House today and to
morow from 2 to 5 p.m.
