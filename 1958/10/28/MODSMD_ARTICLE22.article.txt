# Get-Acquainted
##

sponsored by Palo Alto YWCA
for single adults of 31 to 19 years
is to give a "Holloween Party"
dance at the Women's Clubhouse
Nov. 1, 9 p.m. to 1 a.m.
Non-members may buy $1 tick
ets at the door.
