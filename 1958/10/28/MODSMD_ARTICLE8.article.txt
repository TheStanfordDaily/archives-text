# Investment Club To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Investment Club
will hold its first meeting of the
year this evening at 7 in Bowman
Alumni House, according to Jim
Pigott, club president.
The club's purpose is to study
the financial world. Members
vole a small sum of money for
dues at the start of the year,
form committees to study invest
ments. and make nominal invest
ments which they redeem at the
end of the year.
