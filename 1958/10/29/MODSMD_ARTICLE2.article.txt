# Nominations Open Today
## 
### 
Nomination blanks for offices
to be decided in the fall election
may be picked up beginning this
morning at the ASSU office.
The blanks should be filled out
before signatures are requested.
Only people from the living area,
class or school may sign the nomi
nation slips. Completed slips are
to be returned to the ASSU office
by 5 p.m. next Tuesday.
LASSU living group represent
atives to be elected are two from
Wilbur and one each from Bran
ner, Roble. Lagunita, Union, off
campus, Men's Row, Women's
Row and Stern.
Two representatives from the
combined Schools of Humanities-
Sciences and Mineral Sciences and
one representative from the Engi
neering School will be elected.
Also to be elected are Class of
'61 president, vice president and
business manager, two represent
atives to Women's Council and
two to Men's Council.
