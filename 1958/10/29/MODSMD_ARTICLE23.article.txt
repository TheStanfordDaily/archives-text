# Professors To Aid Tokyo U.
## 
### 
Stanford University will select
American personnel to work with
the University of Tokyo in an
evaluation of educational reforms
launched in Japan since World
War 11.
The two-year study, supported
by the Ford Foundation, will be
co-ordinated within the Stanford
School of Education by Paul R.
Hanna, professor of child educa
tion, and Arthur P. Coladarci, as
sociate professor of education.
