# Gaieties Tickets
##

Today is the last day to pur
chase block tickets for the 1958
Ram's Head Gaieties, according to
Carole Cosgrove, business man
ager.
Tickets are available at the
Ram's Head office from 11 a.m.
to 12 noon and from 4 to 5 p.m.,
and a 15 per cent discount will be
given to block ticket purchasers
for the Tuesday and Wednesday
night performances.
