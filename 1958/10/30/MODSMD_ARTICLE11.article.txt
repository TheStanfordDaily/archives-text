# IIR to Present Vice-Consul
## 
### 
Hanning von Hassel, German
vice-consul in San Francisco, will
speak on "The Contemporary
Scene in Germany" in the second
of the llR's cultural talks series
this afternoon at 4:15 in Bowman
Alumni House.
A color film, "Lovely Land
scapes," will be shown along with
von Hassel's discussion of cultu
ral, political and economic im
pressions.
A native of East Prussia, von
Hassel escaped to Schleswig-Hol
stein in 1915. After extensive
legal training he served two years
with the Bonn government's For
eign Office before coming to San
Francisco in June of this year.
