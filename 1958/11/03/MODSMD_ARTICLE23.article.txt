# Foreign Students To Study Election
## 
### 
Stanford international students
will have an opportunity to see
election machinery in operation
and find out the significance of
California state and national elec
tions on Tuesday, election day.
Two tours of polling stations,
arranged by the League of Wom
en Voters, will leave from the In
ternational Center at 11 a.m. and
2 p.m.
Prof. Cornelius Philip Cotter
of the Department of Political
Science will discuss election prin
ciples and procedure in a talk in
the International Center lounge at
7:30 p.m.. and election returns
will be observed on TV.
