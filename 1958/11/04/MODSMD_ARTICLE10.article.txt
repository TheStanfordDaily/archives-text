# LASSU Plans Finance Study
## 
### 
Legislature is forming a Uni
versity Fund Committee to study
fund - raising methods and poli
cies, a representative announced.
The new committee will prob
ably be composed of four fresh
men, two sophomores, one jun
ior and one senior. The committee
will study fund-raising at Sanford
and other schools, and serve as a
liaison between students and the
administration on money - raising
policies.
Students who are interested in
the scholastic achievement of
Stanford are invited to contact
Legislature members, student
body officers or John Nickerson
at 113 Toyon.
