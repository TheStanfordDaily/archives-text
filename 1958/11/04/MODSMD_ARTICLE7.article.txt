# 'I' Center to Mark First Birthday
## 
### 
A birthday party commemorat
ing the first anniversary of the
International Center will be held
Wednesday at the Center from
4:30 to 5:30 p.m.
The party is open to all Stan
ford students, staff and faculty,
according to the Community
Committee for International Stu
dents, which is sponsoring the af
fair.
The candle will be lit, and the
cake will be cut, at 5:15 p.m.
