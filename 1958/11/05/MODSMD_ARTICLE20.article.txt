# 'Orpheus' Reopens In Little Theater
## 
### 
Entering its second week of
performances, "Orpheus Descend
ing," Stanford Players' produc
tion of the Tennessee Williams
drama, reopens tonight in the Lit
tle Theater at 8:15 for its second
four-night run.
Colon Brown, Margaret Landry
and Carol Moscrip star in the
play, directed by Dr. Robert Lo
per.
Tickets for the performances
have been sold out since last
week; however, interested per
sons may call Memorial Audito
rium box office to claim last-min
