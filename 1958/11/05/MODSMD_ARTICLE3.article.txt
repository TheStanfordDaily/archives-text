# Mothers Ready Tea, Art Show
## 
### 
The Stanford Mothers' Club
will hold its traditional Chrysan
themum Tea at the Stanford Mu
seum on Thursday, 2-4:30 p.m.
The tea will feature floral dis
plays and the new museum exhi
bition of paintings from Thailand
and embroideries from Burma.
Tickets are $1.50 and may be
obtained from the ticket chair
man, Mrs. Harvey Hall, or may
be purchased at the museum the
day oL the tea.
