# Debate Team Ties For First Place
## 
### 
One of Stanford's debate teams
tied for first place in the North
ern California Forensic Associa
tion's tournament at the College
of Pacific last Friday. Members
of the winning team were Mike
Droscoll, Don Proud, Darrell
Trent, and Mike Willemsen.
Another of Stanford's teams,
composed of Gloria Bianchi, Har
old Griffin, Lester O'Shea and
Jack Rolls, took a third place.
It was reported in The Daily
Wednesday that the Stanford
team took second place. The
above results are the correct, re
vised ones.
