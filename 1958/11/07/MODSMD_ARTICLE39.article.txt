# Opera Company To Present Verdi
## 
### 
The West Bay Opera Associa
tion will present its first perform
ance of the season today and
Saturday at the Palo Alto Com
munity Theatre, at 8:13 p.m.
Outstanding soloists and a cho
rus of approximately 50 voices
will sing Verdi's "Othello," the
first act; Strauss's "Der Rosen
kavalier," the "presentation of the
rose" scene; and Bernstein's one
act opera "Trouble in Tahiti."
Tickets are available at the Pe
ninsula Box Office, Town & Coun
try Village, for $2 center section
and $1.50 side scats.
