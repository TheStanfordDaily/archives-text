# Special Discounts On 'Old Man' Film
## 
### 
A special discount of 50 cents
on any ticket is offered to Stan
ford students for the film version
of Ernest Hemingway's "Old Man
and the Sea," playing at the Cor
onet Theater in San Francisco.
Presentation of the student
body card assures the special
prices. Performances are nightly
at 8:40, Sundays at 7, with mati
nees on Wednesdays, Saturdays
and Sundays at 2:30.
