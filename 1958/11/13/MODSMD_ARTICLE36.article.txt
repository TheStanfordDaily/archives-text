# School Offers Two Scholarships
## 
### 
Two 3959-60 national scholar
ships for senior college women
are offered by the Katharine
Gibbs School.
Each scholarship consists of
full tuition for the secretarial
training course, plus an addi
tional cash award of $500. Stu
dents interested in competing for
one of these awards may obtain
further information from the
Placement Bureau.
The awards were established as
a memorial to Mrs. Katharine M.
Gibbs, founder and first president
of the school.
