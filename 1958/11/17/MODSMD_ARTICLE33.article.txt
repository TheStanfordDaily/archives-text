# Signs Erected on Alvarado
## 
### 
New stop signs on Alvarado
Row at the corner of Coronado
Avenue were erected last week.
Lieutenant John VV. Gilkey of the
Stanford Police Department told
The Daily that an increasing
number of homes and young chil
dren in the area made the instal
lation necessary.
Since development of the Pine
Hills residential -area, Alvarado
has been cut through to Stanford
Avenue. As a result, it has be
come a speedway for cars enter
ing and leaving campus by the
back way.
Following several accidents at
the corner of Alvarado and Coro
nado, Stanford Safety Director

Robert Love investigated condi
tions and recommended the instal
lation of stop signs.
