# Game Libe Hours
## 
### 
On Monday through Wednes
day the Reserve Book Room and
the main reading rooms of the
Main Library will be open until
11 p.m. Thursday and Friday will
have regular hours, but on Satur
day the Library will be closed
all day. Regular hours will be re
sumed on Sunday.
The Western Civilization Li
brary will maintain its regular
hours during the week.
