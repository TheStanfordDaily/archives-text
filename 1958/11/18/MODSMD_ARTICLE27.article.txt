# Moot Court Topic: Labor Disputes
## 
### 
The annual Marion Rice Kirk
wood Competition will be held in
the Stanford Law School Moot
Court room from 7:30 to 9:30 to
night, Wednesday and Thursday.
A labor dispute problem will
be discussed tonight and Wednes
day concerning picketing of an
employer by a union that had
been rejected by the employees.
Violation of the narcotics laws,
federal and state, will be the topic
Thursday.
All meetings of the first session
are open to the public and visitors
are invited to attend.
