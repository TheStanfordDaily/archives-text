# Holiday Weekend Planned by IIR
## 
### 
A Thansgiving weekend trip
Nov. 28/30 to Big Sur for foreign
and American students is being
arranged by the Host Committee
of the lIR.
Students can spend Thanksgiv
ing Day at home or as guests of
American families. The group
will stay at Hot Springs Lodge in
the coast country of Monterey,
where Charles Hepburn of the
History Department and his wife
will act as hosts.
Transportation will be by car,
and the total cost of the weekend
will be $11. Reservations may be
made at the"I" Center before
Nov. 21.
