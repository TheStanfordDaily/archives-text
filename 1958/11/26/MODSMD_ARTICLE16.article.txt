# Soprano to Give Benefit Concert
## 
### 
Mezzo - soprano Mae Williams
will give a concert to benefit Ne
gro students attending local high
schools Sunday at 8 p.m. in Din
kelspiel Auditorium.
The program will include mod
ern and classical songs as well as.
spirituals.
Mrs. Williams was graduated
from Howard University School
of Music and has studied at the
Paris Conservatory. Tickets for
the performance are available at
the Peninsula Box Office for
$1.25.
