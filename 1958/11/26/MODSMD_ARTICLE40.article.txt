# Photos on Display
## 
### 
Photographs of Stanford scenes
by Moffatt Hancock, professor of
law, are on display in the lobby
of the University's Planning De
partment.
Prof. Hancock's views include a
flight of steps in Frost Memorial
Amphitheater, Hoover Institution
tower, the rain-swept Quad, a
storm-blasted oak in the hills be
hind the University, and studies
of Lake Lagunita.
