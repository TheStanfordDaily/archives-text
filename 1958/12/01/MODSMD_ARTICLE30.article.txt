# Shell Exhibit
## 
### 
An exhibit representing more
than 20,000 species of shells is lo
cated on the third floor of the
Geology Building. The shells,
which range in size from tiny
spiral shells to enormous clam
shells, are as old as 60 million
years.
The exhibit is open Monday
through Friday from 9 to 5, ex
cept during the lunch hour.
