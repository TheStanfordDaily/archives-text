# Dance Sponsor Applications Due
## 
### 
Organizations interested in
sponsoring the winter quarter
Registration Dance should apply
by tomorrow.
Applications may be obtained
at the ASSU office and should be
submitted to the Social Coordinat
ing Board, according to ASSU
vice-president Carolee Houser.
The board will select a sponsor
for the dance on the basis of
financial need and merit of dance
plans as shown in the application.
