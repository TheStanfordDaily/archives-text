# Research Doctor Chosen for Post
## 
### 
Dr. Alan K. Done, a physician
prominent in rheumatic fever re
search, has been appointed assist
ant professor of pediatrics at the
Medical School.
Dr. Done has researched widely
and published many professional
articles on studies of rheumatic
fever in children.
Also receiving new appoint
ments were William G. Madow,
Robert FO. Bechofer and Benjamin
Kpsfein, all statisticians.
