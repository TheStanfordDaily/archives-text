# SRI Enlarges Biology Staff
## 
### 
Stanford Research Institute has
added three senior scientists to
the Physical Sciences Division
staff, announced Director Dr.
Donald L. Benedict.
New staff members, who will
participate in SRl's expanding
biological sciences program, are
Dr. Gerald A. LePage, program
director in biochemistry; Dr. Jo
seph Greenberg, program director
in microbiology; and Dr. Joseph
D. Mandell, microbiologist and cy
