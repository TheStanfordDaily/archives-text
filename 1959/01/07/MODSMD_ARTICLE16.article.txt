# Ehlers, Heinitz Will Perform
## 
### 
Selections by Bach, Couperin
and Marin Marais will be featured
at the concert given by Alice Eld
ers and Eva Ileinitz Friday in Din
kelspiel Auditorium at 8:30 p.m.
Tickets at $2 are on sale at both
Dinkelspiel and the Peninsula
Box Office.
Ticket stubs from the evening
performance will admit hearers to
a Saturday workshop at 10 a.m.
in Dinkelspiel given by Miss Eld
ers and Miss Ileinitz. Those wish
ing to attend the workshop only
may buy tickets for 50 cents at
the door.
