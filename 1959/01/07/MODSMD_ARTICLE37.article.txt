# Card Crew Sets Workouts Today
## 
### 
Varsity and frosh crew work
outs start today. All oarsmen,
coxswains and managers should
report to the boalhouse at 4:15
p.m.
Those interested in turning out
for the varsity or frosh crews,
and who have not already signed
up, should report at this time or
should contact crew captain
Chuck Brothers.
