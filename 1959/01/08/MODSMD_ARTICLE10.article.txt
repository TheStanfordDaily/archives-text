# Four Preps to Be At Soph Cotillion
## 
### 
The Four Preps head the enter
tainment schedule for this year's
Sophomore Cotillion Jan. 1(5, with
dancing music provided by Rudy
Salvini and his band.
The theme of the cotillion, to
he held at the St. Francis Hotel
in San Francisco, will be "Silhou
ette in Silver." The dance will be
semi-formal, lasting from 9 p.m.
to 1 a.m. Tickets are $1 per couple
to class card holders and $4.50
per couple for those without class
cards.
