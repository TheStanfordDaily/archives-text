# Riding Director Tops Designers
## 
### 
First award in the new Ameri
can Horse Shows Association's
annual course designers' compe
tition will be made to Colonel
Alex Sysin, director of Stanford
University's riding school, in New
York City next Thursday.
Colonel Sysin's winning entries
included courses at the 1958 Na
tional Horse Show at the Cow Pal
ace, San Francisco, and at out
standing events.
