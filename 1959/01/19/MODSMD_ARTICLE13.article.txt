# Former Student Wins Novel Prize
## 
### 
Robin White, who hold a Wal
ter Stegner fellowship at Stan
ford in 1936-57, has been an
nounced as this year's winner of
the $10,000 Harper Prize Novel
contest.
His book, "Elephant Hill," the
story of an American woman in
present-day India, was published
on Jan. 5 by Harper & Brothers.
It was selected from more than
800 entries.
White spent most of his early
life in India where his father was
a missionary.
