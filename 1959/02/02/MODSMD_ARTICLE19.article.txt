# Profs to Participate In Geophysicist Meet
## 
### 
One hundred fifty geophysicists
will gather at Stanford Thursday
and Friday for the annual Pacific
Southwest regional meeting of
the American Geophysical Union.
General chairman is Joseph B.
Franzini, associate professor of
civil engineering at Stanford.
Opening lecture sessions will
be held in the Little Theater on
campus beginning at 9:30 a.m.
Thursday and subsequently in
various rooms of the Education
Building.

Ronald N. Braeewell, associate
professor of electrical engineer- j
ing. will speak on "The Future of
Satellites" at Thursday's lunch.
