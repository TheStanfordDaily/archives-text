# Prof Emeritus Gives Lectures
## 
### 
Thomas S. Barclay, emeritus
professor of political science, has
been appointed by Phi Beta as its
representative on a nationwide
lecture tour.
He is currently visiting univer
sities in Missouri, Michigan, Ill
inois, and New York. In addition
to formal lectures, he is address
ing seminars, group meetings,
and holding conferences with stu
dents and faculty.
A Phi Kappa at the University
of Missouri, Barclay earned his
doctorate from Columbia. He
came to Stanford in 1927 and re
tired last year.
