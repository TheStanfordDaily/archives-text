# Student to Give Organ Concert
## 
### 
Robert Sehuneman, Stanford
University graduate student in
music, will present a free organ
concert in Memorial Church to
morrow at 8:15 p.m.
Sehuneman, a pupil of Univer
sity Organist Herbert B. Nanney,
is organist and choir director at
the Bethel Lutheran Church, San
Francisco.
