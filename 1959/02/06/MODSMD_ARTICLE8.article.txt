# Pomona College Concert Sunday
## 
### 
The Pomona College Glee Club
will present a vespers concert at
4 p.m. Sunday at Memorial
Church. The concert is open to
the public.
The 70-voice glee club will sing
the "Deutsche Magnificat" of
Heinrich Schutz and three sacred
choruses by Mendelssohn. Works
by Handel. Patestrina and Haydn
will also be presented.
The group is under the direction
of Professor William F. Russell,
chairman of Ihe music depart
ment a I Pomona.
