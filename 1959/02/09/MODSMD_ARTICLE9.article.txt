# Drive Nears $1500 Mark
## 
### 
To date, $1409.02 has been re
ceived by the Winter Drive Com
mittee as a result of last week's
Red Cross - Community Chest
drive, reports Mike Praetorius,
drives treasurer.
In the women's living groups,
the highest contributor was Un
ion. El Toro was first among the
eating clubs. The four highest
contributors among the fraterni
ties were Beta Theta Pi, Theta
Chi, Phi Kappa Sigma and Kappa
Sigma.
