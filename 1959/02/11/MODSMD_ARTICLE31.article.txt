# Sailors Plan Eastern Trip
## 
### 
Stanford's sailing team is mak
ing plans to travel east to the
National Intercollegiate Cham
pionships, with Occidental, and
will represent the West Coast at
the Regatta to be held in Provi
dence, R. 1., during ihe latter part
of June.
The team, represented by Dick
Landes, George Walker, John
Harrington and John Eastman,
gained ihe trip by placing second
in the Pacific Coast Intercolle
giate Championships held last
Christmas vacation.
The sailors defeated Cal, San
Francisco State and Menlo JC in
the team race regatta held at Ber
keley Yacht Harbor last Sunday.
