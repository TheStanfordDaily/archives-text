# Slide Rule Course Begins Next Week
## 
### 
Tau Beta Pi's annual slide rule
course will begin Thursday, Feb.
19, with a lecture in Room 273 of
the engineering building at 7 p.m.
Gary Dcley, senior electrical
engineering student, will conduct
the three-part series for all per
sons interested in having a useful
familiarity with the slide rule.
The first lecture will include
an introduction to the slide rule
and the reading of the C, D, CI
and DF scales and their use in
ordinary multiplication and divi
sion.
The two remaining lectures will
be on Feb. 26 and March 5.
