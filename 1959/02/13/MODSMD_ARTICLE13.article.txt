# Frosh Will Hold Friday 13th Dance
## 
### 
Taking advantage of Friday the
13th as a theme, the freshman
class will hold its first dance of
the season tonight in the Wilbur
dining halls immediately follow
ing the UCLA basketball game,
freshman class president Vince
Occhipinti said.
Admission is free to holders of
freshman class cards or is $1.50
without. One member of a couple
must be a freshman. Cards ate
still available from freshman ex
ecutive committee members.
