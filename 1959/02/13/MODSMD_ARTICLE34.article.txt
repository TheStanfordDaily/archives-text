# Chinese New Year
## 
### 
The Chinese New Year Festival
will be presented in San Francis
co's Chinatown tomorrow, Satur
day and Sunday and will feature
a New Year's parade Saturday at
7 p.m.
Other activities will include a
fashion show with apparel from
ancient and modern China, au
thentic folk dancing, a Chinese
chess tournament and a street
carnival.
Planned exhibits include Chi
nese art and paintings of the
Sung dynasty and a collection of
antique tomb jade.
