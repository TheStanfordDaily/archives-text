# Citizen Award Given to Sterling
## 
### 
President Wallace Sterling was
given the Distinguished Citizen
Award of the Palo Alto Chamber
of Commerce yesterday at a
luncheon at Rickey's Studio Inn.
He was voted the award in ap
preciation of his "dynamic leader
ship" as University president and
for "his many contributions to the
Palo Alto Stanford community."
Dr. Sterling, who was present to
deliver a Lincoln's Birthday ad
dress, received the fourth award
to be made by the Chamber in its
'18-year history. Herbert Hoover
received the award in 1.948.
