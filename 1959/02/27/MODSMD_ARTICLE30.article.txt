# Tiddlywinks, Anyone?
## 
### 
CAMBRIDGE. England, Feb. 20
(/!'; - Tiddlywinks today became
an official intervarsity sport be
tween Oxford and Cambridge.
I( now takes its place beside the
boat race, rugby union, track and
field, soccer, boxing, and other

athletic activities.
Cambridge tiddled the winks
more skillfully and defeated Ox
ford 64-48 to take the first offi
cial title.
