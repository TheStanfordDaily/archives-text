# All Study Lists Now Available
## 
### 
Freshmen and sophomores will
begin conferring with their advis
ers today to plan study lists for
spring quarter pre-registration.
They will obtain their official
study lists from their advisers,
fill them out and obtain their ad
visers' signature before they may
begin signing class lists, accord
ing to Registrar Ilarvey Hall.
Juniors, seniors and graduate
students will continue pre-regis
tration today. Class lists must be
signed in the Registrar's Office,
Room 111-A, for all courses
marked "8" in the winter quarter
time schedule. Freshmen and
sophomores in;iy sign class lists
beginning tomorrow only after
the time stamped on their study
lists.
Students may file their study
lists with the check-out clerk iti
the Registrar's Office between 8
a.m. and noon and between 1 and
5 p.m. daily. The deadline for fil
ing study lists is 5 p.m. Monday.
A student's name on a class list
guarantees him admission to the
class if he attends the first ses
sion, according to !he Registrar's
Office.
