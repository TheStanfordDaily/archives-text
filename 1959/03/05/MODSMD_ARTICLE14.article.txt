# Shots Continue as 2100 Immunized
## 
### 
Some 2100 students have been
immunized against Asian influen
za during the past two weeks, the
Health Service reports.
The flu is expected to hit about
Dead or Finals week. Students
who have not been vaccinated or
were immunized over six months
ago are urged to get shots.
The Health Service will give
shots daily through March 13.
