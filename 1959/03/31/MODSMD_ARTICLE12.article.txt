# Belgian Prize To Biologist
## 
### 
Professor Clifford Grobstein, a
Stanfo rfl biologist, has been
awarded the Albert Brachet Prize
of the Belgian Royal Academy.
The prize is given every three
years for the most outstanding
work in exeprimental embryoolgy
during that period. It includes a
cash award of 20,000 Belgian
francs about $500.
Professor Grobstein is the first
American scientist to be awarded
the prize. His work proved that
embryo cells need only be close
together, not actually touching, in
order to interact upon one another
during development.
