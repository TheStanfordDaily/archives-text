# Huff Wins Post
## 
###

nancial aids at Stanford Univer
sity, has been named one of eight
national consultants to the U.S.
Office of Education.
He attended a meeting in Wash
ington, D.C., Monday and Tues
day to discuss administration and
reporting methods of the colle
giate loan program under the new
National Defense Education Act.
