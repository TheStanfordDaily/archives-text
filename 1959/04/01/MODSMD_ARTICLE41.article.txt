# Pool Hours Told
## 
### 
Spring quarter hours for the
women's pool have been an
nounced and are as follows:
Monday, Wednesday, Friday—
4-5:30 p.m..
Tuesday, Thursday—l-2, 3:15-
4:15 p.m.
Saturday, Sunday —1-4 p.m.
Use of the pool at these hours
will be restricted to registered
women students. On Saturday
and Sunday, they will be allowed
two guests of at least college age.
Locker facilities will not be
available on the weekends.

Deadline for "Once Around
(he Quad" items is 3 p.m. of the
day before publication.
