# Peace Adherents Will Meet Here
## 
### 
A meeting for all those inter
ested in aiding in the "achieve
ment of a just peace" will he held
from 2 to -1 p.m. today at Bowman
Alumni House.
"This is not a pacifist meeting,"
according to Brent Barksdale,
program committee member. A
discussion session will follow a
speech by the Rev. John Swomley
on "alternatives to present U.S.
foreign policy."
