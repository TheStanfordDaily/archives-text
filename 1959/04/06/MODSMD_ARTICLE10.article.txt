# Council Notes Sterling's 10th Anniversary
## 
### 
A resolution observing the 10th
anniversary of President Ster
ling's term as president of the
University was presented to the
Academic Council Friday by Dr.
James Wat kins on behalf of 20
professors who have been at Stan
ford during Sterling's entire term.
The resolution passed by the
Council said:
"Resolved, that the Academic
Council notes with pride the 10th
anniversary of its meeting of
April 6, 1949, when J. E. Wallace
Sterling first presided as fifth
president of Stanford University
and chairman of this Council.
"It recalls the achievements of
the University during the past 10
years under his leadership and
looks forward with confidence to
the years that lie ahead."
