# Hoover Antiques Destroyed in Fire
## 
### 
Antiques and art objects col
lected by former President Her
bert Hoover were destroyed as
the home of Hoover's late brother
Theodore burned to the ground
last Friday night.
The four-story, 30-room ranch
home was situated on the coast
15 miles north of Santa Cruz.
Mrs. Hulda McLean, Theodore's
daughter, estimated the loss at
$50,000.
No one was at home at the time
of the fire, which was discovered
by a Cal student 011 a botany
field trip.
