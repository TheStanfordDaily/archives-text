## 
###

tickets may be purchased for the
musical play "Pal Joey," 1o be
presented by Ram's Head May 6

offered on a block of 20 or more

and 10 per cent will be given for
a block of 30 or more tickets for
the May 7 show.
Tickets may be purchased at the
Ram's Head office from 11 a.m. to
12 noon and from 2 to 3 p.m.
today.
