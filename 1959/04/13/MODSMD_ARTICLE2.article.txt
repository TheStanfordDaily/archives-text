# Spaces Open For 'Sing' Tour
## 
### 
Three openings are still avail
able in a college singing group
which is traveling through west
ern and central Europe this sum
mer for 1 two months. The group,
known as the "California Sing
ers," consists of 28 students from
Stanford, San Francisco State
and the University of California.
They will be directetd by Robert
P. Commanday. conductor of the
Glee Club and Treble Clef Society
at the University of California.
The students, who are already
rehearsing, are each paying their
own share of the expenses. They
will be appearing in many Euro
pean cities, singing madrigals and
folk songs.
Anyone interested in applying
for the three remaining places
may call Joyce Firstenberger, La
gunita.
