# Guerard Writes French History
## 
### 
A new book by Professor Al
bert L. Guerard, "France: A Mod
ern History," is a distillation of
the many courses he has given at
Stanford since he first came in
1906.
It has been published by the
University of Michigan Press and
is one of a series titled "The His
tory of the Modern World."
Guerard, retired professor of
general literature, has received in
ternational acclaim for his teach
ing and writings, which include
22 books, many on the subject of
his native France.
He retired in 1946 and lives, on
the Stanford campus.
Guerard is regarded as an
American who has done much to
better Franco-American relations
in the past half-century.
