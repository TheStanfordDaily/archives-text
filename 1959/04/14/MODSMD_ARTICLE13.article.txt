# Climbers Will Show Slides
## 
### 
Three members of the Himala
yan expedition which climbed
Hidden Peak, the highest summit
ever reached by Americans, will
show color slides of their ascent
in Cubberley Auditorium tomor
row at 7:30 p.m.
Thomas MeCormack, Gilbert
Roberts, and Robert Swift, all
Stanford graduates, were part of
the 10-man party which climbed
the 26,150-foot mountain in July
1958.
The slides and lecture arc be
ing presented by the Stanford
Alpine Club. Admission is 50
cents.
