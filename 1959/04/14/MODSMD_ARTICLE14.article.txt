# 'Joey' Block Seats
## 
### 
Today is the last day that
groups may make block ticket
purchases for "Pal Joey," the
Ram's Head production of the
Rodgers and Hart musical to be
presented May 6-9.
Tickets may be bought at the
Ram's Head office from 9 a.m. to
12 or from 1 to 5 p.m. Although
orders must be made today,
checks will be accepted until Fri
day.
