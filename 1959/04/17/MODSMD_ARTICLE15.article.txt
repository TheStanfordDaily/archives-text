# 'Major Barbara'
##

April 23-25 are the dates that
"Major Barbara" will be present
ed by the Stanford Players in Me
morial Auditorium at 8:15 p.m.
Ticket sales continue today for
the George Bernard Shaw com
edy at Memorial Auditorium box
office between 12:30 and 4:30 p.m.
Starring as Barbara, a major in
the Salvation Army and daughter
of a manufacturer of munitions,
will he Sally Pence, who appeared
in "Call Me Madam" last spring
and "Orpheus Descending" this
year.
