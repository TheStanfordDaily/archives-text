# Tickets on Sale For 'Count Ory'
## 
### 
The West Coast premiere of
"Count Ory," a full-scale Rossini
opera, will be presented by the
Music Department May 21 23 and
May 28-30 in Dinkelspiel Audito
rium.
Tickets are now on sale at the
Dinkelspiel Auditorium box of
fice at a student rate of .$1.75 and
general admission of $2.25 for
side and $2.75 for center scats.
