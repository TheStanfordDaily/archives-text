# Odegard to Talk At Phi Beta Fete
## 
### 
Stanford Phi Beta Kappa will
hold a seminar tonight at 8 in
Rickey's banquet room.
Peter Odegard, political science
professor from the University of
California at Berkeley, will speak
on "New Directions in Education
in the U.S. and U.S.S.S.R."
Odegard, past president of
Reed College, has recently re
turned from a trip to the U.S.S.R.
Reservations may he secured
by contacting Professor John Lof
tis, chapter secretary. Reserva
tions are preferred, but not abso
lutely necessary, according to
Loftis.
