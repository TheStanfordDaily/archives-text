In Saturday's match the In

Dick Ogden.
Nelson had his usually top
serve working better than ever.
The husky Indian captain mixed
up some neat twist and spin
serves with a speedy flat serve
and whipped Cal's leading not tor
Art Kono 6-3, 6-2.
Ogden, making a minimum of
mistakes, proved much too
strong for an erratic Warren Sis

The hero for the Stanford
freshman team on Saturday was
Chuck Goodwin, the number six
freshman from Eureka, Calif.
Goodwin won the only match for
Stanford in what proved lo bo a
dismal day for the Papoose net
