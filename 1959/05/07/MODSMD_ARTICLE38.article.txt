Applications for Cardinals
Board are available in the ASSU
office. Cardinals is responsible
for the administration and or
ganization of all-campus activi
ties including Big Game Week,
Homecoming Week, Con Home
Week, High School Day and Par

Cardinals Board consists of 10
members, each chosen for a term

two junior women, three senior
