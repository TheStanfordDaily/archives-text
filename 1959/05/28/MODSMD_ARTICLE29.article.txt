# IIR Publishes Travel Booklet
## 
### 
A forty page booklet on travel
tips will be available at no cost
Friday and thereafter at the lIR
Shark for all students going
abroad.
This collection of money-saving
hints, dates of important events,
and information on how to travel
is published by the Stanford Over
seas Information Service of the
lIR. Also included in the booklet
is a brief summary of European
and several Asian countries and
what may be found in each.
As supply is limited, students
have been urged to pick up a copy
early.
