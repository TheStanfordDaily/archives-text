# Union 'Not for the Birds'
## 
### 
I'ltil Mill's, leader of u demonstration held yes
terday afternoon in the Inner Quad, explains
how the swallows who live there are flaunting
the Fundamental Standard, lie stated that the
birds are "dumping on Inner Quad" and railed

for immediate action either to jjet rid of the
birds or to make their winter home so enjoyable
that they would rather stay there than come
'here. Incidentally, no swallows showed up for
the demonstration.
