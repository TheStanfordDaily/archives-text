# Off for Six Months in Stuttgart
## 
### 
Student body president David Fischer (left) heads the third term of students to leave for Stan
ford-in-Germany. Other autumn officers for the group of 33 men and 30 women are '(continuing
from left) secretary Gloria Geisler, activities chairman Keith Chrisman and (far right) business
manager Hon Casentini. With them are Mia Ahrens. Andree Whitney is public relations chair
man. The group, which will study at Stuttgart for six months, left Tuesday morning from San
Francisco International Airport. Their Pan Am Titan charter flight stopped at Frobisher Bay,
Baffin Island, en route over the North Pole. Associate Professor of Political Science Hubert Mar
shall and Professor of German Fredrick Strothmann will serve on the Stuttgart summer faculty.
