# Students Invited To Meet Arrivals
## 
### 
Foreign visitors and students
have been invited by the I Club to
meet Monday at 4:30 p.m. with a
group arriving at Stanford to take
part in an international program
in small-industry management.
Men from Burma, Ilong Kong,
Indonesia, Iran, Japan, Liberia,
Mexico and Pakistan will spend
three months at the Business
School and three months at Stan
ford Research Institute studying
management.
