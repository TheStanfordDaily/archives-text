# Harvard Prof Will Speak on Russia
## 
###

and Literature" will be the topic
of a speech to be given by Profes
sor Roman Jakobsen Wednesday
at 8 p.m. in Cubberley Audito
rium.
He is Samuel H. Cross Profes
sor of Slavic Languages at Har
vard and is presently a Fellow at
Stanford's Center for Advanced
Study in Behavioral Sciences.
