# New Italian Show At Art Gallery
## 
### 
"Painting in Post-War Italy,"
a new show of 40 canvases on its
first tour of the United States,
will be on display at the Stanford
Art Gallery through August 5.
The display represents the 12-
year period of Italian modernists
from 1945 to 1957. Three genera
tions of Italian painters are in
cluded. It is sponsored by the
American Federation of Arts, Co
lumbia University, and the Italian
government.
The Art Gallery is open from
10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Tuesday through
Saturday, and from noon to 4
p.m. on Sundays. It is closed
Mondays. Admission is free.
