# Doctor Kays Gets Fulbright
## 
### 
William M. Kays, professor of
mechanical engineering, has been
granted a Fulbright Fellowship
to lecture at the University of
London for the 1959-60 academic
year.
Dr. Kays, a specialist in ther
modynamics, will lecture on heat
transfer.
A Stanford alumus, he earned
his advanced engineering degree
here after serving as a captain
in the Corps of Engineers at
Omaha Beach and the European
Theater during World War 11.
