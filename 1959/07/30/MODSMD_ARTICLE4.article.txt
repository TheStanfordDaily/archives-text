# Alumnus Will Speak Sunday
## 
### 
A Stanford alumnus will preach
on "Incarnation and Transfigu
ration" Sunday at 11 a.m. in Me
morial Church.
Canon David Forbes of Grace
Cathedral in San Francisco, the
speaker, received his B.S. and M.S.
here in mineral sciences. After
switching to the ministry, he re
ceived his theological degree from
Virginia Theological Seminary.
He has been at Grace Cathedral
since 1953, where he is responsible
for the worship life of the Cathed
ral and is chaplain of the Cathed
ral School for Boys.
In addition to the 11 a.m. serv
ice, there will be a Communion
service at 9 a.m. This is the last
Communion service of the quar
ter. Wednesday's 9:30 a.m. Candle
light service is also the last of the
quarter.
