# KZSU Continues Daily Broadcasts
## 
### 
Broadcasting on campus radio
station KZSU will continue
throughout next week under di
rection of the Stanford Radio-
Television Institute. The station
opened operations Monday for its
annual two-week summer session.
Programming features jazz and
classical music, news round-ups,
and a nightly half-hour "Stanford
at Night" show. The station broad
casts Monday through Friday 7-
10 p.m.
