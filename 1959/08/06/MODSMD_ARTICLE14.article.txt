# Presbyterian at Memorial Church
## 
### 
The guest preacher in Memorial
Church Sunday, for the last serv
ice of the summer, will be Dr.
Paul S. Heath, executive of the
Presbyterian Church in the Bay
Area.
Dr. Heath will speak on "The
Impartiality of God" at the 11 a.m.
service. Before coming to his
present position, he held a similar
one in New York. lie is past pres
ident of Auburn Theological Sem
inary.
