# Thalmann in Chile
## 
### 
Professor Hans E. Thalmann of
Stanford University left recently
for Santiago, Chile, to spend 18
months assisting the University
of Chile in developing its two
year-old School of Geology.
Dr. Thalmann will be Chief of
Party of Stanford's new two-year
contract with the International
Cooperation Administration of
the U.S. State Department and
the University of Chile.
