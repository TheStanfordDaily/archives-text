# Soph Reunion
## 
### 
The sophomore class will hold a
reunion today at Adobe Creek
from 2 p.m. to 1 a.m. Sylvester
Grisby plays for dancing. There
Will also be swimming, football,
basketball and volleyball. Maps
will be posted at the Post Office
and on Quad. Fifty cents with
class card, $1 without. Bring
I.D.'s.
