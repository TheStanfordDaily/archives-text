# AWS Models Fall Fashions Tonight
## 
### 
A fashion show sponsored by
the Associated Women Students
will be hold tonight from 7 to 0
p.m. in Town and Country Vil
lage.
Members of AVVS will model
fall clothes donated by the Vil
lage shops. All modeling will be
done informally under the walk
ways.
Models will be Robbin Daniels,
Sally Drinker, Tara Fowler, Jan
Kerr, Jane Marks, Nancy Merik,
Molly Merrill, Judy Muller, Bar
bie Norman and Mareia See.
