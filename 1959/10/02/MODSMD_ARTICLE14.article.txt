# The 						Wandering Wonka Returns
## 
### 
A year never passes but War
ren G. Wonka's exploits come
to the fore . . . but this time he
started early, as evidenced by
the following which appeared
among the staff writeups in the
Stanford Hand handbook this
tall:
"Warren G. MacWonka: head
Scotch bass section; holder
Shuchat Legion of Merit
Award; professor of triangle,
tissue paper and comb, and ka
zoo, sez."
