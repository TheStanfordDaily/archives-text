# Rally Com Tells Plans
## 
### 
Something new has been added
to the card stunt section at all
home football games this year.
They're cinemascope cards which
will be used for special tricks and
effects.
Rally Com also sponsors the
gala Spring Sing held every spring
quarter. White -said that Rally
Com is considering the revival of
an old Stanford tradition, holding
a Winter Sports Rally.
Freshmen interested in joining
Rally Com can sign up'for inter
views in the ASSU office. They
will be interviewed October 12
through 14 from 1 to 6 and 7 to
10 p.m. Boasting a membership
of 65, the Rally Com plans to se
lect 21 freshmen.
Other officers this year are
Norma Auer, committee secre
tary; Pete Erlin. chairmart of
sales; A 1 Chambers, director of
cards; Taffy Simons, head of pub
licity; Steve Webster, director of
rallies; Lilly Glinsky. social chair
man; and Jack Chavey, head
usher.
