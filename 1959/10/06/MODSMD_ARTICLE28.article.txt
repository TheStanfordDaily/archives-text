# Witticisms Wanted In Gag Greetings
## 
### 
Campus comedians will have
an opportunity to make their wit
pay dividends as Rust Craft-Vol
land Greeting Card, Inc., looks
for greeting card gags.
Either actual illustrations or
captions with suggested illustra
tions will net contributors $15
apiece. Efforts should be mailed
in care of Dedham, Mass.
