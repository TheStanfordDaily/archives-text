# AWS Groups To Be Chosen
## 
### 
Interviews for selection of AYVS
committee members for the 1959-
60 year will be held from 7:30-9
p.m. today through Thursday in
room 10 of the Women's Club
house.
Applicants may sign up at the
ASSU office and in freshman
women's dormitories.
The scholarship committee is
in charge of raising money by
rummage sales and fashion shows
for a scholarship awarded to a
student active in campus govern
ment.
The housing committee studies
and recommends improvements
in women's living quarters. The
social regulations committee de
termines policies of women's late
leaves, overnights, and the de
merit system; and the publica
tion-elections committee is in
charge of AWS elections and pub
licity.
