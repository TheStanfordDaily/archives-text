# Swim Pool Hours
## 
### 
Open hours for the swimming
pool in the women's gym have
been announced. The pool will be
available to women on Tuesdays
and Thursdays from 2 to 4
p.m.; Fridays, 4 to 5 p.m.; and
Sundays and Saturdays of away
football games, 1 to 4 p.m.
Men may swim in the pool only
on weekends, accompanied by a
registered woman student. Dress
ing room facilities will not be
available on weekends, and swim
mers must provide their own suits
and towels.
Women must provide their own
caps.
