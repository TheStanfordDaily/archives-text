# Satchmo' Here Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Club of Palo Alto
will present Louis "Satchmo"
Armstrong at 8 p.m. tonight in
Memorial Auditorium. The con
cert is for the benefit of the Stan
f or d unrestricted scholarship
fund.
A limited number of $3.50 and
$5.00 tickets are still available for

tonight's performance. They may
be purchased at the Memorial
Auditorium box office between
12:30 and 4:30 p.m. Tickets will
be sold at the door if any are left.
Last year, $3000 in scholarships
was raised by Armstrong's ap
pearance. Because of this suc
cess he was asked to return.
