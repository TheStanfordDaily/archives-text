# Speech Assn. Holds Contest
## 
### 
The Stanford Speech Associa
tion is sponsoring the Ninth An
nual National Contest in Public
Discussion. This year's topic will
be "What Should Be the Role of
Government in Regulating Or
ganized Labor?
Five students will be selected
to represent Stanford against
schools throughout the U.S. The
contest is unique in that entries
will be tape recorded. The tapes
twill then he sent: to Chicago to
be judged by national speech
authorities.
The contest is open to all stu
dents. Interested persons may
contact Henry McGuckin, speech
department, mlo3 of Memorial
Auditorium on Monday, 9 a.m. to
11 a.m.
