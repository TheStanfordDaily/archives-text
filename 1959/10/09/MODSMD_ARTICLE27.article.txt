# Sports Car Rally
## 
### 
The Stanford Sports Car Club
is presenting its inaugural rally
of the season, The Liltle Big
Game Rally, Friday night, Oct. 9.
The rally will start from the
stadium parking lot at 2:30 p.m.
Rallyemaster Bill Cook describes
it as a simple time and distance
event designed especially for the
beginner. Entrance fee will be
50c and plaques will be given for
the first three places. Member
ship in SSCC or the Student Body
is required for entrance.
