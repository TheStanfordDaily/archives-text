# Stanford Faces Loyola Sunday In GE Bowl
## 
### 
Stanford faces Loyola of Los
Angeles Sunday at 1:30 p.m. in a
game of intellectual football on
the G.E. College Bowl. The pro
gram will be televised from Din
kelspiel Auditorium.
Last week the Stanford team
won the right to appear for the
second time on the program when
ihey defeated the University of
Oregon 195 to 110 last week.
The winning team will receive
$1500 for their scholarship fund
and the light to act as the "home
team" next Sunday. The losing
team will receive $500.
Tickets are being given out to
day at Engineering corner, the
Cellar and at Dinkelspiel Audi
torium box office which is open
from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m. Tickets
are free, but must be shown at
the door for admittance.
Professor Jon Ericson of the
Stanford Speech and Drama De
partment is coaching the Stan
ford team of Del Simonson, Roger
Dingman, George Green and Dan
White.
