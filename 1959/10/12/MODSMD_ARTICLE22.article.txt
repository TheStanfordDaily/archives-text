# Soccer-Polo
##

California trounced the Stan
ford tankmen, 23 to 5. Stan
ford's soecermen edged the
California Aggie team, 3-2 in
a hard-fought soccer match on
Saturday. Details of these
names will appear in the sports
section of tomorrow's Daily.
