# Ten Nominated For Moot Court
## 
### 
Ten second-year law students
have been named candidates to
the Moot Court Board. Selection
is made on the basis of high scho
lastic standing at the end of the
first year.
The Moot Court program offers
law students a chance to prepare
and argue hypothetical cases.
Semifinal and final rounds are
held before California state and
federal court judges.
Moot Court Board candidates
are Harry Donkers, Win Foster,
Chuck Grobc, Chuck Hurowitz,
Tom Ingledue, Larry Jones, Rob
Lancefield, Bob McGrouther, Tom
O'Keefe and Marv Siegel.
