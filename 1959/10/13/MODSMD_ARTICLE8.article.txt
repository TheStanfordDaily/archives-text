# String Quartet Appears Friday
## 
### 
The Stanford Music Depart
ment will open their 1959-60 con
cert season with a presentation
of 1 ho Feld string quartet Friday
at 8:30 p.m. in Dinkelspiel Audi
torium.
Tickets are available at Ihe
Peninsula Box Office in Palo Alto
and Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
This is the quartet's second
year of. concert appearances in
the United States, recently re
turning from a tour of Australia
and New Zealand.
The founder and director, Otto
Feld, plays first violin. The oth
ers are Janos Forgaes, second
violin; Gyorgy Szende, viola; and
Wladyslaw Przybyla, cello.
