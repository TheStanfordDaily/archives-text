# Grant Made to Hoover Library
## 
### 
The Hoover Institution on War,
Revolution and Peace is the re
cipient of a $250,000 grant from
the Max C. Fleischmann Founda
tion of Reno.
This latest award brings the
total of Fleischmann Foundation
gifts to Stanford University to
$851,000, of which $300,000 has
been to the Hoover Institution.
