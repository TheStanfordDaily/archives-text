# New Center Gets Medical Library
## 
### 
The largest library move un
dertaken at Stanford since the
Main Library was occupied in
1919 occurred last month when
the Lane Medical Library was
transferred from San Francisco
to its new quarters in the Med
Center.
At a cost of $17,963.38, 3K van
loads of books and periodicals
weighing over 500,000 pounds
were moved.
