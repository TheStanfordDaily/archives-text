# Professor to Give Oriental Art Talk
## 
### 
A world-famous authority on
the history of art, Professor Yu
kio Yashiro, will speak on "Budd
hism and Oriental Art" at 4:15
p.m. today in Cubberley Audito
rium.
This illustrated lecture will be
Yashiro's only appearance open
to the general public. He will
speak to faculty members and
students again Oct. 19 at 8 p.m.,
in Schultz Auditorium.
An authority on both Japanese
and Western Art, Yashiro is at
Stanford during October studying
the university's and other Bay
Area Oriental collections.
The Deputy Chairman of Ja
pan's National Commission for
Protection of Cultural Proper
ties, he is administrator of all
Japanese national museums.
