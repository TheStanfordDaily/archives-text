# O'Brien to Direct Fund Development
## 
### 
Richard F. O'Brien, former as
sociate general secretary at Stan
ford, has been named to the new
position of director of develop
ment.
He will be in charge of all
phases of the University's fund
raising program with the excep
tion of special gifts and bequests
to meet capital needs.
O'Brien holds advanced degrees
from Stanford and an A.B. from
the University of California at
Santa Barbara. He has been on
the staff here since 1918.
