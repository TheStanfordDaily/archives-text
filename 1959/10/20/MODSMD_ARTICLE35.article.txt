# Museum to Display New Acquisitions
## 
### 
One of the largest shows in
recent years, "Major Accessions,
1954-59," will open today at the
Stanford Museum.
The event, supported mainly by
the Committee for Art at Stan
ford, marks the fifth anniversary
of the museum's reopening.
More than 200 items will be in
cluded in the show, representing
gifts of (30 or more donors. They
range from pre-historic pottery to
contempory California paintings,
including works from the Orient,
Europe and America. Many of the
works have never before been
shown.
Museum hours are from 1 to 5
p.m. daily.
