# Indian Consul To Tour Campus
## 
### 
Indian Consul General C. J.
Stracey will arrive on campus to
morrow morning for a day of talk
and sightseeing with Stanford
faculty and students, Foreign
Student Coordinator Mrs. Barb
ara Given announced yesterday.
Stracey will start his tour at
Hoover Tower at 11:3 d, attend a
faculty luncheon al Union, view
the Medical Center and wind up
his day with an open house at I
p.m. at the International Center,
