# Symphony to Play Work by Ratner
## 
### 
Professor Leonard G. Ratner,
Professor of Music, will have his
"Harlequin Overture" performed
by the Monterey County Sym
phony under the baton of Earl B.
Murray on Oct. 28 and 29.
The overture had its premiere
performance in November, 19.17,
by tho Stanford Symphony Or
chestra and was played by the
San Francisco Symphony last
last spring.
His newest composition, "Con
certino for Trumpet and String
Orchestra," will be performed by
the San Francisco Symphony Or
chestra early next year.
