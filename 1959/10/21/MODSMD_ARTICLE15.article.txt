# Talk to be Given By TV Actress
## 
### 
Jan Mason, one of San Fran
cisco's leading women broadcast
ers and a former Stanford stu
dent, will discuss the "The Wom
en's Touch in Television," today
at 1 p.m. in room 204, Memorial
Ilall.
During the talk, she will show
filmed excerpts from her new
children's program, "Linkage."
The discussion is one of a series
conducted for students of broad
casting by Professor Stanley T.
Donner, director of radio and tele
vision. Interested students are in
vited to attend.
