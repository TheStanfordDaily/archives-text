# Steiner Takes New Position
## 
### 
Dr. Kurt Steiner, associate pro
fessor of the Department of
Political Science, has been made
Associate Executive Head of the
department.
Steiner's appointment was
made Sept. 1. He assists Dr. Rob
ert Walker, head of the depart
ment.
Dr. Steiner is an authority on
Japanese law and municipal gov
ernment. He served as a prose
cutor in the trial of the major
Japanese war criminals before
the International Military Tri
bunal in Tokyo in 1946-48.
He has written "Local Govern
ment in Japan" and is co-author,
with Professor Nobushige of
Toyo, of the forthcoming "Law
and Social Change in Japan."
