# USC Plane Ticket Pickup at Airport
## 
### 
Tickets for the Rally Commit
tee's plane to the USC football
game will be available at the air
port at the time of departure,
chairman Maynard Parker an
nounced yesterday.
The plane leaves Friday at 2:15
p.m. from San Francisco Inter
national Airport and arrives at
: Lockheed Air Terminal at 3:55
p.m. Students should claim their
j tickets by 1:45 p.m. at the Pacific
Southwest Airlines counter.
