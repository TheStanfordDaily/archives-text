# Off-Campus Club Offers Relaxation
## 
### 
The Off-Campus Club offers a
lounge where all off-campus stu
dents may gather for discussions
and refreshments. Located in the
Nitery just above the Cellar, the
lounge is open from 8 a.m. to 1
a.m., daily.
All off-campus men and women,
graduates and undergraduates,
including residents of the Stan
ford Village and campus people
who do not live in the dorms,
have been invited to stop by at
any time. The lounge provides a
co-op where soft drinks and food
are available.
Off-campus students may join
the club free for one quarter and
thereafter pay $.1 per year to con
tinue membership.
