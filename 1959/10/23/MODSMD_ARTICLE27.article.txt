# Woodcut Exhibit To Open Tuesday
## 
### 
Woodcut illustrations for
Dante's "Divine Comedy," pro
duced by the Polish artist Stefan
Mrozewski, go on display at the
Stanford Art Gallery Tuesday
and will stay through Nov. 15.
The show is the first exhibition
of the complete set of illustrat
ions, which took Mrozewski near
ly 20 years to produce.
