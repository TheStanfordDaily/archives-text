# Native Moroccan Art Exhibit Ends
## 
### 
Sunday will be the final day of
the Stanford Art Gallery's ex
hibit of native Moroccan art. On
display are rugs, fabrics, jewelry,
brass and woodwork which depict
Spanish, Moorish and Mid-East
ern styles in colorful and geomet
ric motifs.
The gallery is open Tuesday
through Saturday from 10 a.m. to
5 p.m., and Sunday from noon to
4 p.m. It is closed Mondays.
