# New Book Sale At Library Today
## 
### 
More than 700 new books will
go on sale in the lower lobby of
the Main Library at 8 a.m. today.
The books are all 1959 editions
and will be sold at half retail
price.
Books will be sold unlil closing
time at 10 p.m. today. If any are
left over, they will be sold to
morrow beginning at 8 a.m.
