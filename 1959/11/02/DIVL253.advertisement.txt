_Airline Stewardesses
UNITED AIR LINES offers its Steward
esses an opportunity to travel, visit beau
tiful cities and meet interesting people.
If you are attractive and personable, between 20 and
26 years of age, single and between 5'2" and s'B", you
may qualify.
Openings available in Winter, Spring and
Summer classes.
Please contact the Stanford University Placement
Office for additional information and application.
