# '60 Calendar Sale Will Start Today
## 
### 
The 1960 Cap and Gown cal
endars will go on sale for $1.73
today at the Post Office, the
Cellar, and at Engineering
Corner.
They will be sold for two
weeks, Monday through Friday
from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m., to raise
funds for the support of an ASSU
foreign scholar and also for a
scholarship for an American stu
dent under the sponsorship of the
Cap and Gown, women's honor
society.
