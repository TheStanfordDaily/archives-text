# Lecture on India Re-set for Friday
## 
### 
Madame Vijaya Lakshmi Pan
dit, India's high commissioner to
London, has re-scheduled her
Stanford lecture, which she can
celed last week.

Obligations," is now set for Fri
day at 4:13 p.m. in Memorial Au
ditorium.
Tickets are once again on sale
at Memorial Auditorium box of
fice at $1 for students and $1.23
for general admission.
