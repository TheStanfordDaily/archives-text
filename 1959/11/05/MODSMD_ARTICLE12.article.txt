# Challenge Debates Open to Students
## 
### 
A "Challenge Debate Series"
to provide the general student
body an arena for debating any
vital issues is being sponsored by
the Speech Association.
Students wishing to express
opinions on national or interna
tional affairs will be provided
with a room and a chairman by
the association.
To register a challenge or to
accept one, call or write the presi
dent of Speech Association, Mike
Driscoll, at 742 Stern.
