# Folk Musicians To Play Tonight
## 
### 
The Southern folk music con
ceit featuring Brownie McGhee
guitarist, and Sonny Terry, har
monica player, will be held to
night at 8 p.m. in Cubberly Aud
itorium.
The program will feature spirit
uals. blues, and ballads.
McGhee, from Tennessee, sang
and acted in the Broadway version
of "Cat on a Hot Tin Roof." Terry,
who lost his sight in a childhood
accident, has made his debut in
Carnegie Hall.
The team, who records for Folk
ways and Elect ra labels, has re
cently returned from London and
will soon leave for a slate depart
ment tour of India.
Tickets cost $2.00, $1.50 for stu
dents and will be sold at the door.
