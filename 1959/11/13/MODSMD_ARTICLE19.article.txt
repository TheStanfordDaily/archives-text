# 'History's Key' Lecture Topic
## 
### 
Sir George Trevelyan, British
social historian, will lecture on
"Architectural Style as a Key to
History" at 7:30 p.m. tonight in
Cubberley Auditorium.
Trevelyan is warden of the
Shropshire Adult College at At
tingham, one of a group of Brit
ish adult education centers which
offer varied short courses. A
Cambridge graduate, Trevelyan
has taught history, literature, and
allied subjects.
A leader in the movement to
save the historic houses of Eng
land, Trevelyan was a co-founder
of a national trust for their
preservation. His school at At ting
ham is a former Georgian man
sion.
