# Letters to The Editor
## 
### 
Pigs Hike Costs

Editor, The Daily:
May I vehemently concur in
the disgust Mr. Meiner feels
about the waste of food in our
Universities? I wish it would oc
cur to some of these pigs that
they are part of the reason the
cost of room and board is so
high.
Karen Kraushopf
