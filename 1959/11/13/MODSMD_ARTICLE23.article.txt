# Fresno Tourney Draws Debaters
## 
### 
This weekend the Stanford de
bale squad will go to the Fresno
Invitational debate tournament,
trying for a repeat of last year's
first place position.
This, the first major tourna
ment of the year, will include 35
schools and colleges from five
western states. Stanford is send
ing ten teams, each of which will
debate eight rounds. Each team
will receive oral and written
critiques on each round.
