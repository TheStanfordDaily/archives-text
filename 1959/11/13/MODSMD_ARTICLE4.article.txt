# Sunday Night Flicks Announced
## 
### 
The Committee on Public Ex
cercises has announced the Sun
day night flicks for the rest of
autumn quarter.
This Sunday's flick will be "The
Tunnel of Love," the story of
Doris Day's and Gig Young's at
tempts to adopt a baby.
The remaining flicks during
this quarter will be: Nov. 22, "The
Proud and the Profane" with Bill
Holden and Deborah Kerr; Nov.
29, "Shane" starring Alan Ladd;
and Dec. 6, "The Mating Game,"
with Debbie Reynolds and Tony
Randall.
