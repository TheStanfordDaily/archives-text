# Talk on Pacifism
## 
### 
Arlo Tatum, general secretary
of the War .Resistors' Interna
tional, will speak at the Women's
Clubhouse tonight at 7:30.
The speech will be sponsored
by the Socialist Caucus of the
Political Union. Tatum's talk will
be on the subject of "Pacifism
and the Cold War." The public
is invited and a general discussion
will follow the speech.
