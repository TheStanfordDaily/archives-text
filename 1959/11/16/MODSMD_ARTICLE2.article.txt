# Big Game Calendar
## 
### 
Here's a running schedule of Big Game Week activities; tape it to
your mirror so you won't miss a single event of the year's most
hectic week.
TODAY
Football Night. Varsity team members dine with women in their
dorms. So you can better "Prepare for the Bear," the Library will
stay open until 11 p.m.
TUESDAY
Red and White Day. Students, faculty and staff will dress in Stan
ford color, cardinal (which can be stretched to include common red
if that's all you have), set off with white. Frosh Rally in Wilbur-
Branner area at 9:30 p.m., with band, cheer leaders, and pompon
girls. "Big Game Gaieties" opens 1959 run in Memorial Auditorium,
8:30 p.m. Libe again open until 11 p.m.
WEDNESDAY
Gaieties second performance, Mem Aud, 8:30 p.m. Last night on
which Libe will stay open 'til 11.
THURSDAY
Axe Day. "Bury the Bear" funeral procession leaves Inner Quad
at 11:50 a.m. for the Post Office where Bill Kartozian will officiate
at "bearial" ceremony. Axe Society distributes "Beat Cal" buttons
on Quad. Gaieties, Mem Aud, 8:30 p.m.
FRIDAY
Burn the Bear Day. Matchbooks distributed on Quad. Early
dinner in dorms. Week's final rally at Boathouse, 6:30 p.m. with
Stanford band, cheer leaders, pom pon girls, the story of The Axe,
followed by lighting of traditional bonfire in lake bed. All-campus
Big Game Dance, music of Sylvester Grisby, Campus and Lasuen
Street on the Row. Gaieties final performance, Mem Aud, 9 p.m.
SATURDAY
House decoration judging, 9:30 a.m. Living group open houses.
62nd annual Big Game, Stanford Stadium, 1:30 p.m.

which Libe will stay open 'til 11.
THURSDAY
Axe Day. "Bury the Bear" funeral procession leaves Inner Quad
at 11:50 a.m. for the Post Office where Bill Kartozian will officiate
at "bearial" ceremony. Axe Society distributes "Beat Cal" buttons
on Quad. Gaieties, Mem Aud, 8:30 p.m.
FRIDAY
Burn the Bear Day. Matchbooks distributed on Quad. Early
dinner in dorms. Week's final rally at Boathouse, 6:30 p.m. with
Stanford band, cheer leaders, pom pon girls, the story of The Axe,
followed by lighting of traditional bonfire in lake bed. All-campus
Big Game Dance, music of Sylvester Grisby, Campus and Lasuen
Street on the Row. Gaieties final performance, Mem Aud, 9 p.m.
SATURDAY
House decoration judging, 9:30 a.m. Living group open houses.
62nd annual Big Game, Stanford Stadium, 1:30 p.m.
Here's a running schedule of Big Game Week activities; tape it to
your mirror so you won't miss a single event of the year's most
hectic week.

Football Night. Varsity team members dine with women in their
dorms. So you can better "Prepare for the Bear," the Library will

girls. "Big Game Gaieties" opens 1959 run in Memorial Auditorium,
8:30 p.m. Libe again open until 11 p.m.
