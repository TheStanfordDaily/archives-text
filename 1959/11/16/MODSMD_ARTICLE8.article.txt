###

last days seniors may have color
portraits taken for the Quad.

ment of Burbank Mouse. Wing S of
Stern Hall. No appointment is
necessary. The sitting fee is $2.50.

undergraduate portraits will be I
taken. Frosh Books may be picked
up at the Quad office Wednesday
and Thursday from 9 to 12 a.m.
and 1 to 6 p.m.
