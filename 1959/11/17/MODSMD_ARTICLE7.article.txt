# Orchestra, Choir Tickets Available
## 
### 
Tickets are now on sale at the
Dinkelspiel box office for the two
December concerts of the Stan
ford symphony orchestra and the
chorus. The office is open daily
from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m.
The 75-piece orchestra will pre
sent its concert on Friday, Dec.
4, in Dinkelspiel Auditorium at
8:30 p.m.
The 200-voice chorus will ap
pear Sunday, Dec. 6, at 3:30 p.m.
Prices are 50 cents general ad
mission and 25 cents for students.
