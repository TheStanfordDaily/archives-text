# Behavior Lab Set for March
## 
### 
A laboratory for the study of
the behavior of small groups will
be ready for use by University so
ciologists by March, 1960, accord
ing to Sanford M. Dornbusch,
head of the sociology department.
The new laboratory will resem
ble a conference room, with a
table and easy chairs. It will be
fitted with devices to record and
photograph the reactions of the
small groups being studied.
Sociologist's will observe the
groups through one-way mirrors.
A computer will help researchers
analyze basic behavior patterns.
