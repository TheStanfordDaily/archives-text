# Skip Face Named Top Back of Week
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO (/P) Stan
ford's Skip Face, who scored 27
points against Oregon Slate last
Saturday. Monday was named the
Big Five's Back of the Week.
The speedy fullback scored
three touchdowns, kicked four
conversions, tallied a two-point
conversion, and hooted a field
goal in his team's 39-22 victory.
UCLA end Marv Luster was
named lineman of the week for
his play against North Carolina
State.
