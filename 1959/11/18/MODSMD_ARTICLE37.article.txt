# Freedom Leader To Discuss Crisis
## 
### 
Joshua Nkomo, president uf the
African National Congress of
Southern Rhodesia, will speak on
"The Crisis in Central Africa" to
morrow at 4:13 p.m. in Cubberly
Auditorium.
Nkomo, one of the leaders of
the African independence move
ment, has held his present posi
tion since 1954, and has also
served as head of the African
Railway Workers Union. Last
year he attended the All African
People's Conference and was
elected to the Steering Committee.
The talk is sponsored jointly by
the Institute of International Re
lations and the Political Union.
