# Refs Named For Contest
## 
### 
How important are Ihe officials
in a football game? Just ask any
one who went to last year's Big
Game.
This year, however, the Stan
ford athletic department has ac
quired the services of the besl in
the business. Cal will have to win
without any outside help from
men dressed as zebras.
Head referee for Big Game is
Peter Bateye. He only works one
game a year and goes into prac
tice blowing his whistle and doing
his eye exercises about three
weeks before the game.
Other officials are Joseph Sam
is from Changeable, Mo., Hooberl
Hecver from Bad Luck, Ark.,
Phillip Hangnail out of Fountain,
Penn., and Jack Infraction from
Clipping. Mich.
