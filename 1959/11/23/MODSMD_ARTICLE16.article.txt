# Moot Court Cases Presented Tonight
## 
### 
Twelve third year law students
will present oral arguments to
night and Tuesday and Wednes
day evenings on the case of
McElroy, Secretary of Defense, v.
United States ex. rel. Guagliardo,
a case now pending before the
Supreme Court of the United
States.
This is the Marion Rice Kirk
wood Competition, designed to
give law students an opportunity
for training and practice in brief
ing and arguing cases on appeal.
It poses the question whether
civilians working abroad with the
armed forces may be subject to
court martial jurisdiction.
The Stanford Law Society of
Northern California awards prizes
to the winners of the competition
which is administered by the
Stanford Moot Court Board.
