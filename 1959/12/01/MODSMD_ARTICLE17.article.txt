# 'Nothing to Tell' On Opsahl Death
## 
### 
Deputy District Attorney Ernest
Schmidt yesterday refused to
comment on the death of John
Opsahl. "There is nothing I can
tell you at tlie present time" he
said. "I will make no further
comments until there are some
new developments."
Schmidt is handling the case
for the Santa Clara County Dis
trict Attorney's office. The case
was turned over to the District
Attorney last week by Stanford
Police.
