# Symphony Ticket Exchange Planned
## 
### 
The Concert Series Office has
announced a ticket exchange for
those persons wishing to buy or
sell tickets to any San Francisco
Symphony or Stanford Concert
Series performance.
The Concert Series Office will
attempt to sell any tickets turned
in if they are brought to tho
Nitery before the performance.
Those desiring to purchase indi
vidual tickets should come to the
Nitery.
