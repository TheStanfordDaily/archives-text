# Composer to Talk On Persian Music
## 
### 
Hermoz Farhat, authority on
Persian music, will lecture on his
specialty at 4:15 tomorrow at
Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Farhat, presently doing re
search at UCLA, has studied
under the composers Darius Mil
haud, at Mills, and Lukas Foss,
at UCLA. He has several compo
sitions to his credit both in eon
temporary and Persian styles.
The lecture is free, open to the
public, and is sponsored by Ihe
Music Department.
