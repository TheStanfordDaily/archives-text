# Arab Publicist To Speak Tonight
## 
### 
Mohammad T. Mehdi, director
of the Arab Information Center
in San Francisco, will speak on
"The U.S.S.R., the U.S.A., and the
Middle East" tonight at 8 p.m. at
ihe Palo Alto Unitarian Church.
The talk is sponsored by the Palo
Alto Unitarian Laymen's League
and is open to the public.
Mehdi, a native of Iraq, has
taught Political Science at the
University of California. He is the
author of many articles on Amer
ican-Arab relations.
