# Curtain to Lower On O'Casey Play
## 
### 
The last Iwo performances of
Sean O'Casey's "I Knock at the
Door" will be given tonight and
tomorrow night at 8:30 by the
Stanford Players in the Little
Theater of Memorial Auditorium.
The drama is in the form of a
series of dramatic flashbacks,
woven together by a narrator,
played by Juris Svendsen, and is
directed by Dr. James Kerans.
Other members of the cast of
six, who play a total of 31 roles,
are Paul Hennig, Eugene Pey
roux, Steven Gilborn, Elizabeth
Forest, and Dinah Peyroux.
Tickets priced at $1.25 and $1.75
are on sale at Memorial Auditor
ium Box Office.
