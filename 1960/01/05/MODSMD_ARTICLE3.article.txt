# 'The Entertainer' On Tap Tomorrow
## 
### 
John Osborne's popular "The
Entertainer," featuring Robert
Symonds as Archie Rice, a down
at-heel English music hall actor,
will be enacted tomorrow at
8:30 p.m. in Memorial Auditorium.
Symonds will be supported by
a cast from the San Francisco
Actor's Workshop. Students may
purchase tickets for $1.75 at
Memorial Hall box office. Tomor
row's performance will be the
only one.
