# Workshop Offers 'The Entertainer'
## 
### 
A rather downtrodden English
music hall actor played by Robert
Symonds will be the principle
figure in Memorial Auditorium
tonight when the Actor's Work
shop presents John Osborne's
"The Entertainer."
An original Actor's Workshop
production, the play will begin
at. 8:30 p.m. Tickets are still
available at Memorial Hall Box
Office at a student rate of $1.75
for the single performance.
