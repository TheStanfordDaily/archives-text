# A 						Phi O Exchange Open for Last Day
## 
### 
Today is the last day of the A
Phi O book exchange in the tem
porary building A-30.
Books will be sold from 9 a.m.
to noon and 1 to 4 p.m. New books
to be sold will only be received
until noon.
Books needed at the exchange
include Sears & Zam, Universal
Physics, psychology 1 books, busi
ness law books, German 1 books,
and general zoology books. Also
needed are history books other
than civ and books for religion
courses.
