# Museum, Gallery Show Art Items
## 
### 
Western Serigraphs, (silk
prints) will be exhibited at the
Stanford Art Gallery through Jan.
17. Permanently featured is the
Mortimer C. Leventritt Collection
of European and Oriental Art.
The Gallery is open daily 10-5;
Sunday 12-4. 11 is closed Monday.
The Stanford Museum is now
featuring art objects acquired
during the last five years. They
include collections of classical and
Oriental art objects and various
anthropological collections.
The Museum is open daily 1-5;
faculty, students, and University
personnel are admitted free of
charge.
