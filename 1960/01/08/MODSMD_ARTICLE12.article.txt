# Pro Musica Plays Sunday
## 
### 
Medieval music from Spain,
Germany and France will be
heard in Dinkelspiel Auditorium
Sunday afternoon when the New
York Pro Musica presents a con
cert at 3:30 p.m.
The program will also include
English madrigals and sacred
church music of the middle ages
The ten-member group consists
of both instrumentalists and vo
calists and is under the direction
of Noah Greenberg.
Rated as the foremost protlay
ers of medieval. Renaissance and
baroque music, the New York Pro
Musica has been featured on the
"Omnibus" television series.
Tickets for the event are still on
sale at Dinkelspiel Box Office at
$2 for students.
