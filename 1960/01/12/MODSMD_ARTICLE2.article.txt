# 'Mass Education' Theme of Hostel
## 
### 
"The College Student in an PJra
of Mass Education" will be the
theme of this weekend's hostel, to
be held at the YWCA Camp near
La Honda. Discussions will be led
by H. Donald Winbigler, Dean of
Students, and Chester Berry,
director of the Student Union.
There is room for 15 students
on the hostel. Those interested
may sign up in the ASSU office
today and tomorrow and until
noon on Thursday. Cost is $3.50
per student.
Discussions will center on gen
eral needs of students in univer
sities throughout the county. Such
problems as growing enrollments,
rising entrance requirements, and
student-faculty relationships will
be considered.
