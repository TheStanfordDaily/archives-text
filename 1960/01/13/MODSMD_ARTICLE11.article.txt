# Tickets Available For Soph Cotillion
## 
### 
Tickets for- "An Evening in the
City," the Sophomore Cotillion to
be held Friday night in the St.
Francis Hotel, are now on sale at
Engineering Corner for $4.50 per
couple with a sophomore class
card and $5 without.
The Cotillion, an all-campus
dance, will feature songstress Ella
Fitzgerald and music by Ray
Hackett and his Orchestra.
