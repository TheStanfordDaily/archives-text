# IIR Sponsors Coffee Break
## 
### 
The lIR is sponsoring a coffee
hour this afternoon at Bowman
Alumni House, 4:30-6:30 p.m. Re
freshments will he served and
the atmosphere informal.
Saturday night from 8:00 to
12:00 p.m. there will he a Danish
Fireside at tlie I-Center. These
informal gatherings are weekly
activities of the lIR and feature
a specific country each week.
