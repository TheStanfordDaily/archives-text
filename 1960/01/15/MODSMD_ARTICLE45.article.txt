# Show Displays New Art Gifts
## 
### 
A rare Flemish triptych and a
Belgian portrait tapestry are
among recent gifts of art to the
Stanford University Muse u m
from Mr. and Mrs. Arthur Wies
enberger of New York City.
They will be displayed with
other works through Jan. 31 as a
part of the "Major Accessions"
show of the Committee for Art at
Stanford.
The triptych, dated Flanders
1520, is a three panel oil-on-wood
painting of the "Adoration of the
Magi." It is the work of an un
known 16th century Antwerp
Man nerist.
The Belgian tapestry portrait
was done from a 17th century
Rubens painting.
