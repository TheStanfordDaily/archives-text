# Adv. 18 Page 4

The story of a great university from the laying of the
cornerstone for its first quadrangle to the breaking of
ground for the Stanford Medical Center, a biography not
only of an institution but also of the dedicated men and
women who built it. Illustrated with photographs.
Edith R. Mirriclecs, herself a graduate of Stanford, has
been a member of the English faculty since 1909.
