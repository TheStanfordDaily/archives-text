# Braves Win Two Weekend Encounters
## 
### 
The Stanford Braves basketball
team came out victorious in its
two weekend encounters. Friday
night they trounced Moffett Field.
63-40. Saturday night the Braves
trimmed Modesto J. C., 63-50.

Nels Christenson and Mike
Ledgerwood led the Braves in
scoring Saturday night with 14
points apiece, followed by Bill
Cover with 13.
