# Gallery Features 'Northwest' Show
## 
### 
An exhibit of thirty-five paint
ings selected from the Seattle
Art Museum opens today at the
Art Gallery. The display, "North
west Painters Today," will be
shown through Feb. 4.
Prize-winning paintings include
"Mountain Stream" by Neil Meitz-
J ler, "Summer in Kyoto" by Paul
I Horiuchi, "Mountain Greenery"
I by Boyer Gonzales, and "Great
Circle" by James McGarrell.
The Art Gallery is open Tues
day through Saturday, 10 a.m. to
5 p.m., and Sunday from noon to
4 p.m. Admission is free.
