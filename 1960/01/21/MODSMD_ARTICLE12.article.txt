# Film Series Puts On Show Today
## 
### 
"The Devil's General" and"The
Bespoke Overcoat" will be shown
today at 4:15 and 7:30 p.m. and
tomorrow at 4:15 p.m. in Cub
berley Auditorium as the third
program in the Revival Film Ser
ies.
"The Devil's General" is based
on the life of German air ace
Ernst Udet and depicts the politi
cal struggle between Goering and
Himmler for control of the air
force and the decadent Nazi so
ciety in Berlin.
"The Bespoke Overcoat," an
English film, is a dramatic fan
tasy of a tailor revisited by a
deceased friend.
