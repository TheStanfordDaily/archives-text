# Frosh to Get Extra Units
## 
### 
Freshmen entering Stanford
next fall may be allowed up to 45
units credited toward their bache
lor's degree if their qualifications
warrant it.
The new University policy,
which can award sophomore
standing on entrance, is an in
crease of 30 units over the 15
allowed talented freshmen since
1956.
Applicants' qualifications will
be based on results of the Uni
versity's foreign language and
mathematics placement tests or
those of the National College
Board Advanced Placement ex
aminations in all fields.
Last fall. 108 entering fresh
man submitted advance placement
exams, of whom 69 received vary
ing amounts of credit.
