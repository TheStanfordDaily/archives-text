# Teacher Study To Be Offered
## 
### 
High school and college level
mathematics and science teachers
will have a chance this summer to
take a new look at modern teach
ing concepts in six-week courses
to be given at Stanford.
The 100 math teachers and 60
physics teachers selected will
have all tuition and fees paid, a
stipend of $75 a week, and allow
ances for dependents and mileage.
Support for the program comes
from the National Science Foun
dation, whose aim is to refresh
the teachers in their respective
fields as well as to increase their
teaching skills. Credit toward an
advanced degree will be given.
Professor Harold M. Bacon will
be the director of the mathe
matics institute, and Professor
Paul H. Kirkpatrick will be di
rector of the physics institute.
