# Joffre Debate Tryouts Set
## 
### 
Tryouts for the 66th Annual
Joffre Debate between Stanford
and Cal will be held Thursday at
5 p.m. in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
This is the first time in the his
tory of the debate that all stu
dents are eligible to tryout.
Topic of the debate is, "Re
solved: that the powers of the
assembly should be increased."
J. N. Ericson, in announcing
the tryouts, advised that speakers
could debate either side of the
topic, and that speech length
would be restricted to seven min
utes, with a penalty for over
time. He suggested that prospec
tive speakers sign up on the
bulletin board outside of room 103
in Memorial Hall.
