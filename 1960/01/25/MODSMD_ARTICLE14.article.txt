# Vaccination Slips Due at Reg Office
## 
### 
Feb. 1 is the deadline for filing
vaccination certificates without
fine, according to the Registrar's
Office. Students filing certificates
between Feb. 1 and Feb. 8 will
be fined $2.
Students who have not filed
certificates by Feb. 8 may have
their registration canceled. This
applies only to students register
ing for the first time this quarter.
