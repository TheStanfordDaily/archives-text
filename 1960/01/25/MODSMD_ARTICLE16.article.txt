# Play Tickets Still on Sale
## 
### 
Tickets will continue on sale
this week for the forthcoming
production of Dylan Thomas'
"Under Milkwood," to be given by
the Stanford Players in Memori
al Auditorium Thursday through
Friday nights at 8:30.
The play takes place in a small
Welsh sea coast town and con
sists of short glimpses into the
lives of its more-than-50 inhabi
tants who will be played by a cast
of 20 actors and actresses.
The dramatic sequences are
knit together into a complete pic
ture of the town by two narrators
played by Alec Ross and Gordon
Adams. Directing the play is A.
D. Sensenbach.
Seats, priced at $1.25 and $1.75,
may be reserved at Memorial
Auditorium Box Office. DA 3-0665,
weekday afternoons between
12:30 and 4:30.
