# Frosh Scheduled To Discuss Bible
## 
### 
All Freshmen living groups are
holding religious discussions this
week, the third week in a six
week Bible Colloquia sponsored
by nearby college chaplains.
Many of the groups have
changed the time they will meet.
On Monday at 9 p.m. Soto will
meet in their lounge and at 10
p.m. Junipero will meet in J 112.
Cedro will meet on Tuesday at
9:15 p.m. in their lounge. Both
Branner and Roble will meet on
Tuesday at 9:30 p.m. in the Bran
ner library and in R 174. At 10
p.m. Tuesday Arroyo will meet in
the Faculty resident's home and
Rinconada will meet in the
lounge.
Otero will meet in their lounge
on Wednesday at 7 p.m. and Ma
dera will meet on Wednesday at
7:15 p.m. in M 210.
