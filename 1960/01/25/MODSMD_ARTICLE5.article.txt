# Segovia Slates Stanford Concert
## 
### 
Andres Segovia, world-famous
classical guitarist, will give a con
cert here on Feb. 7 in Memorial
Auditorium.
Tickets, at $2.75 apiece, go on
sale today at 1 p.m. in the Nitery.
Geoffrey Naab, who is in charge
of concert series, expects to be
sold out within an hour and ad
vises interested people to come
early; only 75 tickets are avail
able.
Segovia will play numbers by
Bach, Haydn, Mendelssohn, Tor
rcga, Sor, San/., and many other
well-known composers.
