# McAfees to Lead ‘Y’ Seminar Today
## 
### 
Professor and Mrs. Malcolm
McAfee will discuss the "Expecta
tions and Responsibility Between
Men and Women," today at 4:15
on the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse.
The discussion is the first of
three Monday afternoon seminars
to be presented by the YWCA as
a follow-up to the talk given by
Dr. and Mrs. Harry Rathbun on
Jan. 11 that dealt with the prob
lems of the sexes.
On Feb. 1, Dr. and Mrs. Jeffrey
Smith will discuss "Before Mar
riage: Inevitable Loneliness," and
concluding the series will be Dr.
and Mrs. Edwin Good who will
analyze "Pre-Marital Morality."
