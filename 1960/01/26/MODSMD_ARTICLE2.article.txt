# Volkart to Speak On Society, Disease
## 
### 
Professor Ed m un d Volkart,
head of the recently formed Stan
ford Program in Medicine and
the Behavioral Sciences, will
speak tonight at 7:30 on "Disease
and Social Stress" in the Physics
Lecture Hall.
Volkart, former head of the
Sociology Department who came
to Stanford in 1951, will analyze
cultural, political and religious
factors which tend to increase
or decrease various diseases.
The lecture is sponsored by
Sigma Phi Sigma, pre - medical
fraternity.
