# Post Office Plans Opening Feb. 11
## 
### 
The new Stanford Post Office
is expected to open on Feb. 11
according to Superintendent John
Callahan. He stated that as the
new building contains over 4.000
boxes, every student will be re
quired to rent one. General de
livery will be reserved for tran
sients who are staying 30 days
or less.
Students who do not have a box
at present can obtain one at either
stamp window from 9 a.m. to 4
p.m. Rates will remain at 35 cents
per quarter until July 1 when
they will go up to $1.20. For
people who wish to notify cor
respondents. yellow change-of
address cards are available.
