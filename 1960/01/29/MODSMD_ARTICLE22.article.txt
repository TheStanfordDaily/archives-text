# Museum Chief to Speak
## 
### 
George Culler. Director of the
San Francisco Museum of Art,
will speak tonight at the museum
on its current show of Modern
European Painting. This is the
first of a series of lectures for
the Stanford Student Art Asso
ciation.
Members and other interested
students are to meet at the Stan
ford post office at 7 p.m. for trans
portation, or at the museum in
the War Memorial Building, Civic
Center at 8 p.m.
