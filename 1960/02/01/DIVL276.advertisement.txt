OPPORTUNITIES
for
ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONIC
ENGINEERS
A newly organized Southern California Corporation is being staffed
with competent engineering personnel who will participate in the following
programs:
Consultants to Navy Spccial Projects Office on the
Polaris Missile System.
Design of Training Aids for Electronic Test Equipment.
Preparation of Operation and Maintenance Information.
Company Paid Benefits Include: Company is staffed with engineers,
Life insurance administered by engineers, and is
Health Insurance housed in an engineering facility.
Key employee stock options
Interviews will be held February 3, 1960 at the Placement
Center. Further information is available at the Placement
Center.
