# Earth Series Shuts Today
## 
### 
One of Ihe nation's top geolo
gists who is also provost of Rico
Institute of Houston, Dr. Carey
Croneis, will deliver the fifth in
a new series of Earth Sciences
Lectures on campus today.
He will discuss "Mankind in
Scientific Perspective," combining
biological and geological interests,
at 4 p.m. in Dinkelspiel Audito
rium. The public is invited, ad
mission free.
Tomorrow Dr. Croneis also will
present the succeeding lecture in
the series at 7:30 p.m. in Memor
ial Auditorium. His subject then
will be "South America People,
; Resources, and Problems."
He is the author or coauthor
of "Down to Earth," "Paleozoic
Geology of Arkansas," and num
erous professional articles. He is
editor of the Bulletin of the Amer
ican Association of Petroleum
Geologists, associate editor of the
Journal of Geology, and editor of
i Harper & Bros. Geoseience Series.
