# SCF Sponsoring Snow Weekend
## 
### 
A snow hostel sponsored by the
Stanford Christian Fellowship
will take place Feb. 12-11 at
Zephyr Cove, Lake Tahoe, Ne\.
The cost of $10.25 includes lodg
ing, food and insurance.
All students are invited to par
ticipate in this weekend of ex
ploration into the nature of the
Christian faith. The main speaker
will be Earl Palmer, a graduate
of IJ.C., presently serving as
Minister to Students at First
Presbyterian Church in Seattle.
Plenty of time will be allotted
for leisure activities such as ski
ing at nearby Heavenly Valley;
the Olympic trials are less than
an hour's drive away.
Further information can be ob
tained by calling Bill Carr, DA
6-8871.
