# Travel Week Starts Mon.
## 
### 
The Stanford Overseas Infor
mation Service has many pro
grams planned during its "Travel
Week," Feb. 8-12, for students
interested in foreign travel and
education.
Dr. Robert A. Walker opens
the program Monday night at 8
p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium by
discussing aspects of the three
Stanford overseas campuses.
Tuesday afternoon at 4:30 in
Cubberley Auditorium members
of the Information Service will
talk about other foreign study
progams offered.
Bernie Pechter, West Coast Di
rector of the United States Na
tional Student Association, will
show a film concerning his or
ganization's foreign education
trip plans Wednesday night at
7:30 in Cubberley Auditorium.
For the last of the four pro
grams, a student panel will dis
cuss the American student
abroad, followed by a question
and answer period.
