# Students To Pre-Reg Next Week
## 
### 
Feb. 29-March 5 are the dates
for Spring quarter preregistra
tion, the Registrar's Office an
nounced today. All students must
consult their advisors on Wednes
day, Thursday and Friday of next
week. Students may obtain their
study-list cards at this lime.
Students who do not see their
advisors on one of these three
days will forfeit the privilege of
preregist ration.
Spring quarter times schedules
will be available in Ihe Informa
tion Office, room 111-A. Saturday
from 8 a.m. till noon. Class lisls
must be signed for each course
marked in the lime schedule.
Juniors, seniors and graduates
will sign class lists on Feb. 29
and March 1 according to the fol
lowing schedule:
L-R Monday morning.
S-Z Monday afternoon.
A-D Tuesday morning.
E-K Tuesday afternoon.
Freshmen and sophomores will
sign class lists March 2 through
noon March 7. As usual, times
will be stamped on their study
lists. They may sign class lists at
this time or any time thereafter.
Students not required to .sign
class lists may file study lists in
the Information Office at any
time. Deadline for filing is .noon
March 7.
