# Commuter Bus
## 
### 
Stanford students and faculty
are invited to ride a chartered
commuter bus between San Fran
cisco and Stanford Village in Men
lo Park. The bus leaves weekdays
from Van Ness Ave. in the City
at 7:10 a.m.. arriving in Menlo
Park at 7:55. The return trip
leaves at 5:05 p.m.
Riders can buy monthly tickets,
half-monthly tickets or single
rides. Those interested should call
C. J. Maisel, DA 6-6200, ex. 2261.
