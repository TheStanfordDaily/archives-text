# 'Liberalism' Topic Of Demo Program
## 
### 
Stanford Area Young Demo
crats will hold the first of their
new series of programs at 7:30
tonight in Burbank Lounge, Wing
8 of Stern Hall.
Dr. William McCord, assistant
professor of sociology and assist
ant dean of the humanities and
sciences, will speak to the group
on the subject of "Liberalism: the
Creative Force in American Poli
tics." Following the speech there
will be opportunities for ques
tions and discussion.
