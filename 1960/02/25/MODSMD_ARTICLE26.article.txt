# Arabic Professor To Speak Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. Irfan Kawar, Professor of
Arabic from UCLA, will speak
tonight on "Muhammad and Alex
ander A Chapter in Cultural
Encounters" at 8 p.m. in Room
320, Geology.
The talk, sponsored by the De
partment of Religion and the De
partment of Asian Languages,
will deal with the interaction of
Hellenistic and Islamic cultures.
Kawar, originally from Pales
tine, did his undergraduate work
at Oxford and completed his
graduate studies at Cambridge.
The talk is open to the public.
