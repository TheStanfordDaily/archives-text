# 3 Performances Set for Weekend
## 
### 
Daniel Nagrin, celebrated mod
ern dance artist, will give a solo
performance in Dinkclspiel Audi
torium tomorrow at 8 p.m.
Nagrin, who received Broad
way's acclaim with his Sioux In
dian dance in "Annie Get Your
Gun," will perform a series of
seven dance portraits.
The dancer will also be the
guest artist at the Northern Cali
fornia Annual Dance Symposium
to be held in the Women's Gym
nasium tomorrow al 1:30 p.m.
Approximately 125 students from
nine colleges will take part in
the Symposium.
Sunday, a chamber music con
cert featuring a solo by Pianist
Irene Jaeobi will be presented in
Dinkclspiel Auditorium at 3:30
p.m. Also on the program will be
the Stanford Chorale under, the
direction of Professor Harold .
Schmidt.
