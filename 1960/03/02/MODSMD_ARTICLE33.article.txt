# Women's Housing Draw Tomorrow
## 
### 
The end-quarter drawing for
women who wish to change cam
pus residence and for off-campus
women who wish to move back
will be held tomorrow in the base
ment of the Women's Clubhouse
from 9 a.m. to 12 noon and from
1 to 4:30 p.m.
A list of the numbers drawn
will be posted in the Housing
Office. Room 101 a, on Inner Quad
Friday. Women will be allowed
to withdraw from the drawing at
that time.
Information on the drawing
procedure is posted in all houses.
