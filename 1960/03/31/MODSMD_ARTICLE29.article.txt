# Open Hours Listed For Women's Pool
## 
### 
Open hours for the swimming
pool in the Women's Gym have
been announced as the following:
Monday, Wednesday, Friday—
11 a.m. to noon, 4:15 to 5:45 p.m.
Tuesday, Thursday — 3:30 to
4:15 p.m.
Saturday, Sunday Ito 5 p.m. j
The pool is open on weekdays
to all registered women students. I
Suits and towels are provided,
but swimmers must bring their
own caps. Women must wear!
bathing caps.
Men may swim on Saturdays i
and Sundays if accompanied by
a registered woman student. No!
dressing facilities are available
then, and swimmers must pro
vide their own suits and towels.
