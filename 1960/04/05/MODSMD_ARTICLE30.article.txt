# 'Population' Talk Scheduled Today
## 
### 
A youthful Catholic priest who
has served extensively in Japan
will speak on "The Population
Question" at 4:15 p.m. today in
Room 300.
The speaker is Anthony Zim
merman. S.V.D., S.T.D., who
earned his doctorate at Catholic
University of America on the
subject : "Overpopulation: A
Study of Papal Teaching on the
Problem with Special Reference
to Japan."
His appearance is sponsored
jointly by the Newman Federa
tion of America and the Stanford
Committee on Religious Studies.
