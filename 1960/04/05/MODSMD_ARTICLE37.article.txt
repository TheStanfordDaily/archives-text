# Students Offered WAC Scholarship
## 
### 
Scholarships are being offered
to students of the Bay Area to
attend the World Affairs Coun
cil's 14th annual conference at
Asilomar May 6-8.
Applications must be obtained
and turned in by tomorrow to Dr.
Martin Travis in the Political
Science department or the lift
shack.
Scholarships cover room, board
and registration. Speaking at the
confab will be the King and
Queen of Nepal; Richard C. Hott
let, top CBS foreign correspon
dent; and Dean Rusk, president
of the Rockefeller Foundation.
"Competitive Co - Existence
Dilemma of the 60's" is this year's
conference theme. Organizers of
the affair expect students, leaders
and scholars from 25 countries.
