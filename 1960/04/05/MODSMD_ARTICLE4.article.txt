# Sidewalk Cafe Opens
## 
### 
Spring lias definitely arrived oil the Stanford campus as the
recent warm weather and today's opening of the I-Center's Side
walk Cafe indicate. Tony White and Bridget Hursley ready the
Cafe's espresso coffee machine in preparation for the 2:30 opening
this afternoon. Alan Rosenthal, English guitar player and folk
singer will entertain at the opening. Hours will be 8:30 p.m. to II
p.m. Sunday—Friday; 2:30 to ;» p.m. Monday—Friday; and 1 to 1
p.m. Sunday afternoons. Danish pastry and fruit beverages are
included on the menu.
