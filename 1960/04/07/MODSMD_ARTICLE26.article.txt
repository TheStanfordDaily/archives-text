# Menlo Decisions Frosh Net Squad
## 
### 
Great performances by Chris
Crawford and Yoshi Minegishi
paved the way for Menlo College
to end the Stanford frosh netters'
one-game win streak at one. The
Oaks fought their way to a 4-3
victory here Tuesday.
Crawford defeated Stanford's
Bill Burley in the feature singles
match 6-0, 6-2 and teamed with
Hap Robinson to turn back Bur
ley and Pele Snook 6-4, 9-7 in a
doubles contest.
Minegishi handed Snook a 6-0,
6-3 defeat in second singles and
helped teammate Tom Rippner to
defeat Tony Dauphino and Wes
Petit in doubles by a 3-6, 6-3, 6-4
margin.
In other matches Dauphino
downed Chips Potter of Menlo
6-0 and 6-2; Petit tripped the Oaks'
Robinson 6-1, 6-1; and Stanford's
West Root turned back Lenny
Roos of Menlo 6-4, 6-0.
