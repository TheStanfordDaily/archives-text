# Services Added During Holy Week
## 
### 
Holy Week and Easter observ
ances have been announced for
this week. Continuing each morn
ing is the 7:35 a.m. prayer service.
In the evenings today, Tuesday,
Friday and Saturday there will be
a service of compline at 9:30 p.m..
while Wednesday evening at 9:30
p.m. a candlelight service will be
held. The communion service on
Maundy Thursday ai 8 p.m. will
have special music by the Stan
ford Memorial Church Choir.
On Good Friday there will be a
service from 12 noon to 3 p.m.
Easter Sunday Service on Inner
Quad will take place at 6 a.m. A
communion service at 7:30 a.m.
Easter morning will be followed
by two Easter services at 9 and 11
a.m. Bishop Hans Lil.je of the
Lutheran Church in Germany lias
been announced as speaker for
those services.
