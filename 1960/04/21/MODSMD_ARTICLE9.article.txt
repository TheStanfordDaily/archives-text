# Avant-garde Films Slated Tomorrow
## 
### 
Maya Deren, the first woman to
win the International Prize for ex
perimental films at the Cannes
Film Festival, will demonstrate
her films and discuss them in a
lecture in Cubberley Auditorium
tomorrow at 7:30 p.m.
Admission is 75 cents. Tickets
are on sale at Memorial Auditor
ium box office.
The avant-garde short films
"Meshes of the Afternoon," "A
Study in Choreography," "The
Very Eye of Night," and "Ritual
in Transfigured Time" will be
shown.
