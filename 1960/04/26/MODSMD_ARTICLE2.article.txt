# James Warburg To Give Lecture
## 
### 
James P. Warburg, a member
of FDR's original "brain trust"
will speak tomorrow at 4:15 in
Cubberley Auditorium.
As deputy chief of the Office of
War Information during World
War 11. Warburg was in charge
of psychological warfare policy in
the European theater.
He was educated at Harvard
and served as president of the
International Acceptance Bank of
New York and vice chairman of
the board of the Bank of Man
hat ten Co. before 1935.
Warburg is a member of the
board of directors of the Ameri
can Academy of Political and So
cial Science, and chairman of the
board of the Juilliard School of
Music.
Since 1935 he has been a writer
and pamphleteer on world affairs,
domestic politics, and economcs.
Warburg has lectured exten
sively in the U.S. and Canada.
The title of his talk is "Reveille
for Rebels,"
