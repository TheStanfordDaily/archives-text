# Dancing Time
## 
### 
The Kirby Stone Four will be the featured attraction at the Junior
Prom to be held Friday night, 9 p.m. to 1 a.in, in the Mural Room
of the St. Francis Hotel. Their latest record, "Everything's Com
ing Up Roses" supplies the theme for the Prom. The quartet has
recently performed at New York's Copaeabana and at Las Vegas
Hotels. Tickets, priced at $5 per couple or $4.50 with a class card,
will continue to be on sale until Friday at Engineering Corner.
