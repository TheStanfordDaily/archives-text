# Poets Will Receive Awards for Work
## 
### 
Two poetry contests will give
aspiring writers an opportunity to
submit their original works.
The Academy of American
Poets will award $100 to the
graduate or undergraduate who
submits the best poem or poems
in the Academy's poetry contest.
The Clarence Urmy Poetry Con
test will also award $40 to the
writer of the best poem or poems
submitted in this contest.
Contributors may obtain furth
er details by going to room 45
in the English building. Deadline
for manuscripts is May 10.
