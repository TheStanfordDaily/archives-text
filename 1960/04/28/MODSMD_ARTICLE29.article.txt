# Writing Contest
## 
### 
Grove Press Inc. has announced
opening of the Evergreen Award
competition, a writing contest
open to any college student.
The contest opens Sunday, and
closes Sept. 30, 1961.
The winner of the contest will
receive a cash prize of $500.
Manuscripts, which may include
stories, poems, plays, and essays,
must he accompanied by proof
from the registrar or a teacher
to verify the writer's college sta
tus.
Manuscripts should be sent to:
The Editors, New Campus Writ
ing, Antioeh College, Yellow
Springs, Ohio.
