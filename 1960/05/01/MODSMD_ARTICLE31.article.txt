# Choral Festival
## 
### 
Choirs, mixed choruses and vo
cal groups from 1-4 Northern Cali
fornia junior colleges will sing in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium from 2 to
5 p.m. today.
The event is the 26th Annual
Spring Chorus Festival of the
Northern California Junior Col
lege Music Educators Asociation.
The public is invited to attend.
