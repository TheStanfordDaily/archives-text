# Mitchell, Swyter Named to Offices
## 
### 
Hon Mitchell has been appoint
ed next year's student police chief
and Han Swyter has been named
assistant financial manager of
the ASSU, it was announced yes
terday. The two were appointed
by next year's financial manager,
Todd Stewart.
Mitchell, a Theta Chi junior,
will take office during pre-reg
week next fall at a salary of $125
per quarter. His job is to provide
student police for the Big Game,
ASSU functions, dorm dances,
and other occasions.
Swyter, who will be working
under the direction of Stewart,
is a freshman.
