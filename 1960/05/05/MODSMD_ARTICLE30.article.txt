# Traynor to Speak At Law Banquet
## 
### 
Stanford Law School alumni
and former members of the Law
Review staff are invited to the
annual banquet of the Stanford
Law Review Saturday, May 14.
The banquet, held at Sabella's,
will feature Justice Roger Tray
nor of the State Supreme Court
as speaker. Social hour will start
at 6 p.m., dinner at 8 p.m.
