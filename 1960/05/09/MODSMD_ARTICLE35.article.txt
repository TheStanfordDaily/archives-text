# Axe Society Elects
## 
### 
Don Roomer was chosen 1960-61
Axe Society president in elections
held May 4. Other new officers
are Steve Williams, vice-presi
dent; Melinda Howe, secretary;
and Jim Avedision, business man
ager.
New members of the organiza
tion will he selected in try-outs
during autumn quarter.
