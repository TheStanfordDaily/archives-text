# History Teachers Will Study Here

###

schools have received William
Robertson Coe Fellowships for
study this summer in the Stan
ford Institute of American His
tory.
Coming from 12 states in all
parts of the country, the teach

Professor George H. Knoles of
the History Department is direc

The fellowships provide $750
each, with an additional $250
available for those whose families
accompany them.
The fellowships were estab

late William Coe, a New York
