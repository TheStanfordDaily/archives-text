# Law Forum Talk
## 
### 
Melvin Belli, tho notorious law
yer, "king of torts," will speak at
the law forum today at 11 a.m.
on "Jury or Commission."
He will discuss whether it is
better to have a jury or a com
mission composed of trained, well
educated people to decide such
matters as how much should be
awarded for a broken leg.
