# NAACP Organizes Membership Drive
## 
### 
Applications for membership in |
a Stanford chapter of the Na
tional Association for the Ad
vancement of Colored People will
be available at Engineering Cor
ner from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. today.
Fifty members are needed in
order for the group to be granted
a charter from the national or
! ganization.
The Stanford chapter- is being
organized by students who have
taken an interest in recent sit-in ;
strikes in the South. They plan 1
to fill a local need, also, in the
area of housing and student dis- i
i tribution.
A meeting of the NAACP will
be held next week to formulate!
plans for fall quarter.
