# Tours, Florence Officers Chosen
## 
### 
Ken Bishop was chosen student
body president of the overseas
campus at Florence, and Harlan
Robinson, president of the Tours
group of elections held last week.
Other officers of the Florence
student body are Marietta Scurry,
social coordinator; Jonnet Kerns,
public relations; John Huber, ath
letic chairman, and Chuck Gilette,
business manager.
Tours officers are Roan Con
rad, public relations; Nancy Bas
kin, social coordinator; John
Jones, athletic chairman, and Rob
ert Shatzem, business manager.
Both the Tours and Florence
groups leave this fall for two
quarters of study overseas. The
Stuttgart group, which leaves in
June, is scheduled to elect officers
Monday night.
