# ASSU Reg Dance Tonight on Patio
## 
### 
All Summer session people will
have a chance to cool off to
night at the informal registration
dance outdoors at Bowman
Alumni House.
The dance is open to all age
groups and ladies are encouraged
to come, although according to
Bob Sankster, Dance Chairman,
babysitting services will not be
provided. Those taking part in
the golf tournament are given a
special invitation.
Clayton Graham and his band
will present "Music that Swings"
and refreshments will be served.
No admission is charged for the
dance which runs from 9-12.
