# ASSU Plans Tennis Doubles
## 
### 
Mixed tennis doubles are a com
ing attraction for both July Bth
and July 11th. "This is not a tour
nament," explains Beau Bianchi,
ASSU Intramural representative,
"but just organized fun."
Participants should sign up
with a partner at the ASSU office
by July 7 for the July 8 matches,
and by July 10 for the following
day's games. The matches will be
held on the courts behind Encina
Gym, opposite Angell Field, at
3:30 on both days. Players pro
vide their own equipment.
