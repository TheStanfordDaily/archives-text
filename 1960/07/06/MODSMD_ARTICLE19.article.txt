# Hungry? Try These Places
## 
### 
Stanford DOES have facilities
for feeding those whose living
groups do not serve meals.
For fast service, the Union
Snack Bar outside the ASSU Of
fice is equipped with sandwiches,
pies, donuts, coffee, milk and a
variety of fruit drinks. The Snack
Bar is open daily except Satui - -
day. Coffee and refreshments are
served every day but Saturday
and Sunday in the Encina Coffee
Shop, located in the basement of
Encina Hall.
For a greater variety of food,
the Union Cellar across from the
bookstore offers continuous food
service daily except Sunday. The
Union Cafeteria inside Union
courtyard serves lunch and dinner
daily except Saturday. On Sunday
a brunch is served instead of
lunch.
