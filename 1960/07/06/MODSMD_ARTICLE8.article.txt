# Japan Subject Of Convocation
## 
### 
The second of a series of three
lectures to be held during this
Summer Education Convocation
will be given today by Dr. Toki
omi Kaigo, a visiting professor
from the University of Tokyo.
Dr. Kaigo, who was formerly
Dean of the School of Education
at the University of Tokyo, will
speak on "Post War Educational
Changes in Japan" at 10:00 a.m.
in Cubberley auditorium. All Ed
ucation classes will be dismissed
for the convocation.
