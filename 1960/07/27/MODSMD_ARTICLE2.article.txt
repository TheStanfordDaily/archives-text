# Film Shown On Hawaii
## 
### 
Nicol Smith, explorer of strange
places, will narrate his color film
on Hawaii at 8 p.m. Monday in
Memorial Auditorium.
Smith's film is a personal movie
of people rather than of the in
terests of an archeologist or his
torian. He has tried to find the
unusal in people, and he collects
bizarre and amusing stories about
them.
The film presentation is spon
sored by the Committee on Public
Exercises. Admission is 75 cents.
