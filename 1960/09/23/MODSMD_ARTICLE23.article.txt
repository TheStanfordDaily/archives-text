# Engineers Work Toward Degrees
## 
### 
Eight International Business
Machines Corp. engineers from
the technical center in San Jose
will return on a full time basis
this fall to work toward the Ph.D.
and M.S. degrees.
They are among a number of
IBM engineers and scientists from
throughout the country who were
selected for full time scholarships
under a postgraduate education
program.
The eight are John W. Beck,
Russell K. Brunner, James A.
Chrysler, John M. Harker, John
A. McLaughlin, Alfred G. Oster
lund, Arnold P. Richter, and
Ralph E. Maris.
