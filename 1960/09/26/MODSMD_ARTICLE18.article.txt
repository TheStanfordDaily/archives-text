# Company Training To Be Evaluated
## 
### 
A national survey to evaluate
"in-company" training programs
will be conducted by Dean J.
Hugh Jackson, Dean Emeritus of
the Stanford Graduate School of
Business.
The project will be financed by
a research grant from the Ameri
can Management Association of
Now York.
Its object is to measure the ef
fectiveness of training programs
conducted within industrial, com
mercial, and financial corpora
tions for management personnel
below the top executive level.
Jackson will interview ap
proximately 100 heads of major
corporations in San Francisco,
Los Angeles, Minneapolis, Chi
cago, Detroit, Pittsburgh, Roch
ester, New York City, Wilming
ton, and elsewhere throughout the
United States.
