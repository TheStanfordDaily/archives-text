# WISCONSIN INTERCEPTS A STANFORD PASS
## 
### 
Badger halfback Ken Montgomery intercepts a pass thrown
by Stanford quarterback Rod Sears in the fourth period of
Saturday's game in the Stadium. Montgomery returned the
ball 40 yards to the Stanford 1 1 and three plays later Wis
consin scored. The intended receiver is Dale Ostrander.
