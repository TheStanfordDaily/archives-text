# KZSU Will Hold Open House Today
## 
### 
KZSU, the campus student
operated radio station, will hold
an Open House this afternoon
from 2 to 6 p.m. downstairs in
the back of Memorial Hall. In
formal tours of the station will
be conducted, and refreshments
will be served. Simulated broad
casting will be in progress.
KZSU boasts one of the most
completely equipped college sta
tions on the West Coast. Facili
ties include two broadcasting
studios, four Ampex tape record
ers, disc recording equipment, and
an extensive record library.
According to Station Manager
Richard Gilbert, there are many
positions available to new stu
dents due to t lie loss of last year's
graduating seniors.
