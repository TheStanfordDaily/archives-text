# New Students to Glimpse Activities
## 
### 
Freshmen and transfer students
will got a glimpse of the many
activities available at Stanford
at the Axe Society Activities Day
Friday from 3 to 5 p.m.
Each of the clubs on campus
will be represented by two to
four members who will answer
questions about their clubs and
make lists of those interested so
that they may be contacted for
future interviews.
The representatives will sit at
tables which vvill be systematic
ally arranged in the patio. A di
rectory will be placed near the
entrance to help students find the
activities of their interest.
Refreshments and recorded
music will be provided in another
part of Bowman.
