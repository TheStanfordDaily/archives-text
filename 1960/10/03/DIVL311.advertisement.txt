THE COFFEE HOUSE
AS UNIVERSITY
Historically the coffee house
has served as a center for
thought and discussion, the
proper setting for good con
versation.
Kepler's coffee house is an
integral part of the book
store: a storehouse of man's
greatest (and pettiest)
thoughts.
We invite you to come to
browse, to stay for lunch or
after theater snacks, and to
share in the freedom of our
coffee house university.
