# Bowker Elected IMS President
## 
### 
Dr. Albert 11. Bowker, dean of
the Graduate Division, is the new
president of the Institute of Math
ematical Statistics.
He was elected at the annual
meeting of the Institute held on
campus in late August.
Dean Bowker is also a member
of the board of directors of the
American Statistical Association
and director of the Applied Mat he
matics and Statistics Laboratories.
