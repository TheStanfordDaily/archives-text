# Art Gallery Holds Home Design Show
## 
### 
An exhibition of California
designed home furnishings will be
at the Art Gallery from Oct. 3
through Oct. 23. This display.
"California Design 6," includes
more than 100 items of furniture,
fabrics, appliances, ceramics, and
other accessories assembled by
the Pasadena Art Museum. It won
the distinguished service award of
the Furniture Manufacturers As
sociation.
The gallery is open daily except
Mondays from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.,
and Sundays from noon to 4 p.m.
