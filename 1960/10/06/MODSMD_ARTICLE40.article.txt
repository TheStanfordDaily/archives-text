# Med College Test Date Set
## 
### 
The Medical College Admission
Test, sponsored by the Association
of American Medical Colleges, will
be administered on October 20,
1960, for the benefit of the 1961
Medical School applicants who
failed to take the test when it was
given in May.
Application blanks and copies of
the bulletin of information may
be obtained at the Medical Admis
sions Office, room 112 D. To be
guaranteed acceptance, applica
tions and changes in registration
must be received by the Psycho
logical Corporation before Oct. 14,
1960.
Only applicants for admission
to classes beginning the study of
Medicine in the Fall of 1961, who
have not taken the test previ
ously, should take it at this time.
Application blanks for the Stan
ford School of Medicine for the
class which enters the Fall of
1961 are available at the Medical
Admissions Office. Students are
urged to submit applications as
soon as possible.
