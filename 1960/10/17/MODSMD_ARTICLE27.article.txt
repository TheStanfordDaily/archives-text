# Young Democrats Choose Officers, Discuss Debates
## 
### 
Stanford Young Democrats
elected new officers at their first
meeting of the quarter held last
Thursday evening in the Women's
Clubhouse. Jim Lyons was elected
president; Andy Silver, vice presi
dent; and Dennis McFarland, sec
retary-treasurer.
State Young Democrat treas
urer, Jerry Jackson, called the
meeting in conjunction with the
third Kennedy-Nixon debate. Fol
lowing the business meeting, the
forty students present discussed
the debate between the presiden
tial candidates.
Lyons announced that plans are
being made to hold a "New Fron
tier's Rally" and bring a name
speaker to this campus in the
immediate future.
