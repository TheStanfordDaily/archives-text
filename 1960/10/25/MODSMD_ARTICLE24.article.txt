# Gallery Displays Water Colors
## 
### 
Water colors by Charles Paine,
Redwood City teacher, will be on
display at the Art Gallery from
today until November 13.
Paine is a mathematics teacher
at Sequoia High School in Red
wood City. A graduate of Stan
ford, he began painting ten years
ago. His work reflects a particu
lar interest in the California coast
and, more recently, in the Colo
rado Rocky Mountains.
An exhibit of recent designs by
California architect Warren Cal
lister will be in the Small Gallery.
Prints from the University Col
lections will be displayed in the
Foyer.
