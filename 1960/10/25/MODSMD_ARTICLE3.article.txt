# Radford Talk Set Thursday
## 
### 
Admiral Arthur V. Rad
ford, former chairman of the
Joint Chiefs of Staff, will
speak in behalf of the Nixon-
Lodge ticket Thursday in Me
morial Auditorium at 4:15
p.m.
His visit is sponsored by the
Stanford Political Union and
the public is welcome. It will
be his only Bay Area appear
ance.
He was graduated from the
Naval Academy in 1916, and
after air training at Pensa
cola, served in various sea
and air commands until May
of 1944 when he was named
deputy chief of naval air op
erations.
He was named commander
in chief of the Pacific Fleet
in 1949. He holds among other
awards the Distinguished
Service Medal and the Legion
of Merit with gold star.
