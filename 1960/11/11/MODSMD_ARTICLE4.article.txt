# Quad Picture Deadline Here
## 
### 
Senior pictures are being taken
at the Quad office through next
Tuesday. All those who have
missed appointments or have not
had their picture taken should
sign-up at the Quad Office today
before 5:30 p.m.; according to
Editor Rosa Hahn.
Senior pictures will be taken
only of those people who have
appointment cards or have signed
the sheet in the Quad Office. The
portrait fee is $2.25, payable in
advance only.
Senior men are requested to
not wear white jackets. Senior
women may wear any color
sweater other than black or
white.
