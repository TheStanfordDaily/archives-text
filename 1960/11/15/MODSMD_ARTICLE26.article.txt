# I-Center Will Sponsor jaunt to Asilomar
## 
### 
The International Center is
sponsoring a trip to Asilomar on
the Monterey Peninsula over
Thanksgiving vacation (Friday
Nov. 25th thru Sunday the 27th).
The cost per person is $15 for
six meals and two night's lodg
ing. The group plans to go down
by private car, and there will be
opportunities to hike, sightsee, or
just relax.
American, as well as foreign,
students are invited to sign up as
soon as possible in the upstairs
office at the International Center.
