# Last Day for Senior Photos
## 
### 
Today is the last day seniors
may have their pictures taken for
the 1961 Quad.
The Quad office will be open
from 9 a.m. to noon, and from
1 to 5:30 p.m.
Those with appointments will
be taken first, but seniors who
do not have appointments may
also be photographed. The cost
of the pictures is $2.25.
Men are not to wear white
any color sweater except black or
sport jackets. Women may wear
white.
