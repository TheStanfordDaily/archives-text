# KZSU to Make Gaieties Discs
## 
### 
KZSU, Stanford's student radio
station, will supply disc record
ings of Ram's Head's Big Game
Gaieties of 1960. To reserve your
copy, sign-up before or after to
night's performance in the Me
morial Auditorium lobby.
The records will be available in
about two weeks. Price will be
about $4.75, depending on the
number of orders received.
