# Cuba Topic Of Baran Talk
## 
### 
Stanford Economics Professor
Paul Baran will talk tonight at
Cubberley Auditorium on Cuba,
Fidel Castro, and Cuba's relation
ship with the Soviet Union. The
talk is set for 7:30 p.m., and is
sponsored by the Institute of In
ternational Relations. The speech
is open to the public, and admis
sion is free.
Baran returned last month
from a three-week stay in Cuba.
While there he surveyed the eco
nomic situation and interviewed
top government officials. He
talked with Fidel Castro, and got
his views on the revolution.
Baran, at Stanford since 1949,
is a specialist in comparing eco
nomic systems. A frequent con
tributor to leading economic jour
nals, he is the author of a book,
The Political Economy of Growth.
A rccent articlo, "The Economic
Hazards of Arms Reduction," ap
peared in the March 28, 1959, is
sue of The Nation.
Before coming to Stanford he
was senior economist in the re
search department of the Federal
Reserve Bank of New York.
