# Naval Aviators At Pavilion Today
## 
### 
A Naval Aviation Information
Van will lie parked south of the
Pavilion today and tomorrow to
explain opportunities in Naval
aviation to Stanford men.
Lt. Commander Walt McGreevy
and Don Camburn from the U.S.
Naval Air Station, Oakland, will
conduct interviews and answer
questions aboard the van, which
is equipped with modern naval
aviation displays.
Mental and physical examina
tions for the Aviation Officer
Candidate, Aviation Officer Can
didate (non-pilot), and the Naval
Aviation Cadet programs are
given at the Oakland Naval Air
Station at 8:30 a.m. and 12:30
p.m. daily except Monday and
Tuesday. The mental examination
may be taken on campus for j
convenience.
