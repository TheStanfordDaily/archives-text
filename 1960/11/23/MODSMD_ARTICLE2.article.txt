# Debate Team Reaches Finals
## 
### 
CORVALLIS, Oregon (Special
to the Daily) Stanford's debate
team has advanced all ten of its
contestants into the finals of the
Western Speech Association Tour
nament being held here.
This is the first time in the
long history of the tournament
that any school has advanced all
of its members into the finals.
Fifty-six schools from all over
the West are competing in the
event, the finals of which will be
held today.
The debate squad also garnered
21 places in the finals, also a new
record. Four students have made
the finals in all three of their
events.
