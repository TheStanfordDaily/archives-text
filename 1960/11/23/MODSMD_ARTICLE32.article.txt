# Med School Gets New Professors
## 
### 
Two new assistant professors
of neurology will join the faculty
of Stanford's School of Medicine
next Thursday, Dean Robert H.
Alway announced yesterday.
They are Dr. Gilbert S. Frank,
now in practice in San Francisco,
and Dr. Anthony M. lannone, now
at the University of Minnesota.
Alway also revealed that Dr.
John E. Connolly, instructor in
surgery at the Medical School,
has been promoted to the rank
of assistant professor of surgery.
