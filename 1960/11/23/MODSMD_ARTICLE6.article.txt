# Corps Petition Gains Support
## 
### 
Signatures of 850 Stanford stu
dents were obtained on Quad yes
terday by the lIR Project Peace
Corps Committee, on a petition
endorsing a plan for the forma
tion of a civilian corps for aid
to underdeveloped nations, ac
cording to committee chairman
Bill Hawthorne.
The petition will be circulated
in living group and at the lIR
during next week, Hawthorne
said. Plans are then to send
copies of the signed petition to
