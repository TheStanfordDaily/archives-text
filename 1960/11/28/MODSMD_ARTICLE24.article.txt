# Initial Ski Club Meet Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Stanford Ski Club will hold
its first meeting of the year to
morrow at 7:30 p.m. in Cubberley
Auditorium to announce plans for
this year's Winter Carnival.
European ski movies will be
shown and local ski shops have
donated door prizes worth about
$25 to be given away at the meet
ing.
It will be the first chance for
students to obtain or renew mem
berships in the organization.
Club officers invite everyone,
including those who have never
been on skis, to the meeting.
