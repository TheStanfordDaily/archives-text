# Douglas Talk To Be in S.F.
## 
### 
U.S. Supreme Court Justice
William O. Douglas will present
his first major address, "Political
Conformity: Democracy vs. Com
munism in Asia," since his public
support of Red China's admission
to the U.N., tomorrow night in
the Masonic Memorial Temple at
8:30 p.m.
Douglas' lecture will deal with
the Communist threat and the
rise of democratic institutions in
that section of the world. He also
has consented to answer ques
tions from the floor concerning
his support of Rod China's ad
mission.
Tickets may be secured at the
Sherman Clay box office, Sutter
and Kearny St reefs, San Fran
cisco, for the lecture, the first
sponsored by the San Francisco
Lecture Series.
