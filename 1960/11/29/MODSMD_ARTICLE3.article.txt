# STANFORD SADIE EMERGES FROM OBSCURITY
## 
### 
Living in secrecy and mystery all quarter, sultry-voiced
Stanford Sadie has finally been revealed to her listeners as
Judy Hughes, a junior from Lagunita. KZSU, Stanford's ra
dio station, 880kc, presents Stanford Sadie annually to col
lege listeners. The fall quarter presentation of the Siren of
the Airways came to a close with last night's broadcast.
