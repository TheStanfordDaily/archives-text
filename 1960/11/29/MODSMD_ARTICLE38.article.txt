# Philippine Night Planned by HR

### 
Filipino students will celebrate
"Philippine Night" Saturday at
7:30 at Bowman Alumni House
with a program including the
showing of colored slides, a short
documentary film of every-day
Philippine scenes, and a "fire
side" by Roberto M. Bernardo,
this year's ASSU grantee from
the Philippines.
Climaxing the night will be the
interpretation of a number of
typical folkdanees done in origi
nal and authentic costumes, and
ballroom dancing.
