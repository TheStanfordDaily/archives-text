# Last Daily of Year Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tomorrow will mark the last
Daily to appear at dorm break
fast tables this quarter as staff
members take a holiday and pick
up their dusty, unopened books.
All announcements of coming
events for tomorrow's paper must
be turned in to the Daily Shack
by 3 p.m. today. The Daily will
resume its usual publishing sched
ule Jan. 3.
