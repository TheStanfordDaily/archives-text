# Promoters Say No Gotham Bowl
## 
### 
The inaugural Gotham Bowl
football game, scheduled to be
played Dec. 10 in Yankee Stadium
was canceled by the promoters.
Oregon State had agreed to
play in the new bowl game but
a suitable opponent was unable
to be lined up. Holy Cross was of
fered a bid but the offer was re
tracted before school officials
could reply.
