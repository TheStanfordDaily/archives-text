# Greek Exhibit At Art Gallery
## 
### 
A Greek government-sponsored
exhibition to introduce the art of
present-day Greece to America is
currently on display at the Stan
ford Art Gallery.
Assembled at the request of the
Smithsonian Institution, 11 paint
ings by eight contemporary Greek
artists constitute the show, which
will end Jan. 15.
All the artists have exhibited
throughout Europe, and some in
New York. The gallery is open
daily from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m., ex
cept Mondays, and from noon to
4 p.m. Sundays.
