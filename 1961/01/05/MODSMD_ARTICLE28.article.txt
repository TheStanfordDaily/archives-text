# S.F. Symphony Here Saturday
## 
### 
The San Francisco Symphony
Orchestra, with Enrique Jorda
conducting, will play at Stanford
Saturday evening.
One in the ASSU concert series,
the symphony program will be
gin at 8:1.") in Memorial Audito
rium.
Wagner's overture to "The Fly
ing Dutchman" will open the pro
gram. The other selections will
be "Symphony No. 40 in G Minor"
by Mozart, "Symphony No. 5 in
D Major" by Vaughan Williams
and selections from "Romeo and
Juliet" by Prokofieff.
Tickets are on sale at the Nitery
in the Union.
