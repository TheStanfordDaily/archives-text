# Speaker to Outline Friends Projects
## 
### 
Summer and local projects of
the American Friends Service
Committee Will he discussed by
Glenn Kitzenberger, regional Col
lege Secretary of the Committee,
at a meeting to be held Sunday
evening, 7:30, at 2081 Amherst in
Palo Alto.
Three participants in recent
AFSC projects, Bruce and Beverly
Busching of Stanford and Joel
Freis of Ohio will also participate
in the discussion. The Buschings
led an AFSC work camp at the
Tule River Indian Reservation in
California last summer.
The program, sponsored by the
Friends student group, is open to
anyone interested in the AFSC
and its work.
