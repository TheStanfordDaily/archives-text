# Tigers Sign Two
## 
### 
DETROIT (UPI)-Pitcher Paul
Foytack and first baseman Larry
Osborn signed their 1961 contracts
with the Detroit Tigers yesterday.
Foytack, who won 58 games be
tween 1956 and 1959, had a 2-11
season last year due to a muscle
failure in his shoulder.
Osborn is bidding for the reg
ular first base job after batting
.342 with Denver of the American
Association last year. He hit 34
homeruns and batted in 119 runs.
