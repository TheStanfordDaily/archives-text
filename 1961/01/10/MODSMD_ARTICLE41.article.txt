# Rankin Wins Writing Award
## 
### 
Jerry Rankin, associate editor
of The Stanford Daily, was one
of the top 10 winners of ihe No
vember news-writing competition
for college journalists sponsored
by the William Randolph Hearst
Foundation.
Rankin, a junior journalism
major from Redlands, won with
his story of stunts pulled by Cal
and Stanford students before this
year's Big Game.
The six-month national contest
is set up on a point basis and will
give $29,800 in fellowships and
grants to the 10 students and
universities receiving the highest
number by the end of April.
