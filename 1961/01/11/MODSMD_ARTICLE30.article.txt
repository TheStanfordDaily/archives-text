# Krips to Lead S.F. Symphony
## 
### 
Joseph Krips, the first of three
guest conductors to appear with
the San Francisco Symphony this
season, will conduct next Wednes
day and Thursday at 8:30 p.m.
and Friday at 2:15 p.m. at the
Opera House.
Krips, horn in Vienna, is the
regular conductor of the Buffalo
Philharmonic.
