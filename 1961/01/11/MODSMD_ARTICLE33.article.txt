# Dance Program To Feature India, Spain, Israel
## 
### 
Folk Dance International will
continue al Stanford during tiie
winter quarter with a series of
eight seminars on the rhythm
and dance of Spain, Israel, and
India. The series will start tonight
at 7:30 in the Women's Gym
fiance studio, and will continue
on ensuins Wedensday nights.
Those wishing to attend the first
performance must register today.
The first four seminars will be
on Spanish dance classical and
folk and will include Flamenco
and the use of castanets. The next
two programs will be on Israel,
and the final two on India.
Wearing authentic costumes,
La Mariquita, professional dancer
of Menlo Park, will teach the
Spanish programs.
