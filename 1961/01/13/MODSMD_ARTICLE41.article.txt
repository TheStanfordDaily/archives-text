# Positions in Band Open
## 
### 
The 72-piece Stanford Sym
phonic Band, which has just
begun winter quarter re
hearsals, has sent out a call
for french horn and obe
players.
The band announced yes
terday that the positions are
open to any student and that
tryouts will be held Tuesday
at 7:15 p.m. at the Band
House. A few other positions
aside from french horns and
obes are also open.
The symphonic band, which
is conducted by Julius Shu
chat, plays both classical and
contemporary works for band
by such composers as Bach,
Hindemith, and Shumann.
