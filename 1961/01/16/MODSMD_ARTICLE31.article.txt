# Algerian Crowd Kills Europeans
## 
### 
ALGIERS (UPI) Police said
yesterday that a Moslem mob
killed three Europeans and burned
their automobile in a riot that
flared in the Algiers suburb of
Baraki Saturday night.
Eight other persons were in
jured in the riot. One of them, a
European, was reported to be in
serious condition.
Police said the rioters, who
poured out into the streets by the
hundreds, slashed the throat of
Siegfried Schramm, 31-year-old
German chauffeur for a local
