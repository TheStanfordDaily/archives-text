# Seniors Offered Several Awards
## 
### 
The Graduate School of Busi
ness at the University of Chicago
will give a tuition honor scholar
ship to a Stanford senior for the
coming academic year, according
to Robert P. Huff, director of
financial aids.
Also available are Continental
Grain Fellowships at the Wharton
School of Finance at the Univer
sity of Pennsylvania and the
Graduate School of Business of
Northwestern University.
Interested seniors should call at
the Graduate Awards Office,
Room 102-B, for details.
