Daily photo by Bill Koepke
POSTERS CO UP ON CAMPUS FOR SOPHOMORE COTILLION TO BE HELD IN S.F.
Floss Haines, vice-president, and Steve Miller, president of the Sophomore Class, inspect
a poster advertising the upcoming Sophomore Cotillion to be held Friday, Feb. 3, at the
Sheraton-Palace Hotel. Music for the annual, all-campus dance will be provided by Bob
Emerson's Band. Entertainment for the evening will include the popular Gateway Sing
ers and jazzman Cal Tjader. Tickets are now on sale for $5 with Sophomore Class card
and $5.50 without.
