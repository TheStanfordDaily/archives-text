# Kitto to Talk On Sophocles
## 
### 
H. D. F. Kitto, professor of
Greek at the University of Bristol
and currently Sal her Lecturer in
Classics at Berkeley, will speak to
day on "The Oedipus Tyrannus of
Sophocles." The lecture, sponsored
by the Stanford Philological Asso
ciation, will be in the Upper
Lounge of the Women's Club
house at 4:15 p.m., preceded by
refreshments.
Educated at Cambridge, Eng
land, Professor Kitto has earned
international fame for his studies
of Greece and the Greek drama.
His "Form and Meaning in Dra
ma" goes* beyond the Greeks to
consider the entire genre of the
drama. His most recent book is
"Sophocles: Dramatist and Phi
losopher." In addition to teach
ing at the University of Califor
nia, Professor Kitto has taught
or lectured in this country at
Brandeis University and at the
University of Indiana.
