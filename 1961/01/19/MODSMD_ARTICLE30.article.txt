# Tours Planned For Pre-meds
## 
### 
Faculty members and medical
students will direct tours for pre
med students through the Stan
ford Medical School today and to
morrow.
Limited numbers of sopho
mores, juniors and seniors will
be taken around today between
4-5 p.m. and tomorrow between
3:45-5 p.m. Freshmen may attend
an 8-10 a.m. medical class Satur
day and then take a tour with a
faculty member and med students
until lunch.
Signup sheets and information
sheets are outside Room 460, Bi
ology Corner.
