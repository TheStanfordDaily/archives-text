# Socialist Editor Views Cold War
## 
### 
James Aronson, editor of the
Socialist National Guardian, will
speak on the world situation with
the striking title "Hot News in
the Cold War" for his topic.
Editor of the Guardian for the
last 12 years, Aronson is pres
ently touring U.S. colleges and
universities. He has worked for
many papers, including the New
York Times, and is a graduate of
Columbia and Harvard.
Political Union is sponsoring
his talk at 7:30 p.m. tomorrow
night in the Women's Clubhouse.
