# PHIL KELLY, CARD HANDYMAN
## 
### 
Stanford eager Phil Kelly—the "sixth man" on the team
—will be at guard or forward as the Indians journey north
to play the Huskies on their home court in Seattle. Stan
ford needs the win to stay in contention for the Big Five
title as USC with a 4-0 record and UCLA with 2-1 play two
games in L.A. in other Big Five cage action.
