# Socialist Editor To Speak Tonight
## 
### 
"Hot News in the Cold War,"
will be the subject of a talk to be
given tonight by James Aronson,
editor of the Socialist National
Guardian.
Aronson. who is presently tour
ing U.S. colleges and universities,
has been editor of the Guardian
for the lasl 12 years. He has
worked for many papers, includ
ing the New York Times, and is
a graduate of Columbia and
Harvard.
Political Union is sponsoring
the talk which will be given at
7:30 tonight in the Women's
Clubhouse.
