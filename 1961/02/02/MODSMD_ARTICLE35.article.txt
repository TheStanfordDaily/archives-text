# A 						Phi O Gives Books Checks
## 
### 
Checks and unsold books
from the A Phi O book ex
change will be returned today
and tomorrow from 9-1 in
Union Lobby.
All persons who gave books
to the exchange must pick up
their books or checks, or they
will forfeit all rights to them,
said Jerry Kovacic, publicity
chairman.
The Winter Quarter book
exchange was held Jan. 3-."5.
