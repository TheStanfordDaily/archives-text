# Buy! Buy! Buy! Quads! Quads!
## 
### 
Those who want to buy
Quads at the low price of
seven dollars must do so by
Thursday, the Daily is in
formed, because Friday the
price will soar to eight dol
lars.
Thursday is also the last
day that the Farm's egotists
can have their names imprint
ed on the cover of their Quad.
Cost of imprinting is one dol
lar.
Quads will be •sold at the
post office and at Engineer
ing Corner on Thursday from
9 to 5.
