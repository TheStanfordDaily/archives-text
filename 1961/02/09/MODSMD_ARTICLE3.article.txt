# African Work Meeting Today
## 
### 
A meeting to investigate em
ployment possibilities in Africa
will be hold this afternoon at 5
o'clock at Bowman Alumni House.
Anyone who is interested in
teaching or other work in Africa
is invited to attend. Upperclass
men, graduate students, and mar
ried couples are especially en
couraged to come.
Any student with an A.B. or a
B.S. is eligible to teach immedi
ately in African secondary schools.
Elementary courses in English,
history, geography, and the sci
ences are in demand.
Stanford is beginning a pro
gram to send teachers to Africa
this coming year. Transportation
funds may be provided.
Today's meting is an answer to
the challenge Yale University
Chaplain William S. Coffin pre
sented to Stanford last month.
