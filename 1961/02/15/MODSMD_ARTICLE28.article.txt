# Gymnasts In Action
## 
### 
The Stanford gymnasts meet
San Francisco State College to
night at 7:30 in the S.F. State
gymnasium.
With only three veterans on the
Card team, rough going is ex
pected against some of the finest
competition in the nation.
Rich Hawkins, Bill Bednar and
Jim Lathrop, with their experi
ence in competition, are expected
to give polished performances in
free exercises, trampoline, and
side-horse routines, respectively.
A total of 11 men will com
pete on the trampoline, horizontal
bar, side horse, still rings, rope
climb, tumbling and free exer
cise.
With three men needed in each
event, most of the boys will be re
quired to perform double and
triple duty.
