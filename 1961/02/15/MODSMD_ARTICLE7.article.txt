# Frosh Sinners Hire Apaches
## 
### 
The Class of 64 will pres
ent its first dance, "Sin on
Hie Seine," Tuesday from 9
p.m. to 1 a.m. at the Alpine
Hills Club in Portola Valley.
Tickets to the event are on
sale at Engineering Corner
and the post office for $1.50
with a frosh class card, and
$2.50 without. Tickets also
will be sold at the door.
Walt Tolleson's band will
provide music, and additional
entertainment will come from
the "We Three" trio and a
traditional Parisienne Apa
che dance.
