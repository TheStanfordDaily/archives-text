# FROSH APACHES CAPTURE PREY ON FARM
## 
### 
Four members of the Class of '64 get in shape for the first
freshman dance, scheduled for Tuesday from 9 p.m. to 1
a.m. at the Alpine Hills Club. Tickets to the event are on
sale at Engineering Corner and the post office, priced at
$1 .50 with a class card and $2.50 without. On the ground,
left to right, are Diane Bell and Eileen Reekie. Their cap
tors are Bob Friese and Richard Such.
