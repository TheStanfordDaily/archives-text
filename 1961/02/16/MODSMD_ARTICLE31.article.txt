# Frankenstein Speaks Today
## 
### 
Alfred V. Frankenstein, mu
sic and art critic of the San
Francisco Chronicle, will
speak on "Carlos Chaves,
Mexican Composer" today at
7:30 p.m. in Dinkelspiel Re
hearsal Hall.
Frankenstein is a member
of the faculties of the Univer
sity of California and Mills
and has been a frequent lec
turer at Stanford.
The public is invited to at
tend the lecture sponsored by
Sigma Delta Pi. national hon
orary Spanish society. There
is no admission charge.
