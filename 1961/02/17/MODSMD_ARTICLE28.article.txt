# Gymnasts Lose at S.F.
## 
### 
The Stanford gymnasts bowed
to San Francisco State Wednes
day night by the score of 76-52.
There were some bright spots
tor the Tribe however as Russ
Burmester swept to a first in the
rope climb in the time of 4.7 sec
onds. This broke his own per
sonal record by half a second.
Burmester was high man for the
Cards with 11 points.
Bill Bednar took a first in the
trampoline and Jim Lathrop took
top honors in the side horse com
petition for Stanford.
The team meets Cal at Berke
ley tomorrow in what should be
a rough meet for the Indians. Cal
was ranked nationally last year.
