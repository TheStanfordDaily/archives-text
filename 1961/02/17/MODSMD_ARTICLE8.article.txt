# Russ Chorus to Perform Tonight
## 
### 
A 30-member choir from the
I United States Army Language
School in Monterey will present
an all-Russian program of folk
and military music in Dinkelspiel
Auditorium tonight at 8 o'clock.
The program is sponsored by
the Stanford Russian Conversa
tion Club. Choir members are
soldiers attending the Monterey
school while stationed at Fort Old.
