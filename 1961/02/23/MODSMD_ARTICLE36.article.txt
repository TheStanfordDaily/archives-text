# Election Com Positions Open
## 
### 
The Elections Commission
announced that positions are
still open involving work for
the spring elections.
A spokesman said that the
commission is understaffed
and is being presented with a
heavier than usual workload.
Two full-time, polling places
have been added, a number of
referendums have been placed
on the ballots, and most stu
dent body offices are to be
filled, he said.
Persons interested in work
ing may try out today from 2-
4 p.m. in the ASSU Ex-Com
room.
