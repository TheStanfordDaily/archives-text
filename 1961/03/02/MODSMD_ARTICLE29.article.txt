# Swimmers Lose
## 
### 
Stanford's swimming team was
handed a stunning upset by San
Jose last night as the Spartans
won eight first places and the
meet by a 56-39 score.
The Indians only took three
firsts, one a pool record in the
200-yard breaststroko by Olym
pian Paul Hait. Hait swam the
distance in 2:25.9. Tom Redfern
won the 200-yard backstroke and
both Hait and Redfern helped
with the medley relay.
Stanford hosts Cal for the last
home meet of the season in the
pool Saturday at 2 p.m. The frosh
will meet the Cal and San Jose
frosh at tho same time.
