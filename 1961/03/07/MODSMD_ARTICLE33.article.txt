# Mansour Wins Medical Grant
## 
### 
Tag E. Mansour of Stanford is
a winner of one of the new five
year Senior Fellowship Grants.
The grant, awarded by the U.S.
Public Health Service, is in recog
nition of Mansour's work in phar
macology.
The 32 new grants were made
under the program administered
by the Division of General Medi
cal Sciences. The Senior Fellow
ship Program is not oriented to
ward a specific disease, but to
ward basic biomedical and health
related sciences.
