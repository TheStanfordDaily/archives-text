# Philippine Dance Workshop Slated
## 
### 
Mrs. Francisco Reyes, described
by her countryman Ambassador
Carlos Romulo as "the best we
have in Philippine folk dancing,"
is touring the United States and
will lead a workshop here tomor
row.
The workshop has been set for
10 a.m. to 2 p.m. in the Women's
Gym Dance Studio, sponsored by
the Women's Physical Education
Department. Teachers and experi
enced folk dancers are welcome.
