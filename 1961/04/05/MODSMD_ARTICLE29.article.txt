# Spring Hostel Signups Now
## 
### 
Signups for spring quarter
ASSII hostels are available
at the ASSU office in the
Union beginning today. The
first hostel is set for April
14-15, on the subject of air!
for underdeveloped areas of
the world.
Participants may sign up
for all spring hostels. A com
plete schedule is available at
the ASSU office. Payment of
$3 per night is required at
signup time.
