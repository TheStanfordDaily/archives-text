KEEP KOOL WITH KEPLER*
When you have tried everything else first (including the
discount houses and book clubs). come to the Peninsula's
only complete paperback bookstore.
If we don't have it, it must be Holiday Magazine
You will find your required and recommended paperbacks
here - plus thousands of others. Browse through and then
pause for a cool drink in our Browser Room.
Meanwhile Kepler will be pocketing the profit and the
free enterprise system will be safeguarded. Moreover, all
the little Keplers will wax fat.
So buy at Kepler's and help stamp out Guided Missiles.
Keeper and Feeder of all the little Keplers.
