# Therapy Work Meeting Set
## 
### 
Students interested in a project
of occupational therapy and visit
ing should attend a brief meeting
today at 1 p.m. in the Y lounge,
third floor of the Women's Club
house.
Participating once or twice a
week on Tuesdays and Thursdays
from 1:45 to 3:30 p.m., students
visit and help patients with craft
work in the West wood Convales
cent Hospital on Woodside Road.
Materials, instruction and super
vision are provided by San Mateo
County through a Federal grant
for study of the aged.
The Y will arrange transporta- j
tion and activity credit.
