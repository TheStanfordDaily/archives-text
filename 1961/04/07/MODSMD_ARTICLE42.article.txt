# P.E. Teacher Wins Honors
## 
### 
Mrs. Maud Lombard Knapp,
professor of education and physi
cal education, has been awarded
the Verne S. Landreth Honor
Award for distinctive service in
the field of physical education.
The Landreth honor goes to an
individual who is retiring or has
retired from active teaching. Mrs.
Knapp will retire in June.
Mrs. Knapp's special interest
has been teaching and program
development for women's physi
cal education majors. She has also
directed the student teaching pro
gram in secondary education for
the School of Education.
