# Openings Now In Flying Club
## 
### 
The Stanford Flying Club offers
opportunities for students inter
ested in economical flying and in
struction. Openings are now avail
able in the club's membership for
students with or without previous
flying experience.
Through direct participation,
the club's 45 members learn about
the airframe, powerplant, and
maintenance of planes. Club mem
bers fly two Aeronca Champs,
light, two-place planes which
cruise at about 75 mph.
The club invites students to its
meeting tonight in Room 273 En
gineering, at 7:30. Prospective
members will be introduced to the
possibilities in flying, and the op
portunities offered by the club.
