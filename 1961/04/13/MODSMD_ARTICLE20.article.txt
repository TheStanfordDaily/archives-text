# Indian Racqueteers Win Two Shutouts
## 
### 
The Stanford tennis team lost
only one set in two meets this
week, beating both Santa Clara
and San Jose State by the score
of 9-0; both meets were held on
the Stanford courts.
IN THE match with San Jose
State yesterday afternoon the
team only lost one singles set and
won all doubles sets by lopsided
margins.
The Cards won all of the sets in
the meet with Santa Clara on
Tuesday despite the fact that they
played without the services of
their top four team members.
