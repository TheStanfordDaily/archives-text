# Club Plans Talk About Overseas Campuses
## 
### 
Students recently returned from
Stanford's foreign campuses will
tell their experiences and impres
sions of student life abroad at
the Stanford Mothers' Club at 2
p.m. Tuesday in the Women's
Clubhouse.
A tea and social hour will fol
low the meeting.
A no-host luncheon in Union
dining hall at noon will precede
the meeting. For reservations call
Mrs. Frank Richey, DA 2-4861,
or Mrs. Carol Alden, DA 5-1744.
