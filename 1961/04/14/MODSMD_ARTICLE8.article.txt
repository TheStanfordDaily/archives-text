# Young Pianist Plays Tonight
## 
### 
John Browning, young Ameri
can pianist who has been critic
ally acclaimed here and abroad as
"brilliant, electrifying and dra
matic,"," will appear on the final
program of the ASSU Concert
Series tomorrow night at 8:15 in
Memorial Auditorium.
He will play Bach's "Concerto
in the Italian Style," Beethoven's
"Sonata No. 21 in C Major, Opus
53 (Waldstein)," Chopin's "Scher
zo in C Sharp Minor and four
mazurkas, Albaniz' "Evocation"
and "El Puerto" and Barber's
"Sonata, Opus 26."
