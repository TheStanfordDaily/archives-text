there lived a creep
named Rumplcsuit*
skin. Frankly he was
a mess. One day he
heard about the
Haspcl Cord suits
at R/A for only
42.50 so he hopped
right over and
bought one. Now
he’s known as
Smoothskinsuit
the mad blade
on campus.
