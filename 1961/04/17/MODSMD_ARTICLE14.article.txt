# Spikers Trounced By UCLA,96-35
## 
### 
Despite smooth striding, hard jumping Dan Moore's 25-1 effort
in the broad jump, Stanford was soundly trounced by UCLA, 96-35,
in a dual track meet Saturday in Los Angeles.
The defeat was the worst suffered from UCLA in a series that

dates back to 1934.
ASIDE FROM Moore's prodigi
ous leap and John Pontius’ tying
for first in the high jump (6-4),
Stanford took no other first
places.
Saturday the Cards will host
powerful USC and Oregon in what
should be one of the finest meets
in the country this year.
