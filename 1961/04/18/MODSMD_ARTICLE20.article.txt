# Hawks Beat Wings
## 
### 
DETROIT (UPD- The Chicago
Black Hawks, possesors of the
Stanley Cup for the first time
since 1938, may be spearheading
a “new era'’ in big league hockey.
The Black Hawks, already in
orbit, flew home with Lord Stan
ley’s treasured trophy today after
handing the Detroit Red Wings a
5-1 drubbing here Sunday.
