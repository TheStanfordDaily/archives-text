# Prom Ducats Still On Sale
## 
### 
“Students of all classes
should be reminded that the
Junior Prom tickets are still
on sale,’’ said Bob Garretson,
prom chairman yesterday.
The gala affair will be held at
Bay Meadows on Friday,
April 28. from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m.
Color will be provided by
the entertaining anecdotes of
Shelley Berman, night-club
entertainer, and music for
dancing will be by the Ray
Hacked Orchestra,
Tickets for the function are
available in limited numbers
at Engineering Corner and
the Post Office. The charge
is $5.50 per couple with a
Junior Class Card, and $5.95
without the Class Card.
