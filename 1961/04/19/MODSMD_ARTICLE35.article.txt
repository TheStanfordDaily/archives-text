# Netters Top SJS
## 
### 
| Stanford netters swamped San
! Jose State yesterday, 9-0 in the
j Spartans home courts. Card num
j her one man, Dick Ogden, led the
i Indians by defeating the Spartans’
I Gordy Stroud, 4-6, 6-1, 61.
Yoshi Minegishi defeated Bill
I Shaefer, 6-1, 6-3; Norm Karns
whipped Lee Junta 4-6, 6-1, 6-3;
George Bates defeated Stillson
Judah 6-1, 6-2; Phil Odegard heat
Larry Widens 6-1, 5-7, and 6-4;
Peter Snook defeated Stew Jones
6-0, 6-4, to round out Stanford’s
dominance of singles matches.
The Indian doubles teams also
swept their three matches to give
them their seventh win against
three defeats for the season.
