# Card Netters Lead USC As Rain Halts Match
## 
### 
Stanford’s tennis team lost to
UCLA Friday but came on Satur
day to take a 4-2 lead over USC
before the match was called be
cause of rain.
The match with the Trojans will
be completed Wednesday at Ojai.
The Cards will have to win only
one out of three doubles matches
in order to win the team competi
tion.
Yoshi Minegishi, George Bates,
Bill Burley and Phil Odegard all
won their singles matches against
the well-regarded Trojans.
The final score against UCLA

was 8-1 but there were several
matches in which the Bruins were
pushed to the limit.
Stanford picked up its only vic
tory when Norman Karns and
George Bates teamed to beat
Norm Perry and Roger Werks
man, UCLA’s number two dou
bles team.
This afternoon the Stanford
freshman team will host San
Mateo City College.
