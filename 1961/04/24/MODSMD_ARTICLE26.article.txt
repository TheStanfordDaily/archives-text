# Self Evaluation Hostel Scheduled
## 
### 
Prof. Willis Harman and Guy
H. Browning will lead an ASSU
Hostel this Friday and Saturday
concerning self-evaluation and de
termination.

Harman, head of Ihe depart
ment of electrical engineering,
suggested the topic to the Hostel
Board himself as one in which he
had a great deal of interest and
information gained from advising
students, said Gregg Hopkins,
hostel chairman.

Browning has been a student
counselor for the University for
several years.
Cost of the hostel is $3, which
covers meals and accommodation
beginning Friday at 4 p.m. and
ending Saturday noon. Payment
must be made at the time of sign
up at the ASSU Office.
