# Poetry Prizes Offered Here
## 
### 
Two cash prizes are being
offered to Stanford students
for the best unpublished
poem or group of poems by a
graduate or undergraduate
student.
The Irene Hardy award of
$5O will go for the best single
unpublished poem by an un
dergraduate done this year.
Individuals may submit as
many as three poems. Details
are available in the English
Department office. Deadline
for entries is May 10.
