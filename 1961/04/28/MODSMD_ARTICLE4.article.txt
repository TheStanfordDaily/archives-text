# MUSS To Pick Fall Senators
## 
### 
The interviewing committee of
Stanford’s Model United States
Senate will select students next
week to be next fall’s senators.
Sign-up sheets for interviews
to be held next week are posted
in the ASSU Office as of today.
Application blanks for students
interested in participating in
MUSS will be available through
Wednesday in the Political Union
office, now located in the Nitery.
MUSS will meet weekly
throughout next year to debate
national and international affairs
and to pass bills. MUSS will op
erate under the same basic rules
and procedures as the U.S. Senate.
