# Palmer Receives Stillwell Saber
## 
### 
The General Joseph Stillwell
saber recognizing the California
ROTC cadet who stood highest in
performance at the 1960 summer
camp was presented to Stanford
cadet Col. Harry D. Palmer re
cently.
Palmer was rated superior in
leadership, job performance and
use of weapons. In me final camp
standings he rated first in his
platoon, his company and first out
of 1,300 ROTC cadets from some
40 colleges in the West.
