“PEER GYNT” PREPARATIONS BEGIN TO ROLL
Costume designer Irene Griffin puts the finishing touches
on Robert Harden's costume he will wear in the Stanford
Players’ production of Henrik Ibsen's "Peer Gynt" to be
presented May 25, 26 and 27. The show will be staged in
Frost Amphitheatre and will begin at 8:30 p.m. Tickets are
on sale at Memorial Auditorium Box Office at $2.50 and $2
for the public and $2 and $1.50 for students.
