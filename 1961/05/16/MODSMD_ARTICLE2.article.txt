# Doctor Talks On Transplantation
## 
### 
One erf man’s oldest dreams—
the replacing of wornout or dis
eased body tissues and organs—
will be discussed at the Tuesday
Evening Series program tonight
by Dr. Halsted R. Holman.
Holman, who came to the Stan
ford Medical School last year as
executive head of the Department
of Medicine plans to trace the his
tory of transplantation of tissues
and organs from one human being
to another, the causes of failure
thus far, and the possibilities for
success in the future.
