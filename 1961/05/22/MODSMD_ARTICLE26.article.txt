# Uchida to Talk On Japan Art
## 
### 
Kunio Uchida will give a guest
lecture on contemporary Japa
nese design in crafts and architec
ture today in the Little Theater
at 7:30 p.m., sponsored by the
Keio Affiliations Committee of
UR.

Uchida. one of the foremost
authorities in his field, will show
slides and discuss the dynamic as
pects of Japanese crafts and arch
itecture, not so much from the
point of view of the professional
artist but more as to their impor
tance in the life of the layman.
The lecture is free to the public.
