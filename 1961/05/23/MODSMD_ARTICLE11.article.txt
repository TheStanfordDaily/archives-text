# Peace Corps Man on Farm
## 
### 
Paul Slawson, special as
sistant to the Director for Re
cruitment for the Peace
Corps, will be on the Farm
tomorrow to discuss opportu
nities in the Peace Corps,
according to Dean H. Donald
Winbigler.
Slawson will speak at an
open meeting for students
and faculty in Dinkelspiel
Auditorium at 10 a.m.

A second meeting will be
held in Dinkelspiel at 3:15
p.m. Slawson will be avail
able to talk with individual
students in Winbigler’s office
at 11 a.m. and 2 p.m.
