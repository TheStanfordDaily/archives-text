# Student Flicks Shown Today
## 
### 
Films made by Stanford
graduate students in the De
partment of Speech and Dra
ma will be shown at 4:30 and
8:30 p.m. today in the Mem
Aud Little Theatre.

The program, open free to
the public, is sponsored by
Act and Idea, an organization
of graduate students in the
drama department. There will
be a discussion and critique
of the films after the 4:30
showing, led by Dr. Henry
Breitrose of the film division
in the department.
