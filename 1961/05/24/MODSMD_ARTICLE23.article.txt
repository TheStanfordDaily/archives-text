VOW BOY REYNOLDS IN GRID HALL OF FAME
Robert O. "Horse" Reynolds is the newest member of the
National Football Hall of Fame, Reynolds was a tackle on
Stanford's celebrated Vow Boys teams of 1933-4-5. He was
an All-American. He created a record which, if tied, will
never be beaten. He played 180 minutes of Rose Bowl com
petition. Reynolds is Stanford's fifth Hall of Famer. The
others being Ernie Nevers, Bill Corbus, Frank Albert and
Bobby Grayson. Pop Warner made it into the Hall of Fame
as a coach.
