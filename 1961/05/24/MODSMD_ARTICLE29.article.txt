# Six Fox Award Winners Chosen
## 
### 
The Music and Biology Depart
ments have chosen six students to
receive the Stephen Fox Memorial
Award for 1960 - 61. William
Grothkopp Jr., Martha E. Krugg
and Janet M. Levy in music and
Julia Herskowitz Cohen. Kathryn
Klitz Brown and Nancy Jean Reed
in biology are recipients of the
awards.
The winners will receive books
of their choice. Funds will also
be given to the departments to be
used for the libraries.
The award, in memory of Ste
phen Fox, who died in an auto
accident during the summer of
1954 between his freshman and
sophomore year at Stanford, is
annually given to students chosen
by the Music and Biology Depart
ments.
