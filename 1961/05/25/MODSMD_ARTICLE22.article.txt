# AAWU Meeting Slated
## 
### 
Thomas J. Hamilton, director
of the Athletic Association of
Western Universities, announced
yesterday that the association’s
annual summer meeting will he
held June 13-16 In Seattle.
The University of Washington
will host the conference, made up
of athletic directors, faculty rep
resentatives and league directors.
