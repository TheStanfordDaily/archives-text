# '61 Quads Will Arrive Monday
## 
###

uted Monday and Tuesday at the
Quad office. They will also be on
saje at the office for $B.
Only a limited number of Quads
are available, so the Quad staff
urges people who have not or
dered a Quad but want one to
come early. The office will be
open from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. only.
Items for Once Around the
Quad must be submitted by 3 p.m.
the day preceding publication.
