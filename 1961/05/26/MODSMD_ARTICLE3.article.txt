# Inner Quad Rocks Tonight
## 
### 
That rumbling sound you
hoar is the Stanfords turning
over in their tombs at the
news the freshman class will
hold on all-campus rock ’n’
roll dance in the middle of In
ner Quad from 9 p.m. until
midnight today.
The dance—which will fea
ture the “Velveteens”—is free
and is billed as stag or drag.
Campus observers noted
that it is probably the first
such dance ever held on Inner
Quad another Stanford first.
