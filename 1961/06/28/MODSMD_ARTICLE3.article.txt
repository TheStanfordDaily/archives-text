# 'Hawaii Calls'— To Old Mem Aud
## 
### 
An historical sketch of Hawaii
through narration, song and
dance will be presented by the
Kamehameha Schools’ Concert
Glee Club next Wednesday at 8
p.m. in Memorial Auditorium.
Ukuleles, bamboo sticks and
conch shells will be used as ac
companiment for some of the Ha
waiian numbers. In addition, the
Hawaiian group will sing sacred
and secular selections from the
finest of male chorus literature.
All members of the club are of
Polynesian extraction. The club
is on a month-long good will tour
of the mainland.

Admission to the performance
will be 25 cents.
