# Lots to Do During Summer Quarter
## 
### 
Summer quarter at Stanford
offers a wide variety of activities
for participation by students and
faculty in those non-studying or
non-teaching intervals.
Recreational opportunities
range from golfing on one of the
state’s top courses to Sunday’s
first-run films, offered at 25 cents
admission.

And for those hot spells, which
do occasionally simmer the cam
pus, the Farm swimming pools
beckon.
FOLLOWING is a run-down on
the various eating, relaxing and
studying places on campus, and
the hours they are open:
The Main Library is open Mon
day through Thursday, 8 a.m. to
9 p.m. Fridays its hours are 8 a.m.
to 5 p.m., Saturday 8 a.m. to noon
and Sunday 1 to 5 p.m.
Cubberley library in the School
of Education building is open
Monday through Thursday from
8 a.m. to 10 p.m. Friday it opens
at 8 a.m. and closes at 6 p.m. Its
Saturday hours are 8:30 a.m. to
4 p.m., and Sunday from 1 to 5
p.m.
THE STANFORD bookstore is
open Monday through Friday
from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., and Satur
day from 8 a.m. to noon.
The Union store is open Monday
through Saturday from 7 a.m. to
7 p.m., and is closed Sunday.
The outside snack bar at the
Union is open every day except
Saturday from 7 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
THE POPULAR Union Cellar
is open Monday through Thurs
day, 7:15 a.m, to 10:30 p.m. Fri
day and Saturday its hours are
7:15 a.m. to 7 p.m. It is closed
Sunday.
The Union cafeteria is open
Monday through Friday, noon to
1 p.m. and 6 to 6:30 p.m. Sunday
it’s open from 6 to 6:30 p.m.
For those who want to take a

dip in one of the Stanford pools,
the Women’s pool is open to
women only Monday through Fri
day. Its hours are noon to 1 p.m.
for faculty wives and women em
ployees.
FROM 1 to 2 and 4 to 5 p.m. it
is open to women students. From
5 to 5:45 p.m. conference -uests
resident on campus can swim if
they arrange it previously.
On weekends, the swimming is
free. Saturdays from 2 to 5 p.m.
women students and two guests;
married male students and their
wives; University faculty and
staff members with wife or hus
band; and resident conference
guests may swim.
Sunday from 1:15 to 2:45 p.m. is
family hour. Children of regis
tered students, faculty and staff
members and their parents are
allowed to swim.
SUNDAYS from 3 to 5 p.m. is
the same as Saturdays in that
period.
At the Men’s pools, faculty, fac
ulty sons if accompanied by fath
ers, and students may swim with
out charge from noon until 2 p.m.
Monday through Friday.
On Monday through Friday un
til Aug. 5, male staff, faculty and
students may swim from 2 to 4
p.m. if it does not interfere with
the coaching camp.
FROM 5 to 7:30 p.m., Monday
through Friday, faculty, staff and
families, and students, may swim.
Saturdays and Sundays from 1
to 4 p.m. is the same as Monday
through Friday from 5 to 7:30
p.m.
The Stanford golf course is
open daily from 7:30 a.m. to 6:30
p.m.
ON SUNDAY evenings at 7:30
in Memorial Auditorium, current
movies are shown. Admission is
25 cents for students, staff and
faculty members.
