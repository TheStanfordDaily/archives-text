# 2 Farm Golfers Get All-America
## 
### 
One Stanford golfer has been
named to the first collegiate All-
American golf team and another
made the second team, picked for
the Wheaties Sports Federation.
Steve Smith, who was the run
ner-up in last year’s college golf
championship and who made it
into the semi-finals this year, was
picked for the six-man first team.
Kent Winton, another member
of Stanford’s top-ranking golf
squad, was chosen for the second
team.
Chairman of the selection com
mittee was Bud Finger, Stanford
golf coach and chairman of ar
rangements for the 1961 college
golf championship tournament.
