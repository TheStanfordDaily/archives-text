# Hall Chorale, Gondoliers' Ready for Dink
## 
### 
Chorale singing and light opera
will take over the stage at Dinkel
spiel Auditorium Sunday and next
Wednesday.
This Sunday, the famed William
Hall Chorale from Los Angeles
will present a program at Dinkel
spiel of various chorale selections.
The program will start at 3:30
p.m.
Admission is $2 for the general
public and $1.50 for summer
school students.
Tickets are on sale at the Din
kelspiel Box Office and Peninsula
Box Office in Town and Country
Village.
The Department of Music is
sponsoring the program.
Next Wednesday, the Commit
tee on Public Exercises will pre
sent Gilbert & Sullivan’s “The
Gondoliers.”
The Lamplighters from San
Francisco, favorites of Stanford
summer audiences, will produce
the play.
The program will start at 8 p.m.
and will be given the one night
only. It, too, will be in Dinkel
spiel.
Admission will be $2 for the
general public, and $1.25 for Stan
ford summer students.
Tickets are on sale at the Me
morial Auditorium Box Office,
12:30 to 4:30 p.m., Monday
through Friday.
