# Italian Singing Set Next Week
## 
### 
“An Evening with the Italian
Madrigal” will be presented in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium next
Wednesday by the Stanford Cho
rale and Choral Workshop.
Put on by the Department of
Music, the program will start at
8:30 p.m., and is free.

Prof. Harold Schmidt will di
rect the program, and will be as
sisted by instrumentalists.
Numbers will be sung by the
workshop students, by the cho
rale and by soloists.
Italian composers whose works
will be heard include Archadelt,
Willaert, Marenzio, Luzzaschi,
Monteverdi, Gastoldi, Steffani,
Orlando di Lasso and Banchieri.
