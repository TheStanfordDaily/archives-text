# Tresidder Dedication
## 
### 
Stanford’s Board of Trustees
will dedicate the site of Tresidder
Memorial Union next Thursday
at 11:30 a.m. This is the occa
sion of the board’s annual meet
ing on campus.
Ceremonies will be held under
the big oak on the Dinkelspiel
Auditorium parking lot.
Speakers will include President
Wallace Sterling, Trustee Presi
dent Thomas P. Pike and Larry
Royse, last year’s student body
president.
A special guest will be Mrs.
Mary C. Tresidder, widow of Don
ald B. Tresidder. the University’s
fourth president.
Work on the new union began
recently across from Dinkelspiel.
