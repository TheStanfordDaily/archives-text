# Laughs Set For Tonight
## 
### 
Buster Keaton’s all-time com
edy hit, “The General,” will kick
off the summer film series to
night at 8 in Memorial Audito
rium.
Admission is by series ticket
only. No single admission tickets
will be sold.
Tickets, at $1.50, are currently
on sale at the Memorial Audito
rium box office.
The Mem Aud box office is
open from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m.
daily.
Starting next week, the series
will be on Wednesday nights at
the same time and place.
Other films scheduled are
“Duck Soup,” “The White Sheik,”
“Smiles of a Summer Night” and
“Assassins et Voleurs.”
