OATQ
CANTERBURY: Noon com
munion and lunch each Tuesday
at Canterbury House, 1176 Emer
son St., Palo Alto. Rides leave
post office at noon, back by 1 p.m.
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE: Testi
mony meeting, 7:15 p.m. each
Thursday, upstairs in Women’s
Clubhouse.
GRADUATE COFFEE HOUR:
Every Thursday, 4 to 5:30 p.m.,
lower lounge of Women’s Club
house; all grad students welcome.
I-CLUB: Coffee hour, 5 to 6

dents.
STANFORD FOLK DANCERS:
Each Friday, 8 p.m., Dinkelsplel
parking lot.

ern with the late Gary Cooper
and a haunting song, will be
shown at Memorial Auditorium
Sunday at 7:30 p.m.; admission
20 cents.
