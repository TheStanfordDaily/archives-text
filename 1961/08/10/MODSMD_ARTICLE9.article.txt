# Final Exams Scheduled for Aug. 18 and 19
## 
### 
Once again, the quarter draws
to a close and final examinations
are just over the academic hori
zon.
The tests are scheduled from 8
a.m. to 6 p.m. next Friday and
Saturday. Dead week begins this
Monday.
For Stanford students return
ing for fall quarter, registration
days are Monday and Tuesday,
Sept. 25 and 26. Instruction starts
the next day.
Following is the schedule for
final exams, which holds unless
instructors say otherwise:
FRIDAY;
8 a.m. to 10 a.m. 8 o’clock
courses.
10:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. 4
o’clock courses and History 10
group examinations.
1:30 p.m. to 3:30 p.m. 10
o’clock courses.
4 p.m. to 6 p.m. 2 o’clock
courses.
SATURDAY:
8 a.m. to 10 a.m. 9 o’clock
courses.
10:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. 3
o’clock courses.
1:30 p.m. to 3:30 p.m. 11
o’clock courses.
4 p.m. to 6 p.m. 1 o’clock
courses.
A large number of courses are
exempt from final examinations.
They range from one course in
Asian languages, geography, the
Law School and political science,
to 14 in the Art Department. Stu
dents in Hispanic American Luso-
Brazilian studies have no finals.
