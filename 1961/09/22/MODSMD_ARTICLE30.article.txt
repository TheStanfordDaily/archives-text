# Hodges Predicts Jobless Decline
## 
### 
WASHINGTON (UPI) Com
merce Secretary Luther H.
Hodges yesterday forecast a
steady decline in the nation’s job
less rate. He said “reasonably full
employment’’ may be achieved
next year.
Hodges said government figures
show that the last recession was
the mildest and recovery from it
the fastest, of any business down
turn since the end of World War
11.
“There is momentum and con
fidence in almost every part of
the business community.” Hodges
said in a speech prepared for a
conference sponsored by the
American Forest Products Indus
tries.
