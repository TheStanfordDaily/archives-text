# Revival Film Tickets on Sale
## 
### 
Tickets are now being sold at
Memorial Hall Box Office for
Stanford’s three film series. The
Box Office is open from 12:30
p.m. to 5 p.m. Monday through
Friday.
In addition to the annual travel
and revival film series, an annual
series of Audubon Society wildlife
films will bo offered. This series
is composed of five natural his
tory color films and will be nar
rated by the outstanding natural
ists who produced them.
All are scheduled for 4:30 p.m.
in Cubberley Auditorium. The
opening film, “Ranch and Range,”
will be shown Oct. 4.
The fall quarter’s revival film
series will include four film
studies of children. These will be
shown on Thursday at 4:15 p.m.
and Fridays at 7:30 p.m., also in
Cubberley Auditorium.
“Forbidden Games” is scheduled
for Oct. 5 and 6; “400 Blows,” Oct.
12-13; “Ditte, Child of Man,” Oct.
19-20; and “Zero for Conduct” and
“The Quiet One,” Oct. 26-27.
The popular travel series begins
Oct. 25 with “Australia.” All pro
grams are set for 7:30 p.m. in
Memorial Auditorium.
“Austria in Four Seasons” will
be shown Nov, 13; “Persia” on
Jan. 10; “Africa” on Feb. 5;
“France" on March 5; “Norway”
on April 11; and "Adriatic Holi
day” on May 2.
