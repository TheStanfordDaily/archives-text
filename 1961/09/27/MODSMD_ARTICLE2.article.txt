# '63 Reunion At Searsville
## 
### 
The junior claws (’63) is holding
a reunion and general campus got
together at Searsvilie Lake to
night at 7:30. The Zodiacs will
provide music for dancing and
refreshments will be on sale.
Class President Dave Williams
said, “We are trying to make this
an all campus reunion, not just a
junior function as it has been in
the past.”
Admission will be 50 cents with
a junior class card and 75 cents
without. Rides will leave the front
of Dinkelspiel from 7:15 to 7:45
p.m.
