# Hemingway First Bender Exhibit
## 
### 
The Bender Room is currently
showing a group of first editions
of the works of the late Ernest
Hemingway, fncluded are the
Paris and Dijon editions of “Three

Stories and Ten Poems” of 1923.
The exhibit is a gift from Mr.
Christian de Guigne 111 of Hills
borough to the Charlotte Ashley
Felton Memorial Library and will
run through Friday.
