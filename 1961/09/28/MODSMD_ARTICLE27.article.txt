# Laotian Princes Agree To Choose Premier
## 
### 
BAN NAMONE, Laos (UPI)
The three contending Laotian
princes will meet within the next

two weeks to choose a coalition
premier for this divided Southeast
Asian kingdom, their representa
tives agreed here yesterday.
Leaders of this country's three
factions rightwing, neutralist

and pro-Communist will confer
during the first week of October
at Ban Hin Heup village. There,
the Nam Lik River marks the
firing line between rebel and gov
ernment armies.
