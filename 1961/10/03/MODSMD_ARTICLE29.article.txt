# Men's Glee Club Invites Members
## 
### 
All singing Stanford men are
invited to join the Men’s Glee
Club, a campus organization with
a long standing tradition. Activi
ties include serenading, singing
formal concerts here and on tour,
and performing on the broadcast
media.
Prof. Harold Schmidt, Director
of Choral Music, has announced
that the Men’s Glee Club is now
on equal footing with the other
choral groups on campus. One
unit of academic credit or an ac
tivity credit can be earned.
Rehearsals are held in Dinkel
spiel rehearsal room Tuesday eve
nings from 7-8 and Thursday af
ternoons from 4:15-5:30.
