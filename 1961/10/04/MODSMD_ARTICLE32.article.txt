# Vagabond' Crew To Meet Today
## 
### 
An organizational meeting for
1962’s edition of “The Vagabond,”
student traveler’s guide, will be
held at 4:30 p.m. today in the
HR Shack.
The booklet is published by the
Stanford Overseas Information
Service, a division of the Institute
of International Relations.
Anybody who has traveled,
studied or lived abroad, especially
in Latin America, Africa or Asia,
is urged to contribute his knowl
edge and ideas. Activity credit is
offered for work on the booklet.
Interested parties Who cannot
attend the meeting should contact
George Corfino at DA 3-9451 after
5 p.m.
