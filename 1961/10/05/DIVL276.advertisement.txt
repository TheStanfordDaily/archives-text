Senior Movie
Shown Tonight
The Senior Class has announced
the first in a series of movie
nights for Seniors to be held at
the E. Rossotti Country Club to
night at 9:30.
“Hell Below Zero’’ with Alan
Ladd plus a Charlie Chaplin spec
tacular will be shown. Admission
is 25 cents. Proof of age must be
supplied.
1961 Quads
Still Remain
A number of 1961 Quads remain
to be picked up by the individuals
or organizations that ordered
them, Student Financial Manager
Bud Ratts reports.
He urges anyone mising an or
dered Quad to stop by the ASSU
office and check to see if it is
there.
