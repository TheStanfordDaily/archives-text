# African Politics Theme Today
## 
### 
Hoover Institute is sponsoring a
talk by Dr. Cyril Rogers of Uni
versity College of Rhodesia and
Nyasaland this afternoon at 4:15
in the Chinese room of the Wom
en’s Clubhouse.
Dr. Rogers will speak on “Race
and Politics in Central Africa,”
then will answer questions over
coffee at the International Center.
Dr. Rogers just completed a
year’s study at University College
of the West Indies and is now
touring the U.S. on a fellowship
visiting African study centers. His
book on race relations in Central
Africa, written in collaboration
with Dr. Duignan, of the Hoover
Institute, will soon be published
by Yale University press.
