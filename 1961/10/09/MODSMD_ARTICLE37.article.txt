# Faculty Women's Program Planned
## 
### 
“Stanford Yesterday and To
morrow” is the title of the pro
gram to be given by Miss Edith R.
Mirrielees, Pimentos Professor of
English, and Mrs. Allan E.
Charles, member of the Stanford
Board of Trustees, at the first fall
meeling of the Faculty Women’s
Club today at 2 p.m. in the Wom
en’s Clubhouse.
Preceding the meeting, a “Wel
come Luncheon” for newcomers
to the Faculty Women’s Club will
be held at 12:30 p.m. in Bowman
Alumni House.
