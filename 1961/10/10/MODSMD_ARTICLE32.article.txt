# Author Ken Boulding To Speak Today
## 
### 
Economist Kenneth Boulding of
the University of Michigan will
speak on “The Economics of Re
armament” at 8 p.m. tonight in
Physics 100.
Boulding, who is being present
ed by the Political Union, is the
author of “The Organization Rev
olution” and “The Image,” which
he wrote while at the Center for
Advanced Study in the Behavior
al Sciences at Stanford in 1958.
