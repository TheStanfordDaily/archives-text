# Stu-Fac Board Spots Open
## 
### 
Student-Faculty Board is
opening tryouts for Freshman
and transfer students this
week.
The Board consists of mem
bers from the administration,
faculty, graduate division and
each of the undergraduate
classes.

Hostels, firesides, faculty
associates and the “Stanford

indicative portion of the

the ASSU office. Members
