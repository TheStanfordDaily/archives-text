# I-Club Plans First Party
## 
### 
The International Club will hold
their fii’st party of the season Fri
day, 8 p.m., at 1247 Cowper St.,
Palo Alto.
Rides will be provided from the
I-Club. Come stag or drag.
Admission is free for those with
membership cards and 50 cents
for those without.
