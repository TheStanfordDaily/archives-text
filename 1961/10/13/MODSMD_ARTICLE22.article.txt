# Peace Corpsmen Arrive in Manila
## 
### 
MANILA (UPD—A force of 128
members of the U.S. Peace Corps
arrived in Manila yesterday for
two years of voluntary teaching
service in rural Philippine schools.
John C. Kennedy, 57, and his
wife, 54, were the oldest members
of the group, the first Peace Corps
volunteers to he assigned to Asia.
The others, including about 70
women, were in their 20s.
Kennedy took a leave of absence
from his administrative post at
Ohio’s Oberlin College to join.
