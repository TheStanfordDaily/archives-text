# Engineers' Exam Review to Begin
## 
### 
Review sessions for the Nov. 18
Engineer-in-Training Examination
will begin next Tuesday under the
sponsorship of Tau Beta Pi.
Four weekly sessions on Tues
day evenings from Oct. 24 through
Nov. 14 will cover, in order, Stat
ics and Strength of Materials,
Fluid Mechanics. Thermodynam
ics and Electrical Engineering. In
terested engineers should sign up
at the Tau Beta Pi bulletin board
in Engineering Corner.
