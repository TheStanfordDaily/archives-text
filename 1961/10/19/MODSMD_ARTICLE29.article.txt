# Guerard to Talk About Novelists
## 
### 
A. J. Guerard, professor of
English, will speak on “The Be
ginning Novelist’s Self-Discovery
—Faulkner and Conrad” before
the Stanford Philological Associ
ation today at 4:15 in the lower
lounge of the Women’s Club
House.
A Philological Association
spokesman said that Guerard will
discuss the “dark fictional worlds
and the techniques of indirection”
that Faulkner and Conrad dis
covered.
