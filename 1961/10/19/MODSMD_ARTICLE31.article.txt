# Breathe Easier Now: Snake Recaptured
## 
### 
The rare albino gopher snake
missing from its cage at the Stan
ford Natural History Museum
since Sept. 28 was recovered yes
terday in the museum building.
The snake is harmless. It is
blind and never learned to bite
defensively, museum staffers said
with a sigh of relief as the 3-week
snake hunt ended.
