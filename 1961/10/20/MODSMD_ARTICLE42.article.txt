# WRA Asks Girls To Open House
## 
### 
All Stanford women are cor
dially invited to attend the WRA
open house, to be held tomorrow
from 9:30 to 11:30 in the Women's
Gym. Dress is informal—bermu
das, slacks, or skirls and refresh
ments will be served.
Information concerning the
women’s tennis, golf, swimming
and riding clubs and about winter
quarter ice skating classes will he
available. Fencing, swimming,
hockey and archery demonstra
tions are planned.
