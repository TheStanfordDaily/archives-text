# KZSU Plans Sports Series
## 
### 
KZSU will begin this year's
sports coverage with a broadcast
of the Stanford-San Jose frosh
football game this Saturday at
10:15, with Bill Smith and Ken
Johnson announcing.
KZSU plans to cover the re
maining frosh football games and
varsity soccer and water polo this
quarter. Ken Johnson, KZSU
sports director, stated that varsity
football will not be broadcast as
everyone is expected to attend the
games.
Frosh and varsity basketball
will bo broadcast winter quarter,
and plans are being made for base
ball and track coverage this
spring.
