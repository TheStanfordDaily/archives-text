# Four Tapped by AFROTC Group
## 
### 
Four Air Force ROTC students
were elected to membership in
the AFROTC honor organization,
Arnold Air Society, last night.
The four cadets are Ronald Al
bo, a junior; Nield Mercer, a sen
ior; Stephen Murata, a junior; and
Gerald Tucker, a senior.
In announcing the new mem
bers, Arnold Air president John
Chiorini said the cadets were
elected for having shown “excep
tional leadership ability.”
