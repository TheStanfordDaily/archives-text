# AWS Rummage To Sell at Stern
## 
### 
Need a formal? Skis? A type
writer? Go on a shopping spree
in Stern basement.
Sponsored by AWS, a rum
mage sale will begin tomorrow
in the Holladay basement of
Stern.
Articles, some of high value,
that were not claimed by students
who left them in dorms last year,
arc to be disposed of by AWS as
a fund-raising measure.
The sale will be on from 1 to 5
tomorrow afternoon and Thurs
day, and from 11 to 5 on Wed
nesday.
The sale will close Thursday.
