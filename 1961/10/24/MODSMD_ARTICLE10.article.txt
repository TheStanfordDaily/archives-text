# Quad to Take More Pictures
## 
### 
All residents of Toyon,
Stern and Lagunita, except
seniors, may come to the
Quad Office today prepared
to have their Quad picture
taken. Tomorrow, all off-cam
pus men may do the same.
White shirt, dark coat and tie
for men; black sweater for
women. Cost is $2.25. Time:
9:30-12 and 1-5:20.
