# Wilson Deadline Wrong in Daily
## 
### 
Deadline for faculty members
to nominate students' for Wood
row Wilson Fellowships is Oct. 31,
NOT yesterday, as incorrectly re
ported in Monday’s Daily.
Faculty members wishing to
nominate a student for one of the
awards should send the student’s
name to Dr. Robert S. Kinsman,
English Department, UCLA.
Kinsman, the regional director
for the program, will in turn send
application papers to the student.
