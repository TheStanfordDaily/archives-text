# Football Rally Set for Frost
## 
### 
A rally to whip up spirit for
the UCLA game will be
staged Friday night in Frost
Amphitheater.
The rally, planned to start
at 7:30 p.m., will feature the
Stanford Band, the pom pom
girls, and Tim Laddish and
his followers.

Sections of the band will
leave from Branner and Roble
at 7:15, converge at Stern
Hall, and march from there
to Frost. Feathers, pom poms
and hats will be on sale all
day Friday at Engineering
Corner.
