# Music Department Sponsors Recital

The Department of Music will
present a piano recital by Robert

kelspiel Auditorium. Today's pro
gram will bo the second in a series

Below, a 27-year-old pianist, is
assistant professor of music at
UC-Davis, and holds a B.A. and
M.A. in music from the University
of Louisville, where he was a stu
dent of Dwight Anderson.
Below graduated with the high
est average in the history of the
music school and was awarded a
Fulbright fellowship to study in
Germany. In 1960 he won the Kon
zertdiplom, awarded by the Hoch
scule fur Musik.
