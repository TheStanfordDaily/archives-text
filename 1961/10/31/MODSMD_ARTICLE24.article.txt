# Student March Protests Bomb
## 
### 
An all-student peace march will
begin at 11 on Saturday morning
at Cogswell Square in Palo Alto.
The purpose of this march is to
protest the recent Russian bomb
tests and to urge the U.S. and the
rest of the world to ban further
bomb tests.
The theme is “A World for All
Children.”
A high school discussion group
sponsored by the Peninsula Peace
Center is organizing the march.
Students, elementary through I
college, will walk from Cogswell I
Square, up University Avenue, to j
the Town and Country Shopping
Center, and, from there, to the
Stanford Shopping Center.
The sponsoring group will wel
come all students who wish to join
the march. For further informa
tion call DA 1-2220.
