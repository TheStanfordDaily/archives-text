# Ticket Sales End Thursday
## 
### 
Final sales of San Francis
co Symphony season tickets
will be today, tomorrow and
Thursday from 9-12 in the
Nitery. Tickets for the 19
symphonies are being sold at
half price. The tickets are
available in the orchestra
grand tier and dress circle for
$27.75 and in the balcony and
balcony circle for $21.85.
