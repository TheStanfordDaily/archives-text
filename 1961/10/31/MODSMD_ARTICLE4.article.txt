# Lag to Host H'ween Dance
## 
### 
Lagunita will host an all
campus Halloween dance to
night from 8 until midnight.
The dance will feature the
music of the Lancers, a rock
and roll band. Cider and
doughnuts will be served. Ad
mission is free to all students.
Lagunita courtyard will be
the scene of the dance, and
will be given a Halloween at
mosphere by eerie lighting in
the trees. Bermudas and
slacks are appropriate dress
for the women attending, be
cause the weather should be
cold.
