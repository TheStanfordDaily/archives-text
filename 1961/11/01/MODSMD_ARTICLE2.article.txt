# Douglas to Speak On Berlin Friday
## 
### 
Paul H. Douglas, senior sena
tor from Illinois, will deliver a
review of his recent trip to Ber
lin in Memorial Auditorium Fri
day.
His speech, which is free and
open to the public, begins at 4:15
p.m.
Douglas, who was first elected
from Chicago on the Democratic
ticket in 1948, is sponsored by
Political Union and the Model
United States Senate.
Douglas was educated at Bow
doin and holds a doctorate in
economics from Columbia Uni
versity. He was a member of the
faculty at the University of Illi
nois when he first ran for the
Senate.
He has taught at Reed College,
the University of Washington and
Amherst, and holds more than a
half-dozen honorary degrees from
other institutions.
He is a member of Phi Beta
Kappa, the American Academy of
Arts and Sciences and the Econo
metric Society.
