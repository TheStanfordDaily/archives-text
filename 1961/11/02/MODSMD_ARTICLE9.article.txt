# Mayhew Named Prof, of Education
## 
### 
Lewis B. Mayhew, a prominent
specialist in junior college edu
cation. has ben named to the
Stanford faculty.
Mayhew will assume his new
duties as professor of education
on Feb. 1, 1962. He will come
from the University of South
Florida, Tampa, where he is now
director of institutional research
and evaluation services.
He was educated at the Uni
versity of Illinois and received a
doctorate in history at Michigan
State University. He has been a
faculty member at the latter and
a consultant at Stephens College.
