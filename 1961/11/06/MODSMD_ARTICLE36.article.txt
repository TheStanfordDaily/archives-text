# Authors Sought By Poetry Sheet
## 
### 
“Penny Poetry,” a thrlce-week
ly poetry sheet, needs poetry for
this year’s publication, planned
to start after Big Game week end.
The editors promise a liberal to
non-existent editorial policy, try
ing neither to establish nor break
poetic standards. They feel their
function is to give an audience to
aspiring poets.
Poetry from students, faculty,
etc. is welcomed. It should be
sent to Penny Poetry, Box 7564.
Stanford, with a self-addressed
stamped envelope if the author
wants the manuscript returned.
