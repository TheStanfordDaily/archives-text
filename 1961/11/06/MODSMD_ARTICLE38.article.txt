# Director to Speak Of Film in Culture
## 
### 
Leopoldo Torre Nilsson, leading
Argentine film director, will
speak on “Film and the South
American Cultural Revolution”
today at 4:15 p.m. in Physics 101.
Nilsson is one of South Ameri
ca’s best known film directors,
his pictures, including “House of
Angels,” having won awards at
European film festivals.
His talk will be co-sponsored by
the Institute for Hispanic-Ameri
can and Luzo-Brazilian Studies
and the Radio, Television, Film
•section of the Speech and Drama
Department.
