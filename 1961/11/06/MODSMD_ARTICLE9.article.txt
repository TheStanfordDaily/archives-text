# Players to Stage Satiric Comedy
## 
### 
The Stanford Players will
blend live theater technique with
cinema in their production, “The
Marriage of Mr. Mississippi,” an
avant-garde, satiric comedy open
ing Thui’sday night at 8:30. The
play will be staged in Memorial
Theater.
Written by Swiss playwright
Friedrich Durrenmatt, “Missis
sippi” deals with a powerful wom
an and four men representing
foolish idealism, practical expedi
ency, communism and Mosaic
conservatism.
The effect of the play will be
increased with film sequences di
rected by Raoul Piezer.
