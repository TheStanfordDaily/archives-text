# Anglican Canon To Visit Campus
## 
### 
Canon Bryan Green, the famed
Church of England clergyman,
will begin a four-day series of
lectures on Basic Christianity
next Monday and will preach in
Memorial Church next Sunday.

Canon Green, who spoke to
turn-away crowds totalling 20,-
000 in a five-day visit to Stanford
in 1954, will oat lunches and din
ners at campus living groups and
will lead discussions at residences
following his evening lectures.

Canon Green still has some
open dates for meals and evening
discussions. Living groups inter
ested in inviting Canon Green for
a meal or discussion should phone
the Memorial Church office (DA
2-5211).
