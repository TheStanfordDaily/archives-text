# NSF Winners Like SU
## 
### 
Stanford ranked first national
ly last year as a study center for
winners of science faculty fellow
ships from the National Science
Foundation (NSF).
Twenty faculty fellowship win
ners picked Stanford, 16 the Uni
versity of California at Berkeley,
and 13 the University of Wiscon
sin, according to an NSF report.
In the NSF cooperative graduate
fellowship program, Stanford
ranked sixth nationally both in
number of winners from its stu
dents and in the number of win
ners choosing to study here.
