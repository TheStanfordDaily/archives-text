# Sterling Asked to Join Taft Institute Council
## 
### 
The Taft Institute of Govern
ment has asked Stanford Presi
dent Wallace Sterling to join its
Educational Advisory Council.
The Taft Institute, established
in memory of the late Senator
Robert Taft, is designed to pro
mote better public awareness of
the principles and problems of
constitutional government. Law
rence A. Kimpton, a Stanford
trustee, is chairman of the advis
ory council.
