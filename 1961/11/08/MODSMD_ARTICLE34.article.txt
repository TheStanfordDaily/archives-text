# Foreign Students To See Schools
## 
### 
Tours of the Hillview Elemen
tary School in Menlo Park and the
Cubberley High School in Palo
Alto are planned tomorrow for in
terested foreign students.
Cars will leave the I-Center at
10:15 a.m. and will return about 2
p.m. Stanford students will have
an opportunity to observe class
rooms and join students for lunch.
A sign-up sheet is posted at the
1-Center.
