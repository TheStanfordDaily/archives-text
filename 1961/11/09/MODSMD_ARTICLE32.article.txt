# Mexican Unrest Analyzed Tonight
## 
### 
The causes of present day un
rest in Mexico will be the topic of
a seminar conducted by Pedro
Neustaedter from 7:30 to 8:30 to
night in Room 241-L. Modern Eu
ropean Languages building.
Neustaedter, a native of Mexico,
is a chemistry graduate student at
Stanford.
The program is being given as
part of a seminar series sponsored
by the Latin American Desk of the
Institute of International Rela
tions.
