# Quad Gives Last Chance
## 
###

wish their Quad pictures
taken (color) may come to the
Quad office today from 9:30-
12, and 1-5:20. Men wear a
white shirt, coat and tie. Wom
en wear a pastel sweater. Cost
is $2.25. On-campus seniors
who have not made appoint
ments may do so at the Quad
office.
