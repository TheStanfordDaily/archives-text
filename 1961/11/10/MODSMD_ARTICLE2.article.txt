# Indian Sitarist To Appear Here
## 
### 
Indian musical virtuoso Ravi
Shankar will appear with his trio
at 8:30 p.m. next Wednesday in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Shankar, who composes and
plays the sitar, will bo accompa
nied by Kanai Dutta, drummer,
and IN'. C. Mullick, tambourist.
The sitar is a complicated six
stringed mandolin-like instru
ment.
Shankar is musical director of
All-India Radio and has appeared
on European and American tele
vision, both as the leader of his
group and as a lecturer explain
ing the ancient mysteries of clas
sical Indian music.
