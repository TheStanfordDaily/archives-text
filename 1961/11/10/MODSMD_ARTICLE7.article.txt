# Integrationist in Political Talks
## 
### 
“Controversy,” a newly organ
ized student political discussion
group, will feature The Rev. Ash
ton Jones at its second meeting
to be held tonight at 8 p.m. at
855 Partridge Ave. in Menlo Park.
Jones is currently suing Shreve
port, Ala., officials for damages
allegedly resulting from a pro
longer Jailing, severe beatings
and destruction of his "brother
hood bus" which toured the South
as part of the freedom rides.
He will discuss his own experi
ence as a southern integration
alist as well as the nature of
, southern Negro movements.
