# Cap and Gown Opens Drive
## 
### 
Cap and Gown Society will
launch its annual scholarship
fund-raising campaign offici
ally today at: a luncheon hon
oring Dean Lucile Allen.
The informal luncheon at
Bowman Alumni House will
introduce to Peninsula Cap

and Gown alumnae the 1962
engagement calendars and the
new notecards, sold each year
to support women scholars at
Stanford, and will bring to
gether for the first time local
members and the new Dean of
Women.

Calendars. $1.25 each, and
cards, 15 cents each, may be
purchased at the Bookstore,
the Cellar and from individual
Cap and Gown members.
