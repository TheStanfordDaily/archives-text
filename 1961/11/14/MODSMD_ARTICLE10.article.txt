# Christmas Ski Week Sales Continue Today
## 
### 
Christmas Ski Week ticket sales
continue today at Engineering
Corner, sponsored by the Stanford
Ski Club.
The price, $37, includes meals
and lodging for five days at Squaw
Valley, a special party for Stan
ford skiers, 10 per cent reduction
on lifts and ski lessons, swimming
pool privileges and free skate
rental at the Olympic Arena.
