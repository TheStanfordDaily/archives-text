# Socialist Editor To Speak Today
## 
### 
The Socialist Caucus of Political
Union will present a talk on “The
Rise of the New Right” at 8 p.m.
tonight in Room 61-H.
Michael Harrington, editor of
“New America” and a frequent
contributor to “Dissent,” “Com
mentary,” “Commonweal” and
“The Reporter," is the speaker.
Following his talk, there will be
a Socialist Caucus meeting with
I Bogdan Denitch, Socialist Party
representative to the Berkeley
1 campus.
