# Green to Probe Sex Morality
## 
### 
Canon Bryan Green will con
tinue his whirlwind schedule of
lectures and discussions today,
speaking tonight at 7:30 in Me
morial Church on “Is a Personal
Experience with God Possible?”
He will also give the third of his
four-lecture series on “Love,
Friendship, and Marriage a Ba
sis for Sex Morality” at 4:15 p.m.,
in Dinkelspiel Auditorium. To
day’s topic is “Five Plain Reasons
Why Sexual Intercourse before
Marriage Is Wrong.”
In the main lecture on basic
Christianity in the evening, Canon
Green will discuss how a personal
experience with God can bo found.
He will also speak on the problem
of sin and evil and how they are
reconciled with a good God. Fur
ther, he will answer the question:
What commitment and involve
ment is necessary to be a good
Christian?”
Canon Green, who received an
honorary doctorate last week at
the University of Manitoba in
Canada, will also continue his
visits to the living residences.
He wil eat lunch at Soto in Wil
bur and, then, dinner at Phi Sig
ma Kappa, He will address the
Newman Club at 8:45 p.m. and La
gunita at 9:15 p.m. and a men’s
residence, yet to be determined,
t heron flcr.
