# Schwabacher Sings Tonight
## 
### 
Tenor James Schwabacher will
appear in concert tonight at 8:30
in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
His concert, which is open to the
public without charge, will include
selections from the English and
Italian maroque periods, and
songs by Franz Schubert, Ralph
Vaughan Williams, and Francis
Poulenc. He will bo accompanied
by Alden Gilchrist, pianist, and
Germain Provost, violinist.
