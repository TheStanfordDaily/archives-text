# Stanford Starters in Big Game
## 
### 

STANFORD HOPES of bringing back the Axe to the protecting
confines of the Farm ride on these eleven Indian gridmen, who
will open for the hometowners in Saturday's Big Game. Start
ing for the Tribe are: left end, George Honore; left tackle, C. B.

Simons; left guard, Erroll Scott; center, Chris Cottle; right guard,
Tom Walsh (co-capt.); right tackle, Chuck Buehler; right end,
Frank Patitucci; left halfback, Danny Spence; fullback, Ed Cum
mings; quarterback, Rod Sears; right halfback, Gary Craig.
