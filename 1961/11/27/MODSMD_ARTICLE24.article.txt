# 'Six Characters' Seeks Players
## 
### 
Open tryouts will be held to
night, tomorrow night and Wed
nesday at 7:30 for the Stanford
Players’ production of "Six Char
acters in Search of an Author.”
Director Michael Addison in
vites all interested students to
come to room 205 of Memorial
Auditorium to try out for the
Luigi Pirandello modern tragic
comedy. Parts are available for
12 men and 10 women.
The play will be produced in
the Little Theater on Jan. 24, 25,
2G, 27 and 31, and on Feb. 1, 2
and 3. It will also tour to the Uni
versity of California at Davis on
Feb. 4.
