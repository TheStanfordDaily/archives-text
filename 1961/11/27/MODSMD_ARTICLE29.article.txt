# Wade Pass Beats Rams
## 
### 
CHICAGO (UPll—Billy Wade
tossed a 55-yard touchdown pass
to Willie Galimore with two min
it.es to play yesterday to give the
Chicago Rears a 28-24 National

Los Angeles Rams.
Wade’s game-winning pass

for the Bears. It came less than
one minute after the Rams had
rallied to go ahead 24-21.
