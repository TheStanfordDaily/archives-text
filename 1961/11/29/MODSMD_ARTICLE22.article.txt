# FROSH PRAISED
## 
### 
Editor, The Daily:
The work of all the freshman
men who constructed the Cal Cam
panile for the Big Game bonfire is

greatly appreciated. The fire has
seldom before had a definite shape.
Your efforts made the bonfire a
great success. Congratulations,
and many thanks for a fine job.
Tile Koble Girls
