# Winter Carnival Ski Club Topic
## 
### 
The Stanford Ski Club will an
nounce its 1962 Winter Carnival
at a meeting tonight at 7:30 in
Cubberley. Bill Shurtleff, Winter
Carnival chairman, will give the
place and date of the event and
name the entertainment for the
Snow Ball, to be hold as part of
the weekend.
Additional information will also
be available on the Christmas Ski
Week and the Race Team. This
will be the last time* that tickets

will be sold for the vacation Ski
Week.
Four ski movies will be shown
at the conclusion of the meeting.
