# Players Present Sartre One Act
## 
### 
“No Exit,” a one-act play by
Jean-Paul Sartre, will be present
ed as part of a student directing
project of the Stanford Players
in the Little Theater on campus
today at 8:30 p.m. The play
is open to the public without
charge.
The play is concerned with I
three people who are doomed to
live with each other in one room
through all eternity.
Directed by William Louis, the
play has been cast with Dale Wil
son as the valet, Michael Rewa
as Garcin, Gretchen Buterbaugh
as Inez, and Diane Bischoff as
Estelle.
