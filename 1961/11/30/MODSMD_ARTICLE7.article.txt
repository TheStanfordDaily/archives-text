# Warsaw Study Program Open
## 
### 
Scholarships for study at the
University of Warsaw are now
open for undergraduates and grad
uates for this year and next, the
HR announced today.

One scholarship runs from Jan
uary through August 1962, while
the other begins in September and
runs through June 1963.

All those interested in applying
should contact Jim Morrison in
22J, Thursday from 12 to 2 p.m,
and Friday from 9 to 10 and 11 to 2,
or see Don Light at the HR Shack
any day between 5 and 6 p.m.

Deadline for applications is
Dec. 4.
