# SU Electronicsmen Honored by Institute
## 
### 
Four Stanford electronics ex
perts have been honored by the
Institute of Radio Engineers.

Profs. Hubert Heffner and Al
len Peterson of the Stanford Elec
tronics Laboratories have been
named IRE Fellows, the top mem
bership rank of the Institute.
Prof. Marvin Chodorow and Dr.
Tore Wessel-Berg of the Stanford
Microwave Laboratory were
named to receive the W. G. R.
Baker Award for the best paper
published in IRE Transactions,
professional journal of the Insti
tute, during the past year.
