# Saints Sign Allain
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO (UPl)—The
San Francisco Saints of the
American Basketball League an
nounced yesterday the signing of
Jack Allain, former rebound ace
of Idaho State.
The 6foot-9, 225-pound center
brings the squad up to its full
complement of 10 men.
