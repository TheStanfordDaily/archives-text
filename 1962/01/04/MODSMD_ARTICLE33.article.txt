# Author to Review Red China Visit
## 
### 
Special student rales of $1 have
been announced for students to
hear an address by Edgar Snow
entitled “Red China Inside Out”
before the fourth meeting of the
World Affairs Council of North
ern California’s Forum Lecture
Series on Tuesday, Jan. 16, at 8:15
p.m. in the Gold Room of the
Fairmont Hotel.

Snow’s lecture will analyze im
pressions of a six-month visit to
Red China during which he inter
viewed Mao Tse-tung and Chou
En-lai. He is the author of “Red

Star Over China.”
Reservations should he made at
the World Affairs Council of
Northern California, 421 Powell
Street, San Francisco.
