# KZSU Opens New Series
## 
### 
Chris Von Saltza, Olympic gold
medal winner will be the featured
guest on “The Sound of Music”
tonight at 8 on KZSU. Chris will
be interviewed by host Bob Suffel
about her swimming experiences.
The program will be the first in
the series and will feature record
ed music and the special event
“This Week in History.”
KZSU will initiate an on-the-air
forum for campus views on any
and all topics on “Outlook” pre
miering tonight at 7:30. Telephone
opinions will be broadcast.
Calls will be taken at DAven
port 5-0880. All students are in
vited to comment on any issues
of local, national or international
significance.
