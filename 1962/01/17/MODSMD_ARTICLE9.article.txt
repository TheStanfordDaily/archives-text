# Ballet Presents Modern Work
## 
### 
“Original Sin,” a contemporary
ballet premiered last year by the
San Francisco Ballet, will appear
at Memorial Auditorium tomor
row.

The ballet was written by John
Lewis, leader of the Modern Jazz
Quartet. “Original Sin,” the story
of Adam and Eve, received rave
reviews for its dance techniques
and emotional impact.
Also on the program are Act II
of Tschaikovsky’s “Swan Lake,”
Auber's “Divortiseement,” and
“Caprice” by Franz VonSuppe.
Tickets are on sale at the box
office.
