# Like Hours Reviewed
## 
### 
During Winter Quarter the
library will be open the following
hours in the Reserve Book Room,
the Main Loan Desk and Stacks,
and the Humanities and Social
Science Rooms.

Monday-Friday .. 8 a.m.-ll p.m
Saturday 8 a.m.- 5 p.m
Sunday 1 p.m.-11 p.m

Note that the above depart
ments are open Sunday evenings
this quarter. The other depart
ments of the Main Library will
maintain their regular hours.
The Western Civilization Libra
ry will be open until 10:15 p.m.
seven nights each week.
