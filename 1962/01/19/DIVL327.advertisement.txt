INDIANS*
While I see many
hoofprints going
in, 1 see none
coming out. Know
why? Everyone is
galloping down to
R/A for the big
January Clearance
sale of famous
brand name men’s
shoes. Prices are
cut from 15% to
35% now! Prance
down and shoe up
now. Leave no
hoof-marks!
roos/atkins
