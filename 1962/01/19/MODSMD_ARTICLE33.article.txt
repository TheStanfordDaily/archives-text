# Jungian Analyst to Speak Sunday
## 
### 
Dr. Elizabeth Howe, author,
lecturer and practicing Jungian
analyst in San Francisco, will
speak this weekend on “Religion
and Self-Acceptance.”
The program, sponsored by the
college-age Friends (Quaker)
group, will be hold Sunday, 7:30
p.m., at 2081 Amherst, in College
Terrace. The public is invited.
Dr. Howe, a graduate of Stan
ford and the University of Cali
fornia, studied under Jung in Zu
rich. She has since heen an active
spokesman for the Jungian school
of psychology, lecturing at col
leges and universities and writing
several books and many articles.
