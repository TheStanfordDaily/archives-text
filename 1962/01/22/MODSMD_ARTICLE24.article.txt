# Pre-Reg Tryouts
## 
### 
Tryouts for next fall’s Pro-Reg
Committee, which is responsible
for the freshmen orientation pro
gram the week before fall regis
tration. will be held from this
Wednesday until next Wednes
day, according to Randy Deven
ing. chairman of the Pre-Reg
Committee.
Tryouts will be hold in the His
torical Room of the Bowman
Alumni House from 1 p.m. to 5
p.m. and from 7 p.m. to 10 p.m.
On Saturday they will he held
from 9 a.m. to 12 p.m.
Thirty-four places on the Com
mittees are available for inter
ested freshmen, sophomores and
Juniors. Sign up lists are posted
outside the door of the ASSU
office.
