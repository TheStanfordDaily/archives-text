# AFS Seeks Students To Chaperon Bus Trip
## 
### 
The American Field Service is
putting on a program tonight at
9 in the Women’s Clubhouse for
'juniors, seniors, and graduate
students interested in applying
for jobs as chaperones on the
Service’s summer bus trips across
the country.
Interviews for those interested
will be held tomorrow and
Wednesday. Sign-ups are in the
ASSU office.
