# Art Print Rental At Union Thursday
## 
### 
Want a print of a famous con
temporary painting to brighten
the dull walls of your room?

You can rent one for the rest
of the school year for only 25
cents. You can take your pick
from 200 prints tomorrow only
from 9 to 5 in the lobby of Union
residence.

The program is sponsored by
the ASSU Art Board. The collec
tion of masterpieces was former
ly distributed by the university
Library. Art board is handling it
for the first time this year.
The rental fees will go towards
repairing and enlarging the col
lection.

ASSU students will be given
first choice in selecting prints.
