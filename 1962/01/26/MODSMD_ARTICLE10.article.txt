# New Prof in Pulpit Here
## 
### 
Dr. Robert McAfee Brown will
speak in Memorial Church at the
11 a.m. service Sunday. His topic
will be “Joy and Concern: a
Christian Dilemma.”
Succeeding the late Alexander
Miller, Brown will begin teach
ing here next fall quarter. He is
presently Auburn Professor of
Systematic Theology at Union
Theological Seminary, New York
City.
There will be a service of Holy
Communion Sunday at 9 a.m. Aft
er the 11 service there will be a
coffee hour in the Vestry so that
students may meet Brown.
