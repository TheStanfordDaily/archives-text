RECENT preliminary investigations re
veal an enzyme, isolated from Bixales
atlansis near the Hodonin (Czech.) oil
fields, catalyses the novel rearrange
ment of two isoprenoid units into the
basic adamantane structure. Further
work will follow on the details of the
unusual behavior of this enzyme (sug
gested name, adamantase).
