# Ticket Sale Set For Folk Singers
## 
### 
Miriam Makeba and the Chad
Mitchell Trio will appear in a
concert in Memorial Auditorium
at 8:30 Monday, Feb. 26. The con
cert is sponsored by the Senior
Class for the benefit of the Heri
tage Fund.
Miss Mekeba, a favorite per
former in her native South Africa,
has been well received here. Both
she and the Chad Mitchell Trio
are “true folk singers”, using
songs handed from generation to
generation by people from all
parts of the world.
Tickets will go on sale in the
Mem Aud box office Monday, both
as singles and in blocks.
