# Frosh Books Now on Sale
## 
### 
Frosh Books will be on sale to
day from 9-5 at Eng. Corn, and
in front of the P.O. There are
many excellent reasons why you
should buy a Frosh Book now
(see yesterday’s Daily). A prac
tical reason might be because
there are only a limited number
left.
Last year there were many peo
ple who wanted to buy one later
in the year, only to find them
sold out. Besides, there are more
people in the 1962 book than ever
I before, thus making it a collec
tor’s item.
