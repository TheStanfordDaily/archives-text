# Law Scholarship Open To Graduate Women
## 
### 
The Soroptimist Club is offer
ing a special scholarship to a
woman working for a degree in
law. as well as a fellowship open
to women in other areas.
Further information may be ob
tained in the Financial Aids Of
fice (102-B).
The deadline for application is
March 1.
