# Escondido Village To Be Expanded
## 
### 
A federal loan to SI an ford of
$6.75 million for construction of a
“second Escondido Village” was
approved today.

The money will finance con
struction of 333 apartment units
on a 32-acre site next to the first
Escondido Village unit on Stan
ford Avenue.
The 82 throe-bedroom, 188 two
bedroom, and 63 one-bedroom
apartments will open on a land
scaped area.
The budget includes installa
tion of electric kitchen units,
landscaping, parking lots, and
lounges and halls for group
events.
The loan was granted by the
Community Facilities Adminis
tration of the Federal Housing
and Home Finance Agency.
