# Oh Captain!' Cast Will be Chosen
## 
### 
Singers, actor s, and dancers will
match skills next Tuesday and
Wednesday, Feb. 20 and 21, when
tryouts are held in Mem And for
Ram’s Head Spring Show, “Oh
Captain!”
Auditions will be held from 9-11
p.m. on Tuesday and from 7-11
p.m. on Wednesday for the three
starring roles, four supporting
roles, and chorus. Copies of the
script are available at the Current
Periodicals Desk of the Humani
ties Room and in the Ram’s Head
Office in the Union Building.
Signups for technical crews and
production staff will also be held
at the tryouts.
