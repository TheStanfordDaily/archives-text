# Tickets on Sale For Boys Choir
## 
### 
Tickets for the Vienna Boys
Choir benefit concert sponsored
by the HR at 8:15 tonight are on
Sale in the Nitery and the ASSU
Office from 2 to 5 p.m. and at the
Memorial Auditorium Box Office
at 7:30 p.m.
The 22 boys, aged eight through
14, will sing religious, folk and
operetta pieces. The Vienna Boys
Choir was founded in 1498, and
Hayden and Schubert, as boys,
sang with the group.
Proceeds from the performance
will go towards scholarships for
two foreign students.
