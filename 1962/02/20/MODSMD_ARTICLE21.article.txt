# Students to Plan Strike for Spring
## 
### 
WASHINGTON (UPI) Flans
were announced Sunday for a one
day strike by peace-campaigning
college students all over the coun
try this spring.
A leader of a massive week end
student demonstration here said a
day would be set aside later for
students interested in the “Turn
Toward Peace” movement to boy
cott classes.
Peter Goldmark, New Canaan,
Conn., head of a student peace
organization at Harvard which
organized Friday and Saturday’s
picketing and demonstrations
here, said an effort would be
made to make the one-day stu
dent strike in the spring world
wide.
