# Algerian Crisis Will Be Covered
## 
### 
Miss Kathryn Updegraff, soci
ologist and historian, will speak
on “Algeria Prospects and
Problems’’ this afternoon at 4:15
in Physics 100.
In 1957 Miss Updegraff spent a
week behind rebel lines in Algeria
with a medical unit of the Algeri
an Army of Liberation. In 1961
she was in Tunisia during the Bi
zerte crisis, working as editor
and broadcaster for Radio Tunis.
Her interviews with Algerians,
from top nationalist leaders to
peasants in the fields, have given
her an intimate knowledge of the
thoughts and aspirations of the
Algerian people in their struggle
for independence.
