# Spring Quarter Pre-Reg Will Begin Next Week
## 
###

advisers for preregistration pro
gramming on Wednesday, Thurs
day, and Friday, February 28-
March 2. Advisers’ special office
hours should be posted outside
their offices and at department
or school headquarters. Students
who do not come for assistance
on one of these three days will
forfeit the privilege of preregis
tration.
