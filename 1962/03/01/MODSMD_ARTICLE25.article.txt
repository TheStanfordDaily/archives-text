# Frosh Dunked
## 
### 
The Santa Clara Swim Club
defeated Stanford frosh 51-40 in
a swim meet held yesterday in the
Stanford pool.
Three records were set, two by
Stanford. Marty Hull swan the
200 meter individual medley in
2:08.4, bettering the listed frosh
collegiate record. Illka Suvanto
swam the 220 freestyle 2:04.2 to
break the listed mark for frosh.
In the 220 freestyle, Don Schol
lander of SC swam a 2:03.8, to
defeat Suvanto, and set a Stan
ford pool record.
