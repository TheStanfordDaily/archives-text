GUY F. ATKINSON COMPANY
Prospective Civil Engineering Graduates interested in careers
in the general construction field are invited to interview with
the Employment Manager of that firm, James A. Ferren.
Mr. Ferren will be at the Civil Engineedng office, Engineering
Building, Room 315, on March Bth to set up the sign-up
schedule preliminary to interview contacts.
Guy F. Atkinson Company's operations are world-wide and
its schedule normally includes a variety of heavy projects
such as hydroelectric and water conservation works, freeway
programs, harbor facilities, etc. The Company welcomes the
chance to help Stanford graduates develop their skills toward
the management of such projects.
