# Red China Topic For Film, Debate
## 
### 
A film and open debate on
United States policy toward Red
China will be sponsored by the
Conservative Caucus this Thurs
day at 7:30 p.m. in Cubberley Au
ditorium.
The film “Red China Out
lawed” is narrated by Lowell
Thomas and includes excerpts
from speeches by Senator Paul
Douglas, Son. Thomas Dodd, and
Rep. Walter Judd, as well as docu
mentary accounts of atrocities
against U.S. troops in Korea.
