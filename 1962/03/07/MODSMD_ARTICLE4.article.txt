# Technical Crews Needed for Show
## 
### 
Tom Cruse, Technical Director
for Ram’s Head’s 1962 Spring
Show, “Oh Captain!” announced
today that technical crews are
needed for the productoin.
“Oh Captain!” is going to be
one of tlio most elaborate pro
ductions ever’ done at Stanford,”
Cruse said. “We are going to
need at least fifty people for sot
construction and painting. No ex
perience is necessary you don’t
have to be a carpenter or an art
major. We also need girls for
work on costumes; no experience,
other than ability to use a sew
ing machine.”
All students interested in earn
ing an activity credit by working
one or two evenings a week may
sign up for crews all this week
in the Ram’s Head Office on the
second floor of the Union. The
work will not start until the first
week of spring quarter.
