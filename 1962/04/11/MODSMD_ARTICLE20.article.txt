# Modern Dancers Perform Tonight
## 
### 
Dance • choreography Mary
Tiffany and her UC-Santa Bar
bara Dance Workshop troupe will
present a contemporary concert
at 7:45 tonight in the Women’s
Gym.
Miss Tiffany has studied at
many colleges and has appeared
on stage and screen. The program
she will present includes Genesis,
several short numbers and a
dance from Sappho’s poetry.
