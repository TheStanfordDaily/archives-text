# WRA to Sponsor Girls' Trail Rides
## 
### 
WRA will sponsor three trail
rides this quarter at the Stanford
Red Barn. Rides are for women
students only and will be held on
April 22, May 6, and May 20 from
3-5:15. The cost per ride will be
$2.50. Since rides are limited to
ton riders each, sign-ups will bo
on a first come, first served basis.
Sign-ups will be held at the Wom
en's Gym today 11:30-12:15 and
tomorrow 910 a.m. and 1-1:30
p.m.
