# Goheen to Talk on Japanese Students
## 
### 
The Community Committee for
International Students announces
the second of its series in inter
national lectures today at the
Women’s Clubhouse at 1 p.m.
Professor John Goheen will speak
on “The Japanese Student.”

Professor Goheen heads the de
partment of Philosophy at Stan
ford. He has been awarded the
Japanese Order of the Sacred
Treasure for his contributions to
United States and Japanese cul
tural and educational relations. In
addition to lecturing at various
Japanese universities in recent
years. Professor Goheen has
helped in establishing a study

program at Stanford which
brings together American and
Japanese specialists for collabor
ative research. He was the first
director of the Stanford Center
for Japanese Studies in Tokyo.
