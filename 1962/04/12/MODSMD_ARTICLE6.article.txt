UNIVERSITY OFFICIALS YESTERDAY MET with the Santa Clara County Board of Supervisors to explain
Stanford's opposition to the proposed Ladera Dam project. Hoping that the Board would withdraw
their support of the project, President Wallace Sterling, backed by Ray Linsley, head of the Depart
ment of Civil Engineering, said there is not enough rainfall in this area to make the project worth-while
for water conservation or as a recreation area, and that as a flood control measure it is greatly
overdesigned.
