# Golfers Host Bears Today
## 
### 
Stanford’s golfers, fresh from
lopsided wins against San Diego
and Santa Clara last week, meet
Cal here today at 1 p.m. and to
morrow face the Stanford Golf
Club at noon. The frosh squad
meets Bellarmine High School of
San Jose today at 2 p.m. on the
Stanford Golf Course.
