# Stanford Art Patrons Offer Gallery Display
## 
### 
Favorite, contemporary works
of art owned by members of the
Stanford Committee for Art are
now on display at the Stanford
Art Galery in a show entitled
‘‘Proud Possessors.”
The exhibit brings together the
diversity of taste of more than
50 art patrons. There is art from
France, Japan, Holland, China,
Mexico, Italy, Israel, Spain, Ger
many, and the U.S.
The Art Gallery is open from
10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Tuesday through
Saturday and on Sunday from 10
a.m. to 4 p.m.
