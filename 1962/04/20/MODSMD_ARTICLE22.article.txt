# Freshman Trackmen Test Sequoia, Menlo
## 
### 
The Stanford freshman track
team hosts College of Sequoias
and Menlo College today at 4 p.m.
on Angell Field.
Leading the Cards will be
sprinter Larry Quested, shotput
ter Jack Chappie, and distance
runners Weym Kirkland, Harlan
Andrews, Paul Schlicke and Dave
Stineback.
