# African Authority To Give Seminar
## 
### 
Dr. Virginia Thompson, noted
authority on French Africa, will
address a Seminar in the Hoover
Tower today at 4:15 p.m.
Dr. Thompson is presently a
Visiting Professor at the Univer
sity of California. She plans to
do research in Madagascar this
summer for a book.
With her husband, Richard Ad
loff, Dr. Thompson has written
on both Southeast Asia and Af
rica. Their books on “French
West Africa” and “The Emerging
States of French Equatorial Af
rica” were published by the Stan
ford Press.
