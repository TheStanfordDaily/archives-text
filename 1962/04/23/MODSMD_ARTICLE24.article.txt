# Prof to View Conservatism
## 
### 
Dr. Willmoore Kendall, profes
sor of political theory at George
town University and an associate
editor of National Review, will
speak tomorrow at 4:15 p.m. in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Kendall’s talk, entitled “What
Is Contemporary Conservatism?”,
is being presented by the Con
servative Caucus of the Political
Union.
A' visiting professor in the Po- j
litical Science Department, Ken |
dall is remembered largely for I
his debate with former visiting
professor, Mulford Sibley, and
various other encounters during
his controversial year on campus.
The professor is the author of
several books, including “Democ
racy and the American Party
System” and “John Locke and
the Doctrine of Majority Rule.” |
He is presently working on three
other books.
There will be a reception and
dinner for Kendall at the Pioneer
Hotel in Woodside beginning at j
6 p.m.
