# Student Injured In Car Crash
## 
### 
Stanford sophomore Ward
Wood Jr. received a fractured
jaw and scalp cuts when his car
crashed into a concrete bridge
railing on Junipcro Serra near
Alpine Road Friday night.
Wood's ’54 auto was probably
totaled by the crash, police said.
Wood, who lives at Beta Theta
Pi fraternity, was driving west
on Junipero Serra when he lost
control of the car. Menlo Park
Police Sergeant John Spencer
said he noticed alcohol on Wood’s
breath but could not tell how

much he had been drinking. Wood
refused to take a blood test.
Police said they would not de
cide whether to cite Wood until
after an investigation.
