# Peace Corps Test Saturday
## 
### 
Another Peace Corps entrance
examination will be offered at
8:15 a.m. this Saturday in Room
47 of the Men's Physical Educa
tion Building.
The Washington headquarters
ordered the special test when it
was learned that an overflow
group of 69 showed up for the
test last Saturday, and there were
only 60 examinations available.
One need not sign up in advance
to take the tost.
Over 140 Stanford students
have taken the Peace Corps Exam
this year, one of the top records
of any university in the country.
