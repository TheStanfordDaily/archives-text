# Students to Give Spanish Drama
## 
### 
The Spanish play, “En la ardi
cnte oscuridad,” will be presented
at Stanford on April 28 and 29.
Professor Isabel M. Schevill’s
dramatic class will give the
drama in Spanish.
The play which is written by a
contemporary playwright of
Spain, Buero Vallejo, concerns
the difference between the people
who accept what is easily obtain
able in life and those who strive
for the impossible.
The sotting is an institution for
the blind from birth where an il
lusionary peace and happiness
exist formed by the philosophy of
optimism in the institution.
The performances are at 8:15
p.m. April 28 and 2:15 April 29
in Cubberley Auditorium. Admis
sion is free.
