# Honor Student Ends Own Life
## 
### 
A San Francisco boy who had
received a scholarship to Stan
ford but failed to receive one
from Harvard committed suicide
yesterday.
Gary Hooley, a senior at Lin
coln High School, shot himself in
the head with a .22 automatic
pistol at his home. His mother
was in the yard and found him
dead at 3 p.m. yesterday.
The boy was home from school
because ho “had not been feeling
well,” his father said. The father
continued that his son had been
despondent “because he was
turned down for a scholarship at
Harvard.”
Hooley was an honor student
interested in physics. A friend
Mike Savan has received a schol
arship to Harvard.
