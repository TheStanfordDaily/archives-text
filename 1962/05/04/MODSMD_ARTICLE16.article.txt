# Cards Favored For Harback Cup
## 
### 
The Harbach Cup is at stake
tomorrow, as the Stanford crew
goes to Los Angelos to compete
against the crews of the UCLA
Bruins USC Trojans.
The Cup is given to the winner
of the traditional Race between
the three schools at San Pedro.
The Cards are heavy favorites
to win the trophy, as they beat
both schools handily earlier this
year. Back on April 7, Stanford
beat them both while in a meet
also Including Oregon State.
Stanford beat SC again a week
ago in San Diego.
