# Budget LASSU Topic Tonight
## 
###

year, the budget for next year,
and people who have been elected
for next year will occupy LASSU
tonight.

ager and assistant manager and
program for next year’s concert
series, and it will be asked to
consider a change in the By-laws
covering the concert series.
The election of the UR presi
dent, the Democratic Caucus
chairman and Rally Comm offi
cers will be approved.
AWS, Executive, Judicial. Rally
Com and speech budgets will be
considered.
