# Netters Beat BC; Meet SF Today
## 
### 
The Indian netmen rambled to
an easy 10-0 victory over the
University of British Columbia
yesterday on the Stanford courts.
The Cards did not use their top
two men as only one match went
to three sets in the impressive
win.
The Indians will play the Uni
versity of San Francisco today to
close out their regular season.
Last week the netters beat USF
9-0. The match will he held in
San Francisco and will start at
2:30 p.m.
