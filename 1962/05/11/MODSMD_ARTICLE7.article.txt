# KZSU to Broadcast 'Merry Melodies'
## 
### 
KZSU will broadcast the
Spring Sing both live and de
layed. The direct program
from Frost will begin at 7:15
p.m. The delayed broadcast
on Tuesday at 8:80 p.m. will
present only the singing
groups (without the pauses)
and will allow performers a
chance to hear themselves.
