# GOP Candidate To Discuss Votes
## 
### 
Lloyd Wright, candidate for the
Republican senatorial nomination
against incumbent Thomas Ku
chel in the June party primary,
will speak Monday at 4:15 p.m. in
Cubberley Auditorium.
Wright, a conservative, will
critically comment on Kuchel’s
voting record in a talk entitled,
“The Metamorphosis of Tom Ku
chel.”
The candidate’s appearance is
under the sponsorship of the Con
servative Caucus of the Political
Union.
