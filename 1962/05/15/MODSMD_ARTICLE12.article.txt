# Players Will Hold Beckett Tryouts
## 
### 
The Stanford Players will hold
open tryouts this evening and to
morrow evening from 7:30 to 9:30
for the West Coast premiere of
“Happy Days” by Samuel Beck
ett.

The tryouts, according to di
rector James Kerans, will be held
in the Little Theater tonight and
tomorrow night in Room 129 of
Memorial Theater.
