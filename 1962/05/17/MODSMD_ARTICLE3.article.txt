# Stanford Host To Venezuelans
## 
### 
Fifteen Venezuelan newsmen
are now at Stanford for a 10-day
visit during a nation-wide tour.
They arrived Friday.
The newsmen are the guests
of Prof. Clifford Weigle, execu
tive head of the Communication
and Journalism Department, and
the Stanford faculty.
During their stay they will meet
with faculty members, visiting
professors. San Francisco offici
als and newsmen in a series of
seminars devoted to Latin Ameri
can problems.
The visit is designed to give
the newsmen an opportunity to
see the U.S. and to promote good
will. The tour is sponsored by the
North American Assn, of Vene
zuela, a group of U.S. firms with
branches there.
