# One Act Play, 'Form' To Be Presented Today
## 
### 
“The Form,” a one-act satire
by N, F. Simpson, will be pre
sented in the Little Theatre this
afternoon at 4:30. The program
is open to the public without
charge.
The farce is directed by Victor
Mitchell, a graduate student in
the department of speech and
drama. The cast includes Donna
Stone, Karen Seyfert, Foster
Hirsch and Carl Langguth.
