# Peace Corps Adds 3 More
## 
### 
Three more Stanford students
have been selected to begin train
ing for the U.S. Peace Corps this
summer, bringing the total to 14
since the beginning of the quar
ter.
Scott Palmer, who is doing
graduate work in Hispanic-Amer
ican studies, has been selected for
Peru to work in the field of
community development. Marty
Welsh, a graduate student in bi
ology, will teach in Pakistan, and
Phil Littmann, a senior in biolo
gy, will teach secondary school in
Ethopia.
