# Palo Alto Boy Scouts To Have Campout Here
## 
### 
Some 275 Boy Scouts and their
leaders will stage a three-day
Camporee in Stanford’s “forest”
otherwise known as the Arbor
etum.
Scouts from the South Palo
Alto District will pitch their tents,
do their own cooking and practice
their Scout lore today, tomorrow,
and Sunday.
