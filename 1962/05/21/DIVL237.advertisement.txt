PACE Drives
For Culture
In an effort to keep pace
with forward looking Stan
ford, Smith College has
adopted a PACE program of
its own.
Unlike Stanford’s program
which is concerned with the
pursuit of a higher objective,
money. Smith’s program, call
ed Project for Advancement
of Cultural Exchange, is con
cerned with culture.
