# Chief of Student Police Appointed
## 
### 
■ ■
Martin Gibson, a sophomore
Theta Chi, will head the student
police next year.
A member of the student police
this year, Gibson was appointed
on the basis of experience and
leadership by Mike Wells, current
student police head and by Fred
Nelson, student financial man
ager designate.
“The position of the Student
Police Force is derived from the
respect of the student body. In
the coming year we will attempt
to prove worthy of the respect
and full cooperation shown us this
year,” Gibson told The Daily.


MARTIN GIBSON
New Police Chief
