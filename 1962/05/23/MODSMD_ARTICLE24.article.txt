# Noon Piano Recital Of Bach At Musical
## 
### 
Pat Mersman will give a piano
recital at the Noon Musicale to
day in Dinkelspiel Rehearsal
Hall.
The program includes “Prelu
dio in E minor" by J. S. Bach,
“Fughetta in E minor” by W. F.
Bach, “Eleven Bagatelles,” op.
119 by Beethoven and “Minia
tures” by Dimitri Nenov.
