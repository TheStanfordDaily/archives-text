# Bailey to Discuss 'Measure' Play
## 
### 
Dr. Margery Bailey, professor
emeritus of English, will give a
lecture on "Measure for Mea
sure" tomorrow at 4:15 p.m. in
Ihe Little Theatre.
Act and Idea, graduate student
drama organization, is sponsoring
Dr. Baileys’ lecture in connection
with the opening tomorrow eve
ning of Shakespeare’s "Measure
for Measure” presented by the
Stanford Players in Memorial
Auditorium. The play will also he
given Friday and Saturday eve
nings.
Tickets, priced at $2.50. $2.
$1.50 and $1.25 are on sale at
Memorial box office.
