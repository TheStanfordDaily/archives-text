# Pool Hours Announced
## 
### 
The Athletic Department has
announced the following pool
hours:
MEN’S POOL will be open (men
only) Monday through Friday,
June 24-August 24, from noon to
2 p.m. Bring your own towel.
There is no charge.
WOMEN’S POOL will be open
(women only) Monday through
Friday, from 12 to 1 p.m. $2.50 per
quarter, or 15tf a swim.
MEN’S POOL. A recreational
swimming program is open to
men, women, Stanfordites, and
their families. The charge is $3
per person for a Quarter card. In
dividual swimming is 75tf for
adults, 35tf for children.
Hours are Monday through Fri
day, June 25 to Sept. 7, 5 to 7
p.m., and Saturday and Sunday
from 1 to 4 p.m. Cards may be
purchased now at Pool or Ath
letics Dept. The pool will be
closed July 21-22 during the
U.S.A.-U.S.S.R. track meet.
A similar recreational swim
ming program will be held at the
Women’s Pool on Saturdays, from
2 to 5 p.m., and on Sundays from
1:15 to 2:45. Bring your own
suits, caps, and towels. There is
no charge. Dressing rooms are
available.
