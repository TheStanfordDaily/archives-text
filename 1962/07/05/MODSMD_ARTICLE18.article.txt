# Choir Asks Songsters
## 
### 
All Stanford students, faculty,
and adult members of their fam
ilies are Invited to join the Sum
mer Choir of Memorial Church,
June 26 to August 12.
The choir will meet regularly
on Tuesdays from 4-5:30 p.m.,
Thursdays from 7-8:30 p.m., and
Sundays from 10 a.m.-12 noon.
In addition to providing music
for the Sunday services In Me
morial Church, the choir will join
choirmaster Thomas Harmon in
presenting a Concert of Organ
and Choral Music on the after
noon of Sunday, August 12.
All students registered for the
summer quarter may take the
course for one unit of credit or
for activity credit.
