# ACLU Notes 'Prayer Rule'
## 
### 
Ernest Besig, Executive Direc
tor of the Northern Californian
Branch of the American Civil
Liberties Union, will comment on
the recent U.S. Supreme Court
New York school prayer decision
and the California narcotic law
decision at a reception Sunday.
The reception, open to the pub
lic and sponsored by the Mid-
Peninsula Chapter of the A.C.L.U.
will be held from 3 to 6 p.m. at
27060 Old Trace Road, Los Altos
Hills. Direction signs will be
posted from Arastradero and Fre
mont Roads.
