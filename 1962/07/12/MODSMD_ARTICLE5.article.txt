# Stegner Honored
## 
### 
Professor Wallace Stegner ot
the Stanford English department
has been awarded a silver medal
for his novel “A Shooting Star”
in the Commonwealth Club of
California’s annual competition.
The competition, restricted to
California authors, had 187 en
tries in 1961.
Gold medal winners for 1961
were Cal English Department
chairman Mark Schorer for “Sin
clair Lewis: An American Life,”
and Irving Stone for “The Agony
and the Ecstasy.”
Other winners were Theodora
Kroeber for "Ishi in Two
Worlds” and Leon Urls for
“Milia 18.”
