# Art and Music, Youth Concert Scheduled
## 
### 
The Department of Art and Ar
chitecture announces a lecture by
Walter Landor on the “Develop
ment of Designs for Marketing.”
The lecture, presented as part of a
series on the various phases of
the designing field, will be next
Wednesday, in Cubberley Audi
torium at 8 p.m.
The Department of Music an
nounces a similar continuation of
its summer series, “The Sound
and Sense of Contemporary Mu
sic,” with a lecture by Andrew
Imbrie, composer and member of
the University of California,
speaking on “The Composer and
the Past” at 4:15 p.m., Monday
in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Both lectures are free of charge.
The Department of Music will
present the Summer Youth Or
chestra under the direction of
Wolfgang Kuhn, on Sunday, July
15, at 3:30 p.m., in Dinkelspiel
Auditorium. There will be no
charge.
