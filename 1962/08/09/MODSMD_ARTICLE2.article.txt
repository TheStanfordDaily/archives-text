# Director Appointed
## 
### 
Elmer M. Grieder, associate di
rector of libraries at Stanford, has
been appointed acting director of
libraries effective August 1.
He assumes the position vacat
ed by Raynard C. Swank, who
has resigned to accept a dean
ship at the University of Cali
fornia.
It is Grieder’s second stint as
head of the Stanford library sys
tem. He served as acting direc
tor from 1959 to 1961, when
Swank was on a world assign
ment for the American Library
Association.
Grieder, educated at the Uni
versity of Dubique and Harvard
as an historian, holds a degree in
library science from Columbia
University.
