ON BEING NAUGHTY
Here’s a term peculiar to Stan
ford that freshmen ought to
know: “Con Home hours.” That’s
time you may get to spend lend
ing a helping hand at the Stan
ford Convalescent home for
youngsters. This is the traditional
punishment at the Farm for stu
dents who are violating the rules.
