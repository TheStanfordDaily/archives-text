# 289 Transfers Get Oriented
## 
### 
Orientation for 289 transfer
students starts tomorrow at 9
a.m. with registration in front of
Dinkelspiel Auditorium. President
Sterling will address the trans
fers in the auditorium at 10
o’clock.
They’ll be given tours of the
campus at 11.
James T. Watkins IV. popular
professor of political science, will
address the transfers during a
banquet set for 6 p.m. at Tresid
der Union.
Tomorrow evening the transfer
students will be the only non
freshmen (besides pre-reg com
members) at the traditional Jolly
up at Wilbur courtyard. The
dance starts at 8 p.m.
Dean of Students H. Donald
Winbigler will address the trans
fers in Dinkelspiel at 1:15 p.m.
Sunday on registration procedure.
Attendance at this assembly is
imperative, according to pre-reg
chairman Randy Devening. Stir
ling Huntley, associate director of
admissions, and Lucile Allen,
Dean of Women, will also speak
at the assembly.
All woman transfer students
are required to meet at 4:30 p.m.
Sunday at Tresidder Union for an
explanation of Stanford social
regulations.
