A-PHI-0 BOOK EXCHANGE
OPENS TODAY IN OLD UNION
The A-Phi-O book exc hange opens this morning at the old Union
lobby across from (lie Stanford Bookstore. The exchange will he open
from 8 a.m. to noon and 1 to 4 p.m. through Thursday.
A-Fhi-O national service fraternity invites students to bring in
their used books to he sold at prices asked by the owners. The ex
change is a great opportunity for students buying books to get bar
gain prices, according to A-Fhi-O Allen Gammoml. Members of the
service fraternity sell the books and take a 10-cent commission on
each book handled.
The exchange is held traditionally at the beginning of each
quarter.
