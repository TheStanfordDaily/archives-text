PROS H!
Don’t do another thing until you stop at PREUSS
PHARMACY at STANFORD SHOPPING CENTER
to open your STUDENT CHARGE ACCOUNT.
ADVANTAGES?
* Accurate record of expenses while at college.
* Speedy identification for check cashing.
* Deliveries can be made at living quarters dur
ing class hours.
* Monthly bill sent either to you or to your home.
* Your Student Body Card is your only reference
needed.
Preuss Pharmacy serves Stanford with a complete
selection of drugs, prescriptions, cosmetics, per
fumes, magazines, smoking needs, greeting cards
and camera supplies.
