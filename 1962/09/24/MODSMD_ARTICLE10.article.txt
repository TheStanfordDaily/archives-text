# SU Given Grant For Laboratory
## 
### 
Stanford’s Medical Center has
received a $535,000 grant from the
National Aeronautics and Space
Administration for a biomedical
instrumentation laboratory here.
NASA’s new grant will permit
further research in the fields of
space biology and biomedical in
strumentation begun under a
three-year grant of $380,000. Di
rector of the entire program is
Dr. Joshua Lederberg, head of
Stanford’s Department of Gene
tics and a Nobel prize-winner.
Advance plans for the new
grant include devices to determine
life on other planets. The labora
tory will be located in the $5.5
million Clinical Sciences Building
scheduled to open in 1964.
