# Police Warn Car Owners
## 
### 
Car owners have until Wednes
day to put university parking
stickers on their automobiles, ac
cording to Gordon Davis, chief of
the Stanford police.
Although parking stickers will
be on sale at the Pavilion during
registration, Chief Davis urges all
drivers to get parking stickers
before the rush to purchase them
at the Police Department. The
cost is $lO per year.
Stanford police will begin to
issue citations Wednesday to all
cars without stickers, and to those
parked in improper zones.
