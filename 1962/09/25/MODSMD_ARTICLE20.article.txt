# Exam for Med School Set Again
## 
### 
The Medical College Admision
Test, sponsored by the Psychologi
cal Corporation, will be adminis
tered on Oct. 20, 1962, for the
benefit of the 1963 Medical School
applicants who failed to take the
test when it was given in May.
Application blanks and copies
of the bulletin of information may
be obtained in the Medical Admis
sions Office, Room 112 D, Inner
Quad.
Applications for the test must
be received by the Psychological
Corporation on or before October
5, which is the final date for re
ceiving applications.
Only applicants for admission
to classes beginning the study of
medicine in the fall of 1963, who
have not taken the test previously,
should take it at this time.
Application blanks for the Stan
ford School of Medicine, for the
class which enters the fall of
1963, are available at the Medical
Admissions Office. Students are
urged to submit applications as
soon as possible.
