YANKS WIN
NEW YORK (UPl»—The Now
York Yankees clinched their 27th
American League pennant last
night in an 8-3 victory over the
Washington Senators.
It was the Yanks’ 12th pennant
in the last 14 years and two in
two years for Manager Halph
Houk.
