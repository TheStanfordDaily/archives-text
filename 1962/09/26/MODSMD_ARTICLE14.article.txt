# German Surveys Common Market
## 
### 
“Germany and the Common
Market” will be Dr. Kurt Birren
bach’s topic today when he takes
the rostrum at 4:15 p.m. in Cub
berley Auditorium.
Dr. Birrenbach is presently a
member of the House of Repre
sentatives of the Federal Repub
lic of Germany. His knowledge of
Common Market affairs also
stems from his position as a
member of the Parliament of the
European Economic Community
in Strasbourg.
Dr. Birrenbach received his
Ph.D. in Law and Economics
from the University of Tuebin
gen in 1933. He has also studied
at the Universities of Geneva,
Paris, and Munich.
