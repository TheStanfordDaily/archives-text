# Junior Class Reunion Slated for 3—10 p.m.
## 
### 
The Class of ’6l is sponsoring
an all-campus party and junior
class reunion today between 3
and 10 p.m. at Adobe Creek
Lodge. Admission is free for
class card holders and $1 for
others.
Hamburgers will be available,
there will be swimming, and the 1
Zodiaks will provide dance music.
