# SU Gallery Now Rents Original Art
## 
### 
Want an original etching hang
ing from your otherwise drab
dorm wall? Or what about a
wood-cut just inside the door of
your fraternity house?
The Stanford Art Gallery has
announced that on Oct. 2 and 3
it will be possible to rent original
etchings, engravings, lithographs
and wood-cuts from the Gallery’s
collection.
The prints are available in
•color and in black and white, and
are from the works of artists
from twenty-eight different coun
tries. They may be rented by
Stanford students, faculty, and
staff on a first-come first-served
basis.
The rental price, per print, is
$1.25 per quarter or $3 for three
quarters.
