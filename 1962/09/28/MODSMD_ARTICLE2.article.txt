# Seniors to Race In Togas to Zott's
## 
### 
The Senior Class Reunion, set
for 2:30 today at Zotts. will be
preceded by the 47th annual re
lay race between the rival Class
of ’63 and ASSU teams.
The toga-clad teams captained
by Beau Bianchi and Roger Sal
quist will dash from the old Post
Office to Zotts, and the winning
team will celebrate with free
beer.
A truck will follow the runners
to pick up all of those who don’t
have transportation to the fes
tivities.
Entertainment will be furnish
ed by the Zodiacs.
All students are reminded to
bring proof that they are 21.
