# First I-Club Party Planned
## 
### 
The first I-Club party of the
quarter will be held today from
8:30 to 12 p.m. at Armin’s Pad,
323 Santa Maria Ave., Portola
Valley.
Sign-up lists are posted at the
I-Center in the old Union build
ing for those who need transpor
tation or are able to furnish rides
for others. Maps will also be post
ed giving directions on how to
reach the dance.
Music will be furnished by Dick
Kress and his band.
The first regular coffee hour
will be held today in the Union
Courtyard, from 3:30 to 5:30.
Orestes Villafurte of Peru will
show colored slides on places of
historical interest of his country,
along with authentic Peruvian
music, at the I-Club’s first “Eve
ning Abroad,” Saturday, 8:30 p.m.
at the I-Center.
