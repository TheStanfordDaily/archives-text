# New List of Students Set for Stanford Campuses in Europe
## 
### 
Dr. Robert Walker, director of the Overseas Campuses, has an
nounced the names of the students who have been selected to attend
the next sessions of Stanford’s three study centers in Europe.
Students attending Stanford-in-Germany’s tenth session will
begin their six months of studies in Europe next January. Those
going to the study centers in France and Italy will leave in March.
The alternates will attend the immediately following sessions if
they are not needed to fill openings in the groups.
