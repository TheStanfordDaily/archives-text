# Dr. R. J. Wert Honored By Education Council
## 
### 
Robert J. Wert, vice provost of
Stanford University, was recently
elected as vice chairman of the
American Council of Education, it
was disclosed in Chicago this
weekend. The ACE is the nation’s
top non governmental agency for
colleges and universities, com
prising 100 educational associa
tions and 1,000 individual institu
tions.
Wert is president of California’s
Coordinating Council for Higher
Education. He also serves as a
Mills College trustee and as a
consultant on higher education to
the Ford Foundation.
