# Medical School Plans Reception for Students
## 
### 
To acquaint third and fourth
year pre-meds with Stanford’s
medical school, the med students
will sponsor a reception, Wednes
day evening, October 17, 7:30 p.m.
at the Medical School.
Dr. William Creger will speak
on “The Physician as Sherlock
Holmes.” An informal discussion
between the meds and pre-meds
will follow the lecture.
Refreshments will be served.
Dress for the reception will be
slacks and sport shirts.
