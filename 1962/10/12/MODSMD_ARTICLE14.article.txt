# Macbeth Director Out; Title Role to Gilborn
## 
### 
Theodore Hoffman, visiting pro
fessor in the Speech and Drama
Department, has been named to
replace Robert Loper as director
of the play “Macbeth.”
Dr. Loper, who was to have
played the title role in the pro
duction in addition to directing
the play, has contracted a virus
and will be unable to direct or
star.
The title role of Macbeth will
be played by Stephen Gilborn,
whom Loper had selected to play
Mac Duff.
