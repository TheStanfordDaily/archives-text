Fifty Years Ago . . .
In October 191 2, ‘Abdu'l-Baha spoke at Stan
ford at the invitation of President Jordan. As the
leader of the Baha'i World Faith, this Persian
religious teacher was touring the Western World
to spread the message of Universal Brotherhood,
International Peace and Religious Unity of his
new Faith. At Stanford he gave one of his defi
nitive addresses on the relationship between
science and religion.
Tomorrow night, a public meeting is being
held in Palo Alto commemorating ‘Abdu’l-
Baha’s visit to this area. The Baha’i Club of Stan
ford University will provide transportation for
those who need it.
For Information and Rides Call Arthur Dahl at
DA 3-3121, X 550, from 6:30 to 7:30 p.m.
OCTOBER 19 at 8:00
PALO ALTO ART CLUB
855 Cowper Street
