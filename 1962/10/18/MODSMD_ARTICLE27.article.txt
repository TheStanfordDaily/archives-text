# Dornbusch Defines SU As 'Men's University
## 
### 
Professor Sanford M. Dorn
busch in a speech for the annual
Parents Day program last week
end described Stanford as “a
men’s university attended by
some women.” He added that “if
it is a man’s world this is a good
place for girls to learn how to run
it.”
Dornbusch stated further that
“college represents a time of ex
perimentation and transition for
the student” and that the main
contribution of a college is “to
create an environment with di
verse opportunities for individual
growth.”
