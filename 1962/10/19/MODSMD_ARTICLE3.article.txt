# Pike Will Present Talk on 'Regret'
## 
### 
The Right Reverend James A.
Pike, Episcopal Bishop of Cali
fornia. will preach on “Regret”
at the 11 a.m. service in Memorial
Church Sunday.
Pike, who holds a law degree
from Yale, graduated from U.S.C.
and received his theological
training at the Union Theological
Seminary in New York.
There will also be a service of
Holy Communion at 9 a.m. Pro
fessor Herbert B. Nannoy will
present a recital of German Or
gan Music at 3:30 p.m.
