# Crosland to Discuss British, U.S. Economy
## 
### 
British Parliament member C.
Anthony R. Crosland will speak
on “Britain and the European
Economic Community” on Nov. 8.
The lecturer, sponsored by the
Ford Foundation, will also discuss
the process of social change in
the United States and its effects
on political policy, as well as
American development in educa
tion, trade unions, and private
enterprise.
Crosland. chairman of the Fa
bian Society, is the author of
"Britain’s Economic Problems”
and “The Future of Socialism.”
The location and time of the
speech will be announced later.
