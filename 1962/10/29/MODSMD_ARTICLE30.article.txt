# YWCA Panel to Meet
## 
### 
“Time, Talents, and the
Clothesline”: tonight the YWCA
presents a panel of four women
who will discuss the various roles
offered to women after finishing
college. The speakers are: Mrs.
William Kays, Mrs. Dandridge
Gray, Miss Georgia Williams, and
Mrs. Allan Griggs.
The panel will be held at 7:45
in the “Y” Lounge, third floor,
Women’s Clubhouse. All women
students are invited to participate
in the discussion.
