# Cardinals Complete Homecoming Plans
## 
### 
The activities for Homecoming
Weekend next week have been
planned by the Cardinals and
will begin on Friday night, with
decorating by individual houses
and end with a rally in Frost Am
phitheater and a dance at Tresid
der.
On Saturday the house decora
tions will be displayed and
judged. Immediately following,
the official university dedication
ceremonies for the new fraternity
houses will take place. The ‘Stan
ford of the Future’ model in En
cina Hall and other campus cen
ters of interest will be open for
visitors in the morning.
