# USC's Finn to Discuss Technology in Education
## 
### 
Dr. James Finn, professor of
education at the University of
Southern California, will present
an illustrated lecture entitled
“Technological Impact in Educa
tion” today at 4:15 p.m. in Cub
berley Auditorium.
The talk, open without charge
to the entire campus, will deal
with, the revolution in our schools
which is caused by newer tech
nology and the dramatic advance
in the communication media.
