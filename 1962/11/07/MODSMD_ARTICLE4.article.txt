# Dean Stowe to Discuss Stanford Medical School
## 
### 
Dr. L. M. Stowe , Associate
Dean and Chairman of the Com
mittee on Admissions of the Stan
ford Medical School, will speak in
Room MlO6 in the Medical School
Building at 7 p.m. tomorrow.
Stowe will discuss preparations
for medical education, Stanford’s
requirements, admission to the
medical school, and the Stanford
plan of medical education. He will
conclude with a consideration of
students’ selection of a medical
school.

The meeting is open to inter
ested students; after the short
formal presentation will be a
question-and-answer period.
