Two Happy Hours
Tin* Graduate Students'
Club will host the third in its
series of bi-weekly “Happy
Honrs” today from 5 to 8
p.m. at the Pioneer Inn in
Woodside. All grad students
are cordially invited to at
tend.
The Senior Class will also
host a “Happy Hour” Unlay
from 3 to 6 p.m. at “The
20’s.” The major attraction
will be tryouts for the Senior
Drinking Team which will
compete with Cal before Big
Game.
