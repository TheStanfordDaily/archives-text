# Stegner to Discuss Man, Environment
## 
### 
“Man, a Disease of His En
vironment” will be the topic of
Professor Wallace Stegner’s
speech this afternoon at 4:30 p.m.
in Tresidder Union. Ho will dis
cuss man and his environment,
with particular reference to the
result of uncontrolled progress.
He feels that progress is the
greatest danger to the “good life”
and is poisoning our environment.
Stegner’s activities reflect his
interest in the conservation of
natural environment. In addition
to being Director of the Creative
Writing Program at Stanford,
Stegner has served as an assist
ant to the Secretary of the In
terior. He is also a member of
the National Park Advisory
Board.
