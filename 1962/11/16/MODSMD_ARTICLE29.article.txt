# Tribe Shooters Win
## 
### 
The Stanford rifle team beat
the San Jose Spartans, 1411-1394.
yesterday in tho season’s opening
match. Mike Dunia paced the
Stanford scoring with a total of

288. Bill Kindred led the San Jose
shooters with 286.
Stanford finished second in the
conference last year with an 8-2
record.
