# Five Avant Garde Films Scheduled at Tresidder
## 
### 
Tresidder Union has scheduled
a second program of avant garde
films to be shown today at 4, 7.
and 9:30 in room 273.
This program concerns itself
mainly with older films which
were produced by film makers
seeking some new form or meth
od of expression. The five films
in the varied program are: “Blood
of the Beasts,” “Looney Tom,”
“Bridges Go Round,” “Fiddle-dee
dee,” and “Geography of the
Body.”
Admission is 25 cents. Adults
attending are advised that chil
dren will not be admitted.
