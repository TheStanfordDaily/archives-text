# Frosh Turnout Heavy As 1200 Students Vote
## 
### 
Fall Elections Chairman Alan
Holroyde reported yesterday that
about 1200 students had voted in
the first day of the election. The
Freshman vote was especially
heavy, amounting to half the
turnout.
POLLS WILL BE open today
from 8 a.m. to 3 p.m. at the Li
brary, the Post Office, and En
gineering corner, and from 9
a.m. to 1 p.m. in front of the
Physics tank.
The Constitution, requiring a
2/3 majority, Freshman class of
ficers, and LASSU representa
tives from Wilbur, Roble, Bran
ner, and Stern are being voted
upon.
