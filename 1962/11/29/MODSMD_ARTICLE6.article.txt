# Sigma Nu Ex-Officers Support 'Going Local'
## 
### 
Five former presidents of Stan
ford’s Sigma Nu chapter yester
day hacked the decision of its
undergraduates to withdraw from
the national organization and be
come a local fraternity. Beta Chi.
In a letter mailed to the chap
ter’s 630 alumni, the ex-presi
dents said: ‘‘There is no question
about the sincerity of the present
Beta Chi actives . . . Looking to
the future of America, they arc
right.’’
The chapter’s 67 members
voted unanimously a week ago
Tuesday to leave the national be
cause of its restrictive clauses
against Negro and Oriental mem
bers.
