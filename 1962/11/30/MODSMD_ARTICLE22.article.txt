## 
### 
Five teams have emerged as
league champions after the first
half of the intramural bowling
season. Kappa Alpha, AKL 11,
Burbank, Theta Chi, and Junipero
lead in the five leagues.
High individual average be
longs to Jeff Armstrong of Sig
ma Chi. whose 184 average hasn't
helped his team much, Sigma Chi
having lost 18 of 21 games.
