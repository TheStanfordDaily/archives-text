# Bank Official to Deliver Report on Advertising
## 
### 
“Bank of America’s break
through in financial advertising”
is the topic to be presented by
Charles R. Stuart, Advertising
Manager for the Bank of Ameri
ca, Friday, Jan. 18, in Room 121.
Stuart is the President of
the San Francisco Advertising
Club and is the originator of a
new approach in financial adver
tising using stuffed teddy boars,
dolls and cookies for his bank’s
advertising program. His talk
will include both a history of con
ventional advertising and a re
port on the effectiveness of his
unconventional advertising pro
gram in the banking market.
