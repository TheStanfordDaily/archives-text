oAre You Interested In ...
AN INDUSTRIAL SALES POSITION
LEADING TO TOP MANAGEMENT?
A representative from Ducommun Metals & Supply Co. in Los Angeles
will be on campus January 28,1963 to interview Business Administra
tion majors for positions in sales and merchandising at its Los Ange
les Division.
Ducommun is the West’s largest independent industrial distributor,
merchandising steel, non-ferrous metals, industrial supplies and tools
and abrasives to industry. Ducommun has 113 years of industrial dis
tribution experience behind its eight-state operation.
For further information, contact the Placement Office, Encina Hall.
