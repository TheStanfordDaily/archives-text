# Two Professors Win Research Fellowships
## 
### 
Two Stanford professors have
won postdoctoral research fellow
ships in the humanities and re
lated sciences awarded by the
American Council of Learned So
cieties.

Professor Herbert L. Packer of
the Law School will prepare a
book dealing with the limits of
criminal law as a means of social
control, and Wesley W. Trimpi,
Jr. of the English Department
will prepare a monograph on cer
tain stylistic and structural prin
ciples of Renaissance literary
works and literary theory.
The Council, a private non
profit federation of 31 national
associations, awarded a total of
54 fellowships for the coming aca
demic year.
