M.S. and Ph.D. graduates will find room to expand at STL’s new ultra-modern TeeTinolo'rv Center,
Redondo Beach, California, near Los Angeles International Airport.
Here, in an environment conducive to expanding your special interests, you will find thought provoking prob
lems requiring the optimum of your talents. And, while associating with recognized authorities in your field,
you will have the opportunity to sec developed products of your endeavors in such vital space programs as
OGO (NASA’s Orbiting Geophysical Observatory), and in Titan and Minutcman ballistic missile programs.
If > 'ou arc searching for space to expand your ideas, theories and thoughts, in a campus-like atmosphere, we
invite you to investigate the technical specialties listed below: There is creative space for you at STL. The
oretical Physics • Systems Engineering • Radar Systems • Experimental Physics • Applied Mathematics • Space
Communications • Antennas and Microwaves • Inertial Guidance • Analog Computers • Solid State Physics •
Computer Design • Telecommunications • Space Physics • Digital Computers • Guidance & Navigation • Elcc
tro-mechanical Devices • Engineering Mechanics • Applied Aerodynamics • Propulsion Systems.
Please make arrangements with your Placement Office for interview appointment. Or you may write to: College
Relations, Space Technology Laboratories, Inc., One Space Park, Redondo Beach, California. STL is an
equal opportunity employer.

M.S. and Ph.D. graduates will find room to expand at STL’s new ultra-modern TeeTinolo'rv Center,
Redondo Beach, California, near Los Angeles International Airport.
Here, in an environment conducive to expanding your special interests, you will find thought provoking prob
lems requiring the optimum of your talents. And, while associating with recognized authorities in your field,
you will have the opportunity to sec developed products of your endeavors in such vital space programs as
OGO (NASA’s Orbiting Geophysical Observatory), and in Titan and Minutcman ballistic missile programs.
If > 'ou arc searching for space to expand your ideas, theories and thoughts, in a campus-like atmosphere, we
invite you to investigate the technical specialties listed below: There is creative space for you at STL. The
oretical Physics • Systems Engineering • Radar Systems • Experimental Physics • Applied Mathematics • Space
Communications • Antennas and Microwaves • Inertial Guidance • Analog Computers • Solid State Physics •
Computer Design • Telecommunications • Space Physics • Digital Computers • Guidance & Navigation • Elcc
tro-mechanical Devices • Engineering Mechanics • Applied Aerodynamics • Propulsion Systems.
Please make arrangements with your Placement Office for interview appointment. Or you may write to: College
Relations, Space Technology Laboratories, Inc., One Space Park, Redondo Beach, California. STL is an
equal opportunity employer.
