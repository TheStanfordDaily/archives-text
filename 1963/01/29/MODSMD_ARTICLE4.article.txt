DR. CHARLES BEYE, arch-classicist, will play Demipho in the forth
coming Ram's Head production of Terrence's "Phormio." The play
is a Roman comedy translated by Stanford graduate student in
the classics Dan Arnaud and directed by Steve Gilborn. It will be
presented in the Ram's Head Winter One-Acts together with two
other student written and directed plays.
