New Rules
There are a number of new rules regarding the pub
lication of material for volume 143. These so Into effect
immediately.
All publicity for any events—speeches, meetings,
afternoon leas, etc. —must be submitted to the Managing
Editor by 3 pm. two days before the event. The notice
of all such events will appear in an enlarged Once
Around the Quad. Some of these notices will also appear
on page one, entirely at the discretion of The Daily.
All letters must be submitted to The Daily by 2 p.m.
the day before desired publication. They must he type
written.
No exceptions will be made to the above rules.
