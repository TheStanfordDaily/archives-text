# Buss to Deliver Speech At Opportunities' Night
## 
### 
Claude A. Buss, Stanford pro
fessor of history, will speak to
morrow night at 7:30 p.m. in the
Tresidder Lounge in the UR
“Opportunities Evening.”
A panel of representatives from
International Voluntary Service,
DATA International, Kibbute pro
grams. Experiment in Interna
tional Living, and other programs
will be available for discussion
and questions.
Opportunities are of two gen
eral types: workcamps and teach
ing positions. Workcamps extend
from a single weekend to three
years in length. A bachelor’s de
gree is the main qualification for
a teaching job in an underdevel
oped country.
Specific information by area is
available in the HR loft as fol
lows: Africa, Thursdays, 4:30-5:30
p.m.; Asia, Tuesdays, 2:30-3:30
p.m,; Latin America, Wednes
days, 3:30-4:30 p.m.; and Europe,
Wednesdays, 4:15-5:15 p.m.
