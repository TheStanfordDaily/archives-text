Owned and published daily except Saturday and Sunday by the Associated Students
ot Stanford University. Represented for national advertising by Nation Advertising
Service, Inc., San Francisco, 18 East 50th St., New York. Entered as second-class
matter at the post office at Palo Alto, Calif., under the Act of March 3, 1879.

The Stanford Daily, while owned by and operated under the auspices of the
Associated Students of Stanford University, is editorially independent from both
the Associated Students and the University. Editorial opinions do not necessarily
express the views of either the Associated Students or the University.
