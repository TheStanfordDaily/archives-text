# Riflemen Beaten by Cal; SU Girls Blast SFS Men
## 
### 
Stanford’s rifle team finished
a distant second to Cal in an
1 eight-school tournament in Ber
keley on Saturday. Cal shot 1433
to Stanford’s 1404. Davis was
third with 1402.
Bruce Lueck and Mike Dunia
led the Tribe shooters, with 286
and 284, respectively.
Led by Carlisle Haley (276),
the Stanford girls team shot
down the San Francisco State
KOTC squad, 1223-1174.
