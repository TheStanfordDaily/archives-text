# Baez to Give Concert Sponsored by Seniors
## 
### 
Joan Baez will present an eve
ning of folk music March 4 at
8:30 p.m. in Memorial Auditor
ium. Miss Baez, who is formerly
of Palo Alto, is sponsored by the
senior class and the Stanford Folk
Music Club.
Tickets for the concert go on
sale this morning at the Tresid
der Union Box Office. Seats will
he reserved and prices will range
from $2.30 to $4.50.
