SCHOOL FOR SOVIET STUDIES
A school specializing in Soviet
area studies will be established in
Munich, Germany, this summer,
under the joint administration of
the University of Oklahoma and
the Institute for the Study of the
USSR. The school, which will be
in session from Juno 17 to July
26, 1963, at the Institute building
in Munich, will provide qualified
students from the United States
and other countries an opportun
ity for Russian-language and area
studies. Additional information
and application forms may be ob
tained from: Professor John G.
Eriksen, Chairman, Russian Stud
ies Committee, University of Ok
lahoma, Norman, Oklahoma.
