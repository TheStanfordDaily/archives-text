# S.U.N. to Continue Today With Red China Debate
## 
### 
Today’s session of Stanford
United Nations opens at 4:15
p.m. in Memorial Auditorium
with a debate and motions open
on the China issue. Following
this will be closure of debate and
voting. The afternoon session
will end at 6 p.m.
Ambassador Frederick Boland
of Ireland will speak on world
government at 7:30 p.m. in Me
morial Auditorium. After Bo
land’s speech debate will begin
and continue until the end of the
evening session.
Delegates to SUN must attend
the sessions, and activity credit
for all delegates is being held
back until the end of the sessions.
All speeches and debates at
SUN are open to the public.
