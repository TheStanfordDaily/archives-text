# FOOTBALL PRACTICE
## 
### 
Spring football practice will
open on April 20, and will be held
Monday, Wednesday, Friday, and
Saturday with the following
schedule: 3 p.m., individual work
out; 3:20, chalk talk; 3:43, team
practice; 3:45, showers. The time
of Saturday practices will vary.
Coach Ralston has emphasized
that practice will be open to all
interested students.
