# SU Chorus, Symphony To Offer Concert Tonight
## 
### 
Mozart’s “Requiem” and Bee
thoven’s “Seventh Symphony”
will be the program for a joint
concert of the Stanford Chorus
and Symphony Orchestra tonight
at 8:30 in Dinkelspiel Auditori
um.
Four soloists will join the
Chorus and Orchestra for the
"Requiem.” The soloists are Carol
Burgard, soprano; Carol Burche,
alto; Tommy Goleeke, tenor; and
Horace Guittard. baritone.
Tickets are available at the
Tresidder Box Office, students
$l, others. $1.50.
