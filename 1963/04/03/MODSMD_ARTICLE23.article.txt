# Martin Tops Dose For Scoring Crown
## 
### 
Stanford’s All-American center
Tom Dose lost the scoring title of
the AAWU to USC’s Gordon Mar
tin by a scant five points over
the 12-game schedule. Dose’s 21.6
average fell just short of Mar
tin’s 21.7 pace.
Dose led the Big Six in re
bounding, however, topping Ed
Corell of Washington by a wide
margin. California’s Dick Smith
led the conference in free throw
percentage. Just to show how ri
diculous these statistics can some
times be, Charlie Hart and Clint
Peeples (who?) of Washington
were the best in the Big Six in
field goal percentage.
