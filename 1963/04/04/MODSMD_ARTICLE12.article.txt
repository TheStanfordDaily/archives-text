# Public Enemy' is First Of Films in TMU Series
## 
### 
"Public Enemy,” starring
James Cagney, will be the first
of a series of films to be shown
at Tresidder on each Friday of
Spring Quarter. It will be shown
at 4:15, 7:30, and 9:30 p.m. to
morrow. Tickets are 40 cents and
will bo on sale at the Tresidder
Box Office starting today. They
will be sold on a separate basis
for each film in the series.
A documentary short, “Bow
Bells,” will be shown with the
movie. Created by the photog
rapher of “A Taste of Honey,” it
shows the Limehouse section of
London.
