# Tresidder Dance To Be Held Friday
## 
### 
A free and informal TGIF
dance will be held on the back
porch of Tresidder Friday after
noon from 4 to 6 p.m.
The dance is presented by the
Program Division of Tresidder
and is an extension of the TGIF
jazz concerts. Palmer Sesscl and
his “Pastels” will play a wide
variety of music, including rock
’n roll, bossa nova, jazz, and slow
music.
