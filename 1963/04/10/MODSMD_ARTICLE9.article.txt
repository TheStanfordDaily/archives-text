# Noon Musicale to Show Slides of Japanese Play
## 
### 
Visiting Professor Shigeo Ki
shibe of the University of Tokyo
will present slides and recordings
of a Japanese Noh play at the
Noon Musicale in Dinkelspiel
Auditorium at 12:15 p.m. today.
The Noh play, an ancient Japa
nese tradition, is staged with
elaborate costumes and stylized
actions accompanied by flutes
and drums.
