INDIANS
This week is a fateful
one for most of us,
the time when the
bagmen call for the
tax comeuppance,
a time when things
financial come to
mind. One of the
friendlier money
arrangements has to
be R/A’s own
Super/Charge plan,
the system that lets
you buy what you
need, when you need
it and take many
months to pay on
modest installments.
This, truly, is the only
way to buy. Your reg
card is all it takes to
qualify.
