NELDA HALKETT as The Princess Kosmonopolis and Phillip Angeloff
as Chance Wayne are stars in the Stanford Players production
"Sweet Bird of Youth." The play was written by Tennessee Williams.
It will be presented in Memorial Auditorium at 8:30 p.m. tomorrow
and Saturday, and on May 2, 3, and 4. Tickets are on sale at the
Tresidder box office; there is a 50-cent reduction on all student
tickets.
