# Fencing Championship
## 
### 
The Women’s Collegiate Fenc
ing Association Meet will be held
tomorrow at Stanford. The ten
top teams from the West Coast
will compete beginning at 9 a.m.
in the Women’s Gym. The field
includes Stanford, Cal, UCLA,
Pomona, and Arizona.
Round-robin competition will
be held in three divisions, A, B,
and C, and the team champion
ship will be decided by the num
ber of points earned in all di
visions.
