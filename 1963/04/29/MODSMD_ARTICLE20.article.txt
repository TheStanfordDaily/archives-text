# Comedy Tryouts Today
## 
### 
Open tryouts will be held in
Room 129 of Memorial Hall, to
day and Wednesday at 7:30 p.m.
for a “Studio ’63” presentation of
two one-act comedies by A. A.
Milne, “The Artist” and “The
Ugly Duckling." The two comic
spoofs of the game of love and
courtship will be presented in the
Studio Theatre May 21-23, and
will be directed by Herman
Poppe. a graduate student in the
Department of Speech and
Drama.
