# Reserves Win Mock Meet
## 
### 
While Stanford was competing
in the Drake Relays Friday, the
remainder of the varsity squad

and the frosh held an informal
meet at Angel Field. Although
no point totals were kept, it ap
peared that the varsity reserves
won, the frosh finished second,
and a two-man Los Angeles State
squad was last.
The top event of the meet was
the mile, won by the varsity’s
Kirkland in 4:13,1. The frosh’s

Dave Deubner took second with a
4:13.5, and the varsity’s John
Beck was third at 4:13.6.
