# Race Relations Hostel Scheduled for May 10,11
## 
### 
A hostel on the subject of race
relations will be held on May 10
and 11.
Frank Quinn, chairman of the
San Francisco Committee on
Civic Unity, will speak on hous
ing discrimination in the Bay
Area. In addition, Professor
S/.ego of the University of Santa
Clara will discuss the background
of the racial problem in the area.
Szego, founder of the former
Stanford N.A.A.C.P., is presently
chairman of the Palo Alto
N.A.A.C.P. John Howard, gradu
ate in sociology, will lead the hos
tel.
The hostel runs from 5:30 p.m.
on Friday to 11:30 a.m. on Satur
day, Applications are available to
all interested students at the
main desk of Tresidder and at the
YWCA on the third floor of the
Women’s Clubhouse.
