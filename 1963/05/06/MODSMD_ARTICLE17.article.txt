# RECEIVES AWARD
## 
### 
Thomas W. Ford, director of
land development for Stanford
University, has received a golden
mike award from radio station
KXRX, San Jose, for his work as
head of the Santa Clara County
United Fund. The award is made
“in recognition of outstonding
community and civic service."
