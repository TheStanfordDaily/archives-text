# AWS Recording Secretary
## 
### 
The office of AWS Recording
Secretary involves a great deal of
time and responsibility - time for
the technical and administrative
work of keeping accurate minutes
and responsibility of sitting as an
active member of the AWS Cabi
net and Board.
By becoming a candidate for
this office I have declared an ac
tive rather than passive interest
in student government; and I am
both willing to assume and I be
lieve able to fulfill the responsi
bilities involved in this office.
Mary Ellen Nemeth
