# Girl Netters Win
## 
### 
Stanford women swept past Cal
last weekend in the Northern Cali
fornia Women’s Invitational Ten
nis Tournament at Stanford. Lin
da Zinn, Mary Campbell, and Julie
Heldman led Stanford to first
place among the 12 schools
present.
Miss Zinn and Miss Campbell
downed Cal’s Mim Easton and
Winnie Camp in the doubles final,
6-3, 6-3, while Miss Heldman was
beating Cal’s Cathy Casel for
the singles crown, 6-4, 6-3.
