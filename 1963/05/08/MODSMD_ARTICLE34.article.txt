# Weill, Cummings Honored
## 
###

been awarded the AAWU Out
standing Senior Award, given to
the graduating athlete exhibiting
the greatest achievement in
scholarship, athletics, and leader
ship.
Football linebacker Ed Cum
mings has received the J. Gim
bel Athletic Attitude Prize,
awarded to the senior student
showing the best mental attitude
toward athletics.
