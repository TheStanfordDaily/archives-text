# Women to Draw Today For Next Year's Dorms
## 
### 
The AWS Housing Drawing for
all freshman women and upper
class women wishing to change
residence will he held today from
9 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. All freshman
women, except those definitely
going to a foreign campus, must
fill out a drawing card today.
Alternates must draw.
Upperclass women dissatisfied
with their number will be able to
drop out tomorrow or Friday.
The lists will be posted Friday
in the Housing Office, room 101A.
Inner Quad.
All upperclass women present
ly living at Mariposa or Huston
Houses must enter the drawing
unless they have already regis
tered for placement with Mrs. El
wood in the Housing Office.
