# Hauser, Martin to Run For Stern President
## 
### 
James Hauser and Skip Martin
will vie next week for president
of the Inter House Council, a
primary election determined yes
terday.
Glenn Sampson, IHC president,
said that the exact date for the
election will be set at Sunday’s
IHC meeting, but that it will
probably be next Wednesday or
Thursday.
Martin gained 129 votes, and
Hauser 96, while Odin Knudson
received 70, and Ken Johnson 69.
The president serves as the of
ficial representative of the 600
independent members in Stern’s
eight houses.
