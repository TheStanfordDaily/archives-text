# Imamura to Play
## 
### 
Hiro Imamura. pianist and jun
ior at the University of Califor
nia. will perform Beethoven’s
Concerto for Piano and Orchestra
No. 3 in C Minor, opus 37, in con
certs with the San Francisco
Symphony Orchestra at 8:30 p.m.
tonight and tomorrow and at 2:15
p.m. Friday.
The orchestra, under the direc
tion of Enrique Jorda, also will
perform Vaughn Williams’ Fan
tasia on a Theme by Thomas Tal
lis, “Erosao” by Villa-Lobos,
Haydn’s Symphony No. 45 in F
sharp Minor (Farewelli and the
West Coast premiere of Guido
Turchi’s “Piccolo Concerto Not
turnc. ”
