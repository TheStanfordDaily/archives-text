# Ziadeh to Speak On Syria, Egypt
## 
### 
Professor Nicola A. Ziadeh
from the American University,
Beirut, Lebanon, will speak to
night on “Historical Bonds be
tween Syria and Egypt.” His talk
will be given at 8 p.m. in Room
282, Tresidder Union.
Ziadeh received his Ph.D. from
the University of London and was
a visiting lecturer at Harvard.
He has served on the faculty of
Oriental Languages at Cambridge
University, and has held govern
mental posts in Palestine and
Beirut.
Professor Ziadeh is a guest of
the Department of History and
the Hoover Instittuion.
