“IPHIGENIA IN TAURIS," an opera by C. W. Gluck, wil be presented
by Stanford Opera Theatre at 8:30 p.m. May 30, 31, June 1 and 2
in Dinkelspiel Auditorium. Barbara Patton from Los Angeles will
sing Iphigenia, and Shapleigh Howell of Palo Alto will sing the role
of her brother Orestes. Tickets are on sale now at Tresidder Memorial Union box office at $2.50, $2, and $1.50.
