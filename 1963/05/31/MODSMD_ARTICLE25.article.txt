# KEIO Program Sends 11 Students to Japan
## 
### 
Eleven Stanford students will
spend this summer in Japan as
members of the annual Stanford-
Keio summer exchange program.
They are: Jack Schuler (group
leader), Mike Walsh. Carrol Wet
zel. Frank Borse, Bill Miller,
Carolyn Mullikan, Sheila Rick
etts, Jane Reed, Margaret Shel
ton. Lucille Salter, and Elaine
Hill.

These students will live with
Japanese families and travel
throughout Japan with Japanese
students from July 1 to Septem
ber 1. Twelve Keio students will
come to Stanford and the U.S.
for the period of Feb. 15 to April
15, 1964.
