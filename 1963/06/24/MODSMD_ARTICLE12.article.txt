# Festival to Give Moliere's 'School'
## 
### 
Moliere’s “School For Wives,”
the first of the Summer Festival
of Arena Plays at San Francisco
State College, will open June 27
and will have eight performances
through July 6.
The play will be presented, be
ginning at 8:30 p.m., in an arena
setting on the Main Stage. Tickets
are $1.50. Further details and res
ervations are available at the
Creative Arts Box Office at the
College, telephone JUniper 5-7174.
The second play in the series,
for which tickets are also now
being sold, is Eugene Labiche’s
"An Italian Straw Hat,” which
opens July 18.
