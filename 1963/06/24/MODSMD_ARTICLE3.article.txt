# President Sterling issues Welcome
## 
### 
Welcome to Stanford! To some
of you, the appropriate greeting
should he “welcome back!” In both
instances, the greeting is cordial
and is joined hy my good wishes
for a Summer Session that is re
warding and enjoyable.

Stanford offers a varied sum
mer program of high academic
o.uality, complemented by special
conferences and workshops, and
an engaging schedule of cultural
events. I hope that each of you
will take advantage of these offer
ings according to your need and
choice.
