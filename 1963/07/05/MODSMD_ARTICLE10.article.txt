# Kenya Missionary Church Speaker
## 
### 
The Rev. David B. Barrett, a
missionary from Kenya, will
preach at the 11:00 a.m. Service
of Worship at the Stanford Church
this Sunday.
A graduate of Cambridge, the
Rev. Mr. Barrett has been sta
tioned in a remote part of Kenya
for the past four years. He has
been studying African communi
cation problems, particularly in
the provinces of southern Nyanza.
Bishop Stephen Neill describes
David Barrett as “one of the most
promising young missionaries I
have met in any part of the world.”
This summer the Memorial
Church Choir is directed by Har
old Schmidt and the Acting Or
ganist is Hedley Yost.
