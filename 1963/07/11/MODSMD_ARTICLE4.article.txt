# Odegard Speech Slated
## 
### 
Dr. Peter Odegard, University
of California at Berkeley political
scientist, will speak on “The So
cial Sciences in the Twentieth Cen
tury’’ in next week’s Tuesday Eve
ning Series.
The program will be at 8 p.m.
in Memorial Auditorium and is
open to the public.
Dr. Odegard’s speech will also
be the opening talk of the 1963
Cubberly Conference.
