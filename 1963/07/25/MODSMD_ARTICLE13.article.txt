# Library Exhibits Oriental Printing
## 
### 
An exhibit of Chinese and Japa
nese color prints opens today in
the Bender Room of the main
library.
The show traces the develop
ment of oriental prints to the
1700’s. Books and prints are uti
lized to show the development of
the color print in China from the
ink rubbing technique to the per
fection of the seventeenth cen
tury.
THE JAPANESE print and the
Ukiyo-ye School are traced from
their beginnings. The discovery of
the polychrome printing tech
nique is also illustrated.
The exhibition was prepared by
David Sensabaugh, son of Pro
fessor and Mrs. George Sensa
baugh.
The show, to continue through
September, will be open to the
public Monday through Friday
9 a.m. to 5 p.m. and Saturday
from 9 a.m. to noon.
