# W. Hewlett Named To Trustee Board
## 
### 
William R. Hewlett, executive
vice-president, director, and co
founder of the Hewlett-Packard
Co., has been named to the Stan
ford University Board of
Trustees.
He joins, among others, his
partner David Packard, a Univer
sity trustee since 1954.
Hewlett now serves as a direc
tor or trustee of eight other insti
tutions. He holds patents on re
sistance-tuned oscillators and
other electronics devices.
