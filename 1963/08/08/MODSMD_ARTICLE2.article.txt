# BRECHT SYMPOSIUM TODAY
## 
### 
"Brecht: A Symposium on 'Der Hofmeister'" will be
given this afternoon at 4:15 in the Little Theater as a
prelude to the English-language premiere of the Brecht
play this weekend. (See preceding story.)
Leaders of the symposium will be two eminent autho
rities on Brecht's work: Martin Esslin, author of "Brecht
and the Theater of the Absurd," and Dr. Gerhard Nell
haus, a personal friend of Brecht and the principal
translator of Brecht's works into English.
Each man will give a brief talk, followed by a dis
cussion with the audience. There is no admission
charge.
