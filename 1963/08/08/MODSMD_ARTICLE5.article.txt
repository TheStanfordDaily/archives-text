# VIOLIN CONCERT SATURDAY
## 
### 
The Visiting Teachers and Scholars Associa
tion will sponsor a free concert open to the
public on Saturday, August 10, at 8 p.m. in
Tresidder Union lounge.
Hungarian violinist Valos Balogh will make
his California debut. There will be an oppor -
tunity to meet the artist after the concert.
