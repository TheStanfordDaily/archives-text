# Credit to Be Given For Hospital Work
## 
### 
Students who are interested in
doing volunteer work at the Hos
pital Center can earn activity
credits. Thirty hours a quarter or
in two successive quarters are re
quired for one credit.
The annual dues to belong to
the Palo-Alto-Stanford Hospital
Center Auxiliary are $1.
All interested should attend the
orientation session in the back of
the Hospital Cafeteria on Tues
day. October 1. at .3:00 p.m.
