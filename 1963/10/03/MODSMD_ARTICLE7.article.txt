# Academy in Rome Offers Fellowships
## 
### 
The American Academy in
Rome is offering a limited num
ber of fellowships of $3,000 each
for student study in Rome begin
ning October 1. 1964.
Fellowships will be awarded
in the fields of architecture,
landscape architecture, musical
composition, painting, sculpture,
history of art, and classical stud
ies.
All inquiries should be ad
dressed to the Executive Secre
tary, American Academy in
Rome, 101 Park Avenue. New
York 17, N.Y.
The academy was founded in
1891 to "further the development
of the fine arts and classical
studies in the United States."
