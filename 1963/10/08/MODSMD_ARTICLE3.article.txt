# Peter, Paul, Mary To Raise Funds
## 
### 
Tickets for the Peter, Paul, and
Mary performance will go sale
at the Tresidder Box Office
Thursday at $4 each.
The performance of folk-sing
ing is sponsored by the Stanford
Club of Palo Alto as a fund-rais
ing show. Profits, estimated at
$2,500, will go toward four schol
arships given by the Club each
year.
No reserve tickets will be sold
for the event, but a small section
adjacent to the stage will be
available for So.
The trio, originally scheduled
to appear in Memorial Audito
rium. was moved to the Basket
ball Pavillion as the Pavillion
holds approximately 3,000 while
the Auditorium seats only 700.
