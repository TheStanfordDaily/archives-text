# Marshall Deadline
## 
### 
Recent national publicity in
U.S. journals has focused at
tention on the Marshall Scholar
ships. which give 24 U.S. students
every year free tuition at any
British university.
The "Marshalls" are now ac
cepted as Ihe most valuable
awards which any U.S. student of
either sex can win to any British
university. "Marshalls" offer not
only free tuition, fares, living and
other allowances but also unex
celled prestige. They are given
by the British Government as a
gesture of thanks for Marshall
aid.
Applications for the 1961-1966
awards, of which the Western
states of the U.S. are guaranteed
at least four and probably more
Marshall Scholarships, close on
October 22. 1963.
