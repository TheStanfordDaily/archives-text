# Riles for Jellinek To Be Held Sunday
## 
### 
Funeral services for Prof. E. M.
Jellinek, visiting professor of
psychiatry and research associate
at the Institute for the Study of
Human problems, will be held at
4 p.m. Sun d a y in Memorial
Church. He had been at Stanford
since 1962, and was a member of
the Cooperative Commission on
the Study of Alcoholism.
He had studied at the Univer
sity of Grenoble and held an hon
orary M.D. degree from the Uni
versity of Chile. In 1959, he was
awarded the gold key award of
the National Council on Alcohol
ism. He was a fellow of the
American Academy of Arts and
Sciences.
From 1941 to 1930 he was sec
tion director of Yale University's
Studies on Alcohol and previously
held a similar research position
at New York University School
of Medicine.
