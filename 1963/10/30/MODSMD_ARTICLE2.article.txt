# Assault on Roble Injures 3 Girls
## 
### 
Three Roble girls suffered
minor injuries and five Stern
Hall men were cited to the dean
of men's office by campus police
Monday night after an assault on
Roble Hall.
Chief of Police Gordon Davis
said that five of a group of 25
Stern Hall raiders allegedly threw
a water balloon through a second
floor front window of Roble, in
juring Lani Lee Alleman. Mary
Straube, and Jennifer Buss. All
suffered shock and cuts.
They were treated and released
at Palo Alto-Stanford Hospital.
