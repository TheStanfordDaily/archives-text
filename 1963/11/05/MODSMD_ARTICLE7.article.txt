# Prof. Bracher To Speak Tonight
## 
### 
Germany after Adenauer will
form part of the discussion of
"Problems of Parliamentary De
mocracy in Western Europe,"
the Tuesday Evening Series talk
by Prof. Karl D. Bracher, tonight
at 7:45 in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Dr. Bracher, currently a fellow
at the Center for Advanced Study
in the Behavioral Sciences, is pro
fessor of political science and con
temporary history at the Univer
sity of Bonn.
Dr. Bracher is vice-chairman of
the research commission of the
German Society for Foreign Pol
icy and is the author of several
definitive works, especially "The
Collapse of the Versailles System
and the Second World War," and
"The Foreign Policy of the Fed
eral Republic of Germany." He is
co-editor of "Conscience in Re
volt," which deals with anti-Naz
ism in Germanv.
