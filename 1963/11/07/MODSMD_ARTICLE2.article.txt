# Women's Council Extends Tryouts
## 
### 
The date on which written ap
plications for Women's Council
arc due has been extended to
Monday, November 11 at 5 p.m.
The applicant should signup for
an interview at the Judicial Coun
cil Office when she turns in the
application. Interviews will bo
held on November 12-14, Tuesday
afternoon. Wednesday night, and
Thursday afternoon.
Any woman student enrolled at
Stanford is eligible if she has
completed 90 units of study or
has had six quarters in residence,
two of which must be immedi
ately previous to applying for
office. The term of office extends
from winter quarter through the
following fall quarter. Women
who are seniors now and will
be graduate students for part of
the term may apply, however.
Applications are available in
the AWS Desk or at the Judicial
Council Office in Tresidder
Union.
