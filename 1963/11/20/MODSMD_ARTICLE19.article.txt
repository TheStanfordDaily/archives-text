# Cross Country Conquers Bears
## 
### 
Stanford's cross country team
ran past California's harriers
yesterday, 16 -17. Despite constant
rain and a soggy course the first
four finishers turned in respec
table times.
The first four across the end
line were all Indians. They were:
Wayne Kirkland, 20:33.1; Harry
McCalla, 20:53.7; Paul Schlicke,
21:00.8; and Bill Pratt, 21:05.0.
Martin Hill and Bruce Johnson
also did a good job for the In
dians although they didn't finish
in tho top five.
Coach Pay ton Jordan said he
was pleased with the way the
whole team ran.
