# Run-off Is Needed For Cheerleader
## 
### 
The results of yesterday's elec
tion for head cheerleader for the
coming season indicated the ne
cessity for a run-off election be
tween Tom Parker and Carey
Cook, the two candidates receiv
ing the highest amounts of votes.
According to J. D. Lodato, chair
man of Elections Commission,
Parker received 318 votes and
Cook received 311.
A total of exactly 1000 votes
was cast, Lodato said. J. Weaver
received 178; 13 of the votes were
scattered among various candi
dates. Charlie Price, ineligible
because a freshman, nevertheless
received 180 votes as a write-in
candidate.
