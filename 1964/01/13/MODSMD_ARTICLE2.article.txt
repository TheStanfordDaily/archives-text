# Air Force Plans Lecture on Space
## 
### 
I
Three representatives of the
United Stales Air Force will give
a fifty minute briefing on the
United States Mission in Space at
4:15 p.m. on January 22 in Cub
berley Auditorium.
The briefing team is composed
of Lt. Colonel Richard B. Olney,
Major Bill F. Francis, and Major
Jean B. Pitner who travel from
school to school to discuss the
latest developments in the space
program. Colonel Olney. while
serving with the Air Rescue Ser
vice, developed and perfected an
air rescue kit currently being
used to recover astronauts and
their Mercury capsules from the
ocean.
