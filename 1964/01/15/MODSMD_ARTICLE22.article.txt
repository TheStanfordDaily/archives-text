# Sailors Here
## 
### 
Stanford's wrestlers open their
1964 campaign this evening at
seven o'clock in the Men's Gym
against Mare Island and Moffett.
Field.
The Indian mat men, competing
under Coach Bill Leland for the
first time, will be out to improve
Iheir 4-6 showing of last season.
Leland says his team will be
in for a tough match, as Moffett
Field beat the Tribe last season
and has improved considerably.
But, says the coach, he expects
his men to come out on top.
