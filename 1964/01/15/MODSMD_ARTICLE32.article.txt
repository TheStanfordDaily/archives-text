# Students Will Hear Professor Levin
## 
### 
The Stanford Philological Asso
ciation will hold its 'L'37th meet
ing tomorrow evening, Thursday,
January 16, at the Lower Lounge,
Women's Clubhouse. The meeting
will open at 4:15 p.m.
The speaker will be Professor
David Levin, Associate Professor
of English, speaking on "The
Problem of Re-creating History
in William Faulkner's Absoloin,
Absoloin!"
Refreshments will be served.
