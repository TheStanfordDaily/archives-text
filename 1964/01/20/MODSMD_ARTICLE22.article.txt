# Casting Complete For Winter Plays
## 
### 
Casting has been completed and
rehearsals are now underway for
Ram's Head's 1964 Winter One
Acts. Producer Jay Weaver said,
however, that people are still
needed to help on the technical
and production staffs.
Anyone interested in Helping
on the technical and production
staffs should leave a message for
Weaver at the Ram's Head office,
DA 1-2300, ext. 4336.
Tickets for the production, to
be presented February 7, 8, 13.
14 and 15, will be available next
week at ihe Tresidder Ticket
Office.
