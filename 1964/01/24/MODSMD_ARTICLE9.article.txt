# I-Center to Have 'Evening in India'
## 
### 
The International Center will
present "An Evening in India,"
tomorrow night in Tresidder
Lounge, with the "insight into
India and its culture" scheduled
to begin at 8 p.m.
One of a series of Friday night
firesides, the program will be a
"veritable feast, with a dish
seemingly from every zone of In
dia," reports Dhanjoo Ghista.
"The delicate dancing movements
will bring to mind the grace of
the swaying palms. The legends
of the symbol of divine love will
be expressed in native dance,"
said Ghista.
