# Coed Is Topic for AWS Symposium
## 
### 
"The Change and Choice for
the College Woman" will be the
topic of Dr. Pauline Tompkin's
speech today, the second major
address of the A.W.S. Symposium.
Dr. Tompkins, presently Director
General of the American Associa
tion of University Women, has a
Ph.D. in Law and Diplomacy. She
is a former Professor of Political
Science, History, and Government
and is a Visiting Specialist of the
United Board for Christian Higher
Education in Asia.
Dr. Tompkins will be speaking
at 4:15 p.m. in the main lounge of
Tresidder. All are invited to at
tend.
