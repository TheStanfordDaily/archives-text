# New Group At I-Center
## 
### 
Tonight the Discussion Club of
the International Center will hold
its first meeting at 9. Subsequent
meetings of the club will be held
each Monday at 9.
This group is to be composed
of students interested in problems
which human being face in every
day life.
At tonight's meeting the topic
will center around the intellectual
and his role in modern society.
Dr. Werner Warmbrunn, Foreign
Student Adviser, will lead the dis
cussion on "Is Every Stanford
Alumnus an Intellectual?"
Stefan Kwiatowski, Polish grad
uate student in social sciences, is
the co-ordinator for the club and
he welcomes ideas for future
topics. He may be reac he d
through the International Center.
