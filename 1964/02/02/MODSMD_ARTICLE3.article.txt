# Greek Symposium Lectures To Start
## 
### 
Four professors prominent in
the fields of Greek philosophy,
poetry, and drama will participate
in a two-day symposium on Greek
tragedy at Stanford which begins
this afternoon.
TIIK FIRST lecture will be de
livered by Professor Bruno Snell
at 4:15 p.m. in Cubberley Audi
torium. Professor Snell will speak
on "Socrates and Euripides."
At 8:00 this evening, also in
Cubberley Auditorium, Professor
11. D. F. Kit to will discuss "The
Poetics and the Nature of Fourth
Century Greek Tragedy."
Tomorrow afternoon at 4:15 in
the main lounge of Tresidder
Memorial Union, Professor T. B.
L. Webster will present "The Pro
duction of Greek Tragedy."
