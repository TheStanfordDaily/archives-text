# Study Grant Won By Civ Instructor
## 
### 
Richard L. Rapson, instructor in
Western Civilization and faculty
resident of Otero House, Wilbur
Hall, has been awarded a Dan
forth Teacher Grant to complete
his graduate study. The $4,800
allowance will permit Rapson to
finish his work for a doctorate
in history at Columbia University
during 1964-65.
A graduate of Amherst Co I -
lege. Rapson was chosen from 446
applicants nationwide on the
basis of academic ability, future
promise in the teaching field and
other factors.
