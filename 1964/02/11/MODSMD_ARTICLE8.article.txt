# Finer To Speak
## 
### 
Dr. Herman Finer, professor of
political science at Chicago Uni
versity. will speak tonight at 7:45
p.m. for the Tuesday Evening
Series in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Dr. Finer, a graduate of the Uni
versity of London and interna
tional authority on governmental
relations, will speak on "Scientists
versus Politicians: Who Shall
Rule?"
The noted author and scholar's
work include "Mussolini's Italy,"
"The Road to Reaction" and
"America's Destiny."
