SCOTT PAPERCOMPANY AWARD:
Application blanks arc now available in the
Dean of Men's Office for students wishing
to apply for the Scott Paper Company
Foundation award. Candidates must be en
rolled in the School of Engineering or the
Department of Chemistry. Only those per
sons are eligible who will complete their
undergraduate program over two full years.
Application is for the academic year 1964-

65 and should be filed on or before March
6th and is renewable for the second year.
The award is for tuition for each of tin
junior and senior years. Financial need will
be considered in the amount of the award
but in no circumstances will the amount
be less than $500.
To be eligible for the award, a student,
male or female, must have signified an in
tention of pursuing a career in business or
industry and must have specifically demon
strated in extra-curricular activities as well
as in the class room those qualities associated
with the well known Rhodes Scholar.
Candidates are not to be restricted by
reason of their family income level because
the primary purpose of the award is to give
status. Selection of the candidates will be
made by March 15th or shortly thereafter.

SCOTT PAPERCOMPANY AWARD:
Application blanks arc now available in the
Dean of Men's Office for students wishing
to apply for the Scott Paper Company
Foundation award. Candidates must be en
rolled in the School of Engineering or the
Department of Chemistry. Only those per
sons are eligible who will complete their
undergraduate program over two full years.
Application is for the academic year 1964-

65 and should be filed on or before March
6th and is renewable for the second year.
The award is for tuition for each of tin
junior and senior years. Financial need will
be considered in the amount of the award
but in no circumstances will the amount
be less than $500.
To be eligible for the award, a student,
male or female, must have signified an in
tention of pursuing a career in business or
industry and must have specifically demon
strated in extra-curricular activities as well
as in the class room those qualities associated
with the well known Rhodes Scholar.
Candidates are not to be restricted by
reason of their family income level because
the primary purpose of the award is to give
status. Selection of the candidates will be
made by March 15th or shortly thereafter.

with the well known Rhodes Scholar.

the primary purpose of the award is to give
status. Selection of the candidates will be
made by March 15th or shortly thereafter.
