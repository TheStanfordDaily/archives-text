# Tours Alumni Aid Asked On Finance Folio
## 
### 
The Overseas Campus Board is
preparing a fifty-page handbook
for students going to Stanford-in-
France. The booklet will discuss
such varied topics as "Travel,"
"Recreational, Social, and Intel
lectual Life at the Center," and
"A Brief History of Tours and
the Loire Valley." In addition, the
Board plans to include several
short essays concerning personal
reactions to the Stanford-in-
France experience. All alumni
of Tours Campus interested in
compiling this booklet are asked
to meet in Tresidder, Room 274
at 4:15 on Wednesday.
