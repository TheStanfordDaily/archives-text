# Baseballers Face Cal Aggie Nine
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity baseballers
face the University of California
al Davis this afternoon at 3 on
the Sunken Diamond.
The Indians, with second base
man George Thacher once again
an extremely doubtful starter,
will be going after their seventh
win without defeat this season.
Dave Dickson will start on the
hill for the Cards. He is sched
uled to work three innings, as are
Dick Kovasevieh and John Mason.
