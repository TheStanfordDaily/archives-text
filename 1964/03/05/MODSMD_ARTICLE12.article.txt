# Red Vest Band To Play At TGIF
## 
### 
Stanford's Red Vest Band will
play an informal T.G.I.F. concert
of Big-Band jazz in Tresidder
Union tomorrow, from 4 to 6 p.m.
This Friday's T.G.I.F. will be
the first on-campus appearance of
the Red Vest Band outside of the
basketball games. Selections will
be taken from Count Basie,
Woodie Herman, and Dizzy Gil
; lespie arrangements.
