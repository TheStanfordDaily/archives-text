# Volunteer Credit
## 
### 
Students can earn activity
credits by doing volunteer work at
the Palo Alto-Stanford Hospital
Center, according to Mrs. Arthur
Bridgett, Hospital Auxiliary Presi
dent.
Thirty hours during the Spring
Quarter are required for one
credit; or a student may work a
total of 30 hours in two succes
sive quarters for one activity
credit. Spring and Fall will be
counted as consecutive quarters.
Annual dues to belong to the
hospital auxiliary are $1.00.
All interested students should
attend the orientation session in
Room W-002 of the hospital on
Thursday, April 2.
