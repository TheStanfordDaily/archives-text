# MURALS
## 
### 
Intramural Director Elwyn
Bugge announced plans yesterday
for a co-recreational volleyball
league competition U> begin April
21.
Signups for this tournament
are on the Intramurals Board at
the men's gym and on the foyer
of the women's gym, and inter
ested men or women can sign up,
whether affiliated with a team or
not, until April 16.
Teams will consist of three men
and three women to a side, with
an alternate per team, and will
play Tuesdays and Thursdays at
the Encina Gym Courts at 2:00
p.m. and 3:00 p.m.
