PHI BETA KAPPA: Stanford chapter
announces its annual dinner at L'Omelette
on Thursday, April 9. Cocktail hour from
5:30 to 6:30 will be followed by dinner at
6:45. The Provost of the University, Fred
erick E. Terman, will deliver the address.
$3.50 per person, tax and tip included. All
members of Phi Beta Kappa who are now
members of the Stanford community, regard
less of where they were elected, are eligible
to attend with their husbands or wives.
Those who wish to come to the dinner should
respond to Professor Christina Phelps
Harris, Dept. of Political Science, no later

than Friday afternoon, April 3.
RA APPLICATIONS for the coming
academic year may now be submitted to the
Dean of Men's Office. There are 56 of these

Mr. Moore, the Assistant Dean of Men,
will be glad to talk with anyone who is
interested in applying for one of these posi
tions.
