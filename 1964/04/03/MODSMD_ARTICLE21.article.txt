SAN FRANCISCO
David Niven, Robert Wagner, and Capucine star as three jewel

The detective on their trail, and the wife of the third thief, is Peter

are 1, 3, 5, 7, 9 p.m.
At the Bridge Theater is "Sound of Trumpets," an Italian film
that has won several awards. It stars Sandro Panzeri as a youth
meeting Loredana Detto on his first job in an industrial plant in
Milan. "The Fiances," another picture by the same director, Erman
no Olmi, is running with it. Show times for "Trumpets" are 1:15. 4,
6:45, 9:30 p.m.
