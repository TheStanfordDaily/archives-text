# Concert To Aid Con Home Drive
## 
### 
Cal Tjader, Judy Henske, the
Modern Folk Quartet, and balla
deer Steve Pe Pass will star in
a folk and jazz concert for th e
benefit of Con Home Monday,
April 13, in Memorial Auditorium.
Tjader, formerly with George
Shearing, specializes in Latin
Jazz with his quintet. Miss Hen
ske was once a member of the
Whiskey Hill Singers.
