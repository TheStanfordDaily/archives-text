# Snead Takes Lead
## 
### 
GREENSBORO, N.S. (API
Sam Snead. who will be 52 years

old next month, shot a seven
under-par 61 Thursday to equal
the Greater Greensboro Open
Golf Tournament record and lake
the early first round lead.
The ageless wonder, who has
won here seven times, including
the first GGO in 1938, notched
four birdies on each nine. He
put together scores of 33-31 over
the 36-35 par course of the Sedge
field Country Club.
