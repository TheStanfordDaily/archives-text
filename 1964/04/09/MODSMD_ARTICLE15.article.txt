# Stanford Auction Slated Next Week
## 
### 
More than 700 items donated
to the Stanford Auction will be
offered up for bids at 7:30 p.m.
on April 16 and 17. A preview
of the collection, which includes
books, household furnishings,
prints, and jewelry, will be held
in the museum lobby at 1-5 p.m.
from April 10 through April 14.
Proceeds will be used for mu
seum acquisitions.
