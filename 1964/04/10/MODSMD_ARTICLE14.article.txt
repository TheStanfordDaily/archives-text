# I-Club Sponsors Ball At Sheraton
## 
### 
The I-Club sponsored Interna
tional Ball will be held April 18
in the Rose Room of the Shera
ton-Palace Hotel. This is an an
nual I-Club function and consuls
from the San Francisco area are
expected to attend.
Irish dances, Latin American
folksings, the Philippine Lamp
Dance, and a Burmese spiritual
will highlight the evening's enter
tainment.
Tickets are $4 per couple and
will be sold at the I-Center.
