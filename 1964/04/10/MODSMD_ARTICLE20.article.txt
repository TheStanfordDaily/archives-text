# Crew Races Open
## 
### 
Crew competition begins at 9
a.m. Saturday at tht Redwood
City Yacht Harbor, with the first
race between four-man boats
from Stanford and USC. At 10
the usual eight-man boat races
begin, and will feature crews
from Oregon State in addition to
USC and Stanford. Races will be
between J.V.'s, frosh, and varsity
crews, in that order.
