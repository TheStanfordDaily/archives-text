# Students Training At German School
## 
### 
Nine Stanford students, future
teachers of college-level German,
are spending spring and summer
terms in Hamburg for intensive
study of the language.
The following students will
study under Stanford Prof. Ed
gar Loli nor, resident director in
Hamburg: Philip H. Beard. Maria
A. Bradford. Mark L. Groathouse,
Hope A. Hague, Eddi Irmingard
Kosin, Joyce E. McKce. Dorothy
I. Milford, Barbara Tinder, Al
