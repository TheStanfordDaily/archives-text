Block ticket sales for "Guys
and Dolls." Ram's Head's spring
production to be presented
May 2. 3. 7. 8 and 9 have been
extended until Thursday at the
Ram's Head Office, 321-2300, ext.
1336.
Ram's Head will provide thea
ter-party block seating arrange
ments for groups of any size for
any of the five performances.

discount.
