# Musical Meet Begins Friday
## 
### 
Student composers from ten
Western colleges and universities
will present five public concerts
of their work during their annual
symposium at Stanford Friday,
Saturday, and Sunday. Readings
of orchestral works will be pre
sented at the first program at 8
p.m. Friday.
On Saturday original works
will be offered ranging from
three-minute piano compositions
to a concert performance of Stan
ford student Peter Ford's opera
'Buddha," based on surrealist
texts and ideals.
The final program, at 3:30 p.m.
Sunday, will be mostly music for
wind ensembles and string quar
ets. The programs will be given
n Dinkelspiel Auditorium an d
are open to the public.
