ogist and counselor from I*, of Cal. will
speak on "Solutions to the American Dilem
ma—Minority Rights."

4:15, Monday, Tresidder 281. "Rockefeller

HEALTH COMMITTEE: 5 p.m.. Mon

field trip.

HILLEL FOUNDATION: 7:30 p.m.,
Monday. SSO Emerson Street, Palo Alto.
Advanced Hebrew. Ora Slor, Instructor.
INDIA ASSOCIATION: India Asso
ciation presents "India Week." There will
be short films daily from 4:45 p.m. to 5:15
p.m. in Tresidder. Evening seminars related
to national history and culture will be pre
sented from 7:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m. in
Tresidder. Today's panel will discuss "India
Politics and Government."
JOURNAL CLUB: 4 p.m., Monday,
Rm. 320 Geo. Corner. Peter 11. Stauffer
of Stanford will speak on "Paleogene Sedi
mentation in the TransVerse Ranges, Cali
fornia." Carl K. Seyfert will speak on
"Compositional Variation in the English
Peak Batholith, Klamath Mountains, North
ern California."
PEACE CAUCUS: 11:30 a.m. to 1:30
p.m., Monday, White Plaza. Literature and
information on conscientious objection, reli
gious and non-religious grounds available.
STANFORD CHRISTIAN COUNCIL:
7:45 p.m., Monday, Memorial Church Choir
Room. Discussion of joint study program,
possibilities of ecumenical worship, and Sum
mer Community House Project.
