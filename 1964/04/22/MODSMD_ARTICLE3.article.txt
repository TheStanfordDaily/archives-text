# Consul Will Discuss Israel's Progress
## 
### 
Gideon Saguy. Consul General
of Israel, will speak tomorrow
evening at 8 p.m. at the Palo
Alto YWCA. 1161 Alma Street.
Saguy will discuss "Israel's
Image." He will give an analysis
of Israel's development and pre
sent the country's position as an
intermediary buffer in the West's
relation with the African na
tions. The talk is open to the
public and admission will be free.
