# Associated Alumni Sponsor Panel
## 
### 
The Associated Alumni of the
Overseas Campuses will sponsor
a panel discussion on the films of
Fedinio Fellini Thursday at 4:30
in the Large Lounge of Tresidder.
Participating will be Dr. Harry
Brilnare of the Communications
Dept., Dr. James Kerans of the
Speech and Drama I)ept., and Dr.
Casimo Carsana of the Italian
Dept.
Each will discuss an aspect of
Fellini's work.
