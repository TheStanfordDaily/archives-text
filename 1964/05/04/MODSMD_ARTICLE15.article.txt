# Psychic Powers To Be Explored
## 
### 
Dr. Hugh Lynn Cayce will give
a talk entitled "Venture Inward"
at 8 p.m. in Tresidder Union's
Large Lounge.
Cayce's speech will concern his
father, Edward Cayce, who has
gained national recognition as
possessing unusual psychic fac
ulties.
CAYCE will discuss his father's
ability to answer questions re
quiring specialized knowledge
while under a trance; knowledge
which Cayce says he elicits from
other persons' unconscious minds.
"Venture Inward" is the third
in a series of lectures on para
psychology sponsored by the Pro
gram Division of Tresidder Union.
