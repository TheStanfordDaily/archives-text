# YWCA Plans Speaker Series
## 
### 
The YWCA is planning a series
of speakers on Peace and Disarm
ament as part of its regular Fri
day afternoon Public Affairs
Luncheons at the Women's Club
house. This Friday Mr. Robert
Lyon will speak on the Geneva
Disarmament Conference, which
he attended.
Later talks will stress political,
economic, religious, and idealis
tic aspects of disarmament.
Speakers will include Bob Mang,
Dr. Carl Stover. Rev. Richard
I Roe, and Ira Sand perl.
