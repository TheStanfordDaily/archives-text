# Folk Singer Odetta To Give Concert
## 
### 
Folk and blues singer Odetta
will present a concert tomorrow
night at Foothill College Audito
rium, under the sponsorship of
the Palo Alto Fair Play Council.
Proceeds from the concert
will be used to perpetuate the
Council's efforts.
Tickets are available at the
Tresidder Box Office.
