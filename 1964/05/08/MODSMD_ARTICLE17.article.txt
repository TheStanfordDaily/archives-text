# Rev. Bredesen To Discuss Revival
## 
### 
The Rov. Harold Bredesen, pas
tor of the First Reformed Church
of Mt. Vernon, New York, will
preach at the 11 a.m. Service of
Worship at the Stanford Memor
ial Church on Sunday, May 10.
Rev. Bredesen is on a tour of the
West Coast speaking about the
great spiritual revival in the
Church throughout the world.
Sunday afternoon at 3 p.m. he
will speak at the Christian Cen
ter, 3201 Middlefield Road, Palo
Alto.
