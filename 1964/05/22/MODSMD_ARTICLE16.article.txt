# Sterlings Begin European Trip
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Wallace
Sterling arrived in London yester
day on the first stop of their Eu
ropean vacation.
After a week's stay in England,
the Sterlings will go to Southern
Bavaria. In late June, Dr. Sterling
will participate in meetings of
presidents representing the Asso
ciation of American Universities
and the West Germany Rectors'
Conference. He is a former AAU
president.
Dr. Sterling will later tour sev -
eral West German universities.
The Sterlings will return to
California late in July. The presi
dent will resume his duties at
Stanford in September.
Provost Frederick E. Terman,
vice-president for academic af
fairs, will continue to serve as act
ing president in his absence.
