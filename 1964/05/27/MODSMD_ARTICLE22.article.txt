# Journalism Grant
## 
### 
Five junior-year journalism
scholarships of $700 have been
made available for 1964-65, it was
announced today by Dr. Nathan
Maccoby, acting executive head of
the Department of Communica
tion.
The scholarships will be given
to juniors who will be majors in
the Journalism Division of the de
partment in 1964-65. Application
information on the scholarships
is available at the Department of
Communication office, room 61c.
