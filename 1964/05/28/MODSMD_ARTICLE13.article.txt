# Zeitgeist Magazine On Sale Monday
## 
### 
Stanford's freshman literary
magazine, the Zeitgeist, will be
distributed Monday. Copies will
be on sale for 3j cents at the
Post Office and Engineering Cor
ner.
The issue is the second for the
newly formed magazine. It will
include the winning poems from
a freshman contest by Helen
Lynnc Williams, John O. Green,
and Laura Meger. The magazine
will also contain ficton, satire,
and exposition.
Mr. Ransom, the faculty adviser
for the Zeitgeist, commented that
he liked the material included
and commended the staff for
quick and efficient publishing.
