# ASSU Publishes Bay Area Guide
## 
### 
Newly published "Places To Go
And Things To Do In San Fran
cisco And The Bay Area" goes on
sale this week for 10 cents at the
ASSU Office and at the Social Co
ordinator's Office.
The guide to restaurants and ac
tivities in the Bay Area was com
piled by the Social Coordinator
and published by the ASSU.
It includes lists of restaurants
and their specialities, entertain
ment, sport facilities, amusement
parks, art galleries, picnic grounds
and theaters.
