# Journalism Profs Studying In Kenya
## 
### 
Dr. Wilbur Schramm, director
of the Institute for Communica
tion Research, and William Rivers,
associate professor of communica
tion, have left for Nairobi, Kenya,
where they will begin a study of
modern technological approaches
to East African education.
They are part of a five man re
search team under the auspices of
the U.S. Agency for International
Development.
