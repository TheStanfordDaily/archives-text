# Former Organist Dies From Illness
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, organist and
music professor at Stanford for
32 years, died last week at the age
of 78.
Allen, who retired from Stan
ford in 1950, died in Olympia, fol
lowing a long illness.
He transferred to Stanford in
1918 from College of the Pacific,
then located in San .Tose, and re
ceived his bachelor's degree in
1934 while teaching.
He made his last appearance
at Stanford in 1953 as guest or
ganist at the 50th anniversary of
Memorial Church.
