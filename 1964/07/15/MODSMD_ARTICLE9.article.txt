# New Football Ticket Offers
## 
### 
Applications for season tickets
to Stanford's five home football
games are available at the Ath
letic Director's office. This year's
application features a new eco
nomically-priced reserved seat
ticket providing choice seats on
the East side of the stadium for
$16.50.
Also available are three other
types of tickets:
• Reserved, West side, $20.
• Family plan, End zone, $20.
• High school, junior college or
children, North or South end, $4.
Individual reserved tickets for
each game are also on sale for $4.
Nonreserved end zone seats sell
for $2.50.
The 1964 schedule includes
home games with San Jose State,
Rice, Washington, USC, and Ore
gon State.
