# Church Plans Bard Service
## 
### 
Memorial Church will sponsor
a special musical service as a part
of the Summer Shakespearean
Festival tomorrow afternoon at
4:30.
Music from the Elizabethean
Communion Service will be pre
sented by the Church choir under
the direction of Professor Harold
Schmidt.
The music will be set in the
service of Ante-Communion and
will reflect the liturgical choral
work of the Elizabethean period.
Rev. Robert Minto will deliver
the sermon Sunday morning at
11 a.m.
The weekly candlelight services
on Wednesday evening will now
be held at 8:30 p.m., instead of
9:30 p.m. The change will be efec
tive this evening and will continue
for the remainder of the summer.
