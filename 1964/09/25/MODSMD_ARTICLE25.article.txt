# 'Players' Plan Fall Tryouts
## 
### 
Stanford Players will hold open
interview-tryouts next week for
production of Chekov's "The Sea
Gull" and for Anouilh's "Time
Remembered."
Tryouts are scheduled on Mon
day from 7:30 to 10 p.m. and on
Tuesday from 3:30 to p.m. and
from 7:30 to 10 p.m. Final audi
tions and call-backs will be held
on Wednesday from 7:30 to 10
p.m. All tryouts and auditions will
be conducted in Memorial Hall's
Little Theatre.
PRODUC TION DATES for "The
Sea Gull" are November 4, 5, 6.
7, 9, 10, 11 and 13. "Time Remem
bered" will be performed on De
cember 3, 4, and 5.
Gerald Hiken, visiting director
in the Speech and Drama Depart
ment will direct "The Sea Gull."
Hiken appeared recently in the
New York Actors Studio produc
tion of "Three Sisters" and has
performed in other Off-Broadway
Chekov plays.
"Time Remembered" will he di
rected by Judith Burgess, a gradu
ate student in directing.
STUDENTS INTERESTED in
trying out for either of the Play
ers' productions should familiarize
themself with the plays, but no
prepared scenes are necessary.
Rehearsals for "The Sea Gull"
will begin October 1.
