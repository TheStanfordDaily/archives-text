# Clarinetist Joins Music Department
## 
### 
Philip Fath of Mill Valley, solo
clarinetist of the San Francisco
Symphony Orchestra, has been
appointed lecturer in music at
Stanford.
He will teach clarinet from be
ginning through advanced and
master classes, said Dr. William
L. Crosten, head of the Music De
partment.
Fath is a native of New York
City, who studied there and
played assistant solo clarinet un
der George Szell and the Cleve
land Orchestra from 1954 to 1956.
He is also a faculty member at
Ihe Dominican Conservatory of
Music, Dominican College, San
1 Rafael.
