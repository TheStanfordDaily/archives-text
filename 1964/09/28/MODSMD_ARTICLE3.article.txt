# Fraternities' Rights Upheld
## 
### 
BUFFALO (CPS) A New
York State Supreme Court justice
has issued temporary injunction
preventing the University of
Buffalo from interfering with the
activities of fraternities and so
rorities on its campus.
Dean of Students Richard A.
Siggelkow was ordered not to in
terfere with the rushing, pledg
ing, and initiation of students by
college social organizations.
