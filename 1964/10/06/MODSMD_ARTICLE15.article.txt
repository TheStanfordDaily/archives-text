# New Director For ICAME
## 
### 
Thomas Ashley Graves, Jr., the
former director of an interna
tional development, institute, has
been appointed associate dean of
the Stanford Graduate School of
Business and director of the In
ternational Center for the Ad
vancement of Management Edu
cation (ICAME).
Financed by a 53.5 million Ford
Foundation grant, ICAME's pur
pose is to increase the effective
ness of business schools in Latin
America, Asia, and Africa by
training their teachers and by
helping to improve the content
and relevance of their curriculum.
The Center is staffed by a faculty
assembled from leading business
schools in the United States.
