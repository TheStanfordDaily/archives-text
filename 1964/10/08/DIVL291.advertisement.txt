Ken Kesey to Speak at Kepler's
Ken Kesey, nationally-acclaimed author of Sometimes A Great Notion, will speak j
at Kepler's Books, 825 El Camino Real, Menlo Park, on Thursday, October Bth at
8:30 p.m.
Mr. Kesey, who is a Peninsula resident, won attention in 1962 as one of the
country's foremost younger writers with the publication of his first novel, One
Flew Over the Cuckoo Nest. He is working at the present time on a film adapta
tion of that book; actor Kirk Douglas will portray the role of McMurphy, Kesey's
memorable protagonist.
Mr. Kesey will talk about writing and its problems. Following the talk and
discussion, he will be available to autograph copies of his book.
Admission will be a nominal 25c.

Ken Kesey to Speak at Kepler's
Ken Kesey, nationally-acclaimed author of Sometimes A Great Notion, will speak j
at Kepler's Books, 825 El Camino Real, Menlo Park, on Thursday, October Bth at
8:30 p.m.
Mr. Kesey, who is a Peninsula resident, won attention in 1962 as one of the
country's foremost younger writers with the publication of his first novel, One
Flew Over the Cuckoo Nest. He is working at the present time on a film adapta
tion of that book; actor Kirk Douglas will portray the role of McMurphy, Kesey's
memorable protagonist.
Mr. Kesey will talk about writing and its problems. Following the talk and
discussion, he will be available to autograph copies of his book.
Admission will be a nominal 25c.
