# Irvine Memoriam To Be Held Today
## 
### 
Memorial services for Prof.
William Irvine of the English De
partment will be held in Memorial
Church Wednesday, October 14,
at 4:30 p.m. The Reverend Robert
M. Minto, University chaplain,
will officiate.
Prof. Irvine, a nationally-known
authority on Shaw, Darwin, and
the Victorian era, died at his cam
pus home October 7. He is sur
vived by his wife and son.
