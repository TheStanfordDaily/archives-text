# Cultural Booklet Now Available
## 
###

University's artistic, musical and
cultural events for the coming
i year is now available free from

cises, Encina Hall.

hibits. dramas, flims, lectures and
musical presentation. The book
let also includes order forms for
the purchase of season tickets.
