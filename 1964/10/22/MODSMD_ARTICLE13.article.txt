# Ram's Head Seeks Scripts
## 
### 
Ram's Head Productions today
announced that it is seeking ma
terial for the organization's Win
ter One Acts. According to One
Acts' Producer Louise Loosli,
original works are preferred but
original adaptations will be given
consideration. Three plays are to
be selected for presentation.
Miss Loosli urged that authors
submit their scripts to Ram's
Head by November 22.
