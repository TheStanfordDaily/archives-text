# Catholic Mass At Stanford
## 
### 
A Catholic service will be held
in Memorial Church this after
noon at 5 p.m. as part of the new
Liturgical Series.
The service will be a traditional
Catholic mass. Father Duryea,
who will conduct the mass, will
explain the parts of the service
as he performs them.
The service is open to all
Catholic and non-Catholic stu
dents. It is intended to explain
the Catholic ceremonies to non
church members.
Other services of other churches
will follow this service in the
series.
