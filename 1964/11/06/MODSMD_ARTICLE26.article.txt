# Basketball Tickets
##

Beginning Monday arul continuing through Friday from 8:30
a.m. to 4:30 p.m., season ticket books priced at $6 will be on sale.
They will be sold at ticket booths in the Pavilion for students, fac
ulty, and staff.
The book includes tickets for the eight home basketball games
during winter quarter. Individual tickets for the Santa Clara, Tu
lane, and Wyoming games to be played this quarter and during quar
ter break are 75c.
Because of the limited seating capacity of the Pavilion, only 750
student season ticket books will be available and only 250 for facully
and staff.
