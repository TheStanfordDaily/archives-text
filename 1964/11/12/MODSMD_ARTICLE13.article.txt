CORPS WOMAN BARGAINS - Former Stanford
graduate student, Janet Mueller, of Kearney,
Nebraska, bargains with a merchant in Kabul,
Afghanistan. She has served with the Peace
Corps in that country since September of 1962.

Miss Mueller received her M.A. in English in
1960 and taught at Palo Alto High School before
joining the organization. Peace Corps workers
are recruiting volunteers this week in front of
Tresidder Memorial Union.
