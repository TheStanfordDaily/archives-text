Quarter is being held this
week. Students may consult
their advisers for program
ming today and tomorrow.
Advisers' s pee i a 1 office
hours arc posted outside
their offices and at depart
mental or school headquart
ers.

their study list cards on one
of these days will forfeit the
privilege of preregistration.
