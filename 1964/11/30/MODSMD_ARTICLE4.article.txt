# McClure To End Poetry Series
## 
### 
The last poet to read his own
poetry in the Poetry Series is
Michael McClure. He will read his
poetry at 8 p.m. tonight in Tresid
der's small lounge.
McClure is a noted Bay area
resident whoso volumes include
Hymns to St. Geryon and Dark
Brown. He has been placed by the
Reader's Encyclopedia to Amer
ican Literature, in "the second
wave of the Bay Area awaken
ing."
