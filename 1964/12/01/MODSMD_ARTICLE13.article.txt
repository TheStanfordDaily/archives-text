# Ski Club Slates Meeting, Flicks
## 
### 
The Stanford Ski Club will hold
its first meeting today at 7:30
in Cubberley Auditorium. Infor
mation will be given concerning
Winter Carnival including the
procedure for block ticket sales
and the entertainment.
Kim Novak has been invited to
attend again, in addition to the
regular entertainment. Other ski
club activities for the year will
be outlined. The meeting will be
followed by two fifteen minute
Warren Miller flicks. Membership
cards will be sold but will not
be necessary for this meeting.
