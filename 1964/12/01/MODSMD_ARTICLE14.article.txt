# Szalai To Talk In Tresidder
## 
### 
Alexander Szalai, Professor of
Sociology for the Univei'sity of
Veszpren in Hungary, will speak
today at 4:15 in Tresidder on
"Current Hungarian Research in
Sociology and Organization
Theory."
After a number of run-ins with
the government, he was recalled
to do research in 1960 and has
been allowed since 1962 to travel
abroad. He is Hungary's chief ex
