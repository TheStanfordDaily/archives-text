# Groups Show Their Wares
## 
### 
Freshmen who were reluctant
to commit themselves to extra
curricular activities last quarter
will get a second chance this
week.
The Stanford Today and To
morrow Campus Activity Pro
gram will cover five areas of ex
tracurricular activities: political,
service, publications, creative, ath
letics and special interest.
A program will be given tonight
in Branner and the front houses
of Wilbur, and tomorrow in
Roble. It. will begin at 7:30 p.m.
