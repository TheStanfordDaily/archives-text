THE GRAND DETOUR-For many a Stern, Wilbur and Branner
resident this sealed-off area means nothing but a long detour.
But it's also the site of construction for the new undergraduate
library. The old, temporary classrooms are gone. The new

library will stand beside Cubberley Auditorium and the old
library (lower right), which will continue to serve students.
The new building was designed by John Carl Warnecke.
