# Manna Appointed To Hawaii Post
## 
### 
Prof. Paul R. Hanna, director
of Stanford's Comparative Educa
tion Center, has been appointed
a senior research fellow at Ha
waii's East-West Center.
He will serve there from Janu
ary to June, 1965, in a continuing
seminar sponsored by the Center
on "Education as an Instrument
of National Goals."
Dr. Hanna holds the Lee L.
Jacks professorship of child edu
cation.
At Stanford since 1935, he is
an internationally known educa
tor, consultant to and former
official of the Agency for Inter
national Development and
UNESCO, and author or editor of
many social studies textbooks.
