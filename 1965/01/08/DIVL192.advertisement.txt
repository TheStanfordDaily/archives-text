DO YOU HAVE TWO MORE YEARS
OF ACADEMIC WORK
TO COMPLETE?
I
AIR FORCE ROTC OFFERS YOU A
SECOND CHANCE
A new two-year Air Force ROTC program leading
to a career as an Air Force Officer is open to all
male students who have two or more years of
academic work remaining for completion of a bac
calaureate or graduate degree—providing they can
complete such work prior to their 28th birthday.
The Air Forces offers you a CAREER which will
utilize your ACADEMIC MAJOR.
COMBINE CAREER PROGRESSION
and your
MILITARY SERVICE
SEE: Lt. Col. Terry, Bac 41
or Call 326-0265
You must apply not later than February 15, 1965
to enter next Autumn Quarter
