The Episcopal Community at Stanford University
The Inquirer's Group
will hold its first session
THURSDAY, JANUARY 14
7:30 to 8:,30 P.M.
CANTERBURY HOUSE
Purpose: a careful study of the doctrine, discipline, and
worship of the Episcopal Church, open to all interested
in the Episcopal Church and/or Confirmation, and for
Churchmen who want to know more on an adult level.
The leader of this group is
THE REV. JOHN W. DUDDINGTON
formerly Professor of New Testament, St. Aidan's
Theological College, Birkenhead, England
ALL ARE WELCOME
The only obligation is frank and full inspection and
discussion of Christian thought.
