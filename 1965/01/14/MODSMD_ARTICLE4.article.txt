# Registration At Wintertime High
## 
### 
The highest registration for
winter quarter in the history of
the University was reported by
Registrar Harvey Hall.
He put the figure at 10,452 stu
dents, an increase of 241 over the
same period last year. Of the cur
rent total, 7,99;"> are men, 2,4.77 are
women.
Terminal graduate registration
those who will conclude their
Stanford schooling in March
was 361 students, itself an in
crease over last year's 339.
Dr. Hall's figures included both
graduate and undergraduate stu
dents.
