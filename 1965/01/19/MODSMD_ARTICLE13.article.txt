# Freshman Wins Basketball Contest
## 
###

man, has won a round-trip ticket
between San Francisco and Los
Angeles in the first Stanford
Daily-Southwest Airlines Basket
ball Contest of the season.
Kerns correctly named the win
ners of eight of the 10 contest
games to win the ticket.
Eight students finished in a
tie lor second place with seven
correct picks. They were Craig
Simpson, Louis Holdeman, Wil
liam Delaney, Christine Hoover,
Chuck Taubman, Sal Bizzarro,
Ellen Mantaliea and Bill Kireher.
