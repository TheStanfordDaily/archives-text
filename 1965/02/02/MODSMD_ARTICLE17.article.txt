# Photo Panels On Hoover Tower
## 
### 
Starting next week small black-and-white panels will be displayed
briefly from time to time on the south side of the observation deck
on Hoover Tower.
The four-by-six-foot panels are being used to test high resolution
photograpic equipment being developed by Vidya Division of Itek
Corporation. The company is under Defense Department contract.
Its laboratories are located south of the tower.
