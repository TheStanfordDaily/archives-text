# Paintings Now On Display
## 
### 
Florence Moore Hall is currently exhibiting in its dining halls
paintings and drawings by Lynn Bostick, who recently received a
master's degree in painting from the Stanford Art Department.
Miss Bostick, who graduated with honors from the University
of Kentucky in 1962 where she studied with Frederic Thursz and
Raymond Barnhart, won a scholarship to study painting at Stanford
in 1962-1963 and worked here with Keith Boyle.
