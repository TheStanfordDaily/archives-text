# Sharpshooters Win First Match
## 
### 
Stanford's Varsity Rifle team
won its first match of the season
last Wednesday, downing Santa
Clara, 1,386 to 1,368.
Leading the sharpshooters was
Greg Hopwood, who fired a 283,
high score of the match. Next
was Francie Morris, at 282. She
was followed by Bill McElroy
(277), Morgan White (275), and
Dave Jefferson i269>.
Next match for the team is
Saturday, when Stanford hosts an
invitational match for teams as
far away as Reno, Nevada.
