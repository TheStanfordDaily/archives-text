WRESTLING
Here are the wrestling rules for the tourna
ment to be held this week and next week.:
1. All competitors must make their weight on
the night of their first match.
2. A competitor must make weight for each
match; no weight, allowance lor advance
ment to the next round is allowed.
3. Failure to show up on time or make weight
is a forfeit.
4. A team having a member forfeit a match
will be penalized three points from its
grand total.
5. Competition will be Mon-Thurs. evenings,
7-9 p.m. It may he necessary to schedule
matches from 9-10 p.m.
6. Wrestling period will be 1, 2, and 3
minutes for each match.
7. Competitors are asked to report 15 minutes
prior to their scheduled match time.
8. Wrestlers and team managers are respon
sible for knowing the date and time of
each match.
