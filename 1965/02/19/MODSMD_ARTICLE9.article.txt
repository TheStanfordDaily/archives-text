# No Daily Monday
## 
### 
In honor of George Washing
lon's 233 rd birthday, the Daily
will not make, its usual appear
ance on Monday. Barring unfore
seen circumstances, the "Pen
insula's only morning newspaper"
will resume publication on Febru
ary 23.
