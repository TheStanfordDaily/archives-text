# MURALS
## 
### 
GYMNASTICS
Intramural gymnastics will be held March
2, 3, & 4 at 7:30 p.m. Signups will be taken
on the first night of competition at 7:30 p.m.
Unit teams will be 5 men. There is no limit
to the number of events per man. The seven
events are:
Free exercise
Side horse
Trampoline
Horizontal bar
Side horse vault
Parallel bars
Rings
WRESTLING
Today's matches are posted on the intra-
mural bulletin board.
Wrestling finals will be held l Tues., Mar. 2,
at 7:30 p.m.
BASKETBALL
The basketball final tournament will start
the second week of spring quarter.
BILLIARDS
7:00 Winner Alpha Biz - KA vs.
Loser Theta Chi - Donner
8:00 Winner El Capitan - DKE vs.
Loser Alpha Delt-Alpha Sig.
9:00 Winner Alpha Delt - Alpha Sig vs.
Winner Theta Chi - Donner.
