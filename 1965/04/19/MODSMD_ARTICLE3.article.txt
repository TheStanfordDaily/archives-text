# Sanford To Be Guest at Wilbur
## 
### 
Terry Sanford, former governor
of North Carolina, will be guest-in
residence at Wilbur Hall for throe
days beginning Wednsday, April
21.
Sanford, recently named to
head a privately supported study
of the relationship between Fed
eral and state governments, will
give a public address on "State
Government Leadership" at 7:30
p.m. Wednesday in Dinkclspiel
Auditorium.
Sanford will also speak in the
large lounge of Tresidder Memo
rial Union at 4:15 p.m. Friday on
race relations: "The Many Souths
in Transition." He will visit pub
lie administration and history
courses for classroom participa
tion, and meet with student semi
nars in Wilbur.
