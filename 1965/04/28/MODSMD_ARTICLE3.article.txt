# Jackson Elected Newman Director
## 
### 
Tom Peterson, President of the
Stan 112 or d University Newman
Club, announced at the club's
weekly meeting, last Monday, that
Jerry Jackson had been elected
San Francisco Regional Director
for the National Newman Club
Federation, at the Central Pacific-
Province Convention held last
week-end in Modesto.
Jackson has been active in New
man activities for over a year,
and led the Stanford delegation to
the convention. He is the first
Stanford student to hold a Pro
vincial office.
