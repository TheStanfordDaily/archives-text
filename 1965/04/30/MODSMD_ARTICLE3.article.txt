# Constitutional Amendment
## 
### 
An amendment to the ASSU
Constitution will be oil the bal
lot in Monday's election. Article
111, Section (i presently reads:
"The members of the student
legislature shall elect a speaker
from among their number at the
beginning of theaeademieyear."
The words "at the beginning
of the academic year." are to
be struck if the constitutional
amendment is approved. If the
amendment passes, the legisla
ture's by-laws will determine
when the speaker is elected.
