# Marshall, Rhodes Scholarships
## 
### 
Professor Gordon Latta, who
will be the campus adviser for the
Marshall, Rhodes and Churchill
Scholarships for the coming year
will hold a meeting on Wednes
day evening at 7:30 in Tresidder
Memorial Union, Room 271, for
all students who are interested in
applying for these scholarships
for the academic year 1966-67.
The Rhodes Scholarship pro
gram, for men only, awards 32
scholarships yearly for two years
of study at Oxford, in any field
that can profitably be studied
there. Students must be, and re
main, unmarried for the tenure
of the scholarship. The awards
are made regionally, four scholar
ships from each of eight regions,
and application is made in the
region either of legal residence
or where the applicant has com
pleted at least two years of col
lege. Applicants must be between
18 and 25 years of age.
The Marshall Scholarships,
established by the English Gov
ernment in 1953, are tenable in
any university in England, Scot
land, or Northern Ireland, for
two years of graduate study in
any field. Both men and women
are eligible for Marshall Scholar
ships, and married students may
qualify. also. Awards are made
regionally, four scholarships in
each of five regions and four at
large.
The Churchill Scholarship pro
gram is for graduate study in the
field of science at Churchill Col
lege, Cambridge University. The
scholarships arc restricted to
male students between 18 and 25
who have attained at least third
year standings in a recognized
degree-granting college. The per

iod of study will be one or two
years with possibility of an ex
tension.
