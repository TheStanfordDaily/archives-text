# Preregistration Begins Today

###

day for all students. Advisors

and study lists signed last
week in order to prcregister
at these times:
S-Z today, a.m.
A-D today, p.m.

Freshmen and sophomores

list card or thereafter.
Study lists may be filed in
the Registrar's office any
