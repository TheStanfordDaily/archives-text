# Library Sale
## 
### 
The Main Library will hold a
sale this Monday, July 26, lasting
all day. Old books on all sub
jects will be on sale.
Until 11 a.m., all books will
sell for fifty cents. From 11 a.m.
until 2 p.m. the remaining books
may be purchased for twenty
five cents. After 2 p.m., all books
will sell for ten cents.
In past sales, many interesting
books have been found, and
those interested are advised to
come early.
