# New Director for Hillel Foundation
## 
### 
The B'nai B'rith Hillel Commis
sion has appointed Rabbi Charles
Familant to the position of Direc
tor of the B'nai B'rith Hillel Foun
dation at Stanford University and
the Counselorship at San Jose
State College.
Rabbi Familant, a native of
Philadelphia, received his B.A. in
Sociology from the University of
Pennsylvania.
He recently returned from Eng
land with his wife and fifteen
month old son to assume the re
sponsibilities of his appointment.
He will officiate at the Hillel
High Holy Day Services and in
vites all Jewish college students
to attend: Rosh Hashanah, Sep
tember 26 at 8:00 p.m., September
27 at 10:00 am.; Yom Kippur, Oct.
5 at 7:00 p.m., and Oct. 6 at 10:00
a.m., afternoon service to be an
nounced; at the Children's Audi
torium, Palo Alto Community
Center, 1305 Middlefield Road,
Palo Alto.
