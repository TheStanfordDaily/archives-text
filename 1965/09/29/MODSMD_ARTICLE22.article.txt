# MURALS
## 
### 
Intramural sign-up sheets posted in En
cina Gymnasium and Intramural managers
should register their teams as soon as it is
convenient.
All persons interested in being football
officials this fall should sign up in the En
cina Gymnasium. Pay for these officials will
be at the rate of two dollars per game.
There will be an intramural managers
meeting this coming Thursday, September 30,
in room 350 in the Encina Gymnasium. The
meeting will take place at 7:30 p.m.
