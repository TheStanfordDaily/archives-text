# Row Dates Announced
## 
### 
The Western Intercollegiate
Crew Coaches' Association re
cently announced this year's
Stanford rowing schedule. Stan
ford will host three regattas this
year on their home course in the
Redwood City Harbor, and will
participate in five regattas away
from home.
Stanford will open up the sea
son on April 9 at home in a four
way race with Oregon State,
USC, and Santa Clara. On April
16 Stanford will host Long Beach
State College. April 30 will see
the Indians traveling to Corvallis
to meet the University of Wash
ington and Oregon State. May 7
is the date of the Harbach Cup
Regatta in Los Angeles where
Stanford will meet USC and UC
LA.
