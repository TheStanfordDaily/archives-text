# Tutors Begin NSA Project
## 
### 
The Stanford NSA Tutorial
Project will begin its training of
tutors at an information meeting
today at 7:30 p.m. in Room 2,
Cubberly.
The project, which places
Stanford students in a variety
of tutorial programs in the Palo
Alto-Ravenswood area, requires
two hours per week and offers
activity credit. The meeting will
include a summary of the proj
ect and a discussion of the com
munity and educational prob
lems that the tutor will encoun
ter.
The meeting is open to stu
dents interested in tutoring.
