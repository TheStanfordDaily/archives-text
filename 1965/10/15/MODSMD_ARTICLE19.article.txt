# Native Homes Devastated
## 
### 
Last September 28, Mount
Taal in the Philippine Islands
ended a 54-year silence and shot
a giant fireball of molten lava
two miles into the sky, destroy
ing the surrounding native vil
lages.
Stanford students may aid dis
aster victims by contacting the
"Committee for Assistance to
the Taal Volcano Disaster,"
headed by Major Lester Delum
pa, U. S. Army (ret.).
The committee, which is so
liciting funds, clothes, and can
ned food, will sponsor talks by
Major Delumpa and Philippine
students in campus living
groups; contributions may be
made there.
