# Junipero Flushed
## 
### 
Certain Arroyo House men,
armed wiih the knowledge that
if you flush all the house toilets
at once, the ones on the lowest
level will overflow, entered Juni
pero House Sunday at 11:30 p.m. I
The result: six inches of water'
on the ground floor.
The halls quickly became
awash as the toilets spewed
forth great volumes of water,
and startled Freshmen looked on j
as the flood seeped under doors j
and into their rooms.
However, an army of Arroyo i
men equipped with mops and :
towels soon appeared on the i
scene and after forty-five min-;
utes of spirited janitorial work j
had the situation under control.
It may be some time, though, I
before Junipero House men and \
toilets fully recover from the;
shock. Arroyo. House may have |
its share of shock too. for com
ing soon is a plumbing bill for
an undisclosed amount.
