# Chancellor Cancels
## 
### 
Austrian Chancellor Dr. Josef Klaus
has cancelled his scheduled address
al Stanford November 3, due to an
Austrian government crisis.
His coalition government resigned
last week after failure to agree on
next year's budget. President Franz
Jonas dissolved the government at its
wish, but urged the coalition to act
in a caretaker capacity until elections
are held in the spring.
Klaus' visit was to have been an
exchange in honor of the new over
sets study center in Semmering. Aus
tria. The chancellor's speech was
scheduled for the same day as the
foreign campus dedication.
He was also to have addressed the
United Nations.
