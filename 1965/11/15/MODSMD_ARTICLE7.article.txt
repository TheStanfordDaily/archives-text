# Department Offers Graduate Awards
## 
### 
The Stanford University Depart
ment of Communication is now receiv
ing applications for graduate scholar
ships for the 1966-67 academic year.
The awards carry stipends from $1,575
to $4,575.
Approximately 25 scholarships
more than double the number last
year are available for students pre
paring for careers in editorial .jour
nalism, mass communications rc
search, and broadcasting and film.
The increased number of awards is
due to a $975,000 grant to the depart
ment made by the Ford Foundation.
Part of this grant will provide 12
National Honors Fellowships for A.M.
candidates who demonstrate a strong
interest in newspaper and broadcast
journalism.
