# Schoolers Romp
## 
### 

500 students, and a million (conserva
tive estimate) pro-Paly lligli girls and
their brothers and sisters, danced to
the music of tlie Trolls at last Fri
day's dance at the KA house. High
and dry on the asphalt parking lot,
after 8 days of rain, there was no
threat of further torrents and gol
oushes wore not necessary.
