# Pennel, Ryun Set Records
## 
### 
In the San Francisco Exam
iner Track and Field Invitational
Saturday night, Stanford was
able to place four of its fifteen
entrees. The meet was highlight
ed by the record breaking per
formance of Kansas freshman
Jim Ryun in the mile and vet
eran John Pennel in the pole
vault.

The best Stanford finish was
Terry Frederickson's second
place finish in the 440. The mile
relay team composed of Dell
Martin, Mike Hughes, Jack
O'Brian, and Frederick son
placed third. The other Indian
points came from Roger Cox in
the 60 yard dash and Bud Walsh
in the 60 yard hurdles.
