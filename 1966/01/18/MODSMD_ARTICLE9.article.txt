# Sociology Prof To Talk Tonight
## 
### 
Amitai Etzioni, a sociologist who
specializes in international relations
and world peace, will talk on "The
Combination of Scholarship and So
cial Action" tonight at 7:45 p.m. in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium. The lecture
is part of the Tuesday Evening Se
ries.
Professor Etzioni, a fellow at the
Center for Advanced Study in the Be
havioral Sciences this year, is asso
ciate professor in the Department of
Sociology at Columbia University and
a research associate in Columbia's In
stitute of War and Peace Studies.
He was educated at The Hebrew
University and won his doctorate in
sociology at the University of Califor
nia at Berkeley in 1958. Since that
time he has been a faculty member at
Columbia.
