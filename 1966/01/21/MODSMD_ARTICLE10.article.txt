### 
Saad Raheem To Speak

Saad Raheem, graduate student in Communications and former
president of the National Union of Students and the United Nations
Student Association of Kenya, will be the third speaker in the "Y"
luncheon series "Student Action around the World." Raheem, who
organized the Pan-African Student Congress in Nairobi and partici
pated in international student conferences in Eastern Europe, will
give a short talk and then lead a discussion on the student move
ment in Kenya at noon, Friday at the "Y" lounge, third floor Wom
en's Clubhouse. Lunch is 50 cents or bring your own.
