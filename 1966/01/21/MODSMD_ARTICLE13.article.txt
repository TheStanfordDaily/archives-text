# Memorial Service
## 
### 
A memorial service for Dr.
Arthur W. Mayer, who died yes
terday at the age of 92, will be
held at 3:30 this afternoon in
Memorial Church. The Reverend
R. M. Minto will conduct the
service.
Dr. Meyer was professor of
anatomy at Stanford 28 years
and was head of the department
before his retirement in 1938.
