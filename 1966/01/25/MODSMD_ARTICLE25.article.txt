# WEEK'S SCHEDULE
## 
### 
STANFORD UNIVERSITY —
January 24—Stanford's red-hot
basketballers have a pair of
tough road games on tap this
weekend when they travel into
Arizona to face the University
of Arizona at Tucson, Friday
night, and Arizona State Uni
versity at Tempe, Saturday
night. The home schedule shows
contests in Wrestling, Rugby,
Swimming, and Gymnastics.
The complete schedule:
Thursday, January 27
•WRESTLING — Stanford v.
University of Santa Clara, Stan
ford Pavilion, 8:00 p. m.
Friday, January 28
BASKETBALL — Stanford v.
University of Arizona, Tucson,
Arizona, 8:00 p.m.
Saturday, January 29
BASKETBALL — Stanford v.
Arizona State University, Tem
pe, Arizona, 8:00 p.m. Stanford
Frosh v. University of Santa
Clara Frosh! San Jose, 6:00 p.m.
GYMNASTICS — Stanford v.
Sacramento State College, Stan
ford Pavilion, 7:30 p.m.
RUGBY — Stanford v. San
Francisco Olympic Club, Stan
ford Rugby Field, 1:30 p.m.
Stanford Jr. Varsity v. Highland
Rugby Club, Stanford Rugby
Field, 3:30 p. m.
SWIMMING — Stanford v.
Santa Clara Swim Club, Encina
Pools, 2:00 p.m. Stanford Frosh
v. Santa Clara Swim Club, En
cina Pools, 2:00 p. m.
♦Denotes event for which ad
mission of $1.00 adults and 50c
students and children is charged.
