# AT WILBUR MEALS
## Associate Plan 'Success'

Most Wilbur student associ
ates, who eat lunch or dinner
with the freshmen men, feel the
associate program has been a
success this year.
The associates, chosen by a
committee of sponsors on the
basis of interest and awareness,
are intended to be a laison be
tween the freshmen and upper
classmen.
