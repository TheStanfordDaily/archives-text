# Coed Aids In Rescue
## 
### 
Stanford co-ed Ellen Holmes was
credited yesterday for helping police
clear 10 persons from a two-story
house threatened by fire.
Miss Holmes said she saw smoke
spilling out of the window of her
apartment as she drove up to the
building at 454 Hamilton Ave., Palo
Alto, around midnight.
Fire Batallion Chief Charles Chal
lenger said that blaze, which was con
fined to Miss Holmes' apartment,
could have involved the entire wood
en structure in a matter of minutes.
Damage, estimated at $500, was con
fined to a couch and floor and wall
areas.
