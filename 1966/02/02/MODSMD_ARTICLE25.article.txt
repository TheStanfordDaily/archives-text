# LETTER POLICY
## 
### 
The Daily Is happy to accept
and print letters to the editor. As
a general rule, unsigned letters
will not be printed, but names
will be; withheld under certain
circumstances upon request.
It is helpful if letters are type
written on 65-space lines and dou
ble spaced. They should not ex
ceed 300 words in length.
The Daily reserves the right
to edit all letters for grammar
and also to fit available space.
