# Campus News Briefs
## 
### 
Novak To Speak At Newman Center
The Christian Family Movement is sponsoring a workshop this
Saturday afternoon from 1-5 at the Newman center, St. Albert's
parish, 1093 Channing, Palo Alto on the theology of marriage. Pro
fessor Michacl Novak of the Humanities Department will be the
principal speaker.
Violin And Harpischord Recital
A recital of sonata music will be presented by Winifred Roberts,
violin, and Geraint Jones, harpsichord, at 8:30 tonight at Dinkel
spiel Auditorium. Tickets are available at Tresidder box office.
Rabbi To Give Sunday Service
Rabbi Sanford E. Rosen of the Peninsula Temple Beth El, San
Mateo, will be the guest preacher at the 11:00 a.m. Service in the
Memorial Church Sunday. Rabbi Rosen comes to Stanford under
the auspices of the Jewish Chautauqua Society, a national organiza
tion which promotes better understanding of Jews and Judaism
through education.
The Memorial Church Choir, under the direction of Professor
Harold Schmidt, will sing selections from Ernest Bloch's Sacred
Service with the Cantor parts sung by Edward Shapero. Student
members of the B'nai B'rith Hillel Foundation will read the lessons.
