# CSM To Run Vietnam Talk
## 
### 
Four Stanford instructors will
be among those participating in
a Symposium on Vietnam to be
held today at the College of San
Mateo from 11 a. m. to 4 p. m.
Those four are Dr. Christian
Bay, Dr. H. Bruce Franklin, Dr.
Kenneth Mills, and David Ran
som. Franklin and Mills will
speak in the morning, and Ran
som and Bay will speak in the
afternoon. Other speakers in
clude William Mandel, author of
several books on the Soviet
Union, and Felix Greene, com
mentator and filmmaker. Dr.
Rudolph Lapp will be modera
tor.
