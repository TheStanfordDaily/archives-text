# Netmen Romp- Whitewash SFS
## 
### 
Stanford's Varsity netmen
blanked San Francisco State
6-0, this Tuesday. The match,
the Indian's second one thus far
this season, lasted until 10:00 in
the evening before it was called
in because of rain.
The outmanned SF State
squad managed to pull in only
one set, by a fairly small mar
gin. Otherwise, they never even
came close to winning a match.
Volleying victoriously for Stan
ford were Alan Loeb, who won
his sets 6-1 and 6-0; Rich Reed,
winning at 6-2 and 6-1; Mike
Befler, 6-2, 6-1; John McCabe,
6-0, 6-3; Jim Beste, 9-7, 6-1; and
Craig Baise, 6-0, 3-6, 6-1.
Stanford was also ahead in all
three doubles matches when the
meet was rained out.
