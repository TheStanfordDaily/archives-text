# 'Night' Continues Extra Showings
## 
### 
Two additional performances of
the Shakespearean comedy "Twelfth
Night" have been scheduled for Fri
day and Saturday of next week.
The performances will begin at 8:30
p. m. in the large lounge of Tresidder.
Tickets for the shows go on sale to
day.
The extra performances were added
after all the regular shows and an
additional performance set for Sun
day were sold out.
