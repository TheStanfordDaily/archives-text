# Tangent Sets Performances
## 
### 
The Illegitimate Theatre at
the Tangent is presenting a va
riety of performances every
Thursday through Sunday
nights.
Dramatic readings from Salin
ger, Philip Roth, and others,
read by Philip Schultz, and Val
erie School will be featured in
a Thursday series, and Friday
and Saturday evening perform
ances are devoted to improvisa
tional theatre, or instant thea
tre, plus guest folk singers.
This Sunday only, a folk-rock
party is offered free to Stafford
students. All entertainers (folk,
blues, etc.) will be welcome to
participate.
The Thursday, Friday and
Saturday performances will
start at 9 p. m. The Sunday eve
ning show will begin at 7:30
p. m.
