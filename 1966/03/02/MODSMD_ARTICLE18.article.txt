# Medical Center To Exhibit Art
## 
### 
The Palo Alto-Stanford Hos
pital Center will sponsor its
third annual art show March 13
through 22.
The show will feature work
by Hospital and Stanford medi
cal school employees, medical
staffs, students, and volunteers.
The public is invited to visit the
show which will be in the Hospi
tal Core Building and Stanford
Pavilion waiting areas on the
first and second floors.
Paintings, sculpture, photo
graphs, mosaics and prints will
be included in the display. Rib
bons will be awarded based on
the popular vote of viewers.
