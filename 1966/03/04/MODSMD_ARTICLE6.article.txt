# Wolfe Will Talk About Leninism
## 
### 
Prof. Bertram D. Wolfe of Stanford
University's Hoover Institution will
on "Lenin's Sovereign Remedy for
Economic Difficulties" at a luncheon
meeting in the Campus Center of
Foothill College tomorrow.
Wolfe, noted historian of Soviet
Russia and best-selling author of
"Three Who Made a Revolution," is
a senior research associate at the
Institution. His work there includes
contnuing research and writing on
Lenin, who seized power in the Rus
sian revolution of October 1917.
