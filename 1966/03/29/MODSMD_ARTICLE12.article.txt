# British Historian Joins Faculty
## 
### 
Professor Francis L. Carsten
joins the Stanford faculty this
quarter as Kratter Professor of
History, the History Department
announced yesterday.
Carsten, who is Masaryk Pro
fessor of Central European His
tory at the University of Lon
don, has also taught at Oxford
University and is a widely
known authority in his field.
His works include Origins of
Prussia, Princes and Parlia
ments in Germany from the Fif
teenth to the Eighteenth Cen
tury, and The Ascendency of
France. He also served as editor
of the fifth volume of the New
Cambridge Modem History.
