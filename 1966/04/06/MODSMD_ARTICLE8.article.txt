# Art Lecture By Ackerman
## 
### 
Gerald Ackerman, a member of the
Art Department faculty, will speak
on "Manot and Gerome," at 8 p.m.
in Room phlOO, Physics Lecture Hall.
Ackerman will be speaking at the
second lecture of a six part series
sponsored by the Department of Art
and Architecture and the Committee
for Art at Stanford.
He has done special work on man
nerist art theory, publishing several
articles on the subject. He is also in
terested in 19th century painting, es
pecially the work of Jean Leon Ger
ome.
He is exploring the little understood
problem of the relationships between
painters of the academy and those of
the avant-garde during the latter half
of the century.
