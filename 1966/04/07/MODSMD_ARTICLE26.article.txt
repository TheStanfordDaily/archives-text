# Co-ed Team Competition In Volleyball
## 
### 
An all campus co-ed volleyball
tournament will be sponsored
by the Women's Recreation As
sociation this quarter.
Teams may be organized be
tween men's and women's living
groups or independently, with
three men and three women on
each team. Partners will be
found where needed.
Each team will play weekly
at a time and place agreed upon
by the team captains. Players
are to officiate their own games
according to official men's
rules.
Entry blanks are available at
the Men's and Women's Gyms
and have been sent to the living
groups. Entries must be re
turned by tomorrow, April 8, to
the Women's Gym.
The draw will be posted in
both gym locker rooms on April
11.
