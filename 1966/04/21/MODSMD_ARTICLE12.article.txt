# Row Open House For Frosh Girls
## 
### 
All eight Row houses will be
welcoming freshmen at open
houses this afternoon from 2 to
5.
The open houses are being
held to acquaint freshmen with
the living facilities before they
make their choices of residence
in the forthcoming drawing.
