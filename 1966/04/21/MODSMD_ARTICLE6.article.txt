# 'Quad' Has New Editor
## 
### 
Pat Newport has been elected Editor
of the 1967 Stanford Quad, according
to Joellyn Kapp, present editor.
From Menlo Park, she served as
Layout Editor of the 1966 Quad. She

will assume office as editor on May
25, the day set for distribution of this
year's Qwul.
Miss Newport will be the third con
secutive female editor, but only the
sixth altogether since 1898.


PAT NEWPORT
