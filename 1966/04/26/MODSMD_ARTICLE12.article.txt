Art Gallery Open Special Hours
The African Sculpture Exhibition currently being held at the Art
Gallery will have special hours today and tomorrow. The gallery
will be open until 6 p.m. and from 9 to 10 p.m. on both days.
Last Day To Buy Quad
Today is the last day students and staff may reserve a copy of the
QUAD. Tables will be at the post office and engineering corner all
day, and price is $8. Distribution is May 26.
Prof To Talk On Aesthetic Education
Elizabeth Mary Wilkinson, Professor of German Literature at
University College, University of London, will give a lecture on
"Whole Man or Citizen? Differentiation or Alienation?" tonight at
8:30 p.m. in Tresidder Room 270.
She will deal with Schiller's doctrine of aesthetic education in the
context of theories of culture and society from Rousseau and Her
der to Coleridge and John Stuart Mill.
