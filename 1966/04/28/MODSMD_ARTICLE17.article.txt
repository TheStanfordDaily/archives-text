# Indians Bow To Spartan Netmen
## 
### 
San Jose State's powerful ten
nis squad humbled Stanford's
visiting netmen yesterday,
2V2, for its second win over the
Cards this year. Jamie Carroll
took a 6-2, 6-2 decision in the
sixth singles slot for Stanford's
only singles point, while Rick
Reed and Denny Law picked up
a point for the Cards in the dou
bles.
Carroll and Craig Baise
picked up one-half point in the
second doubles match after split
ting sets with Contreras and
Bob Murio. The third set was
not played as it did not affect
the outcome.
