# Orchestra To Return
## 
### 
The Oakland Symphony Or
chestra will perform in a return
engagement to Stanford Sunday,
May 1, at 3:30 p.m. in Dinkel
spiel Auditorium.
The orchestra, conducted by
Gerhard Samuel, will provide a
free concert of music by modern
American composers, among
them Richard Hoffmann, visit
ing professor of music at the
University of California and
Richard Feliciano, San Francis
co composer.
The concert is supported by a
$17,000 Rockefeller Foundation
grant to the Oakland Symphony.
