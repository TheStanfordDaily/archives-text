# Writing Center Hosts Bellow
## 
### 
Saul Bellow, author of "Hcrzog,"
will spend three days on the Stanford
campus starting today as a guest of
the Creative Writing Center.
He will deliver a public lecture on
"Fond and Wayward Thoughts About
Fiction" at 7:30 p.m. Wednesday in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Bellow, a graduate of Northwest
ern University, is now professor of
English at the University of Chicago.
He is also the author of "Henderson
the Rain King." "The Adventures of
Augie March," "The Victim," and
' The Dangling Man."
Bellow has taught at the University
of Minnesota and served as a Creative
Writing Fellow at Princeton.
