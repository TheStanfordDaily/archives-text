# KQED Slate
## 
### 
On May 8, 9, and 10, KQED
will broadcast a convocation on
"The University in America",
presented under the auspices of
The Center for the Study of
Democratic Institutions.
The three-day dialogue will be
carried on in Beverly Hills, Cali
fornia, beginning Sunday eve
ning. Channel 9 will present live
the opening night session from
8-9:30 p.m. The highlights of
Monday's and Tuesday's ses
sions will be taped and shown
on KQED those evenings at
7:30 p.m.
