# Wert Discloses Seminar Plan
## 
### 
Students and faculty at Stan
ford now can arrange special
seminars on topics of mutual
interest for academic credit,
Undergraduate Dean Robert J.
Wert disclosed recently.
Designed to encourage more
flexibility in the curriculum, the
special seminars will carry up
to two hours of academic credit,
with grades.
Proposed seminars must be
approved by the Committee on
Undergraduate Education
(CUE), then listed as under
graduate special courses in the
time schedule.
Seminar size will be deter
mined by the professor.
