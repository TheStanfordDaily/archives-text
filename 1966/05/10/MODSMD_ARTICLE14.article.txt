# Alums Choose New Officers
## 
### 
Robert M. Golden, San Diego
builder in civic affairs, was
elected president of the Stan
ford Alumni Association for
1966-67 here Saturday.
He succeeds Lewis Fenton,
Monterey attorney, as head of
the 27,000 member association.
Stanford has the highest level
of voluntary participation in its
alumni organization of any ma
jor university.
Other officers elected by the
Alumni Association's Executive
Board were Crawford Cooley of
Atherton, first ice-president,
and Mrs. Richard M. Blois of
Menlo Park, second vice-presi
dent.
