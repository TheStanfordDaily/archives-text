SPEECHES
REV. FREDERICK C. COPLESTON,
S.J.: Wednesday, 8:15 p.m. TMU Small
Lounge. ECC sponsors a lecture on "Ber
trand Russell as a Humanist" by the Rev.
Copleston, Dean of Faculty at Heythrop
College, England. Refreshments and in
formal discussion following.
EAST ASIA SEMINAR: Wednesday,
4 p.m. refreshments, 4:30 p.m. lecture,
TMU Rm. 274. George H. Kerr, former
Research Associate at Hoover Institution,
will speak on "Formosa: A Frontier Is

May 18, 7:30 p.m., Dinkelspiel. Dr. Ruel,
consultant for the book. Sex and the Col
lege Student, will talk on "Changing View
point Toward Sex", the last in a series of
speeches on student behavior.

Minister at the British Embassy in Wash
ington, D.C. and Past Director of the Batik
of England will speak at 4:15 p.m. in the
Small Lounge on "The United Kingdom
Economic Situation" and at 7:30 p.m. in
the Largie Lounge on "Current Problems of
International Finance."
