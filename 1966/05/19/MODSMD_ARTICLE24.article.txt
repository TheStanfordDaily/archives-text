# Three Named To N. State Cricket
## 
### 
Three members of Stanford's
cricket club have been named to
the Northern California Cricket
Team, which will meet the All-
Southern Cal squad on Memorial
Day weekend at Tilden Park,
Berkeley.
Mark Feldman, captain of
Stanford's side, Mike O'Meara
and Clifford Severn, who played
for the United States last year
against Canada, will be in the
match for the second year in a
row.
Stanford's squad, sporting a
6-0 record, is leading the league.
They will meet Cal Davis this
Sunday at 1:00 p.m. on the I-M
field.
