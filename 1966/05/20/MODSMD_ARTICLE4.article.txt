# Paul Goodman To Open Series
## 
### 
The Stanford Committee for a Free
University is sponsoring a series of
symposia today and tomorrow on
"The New Student: Pot and/or Poli
tics."
The series is designed to "create a
symposium on the matters which are
agitating today's students." According
to a spokesman of the Free Univer
sity, the topics to be covered reflect
student concern as indicated by the
election of Dave Harris as ASSU pres
ident.
The featured event of the program
is a speech today at 4:15 in Memorial
Auditorium by Paul Goodman, of San
Francisco State College. His speech is
entitled "Radical Education in a Time
of Crisis."
