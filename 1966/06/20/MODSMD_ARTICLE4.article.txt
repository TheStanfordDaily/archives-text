# 2 students Die near Carmel
## 
### 
Two Stanford students
were killed and two injured
when their car plunged over
a thousand foot embank
ment near Carmel on June 8.
Killed were David M.
Kauffman, 19, of Millbrae,
and Frederick W. Walker
111, 19, of Hinsdale, Illinois.
Injured were Imogen M.
Dean, 19, of Vancouver, 8.C.,
and Mary E. McEnerney, 19,
of Elmhurst, Illinois. All
four had just completed
their sophomore year.
