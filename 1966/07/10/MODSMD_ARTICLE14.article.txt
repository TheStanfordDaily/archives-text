# Summer Queen Judging
## 
### 
Within a few weeks, the Sum
mer Weekly staff will elect
the 1966 Summer Queen. The
Queen's picture will be print
ed in the last edition, tradi
tionally known as the Queen's
Journal.
If you are female, and are
relatively attractive, write to
The Stanford Summer Weekly,
C/O Stanford University, re
questing that you be considered
in the judging. The final decision
will be made on the basis of
beauty, friendliness, loyalty,
trustworthiness, bravery, and
a good body.
If you don't feel like writing,
call us at 321-2300, ext. 4632
and apply.
