# The 						Weekly
## 
### 
The Stanford Summer Weekly
is published by the Associated
Students of Stanford University
once a week during summer
quarter, with the exception of
finals week. If it isn't printed
in The Weekly, it either didn't
happen or was ruled confidenti
al by the administration.

Benton
