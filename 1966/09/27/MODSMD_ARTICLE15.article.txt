# ATHLETIC MEETINGS
## 
### 
CREW
Upperclass varsity candidates
and inexperienced upperclass
men meet this afternoon at the
Stanford Stadium at 5:15 p.m.
for a short meeting.
WRESTLING
Varsity and prospective fresh
man wrestlers meet Wed., Sept.
28, at 4:30 p.m., in the wrestling
room of Encina Gym.
BASKETBALL
Frosh basketball candidates
meet Wed., Sept. 28, at 4:30 p.m.
in Room 50 of the BAC.
Varsity basketball players
meet Thurs., Sept. 29, at 4:30
p.m. in Room 50 of the BAC.
FROSH WATER POLO
Freshmen interested in trying
out for water polo should meet
at the Encina Pool at 4:00 p.m.
this afternoon or contact Coach
Jimmie Smith.
FOOTBALL MANAGERS
Freshman and upperclass stu
dents interested in trying out for
football manager should contact
Bob Butler at the BAC as soon
as possible.
INTRAMURAL OFFICIALS
Students interested in officiat
ing intramural sports ($2 per
game) this Fall should contact
Director Gary Ness at ext. 4270
or drop by the IM office, Room
354, Encina Gym.
Openings are also available
for office assistants interested
in sports management.
