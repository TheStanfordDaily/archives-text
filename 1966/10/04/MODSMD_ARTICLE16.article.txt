# Campus GOP To Hear Talk
## 
### 
Paul Bruce Smith, Republican
candidate for the State Board of
Equalization, will speak to the
Stanford Young Republicans at
4:15 in Tresidder Room 270 to
day.
Smith, 29, plans to discuss the
function of the Board as well as
problems involved for a younger
man running for political office.
He is challenging Democrat
George Reilly, who has held the
present office for 28 years.
The State Board of Equaliza
tion raises revenue for the state
and disburses funds. The pres
ent Board has six members, all
Democrats, and is chaired by the
State Controller, Alan Cranston.
The first district, for which
Smith is running, extends 300
miles from San Francisco to the
Santa Barbara County line.
