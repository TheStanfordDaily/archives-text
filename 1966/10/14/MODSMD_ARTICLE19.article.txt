# Funeral Services Held For M. C. Leventritt, '99
## 
### 
Funeral services for Morti
mer C. Leventritt, 90, a mem
ber of the Stanford Class of '99,
were held yesterday in the Home
of Peace Cemetery, San Fran
cisco.
Leventritt was a connoisseur of

Oriental art and gathered more
than 300 pieces of the
objects of the Far East. He gave
his collection to the University
in 1941, and it is now on display
in the Stanford Museum.
