# Axe Comm Rally To Feature Kegs
## 
### 
A keg rally for the USC game will
kick-off at 3 p.m. today at Searsville
Lake.
Sponsored by Axe Comm, the rally
will feature various coed contests
among the living groups, with the
overall winner receiving a 16 gallon
keg.
Another keg will go to the group
that comes up with the most original
cheer for the game.
A committee of cheerleaders, pom
pon girls, and Axe Comm representa
tives will judge all entries.
The Six-Packs, a rock 'n' roll
band, will provide music at the rally.
Tickets for the rally are on sale for
75 cents near the Post Office.
