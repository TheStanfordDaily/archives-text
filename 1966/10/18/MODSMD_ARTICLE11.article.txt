# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
Dave Harris To Speak At [-Center
Dave Harris, ASSU President, will speak at the Current Events
Coffee Hour at 4:30 p.m. today at the I-Center. His talk will be con
cerned with the student's relation to the university. Coffee
and cookies will be served. The event is open to the public.
Vietnam Election Critic Scheduled
Marshall Windmiller, Associate Professor of International Rela
tions at San Francisco State College, will speak tonight at 8 p.m.
in room 214 on the recent constituient assembly elections in Viet
nam. Windmiller, who was in Vietnam during the elections, has
stated in "The Sunday Rampants" that the reported 80 per cent turn
out of voters is not a true indication of Dolitical conditions.
