# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
Experiment Sets Planning Session
The Experiment will hold its first organizational meeting Wednes
day at 4:15 p.m. in Room 270 of Tresidder Union to discuss present
activities and to consider future ones.
Members of The Experiment who are interested in participation
beyond seminars are especially encouraged to attend. The meeting
will be short.
Air Force To Seek OTS Candidates
The U.S. Air Force Officer Selection Team will visit Stanford
October 25 and 26 in the Placement Service Office, from 9 a.m. to
4:30 p.m. The selection team will be here primarily to seek qualified
men and women to attend Officer Training School, a three-month
course leading to a commission and possible technical training in
the Air Force.
Berlin, Beirut Scholarships Offered
An informal presentation of the year-long student exchanges to
the Free University of Berlin and the American University of Beirut
will be given this evening at 7:30 in the Small Lounge of
Tresidder Union. The German and Lebanese exchange students cur
rently at Stanford will discuss student life at their universities;
Stanford returnees will relate their experiences abroad.
The exchanges are full scholarship programs open to any in
terested Stanford student, undergraduate or graduate. Application
information will be provided.
Grad Record Exam To Be Held Here
Contrary to information printed regarding scheduling of centers
in the G.R.E. Bulletin, Stanford will serve as a testing center for
the national administration of the Graduate Record Examination
on December 17.
The deadline for the exam is November 29; applications must be
received by the Educational Testing Service in Berkeley by then.
Seniors Present Law Panel Today
The Senior Class will present the Law School Admissions Panel
at 4 p.m. today in the Tresidder Large Lounge. Dean Keough of
the Stanford Law School, and representatives from Boalt Hall
(Berkeley), Hastings, USC, UCLA, and the University of Chicago
law schools will discuss such matters as admissions policies and
curriculum, and will answer questions about their schools.
