# Cards Tie For Sailing Crown
## 
### 
Captain Kim Desenberg led
the Stanford Sailing team to an
upset over favored USC at Long
Beach last weekend. The In
dians sailors tied with California
State at Long Beach for first
place.
The Stanford team lost only
its first race with Cal State. Also
racing in the Douglas Cup com
petition were teams from the
U.S. Naval Academy, Tulane,
and Western Washington State
College.
