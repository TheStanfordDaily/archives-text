# Golf Tourney Play Begins
## 
### 
Stanford's All-University Golf
Tournament started yesterday,
on the Stanford Golf Course.
Golf Coach Bud Finger
stressed that the tournament is
open to graduate students and
faculty as well as undergrad
uates.
There is one qualifying round
of eighteen holes which can be
played any time between now
and November 5. To enter, con
testants have only to stop by
the pro shop and announce the
day they wish to play the qual
ifying round.
When the qualifying rounds
have been completed, the play
ers with the sixteen lowest
scores will begin match play to
determine the university cham
pion.
There is no entry fee for the
tournament.
