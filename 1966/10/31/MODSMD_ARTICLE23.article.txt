# 'Shoe' Fits Through
## 
### 
Daily photo by Charles Kite
Stanford halfback Bill Shoemaker bursts through
a huge hole in the Washington defensive line.
End Tom Weingartner (88) and flanker Bob Blunt
(29) block on the play, while Husky defensive back
A 1 Worley (12) closes in to make the stop. Shoe
maker carried 16 times for a 5.25 average and
helped set up the final Card touchdown with a 12
yard run to the two.
