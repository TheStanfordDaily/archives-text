# Red Hot Prof
## 
### 
At the end of the first day's voting
in the Red Hot Prof contest Charlie
Beye, formerly of the Classics De
partment, held a 150-vote margin
over B. Davie Napier, Dean of the
Chapel.
Other leaders were Verdel Kolve
(English), Albert Hastorf (Psychol
ogy), and Arthur Schauiow (Phys
ics).
The winner of the contest will
lead a half-time cheer at Saturday's
homecoming game against Air
Force.
Votes are a penny each with pro
ceeds going to the International
Student's Fund for schools in Asia
and Africa. The contest continues
through Thursday.
