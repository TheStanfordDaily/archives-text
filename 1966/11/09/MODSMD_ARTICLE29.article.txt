# 'Stratagem' To Open
## 
### 
The undergraduate production
of George Farquhar's "The
Beaux's Strategem" opens to
night at 8:30 p.m. at the Nitery
Theater in the old student union
building.
This all-student presentation
will play through Sunday. Tick
ets are on sale at the Memorial
Auditorium box office. They will
also be available at the door.
William Sharp, associate pro
fessor of drama who directed
last season's Stanford Repertory
Theater production of "All's Well
That Ends Well" directs this res
toration comedy.
