# Cadets Conquer Berkeley Bears
## 
### 
Army fullback Chuck Jarvis
ran six yards around right end
in the first period to give the
Cadets a 6-3 victory over the
University of California at
Berkeley Saturday. Army totaled
19 first downs to the Bears
7, 323 yards to the Bears' 157.
Bear end Jerry Bradley drop
ped a sure touchdown pass in
the second period, but later set a
school record for yards gained
on passes. Ron Miller kicked a
44 yard field goal for the Bears
