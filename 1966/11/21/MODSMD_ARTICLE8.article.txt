# Students Act As Advisors
## 
### 
During Winter Quarter pre
registration, the ASSU commit
tee on departmental advising
will begin a student advising pro
gram in the Economics, Political
Science, History, German, and
English Departments. Several
students from each department
will hold office hours this week
to enable students who are con
sidering majors in one of these
fields to become better acquaint
ed with the professors and areas
of study open to them.
The student advisors are pre
pared to discuss courses which
are required by or related to a
specific major, honors or special
programs, summer jobs, course
sequence or any other individual
problems.
A student may contact the de
partment he is intersted in for
informaction concerning the
specific advisors and when and
where they will be available.
