# Overseas Deadline Thursday
## 
### 
The deadline for submitting
applications for the forthcoming
sessions at Stanford's Overseas
Campuses is 5 p.m. this Thurs
day, December 1.
Selections will be made at the
end of this quarter for the fol
lowing groups: Stanford in Aus
tria V, Stanford in France XV,
and Stanford in Italy XV, all
September 1967 -March 1968;

Stanford in Britain IV, and Stan
ford in Germany XIX, June - De
cember 1967.
Application cards and course
lists for all the above sessions
may be obtained in the Overseas
Campuses Office, Room 1-C, In
ner Quad. No applications for
these sessions will be accepted
after December 1.
