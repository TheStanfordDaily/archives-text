# LETTER POLICY
## 
### 
The deadline for letters to
the editor Is noon the day be
fore publication. The Dally
attempts to print letters as
quickly as possible; however,
there Is no guarantee that a
letter will be published Im
mediately.
Letters should be typed on
a 65-space line and should not
exceed 300 words. All letters
will be subject to editing.
Anonymous letters will not
be accepted; however, under
certain conditions names will
be withheld upon request
