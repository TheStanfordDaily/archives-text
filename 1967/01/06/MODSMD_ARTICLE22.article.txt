# World Record Holders Open Fire Today
## 
### 

Greg Buckingham broke Dick Roth's 200 meter
individual medley world record last summer (It's
now 2:12.6). In addition he holds the national
record for 500 freestyle (4:41.1) and 400 yards IM
(4:08.3). Buckingham will concentrate on Individ
ual medley and distance freestyle this season.

Luis Nicolao holds the world record for 100 meters
butterfly (57.0). Nicolao will be after national rec
ords for 100 and 200 yards butterfly this year, and
will also work in the freestyle sprints. Dick Both,
Olympic gold medalist in the 400 meters IM, com
pletes the trio of Stanford world record holders.
