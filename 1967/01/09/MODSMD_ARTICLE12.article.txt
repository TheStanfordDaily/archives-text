# Free University Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
The Free University of Palo
Alto will hold an orientation
meeting for the Winter Quarter
today at 8 p.m. The meeting will
be held at 1765 Fulton in Palo
Alto so instructors and other in
terested persons can discuss
courses.

Those unable to attend this
meeting may register for
courses at Kepler's Bookstore
in Menlo Park from 8 p.m., Fri
day, January 6, through Satur
day, January 7. Those interested
in acting or technical work
should contact Jack Smock at
367-3669 during business hours.
