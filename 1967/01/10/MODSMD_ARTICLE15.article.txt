# Guest Lecturer Dies in Italy
## 
### 
Gori Montaneili, guest lecturer
in architecture at Stanford-in-
Italy, died last December 23 of
cancer. Dr. Montaneili had been
undergoing a series of opera
tions this fall.
He was a favorite of many
Italy groups over the last few
years, lecturing on architecture
and accompanying the groups on
field trips. Group XI made him
an honorary member of their
group.
