# Kimber Offers Music Prize
## 
### 
Fifty year graduate of Stan
ford John E. Kimber is offering
a $5000 first prize and gold medal
for the winner of the 15th an
nual Kimber Award in Instru
mental music in competition to
be hosted by Stanford's music de
partment this spring.
The competition is open to
violinists, violists, and cellists
who are both residents of Cali
fornia and not over 25 years
old. The contest will be held in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium May 6
and 7.
