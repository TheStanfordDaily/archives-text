# Overseas Dates Set
## 
### 
Applications for forthcoming
sessions at Stanford's overseas
campuses are now available and
will be accepted beginning Mon
day, January 16.
Selections will be made at the
end of the current quarter for
Austria VI, France XVI, and
Italy XVI, all running from
March through September, 1968,
and for Britain V and Germany
XX, runnirg from January
through June, 1968.
The deadline for applications
is Thursday, March 9. Lists of
courses to be taught at the var
ious campuses as well as appli
cations are available at the Over
seas Campus Office.
