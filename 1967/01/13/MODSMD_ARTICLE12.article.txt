# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
Chiefs' Fans Invited To Watch Game
All people from the greater Kansas City area are invited to a
BYO Wolfpack meeting to watch the Chiefs meet Green Bay in the
Superbowl this Sunday at 12:30 p.m at Nick Davis' house, 2931
Louis Road, Palo Alto. Call 321-9641 for information.
I-Club Presents Australia Program
Saturday the International Club and the International Center will
present an "Evening Abroad in Australia and New Zealand," pro
duced by students from those countries. The program, which will
include films, songs and skits, begins at 8 p.m. and will be followed
by refreshments.
Top Indian Film To Be Shown
The Stanford Indian Association will show a Hindi film "Aasman
Mailed," which has won a President of India Award, Saturday at 8
p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium. Admission is 50 cents. There are
no subtitles but a short synopsis of the film will be distributed.
