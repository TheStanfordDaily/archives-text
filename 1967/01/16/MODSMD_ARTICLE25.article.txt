# Gymnasts Win 2; Wrestlers Lose
## 
### 
Stanford's gymnastics team,
off to its best start in years, beat
Chico State 139.15 to 122.60 and
Cal Poly 139.15 to 90.10 for a
season record of 3-0.
Sophomore Jim Petersen took
first in the long horse and ac
cumulated 42.65 points in all
around routine to lead the In
dians. Rich Guess earned 23.70
points for the Tribe.
Stanford wrestlers did not fare
so well at the hands of San
Jose State, losing most of the
lower weights. Len Borchers and
Burt Ambler were the few
bright spots.
Further details In Tuesday's
Dally.
