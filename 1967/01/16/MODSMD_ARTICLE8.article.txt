# Campus Roundup

Hoover Tower To Reopen To Public
Hoover Tower, landmark of Stanford and the mid-Peninsula, and
location of the famous Hoover War Library, will re-open for public
visitors today.
The tower will be open from 10 a.m. to noon and 1 to 4 p.m. week
days; 10 to noon and 1 to 4:45 p.m. Saturdays; and noon to 4:45 p.m.
Sundays.
It has been closed since 1965, pending installation of protective
grill work.
Openings Remain In Creative Writing
The Creative Writing Center has announced that there still sev
eral openings left in the English 133 class meeting Mondays and
Wednesdays at 2:15 p.m. Those interested in signing up for the class
are requested to come to the Creative Writing Office, Room 51C.
Winter Hours Set For Women's Pool
Open hours for the women's pool, located next to the women's
gym, have been set for Winter Quarter. They are as follows: Mon
day 3-4 p.m.; Tuesday 2-3 p.m.; Wednesday 34 p.m.; Thursday 2-3
p.m.; Friday 1-2, 2-3 p.m.; and Saturday and Sunday 1-3 p.m.
The pool is open Monday through Friday for women students
only, and on Saturday and Sunday for women students and their
college-age or older guests.
