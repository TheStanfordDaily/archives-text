# Sterling To Talk To Mothers' Club
## 
### 
President Sterling will address the
Stanford Mothers' Club with his an
nual report on University activities
this afternoon at 1:30 p.m. in the audi
torium of the Graduate School of
Business.
Sterling will report on student
activities, building programs, and the
curriculum. The meeting is open to
the public.
The Mothers' Club is composed of
mothers of present and former stu
dents and others interested in the
University's future.
