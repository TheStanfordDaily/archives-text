Hurley To Speak On Heart-Surgery
Heart surgery will be the subject of a public lecture to be given
tonight at 8 p.m. in room MlO6 of the Medical School Building.
Dr. Edward J. Hurley, assistant professor of surgery, will pre
sent the lecture, one in a series of lectures entitled Progress in
Medicine.
Dr. Shaull To Discuss Latin America
Dr. Richard Shaull, Professor of Ecumenics at Princeton Theolog
ical Seminary, will conduct an informal discussion of "The Christian
Left and Recent Developments in Latin America," at 8:15 p.m. to
night in the small lounge of Tresidder Union.
