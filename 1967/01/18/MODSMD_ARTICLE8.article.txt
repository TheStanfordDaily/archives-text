# Gay Opening
## 
### 

"The Beggar's Opera," Stanford Repertory Theater's latest production, opens
tonight at Memorial Auditorium. John Gay's musical drama Is directed by
John Wright as his master's project and stars Paul E. Richards as Macheath,
an 18th century highwayman. Another MA candidate, Isaiah Jackson, was in
charge of Music Department graduate students reconstructing the original
score. The modern "Threepenny Opera" is baned on this play.
