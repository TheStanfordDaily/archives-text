# Ex-Editor Ends VISTA Training
## 
### 
A former Editor of The Stan
ford Daily, William J. Moore,
has graduated from a VISTA
Training Program in Cayey,
Puerto Rico. Moore will spend
one year working with the Mon
serrate Community Center in
Puerto Rico.
Moore attended Claremont
Men's College for a year and
then transferred to Stanford
University where he received his
B.A. in communications and po
litical science in 1966. Besides
his work on The Daily, Mooro
served as a correspondent for
the San Francisco Chronicle for
six months.
While at Stanford, Moore was
the holder of a Bush-Copley Fel
lowship and winner of three
Hearst Gold Medal awards in
collegiate journalism.
