# Daily Banquet
## 
### 
Thou shalt know that the semi-an
nual Daily banquet will be held Fri
day, January 27, at Ming's in Palo
Alto. Thou shalt call the Daily office
by Monday, January 23, and tell the
Lord thy Staff Editor whether thou
wilst come or not.
The banquet, to honor thy outgoing
and thy incoming editorial boards, be
gins with Cocktails at 6:30 p.m., Din
ner at 7:30 p.m., and will cause thee
to part with four dollars of the realm.
