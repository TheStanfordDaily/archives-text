# BOXING
## 
### 
Stanford's boxing team, after
a 5-3 win over the Navy in their
season opener two weeks ago,
travels to meet Cal tonight at 8.
Tom Jenks, defending league
champion, Jim Marlar, and
heavyweight Eric DeGooyer
pack the heavy hands for the
Indians.
Dave Oswald, John Price, and
Barney Adler round out what
coach Ray Lunny hopes to be
one of the Tribe's strongest box
ing squads in recent years.
