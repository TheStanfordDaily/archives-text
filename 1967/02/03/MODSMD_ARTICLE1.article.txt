# Milky Way
## 
### 

The Bishop's Company will present Karl Wittlinger's "Do You Know the
Milky Way" Sunday evening: in Memorial Church. The performance will be
gin at 7:30 p.m. and will be followed by a discussion with the cast in the
Round Room- The play, a tragi-comedy, is the story of a returning war vet
eran who attempts to find his true identity. He explores the questions of
"Who Is man?" and "Is he a child of God." The performance Ls sponsored
by the Dean of the Chapel and the Stanford United Ministry. There will be
no admission charge.
