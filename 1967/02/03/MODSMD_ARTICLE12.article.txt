Music Students To Perform Sunday
The Department of Music will present a free concert of chamber
music at 3:30 p.m. Sunday in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
The Stanford Wind Ensemble under the direction of Dr. Arthur
Barnes will perform "Concert Music for Wind Orchestra" by Paul
Hindemith. Pianist Patricia Caballero will present "Five Piano
Pieces, Op. 23" by Arnold Schoenberg. Debussy's "Premiere Rhap
sodie," will be performed by Arthur Tollefson, piano, and Ann Owen,
clarinet.
