# Film Profit To Aid Art
## 
### 
Bernardo Bertolucci's film "Be
fore the Revolution" will be
shown Friday at 8 p.m. in Cub
berley Auditorium. Proceeds will
be sent to Florence, Italy to help
restore art monuments damaged
in the recent flood.
The show is sponsored by the
art and architecture department.
Tickets—s2 for adults and $1 for
students — may be purchased
through the mail until tomorrow
by sending a stamped, self-ad
dressed envelope to the Com
mittee of Art at the Stanford
Museum. They will also be sold
at the door.
