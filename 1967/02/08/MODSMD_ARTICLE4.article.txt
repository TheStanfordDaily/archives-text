# No Conclusion On Student Members To Packer Comm
## 
### 
The Committee of 15 yesterday
came to no conclusions on the prob
lem of student selection for Vice-Pro
vost Herbert Packer's committee to
review Stanford education.
Professor Philip Rhinelander, chair
man of the committee, reported that
after a long period of discussion, the
group reached no conclusions. He
added that he expected discussions to
continue at the next meeting of the
committee.
Rhinelander also said that a pro
posal was brought up at the meeting
and voted down but that he could
not disclose the nature of the pro
posal because "we can only publicize
actions."
