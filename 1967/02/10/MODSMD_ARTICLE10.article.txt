# Committee To Perform
## 
### 
Friday, February 17, at 4:15
p.m. in Dinkelspiel Auditorium,
Interact Club will present the
first in a series of programs
aimed at raising scholarship
money for Ravenswood High
School in East Palo Alto.
Co-chairmen Gene Washing
ton and Ron Miller have an
nounced that the first program
will feature the "funnier than
school" Committee from San
Francisco. The Committee,
which recently changed its en
tire show, will present a full
length program beginning at
4:15 p.m. Tickets are on sale at
Tresidder ticket office. Donation
to the program is $2.
