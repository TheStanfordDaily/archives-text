# Open Houses Begin
## 
### 
The first of the annual fraternity
open houses is being held tonight
from 7:15 to 9:45 p.m. During this
time all the fraternities will be open
to prospective members: freshmen,
transfer students, and non-fraternity
men.
Subsequent open houses will be
held at the same time on Monday,
February 20, and Thursday, Feb
ruary 23, and from 1-5 p.m. on Sun
day afternoon, March 5. There is no
format for the events; the initiative
to visit the fraternities is left to
Individuals.
