# Art Dealer To Discuss Altoon Watercolors
## 
### 
David Stuart, Los Angeles art
dealer, will give a dealer's view
of the John Altoon show, cur
rently at the Stanford Gallery,
tonight at 8 p.m. at the Gallery.
The exhibition of watercolors
and drawings will remain at the
Gallery until February 26.
In addition to discussing the
works in the show, Stuart will

give some background of the
artist's development. The talk
will be informal, with questions
and comments from the audience
forming the basis for much of
the discussion.
The Committee for Art is spon
soring the talk. All members
of the community are welcome.
There is no admission charge.
