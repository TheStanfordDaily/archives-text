# Preregistration
## 
### 
Preregistration continues today and
tomorrow. Students who have not ob
tained their study lists by Friday will
forfeit the privilege of preregistra
tion. Freshmen must obtain their ad
visors signatures on their study lists.
Signing of class lists for juniors,
seniors, and graduates will take place
March 6 and 7. Freshmen and soph
omores will have the earliest times
at which they can sign lists stamped
on their cards.
