## Rissa White, Editor Symphony To Play All-Gershwin Show 
### 
In commemoration of the 30th
anniversary of composer George
Gershwin's death, the San Fran
cisco Symphony Association will
present an all-Gershwin concert
tomorrow at 8:30 p.m. in the
Civic Auditorium.
American conductor Henry
Lewis will direct. Soloists will
be soprano Veronica Tyler, bass
Simon Estes, and pianist Earl
Wild.
Gershwin, one of America's
most creative composers, is
famed for his "Rhapsody in
Blue," "An American in Paris,"
and the opera "Porgy and Bess."
Miss Tyler and Estes will be
accompanied in "Porgy and
Bess" by the San Jose State
College A Cappella Choir.

Swiss conductor Ulrich Meyer

8:30 and Thursday at 2 p.m. at

major; and Franck's Symphony
