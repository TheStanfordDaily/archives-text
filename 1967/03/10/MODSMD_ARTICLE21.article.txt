# L. Borchers T. Crenshaw Go To NCAA's
## 
### 
Stanford's wrestling team will
step into the national arena two
weeks from now when Captain
Len Borchers wrestling in the
152 pound division and Terry
Crenshaw in the 191 pound
class travel to the NCAA cham
pionships at Kent State College
in Ohio, March 23-25.
Both boys are sophomores and
will be facing their stiffest com
petition of the year in the tour
nament. Varsity Coach Jerry
Budzik will accompany the two
to Ohio. Their appearance in the
tournament will mark the first
time in the history of Stanford
wrestling that any Card wrestler
has represented Stanford in the
nationals.
