# 'Evidence' Opens At Rep Theater
## 
### 
The Repertory Theater's
Northern California premier of
John Osborne's "Inadmissible
Evidence" opens at 8:30 p.m.
Wednesday, April 5, in the Little
Theater.
Performances will continue
nightly, except Sundays, through
April 15, with 2:30 matinees on
Saturdays.
The play is directed by Robert
Loper, head of the Speech and
Drama Department and SRT's
producing director, and centers
on an attorney, portrayed by
Gerald Hiken, who is confronted
with the immorality of his per
onal and business conduct.
Tickets may be reserved at
the Memorial Hall box office.
