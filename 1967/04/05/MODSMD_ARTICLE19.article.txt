# Assoc. Editor Wins Hearst News Award
## 
### 
Dally Associate Editor Maggie
McComas was named February
winner in the spot news writing
competition of the William Ran
dolph Hearst Foundation's Jour
nalism Awards Program.
She received a $500 scholar
ship for her entry, an article on
the student march to Sacra
mento published in The Daily
early in February.
Miss McComas is a senior
Communication major from Elk
City, Oklahoma.
