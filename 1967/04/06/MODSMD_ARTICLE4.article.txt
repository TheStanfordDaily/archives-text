# Howard Editor Of 1968 QUAD
## 
### 
Bruce Howard has been elected Edi
tor of the 1968 Quad according to Pat
Newport, 1967 Editor. Howard won in
a close election against Dave Garvin,
a freshman, who will serve as next
year's managing editor.
A sophomore from Millbrook, New
York, Howard served on the 1966
Quad staff as a photographer and is
photo Editor for the 1967 edition.
Dann Boyd will serve Howard as Busi
ness Manager of the 1968 edition.


BRUCE HOWARD
