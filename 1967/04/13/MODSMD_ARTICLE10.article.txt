# ASSU Elections
## 
### 
Run-off elections will be held today
and tomorrow for ASSU vice-presi
dent. The two candidates are John
Trimbur and Bill Stone.
Students may vote at the post of
fice and Engineering Corner between
8:50 a.m. and 4 p.m. The results of
the election will be announced next
week.
