# Railsback Reels Back
## 
### 

Daily Photo By Dave Bitts

UCLA pole vaulter Don Railsback reels back from
the bar as he clears 17'4" in an unofficial jump at
the end of the Stanford-UCLA track meet in Los
Angeles Saturday. Railsback contributed to the
Bruins 115-28 win with a first place jump of
16'6 beating teammate Rick Sloan on fewer
misses.
