The Jake Gimbel lectures were

chology and related fields. Two
years ago the lectures were
given jointly at Stanford by

4:15 in Dinkelspiel with Margar

UCCM Minister. After-dinner

Dr. Snyder has been in his
post at M.I.T. since 1959. He is
also an instructor in psychiatry
at Harvard Medical School, and
a consultant for the National
Institute of Mental Health.
