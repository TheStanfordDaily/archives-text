# LASSU Appoints Finance Director
## 
### 
Richard Ware has been named
ASSU Student Financial Man
ager, to serve from June 1967 to
June 1968. He will replace Terry
Murphy, graduating senior who
has been Financial Manager for
the past year.
The appointment was made
by Dean of Students Joel Smith,
financial director of ASSU, and
was ratified by the Legislature.
A junior majoring in Political
Science, Ware worked as as
istant to Murphy this year. He
will soon be seeking applicants
for his assistants and other posi
tions on the LASSU finance
committee for the '67-'6B school
year.
