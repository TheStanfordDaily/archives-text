# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
The STANFORD WESTERN POLITICAL SOCIETY will hold a
panel discussion on "THE UNIVERSITY AND THE PROBLEM
OF ITS REFORM" tonight at 8:15 in the small lounge of Tresidder.
Panel members will be Charles Bell, President of the Political
Union, James Binns, President of the Senior Class, and Prof. James
T. Watkins, IV. The discussion will consider change in which order
is maintained as opposed to change where the academic routine is
disrupted.
★ ★ ★
The Stanford Med School will host Dr. Herbert C. Kelman at the
weekly research seminar in biobehavioral sciences, to be held today
from 4:15-5:30 in Room M-114. The subject for discussion is THE
HUMAN USE OF HUMAN SUBJECTS: THE PROBLEM OF DE
CEPTION IN SOCIAL RESEARCH. Dr. Kelman is Professor of
Psychology at the University of Michigan and Fellow, Center for
Advanced Study in the Behavioral Sciences at Stanford. Coffee will
be served in the Dean's Courtyard preceding the seminar at 3:45.
