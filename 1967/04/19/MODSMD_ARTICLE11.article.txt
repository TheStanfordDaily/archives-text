A meeting concerning the draw system will be held tonight in
Rohle Lounge at 7 p.m. All freshman and transfer students who
want to know about the draw are encouraged to attend. Sue Hein
berc AWS Housing Chairman, will be available for questions.
★ ★ ★
"The B«*ard", by Michael McClure, will be performed tonight in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium at 8:30. Presented under the sponsorship
of The Experiment, the play involves a confrontation between Jean
Harlow and Billy the Kid rooted in the battle of the sexes.
"Mem What?" will be open immediately after the conclusion of
the play for a discussion on the play. Professors Michael Novak
and Larry Friedlander will participate.

now on sale at the Tresidder Box Office.
