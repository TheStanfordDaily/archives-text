# Wendy G. Wonka
## 
### 
Best Foods National College Queen Contest
gives every student the opportunity to cast a vote
for the true trend of college life.
Too often American society looks at the Uni
versity as the breeding place of bearded and
sandaled trouble-makers. Yet here is an oppor
tunity to show that Stanford is not merely a place

where the Vice President is rioted against. A
vote for Miss Wendy Wonka is a vote for our
real concerns.
Miss Wonka comes from an illustrous family
that has served the University well; she deserves
the united support of the community. Clip the
ballot from the full page ad in today's Daily and
cast your vote for Wendy.
