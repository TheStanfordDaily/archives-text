# Lacrossemen Win 2 Games
## 
### 
Stanford's Lacrosse Club
boosted its season record to two
wins and one loss during a re
cent trip to Colorado with an
8-5 win over Colorado College
and a 6-4 loss to Air Force.
The team defeated Denver
University 9-2 in a scrimmage,
and a game with Colorado Uni
versity was cancelled due to
snow.
