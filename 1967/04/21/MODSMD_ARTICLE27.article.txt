# Giddy Soaring
## 
### 

Daily l'hoto by Dave Bitts
Stanford high jumper Dan Giddens soars high
over the bar in a recent track moet with UCLA
in Los Angeles. Giddens and his. high-jumping
teammate Peter Boyce, who has cleared 6'11'4"
this year, will compete against L'.S.C. Saturday
at Foothill J.C.'s all weather track.
