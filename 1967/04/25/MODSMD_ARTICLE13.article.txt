# 'Resistance' Has Third Issue
## 
### 
Tho third issue of Resistance,
The Experiment's bi - weekly
newspaper, is out today and is
available on top of The Daily
boxes around quad and in most
of the major living groups.
The eight page issue features
articles on the Upward Bound
controversy, the U.S. role in
South Africa, the high school
United Student Movement, and
essays by editors Barry Green
berg and Marc Sapir on "The
Quest for Certainty" and"The
Problems of Liberalism."
