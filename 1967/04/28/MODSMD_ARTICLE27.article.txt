# Tribe Names New Coach
## 
### 
The Stanford Athletic Depart
ment announced yesterday that
Paul R. Neumann, one of Stan
ford's greatest guards, and for
the past three years backcourt
ace of the San Francisco War
riors, will be the assistant bas
ketball coach for the Tribe next
year.
Neumann, now 29 and a resi
dent of Sunnyvale with four
children, captained the Card
team in 1959, and ranks as the
seventh best Redskin scorer. He
succeeds Bill Turner, who left
to become head coach at Port
land University.
