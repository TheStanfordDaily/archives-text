# ASSU Elections Scheduled Today
## 
### 
Elections begin today for ASSU
President and Vice President as well
as other offices. Also included, will be
two referendums: one for men only,
on class ranking, and another con
cerns the Fundamental Standard.
The polls today and tomorrow will
be open from 8:50 a.m .until 3:30
p.m. at the Post Office and Engineer
ing Corner.
There will also be a "mobile polling
place" today that will make several
stops:
8:50 a.m. to 10:30 a.m. — Law
School.
10:30 a.m. to 11 a.m. — Business
School.
12 to 1 p.m. — Medical School.
1 p.m. to 2 p.m. — Physics Tank.
2 p.m. to 3:30 p.m. — Union
