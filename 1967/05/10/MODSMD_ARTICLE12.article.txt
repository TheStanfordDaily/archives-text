# Ringer Memorial Service Tonight
## 
### 
A memorial service for Lee
Ringer will be held tonight in
Memorial Church at 8 p.m.
Dean of the Chapel B. Davie
Napier will officiate.
Ringer, a sophomore in Beta
Chi died Saturday after an
automobile accident.
The members of Beta Chi
have established a memorial in
his name at their house.
In addition, friends wishing
to make contributions have been
requested to donate them to
Volunteers in Asia (VIA) in
Ringer's name. They can be
mailed to VIA, c/o Internation
al Center, Stanford.
