# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
At 8:15 in TMU 274, the I-Center will show the Swedish film
ONE SUMMER OF HAPPINESS, directed by Arne Mathson, which
earned the Grand Prix from the Cannes Film Festival.
Coffee will be served at the I-Center following the showing of the
film.
★ ★ ★
There will be a lecture by Kingman Brewster, President of Yale
University at 4:15 in Dinkelspiel. The lecture entitled "THE ACA
DEMIC PROCESS: THE STATE" is part of the Harry Camp
Memorial Lecture Series.
★ ★ ★
The Stanford Repertory Theater will present "ONCE IN A
LIFETIME" at 8:30 in Memorial Auditorium. Admission for the
general public is $4.25, students $1.50.
★ ★ ★
LLOYD MacMURRAY, a San Francisco attorney who specializes
in civil rights of military personnel, will be a guest participant in
a forum at Junipero House. The forum will begin at 7:00 and will
deal with alternatives to accepting service in Vietnam.
