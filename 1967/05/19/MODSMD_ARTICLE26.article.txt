# Champions All
## 
### 

Daily Photo by Dave Bitts

Kappa Sigma's medley relay of Walt Poterbln,
Mike Daly, Mike Nielsen, and Wells Shoemaker
won the opening event of the intramural swim
ming championships. Kappa Sig also had three
individual champions. Kappa Alpha, with a record
setting free relay and two individual champs,
finished one point behind the Kappa Sigs.
