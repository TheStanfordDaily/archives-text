An open forum on the role of medical students in the Vietnam
war will be held at the Medical School today. The forum will be
held in Room M-106 at 8:30 p.m.
Participants will include Dr. Albert G. Clark, president of the
San Francisco Medical Society, and Dr. Halsted Holman, head of
the Department of Medicine at Stanford.
