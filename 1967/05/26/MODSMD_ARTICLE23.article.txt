Interviews for positions on ASSU JUDICIAL COUNCIL will be
held next Tuesday and Thursday evening. Applications will be avail
able at the Council Office starting today. Sign up for interviews at
the same time. Graduate students (especially in law) and under
graduates are encouraged to apply. Those with questions call Judy
Bader, 327-4491.
