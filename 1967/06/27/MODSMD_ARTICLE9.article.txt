# Wonka In Vermont
## 
### 
Warren G. Wonka, Stanford's
most famous graduate, is no
longer in Antartica, but in Mont
pelier, Vermont, as member of
the faculty of Green Mountain
College, a small, liberal arts in
stitution.

The Advisory Council of Col
lege Chemistry discovered Mr.
Wonka on their list of chemistry
teachers in two-year colleges
across the nation, according to
Council Administrative Aide
Mrs. Martha Press.
