# Entertainment Editor Chosen
## 
### 
Hie Stanford Daily is happy
to announce the selection of an
Entertainment Editor for the
rest of the Summer Quarter. She
is Barbara Deutsch, a junior,
from Skokie, Illinois.
Miss Deutsch will be ably
assisted by the efforts of Mark
and Jane Ciabattari, last sum
mer's drama reviewer and En
tertainment Editor.
An entertainment page will
appear each Friday in the Daily,
and will contain reviews, stories
and a calendar of coming events
in the Bay Area. If there is any
thing in particular in the fine
arts field which you would like
to see appear here please con
tact Miss Deutsch through the
Daily office.
