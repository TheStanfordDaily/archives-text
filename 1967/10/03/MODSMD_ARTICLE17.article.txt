# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
Jackie Henderson, an editor from MADEMOISELLE MAGAZINE,
will be on campus tomorrow to discuss the Annual Mademoiselle
College Competition at a sherry party at 5 p.m. in Hoskins 6G.
Competition awards include 20 guest editorships with Mademoiselle,
cash prizes, and publication of fiction, poetry, photography and
illustrations.

BUCKMINSTER FULLER, inventor of the geodesic dome, will
lecture tonight at 7:30 p.m. in Memorial Auditorium. Fuller is the
keynote speaker in Tresidder Union's Century 21 program. His lec
ture is Human Values in Technological Society." Fuller will con
duct an informal question and answer session about his life and
work at 7:30 p.m. Wednesday in Dinkelspiel Auditorium. Fuller
holds honorary degrees from the University of Michigan and
Washington University, St. Louis.

THE NEW AFRICAN LITERATURE AND THE ARTS. Two years
