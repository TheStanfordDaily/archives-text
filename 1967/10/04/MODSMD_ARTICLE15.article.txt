ANTI-WAR SATIRE: Kent Minault (left) as Corporal Espada, R.G. Davis as General Garcia, and
Jason Marc-Alexander as Sergeant Brighelle are three members of the San Francisco Mime Troupe
that will perform their controversial anti-war satire, "L'Amant Militaire", on Friday in Dlnkelspiel
Auditorium. The Troupe has attempted to follow the practice of the roving Renaissance actors by
performing improvised comedies in streets and playgrounds with commedia masks which identify
each character. The Troupe will give two performances of "L'Amant Militaire" on Friday at 7:30
and 10:00 p.m. Tickets are $2 and are available a t the Tresidder Box Office.
