# Foreign Study
## 
### 
George Stoney, an associate
professor of communications at
Stanford and maker of film docu
mentaries, left recently for Mu
nich with a group of 18 college
students and two professors to
study family life abroad.
They will live and study in
West Germany, India, and Japan
during the eight-month tour.
Sponsored by the International
Honors Program, the group will
keep journals as well as doing
some creative writing and docu
mentaries.
