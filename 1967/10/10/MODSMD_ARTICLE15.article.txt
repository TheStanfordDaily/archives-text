# Felcyn Appointed Jounalism Fellow
## 
### 
Keith Gabriel Felcyn, 36, man
ager of the Chicago bureau of
Business Week magazine, has
been appointed a Professional
Journalism Fellow here for the
winter and spring quarters of
the 1967-68 year.
He joins eight other newsmen
and women appointed for the
same 23-week period, beginning
in January. Thirteen other fel
lows are already at the Univer
sity.
Stanford Professional Jour
nalism Fellows are mid-career
newspapermen, news broadcast
ers, or staffers from magazines
of general interest. Those select
ed, aged between 25 and 40, may
study anything but journalism.
