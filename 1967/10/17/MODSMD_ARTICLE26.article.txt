# Eight Students Receive Sloan Scholarships
## 
### 
Eight Stanford freshmen are
among 151 winners of four-year
scholarships from the Alfred P.
Sloan Foundations.
They were selected on the ba
sis of academic excellence, per
sonal integrity and demonstrated
potential for leadership.
The winners are Raymond S.
Baxter, Donald W. Schwindt,
Charles H. Marz, Dennis Joe Cue,
David D. Thomas, Louis E. Is
las, Steven C. Getman and Ste
ven Lachowicz.
Stipends range from $200 to
$2000 a year.
