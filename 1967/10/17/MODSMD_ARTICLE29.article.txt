All returned PEACE CORPS VOLUNTEERS now at Stanford will
meet tonight at 7.45 in the International Center lounge. The volun
teers will discuss the possible organization of a campus Peace
Corps group similar to one in operation last year. An approach
to a Peace Corps Week at Stanford will be considered.

The STANFORD ALPINE CLUB is showing slide of Mount
Lucania, Mount Steele and Mount McKinley tonight at 7:30 in Room
320. The show will include pictures of a second ascent of Lucania
last summer by a new route.
Beginners as well as experienced mountaineers are welcome at
tonight's show. The club plans weekend practice climbs and a trip
to Yosemite later this quarter.
