l-CLUB POLICY BOARD ELECTIONS
Notice is hereby given that the Annual Elections for the Inter
national Club Policy Board, the governing body of the Interna
tional Club, will be held at the International Center on Friday,
October 20, 1967, from 10:00 a.m. until 6:00 p.m. Any mem
ber of the International Club, foreign or American, who is a
registered Stanford student is eligible to stand for election and
to vote.
Nominations should consist of the following:
1. The signature of the nominee
2. The signature of at least one nominator who should
also be a member of the International Club and a reg
istered Stanford student
3. The country of citizenship of the nominee.
Nominations must be lodged at the International Center before
12:00 noon on Thursday, October 19, 1967.
DAVID J. S. RUTLEDGE
President, International Club
