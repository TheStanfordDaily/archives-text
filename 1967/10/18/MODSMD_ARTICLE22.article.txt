# Women's Open Pool Hours
## 
### 
Women's open pool hours for
the fall quarter are: Monday,

11-12, 5-6; Tuesday, 1-3, 5-6;
Wednesday, 11-12, 5-6; Thursday,
1-3, 5-6; Friday, 11-12, 3-6; Satur
day and Sunday, 1-5.
Weekday hours are for Stan
ford women students only, but
the pool is open to Stanford
men accompanied by Stanford
women on Saturday and Sun
day. Faculty and staff may use
the pool Saturday and Sunday
for 25c and may not bring
guests.
Married Stanford students
may use the pool Saturday and
Sunday, but can bring no one
under college age.
