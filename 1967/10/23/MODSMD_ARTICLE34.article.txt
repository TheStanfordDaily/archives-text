# Frosh Seminar Slots Still Open
## 
### 
Applications for winter-spring
freshman seminars will close No
vember 1. "They have been open
for two weeks and the response
so far has been poor," the fresh
man seminar office announced.
Those turned down for fall
winter seminars because of lack
of space are especially encour
aged to reapply.
There have been some changes
in the seminar program since the
catalog was published. Seminar
FS 163 has been dropped, and
FS 136: "International Rela
tions" and FS 82: "The Theory
of Machines," have been added.
Students receive six units of
credit for a two-quarter seminar.
Applications are now being
taken in the freshman seminar
office, Room 111 F.
