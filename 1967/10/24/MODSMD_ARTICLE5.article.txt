# Protesters Call Rally
## 
### 
A group of the Stanford stu
dents arrested in last Monday's
sit-in at the Oakland Induction
Center announced that a rally
will be held today at noon in
White Plaza to report on their
actions to the Stanford commu
nity.
Speakers at the rally will dis
cuss the reasons for the sit-in
and their experiences in jail, and
will attempt to asses the results
of their experiences.
They will include Mary Han
son, a senior social thought ma
jor, John Shippee, political sci
ence graduate student and David
Pugh, a sophomore Phi Psi.
In addition to discussing past
actions, the rally will outline
plans for a car caravan to Santa
Rita Thursday morning to wel
come demonstrators who will be
freed that day.
