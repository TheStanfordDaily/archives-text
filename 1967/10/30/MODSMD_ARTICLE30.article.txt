# Weekend Results
## 
### 
The frosh football team con
tinued its winning ways with a
18-17 victory over the Southern
California fflosh Saturday. The
little Indians scored three touch
downs in the second half to gain
the come-from-behind win.
Soccer Fortunes
The Stanford soccer squad led
Air Force 1-0 for three quarters

on the strength of Bill Palmer's
corner kick but just ran out of
steam and fell prey to the Fal
cons 3-1.
The JV's also lost, 5-1 to Mer
ritt College; but the Frosh sal
vaged a little Stanford glory by
downing a team from Robert
Louis Stevenson School 6-0.
