★ ★ ★
DR. DAVID MERRILL, professor of art history at the University
of Washington, will speak at Stanford at 8:00 p.m. Wednesday,
Nov. 1, in the Physics Lecture Hall. His talk, "The Damon-Hawks
moor Parallel," will be concerned with Isaac Damon's relationship
to the renowned 18th century English architect, Nicholas Hawks
moor. It opens the five-program Lectures in Art series for autumn
quarter. It is open to the public without charge.

from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. in the lobby of the Knoll, behind the Florence
Moore dormitories. Records will be sold for 10 cents each all day.
Scores and sheet music will be sold at 50 cents per item from 9-11
a.m., 25 cents between 11 a.m. - 1 p.m., 10 cents between 1-3 p.m.,
and 5 cents between 3-5 p.m.
