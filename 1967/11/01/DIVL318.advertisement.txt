OUR UNIVERSITY MAN'S
JACKET, SWEATER AND SLACKS
ARE COLOR COORDINATED
We know our University Man well. And with good
reason, whai with our on-campus shops at Yale,
Princeton, Harvard, University of Michigan and Stan
ford University keeping us posted on exactly what's
happening. That's why we have made a special effort
to color coordinate our boldly plaided jackets, and
worsted twill slacks, with an exceptional group of
lurileneck lambswool sweaters. (The sweaters, of
course, are full-fashioned, and saddle shouldered!)
And everything has been made to our exacting speci
fications. Jackets, $ 55 to *80. Slacks, $22. Sweaters, $16.
