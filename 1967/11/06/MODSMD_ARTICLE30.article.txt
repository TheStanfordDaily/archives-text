# Student's Drama To Be Produced
## 
### 
"The Man Behind the Mask,"
the season's second Theater at
the Museum production, opens
Thursday, November 9, at the
Miedel Room, Stanford Museum.
It will be presented at 8:30 p.m.
nightly through November 11.
The production is the work
of Robert Morgan of Indianapo
lis, Indiana, a second-year mas
ter of fine arts student in direct
ing. The cast includes Ruth
Hunt, Sergei Tschemisch, Jenni
fer Haffele, Salley Kemp, and
Kurtwood Smith.
All seats are $1. Tickets are
available in person or by mail
at Tresidder Memorial Union
box office.
