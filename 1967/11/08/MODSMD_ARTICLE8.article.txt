# ELECTION FACTS
## 
### 
All students can vote on (1) resolution supporting "peace vigil"
and (2) election for head yell leader.
Undergraduates who do not live In a University residence hall
and are not affiliated with an eating club or fraternity may vote for
20 Legislature representatives.
Graduates In the school of humanities and sciences may vote for
25 Legislature representatives.
Polls are open today and Thursday at engineering corner and
outside the post office, 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.
