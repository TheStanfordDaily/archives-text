IM FOOTBALL SCHEDULE
Saturday, Nov. 11, 1967
The quarterfinals of the "A" league
round-robin elimination tournament are
tomorrow. The finals of the "B" league
eliminations are Tuesday on field 5 at 3:45.
Field 1: Phi Delt vs. Dislocations
Field 2: Whiteacre vs. Zetes
Field 3: SAE vs. winner of (Alpha Biz—
Junipero) game
Field 5 : Theta Delt vs. winner of
(El Cuadro-Delts) game
All games at 9:00.
Teams in parentheses played Thursday.
