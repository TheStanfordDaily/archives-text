# Stereo Stolen From Dorm
## 
### 
A $320 stereo combination was
stolen Wednesday from the Las
sen Lounge. Mike Natlock, a
member of Lassen, stated that
the equipment was taken be
tween 1:30 and 11:30 a.m.
A member of the house noticed
the combination was missing
from its shelf and reported the
loss at 11:30 a.m. yesterday. The
set consisted of an amplifier
tuner and a Gerrard turntable.
