# AMA Head To Address Alums
## 
### 
Dr. Dwight L. Wilbur, presl
dent-elect of the American Medi
cal Association, will be principal
speaker at the Stanford Medical
Alumni Association's annual
get-together tomorrow.
Dr. Wilbur will speak on
"Medicine and the Government"
during the afternoon program
to be held at the Cabana Hotel
in Palo Alto.
The new AMA president, who
completed his undergraduate
work at Stanford in 1923, is a
clinical professor of medicine at
the medical school. Dr. Wilbur
received his M.D. degree from
the University of Pennsylvania
in 1926 and an M.S. in medicine
at the University of Minnesota
in 1933. He also was a Fellow
and later a consulting physician
at the Mayo Clinic before enter
ing private practice in San Fran
cisco in 1937.
