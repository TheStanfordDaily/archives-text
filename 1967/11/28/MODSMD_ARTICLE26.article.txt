Daily photo by Ernie Holzmann
THOMAS M. STORKE, Pulitzer prize-winning publisher and editor
of the Santa Barbara News Press, visited campus yesterday and was
honored at a private dinner with University President Wallace
Sterling last night.
Storke, who was the principal donor of the Storke Student Pub
lications Building, celebrated his 91st birthday on Thanksgiving
Day. The building, completed in 1964, houses the offices of The
Stanford Daily, the Quad, Chapparal, Blotter, and Sequoia, and
Workshop.
