# Band Record
## 
### 
The Stanford Band, has re
corded Stanford University
songs—"Come Join the Band,"
"The Scalp Song," "The Car
dinals Are Waving," "Hail!
Stanford, Hail! etc. — plus
the arrangement of the
Star Spangled Banner" which
wowed the audience at the
Notre Dame-Stanford game in
1963.
Band Director Arthur
Barnes says the record, com
patible stereo, is available at
the Stanford Bookstore. The
price: $1.50.
