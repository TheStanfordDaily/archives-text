# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
This Saturday at 8 p.m. the I-Center will present a fireside concert,
featuring the GYPSY TRIO, led by Dr. Louis F. Krasno of the
Medical School. The trio will play Hungarian and Rumanian music;
refreshments will be served.
★ ★ ★
Students interested in participating in MUSIC DEPARTMENT
CHAMBER ENSEMBLES may enroll in Music 171 for one unit of
credit with the permission of the instructor, Professor Sandor Salgo.
First meeting of the class will be held at 4:15 p.m., on Monday
in the rehearsal hall in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
★ ★ ★
Dr. Glenn C. Werth, who heads the AEC's Plowshare Program,
will be the first guest lecturer today in the NUCLEAR CIVIL
ENGINEERING course given during winter quarter by Stanford
Professor Paul Kruger.
His talk is scheduled for 4:15 p.m. in Room 268, Engineering
Corner, and is open to the public.
★ ★ ★

get there, location of motels, and the schedule of events are avail
able today at the Ski Desk in the ASSU Office.
