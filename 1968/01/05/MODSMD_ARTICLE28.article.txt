# Observer Editor Gets New Post
## 
### 
Harry Press, editor of The
Stanford Observer has been
named to the additional post of
assistant director of Stanford's
professional journalism fellow
ship program.
He succeeds Julius Duscha,
former assistant to director Her
bert Brucker. Duscha has been
named director of the Washing
ton, D.C., Journalism Center.
Press, a former Nieman fel
low at Harvard, will continue as
editor of the monthly Observer,
a newspaper published by the
university news bureau.
