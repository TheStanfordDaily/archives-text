# Black Union Rally Today
## 
### 
The chairman of the Black
Panther Party for Self-Defense
will speak today at White Me
morial Plaza at noon.
Chairman Bobby Scale will
speak on behalf of Huey P. New
ton, minister of Self-Defense for
the Black Panther Party. New
ton is charged with murder in
Oakland.
Ken Washington, chairman of
the Stanford Black Union, will
speak on related topics.
