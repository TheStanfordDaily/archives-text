# Math Classes Cancelled
## 
### 
AU mathematics classes
have been cancelled today out
of respect for Prof. Charles
Loewner, Internationally
known mathematician, who
died Monday at the Stanford
Medical Center.
Funeral services for Dr.
Loewner, who was 74, will be
held at 3 this afternoon at
gTaveside In the Alta Mesa
cemetery, Palo Alto.
Dr. Loewner came to Stan
ford In 1951 and continued to
teach after his official retire
ment in 1959. Colleagues said
he probably had taught more
Stanford graduate mathema
ticians than any other profes
sor In the department He
taught a graduate seminar
last fall.
Those wishing to contribute
to a memorial scholarship
fund may do so care of the
Mathematics Department at
Stanford.
