# Sterling To Address Convocation Today
## 
### 
President J. E. Wallace
Sterling will deliver his final
convocation address to the
University today at 11 am. in
Memorial Auditorium.
All classes will be cancelled
at that hour to allow students
to attend the speech.
Sterling's address on
"Changes and Chances" Is
sponsored by Stanford Today
and Tomorrow, an alumni as
sociation, to promote student,
faculty and alumni relations.
The annual convocation has
been used in the past by Ster
ling for a kind of "State of
the University" speech. In
past convocations he has com
mented extensively on growth,
change and reform at Stan
ford.
