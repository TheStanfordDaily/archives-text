# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
CHARLES DREKMEIER, professor of political science, will join
the director and members of the cast of Candaules, Commissioner
tonight for a post-performance discussion with the audience at the
Nitery. Curtain time for the Stanford Repertory Theater production
of this new anti-war play is 8 p.m. Tickets are available at TMU
Box Office.
★ ★ ★
SENIOR HAPPY HOUR is scheduled for 9 p.m. to midnight at
the Rat's in Menlo Park tonight. The place will be closed to the
general public, and seniors attending will enjoy discounts. Class
Vice President Jere Horowitz said the happy hour is "kicking off
the senior activities for this quarter."
★ ★ ★

music, dancing. The evening begins at 8 p.m. at the I-Center; re
freshments will be served.
