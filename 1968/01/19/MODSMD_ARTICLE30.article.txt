Applications for 1968-69 are now avail
able in the Financial Aids Office, Student
Services Building, Room 214. All students
who wish to apply for renewal of their
scholarships for next year, as well as
those not presently holding awards, must
file their applications by May 1. The
deadline for those students who are going
overseas spring and summer is Feb. 1.

on the Bulletin Board at the right of the
Transcripts Window in the lobby of the
Office of the Registrar, Old Union. New
transfer students do not need to petition
since their credit has already been al
lowed.
