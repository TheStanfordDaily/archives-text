# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
Wednesday, Jan 31, is the deadline for filling CANDIDACY AP
PLICATIONS for June A.M., M.S., and Engineer Degrees, and for
September Ph.D Degrees. Applications must be signed by major
departments before being submitted to Graduate Study Office, Room
128, Old Union Building.
★ ★ ★
The Hillel Foundation is sponsoring the showing of THE LIFE
OF EMILE ZOLA with Paul Muni, Joseph Schildkraut, and Lois
Alhern, relating the defense of Alfred Dreyfus, and a short docu
mentary, "The Price of Silence," concerning the plight of Soviet
Jews, Sunday, Jan. 21, 8 p.m., Cubberley Auditorium. General Ad
mission $1.00, Students 75c, Hillel members 50c.
MUSIC AT Tire MUSEUM SERIES, sponsored by the Art De
partment, will present a chamber music conert Sunday at 8 p.m.
in the Miedel Room of the Stanford Art Museum. Admission is free.
Music by Bach, Handel, Kodaly, and Stanford graduate students
Marty Bresnick and Ira Mendlowitz will be performed.
★ ★ ★
The STANFORD INDIA ASSOCIATION will present the Mindi
movie Jhanak Jhanak Payal Baaje at 8 p.m. on Saturday in Cub
berley Auditorium. Admission for members and students is $1,
$1.50 for non-members.
★ ★ ★
PROF. ABDUL HAMID ALGAR of the University of California
(Berkeley) will present a talk on "The Place of Mysticism in Islam"
tonight in Tresidder small lounge at 7:30 p.m. The talk is sponsored
by the Stanford Islamic Society.
★ ★ ★
The Arnold Air Society PLAYBOY CONTEST will begin at noon
on Wednesday, Jan. 24 in White Plaza and continue until Friday,
Jan. 26. Nominations will be 50 cents each and votes one cent. The
winner will be awarded a free evening at the San Francisco Playboy
Club.

September Ph.D Degrees. Applications must be signed by major

128, Old Union Building.
