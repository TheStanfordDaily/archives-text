# VP Nominations Now Open, Rules Meeting To Be Held
## 
### 
Candidates petitions for the
ASSU vice presidential election
must he submitted to the ASSU
elections commissioner by 4:30
p.m. Friday. The election will be
held Feb. 14 and 15.
The vice presidency has been
vacant since Cesare Massarenti
succeeded Peter Lyman to the
presidency. Lyman resigned that
post in November.

Students wishing to run for
vice president may pick up
blank petitions at the ASSU of
fice, upstairs in Tresidder Un
ion.
An information meeting for
all candidates will take place
Jan. 29 at a time and place to
be announced. All candidates
will be required to attend that
meeting.
