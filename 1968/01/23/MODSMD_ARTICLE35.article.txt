# Extend ROTC Deadline
## 
### 
The deadline for applications
to the Stanford Army ROTC
twoyear program has been ex
tended to Feb. 9, according to
Maj. Toraki Fukushima.
Fukushima points out that
the Army ROTC requores two
years active duty after com
missioning, unlike the Navy
and Air Force programs,
which require four years' ser
vice as an officer.
