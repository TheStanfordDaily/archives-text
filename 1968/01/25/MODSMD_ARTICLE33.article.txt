"The Place of FRATERNITY SELECTION in the University" will
be discussed in the Delta Chi evening series tonight at 7.
Dan Williams, assistant dean of students for fraternities and
eating clubs, and SES Residence Committee members Dean B.
Davie Napier, Sidney Verba, Ed Scoles, Dave Pugh (last year's out
standing freshman), and Rick Levin will participate.
Carter Newton, Inter-Fraternity Council rush chairman, John
Trimbur, last year's ASSU Housing Commission chairman, and
English professor David Levin will also join the discussion on the
merits of selective rush.
The program, to be held at Delta Chi, 586 Mayfield, is open to the
public.
