# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
THE STANFORD CONVERSATION GROUP is sponsoring a
lecture on the proposed north Cascades National Park tonight at
7:30 in room 370, outer Quad. Rowland Tabor of the U.S. Geological
Survey will speak and show color slides of the areas being con
sidered for the park.
★ ★ ★
The FRESHMAN FILM SERIES will show "Casablanca," a 1942
drama starring Humphrey Bogart, Ingrid Bergman, Paul Hen
reid, Claude Rains, Conrad Veidt, Sydney Greenstreeti Peter Lorre
and S. Z. Sakall tonight at 8 and 10 in Room 320, Geology corner.
★ ★ ★
The Catholic Graduates Club of Palo Alto has planned a WEEK
END SKI TRIP TO HEAVENLY VALLEY on February 9 11. The
cost, $21.00 per person includes round trip charter bus transpor
tation, two nights motel lodging, lift discounts at Heavenly, and
daily shuttles to ski area. Reservation forms may be obtained from
Dick Wieckowicz, 151 Calderon No. 281, Mountain View. Forms and
full payment are due at the above address by Feb. 1.
