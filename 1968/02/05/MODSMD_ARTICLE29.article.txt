# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
USHERS are needed for "The Roar of the Greasepaint — The
Smell of the Crowd," Feb. 14-17, and"The Maids," Feb. 22-24. Sign
up lists will be posted on the door of the Ram's Head office, located
In the loft of the ASSU office in Tresidder Union.
★ ★ ★
"M," a movie staring Peter Lorre, will be shown in the Physics
Tank on Feb. 5 at 7:30 p.m. It is open to the university community
with no admission charge. It is sponsored by Gordon Craig, pro
fessor of German history.
★ ★ ★
Today is the final day for LEGAL REGISTRATION OF VE
HICLES for operation in California during 1968. Thereafter penal
ties for delinquent registration will be imposed. Offices of the Dept.
of Motor Vehicles as well as bank stations, which will only accept
pre punched statement cards, will accommodate last minute crowds
today. Applicants may also mail their forms and fees. There will
be no penalty if the envelopes are postmarked on or before Feb. 5.
