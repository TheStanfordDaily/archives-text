# Century 21 Speaker
## 
### 

COMPUTERS IN EDUCATION will be the topic of Professor Pat
rick Suppes' Century 21 lecture in the Tresidder Lounge at 7:30
tonight. Supper, chairman of the Department of Philosophy, believes
that in spite of certain problems of course programming and In
spiring students to learn, the individualized Instruction of com
puters can be "another significant step toward realizing the full
learning potential of our children."
