BSCE, Duke, has had
a taste of structural
drafting and engineering,
shop operations, and
steel erection
since joining Bethlehem's
1961 Loop Course.

in the electric transmission
tower shop at our
Leetsdale, Pa., Works,
where he helps solve
a variety of production
and engineering problems.
