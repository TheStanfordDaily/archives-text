SALVATORE GUILIANO, TMU s scheduled
Friday film, has regretfully been cancelled. In
its place will be William Wyler's THE COL
LECTOR, with Samantha Eggar and Terence
Stamp. Tickets may be purchased for 50c at the
TMU box office and at the door.
★ ★ ★
The deadline for submitting petitions to Dean
Hind's office for SPRING QUARTER SEMI
NARS is February 14. Anyone interested in or
ganizing a seminar based on the Century 21
series should contact Libby Stevenson in the
Tresidder Union Board Office for information as
soon as possible.

The Sierra Club Film GLEN CANYON will be
shown today at 4:15 and 7:00 p.m. in Tresidder
room 270. Admission is free.
★ ★ ★

formances of THE ROAR OF THE GREASE
PAINT — THE SMELL OF THE CROWD, in

will be sold for $2 at the TMU Box Office.

dinner at the Coatepeque restaurant and Murry
Schiskol's LUV at the Playhouse on Friday,
February 9. A discussion will follow led by a
Stanford graduate in drama. Tickets will be sold
at the I-Center.

Jean Renoir's LES BAS-FONDS (The Lower
Depths), starring Louis Jouvet and Jean Gabin,

274.
