speak to pre-medical students on "MEDICAL SCHOOL APPLICA
TION," tonight and Wednesday at 8:15 in Tresidder room 275.
"At
Stanford McCarthy-for-President will sponsor a REGISTRAR
CLASS on Feb. 27th, 7:30 p.m., TMU 271. Anyone interested should

and registered or able to register in this country.

In preparation for Cesar Pelli's speech this Wednesday, the CEN
TURY 21 FILM SERIES has scheduled a special showing of the
CBS film "Cities of the Future." It will be shown tonight at 7:00
p.m. in Tresidder Room 270. Dr. Clark Reynolds of the Food Re
search Institute will lead a discussion following the film.
★ ★ ★
The UNION BOARD Is conducting interviews Monday and Tues
day of this week for membership on the Union Board as well as
for the positions of Program division chairman and Cultural Arts
division. Interested persons are urged to sign up for appointments
at the Tresidder information desk.
DR. GUSTAF HILLESTROM head of the Drottningholm Theater
Museum in Sweden will lecture and show a color film on the Drott
ningholm Theater at 4:15 in TMU Room 270. A portion of Gluck's
"Iphigenia in Aulis" with Elisabeth Soderstrom is included in the
film.

SCHOLARSHIPS in the amount of $350 each for the 1968-69 aca
demic year. An applicant or his parents must have been born in
Hawaii, be an undergraduate with an average of 2.0 and be a full
time student. Applicants are asked to call or write David Akina,
751 Budd Avenue, Campbell, California 95008, or Gilbert Wong,
1023 Craig Drive, San Jose, 95129. Deadline for all required mater
ial and applications will be April 30, 1968.

film.

The Hawaiian Civic Club of the Bay Area is offering TWO
SCHOLARSHIPS in the amount of $350 each for the 1968-69 aca
demic year. An applicant or his parents must have been born in
Hawaii, be an undergraduate with an average of 2.0 and be a full
time student. Applicants are asked to call or write David Akina,
751 Budd Avenue, Campbell, California 95008, or Gilbert Wong,
1023 Craig Drive, San Jose, 95129. Deadline for all required mater
ial and applications will be April 30, 1968.
