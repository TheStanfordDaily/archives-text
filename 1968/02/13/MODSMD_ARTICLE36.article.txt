# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
Cedro House will sponsor a panel discussion tonight at 7:00 p.m.
on WHAT'S WRONG WITH THE GENERAL STUDIES PRO
GRAM? in the Cedro lounge, Wilbur Hall. Panel members include:
Prof. F. W. Strothmann, chairman of the Modern European Lan
guages; Prof. Patrick Suppes, chairman of the Philosophy depart
ment; Physics Prof. Walter Meyerhom; Associate Prof, of Hu
manities Edwin Good; Italian Instructor Bruce Merry; Mike
Menke, student member of SES; and moderator Dean Robert Hind,
staff director of SES.
★ ★ ★
THE FRESHMAN FILM SERIES will present tonight Freaks,
a 1932 horror film starring Wallace Ford, Leila Hyams, and Olga
Baclanova, and I'm No Angel, a 1933 comedy starring Mae West,
Cary Grant, Edward Arnold, and Ralf Harolde. The former will be
shown at 8 p.m. and 10:30 p.m., the latter at 9 p.m. and 11:30 p.m.
In Geology room 320. Admission is free. ■
★ ★ ★

topic "America, a Racist Society?" today at 4:15 p.m. at the I-
Center. Providing introductory remarks will be Harry Cleaver from
Committee X, Harry Hukari from Young Conservatives, and Ken
Washington from the Black Student Union.

In Urban Problems."

The program will include selections from"The Mikado," Handel's
"Jeptha," and American and German folk songs.
Cedro House will sponsor a panel discussion tonight at 7:00 p.m.
on WHAT'S WRONG WITH THE GENERAL STUDIES PRO
GRAM? in the Cedro lounge, Wilbur Hall. Panel members include:
Prof. F. W. Strothmann, chairman of the Modern European Lan
guages; Prof. Patrick Suppes, chairman of the Philosophy depart
ment; Physics Prof. Walter Meyerhom; Associate Prof, of Hu
manities Edwin Good; Italian Instructor Bruce Merry; Mike
Menke, student member of SES; and moderator Dean Robert Hind,
staff director of SES.

a 1932 horror film starring Wallace Ford, Leila Hyams, and Olga
Baclanova, and I'm No Angel, a 1933 comedy starring Mae West,
Cary Grant, Edward Arnold, and Ralf Harolde. The former will be
shown at 8 p.m. and 10:30 p.m., the latter at 9 p.m. and 11:30 p.m.
In Geology room 320. Admission is free. ■
