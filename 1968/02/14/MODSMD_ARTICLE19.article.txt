# Harris Gets Postponement
## 
### 
The arraignment of David
Harris, former Stanford student
body president, on charges of
refusing to report for draft in
duction, has been postponed un
til March.
Harris had been scheduled to
appear yesterday before U.S.
District Court Judge Alfonso J.
Zirpoli in San Francisco. The
date was postponed at the re
quest of Harris.
Harris, 22, the organizer of a
national antidraft organization,
The Resistance, was indicted
by a federal grand jury in San
Francisco Jan. 31 for refusing
induction at the Armed Forces
Induction Center in Oakland.
