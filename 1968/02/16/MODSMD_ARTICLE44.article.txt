# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
STOKELEY CARMICHAEL will make his first speaking ap
perance since his return to the United States tomorrow, February
17, at 8:00 p.m. at the Oakland Auditorium Arena at Twelfth and
Fallon Streets in Oakland. Student tickets are $2.00 each. A mixed
audience is expected.
★ ★ ★
The .Row Houses will sponsor a WINE TASTING PARTY this
afternoon from 4:00 to 6:00 p.m. to raise money for a school in
Hong Kong. Wine and hors d'oeuvres will be served at all houses,
except Lathrop. Contributions entitle the giver to progress from
one house to another.
★ ★ ★
The award-winning film, THE WAR GAME, produced by the
8.8.C. will be shown in Cubberley on Saturday night, hourly from
6:00 to 11:00 p.m. and on Sunday, hourly from 3:00 to 11:00 p.m.
The movie, too realistic for television release, depicts a nuclear
attack on Britain. The admission is $1.50 general and $1.00 for
students.
