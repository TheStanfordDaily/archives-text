FINANCIAL AIDS: The Northern
California Section of the Institute of
Food Technologists is inviting applications
for the Wm. V, Cruess Undergraduate
Scholarship for the 1968-69 academic year.
To be eligible, a student must be a full
time undergraduate (seniors are eligible if
they plan to do graduate work) enrolled
in a bona fide college or university situ
ated within the geographical boundaries of
the Northern California Section of I.F T.,
be enrolled in a department of Food Sci
ence, Food Technology or clasely allied
field, and intend to continue his studies in
food science. The scholarship is $500 at a
minimum hut generally amounts to more
than $1,000. Applications should be made
by letter to the Chairman of the Cruess
Award Committee, Mr. Bernard Feinberg
at 800 Buchanan Street, Albany, Califor
nia 94710, by April 1.
