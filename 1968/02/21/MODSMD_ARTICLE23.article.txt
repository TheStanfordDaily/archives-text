# Quaker To Talk
## 
### 
Douglas Steere, one of the
best known Quaker leaders of
the present time, will speak to
night at 8 p.m. in the Friends
Meeting House, 957 Colorado
Avenue, in Palo Alto.
Steere is professor emeritus
of philosophy at Haverford Col
lege and chairman of the Friends
World Committee. He has gone
on numerous missions to Africa,
Europe, and Asia for the Amer
ican Friends Service Committee,
and was an observer at the Sec
ond Vatican Council.
