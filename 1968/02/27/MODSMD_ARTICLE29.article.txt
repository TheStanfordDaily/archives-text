# Services Set For Miller
## 
### 
Funeral services for Stanford
scientist Robert E. Miller will
be held today at 2 p.m. at the
Palo Alto All Saints Episcopal
Church.
The 42-year-old associate di
rector of the Stanford electron
ics laboratory was found dead
from suicide Saturday in a Gil
roy motel. Investigators said

Miller had tied a plastic bag
around his head and suffocated.
No notes were found.
Church services are open to
friends of the family and will
be followed by private inter
ment.
Miller, a research engineer at
Stanford for 18 years, lived with
his wife and family at 3770
Louis Road, Palo Alto.
