The fifth in this quarter's series of Western Civ movies, HENRY
V, starring Laurence Olivier, will be shown today in Cubberley Au
ditorium at 4:15 and 7:30. The film is in color.

A committee to organize and coordinate the ASSU CONVENTION
is being established, and interested students may sign up in the
ASSU office until this Friday, Mar. 1. Anyone who has specific
proposals for. reorganizing student government can have them
included as part of the syllabus of the convention.

Tryouts for the Ram's Head Spring Show, CARNIVAL, will be
held this evening in room 129 and 205 of Memorial Auditorium at
7:30. Actors, dancers, singers, and musicians are needed. The show
will have six performances, May 16, 17, 18, 23, 24, and 25.

145, Memorial Auditorium.
