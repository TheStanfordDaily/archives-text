# 'Slow Dance' Held Over
## 
### 
Slow Dance on the Killing
Ground, the widely acclaimed
play by William Hanley, has
been held over another weekend
at the Manhattan Playhouse in
Palo Alto. The gripping three
act production stars Leonard Si
mon as Randall, a brilliant Ne
gro youth fleeing from the po
lice in Brooklyn. Simon is a
student employed at the Stan
ford Museum.
Slow Dance is set in a Brook
lyn candy shop. The play's other
characters are Mr. Glas, the
store's proprietor, played by Wil
liam Chestnut, and Rosie, a stu
dent, played by Diana Ewing.
