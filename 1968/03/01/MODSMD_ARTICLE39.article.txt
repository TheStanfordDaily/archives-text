# Visiting Prof Talks Monday
## 
### 
Chicago Professor of Law
Harry Kalven, Jr., will discuss
"The First Amendment in the
Last Decade: Libel and Obscen
ity" in Tresidder Large Lounge
Monday at 7:30.
The speech is the first in the
Political Science Department's
"Lectureship in American De
mocracy" series in legal and po
litical philosophy.
Professor Kalven, who will
be at Stanford from March 4
to March 8, has written many
books and articles including The
Negro and the First Amend
ment, and The Uneasy Case for
Progressive Taxation. Students
wishing to talk with him may
make appointments through the
Political Science Department.
