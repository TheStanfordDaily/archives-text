Five ski films will bo shown by the STAN
FORD SKI CLUB Wednesday from 7 to 9 p.m.
in Dinkelspiel. Admission is 25c. The club con
tinues to sell ski lodging and lift reduction tickets
for weekends on Thursday for $8 in the ASSU
office.
★ ★ ★
Inmportant changes in rush policy will be con
sidered at the INTERFRATERNITY COUNCIL
MEETING at Alpha Delta Phi, at 8 p.m. Quad
pictures will also be taken.
★ ★ ★
Call back lists for CARNIVAL, Ram's Head Pro
ductions' Spring show, are posted in the Ram's
Head office (upstairs in the ASSU office) and in
the back hall of Mem. Aud. All those who tried
out please check the lists.
★ ★ ★
Thursday, March 7, at 5 p.m. is the last day for
applications for OVERSEAS CAMPUSES groups.
Applications and course lists may be obtained
at the Overseas Campuses Office. Applications
are being accepted for Austria, France, and Italy
groups beginning in March, 1969, and Britain and
Germany sessions beginning in January, 1969.

call editor, Dave Hibbard, at ext. 4637.
