# Anti-Draft Has Office Hours
## 
### 
The Stanford Anti-Draft Un
ion has scheduled office hours
for draft counseling.
Medical standards, emigration
to Canada, conscientious objec
tion, and induction procedures
will be discussed in individual
sessions by five advisers.
The sessions in the ASSU
office in Tresidder will be 9 to
10 a.m. Monday through Friday,
12 to 1 p.m. Monday and Wed
nesday, and 2 to 4 p.m. Monday
through Thursday.
The advisers are Leonard
Schein, Leonard Siegel, Tim
Jackins, Duncan Thomas and
Bill Meyers.
