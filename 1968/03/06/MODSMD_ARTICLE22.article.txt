# KQED Plans Youth Show
## 
### 
A special show on college-age
youth and their relations with
parents is being planned by tel
evision station KQED.
The show will consist of a
montage of family snapshots,
home movies, letters between
parents and children, and film
footage of events such as the
recent Oakland anti-draft demon
strations.
Produced by commentator
Ernest Lowe, the program will
be one in the KQED series
"Nothing Goes Over the Devil's
Back That Don't Buckle Under
His Belly."
Lowe has asked Stanford stu
dents for family snapshots,
home movies, and letters be
tween parents and children.
Students who wish to vol
unteer material should send
them to Lowe to KQED in San
Francisco.
