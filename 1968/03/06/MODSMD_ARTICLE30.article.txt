# City Planning Topic Of Talk
## 
### 
David Gebhard will speak on
"The Planned City in Southern
California, 1915-30" at 8 p.m. in
the Physics Lecture Hall.
Gebhard has pointd out that
the developments in architecture
and city planning around the
turn of the 19th century wre
of importance in establishing the
fundamental premises of today's
architecture and urban develop
ment.
Gebhard has published widely
on this period of architecture in
both America and Europe. Since
coming to California as Director
of the Art Gallery of the Uni
versity of California at Santa
Barbara, he has explored aspects
of local and regional history of
California architecture.
