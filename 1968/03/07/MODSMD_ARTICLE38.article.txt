SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATIONS FOR 1968-1969 are now avail
able in the Financial Aids Office, Student Services Building, Room
214. All students who wish to apply for renewal of their scholar
ships for next year, as well as those not presently holding awards,
must file their applications by May 1. The deadline for those
students who are going overseas summer and autumn is April 15.
