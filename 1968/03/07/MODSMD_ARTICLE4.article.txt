# Overseas Deadline
## 
### 
Today is the last day for
submitting applications for
forthcoming sessions at Stan
ford's Overseas Campuses.
Groups for which applications
may be made are Britain VII
and Germany XXII (next Win
ter and Spring Quarters) and
Austria VIII, France XVIII,
and Italy XVIII (Spring and
Summer Quarters of next
year). Course lists and appli
cations will be available at the
Overseas Campuses Office on
'Inner Quad, Room IC, and
applications may be turned in
up to closing time at 5 p.m.
