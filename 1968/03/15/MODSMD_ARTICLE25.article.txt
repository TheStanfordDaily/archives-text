# Walk For Peace Set
## 
### 
About 50 students will parti
cipate in a "Walk for Peace"
from Stanford to San Francisco
Mar. 23-26.
The walk will leave a week
from tomorrow morning at 9
and will stop in various towns
in order to leaflet and to speak
to individuals and groups about
the war in Vietnam.
The walk will end Tuesday
at San Francisco's Federal Build
ing. According to organizer Bill
Wachob, there will be housing
for all the walkers, but he sug
gests they bring sleeping bags
with them. Some meals will be
provided along the route.
Saturday night the walkers
will spend in Belmont, Sunday
night in Burlingamc, and Mon
day night in Daly City.
