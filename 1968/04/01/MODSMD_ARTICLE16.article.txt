# Three Daily Editors Chosen
## 
### 
Three new Daily staff appoint
ments have been announced by
editor Michael Sweeney.
Philip Taubman, a sophomore
from New York City, will rejoin
the Editorial Board as associate
editor, a position he held au
tumn quarter.
Michael Kuhl, a sophomore
from Plymouth, Calif., will also
become an associate editor and

member of the Editorial Board.
Formerly he was assistant edi
tor.
The Editorial Board decides
by majority vote the opinions
expressed in Daily editorials.
Gary Cavalli, a freshman
from Cupertino, was appointed
sports editor. Previously he
worked as a reporter on the
sports staff.
