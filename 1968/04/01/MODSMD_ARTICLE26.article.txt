# New Draft Aid Center
## 
### 
The Law Students' Civil
Rights Research Council is op
ening a Draft Project office to
day to provide information and
answer questions about the
draft. Staffing this service are
twenty law students who are
working in conjunction with all
of the attorneys in the Bay area
who are presently handling draft
cases.
Anyone seeking information
may stop by their office, Room
33 in the basement of Crothers
Hall, between ten and five.
