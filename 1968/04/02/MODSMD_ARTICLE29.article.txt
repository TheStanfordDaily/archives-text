# Concert Set At Dinkelspiel
## 
### 
Catherine Crozier, chapel or
ganist at Rollins College, Flori
da, will be presented in concert
by Stanford's Music Department
at 8:30 p.m. Tuesday, April 2,
in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Tickets are available at Tre
sidder box office.
The program will include a
selection of works by J. S. Bach.
"Passacaglia and Fugue in C
Minor," "Sonata No. 1 in E-flat,"
and a group of organ chorales;
Clerambault's "Suite du Pre
mier Ton"; and three works by
Buxtehude, "Chaconne in E
minor," "Fugue in C," and the
chorale, "Come, Redeemer of
Our Race."
Miss Crozier has won interna
tional recognition throughout
the U.S. and Europe. She has
played in major music festivals
and has performed for radio in
France, Belgium and England,
and on television in Denmark.
