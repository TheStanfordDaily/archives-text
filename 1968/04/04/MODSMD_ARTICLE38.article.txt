# Travel Guide Offers Hints
## 
### 
Stanford students in the Insti
tute of International Relations
have published a travel guide,
The Vagabond.
It contains helpful sugges
tions on how to travel inexpen
sively in Europe, Northern Af
rica, and Asia this summer and is
recommended for students going
to overseas campuses.
The 60 page booklet will be
on sale at the Sequoia Travel
Service and at the Bechtel Inter
national Center. On Wednesdays
and Fridays it will be sold in
White Plaza from 12 to 1 p.m.
