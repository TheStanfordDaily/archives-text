# Tennis Team Drops Pair
## 
### 
The expected proved to be the
rule in weekend tennis action,
as the nation's two top teams —
UCLA and USC—whipped the
hustling Stanford squad 8-1 and
6-3.
Friday afternoon Chuck Alloo
and Jamie Carroll provided the
Tribe's lone marker against
UCLA, winning the third dou
bles match 5-7, 6-3, 6-3.
Alloo and Carroll repeated
their victorious performance
Saturday afternoon, upending
Bob Eisenberg and Gary Tarna
4-6, 6-2, 6-1. Aloo also won his
singles match, downing Tarna
6-4, 6-1.
Brad Cornell supplied Stan
ford's other point in Saturday's
competition, winning the fifth
singles match. Cornell beat Ei
senberg 6-4, 0-6, 6-4.
Tribe mentor Dick Gould was
pleased with his team's perform
ance, commenting "we've got a
lot of work to do before we even
belong on the same court with
those guys."
Gould believes the weekend
series helped his improving net
men, noting "We've come a long
way since the beginning of the
year, and we're beginning to de
velop a lot of team spirit."
