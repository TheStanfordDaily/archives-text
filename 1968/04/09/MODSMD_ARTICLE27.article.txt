# Speech And Drama Grads Fill Lead 'Carnival' Roles
## 
### 
Graduate students in the
Speech and Drama Department
have taken most of the lead
roles in "Carnival," Ram's Head
Productions' Spring Show.
Gretchen Grant has been se
lected as Lili, Pieter Van Niel
will play Paul, Sergei Tscher
nisch will be Marco, Lucille
Medalis will have the part of
Rosalie, and Stan Rutherford
has been cast as Jaquot.
Miss Grant is a first year
Master of Fine Arts (MFA) stu
dent, Tschernisch a second year
MFA, Mrs. Medalis a special
student in the MFA program,
Van Neil a Ph.D. student in
dramatic criticism, and Ruther
ford the only non-Speech and
Drama student a senior Fn
glish major.
"Carnival" will have six per
formances at Stanford, on the
nights of May IG, 17, 18, 23, 24,
and 25, on Main Stage in Me
morial Auditorium.
Other members of the cast
include Charlie Edelman as
Eehlegel, Gary Levinson as Gro
bert, John Pasqualetti as Dr.
Glass, Rev Galley as Olga, and
Lili Pratt as Greta.
