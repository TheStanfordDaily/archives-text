★ ★ ★
A panel will discuss IS WHITE EDUCATION RELEVANT TO
BLACK PEOPLE? tonight at 7:30 in Tresidder Large Lounge.
Panelists include Bob Hoover, special administrative assistant to
the President of the College of San Mateo and member of the
school board of Sequoia Union District; Warren Heyman, vice
principal of an East Palo Alto elementary school; Frank Satter
white, former director of Stanford Area Tutorial Project; Letitia
Carter and Joyce King, Stanford students.
★ ★ ★
MUSICAL COMPOSERS and lyricists are invited to participate
in a seminar to write the score for the 1968 Gaieties. The first
meeting for the group is today at 2:30 p.m. in the Tresidder Piano
Room. For more information call Coco Pratt, 327-8164, or Alan
Mokler, 321-2300, ext. 334.
