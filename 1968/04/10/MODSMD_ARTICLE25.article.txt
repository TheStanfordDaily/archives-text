# French Prof. Wins Honor
## 
### 
John C. Lapp, Stanford pro
fessor of French and executive
head of the department of
French and Italian, has been
named by President Wallace
Sterling as the first William H.
Bonsall Professor of French.
The professorship is the first
of three which will bear the
Bonsall name. The newly-en
dowed chairs, which also in
clude a professorship in history
and a visiting professorship in
humanities, were made possible
by a bequest and gift of more
than $3.5 million from Mrs.
Samuel N. Bonsall of Carmel
and her late husband.


JOHN C. LAPP
