"CHROME ON CRASS": A Celebration of Spring in the Old Union
Courtyard
Variation I on Winter Quarters "Chrome Implosion", by the Vinyl Kadets. An electric ritual
played out in the Gothic confines of the Old Union Courtyard, with south-side blues and
rock by Charlie Musselwhite and the Black Shit Puppy Farm. Special effects by the Vinyl
Kadets will blend into a setting already rich in spring lawns and sheer sky. Tickets and
special instructions for celebrants will be available Monday, April 22 at the Tresidder Box
Office and on White Plaza at noon.
