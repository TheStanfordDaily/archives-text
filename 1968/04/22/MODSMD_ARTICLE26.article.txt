# Campus Roundup

THE CECIL TAYLOR SEXTET continues its residence at Sta«
ford this week. Cecil Taylor will speak this afternoon at 3:00 in
Tresidder Union on WHAT'S HAPPENING. The group will also

radio. Destination unknown.

DAYS OF RESISTANCE will begin its Stanford phase with a
"Teach-In on U.S. Imperialism" sponsored by the Students for a
Democratic Society tonight at 8:00 in Cubberley Auditorium. Speak
ers will include James O'Connor, professor of economics at San
Jose State College and Martin Nicolaus, professor of sociology at
Simon Frazer University (Vancouver, 8.C.). 4 member of the
Black Panther Party for Self Defense will also discuss "domestic
imperialism."
