# Siren Test
## 
### 
Stanford University firemen
will test a 300 horsepower super
siren as part of the quarterly
country-wide siren test tomor
row.
The siren will be temporarily
located at the site of the Univer
sity's new fire and police build
ing at the corner of Serra St.
and East Campus Drive.
It is scheduled to sound off
