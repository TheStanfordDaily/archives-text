# Schlesinger To Speak Here Sunday
## 
### 
Arthur M. Schlesinger, Jr., shown hero with President John
Kennedy, will be on campus Sunday speaking on "Robert Kennedy
and the 1968 Election." His talk is scheduled for 2:30 in the Tresid
der Large Lounge and is sponsored by the Students for Kennedy.
Prof. William Rivers of the Communications Department, former
member of the Washington Press Corps and author of The Opinion
makers, will introduce Schlesinger.
Schlesinger was a member of the campaign staff of Adlai Ste
venson in 1952 and 1956 and was a special assistant to the Presi
dent of the United States from 1961 to 1964.
