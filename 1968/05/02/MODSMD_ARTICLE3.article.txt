# Asia Scholar Speaks Today
## 
### 
Franz Shurmann, one of the
nation's leading China scholars
who has just returned from a
two week trip to Hanoi, will
speak in room 214, History Cor
ner, tonight at 8 p.m. His topic
will be "Report from North
Vietnam."
Prof. Shurmann, from the
history and sociology depart
ments at Berkeley, travelled to
Hanoi with Mary McCarthy. He
has been a key critic of the
Vietnam War.
Shurmann is author of Ideol
ogy and Organization in Com
munist China, and co-author of
The Politics of Escalation.
