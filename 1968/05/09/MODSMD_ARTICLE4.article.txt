# Harris Signs With Seattle
## 
### 
Stanford's Art Harris was
drafted and signed by the Seat
tle Supersonics in yesterday's
National Basketball Association
collegiate draft in New York.
The Sonics, picking second in
the draft after San Diego, chose
Harris on the second round of
the NBA draft. The first round
was held five weeks ago.
Harris was also drafted Mon
day by the Oakland Oaks of the
rival ABA.
