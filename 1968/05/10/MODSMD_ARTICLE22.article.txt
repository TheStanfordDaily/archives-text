# Stanford-Cal In 'Big Row'
## 
### 
Tomorrow morning the Stan
ford crew will race Cal at Red
wood City in the annual "Big
Row." First race starts at 8:45
a.m.
Although the Stanford boats
have a better record this year
than Cal, tomorrow's competi
tion promises to be anyone's
race in all three divisions. In

addition to the varsity first and
second boats and the first frosh
boat, the varsity third boat and
the second frosh shell will be
racing.
This year the winner of the
"Big Row" (on a point sys
tem) will be awarded the Jim
Reynolds Trophy, to be pre
sented by the Stanford Ath
letic Department
