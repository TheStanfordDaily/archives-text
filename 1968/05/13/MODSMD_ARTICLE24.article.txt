# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
CARNIVAL! USHERS —Persons Interested in ushering the Ram's
Head Spring Show should sign up on a list on the Ram's Head
office door in the ASSU loft. Performance dates are May 16, 17, 18,
23, 24, and 25. Ushers will receive free admission to the show.
★ ★ ★
NOTICE FOR ALL SENIORS: Please sign the 1968 Senior Class
Scroll (to be placed in the time capsule under the Class plaque in
the Quad). Also, submit nominations for the two most inspirational
professors, who will make brief speeches at Class Day exercises.
A desk will be set up by the bookstore today and tomorrow from
9 a.m. to 3 p.m. to take nominations and signatures for the Scroll.
★ ★ ★
LES GUIGNOLS, Stanford's French amateur troupe, will per
form Moliere's L'Amour Medecln (Love's the best Doctor) In
French May 13 and i.5 at 5 p.m. in the Little Theater. Admission
is free to the 30 minute show.
★ ★ ★
The French-Italian Film Series will present LA TERRA TREMA,
by Italian director Lucchino Visconti, tonight at 7:30 and 9:30
in the Business School Auditorium. The film has English sub-titles.
Series tickets, $1.50; single tickets, $1.00.
★ ★ ★
The Latin American and Spanish Film Series will present
CALLE MAYOR tonight at 8:15 in Tresidder. Admission to the
sub-titled Spanish film, written and directed by Juan Bardem, is
50c.
