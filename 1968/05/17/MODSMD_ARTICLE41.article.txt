"REMEDY FOR A RIOT" will bo shown tonight at 7:30 at the

Stanford's 27th annual SPRING SING will present "The Rights
of Spring" in Frost Amphitheater at 7:30 Sunday evening. Four
teen choral groups will be vying for awards before an anticipated
crowd of 8,000 persons.
Sponsored by the Axe Commission, the show will also include
Stanford's professional singing group, "The Mendicants." Near
the end of the show at 9:30 there will be a traditional match-light
ing ceremony.
