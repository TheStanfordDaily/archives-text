# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
Marcello Mastroianni stars in both of this weekend's TMU films.
The Friday show is "THE 10th VICTIM" which co-stars Ursula
Andress, and the Saturday showing is "CASANOVA 70" with Virna
Lisi.
★ ★ ★
MRS. MICHAEL NOVAK, noted painter and lecturer, will be the
speaker at today's Stanford YWCA lunch-discussion meeting. Mrs.
Novak will discuss the role of women in the arts and the opportu
nities open to them. An informal luncheon will be served or BYO.
★ ★ *
The Western Civ Flick, "WAR GAME" shows today at 4:15 and
7:30 in Cubberley Auditorium. Admission is free to the Stanford
community.

at 5:30 p.m. for dinner in the City and to see "The Fantasticks!"
Sign up at the I-Center.

TMU today at 12:15.

Studies Association will sponsor a discussion period following the
talk.
