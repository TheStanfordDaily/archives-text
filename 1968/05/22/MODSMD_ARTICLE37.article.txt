# Taketa Plans Campus Talk
## 
### 
Congressional candidate Gray
son S. Taketa, who this week
denounced a House Un-Ameri
can Activities Committee pro
posal that detention centers be
readied for black militants, will
speak at Stanford on the results
of his efforts to ease racial ten
sion and will report on the Poor
People's March on Washington,
May 22 at 4:15 p.m. in Tresid
der, Room 270.
Taketa is a Democratic can
didate in the June 4 primary and
seeks to oppose Charles Gubser,
Republican incumbent in the
10th Congressional District.
