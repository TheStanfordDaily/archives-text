# 'Falstaff' Ends Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford Opera Workshop's
production of Verdi's "Falstaff"
in English will be given its final
performance tomorrow at 8
p.m., in Dinkelspiel auditorium.
Musical direction is by Prof.
Sandor Salgo, with accompani
ment by the Stanford Symphony
Orchestra. Stage director is Miss
Claramae Turner of the New
York City Opera Company.
The 25-member chorus is di
rected by Prof. Harold Schmidt.
