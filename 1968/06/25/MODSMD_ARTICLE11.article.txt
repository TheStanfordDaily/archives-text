# Choir

The Summer Choir, which held
its first rehearsal last night, is
still looking for more members.
No auditions are necessary to
join the group, according to di
rectors Harold Schmidt and Rob
ert Rockabrand.
The choir \% ill hold two re
hearsals per week, from 7 to
8:30 p.m. Mondays and Thurs
days, and will sing in Memorial
Church Sunday mornings from
10 a.m. to 12 noon.
The choir will participate in
the Summer Festival, singing
with the Don Ellis Orchestra
on August 4th in Frost Amphi
theater.
Choir may be taken for cre
dit if desired.
