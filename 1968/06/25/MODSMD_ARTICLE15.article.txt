# Student Falls To His Death
## 
### 
Ernie Milburn, a sophomore in
General Studies, plunged 1000
feet to his death last week while
mountain climbing in Yosemite
National Park.
An 18-year-old from Oakland,
California, Milburn associated
with Beta Chi. He is survived by
his parents and his sister, Lucia,
a senior in Physical Sciences at
Stanford.
