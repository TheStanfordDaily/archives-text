# Nixon Aid
## 
### 
Richard V. Allen, foreign affairs
specialist at Stanford's Hoover Institu
tion on War, Revolution, and Peace,
has been named 112 oreign Policy Re
search Coordinator for Republican
Presidential candidate Richard M.
Nixon.
One of his principal tasks will be
the formulation and coordination of
positions and views on foreign policy.
The announcement Wednesday
from the former Vice President's New
York headquarters said that Allen's
duties also would include "collecting
research data and materjals and con
tacting specialists from the academic
and research communities."
While participating in the Nixon
campaign, Allen, 32, will be on leave
from Stanford. He is a senior staff
member at the Institution and editor
of its new "Yearbook on International
Communist Affairs."
