# Textbook Exhibit
## 
### 
Twenty-five publishing companies
will exhibit their latest textbooks and
instructional materials in the lobby of
the School of Education building to
morrow and Thursday.
The latest developments in instruc
tional materials for reading and litera
ture, the new mathematics, social
studies, foreign languages, programmed
learning, audiovisual teaching aids, and
many other innovations will be on dis
play.
The display may be viewed from 9
a.m. to 4 p.m., torn rrow and from 9
a.m. to noon, Thursd y.
