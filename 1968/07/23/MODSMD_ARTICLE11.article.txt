# Research Conference
## 
### 
Approximately 4UU scientists, en
gineers and electronics specialists from
industry and government will meet on
the Stanford campus next month for
the annual conference to review ad
vanced research of the Stanford Elec
tronics Laboratories.
New radio antenna systems, radar
astronomy techniques, laser imaging
schemes and computer applications in
medicine will be among the subjects
discussed by Stanford University en
gineers. Nobel laureate William
Shockley will be among those present
ing papers.
The unique conference has been
held each year since the early 1950's at
the request of government agencies
supporting the Stanford research.
