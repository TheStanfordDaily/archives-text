# Recall Reagan Aid Sought
## 
### 
Stanford Students to recall Reagan
maintains a table in White Plaza, where
members of the Stanford community
may sign petitions and offer aid to re
call California's governor.
Volunteer help is needed for circula
ting petitions in Santa Clara and San
Mateo counties and for looking up pre
cinct numbers of signatures already
collected. Anyone can precinct, but
only registered voters are permitted to
circulate petitions. More than 500 sig
natures have been collected by Stan
ford Students to Recall Reagan.
