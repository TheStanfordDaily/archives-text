# Peace Proposition Needs Volunteers
## 
### 
A group of citizens of San Mateo
County is currently conducting a drive
to place a peace proposition on the
November ballot.
The proposition urges withdrawal
from Vietnam.
In order to place the proposition on
the county ballot, 20,000 valid signa
tures of registered voters of San Mateo
County are needed by August 15.
Volunteers are needed to help man
tables and circulate petitions. San
Mateo County residents interested in
helping should call Ned Groth,
325-3305.
