# I-Center To Present Marx Brothers Movie
## 
### 
As part of its series of entertain
ment for the whole Stanford commun-.
ity and as a service for lonesome,
foreign students, the International
Center will present the most consisten
tly uproarious of the vintage Marx
Brothers comedies, "Duck Soup."
The setting of the movie is in the
tiny warring kingdom of 1 redonia
which is ruled by Queen Margaret
Dumont and Prince Groucho. Harpo
and Chico are professional spies work
ing for both sides and causing the usual
confusion.
The film begins at 9 p.m. this Satur
day at the International Center. "Duck
Soup" will last about 75 minutes.
Coffee will be served by charming girls.
