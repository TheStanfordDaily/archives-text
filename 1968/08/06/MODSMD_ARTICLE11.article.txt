# Music Critic To Study Here
## 
### 
Paul Hertclendy, music critic of the
Oakland Tribune, has been appointed a
Professional Journalism Fellow at Stan
ford.
He is in addition to the 25 fellows
previously appointed for the 1968-69
academic year from newspapers, maga
zines, and broadcasting organization in
the U.S. and Canada.
Hertelendy, 35, holds a Ph.D. in
engineering, but switched to music
criticism four years ago. He will study
the history and literature of music in
the winter and spring quarters of
1968-69.
