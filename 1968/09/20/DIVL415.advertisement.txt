LOX & BAGEL BRUNCH FOR JEWISH FRESHMEN
B'nai Rabbi Charles Fami ant, Director of the B'nai B'rith Hiliel
. - Foundation, invites all Jewish Freshmen to a Lox and Bagel
D rith Brunch, Sunday, Sept. 22, noon, at the International Center.
HIGH HOLIDAY SERVICES WILL BE HELD AT
LJ THE WOMEN'S CLUBHOUSE
The Clubhouse is located across from the Bookstore on
White Memorial Plaza.
. ROSH HASHANNAH:
Liberal Conservative Service—in the Auditorium
Conducted by Rabbi Charles Familant
Cantor: Jess Novick
L Sunday, Sept. 22—8:00 p m.
Monday, Sept. 23—10:00 a.m.
Orthodox Service — Lower Lounge
Sunday, Sept. 22—6:45 p.m.
L Monday, Sept. 23—9:00 a.m. and 6:45 p.m.
Tuesday, Sept. 24—9:00 a.m.
YOM KIPPUR:
_ Liberal Conservative Service—in the Auditorium
E Cnoducted by Rabbi Charles Familant
Cantor: Jess Novick
Tuesday, Oct. 1—8:00 p.m.
L Wednesday, Oct. 2—10:00 a.m. and 4:30 p.m.
Orthodox Service—in Lower Lounge
Tuesday, Oct. 1—6:45 p.m.
Wednesday, Oct. 2—9:00 a.m. (all day)
Immediately following the concluding service a Break-the-
Fast Meal will be given at the International Center.
