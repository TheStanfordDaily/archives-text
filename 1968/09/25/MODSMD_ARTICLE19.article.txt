# Dean's List
## 
### 
The Dean's office clarified
yesterday the requirements of
the "Dean's List." Beginning this
year, a student must have com
pleted a minimum of 39 units
of letter-graded work in the pre
ceding three quarters in order
to qualify.
A position on the Dean's list
is an honor conferred upon un
dergraduates for maintaining no
less than a 3.20 grade point aver
age for three consecutive quar
ters.
