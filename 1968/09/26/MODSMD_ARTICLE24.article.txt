# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
A committed of two graduate and two undergraduate students
is being formed to investigate university sponsorship of LOW COST
HOUSING. Those interested should submit letters of application
to Denis Hayes in the ASSU office immediately.
★ ★ ★
Members of the Stanford community interested in enrolling in the
DEVELOPMENTAL READING PROGRAM of the Counseling and
Testing Center should come to an organizational meeting tonight at
7 o'clock in Room E-2, School of Education.
★ ★ ★
DON QUIXOTE, a Soviet film directed by Grigory Kozintsev, will
be shown at 8:15 tonight in Tresidder Memorial Union. Admission
is free to International Association members.
★ ★ ★
Applications and interview sign-up sheets for the new AXE
COMMISSION are in the ASSU office. Commission members de
velop card stunts, sponsor rallies and Spring Sing, and generally
work toward involving Stanford students in supporting their ath
letic teams.
★ ★ ★
Musicians interested in performing in a WEDNESDAY NOON
MUSICALE this fall are invited to sign up in the basement of
Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
★ ★ ★
The Women's Recreation Association has announced its Fall
Quarter SWIMMING SCHEDULE. Monday and Wednesday hours
are 11-12 and 5-5:45. On Tuesday and Thursday women may also
swim from 1-.3. Friday, Saturday, and Sunday hours are 1-5.
Women may bring guests to the pool only on weekends.
