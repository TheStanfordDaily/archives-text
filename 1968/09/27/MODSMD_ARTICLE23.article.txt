# Fiji's Roll Football Film
## 
### 
This fall the Fiji House, in
conjunction with the Athletic
Dept., will present the official
films of each week's football
game the following Monday
night at 7:15 in Cubberley Audi
torium.
Films of the games will be
narrated by head coach John
Ralston and sportscaster Bob

Murphy. The outstanding Stan
ford players will also be present
for a question-answer period fol
lowing the films.
The series will begin Sept. 30
with films of the Stanford-Ore
gon game, plus a scouting report
on Air Force, the next opponent
on the schedule of the Pacific-B's
dark horse.
