# Campus Roundup

### 
THE VIRGIN SPRING, first
of a series of foreign films, will
be shown at 7 and 9 tonight in
Cubberley Auditorium.
The film is directed by Ingmar
Bergman and stars Gunnel
Lindblom. Admission price is
$1.00.
Tickets are on sale at TMU,
or may be purchased at the
door.
