Students intending to take the MEDICAL COLLEGE ADMIS
SION TEST are reminded that the next testing date is October 19.
There will not be another administration of the MCAT until May,
1969.
Applications for the October test must be received at the Psycho
logical Corporation in New York on or before October 4.
Applications and brochures concerning the test are available at
the Counseling and Testing Center, Third Floor, Old Union.

Art Melville, a former Catholic priest who was expelled from
Guatemala in December, 1967 for his support of the Guatemalan
revolutionary movement, will speak Monday at 4:15 p.m. in Dinkel
spiel Auditorium. His topic will be "GUATEMALA: THE NEXT
VIETNAM?"
Melville's Stanford lecture is being co-sponsored by the University
Christian Movement and the Resistance.
