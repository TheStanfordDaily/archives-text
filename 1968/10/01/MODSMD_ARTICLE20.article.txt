The FRESHMAN FILM SERIES will present "One Hour With
You" starring Maurice Chaevalier, Jeannette Mac Donald and Charlie

at 9:30. Movies will be shown in Geology 320. Admission is free.

The Sequoia, Stanford's literary magazine, will sponsor JOHNIE
SCOTT in a reading of his poetry and prose in the TMU Large
Lounge tomorrow at 4:15. There will be musical accompaniment
featuring flute, congas, and saxophone.
★ ★ ★
A general meeting for all undergraduates interested in the pro
gram of the MATHEMATICS DEPARTMENT will be held tomor
row at 4:15 in Room 380 C. Members of the faculty will be present
to discuss aspects of the undergraduate program and to hear stu
dent views about life in the Mathematics Department.
Among the topics to be discussed will be the honors program,
course requirements and recommendations, methods of obtaining
information about graduate schools and fellowships, student-fac
ulty relations, and the possibilities for a math major to attend a
foreign campus.

uate students are cordially invited.

Anyone interested in working on the ASSU SPECIAL EVENTS

★ ★ ★
Representatives from MADEMOISELLE MAGAZINE will be on
campus tomorrow at 4:15 in TMU 271 to discuss the annual writing
competition for women sponsored by the publication.
Categories include fiction, poetry, art and photography and a
special College Board Competition which includes a trip to New
York and the opportunity to edit a special college issue of the
magazine next August.
Cash awards range from $100 to $500 for winners in the writing,
art and photography divisions.
