Arthur H. King, Controller of the Education
Division of the British Council, will speak in the
Forum Room of the Meyer Memorial Library
(ULib 124) tonight at 8:00. Sponsored by the
Committee on Linguistics, his talk will be fol
lowed by a discussion period. The public is in
vited. Dr. King is on an extended world tour in

vestigating problems related to the use and
teaching ol the English language abroad.
