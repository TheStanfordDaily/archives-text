# Referendum Set
## 
### 
AASU president Denis Hayes
yesterday announced final plans
for a referendum on a new con
stitution and Judicial system.
The campus-wide vote is sched
uled for next Monday and polls
will be set up on White Plaza
and elsewhere.
The students will have the op
tion of accepting or rejecting
the new constitution which has
been in preparation over the
summer. In the vote on the ju
dicial system students will have
the option of rejecting, accept
ing or temporarily accepting the
Committee of Fifteen agree
ments.
Both the new Constitution and
the judicial proposals will be
printed in the Daily this week.
