# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
A STANFORD-IN-WASHINGTON meeting and election of new
officers will be held at 5:00 p.m. on Tuesday, Oct. 8, in room 274 of
Tresidder Union. It is important that all past S.I.W. interns who
worked in either Sacramento or Washington, D.C. over the summer
attend.
★ ★ ★
The STERN BIKE ROOM will be open during the following hours
this week: Monday, 7-9 p.m.; Wednesday, 7-9 p.m.; Friday, 3-5 p.m.;
Sunday, 3-5 p.m. If you have stored a bike in Stern, please pick it
up this week.
★ ★ ★
Italian scholar and MEXICAN SOCIAL WORKER, Dr. Joseph
Facci, will speak on Latin American problems at noon Monday on
White Plaza.
★ ★ ★
Students interested in using their creative talents in the STU
DENT DRAMA GROUP should attend a meeting at 4 p.m. today in
room 275 Tresidder Union.
★ ★ ★
Any students interested in serving on the ROTC and STUDENT
RELATIONS COMMITTEES of the Academic Senate should obtain
applications today in the ASSU office and return them to Denis
Hayes by Wednesday, October 9.
* * ★
The SDS will hold a formative ACTION MEETING in the Bran
ner dining hall tonight at 7:30 to formulate a petition of student
grievances and demands to the Administration.
★ ★ ★
MARK RUDD, head of Columbia's chapter of the SDS, will speak
Wednesday, October 9, in Memorial Auditorium. His talk will follow
a film on the riots at Columbia presented at 4 p.m. Admission to
the film is one dollar.
★ ★ ★
San Francisco' Mayor JOSEPH ALIOTO will be the main guest
at an evening social tonight at 6:30 at Rickey's Hyatt House.
Alioto will talk informally on this year's campaigns. Donations
of $10 per person or $15 per couple are requested.
