# Campus Roundup

A discussion of STANFORD-IN-FRANCE will be presented in
the Tresidder Large Lounge tonight at 7:30 p.m. as a feature of
Overseas Campus Week. All students interested in applying to the
French campus are encouraged to attend. Tomorrow's program will
feature Stanford in Germany, followed by Austria on Thursday, and
Italy on Friday.
Mr. Jerome Vogel, Director of the OPERATION ( ROSSROADS
AFRICA Overseas Program, will speak tonight at 7 p.m. in the
Serra House Lounge. OCA conducts summer friendship and aid
programs in Africa, involving American, Canadian, and Latin Amer
ican students.
★ * ★
The FRESHMAN FILM SERIES will feature "Horse Feathers"
starring the Marx Brothers tonight at 8 and ' I arzan, the Ape Man
starring Johnny Weissmuller and Maureen O'Sullivan at 9:15 p.m.
Both films will be shown in Geology Room 320, and admission is
free.

and 2.
