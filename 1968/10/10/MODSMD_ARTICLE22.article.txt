# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
Czech film, "THE FABULOUS BARON MUNCHAUSEN" will be
shown tonight at 8:15 in Cubberley Auditorium. Admission is by In
ternational Association membership card only; cards will be sold in
the International Center and at noon on White Plaza prior to the film.
There will be coffee and discussion at the International Center fol
lowing the film.
★ ★ ★
Former Stanford student Mike Locks, an airman on active duty
in the Air Force and key organizer in Saturday's Gl's MARCH
FOR PEACE in San Francisco, will speak on anti-war sentiment
within the armed forces this afternoon at 4:15 in Dinkelspiel Audi
torium. The Gl's march will begin at 11:00 a.m. at the Panhandle
of Golden Gate Park. Supporting Stanford students will be leaving
in carpools from the TMU parking lot at 10:00 a.m. Saturday morn
ing.
★ ★ ★
A discussion of STANFORD-IN-ITALY will be presented in the
Tresidder Large Lounge tonight at 7:30 p.m. All students interested
in attending the Italian campus are urged to attend.
★ ★ ★
The Tresidder Social Division presents Vanguard recording art
ists THE CLEANLINESS AND GODLINESS SKIFFLE BAND in
a casual concert Friday afternoon at 4:00 p.m. in the Large Lounge.
Tickets are $1.00 at the Tresidder Box Office.
