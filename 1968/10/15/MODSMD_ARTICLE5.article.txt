# Grape Boycott
## 
### 
More than 155 faculty and
staff members have signed a
petition supporting the grape
boycott and urging "all mem
bers of the Stanford commu
nity to join us." A referendum
on the boycott is on the ballot
in the ASSU elections which
end today.
Signers of the petition inlcude
Dean of the Chapel Davie Na
pier, Dean of Students Joel
Smith, Vice-Provost Herbert
Packer and Associate Dean of
Students Bill Wyman.
Wyman, Napier and 11 other
faculty members initiated the
petition.
