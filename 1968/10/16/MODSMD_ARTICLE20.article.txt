ESALEN AT STANFORD, Experimental Edu
cation, still has places open in the weekend work
shops for interested people. Please call Martine
Boot at 854-5681 for further information about
these Encounter Groups, Gestalt workshops, Psy
chodrama marathons, movement workshops, sen
sory awareness, and theater games workshops.
★ ★ ★
The second in the Fall quarter series of stu
dent faculty staff OPEN FORUM LUNCHEONS,
sponsored by the Stanford Y, will be held in the
Clubhouse, third floor lounge on Thursday, Oc
tober 17. Discussions will be informal, with em
phasis on the presentations of resource people
from the various concerned campus groups.
Luncheon 50c or BYO.
★ ★ ★
On Wednesday, October 16, PROFESSOR
PIfIMP FEHL will speak on "The Artist as
Psychologist: Notes on the Representation of
Character in Renaissance Sculpture." The lec
ture will take place in the Physics Lecture Hall
at 8:00 p.m. It is open to the public.

scholars and artists, many of whom face pos

COMMITTEE and submit any contributions to
the ASSU Office.
