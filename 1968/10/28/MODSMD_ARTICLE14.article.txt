# Work Abroad Organization Meets Today
## 
### 
The Stanford chapter of
AIESCEC will hold an organiza
tional meeting tonight at 8 in
Tresidder Union. The totally stu
dent-run organization arranges
8 week to 18 month management
internships and work programs
with foreign companies.
Starting in 1948 as an associ
ation of students representing
European countries and ex
changing less than 100 jobs, it
has grown into a world-wide or
ganization operating on over
350 campuses in 45 countries on
6 continents.
The United States has been
active in AIESCEC since 1958.
