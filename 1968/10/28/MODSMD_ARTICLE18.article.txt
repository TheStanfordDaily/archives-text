The deadline for INTERNATIONAL ASSOCIATION POLICY
BOARD nominations has been extended to noon Wednesday, Octo
ber 30. Places for both foreign and American students are available.

AFRICAN TABLE will meet today at noon at the International
Center. Elon Gilbert will speak on: Food Marketing and Economic
Development in Northern Nigeria. Bring your own lunch. Coffee
provided.

Anyone interested in USHERING for SRT performances of
"Andromache" October 30 to November 2 and November 6 to 9

The deadline for filing PETITIONS TO CHANGE EXAMS is
November 18. Petitions may be obtained in Room 116 of the
Old Union.

THE WORLD WITH FLOWERS, FOOD AND FASHION at the
annual Chrysanthemum Tea this Wednesday from 1:30 to 4 at
Lagunita Court. A fashion show, scheduled for 2:30 to 3:30,
will highlight the program.
Tickets will be on sale at the door. The money goes to the club's
welfare activities.
