The Director of Admissions of the SCHOOL OF EDUCATION AT
HARVARD will meet with students interested in Harvard Education
today from 2:30 to 5 p.m. at 560 Alvarado Street.
★ ★ ★
LYLE FULKS, Northern Ca'ifornia Regional organizer of the
Young Socialist Alliance, will speak today on the subject of "How
to fight the three little pigs" at 12 noon in Tresidder Union room
271.

A Stanford Conservation Group BEACH OUTING to Pigeon
Point will leave from History Corner at 10:45 a.m. this Saturday.
All welcome. Bring a lunch.
★ ★ ★
Professor HERBERT BRUCKER, political journalist and mem
ber of the Department of Communications, wi'l analyze the char
acter of American Politics today at 4:15 p.m. in the International
Center Lounge.
★ ★ ★
Students are reminded that applications for HEAD YELL
LEADER may be picked up in the ASSU offices. The applications
require fifteen signatures and must be returned by 4 p.m. Friday,
Nov. 1.

members will meet at 4:15 p.m. today in Tresidder Small Lounge to
discuss the Student-Faculty Committee.

pus today from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Interviews can be arranged through

BANQUET tomorrow evening at Rickey's Hyatt House. Professor

$3.00 per person.
