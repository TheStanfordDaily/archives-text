# Lag Seca Car Races
## 
### 
The Sixth Annual Lagunita
Seca car race sponsored by Zeta
Psi will make another appear
ance this year as part of Home
coming activities November 10
at Lake Lagunita.
Practice runs will begin at
10 a.m. and end at noon with
the scheduled race to start at
1:30. Entry fees are $10 per
car with different classifications
open for varying entries.
The event involves a race
against time with a course laid
out across the new dry lakebed
with various obstacles and
jumps to challenge the drivers
abilities and the cars capabili
ties.
Deadline for applications is
Nov. 6 and forms are available
at the Zeta Psi house.
