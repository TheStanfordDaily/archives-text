ATTENTION—
|
GRADUATE STUDENTS!!
Interviews with a representative
of the
COOPERATIVE COLLEGE REGISTRY
may be scheduled in the University Placement Service, White
Memorial Plaza on Monday thru Wednesday, November
11, 12, 13, 1968.
9:00 A.M. to 4:30 P.M.
, Masters and doctoral students interested in college
administrative and teaching positions for the aca
demic year 1968—69 are invited to make an appoint
ment. Experienced college faculty particularly desired.
The Cooperative College Registry is a free service to
over 200 private liberal arts colleges throughout the
United States.
Descriptive brochures and registry
forms are available from
the University Placement Bureau
