# Red Hot Prof Contest Begins
## 
### 
The Red Hot Prof contest,
sponsored by the International
Student Fund, begins today and
will continue through Thursday.
In the contest, students elect a
professor to lead a cheer in Sat
urday's football game.
Voting takes place from 9 a.m.
until 3:30 p.m. daily. A positive
vote costs one cent, a negative
vote a nickel. To nominate some
one costs a quarter..

Last year's winner was Ernest
Arbuckle of the School of Busi
ness. Running third to Fifth for
most of the week, Arbuckle
surged ahead of the leaders on
the last day of balloting.
All proceeds of the contest are
used for scholarship and self
help projects for students in
Asia.
