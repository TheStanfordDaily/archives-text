# Stanford Union Joins CSEA
## 
### 
The Stanford Employees As
sociation committee, which was
appointed recently to study the
benefits of affiliating with the
California School Employees As
sociation (CSEA>, voted in fav
or of such action.

CSEA has over 500 chapters
throughout the State of Cali
fornia. The granting of a char
ter to Stanford is a new venture
on their part. In the past 20
years only public schools were
represented.
