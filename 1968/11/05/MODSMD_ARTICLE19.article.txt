TIONS will be available in the Overseas Campus
office on Quad beginning Wednesday, Nov. 6
through Nov. 13. The Board consists of Overseas
Campus alumni interested in working on the
program. Details are on the application.

and the INTERNATIONAL ASSOCIATION are

TV, and political commentary.
