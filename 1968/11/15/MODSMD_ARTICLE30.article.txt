# Minority Program
## 
### 
The Graduate School of Busi
ness is launching a new program
to recruit disadvantaged minori
ty students for its master of busi
ness administration (MBA) pro
gram.
The new "Leadership Program
in Management for Disadvan
taged Minorities" commits the
school to admit ten or more mi
nority students who may not
meet all the regular academic
standards to the class starting
September, 1969.
Minority students holding a
bachelor's degree in any field
can apply for the program. Those
selected will be eligible for sum
mer employment, tutorial assist
ance, and financial aid for tui
tion and living expenses.
Selections will be based on rec
ommendations, experience, and
leadership as well as the impor
tant factor of motivation.
