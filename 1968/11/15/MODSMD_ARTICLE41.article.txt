# Visiting Chaplain To Preach Here
## 
### 
Dr. Beverly A. Asbury, chap
lain at Vanderbilt University,
Nashville, Tennessee, will preach
at Memorial Church this Sun
day. He will also be present for
an informal discussion in the
Round Room, Sunday night at
7:30 p.m.
Asbury has been a close friend
of Stanford's Dean B. Davie Na
pier's since his student days at
Yale Divinity School. He has
been Chaplain at Vanderbilt
since January, 1967. He spoke at
Stanford last spring.
