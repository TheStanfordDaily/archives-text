# Campus Roundup

TIME SCHEDULES FOR WINTER QUARTER
are now available at the information window of
the Old Student Union. Students will begin pre
registration by obtaining study list cards from
advisers on Monday, Tuesday, or Wednesday. Stu
dents who neglect to pick up study lists on one
of these days cannot pre-register.

Schedule.
