# SOS Plans Action In PA Courtroom
## 
### 
The Stanford Chapter of Stu
dents for a Democratic Society is
planning to "fill the courtroom
and steps of the courthouse"
during the arraignment of six
people arrested in connection
with the recent protest at Palo
Alto's Lytton Plaza.

The purpose, according to an
SDS handout entitled "Our Day
In Court", is to "show solidarity
with those arrested and our de
termination to end political ha
rassment".
The arraignment is scheduled
for 10 a.m. today at the North
County Courthouse.
