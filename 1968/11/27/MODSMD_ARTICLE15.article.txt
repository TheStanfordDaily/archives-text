# Jazz Group To Perform
## 
### 
"Smoke" will be playing a free
concert in the Tresidder Large
Lounge today at 4:00 p.m. They
are returning by demand after
playing a concert with Big
Black's septet Sunday in Dinkel
spiel Auditorium.
The members of Smoke are
Fred Berry (trumpet, flugle
horn, melodica, tamborine,
space-whistle), Cris Cristy (bass,
cello), John Felder (drums,
Chinese percussion), Kenny
Washington (tenor and soprano
saxophones, marachis, temple
blocks), and Woody Webb (vi
braharp, marimba, African per
cussion).
