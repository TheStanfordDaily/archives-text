# Rescue
## 
### 
Two off-duty Stanford firemen
have been instrumental in effec
ting the rescue of a woman
trapped in a Volkswagen follow
ing a head-on collision in Portola
Valley Dec. 23.
Jim Ward and John Cole used
Woodside Fire Department equip
ment to free a woman whose
name they still do not know from
the car which collided with a lar
ger car on Golden Oak Drive in
Woodside.
The pair said they used proced
ures learned under Stanford Fire
Chief John Marston.
Cole attends Colorado State
where he is a senior but works as a
Stanford fireman as well as at
tending summer school here.
