# Art Lecture Set For Tonight
## 
### 
John van der Marck, Director of
the Museum of Contemporary
Art, Chicago, will be the featured
speaker of the Wednesday Night
Art Lecture Series tonight at eight
p.m. in Bishop Auditorium of the
Graduate School of Business.
Topic of Mr. van der Marck's
address will be "The Art of Sub
version in Art."
Author of many articles and
catalogues, Mr. van der Marck re
ceived his PhD. at the University
of Rymegon and serves as a
member of two committees of the
National Council on the Arts.
The Series is jointly sponsored
by the Department of Art and the
Committee for Art at Sanford and
is open to the public.


JON VAN DER MARCK
