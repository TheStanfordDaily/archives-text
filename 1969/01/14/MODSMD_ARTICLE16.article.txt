# Black Holiday
## 
### 
The Black Student Union has
designated Wednesday a Black
Holiday at the Stanford campus in
honor of the birthday of Martin
Luther King.
The holiday has been pro
claimed by the BSU for the Stan
ford campus only.
In addition the BSU has elec
ted new co-chairmen for the com
ing quarter. The pair include Nate
Kirtman, who was re-elected, and
Leo Bazile.
