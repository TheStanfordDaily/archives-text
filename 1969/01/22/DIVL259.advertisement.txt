BOYCE NAMED
TRACK CAPTAIN
High Jumper Peter Boyce
has been elected Captain of
the 1969 Stanford Track and
Field team.
Boyce, a senior from Mel
bourne, Australia, repre
sented his country in the
1968 Olympic games in
Mexico City. Last season he
set a new Stanford record in
the high jump with a mark of
7' 3" and was third place
finisher at the NCAA
Championships.
