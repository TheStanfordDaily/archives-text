# Campus Roundup

Hillel Foundation is sponsoring a weekly series of
ENCOUNTER GROUPS for all faiths and back
grounds. "The Campus Explosion" meets Tuesdays
at noon (bring sack lunch); "On Israel" meets Tues
days, 8 p.m., with a short presentation on current
problems from Israel's standpoint as a focal point
for discussion. Meetings are third floor lounge of the
Clubhouse.
