# Taubman Elected Vol. 155 Editor
## 
### 
Philip Taubman, a junior in his
tory from New York City, was
elected editor of Volume 155 of
The Stanford Daily Friday.
Taubman, who has served as as
sociate editor this volume defeat
ed Managing Editor Mark Wein
berger, a junior in political sci
ence, 55 votes to 41, in a staff
election that drew the largest
turnout in memory.
The new editor will take office
Friday and serve for the remain
der of the school year. The edi
tor-in-chief appoints all other edi
tors to their positions and is
charged with the management of
the editorial side of the news
paper.
