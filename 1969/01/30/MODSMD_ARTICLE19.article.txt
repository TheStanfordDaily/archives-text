# Artist To Discuss Work
## 
### 
Mrs. Karen Novak, a nationally
known artist, will speak in Roth
House Saturday afternoon at 1:00.
Mrs. Novak has had one-man
shows in Boston, New York,
Chicago, Des Moines, Vancouver,
San Francisco and many univer
sities around the country includ
ing Harvard, Carleton and Stan
ford.
The work of Mrs. Novak has
been selected for traveling shows

of the American Federation of the
Arts and the National Association
of Women Artists.
When discussing her art, Mrs.
Novak uses slides of her own
paintings and prints in order to
compare the early stages with
later ones.
Mrs. Novak is married to
Michael Novak, a former assistant
professor of Religious Studies at
Stanford.
