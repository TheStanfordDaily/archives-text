# Convocation
## 
### 
The Black Student Union has
called a convocation of all blacks
on campus for noon today in
Bishop Auditorium in the business
school.
The meeting is planned for
black students, administrators,
faculty and employees "to discuss
and lay plans for future courses of
action to be taken by Black peo
ple collectively at Stanford," ex
plained a BSU statement submit
ted to The Daily.
The statement suggested that
"in many ways, hostility and re
pression is manifesting itself quite
openly on the campus. Parallels to
the situation at San Francisco
State and other campuses across
the nation are quite obvious."
The BSU "plans to keep every
one aware of exactly what is hap
pening concerning the racial situ
ation at Stanford."
