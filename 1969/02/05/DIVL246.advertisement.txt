Been
Astonished
Lately?
IF life holds no surprises for you anymore,
come in and listen to the KLH Model
Twenty-One radio.
You may have a hard time finding it
among the big consoles and the shiny stereo
components. It's just a small FM radio in an
unobtrusively handsome walnut cabinet.
But search it out, and turn it on.
The amount of sound that results will be
unreasonable. So will the quality. Out of all
proportion to the size and price. It will
amaze you. Repeat, amaze you.
The KLH Model Twenty-One is even
more than meets the ear. Besides being a
self-contained radio, it has outlets for
external speakers and for making tape
recordings.
It won't do everything. It isn't' stereo,
and it doesn't play records. It's just the best
FM radio you ever heard.
I in
And if you don't think something that
simple can astonish you, come in and put
yourself to the test.
You're not as jaded as you think.
