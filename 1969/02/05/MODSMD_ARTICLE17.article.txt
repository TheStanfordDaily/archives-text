# RAIDER COACH
## 
### 
OAKLAND (AP)-The Oak
land Raiders once again dipped in
to their own ranks for a head
coach Tuesday naming assistant
John Madden to the job.
Madden, who at 33 becomes
professional football's youngest
coach, replaces John Rauch at the
helm of the American Football
League club which won the
championship in 1967 and the
Western Division title in '68.
