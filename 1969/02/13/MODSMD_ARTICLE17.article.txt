# Chem Lab Damaged
## 
### 
At 3:10 P.M. Monday, a seri
ous explosion damaged portions
of the Organic Chemistry Lab in
Stauffer Chemistry Dept. Accord
ing to Stanford fire and police re
ports, a graduate student was mix
ing an unknown substance and
left the mixture unattended.
The mixture exploded, blow
ing out two windows in the lab
and blowing the door open. Pro
fessor Richard Eastman of the de
partment was slightly injured by a
piece of flying debris. Damage to
the lab, including the windows of
the building was approximately
$200.
