# TMU Hours Announced
## 
### 
Tresidder Memorial Union has
announced new hour changes ef
fective immediately.
Monday through Sunday, will
be: the food service, 7 a.m. to 10
p.m.; the information desk, 8 a.m.
to 10 p.m.; the store, 7 a.m. to 10
p.m. and browsing area 9 a.m. to
10 p.m.
The games area will be open 9
a.m. to 10:30 p.m., Monday
through Thursday and 9 a.m. to
midnight Friday and Saturday.
The coffee house will maintain
its hours of 11 a.m. to 2 a.m.
Monday through Sunday.
