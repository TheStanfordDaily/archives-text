# Mermen Nip USC
## 
### 

1 KIPLE WINNER- Sophomore swimmer John Ferris contributed 17 vital points to Stanford's
57-56 win over USC Saturday. The upset snapped an SC dual meet winning streak that
stretched over 12 years and 108 meets. Eerris won his specialty, the 100 meter butterfly, in
1:54.4 and added a first in the 200 individual medley in 2:00.2. He also teamed with Ered
Haywood, Morgan Manning, and Ered Jamison to take the 400 medley relay in 3:31.6. (See
story on page seven.)
