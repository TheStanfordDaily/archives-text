# New Heart
## 
### 
Dr. Norman Shumway and his
surgical team successfully per
formed their twelfth heart trans
plant operation Monday.
The Stanford Medical Center
reports that the patient, forty
three year-old, Sasha Gilien is in
satisfactory condition. Mr. Gilien,
a script writer for television's "Big
Valley," has a wife and 18 month
old daughter at home in Los
Angeles.
Mr. Gilien's transplant follows a
similar operation which was per
formed on February 1 on a Doug
las Allan of Menlo Park. Mr. Allan
is also in satisfactory condition.
