# Campus Roundup

### 
Cardinal's Board will sponsor an informational program on summer
and spring break work projects at 7:30 this evening in the TMU small
lounge. Richard Young will discuss the spring break Mexicali work
project. Dave Schilling and Beryl Ramsey will speak about YWCA
summer projects, which include a Washington Citizens Seminar, a N.
Y. City project, a Berkeley project in Theology and Social Change,
Overseas and International programs.
