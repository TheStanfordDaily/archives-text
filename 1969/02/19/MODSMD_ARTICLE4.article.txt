# GSA Forum
## 
### 
The Graduate Student Association
will sponsor a panel discussion tonight
in TMU 270-1 on "The Problems and
Prospects of Graduate Education in
America." Featured speakers are
Herbert Packer, vice-provost and SES
chairman, Dr. Joseph Katz, executive di
rector of the Institute for the Study of
Human Problems, Virgil Whitaker, dean
of the graduate division, Richard
Young, T.A. in political science and
A.F.T. member, and Barry Askinas,
chairman of the Graduate Student As
sociation.
Tomorrow night at 8 p.m. there will
be a second panel in the TMU Large
Lounge with Dr. Robert Glaser, Dean
of the Medical School, Herbert Packer,
and Barry Askinas.
