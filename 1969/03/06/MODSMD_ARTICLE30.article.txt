# Fencing
## 
### 
The Stanford Fencing
Association will host the Men's
Western Intercollegiate Fencing
Championships Friday and
Saturday in the old Pavilion.
Representatives from 45 Western
collegiate fencing teams will be
battling for top honors.
Competition will start at 9:00
a.m. on both days, with foil finals
set for 6:00 p.m. Friday, eppee
finals at 2:00 p.m. Saturday, and
saber finals 6:00 p .m. Saturday.
Representing Stanford will be
John Keller, Brent Vernon and
Rod Perry. The Indians have a good
chance of finishing high both in the
team championships and the
individual championships. The
winning team will journey to Notre
Dame for the NCAA finals during
spring break.
