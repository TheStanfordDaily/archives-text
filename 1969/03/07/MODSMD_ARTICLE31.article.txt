# Church History
## 
### 
The Pacific Coast of the
American Society of Church
History will hold its spring meeting
on Friday, March 14, in room 214
in the History corner. At 4:00, Dr.
Abraham Friesen of the University
of California at Santa Barbara will
lecture on "Zimmermann and the
Origins of the Marxist
Interpretation of the
Reformation," to be followed at
5:00 by a panel discussion on
"Church History-an Authentic
Independent Discipline." Another
lecture at 8:00, entitled
"Witchcraft and Religion in
Southwest Germany: in the
Sixteenth Century" to be given by
H.C. Erik Midelfort.
