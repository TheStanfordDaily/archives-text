# Packer I11
## 
### 
Herbert L. Packer, vice provost, pro
fessor of law, and chairman of the
Study of Education at Stanford (SES),
suffered a series of life threatening
strokes during the past two weeks.
Doctors say he is "seriously ill, but
there has been some improvement in the
past few days." Packer's first stroke
occured on March 13. He has been at
the Stanford Hospital since.
Packer is in his mid forties.
