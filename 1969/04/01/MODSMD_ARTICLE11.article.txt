# McMahon Assumes New Post
## 
### 
Patrick McMahon, Daily
executive editor, is taking a
position as senior writer effective
today, at his own request.
McMahon will devote most of
his time to several long-range
editorial projects. He will remain
chairman of the Stanford Daily
Publishing Board.
Arrangements for replacing
McMahon have not yet been
completed, but will be released
soon.
