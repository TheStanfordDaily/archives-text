# Scholar Picked
## 
### 
Dr. G. Robert Mason, assistant
professor of surgery at Stanford
University School of Medicine,
has been named a Markle Scholar
in Academic Medicine, the John
and Mary R. Markle Foundation
of New York announced today.
Dr. Mason is one of 25 young
medical scientists in the United
States and Canada chosen for the
honor. As part of the Markle
Scholar Program, the Foundation
awards a five-year stipend totaling
$30,000 to Stanford to "assist in
the development of the Scholar as
a teacher, investigator, or
administrator."
Mason has been a member of
the Stanford faculty since 1966.
