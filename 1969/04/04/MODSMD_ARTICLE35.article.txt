# European Service Issues Catalogue
## 
### 
Stanford European Auto has
published its European Delivery
Catalogue, which is available at
the Sequoia Travel Center. The
catalogue contains detailed
information on the price,
equipment, and delivery of 18
makes of European cars. The
catalogue also describes a car
leasing plan available through
SEA.

Stanford European Auto offers
a variety of services, also
described in the catalogue, in
addition to ordering and arranging
for the delivery of any European
car. They offer comprehensive
automobile insurance without a
surcharge for drivers under 25,
written by the world's largest
insurance company.
