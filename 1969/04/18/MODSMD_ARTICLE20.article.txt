# Dr. Njoka Delayed
## 
### 
The speech on "Bialra's Case
for Self-Determination" by Dr.
Eni Njoku, Vice Chancellor of the
University of Biafra, which was
scheduled for 4 p.m. today in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium has been
postponed indefinitely.
Informed sources said that, due
to the nature of his mission in
Washington, Dr. Jm Njoku would
not be on the West Coast until
next week.
