# Baseball Broadcast
## 
### 
This weekend's baseball
games will be broadcast at
KZSU (90.1 fm, 880 am) from
the Sunken Diamond. Friday's
game against USC will go on
the air at 2:55 p.m. and
Saturday's broadcast of the
UCLA doubleheader will begin
at 12:25 p.m. John Arthur Will
handle the play-by-play.
