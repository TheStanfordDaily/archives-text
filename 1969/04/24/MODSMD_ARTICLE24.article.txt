# Art Exhibit
## 
### 
An exhibition of early Coptic
textiles will be displayed at the
Stanford Art Gallery May 4-May
25. The show includes 50 pieces
of Egyptian ornamental weaving
dating from the late third to the

mid-seventh Century A.D. and
tunic decorations from the late
Roman and Byzantine periods.
The Stanford Art Gallery is
open to the public without charge
from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Tuesday
through Saturday, and from 1 to
5 p.m. Sunday, but is closed
Mondays.
