# Campus Roundup
## 
### 
UNDERGRADUATE SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATIONS for 1969-70
are available in the Financial Aids Office in the Old Union. All
undergrads who wish to apply either for renewal or new awards must
file by Mav 1.
♦ * *
An AFRICAN STUDIES seminar on the Ndendeuli of Southern
Tanzania will be held at 4:15, Tresidder 271.
* * *
A meeting for UIOLOGY MAJORS to discuss core course sequence
and to elect new representatives to the Undergraduate Studies
Committee starts at 4:15 in Jordan Room, Herrin Biology Building.
* * *
The l-CENTER is planning to visit Hewlett-Packard industrial plant
on May 2. Interested engineering students sign up at the I-Center this
week.
* * *
Color and sound highlights of the MEXICO OLYMPICS will be
shown April 30 and May 1, 7:30 and 9:00 at Palo Alto High School.
Admission is $ 1.00.
*, * *
TRYOUTS FOR CON HOME HYDROFOLLIES this Saturday, May
3, at Lake Lag. Contact Ray Rooker, x 2460 evenings only.
* * *
Dr. Y. Zalcstein will speak on SCIENTOLOGY AUDITING at the
weekly Scientology Stanford meeting, today, 7:30 in 270 Tresidder.
* * *
"The Term and Concept of SYMBOLISM IN LITERARY
HISTORY" is the subject of a lecture by Yale professor Rene Wellek,
4:15, Tresidder Large Lounge.
