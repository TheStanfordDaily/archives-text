# Profs Wife Dies
## 
### 
Mrs. Vivian Hoff, wife of Prof.
Nicholas Hoff (Chairman of the
Department of Aeronautics and
Astronautics), died of cancer
today at Memorial Hospital in
New York City, New York.
Before she married Prof. Hoff, she
was secretary to the Dean of Men
at Stanford. Memorial services in
Palo Alto will probably be held on
Thursday, May Ist.
