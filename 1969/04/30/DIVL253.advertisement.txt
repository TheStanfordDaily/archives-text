ELECTIONS. NOMINATIONS ARE NOW BEING

• President
• American Vice-President
• Foreign Vice-President
• 6 American Policy Board Members
• 4 Foreign Policy Board Members

members of the IA must be filed at the I-Center before noon
Friday, May 2.
