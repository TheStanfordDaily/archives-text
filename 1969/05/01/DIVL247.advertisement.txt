I Visit Our
I New Store!
We have a beautiful new store in the
Mt. View Shopping Center, where Grant
Road meets El Camino Real.
There's much more for you to choose
from, with over twice as much space as in
our old Sunnyvale store, now closed. More
of everything — tape recorders, stereos,
parts, CB — and more things, too, such as
pre-recorded tapes in cassettes and 4 and 8
track cartridges.
From now through the end of May we're
celebrating with prize drawings, special
sale prices on many items, and factory
demonstration days.
Come in and help us celebrate—help
yourself to bargains!
