# Girls Track Meet
## 
### 
An intramural men's and
women's track meet will be held
Monday and Wednesday of next
week. Both days' events will begin
at 3:00 p.m. at Angell Field.
Women's events include
running competition in the 50,
100, 220, 440, and 880. Also
scheduled are 440 vard relav

teams and an 880 yard medley
relay. Field events are the high
jump and long jump.
Participants are asked to sign
up with Miss Kalus at the
Women's Physical Education
Department or Barbara Underhill
at Flo Mo (ext. 141). Deadline for
signups is today.
