# Staff Memo
## 
### 
Special message to The Daily
staff: Everyone is invited to Bill's
for a picnic tomorrow.
Stop by the office for details.
Departure time is 12:30 p.m.
Bill's place is in the
mountains- lots of open fields and
lakes, even clear air.
Bill is really keyed. The entire
staff has to come.
Who's Bill?
Come and find out.
