# Journalist Here
## 
### 
Gene Lichentenstein, a
correspondent for the London
Economist will be staying at the
Alpha Sigma Phi House as a guest
resident Sunday and Monday.
A journalist and film maker,
Mr. Lichentenstein will give two
showings of his documentary film
on college students and drugs
entitled "A Nice Kid Like You."
One showing will be Sunday night
at 7:30 at the Delta Kappa
Epsilon House and the other will
be at 4:15 Monday in Tresidder,
Room 214.
