# Paddlers
## 
### 
The first annual Stanford Surf
Club paddling contest results have
been announced. Individual
overall winner was Bruce
Zimmerman in a time of 5:45.
Kirk Shoemacher (5:55) was
second, and Fred Haywood (6:00)
was third. The winning relay team
was Shoemacher, Haywood, and
Homer Oatman.
