# SDS Meeting Sunday Night
## 
### 
Stanford SDS will meet in the
small lounge of Tresidder
Memorial Union this Sunday
evening at 7 :30 p.m. The chapter
will discuss the upcoming
convention called by the Black
Panther Party.
Entitled "The United Front
Against Fascism," the conference
will convene to discuss such topics
as community control of the
police and the current repression
against militant blacks and
students. All interested members
of the community are invited.
