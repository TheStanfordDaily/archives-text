# New Group Of Employees
## 
### 
An as-yet unnamed group of
Stanford employees, in opposition
to the Stanford Employees
Association (SEA), will hold its
first meeting Monday night at
7:30 in TMU.
The purpose of the meeting is
to discuss employees' needs and
to organize the new group.
Problems such as wages, child
care, medical care, on-campus
housing, job security, and tenure
will also be discussed.
Roland Finston is temporary
chairman of the organization.
