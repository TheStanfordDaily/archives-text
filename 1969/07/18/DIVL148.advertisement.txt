SUNDAY, JULY 20, 1969
9:00 A.M.
COMMUNION CELEBRATION
11:00 A.M.
UNIVERSITY PUBLIC WORSHIP
Preacher: Dr. Wayne R. Hood
Pacific School of Religion, Berkeley
5:00 P.M.
MASS
7:30 P.M.
FILM SERIES: "The Hangman"—a brilliant color fantasy based
on Maurice Ogden's famous poem. It deals very effectively
with the issue of corporate guilt through inaction. The Hang
man's final words, "First the alien, then the Jew/I do no
more than you let me do."
"The Whole World is Watching''—a recent release dealina
with the events at the Chicago Democratic Convention. It
provides a good blend of film taken at the demonstration
with an attempt to obtain the political and philosophical
rationale of what took place from various 'eaders in the
Mevement.
