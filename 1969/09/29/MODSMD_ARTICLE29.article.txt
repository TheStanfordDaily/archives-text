# Sports Shorts: Braves Clinch Tie
## 
### 
ATLANTA (AP>-The Atlanta
Braves, streaking to their ninth
straight victory, clinched a tie for
the National League West Division
championship Sunday with a 4-2
triumph over San Diego.
San Francisco kept its slim
hopes alive by beating Los Ange
les 8-1. The Braves need a victory
in either of their last two games
against Cincinnati or a San Fran
cisco loss in any of its last three
games against San Diego to clinch
the crown.
