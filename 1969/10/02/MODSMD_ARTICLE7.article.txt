# Correction
## 
### 
Contrary to various reports,
including one in yesterday's Daily,
there is no University foreign
language requirement for
graduation at the present time.
The Academic Council will
vote tomorrow whether to submit
a suggested new language
requirement to an all-faculty mail
vote. Any new requirement would
not be in effect until sometime
after the results of the vote is
made known, H. Donald
Winbigler, Academic Secretary,
told (he Dailv yesterday.
