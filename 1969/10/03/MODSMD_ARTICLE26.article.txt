# Six Groups In Concert Sunday
## 
### 
On Sunday, Oct. sth, SCORE
(Stanford Committee on Radical
Education) will present in
concert: It's A Beautiful Day,
Cold Blood, Mike Bloomfield,
Sanpaku, Old Davis, and Stone
Rock Outcrop. The benefit,
produced by the Midpeninsula
Free University, will go on from
12:30-6:00 p.m. in Frost
Amphitheater and promises to be
a beautiful day. Donations of
$2.00 are requested as a
contribution to A3M's Legal
Defense Fund. Stemming from
the SRI demostrations last Spring,
over 100 people are facing trials
and heavy penalties. Money for
bail and legal counsel is
desperately needed.
