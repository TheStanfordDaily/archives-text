# Council Has Student Staff
## 
### 
This year the ASSU Council of
Presidents will have a staff of
students engaged in general
administrative work as well as
specific projects.
All students and freshmen, in
particular, are encouraged to
apply for a staff position.
Applications are now available in
the ASSU office in Tresidder
Union and are due on Wednesday,
October 8.

Some of the projects involving
staff members will concern
student evaluation of teaching and
courses, organizing student
participation in the Stanford
chapter of the Urban Coalition,
writing articles for the Daily, and
investigating the possibilities for
setting up a child day-care center
and a student co-op.
A staff appointment will
involve up to six hours of work
per week.
