# Taylor Group
## 
### 
The West Coast premier of Paul
Taylor's dance "Private Domain"
will highlight the appearance of
the Paul Taylor Modern Dance
Company in Mem Aud Monday
and Wednesday evenings.
Monday's performance will
include "Piece Period," "Private
Domain," and its complimentary
work, "Public Domain."
''Post Meridian,''
"Apocalypse," and"From Sea to
Shining Sea," will comprise
Wednesday's performance.
Tickets can be purchased at
Mem Aud or Tresidder, Student
prices are $3, $2.25, and $1.50.
General Admission is $3.25,
$2.75, and $2.
