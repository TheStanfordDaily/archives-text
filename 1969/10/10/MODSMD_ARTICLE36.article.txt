# 'Money' Playing In Palo Alto
## 
### 
"Money," an off-Broadway
musical comedy which follows the
adventures of a modern Candide
trying to please the girl of his
dreams, is currently appearing at
8:30 P.M. Fridays and Saturdays

at Rudolfo's Steak House, in Palo
Alto.
The Daily News, a new theater
group in the Palo Alto-Menlo Park
area which plays cabaret theater,
is presenting the musical.
