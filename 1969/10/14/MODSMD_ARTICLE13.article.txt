# Contribute!
## 
### 
THE DAILY will gladly accept all
written contributions to the opinion
page from all sectors of the Stanford
ommunity. Letters, columns, poems,
and other articles should be
typewritten, double-spaced, and typed
011 a 60-space line. Although space
limitations sometimes necessitates
editing of copy, THE DAILY will
attempt to preserve the form and
content of all material submitted for
publication.
