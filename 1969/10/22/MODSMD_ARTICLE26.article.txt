# Tickets
## 
### 
There will be a very limited
number of tickets to the rooting
section available to Stanford
students for the UCLA game.
These tickets will go on sale at
10:00 a.m. on Saturday at the
Special Student Booth at the
Stadium. Cost is $1.50 each.
Big Game tickets for the
rooting section are also available.
These tickets will be sold
beginning Monday, October 27,
and will cost $2.50 each.
