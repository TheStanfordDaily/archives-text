# Davis, Nader, Finch To Speak Locally
## 
### 
Robert Finch, Angela Davis,
and Ralph Nader will each be
speaking this weekend in the Bay
Area.
Finch, appearing as a guest of
the Stanford Young Republicans,
will speak briefly and answer
questions in Dinkelspiel
Auditorium tomorrow at 4:15
p.m. Last Saturday Finch
announced that cyclamate, the
dietetic sweetner in soft drinks,
would be banned from sale
starting next year.
Consumer protector Ralph
Nader will discuss "Product
Safety" as it relates to autos and
household appliances at a public
meeting tomorrow at 8 p.m. in
Marina Junior High School, San
Francisco.
Nader's appearance is being
sponsored by the Association of
California Consumers.
Nader is nationally known for
his crusades against unsafe
automobiles, deceptive packaging,
worthless warranties and other

forms of dubious business
practices.
Angela Davis, the black
Assistant Professor at UCLA
whose philosophy course regained
credit yesterday, is making a four
day speaking tour of the Bay
Area. Today she is speaking at
11:00 a.m. in the Peristyle of
California State College at
Hayward, and at 1 :00 p.m. in
Concert Hall of the Music
Building at Mills College in
Oakland.
Tomorrow Miss Davis will be
speaking in Sproul Plaza at the
University of California, Berkeley,
at noon, and at 8:00 p.m. in the
auditorium of Roosevelt Junior
High School, San Jose. Sunday
she will conclude her tour with
appearances at 11:00 a.m. in
Glide Memorial Methodist
Church, and at a 3:00 p.m.
reception for the black
community hosted by the
California Association for
Afro-American Lducation at the
Sun-Reporter building, 1366 Turk
Street, San Francisco.
