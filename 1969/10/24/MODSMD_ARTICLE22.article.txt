# Notes
## 
### 
The man who panned
cyclamate as a sweetener in diet
soft drinks and low-calorie foods
speaks on campus this afternoon.
Robert Finch, Secretary of
Health, Education and Welfare,
speaks in Dinklespiel Auditorium
at 4:15 p.m.
The question-and-answer
session is sponsored by the
Stanford Young Republicans.
Finch is no stranger to higher
education in California. He was
formerly on the UC Board of
Regents, the Board of Trustees for
the state college system, and an
instructor at Palos Verdes and
Marymount Colleges.
