# Girls Swim
## 
### 
The Stanford girls' swim team
was winner of the Northern
California Women's Intercollegiate
Council Swim Relays
Championship. In the meet held
on Friday, October 24, at Chico
State, the girl's swim team scored
80 points to tie with San Jose.
The team took two firsts, three
seconds, one third, one fourth and
two fifths. The team is coached
by Marianna Fowler. The next
home meet will be tomorrow
against University of the Pacific,
UC Davis, and University of Santa
Clara. The meet will be held in the
Women's Pool and everyone is
invited.
