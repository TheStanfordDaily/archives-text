# Guest Sermon
## 
### 
Dr. Tom Driver, Professor of
Theology and Literature at Union
Theological Seminary, drama
critic, and published author will
preach at University Public
Worship in Memorial Church this
Sunday, November 9, at 11:00
a.m.
A frequent lecturer on college
and university campuses whom
Dean Napier calls "really unusual
and interesting," Dr. Driver will
also give a public lecture on
"Drama and Theology" Monday,
Nov. 10, at 2:15 in History 214.
