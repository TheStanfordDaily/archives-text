# Zodiac Calls
## 
### 
Palo Alto police received a call
on their emergency line at 9:05
p.m., Tuesday from an indicidual
claiming he was Zodiac, who said
he planned to come on the
Stanford campus in ten minutes.
Two hours later nothing had
happened. Palo Alto police
received a report of sounds of a
firearm discharge in the vicinity
of the Veteran's Building near the
Southern Pacific depot, but this
was not confirmed on
investigation.
Sgt. Herman W. Williams of the
Stanford Police Department said
all available units were alerted and
patrols intensified.
