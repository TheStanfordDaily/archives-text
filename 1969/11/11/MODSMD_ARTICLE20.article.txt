# News Notes
## 
### 
Dr. Robert Skidelsky will
deliver a lecture "The Future of
America in Europe" tonight at 8
p.m. in Tresidder, Room 270. He
will trace the beginnings of
America's political and economic
commitment after World War 11,
will define the present situation,
especially in relation to the NATO
and Germany questions and the
continuing effect of De Gaulle's
policies, and will explore the
possible directions for future
action.
A research fellow for Oxford's
Nuffield College, Dr. Skildesky
presently is a visiting professor at
the John Hopkins School for
Advanced International Studies in
Washington.
