# Senate Tonight
## 
### 
There will be a special meeting
of the ASSU Senate tonight in
room 75 of the Business School.
First on the agenda will be the
selection of the Nominations
Committee, which nominates
students for appointment to all ad
hoc and standing committees of
the University President and the
Academic Senate. Applications
for this Committee, due today,
are available in the ASSU office.
Students seeking positions should
attend tonight's meeting. •
