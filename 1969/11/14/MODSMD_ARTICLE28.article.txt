# Gaieties Returns
## 
### 
Ram's Head "Gaieties" is
returning to Stanford after a
year's absence. The
student-written musical
comedy extravaganza, which
was endowed with national
prizes two years ago, is this
year called "The Carnival
Down Below."
Tickets for the show are on
sale now at White Plaza and
Tresidder Box Office.
Performance dates are
November 19, 20, 21 and 22.
Curtain time for all shows
except Friday is 8:30 p.m.
Go to the Bonfire on Friday
and then come at 9:00.
