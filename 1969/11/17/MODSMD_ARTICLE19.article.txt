# SDS To Look At US, Japan
## 
### 
Tonight at 7:30 in Tresidder
large lounge, two speakers,
Seiichiro Takagi and James Shoch,
will discuss the U.S.-Japan
economic involvement in Asia and
the controversial U.S.Japan
Security Treaty. The program,
sponsored by Stanford SDS,
coincides with the arrival in
Washington of Japan's Prime
Minister Eisaku Sato.
Sato is journeying to the capital
to negotiate with President Nixon
about the return of the Ryukyu
Islands, particularly Okinawa, to
Japan.
