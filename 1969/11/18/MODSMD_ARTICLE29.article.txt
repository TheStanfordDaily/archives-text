# Suicide
## 
### 
Dr. Leo A. Sapirstein, 50, a
radiologist at the Stanford
Medical School, was found dead
in his BarronPark home Sunday,
the apparent victim of suicide,
according to Santa Clara County
sheriff's deputies.
Dr. Sapirstein's death was
discovered by his son Julian, 20,
who said he last saw his father at
1:30 p.m. when he left their
home. When Julian returned at 5
p.m., he found his father lying on
a bed in his bedroom-office. A
bottle of diurnal was by the bed
and several Seconal capsules were
on a desk, deputies said.
Dr. Sapirstein was dead on
arrival at Stanford Hospital.
