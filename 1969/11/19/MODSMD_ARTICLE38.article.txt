# Award
## 
### 
At the American Institute of
Chemical Engineers Award
Luncheon held October 27th, Mr.
Robert W. Dunn of Stauffer
Chemical Company presented
Rand Hunt Shipley with the
Stauffer Engineering Award of
$1,000 for "outstanding Senior
Chemical Engineer" at Stanford.
A gift of $2,000 was also given to
the Department.
Shipley, who is at the top of his
class in Chemical Engineering, is a
member of Tau Beta Pi, honorary
engineering society, Kappa Sigma
fraternity, and has studied for six
months at Stanford's Oerseas
Campus in Britain.
