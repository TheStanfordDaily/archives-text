# Lacrosse
## 
### 
The Stanford Lacrosse Team is
holding its fall quarter
organization meeting tonight at
7:30 p.m. in Room 275 of
Tresidder Memorial Union. The
team is looking for new players
and invites all interested people,
both those experienced and those
wishing to learn, to attend this
important meeting.
Team membership is open to all
undergraduate students. The team
is a member of the Northern
California Lacrosse Association
and last year enjoyed an 8-2-1
season, for the third straight year
capturing the West Coast
championship. This year plans are
in the making for weekend trips
to Utah, Denver, and Los Angeles.
