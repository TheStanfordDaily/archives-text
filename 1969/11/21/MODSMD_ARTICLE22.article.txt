# 'The Wager'
## 
### 

UNUSUAL RELATIONSHIP-A comic drama of temptation,
seduction and non communication, "The Wager" will return to
Stanford for eight performances November 26-29 and December 3-6.
The show is again directed by William Frank father and features
Frank father. Bob Bates and Sheila Mahoney and Gene Engene
(above). The play explores the curious romantic circumstances
surrounding the lives of four graduate students.
Tickets are available at Memorial Hall Box Office.
