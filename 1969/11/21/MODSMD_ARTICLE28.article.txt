# Appointment
## 
### 
Economist Rita R. Campbell, a
senior fellow at the Hoover
Institution, has been named
chairman of one of three principal
study groups of the Presidential
Citizens' Advisory Council on the
Status of Women.
President Nixon appointed the
20-member advisory group in
August, to serve as a clearinghouse
for private institutions,
organizations and individuals
interested in improvement of
economic and social conditions
for women.
