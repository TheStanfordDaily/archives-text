# Surf Club
## 
### 
The Stanford Surf n Club
dropped their first meet of the
year on Sunday to the University
of Santa Clara Broncos.
Santa Clara topped the Indians,
356-233, in the meet held at
Waddel Creek, 15 miles north of
Santa Cruz. Surf conditions
featured 3-6 foot waves with poor
form.
High man for Stanford was
club vice-president Homer
Oatman.
