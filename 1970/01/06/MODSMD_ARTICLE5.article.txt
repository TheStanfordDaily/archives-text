# Phil Ochs
## 
### 
Folksinger Phil Ochs will
perform Thursday night at 8
p.m. in Memorial Auditorium
in a benefit concert for the
Stanford Legal Defense Fund.
Ochs is touring the country
on behalf of the Chicago
Conspiracy 7, who are now on
trial for violating the Civil
Rights Act.
Ochs, a 29-year-old Texan,
sings folksongs, protest songs,
satirical songs, sad songs, and
happy songs. He performed at
the November moratorium
rally in San Francisco. 1
Tickets are $1.50 at the <
door.
