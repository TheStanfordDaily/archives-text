# Tribe on TV
## 
### 
The Stanford Indians meet the
University of Washington Huskies
at Seattle, Saturday, January 10,
on the first of a series of
live—and—direct basketball
telecasts on KBHK TV, Channel
44.
Airtime is 8:00 PM to
conclusion. The sportscast is in
color.
Bob Murphy will provide game
play—by—play from the Hec
Edmundson Pavilion, Seattle. The
telecast is a production of Kaiser
Sports.
