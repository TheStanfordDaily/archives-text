# Glaser Speaks at Tresidder
## 
### 
Dr. Robert J. Glaser, Dean of
the Stanford Medical School and
former acting president of the
university will be speaking tonight
in Tresidder Union. Doctor Glaser
is a member of the National
Academy of Sciences 15 man
National Advisory Committee on
Higher Education which explores
relationships between colleges,
universities and the federal
government. He has been
instrumental in effecting major
curricular revisions in the medical
school and has taught several
freshman seminars.
His talk is sponsored by the
Stanford Pre-Medical Society. All
members of the Stanford
