# Anli-ROTC Rally
## 
### 
J
Liberation week activities
today include an anti-ROTC
rally, a march to President
Pitzer's office, and a tour of
the ROTC facilities. Speaking
at the noon rally in White Plaza
will be Art Busse and former
ROTC cadet Paul Rupert.
Afterwards petitions calling for
the elimination of ROTC will
be presented to Pitzer.
