# Rep. Lowenstein To Speak On Monday
## 
### 
Allard Lowenstein, Democratic Congressman from the sth district
of New York, will be on campus Monday, February 23. Congressman
Lowenstein was a leader in the Dump Johnson campaign in 1968 and
also served on the faculty of Stanford and the University of North
Carolina. He is also the author of the book, Brutal Mandate. He will
be eating dinner Monday in Trancos House, Wilbur Hall and is
tentatively scheduled to speak at 8:00 P.M. in Memorial Auditorium.
