# Overseas Campus Subcommittees Set
## 
### 
Appointments to the subcommittees representing each of the
overseas campuses in the Committee on Foreign Study Programs have
been made. The chairman of the committee, professor Morris
Zelditch, urges students for ideas for changes in policy on individual
campuses and specific questions about such policies to consult the
appropriate subcommittee.
—AUSTRIA: Professor Walter Sokel (German), Chairman; Prof.
Lorenz Eitner (Art); Prof. David Halliburton (English); Prof. Lewis
Spitz (History); Joe Cordes, John Hughes, Dorothy Rosenberg.
-BRITAIN: Prof. Raymond Wolfinger (Poly Sci.), Chairman; Prof.
Paul David (Economics); Prof Ronald Rebholz (English); Prof. Peter
Stansky (History); Mike Duncheon; Mike Fraser; Dave Reinhard.
—FRANCE: Prof. Michael Cartwright (French), chairman; Prof.
Albert Elsen (Art); Prof. Albert Guerard (English); Prof. Ronald
McKinnon (Economics); Renton Rolph; Bill Durham; Mike Schwartz.
—GERMANY: Prof. Kurt Forster (Art), Chairman; Prof. Peter
Foulkes (German); Herbert Lindenberger (Comparative Literature);
Prof. Hans Weiler (Poly Sci and Education); Forrest Frank; Carl
Anderson; Carol Jessop.
— ITALY: Prof. Lorie Tarshis (Economics), chairman; Prof. John
Goheen (Philosophy); Prof. Dwight Miller (Art); Prof. Wilfred Stone
(English); Rich Ware; Gordon Davidson; Kim Garlin.
