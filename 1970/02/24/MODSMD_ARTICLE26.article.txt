# Film Program Slated Tonight
## 
### 
Being presented tonight at 8
p.m. in Memorial Auditorium is a
program of films from the
distinguished National Film Board
of Canada. All films are in 35mm
and represent the work of visiting
communications instructor Ron
Alexander.
The National Film Board is
recognized as among the best
producers of documentaries in the
world and this evening's program
offers films on widely varying
subjects. There is no admission
charge.
