# Prereg
## 
### 
Time schedules for spring
quarter are available at the
Information window of the Old
Union.
Students will begin
pre-registration by getting their
study list cards from their advisers
on Wednesday, Thursday, or
Friday, February 25, 26, or 27.
Those students who neglect to
pick up their study list card on
one of those days cannot
preregister.
Signing of class lists in the
Registrar's Office will take place
on Monday, March 2 beginning at
8:00 a.m. through Friday, March
6 ending at 5:00 p.m. Juniors,
seniors, and graduate students
with initials L-R will sign lists
Monday morning, March 2; S-Z
Monday afternoon; A D Tuesday
morning, March 3; and E-K
Tuesday afternoon.
Freshmen and sophomores will
sign class lists according to the
time stamped on their study list
cards.
