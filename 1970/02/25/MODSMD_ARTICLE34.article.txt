# Revenue Sharing Panel
## 
### 
The Graduate Economic
Society is sponsoring a conference
on "Federal Financing of State
and Local Governments" to be
held on Friday, February 27 at
Tresidder Union (Room 271)
beginning at 1:15 p.m. Professor
Coen of the Department of
Economics will be moderating a
panel of speakers that includes

Joseph Pechman of the Brookings
Institute, George Break of the
Department of Econ at Berkeley,
Henry Levin of the School of
Education at Stanford, and Brian
Fry of the Department of Political
Science at Stanford.
All are welcome to attend and
participate.
