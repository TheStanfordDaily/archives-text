a.m. University Public Worship Service this Sunday will be
Congressman Pete McCloskey. Currently representing the 11th
District, Rep. McCloskey received his B.A. and LL.B degrees from
Stanford. He is well known for his interest in ecology, the War, and
sinking the Good Ship Lollipop.
