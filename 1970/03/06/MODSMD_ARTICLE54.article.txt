# Student Killed
## 
### 
A senior at the French
overseas campus died Tuesday
in Rome from injuries he
received in a motorcycle
accident.
Richard Allen Clark, 21,
was with a group of other
students on a field trip to
Rome. He and a companion
had rented motorcycles and
were touring outside Rome
\yhen he was sideswiped by an
automobile.
Clark, an economics major,
received massive head and
body injuries. He never
recovered consciousness and
died at a Roman hospital.
Clark was a resident of San
Diego, Calif.
