# Council Meeting
## 
### 
The Academic Council moots
today in its regular quarterly
meeting at 4:15 p.m., in the
Physics Lecture Hall (ph 100).
The only business scheduled is
a report from the President
although the anti-ROTC
movement has declared its
intention to attend the meeting
and express its views.
Results of the mail ballot of the
Council on the Army ROTC
credit issue were released Monday.
The Council voted to allow
limited credit for Army ROTC
courses bv a vote of 390 to 373.
