# Come And Get It
## 
### 
Obviously excitement doesn't
lure many of you into our offices.
How about money? We have it,
and you no doubt want it. If
you're shy about being greedy,
don't worry. We require
remarkably little groveling to earn
it.
Come to our Monday meetings
at noon. The lunches are free, and
we'll even let your dog stay. And
remember folks, it's just a shot
awav.
