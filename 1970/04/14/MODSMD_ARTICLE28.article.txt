A Special feature on military
involvement in Stanford
University policy will be
presented on KZSU night news
report tonight. Cynthia Cherbak
will interview Harry Cleaver,
economics graduate student and
author of the SRI minority
report.
Cleaver will deal with questions
such as how do local industries
deal with Stanford, particularly in
the closed circuit television
research which will illustrate how
Stanford is bought by the military
and industry.
