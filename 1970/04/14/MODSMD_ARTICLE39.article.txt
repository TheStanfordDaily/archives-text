# Free Concert
## 
### 
Cellist Neal LaMonaco and
pianist Marilyn Thompson will
present a free concert at 8 p.m.
tonight in Dinkelspiel
Auditorium.
The program will consist of
seven variations on the theme
from Mozart's "Magic Flute" by
Beethoven, "Sonata No. 3 in G
Major" by Boccherini, "Sonata
for Cello and Piano" by
Hindemith, a group of dances
entitled "Balletti, Giga and
Corrente" by Degli Antoni, and
"Sonata No. 1 in E Minor" by
Brahms.
