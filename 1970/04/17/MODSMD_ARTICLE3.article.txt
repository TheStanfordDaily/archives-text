# Church Speaks Tonight
## 
### 
U.S. Senator Frank Church
(D.-Idaho) will address a public
meeting at Stanford today at 8
p.m. in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Senator Church, a Stanford
alumnus, will discuss "Of
Presidents and Caesars: The
Decline of Constitutional
Government in America."
His talk marks the opening of a
two-day Golden Anniversary
celebration of Stanford's Political
Science Department.
The youthful senator will be
the guest of honor at a Faculty
Club coffee hour after his talk.
Prof. Gabriel A. Almond will
convene the Saturday session at
9:30 a.m. in Tresidder Union.
Panel discussion of "The
Challenge to Pluralism in
American Life" follows.
