# USG Applications
## 
### 
A course which is outside of
regular departmental offerings
may be initiated by faculty, staff
and students and proposed as an
Undergraduate Special. Proposals
must be consistent with the
academic standards of the
University, fulfill the conditions
of approval, and be sponsored by
a member of the Academic
Council.
The new, more detailed

application form for
Undergraduate Specials is now
available from Pam Wright in
600-T, the temporary building
next to Memorial Church.
Applicants wishing
pre-registration signup and
publicity should return their
completed proposals by May 1 for
early consideration. The FINAL
DEADLINE for Fall Quarter
courses is May 13.
