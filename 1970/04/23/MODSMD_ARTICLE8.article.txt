# Election Today
## 
### 
Voting on ASSU offices and .student referenda "fids today at 4 o'clock".
Yesterday 3271 students voted during the first day of balloting, 69 more
than for the first day of the primary elections.
Students are voting on student body officers, senior class officers, an
ASSU program fee, and referenda on the speech and drama department at
Stanford and the status of the University's General Motors stock proxies.
Polling places are as follows: Post Office, Engineering Corvier, and
Physics Tank open 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.; Computation Center and Medical
School Lounge open 11 a.m. to 2 p.m.; Law School Lounge and Business
School Lounge open 11 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Elections Commissioner Jan Westfall stressed last r.ight that no write-in
votes are permitted and asked that voters not fold their ballots, so thai
counting will be expedited.
