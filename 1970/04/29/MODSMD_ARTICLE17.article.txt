# BSU
## 
### 
The Stanford and San
Francisco State Black Student
Unions are co-sponsoring a "Black
Unism" conference this weekend
on campus. More than 300
students from across the country
are expected to attend the
three-day conference which will
feature Nathan Hare, editor of the
Black Scholar, Carleton Goodlet,
publisher of the Sun-Reporter, St.
Clair Drake of the Afro-American
Studies Program, Jim Garrett,
Head of Black Studies Dept. of
Federal City College, and Hosea
Williams of the SCLC.
