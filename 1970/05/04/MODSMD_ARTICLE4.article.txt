# SCLC Results
## 
### 
Two freshman and one
sophomore have been elected to
the SCLC (Student Conduct
Legislative Council) according to
results released yesterday.
Rocky (James L.) Barber,
freshman in Branner; Cynthia
Cherbak, sophomore in Grove;
and Chris Hocker, freshman in
Branner were elected in balloting
held April 16 and I 7.
Results from the election were
delayed due to the complicated
computer program that was
necessary to count the votes by
the Hare System of proportional
representation.
