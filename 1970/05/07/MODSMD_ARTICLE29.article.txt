# Paychecks
## 
### 
Paychecks will be
distributed Thursday to all
departments which are open,
through Interdepartmental
Mail. If a department is closed,
checks will be brought to the
I.D. mail office in the
basement of Encina Hall, from
which they will be distributed
at 1:30 p.m. Entrance to the
I.D. mail office should be
through Encina's back door,
and identification will be
required before checks will be
distributed.
Controller's Office
