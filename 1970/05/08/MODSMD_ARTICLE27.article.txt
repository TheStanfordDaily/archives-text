# Passport Photos
## 
### 
The Stanford Daily Passport
Photo Service gives you 12
photos for $3.50 with a rate
reduction after that. We're
open 1 to 3 Mon-Fri. Located
in the Storke Publications
building where the Daily is put
together. Appointments not
needed. 5 days delivery or
money back. Information, call
(afternoons and evenings only,
please) 321-2300 ext. 4634.
