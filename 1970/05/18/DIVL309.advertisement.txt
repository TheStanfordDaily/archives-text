Today's anniversaries are watershed marks for the
United States. Seventy four years ago the Supreme
Court reached what can he called a low-water mark in
the Plessy vs Ferguson decision ("separate hut equal").
Thirty seven rears ago the Tennessee Valley Authority
was created. It was literally and figuratively a high water
mark.
