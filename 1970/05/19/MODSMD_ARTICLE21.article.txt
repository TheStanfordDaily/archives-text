# Pre-Reg
## 
### 
Time schedules for autumn
quarter are available at the
Information window of the
Old Union.
Students will begin
preregistration by getting their
study list cards from their
advisers on Wednesday,
Thursday, or Friday, May 20,
21, or 22. Those students who
neglect to pick up their study
list card on one of those days
cannot preregister.
Signing of class lists in the
Registrar's Office will take
place on Monday, May 25
beginning at 8:00 a.m. through
Friday, May 26 ending at 5:00
p.m. Juniors, seniors, and
graduate students with initials
S-Z will sign lists Monday
morning, May 25; A-D Monday
afternoon; E-K Tuesday
morning, May 26; and L-R
Tuesday afternoon.
Freshmen and sophomores
will sign class lists according to
the time stamped on their
study list cards.
Juniors who have not yet
declared a major (Division 3,
00) will have to pick up their
study list cards in the
Undergraduate Advising Office,
Old Union, Room 131, in
order to Pre-reg.
