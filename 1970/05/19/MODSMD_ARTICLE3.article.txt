# Hearing Set
## 
### 
The President's Advisory
Committee on ROTC Affairs
will hold its second hearing on
Friday from 4 to 6 p.m. in
Tresidder.
Testimony will be heard on
basic issues of the termination
or retention of ROTC and
credit arrangements needed hy
students currently enrolled in
ROTC programs.
Individuals or group
representatives who wish to
testify should write the
Committee before Thursday at
P.O. Box 2329, Stanford.
Letters should include the
specific questions which will be
addressed and the main points
of the intended testimony.
