# New Phi Beta Kappa Members
## 
### 
At its mooting yesterday, the Stanford chapter of Phi Beta Kappa
elected 140 new members from the class of 1970. These new
members, together with those elected last fall, constitute 12% of the
class. The approximate gradepoint average necessary for election this
year was 3.60. All new members elected this year will be initiated at a
ceremony to be held at Dinkelspiel Auditorium at 8:00 p.m. on
Saturday, June 13th. Philip Rhinelander, Professor of Philosophy and
Humanities, will give the address. A reception at Tresidder for new
initiates and their families will follow the ceremony. The new
members are:
