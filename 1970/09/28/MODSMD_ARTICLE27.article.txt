BOWLING:- The Stanford
Intercollegiate Bowling Teams will
be holding a get-acquainted night
at the Tresidder Memorial Union
Lanes between 8:30 and 10:00
p.m. tonight. All twelve spots on
both intercollegiate teams are
open for qualifiers.
Team members will be selected
on the basis of four criteria: past
bowling experience, natural
ability, attitude, and scoring in a 9
game rolloff to begin Monday,
October 5 th.
Two highly qualified
instructors will be coaching the
bowling teams this year. Bill
Bradbury, a teaching pro at Mel's
Bowl in Redwood City, will be
joining Mrs. Shirley Schoof of the
Women PE Department.
All interested bowlers
(minimum average is 170 for men
and 130 for women) should bring
a resume of past bowling
experience to the 'fresidder Lanes
tonight at 8 :30.
