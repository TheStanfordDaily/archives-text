# OVERSEAS CAMPUS WEEK
## 
### 
Overseas Campus Week,
sponsored by the Overseas
Campus Board will be held
October 11-17. As part of the
week-long program, presentations
will be given on each campus.
Presentation Schedule
Austria—October 11
Germany-October 1 2
Britain—October 13
France—October 14
Italy -October 15
All five presentations will be
held in Room 281 of Tresidder
Union at 8 p.m.
Today's articles on France and
Austria will be followed by stories
on the Stanford campuses in Italy,
Germany and Britain in next
week's Daily.
