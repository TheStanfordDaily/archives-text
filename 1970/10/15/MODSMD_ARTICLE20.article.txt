# Barbara Ward To Lecture Here
## 
### 
Barbara Ward, British writer
and contributing editor of The
Economist, will deliver the Camp
Memorial Lectures this quarter.
Miss Ward, the Albert
Schweitzer professor in
humanities at Columbia
University, will give three lectures
on the theme, "A Human
Planet?"
She will speak at 8 p.m. Oct.
16 on "A World Out of Balance";
at 3 p.m. Oct. 18 on "The
Penalties of Second Place"; and at
8 p.m. Oct. 19 on "Development
and Survival." All of the lectures
will be given in the auditorium of
the Nathan Cummings Art
Building and are open to the
public.
Miss Ward has worked at the
BBC, was a visiting scholar at
Harvard, and is a member of the
Pontifical Commission For Justice
and Peace.
