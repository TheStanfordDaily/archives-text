# Ski Note
## 
### 
The Stanford Ski Club will
have its first meeting tonight at 8
p.m. in the Tresidder large lounge.
A ski flick will be shown to get
fanatic skiers into a skiing frenzy.
Refreshments will also be served.
This year as in the past the
club has a large cabin at Lake
Tahoe for use of members. Later
in the year the club will host its
Bth annual Winter Carnival which
last year attracted over 400
students to the slopes of Squaw
Valley.
