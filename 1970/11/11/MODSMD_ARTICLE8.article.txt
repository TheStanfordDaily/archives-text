Mervin Field
Mervin Field, head of the
California Poll which predicted
Wilson Riles' defeat of Max
Rafferty, will analyze the results
of last week's election at 8 p.m.
tonight in the Art Auditorium.
The event is sponsored by
Stanford Community Forum '70.
"Political Prisoners"
A forum and workshop
on political prisoners will be held
tonight at 8 p.m. in the ZAP
House. The program will include
speakers on Los Siete, the Soledad
Brothers, the California prison
strike, and local political
prisoners.
During the evening, workshops
will be formed to draw up
proposals for the Revolutionary
People's constitutional
Convention to be held in
Washingotn, D. C., at the end of
November.

Murray Bookchin
The ecological advantages of
decentralized social organization
and technology figure
prominently in the politics of
Murray Bookchin who will speak
tonight at 7:30 p.m. in Tresidder
Large Loung. He will discuss
"Ecology and Post-Scarcity
Anarchy."
Bookchin was a pioneer in the
ecology movement. The author of
two books on the subject written
in the mid-60's, he also has been
responsible for bringing a radical
perspective to bear on the
problem.
After his speech, there will be
an informal discussion at the Full
Circle Coffee House on University
Avenue.
