# New Editor
## 
### 
Felicity Barringer was elected Editor
of Volume 159 of the Daily in a staff
election yesterday.
Barringer is a third-year
undergraduate majoring in English. She
has worked on the Daily for over two
years, serving as Editorial Page Editor
and Feature Page Editor during her
tenure. Barringer covered Faculty
Senate meetings last quarter for the
Daily as well as serving on the panels
for Lyman's press conferences.
Barringer will serve as Editor for
Volume 159 of the paper which is
printed from February 1 until June.
