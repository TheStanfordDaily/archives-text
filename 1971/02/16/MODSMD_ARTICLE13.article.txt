THE STANFORD DAILY is owned and published
by the Associated Students of Stanford University
Monday through Friday during the academic year
except dead and finals weeks.
Subscription: $7 for one quarter, $20 for three
quarters.
(Entered as second class matter
at the Post Office of Palo Alto,
California 94302, under the Act
of March 3, 1879.)
