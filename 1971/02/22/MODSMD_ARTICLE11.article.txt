PETITIONS: CHANGING STUDY
LIST: With the change in the grading
system, the need for petitions has
changed. Please observe the following
guide lines. DO PETITION: 1) when
you add a course, 2) when you alter
the unit value, 3) when you change
sections, 4) when you want to drop a
course which will take you below 12
units. DO NOT PETITION: when you
want to drop a course but your course
load will still be 12 units or more. (Be
sure to inform the instructor when you
drop his course.) A petition may do
either of two things; inform the
Registrar's Office of a change (1,2, or
3) or ask permission for a change (4).
UNDERGRADUATES ONLY: A

petition to carry fewer than 12 units
will only be approved for reasons of
health (recommended the Student
Health Service), employment or
domestic responsibility (recommended
by Dean of Students Office). The only
effect of such an approved petition is
to relieve the student of responsibility
for the quarter in question so tar as
academic standing in concerned.
