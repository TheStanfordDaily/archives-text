# Pre-Reg
## 
### 
Time Schedules for Spring quarter
are available at the information window
of the Registrar's Office in the lobby of
the Old Union.
Students begin preregistration by
getting their study list cards from their
advisers on Wednesday, Thursday or
Friday, February 24, 25 or 26.
Signing of class lists in the
Registrar's Office will take place next
Monday, March 1 beginning at 8:00
a.m. through the following Monday,
March 8. Juniors, seniors and graduate
students with initials L-R will sign lists
the first Monday morning, March 1; S-Z
Monday afternoon; A-I) Tuesday
morning, and E-K Tuesday afternoon.
Freshmen and sophomores will sign
class lists according to the time
stamped on their study list cards or
thereafter.
Juniors who have not yet declared a
major (Division 3, Major 00) will have
to pick up their study list cards in the
Academic Information Center, 3rd
floor in the Old Union, in order to
pre-reg.
Advisers must sign the study list
cards of all freshmen.
