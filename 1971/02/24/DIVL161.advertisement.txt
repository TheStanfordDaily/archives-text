SIBERIAN EXPEDITION
An extremely difficult, semi-documentary zoological film will
be made jointly with the Russians in some of the most remote
areas of Eastern Siberia. The undertaking might continue 18
months. Our newly formed film company has the cooperation
of the International Union for the Conservation of Nature
(lUCN), The Wildlife Fund, The National Geographic Society,
and the National Science Foundation.
We need a female executive director with the following quali
fications:
1) College graduate or better with a knowledge of anthropological and biolog
ical sciences.
2) Approximate age, 23—30
3) Single
4) A knowledge & interest in drama
5) Bi lingual or better (Russian not necessary)
6) Exceptionally attractive physically, will act" in the film. Excellent health.
7) Willing & able to pay own travel expenses to Russia for shares in the film.
The above qualifications are all of equal import. Include honest, large photo
graph and write:
P.O. Box 950
Monterey, Ca. 93940
