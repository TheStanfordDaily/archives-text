PEACE CORPS HAS AN OVERSEAS
ASSIGNMENT FOR YOU
Teaching, Business, City Planning
Barbados, Antigua, St. Kitts and Grenada have asked
Peace Corps for Volunteers with degrees in secondary
education to work in teacher training colleges in the East
Caribbean. Senegal, Togo, Niger and Chad need French
speaking English teachers. Peru and Colombia want
Volunteers with degrees in business administration to aid
in cooperative development.
Physical science majors are needed to teach in second
ary schools in Africa. Venezuela and Iran request city
planners to work in provincial municipal public works
offices.
Many assignments are open, in Nepal and Morocco,
for liberal arts graduates.
Apply now for summer programs.
