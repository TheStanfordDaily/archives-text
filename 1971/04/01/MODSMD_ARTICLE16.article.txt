# Elections
## 
### 
All referendums and proposals
going on spring primary ballot
must be in by today at 4:00 p.m.
ASSU election petitions are
available in the ASSU Office for
ASSU President and Vice
President and for Senior Class
Officers. Petitions are due 4:00
p.m. Friday, April 2. Candidates
may withdraw no later than 4:00
p.m. April 3. The Elections
Commissioner will be in the
ASSU Office between 4:00 p.m.
and 5:00 p.m. Friday to
announce and post the names of
those who have filed valid
petitions, and between 4:00 p.m.
and 5:00 p.m. Saturday to
announce and strike out names of
those who have withdrawn. The
primary election will be held on
Wednesday and Thursday, April 7
and 8. The ballot will list tickets
for ASSU President and Vice
President, tickets for Senior Class
Officers, choices for the program
fee, and a few referendums.
