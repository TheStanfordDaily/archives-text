# Music Box
## 
### 
Easter Concert
Country Weather, Aum, and Page St. Evolution will play in an
Easter Sunday Rock Music Service in Dinkelspiel Auditorium at
1:00 p.m. April 11. Tickets are available at Tresidder Box Office and
at the door. The concert is sponsored by Education as Awareness to
help pay for their seminars in alternative education and
self-integration.
Good Friday Music
The Stanford Renaissance Wind Band and Early Music Chorus will
perform Heinrich's Schultz's "German Requiem," tonight at 8 p.m.
in Memorial Church. They will use early vocal techniques and
instruments such as the cornett, sackbut, recorder, flute and viola da
gamba.
