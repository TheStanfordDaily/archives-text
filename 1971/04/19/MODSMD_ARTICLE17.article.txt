# Crew Finishes Behind UW
## 
### 
EUGENE (AF) - The
University of Washington
swept the three crew events
in racing yesterday at Dexter
Reservoir near Eugene.
Varsity time were
Washington 6:25, Stanford
6:38; Oregon State 6:41 and

Oregon 6:43.
Junior varsity teams were
UW 6:24; OSU 6:41;
Stanford 6:42 and UO 6:51.
The winning freshman
team from UW won the 6:37
followed by Stanford with
6:48.
