# KELLEY COMING TO STANFORD
## 
### 
Basketball star Rich Kelley
of Woodside High School
signed a letter of intent
Wednesday to attend
Stanford. The 7-foot Prep
All-American averaged 19
points and 18 rebounds a
game this year and led
Woodside to its third
consecutive league
championship.
Kelley, who was named to
the High School All-American
team yesterday by Coach and
Athlete magazine, was
recruited by many colleges
including USC and UCLA.
