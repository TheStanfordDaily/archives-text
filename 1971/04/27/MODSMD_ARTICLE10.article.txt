# SCLC Elections
## 
### 
Yale Braunstein, Ray White and
Rocky Barber outpolled seven other
candidates in the April 14 and 15
elections for SCLC representatives,
elections commissioner Louis
Humphreys announced yesterday.
The SCLC election was conducted

on the Hare system, and its results were
determined by computers.
Alternates for the seats, in order,
were: first alternate, Buzz Thompson,
second alternate, Ernesto Rael, and
third alternate Herb Borock.
