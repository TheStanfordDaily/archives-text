# Bomb Threat
## 
### 
There was a false bomb threat at
the Stanford Hospital last night. A
male caller at 7:55 p.m. said that a
bomb was in the hospital which
would go off between 9:00 p.m. and
12:00 p.m.
Santa Clara and Palo Alto
authorities investigated but found
no bomb. It was the 12th bomb
threat on the campus in the past
month.
