Church Talks
Dr. Ivan Illich, a former
Catholic priest who is now an
educator, writer and expert in
international relations, will
speak at 11:00 a.m., Sunday,
May, to a university public
worship service in Memorial
Church.
He will address a general
audience at 3:00 p.m. Sunday
in the church, after meeting
with students in Arroyo
Lounge at 7:30 Saturday
night.
