# Tanner, Mayer Lose
## 
### 
Stanford's Roscoe Tanner and
Alex Mayer lost to UCLA's Jeff
Borowiak and Modesto Vasquez,
3-6, 6-4, 2-6, 7-6, 1-6, in

yesterday's Cal State doubles
finals.
To reach the finals, the
Tanner-Mayer tandem had
defeated eighth-seeded Hugh
Ditzler and Cliff Mayne in the
quarter-finals and second-seeded
Barr> McKay and Jim McManus
6-2, 2-6, 6-1 in the semi-finals.
The biggest upset of the
tourney came in the round of 16
when Stan Pasarell and Rick
Evans downed top-seeded Dick
Leach and Hob Potthast, 7-6, 4-6,
7-6.
In the singles, Tanner reached
the semis after upsetting McKay,
6-1, 6-4.
Meanwhile at the Nor Cal
Women's Intercollegiates,
Stanford's women's tennis team
compilled 11 team points to win
the team title over the closest
competitor, Berkeley with five
points. Sally Ride and Maureen
O'Keefe led the Tribe in singles by
reaching the semi-finals, while
Susan Mosely and Terry Albert
were finalists in the doubles.
