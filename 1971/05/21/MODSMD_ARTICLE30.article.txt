# Bershader Elected Senate Chairman
## 
### 
Daniel Bershader, associate chairman
of the Department of Aeronautics and
Astronautics, yesterday was elected
chairman of next year's Faculty Senate.
By a 26 to 22 vote, Bershader
defeated Edwin M. Good, a University
Fellow and professor of religion, at the
organizational meeting of the Fourth

Academic Senate.
In other business before the Senate,
a new steering committee was elected
including: professors Paul A. Brest, law;
William M. Chace, English; Gerald J.
Lieberman, Chairman of Operations
Research and professor of Statistics;
and Halsey L. Royden, mathematics.
