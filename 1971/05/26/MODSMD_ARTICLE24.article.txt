# P.P.R.C. Meeting
## 
### 
The Public Policy Research Center
Inc., an non-profit research
organization, will have an introductory
meeting Monday June 1 at 8:00 p.m. in
TMU 274 and on Wednesday June 3 at
3:00 in TMU 214 for all people
interested. The Center, headed by
Professor Arthur Auble, will emphasize
projects in foreign policy, population
and air and water quality. The group is
formally related to SWOPSI. Further
information can be obtained from Nick
Corff, SWOPSI (x 4305) or from Mr.
Auble at 364-0233, for summer
research projects.
