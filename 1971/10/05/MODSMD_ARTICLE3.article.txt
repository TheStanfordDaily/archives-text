# SENATE MEETING
## 
### 
There will be a special meeting
of the ASSU Summer Senate to
be held on October 6, this coming
Wednesday 12:00 p.m. in the
ASSU office.
The purpose of the meeting
will be to select a new ASSU
elections commissioner and an
ASSU elections commission.
These positions are open to all
members of the Association, all
interested students are urged to
attend.
