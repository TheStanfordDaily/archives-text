# Entertainment Notes
## 
### 
Tonight and Saturday night at
7 and 10 p.m. in Memorial
Auditorium, Rainbow Bridge, will
be screened in a benefit for
KZSU. Admission to the film,
which features Jimi Hendrix, will
be $2 at the door.
* * *
Tonight at 7:30 and 9:30 p.m.
the Law School Film Society will
screen Shadows of Forgotten
Ancestors in Bishop Auditorium.
Flash Gordon—Part 2 will also be
shown with admission $1.
* * *
Playing tonight at In Your Ear
in Palo Alto will be the Gary
Smith Blues Band. The Real
Charles Ford Band will do a guest
jet tonight and then will appear
by themselves on Friday through
Sunday nights. All shows begin at
3 p.m. and admission is $2.
* * *
Last night, Phillip Berrigan's
play, Hie Trial of the Catonsville
Nine opened at the New
Committee Theater in San
Francisco. The performance
schedule for this weekend is:

Tonight and tomorrow night at
8:30 p.m., Saturday night at 7
and 10:30 p.m. and Sunday night
at 7:30 p.m.
Tickets are priced at $5.50, $6
and $6.50 on weeknights and $1
higher in each category on Friday
and Saturday nights. Students can
get a $1 discount on any category
if they purchase tickets in
advance. Student rush tickets at
half-price go on sale 15 minutes
before curtain time if available.
