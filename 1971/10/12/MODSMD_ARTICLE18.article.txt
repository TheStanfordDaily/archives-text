# Entertainment Shorts
## 
### 
Tonight at 8 p.m. in the Cummings Art Building
Auditorium the Stanford Music Department will present
violinist Andor Toth and pianist Adolph Bailer in a concert of
violin-piano sonatas. Toth and Bailer will perform Beethoven's
"Sonata No. 1 in D major," Bartok's "Sonata No. 1 " and
Prokofiev's "Sonata in D major." Admission will be $2 general
andsl for students.
* * *
The Royal Winnipeg Ballet will perform tonight at 8 p.m. in
Zellerbach Auditorium on the UC Berkeley campus. Their
program will consist of "Aimez-Vous Bach?", "Sebastian,"
and "Rondo." Tickets are priced from $3.5 0 to $6.5 0 for the
general public and from $2.50 to $5.50 for students.
Tomorrow night the ballet company will appear at Flint
Center on the campus of De Anza College in Cupertino at 8 :30
p.m. The Cupertino program will consist of "Pas De Dix,"
"The Ecstasy of Rita Joe," and "Variations." Tickets will be
priced from $4 .50 up to $7.50.
* * *
A 1962 film version of Sophocles Antigone, starring Irene
Pappas will be shown tonight at 10 p.m. in the Branner Hall
Dining Room. Admission is free.
