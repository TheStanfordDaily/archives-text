Tonight the Winnipeg Ballet
Company will be performing The
Ecstasy of Rita Joe at Flint
Center in Cupertino at 8:30 p.m.
The Ecstasy. . . is a ballet about a
young Indian girl in Canada who
goes to the city and finds only
problems and hostility.
The ballet was choreographed
by Norbert Vesak, who based the
work on a play written by George
Ryga. The ballet includes both
classical ballet and modern dance.
Pas de Dix and Variations are also
on the program. Ticket prices
range from $4.50 to $7.50.
