# Mercouri Talks Here Sunday
## 
### 
International film star Melina
Mercouri will discuss the cause of
Greek Democracy Sunday at a
champagne reception at a home
on the Stanford campus.
Miss Mercouri, honorary
chairman of the Committee for

Democracy in Greece, will be at
the home of Mrs. Paul Wallin, 736
Frenchman's Road, Stanford,
from 3 p.m. to 5 p.m.
Reservations are available.
Greece is now controlled by a
military dictatorship.
