# Daily' Welcomes Statements
## 
### 
1
Each candidate for the Student Senate may submit a short
statement to the Daily which will be published in the newspaper
before the.election.
Statements must be typewritten, double-spaced and no longer
than 14 62-space lines. Each statement must be accompanied by
the candidate's name, year, and major, and must be delivered to
the Daily office by 4 p.m., Friday, Oct. 15
Slates of at least four people may submit one 50-line
statement for the slate. If the group chooses to do this, no
candidate on that slate may submit a separate statement. No late
submissions will be accepted.
