# Collins To Talk
## 
### 
This Monday the Stanford
community will hfive the opportunity
to see and hear Virginia Collins, a
woman who has been a dynamic force
in the civil rights and peace movements
for over thirty years.
An important part of Collins' work
on draft resistance relates to the case of
her son Walter, a resister who is
currently serving five five-year
sentences concurrently in the federal
penitentiary at Texarkana Texas. It is
this case, and the issue of amnesty,
which she will be discussing during her
visit here.
Collins will speak in room 270
Tresidder at 1:30 p.m. Monday
November 1.


Virginia Collins
