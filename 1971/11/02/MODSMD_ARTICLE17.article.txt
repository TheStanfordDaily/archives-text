# Entertainment Shorts
## 
### 
The International Business
Club will show "Cat Ballou"
tonight at 7:15 and 9:15 p.m. in
Bishop Auditorium. Admission is
$1.
* * *
In a triple feature tonight in
Cubberley Auditorium, film buffs
will be able to see Richard
Nixon's 1952 Checkers speech, an
interview of Lenny Bruce by
Steve Allen and "Pull My Daisy"
by Alan Ginsberg. Showings will
be at 7, 9 and 11 p.m. with tickets
$1.
* * *
Vince Guaraldi and his quartet
will be appearing tonight only at
In Your Ear in Palo Alto. The
show begins at 9 p.m. and
admission is $2.00. Minors are not
admitted.
* * *
Due to the withdrawal of
production rights by Track Music,
the Electronic Simulsense Theatre
Company was forced to end its
production of "Tommy" last
Saturday, a week earlier than
scheduled. Tickets which had

been purchased for the remaining
scheduled performances are
refundable at the ticket agencies
from which they were purchased.
