# Entertainment Shorts
## 
### 
The current Stanford Drama
Department production of Jean
Genet's "The Maids" has added
two extra performances at the
Nitery. Regular performances will
be given tonight, tomorrow night
and Saturday night at 8 p.m.
Added performances will be given
next Monday and Tuesday nights,
also at 8 p.m. Tickets priced at $2
general and $1 for students, go on
sale in the lobby at 7:30 p.m.
* * *
Tonight at 7:30 p.m. in
History 214, the Stanford
Workers League Club will sponsor
the U.S. premiere showing of a
film on the civil war in Ulster.
Speaking at the showing will be
Tim Wohlforth, National
Secretary of the Workers League,
and Lucy St. John, editor of the
Bulletin, weekly newspaper of the
Workers League. Admission will
be $1 and "Workers Press" a film
of the launching of the World's
first Trotskyist daily newspaper,
will also be shown.
* * *
Peggy Lee opens a weekend
engagement tonight at 8:30 p.m.
at the Circle Star Theatre in San
Carlos. Special guest star Frank
Gorshin will also be on the bill.
Performances will also be given
tomorrow night at 8:30 p.m.,
Saturday night at 7:30 p.m. and
10:30 p.m. and Sunday at 3 p.m.
and 7:30 p.m. Ticket prices range
from a low of $3.50 on Sunday
afternoon to a high of $7.50 on
weekend nights.
