# Berger Nominated To AAUP
## 
### 
Professor Joseph Berger of the
Sociology Department has been
nominated by petition for one of
the two positions of Executive
Committe Member of the
Stanford AAUP governing board.
Ballots will be mailed to all
AAUP members on Monday,
November 15 and must be
returned by November 29.

The candidates named by the
Nominating Committee for
President (John Goheen, Professor
of Philosophy), Vice President
(Paul Seaver, Associate Professor
of History), and for
Secretary-Treasurer (Elliott
Levinthal, Senior Scientist,
Genetics Department) are all
unopposed.
