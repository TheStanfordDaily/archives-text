# Hunt For Dean
## 
### 
President Richard W. Lyman
has announced the establishment
of the search committee for the
Dean of Engineering at Stnaford
University.
The committee will be chaired
by Provost William F. Miller.
Provost Miller indicated that he
has already received a number of
recommendations for candidates
for the deanship both from within
the University and from outside.
Any person who would like to
submit the name of a candidate
for the deanship should address
his recommendation, with a brief
statement of the qualifications of
the candidate, either to Provost
Miller, chairman of the
committee, or to any other
member of the search committee.
