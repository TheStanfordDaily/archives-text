# Campaign Plane
## 
### 
Supporters of presidential
Candidate Paul N. (Pete)
McCloskey have chartered a plane
for those interested in
campaigning for McCloskey in the
New Hampshire primary.
For students, the price of $170
includes round trip air fair plus all
lodging and transportation in New
Hampshire. The adult price is
$250.
