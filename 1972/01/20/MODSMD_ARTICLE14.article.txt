# Nominations
## 
### 
Nomination petitions for
the 10 unfilled graduate scats
to the ASSU Senate are due at
the ASSU office no later than
4:00 p.m. Friday. Petitions can
be obtained from the ASSU
Secretary.
Elections will be held
January 28. The available seats
are distributed as follows: 6
graduate engineering, 2
education, 1 medical school,
and 1 humanities and sciences.
Balloting in the current
student referendum on the
firing of English Professor H.
Bruce Franklin continues
today from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m
