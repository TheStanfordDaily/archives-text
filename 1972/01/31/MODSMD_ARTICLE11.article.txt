# The 						Stanford Daily
## 
### 
THE STANFORD DAILY is owned and published by the Associated
Students of Stanford University Monday through Friday during the
academic year except dead and finals week.
The opinions expressed herein are not necessarily those of the
University or the student body. Editorials represent the opinion of the
majority of the editorial board.
