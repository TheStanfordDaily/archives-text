# Sociology Meeting
## 
### 
The Department of Sociology
Is hosting a "Sociology Fair"
including speakers, panels and the
reading of papers to be held
Wednesday, Thursday and Friday
of this week. David Horowitz,
Ramparts editor and radical
sociologist, will be on a panel
Wednesday at 3:30 p.m. in the
Sociology' Graduate Library with
Professors Francesca Cancian and
Morris Zelditeh, Jr.
