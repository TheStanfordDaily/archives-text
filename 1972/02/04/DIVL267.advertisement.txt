Mercedes-Ben/ 220 Sedan:
Palo Alto Price: $0426. Ex-Factory Price:
$4335.
The Mercedes-Benz 220 sedan for 1972 is
offered with two markedly different engine
choices — a four-cyclinder gasoline engine
with single carburetor, or a diesel engine with
fuel injection. The diesel model, known as the
220 D, has no spark plugs or points and
operates on low-cost diesel fuel. Four-wheel
power disc brakes, bumper guards front and
rear, anil electrically heated rear window are
standard on both models, while the 220 gas
also has an automatic transmission as
standard.
