# Adv. 4 Page 2
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes
contributions from its readers.
Letters or columns should be
typed, double-spaced, on a 62
space line. All contributions must
be signed, with some identifying
information. Deadline for opinion
page material is 5:00 p.m. two
days before desired date of
publication. The Daily reserves
the right to edit all submitted
material.
