# Poll Watchers Needed
## 
### 
Elections Commissioner
John Lawson is looking for
volunteers to man polling areas
in the upcoming fee
referendum for the Children's
Center of The Stanford
Community.
The polling is from 10 a.m.
to 4:00 p.m. Thursday and
Friday this week, and those
interested in helping should
call Lawson at 321-2300 ext.
4340.
If the referendum is passed

students would pay one dollar
next quarter to the center.
