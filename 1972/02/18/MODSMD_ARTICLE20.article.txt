CAMPUS CONCERT—Victoria will be appearing along with the
Congress of Wonders tomorrow night at 8 p.m in Dinkelspiel
Auditorium. Victoria is a solo folk singer who has performed on bills
with James Taylor, Cat Stevens and who has recored an album with
Herbie Hancock. The Congress of Wonders is a two-man comedy and
satire team who record for Fantasy Records, Credence's label. Tickets
are priced at $2 general and for Stanford students they are $1 in
advance at the ASSU Duplicating Center of $1.50 at the door.
