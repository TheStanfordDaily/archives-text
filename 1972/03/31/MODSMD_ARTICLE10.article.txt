# The 						Stanford Daily
## 
### 
THE STANFORD DAILY is owned and published by the
Associated Students of Stanford University (ASSU) Monday
through Friday during the academic year except dead and final*
week.
Editorials represent the opinion of the majority of the editorial
board.
(Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto,
under the Act of March 3, 1897.)
