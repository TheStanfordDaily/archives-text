# Rhodesian To Speak
## 
### 
Henry Hamadzaripi, a member
of the Revolutionary Council of
Zimbabwe African National
Union (Z.A.N.U.), a Rhodesian
liberation movement, will speak
today at 4:30 p.m. in Room 270
of Tresidder. He will lecture about
the recent rebellions against the
Rhodesian government. The
revolutionary leader will also meet
with the Stanford B.S.U. at 8:00
p.m. in Roble Dining Room.
