THE STANFORD DAILY Is owned and published by the Associated
Students of Stanford University Monday through Friday during the
academic year except dead and finals week.
Letters and Columns on the opinions pages do not necessarily
represent the views of the University Administration, the ASSU, or any
other organization.
Editorials represent the opinion of the majority of the editorial
board.
