MYSTERY THRILLER SLEUTH-At the suggestion of his host,
mystery writer Andrew Wyke, Milo Tindle (Peter Donat), above,
dresses up for an elaborately-plotted crime in the American
Conservatory Theater's Northern California production of "Sleuth".
Under the direction of Ellis Rabb, Anthony Shaffer's suspense-thriller
will now play four extra weeks after the close of the regular season,
until June 3. Tuesday is all sold out except for second balcony seats.
Friday night is completely sold out.


IN SPITE OF HlMSELF—Sganarelle (Wes Finlay) carried away by his
own fakery, deludes Geronte (Peter Silbert) in Latin, which isn't, yet
convinces him that he is a doctor as well as a scholar, which he isn't.
Stanford production of Moliere's "The Doctor In Spite Of Himself"
opens tonight.
