# Law Review Editors
## 
### 
Jerrold J. Ganzfried, a Yale
graduate from Flushing, N.Y.,has
been chosen president of The
Stanford Imw Review for
1972-73.
Former news editor with The
Yale Daily News, he has worked
with the Santa Clara public
defender and with Prof. Anthony
Amsterdam, nationally prominent
expert on criminal law.
Edward E. Burmeister Jr. of
Palo Alto has been named
managing editor; Bruce L.
Cronander, Birmingham, Mich.,
executive editor; John N. Drobak,
Apalchin, N.Y.
managing editor; and Michael A.
Kahn, Mt. View, book review
editor.
