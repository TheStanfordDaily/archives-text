# Chinese Ping-Pong At Maples
## 
### 
It was announced yesterday
that China's touring table tennis
team has scheduled an exhibition
performance at Maples Pavilion
this Friday at 3 p.m.
The exhibition will last one
and one-half hours and will
involve only the Chinese players
In earlier matches on their tou: -
the Chinese faced American
players and won 34 of At
matches.
Headed by three-time world
champion Chuang Tse-tung, the
team has 14 players, including six
women. The total tour group
including interpreters, numbers
about 40.
General admission tickets will
go on sale at the Athletic
Department from 8:30 a.m. to 5
p.m. starting today. They are
priced one dollar for students and
two dollars for all others. Tickets
will be limited to two per
customer.
