# Ho Arrested
## 
### 
Kwonping Ho, a sophomore economics
major and long-time militant third world
activist, was arrested yesterday at the Stanford
Bookstore and charged with burglary.
Ho was apprehended at 3:15 p.m. by a
Stanford security guard who observed him
enter the bookstore carrying a shoulder bag,
allegedly place unidentified merchandise inside
it, and attempt to leave.
