# Williams Acting Dean
## 
### 
President Richard W. Lyman
has announced the appointment
of Daniel A. Williams, 31, as
acting dean of student affairs on
an interim basis, beginning May 8
and continuing until the
appointment of James W. Lyons
as dean of student affairs becomes
effective in July.
Williams has been associate
dean with responsibility for the

residence system since 1970, and
has been a member of the
Stanford staff since 1987.
lie came to Stanford from an
administrative position at the
University of Oregon. He and his
wife are resident fellows at
Madera House on the campus.
Williams is the successor to
Robert Freelen in the acting
deanship.
