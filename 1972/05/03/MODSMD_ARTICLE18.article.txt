# Coed Golf
## 
### 
The Women's Recreation
Association is sponsoring a co-ed
2-ball golf tournament. Teams
may play their round of golf
anytime between April 25 and
May 25. Prizes will be awarded.
Rules and information are posted
in Women's Physical Education
(where sign-up sheets are up), at
the golf course, or may be
obtained from Pat Jenson.
