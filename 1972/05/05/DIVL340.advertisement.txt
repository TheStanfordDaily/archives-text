### 
DEMONSTRATIONS
A joint committee of Human
Relations Commission members
and Youth Advisory Council
members has been chosen in Palo
Alto to review specific complaints
and concerns related to the anti war
demonstrations during the week of
April 17-21 and to prepare a report
of its findings and recommenda
tions to the Chief of Police. The
committee requests that any person
having firsthand information about
those events send a summary of
that information along with name
and address to the Human
Relations Commission at 250
Hamilton Avenue, Palo Alto,
California 94301, or call Pat
Maloney at 329 2476.
