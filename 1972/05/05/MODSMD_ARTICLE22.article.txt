# FILM NOTES
## 
### 
Tonight at 7 and 9 p.m. in
Cubberley Auditorium, the Black
Students Union will show "The
Emperor Jones" starring Paul
Robeson. Admission will be $1.
The film is the first in a BSU
sponsored Black Film Festival
which will include the following
films during the remainder of
spring quarter: May 11—"Green
Pastures"; May 13—"Cabin In The
Sky"; May 18—"Scar of Shame."
All the films will be screened in
Cubberley Auditorium.
* * *
The International Business
Club will screen "Le Guerre Est
Finie" tonight at 7 and 9:30 in
Bishop Auditorium. Admission
will be $1.
Stan ford-in-Washington will
show "The RA" tomorrow night
in Cubberley Auditorium at 7:30
and 9:30 p.m. Admission will b§
$1, and not $1.50 as was reported
to us earlier.
* * *

show "Johny Mera Naam"
tomorrow night at 7:30 p.m. in
Bishop Auditorium. Admission i
$2 for non-members and $1.5
for members.
