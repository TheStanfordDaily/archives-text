BICYCLE SURVEY
The City of Palo Alto needs information about bicyclists
routes. Bicyclists please fill out questionnaires which can be
obtained at the following locations:
Main Library, Meyer Library, ASSU Office, Bookstore,
Engineering Corner, Wilbur, Stern, Roble, Laguanita, Florence
Moore, and deposited at Main or Meyer Library.
