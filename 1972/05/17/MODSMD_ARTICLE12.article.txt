# 'Sleuth' Tickets
## 
### 
Contrary to a report given
to us yesterday which appeared
in the "Who, What, When and
Where" calendar, there are
good tickets available for
"Sleuth" this week at the
Geary Theatre in San
Francisco, except on Friday
and Saturday night when only
$5.90 balcony seats are
available.
"Sleuth" will run through
June 3 with 8:30 performances
on Monday through Saturday
nights and 2:30 matinees on
Wednesday and Saturday.
* * *
"Day of The Triffids" will
be screened tonight in
Cubberley Auditorium at 7:30
and 9:30 p.m. Admission will
be $1.
