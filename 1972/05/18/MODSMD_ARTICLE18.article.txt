# RESEARCH POLICY DISCUSSION
## 
### 
University research policy
will be discussed by a panel of
four professors and a student
today at 4:30 p.m., Thursday,
May 18, in the Annenberg
Auditorium of the Cummings
Art Building at Stanford
Prof. Thomas J. Connolly,
chairman of the Committee on
Research, will preside at the
session, which will open with
comments by Vice-Provost
William F. Massy, the
University's principal research
officer.
