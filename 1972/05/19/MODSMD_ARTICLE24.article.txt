# Entertainment Shorts
## 
### 
A complete stage version of
"Jesus Christ Superstar" will be
presented by the Old First
Presbyterian Church of San
Francisco tonight at 8:30 p.m. in
Memorial Church. Admission is
free.
* * *
Sunday night at 8:15 p.m. in
the Flint Center of De Anza
College in Cupertino, there will be
a George McGovern benefit
featuring Cal Tjader and poet Jim
Marks. General admission is $5
and student tickets are $2.
* * *
Norbert Yancey will give a
concert entitled "Ballads From
Around the World" tonight at 8
p.m. in the Tresidder Large
Lounge. Tickets are $1.50 for the

general public and $1 for
students.
* * *
There will be a benefit dance
for the Chris Laury Defense Fund
featuring The Sterlings, JW Plus 5,
Stash and Christian Black tonight
at 9 p.m. in the Serra Dining
Room in Stem Hall. Admission
will be $1.
* * *
At this Sunday's ASSU Flick
"Carnal Knowledge", Butch Wax,
a Sha-Na-Na type rock band
composed of Stanford Glee Club
members, will play before the two
showings which are scheduled for
7 and 9:10 p.m. and after the
second show. They will begin
playing at 6:30 p.m. and
admission is 50c.
