23-DAY SURVIVAL
EXPEDITION IN COLORADO.
Camp in Sangre de Cristo Mtns.,
climb, traverse snowy ridges for 10
days. Survive 150 miles of desert
for 8 days and downtown Denver
without money or direction. June
19 & Aug. 7. For full info., write
Box 91766, L.A., Calif. 90009
RESEARCH I
ASSISTANT
Within our Long Range Planning
Service, our client inquiry
coordinators prepare replies to
clients on a wide variety of
subject matter. We are looking for
such a coordinator with a BS
preferably in Economics or a
technical field, who enjoys
literature search and can
communicate with the SRI
professionals to obtain needed
information.
This is a permanent position and
offers excellent benefits. Please
send resume to Ms Harriet
McCartney. Personnel Cervices...
STANFORD RESEARCH
INSTITUTE
333 Ravenswood Avenue
Menlo Park, California, 94025
—an equal opportunity employer—
ATTENTION ALL PREMEDS:
The 1972-1973 Stanford
Premedical Handbook, published
by the Dean of Undergraduate
Studies office, will be available
free of charge in the Academic
Information Center on Monday,
June 5, 1972. If you intend to
apply to medical school this
summer, you should refer to this
handbook for information about
implications that will be especially
helpful for you.
Come to the AIC and leave
your name and address if you
aren't going to be at Stanford on
the sth of June. Handbooks will
be mailed to students over the
summer at their request.
