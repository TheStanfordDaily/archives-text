# MEMORIAL SERVICE
## 
### 
Tomorrow at 3 p.m. in Memorial Church there will be a
memorial service for Fred Oakford 11, an honors graduate of
the Class of '71, who died on May 17 in Aberystwyth, Wales.
At the time of his death, Oakford was a graduate student in
Library Science at the University of Wales in Aberystwyth.
Oakford is survived by his parents, Mr. and Mrs. Robert
Oakford, two brothers, Donald Ross Smith and Stephan Allen
Smith, and grandparents, Mr. and Mrs. H. Ross Brown Sr. of
Salt Lake City, Utah. The elder Oakford is a professor in
Stanford's industrial engineering department.
The family perfers that contributions be made to the
Stanford Library.
