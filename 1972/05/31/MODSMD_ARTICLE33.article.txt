# Timoshenko Dies
## 
### 
Retired professor Stephen P.
Timoshenko, a member of the
National Academy of Sciences
and the Royal Society of London,
died Monday of a kidney ailment
at his daughter's home in West
Germany. He was 93.
Timoshenko, also a member of
the Russian Academy of Science,
was a teacher, author and
researcher in theoretical and
applied mechanics.
His textbooks in engineering
have been translated into several
languages and are used around the
world.
Timoshenko taught at Stanford
from 1936 to 1944, when he
reached retirement age, but he
continued lecturing and writing
for another decade.
