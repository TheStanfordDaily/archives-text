# IM Season Concludes
## 
### 
In an extremely close race,
Theta Delt edged out KA for the
University Intramural
Championship. Theta Delt scored
555 intramural points to KA's
517-5/6. W.P. "Dutch" Fehring,
Director of Intramural Activities
remarked that this was the closest
score for championship in recent
years.
Kent Lawson of Alpha Delt set

a new Stanford and National
Intramural swimming record in
the 50-yard fly
The women's intramural season
came to an end with
Cardenal-Alonda winning the
softball title. The Tribe won the
coed-vol ley ball title, and Karen
Cartwright and Jim Pine won the
coed-golf two ball contest.
