# OPINIONS POLICY
## 
### 
The summer Daily will publish an Opinions section in its Friday <
issue, and will continue to publish an opinion page in all issues*
during the next six weeks.
Opinions editor David Thiemann solicits letters, columns, and*
cartoons from all members of the Stanford community.
Letters and columns should be typed on a 62 space line, (
double-spaced, and addressed to the Daily opinions editor.,
Preference will be given to short, well-written articles expressing
unrepetitive viewpoints.
All submissions must be signed, and accompanied by a
telephone number and address for the author. Opinions pieces will
not be published under pseudonyms.
