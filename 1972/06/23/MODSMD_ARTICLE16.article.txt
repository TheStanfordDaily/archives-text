San Carlos has announced the
cancellation of two
forthcoming acts.

scheduled for July 4-9 along
with Gladys Knight and the
Pips, canceled because of a

"major TV conflict," according
to the Circle Star management.

her July 11-16 engagement
because she is expecting a
baby.
