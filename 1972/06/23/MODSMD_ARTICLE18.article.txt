# Cheech And Chong Tonight
## 
### 
Cheech and Chong will appear for two shows tonight in Memorial
Auditorium. Appearing with them will be Elvin Bishop and his
group. Show times are at 7:30 p.m. and 10 p.m.
The comedy team has a current best-selling album on Lou
Adler's Ode Records, distributed by A&M Records.
Tickets are on sale at the Tresidder Union box office, among
others.
Prices are $2.50 advance sale, and $3.50 at the door.
The Elvin Bishop Group is a veteran of the area music wars, who
have also recorded several albums.
Cheech and Chong's appearance is being sponsored by the ASSU
Special Events Board.
