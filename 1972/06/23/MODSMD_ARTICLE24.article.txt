# New Dean Appointed
## 
### 
Ramon Telles, a UCLA graduate
student, will take over as assistant dean
of student affairs when he receives his
degree in late July.
Telles, working on his masters in
Spanish and Portuguese, will report to
Stanford August 1, replacing another
chicano assistant dean, Carlos Jackson,
who is going back to school.
He was selected from a field of 40
candidates. The 29-year old native of
Whittier has been active at UCLA in
the Graduate Students Association,
Chicano Graduate Students
Association, and the UCLA tutoring
program.
Telles received his bachelors degree
from Cal State-Fullerton, and also
attended Cal State-Long Beach.
