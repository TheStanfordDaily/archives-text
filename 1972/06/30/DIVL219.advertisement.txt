Women's Pool
Weekdays:
12 to 1:30 p.m.» faculty and
staff women and wives and
student wives, no fee.
1 p.m. to 2 p.m.: women only
— students and "noon
swimmers", no fee.
2 p.m. to 6:15 p.m.: coed,
open to persons holding a
current Stanford identification
card — no fee for currently
registered Stanford students,
others pay 25 cents per
swim/$1.50 per week/$5 for
the summer.
Weekends:
12 p.m. to 5:45 p.m.: same
rules apply as from 2 to 6:15
weekday hours; one adult guest
per woman student permitted
on weekends.
