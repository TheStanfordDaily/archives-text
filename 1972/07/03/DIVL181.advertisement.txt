4th Of July Events
PARADES: Double Independence Day (U.S.
and Philippine) Parade beginning at 1 p.m. at
Pine and Montgomery Streets in San Francisco.
33rd Redwood City 4th of July Parade
beginning at 10:30 a.m. at Arguello and
Brewster Streets in Redwood City.
BOAT RACING: $4,000 Enduro featuring top
Outdoor Marathon Association drivers
beginning at 1 p.m. at the Redwood City
Municipal Harbor.
FIREWORKS: Entertainment from 8 p.m. with
fireworks starting at 9 p.m. at Candlestick Park
in San Francisco. Fireworks beginning at dusk
at the Redwood City Harbor. Fireworks
beginning at 8 p.m. at Washington Park in
Sunnyvale. Fireworks beginning at 9:15 p.m. at
the Mt. View Recreation Center and the
baylands refuse disposal area in Palo Alto.
SWIM SHOW: 8 p.m. at the Mt. View
Recreation Center.
