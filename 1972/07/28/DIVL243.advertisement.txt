Stanford University Summer Session presents:
The World Premiere of a new American Drama of the Absurd:
INTERMISSION
by Philip Oxman
August 2nd. Wednesday - - August 6th, Sunday, 8:00 p.m.
Little Theater, Memorial Auditorium
Tickets $1.00 at the door; Reservations SU ext. 4439
BASIC TRAINING
A Frederick Wiseman documentary about draftees and enlisted men going
through nine weeks of training at Fort Knox, Kentucky. The film
documents the transition from civilian to soldier.
5:30 Sunday, July 30
Palo ALto Community Cultural Center Auditorium
1313 Newell Road
$4, includes following discussion with
David Denby, Atlantic Monthly film critic.
For information, phone University of California Extension, Santa Cruz,
(408) 429 2351.
