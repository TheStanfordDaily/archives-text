publication next Tuesday (August
8), and the regular Daily will not
resume publication until late
September.

notices for as many events as
possible which will be occurring
on campus during the rest of the
summer. Information about
entertainment events should be
sent to the Daily Entertainment
Editor, Storke Publications
Building, Stanford, California
94305. Information about other
events should be sent to OATQ,
c/o the above address. The
deadline for all information is
Monday, August 7 at 2 p.m.
