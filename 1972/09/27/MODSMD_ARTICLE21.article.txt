# Child Center Head Chosen
## 
### 
Phyllis Craig has been
appointed Administrative Director
of the Childrens Center of the
Stanford Community.
Craig was director of the
University Cooperative Nursery
School at Princeton for 12 years
prior to becoming director at Palo
Alto's Children's Health Council
Preschool for Multi-Handicapped
Children.
Craig's husband, Gordon, is
Chairman of the History
Department here. The Center,
which offers day care programs, is
located at 591 Mayfield Avenue,
Stanford.
