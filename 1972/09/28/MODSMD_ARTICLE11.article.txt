# Library To Close One Hour Earlier
## 
### 
The Main Library is closing one hour earlier this quarter,
according to David C. Weber, director of the university
libraries.
The Main Library will now close at 10 p.m. Sunday
through Friday nights. It had been open until 11 p.m.,
Monday through Friday. The hours at all other libraries will
remain the same as they have been in the past. Meyer
Undergraduate Library remains open until midnight
everyday of the week.
Weber said the reduction of hours is a "direct result of
trying to use dollars available in the best ways." He said the
decision was based on a very careful study done last spring
of use of the libraries.
He foresees no further changes in library hours.
The main library is now open from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m.,
Monday through Friday, from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. on Saturday
and from 1 p.m. to 10 p.m. on Sunday.
