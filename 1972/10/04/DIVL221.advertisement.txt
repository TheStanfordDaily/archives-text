EFFECTIVE READING CLASSES
Classes in effective reading and related skills, offered by the Studont
Counseling Service, will begin Tuesday, October 3, and continue for six
weeks. Hours are 7-9 p.m. on Tuesday and Thursday. Focus will be placed on
developing reading speed, comprehension, and memorizing techniques.
Orientation and registration will take place in the first and second meetings in
Room E 15 in the School of Education. The classes are non-credit and cost
$25.
