The Daily opinions page welcomes original articles, letters
and cartoons from its readers. Preference is given to short,
imaginative submissions.
Letters and articles should be typed, double-spaced, on a 62
space line. All submissions must be signed with a legible address
and telephone number, and placed in the opinions editor's box
by 2 p.m. two days prior to publication, or mail them to
Opinions Editor, Stanford Daily, Stanford, California 94305.
The Daily will not print any unsigned submissions.
For any questions or additional information, please contact
Daily Opinions Editor Kevin Smith at 321-2300, extension
4632.
