# AAUP Program
## 
### 
The Stanford chapter of the
American Association of University
Professors (AAUP) is sponsoring a
panel discussion of the controversial
Faculty Discipline Report today at
4:30 in the Annenbuig Auditorium of
Cummings Art Building.
Participants will be: Asst. Law Prof.
Paul Brest, Communication Prof.
David Grey, Medicine Prof. Halsted
Holman and Asst. English Prof. Nancy
Packer. Brest authored the report and
Holman is a member of the Faculty
Political Action Group (FPAG), which
opposed the report.
Questions and comments from the
floor will be welcome. This discussion
is the last scheduled community forum
on the report before the mail
referendum is sent out to the faculty
on Friday.
