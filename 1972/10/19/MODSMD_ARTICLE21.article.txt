The Oakland Symphony gives a
concert of works by Beethoven,
Brahms, and Lees at the Oakland
Auditorium at 8:30. Ticket prices
are $2.50 to $7, with student rush
tickets for $1.50. For information
call 444-8575.

COMPANY performs in "TOM
PAINE" directed by Tom
O'Horgan at 8:30 in the San Jose
Civic Auditorium. Additional
performances will be on Friday at
the flint Center in Cupertino at
8:30, on Saturday at the San
Francisco Veterans Auditorium at
the same time, and Sunday at
2:30 in the Oakland Auditorium.

evening performances and $2.50
to $5.50 for the matinee.

the matinee.
