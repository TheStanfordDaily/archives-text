Paul N. "Pete" McCloskey Jr. and James Stewart, 17th District
Congressional candidates, will debate foreign policy issues tonight at 8
p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium.
The public is invited, with no admission charge.
Each candidate will speak for fifteen minutes on foreign policy, the
draft, military preparedness, military spending, and the United Natiorts.
A one-hour question-answer period will follow, with questions accepted
"on any topic deemed appropriate by those present," according to ASSU
co-president Scott Burke.
Announced conservative write-in candidate J. Gordon Knapp
(R-Atherton) will not take part in tonight's debate.
