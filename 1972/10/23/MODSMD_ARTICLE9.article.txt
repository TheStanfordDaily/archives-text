# Untitled Article
## 
### 
All candidates for the student Senate who wish to have
statements regarding their candidacies published in the Daily
must submit statements today or tomorrow for publication in
Wednesday's or Thursday's paper.
Statements will be printed in the order in which they are
received by us. All statements must be typewritten,
double-spaced, and no longer than fourteen 62-space lines.
Each statement must be accompanied by the candidate's
name, year and major, and must be delivered to the Daily
office by no later than 6 p.m. Tuesday, Oct. 24.
Slates of candidates may submit one statement no longer
than 14 lines for each candidate on the slate.
