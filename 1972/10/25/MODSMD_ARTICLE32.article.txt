# Another Bomb Threat
## 
### 
A bomb threat to the Chemistry Building forced the postponement of a 5
scheduled organic chemistry mid-term yesterday.
According to Administrator for the Chemistry Department, Perry West, g
a male caller told secretary Carolyn Kobielus at 9:47 a.m. that "You've 5
got a bomb in there."
The building was evacuated within four minutes and Stanford firemen =
searched the building for about 30 minutes. No bomb was found. =
