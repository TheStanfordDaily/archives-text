# Editorial Policy Defined
## 
### 
The Editorial Board of The Stanford Daily is composed of:
Stuart Ackerman, Bob Bacon, Joe Berman, Dan Brenner, Mike
Chasson, Chrissy Douglas, Charlie Hoffman, Rich Jaroslovsky,
Bob Litterman, Charles Lyle, Frank Miller, Kitty Patterson,
Kevin Smith, Dave Thiemann, Don Tollefson, Jim Wascher and
Stan Wilson.
Editorials represent the majority opinion of the Editorial
Board and do not necessarily represent the views of the entire
Daily staff, the ASSU or Stanford University. The Daily
welcomes submissions of opposing views.
