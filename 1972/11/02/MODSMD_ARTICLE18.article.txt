# Registrar's Notice
## 
### 
Copies of Official Study
Lists for the current Autumn
Quarter are now being mailed
to all registered students and
should be received by
Thursday, November 2.
The list of courses should
be inspected carefully so that
if an error has occurred it will
be corrected by filing a green
Study List Change Petition
with the Registrar's Office.
If a course title is omitted
on the official list, there is an
errpr in the course number as
submitted or the course has
not been established on the
Registrar's file.
A window will be staffed
in the lobby of the Old Union
to provide assistance with
study list problems.
