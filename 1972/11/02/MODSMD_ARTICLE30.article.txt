THURSDAY, NOVEMBER 2
WOODY ALLEN performs tonight through
Sunday at the Circle- Star Theater. Shows on
Thursday and Friday are at 8:30, Saturday
7:30 and 10:30, and Sunday at 4:30 and
8:30. Tickets for Thursday and Sunday
evening are $3.75 to $6.50, for weekends
$3.75 to $7.50, and for the Sunday matinee
$3.50 to $5.50.
GODSPELL, the hit musical, continues at the
Marines Memorial Theater in San Francisco.
Tickets are $5.50 to $7.50 for 8:30 shows on
week-nights, $6.50 to $8.50 for shows Friday
at 8:30 and Saturday at 6 and 9.
ONE FLEW OVER THE CUCKOO'S NEST is
in its third year at the Little Fox Theater, 533
Pacific. For information call 434-4738.
GHOSTS, presented by the Stanford Drama
Department, plays tonight through Saturday
at the Little Theater at 8. Tickets are $2.50
weeknights, $3 weekends general admission,
$1 and $2 respectively for students.
APPLAUSE runs tonight and tomorrow at the
Flint Center at 8:30 and Saturday in the
Marine Veterans' Memorial Auditorium at
2:30 and 8:30. Tickets for evenings are $5.50
to $8.50, and $3.50 to $6.50 for the Saturday
matinee.
TAKE THE MONEY AND RUN, starring
Woody Allen, isscreenedby the Stanford Law
School Film Society in Cubberley Auditorium
at 7:30, 9 and 10:30. Admission $1.
TEN DAYS THAT SHOOK THE WORLD is
presented by the Young Socialists at 7:30 and
10 in Roble dining room. Donation is $1.
ACT presents CYRANO DE BERGERAC
tonight and Saturday at 8:30 and next
Wednesday at 2:30 and 8:30 at the Geary
Theater. Tickets for Wednesday and Thursday

are $4.90 and $6.90, for Saturday $5.90 and
$7.90. For ticket availability call 673-6440.
