# WICKER TO SPEAK AT BISHOP TONIGHT
## 
### 
Tom Wicker, associate editor
and columnist of The New York
Times, will speak at 8 p.m. today
in Bishop Auditorium of the
Graduate School of Business.
Wicker is this year's Carlos
McClatchey Memorial Lecturer. A
lecture is presented annually by
the Communication Department
to honor the memory of the late
founder-editor of the Fresno Bee.
Books written by Wicker
include Kennedy Without Tears
and JFK and LBJ: The Influence
of Personality Upon Politics. His
talk will be entitled "Whose
Freedom Next?".
