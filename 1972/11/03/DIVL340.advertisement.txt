Today In The 'Daily'
Today is Friday, Nov. 3, 1972.
On this date in 1959, two General Motors plants in Oakland
shut down because of steel shortages, laying off 1,334 workers.
The Chevrolet and Fisher Body plants dosed up shop at the end
of the day shift.
If you see General Motors steeling shortages, call our news
office at 321-2300, ext. 4632.
If you have been laid off, and are looking for some way to
get it on, try our business office, at 327-4150.
