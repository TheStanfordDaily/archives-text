Today is Tuesday, Nov. 7, 1972.
Fifty-five years ago today the Communists seized control of
the Russian Government. Two months later, Lenin established a
socialist dictatorship in Russia.
If you see Lenin Russian to seize control of a government,
call our news office at 321-2300.
\If you need to sell a Communist Plot, try our business office,
at 327-4150.
Please don't call us for election results.
Important Daily staff luncheon today at noon. We will be
planning election night coverage. Paychecks will be distributed.
