# Women Swimmers Win Meet
## 
### 
Stanford's women's swim team defeated the
University of California at Davis and San Jose State
University, 134'/2 - 103 - 34V& in a tri-meet here last
Thursday.
Three pool records were broken or tied, and a
Stanford University record was tied. Laurie Gill,
swimming for Stanford, tied a Stanford record with a
15.2 in the 25-yeard backstroke.
