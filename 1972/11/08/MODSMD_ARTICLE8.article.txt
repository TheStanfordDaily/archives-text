# Colo. Drops Bid For Olympics
## 
### 
DENVER (AP) - Colorado voters
snuffed out the Olympic flame in
their state Tuesday for the 1976
Winter Games.
With 647 of 2,045 precincts
reporting, 159,147 ballots had been
cast to cut off state funds for the
Games, and 107,638 persons had
voted to continue funding.
In Denver itself, with 403 of 474
precincts reporting, 102,840
persons had voted to halt state
funds, and 68,773 voted to
continue financing.
