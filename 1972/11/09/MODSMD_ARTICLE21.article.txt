# Rape Attempts Reported
## 
### 
Two attempted rapes have been reported on the
Stanford campus within the last week and a half,
according to the Santa Clara County Sheriffs office.
The incidents occurred on Oct. 31 at 1:15 p.m. in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium, and on Nov. 4 at 11 p.m. at
Toy on Hall.
Sheriffs Sgt. Jon Svilich said yesterday that both
incidents we/e currently under investigation by
county authorities.
