# Indian Education Meeting Begins
## 
### 
The California Indian Education
Association will hold its sixth annual
conference starting today in Tresidder
Union. Registration for the program
will be held from 7 to 9 a.m., and
again starting at 8 a.m. Saturday. The
conference concludes Sunday.
According to Assistant Dean of
Students Gwen Shunatona, the
conference will focus on urban
education and a reassessment of goals
of the Association. Participants will
discuss efforts to introduce native
American cultural materials into the
public school curriculum, work with
news media, and other issues suggested
by the participants. The first general
session is at 9 a.m. Saturday.
