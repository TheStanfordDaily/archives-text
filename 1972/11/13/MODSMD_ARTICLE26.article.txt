# Goldberg To Speak Tonight
## 
### 
Former U.S. Supreme Court Justice Arthur J. Goldberg will speak
| tonight at 8 p.m. in Bishop Auditorium of the Graduate School of
| Business.
Goldberg, whose visit is sponsored by the Stanford Law Forum, will
1 discuss the effects of elections on the rulings of the Supreme Court.
Goldberg served as Secretary of Labor in the Kennedy Administration
| prior to his appointment to the high court in 1962. He resigned from the
| court in 1965 to become U.S. Representative to the United Nations.
| Goldberg lost to incumbent Nelson Rockefeller in the 1970 race for the
| New York governorship. He now practices law in Washington, D.C.
