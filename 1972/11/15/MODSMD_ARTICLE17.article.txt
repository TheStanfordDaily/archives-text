# Modern Thought Conference Features Author John Hawkes
## 
### 
Three presentations of interest to students will be sponsored by
Stanford's Modern Thought and Literature Conference today,
Thursday and Friday.
At 4:15 p.m. today, eight women from four different colleges or
universities will discuss "Thinking about Women: Problems of
Interpretation in Myth, Literature and History." Ellen Rogat, a
graduate student here, will chair the discussion which will be held in
the Tresidder Lounge.
Tomorrow at 4:30 p.m., innovative novelist John Hawkes will read
his works in the Tresidder Lounge.
At 11 a.m. Friday, two students will read reports on "Myth in the
Novels of John Hawkes." Hawkes and the students of English 334 and
361 will then discuss the reports in the Briggs Room of Building 50.
