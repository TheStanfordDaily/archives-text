# Elly Ameling Sings Tonight
## 
### 
Dutch soprano Elly Ameling
will appear tonight at 8 p.m. in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium in the
first of two campus performances
this week. Ameling will also
perform in Dinkelspiel at 2:30
p.m. on Sunday.
Tonight's program will open
with two Lieder of Carl Loewe,
"Der Fischer" and "Der
Mummelsee." The second half of
Ameling's performance will
consist of works by Chausson,
Faure and Erik Satie.
Sunday's concert will consist
of music by Bach and Haydn. The
Stanford Symphony Orchestra
under the direction of Sando
Salgo will perform Haydn's
Symphony No. 67 on Sunday
also.
