Reminisce
By Orange Blossom
The pattern of this ring dates to
the Middle Ages where it was known
as "the ceremonial ring."
The symbols used then are as appropriate
today as they were in the year 1320.
Joined Hearts:
two mortal souls in love.
Clasped Hands:
togetherness for a lifetime.
q<\ Scriptures:
wisdom of the ages for guidance.
Horn of Plenty:
fulfillment and happiness.
Orange Blossoms:
symbols of the dream you share.
Reminisce. A ring as eternal as time.
As young as your dream.
