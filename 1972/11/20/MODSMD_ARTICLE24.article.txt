# Advance Reg Begins Today
## 
### 
Advance registration for winter quarter begins today. Time schedules
are available on the first floor of the Old Union and other registration
materials can be obtained from academic advisers or from a student's
major department.
After a study program is developed, freshmen and engineering graduate
students must obtain the signature of their advisers on their study lists.
Whenever necessary, students must sign class lists either in the Old Union
or at individual departments.
Juniors, seniors and graduate students can advance register from today
until Monday, Nov. 27, following the schedule printed on page two of the
time schedule. Freshmen and sophomores can advance register from
Tuesday, Nov. 28 until Monday, Dec. 4. All students can advance register
on Tuesday and Wednesday, Dec. 5-6.
