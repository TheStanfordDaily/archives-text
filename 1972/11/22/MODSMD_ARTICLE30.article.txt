# HOPKINS DIRECTOR RESIGNS
## 
### 
Acting President William F.
Miller has accepted "with regret"
the resignation of Prof. John H.
Phillips as director of the Hopkins
Marine Station.
Phillips, who joined the
Stanford faculty in 1962, has
served as director of the Station
since 1965. The UC-Berkeley
graduate's research interests have
included comparative
biochemistry and comparative
immunology.
