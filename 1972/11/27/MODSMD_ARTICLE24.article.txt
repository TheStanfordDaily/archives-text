# Merlo In Shrine Game
## 
### 
Senior linebacker Jim Merlo,
named to the All-Coast and
All-Pac Eight all-star teams, was
accorded another honor last week
when he was selected to play in
the 48th annual East-West Shrine

game at Candlestick Park on Dec.
30.
Merlo will be joined in the
classic by linebackers Steve Brown
of Oregon State and Bill Ferguson
of San Diego State.
