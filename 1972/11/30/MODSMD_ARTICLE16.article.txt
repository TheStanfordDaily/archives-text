THURSDAY, NOVEMBER 30
The musical GODSPELL
continues at the Marines'
Memorial Theater. Tickets for
shows at 8:30 Monday through
Thursday are $5.50 to $7.50. For
Friday at 8:30 and Saturday at 6
and 9 tickets are $6.50 to $8.50,
and for Wednesday at 2:30 $4.50
to $6.50.

CUCKOO'S NEXT continues at
the Little Fox Theater, Tuesday
through Fridays at 8:30, Saturday
at 7 and 10:30, and Sundays at
7:30. For ticket information, call
434-4738.

continues at the Orpheum Theater
in San Francisco at 8:30 Tuesday
through Saturday and at 2:30
Wednesday, Saturday and Sunday.
For information call 621-5000.

$3.75-$6.50; for Friday at 8:30

Theater in Berkeley at 8:30

$2 for students.

at Zellerbach Auditorium. Tickets

to $2.50 students).
