# Invitational Swim Meet
## 
### 
Stanford swimmers will
participate in their first
competition of the season this
Friday and Saturday in the first
anual Stanford Invitational swim
meet at the Encina pools.
The meet will consist of three
sessions comprised of the NCAA
championship meet events. The
first session is Friday afternoon at
3:30 p.m. The second session is
Saturday morning at 10 a.m., with
the final session at 3 p.m.
Saturday afternoon.
This meet will be the Cardinals
only local competition until the
regular season starts in January.
Among those participating in the
meet for Stanford is Olympic gold
medalist John Hercker, defending
NCAA champion Brian Job, and
last year's most improved
swimmer Terry Sutton.
