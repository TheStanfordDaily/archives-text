# IM OFFICIALS
## 
### 
All those interested in
officiating at intramural
basketball games this year
should attend a meeting
today at 4 p.m. in Maples
Pavilion. Techniques of
officiating and new rules will
be reviewed at the meeting.
Prospective officials will be
paid two dollars for
attendance at the meeting
and will also receive a fee for
each game at which they
officiate.
