# Happenings
## 
### 
TUESDAY, JANUARY 9
Tonight is opening night for
the ACT production of Ibsen's
The Doll's House at the Geary
Theater at 8:30. Tickets are $4.90
to $6.90, and only a few are left.
For tickets and information call
673-6440.
WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 10
The Western Opera Theater
season opens with two
performances of Rossini's La
Cenerentola at 10 a.m. and 1 p.m.
in the San Jose Civic Auditorium.
The performance will be in
English. For further information
call 861-4074,
Contralto Janet Baker makes

her sixth guest appearance with
the San Francisco Symphony
tonight at 8:30 in the Opera
House. The program includes
Berlioz's Les Nuits d'Ete and
Prokofieff's Scythian Suite. SOLD
OUT.
The English Department and
English Review Club open their
film series tonight at 7:30 in
Cubberley Auditorium with a
double feature: The Philadelphia
Story starring Cary Grant and
Katherine Hepburn, and Christmas
in July with Dick Powell and
Ellen Drew. Admission is $1.


MAESTRO SEIJI OZAWA—the San Francisco Symphony rehearses
for their performance this week. Contralto Janet Baker is guest soloist
for Berlioz's Les Nuits D'Ete. See below for further information.
