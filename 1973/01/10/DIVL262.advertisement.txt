SUMMER GRANTS
TO
LATIN AMERICA
The Center for Latin American Studies offers research grants
providing round-trip air fares and $150 stipends to students com
pleting their junior year at Stanford. The program is open both to
those with considerable Latin American course work and to those
who are "beginners" in the field. Students must possess the
necessary language competency and must have demonstrated the
ability to work independently.
Contact
Bolivar House
582 Alvarado Row
X 4444
Deadline for applications: January 15.
