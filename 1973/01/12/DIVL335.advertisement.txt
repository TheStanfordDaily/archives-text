Dr. David A. Hubbard, our preacher of the morning, is President and
Professor of Old Testament at Fuller Theological Seminary in Pasadena.

the Gospel Broadcasting Association. He has taught Old Testament at
Westmont College, St. Andrew's in Scotland, Cambridge and London.
Those who wish to meet and talk with Dr. Hubbard will have the
opportunity to do so over an informal buffet lunch in the Round Room
Immediately following the service. The service will be broadcast over
