# Chinese Life Discussion
## 
### 
Yale University Professor Gerald Tannebaum, who has just
returned to the United States after 26 years in China, will speak
today at 8 p.m. in the Tresidder Large and Small Lounges.
Tannebaum will discuss "Daily Life in China since the Cultural
Revolution." His speech is sponsored by the Office of Public
Events and the Center for East Asia Studies. Tannebaum was a
"participant in political and cultural affairs at all levels of Chinese
society."
