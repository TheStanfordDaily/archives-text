'Daily' Editor Election
Voting in the election for Editor of Volume 163 will take
place tomorrow from 10 a.m.-5 p.m. in the Daily office.
Candidates will speak at today's staff luncheon at noon. There
will also be another staff luncheon tomorrow at noon. All staff
members are urged to attend both lunches and to vote on
Friday. Staff members who will be unable to vote on Friday,
must request an absentee ballot by 5 p.m. today.
