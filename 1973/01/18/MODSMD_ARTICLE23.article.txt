# Happenings
## 
### 
Information on forthcoming
events for the weekly schedule of
entertainment activities should be
sent to the Daily as soon as
possible. Because of early
deadlines, all entries for
Thursday's "Happenings" must
reach the Daily office no later
than Monday noon. Send all items
to "Happenings," Entertainment
Editor, The Stanford Daily,
Stanford, California, 94305.
