Today In The 'Daily'
Today is Wednesday, January 24, 1973.
On this date in 1848, a gold nugget was found in California's
Sierra Nevada, touching off the gold rush of 1849. Fifty-one
years ago today, the Eskimo pie was patented.
Any fool who finds a shiny chunk of rock in a puddle in
White Plaza should call our editorial office at 321-2300, ext.
4632.
Those who wish to discuss the continuing food price freeze
should contact our business office at 327-4150.
