# Newman Concert Cancelled
## 
### 
The Anthony Newman Concert scheduled for tomorrow at 8 p.m.
in Dinkelspiel Auditorium has been cancelled due to Newman's
contraction of the "London flu." The concert has been rescheduled
for Monday, January 29 at 8 p.m. in Dinkelspiel Auditorium. Friday
tickets will be good for the Monday performance. Those with Friday
tickets who can not attend the Monday performance may obtain a
refund by mail through the Tresidder Union Box Office.
