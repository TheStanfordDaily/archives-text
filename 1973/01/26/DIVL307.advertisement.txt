BICYCLE WARNING
Stanford Public Safety
officer Jack Donals warned
Tuesday that Santa Clara
County Sheriff's deputies will
soon begin citing, and later
impounding, all unlicensed
bicycles on campus.
Donals added that bikes
can be licensed at the Public-
Safety Department on Serra
Street just off Campus Drive
on Mondays, Wednesdays,
and Fridays from 9 to 11
a.m., and on Tuesdays and
Thursdays from noon to 3
