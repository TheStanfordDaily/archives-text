# THE 						STANFORD DAILY
## 
### 
THE STANFORD DAILY is owned and published by the Associated
Students of Stanford University Monday through Friday during the
academic year except dead and finals week.
Letters, columns, and cartoons on the opinions pages represent
only the views of their authors.
Editorials represent the opinion of a majority vote by the Dally
editorial board, composed of 12 editors and four at-large members
elected by the staff.
Nothing on the opinions page represents a position of the Dally
staff, the ASSU, or the University.
Subscription rates: In the U.S., Canada, and Mexico, $15 per year,
$6 per quarter; overseas, $20 per year, $8 per quarter. Cash In
advance only — write the Dally, Storke Student Publications Building,
Stanford, California 94305 for further Information.
(Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto
under the act of March 3, 1897.)
