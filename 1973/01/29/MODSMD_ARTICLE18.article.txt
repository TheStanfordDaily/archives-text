4 servings
slice of ham 1 to IV2 inches thick
l A teaspoon cloves
1 can (1 pound) cling peaches
V2 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon butter
Heat broiler and grease broiler
rack with a piece of ham fat. Gash
the fat in several places to prevent
curling during cooking. Place meat
on the rack, 2 inches from the
source of heat. Broil 20 to 25
minutes on each side. While meat
is broiling, drain can of peaches.
Combine cloves, sugar and
cornstarch. Add to peach juice
and cook over low heat, stirring
until thickened. When ham is
turned, arrange drained peaches
on broiler. Dot with butter and

broil until slightly brown. Serve
with ham. Pass sauce separately.
