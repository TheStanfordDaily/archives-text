# THE 						STANFORD DAILY
## 
### 
THE STANFORD DAILY is an Independent student newspaper owned and
published by The Stanford Dally Publishing Corporation Monday through
Friday during the academic year except dead and finals week.
Editorials represent the opinion of a majority vote by the Daily editorial
board, composed of 12 editors and four at-large members elected by the
staff.
Letters, columns and cartoons on the opinions pages represent only the
views of their authors.
Nothing on the opinions page represents a position of the Daily staff,
the ASSU or the University.
Subscription rates: In the U.S., Canada and Mexico, $15 per year, $6
per quarter; overseas, $20 per year, $8 per quarter. Cash in advance
only—write the Dally, Storke Student Publications Building, Stanford,
California 94305 for further Information.
(Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto under
the act of March 3, 1897.)
