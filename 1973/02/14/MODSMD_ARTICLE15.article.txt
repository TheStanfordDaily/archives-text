# 'SKIN TRADE' AUTHOR
## 
### 
John O'Neill author of
Sociology as a Skin Trade will
speak today at 4:15 p.m., in
Tresidder 270. His talk, "On
the Way to Sociology-," will
relate the philosophy of
Heidegger to contemporary
sociological trends. O'Neill is
the author of several recent
sociological studies including
works on Marx, Hegel and
Merleau-Ponty.
The talk is co-sponsored
by Modern Thought and
Literature and the Sociology
Department.
