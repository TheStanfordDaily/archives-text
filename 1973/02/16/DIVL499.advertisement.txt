INTERVIEW
King Broadcasting Com
pany, a growing communica
tions company, headquartered
in Seattle, Wash., would like
to meet with graduating
seniors and graduate students
interested in broadcasting
Work.
King owns and operates
TV-AM-FM broadcast stations
in Seattle, Portland and Spo
kane and a community an
tenna (CATV) business.
We will be interviewing for
a limited number of entry
level positions in advertising
time sales, promotion, news
reporting, programming, pro
duction, announcing, writing,
photography and film editing.
Preference is given those
with broad liberal arts educa
tional backgrounds and who
have evidenced an interest
in broadcasting through work
on campus newspaper staffs,
radio station staffs, etc., but
such experience is not re
quired.
For further information, you
may consult King's "Fact
Sheet" and the Company's
Policy Manual at your Place
ment Office, 'Where an inter
view with our representative
may also be scheduled.
King is an Equal Oppor
tunity Employer.
