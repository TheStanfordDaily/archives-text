ROBERT McAFEE BROWN
SUNDAY, FEBRUARY 18, MEMORIAL CHURCH
11 A.M. University Public Worship
(Child Care Provided)
"How is Your 'Cantus Firmus'?" will be the topic of the
sermon delivered by Interim Dean of the Chapel Robert
McAfee Brown this week. Dr. Brown will be drawing on the
writing of Dietrich Bonhoeffer, and musical illustration.
Those wishing to meet with Dr. Brown will have an op
portunity to do so over the customary informal buffet in the
Round Room following the service.
The 11 A.M. service is broadcast over KZSU, 90.1 F.M.
9 AM COMMUNION SERVICE
Folk music, readings, and celebration form the context for
the informal communion service. Bread and wine will be
shared, and all are invited, regardless of their
denominational affiliation.
5 P.M., MASS, for the Catholic Community
