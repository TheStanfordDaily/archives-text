# HOFFA SPEECH
## 
### 
James Hoffa, former Teamsters
Union president, will speak here
tonight at 7:30 p.m. on "Prison
Reform."
Hoffa, who was paroled last
year from Federal prison after
serving several years for jury
tampering, has been barred from
any further labor management
activities until 1980.
His lecture is being sponsored
by the Stanford Law Forum, and
will be given in the Annenberg
Auditorium of the Cummings Art
Building. It is open to the public
without charge.
