# Sex, Love, and Utopia Speech
## 
### 
Prof. Ernest van den Haag will speak tonight at 8 p.m. in
Memorial Auditorium on "Sex, Love, and Utopia." His views will
be challenged by a panel of educators, including Dr. Harvey
Caplan, Asst. Psychiatry Prof. Donald Lunde, and Pat Miller,
president of Family Planning Alternative.
Van den Haag's appearance is part of a lecture series sponsored
by the Stanford Conservative Forum, a faculty-staff organization.
