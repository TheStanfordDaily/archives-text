# Opinions Page Policy
## 
### 
Once again, dear friends, the Daily opinions page, in its
perennial aspiration to the lively exchange of significant ideas,
is soliciting articles. Put down this newspaper, leap to your
typewriter, and have at it. We encourage your submission of
letters, articles, and cartoons of every description; everybody
wins a prize.
