# Fisher-Groslimond Lose In Finals
## 
### 
Stanford's first-seeded
doubles team of Rick Fisher
and Gery Groslimond
finished second in the 83rd
Annual Pacific Coast Men's
Doubles tournament in La
Jolla over the weekend.
Fisher and Groslimond
bowed to Jeff Austin and
Bob Kreiss of UCLA 6-7, 4-6,
7-6, 6-2, 6-0 in the final
yesterday, after coming
within a tiebreaker of a
straight-set win.
The other Stanford teams,
Chip Fisher and Jim Delaney,
and Pat DuPre and John
Whitlinger, bowed in the
quarterfinals.
