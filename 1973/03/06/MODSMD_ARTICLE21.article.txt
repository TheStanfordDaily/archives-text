BUNRAKU PUPPETS — from Japan are scheduled for an eight-performance run at the Nourse
Auditorium located at Hayes and Franklin Streets, from March 5 through the 11th. This marks their
second U.S. tour. Tickets for the evenings of March 5-7, and 8 are $3.50-$6.50, and for evenings of March
9 and 10 are $4.50-$7.50. Evening performances are at 8:30. Tickets for matinees at 2:30 on March 10
and 11 are $3.50-$5.75. Tickets for a special student matinee on March 7 at 1:30 are $3.
