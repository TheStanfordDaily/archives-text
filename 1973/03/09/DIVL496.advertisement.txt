FOUNDERS' DAY
SUNDAY, MARCH 11,
MEMORIAL CHURCH
11 A.M. University Public Worship
The sermon for the Founders' Day observance in Memorial
Church will be given by Dr. Philip Rhinelander, Olive
Palmer Professor of Humanities, and Professor of
Philosophy.
9 A.M. COMMUNION SERVICE
5 P.M. MASS, for the Catholic Community
7:30 P.M. EVENING IN THE ROUND ROOM
The Rev. Chris Hartmire, of the National Farmworker
Ministry will show the United Farm Workers film "Si, se
Puede," which chronicles the organizing efforts of the UFW
in Arizona, in response to legislation which threatened to
destroy the union. The Rev. Hartmire will then talk on his
experiences with the California Migrant Ministry, and the
National Farmworker Ministry.
