# Grad Volleyballers Compete At Maples
## 
### 
The Stanford graduate
volleyball team will close out
its league schedule in the next
10 days with two games at
Maples Pavilion. On
Wednesday, March 14, the
grads will take on the
Berkeley YMCA and on
Tuesday, March 20, they will
face the San Francisco
Olympic Club. Both games
will begin at 7:30 and there is
no admission charge.
The Olympic Club won
the USVBA, Region 12, AA
championship last year and is
challenging for titles again
this year at both the regional
and national level.
