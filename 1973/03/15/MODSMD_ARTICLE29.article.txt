# AIM Leader To Speak
## 
### 
Vernon Bellecourt, the National Director of the American Indian
Movement (AIM) and one of the main organizers of the Wounded Knee
takeover, will speak at Memorial Auditorium tonight at 8:30 p.m. There will
be an admission charge of one dollar.
In his talk, Bellecourt will explain the reasons why the AIM is at Wounded
Knee and answer questions from the audience.
Bellecourt, a Chippewa Indina from White Earth, Minnesota, organized the
Denver Chapter of the AIM five years ago and was elected director last year.
| His borther Clyde, who is at Wounded Knee now, founded the AIM in 1968.
