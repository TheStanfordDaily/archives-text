# WATER MAIN REPAIRS
## 
### 
In order to repair a defective valve in a water
main, Plant Services Manager of Utilities H.L.
Davis announced his crew will sharply reduce the
water pressure to most of the Faculty and Staff
private residences for a period of five hours, from
1 a.m. until 6 a.m. on Tuesday, March 20.
The areas or buildings affected will be: San
Juan Subdivision, Pine Hills 11, Frenchman's Hill,
Fraternity Clusters I and 11, Behavioral Science
Center, and the Golf Clubhouse.
