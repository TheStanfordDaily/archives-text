conducted by Seiji Ozawa, greets
pianist Peter Serkin tonight at 8
p.m. at U.C. Berkeley's Zellerbach
Auditorium. Tomorrow and
Saturday the performance will be
at the Opera House. Works to be
played include: Cowell's
Sinfonietta and Bach's Piano
Concerto Number Three. Tickets
are $4.50, $7, and $10.

Memorial Theater in San

with tickets available from $4.50
to $6.50.

Gentlemen of Verona, winner of

Friday and Saturday prices are

on Wednesday, with tickets
costing $2 to $5.75 and Saturday,
with tickets $2.45 to $6.45.

$4.50 to $10.

screens A Clockwork Orange at 7
and 9:30 p.m. Tickets are 50
cents; a season pass is $4.50.

Francisco Symphony's European
Tour. Works include J.C. Bach's
Sinfonia Number Four and
Rusk's Cloud Messenger at the
Opera House at 3 p.m. Tickets are
$4.50 to $10.

Auditorium, the Francesco Trio
with guest French horn player
Earl Saxon, will play the music of

Beethoven, Brahms and others.
Admission is $2, $1 for students.

Community Theater at 6 and 9
p.m. Bobby Womack will also
play at 9 only. Tickets are $3.50,
$4.50 and $5.50.

screens A Clockwork Orange at 7
and 9:30 p.m. Tickets are 50
cents; a season pass is $4.50.
