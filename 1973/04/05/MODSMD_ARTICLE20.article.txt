# Pauline Kael To Speak
## 
### 
Pauline Kael will speak in Tresidder Large Lounge on Friday at 8.
She is film critic for the "New Yorker," and author of "Kiss, Kiss,
Bamg, Bang," "I Lost It at the Movies," and"The Citizen Kane
Book."
She has just returned from two weeks in Hollywood, which
included confrontations with the Academy Awards as well as Rona
Barrett.
Kael's appearance is sponsored by the Communications
Department. Admission is free.
