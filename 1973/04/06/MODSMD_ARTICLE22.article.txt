# Rehnquist To Visit
## 
### 
Assoc. Justice William H.
Rehnquist of the U.S. Supreme
Court will head a panel of
Stanford Law School Alumni
presiding over the finals of the
21st annual Marion Rice
Kirkwood Moot Court
competition. The event will take
place Saturday April 7 at 2 p.m.
in the Cummings Art Auditorium.
The hypothetical case to be
argued involves a newspaper
publisher who is convicted under
the general federal theft law for
publishing secret government
information. The case involves the
same statute currently under
consideration in the "Pentagon
Papers" trial.
