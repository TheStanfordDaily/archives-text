# Walk For Mankind
## 
### 
The Third Annual Project Concern 20 mile Walk for Mankind will
take place on Saturday, April 7. Starting point is Maples Pavilion.
Registration is from 7 a.m. to 10 a.m.
Proceeds benefit the poverty stricken with congenital ' and
traumatic defects in need of reconstructive surgery. Charitable,
non-profit groups are invited to share in the proceeds. A local
non-profit group registering with the Walk Committee can receive 20%
of the amount that their walkers raise on the Walk.
