# Women's Tennis Team Scores Shutout Victory
## 
### PATRICIA LOWDON 
The Women's tennis team
shutout Fresno State Saturday
6-0. Exciting and close matches
characterized the meet, which was
held at the Stanford Women's
Courts.
First-seeded sophomore
Andrea Barnes edged Karen
Furguson 4-6, 7-5, 6-1. Barnes was
down 6-4, 5-3, 30-love before her
backhand started clicking.
Number-two Tracey McNair
out-volleyed her opponent to
triumph 6-4, 7-5. Maureen
O'Keefe outlasted her opponent,

giving Stanford a 6-4, 6-4 victory
at third singles.
Juniors Terry Albert and
Marlene Veach were forced into
three sets before winning 6-7, 6-2,
6-4. Both second and third
doubles won in straight sets.
Freshman Ann Feeley paired with
junior Vicki Van Dyke. Freshman
Kathy Wright and junior Molly
Tyson gave Stanford its sixth
victory.
Stanford now 3-0 on the
season, will host Santa Clara
Thursday, April 12 at 3 p.m. on
the Women's Courts.
