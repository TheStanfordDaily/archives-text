# DEVI PRASAD TO SPEAK
## 
### 
Devi Prasad, the newly elected
Chairman of the War Resisters
International and a former
associate of Mahatma Gandhi in
India, will speak in the Round
Room of Mem Chu tonight at
8:30 p.m.
He will be speaking on the
subject: SATYAGRAHA
(Truth-Force), the Way to
Liberation and Lasting Peace.
Prasad's appearance is being
sponsored by the ASSU Speakers
Bureau.
