# CONSUMER OFFICAL TO SPEAK TONIGHT
## 
### 
Larry Sheahan, Director of
the Santa Clara County
Department of Consumer
Affairs, will speak tonight at
8 p.m. at a meeting of
Consumer's United at the
Palo Alto First Congrega
tional Church. Sheahan will
review some cases he has
handled and discuss plans for
expansion of the department
into other areas besides
grievances.
Sheahan requests that the
public bring suggestions to
aid his department in working
more closely with consumers.
The meeting is open to the
public and free of charge.
