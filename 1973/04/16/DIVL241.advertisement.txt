HOUSING DRAW
Spring' House Draw for undergraduate residences
1973-74 begins today and lasts through Friday,
April 20. Eligible students should pick up draw
cards and supplementary instructions in Residences
Hall Offices. Number can be drawn in the lobby
of the Old Union, first floor, from 9 a.m. to 12
noon and from 1 to 4 p.m.
For further information consult resident staff
or the housing office, room 308, Old Union,
321-2300, ext. 3047.
