# Adv. 2 Page 2
## 
### 
It is election day. Remember that all that is necessary for the
triumph of evil in this world is for enough good men to do
nothing; and vote.
The Daily opinions page will be pleased to consider your
submissions, be they scholarly or whimsical, on all manner of
subjects. Especially when they're double-spaced on a 62-space
line. Do not shirk from being outrageous; extremism in the
defense of liberty is no vice.
