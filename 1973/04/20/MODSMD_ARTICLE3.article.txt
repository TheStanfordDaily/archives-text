# BULLETIN
## 
### 
WASHINGTON (AP) - Former Atty.
Gen. John Mitchell has been
summoned from New York to testify
before the Watergate federal grand
jury in Washington, it was reported
early this morning.
Mitchell repeated his previous
statements that he had nothing to do
with the break-in and bugging of the
Democratic National Headquarters last
June.
Further details were not
immediately available.
