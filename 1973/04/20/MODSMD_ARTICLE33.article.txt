# Ottmar Recovering From Surgery
## 
### 
Reserve quarterback Dave
Ottmar is recovering normally
from knee surgery Monday,
an athletic department
spokesman reported
yesterday. However, the
spokesman added that it wil!
take "about five months," for
the first-string punter to
return to normal condition.
It was also reported that
no decision has been made on
whether offensive tackle
Mark Ferguson will have to
undergo knee surgery.
Ferguson has injured his right
knee several times previously.
Both knee injuries occured
during spring football
practice Monday.
