Overt Stew
6 to 8 servings
2 lbs. beef stew meat
2 tbsp. oil
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
1 carrot, peeled and shredded
1/3 cup red wine
1 (3 oz.) can chopped mushrooms, drained
2 tbsp. dry onion soup mix
Brown meat in oil. Transfer to casserole. Combine remaining
ingredients; pour over meat. Bake, covered, at 350 degrees for 2Vi
hours, stirring occasionally.
FRIDAY
Tomato-Sauced Fish, Brussel Sprouts, Carrot-Pineapple Molded Salad,
Rolls, Chocolate Cake, Beverage
Tomato-Sauced Fish
6 servings
2 lbs. frozen fish filets, thawed
salt
1 (3 oz.) can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 (8 oz.) can tomato sauce
1 tbsp. lemon juice
14 tsp. dried thyme leaves, crushed
Arrange filets in single layer in greased baking dish. Sprinkle with
salt. Top with mushrooms. Blend the tomato sauce, lemon juice and
thyme; pour over fish. Bake, covered, at 350 degrees about 30 to 35
minutes. If desired, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.

Frank and Potato Bake
8 servings
1 lb. frankfurters
2/3 cup nonfat dry milk
1 tbsp. flour
2 cups processed cheese spread, cut up
2 tbsp. minced onion
2 tsp. finely chopped parsley
8 medium potatoes, cooked, peeled and sliced
Reserve 3 franks; slice remaining franks and set aside. Stir milk into
flour until smooth. Add cheese; cook and stir until thick. Add onion,
parsley and x /i tsp. salt. Fold cheese mixture into potatoes and sliced
franks. Put in casserole. Halve reserved franks and arrange them in a
pinwheel on top of the casserole. Bake uncovered for 10 minutes.
