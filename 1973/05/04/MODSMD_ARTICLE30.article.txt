# Stanford Third In Women's Golf Tourney
## 
### 
Stanford freshmen Margie
Leno and Pat Cornett tied for
third in a field of 17 teams
Monday and Tuesday in the
California Women's
Intercollegiate golf
tournament at California
Country Club in Whittier.
Leno finished eighth in the
individual competition on
rounds of 82-86 for a 168
total, 11 strokes behind
individual champion Alex
Reinhardt of New Mexico.
Cornett finished ninth with
169 on rounds of 88-81.
Arizona, represented by
Gayle Runke and Paula Eger,
won the event with a 325
total, two strokes ahead of
defending champions Arizona
State and 12 ahead of
Stanford.
