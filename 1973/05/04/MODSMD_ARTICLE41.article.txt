# I. F. Stone Speaks
## 
### 
Noted journalist I.F. Stone of the
"New York Review of Books" will
speak tonight at 8 p.m. at Memorial
Church to mark the third anniversary
of the killings at Kent State.
Stone will speak on "The Lessons
of Kent State and Watergate."
Communication student and Daily
columnist Daniel Brenner will also
speak.
Admission is free. The service is
sponsored by Memorial Church and
the ASSU Speakers' Bureau.
