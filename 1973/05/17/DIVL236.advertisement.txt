THE STANFORD DAILY is an independent student newspaper I
owned and published by the Stanford Daily Publishing
Corporation Monday through Friday during the academic year
except dead and finals week.
Editorials represent the opinion of a majority vote by the
Daily editorial board, composed of 13 editors and four at large
members elected by the staff.
Letters, columns and cartoons on the opinions pages
represent only the views of their authors.
(Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo
Alto under the act of March 3, 1897.)
