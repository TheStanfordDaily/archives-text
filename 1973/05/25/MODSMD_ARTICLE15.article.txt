# Wolfe to Speak
## 
### 
Author Tom Wolfe will
speak at Memorial
Auditorium tomorrow at 8
p.m. Wolfe wrote The
Electric Kool-Aid Acid Test;
The Pumphouse Gang;
Radical Chic and Mau-Mauing
the Flak Catchers; and The
Kandy-Kolored
Tangerine-Flake Streamline
Baby.
He is a frequent
contributor to New York,
Rolling Stone and Esquire.
Wolfe's discussion of "Class
Warfare Among Youth" is
sponsored by the ASSU
Speakers Bureau.
