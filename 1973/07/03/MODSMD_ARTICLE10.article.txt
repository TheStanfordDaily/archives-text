# THE 						STANFORD DAILY
## 
### 
THE STANFORD DAILY is an independent student newspaper owned anr.
published by the Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation on Tuesday anc
Friday during summer quarter and Monday through Friday during the
academic year, except dead and finals week.
Editorials represent the opinion of a majority vote of the Daily editorial
board, composed of five editors.
Letters, columns and cartoons on the opinions pages represent only the
views of their authors. Nothing on the opinions pages represents a position
of the Daily staff, the ASSU or the University.
Telephone: Editorial, (415) 321-2300, ext. 4632; Business, (415)
327-4150.
Subscriptions: in the U.S., Canada and Mexico, $4 for summer quarter;
$8 for autumn, winter or spring quarter; $20 per academic year. Send
check or money order in advance only to the Stanford Daily, Storke
Student Publications Building, Stanford, California, 94305.
