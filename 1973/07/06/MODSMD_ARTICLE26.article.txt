# Theta Delt Wins IM Championship
## 
### 
Theta Delta Chi won the
1972-73 Intramural
championship, scoring a total of
511-2/3 points, the athletic
department has announced.
A second fraternity, Kappa
Alpha, finished a distant second
with 449 points. JRA was the first
dormitory team, with a score of
353-5/6, Shakey's won the
independent title, with 341, the
Biz II team the graduate title
(264-1/3), and El Toro, the club
championship (223-1/2).
In all, some 230 teams entered
in competition.
