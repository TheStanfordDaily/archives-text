ON CAMPUS
The Tresidder Friday film
series presents THE MISFITS
tonight at 9 and 11:30 p.m. for
$1 in the Tresidder lounges. The
flick stars Clark Gable and
Marilyn Monroe.
The final performances of
Anouilh's THE REHEARSAL,
presented by the drama
department, take place tonight
and tomorrow at 8 p.m. in the
Nitery. Tickets $1 at the door.
Hear the PRESERVATION
HALL JAZZ BAND in a special
outdoor picnic-concert tomorrow
at 2 p.m. in Frost Amphitheater.
(PHJB will perform in Stern
Grove in San Francisco Sunday at
2 p.m.)
See Malcolm McDowell (of
"Clockwork Orange") in IF
tomorrow night at 7 and 9:15
p.m. and the unforgettable
M*A*S*H with Donald
Sutherland and Elliott Gould on
Sunday at 7 and 9:20 p.m. Both
films will be shown in Mem Aud,
brought to you by ASSU Summer
Flicks.

vaudeville-expose THE SAN

on the Wilbur dorm lawn.
Admission free!
