SATURDAY
Chicken livers stroganoff, hot
cooked rice, lettuce and tomato
salad, peach melba and beverage
Chicken Livers Stroganoff
serves four
1/2 lb. chicken livers, halved
2 cups onion, sliced thin
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup butter
1 tablespoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 dash pepper
parsley
Cook onion in butter until
tender but not brown. Add livers.
Season with paprika, salt and
pepper. Slowly brown livers.
Cover and cook over low heat for
10 minutes or until livers are
tender. Add sour cream; heat
through. Serve over hot cooked
rice. Gamish with parsley.
SUNDAY
Glazed ham with potato patties,

molded cucumber salad, hard
rolls, vanilla ice cream, sugar

Glazed Ham with
Potato Patties

serves four
1 (3/4 inch) slice fully
cooked ham, about 1 lb.
1 (12 oz.) pack frozen
potato patties
1/2 cup apricot jam or
preserves
3 tablespoons melted butter
1 tablespoon water
paprika
salt
Mix apricot jam or preserves,
water and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Slash
fat edge of ham slice. Place ham
and potato patties on broiler-pan
rack. Spread half the apricot glaze
on the ham; brush the potatoes
with half the butter. Broil three
inches from heat for six minutes.
Turn ham and patties. Spread ham
with remaining glaze. Butter
patties; season. Broil for six
minutes more.

Broiled Lamb Chops Parmesan
serves six
6 lamb chops
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons butter
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 dash pepper
Broil chops three to four
inches from heat for eight to 10
minutes or until lightly browned.
Turn; broil four to five minutes or
as long as desired. Blend
remaining ingredients; spread on
chops. Broil one to two minutes

or until cheese is lightly browned.

Tangy Potato Salad
1 serves four
1 can cream of celery soup
1/4 cup nonfat dry milk
2 tablespoons swet pickle
relish
2 tablespoons vinegar
1 tablespoon finely chopped
onion
3 cups diced, peeled, cooked
potatoes
5 frankfurters, slicked into
one-inch pieces
parsley
salt and pepper
In a skillet combine first five
ingredients, 1/2 teaspoon salt and
a dash of pepper; cook and stir
until boiling. Stir in potatoes and
franks; heat through. Top with
snipped parsley.

Beef Burgers
serves six
1 egg
1/2 cup fine crushed
saltine crackers
1/4 cup catsup
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 dash pepper
1 lb. ground beef
1 tablespoon shortening
1 can tomato soup
1 cup drained whole
kernal corn
2 tablespoons milk
1/2 teaspoon marjoram
Combine first seven
ingredients; add ground beef and
mix well. Shape into six patties.
Brown on both sides in hot
shortening. Combine remaining
ingredients and pour over meat.
Cook, covered, over low heat for
10 minutes.
