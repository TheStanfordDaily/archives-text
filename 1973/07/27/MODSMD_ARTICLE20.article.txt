ON CAMPUS

Bergman and starring Liv Ullman
and Max Von Sydow, will be

The Stanford Savoyards will
perform TRIAL BY JURY,
Gilbert and Sullivan's only one-act
comic opera, at 7:30 p.m. today
and tomorrow. Refreshments will
be served at the performance in
the Wilbur Hall courtyard. And
it's absolutely free!
The ASSU FLICKS will
present a program of five short
films Saturday at 6:30, 8:30, and
10:30 p.m. in Cubberley
Auditorium. The films include
"The Red Balloon," an academy
award winning French film, "Why
Man Creates," a highly acclaimed
film sequence of explorations,
episodes and comments on
creativity that also won an oscar,
"To See or Not To See," an
award-winning film from
Czechoslovakia, "Evolution," an
animated film that would make
even Charles Darwin chuckle, and
"Solo," a breathtaking film about
the joys of solo mountain
dimbing.
And on Sunday night in
Cubberley, the flicks brings you

YELLOW SUBMARINE starring
the voices of John, Paul, George,
and Ringo.
