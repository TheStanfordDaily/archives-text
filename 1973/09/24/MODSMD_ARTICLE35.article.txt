# Trustee Dies
## 
### 
University trustee Benjamin S.
Crocker Jr., 38, died July 27 at his
Pasadena home.
Crocker, chairman of the board of
Lincoln Savings and Loan Association
of Los Angeles, had been a member of
the Board of Trustees since last year.
He is survived by his wife, Nan; his
children, Nina and Scott; his parents,
Mr. and Mrs. Benjamin S. Crocker Sr.
and his brother, Don. Funeral services
were private.
Crocker graduated from Stanford in
1956. He became vice president of the
Stanford Annual Fund program in
1969 and chairman in 1971. In May,
Crocker received the Golden Spike
Award, the highest honor of the
Annual Fund.
