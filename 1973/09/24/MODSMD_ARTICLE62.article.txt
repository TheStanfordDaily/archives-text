# Season Ticket Procedures
## 
### 
The Athletic Department has outlined the
following procedure for purchasing student
football tickets:
1.) The Season Rooter sale will be conducted
Monday, Sept. 24 and Tuesday, Sept. 25 at
Maples Pavilion from 8:30 a.m. to 4:15 p.m.
2.) Season books will be sold after
registration, only if available, at the Department
of Athletics.
3.) In order to purchase tickets, students must
have a validated Fall Quarter Photo-ID card and a
1973 Athletic-ASSU sticker. These two are
affixed to the card at the final step of
registration.
4.) A student is entitled to bring a limit of
two cards to the window.
5.) The Season Rooter Book contains one
ticket to each of the following games:
San Jose Sept. 29
UCLA Oct. 13
Washington St Oct. 27

Oregon Nov. 17
California Nov. 24
6.) Married students are allowed to purchase a
rooter book for their spouse after completing the
married registration form and signing a married
statement at the Married Student's Table.
7.) USC Rooter Tickets will be sold on an
available basis only at the Department of
Athletics October 3 at 8:30 a.m. A student can
buy only one ticket per student body card. A
married student can purchase an additional ticket
for his/her spouse.
8.) Prices:
Student Rooter Book $9.00
Spouse Rooter Book $9.50
USC Rooter Ticket $3.25
USC-Spouse Rooter Ticket $3.25
9.) Individual student tickets are sold on an
available basis only. If available, individual
tickets will be sold at the stadium on the day of
the game.
