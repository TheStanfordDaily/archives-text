All students will be required to pay
$6 in ASSU fee assessments autumn
quarter. ASSU Special Events, the
Stanford Band, The Stanford Daily,
KZSU, the ASSU Program Fee and the
ASSU Legal Counseling Office (LCO)
will each receive $1 per student.
Each fee is refundable and students
may receive the refunds at the ASSU
office in Tresidder Union from Oct. 1
through Oct. 12 from 12 noon to 4
p.m.
Students who claim refunds for the
LCO may not use that office's
facilities.
