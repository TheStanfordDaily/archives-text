# Untitled Article
## 
### 
Jerry Coleman, Peter Aleshire and Bill Evers, do it once a
week. Ed Kohn and Pete McCloskey do it regularly. You can do
it.
It doesn't take much doing. Combine a typewriter set
double-space for a 60-space line, with your own clever wit and
incisive views and drop off the result in the opinion editor's box
at the Daily Editorial Offices, Lomita and Panama Streets.
Go do it.
