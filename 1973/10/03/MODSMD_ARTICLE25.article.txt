# Fee Refunds
## 
### 
Refunds for student fee
assessments paid during autumn
quarter registration are now
available at the ASSU office in
Tresidder Union. Students may
request their refunds today
through Oct. 12 from 12 noon to
4 p.m.
Assessments for which refunds
are available are: the Daily, ASSU
Special Events, KZSU, the ASSU
Program Fee, the ASSU Legal
Counseling Office and the
Stanford Band.
