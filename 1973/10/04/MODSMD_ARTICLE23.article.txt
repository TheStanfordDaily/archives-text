THURSDAY, OCTOBER 4
LORELEI, starring Carol
Channing, continues nightly at the
Curran Theatre in San Francisco
at 8:30 p.m., with matinees at
2:30 on Wednesdays and
Saturdays.
Dale Wasserman's dramatic
version of Ken Kesey's ONE
FLEW OVER THE CUCKOO'S

the Little Fox Theatre in San
Francisco.

presents CHARLEY'S AUNT,

comedy. The play will be staged
Wednesdays thru Saturdays at
8:30 p.m., with Sunday
presentations at 7. Tickets are $3,
$2 for students.
STEPHEN STILLS and
MANASSAS, along with the SAL
VALENTINO BAND, appear at

night at 8 p.m. Tickets are $5 in
advance, $5.50 at the door.
The Stanford Museum of Art
presents in the Miedel Room the
ARTS OF VENICE IN THE 18TH
CENTURY thru Nov. 18. The
Museum is open Tuesday thru
Friday from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. and
weekends from 1 to 5 p.m.

Ivan Perestiani (1923) will be
screened tonight at 7:30 p.m. in
