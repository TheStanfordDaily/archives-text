# JOB FAIR
## 
### 
Nearly 100 national and Bay
area employers are participating in
a job opportunity fair at Stanford
October 6 and 7. Advance
estimates indicate some 2000
professional and technical
applicants will be present.
Ray Caldera, coordinator of
the event planned in Maples
Pavilion from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.
each day, quoted the anticipated
attendance figure after checking
college placement centers and
community agencies in the Bay
Area and Los Angeles.
Caldera said the purpose of the
fair is to provide employers with
the best selection of technical,
managerial, and professional
talent available.
Although the job fair is open
without charge to everyone, the
emphasis is being placed or
minority, veterans, and womei
job seekers.
