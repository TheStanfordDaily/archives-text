### 
Paul Jacobs on the
Middle East
Writer Paul Jacobs will speak on "War & Peace
in the Middle East" at a public meeting at
Kepler's Books, 825 El Camino, Menlo Park, at
8 p.m. Thursday, Oct. 11th.
Mr. Jacobs is author of the controversial
book, Between the Rock & the Hard Place, and
is one of the few observers who has intimate
knowledge of both Israel and the Arab countries.
The meeting is open to all.
