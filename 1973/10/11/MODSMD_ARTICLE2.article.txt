# Last Day Of Elections
## 
### 
Today is the final day of elections for the ASSU Senate and the
referendum on whether to accept the Committee of 15 recommendation
to replace the Campus Judicial Panel with a new Stanford Judicial Council.
Polling places are located at the history corner, the Graduate School of
Business lounge, the physics tank, and the medical school lounge, all open
from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m., and the post office and engineering corner, both
open from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
