THE STANFORD DAILY is an independent student newspaper owned
and published by the Stanford Dally Publishing Corporation Monday
through Friday during the academic year except dead and finals week.
Telephone: Editorial, 321-2300, ext. 4632; Business, 327-4150 or
$21-2300, ext. 2554.
Editorials represent the opinion of a majority vote of the Dally
editorial board, composed of 15 editors and four at large members
elected by the staff.
Letters, columns and cartoons on the opinions pages represent only
the views of their authors. Nothing on the opinions pagas represents a
position of the Dally staff, the ASSU or the University.
Subscription rates: In the U.S., Canada and Mexico, 915 per year, $8
per quarter. Send check or money order In advance only to the Dally,
Storke Student Publications Building, Stanford, California, 94305.
(Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto under
the act of March 3, 1897.)
