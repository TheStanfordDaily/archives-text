# ENROLLMENT
## 
### 
Stanford University
student enrollment totals
11,196 for fall quarter, the
Registrar's Office reports.
Undergraduates totaled
6417 an increase of five over
last year. Graduate students
totaled 4779.
Graduates in the
University's professional
schools as of the close of
registration in mid-October
were law, 487; medicine, 342;
business, 667; and education,
570.
