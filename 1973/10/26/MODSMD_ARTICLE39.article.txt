# Poloists Host Chico, Santa Barbara
## 
### 
Stanford's water polo team will
try to improve on its 9-5-1 record
this weekend when the Cards host
Chico State and UC-Santa Barbara
at Encina Pool. Chico is today's
opponent at 3:30 p.m. and Santa
Barabara will test the Cards at 10
am. tomorrow.
The junior varsity team also
faces a pair of weekend games, as
the Cards host Fremont High
School today at 4:30 p.m. and
travel to College of San Mateo for
a 10 a.m. game tomorrow.
