# String Quartet To Perform Guarneri
## 
### 
The Guarneri String Quartet plays a return engagement at Stanford at 2:30 p.m. Sunday,
Nov. 11, in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
The young chamber group of Arnold Steinhardt and John Dalley, violinists, Michael Tree,
viola, and David Soyer, cellist, display unique technical virtuosity, beauty of tone, and musical
understanding.
Their program contains three works of sharp contrast: the timely Quartet No. 3, Op. 22, by
Hindemith; the classic and elegant Beethoven Quartet in B-flat Major, Op. 18, No. 6; and the
nationalistic and extroverted Dvorak Quartet in A-flat Major, Op. 105.
