"Braised Short Ribs"
serves 4 to 6
3 lbs. beef short ribs, cut in
serving pieces
3/4 cup uncooked rice

V2 cup chopped onion
cup chopped celery
Va cup chopped green pepper
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
Va tsp. thyme
In skillet brown ribs slowly.
Season with salt. Place in large
casserole. Cover; bake at 325
degrees for 1 hour. In small skillet
combine rice, onion, celery and
green pepper; cook till rice is
lightly browned. After meat has
baked remove ribs from casserole;
pour off fat. Place rice mixture in
casserole; top with ribs. Combine
2Va cups water, 2 tsp. salt,
Worcestershire, thyme, and 1/8
tsp. pepper; heat to boiling. Pour
over ribs. Cover; bake one hour
more.

"Oven Bar-B-Q Swiss Steak"
serves 6
2 lbs. round steak
2 (8 oz.) cans tomato sauce
1 Tbsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. vinegar
1 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 dashes hot pepper sauce
1 medium onion, sliced
Combine 1/3 cup flour, a tsp.

salt and Va tsp. pepper; coat meat
with mixture. Brown on both
sides in hot fat. Spoon off excess
fat. Combine next 5 ingredients
and pour over. Add salt and
pepper to taste. Simmer
uncovered 5 minutes; add onion
slices. Cover and bake at 350
degrees for 114 hours or until fork
tender.

"Meat Loaf"
serves 6 to 8

2 Tbsp. chopped green pepper
IV2 tsp. salt
1 bay leaf, crushed

baking dish. Score the loaf by

"Steak Diane"
serves 4
4 loin beef steaks
1 tsp. dry mustard
Va cup butter
3 Tbsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. snipped chives
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
Pound steaks with meat mallet
to 1/3 inch thickness. Sprinkle
each side with salt, pepper, and
1/8 tsp. of the dry mustard;
pound into meat. Melt butter in
skillet. Add 2 steaks; cook 2
minutes on each side. Transfer to
hot serving plate. Repeat for
remaining meat. To skillet add
remaining ingredients; bring to
boiling. Return meat to pan;
spoon sauce over. Trim with
cooked fluted mushrooms.
