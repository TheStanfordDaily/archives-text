# MEMORIAL SERVICES
## 
### 
A memorial for the late Dr.
Marcus A. Foster will be held
tonight from 7:30 p.m. to 9:30
p.m. in Memorial Church. Dr.
Foster, who was shot and
killed on November 7, 1973,
was the Superintendent of
Schools for the Oakland
Unified School District, and a
member of the Board of the
Stanford Center for Research
and Development in
Education.
Charles Ogletree, ASSU
co-president, Chris Murray,
president, chairman of the
Associated Stanford Students
for Action, and Derek Toliver,
BSU chief, are sponsors and
student speakers for the
memorial, which will be
broadcast live by KZSU.
