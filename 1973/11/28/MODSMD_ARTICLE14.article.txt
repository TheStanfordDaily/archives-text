# S.F. SYMPHONY BUS SERVICE
## 
### 
Charter bus service to the
San Francisco Symphony's
Wednesday series will begin
this evening. Every Wednesday
for the duration of the season,
buses will leave the Tresidder
parking lot at 7:15 p.m. for the
Opera House.
Tickets are $2 round trip,
and should be purchased a
week in advance at the
Tresidder box office. Unsold
tickets, if any, will be available
at the bus, which begins to
load at 7 p.m.
For further information,
contact Carl Sunshine at
329-1486 or Roger Thacker at
Tresidder.
