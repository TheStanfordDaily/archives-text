# The 						Stanford Daily
## 
### 
THE STANFORD DAILY is an independent student newspaper owned and
published by the Stanford Dally Publishing Corporation. Special basketball
issues are published on weekends of Stanford home games, with the
cooperation of the Stanford Department of Athletics.
(Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto under the
act of March 3, 1897.^
Sports Editor Dave Robinson

Baker, Mark White

Athletic Director Joe Ruetz
