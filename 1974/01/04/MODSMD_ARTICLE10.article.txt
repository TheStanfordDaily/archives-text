# UCLA, USC Tickets
## 
### 
A limited number of tickets for next
weekend's UCLA and USC games in Los
Angeles are available at the Athletic
Department ticket office. Tickets for Friday
night's USC game are $3, and tickets for
Saturday afternoon's UCLA game are $4.
Students are reminded that Winter quarter
season ticket books are still available at the
Athletic Department. The books contain tickets
for all six remaining homes games, and insure
admission to the March 2 game against UCLA.
All reserved seat tickets for the UCLA game are
sold out.
