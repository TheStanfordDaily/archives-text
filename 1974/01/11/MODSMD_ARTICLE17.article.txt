# Cheech & Chong Show Set
## 
### 
ASSU Special Events will present its first of two comedy shows
this quarter next Friday night, January 18. Cheech and Chong,
"the world's first Chinese-Chicano comedy team in the world," will
appear in Maples at 8:30 p.m.
Tickets are $3.50 and $4.50, and are available at Tresidder and
most Bay Area agencies.
