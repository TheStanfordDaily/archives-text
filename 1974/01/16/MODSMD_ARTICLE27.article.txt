# McCloskey Discussion
## 
### 
Rep. Paul N. McCloskey, R-Menlo
Park, will meet with his Stanford-area
constituents today from 4-6 p.m. in
the Tresidder Union large lounge.
McCloskey will discuss what he calls
the "two crucial issues facing Congress
when we reconvene: energy legislation
and impeachment."
McCloskey has invited constituents
to express their views on current issues
and to discuss problems with federal
agencies and programs during the
meeting.
