# Parkening To Appear Friday
## 
### 
Classical guitarist
Christopher Parkening will
appear in concert this Friday,
Jan. 25, in Mem Aud.
His program includes works
of J.S. Bach, Weiss, Ravel,
Debussy, Villa-Lobos, Handel,
Lauro and Albeniz.
Parkening is one of the most
highly regarded young
guitarists in the world. He has
studied under Andres Segovia
and attended USC on a music
scholarship. He now heads the
Guitar Department there.
Tickets for the 8 p.m. show
are $4.25 for the general public
and $2 for students, and are
available at Tresidder and most
major ticket agencies.


CHRISTOPHER PARKENING
