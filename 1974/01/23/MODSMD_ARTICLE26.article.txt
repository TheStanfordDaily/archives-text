# Memorial Services
## 
### 
Memorial services for Stanford
Law Prof. Emeritus Joseph W.
Bingham will be held in Memorial
Church today at 4 p.m.
Bingham died Dec. 15 in a rest
home at the age of 95. A
graduate of University of Chicago
and its law school, Bingham
joined the Stanford faculty in
1907 and retired in 1944.
