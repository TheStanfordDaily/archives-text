# Bowling League
## 
### 
Tresidder Memorial Union is
organizing a Tuesday night
men's bowling league.
Open to faculty, staff, and
students, the league will have
its first meeting at 6 p.m.
Tuesday, Jan. 29.
Interested individuals
should plan to meet then at the
Tresidder Bowling Center,
where teams will be formed.
Individuals unable to attend
or desiring further information
should call ext. 4316.
