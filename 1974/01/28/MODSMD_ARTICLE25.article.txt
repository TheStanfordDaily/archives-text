# Fencers Score Three Firsts
## 
### 
Stanford's fencing teams
made a strong showing
Saturday in the UC-Santa
Cruz Invitational, taking first
place in three of four
weapons. Cal Poly-San Luis
Obispo also participated in
the three-way meet.
Sharyl Swope and
Margaret Long led the
women's foil with individual
8-0 records, and David Wong
turned in the team's best
performance in men's foil,
finishing 5-1.
The men's epee team, led
by Richard Saunders (4-2),
edged UCSC for first place.
The men's sabre team, short
one member, placed third.
