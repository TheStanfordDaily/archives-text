## 
### 
Women Cagers
Win Opener
Stanford's women's
basketball team opened its
1974 season last night with a
33-28 victory over California
at the Women's Gym.
The junior varsity was also
victorious, edging the Bears
45-39. Kathy Levinson and
Terri Borchers led the varsity
scoring with 11 and 10
points, respectively, while
Karen Stromsmoe poured in
14 for the JV's. Details
tomorrow.
