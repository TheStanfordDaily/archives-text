THE STANFORD DAILY is an independent student newspaper owned and published
by the Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation Monday through Friday during the
academic year, except during dead and finals week.
Telephone: Editorial, 321-2300, ext. 4632; Business, 327-4150 or 321-2300, ext.
2554.
Editorials represent the opinion of a majority vote of the Daily editorial board,
composed of 13 editors and four at-large members elected by the staff.
Letters, columns and cartoons on the opinions pages represent only the views of
their authors. Nothing on the opinions pages represents a position of the Daily staff,
the ASSU or the University.
Subscription rates: In the U.S., Canada and Mexico, $15 per year, $8 per quarter.
Send check or monoy order in advance only to the Daily, Storke Student
Publications Building, Stanford, California, 94305.
(Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto under the act of
March 3, 1897.)
