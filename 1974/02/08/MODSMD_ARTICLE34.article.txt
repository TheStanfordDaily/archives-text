# DELLUMS TO SPEAK
## 
### 
Congressman Ronald V.
Dellums, D-Berkeley, will be the
featured speaker at a benefit for
the Scholarship Fund for Stanford
Students this Sunday at 2 p.m. in
Memorial Auditorium.
The Gwen Lewis
Afro-American Dance Theater will
also perform.
The benefit, billed as a
celebration of Afro-American
History Week, is jointly sponsored
by the Community Activities
Committee, the Peninsula Chapter
of Links, Inc., the Stanford Black
Students Union and the Palo
Alto-Stanford NAACP.
