# Chao To Speak
## 
### 
Prof. Chi-Chang Chao of the
Dept. of Aeronautics and
Astronautics will report on his
recent trip to the People's
Republic of China this
afternoon at 4:15.
As a member of a group of
seven professors and their
families, Chao visited several
universities and research
institutes. Among them were
the Institute of Applied
Mechanics, the Institute of
Aeronautical Engineering, and
Tsing-Hua University. Several
rural communes were also
visited.
Chao's talk will take place
in Skilling Room 193.
