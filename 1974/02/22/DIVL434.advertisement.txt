AGENDA DIGEST
The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday, February
25,1974 at 7:30 p.m. in +he Council Chamber of the Civic Center, 250
Hamilton Avenue. The complete agenda for the meeting is
available at the Civic Center, all City libraries, the Community
Center, Police and Fire Stations. In addition, packets containing
the Agenda and supporting informative material may be consulted
at the Clerk's Office, 7th floor, and the libraries. Below is a brief
digest of the items which have been submitted. Inquiries con
cerning details of any items should be directed to the City Clerk
(329 2226). Palo Alto City Council Meetings are broadcast live via
KZSU, 90.1 on the FM dial.
1. Finance and Public Works Committee recommends re allotment
of public space in Old Police/ Fire Bldg.
2. Finance and Public Works Committee recommends re proposed
lease between City and La Casa de Nieto y Amigos, Inc.
3. Finance and Public Works Committee recommends re Down
town Parking
4. Planning Commission recommends re directional signs on El
Camino Real & University Ave to Holiday Inn
5. Ordinance amending Section of Municipal Code re Overnight
Parking ( first reading 2/ 11/74)
6. Sewer Collection System Maps Proposed Contracts
7. Testing of Fire Fighter Applicants: Approval of Joint Powers
Agreement
8. Traffic Intersection Project: Approval of Consultant Contract
9. Unfinished business from 4/ 23/ 73 re appointment of a com
mittee to recommend re goals and priorities for use of City
resources and facilities; to explore possibility of increasing
cooperation between City and school district art programs; and
to make recommendations re means of encouraging citizen in
volvement in arts program.
