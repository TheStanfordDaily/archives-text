The Committee on Black
Performing Arts will hold
auditions for a Spring Musical
on Friday, March 9 at 10 a.m.
in Harmony House on Stanford
Campus. The musical will
feature the various styles,
rhythms, and moods of black
and white music of the
1950-1963 era, and will be
produced and directed by John
H. Cochran, Chairman of the
Committee.
Those auditioning are
requested to bring one or two
selections of contrasting music
for eight minute presentations.
An accompanist will be
provided. Sign-up sheets will
be posted at Harmony House.
