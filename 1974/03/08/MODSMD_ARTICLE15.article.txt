# Big Daddy Offers Tasty Week
## 
### 
SATURDAY
"Sausage & Peach Special,"
Choice of Salad, Fruit Pie,
beverage.
"Sausage & Peach Special"
Serves 6 to 8
Drain 1 (No. 2V2) can peach
halves. Sprinkle each peach with
brown sugar. Broil 3 inches from
heat 5 minutes. Put chili sauce in
each peach half; top with Vienna
sausages. Broil 8 to 10 minutes
more.
SUNDAY
"Broiled Steak,'- Duchess
Potatoes, Peas & Carrots, French
Fried Onions, Grilled Tomatoes,
Jello Mold, beverage.
"Broiled Steak"
Serves 4
Choose steak of choice;
Porterhouse, T-Bone, or Sirloin.
Slash fatty edges. Place on broiler
rack; broil top side, season with
salt and pepper. Turn, broil other
side till brown and meat is done
the way you like it. Allow 15 to
20 minutes for rare; 20 to 30
minutes for medium.
MONDAY
"Gourmet Goulash," Tossed
Salad, Fruit Pic, beverage.
"Gourmet Goulash"
Serves 5 to 6
1 lb. ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlie, crushed
1 tsp. salt
3 cups noodles
2'/2 cups tomato juice
V/ 2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
IV2 tsp. celery salt
1 tsp. salt

Dash pepper
1 can beef broth
V2 cup water
1/3 cup chopped green pepper
1 cup sour cream
1 (3 oz.) can mushrooms, drained
Place beef, onion, garlic, and 1
teaspoon salt in skillet over
medium heat. Cook till beef is
brown. Add noodles. Combine
next 7 ingredients; pour over
noodles. Cover; simmer over low
heat for 20 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Add green pepper.
Cover; cook 10 minutes or till
noodles are tender. Stir in
mushrooms, sour cream; heat.
TUESDAY
"Bean Banquet," Green Salad,
Hard Rolls, Ice Cream, beverage.
"Bean Banquet"
Serves 6 to 8
2 (1 lb.) cans pork n' beans
Va cup catsup
2 Tbl. mustard
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 to V/ 2 tsp. liquid smoke
2 (12 oz.) cans luncheon meat
In a baking dish, combine
beans, catsup, and seasonings. Cut
meat in half lengthwise. Cutting
not quite through, slice each half
in 7 slices. Arrange meat on
beans. Bake in 375 degree oven
for 25 to 30 minutes. Place in
broiler a few minutes to brown
meat; brush meat with melted
butter.
WEDNESDAY
"Mexican Chiletti," Tossed Salad,
Fresh Fruit, beverage.
"Mexican Chiletti"
Serves 4
Cook I/4 cup chopped onion in

1 Tbl. butter. Stir in 1 (1 lb.) can
spaghetti with sauce and 1 can
chili without beans. Heat through.
Top with Vi cup shredded sharp
cheese. Spoon over corn chips.
THURSDAY
"Ham-Potatd Soup," Relish Tray,
Cupcakes, beverage.
"Ham-Potato Soup"
Serves 4 to 5
Combine 1 can frozen Cream
of Potato soup, 1 can Cream of
Mushroom soup and 2 soup cans
milk. Stir in 1 can deviled ham.
Heat to boiling.
FRIDAY
"Tuna Curry," Hot Rice, Crisp
Salad, Fruit Compote, beverage.
"Tuna Curry"
Serves 4
Cook 1/3 cup chopped onion,
l A cup chopped green pepper, and
1 clove garlic, minced, in 2
tablespoons butter till tender but
not brown. Stir in 1 cup sour
cream, 1 teaspoon curry powder,
X A teaspoon salt, and dash pepper.
Break 1 can tuna in bite-size
pieces; add. Heat slowly, stirring
often. Serve over rice.
