# Friedman Appointed To Business School
## 
### 
Carol A. Friedman, former
assistant director of the
University's Career Planning and
Placement Center since 1970,
today becomes assistant dean for
placement and career planning at
Stanford's Graduate School of
Business.

The appointment, announced
by Samuel A. Pond, associate
dean for administrative affairs,
follows a three-month search.
Friedman replaces Gerard M.
Peterson, who left the School last
November to return to private
business.

At the University placement
center she has been responsible
for career counseling and for
identifying job opportunities for
undergraduate and graduate
students and alumni. She has also
coordinated a career planning
program for minority students.
