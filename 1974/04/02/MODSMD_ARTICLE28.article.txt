# Student Beaten, Robbed
## 
### 
Stanford and Santa Clara
County law enforcement officers
are investigating the beating and
robbery of a Stanford student near
the Tresidder Union patio around
11:45 Sunday night.
The victim, sophomore David
Stern, was apparently jumped by
four blacks who beat him and then
robbed him of his wallet, Capt.
William Wullschleger said.
Stern suffered bruises and
numerous cuts as a result of glasses
he was wearing. He was treated at
Stanford Hospital and spent the
night at Cowell Health Center.
The police said they have no
suspects.
