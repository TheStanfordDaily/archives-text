The National Association for the
Advancement of Colored People
(NAACP) has filed a suit over the
proposed Frenchman's Terrace
Housing Project, it was learned last
night.
The suit, reportedly alleging that
the project does not meet
conditions for low-income housing
specified by Congress, was filed
against Stanford, the Stanford
Midpeninsula Urban Coalition,
Secretary of the Department of
Housing and Urban Development
(HUD) James Lynn and the local
office of HUD.
More information will follow in
tomorrow's Daily.
