# VIETNAM VISITOR SPEAKS
## 
### 
Berkeley City Councilwoman Ying Lee Kelley
will speak on "Vietnam: Police, Prisons and the
Hope for Peace," Monday at 8 p.m. in Bishop
Auditorium.
Kelley is one of five Americans who spent two
weeks in South Vietnam on a fact-finding tour to
investigate reports of lacking implementation of
important provisions of the Paris Peace Agreements.
"South Vietnam: A Question of Torture," a
British Broadcasting Company Documentary on
Nguyen Van Thieu's police and prison system and
treatment of prisoners also will be shown.
