The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday, April 22,
1974 at 7:30 p.m. in the Council Chamber of the Civic Center, 250
Hamilton Avenue. The complete agenda for the meeting is
available at the Civic Center, all City libraries, the Community
Center, Police and Fire Stations. In addition, packets containing
the Agenda and supporting informative material may be consulted
at the Clerk's Office, 7th floor, and the libraries. Below is a brief
digest of the items which have been submitted. Inquiries con
cerning details of any items should be directed to the City Clerk
(329-2226). Palo Alto City Council Meetings are broadcast live via
KZSU, 90.1 on the FM dial.
Adjourned Meeting of April 15, 1974
6. Planning Commission recommends re amending and adding
various sections to the Palo Alto Municipal Code re dwelling units
in commercial and industrial districts.
7. Community Cultural Center Landscape Improvements: Bid
Award.
8 Landfill Environmental Impact Statement — Consultant
Selection.
9. Palo Alto Flood Basin Project.
10. St. Francis Drive Parking Restriction Modification.
11. Report of City Attorney re Easements/Harrington Property,
1250-1260 Monte Bello Road.
12. (Considered and approved at April 15, 1974 Council Meeting)
13. Request of Councilman Sher re College Terrace trail ic plan.
Regular Meeting of April 22, 1974
1. Ordinance amending Palo Alto Municipal Code relating to
temporary disability payments.
2. Lawn Bowls Clubhouse Lease.
3. Greer Park Expansion and Related Considerations.
4. Old Police Fire Building — Lease of Community Space.
5. Unity House: Report and Contract Amendment.
6. Report of City Attorney re Agreement to Relocate a Sanitary
Sewer Easement of the East Palo Alto Sanitary District.
7. Request of Councilman Clay re cost estimates to implement
Rent Control.
