# Florida Senator Speaks Tonight
## 
### 
Sen. Lawton M. Chiles, D-Fla., will
speak tonight at 7:30 in the Meyer
Library Forum Room on the subject
of campaign finances.
Chiles was elected to the Senate in
1970 after defeating former Rep.
William Kramer, who was asked to run
by President Nixon.
Chiles upset former Gov. Farris
Bryant in the Democratic primary by
walking the entire length of the state
of Florida. He was the first candidate
to use such a walking campaign
strategy.
It was during this race that former
Federal Judge G. Harrold Carswell
unsuccessfully sought the Republican
nomination after he was rejected by
the U.S. Senate for the U.S. Supreme
Court.
