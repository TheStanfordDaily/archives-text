TUESDAY
Bean & Frank Bake," Jello
Salad, French Bread, Fruit Cup,
beverage.
"Bean & Frank Bake"
Serves 4 to 6
1 can red kidney beans, drained
1 can lima beans, drained
V2 cup spaghetti sauce w/mushrooms
1 small onion, chopped
1 cup shredded Cheddar Cheese
4 to 6 Franks
Mix beans, spaghetti sauce,
onion and cheese in ungreased
casserole. Arrange franks on top.
Bake uncovered 30 minutes.
WEDNESDAY
"Skillet Hash," Lettuce Wedges
w/dressing, Ice Cream & Cookies,
beverage.
"Skillet Hash"
Serves 4
2 cups chopped cooked beef
2 cups chopped cooked potatoes
2/3 cup chopped onion
2 Tbl. snipped parsley
V2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
14 cup shortening
2/3 cup water
Combine beef, potatoes, onion,

parsley, salt and pepper. Melt
shortening in large skillet. Spread
meat mixture in skillet. Brown
hash 10 to 15 minutes, turning
often with spatula. Stir in water.
Cover, reduce heat and cook 10
minutes or until crisp.
THURSDAY
"Skillet Sausage & Sauerkraut,"
Tossed Salad, Fruit Compote,
beverage.
"Skillet Sausage & Sauerkraut"
Serves 4 to 6
1 pkg. prepared scalloped potatoes
1 (16 oz.) can sauerkraut,
drained (keep liquid)
V2 tsp. caraway seed
1 pkg. (12 oz.) cooked smoked
sausage links
Empty potato slices and packet
of seasoned sauce mix into skillet.
Stir in amount of water called for
on package. Add enough water to
sauerkraut liquid to measure 2/3
cup; stir liquid, kraut and caraway
seed into potatoes. Heat to
boiling, stirring often. Reduce
heat; cover and simmer 30
minutes. Arrange sausages on top.
Cover; cook 10 minutes longer.
FRIDAY
"Creole Style Fish Fillets,"
Seasoned Green Beans, Hard
Rolls, Sherbet Ice Cream,
beverage.
"Creole Style Fish Fillets"
Serves 4 to 6
2 lbs. Fish Fillets, Flounder
IV2 cups chopped tomatoes
Vicup chopped green pepper
1/3 cup lemon juice
1 Tbl. oil
2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. minced onion
1 tsp. basil leaves
V4 tsp. pepper
4 drops hot pepper sauce
Green Pepper Rings
Place fillets in single layer in
baking dish. Stir together
remaining ingredients except
pepper rings; spoon over fillets.
Bake in 500 degree oven for 5 to
8 minutes or till fish flakes with
fork. Remove to warm platter.
Garnish with green pepper rings.

"Chili w/Corn"

SUNDAY
"Baked Chicken Breasts,"
Buttered Asparagus, Hearts of
Lettuce Topped w/Tomatoes,
Cucumbers & Dressing, Apple Pie
& Ice Cream, beverage.
"Baked Cicken Breasts"
Serves 4
2 chicken breasts, halved
4 tsp. soft butter
1 to 2 Tbl. instant minced onion
V2 tsp. garlic salt
Va tsp. paprika
2 Tbl. snipped parsley
Remove skin from chicken;
place chicken in ungreased baking
pan. Spread 1 tsp. butter on each
piece; sprinkle with onion, garlic
salt and paprika. Bake uncovered
in 425 degree oven for 30 to 35
minutes or till tender. Sprinkle
with parsley.
MONDAY
"Toasty Cheese Bake," Relish
Tray, Fresh Fruit, Brownies,
beverage.
"Toasty Cheese Bake"
Serves 4 to 6
8 slices White bread
butter
Vfc lb. ground beef
X A cup chopped onion
2 Tbl. chopped celery
1 Tbl. prepared mustard
V2 tsp. salt
1 cup shredded sharp
American cheese
1 egg, slightly beaten
3/4 cup milk
V2 tsp. salt
Dash pepper
1/8 tsp. dry mustard
Toast bread; butter both sides.
Cook meat, onion, celery,
prepared mustard and V2 tsp. salt
till meat is brown and onion is
tender. Alternate layers of toast,
meat mixture and cheese in
greased baking pan. Mix remaining
ingredients; pour over layers in
pan. Bake in 350 degree oven,
uncovered 30 to 35 minutes.

"Creole Style Fish Fillets"
Serves 4 to 6
2 lbs. Fish Fillets, Flounder
IV2 cups chopped tomatoes
Vicup chopped green pepper
1/3 cup lemon juice
1 Tbl. oil
2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. minced onion
1 tsp. basil leaves
V4 tsp. pepper
4 drops hot pepper sauce
Green Pepper Rings
Place fillets in single layer in
baking dish. Stir together
remaining ingredients except
pepper rings; spoon over fillets.
Bake in 500 degree oven for 5 to
8 minutes or till fish flakes with

Garnish with green pepper rings.

"Toasty Cheese Bake," Relish
Tray, Fresh Fruit, Brownies,
beverage.
"Toasty Cheese Bake"
Serves 4 to 6
8 slices White bread
butter
Vfc lb. ground beef
X A cup chopped onion
2 Tbl. chopped celery
1 Tbl. prepared mustard
V2 tsp. salt
1 cup shredded sharp
American cheese
1 egg, slightly beaten
3/4 cup milk
V2 tsp. salt
Dash pepper
1/8 tsp. dry mustard
Toast bread; butter both sides.
Cook meat, onion, celery,
prepared mustard and V2 tsp. salt
till meat is brown and onion is
tender. Alternate layers of toast,
meat mixture and cheese in
greased baking pan. Mix remaining
ingredients; pour over layers in
pan. Bake in 350 degree oven,
uncovered 30 to 35 minutes.

"Skillet Hash," Lettuce Wedges
w/dressing, Ice Cream & Cookies,
beverage.
"Skillet Hash"
Serves 4
2 cups chopped cooked beef
2 cups chopped cooked potatoes
2/3 cup chopped onion
2 Tbl. snipped parsley
V2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
14 cup shortening
2/3 cup water
Combine beef, potatoes, onion,

parsley, salt and pepper. Melt
shortening in large skillet. Spread
meat mixture in skillet. Brown
hash 10 to 15 minutes, turning
often with spatula. Stir in water.
Cover, reduce heat and cook 10
minutes or until crisp.
THURSDAY
"Skillet Sausage & Sauerkraut,"
Tossed Salad, Fruit Compote,
beverage.
"Skillet Sausage & Sauerkraut"
Serves 4 to 6
1 pkg. prepared scalloped potatoes
1 (16 oz.) can sauerkraut,
drained (keep liquid)
V2 tsp. caraway seed
1 pkg. (12 oz.) cooked smoked
sausage links
Empty potato slices and packet
of seasoned sauce mix into skillet.
Stir in amount of water called for
on package. Add enough water to
sauerkraut liquid to measure 2/3
cup; stir liquid, kraut and caraway
seed into potatoes. Heat to
boiling, stirring often. Reduce
heat; cover and simmer 30
minutes. Arrange sausages on top.
Cover; cook 10 minutes longer.
