# ADDITIONAL PERFORMANCE
## 
### 
Due to an enormous
pre-sale of tickets for the ten
regular performances of
Rosencrantz and
Guildenstern Are Dead, the
Drama Department has
scheduled an additional
performance for Monday,
April 29, at 8 p.m. in the
Little Theater.
Tickets will go on sale at
the door starting at 7 p.m. on
Monday. Admission will be
$1 for students, $2.50
general. Tickets for the
special performance will only
be sold at the door.
