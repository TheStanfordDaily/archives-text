# Bluitt Named Cornell Coach
## 
### 
Ben Bluitt, father of
Stanford guard Ben Bluitt Jr.,
has been named head
basketball coach at Cornell
University.
Bluitt is a former star
player at Loyola University in
Chicago and coach at Detroit
University. He becomes the
first black head coach in the
history of the 110-year old
Ivy League school, located in
Ithaca, N.Y.
