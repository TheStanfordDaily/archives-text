# MEChA Plans Celebration
## 
### 
The Stanford chicano community will observe traditional Cinco d<
Mayo festivities starting tomorrow.
Wednesday, May 1
7 and 9:30 p.m. Film: Mexico Insurgente
Cubberley Auditorium
Admission: 50 cents
Thursday, May 2
7 p.m. Speaker: Dolores Huerta, executive vice president
of United Farmworkers of America
Memorial Church
Friday, May 3
12-1 p.m. Cultural-Historical Presentation with Music
and Poetry
White Plaza
2:30-4:30 p.m. Poetry, Music, Dancing, Pinata
Casa Zapata Courtyard
5:30-7:00 p.m. Mexican Dinner at Casa Zapata
9 p.m.-l a.m. Cinco de mayo Dance
Tresidder Deck
Music by Los Cisnes
Admission: $1

7-10 p.m. Speakers: Bert Corona, director of C.A.S.A.
and' Los Tres
Singers: La Primavera
Memorial Church
Sunday, May 5
8-10 p.m. Ballet Folklorico de Stanford
Chicano Law Students Conjunto, Teatro, Mariachis
