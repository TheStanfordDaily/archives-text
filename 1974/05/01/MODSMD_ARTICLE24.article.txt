# Women's Pool, Gym Hours
## 
### 
The Lagunita Pool is open to
students, faculty and staff on a
coed basis six days a week. The
pool is closed Sunday for
hyperchlorination. Recreational
swim hours are as follows:
12—1 p.m., Monday-Friday:
Staff and faculty only.

2-3 — 4—5:45 p.m.
Monday-Friday: Students, faculty
and staff.
10 a.m. — 4:45 p.m. Saturday:
Students, faculty and staff.
The women's gymnasium is
available for open recreation 8-9
a.m. Monday—Friday and 5—6:45
p.m. Friday only.
