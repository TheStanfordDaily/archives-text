# Elie Wiesel Here
## 
### 
Author and educator Elie
Wiesel will deliver the annual
Harry F. Camp Memorial Lectures
in Dinkelspiel Auditorium May 5
through 7.
The theme of the lecture series
is "Biblical Heroes : Our
Contemporaries." May 5 at 8 p.m.
Wiesel will speak on "Adam."
"Cain and Abel" is the topic of
his May 6 talk, also at 8 p.m. On
May 7, "The Binding of Isaac"
will conclude the series at 4:15
p.m.
Wiesel, author of prize-winning
novels A Beggar in Jerusalem and
Souls on Fire, is currently
professor of Judaic studies at the
City University of New York.
Born in Hungary, Wiesel is a
survivor of the Auschwitz and
Buchenwald Nazi prison camps
and Jewish extermination centers
of World War 11.
