# Coffin Talk At 8
## 
### 
Yale Chaplain William Sloane
Coffin will deliver the annual Kent
State-Jackson State address tomorrow
at 8 p.m. in Memorial Church. Coffin
will speak on violence.
On Sunday, Coffin will deliver a
sermon in Memorial Church at 11 a.m.
The chaplain has written for The
Nation, Saturday Review, Christian
Century and The Inter collegian. His
background includes serving in the
U.S. Army in World War II and then
becoming a liaison officer with the
Soviet army.
In 1960, he led students to Guinea
to work in "Operation Crossroads."
The following year, he was named the
first director of the Peace Corps
Training Center in Puerto Rico.
