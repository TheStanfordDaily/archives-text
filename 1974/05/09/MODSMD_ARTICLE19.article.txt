# YALOM WINS STRECKER AWARD
## 
### 
Medical School News Service
Dr. Irvin Yalom, professor of psychiatry at the Stanford School of
Medicine, has received the Edward A. Strecker Award for outstanding
contributions in the care and treatment of patients.
The award, consisting of a certificate and an honorarium of
$1000, was announced Tuesday at a meeting of the American
Psychiatric Association in Philadelphia.
Yalom is currently on sabbatical leave at Oxford University in
England. A graduate of Boston University Medical School, he is
known for his studies in group therapy and the relationship of
hormones to behavior.
