# HOUSING CARDS DUE
## 
### 
All students who wish to remain
in the housing draw must turn in
their Round I result cards by 5
p.m. today at their current
residence offices. Off-campus
students and others who picked
up their draw cards at the Old
Union should return them to the
Old Union. Even students who
have decided to drop out of this
year's draw should turn in their
cards if they want to qualify for
the draw next spring.
