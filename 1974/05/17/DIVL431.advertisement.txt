Christmas Charter 1973-74 Refund Schedule
All Stanford members who flew on the ASSU Christmas Charters are eligible
for a $19.25 refund (as a result of a refund to the ASSU TRAVEL SERVICE
from the Airlines)
Refunds will be given according to the following schedule:
DAY FLIGHT You must have
Monday New York Your Stanford
Tuesday New York , D to receive
Wednesday Chicago - - the refund
Thursday Boston/Wash. D.C.
Friday Any Flight
