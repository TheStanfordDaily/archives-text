AGENDA DIGEST
The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday, May 20, 1974 at 7:30
p.m. in the Council Chambers of the Civic Center, 250 Hamilton Avenue. The
complete agenda for the meetings is available at the Civic Center, all City
libraries, Community Centers, Police and Fire Stations. Ip addition, packets
containing the agenda and supporting informative material may be con
suited at the libraries. Below is a brief digest of the items which have been
submitted. Inquiries concerning details of any items should be directed to the
City Clerk (329 2226). Palo Alto City Council Meetings are broadcast live via
KZSU, 90.1 on the FM dial.
1. Policy and Procedures Committee recommends re Qualifications for
Purchasers and Renters of Non Federally Subsidized Housing.
2. Planning Commission adopts Resolution recommending denial of ap
plication of James C. Safer, 4102 El Camino Real.
3. Ordinance extending the suspension of the conversion of apartment
buildings to condominiums.
4. Ordinance amending Title 18 of the Palo Alto Municipal Code.
5. Resolution in support of the passage of County of Santa Clara Measure A.
6. Golf Course Maintenance Building Project: Report of Bids.
7. Request for General Plan Element Time Extensions.
8. Squire House Renovation and Utilization.
9. Old Police Fire Building; Rental of Community Space
10. Park Projects Requiring Ordinance for Substantial Building Con
struction, Etc.
11. Report of Councilman Sher re meetings of the Northern California Power
Agency (continued from May 13, 1974).
12. Resolution in support of the passage of Proposition 0 (continued from May
13, 1974).
13. Request of Councilman Rosenbaum re posting of prices at gasoline
stations.
14. Request of Councilman Beahrs.or adoption of Resolution urging defeat of
Measure B, amending the Charter of the City of Palo Alto to provide for
rent control.
