# STRIKE TEACH-IN
## 
### 
A "teach-in" on issues
raised by the current United
Stanford Employees strike
will be held tonight at 8 in
Bishop Auditorium.
Speakers will include
Assoc. History Prof. Barton J.
Bernstein, Economics Prof.
John Gurley, and Medicine
Prof. Halsted Hoi man.
