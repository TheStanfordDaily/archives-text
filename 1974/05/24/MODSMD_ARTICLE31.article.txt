# WOMEN'S GOLF
## 
### 
Stanford's Pat Cornett won
the Cal-State Hayward
Invitational Golf Tournament
Wednesday at Hayward
Municipal Course by shooting a
two-over-par 74.
Margaret Leonard of Santa
Clara was second with an 82.
Santa Clara won the team
competition with a two-person
total of 170, just one stroke in
front of Stanford's 171.
