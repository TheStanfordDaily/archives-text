# HAY DEN AT MEMCHU
## 
### 
Tom Hayden, a founder of
Students for a Democratic Society
and member of the Chicago
Seven, will speak tonight at 8 in
Memorial Church. His topic is
"Vietnam in 1974: An Ongoing
War and a Plan for Peace."
Hayden, who is married to
actress Jane Fonda, has worked
since 1972 with the Indochina
Peace Campaign, a nation-wide
organization seeking local and
congressional support for
legislation to cut off U.S. aid to
South Vietnam.
His talk culminates a "Week of
Concern for Indochina"
sponsored by the Committee to
Invite Nguyen Huu Thai to
Stanford.
