# QUAD DISTRIBUTION
## 
###

The Stanford QUAD will be
distributed at the QUAD office
in the Storke Student
Publications Building on
Monday thru Wednesday, June
3-5 from 10:00 to 4:00. Bring
receipts and I.D. to pick up
your yearbooks. There are
about 150 books still unsold,
first come first serve on these
books.
