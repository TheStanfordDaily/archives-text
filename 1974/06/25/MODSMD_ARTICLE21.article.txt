# Preservation Band Leads Summer Fare
## 
### 
The Preservation Hall Jazz Band kicks off summer entertainment
on campus next week, July 2 through July 6. Tuesday and Wednesday
concerts will be held at 8 p.m., Friday and Saturday concerts will be
held at 8 and 10 p.m. Thursday will feature a special family picnic
concert in Frost Amphitheater at 5:30 p.m. with gates open at 4:30
p.m.
Another famous jazzman, Benny Goodman, will perform with
Arthur Fiedler and the San Francisco Symphony Orchestra in Frost
Amphitheater on August 25 in a special benefit for the Children's
Health Council of the Mid-Peninsula.
In addition, the Music Department will continue its series of
recitals and performances, beginning with Margaret Fabrizio playing
Stanford's new harpsichord and dulcimelos in Annenberg Auditorium.
Most music department performances are free.
