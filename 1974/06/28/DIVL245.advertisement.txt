AGENDA DIGEST
The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday,
JULY 1, 1974 at 7:30 p.m. in the Council Chambers of the
Civic Center, 250 Hamilton Avenue. The complete agenda for
the meeting is available at the Civic Center, all City
libraries, Community Centers, Police and Fire Stations. In
addition, packets containing the agenda and supporting
informative material may be consulted at the libraries.
Below is a brief digest of the items which have been sub
mitted. Inquiries concerning details of any items should be
directed to the City Clerk (329 2226). Palo Alto City Council
Meetings are broadcast live via KZSU, 90.1 on the FM dial.
1. Election of Mayor and Vice Mayor
2. Finance and Public Works Committee recommends re
Youth Center program
3. Planning Commission (6 in favor, 1 absent) indicates
general approval of Alley 13 Beautification Scheme D
4. Architectural Review Board approval (5-0) of schematic
design for Lane No. 13
5. Alley 13 Resolution of Intention and Resolution of
Preliminary Determination
6. PACDAB Contract
7. Proposed Amendment to Agreement No. 2876 — Sewage
Transportation, Treatment and Disposal. Los Altos Hills
8. Contract for Civic Center Elevator Maintenance
9. Request of Councilman Berwald re burglary & related
crimes within City (continued from Adjourned meeting of
6 17 74 - 6 24 74
10. Request of Mayor Comstock re Council support of AB 3924
(Tippler's Tax — 5 percent of alcoholic beverages con
sumed on premises where sold)
11. Request of Mayor Comstock re appointment of Council
representative to meet with State and Federal Legislators
serving area
12. Request of Councilman Berwald for Executive Session re
personnel matters.
+ + +
There are no Council Committee meetings scheduled for the
week of July 1.
Thursday, .July 4, is a holiday— CITY HALL CLOSED.
