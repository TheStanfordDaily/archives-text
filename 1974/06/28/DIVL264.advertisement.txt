This Petition is for ALL members of the BARGAINING UNIT who wish to express their resentment towards
USE. The Petition Committee asks you to help sign up your fellow staffers. Write your reasons why you are
against USE as this establishes the ma joritys voice. If you would like to help us say so and give times and what
you would like to do.
Why should you sign the Petition Against USE? If you are not saiisfied in any way with the way they are
representing you at the bargaining table with Stanford or railroading you into paying dues, or have no voice in
the matter and want one, sign the Petition and watch what a firm majority of members of the BARGAINING
UNIT CAN do together.
