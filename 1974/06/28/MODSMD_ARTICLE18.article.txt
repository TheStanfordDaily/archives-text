CONTINUING EVENTS
Hand weavings by Gisela Evitt
continue through July upstairs at
Tresidder Union. The exhibit
includes hangings, afghans, and
stoles made from hand-spun
vegetable dyed yarn.
Palo Alto Cultural Center, 1313
Newell Rd., presents a Print
Invitational in the main gallery
through August 10. The small
gallery presents "The Dynamics of
Printmaking; the glass gallery
contains paintings by Katherine
Kahrs; and the lobby contains
Rembrandt etchings through July
10.
RA continues at the Odyssey
Room, 799 E. El Camino,
Sunnyvale, through Saturday.

DAVID LA FLAMME performs
Monday, and RA continues on
Tuesday.
ANDY WILLIAMS and THE
LENNON SISTERS continue at
the Circle Star Theater in San
Carlos through Sunday. They are
replaced Tuesday by HARRY
BELAFONTE.
FRIDAY, JUNE 28
The Tresidder film series presents
A STREETCAR NAMED
DESIRE, starring Marlon Brando,
Vivien Leigh, Kim Hunter, and
Karl Maiden. Shows at 9 and
11:15 p.m. in the Tresidder
lounges, admission $1.
Margaret Rabrizio will play
harpsichord, virginal, and
dulcimelos — three instruments
build for the Music Department —
at 8 p.m. in Annenberg
Auditorium in the art building.
Tickets are available at Tresidder
Box Office.
Chateau Liberte at 22700 Old
Santa Cruz Hwy. near the
Highway 17 summit presents Blue
Cheer. This show continues
Saturday.


—Photo courtesy ASSU Special Events
SITTIN' IN—Would you believe at Frost Amphitheater? ASSU Special Events will present Loggins and
Messina August 3 in another picnic concert. Gates open at noon, and the music starts at 2 p.m. Kenny
Loggins, Jim Messina, and their band turn out foot stompin' sounds ranging from their recent AM hit "My
Music" to the more mellow "Danny's Song" and "Love Song," two Loggins compositions popularized by
Anne Murray.
SATURDAY, JUNE 29
ASSU Saturday Flicks presents
KLUTE starring Donald
Sutherland and Jane Fonda in her
Oscar-winning performance.
Shows at 7, 9, and 11 p.m.
Admission is 50 cents, no
Stanford ID required. This show is
in Cubberley Auditorium.

choreographer, will give a
workshop from 1 to 5 p.m. at
Trinity Church, 330 Ravenswood
Ave., Menlo Park. The workshop
will be followed by a
BALKAN-ISRAELI
FOLKDANCE PARTY at 7:30
p.m. at the Menlo Park Recreation
Center, Alma at Mielke Drive.
SUNDAY, JUNE 30
ASSU Sunday Flicks presents
Woody Allen's BANANAS at 8
and 9:45 p.m. in Memorial
Auditorium. Admission is 50
cents, no ID required.

Wendy Hilton will perform
MUSIC AND DANCE FROM

p.m. in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Tickets are $2 general and $1
student.
