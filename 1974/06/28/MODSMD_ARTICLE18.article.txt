CONTINUING EVENTS
Hand weavings by Gisela Evitt
continue through July upstairs at
Tresidder Union. The exhibit
includes hangings, afghans, and
stoles made from hand-spun
vegetable dyed yarn.
Palo Alto Cultural Center, 1313
Newell Rd., presents a Print
Invitational in the main gallery
through August 10. The small
gallery presents "The Dynamics of
Printmaking; the glass gallery
contains paintings by Katherine
Kahrs; and the lobby contains
Rembrandt etchings through July
10.

Tuesday.

SITTIN' IN—Would you believe at Frost Amphitheater? ASSU Special Events will present Loggins and
Messina August 3 in another picnic concert. Gates open at noon, and the music starts at 2 p.m. Kenny
Loggins, Jim Messina, and their band turn out foot stompin' sounds ranging from their recent AM hit "My
Music" to the more mellow "Danny's Song" and "Love Song," two Loggins compositions popularized by
Anne Murray.
