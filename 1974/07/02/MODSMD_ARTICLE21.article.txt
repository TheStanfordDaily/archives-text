# OLD TIMES OPENS
## 
### 
The Stanford Drama
Workshop production of
Harold Pinter's psychological
play Old Times will open
tomorrow night in The
Nitery. Curtain time is 8 p.m.
The production is directed
by doctoral student Janelle
Reinelt and stars Ellen Mease,
Norman Fisk and Jane
Valentine as Kate Deeley, and
Anna.
The free performances
continue through Saturday
and July 10 through 13.
Curtain each evening is at 8
p.m.
