# Adv. 2 Page 2
## 
### 
The Daily is in need of, and welcomes your contributions to its opinions
page, which is intended as a forum for persons of all viewpoints on the
campus. Here's all you do to see your name in print:
1. Think of an issue to sound off on.
2. Type your contribution on a 62-space line, double-spaced.
3. Sign your name and leave a telephone number.
4. Bring the completed masterpiece down to our office, Storke Student
Publications Building, Panama & Lomita Streets.
Articles should be no more than 70 lines, letters to the editor no more
than 35 lines.
