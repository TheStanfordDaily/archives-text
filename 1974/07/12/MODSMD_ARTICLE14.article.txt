CONTINUING EVENTS
The Stanford Drama Workshop
production of OLD TIMES
continues at The Niterv through
Saturday. Curtain is at 8 p.m.,
admission is free.
The Company presents AS YOU
LIKE IT through Sunday at
Bechtel International Center. This
show will continue Friday
through Sunday next week.
The hand weavings of Gisela Evitt
continue upstairs in Tresidder
Union through the month of July.
FRIDAY, JULY 12
James Stewart, Jean Arthur, and
Claude Rains star in Frank Capra's
classic film MR. SMITH GOES TO
WASHINGTON, showing at 9

p.m. only in the Tresidder
Lounges. Admission is $1.
The Stanford India Association
presents ANUBHAV at Cubberley
Auditorium at 8 p.m. Tickets are
$1.50 for members and $2 for
non-members.
SATURDAY, JULY 13
ASSU presents BULLITT starring
Steve McQueen and Jacqueline
Bisset. Showings are at 7, 9, and
11 p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium.
Admission is 50 cents.
SUNDAY. JULY 14
ASSU flicks presents Barbra
Streisand and Ryan O'Neal in
WHAT'S UP DOC? at 8 and 10
p.m. in Memorial Auditorium.
Admission is 50 cents.

TUESDAY, JULY 16
A scheduled concert by Sarah
Umrdock, pianist, has been
CANCELLED.
WEDNESDAY, JULY 17
The Tresidder Hitchcock Festival
presents SPELLBOUND starring
Gregory Peck, Ingrid Bergman,
and Rhonda Fleming, and
featuring a dream sequence
designed by Salvador Dali.
Screenings are at 9 and 11 p.m.,
and admission is $1.
GeraJd Chappell's scheduled
concert has been CANCELLED.
FUTURE EVENTS
Tickets remain on sale for
LOGGINS AND MESSINA,
August 3 at Frost Amphitheater.
Tickets are $5 in advance, $6 at
the gate. The gates open at noon,
and the music starts at $2.
Max Morath will perform ragtime
music next Friday at 8 p.m. in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium. Tickets
are $4.50 general and $3.50
student. "
