THE STANFORD DAILY is an Independent student newspaper
owned and published by the Stanford Daily Publishing
Corporation Tuesdays and Fridays during summer quarter and
Monday through Friday during the academic year, except dead
and finals week.
Letters, columns and cartoons on the opinions pages represent
only the views of their authors. Nothing on the opinions pages
represents a position of the Dally staff, the ASSU or the
University.
Subscription rates: In the U.S., Canada and Mexico, $6 for
summer quarter; $15 per year, $6 per quarter. Send check or
money order in advance only to the Oaily, Storke Student
Publications Building, Stanford, California, 94305.
(Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto
under the act of March 3, 1897.)
