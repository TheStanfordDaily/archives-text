AGENDA DIGEST
The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday, August 5,
1974 at 7:30 p.m. in the Council Chambers of the Civic Center, 250
Hamilton Avenue. The complete agenda for the meetings is available
at the Civic Center, all City libraries, Community Centers, Police and
Fire Stations. In addition, packets containing the agenda and sup
porting informative material may be consulted at the libraries. Below
is a brief digest of the items which have been submitted. Inquiries
concerning details of any items should be directed to the City Clerk
(339-2226). Palo Alto City Council Meetings are broadcast live via
KZSU, 90.1 on the FM dial.
1. Resolution of Welcome to Oaxaca Exchange Students
2. Presentation of gift from Palo, Leyte, Republic of the Philippines,
by Neighbors Abroad
3. Resolutions of Appreciation to Youth Advisory Council Members
4. Retirement of Dale Hooker
5. Retirement of Robert Woolley
6. Report of Mayor Sher re Stanford, Palo Alto Staff Liaison Com
mittee
7. Planning Commission transmits consensus recommendations re
Transportation Commission on Transportation and Land Use in
Santa Clara County
8. Resolution of appreciation to Palo Alto citizens against Proposition
B for financial contribution to Housing Authority of Santa Clara
County
9. Resolution re compensation plan for Council-appointed officers
10. 1975 Santa Clara County Census
11. Administration Code Changes
12. Downtown Beautification Street Lighting — Demonstration
13. Community Theatre: Agreement with Community Players
14. Consultant Selection Procedures
15. Executive Session Guidelines
16. Proposed Amendments to the 1973 Uniform Building Code,
Uniform Plumbing Code, Uniform Mechanical Code, Uniform
Housing Code, National Electrical Code, and Uniform Fire Code
(continued from 7 15 74)
17. Status Report — AB 2040 — Bay Area Planning Agency (continued
from 7 15, 74)
18. Planning Commission recommends re Fire Zone 1 (continued from
3 11 74, 7 15 74)
19. Proposed Closure of Scott Street (continued from 6 17 74,
7 15 74)
20. Request of Councilman Comstock re SB 2177 (continued from
7 22 74)
21. Request of Vice Mayor Henderson re Dumbarton Bridge
22. Request of Councilwoman Pearson re resolution for responsibility
for employment
23. Motion of Councilman Norton re Apartment Manager Licensing
