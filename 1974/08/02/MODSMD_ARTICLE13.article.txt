CONTINUING EVENTS
REMEMBER WHEN, an original
musical based on key songs from
the late 50s and early 60s,
continues through Sunday, as well
as August 8-11 in a tent pitched
next to Memorial Auditorium.
Tickets are $3.50 general, $2
student, with the first 100 people
in line getting $1 off the regular
price. Advance tickets available at
Tresidder Ticket Office, curtain
time 8:30 p.m.
FRIDAY, AUGUST 2
Organist Pamela Decker concludes
her free performance of Bach's
complete organ concertos and
sonatas at 8 p.m. in Memorial
Church.
Tresidder Films presents Woody

Allen's TAKE THE MONEY AND
RUN at 9 and 10:45 p.m. in the
Tresidder Lounges. Admission is
$1.
SATURDAY, AUGUST 3
ASSU Flicks presents CAT
BALLOU at 7, 9 and 11 p.m. in
Cubberley Auditorium. Admission
is 50 cents.
SUNDAY, AUGUST 4
The Company presents its last
performance of AS YOU LIKE IT
at 6:30 p.m. in Community
Center Park, 201 Rengstorff,
Mountain View. Admission is free,
everyone is invited to bring a
picnic and a pillow.
ASSU Sunday Flicks presents
THE PAPER CHASE at 8 and

10:15 p.m. in Memorial
Auditorium. Admission is 50
cents.
TUESDAY, AUGUST 6
Marie Bird, piano, and Paul
Garritson, clarinet, present
Chopin piano music and clarinet
pieces by Schumann and Leonard
Bernstein in a free concert at 8
p.m. in Dinkelspiel. The Sheridan
Schroeter section of the program
has been cancelled.
