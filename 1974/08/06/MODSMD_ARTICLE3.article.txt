# Ford, Hatfield To Honor Hoover
## 
### 
Vice President Gerald Ford and Sen. Mark Hatfield, R-Ore.,
will speak here Saturday to commemorate the centennial of
Herbert Hoover's birth.
Hatfield will give a talk in Bishop Auditorium at 4:30 p.m.
and Ford will speak in Dinkelspiel Auditorium at 8 p.m. as
part of the Hoover Institution sponsored-invitational program.
As one of the first freshmen here when the University was
newly founded, as a trustee for more than 50 years and as
America's 31st President, Hoover made many valuable
contributions to the University.
During his student days here he helped initiate the first
Big Game, against Stanford's football foe across the Bay in
Berkeley.
He also founded Hoover Institution on War, Revolution and
Peace, helped plan the Student Union, organized the Food
Research Institute and established the Graduate School of
Business.
The Daily will publish a special Hoover Memorial
supplement in Friday's paper.
