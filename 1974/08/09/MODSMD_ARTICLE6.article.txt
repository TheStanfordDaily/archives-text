THE STANFORD DAILY is an independent student newspaper owned
and published by the Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation Tuesdays
and Fridays during summer quarter and Monday through Friday during
the academic year, except dead and finals week.
Editorials represent the opinion of a majority vote of the Daily
editorial board, composed of five editors and four members appointed
by the summer editor.
Letters, columns and cartoons on the opinions pages represent only
the views of their authors. Nothing on the opinions pages represents a
position of the Daily staff, the ASSU or the University.
Telephone: Editorial, (415) 497-4632; Business, (415) 497-2554.
Subscription rates: In the U.S., Canada and Mexico, $6 for summer
quarter; $15 per year, $6 per quarter. Send check or money order in
advance only to the Daily, Storke Student Publications Building,
Stanford, California, 94305.
(Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto under
I the act of March 3, 1897.)
