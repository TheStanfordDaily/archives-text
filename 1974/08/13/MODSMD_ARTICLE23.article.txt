CONTINUING EVENTS
The New Community Theater
production of THE LIBERATION
OF SNOW WHITE continues
tonight at 7:30 p.m. at Bechtel
International Center; Thursday at
7:30 p.m. at Mitchell Park in Palo
Alto; Friday at 9 p.m. at Pan's
Natural Food Restaurant at El
C amino and Arastradero;
Saturday at 3 p.m. at Stanford
Shopping Center; and Sunday at
7:30 p.m. at Mountain View

Recreation Center. All shows are
free.
The Workshop continues its
summer season with THE
COUNTRY WIFE Friday and
Saturday at Stern Center, 1305
Middlefield Rd., Palo Alto. The
Workshops final production,
Shakespeare's THE TEMPEST,
opens August 23 and continues
August 24, 29, 30, and 31. All
curtains are at 8:30 p.m.

CROSSROADS OF THE
SAHARA, an exhibit of color and
monochrome prints of the
mystical tribes of the Sahara
taken by Stanford alumna Doris
Birkland Beezley will be shown
until August 31 in the Tresidder
second floor galleries.
Foothill Summereperatory
presents THE WOMEN tonight,
August 18, and August 21; AND
MISS REARDON DRINKS A
LITTLE August 14, 22; CACTUS

FLOWER August 15, 19, and 23;
and MUCH ADO ABOUT
NOTHING August 16,17, 20, and
24. All shows are at 8:15 p.m. in
the Foothill Theater.
TUESDAY, AUGUST 13
ALICE IN WONDERLAND and
THROUGH THE LOOKING
GLASS will be previewed tonight
and tomorrow night in Branner
Hall at 8:30 p.m. Admission to
these two previews is 50 cents.
Regular performances are August
15 through 18 and August 22
through 25. Admission to regular
performances is $1.
The Odyssey Room, 799 E. El
Camino, Sunnyvale, presents
NORMAN GREENBAUM
tonight; RA Wednesday through
Monday; GREENBAUM August
20; GIDEON AND POWER
August 26; and EARTHQUAKE
September 2.
WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 14
The Tresidder Hitchcock Festival
presents MARNIE starring Tippi
Hedren, Sean Connery, and Diane
Baker. Shows are at 9 and 11:15
p.m. and admission is $1 in the
Tresidder lounges.
FRIDAY. AUGUST 16
Tresidder Friday Film Series
presents SAN FRANCISCO
starring Clark Gable, Jeanette
Mac Donald, and Spencer Tracy.
The film will be shown at 9 p.m.
only in the Tresidder lounges.
Admission is $1.
