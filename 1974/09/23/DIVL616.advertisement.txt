### 
Frenchman's Tower
Dedicated
A bronze plaque
commemorating recognition
of Frenchman's Tower in the
Stanford foothills as an
official state and county
historical "point of interest"
was dedicated Sept. 12.
The plaque, which has
been affixed to a large native
boulder, was donated by the
Committee for Green
Foothills.
