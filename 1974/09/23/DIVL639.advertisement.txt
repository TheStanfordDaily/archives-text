SHORT COMPUTER COURSES
Offered Autumn Quarter
by
Academic Computing Services
Stanford Center for Information Processing
All interested students, faculty and staff are invited to attend these free, non-credit
computer courses taught by the staff of Academic Computing Services. Introductory as well
as advanced courses describe the services, programming languages and applications programs
available on Stanford's IBM 360/67 computer located in Pine Hall, Jordan Quad. In most of
these courses, free computer time is provided for students to do assignments. All classes
meet in Polya Hall (next to Pine Hall).
Registration is required for all courses. You may register for the Introduction to Academic
Computing Services course by calling Ext. 74877 or by going to the Document Sales Office in
Pine Hall during the hours 10 a.m. - Noon and 1:15 - 5 p.m. Registration for all other
courses must be made in person at the Document Sales Office. Early registration is
advisable as enrollment is limited.
For further information on course content, materials used or prerequisites, call Ext. 74877
or see the Academic Computing Services Bulletin available at the Document Sales Office.
