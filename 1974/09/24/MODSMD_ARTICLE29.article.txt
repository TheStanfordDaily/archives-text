Tresidder Carnival Tonight
A wide range of activities—from
belly dancing to bowling—is planned
for tonight's carnival at Tresidder
Memorial Union.
Outdoor booths between Tresidder
and Dinkelspiel Auditorium will
feature carnival games, food, and
entertainment such as Yashti the belly
dancer, jugglers and clowns.
Singin' In The Rain, the classic
musical starring Gene Kelly, Donald
O'Connor, and Debbie Reynolds will
be shown at 8 p.m. in the Tresidder
large lounge for $1.
The evening will close with a

variety show sponsored by the Student
Development Committee from 10 to
11 p.m. in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Throughout the evening, students
may bowl or shoot pool at Tresidder
for half price.
All activities begin at 8 p.m. The
outdoor booths will close at midnight.
CORRECTION
The Daily mistakenly reported
yesterday the date of the appearance
of the Spanish guitar group, the
Romeros. The Romeros will perform
one night only on Friday, Oct. IB, at 8
p.m.
