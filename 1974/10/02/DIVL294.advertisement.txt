## 
### 
Senate Candidates
Candidates for ASSU Senate
have until 4 p.m. Friday, Oct.
4, to submit a brief statement
for publication in the Daily
next week.
Statements should be no
more than two or three brief
paragraphs, and should include
author's name, major, and>
telephone number. The Daily
reserves the right to edit for
style and length. Contributions
may be left in the Opinions
Editors' box in the Daily
office.
