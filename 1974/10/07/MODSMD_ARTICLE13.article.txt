THE STANFORD DAILY is an independent student newspaper owned
and published by the Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation, a
California non-profit corporation, Monday through Friday during the
academic year, except during dead and finals weeks.
Telephone: Editorial, (415) 497-4632; Business, (415) 497-2554.
Editorials represent the opinion of a majority vote of the Daily
editorial board, composed of 18 editors and four at-large members
elected by the staff.
Letters, columns and cartoons on the opinions page represent only
the views of their authors. Nothing on the opinions page represents a
position of the Daily staff, the ASSU or the University.
Subscription rates: In the U.S., Canada and Mexico, $15 per year, $6
per quarter. Send check or money order in advance only to The
Stanford Dally, Storke Student Publications Building, Stanford. Ca.
94305.
(Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto under
the act of March 3, 1897.)
