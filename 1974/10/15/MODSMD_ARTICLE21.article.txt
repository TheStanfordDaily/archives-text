# SPECTRUM
## 
### 
The Stanford Daily publishes a
section previewing Bay Area
entertainment events each
Thursday. Anyone wishing to
submit events for publication in
the Daily's Entertainment
Spectrum should type their name,
type of event, date, admission
costs and any other pertinent
information and place it in the
Spectrum box at the Daily offices,
Storke Student Publications
Building by 1 p.m. on Tuesday on
the week of desired publication.
