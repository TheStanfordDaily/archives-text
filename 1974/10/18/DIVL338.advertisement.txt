Balya'i YVeel*
In Palo Alto
October 20-20, 1974
The Palo Alio Baha'i Community nas arranged to present a
week-long series of events in order to share with the citizens of
Palo Alto a plan for the building of a worldwide, spiritualized, and
peaceful civilization, as propounded by Baha'u'llah, Founder of
the Baha'i Faith.
"Let Your Vision
Information Center at the Palo Alto Cultural Center,
Newell at Embarcadero, in the Lobby. Hours: Sunday
12-5. Monday through Thursday 11 a.m.-5 p.m. or
Call 326-2655 or 493-7818.
