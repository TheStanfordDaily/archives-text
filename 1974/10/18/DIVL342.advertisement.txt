AGENDA DIGEST
The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday, October 21, 1974 at 7:30 p.m. in the Council
Chambers of the Civic Center, 250 Hamilton Avenue. The complete agenda for the meetings is available ...
at the Civic Center, all City libraries, Community Centers, Police and Fire Stations. In addition, packets
containing the agenda and supporting informative material may be consulted at the libraries. Below is a
brief digest of the items which have been submitted. Inquiries concerning details of any items should be dir
ected to the City Clerk (329-2226). Palo Alto City Council Meetings are broadcast live via KZSU, 90.1 on
the FM dial.
Adjourned Meeting of October 7,1974
6. Planning Commission recommends re Yacht Harbor EIR, Master Plan and Revised Lease Agreement
7. Architectural Review Board recommends re Yacht Harbor EIR, Master Plan and Revised Lease Agree
ment
8. Resolution re Proposition 17 (Stanislaus River)
9. Resolution re Expense Allowance Schedule for Members of the City Council, Architectural Review
Board, Human Relations Commission, and Planning Commission
10. Action taken at special meeting 10/10/74
11. Arroyo Road Vacation of Public Utility Easements
12. Action taken at special meeting 10/10/74
13. Action taken at regular meeting 10/7/64
14. Downtown University Avenue Diagonal Parking Experiment
Regular Meeting of October 21, 1974
1. Ordinance re Community Housing, including condominiums, community apartments, stock coopera
tives, and planned developments (Ist reading 9/30/74)
2. Ordinance re Improvement of the Police Pistol Range in Byxbee Park and City Owned Baylands (AKA
John Fletcher Byxbee Recreation Area) (Ist reading 10/7/74)
3. Ordinance renaming Lane 13 E, Commonly Known as Alley 13, as Paulsen Lane (Ist reading 1077/74)
4. Resolution Endorsing Proposition 15 (re Low Rent Housing Projects)
5. Child Care Contract Amendment
6. Barron Park Annexation
7. Flood Basin Mitigation Project
8. Refuse Disposal Area: Lagoon Mitigation Project
9. Request of Councilwoman Pearson re Traffic Solution in Crescent Park Area re Petition
ACTION TAKEN BY THE COUNCIL ON THE ABOVE
ITEMS IS AVAILABLE AFTER 11:00 A.M. ON THE DAY FOLLOWING
