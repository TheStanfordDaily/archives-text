# The 						Romeros Appear Tonight
## 
### 
The Romeros, known on the international concert scene as
"The Royal Family of the Guitar," will make a return
engagement to the Stanford campus at 8 p.m. tonight in
Memorial Auditorium.
The Romeros performed at Frost Amphitheatre in 1970
and in 1973 with Arthur Fiedler and the San Francisco
Symphony Orchestra.
Time magazine described the Romeros as "Indisputably the
best guitar ensemble around."
Tickets are $2.75 for students, $3.50, $4.50 and $5.50 for
all others.
