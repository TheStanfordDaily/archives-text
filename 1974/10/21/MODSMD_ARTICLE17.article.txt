# Mitchell Added To Lecture Series
## 
### 
Edgar D. Mitchell, former
astronaut and the sixth man to
% walk on the moon, has been
added to the ASSU Speakers'
Bureau autumn quarter lecture
series.
Mitchell will speak at 8 p.m.
Thursday in Bishop Auditorium
on "Planetary Futures and Human
Potential."
Other speakers due within the
month are noted heart surgeon
Dr. Michael E. Deßakey and R.
Sargent Shriver, former director of
the Peace Corps and Sen. George
McGovern's running mate in the
, 1972 presidential election.
Deßakey will lecture at 8 p.m.
Thursday, Oct. 31, at Cubberley
Auditorium on ''Social
Responsibility in Medical
Research." Shriver speaks at 8
p.m. next Monday, also at
Cubberley, on a topic to be
announced.
Mitchell, who heads the
Institute of Noetic Science in Palo
Alto, will be a guest-in-residence
at Florence Moore Hall. Deßakey
will also participate in the
guests-in-residence program.
