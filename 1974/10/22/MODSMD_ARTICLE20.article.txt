# BAY AREA SPECTRUM
## 
### 
The Daily publishes a section previewing Bay Area entertainment
events each Thursday. To submit events for publication in Spectrum,
type the name, type, date and admission cost of the event along with
any other pertinent information and place it in the Spectrum box at the
Daily offices, Storke Student Publications Building by 1 p.m. Tuesday
on the week of desired publications.
