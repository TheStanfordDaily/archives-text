Mitchell To Speak
Edgar D. Mitchell, former astronaut
and the sixth man to walk on the
moon, will speak at 8 p.m. in
Bishop Auditorium of the Graduate
School of Business.
Mitchell, the first guest of the
ASSU Speaker's Bureau this year, will
speak on "Planetary Futures and
Human Potential."
