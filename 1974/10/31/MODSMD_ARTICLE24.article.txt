# Briefly...
## 
### 
Heart Surgeon To Speak
Noted heart surgeon Dr. Michael E.
De Bakey will speak at 8 p.m. today in
Cubberley Auditorium on "The Social
Responsibility, of Medical Research."
De Bakey, president of Baylor
College of Medicine, is guest of the
ASSU Speakers Bureau.
Sociology Prof To Speak
Asst. Sociology Prof. John Meyer
will speak on "Uses of the Myth of
Education" at 8 tonight in the
Tresidder lounge. His talk is sponsored
by the Tresidder Program Board.
Lecture on Lasers
Prof. Edward M. Teller of the
University of California's Lawrence
Radiation Laboratory will speak at
4:15 p.m. today in Annenberg
Auditorium of the Cummings Art
Building.
"The Use of High Power Lasers," is
the topic of his talk, which will be the
first John R. Mac Donald Memorial
Lecture.
Graphic Designer To Speak
Sheila de Bretteville will speak
tonight at 8 in Annenberg Auditorium
on "A Feminist Perspective on
Design."
