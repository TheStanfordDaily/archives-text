MONDAY
Oven Stew, Scalloped Potatoes, Garden
Salad, Ice Cream and Cake, Beverage
Oven Stew
Serves 6 to 8
2 lbs. beef stew meat, cut in cubes
2 tablespoons oil
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
1 carrot, shredded
1/3 cup dry red wine
1 (3 oz.) can chopped mushrooms,
drained
2 tablespoons dry onion soup mix
Brown meat in oil. Transfer to 2 Qt.
Casserole. Combine rest of ingredients;
pour over meat. Bake, covered in 350
oven for 2 I A hours, stirring occasionally.
TUESDAY
Pickled Meat Loaf, Buttered Peas, Sliced
Tomatoes, Potato Salad, Rolls,
Cheesecake, Beverage
Pickled Meat Loaf
Serves 8
2 beaten eggs
1/3 cup fine dry bread crumbs
1/3 cup chili sauce
1 (3 oz.) can chopped mushrooms,
drained
1 tablespoon horseradish

IV4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
V 4 teaspoon thyme
2 lbs. ground beef
Dill pickles, quartered lengthwise
Combine first 8 ingredients. Add beef;
mix well. Put half the mixture into a loaf
pan. Top with pickles; cover with rest of
mixture. Bake at 350 for PA hours.
WEDNESDAY
Hearty Hodgepodge, Celery and Carrot
Sticks, Dill Pickles, Sourdough Bread,
Baked Custard, Beverage
Hearty Hodgepodge
Serves 8 to 10
6 slices bacon
1 med. onion, sliced thin
1 lb. beef shank
3 A lb. ham hock
6 cups water
2 teaspoons salt
2 (15 oz.) cans garbanzo beans
3 cups diced, peeled tomatoes
1 clove garlic, minced
1 (4 oz.) link Polish Sausage, sliced thin
In Dutch oven cook bacon till crisp;
drain, reserving 2 tablespoons drippings.
Crumble bacon and set aside. Cook
onions in reserved drippings till tender

but not brown. Add beef shank, ham
hock, water and salt. Cover and simmer
IV2 hours. Remove meat from shank and
ham hock; dice. Skim fat from broth.
Return diced meat to soup; add
undrained beans, potatoes and garlic.
Simmer, covered, 30 minutes more. Add
sausage and bacon. Simmer, covered 15
minutes longer.
THURSDAY
Lamb Loaves, Frozen Potato Puffs,
Brussel Sprouts, Cherry Tomatoes,
Celery Sticks, Blueberry Pie, Beverage
Lamb Loaves
Serves 6
1 lb. ground lamb
V 2 lb. ground beef
2 cups soft French bread crumbs
1 can onion soup
l A teaspoon oregano
Mint leaves
Mix meats, crumbs, soup and oregano,
stirring till blended. Spoon meat mixture
into 12 ungreased muffin cups, pressing
in lightly. Bake in 400 oven about 15
minutes or till browned. Garnish with
fresh mint.
FRIDAY
Tomato Sauced Fish, Baby Lima Beans,

Carrot/Pineapple Salad Mold, Rolls,
Lemon Ice Cream, Beverage
Tomato Sauced Fish
Serves 6
2 lbs. frozen fish fillets
Salt
1 (3 oz.) can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 (8 oz.) can tomato sauce
1 tablespoon lemon juice
l A teaspoon thyme
Thaw fish; arrange in single layer in
well greased baking dish. Sprinkle with
salt. Top with mushrooms. Blend tomato
sauce, lemon juice and thyme; pour over
fish. Bake covered at 350 about 30 to 35
minutes.
SATURDAY
Ham and Cheese Fondue Bake, Buttered
Green Beans, Tossed Salad, Pickles and
Olives, Baked Apples, Beverage
Hani and Cheese Fondue Bake
Serves 4 to 6
2 tablespoons butter
V 2 cup chopped onions
5 slices bread, cut in cubes
2 cups diced fully cooked ham
1 cup shredded American cheese
1 (8 oz.) can tomatoes, drained
3 beaten eggs

VA cups milk
1/8 teaspoon dry mustard
Melt butter. Add onion; cook till
tender but not brown. Add bread, ham,
cheese and tomatoes; toss to mix well.
Pour into baking dish. Combine eggs,
milk and mustard. Pour over ham into
baking dish. Bake at 350 for 25 to 30
minutes.
SUNDAY
Cornish Hens with Mandarin Orange
Dressing, Fluffy Rice, Buttered
Asparagus, Crisp Green Salad, Sliced
Pears, Beverage
Cornish Hens with Mandarin Orange
Dressing
Serves 4
2 cans (11 oz.) Mandarin Oranges,
drained, reserve liquid
1 small can pimento
4 Cornish hens
4 tablespoons soy sauce
4 tablespoons lemon juice
Combine orange sections with
pimento. Use this mixture to stuff hens
lightly. Dress and place in baking pan.
Roast in 350 oven for 50 to 60 minutes.
Baste while cooking with reserved orange
liquid, combine with soy sauce and
lemon juice.

Tomatoes, Potato Salad, Rolls,
Cheesecake, Beverage
Pickled Meat Loaf
Serves 8
2 beaten eggs
1/3 cup fine dry bread crumbs
1/3 cup chili sauce
1 (3 oz.) can chopped mushrooms,
drained
1 tablespoon horseradish

IV4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
V 4 teaspoon thyme
2 lbs. ground beef
Dill pickles, quartered lengthwise
Combine first 8 ingredients. Add beef;
mix well. Put half the mixture into a loaf
pan. Top with pickles; cover with rest of
mixture. Bake at 350 for PA hours.
WEDNESDAY
Hearty Hodgepodge, Celery and Carrot
Sticks, Dill Pickles, Sourdough Bread,
Baked Custard, Beverage
Hearty Hodgepodge
Serves 8 to 10
6 slices bacon
1 med. onion, sliced thin
1 lb. beef shank
3 A lb. ham hock
6 cups water
2 teaspoons salt
2 (15 oz.) cans garbanzo beans
3 cups diced, peeled tomatoes
1 clove garlic, minced
1 (4 oz.) link Polish Sausage, sliced thin
In Dutch oven cook bacon till crisp;
drain, reserving 2 tablespoons drippings.
Crumble bacon and set aside. Cook
onions in reserved drippings till tender

but not brown. Add beef shank, ham
hock, water and salt. Cover and simmer
IV2 hours. Remove meat from shank and
ham hock; dice. Skim fat from broth.
Return diced meat to soup; add
undrained beans, potatoes and garlic.
Simmer, covered, 30 minutes more. Add
sausage and bacon. Simmer, covered 15
minutes longer.

Lamb Loaves, Frozen Potato Puffs,
Brussel Sprouts, Cherry Tomatoes,
Celery Sticks, Blueberry Pie, Beverage
Lamb Loaves
Serves 6
1 lb. ground lamb
V 2 lb. ground beef
2 cups soft French bread crumbs
1 can onion soup
l A teaspoon oregano
Mint leaves
Mix meats, crumbs, soup and oregano,
stirring till blended. Spoon meat mixture
into 12 ungreased muffin cups, pressing
in lightly. Bake in 400 oven about 15
minutes or till browned. Garnish with
fresh mint.

Tomato Sauced Fish, Baby Lima Beans,

Carrot/Pineapple Salad Mold, Rolls,
Lemon Ice Cream, Beverage
Tomato Sauced Fish
Serves 6
2 lbs. frozen fish fillets
Salt
1 (3 oz.) can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 (8 oz.) can tomato sauce
1 tablespoon lemon juice
l A teaspoon thyme
Thaw fish; arrange in single layer in
well greased baking dish. Sprinkle with
salt. Top with mushrooms. Blend tomato
sauce, lemon juice and thyme; pour over
fish. Bake covered at 350 about 30 to 35
minutes.
SATURDAY
Ham and Cheese Fondue Bake, Buttered
Green Beans, Tossed Salad, Pickles and
Olives, Baked Apples, Beverage
Hani and Cheese Fondue Bake
Serves 4 to 6
2 tablespoons butter
V 2 cup chopped onions
5 slices bread, cut in cubes
2 cups diced fully cooked ham
1 cup shredded American cheese
1 (8 oz.) can tomatoes, drained
3 beaten eggs

VA cups milk
1/8 teaspoon dry mustard
Melt butter. Add onion; cook till
tender but not brown. Add bread, ham,
cheese and tomatoes; toss to mix well.
Pour into baking dish. Combine eggs,
milk and mustard. Pour over ham into
baking dish. Bake at 350 for 25 to 30
minutes.
SUNDAY
Cornish Hens with Mandarin Orange
Dressing, Fluffy Rice, Buttered
Asparagus, Crisp Green Salad, Sliced
Pears, Beverage
Cornish Hens with Mandarin Orange
Dressing
Serves 4
2 cans (11 oz.) Mandarin Oranges,
drained, reserve liquid
1 small can pimento
4 Cornish hens
4 tablespoons soy sauce
4 tablespoons lemon juice
Combine orange sections with
pimento. Use this mixture to stuff hens
lightly. Dress and place in baking pan.
Roast in 350 oven for 50 to 60 minutes.
Baste while cooking with reserved orange
liquid, combine with soy sauce and
lemon juice.

Hearty Hodgepodge
Serves 8 to 10
6 slices bacon
1 med. onion, sliced thin
1 lb. beef shank
3 A lb. ham hock
6 cups water
2 teaspoons salt
2 (15 oz.) cans garbanzo beans
3 cups diced, peeled tomatoes
1 clove garlic, minced
1 (4 oz.) link Polish Sausage, sliced thin
In Dutch oven cook bacon till crisp;
drain, reserving 2 tablespoons drippings.
Crumble bacon and set aside. Cook
onions in reserved drippings till tender

but not brown. Add beef shank, ham
hock, water and salt. Cover and simmer
IV2 hours. Remove meat from shank and
ham hock; dice. Skim fat from broth.
Return diced meat to soup; add
undrained beans, potatoes and garlic.
Simmer, covered, 30 minutes more. Add
sausage and bacon. Simmer, covered 15
minutes longer.

Tomato Sauced Fish
Serves 6
2 lbs. frozen fish fillets
Salt
1 (3 oz.) can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 (8 oz.) can tomato sauce
1 tablespoon lemon juice
l A teaspoon thyme
Thaw fish; arrange in single layer in
well greased baking dish. Sprinkle with
salt. Top with mushrooms. Blend tomato
sauce, lemon juice and thyme; pour over
fish. Bake covered at 350 about 30 to 35
minutes.

Ham and Cheese Fondue Bake, Buttered
Green Beans, Tossed Salad, Pickles and
Olives, Baked Apples, Beverage
Hani and Cheese Fondue Bake
Serves 4 to 6
2 tablespoons butter
V 2 cup chopped onions
5 slices bread, cut in cubes
2 cups diced fully cooked ham
1 cup shredded American cheese
1 (8 oz.) can tomatoes, drained
3 beaten eggs

VA cups milk
1/8 teaspoon dry mustard
Melt butter. Add onion; cook till
tender but not brown. Add bread, ham,
cheese and tomatoes; toss to mix well.
Pour into baking dish. Combine eggs,
milk and mustard. Pour over ham into
baking dish. Bake at 350 for 25 to 30
minutes.

Cornish Hens with Mandarin Orange
Dressing, Fluffy Rice, Buttered
Asparagus, Crisp Green Salad, Sliced
Pears, Beverage
Cornish Hens with Mandarin Orange
Dressing
Serves 4
2 cans (11 oz.) Mandarin Oranges,
drained, reserve liquid
1 small can pimento
4 Cornish hens
4 tablespoons soy sauce
4 tablespoons lemon juice
Combine orange sections with
pimento. Use this mixture to stuff hens
lightly. Dress and place in baking pan.
Roast in 350 oven for 50 to 60 minutes.
Baste while cooking with reserved orange
liquid, combine with soy sauce and
lemon juice.

Oven Stew
Serves 6 to 8
2 lbs. beef stew meat, cut in cubes
2 tablespoons oil
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
1 carrot, shredded
1/3 cup dry red wine
1 (3 oz.) can chopped mushrooms,
drained
2 tablespoons dry onion soup mix
Brown meat in oil. Transfer to 2 Qt.
Casserole. Combine rest of ingredients;
pour over meat. Bake, covered in 350
oven for 2 I A hours, stirring occasionally.
