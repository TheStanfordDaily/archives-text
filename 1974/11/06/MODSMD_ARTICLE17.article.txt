# Former Alumni Director Dies
## 
### 
Robert L. Pierce, 49,
former executive director of
the Stanford Alumni
Association, died Saturday in
Carmel after a lengthy illness.
A memorial service will be
held at 2:30 p.m. Sunday in
Memorial Church.
Pierce retired in 1970 for
health reasons. During his 14
years of service, the Stanford
Alumni Association became
one of the leading
organizations of its kind in
the country.
