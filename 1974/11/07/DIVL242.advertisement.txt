Students are cordially invited
by the Officers of the Class of 1975 and the Alumni Association
to share an exciting elegant evening with alumni
in the old style of Stanford Big Game festivities!!!!
The Big Game Dinner Dance
Buffet dining and dancing to music from
the '3os to the '7os
WHERE: Stanford Court Hotel on
Nob Hill in San Francisco
WHEN: Friday, November 22, 1975
7:30 p.m. to 12 midnight
Dress: cocktail attire
Transportation: Round-trip bus service from
campus, charge $3.00. Leaves Bowman
Alumni House at 6:45 p.m., leaves San
Francisco at 12:30 a.m.
Schedule: No-host cocktails at 7:30 p.m.,
dinner at 8:30 p.m., and dancing from 9
to midnight.
Charge: $12.50 per person including dinner
and dancing.
RESERVE NOW!!! SPACE LIMITED.
Make reservations at Bowman Alumni House.
(Sorry, reservations canceled after November 15 cannot be refunded.)
