Synergy Earth Games
Project Synergy will sponsor a New Games
Institute program of "non-competitive sport" today
in White Plaza from 11:45 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. The
program will feature earthballs, dancers with
parachutes and boffing matches. At 12:30 p.m.,
films from the World Games Tournament last year
in San Francisco will be shown on the main floor of
the Clubhouse.
Hudson Gets Dreyfus Grant
Asst. Chemistry Prof. Bruce S. Hudson has
received a $25,000 grant from the Camille and
Henry Dreyfus Foundation of New York to be used
"at his discretion to develop new educational
concepts and to evaluate new research ideas."
The grants are awarded each year to outstanding
young faculty members in chemistry, biochemistry
or chemical engineering.
