HOW TO PLACE CLASSIFIED ADS
HOW up of 32 characters, spaces or
Ads must be placed in person or punctuation marks. Charges are
mailed, with payment in advance. $2.20/ line; $l.lO for students with
No ads by phone! ID.
WHERE Late ads run for one day at $.75
Daily Business Office, Storke per line.
Student Publications Building,
Panama and Lomita Streets. Phone DEADLINES
497-2554. Ads received by noon, Friday
WHEN appear Monday.
10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Ads received by noon,
RATES Wednesday appear Thursday.
Regular ads run for one week. Late ads received by 4 p.m.
