Israeli Journalist To Speak
Peretz Kidron, an Israeli journalist who
represents a "New Left" viewpoint in his
country, will discuss "Israel and the
Palestinians" today at 4:15 p.m. at the Bechtel
International Center.

Columbia Prof. Elliott Skinner, chairman of
Relief for Africans In Need in the Sahel
(RAINS), will speak on "African Drought and
Famine: External Responses to the Drought"
today at 4:15 p.m. in Tresidder 270.
