# BONFIRE ESCORT SERVICE
## 
### 
An escort service will be available for persons working on
the nightshift of the bonfire. Escorts may be arranged by
calling any fraternity:
Working and Guarding Schedule
Thursday
6 a.m. - noon Branner
noon - 6 p.m. FloMo
6 p.m. - midnight Wilbur
Friday
midnight - 6 a.m. Stern
6 a.m. - noon ;.... Roble
noon-6 p.m. all
. People wanting to work in addition to the above times may
come out when ever they wish.
