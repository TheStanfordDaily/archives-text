Stunkard Resigns Chairmanship
Psychiatry Prof. Albert J. Stunkard ,
has resigned as chairman of the
psychiatry department, it was
announced Tuesday.
In a letter to Medical School Dean
Clayton Rich, Stunkard cited personal
reasons for his decision. He plans to
continue as a professor and director of
the newly established eating disorders 1
clinic.
Rich appointed Psychiatry Prof.
Thomas A. Gonda as acting chairman
of the department, effective
immediately. He also appointed a
committee to advise on the selection
of a new chairman. i
