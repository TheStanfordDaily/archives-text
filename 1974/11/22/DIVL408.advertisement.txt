AGENDA DIGEST
The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday, NOVEMBER 25, 1974 at 7:30 p.m. in the Council
Chambers of the Civic Center, 250 Hamilton Avenue. The complete agenda for the meetings is available at the
Center, all City libraries, Community Centers, Police and Fire Stations. In addition, packets containing the agenda
and supporting informative material may be consulted at the libraries. Below is a brief digest of the items which
have been submitted. Inquiries concerning details of any items should be directed to the City Clerk (329-2226).
Palo Alto City Council Meetings are broadcast live via KZSU, 90.1 on the FM dial.
Adjourned Meeting of November 18, 1974
4. Planning Commission recommends re application of Jack Blackwell re 2097 East Bayshore Rd.
,5. Comprehensive Plan Options: Final Report
6. Cooperative Maintenance of School District and City Facilities
7. Veterans' Building
8. Request of Vice Mayer Henderson re Dumbarton Bridge Improvements Alternative Plan
9. Request of Councilman Comstock re Christmas Tree program
Regular Meeting of November 25, 1974
1. Finance and Public Works Committee recommends re Squire House
2. Finance and Public Works Committee recommends re consultant selection procedures
3. Policy and Procedures Committee recommends re Massage Parlor Ordinance
4. Planning Commission recommends re Barron Park annexation
5. Renaming of Wilkie Way Bridge
6. California Avenue Area Off-Street Parking Project No. 71-63
7. Ordinance re Cogswell Plaza Extension (Ist reading 11/11/74)
8. Ordinance re improvement of Cogswell Plaza Extension (Ist reading 11/11/74)
9. Ordinance re contract between Board of Administration of the Public Employees' Retirement System and the
City of Palo Alto (1 st reading 11/11/74)
10. Vacation of an Overhead Power Line Easement 1117 California Avenue
11. Vacation of an Underground Electrical Utility Easement - 611 Hansen Way
12. Vacation of a Public Utilities Easement - 3333 Hillview Avenue
13. Fence Ordinance
14. Finance and Public Works Committee recommends re Fire Zone I Rehabilitation Program and Financial Plan
(Continued from 6/24/74)
15. Request of Councilman Rosenbaum re Model Smoking Ordinance

CITY OF PALO ALTO
NOTICE OF EXPIRATION
OF HUMAN RELATIONS COMMISSIONERS
Pursuant to Section 2.22.015 of the Palo Alto Municipal Code, NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that the terms of
Human Relations Commissioners McAllester and Dreyfus will expire December 31, 1974. Incumbents may request
reappointment.
Applications and nominations from citizens interested in serving on the Human Relations Commission are invited
by the Council. Terms are for the three years commencing January 1, 1975.
Application forms may be obtained from the Office of the City Clerk (329-2571). If your resume is on file in the
City s Talent Bank, please call and indicate that you wish to be considered for these appoinements.
Last date for acceptance of applications in the City Clerk's Office is 5-00 p.m., Wednesday, December 4, 1974.
ANN J. TANNER
City Clerk
