# Last 'Daily'
## 
###

Daily this quarter. A special
basketball issue will be distributed
Saturday, Dec. 21 (Utah State) and
Monday, Dec. 23 (University of
Florida). The Daily will resume
publication on Registration Day,
Monday, Jan. 6, 1975.
Happy Holidays from the entire
Daily staff.
