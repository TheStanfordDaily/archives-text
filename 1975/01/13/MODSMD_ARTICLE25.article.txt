# JV Cagers Win First Of Season
## 
### 
The Stanford junior varsity
basketball team posted its first
victory of the season Friday
night, defeating Sacramento
State JV's 91-81 at
Sacramento.
Sven Walker led the
Stanford scoring with 21
points and 15 rebounds. Scott
Russel chipped in 16 points
while Bill Christopher had 14
and Warner Session got 12.
The Stanford JV's are now
1-2 for the season.
