# Across The Sports Desk
## 
### 
Schweitzer Honored
Stanford forward Ed
Schweitzer was chosen both
Pacific Eight conference and
Northern California basketball
player of the week for his efforts
against Oregon and Oregon State.
Schweitzer scored 54 points

and shot 81 percent from the
floor over the weekend.
Hockey Game
The Stanford Ice Hockey Team
will play Santa Rosa Junior
College in the Oakland Coliseum
before the Seals-Montreal
Canadians game Feb. 2.
Tickets are available at the
Athletic Department and from
team members. While they will be
sold at the regular $5 and $6.50
prices, the team will make a small
profit on each ticket sold.
