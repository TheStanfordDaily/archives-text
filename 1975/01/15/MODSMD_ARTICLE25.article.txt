# Briefly...
## 
### 
Assassination Conspiracy Talk
Radio personality Rusty Rhodes, well-known
conspiracy theorist, will discuss "A Decade of
Conspiracy from Dallas to Watergate" tonight at
7 in Annenberg Auditorium, Cummings Art
Building.
Rodin Tours
Public tours of the Rodin collection in the
Stanford Art Museum will be given on Wednesdays
at 12:15 p.m. and Saturdays at 1:15 p.m. during
January and February with the exception of
Wednesday, February 5.
ASSU Senate Meeting
The ASSU Senate will meet tonight at 7 in Room
75 of the Graduate School of Business. Anyone
interested in serving on the ASSU Nominations
Committee should attend. The meeting is open to
the public.
