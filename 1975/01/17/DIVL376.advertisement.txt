AGENDA DIGEST
The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday, January 20, 1975 at 7:30 p.m. in the Council
Chambers of the Civic Center, 250 Hamilton Avenue. The complete agenda for the meetings is available at
the Civic Center, all City libraries, Community Centers, Police and Fire Stations. In addition, packets con
taining the agenda and supporting informative material may be consulted at the libraries. Below is a brief
digest of the items which have been submitted. Inquiries concerning details of any be directed
to the City Clerk (329-2226). Palo Alto City Council Meetings are broadcast live via KZSU, 90.1 on the FM
dial.
Regular meeting of January 20, 1975
1. Planning Commission re John N. Pappas, 4221 Wilkie Way (cont. from 1/13/75).
2. Planning Commission re Sydney M. Smith, 3801 E. Bayshore (cont. from 1/13/75).
3. Resolution re practice and education of massage practitioners (cont. from 1/13/75).
4. Retirement of Anthony J. Poso.
5. Committees on Finance and Public Works re Greer Park.
6. Policy & Procedures recommendation re Council & Committee Meeting Procedures.
7. Policy & Procedure Committee re Fence Ordinance.
8. Planning Commission re James W. Foug, 2600 E. Bayshore.
9. Ordinance re improvement of Palo Alto Yacht Harbor. (First reading 10/21/74).
10. Ordinance amending title 18 of Municipal Code re cemeteries. (First reading 1/6/75).
11. U.S. Coast Guard draft re Dumbarton Bridge Replacement Project.
12. 1975 Weed Abatement Program (cont. from 1/13/75).
13. Massage Ordinance (cont. from 1/13/75). Administrative procedures.
14. Transportation for Mobility-Impaired Seniors (cont. from 1/13/75).
15. Request of Councilwoman Pearson re DIAL-A-BUS.
16. Request of Vice Mayor Henderson re revenue Holiday Inn. (cont. from 1/13/75).
17. Request of Councilman Norton re ITT property.
COMMITTEE MEETINGS:
Tuesday, January 21,1975 Policy and Procedure Committee 7:30 p.m.
Conference Room Agenda: 1) Campaign Financing.
2) Model Smoking Ordinance.
