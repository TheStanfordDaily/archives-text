# Viola Mini-Concerts
## 
### 
Violist Nobuko Imai will be
performing as part of the
Lively Arts Season on Friday
night at 8 in the Tresidder
Lounges.
In addition to the scheduled
performance, Imai will be
giving two mini-concerts that
will be free and open to the
entire campus community.
One of the concerts will be
at Junipero following a 5 p.m.
sherry hour and dinner on
Wednesday, and the other will
follow a 5 p.m. sherry and
dinner at Toyon, with El Toro
eating club as host.
