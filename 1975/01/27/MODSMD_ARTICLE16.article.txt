Serves 4 to 6
IV2 lbs. ground beef
1 tsp. salt

fine dry bread crumbs

3 tbl. butter
1 (8 oz.) can tomato sauce
4 slices Mozarella cheese
grated Parmesan cheese
Mix meat, salt, and pepper.
