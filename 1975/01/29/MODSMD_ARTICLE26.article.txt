Sex Differences And Psychiatry
Lenore K. Morrell, research fellow
at the Harvard School of Public
Health, will discuss "Sex Differences
in the Odds of Becoming a Psychiatric
Case" today from noon to 1 p.m. in
Room M-114 of the Medical Center.
Voter Registration Deadline
The voter registration deadline for
the March 4 local elections is
tomorrow. The March 4 ballot
includes elections for the Palo Alto
Unified School District and the
Foothill College Board of Trustees, as
well as measures to provide for
continuation of the transportation
district in Santa Clara County and for
support of local school revenues.
