'Star Trek' Showing
The original pilot of the Star Trek
television series, entitled "The Cage,"
will be shown at 7 p.m. tonight in
Memorial Auditorium. Admission is
free and open to the public.
Child Care Discussion
A panel discussion will precede a
town-hall type meeting and child care
tonight at 7:30 p.m. in the Small
Lounge of Tresidder Union.
Journalist To Speak
Stan Bohrman, KPIX television
anchorman, will be guest speaker at
the weekly Communication 2258 class
at 3:45 p.m. today in Redwood Hall
Room Gl9.
