# Kirst, Augsburger Named
## 
### 
Assoc. Education Prof. Michael W. Kirst was
appointed Friday to the state Board of Education by
Gov. Edmund G. Brown Jr.
Kirst has served as consultant on school finance to
the Oregon Legislature and to Florida Gov. Reuben
Askew.
Robert Augsburger, vice president for business and
finance, has been named to a task force sponsored by
the 20th Century Fund to examine "the growing
financial strain for institutions of higher learning."
"The commission will review the suitability of
common stocks for educational endowment funds, and
other problems related to investment and return.
