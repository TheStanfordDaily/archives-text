THE STANFORD DAILY is an independent student newspaper owned
and published by the Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation, a
California non-profit corporation, Monday through Friday during the
3 academic year, except during dead and finals weeks.
Telephone: Editorial, (41 '3) 497-4632; Business, (415) 497-2554.
Editorials represent the opinion of a majority vote of the Daily's
Editorial Board, comprised of 25 editors and four at-large members
{ elected by the staff.
Letters, columns and cartoons on the opinions page represent only
the views of their authors. Nothing on the opinions page necessarily
represents a position of the Daily staff, the ASSU or the University.
S Subscription rates: in the United States, Canada and Mexico, $15
per year or $6 per quarter. Send check or money order in advance to
The Stanford Daily, Storke Student Publications Building, Stanford,
California 94305.
Printed by Noweis Publications, 640 Robie Avenue, Menlo Park,
2 California.
(Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto under
the act of March 3, 1897.)
