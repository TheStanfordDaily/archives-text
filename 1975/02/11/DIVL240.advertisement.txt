STUDENTS LIVING OFF CAMPUS STILL
HAVE TO DRIVE ON CAMPUS
And you off-campus students may not be completely satisfied
with the current parking system.
WOULD YOU RIDE A BUS IF THERE WAS ONE?
ARE YOU WILLING TO PAY FOR A PARKING SPACE?
SHOULD ALL STUDENT LOTS BE UNRESTRICTED,
REGARDLESS OF ON-OR OFF-CAMPUS RESIDENCE?
LET ME KNOW WHAT YOU THINK.
I've been arked to re-examine the parking and transportation
system and recommend changes. To do this, I need your input
There will be a public meeting today at 7 p.m. in the Forum
Room, Meyer to hear your ideas. Everyone is welcome, but
I especially want to hear from the off-campus students.
THIS IS YOUR OPPORTUNITY TO BE HEARO.
MAKE USE OF IT.
