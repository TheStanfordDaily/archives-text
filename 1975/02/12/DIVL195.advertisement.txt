MASTER OF BUSINESS
ADMINISTRATION
with a major in
Transportation
at the University of British
Columbia in Vancouver, Canada.
ÜBC has the largest business
school in Canada and one of the
most prestigious faculties in North
America. Our graduates are in
demand.

physical sciences.
