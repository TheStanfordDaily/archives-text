Theater in England
5 weeks: July 29-Sept 2 (two weeks for travel) OR
3 weeks: Aug. 5-27 (the period of the 3-week course)
• Six units of academic credit (can be transferred to any
institution).
• Twelve or more great plays in London, Chichester, and
Stratford-on-Avon.
• Leading actors and directors from the Royal Shakespeare
Company and the National Theatre Company
(they discuss the plays with you both in class and over a
drink in the evening)
• Trips into English history and walks in the countryside.
• Plenty of free time; this is not a tour, you are not
herded about
• Two teachers: Dr. Stanley Glenn, Director of the Theatre,
UCSB, and author of The Director Prepares; and
Or. Homer Swander, Director of the Institute of Renaissance
Studies, Ashland, Oregon, and teacher of English, UCSB.
For information, write Dr. Homer Swander, Department of English, UC Santa Barbara, 93106.
