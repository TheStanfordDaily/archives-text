# Art Show Opens
## 
### 
'Transcendent Blossoms,"
an exhibition of the works of
11 Japanese-American artists,
will open at the Syntex Gallery
in Stanford Industrial Park on
Feb. 24.
The exhibit will include
paintings, prints, sculptures,
drawings, ceramics, weavings,
illustrations, and graphic and
architectural designs.
The sculptures of Ruth
Asawa, one of the exhibitors,
have been acclaimed nationally
for years by representatives of
such major museums as the
Whitney and the Chicago Art
Institute. Many of her
commissioned sculptures are
on display in locations through
the City: Ghirardelli Square,
Fox Plaza, Joseph Magnin,and
a cast bronze fountain at Hyatt
on Union Square.
