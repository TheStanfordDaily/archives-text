# 'SNACK' SUNDAY
## 
### 
Bill Graham and San
Francisco Mayor Joseph Alioto
have announced that SNACK
Sunday, a benefit for San
Francisco public school
activities, will be held March
23, beginning at 10:30 a.m. in
Kezar Stadium.

Among the musical
performers slated to appear are the
Jefferson Starship, Santana,
Tower of Power, Jerry Garcia
and Friends, Graham Central
Station, Joan Baez and Mimi
Farina.
