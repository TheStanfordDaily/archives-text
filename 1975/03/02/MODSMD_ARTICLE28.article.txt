Shultz To Speak
Former Treasury Secretary and Business School Prof.
George Shultz will speak on "The Conservative Case for a
Strong System of Income Maintenance" tonight at 8 in
Annenberg Auditorium, Cummings Art Building.
Candidates Night
Tonight at 7:30 in Jordan Hall Room 040 there will be a
candidates night featuring all the major contenders for the
seat on the Palo Alto School Board. Candidates for the
three seats on the Foothill Junior College Board of Trustees
will also speak.
