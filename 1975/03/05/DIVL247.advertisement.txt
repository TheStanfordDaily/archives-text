The Stanford Daily
THE STANFORD DAILV is an independent student newspaper owned
and published by the Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation, a
California non-profit corporation, Monday through Friday during the
academic year, except during dead and finals weeks.
Telephone: Editorial, (415) 497-4632; Business, (415) 497-2554.
Editorials represent the opinion of a majority vote of the Daily's
Editorial Board, comprised of 25 editors and four at-large members
elected by the staff.
Letters, columns and cartoons on the opinions page represent only
the views of their authors. Nothing on the opinions page necessarily
represents a position of the Daily staff, the ASSU or the University.
Subscription rates: In the United States, Canada and Mexico, $15 per
year or $6 per quarter. Send check or money order in advance to The
Stanford Daily, Storke Student Publications Building, Stanford,
California 94305.
Printed by Nowels Publications, 640 Roble Avenue, Menlo Park,
California.
