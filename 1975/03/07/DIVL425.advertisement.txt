The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday, MARCH 10, 1975 at 7:30 p.m. in the Council
Chambers of the Civic Center, 250 Hamilton Avenue. The complete agenda for the meetings is available at the
Civic Center, all City libraries, Community Centers, Police and Fire Stations. In addition, packets containing the
agenda and supporting informative material may be consulted at the libraries. Below is a brief digest of the items
which have been submitted. Inquiries concerning details of any items should be directed to the City Clerk's Of
fice (329-2267). Palo Alto City Council Meetings are boardcast live via KZSU, frequency 90.1 on the FM dail.
1. Vacation of a Sewer Easement — East Meadow Circle/Fabian Way
2. Amendment to Child Care Contract
3. 1975 Census — Contract with Santa Clara County
4. Integrated Urban Insect Control Program
5. Solids Handling Optimization Study — Water Quality Control Plant
(NOTE: Items 1-5 Consent Calendar Items)
6. Retirement of Charles De Moulin
7. Determination by lot order of listing terms and candidates' names on the ballot for General Municipal Elec
tion, May 13, 1975
8. Finance and Public Works Committee recommends re Sidewalk Repair Policy
9. Policy and Procedures Committee recommends re Sign Ordinance Revisions
10. Policy and Procedures Committee recommends re Proposed Modifications to Architectural Review
11. Designation of Health Service Area — Santa Clara County
12. Santa Clara Valley Water District — Hetch Hetchy Water Contracts
13. Request of Councilman Comstock re SB 417 (continued from 2/24/75)
14. Request of Councilwoman Pearson re wall murals
15. Request of Councilwoman Pearson re status of Affirmative Action Program
16. Request of Councilwoman Pearson re inconsistencies in citizen utility bill
17. Request of Councilwoman Pearson re closing hours of card clubs
