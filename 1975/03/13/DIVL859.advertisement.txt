AGENDA DIGEST
The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday, March 17, 1975, at 7:30 p.m.
in the Council Chambers of the Civic Center, 250 Hamilton Avenue. The complete agenda
for the meetings is available at the Civic Center, all City libraries, Community
Centers, Police and Fire Stations. In addition, packets containing the agenda and
supporting informative material may be consulted at the libraries. Below is a brief
digest of the items which have been submitted. Inquiries concerning details of any
items should be directed to the City Clerk's Office (329-2267). Palo Alto City Council
meetings are broadcast live via KZSU, frequency 90.1 on the FM dial.
1. California Avenue Parking Assessment District: Award of Construction Contract
2. Foothills Park Storage Building - Contract Award
3. Planning Commission recommends re Miscellaneous Division of Land at 1015, 1025,
and 1085 East Meadow Circle
4. Barron Park Annexation - Resolution of Int tion to Call Election
5. Joint Powers Agreement - Fire Training Center - Mountain View
6. Garden Terrace Annexation No. 4 - Monroe Park Area
7. Participation of Recreation Staff at Mayfield School
8. Request of Councilwoman Pearson re rate increase proposed by PG&E 2/25/75
9. Request of Mayor Sher re proposed new regional postal facility
10. Request of Councilman Berwald re purchase of Squire House and Webster Block
properties
