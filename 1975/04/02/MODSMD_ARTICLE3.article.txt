# Franklin Receives Grant
## 
### 
Fired Stanford English Prof. H.
Bruce Franklin has received a
Rockefeller Foundation
fellowship.
Franklin, dismissed from
Stanford in 1972 for allegedly
inciting students to violence, now
teaches at Wesleyan and Yale.
One of 42 scholars to receive
the newly created fellowships.
Franklin plans to use the
appointment to write a book
entitled. The Victim as Criminal
and Artist: Crimes of Work and
Poverty in American Literature.
Franklin taught a course at
Wesleyan last year with the same
title.
