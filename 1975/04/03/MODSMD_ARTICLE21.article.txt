Residence Staff Positions
The last of three meetings for
students interested in residence staff
positions for the 1975-76 school year
will be held tonight from 7 to 9 p.m.
in Room 281 of Tresidder Memorial
Union.
Petitions Due
Petitions for slates of candidates
wishing to run for either Council of
Presidents or Senior Class Officers are
available in the ASSU office and are
due by 5 p.m. tomorrow.
Two Profs Elected
Political Science Profs. Gabriel
Almond and Robert Ward have been
elected to the American Philosophical
Association.
Ward is director of the Center for
Research in International Studies here;
Almond is former director of the
Institute for Political Studies and
former chairman of the Political
Science department.
