GENERAL MUNICIPAL ELECTION
May 13, 1975
VOTER REGISTRATION: You must be registered by April 13 to be eligible to vote on May 13, Register at
the City Clerk's office, 7th Floor, 250 Hamilton Avenue, Monday thru Friday, 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m. or at any
Palo Alto fire station, seven days a week between the hours of 4:00 - 5:00 p.m. and 6:00 - 8:00 p.m.
Call 321-2141, Ext. 2161, to check your registration status.
ABSENT VOTER BALLOT APPLICATIONS: If you will be out of town on May 13, you can pick up an app
lication for an absent voter ballot in the lobby of the Civic Center or at the City Clerk's office. Last day for City
Clerk to accept application for absent voter ballot is May 6, 1975.
