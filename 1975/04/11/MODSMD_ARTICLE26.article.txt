# HARPSICHORDIST
## 
### 
Leading West Coast
harpsichordist Margaret
Fabrizio, with baroque oboist
George Houle, will present a
concert of baroque music at 8
p.m. Friday, April 11, in
Annenberg Auditorium on the
Stanford campus.
Both musicians are on the
Stanford Music Department
faculty. Houle directs early
music studies here.
