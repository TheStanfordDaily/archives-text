Cherry For Lunch
Miriam Cherry, former associate
Catholic chaplain for the University,

CROW Lecture
Pat Nichols, a graduate student in
linguistics, will speak today at noon in
Room 86 of the Graduate School of
Business on "Language and Sex
Roles." The lecture is sponsored by
the Center for Research on Women.
