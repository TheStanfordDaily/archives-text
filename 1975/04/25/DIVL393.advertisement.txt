Agenda Digest
The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday, APRIL 28, 1975 at 7:30 p.m. in the Council
Chambers of the Civic Center, 250 Hamilton Avenue. The complete agenda for the meetihgs is available at the
Civic Center, all City libraries, Community Centers, Police and Fire Stations. In addition, packets containing the
agenda and supporting informative material may be consulted at the libraries. Below is a brief digest of the
items which have been submitted. Inquiries concerning details of any items should be directed to the City Clerk's
Office (329-2267). Palo Alto City Council meetings are broadcast live via KZSU, frequency 90.1 on the FM
dial.
1. Public Hearing: Refuse Disposal Area Draft EIR
2. Planning Commission recommends re Draft EIR for expansion of Refuse Disposal Area
3. Planning Commission recommends re action to prohibit and block all vehicle traffic in flood basin
4. Planning Commission recommends re restoration of marshland in flood basin
5. Solid Waste Disposal Study Part I - Short-Term Disposal Alternatives
6. Mathematical Model Study of the Palo Alto Flood Basin and Yacht Harbor
COMMITTEE MEETINGS
Finance and Public Works Committee — Tuesday, May 6 — 7:30 p.m. — Conference Room
Agenda: 1975-76 Annual Budget (Utilities)
Policy and Procedures Committee — Tuesday, May 6 — 7:30 p.m. — Council Chambers
Agenda: Proposed Santa Clara County Transportation Improvement Program
