AGENDA DIGEST
#
The Council of the City of Palo Alto will meet on Monday, May 5, 1975 at 7:30 p.m. in the Council Cham
bers of the Civic Center, 250 Hamilton Avenue. The complete agenda for the meetings is available at the Civic
Center, all City libraries, Community Centers, Police and Fire Stations. In addition, packets containing the
agenda and supporting informative material may be consulted at the libraries. Below is a brief digest of the
items which have been submitted. Inquiries concerning details of any items should be directed to the City
Clerk's Office (329-2267). Palo Aito City Council Meetings are broadcast live via KZSU, frequency 90.1 on
the FM dial.
1. Community Housing: Condominium Primer.
2. Barron Park — Proposed Moratorium.
3. El Camirro Park Improvements: Award of Contract.
4. Underground conversion of San Antonio Road.
5. Monroe Mini-Park. (First reading 4/14/75).
6. AB 664 (California Toll Bridge Authority Surplus Revenue).
7. Waverley Street Bicycle/Pedestrian Bridge.
8. Loan Guidelines for Housing Rehabilitation Project.
9. Policy and Procedures Committee Recommends re Long-Term Power Supply. (Cont. from 4/21/75).
10 . Planning Commission requests Council to reconsider its position re purchase of Lytton Plaza.
11. Task Force on Aging — Final Report.
12. Regional Quality Control Plan Modification Contract for Engineering.
13. Water Reclamation Pilot Project.
14. Stop sign Monroe Drive/Miller Avenue Intersection.
15. Inquiry of Councilman Berwald re Silva/Silva Annexation.
16. Request of Councilman Comstock for Council support of SB 634.
17. Request of Councilman Comstock for Council support of AB 1 and SB 2.
