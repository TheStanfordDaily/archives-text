ATTENTION,
CAMPUS PUBLICATIONS
The ASSU Publications Board will be allocating
funds this spring for publications that will be
publishing in Autumn Quarter, 1975. If you
would like to request money from the Board,
you should arrange to make a presentation
before the Board's appropriations subcommittee.
For further information, contact either sub
committee chairman Tim Portwood at 497-4687
(afternoons) or 326-2302 or 328-9637 (evenings);
or Pub Board Chairman Rich Jaroslovsky at
497-4632.
