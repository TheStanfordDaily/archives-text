Hershey Wins Fellowship
Dr. John C. Hershey, assistant
professor of management science in
the health service administration
program at the Stanford Medical
School, has been named a Robert
Wood Johnson Health Policy Fellow
for 1975-76.
Hershey and the five other fellows
will work with members of Congress
on health-care legislation and
programs.
Law Library Moving
The law school's library will close
at 10 p.m. on June 8 and reopen in the
new law school building on June 23.
During that period no law library
services will be available.
