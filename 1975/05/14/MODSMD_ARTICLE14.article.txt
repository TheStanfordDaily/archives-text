# Prison Symposium Schedule Of Events
## 
### 
<
Today is the first day of the Stanford Forum's symposium <
on prisons. Here is the schedule of events:
Sherry Hours
Larry Hatfield, prison reporter for the San Francisco
Examiner, 5 p.m., Naranja, Lagunita.
Michael Snedeker, Attorney for the Prisoners' Union, 4:30
p.m. Rinconada, Wilbur.
Patti Holder, co-director of the Prisoners' Union, 4:30 p.m.
Arroyo, Wilbur.
Willie Holder, president of the Prisoners' Union, 4:30 p.m.
Madera, Wilbur.
Representative of McLaughlin's Halfway House in
Redwood City, 5:15 p.m., Lathrop House.
Guest Speaker
Jessica Mitford, the author of Kind and Unusual
Punishment and The American Way of Death, will speak in
Cubberley Auditorium, 8 p.m.
