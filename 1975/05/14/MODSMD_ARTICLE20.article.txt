# Netters Win
## 
### 
The Stanford tennis team
ended its regular dual-match
season Monday with an 8-1
win over San Diego City
College. The win gives the
Cards a nearly perfect season
record of 19-1.
The only loss of the day
came at third doubles, long
after Stanford had clinched
the match. Craig Johnson
played at first singles, and
beat his San Diego
counterpart in straight sets.
Stanford 8, San Diego CCI
Singles: Craig Johnson (S) d.
Dennis Bond, 7-5, 6-2: Chip
Fisher (S) d. Todd Hagey, 6-0,
6-0; Perry Wright (S) d. Paul
Rubens, 6-1, 6-1; Jake Wards (S)
d. Ron Chapman, 6-3, 6-3; Tim
Noonan (S) d. Todd Harvey, 6-2,"
6-2; Alex Cortes (S) d. Jerry
Nauman, 6-3, 6-2.
