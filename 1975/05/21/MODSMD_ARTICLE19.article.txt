# BASKETBALL BANQUET
## 
### 
Retiring Stanford Basketball
Coach Howie Dallmar and
graduating All-American Rich
Kelley will be honored tonight
at a banquet sponsored by the
PALO Club, a group of local
sports enthusiasts.
To be held at Rickey's Hyatt
House, the banquet will also
honor retiring Pac-8 referee
Ernie Filiberti and Cupertino
High School's Central Coast
Section championship team.
