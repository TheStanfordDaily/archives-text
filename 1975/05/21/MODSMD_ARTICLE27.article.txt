# Bond Cancels
## 
### 
Today's scheduled campus visit and tonight's speech by
Georgia legislator Julian Bond have been cancelled, the
ASSU Speakers Bureau announced yesterday.
Instead, Southern Christian Leadership Conference
Chairman Ralph Abernathy will speak tonight at 8 in
Memorial Church. There will be no admission charge to
his speech, the third annual Kent State-Jackson State
Memorial Lecture.
Bond has been hospitalized with kidney trouble.
