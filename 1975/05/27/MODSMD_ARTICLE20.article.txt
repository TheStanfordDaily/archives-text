# Former Dean Faust Dies
## 
### 
Clarence Faust, 74, nationally known for his work with
the Ford Foundation, Stanford and the University of
Chicago, died last Tuesday of congestive heart failure.
Former dean of the college at the University of
Chicago, Faust served as director of the Stanford
University libraries in 1947-48 and as dean of humanities
and sciences from 1948-51, when he joined the Ford
Foundation as vice pres'' ' of the Fund for the
Advancement of Education.
Faust was acting president of Stanford from January
through March 1949, when Wallace Sterling assumed
office.
