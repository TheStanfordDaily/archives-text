APPLYING TO LAW SCHOOL?
The law boards may presage a great deal for your future. You can prepare for them, and we
can enhance your preparation. Our curriculum is carefully crafted to such pivotal portions
of the LSAT as math, logic,and legal reasoning. We offer classes in Palo Alto on October 4th
and sth. Call us (toll free) for information or to reserve your space. Ask for our sixteen page
brochure, without obligation. If you plan to take our course this weekend, call us now.
John Sexton's LSAT Preparation Center is supervised in California by Joe Angland, a 1975
honors graduate of the Harvard Law School. Mr. Angland, who currently works for the
California Supreme Court, captured first prize in Harvard's writing competition and was
named the outstanding oral advocate in foe moot court competition. Mr. Angland, while a
member of the New York faculty, devised the mathematics curriculum for our nation-wide
LSAT course.
