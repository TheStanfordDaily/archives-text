Might an on campus eating coop interest you?

* enjoy a convenient on-campus meeting place to relax
* like eating good food — far from Jack-in-the-Box
& the dorm food.

by the CO-OP Eating CLUB
on Registration Day, Enjoy a free meal and see if we offer
something you might enjoy. A vegetarian option is offered.
