# Art prints displayed at Fine Arts sale
## 
### 
The Fine Arts Committee is sponsoring a
special exhibition and sale of fine art prints on
the second floor lobby of Tresidder Memorial
Union. On display 9 a.m. to 5 p.m., through
Oct. 10 are the works of Chagall, Dali, Matisse,
Gaugin, Van Gogh, Breughel, Cezanne,
Frankenthaler, Homer, Klee, Miro, Monet,
Magritte, Picasso, Rembrandt, Renoir,
Toulouse-Lautrec, Wyeth, M.C. Escher and
others. More than 1200 different prints are
available for $2.50 each or 3 for $6.
