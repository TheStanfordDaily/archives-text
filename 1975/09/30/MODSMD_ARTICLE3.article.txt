# Stengel dies
## 
### 
GLENDALE, Calif. (AP)—Casey
Stengel, who won a niche in
baseball's Hall of Fame by
managing the New York Yankees to
unsurpassed triumphs and the New
York Mets to amazingly popular
losses, died late last night. He was
85.
