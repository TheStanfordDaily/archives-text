Accounting
Stanford Business School ranks
first in the nation in accounting,
based on the volume of published
research of its faculty, according to
an article in the current issue of
Accounting Review.
The new study ranks the "top 15"
accounting departments, based on the
number of articles published in
leading accounting journals.
