# Langford's kick sinks Trojans
## 
### 

—Daily photo by Mark Funk
DAYTIME DRAMA—Stanford's Mike Langford kicks the game-ending field
goal which gave the Cards their 13-10 victory over USC. Langford later said,
"I was pretty relaxed. . . I didn't feel much pressure." Stanford's win kept the
Cards in the Rose Bowl race and all but eliminated USC.
