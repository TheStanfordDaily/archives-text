# Music, Drama Depts to collaborate
## 
### 
The Stanford Departments of Music and Drama are col
laborating in a production of Mozart's COSI FAN TUTTEto he
performed in Memorial Auditorium on Stanford campus next
May.
This will offer several opportunities for students who are
interested in opera production both as singers and as techni
cians. Those who are interested in working on the production
should contact the Music Department at 497-2811 or the
Drama Department at 497-2576 for information regarding au
dition procedures and opportunities for participation.
The auditions will be held later this year.
