# Briefly...
## 
### 
Fisher dies
Memorial services will be held for
former Hoover Institution Director
Harold H. Fisher at 11 a.m. today at
Roller and Hapgood Chapel, 980 Mid
dlefield Rd., Palo Alto.
Fisher, a national authority on Rus
sian history and politics, died Satur
day at the Medical Center at the age of
85.
Fisher collected original documents
from the Czarist regime and the Bol
shevik government as Herbert
Hoover's assistant during the post
World War I famine relief operations
in Eastern Europe and Russia.
Fisher donated these documents
and some papers concerning Asia dur
ing and after World War I to the Institu
tion. He was Institution director from
1924-55.
