# Bonfire schedule
## 
### 
Big Game Bonfire chairman Bill
Christopher has announced a
schedule of activities in prepara
tion for the Bonfire. He announced
the following schedule:
Wednesday
11 a.m.-3 p.m. — wood pick-up
3 p.m.-12 p.m. —building
Thursday
12 p.m.-7 a.m. — guarding
7 a.m.-12 p.m. —building
Friday
12 p.m.-6 a.m. — guarding
6 a.m.-7 p.m. — building
7 p.m. — Big Game Rally
7:30 p.m. — lighting of the Big
Game Bonfire
Christopher said that anyone
who would like to help with either
the construction or the guarding of
the Bonfire would be welcome at
any time.
