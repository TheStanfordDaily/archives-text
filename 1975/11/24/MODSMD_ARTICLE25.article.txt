'Day of Fasting 1
Numerous activities are planned on
campus today in connection with the
proclaimed "Day of Fasting." The
films Tilt and Hunger in America will
be shown in Tresidder Union from 11
a.m. to 1 p.m. and 5 to 7 p.m.
A slideshow and discussion on
famine relief in the Sahel is scheduled
for 3 p.m. in Tresidder Room 270. A
panel discussion "the contemporary
food crisis" featuring Profs. David
Abernethy (political science), Bruce
Johnson and Tetteh Kofi (Food Re
search Institute) and CARE representa
tive Frederick Shaw is scheduled for
7:30 p.m. in Jordan 040.
An information booth will be open
in White Plaza all day. Tapes of panel
discussions from last April's Food Day
will be available from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
in the Memorial Church Round Room.
Former prof gets deanship
Former Engineering Prof. Louis
Padulo has been appointed dean of
Boston University College of En
gineering and a professor of engineer
ing and mathematics there.
While at Stanford Padulo directed
the minority program in electrical en
gineering. He received the 1971 Walter
Gores award for excellence in teach
ing.
Padulo received his M.S. degree in
electrical engineering from Stanford
in 1962 and has served as an associate
professor at Columbia and Harvard
universities.
