league. Stanford's representatives on the team, chosen by the I

Langford, defensive lineman Duncan McColl and tight end |
Ted Pappas.

Gerald Wilson and Rich Waters, made the second team while 1
running back Ron Inge and defensive lineman John Harris §
received honorable mention recognition.
