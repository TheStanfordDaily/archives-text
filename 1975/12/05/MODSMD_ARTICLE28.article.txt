# Study center hosts meeting
## 
### 
The Center for Russian and East
European Studies here will host a con
ference on "Political Development in
Eastern Europe" today and tomorrow.
Sessions are scheduled throughout
both days in Room 204 of the School of
Education's Research and Develop
ment Building.
Organized by Political Science Prof.
Jan Triska and Hoover Institution Re
seach Fellow Paul Cocks, the confer
ence will feature discussions of on
going changes in Communist East
European countries.
Triska, Cocks and several students
have been studying the subject this
quarter in a political science seminar.
The course was designed to lead up to
the conference.
Professors from universities
throughout the country will speak at
the event.
The American Council of Learned
Societies is co-sponsoring the confer
ence.
