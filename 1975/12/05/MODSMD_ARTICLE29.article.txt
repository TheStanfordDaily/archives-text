Vending machine prices
Prices for a variety of items in cam
pus vending machines will rise by five
cents Jan. 1 at the request of the Can
teen Corporation, the University has
announced. Canned drinks, cigarettes,
pastry, milk, soup and hot canned food
will be affected.
Senate meeting
Consideration of a resolution on
campus parking is scheduled at the
ASSU Senate's meeting Monday at
7:30 p.m. in Room 75 of the Business
School.
Post Office open
The campus Post Office will be open
tomorrow and next Saturday from 8:30
a.m. to 4:30 p.m. because of the
Christmas rush.
Christmas Faire
Tresidder Union's fourth annual
Christmas Faire will be held today
through Sunday at the student union.
School official to speak
San Francisco school superinten
dent Robert Alioto will speak at the
Faculty Club Wednesday at 7:30 p.m.
Reservations can be made with Delora
Wiley, 497-4891.

CORRECTION
The Committee on Services to Stu
dents (COSS) took no formal vote at its
closed meeting Tuesday to advise
President Richard Lyman to make a
statement against discrimination on
the basis of sexual preference.
Yesterday's Daily article on the
meeting incorrectly implied that
COSS had come to a formal decision.
