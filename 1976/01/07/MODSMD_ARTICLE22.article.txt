# Briefly...
## 
### 
Visiting CROW scholar
Cornell Emerita Professor Alice H.
Cook has been selected the first 1976
scholar-in-residence of the Center for
Research on Women (CROW).
Cook will spend one month at Stan-'
ford, kicking off CROW's noon lecture
series today with "Working Mothers:
European Perspectives." The talk is set
in Room 86 of the Business School.
Cook will discuss research she has
completed on the subject in nine coun
tries.
