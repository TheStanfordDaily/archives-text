FRIDAY
"Italian Sauced Fish," Whipped
Potatoes, Wax Beans, Ar
tichokes, Fruit Salad, Italian
Bread, Raspberry-Apple De
ssert, Beverage
"Italian Sauced Fish"
Serves 8
2 (16 oz) frozen fish
filets, thawed
1 (8 oz) can spaghetti
sauce, with mushrooms
2 tbsp. chopped onion
1 (4 oz) pkg shredded
mozzarella cheese
Arrange filets in single layer
on well greased baking sheet.
Sprinkle with salt. Mix spaghetti
sauce and onion; pour over
filets. Bake, uncovered, at 350
degrees until fish flakes with
fork; about 25 to 30 minutes.
Sprinkle with cheese; return to
oven til cheese melts, about 3
minutes.
SATURDAY
"Pineapple Pork Chops," Whole
Kernel Corn, Beets & Pears on
Lettuce Leaves, Hot Biscuits,
Jello Mold, Beverage
"Pineapple Pork Chops"
Serves 6
Trim fat from 6 loin pork
chops; reserve trimmings until 1

tbsp. fat accumulates; discard
trimmings. Brown chops on both
sides in hot fat; drain. Season
with salt and pepper. Drain 1 (2
ozl can pineapple chunks, re
serving 1/2 cup juice. (Mix re
served juice, 1 tbsp. brown
sugar, and pineapple chunks.)
Arrange 2 medium onions,
thickly sliced, over chops; top
with pineapple. Cover, simmer
for 50 to 60 minutes. Remove
chops; skim fat from skillet.
Blend 2 tsp. cornstarch with 1
tbsp. cold water; stir into skillet.
Cook and stir until thick. Serve
sauce over chops.
SUNDAY
"Tomato Sauced Chicken,"
Parsliced Potatoes, Steamed
Broccoli, Cole Slaw, Marinated
Fruit, Beverage
"Tomato Sauced Chicken"
Serves 6
Skin and bone three large or 6
small chicken breasts. Place in
baking dish; sprinkle with 1 tsp.
season salt and dash paprika.
Drain 1 (6 oz) can tomatoes, re
serving 1/4 cup liquid. Arrange
tomatoes in wedges, 1 medium
onion, thickly sliced and sepa
rated into rings; and 1 (4 oz) can
mushrooms, drained over chic

ken. Sprinkle 2 tbsp. snipped
parsley, 1/2 tsp. oregano and 1/2
tsp. celery seed over all. Mix re
served liquid, 1 clove garlic
(minced) and 1 bay leaf. Pour
over chicken. Cover. Bake at 350
degrees for 1 hour. Uncover and
bake 10 minutes more; remove
bay leaf. Spoon juice over chic
ken.
MONDAY
Oven Style Swiss Steak," Broc
coli Salad, Consomme, Saltine
Crackers, Apple/Grapefruit
Salad, Beverage
"Oven Style Swiss Steak"
Serves 8
2 lbs. beef round
steak, 1" thick
1 tsp. salt
I' 4 tsp. pepper
1 medium onion, sliced
1 (4 oz) can sliced
mushrooms, drained
1 (8 oz) can tomato
sauce
Trim fat from meat. Pound
meat with mallet. Sprinkle with
salt and pepper. Place in baking
dish. Top with onion and mush
rooms; pour sauce over. Cook,
covered, at 350 degrees for 1-1/2
hours. Uncover; cook 15 minutes
longer. Baste occasionally. Serve
with sauce.

Skin and bone three large or 6
small chicken breasts. Place in
baking dish; sprinkle with 1 tsp.
season salt and dash paprika.
Drain 1 (6 oz) can tomatoes, re
serving 1/4 cup liquid. Arrange
tomatoes in wedges, 1 medium
onion, thickly sliced and sepa
rated into rings; and 1 (4 oz) can
mushrooms, drained over chic

ken. Sprinkle 2 tbsp. snipped
parsley, 1/2 tsp. oregano and 1/2
tsp. celery seed over all. Mix re
served liquid, 1 clove garlic
(minced) and 1 bay leaf. Pour
over chicken. Cover. Bake at 350
degrees for 1 hour. Uncover and
bake 10 minutes more; remove
bay leaf. Spoon juice over chic
ken.

"Oven Style Swiss Steak"
Serves 8
2 lbs. beef round
steak, 1" thick
1 tsp. salt
I' 4 tsp. pepper
1 medium onion, sliced
1 (4 oz) can sliced
mushrooms, drained
1 (8 oz) can tomato
sauce
Trim fat from meat. Pound
meat with mallet. Sprinkle with
salt and pepper. Place in baking
dish. Top with onion and mush
rooms; pour sauce over. Cook,
covered, at 350 degrees for 1-1/2
hours. Uncover; cook 15 minutes
longer. Baste occasionally. Serve
with sauce.

"Italian Sauced Fish"
Serves 8
2 (16 oz) frozen fish
filets, thawed
1 (8 oz) can spaghetti
sauce, with mushrooms
2 tbsp. chopped onion
1 (4 oz) pkg shredded
mozzarella cheese
Arrange filets in single layer
on well greased baking sheet.
Sprinkle with salt. Mix spaghetti
sauce and onion; pour over
filets. Bake, uncovered, at 350
degrees until fish flakes with
fork; about 25 to 30 minutes.
Sprinkle with cheese; return to
oven til cheese melts, about 3
minutes.

"Pineapple Pork Chops"
Serves 6
Trim fat from 6 loin pork
chops; reserve trimmings until 1

tbsp. fat accumulates; discard
trimmings. Brown chops on both
sides in hot fat; drain. Season
with salt and pepper. Drain 1 (2
ozl can pineapple chunks, re
serving 1/2 cup juice. (Mix re
served juice, 1 tbsp. brown
sugar, and pineapple chunks.)
Arrange 2 medium onions,
thickly sliced, over chops; top
with pineapple. Cover, simmer
for 50 to 60 minutes. Remove
chops; skim fat from skillet.
Blend 2 tsp. cornstarch with 1
tbsp. cold water; stir into skillet.
Cook and stir until thick. Serve
sauce over chops.
