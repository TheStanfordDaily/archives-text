# YESTERDAY
## 
### 
Sixteen years ago in the Daily. . .
Jan. 27, 1960: "The new Stanford Post Office is expected to open on
Feb 11 according to Superintendent John Callahan He stated that as the
new building contains over 4000 boxes, every student will be required to
rent one.
"Students who do not have a box at present can obtain one. . . Rates
will remain at 35 cents per quarter until July 1, when they will go up to
$1.20."
Jan. 28, 1960, opinions column: "If the ASSU Legislature would
stop all this silly business of play government' and sit down to really bite
into a topic, I doubt there's anything they could't solve. But as it is. almost
every representative to the body goes to meetings to hear himself talk."
