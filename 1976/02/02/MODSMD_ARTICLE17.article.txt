"Sweet Sour Pot Roast"
Serves B to 10
2 tbl. shortening
5 lbs. round-bone pot roast
1/2 cup sliced onion
1 cup vinegar
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
Melt shortening in a heavy
kettle. Brown meat. Remove
meat. Add onions and cook 'til
transparent. Return meat to ket
tle. Add remaining ingredients.
Cover and simmer over low heat
'til meat is tender, about 3-1/2
hours.

"Mexican Veal"
Serves 6
6 veal cutlets,
cut 1/2 inch thick
1 egg, beaten
2/3 cup fine dry bread crumbs
1/4 cup shortening
1 can (1 lb.) tomatoes
1-1/2 teaspoons oregano
3 medium zucchini squash
Put meat into egg and then in
bread crumbs. Brown on both
sides in hot shortening in heavy
skillet. Pour off drippings. Add
tomatoes and seasonings. Cover
tightly and simmer 30 minutes.
Slice zucchini in about 1/2 inch
slices and add to veal. Cook 30
minutes more or'til meat is fork
tender.

"Broiled Center Ham Slice"
Serves 4
1 slice of ham, 1 to 1-1/2
inches thick
1/4 teaspoon cloves
1 can (1 lb.) cling peaches
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tbl. corstarch

1 tbl. butter
Heat broiler and grease rack
with a piece of ham fat. Gash the
fat in several places to prevent
curling during cooking. Place
meat on the rack, 2 inches from
the source of heat. Broil 20 to 25
minutes on each side. While
meat is broiling, drain peaches.
Combine cloves, sugar and
cornstarch. Add to peach juice
and cook over low heat, stirring
'til thickened. When ham is
turned, arrange peaches on
broiler. Dot with butter and broil
til slightly brown. Serve with
ham. Pass sauce separately.

"Touch of Dill Lamb Chops"
Serves 4
4 lamb chops, 1/2 inch thick
1 tbl. shortening
1 can celery soup
1/2 cup water
1 tsp. ground dill seed
Brown chops on both sides in
shortening. Add soup, water and
dill seed. Blend well and simmer
35 to 40 minutes, stirring occa
sionally, 'til chops are tender.

"Fish Cutlets"
Serves 4 to 6
1 lb. fish, cooked and flaked
1 egg. beaten
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp. salt
2 tbl. pickle relish
1 cup fine bread crumbs
1/4 cup oil
Blend together fish, egg, milk,
salt, relish and 3/4 cup bread
crumbs.Fry in hot oil 'til golden
brown. Serve with desired
sauce.

"Oven Fried Chicken"
Serves 4
1/2 cup butter
1/3 cup flour
1/3 cup yellow corn meal
1/2 tsp. salt
1 fryer chicken,
cut into serving pieces
1/3 cup evaporated milk
Melt butter in a baking pan in
375-degreeoven. Combine flour,
corn meal and salt in a bowl. Dip
chicken pieces in milk, then roll
in flour mixture. Place meat,
skin side down, in butter. Bake
30 minutes. Turn over and bake
30 minutes more or 'til tender.
