# MIXED BLESSINGS
## 
### 
Two actors, who have long
been involved in Stanford
theater, have formed their
own two-man company cal
led The New Theater. Gerald
Hiken and Paul E.Richard
open their new show, entitled
Mixed Blessings, at the Man
hattan Playhouse on West
Bayshore Road.
Opening night for this
"salad" of light comedy
sketches, poetry and short
plays is on this Friday. Shows
are Fridays and Saturdays at
8:30, and Sundays at 7:30,
through Feb. 29.
