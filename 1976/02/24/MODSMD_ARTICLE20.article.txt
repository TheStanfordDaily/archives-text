# ABT to prance
## 
### 
American Ballet Theatre, with its
company of 150, including the star
dancers, Mikhail Baryshnikov and
Natalia Makarova, offers its 1976 Bay
Area season with performances at the
War Memorial Opera House in San
Francisco, March 8-11 and March
14-18; at Flint Center in Cupertino,
March 12 and 13; and at Zellerbach
Auditorium in Berkeley, March 19-21.
Tickets for all shows are available at
the Tresidder Union Box Office.
