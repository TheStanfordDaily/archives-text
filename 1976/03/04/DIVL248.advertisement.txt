STANFORD STUDENTS
OPENINGS NOW AVAILABLE TO YOU IN THE
\R\IY ROTC2- YKAR PROCRAM
FOR ACAD KM IC
SOPIIOMORKS, JUNIORS* AM) SKMORS*
QUALIFICATIONS
1. Have 2 years remaining (includ
ing Graduate School).
2. Have a "C" average or better.
3. Be in good physical condition.
If you do well enough at camp,
you could win an ROTC scholar
ship which pays full tuition, books
and fees.
(Sfi)
ACT NOW, LIMITED SPACES
AVAILABLE. Enrollment ends
10 April 1976.
TRAINING
1. Attend a 6-Week camp this
summer (all expenses paid
plus $500.00)
2. If you decide to continue,
no obligation incurred from
camp, take ROTC your
junior year (earn $100.00
a month)
3. Attend advanced camp
next summer (all expenses
paid plus earn $500.00)
4. Take ROTC your senior
year (earn $100.00 a
month)
5. Graduate with a degree 3nd
a commission.
FOR MORE INFORMATION CONTACT: * Juniors and seniors who will
MAJOR ERIC MACKINTOSH - be attending graduate school.
University of Santa Clara If you cannot attend camp ask
VARSI HALL, Rm 110 about our compression course.
Phone 984-4281
