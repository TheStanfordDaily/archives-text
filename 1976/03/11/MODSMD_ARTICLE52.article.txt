# Van Cliburn to play
## 
### 
Van Cliburn, world-famous
classical pianist, will be in
concert at Flint Center for the
Performing Arts on Sunday,
March 21, at 2:30 p.m. Bay
Area audiences will have
only one chance this year to
see him.
Cliburn's career in the U.S.
has been distinguished by
some major musical "firsts"
— he was welcomed home
from the U.S.S.R. by Presi
dent Eisenhower with the
first ticker-tape parade ever
given for a classical musician.
