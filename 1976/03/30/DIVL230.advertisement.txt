SCULPTURE SEMINAR for FRESHMAN anri TRANSFER
STUDENTS, Spring Quarter
This is an introductory Sculpture course, available to freshman
and transfer students, taught by an advanced graduate student in
sculpture. The studio work involves an exploration of various
materials and techniques, supplemented by slide talks and dis
cussions with practicing artists. While this is a course for begin
ning sculptors, it will offer an opportunity to explore contem
porary ideas being expressed through sculpture.
Monday, 7:30-9:00 P.M., room ar6 Cummmgs Art Building.
Class lists may be signed at the first class meeting.
