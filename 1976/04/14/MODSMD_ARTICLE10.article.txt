# The 						Stanford Daily
## 
### 
The Stanford Daily is an independent student newspaper owned and published by The
Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation, a California non-profit corporation, published Monday
through Friday during the academic year, with the exceptions ol dead week, when one issue is
published and finals week when no issues are published Special sports issues are published for
-tome football and basketball games and tor certain tennis matches
Telephone Editorial (415) 497-4632, Business (415) 497-2554
Subscription rates In the United States. Canada and Mexico, $15 per year or $6 per guarter
Send check or money order in advance to The Stanford Daily Storke Student Publications
Building, Stanford. California 94305
The Daiy s editorial board is comprised of 22 editors and four at-large members elected by the
staff Editorials represent the opinion ol a maiority ol those editorial board members voting on each
topic
Material on the opinions page is edited tor brevity, clarity and style Letters, columns and
cartoons represent only the views ol their authors Nothing on the opinions page necessarily
represents a position of the Daily stall the ASSU or the University
Printed by Nowels Publications, 640 Roble Avenue, Menlo Park. Calif
Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto under the act ol March 3. 1879
