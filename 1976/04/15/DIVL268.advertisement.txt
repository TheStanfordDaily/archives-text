Political coverage
All national, state and local
political groups who wish to
publicize meetings, events
and speakers should do so
through either the OATQ col
umn or Daily advertisements.
Because of the vast number
of political events they can
not be guaranteed space in
the Daily except in the two
above-mentioned places.
Notices of political events will
not be accepted over the
phone.
