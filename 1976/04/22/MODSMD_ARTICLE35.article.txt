# Bicycle safety day arrives Wed.
## 
### 
A tricycle race in the middle of
White Plaza at high noon?
That's right. The trike race bet
ween the Administration Lackeys
and the Stanford Daily Thunder
turkeys is the highlight of "Bicy
cle Safety Day" next Wednesday
from noon to 5 p.m.
The special program, held to
raise campus consciousness
about bike safety, will feature bike
films, a vehicle inspection station
in White Plaza, displays of bike
locking and lighting systems and a
personal appearance by Sprocket
Man, from the campus comicbook.
According to Julia Molander,
one of the day's organizers, a new
10-speed bike will be rattled off.
