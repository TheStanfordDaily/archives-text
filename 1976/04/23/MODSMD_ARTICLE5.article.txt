# Draw ends today; results due in May
## 
### 
Today is the last day for under
graduates to participate in the first
round of the Spring Housing Draw,
which will determine eligibility for re
sidence in University housing next
year.
Students should bring their draw
cards from their present residences if
they live on campus, or pick up cards
from the Housing Office if they live off
campus.

In addition, students should bring
any priority cards they have obtained.
The draw window will be open from
9 a.m.-12 noon and from 1:30 p.m.-4
p.m. on the second floor of Tresidder
Union.
According to the Housing Office, re
sults from Round One, with tentative
housing assignments, will be available
May 11.
