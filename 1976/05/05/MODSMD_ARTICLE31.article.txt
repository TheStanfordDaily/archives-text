Political science majors
All undergraduates interested in
majoring in political science should
meet today in Tresidder Km. 270 at
7:30 p.m.
Political science professors will
speak on the subfields in the depart
ments and other topics.
Vietnam talk
Earl Martin, who has worked and
traveled through South Vietnam, will
speak today at 8 p.m. in Annenberg
Auditorium on "Vietnam in Transi
tion."
French poetry
James Lawler, an authority on Val

lery and modern poetry, will speak
today on "The Poetry of Rene Char" at
4:15 p.m. in Tresidder Rm. 270.
Econ lecture
Asst. Economics Prof. Michael
Block will speak on "Economics of
Crime" today at 7:30 p.m. in the Meyer
Library Forum Room.
Clarification
The May 4 Daily article on the
Inter-Fraternity Council (IFC) should
have reported that IP'C President Hob
Ottilie sent a personal letter expres
sing his own position to all fraternities
except Theta Xi. No IFC funds were
expended for the printing of the letter.
