Japan conference

Three distinguished Japanese
experts will participate in a
two-day conference on "Japan as
a Business Society." The confer
ence will be held Friday and
Saturday at Bishop Auditorium.
Featured speaker will be Peter F.
Drucker who will discuss
"Japanese Business: Past and Fu
ture," at 1:30 p.m. on Saturday.

Brower to speak

David Brower, president of
Friends of the Earth, will speak
op "Environmental Politics:
How Do We Get to the 21st Cen
tury?" tomorrow at 7:30 p.m. in
Annenberg Auditorium. The
lecture is sponsored by
SWOPSI/ARC.

Senate meeting

The ASSU Senate will meet
tonight at 7:30 p.m. inroom 75 of
the Business School.
