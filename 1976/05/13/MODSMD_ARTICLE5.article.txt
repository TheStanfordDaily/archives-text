# THURSDAY
## 
### 
The Daily publishes Monday through Friday during the academic year, with the exception
of Dead Week when one Tssue is published, and finals or vacation weeks when no issues are
published- Special sports issues are published for home football and basketball games and
certain tennis matches.
Telephone: Editorial (415) 497-4632; Business (415) 497-2554.
Subscription rates: In the United States. Canada and Mexico. Si 5 per year or $6 per quarter.
Send check or money order in advance to: The Stanford Daily. Storke Student Publications
Building. Stanford. California 94305.
The Daily's editorial board is comprised of 22 editors and four at-large members elected by
the staff. Editorials represent the opinion of a majority of those editorial board members voting
on each topic.
Material on the opinions page is edited for brevity, clarity and style. Letters, columns and
cartoons represent only the views of their authors. Nothing on the opinions page necessarily
represents a position of the Daily staff, the ASSU or the Univrsity.
Typeset by ASSU Typesetting Shop.
Printed by Nowels Publications. 640 Roble Avenue. Menlo Park. California.
Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto under the act of March 3.1879.
