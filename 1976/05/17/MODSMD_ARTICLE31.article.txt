# Six arrested over weekend
## 
### 
The Stanford Department of Pub
lic Safety is holding six young
males in connection with a series of
crimes on campus over the
weekend, Public Safety Sergeant
Stephen Lawson said yesterday.
The six were arrested near the
Stadium about 1:30 p.m. yesterday

in an allegedly stolen vehicle. The
Department of Public Safety and the
Santa Clara County Sheriff's Office
were investigating whether the six
are connected with a strong-arm
robbery and a couple of burglaries.
No charges against the six have
yet been filed.
