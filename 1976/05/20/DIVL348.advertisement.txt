WESTERN STATES ARTISTS ON THE MALL
Sixty-five gallery artists will compete for gold medals in
Stanford Shopping Center's annual art show on the mall.
They'll turn the mall into a mammoth live art gallery for
four days.
The artists will show thousands of works ot art paint
ings, watercolors, graphics,metal sculptures and pottery.
Judges will award gold medals for the best exhibit 111
painting and the best exhibit in sculpture on Saturday,
May 22 at 1:00 p.m.
Show hours will be: 10 a.m. to 0 p.m. Friday; 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Saturday; noon till 5 p.m. Sunday and 10 a.m. to 9 p.m. Monday.
