# Police investigate alleged Manzanita rape
## 
### 
The alleged rape of a non-student woman in a Man
zanita Park trailer May 15 is under investigation by the
Santa Clara County Sheriff's detective bureau. Director of
Public Safety Marvin Herrington confirmed last night.
The alleged rape was the second for the victim, accord
ing to two sources. Thefirst, which reportedly took placea
few weeks ago, was not reported to police, the sources
said.
Herrington said there are no suspects in the case, but
said the victim gave police descriptions of three young
white adult males who were allegedly involved.
Herrington refused to give more details, saying he was
attempting to protect the victim.
The same individuals allegedly committed each crime,
the sources said.
