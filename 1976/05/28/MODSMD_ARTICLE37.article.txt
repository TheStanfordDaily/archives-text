# Boston tops Phoenix
## 
### 
From the Associated Press
The Boston Celtics, spurred by
John Havlicek, beat Phoenix,
105-90. last night for their sec
ond straight victory in the Na
tional Basketball Association
championship series.
Havlicek. playing despite pain
from torn muscles in his left foot,
scored 23 points.

The Celtics, in winning their
eighth playoff game in a row at
home this year, broke it open
with a surge in the first five mi
nutes of the third period.
Ex-Celtic Paul Westphal led
the Phoenix Suns with 28 points.
Game No. 3 of the best-of
seven series will be played Sun
day in Phoenix.
