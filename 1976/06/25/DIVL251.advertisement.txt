Kepler's
is a store
for
Students
and
Scholars
There are few
bookstores where you can
find serious and scholarly
books published more than
two or three years ago -
usually you'll find bestsel
lers and current fiction and
non-fiction.
But at Kepler's we stock
a permanent inventory of
paperbacks on every subject
known to human kind. If its
in print, we try to have it.
Browse through a
Kepler's store this week
and every week.
