# Black theater
## 
### 
Don't Bother Me, I Can't Cope,
the long-running Broadway
musical and family hit across the
country, is scheduled to close
July 25 after final performances
at the Marines' Memorial Thea
ter.
The Tom Mallow production,
presented in San Francisco by
the American Conservatory
Theater, was conceived and di
rected by Vinnette Carroll with
music and lyrics by Micki Grant.
An all-black cast of 11 performs
the vigorous song and dance
numbers, 28 in all, which com
prise the popular entertainment.
Final performances will be
held today through Friday at
8:30 p.m., Saturday at 6 and 9:30
and Sunday at 3 and 7:30
through July 25. Student rush
tickets are available 15 minutes
before curtain.
