—Daily photo by Linda Buddenberg
CONGRATULATIONS—The Athletic Department events board lauds Stanford Olympic gold medal
winners Mike Bruner and John Hencken. Hencken won his second gold medal yesterday, swimming
for the U.S. men's 400-meter relay squad, which set a world record of 3:42.22. Bruner won his second
gold medal Wednesday, as part of the U.S. men's 800-meter freestyle relay squad.
