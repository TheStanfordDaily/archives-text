# OPINIONS
## 
### 
The Stanford Daly s an independent student newspaper owned and published by The Stanford
Daily Publishing Corporation a California non-profit corporation published Monday through Friday
dunng the academic year with the exceptions of dead week when one issue is published, and finals
week when no issues are published Special sports issues are published for home football and
basketball games and occasional tennis matches
During the summer quarter the Daiy is published on Tuesday and Friday
The summer subscription rate is $4 tor the quarter
Letters columns and cartoons represent only the views of their authors Nothing on the opnions
page necessarily represents a position of the Dariy staff the ASSU or the University
Printed by Noweis Publications 640 Ftoble Avenue Menlo Park Calif
Entered as second class matter at the Post Office ol Palo Alto under the act ot March 3. 1879
