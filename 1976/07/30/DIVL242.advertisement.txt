Local theater alive
Two local theater musical productions will come alive this
weekend.
The Stanford Savoyards tonight will open with Patience for
Bunthorne's Bride) at 8 p.m. in Dinkelspiel Auditorium. This
Gilbert & Sullivan operetta will also play in Dink tomorrow night
as well as August 1, 6, 7 and 8. All performances are at 8 p.m.
Theatre Works will continue its world premiere run of The
Gibson Girl, a vaudeville musical review, in the courtyard of the
Stanford Barn, part of the Stanford Shopping Center. The show
runs tonight and tomorrow night as well as August 5,6 and 7. All
are at 9:30 p.m.
