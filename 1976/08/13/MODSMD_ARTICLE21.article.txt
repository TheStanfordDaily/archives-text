# Powerhouse rock
## 
### 

Ted Nugent and Kiss will both be coming to the Bay Area this month. Ted Nugent will share the bill
with the popular Texas rockers, ZZ Top this Saturday at 8 p.m. at the Cow Palace in San Francisco.
Kiss will join Uriah Heep and the Detroit wonder-boy. Bob Seger. at the Oakland Coliseum on
Sunday, August 22 at 8:00 p.m.
