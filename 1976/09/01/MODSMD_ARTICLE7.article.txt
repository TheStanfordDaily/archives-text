# Tresidder Union
## 
### 
TheTresidder Union is in many waysthe center
of the Stanford campus. A place to eat, meet
friends and enjoy the legendary California
weather, the services at Tresidder cover almost
any student needs.
Tresidder offers a campus information service.
Located on the second floor, they can give you
information about campus events or direct you to
the geology department, should you need to know
its location. There is also a Campus Events Tape
(call 497-4311).
The Coffee House — The Coffee House features
made-to-order sandwiches, soups, cheese boards
and various coffee drinks.
Like most of the Tresidder food services, the
Coffee House tends to be overpriced. Service and
food are both good, and there is usually plenty of
room for chess or backgammon, but bring your
own set.
Hours: Monday-Friday, 11 a.m.-l a.m.; Satur
day noon - 1 a.m.; Sunday, noon-midnight.
The Transition — The Transition has a full line
fountain, daily hot entrees, burgers, soup and
salad bar, pizza and hot breakfast.
Transition food tends to be greasy and service is
generally lousy, but eating on the large front deck
can make for a pleasant meal.
Hours: Monday-Friday, 7 a.m.-l 1 p.m.; Satur
day and Sunday, 8 a.m.-l I p.m.
The Commons — Perhaps the highest quality eat
ery on campus, The Commons is a full service
cafeteria, and is located behind Encina Hall. Fea
tured are hot breakfasts, lunches, sandwiches,
salads and an ice cream fountain. Reasonable
prices, especially at lunch.
Hours: Monday-Friday, 7 a.m.-3 p.m.
Marcel's Pastry Shop — This is one of the nicest
attractions at Tresidder, featuring delicious pas
tries of all kinds. Special occasion cakes on order.
Hours: Monday-Friday, 7 a.m.-8 p.m., Saturday
and Sunday, 8 a.m.-8 p.m.
Wells Fargo Hank — Wells Fargo offers check
cashing to all students with a Stanford I.D. (no

account necessary). You can also get all other
banking services here. Friendly staff.
Hours: office, Monday-Thursday, 10 a.m.-3
p.m., Friday 10 a.m.-6 p.m., outside windows,
Monday-Friday, 8:30-4:30 p.m.
The Store — Although overpriced on many items,
the Store offers all kinds of magazines. soft
drinks, sundries and snacks.
Hours: Monday-Saturday, 8 a.m.-9 p.m., Sun
day, noon-4 p.m.
Thie Ticket Office — The ticket office has tickets
for most camous and Bay Area entertainment,
plus Ticketron service, campsite reservations and
tickets to area sporting events.
Hours: Monday-Friday, 10 a.m.-5:30 p.m.,
Saturday, noon-4 p.m.
Sequoia Travel Center — Sequoia is a complete
travel service and can make reservations with all
major airlines. You can pick up your tickets on
campus. Also offered are hotel reservations, plus
Eurail and Britrail passes.
Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:30 a.m.-5 p.m.
Stanford Barber Styling — Here you can get a cut
($4.00), trim, shampoo or styling for men and
women. The shop also carries a full line of Redkin
products.
Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:30-5:30 p.m., Satur
day, 8:30-5 p.m.
The Bowling Center has 14 bowling lanes and a
roomful of pinball machines and pooltables.
Hours: Monday-Thursday, 10 a.m.-10:30 p.m.,
Friday and Saturday, 10 a.m.-l 1:30 p.m., Sun
day, noon-8 p.m.
Also available in Tresidder are air-conditioned
meeting rooms equipped with blackboards, sc
reens and sound systems.
In addition, paintings, photography and other
works of art are continuously displayed on the
second floor, as well as in the Coffee House gal
lery. Over 400 framed art prints are available to
rent. A music reading room, with over 900 al
bums, plus newspapers, magazines and televi
sion, is located on the second floor.
