# Brazilian fest
## 
### 
Next Saturday's Brazilian Carnival will feature a samba
band and all the beer you can drink — for $2. Also at the

event, to be held at the Bechtel International Center from
9 p.m. on, will be (I. to r.) Monica Pinheiros, Jorge de
Baula and Eliane de Baula.
