Beef short ribs and cabbage with

Beef short ribs and
cabbage with herbs
3 lbs.short ribs, cut into servings
1 tbls. salt
2 tbls. fat
1/4 tsp. vinegar
1 cup water
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/8 tsp. sage ,
1 tbls. dry mustard
1 bay leaf
1/2 cup sliced onion
1/2 head cabbage, cut in quarters
Brown ribs in hot fat. Sprinkle
with salt and pepper. Add re
maining ingredients except cab
bage. Cover and simmer one to
1-1/2 hours. Add cabbage and
cook about 20 minutes, or until
cabbage is tender.

Friday
Cranberry-orange lamb chops,
steamed rice, buttered green
beans, cream# pie, beverage.
Cranberry-orange lamb chops
4 lamb chops
1 tbls. oil
1 cup cranberry juice
2 tsp. cornstarch
1 tbls. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/2 cup raisins
1 can (11 oz.) orange sections,
drained
Brown chops in hot oil on both
sides. Remove meat from pan
and add cranberry juice. Blend
in cornstarch and cook until
sauce thickens. Add sugar, salt
and chops, simmer 25 minutes.*
Stir occasionally. Add raisins
and orange sections and simmer
10 to 15 minutes, or until chops
are tender.

Saturday
Saturday Night Special, crisp
green salad, fresh fruit, bever
age.
Saturday Night Special
serves 8
1 lb. ground beef
1 can (1 lb.) pork and beans
1 can (1 lb.) tomatoes
1 tsp. salt
1 onion, sliced
bacon strips
1/4 cup brown sugar
Brown ground beef lightly in a
skillet, without adding any fat,
stirring frequently. Add pork
and beans, tomatoes and salt.
Grease a baking dish. Four half
of the mixture into dish, cover
with onion, add remaining
meat-bean mixture. Top with
bacon strips and sprinkle with
brown sugar. Bake in 350 degree
oven for one hour.
Sunday
Cornish Hen in foil, wild rice
stuffing, buttered brocolli, tos
sed salad, apple pie, beverage.
Cornish Hen in foil
(use one hen per serving)
Clean, wash and dry hen. Stuff
with Wild Rice Stuffing {recipe
below). Tie legs to tail. Brush
with melted butter, sprinkle
with salt. Wrap tightly in foil.
Place on rack in shallow pan.
Roast for 1-1/4 hours. For last 15
minutes, open foil for browning.
Wild Rice Stuffing
1/2 cup minced onion
1/4 lb. sausage meat
1 tbls. butter
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. tarragon
2 cups cooked wild rice
Saute onion and sausage in
butter for about 10 minutes. Add
salt, tarragon, wild rice; blend
well. This mixture will stuff
three hens.

Tuesday
Sweet and sour spare ribs,
boiled rice, green salad, cookies

Sweet and sour spare ribs
serves 4 to 6
3-4 lbs. spare ribs
2 tbls. flour
2 cups bouillon
4 tbls. vinegar
2 tbls. brown sugar
1 bay leaf
1/8 tsp. thyme
salt and pepper to taste
Cut ribs into serving pieces.
Brown slowly in a heavy skillet.
Remove ribs and pour off all but
two tablespoons of drippings.
Stir flour into drippings, add
bouillion and cook until thick.
Add ribs and remaining ingre
dients. Cover tightly and simmer
45 minutes.

dill pickle slices
lemon wedges

poons bacon fat for one minute.
