Contributions
The Daily welcomes opinions on topics of interest to the
Stanford community. Materials submitted for publication
must be double-spaced and typed on a 62-space line. We
reserve the right to edit for brevity, clarity and style. Mail
contributions to Opinions Editor, The Stanford Daily, Storke
Publications Bldg., Stanford, CA 94305. Persons wishing to
become regular columnists should submit three sample col
umns to the Daily on or before Oct. 18,1976 for selection.
