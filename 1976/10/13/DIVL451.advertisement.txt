Contributions
The Daily welcomes opinions
on topics of interest to the Stan
ford community. Materials sub
mitted for publication must be
double-spaced and typed on a
62-space line. We reserve the
right to edit for brevity, clarity
and style. Mail contributions to
Opinions Editor, The Stanford
Daily, Storke Publications Bldg.,
Stanford, CA 94305. Persons
wishing to become regular col
umnists should submit three
sample celumns to the Daily on
or before Oct. 4, 1976 for selec
tion.
