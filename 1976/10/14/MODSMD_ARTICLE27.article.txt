### 
Civil liberties talk tonight
Congressman Don Edwards of San Jose, running for
re-election, will speak on "Civil Liberties and the United
States Congress" tonight at 7:30 in CubberleyAuditorium.
Edwards is presently Chairman of the Judiciary's sub
committee on Civil and Constitutional Rights. The sub
committee has considered issues such as whether the
Kennedy assasination investigation should bereopened.
Edwards, a graduate of Stanford Law School, has served
in congress for 14 years. He was an early opponent of the
Vietnam War and served as floor manager for the Equal
Rights Amendment several years ago.
