Contributions
The Daily welcomes opinions
on topics of interest to the Stan
ford community. Materials sub
mitted for publication must be
double-spaced and typed on a 62-
space line. We reserve the right to
edit for brevity, clarity and style.
Mail contributions to Opinions
Editor, The Stanford Daily, Storke
Publications Bldg., Stanford, CA
94305.
The Daily also welcomes edito
rial cartoons. Anyone wishing to
become a guest or regular cartoonist
should contact the opinions editors
and bring in samples of their work.
