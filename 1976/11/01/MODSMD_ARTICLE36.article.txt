# Accident
## 
### 
Cathy Shimizu, a member of the Stanford band, was
struck by an automobile while enroute to a band re
hearsal late Friday afternoon.
According to eyewitnesses, she was northbound on
the bike path bordering Galvez St. when she turned to
cross the street at Memorial Way and was hit by a
northbound car.
Bertram D. Wolfe, professor emeritus of Russian

History, and senior research fellow at the Hoover Insti
tute, was driving the vehicle.
The 79-year-old professor said, "All of a sudden the
bike shot in front of me, I hit the brakes and tried to
swerve."
Shimizu is listed in fair condition at the medical
center.
