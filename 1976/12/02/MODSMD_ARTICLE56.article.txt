SECOND FLOOR SHOPS — Bring your head or
body or both of them to the shops on The Barn's
second floor. Before the turn of the century the
second floor was the fermentation and aging level —

but it is just the opposite now. You can get your hair
cut or a new style at Stanford Barn Barber Shop or
Custom Coiffures. You can have your dress altered
or a new one at Sandy's — formerly Trudie. Now,

women can also receive consultation on their image
or achieve a new one at Images by Suzie.
Take elevator or the handsome new staircase up
through the center of the building to the remodeled
area.
