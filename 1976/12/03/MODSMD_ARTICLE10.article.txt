# Contributions
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes opinions on topics of interest to the Stanford
community. Materials submitted for publication must be double-spaced
and typed on a 62-spaceline. We reservethe right to edit for brevity, clarity
and style. Mail contributions to Opinions Kditor, The Stanford Daily,
StorkePublications Bldg., Stanford, C.A 94305.
Letters,which are more likely to be published than columns, should noi
exceed 35 lines. Columns should not exceed 96 lines.
All contributions must be signed by one or more individuals; anonym
ous organizational contributions will not be published. Individuals may
however, submit contributions "on behalf of" organizations, if they are
willing to have their name appear with the contribution.
