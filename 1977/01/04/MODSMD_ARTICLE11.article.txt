The Stanford Daily is an independent student newspaper owned and pubtshed by The Stanford Daily Publishing
Corporation, a California non-profit corporation Main office Storke Student Publications Bulking, Stanford, CA
94306. Published Monday through Fnday dunng the academic year, with the exceptions of dead week, when one
issue 19 published, and finals week, when no issues are published Special sports issues are published lor home
football games, and for certain home basketball games and tennis matches Published Tuesdays and Fridays dunng
the eight-week summer quarter
Telephone Editorial (415) 497-4632: Business (415) 497-2554
Subacnption rates in the United States. Canada and Mexico. $18 per year or $7 per quarter Send check or money
order in advance to The Stanford Daily Storke Student Publications Building. Stanford. CA 94306
The Daily's editonal board is composed of five editors and four at-large members elected by the staff Editonals
represent the opinion of a majority of those editorial board members voting on each tope
Matenal on the opinions page is edited for brevity, cianty and style Letters, columns and cartoons represent only
the views of their authors Nothing on the opinions page necessanly represents a position of the entire Daily staff or of
