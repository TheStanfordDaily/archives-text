TIAA-CREF meetings
Two representatives of the Teachers Insurance Annuity
Association-College Retirement Equities Fund (TIAA
CREF) retirement annuity organization will hold open
meetings today to answer any questions faculty and staff
might have regarding the program. The meetings will be
held at 2 p.m. and 4 p.m. in Bishop Auditorium, 2 p.m. at
the Stanford Linear Accelerator and at 4 p.m. in Fairchild
Auditorium.
