Tickets for this weekend's
sports events are now on sale at
the Athletic Department. Friday
night at 7:30, the Cards take on
five-time NCAA volleyball
champion UCLA. Tickets for the

$1 for students.

swimmers take on four-time de
fending NCAA champs USC.

Montreal Olympics, John Naber,
winner of four gold medals, will
compete along with four other
Trojan Olympians.
Tickets for the meet are $1 for
adults and 50 cents for students.
