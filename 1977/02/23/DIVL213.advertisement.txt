SUMMER JOBS AT
GENERAL MOTORS
Sophomores and co-terminal juniors in chemical,
electrical, industrial and mechanical engineering
are invited to apply for the General Motors
internship-scholarship program. The program
provides summer employment in 1977 and 1978
and a two year tuition scholarship. For application
forms see Nancy Hatcher, Room 271, Engineering
Corner.
