# Spring Quarter Advance Registration
## 
### 
Currently registered students can pick
up Advance Registration Permits to Regis
ter (PTR's) from their departments or ad
visors Friday, February 25, through Wed
nesday, March 9, and can advance register
in the lobby of the Old Union from Mon
day, February 28, through Thursday,
March 10. PTR's are available for Terminal
Graduate Registration (TGR) students.
Advanced Graduate, and Postdoctoral
Scholars in their major departments. At
tendance Permit and nonmatriculated stu
dents who have been approved to register
Spring Quarter and Postdoctoral Scholars
with no major departments can pick up
their PTR's at the Information Windows,
lobby of the Old Union. Barbara Bolden
(Special Registration Assistant), room 144,
Old Union Lobby, should be contacted if
students (after checking with their depart
ments/advisors) are unable to locate their
Advance Registration PTR's.
The Advance Registration procedure
and the schedule for signing class lists are
on page 2 of the Spring Time Schedule,

Advance Registration Cashier/Student Accounting Hours: 8:15-12 noon & 1-4 p.m

available in the lobby of the Old Union.
Class list signing will be done entirely by
alphabetical grouping and individual excep
tions to the schedule cannot be made. A
senior or graduate degree candidate, how
ever, who is scheduled to sign class lists
toward the end of Advance Registration
and anticipates extreme difficulty in get
ting into a particular class may petition to
sign a list during the beginning of Advance
Registration. Petitions are available at the
Information Windows in the lobby of the
Old Union; they should explain the neces
sity of signing up earlier than scheduled, be
signed by the student's advisor, and re
turned to the Information Window as soon
as possible. Action will be taken on these
petitions at the end of each day.
Students who pick up Advance Registra
tion PTR's but do not advance register
should get new regular PTR's on registra
tion day, March 28, 1977.
Students who intend to register for
Summer Quarter 1977 should indicate this
on their Spring PTR.
