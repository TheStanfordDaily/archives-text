# Contributions

The Daily welcomes opinions
on topics of interest to the Stan
ford community. Materials sub
mitted for publication must be
double-spaced and typed on a
62-space line. Letters, which
should not exceed 35 lines, are
more likely to be published than
columns, which should not ex
ceed 70 lines. We reserve the
right to edit for brevity, clarity
and style. Mail contributions to
Opinions Kditor, The Stanford
Doily, Storke Publications Bldg.,
Stanford, CIA 94305.
