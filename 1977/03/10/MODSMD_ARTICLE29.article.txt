# Women's track
## 
### 
Women interested in train
ing to compete in track and
field or cross country are en
couraged to sign the class list
for the basic track class taught
by Coach Marshall Clark. The
class is offered at 11 a.m. and
2:15 p.m. on Tuesdays and
Thursdays. Clark asks that
those whocan,sign upfor the
11 a.m. class.
Any women interested in
taking the class but having
questions may call Clark at
the track office at 497-4527.
