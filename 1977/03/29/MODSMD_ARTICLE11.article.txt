# COP candidates
## 
### 
The Daily will publish campaign statements by can
didates for the Council of Presidents. Each slate will be
allowed one statement signed by all members of the
ticket; the deadline for statements has been extended
to Monday, April 4 at 5 p.m. The statements may be no
longer than 60 lines.
Should there be a run-off, the two slates will be al
lowed one final statement of 35 lines, due Thursday,
April 14 by 10 p.m. All statements should be typed
double spaced on a 62-space line.
The Daily will not print any opinions material concern
ing the election on election days April 13 and 14 and
April 20 and 21.
