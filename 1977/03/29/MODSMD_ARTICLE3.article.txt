# Suspect held in rape case
## 
### 
Eric Orme, 30, of East Palo Alto,
has been arrested and is detained in
Palo Alto's North County jail on
suspicion of rape and assault with a
deadly weapon. He is held in con
nection with the reported rape of a
26-year-old student here on March
18.
According to a North County jail
spokesman, Orme was arrested by a
Santa Clara County Sheriff's De
puty at 4:45 p.m. yesterday at the
Palo Alto Police Department and is
being held on $3000 bail. As of late
last night he was still in custody.
Orme was turned over to the
Santa Clara County Sheriff's Office
after comments he made to Palo
Alto police indicated he might have
been involved in the reported rape.
