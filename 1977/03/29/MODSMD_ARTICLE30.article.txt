Kaplan's class on the air
Prof. John Kaplan's class "The Criminal Law and
the Criminal System" will he recorded and aired
every Monday, Wednesday and Friday at 5:10 p.m.
on KZSU, 90.1 FM. Each lecture, normally given
MWF at 1:15 p.m. will be broadcast in its entirety
that same day.
KZSU staff meeting
Tonight in Hishop Auditorium, KZSU will hold a
mandatory staff meeting for all returning staffers.
The meeting will begin at 7:30 p.m. and application
forms for all departments must be filled out at that
time. Refreshments will be provided.
Daily staff lunch
There will be a staff lunch at noon for all Daily
staffers. Reporters and photographers who want to
be paid for their work last quarter should show up.
