# Hopping down the bunny trail
## 
### 
Palo Alto Mayor Stanley Norton presents awards to
the contest winners in the Sixth Annual Easter Egg
Contest sponsored by the Stanford Shopping Center
Merchants' Association. This year Denise Mcintosh,

9 (center), took the "Best of Show" award and Anne
Verheyden, 11 (left), won an award for the "Most
Unusual" egg. Both are students at Covington
School in Los Altos.
