# Robert Egan
## 
### 
Born and raised in Washington, D.C., Robert Egan
attended Boston College as an undergraduate, where he
completed a double-major in political economics and
literature. As a graduate, he studied /ihilosophy and
literature at Oxford University in England. At this time
he also traveled extensively throughout Europe.
Egan came to Stanford in 1974, where he is currently a
Ph.D. student in the Drama Department in directingand
literary criticism. He is writing on three young political
playwright in Britain for his dissertation. He has directed
a total of 17 plays, musicals and operas, among them
Bluebeard and The Dumb Waiter, at Stanford. He has
also written three plays. Egan is now 26 years old.
