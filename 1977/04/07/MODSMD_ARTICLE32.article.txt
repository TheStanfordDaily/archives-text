Bern denied tenure
Asst. Psychology Prof. Sandra Bern confirmed last
night that she had been denied tenure.
She said that the Psychology Department had
unanimously voted to recommend tenure but that
School of Humanities and Sciences Dean Halsey
Royden had denied it.
Bern added that she intends to appeal the decision
"within the week," but that she had no further com
ment.
Royden could not be reached for comment.
Investment forum
An open forum on University investment in stocks
of companies which operate in South Africa will be
held tonight at 7:30 in Cubberley Auditorium. The
forum is sponsored by the Committee on Investment
Responsibility.
