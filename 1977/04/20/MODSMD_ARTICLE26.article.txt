# Women's basketball
## 
### 
Stanford has awarded its first
women's basketball scholarship
to Kathy Murphy, a standout
forward from Pittsburgh, Pa.,
coach Dotty McCrea announced
yesterday.
Murphy, 5-IOV2, is a product
of Mt. Lebanon H.S. in
Pittsburgh, where she averaged
22.0 points, 16.0 rebounds and

3.5 assists per game last season.
Murphy was a letterwinner in
volleyball, track and softball, in
addition to basketball.
In McCrea's first year as coach
of the Stanford women's team,
the Cardinals posted an 8-11 re
cord, including the champion
ship of the Stanislaus State
Tournament.
