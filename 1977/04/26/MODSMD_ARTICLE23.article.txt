# Grappelli at Music Hall
## 
### 
Stephane Grappelli, legen
dary French jazz violinist, will
be playing at The Groat Ameri
can Music Hall in San Francisco
tomorrow, Thursday and Friday
evenings.
Grappelli first broke into the
jazz scene in the early 1930s
playing with guitarist Django
Reinhardt, the greatest of his era,
in the Quintet of the Hot Club of
France.
It was not until 1976 that
Grappelli made his first ex
tended tour of the United States.
There will be two shows each
evening at 8:30 and 11.
