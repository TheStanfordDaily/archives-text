BACKPACKING
Saturday, April 30
T*naya Canyon tn Yosemite. Two days.
This trip will involve both backcountry hik
ing and cßmbing. From Tenaya Lake to
Yosemite Valley via Tenaya Creek,
Pywiak Cascade and Mirror Lake; one of
the most impressive and pristine areas in
Yosemite Park. The current drought
should make it possible to follow the creek

falls (dry) will be involved. Contact Bob
Fielding, 387-3945/
Saturday, May 7
Point Reyes. A weekend exploring three
different natural environments of Point
Reyes: Beach and Esteros, Chaparral,
and Evergreen Forests. Stay at Laguna
Ranch Hostel ($4), so all that is needed is
a sleeping bag and daypaek. Bring
cameras: wikJftowers will be out. Contact
David Friedman, Wilderness Exchange,
S.F., 666-2078/
