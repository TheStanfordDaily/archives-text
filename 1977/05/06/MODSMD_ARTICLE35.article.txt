# Briefly...
## 
### 
Labor film
The Stanford Committee for Justice
at J.P. Stevens will sponsor a screening
of the film Testimony, tomorrow at
p.m. in Cubberley. Testimony was de
veloped by the Amalgamated Clothing
and Textile Worker's Union (ACTWI!)
to show what it considered unfair
labor practices of Stevens, a textile
company.
Marguerite loop ends
The Marguerite loop which runs be
tween Campus Shell and Jordan Quad
via Tresidder will end service today.
The loop will be evaluated later for its
effectiveness.
Black premed conference
The Black Premedical Organization
will sponsor the Third San Francisco
Bay Area Premedical Conference to
morrow in Kresge Auditorium. The
theme of the open conference will be
"Beyond the Doctor's Office."
