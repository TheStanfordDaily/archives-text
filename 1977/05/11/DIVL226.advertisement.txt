The San Francisco Brown Bag Opera
comes to Stanford Shopping Center.
This Thursday, Stanford Shopping Center and Citizens Savings & Loan
are proud to bring the San Francisco Brown Bag Opera to the Peninsula for
the first in a series of free, noontime performances.
Don't worry if you forget your brown bag lunch. Our restaurants and delis
are selling sandwiches, hot dogs, and espresso coffee at the performance.
Come Thursday to the Pavilion near Macy's. and add a little Puccini to
your usual pastrami and cheese.
