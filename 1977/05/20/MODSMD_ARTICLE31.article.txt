# Briefly...
## 
### 
Salant speech
Richard Salant, president of CBS news, will be
speaking tonight at 8 p.m. in Kresge Auditorium in
the Law School.
Hawaii on campus
Bechtel International Center will host the Stanford
Luau Hui's third annual feast tomorrow. Eight
course dinner of traditional dishes followed by a 90
minute Polynesian review. Dinner begins at 6:30
p.m. Tickets available at Bechtel: $5.50 presale, $6 at
the door. Call 321-0130 for information.
Correction
The film being shown as part of the Lagunita Film
Series was incorrectly listed in Spectrograph as
being shown on Saturday at 10 p.m. It will be shown
Tuesday, May 31 at the same time.
