NEW MEMBERS OF
PHI BETA KAPPA
The following students were elected May 24, 1 ( J77 to membership in the
Stanford Chapter of Phi Beta Kappa. In selecting its members, the Society
seeks to uphold the traditional values of a liberal education. The basis for
selection is evidence of high distinction in an academic program that
exhibits breadth and substance and in which each of the disciplinary
areas—humanities, sciences, and social sciences—is well represented.
9 1/2% of the Senior Class and 2 1/2% ot the Junior Class were elected. No
more than 12% of each graduating class can be elected to membership in the
Chapter.
