# To be killed?
## 
### 
The Interior Department is considering killing golden eagles,
a rare and protected bird of the American west that some
times preys on livestock, according to Harvey Willoughby,
regional director of the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service in De
nver. U.S. Fish and Wildlife spokesmen emphasize that no
plan has been adopted to kill any birds of prey.
