In ancient Japan the SHO
GUN was the highest ranking
of all samurai (warrior) lords.
Now here on the Peninsula is a
restaurant that will rank high
on your list of excellent dining
places.
Experience the flavor and
tradition of old Japan in eleg
ant Japanese garden surround
ings at the SHOGUN RE
STAURANT in Los Altos. Our
samurai chefs will skillfully at
tack and render the finest cuts
of prime steak, seafood and
chicken into an exquisite meal.
You'll flip for us because our
chefs will literally flip for you
at our specially designed tep
pan tables.
Or, if you prefer, dine in our
Japanese booths and experi
ence Kabuto-yaki, or authentic
samurai "helmet" cooking.
Either way you'll enjoy a
novel experience...
