# Art Notes

### 
Symphony swings

An All-Beethoven Ex
travaganza opens the San Fran
cisco's Symphony Orchestra's
summer season at Concord
Pavilion, Friday August 12.
Edo de Waart conducts "Over
ture to Coriolanus," Symphony
No. 9 (choral). The instrumental,
vocal and choral spectacular in
cludes soloists soprano Sarah
Beutty, mezzo-soprano Nina
Hinson, tenor Neil Shicoff and
bass Douglas Lawrence.
Concert time is 8 p.m. at the
pavilion, 2000 kirker Pass Road
in the Diablo foothills. Tickets
available at the symphony box
office (431-5400).
