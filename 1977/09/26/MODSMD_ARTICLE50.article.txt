# Music auditions
## 
### 
University musical groups,
under the coordination of the
Music Department, will be
mounting an extensive pro
duction program this fall.
Among the works scheduled
for performance are a baroque
opera, "La Dafne" and
Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart's
"Requiem." According to Mar
cia Tanner, Music Department
Publicity Director, there are
still many unfilled positions in
the groups that will be creating
the performances. She says
that any person "looking for an
opportunity to play, sing, or
both" should come to the audi
tions scheduled for Monday
and Tuesday, September 26
and 27.
Tanner also said that the
Stanford Studio Band needs
trumpeters, trombonists, bass
trombonists, saxophonists,
flautists, clarinetists, drum
mers, percussionists, electric
and double bassists, pianists,
guitarists, and vocalists of all
genders.
