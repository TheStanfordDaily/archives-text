IM sports
An organizational meeting
for all IM managers and house
or team representatives kicks
off this quarter's intramural
schedule today at 4 p.m. in
room 350 at Encina Gym.
All-University IM activities
(open to men and women) set
for fall quarter are bowling,
cross country, free throw
competition, golf, pocket bil
liards, tennis, football, two
person volleyball, wrestling,
table tennis and three-on
three basketball.
Women's sports include
cross country, football, golf,
free throw competition, ten

nis, two-person volleyball and
bowling.
Two clinics for football offi
cials, to which every all-
University and women's team
must send a representative, are
scheduled for tomorrow and
Friday at 4 p.m. in room 350 at
Encina Gym.
Football and volleyball sig
nups for both all-University
and women's IMs will be held
tomorrow and Friday from 1 to
5 p.m. in room 350 at Encina
Gym.
Signups for all other ac
tivities (except bowling sig
nups at Tresidder Union) are
posted in the Encina Gym hall
way adjacent to the IM office,
room 352. For further informa
tion stop by the IM office or call
the office at 497-1080.
Basketball
Tryouts for the men's varsity
basketball team will be held on
Oct. 15 from 1 to 3 p.m. in
Maples Pavilion. Anyone in
terested in managing the team
should contact John Iwanski at
328-9629 or 327-6545.

with women's basketball coach
Dotty McCrea on Friday at 3:30
p.m. in Maples Pavilion for all
women interested in trying out
for this year's team. Persons in
terested in managing the wo
men's team are also asked to
attend the meeting.
