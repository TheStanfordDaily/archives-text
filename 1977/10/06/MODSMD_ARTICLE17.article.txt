Thursday, Oct. 6
The Dingoes. Free noon concert in White
Plaza sponsored by ASSU Special
Events. The Dingoes are an Australian
band managed by Peter Rudge (The
Who, The Rolling Stones), who are on a
promotional tour of American college
campuses. Better catch them now. You'll
have to pay to see them the next time
around.

The Stanford Jazz Band. Tresidder Un
ion. Noon. Free.

(second floor). $2.50 general, $2 student,

Los Payas. The internationally-known
Bolivian group will give a UNICEF benefit
concert at 8 p.m. in Kresge Auditorium.
$3.
