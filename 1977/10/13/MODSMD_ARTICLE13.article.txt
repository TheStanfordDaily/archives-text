The Oddand MuMUtn. 1000 Oak St.,
Oakland, 273-3401. A major retrospective
exhibition of the work of renowned
California artist Richard Diebenkorn will
be on view in the Great Hall oeginning
Oct. 15 with the inclusion of over 150
works of abstract and figurative painting
and drawings done from 1943-1976. In
the Art Special Gallery is a retrospective
survey of the recent paintings of Mel
Ramos. Beginning Oct 18, the Museum is
sponsoring a Natural Sciences Open
House, giving visitors the opportunity to
visit the exhibit preparation shop,
aquarium support area and take part in
special theme tours. In the History Special
Gallery will be exhibitions of "American
Patent Models 1836 to 1880" and "Touch
ing the Earth," lithographs and sculpture
by the famed Mexican artist Francisco
Zuniga. Museum hours are Tuesdays-
Thursdays, 10 a.m.-5 p.m., Fridays, 10
a.m.-10 p.m., Saturdays 10 a.m.-5 p.m.,
and Sundays. 10 a.m.-6 p.m.
