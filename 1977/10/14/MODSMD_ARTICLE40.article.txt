Academic Organization Board
There will be a meeting of the Academic
Organization Board today at 4 p.m. in Tresid
der 132.
Committee application deadline
Today at 4 p.m. is the deadline for all applica
tions for committee positions for students.
Positions on the Subcommittee on Minority
Affairs and student representative to the Fa
culty Senate are still open.
Peace Corps recruiting
The Peace Corps will be recruiting Monday,
Oct. 17 through Friday, Oct. 21 in White Plaza.
Interviews will be held on Monday and Tues
day at the Career Planning and Placement
Center.
Black Pre-Law Society
The first meeting of the year of the Black

Pre-Law Society will be held Sunday at 7 p.m. in
Ujamaa large lounge.
Overseas Studies
The ASSU Task Forte on the Place of Over
seas Studies at Stanford is soliciting written
comments on the quality of overseas studies
programs by students who have attended the
overseas centers. The Task Force will eventu
ally make recommendations to the Provost and
Academic Senate. Comments should be
turned into the Task Force at the ASSU office,
second floor of Tresidder.
Benefit dance
There will be a Disco Dance tonight from
9p.m. to 1 a.m. in the Roble Dining Hall, pre
sented by the Southern Atrica Study Action
Croup to benefit the Zimbabwe Relief Fund.
There will be free door prizes and a raffle for an
lan Smith dart board.
