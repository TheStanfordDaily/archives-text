Hal Michelson graduated Phi Beta
Kappa from Stanford in 1971. In addition
to his work with the Band he served on
the ASSU Council of Presidents, in the
ASSU Senate and in Legislature of the As
sociated Students of Stanford University
(LASSU), a now defunct relative of the
Senate. He is also a graduate of Harvard
Law School and now works as a lawyer in
San Francisco.
