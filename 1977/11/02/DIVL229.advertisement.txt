Mark Triska Knows How!
Meet Mark Triska, our new Stanford Community representative for
Northern California Savings. Mark has a question for you:
"How much interest does your checking account earn?"
Mark s answer: "Probably NONE, unless you know about Telephone
Transfer.' With Telephone Transfer you can keep those dollars
earning 5V4% annual interest, compounded-daily, in a Northern
California Savings account. When you need them, give us a call and
your money is transferred back to your checking account that very
day ... no matter where your bank might be. No minimum balance.
No charges or fees. Give Mark a call. He'll be glad to meet with
you and make all the arrangements for Telephone Transfer.
His interest is helping you.
