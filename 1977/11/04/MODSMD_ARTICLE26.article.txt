Bomb threat
Shortly after 9:30 last night, the
Daily received an anonymous tele
phone message stating that a bomb
had been placed in Meyer Library.
The Stanford Department of Public
Safety was informed and the library
was evacuated.
After a 45 minute wait and a visual
search by police officers, students
were allowed to pick up their belong
ings. The library was then closed for
the evening.
