t Directed by Hiroshi Teshigahara !

Man, woman, sand and sun... these are the basic elements ot
this fine adaptation of Kobo Abe's best selling Kafkaesque
novel. A man and a woman held captive in a sand pit provide the
perfect visual metaphor for director Hiroshi Teshigahara's
examination of the fundamentals of existance, rebellion, 1
adaptation and freedom. Outstanding photography and sound
creat a self contained world of particularities. The almost
visceral experience of sand on flesh becomes an entire
universe.
