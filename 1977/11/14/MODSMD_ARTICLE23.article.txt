# The 						Race for the Roses
## 
### 
Three Pacific-8 teams are still in the running
for a berth in the Rose Bowl on Jan. 2. The follow
ing explains how each of the teams can make the
trip to Pasadena:
1. UCLA will represent the Pac-8 if it defeats
USC on Nov. 25.
2. Washington will go to the Rose Bowl for the
first time since 1964 if it defeats Washington
State this weekend and USC beats UCLA on
Nov. 25.
3. Stanford will face the winner of the Ohio
State-Michigan game if it defeats California in
the Big Came, Washington State upsets
Washington this weekend and USC beats
UCLA on Nov. 25.
