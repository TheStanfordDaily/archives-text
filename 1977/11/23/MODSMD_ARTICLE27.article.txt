### 
Thanksgiving Dinner
Persons left without a place to go for the tradi
tional Thanksgiving Day Feast may attend a dinner
sponsored by St. Mark's Episcopal Church. Wine
will be served at 3 p.m. followed by dinner at 4
p.m. The church is located at 600 Colorado Ave. in
Palo Alto (near the intersection of Middlefield).
Phone 326-3800 for reservations.
