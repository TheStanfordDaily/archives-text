A true k delivering milk to Theta Xi yesterday morning slid down the embankment
in front of the house after it had gone up on the curb to avoid cars parked in the
drive. A tow trut k was summoned to pull the truck out of its precarious position.

--Daily photo by Lex Passans
At first, the tow truck succeeded onlv in moving the milk truck into a second
equally precarious position . The truck was finally freed from its perch on the
embankment.
