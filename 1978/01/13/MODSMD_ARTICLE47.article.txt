##

Cal grants due
The California Student Aid Com
mission reminds students planning
to apply for a Cal Grant that the
postmark deadline for mailing an ap
plication is Feb. 1.
Study lists
Study lists or alterations to ad
vance registration confirmations are
due at the Credentials Window at
Old Union by 5 p.m. today.
Send a can
Californians Against Waste will
participate in a national program cal
led "Mail a Can to Carter," designed
to encourage President )immy Carter
to support national legislation which
would require a deposit on beer and
soft drink cans and bottles.
Persons interested in participating
should bring their own can for
mailing to the CAW booth in White
Plaza today from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.
The group will provide labels to use
in mailing. Can donors must provide
postage.
Bloodmobile
The Red Cross Bloodmobile will be
accepting volunteer donations of
blood today at Florence Moore Hall
from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m.
Alien registration
Under the Immigration and Na
tionality Act, each alien in the United
States, except diplomats and persons
accredited to certain international
organizations, must report his ad
dress to the Attorney General during
January.
Reporting may be accomplished
by filling out a card available at the
Post Office. Aliens must report by
Jan. 31.
