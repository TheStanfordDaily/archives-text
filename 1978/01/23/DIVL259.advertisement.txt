# Adv. 5 Page 3
## 
### 
I
The Stanford Daily
announces its
Third Annual
Photography
Contest
Entries for the contest will be accepted in three categories: people, social comment
and scenic. Each contestant may enter up to three prints, mounted or unmounted, black
and white only. The contest is open to all Stanford students, faculty and staff. All entries
must be in the Daily offices by 6 p.m., Friday, February 3 and should have the entrant's
name, address, telephone and photo title (if any) clearly marked on the back. Prizes and
display information will be announced. All entries will be returned following judging.
