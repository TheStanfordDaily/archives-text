# De Bona watercolors on exhibit
## 
### 
Watercolor renderings of
original American quilts and
Indian blankets is being
displayed through January
in the Tresidder art gallery
on the second floor.
The artist is Eve De Bona
of Palo Alto, whose show is
titled "In Praise of American
Quilters and Weavers."
De Bona, who has copied
existing quilts and blankets,
chose her own colors and
proportions, and varied the
traditional designs for opti
cal effect.
De Bona has shown in
Northern and Southern
California art galleries and
museums, designed sets for
the Cliff Keuter dance com
pany in New York, and
taught at the Palo Alto Cul
tural Center.
