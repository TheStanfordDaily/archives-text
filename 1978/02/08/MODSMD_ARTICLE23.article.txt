Correction
The primary election for the 12th
Congressional district will be this
June, not in Novemberas reported in
yesterday's Ronald Davis article on
page one. Rep. Paul McCloskey (R-
Menlo Park) and Davis, Democrat,
will not be running against each
other in the primary, but for their
respective party's nomination.
