# Results of Library User Survey – Graduate Students
## 
### 
During the Spring Quarter (1977) the Librai ies conducted a
survey of graduate students to determine their opinions on
library services here at Stanford. The purpose of this survey
was to evaluate the effectiveness of library collections and
services in supporting the academic program. This graduate
student survey was conducted as a follow-up to the undergraduate
student survey done during the Spring Quarter of 1975.
The results may interest many members of the Stanford
Cormunity, particularly those of you who took the time to
fill out the questionnaire. You can read below the bulk of
the survey's statistical returns. Copies of the unabridged
survey results, containing additional statistical information,
a ccpy of the questionnaire, and the answers to essay-type
questions can be found on the Stanford shelves In Meyer
Library and Green Library Reference Room.
