# CIR forum schedule changed
## 
### 
The Commission on Invest
ment Responsibility's public
forum scheduled for March 8
has been moved ahead two
days to next Monday according
to Tim Warner, secretary to the
commission. The forum will be
in Terman Auditorium from
7:30 to 10 p.m.
"Reservations may be made
but are not required. I would
encourage it if they can be
made though," Warner said
yesterday. They can be made
by calling Warner at 497-4154.
