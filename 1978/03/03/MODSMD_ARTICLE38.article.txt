# Briefly...

Begle dies
Prof. Edward Begle, founder of the
"new math" which captured the at
tention of educators in the 19605,
died early yesterday at the Medical
Center.
Begle, professor of mathematics
education in the School of Educa
tion, was 63. He had been in declin
ing health from emphysema and
complications. A memorial service
will be held tomorrow at 4 p.m. in

Memorial Church.
Black symposium
A Black Liberation Month sym
posium on "Where Do We Co From
Here?" is scheduled for tomorrow
from 1 to 5:30 p.m. in Tresidder 132.
Speakers include James Turner,
lames Boggs and Ed Boston. These
three will also be at a roundtable dis
cussion Sunday at 2 p.m. in Kresge
Auditorium.
