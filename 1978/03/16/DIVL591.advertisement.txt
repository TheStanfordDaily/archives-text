Contributions I
The Daily welcomes letters, columns, and editorial cartoons from its '
readers. All submitted materials must be typed, double-spaced, on a ,

not exceed 80 lines. All submitted materials are subject to editing. The '

Student Publications Building, Stanford, CA 94305. Contributions may i
also be delivered to the Daily offices.
