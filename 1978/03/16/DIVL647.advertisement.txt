HIGH PROFIT SUMMER OPPORTUNITY
WITH THE SOUTHWESTERN CO.,
NASHVILLE, TN.
We are interviewing tomorrow for our high profit full
summer marketing program To qualify, you need no
previous experience or capital to invest. You do need
to be independent, hard-working, and willing to work
away from home (out-of-state) for the entire summer.
Interviews are Friday, March 17, at 4pm and 7pm
in the main Seminar Room of Lagunita Court.
Don't dress up, but come early, Meetings will last two
hours.
