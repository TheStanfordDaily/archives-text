The Lively Arts will be bringing three groups to campus in the next week. The New
York Chamber Soloists (top I.) will perform Bach's six Brandenburg Concerti in
Memorial Auditorium starting at 8 p.m. this Friday. On Sunday at 2:30 p.m. in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium the Beaux Arts Trio (top r.) will appear. The Trio includes
Menahem Pressler, piano; Isadore Cohen, violin; and Bernard Greenhouse, cello.
