M.D. pancake benefit
There will be a pancake breakfast
benefit for the Muscular Dystrophy
dance-a-thon Sunday from 10 a.m. to
1 p.m. at the Sigma Alpha Epsilon
house. The event is being sponsored
by the SAEs and the Kappa Kappa
Gamma sorority and there is a $2 do
nation. Tickets will be available at the
door. For more information, call
Kevin Wilson or any SAE member at
327-9917.
SWOPSI time changes
Meeting times to discuss job re
sponsibilities for the two half-time
student co-director openings for
SWOPSI and SCIRE have been
changed. There will be a general in
formational meeting tomorrow at
noon. Additional SCIRE meetings are
set for next Monday at 10 a.m. and 4
p.m. while SWOPSI has meetings
scheduled for Monday at 11 a.m. and
Tuesday at 2 p.m.
The new co-directors will have a
pay increase and for the first time
since the positions were established,
will be allowed by the University to
enroll as half-time students.
New staff meeting
There will be a meeting for new
Daily staffers in the editorial offices
today at 1:30. No experience is
necessary.
