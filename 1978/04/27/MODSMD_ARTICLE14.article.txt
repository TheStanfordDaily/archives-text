Editorial cartoons such as these were common in newspapers across
the country during the past three months as coal miners in Ap
palachia were out on strike. According to Paul Allen, who covered
the coal mine strikes for KPFA radio station in Berkeley, the issues at
stake were the right to strike and the health care plan. Although

cartoonist Jeff Mac Nelly portrayed the United Mine Workers with
control in their hands, Allen told students here last week that the
mine workers were successful only in that they were able to rally
support from other unions and prove that they could stay out on
strike for 109 days.
