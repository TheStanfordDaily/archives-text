# ASSU election runoffs today
## 
### 
The runoff elections for ASSU Council of Presidents (COP)
and Senior Class President slates end today. All registered stu
dents are eligible to vote in the COP election while only those
who have completed 130 units at the end of Spring quarter may
vote for Senior Class President slates.
The COP race is between the Goldenkranz/Petty/Estes/
Morimoto and Drysdale/Evered/Felt/Bush slates.
The slates of Breck/Butler/Crozier/Peet and Wachtel/
Broussard/Payne/Tepe are facing off in the Senior Class Presi
dents race.
Polling places for both today and tomorrow are:
Graduate School of Business 11 a.m. to 2 p.m.
Physics Tank 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.
Post office 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
French and Italian Corner 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Corner of Campus and Serra 7:30 a.m. to 9:30 a.m.
