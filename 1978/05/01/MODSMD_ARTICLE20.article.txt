Prof gets award
Electrical Engineering Prof.
Stephen Harris will be awarded the
1978 David Sarnoff gold medal and
$1000, the Institute of Electrical and
Electronic Engineers (lEEE) has an
nounced in New York City.
Harris was selected "for scientific
discoveries and device inventions in
the fields of lasers, quantum elec
tronics and nonlinear optics." The
award, named after the late radio
pioneer and chairman of the RCA
Corp., will be presented during the
Device Research Conference in
Santa Barbara June 26 to 28.
