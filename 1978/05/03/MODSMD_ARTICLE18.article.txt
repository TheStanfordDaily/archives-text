# Calendar for Sun Week
## 
### 
Today
Stanford — Solar displays, demonstrations, conservation
ideas, tours, White Plaza, 11 a.m. to 2 p.m.
— Dave Bagshaw of Pacific Sun will give a slide
show describing solar projects in the Bay Area,
Whitman Lounge, 5 p.m.
Stanford Shopping Center — Solar displays, demonstrations,
information. 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.
San Jose State — Don Aitken, director of Center for Solar
Energy Applications, SJSU, speaking on "Project
Heliose," Student Union Ballroom, 12:30 p.m.
— Aitken, speaking on "Solar Energy 1978: Emerging
Perspectives," JCI4I (Journalism Building), 7:30 p.m.
— Solar collector, cooker in Art Quad opposite Student
Union; photo essay on Ist floor of Student Union;
Solar-heated dorm tours — inquireatStudent Union.
Palo Alto — Sunrise service, Nairobi Community Gardens at
bay end of University Avenue, 6:30 a.m.
— Dedication of solar house — Technology Appropriate for
People (TAP) Root House at Garden and Pulgas, East
Palo Alto, noon. Open daily from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.
San Francisco — Sunrise ceremony at Twin Peaks, 6 a.m.,
followed by walk through Golden Gate Park and sun
set bonfire at Sutro Baths beach.
— Solar fest with exhibits, slide shows, entertainment, 7
a.m. to 7 p.m. at Civic Center Plaza.
— Solar art, heating systems at Exploratorium, 1 to 5 p.m.
and 7 to 9:30 p.m. Open daily 1 to 5 p.m.
— Solar displays at Stonestown Shopping Center Mall, all
day.
Berkeley — Rally for solar energy development, Sproul
Plaza, UC-Berkeley, noon, with Jane Fonda and
Country Joe McDonald.
Oakland — Construction of solar greenhouse at Oakland
City Hall, starting at 8 a.m.
(To be continued)
