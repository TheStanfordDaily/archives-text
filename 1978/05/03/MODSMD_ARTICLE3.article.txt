# Off-it day
## 
### 
In conjunction with the Sun Day
activities planned for Stanford
and the rest of the Bay Area, the
University will be observing an
"off-it hour" starting at noon to
day. Non-essential electrical
appliances should be left off dur
ing this time. Outdoor lunches
and classes are encouraged to
conserve electricity.
An off-it hour last spring re
sulted in a 14 percent reduction in
electrical consumption for that
day.
