# Sun Day
## 
### 
Friday, May 5

San Francisco — Lunch with guest speaker, S.F. Ecology
Ctr., 12:30 p.m.
— Sunliner: 11: 30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. at Irving at 25th Ave.,
Petrini's parking lot; 2 to 3 p.m. at Community Educa
tion Center, 3151 Ortega; 3:30 to 4:30 p.m. at Traval
and 20th Ave.; 5 to 7 p.m. at Soul Park, Fillmore at
Ellis.
Saturday, May 6
Palo Alto — "What to wear under the sun" fashion show,
Stanford Shopping Center pavilion, 1:30 p.m.
— Twenty mile "Run for Energy and Health" begins at 7
a.m. from Nairobi Community Gardens.
Stanford — Don Aitken from San lose State speaking on
"Solar Energy 1978: Emerging Perspectives," Cubber
ley Auditorium, 7:30 p.m.
San Francisco — Energy Conference with Amory Lovins and
State Energy Commissioner Ronald Doctor, John
Adams Community College, 1860 Hayes near
Masonic. Call 239-3016.
— Sunliner: 8:30 to 10:30 a.m. at Leland St., Visitacion
Valley; 11 a.m. to noon at John Adams Community
College; 12:30 to 2 p.m. at Japan Ctr., Buchanan Mall
at Post St.; 2:30 t04:30 p.m. at Dolores Park, 18th St.,
Dolores.
Berkeley — Solar Multi-media Theater, Unitarian Fellow
ship Hall, Cedar and Bonita, 8 p.m. Also Sunday.
Sunday, May 7
Palo Alto — Tours of houses, apartments and pools heated
by solar equipment from 1 to 6 p.m. Lists of 10 sites
are available at White Plaza display, Aitken presenta
tion or Stanford Shopping Center display.
Stanford Shopping Center — displays and demonstrations
open from noon to 5 p.m.
