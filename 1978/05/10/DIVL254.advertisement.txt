Fee assessments election today
The special fee assessment election is today and tomorrow for
the eight organizations that failed to obtain the 20 per cent voter
turn out needed to validate in the last election three weeks ago.
Approximately 2400 people must vote in this week's election for
the assessments to have a chance of passage.
The organizations on the ballot are SCOPE, Legal Counseling
Office, Speakers Bureau, Program Fee, Academic Organizations
Board, Graduate Student Council, Guest Professorship Board
and Publications Board.
Polling places for both today and tomorrow are:
Post office 9 a.m. to 3 p.m.
French and Italian Corner 9 a.m. to 3 p.m.
Meyer Library facing Stern 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Terman Engineering Building facing Daily 8:30 a.m. to 2 p.m.
DeGeurre pools entrance noon to 3 p.m.
