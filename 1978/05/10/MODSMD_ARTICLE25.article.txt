Poetry reading
The Creative Writing Center is pre
senting a poetry reading session to
night at 8 p.m. in the Meyer Forum
Room. Prof. Donald Davie will be the
Master of Ceremonies.
Danforth fellowships
Four graduate students and two
undergraduates have been awarded
Danforth Graduate Fellowships for
1978.
Danilo Begonia, Christa Hansen,
William Jones and James Kloppen
bergarethe graduate award winners.
Jon Streeter and Alfredo Jimenez are
the undergraduate winners.
The Danforth Foundation annually
awards fellowships to approximately
100 students from nearly 2000 applic
ants.
Bike-a-thon benefit
A Bike-a-thon benefit for the
Peninsula Center for the Blind and
Visually Impaired in Palo Alto will be
held Saturday, beginning at 10:30
a.m.
Sponsor sheets are available
through recreation departments, at
libraries, police departments and fire
stations, and in many stores in Menlo
Park and Palo Alto. For further infor
mation call 326-0482 or 324-1311.
