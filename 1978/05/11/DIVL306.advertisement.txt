Fee assessments election today
The special fee assessment election ends today for the eight
organizations that failed to obtain the 20 per cent voter turn out
needed to validate in the last election three weeks ago. Approx
imately 2400 people must vote in this week's election for the
assessments to have a chance of passage.
The organizations on the ballot are SCOPE, Legal Counseling
Office, Speakers Bureau, Program Fee, Academic Organizations
Board, Graduate Student Council, Guest Professorship Board
and Publications Board.
Polling places are:
Post office 9 a.m. to 3 p.m.
French and Italian Corner 9 a.m. to 3 p.m.
Meyer Library facing Stern 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Terman Engineering Building facing Daily 8:30 a.m. to 2 p.m.
DeGuerre pools entrance noon to 3 p.m.
(Business School courtyard 11 a.m. to 1 p.m.
Corner of Escondido Road and Campus Drive7:3oa.m. to 9 a.m.
