Bay Area
Sen Francisco Museum of Modem Art.
"Margery Mann: Photographs." 70 works
dating from 1954 until the artist's death in
1977. "Photographs from the Permanent
Collection." A historical survey consisting
of 275 photographs. "Aesthetics of
Grafitti." A multi-media exhibition com
posed of photo documentation of
"natural" grafitti found in the streets,
coupled with work by artists who are di
rectly influenced by grafitti. "Ellen Land-
Weber " An exhibition of photographic
and 3M color work. "Jennifer Bartlett:
Rhapsody . " A large scale grid of 988 cold
rolled steel plates one foot square, each
colorfully painted. Opening May 14:
"Chere Lai Mah: Working at the
Museum." A constantly changing
installation/exhibition produced within the
gallery. Van Ness and McAllister Streets,
S.F. 863-8800. Tuesday thru Friday, 10
a.m. to 10 p.m., Saturday thru Sunday. 10
a.m. to 5 p.m.
