a summer job.
Talk to Manpower.

opportunities for temporary
workers. In factories, ware

and outdoors.
Work as much as you
want. Or as little. It's up
to you.
There's a Manpower office
almost anywhere you're
spending the summer. Stop
in and we'll plan a job
schedule for you.
