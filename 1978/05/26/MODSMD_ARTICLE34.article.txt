# NEW MEMBERS OF PHI BETA KAPPA
## 
### 
The following students were elected May 24, 1978 to membership in the Stanford
Chapter of Phi Beta Kappa. In selecting its members the Society seeks to uphold the
traditional values of a liberal education. The basis for selection is evidence of high
distinction in a coherent academic program that exhibits breadth and substance and
in which each of the disciplinary areas -- humanities, sciences, and social sciences -
is well represented.
