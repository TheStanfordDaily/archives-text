Class List Sign-Up
Autumn Quarter 1978-79
There will not be Advance Registration in
Spring Quarter for Autumn Quarter 1978-
79. Instead there will be a Class List Sign-
Up for current students to sign class lists for
those courses requiring it. Courses will be
listed on Autumn Program Cards that will
be available at the "Autumn Program
Cards" window in the lobby of the Old
Union beginning May 30. Signing of Class
Lists will begin May 30 according to the
schedule in the Time Schedule (available at
the Information window in the lobby of the
Old Union.)
Each student should bring his or her ID
to get a Program Card. No advisors'
signatures will be required on the Autumn
Program Cards and only courses that
require signing class lists should be shown.
Students who do not need to sign class lists
should not fill out and turn in Autumn
Program Cards. The last day to pick up
Program Cards is June 6, but Class Lists
can be signed through June 7. The cards
should be turned in at the Class List
windows by June 7 and will be returned to
students at Autumn Quarter Registration.
