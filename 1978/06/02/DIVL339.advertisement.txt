Special OATQ Deadline
for Dead Week
Once Around the Quad announcements for the
Dead Week issue of the Daily must be submitted to
the Daily office by 1 p.m. Sunday, June 4. No excep
tions to this special deadline can be made. The Daily
publishes announcements for events which are free
and open to the public at no charge in each regular
issue of the Daily. Announcements may be submitted
at the Daily office across Lomita Mall from Terman
Engineering Center. Announcements cannot be ac
cepted over the telephone. The Dead Week issue will
be published Wednesday morning, June 7, and it will
be the last regular Daily of the quarter.
