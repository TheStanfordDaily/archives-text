# Bay Area
## Art Events 
### 
Campus
Stanford University Museum of Art.
An exhibition of works by the contem
porary German sculptor Joachim
Berthold. The gallery is located next to
the Cummings Art Building, and its
hours are Tuesday through Friday, 10
a.m. to 4:45 p.m., and Saturday and
Sunday, 1 to 4:45 p.m. Also, negative
platinum prints by Joan Myers. Both
exhibits run through August 20.
Coffee House GaNery. Black and
white photographs by Lei-Sanne Doo.
Through August 9.
Bay Area
San Francisco Museum of Modern
Art. "Europe in the '70s: Aspects of
Recent Art," a major exhibition of en
tirely avant-garde European art. Van
Ness at McAllister, S.F. Tuesday,
Wednesday and Friday, 10 a.m. to 6
p.m.; Thursdays, 10 a.m. to 10 p.m.;
Saturday and Sunday, 10 a.m. to 5
p.m. General admission $1.25;
seniors and juniors, $.75.
First Annual Bay Area Women's
Graphic Art Competition. At the
Frame Company, 354 Village Lane,
Los Gatos. 35^-2331. Tuesday
through Saturday, 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Young Galery. An exhibition of art
posters by such artists as Calder,
Rauschenberg, Steinberg, Christo,
Chagall and Hundertwasser, among
others. 100 Park Center Plaza, Suite
117 Mall, San Jose. Gallery hours are
Monday through Friday, 10 a.m. to 5
p.m., weekends and evenings by ap
pointment. (408) 295-2800. Runs
through August 11.
Montalvo Center for the Arts. Etch
ings, paintings, photos and sculpture
by Collette Meltzer, John Fronczak,
Dennis Gagarin and John Schelegle,
respectively. Montalvo Galleries,
Saratoga. Hours are 1 to 4 p.m. Tues
day through Sunday, with no charge
on weekdays but $.25 on weekends.
867-3421. Runs through July 30.
Works Galery. Contemporary Art:
Exhibitions/Events, presents an exhib
ition of wood sculpture by San Jose
artist Sam Hernandez. 248 Auzerais
Ave., San Jose. Gallery hours are
noon to 4 p.m. Tuesday through
Saturday. Call 293-4561,292-4622, or
295-2646 to verify. Exhibit closes July
29.
