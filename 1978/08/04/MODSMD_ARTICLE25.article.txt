# Briefly
## 
### 
Blood donors
Nurses and aides from the
American Red Cross-Stanford
University Blood Center will set
up shop for donors on Wednes
day, Aug. 9, in Rm. 282, second
floor, Tresidder Union.
Hours are from 11 a.m. to 2
p.m.
Donors will supply blood to
patients in 29 hospitals in an area
which includes Santa Clara,
Santa Cruz, San Benito and
Monterey counties.
This area requires the collec
tion of 375 pints of blood per
day, officials said, and as always,
the need is great.
