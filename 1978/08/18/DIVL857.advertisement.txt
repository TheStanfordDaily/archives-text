I KIRK'S
361 California Avenue
"Ask the person who's had one 99
t
We've served Stanford students for more than 25
years — specializing in steakburgers and
frankfurters. All our sandwiches are cooked to
your order over live charcoal. They are served
broiling hot and a large condiment bar allows
you to finish them off yourself to taste.
The Menu includes:
Charcoal broiled 1/3 lb. steakburgers,
cheeseburgers or pineappleburger
Charcoal broiled 1/4 lb. frankfurters
Salads — Shakes — Softdrinks
