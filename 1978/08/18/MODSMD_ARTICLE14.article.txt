# Bay Area
## Art Area Events 
### 
Campus
Stanford University Museum of Art.
An exhibit of works by the contempor
ary German sculptor Joachim Berth
old. The gallery is located next to the
Cummings Art Building, and its hours
are Tuesdays through Fridays, 10
a.m. to 4:45 p.m., and Saturdays and
Sundays from 1 to 4:45 p.m. Also,
negative platinum prints by Joan
Myers. Both shows run through Sun
day, August 20.
