# Adv. 11 Page 4
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes opinions, letters and cartoons on topics of
interest to the Stanford community. Materials submitted should
be double-spaced and typed on a 57-character line. We reserve
the right to edit for brevity, clarity and style.
Persons wishing to become regular bi-weekly columnists
should submit one sample column by Friday, October 6. Bring or
send contributions to The Stanford Daily, Storke Publications
Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
