Karel deLeeuw services
Memorial services for the late Pro
fessor of Mathematics Karel de-
Leeuw will be held today at 4 p.m. in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Water shut-off
Water will be shut off in all of the
Meyer Undergraduate library build
ing from 8 a.m. Monday to 9 a.m.
Tuesday so that water lines can be
connected at the new Green library.
Suicide on campus
A 27-year-old man was found dead
yesterday afternoon on the Stanford
campus, the victim of an apparent
suicide.

Santa Clara County Sheriff's de
puties found the man in a garage at
824 Esplanada at 4:45 p.m. He was
seated in an automobile that had a
vacuum cleaner hose running from
the car's exhaust pipe to a rear win
dow.
Deputies reported the man was an
unemployed musician. The Santa
Clara County Coroner is withholding
his name and address until next of
kin can be notified.
Committee applications
The application deadline for stu
dent positions on University commit
tees has been extended from 4 p.m.
today to 4 p.m. on Monday. Applica
tions must be submitted to the ASSU
Office.
