# Once Around the Quad Policy
## 
### 
Once Around the Quad
announcements are
printed free of charge as a
service to the community.
Announcements for any
free, public event will be
published. Announce
ments must be submitted
in person no later than 1
p.m. two business days
before publication at the
Daily office, Storke Publi
cations Building, Santa
Teresa and Lomita across
from the Terman Engineer
ing Center. Announce
ments will not be accepted
over the telephone.
