# Briefly
## 
### 
Gregorio reception
State Sen. Arlen Gregorio will be
honored at a reception at the Stan
ford Faculty Club on Sunday from 4
to 6 p.m. Wine, cheese and music
will be featured. The event is open to
the public.
Red Cross Bloodmobile
The Red Cross Bloodmobile will be
at the Graduate School of Business
today from 8:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m.
and will be at Escondido Village to
morrow from 9 a.m. to 12:30 p.m.
McCloskey gathering
Congressman Pete McCloskey
(R-Menlo Park) will be on campus
Sunday from 1 to 3 p.m. for an infor
mal question-and-answer session in
the grove behind Bowman Alumni
House. The gathering is sponsored
by the Stanford Bay Area Juniors.
