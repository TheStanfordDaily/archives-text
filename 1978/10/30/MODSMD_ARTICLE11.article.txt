or of the Leland Stanford junior University.
. The DaHy's editorial board is composed of six editc sand three at-large members

regrets that it can not guarantee the return of any article submitted. All submitted articles
are subject to editing.
Subscription rates: in the United States, Canada, and Mexico, $21 per year or $B per
quarter. Send check or money order In advance to: The Slanford Daily, Storke Student
