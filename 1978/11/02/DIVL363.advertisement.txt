Some people would like a
DUMMY*
for
CHIEF JUSTICE
of the California Supreme Court
(' "ventrioquial puppet" is the technical term)
Several special interest groups have organized to try to
remove Rose Bird as Chief Justice. These groups oppose
her confirmation on frivolous and sometimes racist or
sexist grounds. Beware of these detractors. Follow this
issue and you will realize that Rose Bird is an excellent,
farsighted Chief Justice who is attempting to make needed
changes in the State Judicial System.
Paid tor and authorized by: "Rosebird Hasballs," Bob Baker, chair,
Carol S. Lamoureux, treasurer c 1978, Bob Baker
