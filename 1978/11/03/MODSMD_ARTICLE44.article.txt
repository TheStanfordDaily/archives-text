# Grad student dies
## 
### 
David Richardson, 29, graduate student in
drama and humanties here, died of cancer
Thursday, Oct. 26, at the University Hospital.
He had been ill for some time.
Survivors include his wife, Cathy Clark
Richardson, and his parents Mr. and Mrs.
Thomas Richardson, of Durham, N.C.
Richardson received his B.A. in English at
Stanford in 1972 and his M.A. at the University
of North Carolina in 1973.
A memorial service and mass already have
been held, with burial at a family plot in De
nver, Colorado. The family prefers memorial
contributions in his name be made to the Uni
versity.
