# Big Game rally
## 
### 
The Band will make its annual march through
the streets of San Francisco tonight beginning
about 10 p.m. from the Mark Hopkins hotel at
Taylor and California streets. The Band will go
down Taylor to Pacific, left on Pacific to Leaven
worth, right on Leavenworth to Filbert, left on
Filbert to Hyde, and then all the way down Hyde to
Victorian Park. The Band and Dollies will perform
at the park, which is located across from Ghirar
delli Square.
