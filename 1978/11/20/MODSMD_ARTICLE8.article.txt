# Food Week starts today
## 
### 
Stanford Food Week begins
today with a keynote address,
ecumenical service and begin
ning of a one-day fast.
Frances Moore Lappe, au
thor of Food First and Diet for a
Small Planet, will speak tonight
at 8:30 p.m. in Memorial Au
ditorium on "Aid for Self-
Reliance."
Students participating in the
fast must turn in their meal
cards at dinner today. The Uni
versity Food Service will give
the rebates from each card to
the Committee on Hunger,
said Steve Hansch, a commit
tee member.
Other Food Week activities
include a noon rally tomorrow,
ecumenical services tonight
and tomorrow night, and a
"break fast" dinner at Colum
bae House tomorrow.
