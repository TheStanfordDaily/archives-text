# CPA award
## 
### 
William Beaver, CPA, Ph.D., has
won the American Institute of Cer
tified Public Accountants' Journal of
Accountancy literary award. Beaver
is the Thomas D. Dee II Professor of
Accounting at the Graduate School
of Business here.
Beaver received the award of $500
and a scroll for his article in the

January 1978 issue, "Current Trends
in Corporate Disclosure."
