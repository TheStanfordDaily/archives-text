# Book fair
## 
### 
A holiday book fair will be held at
Bing Nursery School, 850 Escondido
Road, Dec. 7-9. The fair will feature a
wide selection of new hardcover
books, records and tapes for pre
school children. Proceeds will go to
the school's scholarship fund The
fair will be open to the public 10 a.m.
to 2 p.m. Thursday and Friday, and 10
a.m. to 3 p.m. Saturday.
