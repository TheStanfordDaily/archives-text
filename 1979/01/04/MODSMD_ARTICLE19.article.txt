# ASSU Senate meeting
## 
### 
Rep. Pete McCloskey (R-Menlo
Park) will speak during the first half
of the ASSU Senate meeting tonight
in an open constituent discussion.
During the second half of the meet
ing the senate will turn to regular
business which includes;
— discussing mascot selection
guidelines;
— establishment of an ad hoc
housing committee;
— discussing possible divestiture

of student funds from the Universi
ty's merged pool B.
The meeting, to be held in CSB 75,
will start at 7:30 p.m.
