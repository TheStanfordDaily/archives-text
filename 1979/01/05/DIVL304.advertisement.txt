San Francisco Bay Area Computer and Computerized
Telephone Equipment Manufacturer seeks talented
individuals interested in
• Telephony
• Microprocessor Applications
• Digital and Analog Design
• CPU and Memory Design
• Operating Systems Design
• Compiler Design
• System Programming Languages
ROLM Corporation, founded in 1969 by four
Stanford graduates, has grown 50-100% each
year and currently has 1600 employees. ROLM's
Telecommunications Division is the leading
independent supplier of computerized PBX's
(CBX) and microprocessor controlled telephones
ROLM's Mil Spec Computer Division offers a com
plete line of rugged general purpose mini-computers
Included in ROLM's outstanding benefits package
is a three month paid sabbatical after six years
(and every seven years thereafter) Company paid
tuition and time off for Graduate Study at Stanford
and flexible working hours
