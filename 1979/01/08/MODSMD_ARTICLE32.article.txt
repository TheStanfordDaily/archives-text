Peace Corps recruiters
Recruiters from Vista and the
Peace Corps will be at Tresidder
today through Thursday, Jan. 8
through 11, from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m.
each day. Advance appointments for
interviews may be made through the
Career Planning and Placement
Center, 497-3963.
Approaching Stanford
Approaching Stanford is looking
for scintillating quotes on anything
pertaining to the Stanford experi
ence. Please turn in your contribu
tions to Kathy Thoelecke at the
Academic Information Center on the
third floor of Old Union by Friday.
Little Eleven delegates
Monday at 5 p.m. is the final dead

line for applications to be a student
delegate to the Little Eleven confer
ence, a student symposium on un
dergraduate education in which the
University of Chicago, the Ivy League
schools and Stanford will be par
ticipating. Applications for the con
ference which will be held in
Philadelphia in February can be
picked up in the ASSU offices on the
second floor of Tresidder Union.
Viennese Ball instruction
Dancing instruction for the Sec
ond annual Viennese Ball will begin
tonight from 7:30 to 9:30 p.m. in
Stern Dining Hall with the Viennese
waltz. Dress shoes are highly re
commended. Instructors should ar
rive no later than 7 p.m. to practice.
