### 
Flicks cancellation
Last night's scheduled showing of
The Last Waltz at the ASSU Sunday
Flicks was cancelled because of a
faulty print, according to Jay Roach,
Flicks manager. He said the Flicks re
grets any inconvenience and will re
fund 65 cents to season pass holders
in the ASSU office today after 1 p.m.
The ASSU plans to reschedule the
film.
