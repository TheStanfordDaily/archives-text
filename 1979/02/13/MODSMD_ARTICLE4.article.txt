# Cards drop game 69-47 to WSU
## 
### 
The Stanford Cardinals drop
ped their third straight basketball
game last night to Washington
State University (WSU), 69-47, in
Pullman, Washington.
The Cards are now 4-9 in
Pacific-10 Conference action and
10-12 overall. Don Collins of WSU
led all scorers with 24 points,
while Stanford's Wolfe Perry
scored 12 points. Turnovers were
decisive to the outcome as the
Cards committed 25 miscues
while the Cougars committed
only 10.
A full game report will appear in
tomorrow's Daily.
