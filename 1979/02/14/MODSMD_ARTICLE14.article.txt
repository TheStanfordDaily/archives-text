The Stanford Daily is an independent student newspaper owned and published by The
Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation. Main office: Storke Student Publications Build
ing, Stanford, CA 94105.
Telephone Editorial (415) 497-4612; Business (415) 497-2554.
Nothing on the opinions page necessarily represents a position of the entire EJaily staff
or of the I eland Stanford lunior University.
The Daily s editorial board is composed of si* editors and three at-large members
elected by the staff. Editorials represent the opinion of a majority of those editorial board
members voting on each topic.
Letters, columns, and cartoons represent only the views of their authors. The Daily
regrets that it can not guarantee the return of anyartir le submitted All submitted articles
are subject to editing
Subscription rates in the United States, Canada, and Mexico, $21 per year or SB per
quarter Send check or money order in advance to: The Stanford Daily, Storke Student
Publications Building, Stanford, CA 94105.
Published Monday through fridav during the academic year, with the exceptions of
dead week, when one issue is published, and finals week, when no issues are published.
Special sports issues are published for home football games, and for certain home
basketball and tennis malches. Published Tuesdays and Fridays during the eight-week
