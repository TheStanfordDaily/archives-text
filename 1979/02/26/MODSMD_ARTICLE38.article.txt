# Briefly
## 
### 
Eclipse
A total eclipse of the sun will occur
this morning beginning at 8:13. The
175-mile moon shadow will take
about IV2 hours to fall across North
America.

Bloodmobile
A Red Cross bloodmobile will be at
Lagunita Court tomorrow from 1 to 4
p.m. and at Serra in Stern Hall March
2 from noon to 3 p.m.
