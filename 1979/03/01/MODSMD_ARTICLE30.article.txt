Senate meeting
The ASSU Senate will meet tonight
at the Graduate School of Business,
Rm. 75 at 7:30 p.m. Visitors are wel
come. Topping the agenda are the
following items:
—discussion of Post Office deliv
ery to dormitories;
—discussion of a University mascot;
—report from the Commission for
Investment Responsibility (CIR);
—-discussion of election procedures
for the spring ASSU election;
—report from the ASSU task force
that visited the Adolph Coors Co. last
week.
Garcia to speak
Newly-elected Republican State
Senator Marz Garcia will speak at
4:10 p.m. Friday in the American
Studies lounge. A question-answer
period will follow Garcia's brief talk.
Faculty and students are welcome.
