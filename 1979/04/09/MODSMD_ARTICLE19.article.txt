# Children's Hospital donation
## 
### 
Alpha Chi Omega recently presented a check for $1000 to Dr.
Norman Lewiston, chief of the allergy-pulmonary service at
Children's Hospital. Pictured (I. to r.) are sorority member
Kelly Brodie, 11 -year-old asthma patient Gary Stanford, Lewis
ton and Tally Cameron, a representative of the alumnae group
of the sorority.
