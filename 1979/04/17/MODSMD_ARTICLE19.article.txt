# Walton to speak here
## 
### 
Professional basketball star Bill Walton will discuss
"Sports in America" here Monday, April 23 in the premiere
of a new campus lecture series sponsored by the Depart
ment of Athletics.
Walton's talk will begin at 7:30 p.m. in the Law School's
Kresge Auditorium. An extended question-and-answer
period will follow his 40-minute address.
Tickets, priced at $3 general admission and $1 for Stan
ford students, are now on sale at the Athletic Department.
They will also be on sale at the door.
