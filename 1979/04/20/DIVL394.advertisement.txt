UNDERGRADUATE HOUSING
Spring Housing Draw
The housing draw is now in progress and those stu
dents who wish ro apply for autumn quarter housing
are encouroged to participate.
Information and draw cards are avaiable at the resi
dence hall offices and at the Single Student Housing
Office.
The final date for entering the Draw is Friday, April
20. Students must have their draw cards when they
go to the draw window which is located in Tresidder
Union, room 270.
The draw window closes at 4:00 p.m., Friday, April
20.
