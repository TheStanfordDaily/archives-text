Senior runoff today
A runoff election between the BASH and Natural Choice senior class
slates is being held today and tomorrow. All students who will have 130
quarter units by next fall are eligible to vote.
Ballots from five of the six overseas campuses came in yesterday,
according to Chris Noonan, assistant elections commissioner. As of 4
p.m. yesterday, Florence's votes still had not been reported, he said.
The BASH slate of Gray Boyce, Alan Alpert, Sandra Shanker and Dave
Hood held a narrow lead over the Natural Choice slate of Mike Kavouk
jian, Connie Ordway, Jeff Merrill and Janet Rappaport, Noonan said.
Noonan said ballots for the runoff election will be rpceived at the
overseas campuses today and results should be cabled back by Satur
day, facilitating a quick tallying of the senior class election.
