# Teller hospitalized
## 
### 
Dr. Edward Teller, Hoover Re
search Fellow here, was reported in
"good" condition yesterday at
Cedars-Sinai Hospital in Los Angeles,
where he was admitted Wednesday
after complaining of an "upset."
Teller, known as the father of the
hydrogen bomb, used the word "up

set" to describe his condition when
he was admitted to the Hospital,
