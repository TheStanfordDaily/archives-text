Last Day to Add Courses or Units
and Declare Pass/No Credit
Wednesday, May 16, is the last day to add
courses or units for Spring Quarter. You should
_ turn in your petition at the Registrar's In-
formation Window —lobby of the Old Union —
with the instructor's signature.
Wednesday is also the last day to arrange with
your instructor to take a course on a Pass/No
Credit basis.
