# SPORTS
## IM track set for weekend 
### Kevin MacGillivray 
Coming up this weekend is the IM track and
field meet, at Stanford Stadium. This meet will
be held on Friday and Saturday, May 18 and 19.
In each event, the All-University division will be
followed by the women's division.
The finals for all the field events and the
two-mile run will be held on Friday. The running
events will be staged in heats, with the finals on
Saturday. If heatsare not required, thefinalswill
be run on Saturday. All competitors must sign
up 30 minutes before each scheduled event. The
schedule for the weekend looks like this:
Friday, May 18
Estimated
Field Events Time
High Jump Final 2:30 p.m.
Shot Put 12 lb. (Women 8 lb.) Final 2:30 p.m.
Shot Put 16 lb. Final 2:30 p.m.
Long Jump Final 2:30 p.m.
Triple Jump Final 2:30 p.m.
Running Events
2 miles Final 3:00 p.m.
400 meter relay Prelim. 3:15 p.m.
800 meter run Prelim. 3:30 p.m.

60 meter int. hurdles Prelim. 3:50 p.m.
60 meter dash Prelim. 4:05 p.m.
400 meter dash Prelim. 4:20 p.m.
100 meter dash Prelim. 4:35 p.m.
110 meter low hurdles Prelim. 4:50 p.m.
200 meter dash Prelim. 5:10 p.m.
1 mile Prelim. 5:25 p.m.
800 meter relay Prelim. 5:40 p.m.
Mile relay Prelim. 6:00 p.m.
Saturday, May 19
400 meter relay Final 4:00 p.m.
800 meter run Final 4:10 p.m.
00 meter int. hurdles Final 4:25 p.m.
60 meter dash Final 4:35 p.m.
400 meter dash Final 4:45 p.m.
100 meter dash Final 4:55 p.m.
110 meter low hurdles Final 5:10 p.m.
200 meter dash Final 5:20 p.m.
1 mile Final 5:30 p.m.
800 meter relay Final 5:45 p.m.
Mile relay Final 6:00 p.m.

All track and field rules and regulations are in
the IM Handbook. Stop by the IM Office and
pick one up for your unit.
