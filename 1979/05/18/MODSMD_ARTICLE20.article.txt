# OATQ Policy
## 
### 
Once around the Quad announcements are run as
a public service by the Daily only for events which are
free and open to the public. Announcements may be
placed at the Daily office, on Lomita Mall across from
the Terman Engineering Center. Deadlines for an
nouncements are two business days before publica
tion at 1 p.m. No exceptions to deadlines can be
made, and an individual form must be submitted for
each day the announcement is to run. No an
nouncements can be accepted by telephone-
Time Schedule Changes are also run as a service,
but changes are run only if submitted through the
Registrar's Office. No announcements of course of
ferings or first meetings will be run unless submitted
by the registrar.
