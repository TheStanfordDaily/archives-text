Stanlord University Museum of Art.
Opening: May 29: "Opium: Pipes, Prints,
and Paraphernalia." More than 40 pipes
made from material like jade, ivory, porce
lain and cloissonne enamels, as well as
bamboo and rough clay. Opium lamps,
boxes and other objects, paintings, prints,
photographs, maps and illustrated books
will also be featured. Continuing: Perma
nent collection including the B.G. Cantor
Gallery of Rodin sculpture. Docent tours:
Saturday at 2 p.m. and Wednesday at
12:15 p.m. Museum hours: Tuesday thru
Friday, 10 a.m. to 4:45 p.m. and Saturday
and Sunday, 1 to 4:45 p.m.
