NEW MEMBERS OF SIGMA XI SOCIETY
The following were elected on May 22,1979 to Associate or Full Membership in the Stanford Chapter
of the Sigma Xi Society. The object of the Society is to encourage original investigation in pure and
applied science. Full Members are elected or promoted based on their noteworthy achievement as
an original investigator in a field of pure or applied science. Associate Members are elected based on
initial research achievement which has shown their aptitude for science research.
