# Women in Harmony
## 
### 
About 25 women participated in the Women's Music Night at the
Tresidder Coffee House Tuesday. It was the first event of its kind
and was organized to give women the opportunity to perform

original works. About 100 persons attended. Piclured above, from
left are Gretchen Steffy, Cathy Gewert./, Glenda Choate and
