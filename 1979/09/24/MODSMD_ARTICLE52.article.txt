# Briefly
## 
### 
License bikes on campus
Beginning this quarter, Tresidder
Recreation Center will take over the
job of licensing bicycles here. Bikers
can get licenses between 4 and 6
p.m. every Tuesday throughout the
quarter.
Music groups recruiting
More than four out of five players
in orchestras, choral groups, band
and other ensembles on campus are
non-music majors. Auditions for the
various groups, open to all students,
begin today. Contact the Music
Dept. for details.
Auditions opening
Auditions for the Danish comedy
"Erasmus Montanus," the Drama
Dept.'s first production this year, will
be held today through Wednesday at
7:30 p.m. in the Little Theater.
The script is available at the Drama
Dept., and may be signed out over
night. Auditions are open to the en
tire community, with preference in
casting given to students.
Daily staffers
All old Daily staffers are strongly
encouraged to come into the office
and rekindle old friendships. There
are lots of assignments running
around the office looking for au
thors.
Persons interested in working for
the Daily are also strongly encour
aged to stop by Storke Publications
Buildings and encouraged to stop by
Storke Publications Buildings and
indulge in one of the Orientation
meetings this week.
Meetings are scheduled for today
at 10 a.m. and again at 2 p.m. Other
introductory sessions will be held
Tuesday at 10 a.m. and 2 p.m., Wed
nesday at 1 p.m., Thursday at 2 p.m.
and Friday at 3 p.m.
For more information call the Daily
editorial office at 497-4632.
