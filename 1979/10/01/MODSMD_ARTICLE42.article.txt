Daily staff lunch
All Daily staffers, old and new, are heartily
invited to partake of Togo's sandwiches and
to help elect at-large members of the edito
rial board, as well as to get to know the
editors and other staffers, at noon Tuesday
in the Daily office.
Volunteers
The Stanford Children's Hospital is re
cruiting volunteers to help,with patient care
and support services. Interested persons 18
or over, should attend an introductory meet
ing at 7 p.m., Oct. 14, in Faber Auditorium.
For more information contact the Volunteer
Services Department.
Daily workshops
The Daily will hold workshops this week to
help strengthen a few important skills, for
both new and old staffers. Stop by even if
you haven't signed up in the office. News
writing: Monday, 4 p.m., Tuesday, 7 p.m.,
Wednesday, 4 p.m. and features/
interviewing: Tuesday, 2:15 p.m., Thursday,
7 p.m. Also reviews/entertainment: Friday, 2
p.m., night editing: Wednesday, 7 p.m. and
wire editing: Tuesday, 4 p.m.
