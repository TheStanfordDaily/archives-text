Organist (ill Mueller will
perform Bach, Buxtehude,
Franck and others in a free
Department of Music con
cert at 8 p.m. Oct. 10 in
Memorial Church.
Featured works include
Bach's Trio Sonata No. 5,
Franck's Fantasie in A and
Durufle's Suite for Organ.
Mueller, a freshman here,
has performed twice before
in University recitals.

Guitarist Charles Fergu
son will play "Themes and
Variations" by baroque and
contemporary composers
in an 8 p.m. concert Oct. 13
in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Ferguson, a University
music faculty member, will
perform works by Fre
cobaldi, Bach, Couperin,
Debussy and others.
Tickets are available at
Tresidder and other major
outlets.
Triple play
The Francesco Trio opens
its eighth season here at
2:30 p.m. Oct. 14 in Din
kelspiel Auditorium. Vio
linist David Abel, cellist
Bonnie Hampton and
pianist Nathan Schwartz will
perform two works by
Schubert and Hampton and
Schwartz will play Poulenc's
Sonata for cello and piano.
Each Francesco concert
will feature a French work.
Tickets are available at
Tresidder and major out
lets.
