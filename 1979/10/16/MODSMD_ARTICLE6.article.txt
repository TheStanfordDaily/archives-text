# ASSU election polling places
## 
### 
Polling places for today and tomorrow's ASSU election will be set up at
various locations around campus and will each be open at different hours.
The nine locations and their respective polling hours are listed below.
Please note that three of the polling places will be open for only one day of
the election.
Post Office 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Tresidder 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.
French & Italian Corner of Quad 10 a.m. to 3 p.m.
Physics Tank 9:30 a.m. to 12:15 p.m.
Earth Science Corner of Quad 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.
Stern side of UGLY 9:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.
UGLY (lobby) 7 p.m. to 10 p.m. Tuesday only
Business School 11:30 a.m. to 1 p.m. Tuesday only
Medical School 11:45 a.m. to 2:15 p.m. Wednesday only
