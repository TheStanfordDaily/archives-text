# ASSU election polling places
## 
### 
Polling places for today's ASSU election will be set up at various locations
around campus and will each be open at different hours. The nine locations
and their respective polling hours are listed below.
Post Office 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Tresidder 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.
French & Italian Corner of Quad 10 a.m. to 3 p.m.
Physics Tank 9:30 a.m. to 12:15 p.m.
Earth Science Corner of Quad 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.
Stern side of UGLY 9:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.
Medical School 11:45 a.m. to 2:15 p.m.
