Harry S. Truman
Scholarship Program
Faculty Representative —
Professor Richard A. Brody, Political Science
Sophomores committed to a career in the public service
may apply to be Stanford University's nominees for a Harry
S. Truman Scholarship. Two nominees may be selected
from Stanford, but they may not both reside in the same
State.
A Truman Scholarship covers tuition, fees, books, and
room and board, up to a maximum of $5000 annually for the
: remainder of a student's undergraduate career and for up
to two years of graduate study. Fifty-three scholarships are
awarded nationally each year. For further information con
tact Virginia Mann in Building 370, Room 372.
Application Deadline— November 12, 1979
