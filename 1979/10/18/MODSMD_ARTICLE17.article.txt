Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation. Main office; Storke Student Publications Build
ing, Stanford, CA 94305.
Telephone: Editorial (415) 497-46.12; Business (415) 497-2554.
Nothing on the opinions page necessarily represents a position ol the entire Daily stall,
or of the I eland Stanford lunior University.
The Daily's editorial board consists of six editors and three at-large members elected bv
the staff. Editorials represent the opinion of a majority of those editorial board members
voting on each topic.
Letters, columns, and cartoons represent only the views ot their authors. The Daily
regrets that it cannot guarantee the return of any artii le submitted. All submitted articles
are subject to editing.
