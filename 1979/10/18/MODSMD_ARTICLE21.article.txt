# A 						Special Event
## 
### 
Tom Scott will play the Coffee House Oct.
25 in the quarter's second ASSU Special
Events concert. Joining him for the 8:30 p.m.
and 10:30 p.m. shows will be Carlos Rios,
Neil Stubenhauf, Ronald Aston and Russell
Ferrante. Two days later, folk musician John
Fahey will entertain at Cubberly Auditorium

in an 8 p.m. show with Reilly & Maloney. Big
Game Night will see Dave Mason in Memorial
Auditorium for a 7:30 p.m. celebration of
Stanford's victory over Cal.
Tickets for all ASSU shows are available at
Tresidder and major outlets.
