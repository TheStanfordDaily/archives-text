112 Contributions
The Daily welcomes expressions of all viewpoints
Irorn readers. Letters should be kept as brief as possible
and are subject to editing. They must include signature
and phone number. Pseudonyms and initials will not be
used. Unpublished letters cannot be acknowledged.
Contributions must be typed double-spaced on a 57-
space line. They should be sent to: Opinions, The Stan
ford Daily, Storke Publications Building, Stanford, CA
i 94305.
