# IN VOGUE
## 
### 




For the fashion-conscious student on a low budget, Vogue has re
leased its famous compendium, In Vogue, in paperback. A record of
the last 60 years of fashion as seen in the pages of Vogue magazine, In
Vogue is a browser's delight. Whether it's tricorne headdresses with
ostrich feathers or Brazilian string bikinis that turn you on, you'll find
it in the pages of In Vogue.
