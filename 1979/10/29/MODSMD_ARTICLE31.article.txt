# Men win Pac-10
## 
### 
In the co-upset of the
weekend, the men's cross
country team snatched first
place in the Pacific-10
Southern Division Champ
ionships Saturday led by
Rod Berry's second-place
finish.
En route to the victory the
harriers tallied 47 points
passing up UCLA with 48,
nationally-ranked Arizona
with 57 and California with
61. Details of the race and
the results will be published
in Wednesday's Daily.
