# Kihn in concert
## 
### 
Greg Kihn (right) will play
the Palo Alto Keystone Hal
loween night at 8:30. Kihn
and hand play a cool, tough
brand of rock that is largely
influenced hy the English and
American power pop and
rock sounds of the late '60s.
Kihn and hand have long
been the best club act in the
Bay Area, and in the last year
have opened shows on the
East Coast for The Cars,
among others.
