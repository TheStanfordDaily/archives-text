# OATQ Policy
## 
### 
Once around the Quad announcements are run as a public service
by the Daily only for events which are free and open to the public.
Announcements should be filled out and placed in person at the
D.iily office, on the Lomita Mall across from theTerman Engineer
ing Center. The deadlines for announcements are two business
days before publication at 1 p.m. No exceptions can be made.
