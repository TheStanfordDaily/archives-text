The Daily will be publishing the second edition of The Stanford Daily Magazine at the end of
November. The subject for this issue will be "Stanford in the 19705." There will be space in the issue
devoted to columns and letters to the general theme of "Thoughts About Life on the Farm in the
Past Decade." Members of the Stanford community are encouraged to contribute to this section
and are invited to submit the columns to the Daily office by Friday, Nov. 16. Contributions should
be typed, double-spaced on a 57-space line, and preferably no longer than 80 lines. Please identify
your position in the Stanford Community.
