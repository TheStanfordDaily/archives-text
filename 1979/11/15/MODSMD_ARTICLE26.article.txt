# Dancing in the dark
## 
### 
The San Francisco Dance
Theater tomorrow will open
its fall concert series, which
will include two premiere
works by artistic director
Penelope Lagios Johnson, the
SFDT choreography debut of
dancer Marika Sakellarious,
and works by Sharonjean
Leeds and Catherine Sims.
Programs begin at 8:30 p.m.
Fridays and Saturdays
through Dec. 1 at the dance
theater, 1412 Van Ness, San
Francisco.
