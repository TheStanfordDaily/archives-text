# Off with her head?
## 
### 
Everyone's falling in love or fleeing the executioner's blade in
the Savoyards production of The Mikado, which opened last
night and runs through Sunday in Dinkelspiel Auditorium. The
Gilbert and Sullivan classic will be performed at 8 p.m., except
for tonight's 9 p.m. show. Tickets are available at Tresidder:
admission $3.50, students $1.50.
