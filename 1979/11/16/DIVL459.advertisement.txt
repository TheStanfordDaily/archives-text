INVITE YOU TO A PUBLICITY WORKSHOP
Dear Friends:
The Stanford Daily and K/.SU cordially invite you or a representative of
your organization, department or living group to a publicity workshop
on 1 uesday, November 20 trom 4 to 6 p.m. in the Meyer Forum Room.
Both the Daily and k/SU are often asked why we failed to cover or
promote a particular event. Too frequently, the answer is due to a lack of
communication between us and the sponsoring group.
We'd like to help overcome this gap by exchanging ideas on how your
organization can best communicate with us. /\ publicitv workshop is a
logical torum tor this exchange.
The Daily and KZSU have complimentary publicity services that can be
ottered to your organization. At the workshop, we will each offer a
half-hour presentation on our respective needs and services, including
mention oi deadlines and news policies. Then we will open the work
shop for questions and suggestions.
We hope this workshop will give you a clearer picture of our organiza
tions.
We look torward to seeing you on November 20.
Sincerely,
Sara Lessley Mike DiMartino
Editor Station Manager
The Stanford Daily KZSU
