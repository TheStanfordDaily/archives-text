# Cagers split at SLO
## 
### 
Stantord's women's basketball team tell to San lose State,
64-59, in the second round of the Cal Poly-San Luis Obispo tour
nament Saturday.
Stanford led at halttime, 34-31, but failed to hold its edge as
Karen Mason, who finished with 21 points, and Elinor Banks, who
collected 16, got hot for the Spartans.
Center Louise Smith paced the Cardinals with 20 points and
leanne Ruark added 15.
Friday night Stanford opened its season in winning fashion,
trouncing UC-Santa Barbara, 69-42.
Details ot the tournament will appear in Wednesday's Daily.
