# Movies
## 
### 
Campus
Thursday, November 29
Adieu Poulet (The French Detective).
Directed by Pierre Granier-Deferre. 8 p.m.
Cubberley Auditorium.
Friday, November 30
The Harder They Come. Jimmy Cliff
stars in this story of an aspiring musician
who runs marijuana to support himself,
kills a cop during a drug bust and is hunted
by police and the local syndicate. 7:30 and
9:30 p.m. Kresge Auditorium.
Sunday, December 2
Close Encounters of the Third Kind.
The film that makes you believe that
someone out there is watching you. 7 and
9:30 p.m. Memorial Auditorium.
Monday, December 3
Day for Night. Francois Truffaut bath di
rects and stars in this film, where he plays
the part of a director of a movie being shot
in Nice. 7:30 p.m. Cubberley Auditorium.
