# Christmas Faire returns to Tresidder
## 
### 
The eighth annual Christmas Fairewill take
place on December 7, 8 and 9 at Tresidder
Union. More than 80 booths featuring crafts
and exotic foods will be open from 10 a.m. to
5 p.m. each day.

STARTS (Student Arts at Stanford) is spon
soring the event, the proceeds of which help
fund musical and cultural programs during
the school year.
