# Whalewatching spree
## 
### 
Thousands of California
Gray whales, reaching up to 40
teet in length, are now passing
along the west coast on their
annual trek from Alaska to the
warm waters of Baja , Mexico.
Whalewatching excursions
are being offered by the

Oceanic Society, with day long
whalewatch trips from San
Francisco to Point Reyes run
ning on weekends. Shorter
trips out of Halt Moon Bay are
offered on Fridays and Mon
days.
For more information, call
Whalewatch at 775-6880.
