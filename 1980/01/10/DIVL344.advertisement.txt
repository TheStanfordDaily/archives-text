Contributions
The Daily welcomes letters and opinions on subjects of
interest to members of the Stanford community, whether
these contributions be social, political, cultural,
humorous or satirical.
All contributions must be typed double-spaced on a
57-space line. They should be sent to or dropped off at:
Opinions, The Stanford Daily, Storke Publications
Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
