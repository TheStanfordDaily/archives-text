# FORUM: Registration and the Draft
## 
### 
Beginning next Friday, the Daily will sponsor a weekly Opin
ions FORUM appearing along with our regular Opinions Page.
The first topic is "Registration and the Draft." We welcome your
opinions on this topic in column, letter or cartoon form.
Submissions should be typed, double-spaced, should not ex
ceed 750 words, and are subject to editing. They should be
brought or sent to the Daily office, Storke Publications Building,
Stanford University 94 ?05 not later than noon on Tuesday, Feb. 5.
