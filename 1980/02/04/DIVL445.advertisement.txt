THE
CHARLES R. BLYTH
FUND OF
STANFORD UNIVERSITY
An autonomous student-managed investment I und, is
now accepting applications for Board of Directors to
serve on the board from April 1, 1980- March 31, 1981.
The Blyth Fund is designed to provide undergraduate students
interested in Finance and Investments with a practical education in the
workings of the stock, bond and securities markets. The Board of
Directors has approximately $86,000 at their disposal and is in
exclusive control of all investment decisions.
The fund has been in operation for one year and has returned a
sizable profit in its investments.
Interest in learning how to manage investments will weigh equally
with experience in the selection of students.
Applications may be picked up at the
Office of Dean of Student Affairs, 323 Old Union
DEADLINE: February 11
Additional information is also available there.
For further inquiries contact:
AH.MAD SAMI 327-8687 HOWARD GLEICHER 327-8573
CORRINE GARZA 322-8227 KEN PEREZ 321-8625
