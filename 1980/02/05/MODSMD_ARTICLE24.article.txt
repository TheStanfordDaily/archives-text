# FORUM:
## Registration and the draft 
### 
This Friday the Daily will begin publishing a weekly Forum
page. The first topic is "Registration and the Draft." We welcome
your opinions on this topic in column, letter or cartoon form.
Submissions should be typed, double-spaced, should not exceed
750 words and are subject to editing. They should be brought to the
Daily office, Storke Publications Building, not later than noon today.
