# Forum:
## 
### 
Are grades reflective of intelligence? Is the bell curve fair? Are
the avenues for fighting a grade open enough? Are Stanford
students victims (or beneficiaries) of grade inflation?
Express your opinion in column, letter or cartoon form and
send it to the Stanford Daily. Storke Publications Building. Stan
ford, CA 94305 by noon Tuesday. Letters and columns should be
typed double-spaced on a 57-space line and are subject to editing.
Columns should not exceed 750 words.
The Forum page, the Daily's newest feature, will continue to
run every Friday in addition to our regular opinions page. Sugges
tions for future topics are welcome.
