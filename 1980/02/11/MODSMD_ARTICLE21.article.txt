# The
						Daily is looking for a few choice words.
## 
### 
We want to know what you
think is wrong (or right) with
Stanford, California, the Un
ited States or the world. A let
ter to the editor is a quick way
to share your thoughts with
15,000 readers in the Stan
ford community.
Letters should he typed,
double-spaced on a 57-space
line and should not exceed 20
lines. Send to the Stanford
Daily, Storke Publications
Building, Stanford, CA
94305. Letters must be
signed and are subject to edit
ing.
