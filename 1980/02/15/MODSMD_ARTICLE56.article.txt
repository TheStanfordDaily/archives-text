# Chicano meeting
## 
### 
All members of the community are
invited to attend an open hearing to be
heldTues., Feb. 19, by the Committee
to Review the Office of Assistant to the
President for Chicacno Affairs.

The meeting will take place in E!
Centre Chicano from 4 to 5:30 p.m.
This will he a fact-finding session to
acquaint the committee with commun
ity views on the office. The committee
is particularly interested in learning
what services community members feel
are being provided by the office at pre
sent. which of these might be provided
in the future by other mechanisms, and
which do not appear to be transferable
elsewhere.
