PLANNING TO MOVE OUT
OF YOUR RESIDENCE
NEXT QUARTER?
Single students w ho arc currently living in a University residence
and wlu) plan not to return for spring quarter should notify their
local residence office of their plans. A "termination of occupancy"
form should he filed by every resident who plans to (a) graduate,
(h) go to an overseas studies center, (c) stop out. or (d) get married.
Studentsare strongly encouraged to file their termination forms by
Monday. I ebiuary 25. I 980, so that students a waiting spring quar
tei assignment can be informed as soon as possible. Students who
file their termination forms after March I. I will lose their $50
housing deposits.
Residents ol Kscondido Village, w ho have monthly rental agree
ments. should plan to give thirty days notice to the Escondido
Village Office before moving out.
