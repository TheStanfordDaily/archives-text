# WANTED: Letters
## 
### 
If you think something is especially wrong (or right) with
Stanford, California, the United States or the world, a letter
to the Daily is a quick way to express your opinion to mem
bers of the Stanford community.
Letters should be typed, double-spaced on a 57-space line,
should not exceed 20 lines, are subject to editing and must be
signed. Send to the Stanford Daily, Storke Publications
Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
