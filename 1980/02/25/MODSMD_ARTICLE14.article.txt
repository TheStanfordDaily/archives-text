Treasure Mart preview
Persons planning to participate in an upcoming Treasure Mart, to benefit the Stanford
Museum Art Acquisition Fund, were at the Stanford Art Museum Thursday evening

Wednesday through Friday, featuring the sale of donated works of art and various
other objects.
