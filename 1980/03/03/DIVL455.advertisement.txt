HAPPY RETIREMENT PAPA DOC!
Any year that begins sailing with the HMS
Mama Leon and saluting bubbly Bacchus
has to be golden.
This is bound to be a mischievious Monday.
HAPPY 25th, AL BUDDY!
Lots of Kisses (besides the chocolate ones). I
Maxee and La Boopa I
