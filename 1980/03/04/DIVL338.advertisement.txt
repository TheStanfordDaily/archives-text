Friday Forum:
Should marijuana be decriminalized?
Is the decriminalization of marijuana a good
idea? How would it affect students and faculty
here?
The California Marijuana Initiative of 1980 is a
proposed statutory amendment. It would allow
adults to possess, transport or cultivate marijuana
for personal use without criminal penalty. The
measure would not affect legislation prohibiting
people under the influence of marijuana from
operating a motor vehicle or engaging in conduct
that may endanger others.
Additionally, a state commission would he es
tablished to study the potential agricultural,
economic and tax benefits of regulating the
marijuana market.
Bring your opinion in column, letter or cartoon
form to the Daily, Storke Publications Building,
by noon tomorrow. Submissions should be typed,
double-spaced, on a 57-space line and are subject
to editing.
