Undergraduate and Graduate Students
Are Invited to Apply for...
STUDENT RESIDENCE STAFF
POSITIONS FOR 1980-81
Informational meetings for students interested In applying
for these positions will be held
Tuesday, March 4, at 7 p.m. , Forum Room, Meyer Libary
Wednesday, March 5, at 7 p.m., CERAS Large Instructional Room
Thursday, March 6, at 4 p.m., Forum Room, Meyer Library
Tuesday, April 1, at 4 p.m. , Forum Room, Meyer Library
(for students who were away from campus Winter Quarter)
A member of the Dean of Student Affairs staff, a current
Resident Fellow, and some student staff members will give a
brief presentation about student staff positions. They will try to
answer your questions about the program. Applications, job
descriptions, and information about the selection process will
be distributed.
We Suggest, in order to keep these meetings as small as
possible, that those whose last names begin with A-J attend
on Tuesday, K-R on Wednesday, and S-Z on Thursday.
All Students Interested in residence staff positions are urged
to attend one of these meetings and to pick up application
materials there.
Application materials will also be available at the Residential
Education reception desk, Office of the Dean of Student
Affairs, room 311, Old Union
All Applications are due by 4 p.m. Friday, April 4
