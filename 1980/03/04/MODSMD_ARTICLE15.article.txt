# Contributions
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes contribu
tions from members of the Stan
ford community. Opinions are ac
cepted at our office in the Storke
Publications Building during nor
mal business hours. All submitted
items must be typed, double
spaced. on a 57-space line. Letters
should not exceed 20 lines and
columns should not exceed 80
lines.
