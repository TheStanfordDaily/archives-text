Stanford Conference y BO
Alumni and parents gathered in Los Angeles on Sunday for an
annual conference featuring lectures by nine University
professors and administrators, plus a luncheon speech by
President Richard Lyman. See page 3.
Aftermath of fire
Women whose rooms were burned in the Storey House fire
talk about how they're coping with the situation, and officials
from both the University and fire department discuss how the
fire fighting was handled. See page 5.
Cards lose to Arizona
The Arizona Wildcats downed Stanford 3-2 in baseball action
Monday, but the Cards still took the series by winning two of
the three games. They also stand at the top of the Pac-10's
southern division with a 2-1 conference record. See page 10.
