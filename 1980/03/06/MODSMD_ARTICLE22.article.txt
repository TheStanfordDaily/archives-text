Hot crusade
Jazz is rolling in a very big way this quarter, with Chick
Corea set for Sunday at 3 p.m. in Memorial Auditorium.
But following that master jazzman will be another brand of
musical excellence, the southern bluesy funk sound of The
Crusaders. The group, featuring dazzling vocalist Randy
Crawford, will play Maples Pavilion, April 12.
