# Senior recital
## 
### 
The Music Depart
ment will present the
Senior Recital ol sop
rano Mary Setrakian.
with pianist Ellen
Limacher on next
Tuesday.
Setrakian will sing
works by Mozart, Bizet
and Walton, featured
will be Schumann's
Liederkreis. opus 39.
and Haydn's Arriunna
a Nassos.
The N p.m. concert in
Dinkelspiel Au
ditorium is tree.
