The Stanford Daily is an independent student newspaper owned and
published by The Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation. Main office:
Slorke Student Publications Building. Stanford, California 94305.
Nothing on the Opinions Page necessarily represents a position of the
entire Daily staff, or of the I.eland Stanford Junior University.
The Daily's Editorial Board consists of six editors and three at-large
members elected by the staff. Editorials represent the opinion of a
majority of those Editorial Board members voting on each topic.
I etters, columns and cartoons icpresent only the views of their au
thors The Daily regrets that it cannot guarantee the publication or
return of any article submitted. All submitted articles are subject to
