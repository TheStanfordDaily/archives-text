Auditing
Students who register on o full-rime basis are considered ro hove rhe privilege of the
University and may, with rhe consent of the instructors, audit lecture courses in addition to
rhe program taken for credit. Students registered on some partial basis (such os half
tuition) may nor audit additional courses without payment of rhe regular tuition rhar
would be charged for credit registration. Changing from auditing basis to credit basis or
vice verso is not permitted after rhe published deadline for changing study lists.
Persons nor registered or Stanford are permitted ro audir only under unusual cir
cumstances; permission may be granted by rhe Associate Registrar on payment of rhe
Attendance Permit fee of $525 per quarter.
Stanford alumni and rheir spouses moy register on Attendance Permir basis on requesr
and upon payment of rhe Attendance Permit Fee.
Faculty may attend another faculty member's class upon his or her invitation.
A member of rhe University staff may audir lecture courses with a letter from rhe
deportment head rhar auditing wi II nor interfere with normal duries and rhar rhe course in
question will contribute to rhe ability ro perform the assignment. Spouses of University
faculty and staff may audir lecture courses. In borh of rhe above cases a Courtesy Cord
should be obtained from rhe Associate Registrar (Room 137). In all cases of auditing rhe
instructor's consent is required.
No person should attend any class unless he or she is in one of rhe classifications above
(Space paid for by News and Publications Service)
