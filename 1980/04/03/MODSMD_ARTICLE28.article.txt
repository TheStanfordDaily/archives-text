# China Night
## 
### 
Dances in colorful costumes, traditional songs and skits and
instrumental music highlight a China Night gala this Sunday in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium. The 7:30 p.m. event, sponsored by
the Stanford Chinese Students' Association, will include a
variety show which features Stanford students, their families
and Bay Area cultural groups.
