# WORLD NEWS
## Shoulder to Shoulder 
### 
SALISBURY, Zimbabwe — Now equipped with Rhodesian army uniforms and
weapons, a rank of former guerrillas from Robert Mugabe and Joshua Nkomo's
nationalist armies stand shoulder-to-shoulder with white and black airmen under
command of a white army drill instructor. They were rehearsing formations in prep
aration for a review of the new nation's combined forces during a ceremony marking
Independence, which begins at midnight tomorrow.


—AP photo courtesy Peninsula Times Tribune
