# Shakespeare fest
## 
### 
The Department of Drama
will mark the birthday of Wil
liam Shakespeare with a gala
program of comic scenes from
his plays.
The 45-minute perfor
mances. beginning at 12:15
p.m.. will run Wednesday.
April 23. through Saturday,
April 26, on the east patio of
Memorial Auditorium.
A Gala Celebration of

Shakespeare's Birthday will
be directed by second-year
doctoral students Debra
Skriba, Jim Carmody and
Tony Curiel and will include
scenes from A Midsummer
Night's Dream, Romeo and
Juliet, The Tempest, As You
Like It, Much Ado About No
thing, and Love's Labours
Lost.
Admission is free.
