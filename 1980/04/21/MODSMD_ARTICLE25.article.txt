# What's your opinion?
## 
### 
Chances are that you're a student, faculty,
staff or community reader who's informed
about today's events. What's your opinion?
It's about time you let the world know
where you stand by writing a letter or column
for the Daily.
Letters should be typed, double-spaced on
a 57-space line and should not exceed 20
lines. Columns should not exceed 80 lines.
All contributions arc subject to editing and
must be signed. Send them to The Stanford
Daily, Storke Publications Building, Stan
ford, CA 94305.
