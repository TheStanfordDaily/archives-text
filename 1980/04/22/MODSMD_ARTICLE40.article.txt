Recycling growth
Because of positive response from
the community, the ASSU Recycling
Center has expanded its operation.
There is now a goodwill center on
Pampas Lane. Drop-off hours will con
tinue to be Monday through Saturday,
from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Seminar
The first seminar of Ujamaa's four
part "Social Progress Due to the Arts
and Professions" series will be held to
night at 7 p.m. in Ujamaa large lounge.
Tonight's discussion will focus on "So
cial Progress through the Medical Pro
fession."
