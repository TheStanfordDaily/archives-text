# Once Around the Quad announcement policy
## 
### 
Once Around the Quad announcements are run as a public service
b\ the Daily only tor events which are free and open to the public.
Announcement forms must be filled out and placed in person at the
Daily office in the Storke building, across Lomita Mall from Terman
Engineering Center. The deadline forsubmitting announcements is 1
p.m., two business days before publication. No exceptions can be
made. A separate announcement form must be submitted for each
day of publication.
