Table tennis
Stanford's Carl Danner, of
Huntington, NY. was one of the
30 finalists at thevjust'concluded
Ninth Annual Intercollegiate
Table Tennis Championships at
the University of Minnesota, in
Minneapolis, Minnesota. Dan
ner placed eighth in the men's
singles competition, having first
won tournaments at college and
regional levels. Altogether, over
1000 colleges and universities
throughout the United States

participated in this national
championship.
