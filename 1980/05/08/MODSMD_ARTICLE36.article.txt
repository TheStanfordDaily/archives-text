# Premiere
## 
### 
Japanese film director
Okamoto Kihachi and Sato
Masaru, composer of the
score, will be on hand for the
first West Coast showing of
Okamoto's latest film. The
Last Game, at 2:30 p.m., Fri
day, at the New Varsity
Theatre in Palo Alto.
The event is sponsored by
the Center for East Asian
Studies and is open to the
public. A 50-cent donation
will be accepted at the door.
