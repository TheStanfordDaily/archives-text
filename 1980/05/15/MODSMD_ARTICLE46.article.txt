# The
						Stanford Daily
## 
### 
The Stanford Daily is an independent student newspaper owned and published by The
Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation, a California non-profit corporation, published
Monday through Friday during the academic year, with the exceptions of dead week,
when one issue is published, and finals week, when no issues are published Special
sports issues are published for home football and other special sporting events.
