Contributions
The Daily welcomes letters and columns
from its readers. All submitted items must he
typed, double-spaced, on a 57-space line. Let
ters should not exceed 20 lines and columns
should not exceed KO lines.

are subject to editing. Send contributions to

ing, Stanford, CA 94305.
