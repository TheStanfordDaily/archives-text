# What do you think?
## 
### 
Hey you! Chances are that you're an intelligent student,
faculty member or community resident who's informed about
today's events. What's your opinion?
Letters should be typed, double-spaced on a 57-space line
and should not exceed 20 lines. Columns should not exceed 80
lines. All contributions are subject to editing and must be
signed. Send them to The Stanford Daily, Storke Publications
Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
