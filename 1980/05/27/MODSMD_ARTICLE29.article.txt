# Once Around the Quad policy
## 
### 
Ihe Daily prints Once Around the Ouad announcements as a
public service to the community. Only announcements tor e\ ents that
are tree and open to the public will be published. All announcements
must be submitted in person two business days before publication at I
p.m. A separate announcement form must be submitted for each day
the announcement is to appear. Due to occasional space limitations,
announcements must sometimes be omitted.
