The Stanford Daily is an independent student newspaper owned and published by The
Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation Main office Storke Publications Building, Stanford.
California 94 305
Nothing on the Opinions Page necessarily represents a position of the entire Daily stalf, or
of the Leland Stanford Junior University.
The Daily's Editorial Board consists of five editors and two at-large members selected by
the staff Editorials represent the opinion of a majority of these Editorial Board members
voting on each topic.
Letters, columns and cartoons represent only the views of their authors The Daily regrets
that it cannot guarantee the publication or return of any article submitted All submitted
articles are subject to editing
