In Summer Quarter, Dead Week consists of the
weekend and the four class days preceding the final
examinations, which take place on Friday and
Saturday of the eighth week. During this period,
no quizzes or examinations should be given, and no
musical, dramatic and athletic events involving
compulsory student participation should be
scheduled.
