FOR BOOKLOVERS!
A recently-published book, cover to cover,
evaluates about 460 bookstores in the San Fran
cisco Bay Area. Of these the author writes:
The best-known and the ones that spring to mind
are the stores such as Kapler's in Menlo Park and
codir* in Berkeley ... they are characterized by
having single-location stores, staffed by know
ledgeable book people who can discuss the topical
areas covered by the store. They are friendly places
and always crammed full of browsers."
if you have not yet visited the Bay Area's top
bookstores, do so today and every week thereafter.
Kepler's is the home of all the paperbacks.
Kepler's Books & Magazines
825 El Camino Real, Menlo Park
free parking - open 7 days & eves
