# Columnists
## 
### 
Persons interested in becoming regular columnists for Volume 178 (from now until the end ot
January) should submit one sample column to the opinion editors by noon, Friday, Oct. 3.
The Daily is looking for columnists from the Stanford community who wish to comment on
the issues of the day, whether political, economic, social or cultural. Humorous and satirical
contributions are also welcome.
