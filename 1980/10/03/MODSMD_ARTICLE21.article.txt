The Stanlord Daily is an independent student newspaper owned and published by
The Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation. Main office: Storke Student Publications
Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
Telephone: Editorial (415) 497-4632; Business (415) 497-2554.
Nothing on the opinions page necessarily represents a position ot the entire Daily
staff. or ot the Leland Stanford lunior University
The Daily's editorial board consists ot six editors and three at-large members elected
by the staff. Editorials represent the opinion ot a ma|ority ol those editorial board
members voting on each topic.
Letters, columns and cartoons represent only the views ot their authors The Daily
regrets thai if cannot guarantee the return ot any article submitted. All submitted
articles are subject to editing.
Subscription rates; in the United States. Canada and Mexico, $25 per year or $12 per
quarter. Send check or money order in advance to: Circulation, The Stanford Daily,
Storke Student Publications Building, Stanlord, CA 94105.
Published Monday through Friday during the a ademic year, with the exceptions ot
dead week, when one issue is published, and finals week when no issues are pub
lished. Published Tuesdays and Fridays during the eight-week summer quarter
