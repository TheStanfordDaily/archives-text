TAKE OFF . . .
AND LEAVE THE CAMPUS
BELOW AND BEHIND!
Inside each of us is a spirit
that wants to escape from the
ordinary .to experience
something special.
For many Stanford stu
dents and staff flying opens
the door to an adventure that
only few share and none
forget.
Now, you can Take Off
and learn to fly. at a very low
cost.
Most students obtain a
Solo License with only 9 to
18 hours of flight time.
Many completed a Solo
License for less than $385.
Come try a lesson. For in
formation call today.
