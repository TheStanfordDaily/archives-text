# Adv. 14 Page 3
## 
### 
Student Directory
1980-81
Tuesday Oct. 14 will be the last day you can request the
Registrar's Office to suppress your address and or telephone
information from the Student Directory.
If you are unsure, or want to confirm what mformaton will
appear, you should check the public roster near the Informa
tion Window in the lobby of the Old Union by Oct. 14.
The 1980-81 Student Directory will be available the third
week in November. The address and telephone information
will be taken directly from the information on your Permit to
Register (PTR), so if you have a new address and/or tele
phone number you should report it at the Registrar s Informa
tion Window by the 14th.
