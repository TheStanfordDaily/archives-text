# Briefly
## 
### 
University committees
The deadline for applications for
student members of ASSU commit
tees has been extended to this Friday
for graduate students only. Also, a
new Committee on Computing and
Information Technology has been
formed, and both undergraduates
and graduates are welcome to apply
through Friday. Application forms
are available in the ASSU office in
Tresidder.
Writing workshops
The Daily is offering all new writers
some workshops to learn some of
the important aspects of writing
news. The workshops will cover the
basics of news and feature writing,
and all those who are interested are
encouraged to attend. Workshops
will be held Thursday, Oct. 16 at
noon, Monday, Oct. 20 at noon and
Tuesday, Oct. 21 at 7 p.m.
Production workshops
The Daily will offer production
workshops to train new staffers in
the basics of night and wire editing.
Topics such as layout, headlines, and
general style will be discussed.
Workshops will be held Thursday,
Oct. 16 and Monday, Oct. 20 at 2
p.m.
American premiere
On Wednesday, Oct. 15 at 8 p.m.
at La Casa Italiana there will be a re
ception for the presentation of Feu
d'artifice, a ballet designed by the
Futurist painter Ciacomo Balla for
Stravinski's music. The public is in
vited to this extraordinary American
premiere.
