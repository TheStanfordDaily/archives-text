GIULIETTA MASIIVA
La Casa Italiana
Wednesday, Oct. 15 8:00 p.m.
Giulietta Masina will be guest of honor at
Simone Carella 1 s presentation of the Balla sets
for Stravinsky's Feux d' artifice ballet.
Giulietta Masiria is best known for her starring roles in such
Italian films as Nights ofCabiria. Giulietta of the Spirits, and La
Strada. She is married to Italian film director Federico Fellini.
This past week Ms. Masina was honored with a tribute by the
San Francisco International Film Festival.
