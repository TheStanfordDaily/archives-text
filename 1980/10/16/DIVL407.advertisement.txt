Harry S. Truman
Scholarship Program
— Faculty Representative —
Professor Hubert R. Marshall, Political Science
Sophomores committed to a career in the
public service may apply to be Stanford Univer
sity's nominees for a Harry S. Truman Scholar
ship. Informational meeting Monday, October
20, 5 p.m., Meyer Forum Room. Interested first
year students may also attend.
A Truman Scholarship covers tuition, fees,
books, and room and board, up to a maximum of
$5000 annually for the remainder of a student's
undergraduate career and for up to two years of
graduate study. Seventy-nine scholarships are
awarded nationally.
If unable to attend meeting, contact Vir
ginia Mann in building 370, Room 372.
Application Deadline — November 17, 1980.
