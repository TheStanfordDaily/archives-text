Contributions
The Daily welcomes expressions of all viewpoints trom
readers. Letters should be kept as brief as possible and are
subject to editing. They must include signature and phone
number. Pseudonyms and initials will not be used. Unpub
lished letters cannot be acknowledged.
All contributions must be typed double-spaced on a 57-
character line on non-corrasable paper. They should be
sent to Opinions, The Stanford Daily, Storke Publications
Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
