# Kalichstein, Laredo, Robinson
## 
### 
Josenh Kalichstein, pianist, Jaime Laredo, violinist, and Sharon Robinson, cellist, make up
the Kalichstein, Laredo, Robinson Trio, which will perform this Sunday at 2:30 p.m. in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium. The concert, sponsored by the Lively Arts at Stanford, will include
works by Mozart, Mendelssohn and Schubert.
