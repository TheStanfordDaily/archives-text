'Daily' staff banquet
The long awaited Daily staff ban
quet is finally here. There will be a
Happy Hour at the Daily offices at 6
p.m. Drinks will be served, and those
who need rides will be able to hitch one
at that time. Dinner will be served at
7 30 at the Winery.
