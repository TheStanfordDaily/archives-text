# Contributions
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes letters to the editor from
its readers. Submissions should be typed,
double-spaced, on a 57-space line, on non-cor
rasable paper.
While every effort is made to consult with au
thors of opinions articles before changes are
made, articles are subject to editing by the
Daily without prior notice.
Please direct letters to the opinions editors,
The Stanford Daily, Storke Publications
Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
Thank you.
