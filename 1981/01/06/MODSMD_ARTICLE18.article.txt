# Selective Service registration
## 
### 
This week men born in 1962 are ex
pected to register with the Selective
Service.Registration forms are avail
able at U S. post offices. Also, men
born in 1963 are expected to register
within 30 days of their 18th birthdays.
Further information may be obtained
from the Selective Service at (202)
724-0790 or the draft information hot
line at (415) 493-4857.
