THE
CHARLES R. BLYTH
FUND OF
STANFORD UNIVERSITY
An autonomous student-managed investment fund is now accepting
applications for membership on the Board of Directors to serve from
April 1, 1981 — March 31, 1982.
The Blyth fund is designed to provide undergraduate students interested in
finance and investments with a practical education in the workings of the stock,
bond and securities markets. The Board of Directors has approximately $93,000
at its disposal and is in exclusive control of all investment decisions.
The Fund has been in operation for 2 years and has returned a sizable profit from
its investments.
Interest in learning how to manage investments and commitment tojhe project
will weigh equally with experience in the selection of students.
