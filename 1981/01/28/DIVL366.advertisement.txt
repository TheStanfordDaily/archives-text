Careers in Publishing
The director of the Radcliffe
Publishing course, a six-week
graduate program in book and
magazine publishing at Harvard
University, will be on campus on
Tues., Feb. 3. A general meeting will
be held at 4:00 PM in Tresidder Rm.
133 to discuss career opportunities in
publishing. For individual interviews
and more information contact
Melinda Kaewert 497-3963.
