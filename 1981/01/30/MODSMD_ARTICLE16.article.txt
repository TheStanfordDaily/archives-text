# Opinions for the masses
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes expressions of all viewpoints from readers. Letters should be as brief
as possible and are subject to editing. They must include signature and phone number. Un
published letters cannot be acknowledged. Unsigned letters will not be published.
All contributions must be typed double-spaced on a 57-character line on non-corasable pa
per. Send all letters to Opinions. The Stanford Dailv. Storke Publications Building. Stanford.
CA 94305.
