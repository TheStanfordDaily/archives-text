# Once Around The Quad
## Once Around The Quad policy 
### 
Once Around the Quad announcements are published as
a public service for events which are free and open to
the public. Announcements must be submitted at the
Daily offices by 1 p.m. two business days before publi
cation. iVo exceptions to deadlines can be made.
