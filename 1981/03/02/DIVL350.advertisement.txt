# Adv. 11 Page 4
## 
### 
El Salvador Forum
The opinions page of the Daily on Wednes
day, March 4, will be devoted to comment on
El Salvador from the Stanford community.
Submissions pertaining to the conflict, the
U.S. role therein, and possible implications of
the issues for Central America and the world
are welcome.
The deadline for articles is Monday, March 2.
Manuscripts must be typed, double-spaced,
on a 57-character line on non-corrasable pa
per.
