# Daily party
## 
### 
All Daily staffers had better attend
the end of the quarter party to be held
this Friday at 8 p.m. at Chez Mum
ford, the home of Richard Martinez.
Tantalizing and intoxicating hors
d'oeurves and libations will be forced
down your throats. Directions to the
party's location can be obtained at the
Daily office.
