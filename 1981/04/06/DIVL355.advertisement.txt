Berlin Scholarship
The Free University of Berlin (FU) announces its annual scho
larship for former participants of the Stanford Program in Berlin.
The Scholarship is for study at the FU during academic year
1981-82 and provides living allowance, rent reimbursement, book
allowance and health insurance.
If you are a senior or first year graduate student and have
participated in the Stanford Program in Berlin for two quarters, pick
up an information sheet from the Overseas Studies Office, 112 Old
Union APPLICATION DEADLINE IS THURSDAY, APRIL 30,
1981.
