I PLAN ON FUEL BEING $2
OR EVEN $3 PER GALLON
A pilot's license can help you save fuel and travel time One of the
modern fuel efficient aircraft available to club members will let you
reduce trip distances by as much as one-third, by flying in a straight
line And it gives you better mileage than a mid-size car at 2to 4 times
the speed If time, reduced distances, or lower fuel costs per trip could
be worth something to you, join us.
Learning to fly is an unusual experience few share and none forget
Many have obtained a Solo-License for less than $432, or in only 9 to
18 hours of flight time To join, or just for free information, call today
