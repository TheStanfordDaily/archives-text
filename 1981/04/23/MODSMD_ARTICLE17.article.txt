Stanford Glee Club: The Stanford
Glee Club will be performing a free
outdoor concert at the Bechtel l-Cen
ter on Sunday, April 26 at 8 p.m.
Songs from hit musicals will be per
formed, as well as classical pieces by
Handel and Purcel. The public is wel
come.
Pi-Pa Concert: An evening of Pi-Pa
music (Chinese stringed instrument)
will be sponsored by the Center for
East Asian Studies on April 28, Tues
day at 7 p.m. ferluring a dist
inguished young oi-pa player,
Guang-zu Li from the People's Re
public of China. The perfomance will
be given in the Assembly Room of
Bechtel l-Center.
