Contributions
The Daily welcomes expressions of all
viewpoints from its readers. letters should
be kept as brief as possible and are subject
to editing, though efforts are made to consult
with the authors before changes are made.
They must include signature and phone
number. Pseudonyms and initials will not be
used. Unpublished letters cannot be ac
knowledged.
All contributions must be typed, double
spaced on a 57-character line on non-cor
rasable paper. They should be sent to the
opinions editors, The Stanford Daily, Storke
Publications Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
