# This weekend at Coffee House
## 
### 

A Little Night Music, a jazz quintet, will perform Friday at the Coffee House. Their music to soothe the
savage beast will begin at 9 p.m. Admission is free.


Who's on First? Who cares? Jay Martel and Stacy Geiken will address these and other questions Satur
day night at the Coffee House. In addition to their usual short stuff, they will premier Shakespeare's
recently discovered tragedy "Mac Muffin." Also billed are Bernie Yatzman and Friends, who will per
form acoustic music between comedy sets. The show begins at 9 p.m., and is free.
