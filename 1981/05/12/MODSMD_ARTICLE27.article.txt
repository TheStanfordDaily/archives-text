### 
The Stanford
Cinematheque presents a
program entitled " 'The War
Game' and Related Films"
tonight at 7:30 in Terman
Auditorium. "The War
Game" is an award-winning,
hypothetical "documen

nuclear attack.

war and its effects will be
shown.
