# The 						Stanford Daily
## 
### 
The Stanford Daily is an independent student newspaper owned and published by The
Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation Main office Storke Student Publications Building.
Stanford, CA 94305
Published Monday through Friday during the academic year, with the exceptions of
Dead Week, when one issue is published, and finals week, when no issues are published
Published Tuesdays and Fridays during the eight-week summer quarter Special sports
issues are published at home football and other special sporting events.
Telephone Editorial (4151 497-4632, Business (415) 497-2554
Entered as second class matter at the Post Office of Palo Alto under the act of March 3.
