# Carillon concert
## 
### 
Miiford Myhre, here shown at a carillon keyboard, will give
a recital next Monday noon on the Hoover Tower bells. Myhre
is the regular carillonneur at the Bok Tower Gardens in Lake
Wales, Florida, and has been described by Stanford's resident
carillonneur, James Angell, as "the most highly regarded
carillonneur in North America." The recital will include
works by Mozart, Marcello and others, and will last approx
imately 45 minutes.
