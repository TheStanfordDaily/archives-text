# Art
## 
### 
Campus
Masters of Photography: Special
Exhibition in Stanford Museum of
Art with selections from Bay Area
collections, including Fox Talbot,
Fenton, Cameron, Bisson Freres, Em
erson, Atget, Stieglitz, Strand, and
Minor White. Docent tours at 12:15
p.m and Sundays at 3 p.m. Museum
open Tues.-Fri., 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.,
and weekends 1 to 5 p.m.
Japanese Prints: Images of For
eigners: Special Exhibition in
Miedel Room of Stanford Museum of
Art May 5 through June 14. Docent
Tours Thursdays at 12:15 p.m. and
Sundays at 2 p.m. Prints from the
McGovern Collection illustrate,
often in a fanciful way, how the
Japanese viewed foreigners from
1615 to 1912.
Master of Fine Art* Graduate Ex
hibition: Will be presented at Stan
ford Museum of Art, May 10 through
June 14. Exhibiting artists will be
Nevin Berger, printmaker; Paul
Gleason, painter; and Richard Putz,
Sculptor. The show consists of
selected works completed during the
artists' two years residence at Stan
ford.
