# 'Working'
## 
### 
Stern Hall's Serra House
will present Working, a mu
sical based on Studs Terkel's
book of the same name May
2 -23 in the Serra lounge.
Working concerns the pain,
frustration, and pride ot the
working class, as each char
acter tries to invest his or her
job with significance, even if
society at large will never
recognize it.
The show is directed by
Lindsay Chag. Admission is
free, but seating is limited.
