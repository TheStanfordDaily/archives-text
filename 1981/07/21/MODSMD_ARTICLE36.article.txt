Staff Lunch
Once again, editors, writers,
and hangers-on are invited to
the fabulous Daily staff lunch.
However, today, in a startling
break from tradition, the lunch
will be held at 1 p.m.! All who
attend this revolutionary meet
ing will thrill to the wisdom of
our esteemed Editor, and M.E.,
while munching on that de
licious Daily popcorn. Happy
belated 21, Bnb! — you may be
out of out sight, but you're never
out of our minds.
