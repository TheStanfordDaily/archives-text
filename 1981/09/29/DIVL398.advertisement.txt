REGISTRATION
Registration for Autumn will be Monday and
Tuesday, September 28 and 29, 1981. Usually
on registration days most students pick up their
registration materials (Permits to Register —
PTRs) at Memorial Auditorium and complete re
gistration inside Maples Pavilion. For this regist
ration we have changed the procedure of the
PTR distribution: on Monday PTRs will be avail
able at Memorial Auditorium and on Tuesday
they will be available outside Maples Pavilion on
the Campus Drive side. Specific registration de
tails are in the Autumn Time Schedule.
