Mural for El Centro Chicano unveiled
MURAL UNVEILED — Bay Area Chicano artist Malaquias Montoya explained the political significance of
his mural at its unveiling yesterday. It is the first mural by a Chicano approved for outdoor display here
and will be on exhibit fall quarter above the entrance to El Centro Chicano.
