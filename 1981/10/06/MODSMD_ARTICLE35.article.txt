Memorials
A memorial service for Jeffery
Smith, emeritus professor of philoso
phy who died Sept. 27, will be held
this afternoon at 4:15 p.m. in Memo
rial Church. Smith, who was 75,
taught at Stanford after being on the
faculty at Columbia University and
use.
A service for former Stanford stu
dent Gordon Jones, who died Friday
evening after an extended illness,
will be held tomorrow at 5 p.m. in
Memorial Church. Jones, a freshman
last year, lived in Donner House. He
was from Gaviote, California.
