# Mideast forum page
## 
### 
Anwar Sadat is dead. President Reagan wants to sell
/XWACS to Saudi Arabia. The Arab world is divided
and Egypt's political future is uncertain. The Mideast
is in turmoil.
What does all this mean? What will happen tomor
row, next month, next year? What is America's role in
this region in the days ahead?
We think these questions are critical. For that reason,
the Daily is planning a special forum page for Tues
day, Oct. 27. Submissions must be typed, double
spaced, on a 57-space line. Optimum length is 750
words. Bring your columns to the Daily opinions of
fice by 2 p.m. Friday, Oct. 23.
This is your forum — tell us what you think.
