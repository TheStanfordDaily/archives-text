HARRY S. TRUMAN
SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM
— Faculty Representative —
Professor Hubert R. Marshall, Political Science
Sophomores committed to a career in the public service may apply to be
Stanford University's nominees for a Harry S. Truman Scholarship.
A Truman Scholarship covers tuition, fees, books, and room and board,
up to a maximum of $5,000 annually for the remainder of a student's
undergraduate career and for up to two years of graduate study.
Seventy-nine Scholarships are awarded nationally.
Contact Andrea Garwood
in the Undergraduate Studies Office
Building 1
