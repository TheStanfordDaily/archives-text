# Adv. 29 Page 41
##

Help us to help you better . . .
We want to help you to help yourselves by telling you how the
Daily handles publicity and decides to cover events and or
ganizations around campus.
We need ideas, and your criticism, to do a better job
spreading the word about what's going on here. We also want to
hear your thoughts about all other aspects of the Daily.
On Tuesday, the Daily will host an open forum meeting to
meet and talk with any interested members of the Stanford com
munity. We'll talk briefly about publicity for your concerns,
then open it up to a general discussion about the Daily.
Open forum meeting —Tuesday, Nov. 17 Tresidder, Room 282
