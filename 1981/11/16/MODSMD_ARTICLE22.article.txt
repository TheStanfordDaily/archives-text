# What do you find funny?
## 
### 
Each week, we devote an entire page exclusively to lighter in
terpretations of both serious and not-so-serious issues; regular
columnists treat their subjects satirically or in a humorous vein.
However, we need your contributions.
Which public figures need a good, irreverent shakedown?
What campus issues need satirical treatment? Tell us, on 57-char
acter double-spaced typed copy, and bring your submissions to
the Daily office.
We are located in the Storke Student Publications Building,
and at least one intrepid staffer can usually be found at almost
any hour of the day or night.
