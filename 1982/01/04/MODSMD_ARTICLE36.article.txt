Daily staffers
Yes, Virginia, the Daily is publish
ing this week. And, yes, we need all
staffers to come in and take story and
photo assignments and sign up for
night and wire editing spots. The first
week back is tough, but you know
what they say: "When the going gets
tough, the tough need help."
Staff Lunch
Everyone is invited to taste the excit
ing new 1982 version of the usual Dai
ly lunch fare. This year's best will be

served up at noon this Thursday at
the Daily offices. If your New Year's
resolution was never to miss another
staff lunch, here's your chance to
make good.
Money
There are still some unclaimed
checks, which may very well find
their way into the Pizza for Starving
Editors Fund. Biz side says that we
can't write off the checks as a tax loss,
so even though you may be amazed
to be paid, drop by for your check
when you pick up a story (hint, hint).
