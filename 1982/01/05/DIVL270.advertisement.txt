Daily needs a flight OWI with a car to IJ
deliver a small package to our printer in San
Jose. The run needs to be made each night the
paper is printed, Sunday through Thursday
from between 1 a.m. and 3 a.m.
The printer is located 25 miles south via
Route 280. A round trip should take around 1
hour. The fee is negotiable. For further
information call Andrew Preston, at 497-2554.
