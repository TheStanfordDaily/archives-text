# Adv. 5 Page 3

Teach in Japan
Persons wirh a degree in such fields as engineering, business
adminisrrarion, finance, pharmacology, linguistics, languages or
education wishing ro reach adults for one or two years in Jokyo
should write to: Personnel Director
International Education Services
Shin Taiso Dldg.
10-7, Dogenzako 2-chome
Shibuyo-ku
Tokyo 1 50
Japan
Persons wirh overseas reaching experience will also be consi
dered.
Instructors employed by I.E.S. will reach Japanese businessmen
and engineers English as a second language and rhe terminology
in their own fields of study or job-experience.
No Japanese-language is required for classroom instruction.
Teaching experience is nor required. An orienrorion and training
are given in Tokyo.
Informarion on salary, transportation and housing can be ob
tained by providing International Education Services wirh a de
tailed resume and a letter indicating an interest in rhe position.
Personal interviews will be held in Seattle. San Francisco and Los
Angeles in May, 1982.
Selected applicants would be expected to arrive in Tokyo from
July, 1982 ro October, 1982.
