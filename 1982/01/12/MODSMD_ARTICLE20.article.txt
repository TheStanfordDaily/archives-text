pression and dancing as well as classi

33. Exercise tor Everyone: relaxation,

from Israel. Old Union Clubhouse,

experience, taught by Stuart Haber,

eight years. Wednesdays, 6-7:30 p.m.,
Old Union Clubhouse Ballroom. Calli
graphy: a study of personal Italic for
mation and Old English lettering, taught
by Billie Odell. Monday evenings, Tres
idder Room 132. Tarot reading: also by
Billie Odell, covering attitudes about
tarot, how to relate the symbols to hu
man experience, and how the cards In

by Mark Hansen. Wednesdays, be
ginners 3-4 p.m., intermediate, 4-5
p.m., and advanced 5-6 p.m. For more
