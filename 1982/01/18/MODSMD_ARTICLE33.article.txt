# Fencers take dual matches
## 
### 
The Stanford varsity men's
and women's fencing squads
competed over the weekend
and both came through vic
toriously.
The men beat Cal-State Full
erton 29-14, and Cal-State

Northridge, 27-16.
The women, led by junior
Pam Hench who was 6-1 on the
day, beat Northridge 9-7,
which was something of an up
set since the Northridge team
had not lost a dual meet in one

and a half years.
In the sabre weapons squad,
Howard Cash, a junior, and Jeff
Gueble, a senior, were both
5-1.
In the epee, sophomore
Nick Blevins was undefeated
