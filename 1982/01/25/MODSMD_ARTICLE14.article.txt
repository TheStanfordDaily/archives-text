# Wanted: 10 Daily columnists
## 
### 
— Who's thinking out there? —
After Feb. 7, the old Daily columnists will be doing the cream chicken circuit
while you get a crack at influencing the Stanford community. The opinions
editors for the new volume will be selecting biweekly columnists. Please
submit a timely sample column, one additional writing sample and a brief
proposal describing the issues that incite you to put your opinions in print.
The column should be typed, double-spaced, on a 57-space line,
approximately 800 words long. Bring your political, social, environmental,
academic, etc. opinions to the opinions office at the Daily in the Storke
Publications Building by sp.m. Monday, Feb. 7.
