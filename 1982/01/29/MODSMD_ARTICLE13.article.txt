Scientists and engineers unite!
Approximately 150 students gathered Saturday in Terman Engineering Building to
talk to recruiters from 29 companies about jobs in science and engineering. The
fifth annual job fair was sponsored by three minority engineering societies: Wom
en in Science and Engineering, Stanford Black Scientists and Engineers, and Stan
ford Society of Chicano Engineers and Scientists.
