Reagan visits FDR exhibit
President and Mrs. Ronald Reagan pay a visit to the National Museum of American
History at the Smithsonian Institution in Washington to view the exhibit "Franklin
Delano Roosevelt: The Intimate Presidency" which opened to the public Jan. 30.
This exhibit commemorates the 100 th anniversary of the birth of FDR.
