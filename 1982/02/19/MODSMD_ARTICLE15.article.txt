The Stanford Daily (USPS 518-420) is an independent student newspaper owned and published by

Sunday and school vacations. Main office; Storke Student Publications Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
Telephone: Editorial (415) 497-4632; Business (415) 497-2554.
Nothing on the opinions page necessarily represents a position of the entire Daily staff, or theLeland
Stanford lunior University. Editorials represent the opinion of a majority of editorial board members
voting on each topic. Ihe editorial board consists of the editor, managing editor, two news editors,
two opinions editors and three at-large members elected by the Daily staff. Af-large members for
Volume 181 are Kathy Bodovit*. Brad Crystal and Sally Newman.
letters, columns and cartoons represent only the views of their authors. The Daily regrets that it
cannot guarantee the return of any article submitted. All submitted articles are subject to editing.
