# Endorsements
## 
### 
Groups seeking Daily endorsement for their fee
assessments in trie spring election should sign up for
an interview and submit an election statement (with
general and financial information) by Thursday. Sign
up at the Daily opinions office. Any endorsement will
be based primarily on the written statement (one
page maximum, please), and there will be space pro
vided for responses to Daily decisions.
