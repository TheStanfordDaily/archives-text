BLACK PERSPECTIVES IN
ENGINEERING
WED 7-9:30 PM ERL 126 (3 Units-VTS)
This course is designed to assist students in understanding
the basic benefits and opportunities available to Blacks in
engineering. Experts will lecture on topics such as social
obligations of students and engineers, problems faced by
Black engineers, Black women in engineering, and
contributions of Blacks in technology.
