# Linksters win
## 
### 
Behind medalist Lori
Castillo's 67, the women's golf
team bettered both San Jose
and Sacramento States in a
three-way meet Friday. The
one-day event was originally
scheduled earlier this month,
but was cancelled because of
rain.
The Card totaled 290 strokes
over the par-72 Haggin Oaks
Golf Course in Sacramento.
San Jose State finished two
shots behind Castillo and com
pany, with Sacramento State a
distant third at 314.
In a three-day tournament
over the weekend, the men's
team placed sixth in the San Di
ego State Aztec Invtational at
Torrey Pines Golf Course.
