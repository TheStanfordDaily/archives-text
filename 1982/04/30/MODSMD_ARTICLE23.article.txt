# Exhibition set
## 
### 
Five members of the San
Francisco 49ers will play in a
special volleyball exhibition at
Menlo-Atherton high school
tonight as part of a benefit for
Partners, a local, non-profit or
ganization that provides in
tervention services for trou
bled youth.
The preliminary match starts
at 7 p.m and will be followed by
the celeberty match featuring
such 49er stars as Bill Ring, Milt
McColl, Rick Gervais, Archie
Reese, and Bobby Leopold.
The feature match of the
evening will be between the
Stanford volleyball team and
the NorCal Champ Village
Host-Capitol Coors team.
