■OFF-CAMPUS STUDENTS—i
If you live off-campus now and will be moving out of
your place in June or September, we need your help.
The Rental Housing Information Office wants to pass
your apartment, house, or room-in-a-house on to
another Stanford student. Please call us at 497-3906,
or stop by our office in Old Union, room 315 to give us
a listing (no charge).
And hurry, our Spring Clearinghouse
starts May 24!
