NOTICE OF ELECTION
Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of Section 5528 of the
California Public Resources Code, that a general District election to be
consolidated with the County-wide General Election will be held in the
Midpeninsula Regional Open Space District on the 2nd day of November,
1982 to choose a successor for four (4) offices of Director to be elected in
Ward(s) 1, 2, 5 and 6.
'Generally, the wards include the following areas:
Ward 1 Los Gatos, Monte Sereno, Saratoga, portion Cupertino.
Ward 2 Los Altos, Los Altos Hills, portions Cupertino and Sunnyvale.
Ward 5 Palo Alto, Stanford, portions Los Altos Hills and East Palo Alto.
Ward 6 Menlo Park, Atherton, Ladera, Portola Valley, Woodside, portions
East Palo Alto, Emerald Lake Hills, Farm Hills and Skyline.
The qualifications required by the principal act for each office for which
candidates may be nominated are: Each Director shall be a resident and an
elector from the ward from which he or she is nominated.
Official nominating petitions for eligible candidates desiring to file for any
of the elective offices may be obtained from the Office of the Clerk of the
District, 375 Distel Circle, Suite »1, Los Altos, CA (Telephone Number
(415) 965- 4717). Said nominations shall be filed in the office of the Clerk of
the District not later than 5 P.M. on Friday, August 6, 1982.
Dated this 7 day of July, 1982.
