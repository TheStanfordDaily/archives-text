PETE F CMP' RENT
Rental applications for the new Peter Coutts Hill Con
dominiums and floor plans of the various unit types are
available from:
Lucille Long, Manager of Faculty/Staff Housing
105 Encina Hall • (497-2238).
Prices range from $840 to $1140
per mo. for 2BR to 3BR/Study units.
ATTENTION:"——
Working Students
Effective August 7, 1982, individuals
exempting themselves from paying FICA
and/or VDI because of student status, will not
be allowed to claim this exempt status unless
they are enrolled as a student during the
same period they are employed here at Stan
ford; i.e., if exempting during summer
employment, they must be enrolled as a
summer student. Individuals who are pre
sently working at Stanford and claiming stu
dent exempt status, but not enrolled as a
summer student will have FICA and/or VDI
withheld on their August 7 paychecks.
When those individuals re-enroll as stu
dents and continue working for.the University,
they should re-submit a new W-4 to re
establish the exempt status to which they are
entitled.
