DEAD WEEK
Dead Week is the week immediately
preceding final examinations in Summer
Quarter. During this period, no quizzes or
examinations should be given, and no mus
ical, dramatic and athletic events involving
compulsory student participation should
be scheduled. No classes should fee held on
the day before finals.
Dead Week dates this quarter: Satur
day, August 7th through Thursday, August
1 2th.
