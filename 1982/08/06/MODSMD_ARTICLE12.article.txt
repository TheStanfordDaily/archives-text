# Once Around The Quad Policy
## 
### 
Once Around the Quad Announcements are
printed Tuesday and Friday during the summer as
a service to the public. Deadline for announce
ments is two business days before publication at 1
p.m. Announcements should be placed in the ap
propriate slot just inside the Daily front door.
Only events which are free and open to the public
may appear in Once Around the Quad.
