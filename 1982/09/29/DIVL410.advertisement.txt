Manage Tresidder Union's
outdoor barbeque, The
Patio Grill, and test your skills!
WHY: To improve Tresidder Union Food Services through
greater student involvement
HOW: Through selection of a student entrepreneurial group to
manage The Patio Grill beginning October 17, 1982. The
group will be expected to develop the menu, hire and train
employees, market the operation, keep inventory, and main
tain Financial statements.
REQUIREMENTS: Reliability, responsibility, efficiency, good
organizational skills and creativity are desired. Marketing
ana some financial experience are highly recommended.
TO APPLY:
1. Pick up application at the Tresidder Union Information
Desk.
2. Attend an informational meeting on Friday, October 1 at
3:00 p.m. in Tresidder Union Room 282.
3. Submit a bid by Monday, October 4 at 5:00 p.m.
