# Columnists
## 
### 
Do you have a fresh perspective —
serious or satirical —on an important
issue?Do you want to express your
views on political, cultural or social
questions to the entire Stanford
community? If so, the Daily
encourages you to become a regular
bi-weekly columnist.
Bring your sample columns to the
Daily office by 2 p.m. Monday, Oct.
3. All columns should be typed double
spaced on a 57-character line.
