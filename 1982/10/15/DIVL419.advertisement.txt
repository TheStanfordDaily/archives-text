HARRYS TRUMAN
SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM
-Faculty Representative-
Professor Hubert R. Marshall, Political Science
Sophomores committed to a career in the public service
may apply to be Stanford University's nominees for a
Harry S. Truman Scholarship. An informational meeting
will be held Tuesday, October 19 at 7:00 p.m. in the
Meyer Library Forum Room. Interested first year stu
dents may also attend.
A Truman Scholarship covers tuition, fees, books, and
room and board, up to a maximum of $5,000 annually for
the remainder of a student's undergraduate career and
for up to two years of graduate study. Approximately 100
Scholarships are awarded nationally.
If you are unable to attend the meeting, contact Andrea
Garwood in the Undergraduate Research office, Build
ing 1, Room 2-U.
APPLICATION DEADLINE NOVEMBER 15, 1982
