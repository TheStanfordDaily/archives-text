# Election '82: Where do you stand?
## 
### 
Voters in the area will have to decide next
Tuesday, Nov. 2, on propositions which would
require stricter gun registration laws and would
urge President Reagan to negotiate a nuclear
weapons freeze with the Soviet Union. They
must also choose between Jerry Brown and Pete
Wilson, Tom Bradley and George Deukmejian,
Tom Lantos and Bill Royer, Emmett Lynch and
Ed Zschau, as well as other candidates. What
are your opinions? Send them to the Daily.
