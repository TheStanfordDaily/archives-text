# Surfers beat Humboldt
## 
### 
The Stanford surfing club,
paced by seniors Jim Lightner's
and Erik Olson's first and second
place finishes, easily defeated
Humboldt State at North
Salmon Creek in Sonoma Coun
ty Saturday.
Stanford freshman Ron
Ratcliffe, who completed a two
second tube ride, finished fourth.
Three surfers competed for
each side in 3-5 foot medium
sized waves.
Stanford travels to Southern
California in December to take
on the University of California at
San Diego.
