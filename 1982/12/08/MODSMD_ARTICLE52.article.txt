# Fencers fall
## 
### 
Highly-touted San Jose State
narrowly defeated both Stan
ford's men's and women's fenc
ing teams Saturday.
The Spartans beat the
Cardinal men 16-11. Stanford
was led by freshman Elliot Chew,
who finished 3-0 in the foil.
San Jose State edged the wom
en 11-5. The Cardinal was led by
freshman Jennifer Yu, who went
3-1 in the foil.
