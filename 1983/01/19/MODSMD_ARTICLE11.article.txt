The outside world intrudes
Sunlight trickles down a wall, signalling afternoon to the population of the CERAS
lobby, home to LOTS and a changing but numerous collection of computer science
students. The skylight is used by the students as a way of keeping track of how many days
they have left before their assignments are due; one period of light and one period of dark
mean that 24 hours, or one 'May,* have passed.
