# Fencers win duals in City
## 
### 
The Stanford fencing teams
steamrolled to two more vic
tories in San Francisco Satur
day, the women blanking San
Francisco State 16-0 and the
men trouncing the University
of San Francisco 18-9.
The women's team was led
by senior Pam Henck and
Cathy Correa, sophomore Ali
son Reid and freshman Jen
nifer Vu, all 4-0. They have now
won 72 of their last 80 indi
vidual bouts.
For the men, freshman Elliott
Cheu rolled to his 24th straight
win without loss to lead the
way for the Cardinal, and
junior Nik Blevins also gained a
3-0 decision in the epee.
