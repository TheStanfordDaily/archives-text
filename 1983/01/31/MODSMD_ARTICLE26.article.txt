# Davis beats Leach; intrasquad at home
## 
### 
Scott Davis qualified for the U.S. Pro Indoor Tennis Champion
ships with a 6-2, 6-2 win over defending NCAA champion Mike
Leech of Michigan yesterday while his Stanford teammates
slugged it out at home in an intrasquad match.
Davis will play another qualifying player today in the first round
and, if he wins, will face Ivan Lendl in the second round. Lendl
has won 55 consecutive indoor matches on the pro circuit.
At the Farm, Stanford's white team defeated the red team 5-4.
Eric Rosenfeld (white) defeated Dan Goldie (red) 6-2, 4-6, 7-6;
Mark McKeen (white) defeated )ohn Letts (red) 6-2, 6-3; )im
Grabb (white) defeated Jeremy Semple (red) 6-2, 6-2; Rod Coull
(red) defeated Geordie Mckee (white) 6-2, 6-3; Andy Winter
bauer (white) defeated Paul Robinson (red) 6-3, 6-4; and Daryl Lee
(red) defeated George Harding (white) 6-2, 6-2.
In doubles, McKeen-Grabb (white) defeated Goldie-Semple
(red) 2-6, 6-1, 6-3; Letts-Robinson (white) defeated Rosenfeld-
McKee (red) 6-2, 6-3; and Coull-Lee (red) defeated Winterbauer
and John DeVincenzo (white) 6-0, 6-4.
Stanford, rated No. 8 in the nation, will play in the National
Team Championships at UCLA Thursday through Sunday. The
Cardinal will face No. 10 Clemson in the first round.
