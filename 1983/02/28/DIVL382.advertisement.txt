The Department of Anthropology
and Overseas Studies
are now accepting
APPUCATIONS for SUMMER 1983
INDEPENDENT FIELDWORK
PROGRAM IN SALAMANCA
Four to six students may participate in this ten week (June
20-August 30, 1983) summer program Participants will
earn 15 units of Stanford Overseas Studies credit while
conducting interviews and observations focused upon an
approved topic of their choice. Possibilities include
observing family structures, subsistence systems, and
local-to-supralocal change in and around Salamanca,
Spain. Those selected will enroll in ANTH 93 during the
Spring Quarter before departure and in ANTH 94 upon
their return to campus Autumn Quarter. A minimum of 5
quarters of Spanish is required before departure.
APPLICATION DEADLINE: FRIDAY, MARCH 4
