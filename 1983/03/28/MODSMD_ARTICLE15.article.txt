# ASSU Candidates
## 
### 
All ASSU candidates who wish to
be considered for a Daily endorse
ment should prepare a written
statement, which is due Wednesday,
March 30 at 10 p.m. For our con
venience, please provide nine
copies of this statement. Statements
hy senate candidates, fee assess
ment sponsors and ballot measure
sponsors should be no longer than
one page, while those by COP slates

should not exceed two pages.
\
Senate candidates must also ar
range an interview with the Daily
editorial board; sign-ups for this will
be posted in the Daily office Tues
day, March 29 at 5 p.m.
COP slates should contact Karen
Springen, Tim Whalen or Steve
Trousdale to arrange an interview
time.
