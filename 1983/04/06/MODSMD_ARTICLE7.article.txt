# NEWS
## Down to the lake in sailboats 
### 

The boathouse has reinstalled their fleet of sailboats in the familiar dock-side berths, another encouraging
sign that spring is finally here. The recent appearance of the sun over Stanford has brought on a flurry of
lake-side activity and a variety of water-craft—from wind-surfers to rubber rafts—have been spotted
re-discovering the reaches of Lake Lagunita.
