# Adv. 21 Page 6
## 
### 
SENATOR PAUL TSONGAS
speaks on
"The Importance of Student
Involvement in Arms Control"
MONDAY at 12:00 NOON
TERMAN AUDITORIUM
Senator Tsongas, D-Mass, is an influential member of the
Senate Foreign Relations Committee. He has led the
opposition to Kenneth Adelman and last year co-sponsored a
Senate freeze resolution.
Tsongas' appearance is sponsored by the
Stanford Arms Control and Disarmament Forum.
