# Football in April?
## 
### 

Stanford football plavers hit both the books and the pads spring quarter with the beginning of spring football. Practices
are every Monday, Tuesday, Thursday and Friday from 3:15 p.m. to 5:15 p.m. and continue through May 7. The
traditional intrasquad scrimmage that ends spring football is scheduled for May 7 at 10 a.m.
