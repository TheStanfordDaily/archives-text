# Brown at Hoover
## 
### 

Greg Webb/Daily
Former Gov. Jerry Brown leaves Hoover Tower yesterday after
meeting with Hoover Director W. Glenn Campbell and Hoover
Senior Fellow Martin Anderson inside the Tower. While on campus,
Brown also met with Sidney Drell, deputy director of SLAC. The
two-time presidential and one-time senatorial candidate is reported
to be touring the Bay Area to discuss arms control with local
experts.
