# Stanford challenge
## 
### 
The second annual Stanford
Challenge is scheduled for
Saturday, beginning at 10 a.m.
at Angell Field.
The "challenge" consists of
six events run in the following
order: 880-yard run, bike race
to Lake Lagunita, quarter-mile
swim, Dish run, bike race to
Angell Field, innertube water
race and finally a run through
an agility course.
Competition can be indi
vidual or in teams of two or six
persons. The Stanford Chal
lenge is free to Stanford stu
dents, faculty, staff and af
filiates, with registration start
ing at 9 a.m. Saturday.
