# Stars shine at Cardinal Club celebrity golf tourney
## Clubs and carts 
### 
Sports stars graced Stan
ford Golf Course Monday at
the Cardinal Club Celebrity
Golf Tournament, with the
proceeds from the event
going to women's athletics at
Stanford. The 180 total
players were grouped into
fivesomes in a best-ball for
mat. Each team was headed
by a celebrity. Clockwise
from upper left: Stanford
quarterback John Elway, San
Francisco 49ers quarterback
)oe Montana, Los Angeles
Raiders quarterback Jim
Plunkett, 49er Randy Cross
and baseball great Willie
Mays.
