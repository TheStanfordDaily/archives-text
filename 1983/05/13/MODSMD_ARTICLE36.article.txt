# THE
						STANFORD DAILY
## EIGHTH ANNUAL PHOTOGRAPHY CONTEST 
### 
Contest Judges:
Tony Grant
Mark Olson
Chuck Painter
The Eighth Annual Stanford Daily Photography
Contest received 1 26 of the finest in black and white
prints produced by the Stanford community. The
winners of each of the three categories were chosen by
a panel of three judges: Tony Grant, MFA graduate
student in photography; Mark Olson, art director of
The Stanford Magazine and Chuck Painter, chief
photographer at News and Publications.
Winners of the competition will receive cash prizes of
$25, $15 and $10.

We would like to thank all the participants of the photo contest
Prints will be held at the Daily office until May 27, 1983
