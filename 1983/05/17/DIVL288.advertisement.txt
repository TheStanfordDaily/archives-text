ENGINEERS! —
Silicon Valley Group, the
leading manufacturers of
microprocessor controled
semiconductor automatic
processing equipment will be
interviewing on campus
MAY 19 for the following
positions.
- ELECTRICAL ENGINEER with
digital circuit, logic design,
computer science courses and
analog coursework
- MECHANICAL ENGINEER
coursework in mechanical
dynamics and machine design
and possibly drafting and/or
computer science background.
- SOFTWARE ENGINEER
editing and coding, some
documentation, and eventual
systems designs.
Please contact the Career Planning and
Placement Center for interview
arrangements!
