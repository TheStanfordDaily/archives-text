# A
						Stanford Ride in space
## 
### 

Dean of Admissions Fred Hargadon and Debbie
Duncan, president of the Stanford Bay Area Pro
fessional Women's Club, unfurl a scroll dedicated
to Stanford alumna Sally Ride. Ride will be the first
American woman in space when she travels
aboard the space shuttle set to be launched June
18. Hargadon will travel to Cape Kennedy, Florida,

Greg Webb/Daily
to present the scroll to Ride who will also take a red
Stanford pennant with her aboard the shuttle. Al
ready containing more than 100 signatures, the
scroll will be available for signing from noon to 1
p.m. in White Pla/a and at Bowman Alumni House
other times during the day.
