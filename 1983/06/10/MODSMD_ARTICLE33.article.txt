# More museums
## Bay Area offers artistic variety 
### 
San Francisco Museum of Modern Art. The San Francisco
Museum of Modern Art, located at Van Ness and McAllister
in San Franicsco, features rotating exhibits. It is open from
10 a.m until 6 p.m. Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday, 10
a.m. to 10 p.m. Thursday, and 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Saturday
and Sunday.
Asian Art Museum. After enjoying roller skating in San
Francisco's Golden Gate Park, stop by the Asian Art
Museum at Eighth Avenue and Kennedy Drive. It is open
every day from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.. The museum is currently
featuring the Treasures from the Shanghai Museum: 6000
years of Chinese Art. Tickets are available for this exhibit
through the BASS ticket agency, located at the Tresidder
Ticket Office.
