# Mosher appeals
## 
### 
Steven Mosher has filed
another appeal in his continuing
battle to win reinstatement at a
Ph.D. candidate in anthropology
here.
The appeal was received July
21 in the Office of Vice-Provost
Gerald Lieberman, dean of
graduate studies and research,
who currently is on vacation
until August.
Mosher was terminated as
Ph.D. candidate in anthropology
by an 11-0 vote of the depart
ment faculty Feb. 24.
