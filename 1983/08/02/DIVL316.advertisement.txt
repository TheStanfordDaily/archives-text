DEAD WEEK
Dead week is the week immediately preceding
final examinations Summer Quarter. During
this period, no quizzes or examinations should
be given, and no musical, dramatic, and athletic
events involving compulsory student participa
tion should be scheduled.
Dead Week dates this quarter are Saturday,
August 6 through Thursday, August 11, in
clusive.
