YOU CAN EXPERIENCE
HYPNOSIS
FREE OF CHARGE.
The Psychology Department is
conducting a search for
individuals who are
hypnotizeable.
If you have ever wondered
what hypnosis is like, or
whether you can be
hypnotized, you now have an
opportunity to find out in a safe,
confidential environment.
Students who are
hypnotizeable will be given an
opportunity to participate in
further studies using hypnosis?
and will receive a small
honorarium (about $5/ hour) for
participation in those additional
studies.
For further information
call 497-2889 from 9-5.
