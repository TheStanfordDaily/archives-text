STANFORD OVERSEAS STUDIES
POLAND
FIELD TRIP!
Students attending Stanford Overseas Centers in Berlin and Vienna this
spring (1984), have an exceptional opportunity to participate in a ten day field
trip to Poland. Two quarters of German enable you to apply for either the
Berlin or Vienna programs.
PLAN NOW! Sign up for German language courses in order to take
advantage of this exciting educational opportunity.
APPLICATION DEADLINE DEC. 12, 1983.
FOR MORE INFORMATION VISIT
OVF.RSFAS STUDIES OLD UNION 112 —or call 497-3555.
