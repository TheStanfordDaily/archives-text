# Have It Your Way
## 
### 
Become a regular
Daily columnist and let the
whole community see
things your way.

Each copy of The Daily reaches more than 20,000
readers. If you become a bi-weekly columnist,
people will be debating your views—or chuckling at
your humor—across campus and throughout the
area. Whether you have a particular specialty or just
like to write, apply for a Daily columnist position
today.
Sample columns should be typed,
double-spaced on a 57-character line. Drop columns
in the opinions box at The Daily or give them to
Kevin or Bob. While you're here, fill out the brief
columnist application. Deadline is Monday.
