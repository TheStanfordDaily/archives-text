INTER-CONTINENTAL
30 college/university students, drawn from across
the United States, will live and study in NAIROBI,
CAIRO, JERUSALEM, and LONDON from Jan. 8
to May 9, 1984. Accompanied by internationally
known senior professors, the selected students will
live with local families and carry a full course load as
they explore how societies represent and interpret
themselves to themselves and outsiders.
The International Honors Program seeks mature,
motivated candidates who are prepared for a
firtfNling academic experience. For further
information and an application, please call, collect,
Marshall Strauss at 617-491-3120.
