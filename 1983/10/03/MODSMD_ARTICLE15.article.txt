# Last Chance
## 
### 
All applicants for the position of biweekly
columnist must turn in a sample cloumn and
brief application by 5 p.m. today. All submis
sions will be reviewed and columnists will be
notified later this week.
Readers are encouraged to submit letters to
the editor. Letters and columns should be
typed, double-spaced on a 57-character line
and deposited in the opinions box at The Daily
office. Letters should be as brief as possible
and are subject to editing.
