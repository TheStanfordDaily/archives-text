# Entertainment
## KZSU Picks 
### 
The KZSU PICKS are a new addition to the
Daily Entertainment section. The records
that will appear here each week are those
that the music staff at KZSU feels have ex

ceptional merit. Tracks off these albums will
be highlighted during ZOO Preview airing
Tuesdays from 5-8 p.m. and on Dance for
Your Dinner Thursdays from 5-8 p.m.
