# Memorial services
## 
### 
A memorial service for Physics
Prof. Felix Bloch wili be held today
at 3:30 in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Bloch, a prominent physicist here
since 1934, received the Nobel prize
in physics in 1952 for discovering,
along with Edward Purcell, the nu
clear magnetic resonance effect.
