HARRY S. TRUMAN
SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM
- Faculty Representative -
Professor Hubert R. Marshall, Political Science
Sophomores committed to a career in the public service may apply to
be Stanford University's nominees for a Harry S. Truman
Scholarship. An informational meeting will be held MONDAY,
OCTOBER 17 at 8:00 p.m. in the Meyer Library Forum Room.
Interested first year students may also attend.
A I ruman Scholarship covers tuition, fees, books, and room and
board, up to a maximum of $5,000 annually lor the remainder of a
student's undergraduate career and for up to two years of graduate
study. Approximately 100 Scholarships are awarded nationally.
If you are unable to attend the meeting, contact
Sally Beckham in the H&S Dean's office, Building 1.
APPLICATION DEADLINE - NOVEMBER 14, 1983
