ANNOUNCEMENT TO
MEMBERS OF THE
ACADEMIC COUNCIL
The Steering Committee of the Sixteenth Senate of the Academic Council is
planning to hold several Academic Council discussion meetings on issues
associated with the Reagan Library proposal. The purpose of these meet
ings will be for individual faculty members to express their opinions to
members of the Senate. Each meeting will be attended by Senate members
and will be chaired by a member of the Steering Committee.
ALL MEMBERS OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL ARE INVITED
TO ATTEND.
Wednesday, November 2, 1983
3:00-5:00 p.m., Building 320 (Geology Corner) Room 334
Chair: Professor Kenneth Arrow (Economics)
Thursday, November 3, 1983
4:15-6:15 p.m. Building 200 (History Corner) Room 2
Chair: Porofessor Sanford Dornbusch (Sociology)
