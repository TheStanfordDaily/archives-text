Fearless Don
That tireless promoter of public
service, that zealous early morning
jogger, that indefatigable arbiter of
partisan disputes, yes, the one and
only fearless leader of the University
will speak at a staff lunch on
Thursday. All staffers should attend.
Training
H. Millar will hold a computer
training session on Thursday at 4
p.m.
Money, money, money
It's a rich man's world ... if
you're lucky enough to get your
paycheck this month. Old staffers
that were on the payroll last year can
pick up their checks from a dam
good man. Younger staffers will
have to wait until November 15 for
their paychecks due to an adminis
trative mix-up. Dam.
