They were Daily people, and they
were good people. They worked
hard and filled their pages with
interesting graphics and copy.
Every day. Then midterms came,
and the men and women of The
Daily had to resort to more dras
tic measures to fill their pages.
Like bad imitation Hemingway
and plagiarizing graffiti from
dormitory blackboards. Don't
make us do this again. Come by
and pick up a story.
