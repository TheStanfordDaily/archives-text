DON'T GET MAD, GET PUBLISHED
I
Angered by a Daily article? Upset by an opinion? World affairs got you down? Get it
off your chest and into print. Readers are encouraged to write letters to the editor or
viewpoint columns. All submissions should be typed double-spaced on a 57-character
line and placed in the opinions box at The Daily. They should be kept as brief as possi
ble. So stop griping and start typing.
