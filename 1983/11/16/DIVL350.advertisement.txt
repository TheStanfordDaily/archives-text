y Don't get mad, get published
Angered by a Daily article? Upset by an opinion? World
affairs got you down? Get it off your chest and into
print. Readers are encouraged to write letters to the
editor or viewpoint columns. All submissions should be
,typed double-spaced on a 57-character line and placed
in the opinions box at The Daily. They should be kept as
brief as possible. §o stop griping and start typing.
