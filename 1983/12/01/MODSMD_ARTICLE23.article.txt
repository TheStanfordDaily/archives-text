# Haller at forum
## 
### 
The Stanford Law School
will host a forum on agents
and their role in sports at
3:30 p.m. today in room 190
of the Law School.
Among the featured
guests will be San Francisco
Giants' general manager
Tom Haller, pitcher Gary
Lavelle and agent Wayne
Hooper. One of Hooper's
clients is UC-Berkeley foot
ball Coach Joe kapp.
