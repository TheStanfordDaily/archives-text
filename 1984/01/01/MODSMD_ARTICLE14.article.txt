# 'Country Club' facilities open for summer
## 
### 
Stanford is fondly known as
"The Country Club" by those
who have learned to take ad
vantage of the tremendous
facilities available. To join this
elite crowd one need only take
a bit of initiative.
And to make it even more
painless, here is a listing of
University recreational
facilities and their hours for
this summer. Most of them re
quire Stanford ID for use, so if
you're anonymous, you should
read no further.
Encina lockers and showers
are open Monday through
Friday 7:30 a.m. to 9 p.m.; 9
a.m. to 6 p.m. Saturday and
Sunday.
Roble weight training lock
ers and showers are open
Monday through Thursday 8
a.m. to 10 p.m., Friday 8 a.m.
to 7 p.m., noon to 5:30 p.m. on
Saturday and Sunday.
Encina Weight Room is
open for weight training Mon
day through Friday 10 a.m. to
2 p.m. and 4 to 8 p.m.; Satur
day and Sunday noon to 5 p.m.
The Roble Gym Floor is
available over the summer for
basketball, volleyball, fencing,

badminton and open recrea
tion. The schedule is pretty
complicated, so pay attention.
Play is limited to recreational
basketball Monday, Wednes
day and Friday 1 to 3 p.m.;
Tuesday noon to 3 p>.m. Bad
minton is the game in town
Fridays from 3 to 5 p.m. The
gym is open for open recrea
tion Monday and Wednesday 3
to 5 p.m. and 9 to 10 p.m.;
Tuesday and Thursday 3 to 6
p.m. and 8 to 10 p.m.; Friday
from 8 a.m. to noon and 5 to 7
p.m.; Saturday 2 to 5 p.m.;
Sunday noon to 5:30 p.m. If
you need more information,
call 497-4895.
Angell Field Track, Olym
pic Gamefield and Perrier
Parcourse (begins at Roble
Gym) are open every day, all
day; for reserved use call 497-
0505. The Angell field
(infield), Taylor Field, and IM
Fields (North and South) must
be reserved by calling 497-
0505.
Maples Pavilion is closed for
the summer.
Roble Pool — Must have ID,
cap, and summer swim card.
Faculty/Staff swim noon to I

p.m. Monday-Friday; open for
all swim card holders Monday,
Wednesday and Friday I to 6
p.m., Tuesday and Thursday 3
to 6 p.m., and Saturday and
Sunday noon to 5 p.m.
De Guerre Pools are open
Monday-Friday noon to 2 p.m.
for faculty, staff and student
lap swim; 2 to 4:30 p.m. for
swim card holders; and 4:30 to
6 p.m. for lap swim only. Satur
day and Sunday noon to 6 p.m.
for swim card holders.
De Guerre Courts consists of
handball, racquetball and
squash courts open Monday
through Saturday from 11 a.m.
to 7:15 p.m. Closed Sundays.
Call 497-1588 for reservations.
Tennis Courts are available
when not scheduled for classes.
Sign up sheets for the next
day's play are posted at the
courts at the following times:
Encina courts, 8:30 a.m.; Roble
courts, 8:30 a.m.; Varsity
courts, 5 to 10 p.m. Roble and
Encina courts are reserved for
faculty and staff from noon to
1 p.m. Monday through
Friday. Courts are open all clay
Friday, Saturday and Sunday.
Encina Volleyball Courts

are open for recreational vol
leyball every clay during
daylight hours.
Roble Field is generally
available for all field activities,
except when it is reserved.
Check the schedule at the
Roble Office.
Roble Gym Special Use
Rooms — Roble has several ad
ditional rooms for more spe
cific uses. To check the
availability of each check at the
Roble Gym Of/ice. The dunce
studio is available for dance
only and no shoes are allowed;
Studio 42 is available for yoga
and mat work; Room 33 can be
used for fencing, ballet
practice and self defense.
The golf driving range has
buckets of balls available for $4
lor a large bucket, $2 for a
small bucket. Irons can be ob
tained at the clubhouse free of
charge. The range is open
Monday-Friday from 8:30 a.m.
to one hour before dark; Sat
urday, Sunday and holidays 7
a.m. to one hour before dark.
Golf course — Golfers must
obtain starting times in person
at the pro shop. For informa
tion call 497-4334. The course

is closed on Mondays, and the
earliest starting time on
weekends and holidays is 1 1
a.m.
July 4 — All facilities will
have their regular Wednesday
schedule except tor Roble Pool
noon to 4:30 p.m.; Roble
Building noon to 5 p.m.; En
cina lockers and showers
closed; Encina weight room
closed.
Break after Summer Quar
ter (Aug. 18 to Sept. 23) — The
Roble complex and pool will be
closed after Aug. 1 7. The com
plex will reopen Sept. 4 to 21
Monday-Friday 9 a.m. to 4
p.m.; Maples will be closed for
the break; Encina weight room
and showers will be open as
usual; l)e Guerre pools and I)e
Guerre courts will be open as
usual.
It you have any questions,
suggestions or comments of
any kind, please contact Mark
Marquess, the summer physi
cal education chairman, at 497-
4528 or 497-4895 or Pete
Leighton at 497-2221. Have a
good time and stay in shape.
