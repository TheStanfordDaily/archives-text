Get Even!! Dinner
Clown Time isn't over. As The
Daily adage says, when you can't Get
Happy!!, it's high time to Get Even!!
In that spirit we offer the Get
Even!! Dinner this Friday evening for
staffers and staff infections. Come
drown your joys and sorrows — in
food, thank you.

For those of you enticed hy the
Get Even!! affair, keep watching
that wire: "Urgent. Details to fol
low by week's end."' And expect to
hear at least three hells. Just for com
parison's sake, Yuri got four.
And in a nearly serious mode,
happy anniversary to we reporters

who carried me through a year of
this.
Looking hack on that year
prompted him to dedicate a song to
all of you: "Now it's much too dan
gerous to stop what you've begun
when everyone in paradise carries
. . ." a reporter's notebook.
