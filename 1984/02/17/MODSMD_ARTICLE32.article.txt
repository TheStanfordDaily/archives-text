The employers listed below are conducting interviews at the Career Planning and Place
ment Center and other campus locations on the dates indicated Sign-up sheets will be
posted TWO WEEKS PRIOR TO the interview date Appointments should be made in
person as soon as possible as the schedules are filled quickly. The number of interview
ing schedules is indicated for each company visit, to enable students to determine the
total number ot available interviews Each schedule has 13 time slots Students are
responsible for providing a copy of their resume each time they sign up
for an interview.
