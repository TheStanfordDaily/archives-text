# Former student dies
## 
### 
Barbie Fields, a former member of
The Daily staff and resident assis
tant at Branner and Alpha Sig, died
Feb. 13 in Malaysia. She was 24.
After graduating from Stanford in
1981, Fields pursued graduate studies
at Yale and traveled to Singapore on
a Fulbright scholarship. She was killed
in an accident involving a truck and a
bus during a visit to Malaysia.

The funeral will be held Monday in
Kansas City.
Her family has established the
Barbie Fields Memorial Scholarship
Fund, directed toward deserving
Stanford students interested in in
ternational relations. Contributions
should be sent in care of the Bro
therhood Bank and Trust, Eighth and
Minnesota, Kansas City, Kansas,
66101.
