BEAUTY
Extra foundation makeup
should be dabbed on only
where you need it — on
dark circles under eyes,
along sides of nose, over
blemishes.
* * *
Always store your knit
dresses folded, never on
hangers.
* * *
Choosing a sport jacket?
Make sure patterns are
matched at pockets, collar
and lapels. That's where
quality shows in men's
tailoring.
* * *
Make your legs look longer
and slimmer by wearing
dark stockings with narrow
ribs or pinstripes.
* * *
To help lose weight, drink a
glass of water instead of
eating a snack when you get
hungry between meals.
• * *
To help look better
whatever your weight, wear
the newest in hairstyles.
