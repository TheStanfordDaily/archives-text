### 
Quad Policy
Once Around the Quad announcements are printed
daily as a service to the public. Deadline for announce
ments is two business days before publication at 1 p.m.
Announcements should be placed in the appropriate
slots just inside The Daily's front door. Only events that
are free and open to the public may appear in Once
Around the Quad.
