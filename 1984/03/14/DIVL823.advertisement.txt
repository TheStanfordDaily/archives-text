DEPARTMENT OF BIOLOGICAL SCIENCES
Job Opening
Biological Sciences
We are seeking someone to coordinate and administer a large (300 student) laboratory course in introductory
biology. In this course the students are exposed to the full range of biological analyses (molecular to ecological),
and they are also encouraged to develop confidence and proficiency in the design of their own experiments.
Candidates should have: a Ph.D. and a commitment to teaching; a grasp of current issues and methods at all
levels of biology; and the ability to supervise laboratory staff and 20-30 teaching assistants. The appointment will
be for the academic year 1984-1985 and may be extended for up to three academic years. The appointment will
garry the title of Lecturer, and salary will be commensurate with experience.
APPLICATION: Submit as soon as possible a detailed resume including names of references to Professor Paul
Green, Department of Biological Sciences, Herrin Biology Labs, Stanford University, Stanford, CA 94305.
