# Prof. Whitaker dies at 75
## 
### 
Prof. Emeritus Virgil Whitaker. a noted
Shakespearean scholar, died at his campus home last
Friday at age 75.
Whitaker. who had been in declining health for
three years, was the founder and director of the
Stanford Summer Festivals from 1964 to 1969 and
the head of the English Department here for 12
years.
Whitaker is survived by his brother Gordon, of Las
Vegas.
The family asks that contributions be to the Heart
Fund. Memorial services will be held April 11 at 5 p.m.
at the Memorial Church.
