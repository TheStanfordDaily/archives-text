# Hoover's Emmerson dies
## 
### 
Hoover scholar John Emmerson. a prominent U.S.
diplomat, died last Saturday at Stanford University
Hospital after suffering a stroke four days earlier. He
was 76.
A 33-year veteran of the U.S. Foreign Serv ice, Em
merson served in Japan during the 1930s, 1940s and
1960s.
He is survived by his wife, Dorothy Emmerson, a
daughter, a son. two grandchildren and a sister.
The family prefers contributions to the John K.
Emmerson Scholarship Fund, YMCA Cardiac
Rehabilitation Program, 3412 Ross Road, Palo Alto,
Ca. 94303.
