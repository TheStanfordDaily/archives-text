# Campbell to stay on at UC
## 
### 
Gov. George Deukmejian has appointed Hoover In
stitution Director W. Glenn Campbell to a new 12-
year term on the University of California Board of
Regents.
Campbell completed a 16-year term last month
after he was appointed to the board by then-Gov.
Ronald Reagan. Campbell served as chairman of the
board in 1982-83.
'T am very pleased by the confidence shown in me
by Governor Deukmejian," Campbell told Stanford
University News Service, "and 1 look forward to the
opportunity to continue to play a role in maintaining
the University of California as the finest public insti
tution of higher learning in the United States."
