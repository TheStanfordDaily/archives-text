# Stock market creeps forward
## 
### 
New York — The stock market edged ahead in moderate
trading yesterday despite the failure of an afternoon rally at
tempt.
The Dow Jones average of 30 industrials rose 0.63 to
116." 53, but its transportation and utility averages retreated.
The industrial average had jumped 13.40 on Tuesday.
