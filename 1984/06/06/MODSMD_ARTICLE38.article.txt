# Hoover names new editor
## 
### 
Walter Lammi has been appointed executive editor
of the Hoover Institution Press. He will oversee the ed
itorial, production and marketing functions of Hoover
Institution publications.
From 1980 until joining the Hoover Institution.
Lammi served ascoeditor of the Journal of Contempo
rary Studies and as editorial director of the Institute for
Contemporary Studies in San Francisco.
Lammi is currently a Ph.D. candidate at Bryn Mawr
College in Pennsylvania.
