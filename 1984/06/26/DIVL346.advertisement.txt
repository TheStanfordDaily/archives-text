RESEARCH ASSISTANT I
POSITION
MANAGEMENT ANALYSIS CENTER, INC.
LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA
This position is designed as a 2-year interim commitment
prior to graduate school. Candidates must have proven qual
itative and quantitative abilities, some experience with com
puters, excellent writing skills, strong interpersonal attributes,
self-motivation, and a commitment to quality.
As a professional member of this international manage
ment consulting firm, the selected candidate will do library re
search, statistical analyses, literature summaries, telephone
interviewing, and report writing, as well as participate in client
meetings.
Number Hiring: 1-2 research assistants
Desired Starting Dates: late July to late August
Please send resumes no later than
THURSDAY, JULY 10, 1984 to:
Grace Delgado
Management Analysis Center, Inc.
515 S. Figueroa St., Suite 1230
Los Angeles, CA 90071
