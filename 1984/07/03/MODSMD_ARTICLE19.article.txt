# Samuel named to staff
## 
### 
Stanford Head Football
Coach Jack Elway announced
Friday that Tony Samuel, de
fensive line coach at Western
Michigan University, has been
named to the same position at
Stanford. Samuel is the final ad
dition to the Cardinal staff.
Samuel has been at Western
Michigan since June 1982. Prior
to that he was an assistant coach
at the University of Nebraska.

"I'm extremely happy that
Tony Samuel is joining our
staff," Elway said. "He was
highly recommended by every
coach he has been associated
with during his playing and
coaching careers."
"This new position at Stan
ford should be an interesting
and fun challenge for me,"
Samuel said.
