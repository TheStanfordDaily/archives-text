Drama Dept Summer Workshop: Eugene
lonesco's 'The Bald Soprano'. A hilarious

minutes. July 6, 11-13 at noon and July 6 &

Union. Admission is free.

is the deadline for SCIRE summer intern

credit. 497-4504.
