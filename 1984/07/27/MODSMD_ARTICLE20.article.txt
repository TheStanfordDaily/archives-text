# SPORTS
## Stanford swimmers to watch for in L.A. 
### 

Stanford swimmers to look out for: (clockwise from upper left) Pablo Morales,
world record-holder in the 100-meter butterfly, will swim in two other events. Jeff
Kostoff searches for gold in the 400-meter individual medley. Susan Rapp, Ameri
can record-holder in the 200-meter breaststroke will also swim in the 100-meter
breaststroke. John Moffet, world record-holder in the 100-meter breaststroke, will
compete in the 200 as well.
