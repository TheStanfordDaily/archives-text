terested in spending the day with a

every Wednesday at 8 p.m. All welcome.
Volunteer Study: Does lower physical activity
relate to the development of overweight in
young children? Help us find out by par
ticipating in the medical Center study with
your 18-month-old child, who will wear a

small, very sophisticated activity monitor
for 2 days during waking hours. Parents
will receive a time-sequence record of
their child's activity for each day. Call
Sherry Low at 497-7928 (alt. 497-5065.)
Yoga at the l-Center: every Thursday, 5-6:30
p.m. All are welcome — improve your
muscle tone and promote your personal
well-being.
