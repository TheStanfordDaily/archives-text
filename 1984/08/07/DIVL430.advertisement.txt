IDEAD WEEK
Dead Week is the week immediately
preceding final examinations in Summer
Quarter. During this period, no quizzes or
examinations should be given, and no
musical, dramatic and athletic events
involving compulsory student participation
should be scheduled. No classes should be
held on the day before finals.
DEAD WEEK DATES THIS QUARTER:
Saturday, August 11 through
Thursday, August 16, inclusive.
