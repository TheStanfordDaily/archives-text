I NEW SEMINAR!
TRUTH: FACT or FICTION?
Are facts and truth one and the same?
What's your opinion? This seminar is intended to get you to
actively explore the relationship of facts to truth. The
discussion will be approached from a media-related angle,
but more than media views will be presented. In addition to
editors, journalists, and a filmmaker, there will be a
philosopher, a poet, and a scientist to lead discussion. Some
facts about the course:
Instructor: Marion Lewenstein, Dept. of Communication, Schiff House
Resident Fellow
Meeting Time and Place: Mondays, 3:30-5:00, Schiff House RF Apt.
Units: 2-3
Grading: Pass/No Credit
Enrollment: Limited to 12; preference given to Schiff House residents
How to Sign Up: Attend first class meeting on Oct. 1; list Indep. Study,
Comm. 199, Section No. 6, Lewenstein, on your Study List.
