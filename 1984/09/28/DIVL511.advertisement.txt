The Stanford Daily
is looking for students to fill the following vacancies
on its business staff:
CREDIT CONTROLLER
The credit controller is responsible for controlling
customer accounts, communicating with advertising
customers, researching problems and liaising with
other staff at The Stanford Daily.
This is a fine opportunity to gain first hand business
experience in an exciting environment.
SUBSCRIPTIONS MANAGER
The subscriptions manager has complete
responsibility for the distribution of paid subscription
copies of The Stanford Daily. Work involves labelling,
sorting and mailing. Attractive compensation.
Applicants should complete an employment application form at the
business office of The Stanford Daily, Storke Publication Building (across
from Terman Engineering) or may call Andrew or Karen at 497-2554 for
further information.
