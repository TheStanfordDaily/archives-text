HARRY S. TRUMAN
SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM
— Faculty Representative —
Professor Hubert R. Marshall, Political Science
Sophomores committed to a career in the public service may apply to be
Stanford University's nominees for a Harry S. Truman Scholarship. An
informational meeting will be held MONDAY, OCTOBER 15, at 7:30 p.m.
in Room 128 of the Education Building. Interested first year students may also
attend.
A Truman Scholarship covers tuition, fees, books and room and board, up to a
maximum of $5,000 annually for the remainder of a student's undergraduate
career and for up to two years of graduate study. Approximately 100
Scholarships are awarded nationally.
If you are unable to attend the meeting, contact
Elizabeth Rafferty, Room 161G, Building 160.
APPLICATION DEADLINE — NOVEMBER 12, 1984
