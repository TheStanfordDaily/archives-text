# Women win third in a row
## 
### 
The Stanford women's soc
cer evened its record yesterday
as it defeated Texas A & M, 1-
0, in Orlando, Fla. yesterday.
The Cardinal, now 5-5 over
all, got its lone goal from a
usual source as senior Sue
Moore scored off an assist by
Jennifer Hill. The team now
has a three game winning
streak and has won four of its
last five.
Stanford goalie Theresa
Greaney, starting only her third
game, had two saves to key the
defense. The Cardinal plays at
St. Mary's on Thursday.
at Orlando, Fla.
