NOTICE OF
NONDISCRIMINATORY POLICY
AS TO STUDENTS
The Childrens Center of the Stanford Community admits
students of any race, color, national and ethnic origin to all
the rights, privileges, programs, and activities generally ac
corded or made available to students at the school. It does
not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national and
ethnic origin in administration of its educational policies,
scholarship and loan programs, and athletic and other
school-administered programs.
