# Golfers take 3rd
## 
### ROB GRAHAM Staff sportswriter 
The Cardinal men's golf
team yesterday placed third in
the 54-hole, two-day Univer
sity of the Pacific Invitational at
Woodbridge Country Club in
Lodi.
The University of Arizona
won the team championship
with a total of 865, two strokes
better than UCLA. Stanford
and Weber State were 10
strikes behind at 875.
The Cardinal was led by
Scott Erickson, whose 214
placed him in a tie for fourth
individually.
