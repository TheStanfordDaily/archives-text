# Women's crew team to race
## 
### 
The Stanford women's crew team
will participate in the Head of the Es
tuary regatta in Oakland Sunday.
The Cardinal will have two eight
woman and two four-woman boats
competing in two-and-three-fourths
mile races. Stanford's competition
will include the University of Califor
nia at Berkeley.
Sunday's regatta will be the wo
men's crew team's second and final
event this fall. The Cardinal's eight
woman entry finished 18th out of 40
boats Sunday in a three-and-one-half
mile competition at the Head of the
Charles races in Boston.
