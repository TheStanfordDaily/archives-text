Rod Searcey/Daily
Heaven can't wait
Sophomore Bob Hess, a Delta Kappa Epsilon pledge, maintains a lonely midnight vigil outside the
Gaieties ticket office in the Old Firehouse. Deke Resident Assistant Rick Hoskins confirmed early this
morning that all the Row houses have agreed to keep at least one person in line 24 hours a day to get
the best seats for the show. "Basically, we're just really psyched about Gaieties," Hoskins said.
