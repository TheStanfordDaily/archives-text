# Hockey players honored
## 
### 
Four Stanford field hockey
players were named to the All-
Northern Pacific Conference
team.
Senior forwards Patsy Hunt
ington and Jen Bleakley, junior
back Karen Chamberlain and
sophomore midfielder Andi
Wolpert made the team.
Huntington, a two-time All-
American, led the Cardinal in
scoring with 18 points, on 17
goals and an assist. She also
tied for second in the NorPac in
goals.
Wolpert amassed 17 points
on nine goals and had eight as
sists this season, and tied for
fifth in the NorPac in goals and
tied for first in assists. Bleakley
had four goals and four assists,
and Chamberlain played well
on defense.
