The Timberland boat shoe has uppers
made of oil-impregnated leathers that won't dry out
or crack. The laces are thick rawhide.
And, to prevent rusting and damage from salt,
the eyelets are only solid brass. But, most important,
the sole is long-lasting, rugged Vibram.' 1
Most boat shoes just hold up well in June, July
and August. The Timberland boat shoe, for men
and women, holds up all year round.
Robert krohn
