Turkey, cranberries and Thanksgiving stuff
Provide a nice break from chile-egg puff.
But as you let out your belt after the meal,
Something is missing, or so you feel,
Your Daily is gone - forever, you fear!
Not to worry. On Monday we'll reappear.
Happy Thanksgiving to friends and others,
To forgiving profs and cooking mothers.
