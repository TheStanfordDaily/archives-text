Rod Searcey/Daily
Player of the Year
Stanford Junior Kim Oden, a 6-foot-2 middleblocker from Irvine, was chosen as the
Player of the Year by the Western Collegiate Athletic Association, it was announced
yesterday. Oden, a two-time All American with a .398 hitting percentage this year,
also won the honor last year. Volleyball Coach Fred Sturm was named WCAA Co-
Coach of the Year.
