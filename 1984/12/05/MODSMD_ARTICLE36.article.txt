# PAGC meet here Saturday
## 
### 
The largest gymnastics meet ever
held in Northern California — the
1984 Pacific Alliance Championships
— will be held Saturday at Maples
Pavilion.
Men's team and all-around compe
tition will be held at 2 p.m. and will
be followed by women's team and all
around competition at 7 p.m. Teams
from the United States and eight

other countries will be taking part.
Individuals include Olympic gold
medal winner Lou Yun and U.S.j
Olympic team member Tracee
Talavera, who will perform an exhibi
tion.
The men's and women's indiviaual
competition finals will be held at the
Cow Palace in Daly City at 2 p.m.
Sunday.
