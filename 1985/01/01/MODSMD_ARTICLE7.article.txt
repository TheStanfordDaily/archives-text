# SPORTS
## 'Country Club' facilities open for summer 
### 
Stanford is fondly known as "The
Country Club" by those who have learned
to take advantage of the tremendous
facilities available. To join this elite crowd
one need only take a bit of initiative.
And to make it even more painless here
is a listing of facilities and their hours for
this summer. Most of them require Stan
ford ID for use, so if you're anonymous,
you should read no further.
Encina lockers and showers are open
Monday through Friday 7:30 a.m. to 9
p.m.; 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. Saturday and Sun
day.
Roble wieght training lockers and
showers are open Monday through
Thursday 8 a.m. to 10 p.m. , Friday 8 a.m.
to 7 p.m., noon to 5:30 p.m. on Saturday
and Sunday.
Encina Weight room is open for weight
training Monday through Friday 10 a.m. to
2 p.m. and 4 to 8 p.m.; Saturday and Sun
day noon to 5 p.m.
The Roble Gym Floor is available over
the summer for basketball, vollyball, fen
cing, badminton and open recreation. The
schedule is pretty complicated, so pay at

tention. Play is limited to recreational bas
ketball Monday, Wednesday and Friday 1
to 3 p.m., Tuesday and Thursday from
noon to 3 p.m. and Friday from 1 to 3 more
recreational basketball; Fridays from 3 to 5
p.m. badminton is the game in town. Wed
nesdays from 3 to 5:30 is recreational vol
lyball. The gym is set for open recreation
Monday 3 to 5:30 p.m. and 9 to 10 p.m.;
Wednesday from 9 to 10 p.m.; Tuesday
and Thursday 3 to 6 p.m. and 8 to 10 p.m.;
Friday from 8 a.m. to noon and 5 to 7 p.m.;
Saturday 2 to 5 p.m.; Sunday noon to 5:30
p.m. If you need more information, call
497-4895.
Angell Field Track, Olympic Gamefield
and Perrier Parcourse (begins at Roble
Gym) are open every day, all day; for
reserved use call 497-0505. The Angell
field (infield), Taylor Field, and 1M Fields
(North and South) must be reserved by
calling 497-0505.
Maples Pavilion is closed for the sum
mer.
Roble Pool — Must have ID, cap, and
summer swim card. Faculty/Staff swim
noon to 1 p.m. Monday-Friday; open for

all swim card holders Monday, Wednesday
and Friday 1 to 6 p.m., Tuesday and
Thursday 3 to 6 p.m., and Saturday and
Sunday noon to 5 p.m.
De Guerre Pools are open Monday-
Friday noon to 2 p.m for faculty, staff and
student lap swim; 2 to 4:30 p.m. for swim
card holders; and 4:30 to 6 p.m. for lap
swim only. Saturday and Sunday noon to 6
p.m. for swim card holders.
De Guerre Courts consists of handball,
racquetball and squash courts open Mon
day through Saturday from 11 a.m. to 7:15
p.m. Closed Sundays. Call 497-1588 for
reservations.
Tennis Courts are available when not
sceduled for classes. Sign up sheets for the
next day's play are posted at the courts at
the following times: Encina courts, 8:30
a.m.; Roble courts, 8:30 a.m.; Varsity
Courts, 5:10 p.m. Roble and Encina courts
are reserved for faculty and staff from
noon to 1 p.m. Monday through Friday.
Courts are open 'all day Friday, Saturday
and Sunday.
Encina Vollyball Courts are open for
recreational volleyball every day during

daylight hours.
Roble Field is generally available for all
field activities except when it is reserved.
Check the schedule at the Roble Office.
Roble Gym Special Use Rooms — roble
has several aditional rooms for more spe
cific uses. To check the availability of each
check at the Roble Gym Office. The dance
studio is available for dance only and no
shoes are allowed; Studio 42 is available
for yoga and mat work; Room 33 can be
used for fencing, ballet practice and self
defense.
The golf driving range has buckets of
balls available for $4 for a large bucket,
$2.50 for a small bucket. Irons can be ob
tained at the clubhouse free of charge. The
range is open Monday-Friday from 8:30
a.m. to one hour before dark; Saturday,
Sunday and holidays 7 a.m. to one hour
before dark.
Golf course — Golfers must obtain start
ing times in person at the pro shop. For in
formation call 497-4334. The course is
closed on Mondays, and the earliest start

a.m.
