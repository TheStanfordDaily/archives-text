New staffers
The Daily will hold three brief informational
meetings for students interested in joining the staff.
We're looking for writers. No journalistic experience
is necessary.
We're looking for night and wire editors. Again, no
experience is necessary.
It's easy to get involved, even in the middle of the
school year.
Com'e to one of our meetings and see what we're all
about.
Thursday, Jan. 10 at 4 p.m.
Friday, Jan. 11 at 4 p.m.
Monday, Jan. 14 at 4 p.m.
The Daily offices are located in Storke Publications
Building, across from the Terman Engineering
Center.
