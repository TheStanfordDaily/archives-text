The Palo Alto LSAT Program 1
Preparation for March 2 LSAT
The Program's 32-hour course to prepare students for the March 2 LSAT
begins on January 17 and meets 8 to 10 times on midweek evenings and
some weekends. All curricular preparation and teaching are carried out by a
former Stanford faculty member who is a Stanford J.D. and a practicing at
torney, and who has prepared Stanford students for the LSAT since 1978.
The Program offers intensive teaching and review, copious homework,
and lots of instructor feedback. We do not use tapes or other passive learn
ing devices. Enrollment is limited to 12 students, and the fee is $300. In
terested in the GMAT? The Program's course for the March -\6 GMAT starts
January 26.
