POSITIONS AVAILABLE WITH
THE ASSU
Elections Commissioner
Has overall responsibility for organizing and conducting the April
general election.
Associate Elections Commissioners (2)
Assist the Commissioner by taking responsibility for one aspect of
the election: publicity (1); operations (1)
Elections Hearing Officer
Oversees the conduct of election campaigning; investigates
alleged violations of election rules.
Program Board Deputy Director
Assist the Director of the Program Board in managing the process
of allocating funds to student organizations.
Senate Secretary
Attend and prepare minutes of the bi-weekly Senate meetings.
Application forms and information are available at the ASSU office.
