WHAT ARE YOU DOING THIS SUMMER?
YOU
CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE!
The Office of the President announces the
Stanford Public Service Fellowship Program. The
fellowships allow students to identify a
community problem or need and then develop an
innovative, practical project addressing that
problem or need.
* Enrolled graduate and undergraduate students are eligible
for up to $ 1,200 grants.
* At least the eqivalent of an eight week full-time
commitment during the summer of 1985 is required.
* Applications and information are available at Owen House
(next to bikestore).
* Application deadline is 12 April 1985.
