UNDERGRADUATE
\ GRANTS for
SUMMER
FIELDWORK
The Department of Anthropology is pleased to offer a limited
number of summer fieldwork grants for undergraduates. These
grants provide for travel expenses plus a fixed portion of living
expenses at the research site. Students of any major may apply,
but some coursework in anthropology is expected.
An information meeting will be held at the Anthropology Lounge,
Room 112R, Bldg. 110, on Wednesday, February 6at 12:00 noon.
For further informawton see Marjorie Wright
Room 111 -G, Bldg. 110, 497-4641.
APPLICATION DEADLINE is FEBRUARY 27, 1985.
