JAMES 11< 11 >
Author of the novel and screenplay DELIVERANCE and several books
of poems, Mr. Dickey has received a Guggenheim fellowship and The
National Book Award for BUCKDANCER'S CHOICE. In 1977 he
was invited to read from his work at President Carter's inaugural
celebration. Mr. Dickey is Poet in Residence and Carolina Professor at
the University of South Carolina.
Mr. Dickey will sign copies of his books
at THE STANFORD BOOKSTORE, Monday, February 11th, from 3-4 PM
and after his reading that evening at Kresge Auditorium.
