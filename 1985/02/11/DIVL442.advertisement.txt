ELECTIONS OFFICERS
NEEDED!!
Two Associate Elections Commissioners are
needed to help run the Spring-1985 ASSU
Election.
— The Associate Commissioner for Publicity will oversee
preparation of the Elections Handbook.
— The Associate Commission for Operations will oversee the
logistical operations of polling sites.
These are paid positions.
Application forms and information available at the ASSU office.
DEADLINE: April 13,1985, 4:00 PM
