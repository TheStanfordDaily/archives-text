MBA Graduates
. Finance and Marketing
At Motorola/Four-Phase Systems we develop,
manufacture, market and service multi-function
computer systems for office and distributed
information processing applications. We have
positions leading to fast track career
opportunities in finance, marketing and product
line management As a subsidiary, we offer the
excitement of an entreprenaural company with
advancement opportunities in a Fortune 100
corporation.
If you are an MBA in marketing or finance and
have the desire to work in a rapidly changing and
dynamic environment, plan to meet with us on
campus Friday, February 15. See your
Placement Office for more details.
If unable to meet with us, please send your
resume to: Motorola/Four-Phase Systems,
Professional Staffing, Dept. 408,10700 N.
DeAnza Blvd., Cupertino, CA 95014. Equal
opportunity is our pledge and practice.
