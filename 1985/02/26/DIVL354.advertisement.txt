WL A JOHN GARDNER FELLOWSHIP PROGRAM
Graduating Seniors are invited to apply for
Fellowships in Public Service
Three successful candidates will be provided with a $12,000
stipend for eleven months and an assignment to work in a
compatible organization (government or voluntary sector in the
United States) with a distinguished mentor.
• Applications and information are available at the Public Service Center,
Owen House (next to the bikestore).
• Application deadline is March 15.
