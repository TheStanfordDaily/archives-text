The Stanford Daily (USPS SIB-420) is an independent student newspaper owned and published by The
Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation. The Stanford Daily is published Monday through Friday during the
academic year with the exceptions of dead week, when one issue is published, and finals week, when no is
sues arc published The Daily is published Tuesdays and Fridays during the eight week summer quarter
Main offipe: Storke Student Publications Building, Stanford, CA 94105
Nothing on the opinions page necessarily represents a position of the entire Daily staff or of l.cland Stan
ford Junior University Editorials represent the opinion of a majority of editorial board members voting on
each topic The board consists of seven Daily editors and three at-large members elected by The Daily staff
I eltcrs. columns and cartoons represent only the views of their authors The Daily regrets that it cannot
guarantee the return of any article submitted All submitted articles are subject to editing
Subscription rates in the United States, Canada and Medico, $45 per year or $18 per quarter Second
class postage paid at Palo Alto, Calif Send check or money order in advance to: The Stanford Daily, Storke
Student Publications Building, Stanford, CA 94305 Postmaster: Send address changes to The Stanford
Daily. Storke Student Publications Building, Stanford, CA 94105.
Production at the ASSU Typesetting Shop, Storke Student Publications Building. Stanford, CA 94305
Printed by Independent Publications, 865 The Alameda, San Jose, CA
