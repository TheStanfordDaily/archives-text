CO-OP
INTERNSHIP
PROGRAM
Through our unique Co-op Internship Program you can combine
your classroom studies with 'hands-on' experience at our state-of
the-art facilities. We will present our special program to sopho
mores, juniors and seniors, in EE, ME, and CS.
Tuesday, March 12, 1985
12:00 P.M.-1:00 P.M.
Terman 217
Part of the presentation will include a special viewing of a Hughes
video of the Satellite Recovery Mission. In addition, a complimen
tary lunch will be provided to all interested applicants.
Students should sign up for an interview at Dean Kenneth Down's
office, Terman 215. Interviews will be conducted on 3/12 and 3/13.
Proof of U.S. Citizenship Required. Equal Opportunity Employer.
