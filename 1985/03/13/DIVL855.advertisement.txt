CABLE
Community Access
Organization
The Joint Cable Working Group of Atherton, East Palo Alto,
Menlo Park, Palo Alto, Santa Clara County (Stanford
University) and San Mateo County (Ladera, Menlo Oaks,
University Heights) has drafted Articles of Incorporation
and Bylaws for a nonprofit Community Access
Organization to govern the use of access channels and
resources for public and institutional access to the cable
system in this service area.
At their meeting on March 19, 7:30 P.M. in Room "R" of the
Palo Alto Lucie Stern Community Center, the Policy and
Procedures Committee of the Palo Alto City Council will
review the Articles of Incorporation and Bylaws of the
proposed corporation. The public will be invited to speak
and all interested persons are welcome to attend.
For further information call Jeanne Moulton at 329-2492.
