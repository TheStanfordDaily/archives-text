BERLIN SCHOLARSHIP
The Free University of Berlin (FU) announces its annual scholarship for
former participants of the Stanford Program in Berlin.
The Scholarship is for study at the FU during academic year 1985-86 and
provides living allowance, rent reimbursement, book allowance, and health
insurance.
If you are a senior or first year graduate student and have participated in
the Stanford Program in Berlin for two quarters, pick up an information sheet
from the Overseas Studies Office, 112 Old Union.
