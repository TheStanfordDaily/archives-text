i I BERLIN SCHOLARSHIP I
/£■. The Free University of Berlin (FU) announces its annual scholarship for I
former participants of the Stanford Program in Berlin.
The Scholarship is for study at the FU during academic year 1985-86 and I
I provides living allowance, rent reimbursement, book allowance, and health I
■ insurance.
If you are a senior or first year graduate student and have participated in I
I the Stanford Program in Berlin for two quarters, pick up an information sheet I
I from the Overseas Studies Office, 112 Old Union.
I APPLICATION DEADLINE IS I
I FRIDAY, APR1L26,1965 I
