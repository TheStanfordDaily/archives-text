OFF-CAMPUS
HOUSING
If you need off-campus housing for this summer or next fall,
the Spring Clearinghouse can help you. We have listings of
rentals, general housing information and a free phone.
The Spring Clearinghouse will be open
MAY 28 to 31, 8 AM-5 PM
in ROOM 309, OLD UNION
Be sure to bring your Stanford ID.
For more information, call 497-3906.
