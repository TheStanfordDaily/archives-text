MARKETING
MANAGER
XIDAK needs a marketing manager for its line of
advanced software development products. You
must be familiar with modern computing con
cepts and able to track rapidly changing market
segments, such as CAD/CAE and applied Al. You
will work directly with the President to identify
new market areas, define new products, and de
vise sales strategies for existing products. You
should have experience in pricing software pro
ducts, identifying distribution channels,
negotiating sales and distributor agreements,
. and managing promotional programs. You must
be smart, hard-working and have impeccable
integrity.
XIDAK is a small, dynamic company, dedicated to supplying the
highest-quality software development environments to the most
demanding software application developers. If you are equal to
the task, then please submit a resume in confidence to:
XIDAK, Inc.,
530 Oak Grove Avenue
Menlo Park, CA 94025
(415) 324-8745.
