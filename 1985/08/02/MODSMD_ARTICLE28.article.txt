# Shuttle sightings
## 
### 
The following is a list of times when the Space Shuttle
Challenger can be seen flying over the Bay Area. Although
the shuttle should be visible to the naked eye, NASA sug
gests the use of binoculars. Times are PDT, and the angle
given refers to the angle of inclination above the horizon at
the initial time. The shuttle will appear as a slow-moving
star.

Day Time Angle Path
Sat. 9:20 p.m. 35° from NW to E
Sun. 9:34 p.m. 82° directly overhead,
from NE to SE
— Rebecca Zeigler
