DEAD WEEK is the week immediately
preceding final examinations in Summer Quarter. During this
period, no quizzes or examinations should be given, and no
musical, dramatic and athletic events involving compulsory
student participation should be scheduled. No classes
should be held on the day before finals.
DEAD WEEK DATES THIS QUARTER:
Saturday, Aug. 10 through Friday, Aug. 16, inclusive.
