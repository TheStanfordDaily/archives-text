# Forging Ahead
## 
### 
First-year coach Sheryl Johnson will try to get the Stan
ford field hockey team into the nation s top 12 and into the
NCAA playoffs.
The Cardinal was ranked 14th and 16th, respectively,
the past two seasons, and All Americans Andi Wolpert and
Karen Chamberlain are two of the 10 returning to lead
Stanford toward postseason play.
