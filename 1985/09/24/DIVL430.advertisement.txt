# Adv. 10 Page 4
## 
### 
We Want You!
to write a semi-weekly column or
to be a member of The Daily's
Editorial Board.
To be considered for weekly columnist, submit a three to
four page, typed, double-spaced sample column to The
Daily offices by 5 p.m. Monday, Sept. 30.
To be considered for The Daily's Editorial Board, submit
a one-page statement to The Daily offices by 5 p.m.
tomorrow and attend a grill session Thursday at noon.
The election will follow, from 1 to 10 p.m.
Join the Few, the Proud, The Daily.
