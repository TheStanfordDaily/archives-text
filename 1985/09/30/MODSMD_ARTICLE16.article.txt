The Stanford Daily (USPS 518-420) is an independent student newspaper owned and published by The Stan
ford Daily Publishing Corporation, Main office Storke Student Publications Building, Stanford, CA 94305
The Daily is published Monday through Friday during the academic year with the exceptions of Dead Week,
when one issue is published, finals week, when no issues are published; and Tuesdays and Fridays during the
eight-week summer quarter
Nothing on the opinions page necessarily represents a position of the entire Daily staff or of the Leland Stan
