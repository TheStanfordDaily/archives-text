# GRADUATE SENATE ELECTION OCTOBER 29th & 30th
## Be the Next Jim McGrath 
### 
A special election to fill seven graduate positions has
been called for October 29 and 30, 1985.
There are three seats available in the school of En
gineering, two seats in the schools of Humanities and
Sciences/ Earth Sciences/ Education, and two seats in
the schools of Business/Law/Medicine.
The term of office will be until May of 1986. The Se
nate meets at 7:00 pm on alternate weeks in room 180
of the Law School; committees of the Senate meet on
alternate weeks between Senate meetings.
Petitions, declarations of intent, and elections
Handbook material are due October 21, 1985. Contact

Elections Commissioner Derek Kampfe or Associate
Commissioner Farid Aitsahlia, 497-4331. Or contact
Senate Chair Carey White.
