HARRY S. TRUMAN —
Scholarship Program
— Faculty Representative —
Professor Hubert R. Marshall, Political Science
Sophomores committed to a career in the public service may apply to be
Stanford University's nominees for a Harry S. Truman Scholarship. An
informational meeting will be held Tuesday, OCTOBER 16, at 5:15 p.m. in
Room 128 of the Education Building. Interested first year students may also
attend.
A Truman Scholarship covers tuition, fees, books, and room and board, up
to a maximum of $5,000 annually for the remainder of a student's
undergraduate career and for up to two years of graduate study.
Approximately 100 Scholarships are awarded nationally.
