CENTER FOR TEACHING AND LEARNING FALL
LECTURE SERIES
The Center for Teaching and Learning announces the second presentation
in its fall lecture and discussion Series on University Teaching. Graduate
students, faculty, and others involved in teaching are invited to attend, and
to bring a bag lunch.
Wednesday, October 30 12-1 p.m. CERAS, room 204
WRITING LETTERS OF RECOMMENDATION:
How to save time and insure your students' futures. Speakers:
Verity Powell, Undergraduate Advising Center
Jonathan Reider, Undergraduate Admissions
Peter Rosenbaum, Medical School
Christopher Shinkman, Career Planning and Placement
■This talk as well as six more to be specified during the academic year, are part
of the Stanford Series on University Teaching. If you attend the series of eight
lectures, you may request that a letter which describes the talks attended and
the nature of your participation be added to your placement file. For more infor
mation on the series, including information on how to view the talks on '
videotape, please call the Center for Teaching and Learning at 497-1326.
