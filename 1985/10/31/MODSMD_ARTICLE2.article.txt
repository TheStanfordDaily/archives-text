# Strike Facts
## 
### 
The University Proposal: Wage increases of 5 percent
the first year, 4 percent the second year and 4.5 percent
the third year. A 2.5 percent raise immediately for
employees in the top step of their wage range.
The Union Proposal: Wage increases of 7.5 percent
the first year, 6.4 percent the second year and 6.2 per
cent the third year.
Other Issues: Job security, affirmative action, pen
sions, layoffs and union access to employees during
working hours.
