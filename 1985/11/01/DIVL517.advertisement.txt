Public
Service
Opportunity
Announcements
will appear on this very page, in this very space. Each
day you will find listings of internships and volunteer
positions offered by organizations that work either at or
in conjunction with Stanford.
All organizations wishing to list themselves should come by The
Daily immediately to fill out an application. Meetings and one-day
events should ordinarily not be submitted for this space. OATQ will
continue to make that type of announcement.
