■■■■■■■LAGUNITA HOT SPOTS PRESENTS A MINI-SERIES ONHBBMH
WAR AND CORRUPTION IN EL SALVADOR
featuring
LEONEL GOMEZ
Mr. Gomez is the former deputy director of the Salvadoran Institute for
Agrarian Transformation . After being appointed to the position in 1980,
he survived eight assassination attempts and came to the US. He was
granted political asylum in 1983. He is an expert on agrarian reform,
army corruption and the negotiation process between the Duarte
government and the guerillas.
Mr. Gomez will speak on the future of El Salvador tonight, Wednesday,
November 13 at 7:30 pm in Building 320, Room 320. Reception will
follow in the Adelfa Lounge.
