samTrans goes to the seals!
Special buses to Afto Nuevo State Reserve: Dec. 14 through March 16, 1986.
Rain or shine, trips leave from San Mateo and Half Moon
Bay to picturesque Ano Nuevo State Reserve for state
guided visits with the massive elephant seals and their
fascinating offspring. Departures from Hillsdale Shopping
Center every Saturday and Sunday in December and March
at 9:45 a.m. and 10:45 a.m. In January and February,
departures every Saturday and Sunday at 9:45 a.m., 10:45
a.m., and 11:45 a.m. All trips return approximately 6 hours
later. First come, first served: send coupon below for your
bus trip/seal reservation card. IF DEPARTING FROM
HALF MOON BAY, PLEASE CHECK BOX UNDER
DEPARTURE TIME. Prepaid reservations cannot be refund
ed or exchanged. The $B.OO fee includes the State Reserve
tour fee.
No trips are scheduled for Dec. 29, Jan. 4, 5, 12.
