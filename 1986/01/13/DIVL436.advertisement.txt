SENIOR CASINO NIGHT
The Stanford Alumni Association
invites all Seniors and their guests to an evening of
fun and games, Friday, January 31,1986.
Tickets go on sale starting Wednesday, January 15, 1986
at 11:00 a.m. in the Bowman Alumni House.
Tickets must be purchased in advance
and are available on a limited basis.
$11.00 per person. (Alumni Association members, $7.00)
Be sure to bring your Stanford I.D. to purchase tickets.
