JOHN GARDNER FELLOWSHIP PROGRAM
Graduating Seniors are invited to apply for
Fellowships in Public Service
Three successful candidates will be provided with a $12,000 stipend for
eleven months and an assignment to work in a compatible organization
(government or voluntary sector in the United States) with a distinguished
mentor.
• Applications and information are available at the Public Service Center, Owen
House (next to the bike shop). Jeanne Halleck 497-0992 or 725-2870.
• Application deadline is February 14, 1986.
