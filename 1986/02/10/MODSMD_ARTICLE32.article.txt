# Goldie upset in Texas
## 
### CHRIS FIALKO Staff writer 
Unseeded Arkansas fresh man Joey Blake upset Stanford senior Dan Goldie
yesterday in the finals of the Rolex National Indoor Intercollegiate Champion
ships in Houston by a 3-6, 6-3, 7-6 score.
Blake edged Goldie, the defending champion, in the final set tie-breaker by
a slim 8-6 margin.
The Stanford senior, who called the Invitational "the biggest tournament of
the winter" was the No. 2 seed going in, while Blake was not even ranked in
the preseason top 50.
Goldie waltzed through the first three rounds without losing a set. He
defeated Houston's Brent Saigeon in the first round on Thursday, 6-2, 6-4.
The top Cardinal player then played two matches on Friday, defeating Matt
Ochesky of Clemson, 6-2, 6-1. and seventh-ranked Rick Leach of USC 6-3, 6-
3.
In Saturday's semifinal, however. Goldie ran into UCLA's Brad Pearce.
Pearce took the first set 6-3 and had a match point in the second before Goldie
fought back to win that set 7-5. Goldie then clinched his spot in the final by
taking the third set, 6-3.
Meanwhile. Blake rallied in his semifinal match to defeat llth-ranked
/Greg Hahn of Alabama. Hahn took the first set 6-3, but Blake came back
strong to win the last two sets, 6-4, 6-4.
