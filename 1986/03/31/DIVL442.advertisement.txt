STANFORD
PUBLIC SERVICE
FELLOWSHIPS
Stanford students are invited to apply for the
Summer of 1986 Public Service Fellowships. The
program provides funding to undergraduates and
graduate students who develop an innovative
project to serve the community during the
summer.
• Enrolled graduate and undergraduate students
are eligible for up to $ 1,200 grants.
• At least the equivalent of an eight week full-time
commitment during the summer of 1985 is
required.
• Applications and information are available at the
Public Service Center in Owen House (next to the
bike shop) - 723-0992.
• Application deadline is 7 April 1986.
