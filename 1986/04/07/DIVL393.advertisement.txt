STANFORD OVERSEAS STUDIES PROGRAM
An Invitation to Tea
is hereby extended to all students returning from the
STANFORD PROGRAM in OXFORD,
as well as to those who were at Oxford during Autumn Quarter.
Please join the Overseas Studies Oxford Panel
for Tea and Conversation on
MONDAY, APRIL 7th, at 4:00 PM
in the English Department Lounge (Building 300, Doom 303).
This is our chance to hear from you about how we can improve our
program. We look forward to seeing you there.
