Robo-men did not observe Senate voting this last
time and that is why the measure to make humans
live in boxes did not pass. So what, who cares. (In
Robospeak, this means, "Humans will live in boxes,
anyway.") In additional voting the Senate also ap
proved all of the following measures: 1) To reinstate
suspended graduate Senator Bruce Winslow; 2) To
amend the divestment bill to include divestment from
the University's Expendable Funds Pool; 3) To pass the
divestment bill amended; 4) To divest from Merge
Pool B (who says we have to do things in order; and 5)
To make every day Wednesday. Here is a list of how
