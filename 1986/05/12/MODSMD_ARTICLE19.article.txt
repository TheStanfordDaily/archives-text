# Stanford Special Olympics 1986
## 
### 




More than 250 developmentally disabled athletes
participated in Stanford's Special Olympics swim
meet, which was held on Saturday. More than 200
Stanford volunteers donated their time to the event,
which lasted from 9 a k m. to about 3:30 p.m. "It was
really fun, and rewarding, too," said one volunteer.
