The Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation Main office Storke Student Publications Building Stanford
CA 94305
The Daily is published Monday through Friday during the academic year with the exoeptions of Dead
Week when one issue is published, finals week, when no issues are published, and Tuesdays and
Fridays during the eight-week summer quarter
Nothing on the opinions page necessarily represents a position of the entire Daily staff or of the Lelartd
Stanford Junior University Editorials represent me opinion of a majority of editorial board members voting
on each topic The Hoard consists of seven Daily editors and three at-large members from the community
elected by The Daily staff
Letterc columns and cartoons represent only the views of their authors Advertisements do not neces
sarily reftect editorial opinion The Daily regrets it cannot guarantee the return of any article submitted All
submitted articles are sub|ect to editing
