VOLUNTEERS WANTED
for the
Sophomore Celebration
and the
Junior Convocation
If you would like to organize the programs, welcome your fellow classmates,
design publicity, or just help out in some way, please leave your name at the
Dean of Undergraduate Studies' Office, Building One, with Maria Villasenor or
Charlie Junkerman. Or call 723-9786. It's fun!
Put the dates on your calendar now
Sophomore Celebration in Frost on September 30
Junior Convocation in Memorial Church on October 3

CLASS OF 1988
Attention Sophomores!
Wanted: Students to give presentations at the JUNIOR
CONVOCATION on October 3 next fall.
Qualifications: Willingness to share your creativity with members of
your class. Projects from engineering, creative writing,
studio arts, drama music, and other fields are all
welcome!
To apply: Stop by the Dean of Undergraduate Studies' Office in
Building One and talk to Ellen Woods or Charlie
Junkerman about your idea. Or call them at 723-9378
or 725-1533. Or write a letter describing your proposal.
DON'T BE SHY!
Special Note: Feel free to nominate a friend!
