Pop/Rock/Jazz
Elsewhere
The New Varsity: Presents Big Bang
Beat, a high-energy Motown sound
guaranteed to shake. Performance is
tomorrow at 9:35 p.m. Palo Alto.
321-1246.
The Stern Grove Midsummer
Music Festival: Presents New Or
lean's famed Preservation Hall jazz
Band currently in its 25th anniver
sary season. Performance Sunday at
2 p.m. Admission free. 1090
Sansome St., San Francisco. 398-
6551.
The Great American Music Hall:
Presents Butch Whacks & The Glass
Packs in their fourth annual
"Farewell Reunion." Performances
tonight, tomorrow and Sunday at 11
p.m. 859 O'Farrell St., San Fran
cisco. 885-0750.
The Rova Saxophone Quartet:
Presents an evening of jazz with co
performance by the Soviet Union's

leading jazz group, The Ganelin
Trio. Performance tonight at 8 p.m.
4320 California St., San Francisco.
548-1817.
Upcoming Bill Graham Presents
Concerts: Depeche Mode, July 5 at
the Greek Theatre; The Beach Boys,
July 18 at the Cal Expo Amphi
theatre; and Jean Luc Ponty, July 19
at the Greek Theatre.
Lectures
Here
The Stanford Jazz Workshop:
Presents the first of its summer
long jazz lecture series on July 3,
featuring Ted Gioia with his discus
sion of "West Coast Jazz Then and

Now." Braun Music Center, 7:30
p.m. 386-8535.
Exhibits
Elsewhere
San Francisco Art Institute:

Presents "Recent Sculptural Paint
ing," highlighting the work of six
Bay Area artists who work three
dimensional surfaces. Runs through
July 19. 800 Chestnut St., San
Francisco. 771-7020.
The Holos Gallery: Presents a
retrospective exhibition of
holograms, photographs, posters
and drawings by the artist co
operative Laser Affiliates. Runs
every Saturday from 6-10 p.m.
through Sept. 28. 1792 Haight St.,
San Francisco. 668-HOLO.
Classical
Here
The Harvard-Radcliffe Collegium
Musicum: Presents an evening of

choral music, featuring the works of
Schutz, Brahms, Poulenc, Hin
demith and Randall Thompson.
The performance will be tomorrow
at 8 p.m. at Memorial Church.
The Stanford Department of Music:
Presents a concert of Baroque cham
ber music at 8 p.m on July 1. Dink
elspiel Auditorium. 723-4317.
Performance
Elsewhere
The Joffirey Ballet: Presents its two
week San Francisco engagement
opening with "Birthday Variations."
Performances July 1-13. War
Memorial Opera House, San Fran
cisco. 552-8011.
Theatre Works: Presents "Little
Shop of Horrors" tomorrow at 8
p.m. Lucie Stern Theatre, 1305
Middlefield Rd., Palo Alto. 328-
0600.
