The Department of Statistics
Stanford University
has a
STATISTICAL CONSULTING
SERVICE
The Department operates a drop-in consulting service in which advanced
graduate students, under faculty supervision, are available as consultants
during specified hours. The Summer 1986 schedule is given below.
You are encouraged to drop in for statistical advice at any stage of
research. You are especially encouraged to come in early in your research
so that the consultants may be helpful at the design stage.
There will be no charge for the consultations
SUMMER HOURS PLACE
Monday 1-3
Tuesday 2:30-4:15 Sequoia Hall, room 120
Wednesday 1-3
Thursday 10-12
The service operates from July Ist until August Bth.
