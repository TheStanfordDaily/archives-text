NOTICE OF ELECTION
Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of Section 5529 of the
California Public Resources Code, that a general District election to be
consolidated with the County-wide General Election will be held in the
Midpeninsula Regional Open Space District Office on the 4th day of
November, 1906 to choose a successor for four (4) offices of Director to
be elected in Wards 1, 2, 5 and 6.
Generally, the wards include the following areas:
Ward 1 - Los Gatos, Monte Sereno, Sratoga and a portion of Cupertino.
Ward 2 - Los Altos, Los Altos Hills, portions of Cupertino and
Sunnyvale.
Ward 5 - Palo Alto, Stanford, portions of Los Altos Hills and East
Palo Alto.
Ward 6 - Menk) Park, Atherton, Ladera, Portola Valley, Woodside,
portions of East Palo Alto, Emerald Lake Hills, Farm Hills and
Skyline.
The qualifications required by the principal act for each office for which
candidates may be nominated are: Each Director shall be a resident and
an elector from the ward from which he or she is nominated.
Official nominating petitions from eligible candidates desiring to file for
any of the elective offices may be obtained from the Office of the Clerk of
the District, Old Mill Office Center, Building C, Suite 135, 201 San Antonio
Circle, Mountain View, California 94040 (telephone number 415 949-5500)
on or after Monday, July 14, 1986. Said nominations shall be filed in the
Office of the Clerk of the District not later than 5 p.m. on Friday, August 8,
1986.
Dated this 25th day of June, 1986
