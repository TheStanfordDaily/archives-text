Fabulous Fendick
Though she was all the way in England, Patty Fendick nevertheless donned her Stanford
tennis cap while playing at the Wimbledon championships. The good-luck omen paid
off handsomely as Fendick and partner Jill Hetherington of the University of Florida
surprised the tennis establishment by advancing to the semifinals of the women's
doubles.
