The Stanford Daily (USPS 518-420) is an independent student newspaper owned and published by The
Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation Main office Storke Student Publications Bulldng, Stanford, CA
94305
Subscription rates: in the United States, Canada and Mexico, $50 per year and $20 per quarter. Send
check or money order In advance. Seoond class postage paid at Palo Alto, CA Postmaster: send address
changes to The Stanford Dally, Storke Student Publications Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
The Daily is published Monday through Friday during the academic year with the exceptions of Dead
Week, when one issue is published, and finals week, when no issues are published. The Dally Is published
Tuesdays and Fridays during the eight-week summer quarter
Nothing on the opinions page necessarily represents a position of the entire Dally staff or the Lelend Stan
ford Junior University Editorials represent the opinion of a majority of editorlel board members voting on
eech topic The board conaists of seven Daily editors and three at-large members from the community
elected by The Daily staff
Letters, columns and cartoons repreeent only the views of their authors Advertisements do not necessar
ily reflect editorial opinion. The Daily regrets it cannot guarantee the return of any article aubrmtted. All sub
mitted articles are subject to editing.
Production at the ASSU Typesetting Shop. Storke Student Publication Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
