# Welcome to the Farm
## Class of 1990 
### 
The Class of 1990, 1,575 freshmen
representing 49 states and 12 foreign
countries, began life on the Farm this
week. Almost 200 orientation
volunteers were on hand to make
them feel at home and to organize five
days of moving-in, acclimatization,
learning and fun.
