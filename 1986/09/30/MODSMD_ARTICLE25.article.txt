# Cardinal in top 20
## 
### 
The 3-0 Stanford football team has broken into the UPI Coaches' Poll Top 20 for
the first time since 1982, it was announced late yesterday.
The Cardinal was ranked No. 20 in the latest UPI poll, which was released Mon
day.
The last time Stanford had made the top 20 was after defeating Purdue and
Ohio State in 1982, John Elway's senior year.
The Cardinal is one of six Pacific 10 Conference teams currently in the top 20.
