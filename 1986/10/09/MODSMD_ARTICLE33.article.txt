# Reference Services...
## 
### 
Reference librarians throughout the
library system are expert in locating
k. elusive library materials, in provid
ing instruction in the use of library catalogs, in
explaining the use of reference sources, and in
answering all questions involving effective use
of libraries. Use this expertise and ask for
assistance whenever necessary. Reference
librarians and curators will also recommend
appropriate strategies or techniques for locating
materials for research projects ranging from term
papers to dissertations. Contact with the subject
specialist in your field will help you find
research materials scattered throughout the
Stanford library system as well as materials held
at other institutions. (To arrange a consultation,
call or stop by the reference or service desk in
the library serving your discipline.)
