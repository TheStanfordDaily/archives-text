The Stanford Daily (USPS 518-420) to an independent student newspaper owned and published by The
Stanford Daly Publishing Corporation. Main office Storke Student Publications BuicSng. Stanford. CA
94305
Subscription rates in the United States. Canada and Mexico. $50 per year and $20 per quarter. Send
check or money order in advance Second class postage paid at Palo Alto. CA. Postmaster send adtfress
changes to The Stanford Daily. Storke Student Publications Building. Stanford, CA 94305
The Daily is published Monday through Fnday during the academic year with the exceptions of Dead
Week, when one issue is published, and finals week, when no issues are published The Daily is published
Tuesdays and Fridays during the eight-week summer quarter.
Nothing on the opinions page necessarily represents a position of the entire Daily staff or the Leland Stan
ford Junior University Editorials represent the opinion of a majority of editorial board members voting or)
each topic The board consists of seven Daly editors and three at-large members from the commirtly
elected by The DaHy staff
Letters, columns and cartoons represent only the views of their authors Advertisements do not necessar
ily reflect editorial opinion The Daily regrets it cannot guarantee the return of any article submitted. All sub
mitted articles are subject to editing.
