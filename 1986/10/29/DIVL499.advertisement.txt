NOTICE OF
NONDISCRIMINATORY POLICY
AS TO STUDENTS
The Childrens Center of the Stanford Community ad
mits students of any race, color, national and ethnic
origin to all the rights, privileges, programs, and ac
tivities generally accorded or made available to stu
dents at the school. It does not discriminate on the
basis of race, color, national and ethnic origin in ad
ministration of its educational policies, scholarship
and loan programs, and athletic and other school
administered programs.
