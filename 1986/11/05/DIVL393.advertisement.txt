ASK Computer Systems Inc.
Comes to Stanford!
ASK is the leading independent supplier of
software for the manufacturing industry. The
software includes manufacturing control,
financial, project management, field service,
quality control, human resources, and managerial
decision products.
On Thursday, November 6, 1986 ASK will host
an Information Session in the Cypress Room at
Tressider Union from 7-9 pm. Desired curriculum
may include engineers with computer science
coursework for Software Engineer opportunities,
Journalism, English and Humanities for
Technical Documentation positions.
ASK will conduct interviews on campus on
Tuesday, November 24. Check with the Career
Planning and Placement office for requirement
criteria and company literature.
