Goldman, Sachs & Co.
Invites all interested Seniors to attend an information
meeting on the two-year Financial Analyst Program. Please
join us next Monday, November 24 from 8-10 p.m. in
Tresidder s Cypress Lounge North and South. Stanford
graduates and other members of the Firm's Investment
Banking Division will offer a brief overview of the Program
and look forward to answering questions and speaking
informally with all attendees. Refreshments will be served.
i % <
Goldman Sachs will be at the C.P .P .C. for two open and
two closed interviewing schedules on Friday, January 23,
1987. Interested Seniors from all academic disciplines are
encouraged to participate.
Please address questions to: L.C. Joseph (Joe) Mosnier ('B4)
Goldman Goldman, Sachs & Co.
85 Broad Street
New York, New York 10004
