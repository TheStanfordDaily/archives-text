The following public service an
nouncements appear courtesy of The
Stanford Daily. For more information,
please contact Sue at the Public Service
Opportunities Clearinghouse, Owen
House, Monday-Friday, noon-4 p.m.,
725-2860.
Michael Stone, a recent graduate in
Latin American Studies will give a slide
presentation called "Through Refugee
Eyes: War and Health in El Salvador"
Monday at noon. The presentation will
be held in the Assembly Room of the In
ternational Center.
Winners of the 1986 Public Ser
vice Summer Fellowships and
Friends of Memorial Church Summer
Scholarships will speak at a forum Mon
day 7-9 p.m. The forum, "Fellowships
for the Public Good," will be held in the
Round Room of Memorial Church, with
a reception following.
Deadline for applications for the
Carnegie Endowment for International
Peace Fellowship is Tuesday at noon.
Application materials are availible at the
Public Service Center. Call Sue Gray at
725-2860 for more information.
Don't forget to donate your house
Christmas tree Thursday, Dec. 18. Bring
trees to the Owen House lawn between
2 and 4 p.m. Trees will be donated to
families in East Palo Alto.
The Ecumenical Hunger Pro
gram needs volunteers during its
Christmas drive. You can pack food
boxes, pick up donations, deliver food
baskets, etc. Contact Nevida Butler at
323-7781 for more information.
