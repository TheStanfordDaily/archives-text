DEAD WEEK
is the week preceding final examination
in Autumn Quarter. During this period,
no quizes or examinations should be
given. No musical, dramatic and athletic
events involving compulsory student
participation should be scheduled.
Dead Week dates this Quarter are:
Sunday, December 7th through
Sunday, December 14, inclusive.
