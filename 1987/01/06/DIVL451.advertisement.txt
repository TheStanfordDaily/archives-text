WALK-IN HOUSING
Walk-in assignments for vacancies in University
housing begin Wednesday, January 7 at 6:00 p.m. on
the second floor of Tressider Union. If you are
registered and unassigned, you are eligible. A valid
Stanford ID is required. Contact the Stanford
Housing Center, Old Union 317, for more details.
