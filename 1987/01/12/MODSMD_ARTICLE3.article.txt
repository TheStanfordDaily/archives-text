# East-West confrontation
## 
### 
Honorary head coach Jonathan Studebaker joined 74,000 other fans in
watching the annual East-West Shrine game, held at Stanford Stadium
on Saturday. The all-star team from the West, which included Stanford

players Thomas Henley and David Wyman, eventually triumphed in the
game by a score of 24-21. The Card pair put on an impressive perfor
mance before the home crowd.
