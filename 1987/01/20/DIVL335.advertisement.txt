MAKE THE SMART MOVE . . . HBEB
TO OAK CREEK APARTMENTS
Palo Alto's premier luxury address within walking distance of
Stanford Shopping Center. Lighted tennis courts and resident
teaching pro, NAUTILUS Health Club, Social Club,
swimming pools, intercoms, security patrol, and much more!
Studios one- and two- bedroom unfurnished and furnished
apartments available for short or long term leasing. (No pets,
please.) One- bedroom Units from $950.
1600 SAND HILL ROAD (415) 321-1701
ASK ABOUT OUR MOVE-IN INCENTIVES
The
Hopkins Marine Station
announces the topic for the 1987
Spring course "Problems in Marine Biology":
Cephalopods
Course work includes field and lab studies, lectures, and
individual projects. There are no formal prerequisites
and all majors are invited to apply.
I
Interested? Find out more at the Biology Student
Services Lounge, Herrin Hall, Room T333, on
Wednesday, 21 January. Come by anytime between
1:00 and 4:30 to talk with faculty members and former
students about this unique research opportunity.
