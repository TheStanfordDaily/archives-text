SENIOR CASINO NIGHT
The Stanford Alumni Association
invites all Seniors and their guest to an evening of
fun and games, Friday, February 6, 1987.
Tickets go on sale starting Wednesday, January 21, 1987
at 11:00 a.m. in the Bowman Alumni House.
Tickets must be purchased in advance
and are available on a limited basis.
$12.00 per person. (Alumni Association members, $8.00)
Be sure to bring your Stanford I.D. to purchase tickets.
