JOHN GARDNER FELLOWSHIP PROGRAM
Graduating Seniors are invited to apply for
Fellowships in Public Service
Gardner Fellows receive a $12,000 stipend for eleven months and an
opportunity to work in a government or voluntary sector agency in
the United States with a distinguished mentor.
• Applications and information are available at the Public Service
Center, Owen House (next to the bike shop) Jeanne Wahl Halleck
725-2870 or 723-0992.
• Applications deadline is Friday, February 13, 1987
