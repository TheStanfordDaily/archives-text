COMMUNICATION BETWEEN PCs AND HOSTS
SU-PC/IP: A HANDS-ON WORKSHOP
Participants will learn how to use the Stanford University Personal Computer Internet Protocol (SU-PC/IP) software for
high-speed communication between IBM PCs and Stanford University's host computers.
Offered twice, 20 participants per session:
Monday, February 23, 2:00 - 4:00 P.M. FULL
Tuesday, February 24, 10:00 A.M. - Noon
Location: PC classroom, ground floor of Sweet Hall (between CERAS and Meyer Library)
Prerequisites: Knowledge of PC operation; knowledge of operating systems on hosts wish to communicate with
SU-Mac/IP: A HANDS-ON WORKSHOP
Participants will learn how to use the Stanford University Macintosh Internet Protocol (SU-Mac/IP) software for high-speed
communication between Apple Macintosh computers and Stanford University's host computers.
Offered twice, 20 participants per session:
Wednesday, February 25, 2:00 - 4:00 P.M.
Thursday, February 26, 10:00 A.M. - Noon
Location: Macintosh classroom, ground floor of Sweet Hall
Prerequisites: Knowledge of Macintosh operation; knowledge of operating systems on hosts wish to communicate with
