ECUMENICAL J WEDNESDAY NOON FORUM
MINISTRIES [EMS] Fellowship & Discussion
AT STANFORD Over Lunch (provided)
SEVEN DEADLY SINS - A CONTEMPORARY PERSPECTIVE
COVETOUSNESS
Catherine Milton, Director, Public Service Center
Old Union Clubhouse, Top Floor, Common Room
Volunteers Sought
Volunteers (between the ages 18-55 years old) who suffer
from recurrent genital herpes sores are needed to participate
in studies aimed at determining the reasons for these
recurrences which may eventually lead to vaccines alleviating
this malady.
Participants will be asked to give 4 blood samples over a
4-5 week period. For further information please contact:
Ron Korh Ph.D. 723-5909
John Torseth M.D. 9 a.m. - 12 p.m.
