PLANNING TO BE IN NEW YORK THIS SUMMER?
Do you need a place to live? Barnard College offers summer housing at moderate rates to
students who plan to work or study in New York City. Dormitory facilities are available from the
end of May through the middle of August.
Located at 116 th and Broadway, Barnard College is adjacent to Columbia University and
accessible to all of the cultural resources of the city.
Barnard's dormitories offer a variety of living arrangements - - singles, doubles and apartments.
