The Palo Alto LSAT Program
LSAT/GMAT Preparation for June Exams
The Program's 10-session courses for the LSAT and GM AT are
under way and meet on Tuesdays (LSAT) and Wednesdays
(GM AT). All substantive instruction will be completed by exam
week. Instructor, a former Stanford faculty member, has 8 years'
experience in preparing students for these exams. Each course
costs $350. Median scores: 39 (LSAT); 670 (GMAT). There are
a few openings in each course.
Call 857-1758
