I REBATE ENVELOPE I
The Stanford Bookstore, a nonrprofit corporation, will pay a rebate
to Stanford Students, faculty, and staff for purchases of BOOKS made
from May 1, 1986 through April 30, 1987.
The rebate amount will be determined by the Bookstore Board of
Directors. It will be derived from net income from sales and paid by
check after October 1,1987. (Exception: special order books. Master
Card and Visa purchases are not eligible for rebate).
I
1. Save all receipts on which you have purchased a book(s).
PLEASE DO NOT CIRCLE OR UNDERLINE AMOUNTS
ON YOUR RECEIPTS.
2. Keep your receipts in this envelope. Stanford Bookstore
Charge Customers keep your charge slip copy in lieu of a
receipt.
3. Before turning the envelope in, be sure to print your
name and mailing address in the space provided. Also,
be sure to indicate your affiliation with Stanford in the
space provided.
4. Turn envelopes in at the Bookstore's customer service
window between April 20th and April 30th, 1987 (ormail
to the Standord Bookstore, Stanford, CA 94305). NO
LATE ENVELOPES WILL BE ACCEPTED. Rebate checks
will be available after Oct. 1, 1987.
