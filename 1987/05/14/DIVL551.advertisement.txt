IBM
in cooperation with
Instruction and Research Information Systems (IRIS)
presents
IBM PERSONAL SYSTEM/2
Stanford faculty, staff, and students are invited to attend a special showing of
IBM's new family of personal computers.
Date: Wednesday, May 20,1987
Time: 10:00 am -12:00 pm Formal Presentation and Demonstrations
12:00 - 2:00 pm Informal Demonstrations
2:00 - 4:00 pm Formal Presentation and Demonstrations (repeated)
Place: CERAS 112 (LGI) and Ground Floor Lobby
The following IBM representatives will be available to answer questions:
Dr. Stephen Romberg, Academic Specialist
Nick Trufyn, Senior System Engineer
Gia Knaus, Marketing Representative
John Marshall, System Engineer
Jim Lynch, Personal System/2 Specialist
Dr. Bill Geoghegan, Scientific Center
Dr. Paul Halpern, Scientific Center
Equipment demonstrations include:
PS/2 Model 30
PS/2 Model 50
PS/2 Model 60
Desktop Publishing System
Scanner
Proprinter
For more information, contact IRIS at 723-1055.
