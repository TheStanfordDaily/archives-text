AVOID THE RUSH!
Don't wait until the last minute to get your car
serviced befor you leave for Summer break. For
efficient and safe driving, remember you car's
needs: „
1) LUBE
2) OIL CHANGE & FILTER
3) CHECK COOLING SYSTEM
4) TIRES CHECKED FOR SAFETY
