THE CLERK
OF STAHTEFORD
A clerk there was of Staneford also,
That unto Englysh hadde longe ygo
Reading Conrad, Austen, Chaucer and much More.
By daye hashed he bihynde the Kytchyn dore.
A pride of Glikbarg Family highte he Steve
And gladly wold he lerne and gladly leve.
from Los Angeles, CA
