The Palo Alto LSAT Program
LSAT/GMAT Preparation for October Exams
The Program's courses for the Oct. 17 GMAT and Oct. 24 LSAT
start the weekend of August 8 and meet primarily Tuesday
(LSAT) and Wednesday (GMAT) evenings. All curricular
preparation, instruction, and review is presented by a former
Stanford faculty member and Stanford JD who is a practicing
attorney with nine years experience teaching these courses.
Enrollment in each class limited to 15 students. The fee is $350.
