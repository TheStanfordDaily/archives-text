I DEAD WEEK! I
I Dead Week is the week immediately preceding final I
I examinations in winter quarter. I
I During this period, no quizzes or examinations should be I
I given and no musical, dramatic, and athletic events including I
I compulsory student participations should be scheduled. I
I Dead Week dates this quarter are I
I Saturday, August 8 - Thursday, August 13, I
I inclusive. I
