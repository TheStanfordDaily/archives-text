Once Around the Quad announcements
are printed as a service to the public.
Deadline for announcements is two
business days before publication at I p.m.
Announcements should be placed in the
appropriate slot just inside The Daily's
front door. Only events that are free,
on campus and open to the public may
appear in Once Around the Quad.
Only events taking place on the day of
publication or the follow ing day will
be printed. Continuing announcements
may only run for a period of two weeks.
