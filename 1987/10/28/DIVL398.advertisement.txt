SENATE HAPPENINGS
At the last meeting
The Senate had its 2nd meeting of Fall Quarter on October 13th. Here are
some of the bills it passed:
1 To allocate funding for the Speakers Bureau to bring Robert Owen to
Stanford.
2. To approve the new students on University committees.
3. To modify the budget of the Program Board so that it could purchase a
Macintosh.
4. To approve the Fall election timetable.
5. To request more student office space from the committee on student
office space.
6. To encourage the Law School to bolster its non-discrimination policy.
The Senate is also looking at issues such as Parking and Western Culture,
and if you think there is an issue that should be addressed by the Senate
come talk to a Senator during
SENATOR OFFICE HOURS
Monday—Friday, 12 —5
Every Monday through Friday, from 12-5, one member of the Senate will
be in the ASSU lounge to answer your questions and help you with your
concerns. So what are you waiting for — come see a Senator today!
