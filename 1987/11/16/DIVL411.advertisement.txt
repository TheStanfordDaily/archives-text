Invites all interested Seniors to attend an information
meeting on the two-year Financial Analyst Program. Please
join us next Monday, November 23 from 6-8 p.m. in
Tresidder's Oak Lounge East. Stanford graduates and other
members of the Firm's Investment Banking Division will
offer a brief overview of the Program and look forward to
speaking informally with all attendees.
Refreshments will be served.
Goldman Sachs will be at the C.P.P.C. for two open and
two closed interviewing schedules on Thursday, January 21,
1988. Interested Seniors (tomall academic disciplines are
encouraged to participate.
Please address questions to: Robert J. Lisbonne
Gold man I Goldman, Sachs & Co.

New York, New York 10004
