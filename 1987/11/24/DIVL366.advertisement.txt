STATE FARM INSURANCE COMPANIES
MONDAY, NOVEMBER 30th
We will be recruiting seniors who will
graduate in May or June, 1988 for our intern
program. This program runs January through
May and is a paid, part-time position, $9.09
per hour. The interns will be trained as claim
representatives. Job duties will include
investigating losses, obtaining record
statements, evaluating coverage, and
negotiating settlements. Positions available
throughout the Peninsula.
If interested, please sign up on the schedule
in the
Career Planning and Placement Center.
Affirmative Action candidates
are encouraged
to apply.
