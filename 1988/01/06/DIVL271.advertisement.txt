The Palo Alto LSAT Program
LSAT Preparation for February 20 EXAM
The Program's short course begins January 7 and meets mostly
on Tuesday evenings and some weekend mornings. All
preparation, instruction, and review are furnished by a former
Stanford faculty member and Stanford J.D. who is a practicing
attorney with 9 years experience in preparing students for the
LSAT. Enrollment is limited to 10 persons. The course fee is
$350.
