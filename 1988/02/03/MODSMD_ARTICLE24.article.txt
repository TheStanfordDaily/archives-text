# Letters policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes expressions of
opinion from its readers. letters are sub
ject to editing and must include name,
phone number and Stanford affiliation,
if any. Bring letters to The Daily's offices
in the Storke Publications Building or
send them to:
letters to the Editor
Storke Publications Building
Stanford, CA 94305
Letters must be typed, double-spaced.
