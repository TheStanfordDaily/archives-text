# Letters policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes expressions of
opinion from its readers. Letters should
be kept as short as possible and are
subject to editing. They must include
name, phone number and Stanford af
filiation, if any. Anonymous letters will
not be considered for publication. Bring
letters to The Daily's offices in the
Storke Publications Building across from
Terman Engineering on the Stanford
campus, or send them to:
Letters to the Editor
The Stanford Daily
Storke Publications Building
Stanford, CA 94305
Letters must be typed, double-spaced.
