# Achebe visits Stanford tomorrow
## 
### 
Chinua Achebe, award-winning Nigerian novelist and
poet, will speak tomorrow at Branner Hall at 7:30 p.m.
His novels include "Things Fall Apart," "A Man of the
People" and, most recently, "Anthills of the Savannah,"
from which he will read. Achebe won the first Common
wealth Poetry Prize in 1972 for his volume of poetry,
"Christmas in Biafra," written during and after the Biafran
War.
Holder of 11 honorary doctorates from American, British
and Canadian universities, Achebe is internationally recog
nized as one of today's great living writers.
