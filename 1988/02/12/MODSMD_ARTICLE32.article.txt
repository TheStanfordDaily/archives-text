Internships and Student Employment
As the summer job search Intensifies, we find that many students are asking the following question ...
Beside Odyssey, the Internship database, what other resources are availablo at the CPPC to aid me with
my summer |ob search?
Answer: Odyssey is a good place to start. In addition, check out the national Internship and summer
employment directories available at the Internship counter. For those of you Interested in designing your
own Internship, pick up a copy of the Guide to Student Employment. Last but certainly not least, sign up
to meet with one of the career counselors.
