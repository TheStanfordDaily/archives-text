U.S. Marine kidnapped — A
Marine officer serving with the
United Nations peacekeeping
force was abducted yesterday in
southern Lebanon. There was no
immediate claim of responsibility
for the kidnapping. The officer was
identified as Lt. Col. William Hig
gins of Danville, Ky. Page 3
Two for two — The baseball team
defeated USF 5-3 yesterday for its
second win in a row, which was
highlighted by pinch hitter Jeff
Saenger's second home run of the
year. Pitcher Bryan Kaiser started
and went four innings in his first
game since an elbow injury he
suffered just before the start of
the season. Page 9
