# Alternative Test Sites
## 
### 
San Mateo County: 573-2516. 225 37th Avenue, San Mateo, 8:30 to 11 a.m.,
Tuesday and Friday. Watch a 10-minute video, ask questions if desired. No
appointment necessary. Free and anonymous. Do not have to be a county resident.
Santa Clara County: 408/299-5913. 976 Lenzen Avenue, San Jose, near the
Alameda. Open 8-11 a.m., L 2:30-3:30 p.m., Monday-Friday except the fourth
Wednesday of each month. Arrive at the beginning of the clinic hours. No
appointment necessary. Free and anonymous.
Palo Alto Red Cross: 322-2143. 400 Mitchell Lime, Palo Alto, across from the
Palo Alto train station, in back of Macarthur Park. $20/ test, includes counseling.
Done every Monday from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. No appointment necessary. Results
available with counseling by appointment two weeks later, also Monday only.
