# Where condoms and foams are available on campus
## 
### 
Cowell Student Health Center (723-4841) sells condoms,
sponges, spermicidal foam and jellies. Open Monday
through Friday 9:30 a.m. to 1 p.m. and Thursday and
Friday 2-5 p.m.
Contraceptive Information Center (723-4870) (2nd floor of
Cowell Student Health Center) sells condoms and
contraceptive sponges. Open Monday through Friday noon
to 5 p.m.

The Store at Tresidder Union (723-9224) sells condoms,
spermicidal foam, jelly and contraceptive sponges. Open
7:30 a.m. to midnight
The Stanford AIDS Education Project (AEP) sells
condoms, "dental dams,' spermicidal lubricants and other
"safer sex" products. AEP Safer-Sex Shoppe operates in
White Plaza and Room 8 of the Old Firehouse. Call
723-0258 for hours.
