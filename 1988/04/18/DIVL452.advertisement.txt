NOTICE OF WRIT
TO: RICHARD TZOUI KAU LI. OTHERWISE
KNOWN AS
RICHARD TZAR KAI U
IN THE SUPREME COURT OF BRITISH CO
LUMBIA, ACTION NO. 12755, KAMLOOPS
REGISTRY. BETWEEN: DARIN DAVID KREPS,
PLAINTIFF, AND: WAI LIM CHAN, ANNIE HUNG
SUEN LO CHAN AND RICHARD TZGUI KAI U,
otherwise known as RICHARD TZAR KAI U,
DEFENDANTS.
TAKE NOTICE that Darin David Kreps has
issued a Writ of Summons in the Supreme Court
of British Columbia, Kamloops Registry, against
you, for:
General damages, special damages, costs, pre
judgment interest and such further and other
relief as deemed meet by the Court, as a result
of injuries which he sustained in a motor vehicle
accident which occurred on September 17,
1985 in the City of Vancouver, in the Province of
British Columbia.
AND FURTHER TAKE NOTICE that you are en
titled within twenty-eight (28) days after publica
tion of this Notice, inclusive of such date, to
enter an Appearance in person or by your
Solicitor at the office of the Registrar of the Su
preme Court of British Columbia. 455 Columbia
Street, Kamloops. British Columbia.
THIS NOTICE is filed by the law firm of Home
Marr Wooruff, Barristers and Solicitors, of Suite
600 - 175 Second Avenue, in the City of Kam
loops, in the Province of British Columbia,
Solicitors for the Plaintiff.
