HEY! Do you live in Flo Mo,
Mirrielees, Lagunita or Branner?
(Or study at Meyer?)
Did you know there's a writing tutor RIGHT THERE to assist you
with paper drafts? Whatever your ability as a writer — whether you're confident
or struggling for words — the undergraduate peer writing tutors can help you to
improve your final product!
If you live in one of the above residences, see your dorm's academic
coordinator for more information.
Tutors are also available at Meyer library, Sunday through Thursday evenings,
in Room 147. Sign up in advance at CTL, 110 Sweet Hall.
For more information, call or stop by CTL, 110 Sweet Hall, 723-1326.
