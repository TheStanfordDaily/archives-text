# FRIDAY
## 
### 
Rumble Seats on the Farm —
Members of the Class of 1938
reminisced at their 50-year reunion
last weekend Page 3

Daily staffers! Happy Hour at 4:30
p.m. today in the offices. Then —
yep, you guessed it — the End
of-the-Year Banquet begins at 7
p.m. at Hyatt Rickey's Coat and
Tie. Dinner and Dance Rides will
be available from The Daily to the
banquet at 6:30 p.m. STOP will
provide safe rides home afterward.
