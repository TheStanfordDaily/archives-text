# Adv. 6 Page 4
##

* * Observations "
"Poor folks steal. Rich folks embezzle. Poor folks get caught; they
go to San Quentin. The perjurer gets caught; he goes to San Clemente.
Poor folks lie. Rich folks prevaricate."
—Jesse Jackson campaigning in California last week.
