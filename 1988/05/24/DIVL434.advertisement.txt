■MPCCTANT
FCC ATI STUDENTS
From June 10, to September 16, 1988, all
university mail will be sent to the summer
address of students, not the local address. If you
do not file a summer address with the Office of
the Registrar, your university mail (including bill
and ID/Study list for Autumn quarter) will be sent
to your PERMANENT ADDRESS. Therefore,
please submit a summer address to the
Registrars Office Information Window in the
lobby of the Old Union as soon as possible.
