# Entertainment
## Diversions 
### Mia Manzulli Staff writer 
Sing, Sang, Sung:
I was talking to graduate student Kevin
Quinn, a member of the esteemed Fleet Street
Singers, and he let me know that the group
has a big show coming up. In fact, the Spring
Concert is tomorrow night at 8 p.m. in Cub
berly Auditorium. The concert is free and
Kevin invited "the whole campus."
Attention all Juniors:
To celebrate our last days as juniors, the
class of '89 is asked to join their new Senior
Class Presidents in White Plaza today from
11 a.m. 'til 1 p.m. for pizza.
Music Abounds:
You have one more chance to hear the Stan
ford Symphony this quarter. Beethoven's 9th
and the Barber Violin Concerto are being per
formed Friday and Saturday night at 8 p.m.
in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Also this weekend is "Temple of Fire," a
"performance art rock musical" presented by
the Stanford Department of Drama. Written
and directed by Simone Genatt with music by
Ed Spiegel of Walkabout, the show takes place
Friday and Saturday at 8 p.m. and Sunday at
2 p.m. in the Little Theater.
