The Palo Alto LSAT Program
SUMMER SCHOOL PREP FOR FALL LSAT/GMAT
The Program will offer special courses for the fall LSAT and GMAT to coincide
with summer school classes. Both courses will begin the weekend of June 25
and will meet Tuesdays (LSAT) and Wednesdays (GMAT) in the evening
through the end of August. These eight-meeting courses will offer thorough
instruction in all phases of the exams. All curricular preparation, instruction, and
review are presented by a former Stanford faculty member and Stanford Law
graduate who is a practicing Palo Alto attorney with 10 years' experience in
LSAT/GMAT teaching. The median scores of Stanford students in past courses
have been: 41 (LSAT); 690 (GMAT). Each class has a maximum enrollment of ten
students, and the course fee will be $375.
Call 857-1758
