Compact. Lightweight. Yet bursting with 286 desktop speed and power operating at 12 MHz with zero

Zenith Data Systems —today s leader in battery-operated portables.
ADVANCED DESKTOP PERFORMANCE TO GO. The Supers Port 286 features a fast 20MB hard disk
and 3.5" 1.44M8 floppy drive which allows you to carry volumes of data and application programs
IMB RAM —expandable to 2MB with EMS and extended memory capabilities to run MS-DOS® and
MS-OS/2® PC XT and AT compatibility to give you the ability to multi-task applications. Plus a detachable
battery with AC adapter/recharger. For 286 desktop standards in a truly portable PC.
BATTERY POWER FOR THE U)NG RUN. The Supers Port 286 even gives you longer battery life for
greater productivity on the road. Thanks to Zenith's comprehensive Intelligent Power Management—a
system that lets you control how your battery power is consumed so you won't waste valuable energy on
components and peripherals not in use.
AN IMPRESSIVE DISPLAY OF QUALITY For crisp text and superior readability, the Supers Port 286
also features Zenith's renowned dazzling back-lit Supertwisl LCD screen with 640 x 400 double-scan
CGA resolution for greater clarity. The screen even tilts back 180-degrees so it lies flat to accommodate
a desktop monitor.
