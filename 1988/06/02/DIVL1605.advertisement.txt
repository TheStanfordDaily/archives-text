CONGRATULATIONS
FELICIA PAIR!
Remember those days when you went around cutting all the seets and blankets in sight,
after you had learned how to use the scissors, or when you cut off your doll's hair because
you though it would grow back? Well, you've come a long way since then. Sadly, those day
are over, along with your years as a student. Now, you are moving on to follow your dream,
; dream you have had ever since you were a child with scissors in your hand.
GOOD LUCK! WE LOVE YOU!
Love, Mom, Dad and Tricia
from New York, NY
