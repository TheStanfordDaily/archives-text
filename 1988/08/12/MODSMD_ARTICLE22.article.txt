# The 						Stanford Daily
## 
### 
The Stanford Daily (USPS 518-420) is
an independent student newspaper owned
and published by The Stanford Daily Pub
lishing Corp., Storke Student Publications
Building, Stanford, CA 94305
Letters, columns, cartoons and adver
tisements do not necessarily reflect edito
rial opinion.
The Daily is published weekdays during
the academic year except Dead Week, when
one issue is published, finals week and
holidays, and biweekly during summer
quarter.
Subscription rates in the United States,
Canada and Mexico, $75 per year and $30
per quarter Second class postage paid at
Palo Alto, CA. Send check or money order
in advance.
Production at the ASSU Typesetting
Shop, Storke Student Publication Build
ing, Stanford. CA 94305 Pnnted by K-P
Press, 37428 Centralmont Place, F-emont,
CA 94536.
