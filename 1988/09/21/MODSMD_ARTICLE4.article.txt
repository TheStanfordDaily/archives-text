The Stanford Daily (USPS 518-420) is an independent student newspaper owned and published by
The Stanford Daily Publishing Corp., Storke Student Publications Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
Letters, columns, cartoons and advertisements do not necessarily reflect editorial opinion.
The Daily is published weekdays during the academic year except Dead Week, when one issue is
published, finals week and holidays, and biweekly during summer quarter.
Subscription rates: in the United States, Canada and Mexico, $75 per year and $30 per quarter.
Second class postage paid at Palo Alto, CA. Send check or money order in advance.
Production at the ASSU Typesetting Shop, Storke Student Publication Building, Stanford, CA 94305.
Printed by F-P Press, 37428 Centralmont Place, Fremont, CA 94536.
Copyright 1988 by The Stanford Daily Publishing Corp., Stanford, California.
