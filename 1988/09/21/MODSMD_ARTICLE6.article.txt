# An up-close look: Freshmen reveal past successes
## 
### 
While she studied the Ute Indian
tribe in Colorado, he began writing
a 200-page novel in New York. One
student was politicking in New
Mexico. Another was gallivanting
through the Italian Riviera. A third
was making scientific break
throughs in Georgia.
Our 1,602 incoming freshmen all
have tales to tell, memories to share
and new challenges to meet. We
can't paint a full picture of all the
talents and diversity in the class of
'92. However, we did manage to
learn about some students' past ad
ventures as well as their hopes for
the future. Here's a look at just a
few of Stanford's up-and-coming
freshmen. Welcome, one-and-all, to
the Farm.
