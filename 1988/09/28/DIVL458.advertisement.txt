# Adv. 7 Page 3
## 
### 
September 28 — 29. ham to 3RIVI
New and Used ★ Texts and Novels -* All Subjects
Anthropology Department ★ Building 110 on the Quad
WALK-IN
HOUSING
Walk-in assignments for vacancics in University housing
begin Thursday, September 29 at 6:00 p.m. on the second
floor of Tresidder Union. Many vacancies arc available.
If you are registered and unassigned. you are eligible to attend. A valid
Stanford ID and $50 housing deposit are required. Contact the Stanford
Housing Center, 110 Old Union, for more details.
CREATIVE WRITING
Creative Writing now offers five sections of Fiction Writing
(Eng. 90) each quarter.
Admission to Intermediate Fiction Writing (Eng. 190) now
depends on submission of a qualifying manuscript. Submit
no later than one week before registration. Late submissions
accepted this quarter only.
New course this quarter: Reading and Writing the Novella
(Eng. 290 A), taught by John L'Heureux. Submit a qualifying
manuscript one week before registration. Lite submission
accepted this quarter only.
