# A 						Recipe for a Change
## 
### 
Spinach Lasagne Special
(from "Diet for a Small Planet" by Frances Moore Lappe)
2-3 tablespoons oil 2 tablespoons chopped fresh
1/2 pound lasagne noodles parsley
2 cloves garlic, minced 1 pound spinach, washed,
1 medium onion, chopped drained and chopped
2 tomatoes, chopped 1 cup low-fat cottage cheese
10 medium mushrooms, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
sliced cheese
1/2 teaspoon each oregano, 8 to 10 oz. grated mozarella
basil and rosemary cheese
Cook noodles al dente, drain and set aside. Preheat oven to
350 degrees Fahrenheit.
Heat oil in a big skillet and saute garlic, onion, tomatoes
and mushrooms. When onion is translucent, add herbs and
spinach, stirring until spinach is wilted. Simmer.
Reserving one-half cup mozzarella cheese, combine cheeses
in a large bowl. Pour vegetables into cheese mixture and mix
thoroughly. Layer noodles alternately with vegetable-cheese
mixture in an 8-by-13-inch baking pan. Top with saved
mozzarella and more Parmesan cheese if desired. Bake for
one-half hour. Let sit five to 10 minutes before serving.
Makes six to eight servings.
