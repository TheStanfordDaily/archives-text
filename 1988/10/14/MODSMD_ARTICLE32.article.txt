# Entertainment
## Hot Stuff

Amnestia Internacional
The final concert in the Amnesty International
"Human Rights Now!" tour will be broadcast
from Buenos Aires, Argentina tomorrow from
noon to 6 p.m. on KFOG 104.5. Performers on
the tour include Bruce Springsteen, Sting, Peter
Gabriel, Tracy Chapman and Youssou N'Dour.
Swimming to Nicaragua
Saturday and Sunday at 8 p.m. in the Elliot
Program Center, student Andy Fleischmann
presents humorous stories about life in Central
Amenca. The show promises to be similar to
the critically acclaimed monologues in "Swim
ming to Cambodia." Admission is free.
You baroque my heart
Tonight at 8 p.m. in Campbell Recital Hall is
the first concert of the Music Department's fall
season, a presentation of baroque chamber
music. The Music Department presents about
10 concerts each month, all of which are free
or cheap. Admission to tonight's kick-off concert
is free.
