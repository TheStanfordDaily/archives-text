NOTICE OF
111 NONDISCRIMINATORY POLICY II
AS TO STUDENTS
The Children's Center of the Stanford Community
admits students of any race, color, national and ethnic
origin to all the rights, privileges, programs, and
activities generally accorded or made available to
students at the school. It does not discriminate on the
basis of race, color, national and ethnic origin in
administration of its educational policies, scholarship
and loan programs, and athletic and other
school-administered programs.
