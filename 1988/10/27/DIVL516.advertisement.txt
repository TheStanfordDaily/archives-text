Edward Abbey has won a devoted following with his caustic meditations on the
destruction of the American West. His novefs and works of non-fiction include:
The Brave Cowboy, The Monkey Wrench Gang, Black Sun, Desert Solitaire.
The Fool's Progress, already hailed as Abbey's opus, promises to launch this
writer of conservation essays and contemporary western fiction into the
mainstream of America's best fiction writers.
