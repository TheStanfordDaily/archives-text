# Portions of the Coalition's "Mandate for Change"
## 
### 
• Establish ethnic studies departments for blacks,
Chicanos, Asian Americans and American Indians.
• Make ethnic studies a graduation requirement.
• Increase funding for the ethnic centers.
• Hire full-time deans for the ethnic centers and increase
the centers' funding and staffing.
• Hire five minority faculty members, Including Jose Cuellar
and Greg Sarris.
• Make racism a Fundamental Standard violation.
• Put one student from each of the four minority groups,
plus four at-large students, on the Board of Trustees.
• Eliminate UJamaa's requirement that no more than half
the house residents be black.
