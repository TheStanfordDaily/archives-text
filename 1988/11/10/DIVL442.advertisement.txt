of litigation and trial work at the fourth largest law firm in the United States.
Gibson, Dunn & Crutcher, California's largest law firm, last year created a unique
two-year trial and litigation internship for 6-10 college graduates.
A team of Gibson, Dunn partners and senior associates will be interviewing students at
Tresidder Union.
Interview Sessions will take place on November 14 and 15 l 1988, from 8:45 a.m. to 4 45
p.m., at the Career Planning and Placement Center.
