# Dashiell Hammett
## 
### 
Dashiell Hammett was born in St. Marys
County, Md in 1894. He grew up in Philadelphia
and Baltimore.
Hammett left school at the age of 14 and
held several kinds of jobs thereafter â€” he was
a messenger boy, a newsboy, a clerk, a
timekeeper, a yardman, a machine operator
and a stevedore.
Hammet finally became an operative for
Pinkerton's Detective Agency.
After World War I, Hammett turned to writing.
In the late 19205, he became the unquestioned
master of the detective story.
In World War 11, Hammett served as a ser
geant in the army, mostly in the Aleutians. He
died in 1961.
