STUDENT OPEN HOUSE
APPLIED DECISION ANALYSIS, INC.
Tuesday, November 29,1988, 3:00 - 5:00 PM
Applied Decision Analysis, Inc. (ADA) wants to meet undergraduates who are
interested in quantitative analysis and management consulting. If your course work
includes mathematical sciences, mathematics, statistics, computer science, industrial
engineering/operations research, physics or other quantitative sciences, we would like
to meet you.
ADA is a small, growing consulting company, located in Menlo Park, that applies
sophisticated analytical techniques to real-world problems. ADA's strength is high
quality work in areas such as forecasting markets for new products, strategic planning,
decision analysis, operations management, analysis of public policy, R&D planning,
and basic research.
We would like to meet undergraduates who have:
• an interest in solving important, complex problems
• skills in a broad range of mathematical techniques
• an ability to design and implement business application software
• communication skills to present analytical results in a clear, concise form
• a high level of enthusiasm for hard work in an informal atmosphere.
ADA Student Open House
Tuesday, November 29, 1988
3:00 - 5:00 PM
Tresidder Union, Cypress Room South
REFRESHMENTS WILL BE SERVED
