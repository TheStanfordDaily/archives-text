SOCRATES
COMPUTER CATALOG OF THE LIBRARIES
FREE INSTRUCTION
The Libraries are offering free hands-on instruction for students,
faculty and staff in the use of Socrates, the online library catalog.
Two types of workshops are available: Introduction to Socrates
(for those unfamiliar with Socrates or new Socrates users), and
Socrates for Humanists and Social Scientists (for experienced
Socrates users interested in efficient ways to search for library
materials in the Humanities and Social Sciences).
Due to limited class size, advance registrations Is required. All
workshops will be held in the McDermott Room (Rm 175) in Green
Libary.
