The Committee on Community Education Initiatives on South Africa (C-CEISA)
is sponsoring
$1750 FELLOWSHIPS
for students to work on South African Issues

or
New York
or

during the Summer of 1989
* Applications will be available after January 25
* Informational Meeting on January 26, at 4:00 pm
in the Public Service Center
* Applications are due February 16
Application will be available at the Public Service Center next to the Campus Bike Store or call
Waseem Noor at 723-0054 for more information.
Students are required to be at Stanford for a year following the internships in order to carry out a
project describing their experiences.
