SUMMER JOB
OPEN HOUSE
Friday, February 3, 1989
10:00 am - 3:00 pm
at the CPPC on White Plaza
★ FOOD ★ DRINK * SUMMER EMPLOYMENT
This CPPC Open House is your golden opportunity to:
★ Explore summer job options
★ Speak informally with counselors on the how-to's of finding
summer employment
★ Obtain help with your resume and cover letter
★ Review summer job listings and internship opportunities
★ Learn about the service and resources at the CPPC
MARK YOUR CALENDARS
AND JOIN US ON THE 3RD.
EATING PROBLEMS
GROUP FOR WOMEN
COWELL STUDENT HEALTH CENTER
For people with problems of:
• Binge Eating • Food Obsessions
• Vomiting • Other related symptoms
NOT for those interested in weight loss.
If this applies to you then this is your opportunity to begin
dealing with these issues in a supportive atmosphere with
other women just like you.
TO BEGIN THURSDAYS 3:00-4:30 PM.
STARTING IMMEDIATELY
An interview with group leaders is necessary
prior to participating in the program,
For Information, call Dr. Huang or Dr. Alexander at 723-3785.
