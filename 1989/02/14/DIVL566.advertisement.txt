CONSULTING OPPORTUNITIES IN EXECUTIVE COMPENSATION
STRATEGIC COMPENSATION ASSOCIATES, a rapidly growing management consulting firm, is seeking candidates for 6 Research
Associate positions in its Los Angeles, New York, and Chicago offices.
We advise senior management and Boards of Directors on how to effectively use executive compensation to
implement business strategy, improve corporate performance and create shareholder value. Our client base
includes a broad cross section of the Fortune 100 D and high growth/high technology companies.
As a Research Associate, you will have the opportunity to work closely with partners in the firm, and will interact
directly with clients. You will be given a first hand view of management decision making at the highest level.
Primary responsibilities of our Research Associates include:
• Collection and analysis of industry performance and compensation information;
• Application of computer and research skills;
• Development of implications from strategic and compensation analyses;
• Interaction with internal and client proiects teams.
Ideal candidates have:
• Undergraduate degree in economics or other analytic discipline;
• Top academic and achievement record;
• Proven analytic and quantitative skills;
• Strong interpersonal skills, with the poise and maturity to interact effectively at senior management levels.
We offer a fast-paced and stimulating working environment, an excellent compensation package, and an
opportunity for significant growth in a young, fast-paced, entrepreneurial consulting practice.
We will be conducting first round interviews at Stanford on March 10. Registration for interviews will be
coordinated through the CPPC.
