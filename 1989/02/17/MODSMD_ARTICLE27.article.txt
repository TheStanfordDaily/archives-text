# Sports
## Stanford at USC 
### 
MONDAY - Radio: KZSU (90.1 FM), KCBS (740 AM). 7:00 p.m. tipoff at
the Sports Arena.
RECORDS — Stanford (20-5, 11-3 and 2nd in Pac-10 play, ranked 17th
by AP), USC (7-17, 0-12 and 10th).
SERIES — All time: Trojans lead 101-88. Recently: Stanford has won nine
straight by an average of 12.6 points. Last meeting: Greg Butler, now
a member of the New York Knicks, had 25 points and nine rebounds to
lead Stanford to a 73-65 win in Los Angeles.
TROJAN UPDATE USC lost at Arizona 93-70 last night and plays Arizona
State Saturday. Five of the Trojans' losses have been by three points or
less. Ronnie Coleman was averaging 14.8 points per game (tied for
12th in the Pac-10) and 7.2 rebounds per game (sth in the Pac-10).
