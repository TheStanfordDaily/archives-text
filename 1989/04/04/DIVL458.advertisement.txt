# Adv. 17 Page 6
## 
### 
MISSED THE RA APPLICANT
INFORMATION MEETINGS?
It's not too late to apply!
If you were away winter quarter or missed the earlier meetings,
you are encouraged to attend an RA information meeting
Tuesday, April 4 at 1:00
in the Aspen room of Tresidder
Application materials are available in Room 306, Old Union so
that you can get started on them immediately. They will also be
available at the meeting and are due
Friday, April 7 by 1:00 p.m.
(Students who were away from campus winter quarter can turn
in applications, Monday, April 10 by 1:00 p.m. if they inform the
central office of their plans by the regular due date.)
For more information, call the Office of Residential Education, 725-2800.
