Adams House In Sterling Quadrangle is the Human Biology Focus House

provide through programming an informal setting for intellectual exchange and faculty interaction.

and social sciences.
The Focus Assistants' role is to implement creative ways to fulfill the goals of both the Focus House and
Human Biology through residential education.
