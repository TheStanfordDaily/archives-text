MERRILL LYNCH
CAPITAL MARKETS
SAN FRANCISCO INVESTMENT
BANKING DIVISION
The Investment Banking Division of Merrill Lynch
Capital Markets has an opening for a financial
analyst in its San Francisco office. Beginning in June,
the analyst will work as part of a team specializing
in equity, debt and merger and acquisition
transactions for financial institutions.
Applicants should be familiar with personal
computers and should have completed course work
in economics, accounting, finance and/or computer
science. A two-year time commitment is required and
salary is competitive.
Applicants should send a resume and brief cover
letter to:
Joseph C. Henri
Merrill Lynch Capital Markets
101 California Street
Suite 1140
San Francisco, CA 94111
