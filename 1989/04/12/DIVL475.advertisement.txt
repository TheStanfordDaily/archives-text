The ASSU is now accepting applications for
the NOMINATIONS COMMISSION for
the 1989-90 academic year.
Nom Com is the body which appoints student
representatives to University committees. It is composed of
seven students - six volunteer members and one paid chair.
The chair is chosen from the seven students selected to be
Nom Com members.
Applications are available in the ASSU, located on the
second floor of Tresidder Union. They are due on Monday,
April 17, by 5:00 p.m. in the ASSU.
