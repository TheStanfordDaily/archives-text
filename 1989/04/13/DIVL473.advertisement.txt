MANAGEMENT INTERN
Salary: $30,000/ year
The City of San Jose is offering a unique and exciting opportunity for an energetic and dynamic person to
gain hands-on experience and insight into top level city management. As an intern, you will directly assist the
City Manager and City Department Heads in organizing, developing and evaluating City services and
programs. You will also be expected to provide creative and innovative ideas for improving the effectiveness
of the City's government. You will be hired as a Staff Analyst I in the unclassified Civil Service
To qualify for this position, you must be in your last semester of a Master's degree Program in Public
Administration or a closely related field. You must have completed your degree requirement by July 1, 1989
To apply, please send your resume, along with the names and numbers of three references (one of which
must be a faculty member), official graduate and undergraduate transcripts and a 3-5 page typewritten paper
on why you are seeking this position to: Mr. Bob Allen, City of San Jose, Personnel Department,
Dept. STAN, 801 N. First Street, Room 215, San Jose, CA 95110. Final filing date: Tuesday, May 16, 1989
