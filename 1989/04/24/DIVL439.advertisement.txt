1989 SPRING
BLACK COMMUNITY SERVICES CENTER
LEADERSHIP RETREAT
When? Friday & Saturday, May 5-6, 1989.
Where? Bosch Baha'i School in the Santa Cruz
Mountains.
Who? At least one representative from each Black
Volunteer Student Organization, and any interested
Black students. Freshmen are encouraged to attend.
How? Sign up in the Black Community Services Center
by May 1, 1989.
Why? Learn, get involved, review year, set goals for
the community, have fun, and it's FREE!!!!!!!!!!
For More Details? Contact Riico Dotson at 723-1587 or
321-7843.
