SPEAK OUT FOR CHOICE
We, the undersigned, believe it is every woman's right to make her own reproductive decisions. Included in that right is the freedom to
choose if and when to have a child.
On April 26th, the Supreme Court will begin hearing arguments in the case of "Webster v. Reproductive Health Services", a Missouri
case which the Court could use to overturn the Roe v. Wade decision guaranteeing women the right to a safe and legal abortion. Even
without completely reversing the Roe decision, the Court could use the Missouri case to deny access to abortion for many women,
through such means as prohibiting public funding for abortions.
The women of America must never be thrown back to that degrading and dangerous time of illegal abortions when women risked their
lives and their health, and thousands died. We therefore pledge to oppose any attempt to interfere with the fundamental right of a woman
to make her own decision about abortion.
