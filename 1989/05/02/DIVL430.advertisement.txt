HI Centre) Chicano cordially invites you to open the
Cinco de Mayo season with a performance of
La Victima
by El Teatro de la Esperanza
Wednesday, May 3, 1989, 8:00 p.m.
Kresge Auditorium, Stanford University
followed by a reception at El Centro Chicano
and
A theater workshop on the History and Development of
Chicano Theater by El Teatro de la Esperanza
Tuesday, May 2, 1989, 3:30 p.m.
at El Centro Chicano
Tickets for Im Victima are available at Irrsidder Ticket Office (-411) 723-4117
and at the door. The workshop is free of charge and open to the public.
