LIVE AND WORK
IN JAPAN
AMVIC Institute of Foreign Languages, an English
Conversation school with 65 branches throughout
Japan will be interviewing for positions beginning
Summer, 1989.
• Minimum BA/BS degree
(All majors considered)
• 1 year contract; 230,000 yen/month
• Paid training
• Housing benefits
• Teaching experience and/or Japanese
language helpful but not required.
Bring resume and 1-page essay
"Why / Want To Live and Work in
Japan" to an info, meeting on
Monday, May 8, 1989 from
6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. at the
Tresidder Student Bidg., Aspen Room,
Interviews will be held on
Tuesday, May 9th.
M. Therese Feehan
Ms. Kyoko Watanabe
Personnel Directors
P.O. Box 25164
San Mateo, CA 94402
(415) 349-7627
