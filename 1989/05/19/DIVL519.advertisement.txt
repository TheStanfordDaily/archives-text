HOAGLAND PRIZE
FOR
UNDERGRADUATE TEACHING
The Hoagland Prize is awarded annually to an
outstanding teacher of undergraduates, if you have had a
teacher you've especially appreciated, send a letter of
nomination to the Dean of Undergraduate Studies,
Building One, Stanford CA 94305. Nominations for this
year's award must be received May 26. (Only associate
and full professors are eligible.)
H you have any questions, please call Pam Ferry at 725-1572.
