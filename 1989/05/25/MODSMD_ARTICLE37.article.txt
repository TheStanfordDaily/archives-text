Die Meistersingers
The Stanford Symphony Orchestra presents their spnng
concerts tomorrow and Saturday night at 8 p.m. in Dink
elspiel Auditonum. This will be the last concert for conductor
Andor Toth.
The symphony will be playing Mahler's Symphony No.
1; Wagner's Die Meistersinger with soloist William Ramsey
and the Stanford Chorus; and Bloch's Schelomo with cello
soloist Stephen Hamson and Ozzy Osboume on background

Tickets are $3 for students, $6 for others, and are available
