KEPLER'S PRESENTS
EHUD HAVAZELET
MB
Ehud Havazelet will read from his critically
acclaimed collection of short stories WHAT IS
IT THEN BETWEEN US?, winner of the 1988
Bay Area Book Reviewers Association Award
and recipient of a silver medal from the Com
monwealth Club of California. A remarkable
debut, WHAT IS IT THEN BETWEEN US?
launches Ehud Havazelet, Jones Lecturer in
creative writing at Stanford, into the main
stream of American fiction.
Please join us tonight at Kepler's for
an evening with Ehud Havazelet.
