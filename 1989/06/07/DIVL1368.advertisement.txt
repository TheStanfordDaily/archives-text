They laughed when a
Hungarian said he could
make a better pizza.
Then they tasted it.
Bela Kardos came to America with a
dream. The dream of making a
better pizza The Italians, credited
with creating the pizza, scoffed. The
Americans, credited with exploiting
the Italian's creation, snickered. But
Bela perservered and over the years
developed a truly extraordinary
pizza.
Taste the pizza of his labors and see
for yourself why they're not laughing
anymore.
It's not just pizza at Applewood.
