THEY PHONED HOME
They phoned home, they phoned friends and they phoned alumni all over the United States to raise money
for Stanford at the Seventh Annual Spring Telethon. They phoned for fun as well as for funds.
More than 375 Stanford students, alumni and friends gathered amidst balloons and bells for a solid week of
calling May 13 through May 20, 1989.
The students' valiant efforts were rewarded by reaching over 3000 friends and alumni for a grand total of
$78,000 in specified pledges to Stanford.
Individuals and groups vied for numerous prizes donated by many generous Bay Area merchants and
organizations, including lunches hosted by Stanford faculty members. Callers walked away not only with a
tangible prize but also with the satisfaction of knowing that they had contributed to something important.
The Office of Development wishes to thank all those who helped make the Seventh Annual Telethon a
great success. In particular, grateful thanks to student Telethon Chair Allyson Campbell, '89, Vice Chair
Jonathan Holloway, '89, members of the student telethon committee, the callers, the clerks and other
Telethon helpers for the many hours thev donated to support Stanford. And please remember ITS FOR
YOU!

THEY PHONED HOME
They phoned home, they phoned friends and they phoned alumni all over the United States to raise money
for Stanford at the Seventh Annual Spring Telethon. They phoned for fun as well as for funds.
More than 375 Stanford students, alumni and friends gathered amidst balloons and bells for a solid week of
calling May 13 through May 20, 1989.
The students' valiant efforts were rewarded by reaching over 3000 friends and alumni for a grand total of
$78,000 in specified pledges to Stanford.
Individuals and groups vied for numerous prizes donated by many generous Bay Area merchants and
organizations, including lunches hosted by Stanford faculty members. Callers walked away not only with a
tangible prize but also with the satisfaction of knowing that they had contributed to something important.
The Office of Development wishes to thank all those who helped make the Seventh Annual Telethon a
great success. In particular, grateful thanks to student Telethon Chair Allyson Campbell, '89, Vice Chair
Jonathan Holloway, '89, members of the student telethon committee, the callers, the clerks and other
Telethon helpers for the many hours thev donated to support Stanford. And please remember ITS FOR

THEY PHONED HOME
They phoned home, they phoned friends and they phoned alumni all over the United States to raise money
for Stanford at the Seventh Annual Spring Telethon. They phoned for fun as well as for funds.
More than 375 Stanford students, alumni and friends gathered amidst balloons and bells for a solid week of
calling May 13 through May 20, 1989.
The students' valiant efforts were rewarded by reaching over 3000 friends and alumni for a grand total of
$78,000 in specified pledges to Stanford.
Individuals and groups vied for numerous prizes donated by many generous Bay Area merchants and
organizations, including lunches hosted by Stanford faculty members. Callers walked away not only with a
tangible prize but also with the satisfaction of knowing that they had contributed to something important.
The Office of Development wishes to thank all those who helped make the Seventh Annual Telethon a
great success. In particular, grateful thanks to student Telethon Chair Allyson Campbell, '89, Vice Chair
Jonathan Holloway, '89, members of the student telethon committee, the callers, the clerks and other
Telethon helpers for the many hours thev donated to support Stanford. And please remember ITS FOR
YOU!
