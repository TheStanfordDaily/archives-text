Sweeping Semioli
Senior Mark Semioli, the MVP on the soccer team for the past three seasons, is plying
his trade for the San Francisco Bay Blackhawks of the Western Soccer League this
summer. Semioli, Stanford teammate Rhett Harty and the rest of the Blackhawks, 6-3,
will face the Arizona Condors, 3-7, on campus at Maloney Field Saturday. The match
begins at 5 p.m. and tickets should be available at the gate.
