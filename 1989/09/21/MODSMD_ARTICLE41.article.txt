# Not for athletes only
## 
### 
Stanford boasts tremendous athletic fa
cilities for use by members of the commu
nity. To take advantage of them one need
only have a bit of initiative and the right
identification.
To make your pursuit of fitness and
relaxation easier, here is a list of athletic
facilities, the hours of each and their usage
guidelines for the fall quarter, beginning
Sept. 25. Most facilities require Stanford
ID, so the safest bet is to carry it with
you any time you go recreating. Enjoy.
DeGuerre pool is open Monday through
Friday 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. and again from
5:30 to 8 p.m. On Saturday and Sunday,
the pool is open 1 to 5 p.m.
DeGuerre courts consist of handball, rac
quetball and squash courts. The courts are
open 11 a.m. to 11 p.m. Monday through
Friday, 9 a.m. to 7 p.m. Saturday and 9
a.m. to 11 p.m. Sunday. Call 723-0728 for
reservations.
The Encina weight room is open for
weight training Monday through Thursday
noon to 2 p.m. and again 3:15 to 10 p.m.
On Friday, the facility is open 11:30 a.m.
to 10 p.m. and on Saturday and Sunday
the hours are noon to 7 p.m.
Encina gym lockers are open Monday
through Friday 7:30 a.m. to 10:30 p.m.
and Saturday and Sunday 9 a.m. to 7:30
p.m.
Beginning Sept. 27, Maples Pavilion will
be open Monday through Friday 11 a.m.
to 12:30 p.m. for recreational basketball
only.

The Stadium track is open Monday
through Friday 11:30 a.m. to 1 p.m. and
