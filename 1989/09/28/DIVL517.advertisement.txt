Undergraduate Program in the School of Education (UPSE)
Offers placements for teaching elementary or high school students one-on-one or as a classroom
teacher's assistant. Call Jamila Rufaro, Program Director at 723-0880 for more information.
Ravenswood-Stanford Tutoring Program (RSTP)
Supports the Ravenswood City School District's efforts to strengthen East Palo Alto Elementary
and junior high students' academic achievement. We need students for one-on-one tutoring using
a literature-based curriculum. Must commit 2 quarters, twice a week. For more information call
Dee Dee Terzian at 723-5786 or visit the Haas Center for Public Service.
Upward Bound
Needs tutors who are interested in assisting with an exciting college preparatory program for
high school students in East Palo Alto and Redwood City. Cat! Arcadio Morales, Program Director
at 725-1663 for more information.

or
