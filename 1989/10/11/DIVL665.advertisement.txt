KEPLER'S PRESENTS
TONIGHT AT 8:00 P.M.
Two recipients of Stanford's prestigious Stegner Fellowship in
creative writing will read from their works. Fenton Johnson, an
'85-'B6 Stegner Fellow, will read from CROSSING THE RIVER,
a wise and comic novel which tells about passions offered and
spent, about a place - both geographical and emotional -
where North and South meet. Erin McGraw, an '88-'9O Steg
ner Fellow, will read from BODIES AT SEA, a stunning debut
collection of stories remarkable for their compassionate
regard for character and sensitivity to language.
All readings are free to the public and will take place at our new
location in Menlo Center. There's convenient underground parking
- enter off Santa Cruz Ave
