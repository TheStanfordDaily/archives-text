Welcome to the dynamic world of the Stanford libraries! Why dynamic?
As you use the libraries of Stanford, you will find that we offer you books AND
MORE from within 18 libraries AND BEYOND!! This Resource Guide will inform
you about our people, catalogs, collections, instruction, services, facilities, and hours.
Library staff are happy to assist you in using the libraries. Ask us! We are eager to
help you get the most out of your libraries.
