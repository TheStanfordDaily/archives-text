Important information
Meeting for all displaced students
Today, 7 p.m. Oak Lounge in Tresidder Memorial Union.
Meetings for students with quake-related academic concerns
Tomorrow 4 p.m. and Thursday 7 p.m. Both in Tresidder, check Tresidder bulletin boards for exact location.
Students moving out of Delta Tau Delta, Phi Psi and Synergy
Go to those houses today between 10:30 a.m. and 6:30 p.m. to collect your belongings under the supervision of
Housing and Food Sen/ices staff.
Theta Xi, Roth and Durand residents
Your dorms will be accessible by tomorrow.
Looking for your mail?
It will be available today between 11 a.m. and 1 p.m at the Row Cluster and Facilities Office (622 Lomita).
Special thanks
The Row Office wants to thank student volunteers who've made this all possible.
