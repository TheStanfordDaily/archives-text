Nov. 8, 8 p.m.: Psychotherapist Dr. Emily Han
cock will read from her new book The Girl
Within" at Printers Inc. Bookstore, 310 Cali

Gilbert Sorrentino will read from "Mis
terioso," the third novel of his trilogy that
also includes "Odd Number" and "Rose
Theatre," at Kepler's Books and Magazines,
1010 El Camino Real in Menlo Park.

scholar at Stanford University, will sign copies
of her first novel "The Dead Magician" at the
Stanford Bookstore.
