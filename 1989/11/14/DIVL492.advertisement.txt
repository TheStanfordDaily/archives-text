WINTER BREAK
HOUSING
All student residences close during the Autumn-Winter academic
recess except Crothers, Crothers Memorial, Escondido Village,
Manzanita Park, Mirrielees, Rains, and Theta Chi. If you live in a
residence that will be closed and need emergency housing during
the entire interim period, contact the Stanford Housing Center,
Old Union 110, before Friday. December 1.
