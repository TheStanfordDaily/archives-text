From glasnost to gladnotes
East meets West in perfect harmony this Sunday at 8 p.m.
when the Soviet Union's leading jazz ensemble pools its talent
with American saxophonist Joe Henderson and vibraphonist
Bobby Hutcherson. General admission tickets for this concert
in Dinkelspiel Auditorium are $15, or $8 for students. They
are available at the Tresidder Ticket Office and the door.
