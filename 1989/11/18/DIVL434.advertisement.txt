TheAmigo Shoot-Out: Beginning Nov 17
You have a chance to win an Isuzu Amigo at every Men's & Women's Home Game!! One Lucky
contestant will be chosen from the audience to shoot four shots in the Amigo Shoot-Out
sponsored by Anderson Isuzu, Pepsi, Tearney's and Frankie Johnnie & Luigi, Too!!
Make the shots and win the following prizes:
~ A lay-up Two Six Packs of Pepsi
~ A free throw -- A Pizza from Frankie, Johnie & Luigi ,Too
~ From the Key -- A clothing certificate from Tearney's
~ Half Court Two airline tickets to any destination in the USA,
USA compliments of Pepsi Cola
Make all four shots and win all four prizes PLUS an Amigo from Anderson Isuzu.
