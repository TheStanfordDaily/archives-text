COMMITTEE ON RESEARCH
OPEN MEETING
ON INDIRECT COST POLICY
All interested members of the Stanford community
are invited to attend an open meeting of the
Committee on Research from 4 to 6 p.m., Tuesday,
December 5, at Tresidder Union, Oak East Room.
The purpose of this meeting is to solicit comments
from the community on Stanford University's
indirect cost policy. Dean of Research, Robert Byer,
will present background to the current University
policy and rates, as well as the process that
determines them, followed by discussion.
TUESDAY, DECEMBER S • 4 P.M.
OAK EAST, TRESIDDER UNION
