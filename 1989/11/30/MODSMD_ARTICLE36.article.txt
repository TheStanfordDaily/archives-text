# HOT STUFF
## 
### 
Dead Week blues?
Turn your hue to Indigo next Saturday night in
Memorial Auditorium. Tickets are still available for the
Dec. 9 Indigo Girls concert, brought to Stanford by the
Concert Network. General Admission to the 8 p.m. show
is $12.50, or a mere $10 with student ID. You just can't
get "Closer to Fine" than this dynamic duo.
How will you choose?
The Stanford Symphony Orchestra performs its fall
concert is Friday evening at 8 p.m. in Dinkelspiel Au
ditorium. The program will feature award-winning stu
dent soloist Renato Zenobi playing Bartok's Concerto
No. 1 for violin and orchestra. Also on the bill is Copland's
"El Salon Mexico," Debussey's "Prelude a l'apres-midi
d'un faun" and Stravinsky's "Petrushka." Tickets for this
not-to-miss performance are $6, not including a $3 stu
dent discount. They are available at the Tresidder Ticket
Office and the door.
You can't lose
The annual Stanford Christmas Faire will hit Stanford
tomorrow morning at 10 a.m., complete with an incred
ible variety of hand made crafts, food and top-notch
holiday entertainment performed by campus and Bay
Area talent. If you haven't done your holiday shopping
yet, you won't have to go any farther than Tresidder
Union for ideas and bargains. Admission is free.
