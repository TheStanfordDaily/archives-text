Associated Press
Ethnic killings continue
A Soviet soldier stands guard yesterday over the body of a man killed during ethnic unrest in
the Azerbaijani province of the Soviet Union. The Soviet Defense Ministry called up reserve
troops yesterday to reinforce 29,000 soldiers engaged in quelling the violence that has killed
at least 66 people.The troops have been authorized to shoot to kill in an effort to enforce order.
