SOUTH AFRICAN
ISSUES
FELLOWSHIP
The Committee on Community
Education Initiatives on South Africa
is sponsoring
$1750 FELLOWSHIPS
for students to work on South African
issues during summer 1990 in
Washington, D.C., New York, or
an office of their choice.
INFORMATIONAL MEETING
Tuesday, January 30 at 3:30 p.m.
at Mariposa House (opp. Bike Shop).
Applications available at the
Clearinghouse at the Haas Center
for Public Sen/ice.
