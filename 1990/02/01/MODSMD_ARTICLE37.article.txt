# HOT STUFF
## An Iris-opening flick 
### 
When Jane Fonda teaches Robert De Niro to read in
"Stanley & Iris," she gets more than she bargained for.
In fact, when she tutors this illiterate cook, Fonda gets
a learning experience in love.
MGM senior vice-president Trevor Fettor, who sailed
on Stanford's varsity team, will bring this world premiere
to Stanford this Saturday night. Shows are at 7:30 and
9:30, with a reception 30 minutes prior to each show.
Food at reception is included in the price of admission.
Tickets cost $6 with Stanford ID and $10 without,
and they will be available in White Plaza today and
tomorrow from 11 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Proceeds will benefit the varsity sailing team.
