Applied Decision Analysis, Inc. (ADA) wants to meet undergraduate and graduate students who
are interested in quantitative analysis and management consulting. If your course work includes
mathematics, statistics, computer science, industrial engineering/operations research, physics or
other quantitative sciences, we would like to meet you.
ADA is a small, growing consulting company, located in Menlo Park, that applies sophisticated
analytical techniques to real-world problems. ADA'S strength is high quality work in areas such as
strategic planning, decision analysis, operations management, analysis of public policy, forecasting
markets for new products, R&D planning, and basic research.
We would like to meet students who have:
. An interest in solving important, complex problems,
. Skills in a broad range of mathematical techniques,
• An ability to design and implement business application software.
. Communication skills to present analytical results in a clear, concise form, and
. A high level of enthusiasm for challenging work in an informal atmosphere.
