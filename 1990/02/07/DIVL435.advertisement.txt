HOUSING JOB OPPORTUNITIES
Spring Quarter
■ Drawing Room Coordinator: Plans and organizes the activities
of the Undergraduate Drawing Room.
■ Housing Assistants: Provide clerical support for the
Undergraduate Draw, Graduate Lottery, and Summer Housing
Allocation.
Spring and Summer Quarters
■ Freshman Information Coordinator: Makes freshman housing,
roommate, CIV, and Freshman English assignments.
Interested students should complete an employment application at
the Stanford Housing Center, Old Union 110, by Friday, February 16.
