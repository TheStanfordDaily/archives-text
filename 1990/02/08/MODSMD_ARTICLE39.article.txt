# Chuck Goes 'Amuck'
## 
### 
It's wabbit season again. Three-time Academy Award winner
Chuck Jones, author of "Chuck Amuck," kicks off the second
week of "Two Weeks of Fun and Contention" tonight in Cubberley
Auditorium at 7 p.m. Jones, animation director for cartoon char
acters like Bugs Bunny and Daffy Duck, created the Road Runner,
Wile E. Coyote and Pepe Le Pew. Jones' works include "What's
Opera, Doc?" "How the Grinch Stole Christmas" and "Buck
Dodgers in the 24 Vi Century." Tonight's program will feature
12 of Jones' finest cartoons along with intervals of commentary.
Th-th-th-that's all, folks.
