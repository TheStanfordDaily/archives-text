THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 15, 8 P.M.
John L'Heureux, Lane Professor of Humanities at Stan
ford and former Director of Stanford's Creative Writing
Program, will read from COMEDIANS. These eleven
short stories and one novella explore the possibilities of
faith and redemption in a secular age They are infused
with a religious, almost supernatural vision of life's mys
teries. The characters are like anybody you might know,
except they are all artists or saints - sometimes both.
From the acclaimed author of Woman Run Mad. COM
EDIANS is funny, shocking, ironic, elegant, and
profound.
