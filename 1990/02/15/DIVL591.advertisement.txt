Want to manage an investment
portfolio fund valued at over
$60,000?
Then apply to join the
Charles R. Blyth Fund
of Stanford University.
- Be one of the student directors who are
placed in charge of managing an investment
fund.
- Gain experience investing real money in
stocks, bonds and options.
- Serve on the Fund from Spring 1990
through Winter 1991.
- No experience is required.
Applications are available in the CPPC
(Career Planning and Placement Center)
Deadline: Tuesday February 20th
