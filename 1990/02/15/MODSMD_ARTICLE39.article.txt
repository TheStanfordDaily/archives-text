# High-fluting program
## 
### 
Francis Blaisdell, lecturer emeritus in the Department of Music at
Stanford, will discuss "Music and Memories" at 8 p.m. on Feb. 20
in Campbell Recital Hall, Braun Music Center. The program
includes vignettes from her musical career as a student at the Julliard
School and as an internationally acclaimed flutist with the New York
Philharmonic Orchestra. Blaisdell will also perform works by Bach,
Gluck and Debussy. Tickets are $10 general admission and $5 for
students. Call 723-4317 for further information.
