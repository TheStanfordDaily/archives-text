# Men's Tennis at National Indoors
## 
### 
THURSDAY THROUGH SUNDAY — Stanford's first match
kicks off at 5 p.m. at the Louisville Tennis Club in
Louisville, Ky.
RECORD — Stanford (4-0, ranked No. 1 by the Volvo
Intercollegiate Tennis Rankings, seeded first at the
tournament).
NOTES — The top eight teams in the country will
attend the National Indoor Team Championships,
touted as the most competitve team tournament next
to the NCAA Championships in May. Stanford is
seeded first, but Georgia, UCLA and LSU should
challenge the Cardinal for the title. In its first round
this Thursday, Stanford will meet the winner of the
Kentucky-Wisconsin match, to be held today Ken
tucky has beaten Stanford two out of the last three
times the teams have met.
