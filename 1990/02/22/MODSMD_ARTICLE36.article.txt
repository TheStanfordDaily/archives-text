# Men's Tennis at National Indoors
## 
### 
TODAY — Stanford will play host-school Kentucky in the second round
of the tournament today. The match begins at 5 p.m. at the Louisville
Tennis Club in Louisville, Ky.
RECORD — Stanford (4-0, ranked No. 1 by the Volvo Tennis/Collegiate
Rankings, seeded first at the tournament).
NOTES — Kentucky has beaten Stanford two out of the last three times
the teams have met. The Wildcats tied for third in last year's event, while
Stanford finished tied for seventh. Stanford last won the National Indoor
Team Championships in 1985.
