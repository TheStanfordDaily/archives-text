# Symphony Soloists Serenade
## 
### 
Heather Hinds, Susan Soong, and Becky Baron, winners of last
spring's Student Soloist Awards, will be spotlighted at the Stanford
Chamber Orchestra/Stanford Symphony concert at 8 p.m. on Feb.
25 in Dinkelspiel Auditorium. Hinds and Soong will perform
Bach's "Concerto for Two Violins in D Minor," and Baron will
play Saint-Saens' "Cello Concerto." Also scheduled for Sunday
night's performance is Haydn's "Symphony #49" and Copland's
"Appalachian Springs." Tickets are $6/general and $3/students,
and are available at the door.
