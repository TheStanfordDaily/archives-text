TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 27, 8 P.M.
Margo Davis, professional photographer and teacher,
and Roxanne Nilan, Curator of University Archives at
Stanford, will discuss THE STANFORD ALBUM. With
000 photographs and an interpretive text, this
photographic history tells the story of Stanford Univer
sity from its creation in 1885 through the Second World
Wah Together text and photographs vividly portray the
dynamics of each era. Through the magic of the camera
lens, a vanished world of college life comes alive again.
All events are free to the public.
