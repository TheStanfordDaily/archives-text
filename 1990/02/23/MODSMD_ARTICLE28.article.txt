# Men's Basketball vs. Arizona, ASU
## 
### 
TONIGHT — Stanford vs. Arizona, live on PSN with a 7:30 p.m. tipoff in Maples Pavilion.
KZSU (90.1 FM) and KCBS (740 AM) will also broadcast the game.
SUNDAY — Stanford vs. ASU, live on channel 5 with a 3 p.m. tipoff.
RECORDS - Stanford (15-8, 7-7 in Pac-10 play, ranked 45th nationally with one vote
in the AP poll), Arizona (18-5, 12-3, ranked No. 21), Arizona State (12-11, 5-9).
INJURIES — Wildcat guard Harvey Mason is recoving from arthroscopic knee surgery,
performed 15 days ago. He played three minutes against Cal Wednesday.
WILDCAT UPDATE - Small forward Jud Buechler leads the Cats in scoring and rebound
ing and is second in steals and assists. With Mason hobbled, freshman Casey Schmidt
is Arizona's only backup guard. So against Cal, Coach Lute Olson had guards Matt
Muehlebach and Matt Othick play the first 33 minutes of the game without a rest.
SUN DEVIL UPDATE - Arizona State is led by the brother tandem of Alex and Isaac
Austin. Alex, the small forward, is leading the team in scoring and rebounds; Isaac
is second in both scoring and rebounding as the starting center.
