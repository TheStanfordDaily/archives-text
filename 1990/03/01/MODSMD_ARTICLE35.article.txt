# Political humor on the Mark
## 
### 
Political humorist Mark Russell will perform at Memorial Au
ditorium on March 4 at 2:30 p.m. Russell is famous for his
nationally-syndicated television series, "The Mark Russell Comedy
Special," which utilizes political satire and piano accompaniment
in his humourous act. Tickets are priced from $16 to $20, and
are available at Tresidder Ticket Office and BASS Outlets. Call
725-ARTS for further information.
