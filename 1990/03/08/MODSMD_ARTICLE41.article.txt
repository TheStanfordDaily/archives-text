# Copland-composed concert
## 
### 
The Stanford Symphonic Band will play its winter concert
Sunday at 8 p.m. in Dinkelspiel Auditorium. The program
features music by 20th-century American composer Aaron
Copland, including "Fanfare for the Common Man,"
"Emblems," and "Outdoor Overture." Tickets are $6 general
admission and $3 for students, and are available at the
Tresidder Ticket Office or at the door.
