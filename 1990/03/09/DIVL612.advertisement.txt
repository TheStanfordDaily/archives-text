KEPLER'S PRESENTS
HAROLD
NORSE
reading from
MEMOIRS OF
A BASTARD ANGEL
SUNDAY, MARCH 11, 8 P.M.
A friend of W.H. Auden, Tennessee Williams, Allen
Ginsberg-, and William Burroughs, Harold Norse has ex
perienced the mainstream culture and the homosexual
subculture of three continents for over half a century.
Norse is an acclaimed novelist and poet whose candid au
tobiography tells an amazing story peopled with trend
setting artists and celebrities from Marc Chagall and
Marlon Brando to the Duke of Windsor and Arnold
Schwarzenegger.
"If light ever enters the hearts of men, Harold Norse will
be one of those who have helped set it there," James
Baldwin.
All events are free to the public.
