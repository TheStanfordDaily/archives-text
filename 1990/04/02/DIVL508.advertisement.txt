THE STANFORD DAILY
invites all interested candidates
to apply for the position of
BUSINESS MANAGER
for the 1990-91 school year.
Responsibilities include:
• Bottom-line performance of The Stanford
Daily Publishing Corporation, with
revenues of over $1.1 million.
• Hiring and management of a 20-person
Business Department.
• Management of growing assets, revenues,
and expenses.
The position runs from June 16, 1990 to June 15,
1991. Full-time commitment over the summer and
part-time commitment during the academic year
are required. General business management
experience is preferred, but not required. All
interested candidates are encouraged to apply.
