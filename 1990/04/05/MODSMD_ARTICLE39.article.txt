# Raised on Radio
## 
### 
Fans of episodes of the "Twilight Zone" will enjoy the premiere
of the new Stanford Masters Film, "Tune in Tomorrow," written,
produced and directed by Stanford student Matthew Rauchberg.
"Tune in Tomorrow," described as "a mystery sci-fi suspense
drama with a message," centers its story around a mysterious
radio which gives broadcasts four hours in advance. The 22-minute
film premieres tonight at 7:30 p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium.
