# Intermission Rating Scale
## 
### 
All Intermission albums and movies are rated on a scale of
one to 10 with 10 being the best possible score. The ratings box
for albums includes the artist and the album title. The ratings
box for movies includes the movie title and the principal actors.
All ratings are chosen by the individual reviewer and are subject
to editorial consent.
