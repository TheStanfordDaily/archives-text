# Men's Tennis vs. California
## 
### 
TODAY — 1:30 p.m at Stanford Tennis Stadium
RECORDS — Stanford (16-0, 4-0 in Pac-10 South, ranked No. 1 by Volvo
Tennis/Collegiate Rankings), California (15-5, 2-1 in Pac-10 South, ranked
No. 2 in the nation).
INJURY — Freshman Jared Palmer sustained a knee injury over the
weekend against UCLA and will not play today.
BEAR UPDATE — Besides its No 2 ranking, California boasts several highly
ranked players Doug Eisenman is ranked No 40, while Bent-Ove Peder
son sits at No 63. Eisenman and Matt Lucena are the top-ranked doubles
team in the country.
PREVIOUS MEETING — Stanford met California in the finals of the National
Indoor Championships Feb. 25.in Louisville, Ky The Bears were the
first team to take the Cardinal to doubles. Stanford won the match 5-2.
NOTES — Students with Stanford identification can attend today's match
against California free of charge The team encourages all students to
attend. This will probably be the only time the No. 1 and No. 2-ranked
teams meet during the year. "It really makes a difference when you have
a huge crowd cheering for you," freshman Jonathan Stark said.
