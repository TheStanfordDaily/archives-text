# Lafayette Quartet
## 
### 
The all-woman Lafayette String Quartet, consisting of Ann
Elliott-Goldschmid and Sharon Stanis on violins, Joanna Hood
on viola, and Pamela Highbaugh on cello, will perform on Sunday
in Kresge Auditorium at 2:30 p.m. Their performance, sponsored
by the Lively Arts at Stanford, is scheduled to include works by
Mozart and Schubert. Tickets are $15 with a $3 student discount,
and are available at the Tresidder Ticket Office.
