# Symphony of Emotions
## 
### 
SansKriti, the Undergraduate Indian Association, offers a dance
presentation of Ramya Harishankar in a solo performance. Ac
companied by an ensemble of musicians, the show, entitled "Sym
phony of Emotions," is choreographed in traditional Indian style.
The performance is Friday at 7 p.m. at Kresge Auditorium, with
tickets $3 for students and $6 general available at the door.
