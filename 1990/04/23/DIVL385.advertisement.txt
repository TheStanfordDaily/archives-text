EUROPEAN STUDIES
presents

Lectures On Soviet Affairs
PERESTROIKA AND WESTERN POLICY
by ED HEWETT, Senior Fellow,
The Brookings Institution
TWO LECTURES
(note new time and place)
Monday, April 23, 1990 Economic Perestroika: Learning
the Hard Way
Tuesday, April 24, 1990 What Can and Should the West Do to Aid
Perestroika?
Both lectures will be held at 5:15 p.m. in Bishop Auditorium Ground Floor Graduate School of
Business, Stanford University They are free and open to the public.
