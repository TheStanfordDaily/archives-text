KEPLER'S PRESENTS
WALLACE STEGNER
Wednesday, April 25, 8 p.m.
Wallace Stegner,
recipient of the
National Book
Award, the Pulitzer
Prize, the
Commonwealth
Gold Medal, and
the Los Angeles
Times Award, will
read from his latest
work, COLLECTED
STEGNER.
"Stegner's short fiction is unparalleled for
the clarity and depth of its human Insights."
— James Dickey
