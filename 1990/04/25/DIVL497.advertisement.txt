27th Year Guadalajara Summer Session
University of San Diego • June 29 - August 2 f 1990
Courses fully accredited by the Western Association ot Schools and Colleges (WASC) Spanish
language at all levels, Bilingual and Special Education including ESL and Reading Education
Art, Folk Dance, Literature. Folklore, History, Sociology, Guitar. Political Science Psychology
Cross Cultural Studies Tuition $575 for 1 8 undergraduate units. $615 for 1 8 graduate units
Room and board with a Mexican Host Family $575 Apartment and Hotel rates from $400 $700
per month.
University of San Diego also has a M A program in Spanish Students may earn credits toward
the degree by attending the Guadalajara Summer Session
