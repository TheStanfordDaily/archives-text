STILL LOOKING
FOR A SUMMER JOB?
The LOCAL GOVERNMENT INTERNSHIP PROGRAM has
something for you!
Part-time and full-time PAID positions are available with the
Cities of Mountain View, Daly City, Campbell, and Sunnyvale.
Design a database, do policy research, develop community
education programs, assist in the development of plans for a
performing arts facility, and more!
Applications and job descriptions are available in the
Clearinghouse at the Haas Center for Public Service.
APPLICATIONS ARE DUE THURSDAY, MAY 10
For more information call 725-2860
