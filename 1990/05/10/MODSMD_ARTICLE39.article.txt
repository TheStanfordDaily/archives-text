# Comic Book Cavalcade
## 
### 
The Fourth Annual Wonder-Con, a three-day convention spot
lighting the world of comic books, will be held May 11-13 at the
Oakland Convention Center in Oakland. The third-largest comic
convention in the country, Wonder-Con activities include movie
previews, dealer tables, an animation festival, celebrity appearances
and an original art auction. Information and tickets are available
at comic book stores in the Bay Area.
