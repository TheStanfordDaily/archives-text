The Hoagland Prize is awarded annually to an outstanding teacher of undergraduates
The winner will receive a monetary prize as well as release from routine duties in order
to pursue new ideas and directions in teaching, or to spend time on special projects.
Nominating is easy If you have had a professor you've especially appreciated, write a
letter describing the gualities that make him or her a fantastic teacher, and send it to the
Dean of Undergraduate Studies at Building One, Stanford. CA 94305 Nominations are due
June 4th (Only associate and full professors are eligible )
If you have questions, please call the Undergraduate Studies Coordinator at 725-1572.
