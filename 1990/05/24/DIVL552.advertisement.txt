Spring 3rd & Beale-ing
Seniors Shelley Harrison and Cliff Goldmacher
will perform a special benefit concert tonight for
the Stanford Homelessness Action Coalition, be
ginning at 8 p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium. The
duo from Memphis will play all the songs from
their hot new album, "3rd & Beale," as well as
plenty of classic covers.
