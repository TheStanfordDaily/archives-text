STUDENTS:
WOULD YOU LIKE TO HEAR
PRESIDENT MIKHAIL GORBACHEV
SPEAK? READ ON ...
1. Pick up a bid form at your housing office or at the counter at the Tresidder
Information Window (first floor, Tresidder Union). Complete the bid form legi
bly. Illegible forms will be discarded. (Note: only one bid form per student will
be accepted.)
2. Submit your bid form to the SOS table in White Plaza on Wednesday,
May 30 and Thursday, May 31 between the hours of 11:00 a.m. and 2:00 p.m.
3. WHEN YOU TURN IN YOUR BID FORM YOU MUST PRESENT YOUR
SPRING QUARTER, 1990 STUDENT IDENTIFICATION CARD.
4. A list of the students whose names were drawn, and a short Wait List, will
be posted on the windows of the ASSU, Student Organization Services, and
the Tresidder Ticket Office on Friday, June 1. The names will also be pub
lished in the Daily.
5. If your name was drawn, you must claim your ticket at the Tresidder Ticket
Office between 10:00 a.m. and 5:30 p.m. on Friday, June 1. YOU MUST
PRESENT YOUR STUDENT ID AT THAT TIME.
6. On June 4, those students on the Wait List may pick up unclaimed tickets
at the Tresidder Ticket Office at 10:00 a.m. on a first-come-first-served basis.
