ABOUT GETTING AIDS?
Do Carriers of the AIDS Virus
Have Symptoms?
A carrier of the AIDS virus may
have no signs or symptoms of the
AIDS disease and may not develop
the disease. Carriers can infect
someone else through sexual con
tact. or sharing needles or syringes
for intravenous drug use, but not
through normal casual contact.
AIDS IS HARD TO CATCH
This information is based upon
data from the U.S. Public Health
Service. For more information, call
your local health department, the
National AIDS Hotline (1-800-342-
AIDS) or your local Red Cross
Chapter.
