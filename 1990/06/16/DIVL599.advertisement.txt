Health Insurance for
Unregistered Students
If you are on the Student Health Insurance plan for Spring Quarter,
you are eligible to extend your insurance coverage over the summer
even if you are not going to be registered.
YOU NEED TO:
Mr Complete and submit with payment the "Request for Unreg
istered Health Insurance for Summer Quarter 1990-91" form,
mailed to all students (currently on the policy) during the
week of 5/21/90.
OR
Mr Come to Cowell Student Health Center and pay the $137
premium.
nr The deadline to pay for summer insurance is June 27, 1990.
Requests for insurance after this date will riot be honored.
PLEASE NOTE:
Mr The purchase of health insurance during an unregistered quar
ter does not provide you with continued free service at Cowell
Student Health Center. If you are returning Fall Quarter, you
may be seen at Cowell on a fee-for-service basis.
Mr If you have graduated, you are eligible to be seen at Cowell on
a fee-for-service basis through August 31, 1990.
Mr The Registration Commitment forms you are now signing for
the 1990-91 academic year enable you to commit for unreg
istered health insurance for Summer Quarter, 1991. For this
summer, however, you must follow the above procedure.
Mr If you are currently on the plan, and you will be registered this
summer, you need do nothing.
