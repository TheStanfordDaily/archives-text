# JUST DID IT.
## 
### 
You just did it! Thanks for your help with the Bth annual Student Telethon.
Nearly 400 volunteers talked to 4,700 alumni and friends all over North America.
There were some great conversations, which led to a pledge total of over $245,000.
The Telethon Incentive Program (TIP), a new feature this year, drew students from
a variety of organizations. TIP was established by gifts from faculty, staff, and alumni
as a way to thank students for their gift of time. Each volunteer designated $100 of
the lund to an area of his or her choice.

There were some great conversations, which led to a pledge total of over $245,000.
The Telethon Incentive Program (TIP), a new feature this year, drew students from
a variety of organizations. TIP was established by gifts from faculty, staff, and alumni
as a way to thank students for their gift of time. Each volunteer designated $100 of
the lund to an area of his or her choice.
