It's the Office of Student Activities (formerly Tresidder Programs and SOS), on the second floor of Tresidder Union.
The newly formed Office of Student Activities plans and supports a variety of activities for Stanford Students including:
•Student Organization Registration, Advising, and Training
•Publicity Resources:
Banner Making Supplies
Tables for White Plaza
•Scheduling Information:
Find out the best space for your event.
•Fraternity and Sorority Advising:
Advising for fraternities and sororities as they plan social, cultural, and community service projects.
•Classes and Outings:
Dance, music, cooking, art and other leisure classes are offered each quarter. Register at the Tresidder Ticket Office.
Backpacking, whale watching, horseback riding, and hiking are some of the many outings offered each quarter in
conjunction with SEAS (Students for Environmental Action at Stanford). Register at the Tresidder Ticket Office.
•Outdoor Resource Library:
Choose your weekend getaway from the extensive library of information on backpacking, camping, hiking,
sight-seeing, skiing, etc. in California.
•Coffee House Entertainment:
Jazz, comedy, folk and light rock are some of the programs presented at the Coffee House on Tuesday and Thursday nights.
•Noon Concerts:
The noon concert series features student and Bay area musicians performing on the Tresidder front patio most
Wednesday and Fridays during the year.
