REBATE ENVELOPE
The Stanford Bookstore, a non-profit corporation, will pay a
rebate to Stanford Students, faculty, and staff for purchases of
BOOKS made from May 1, 1990 through April 30, 1991.
The rebate amount will be determined by the Bookstore Board
ot Directors. It will be derived from net income from sales and paid
by check after October 1, 1991. (Exception: special order books,
Master Card and Visa purchases are NOT eligible for rebate).
1990-91
INSTRUCTIONS
1. Save all receipts on which you have purchased a book(s) from
Stanford Bookstore. PLEASE DO NOT CIRCLE OR UNDERLINE
AMOUNTS ON YOUR RECEIPTS.
2. Keep your receipts in this envelope. Stanford Bookstore Charge
Customers keep your charge slip copy in lieu of a receipt.
3. Before turning the envelope in, be sure to print your name and
mailing address in the space provided. Also, be sure to indicate
your affiliation with Stanford in the space provided.
4. Turn envelopes in at the Bookstore's customer service window
between April 20th and April 30th, 1991 (or mail to the Stanford
Bookstore, Stanford, CA 94305). NO LATE ENVELOPES WILL BE
ACCEPTED. Rebate checks will be available after Oct. 1, 1991
USE THIS ENVELOPE TO
SAVE YOUR RECEIPTS
STANFORD BOOKSTORE
