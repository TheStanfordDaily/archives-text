Are You Protected?
Lock your bike frame to a stationary
object. Lastyear over four hundred bicycles
were stolen from the Stanford campus;
many of those were locked to themselves.
The Law:
You are required by law to register your
bicycle. Take your bike and $3.50 to the
Tresidder Recreation Center (across from
the Tresidder Express). You will receive
a coupon for $5 off a bike light purchased
at the Campus Bike Shop.
CALIFORNIA
2661234
BICYCLE LICENSE
At night, the State of California requires that
all bicycles have a light visible from 300 feet
away. You may be fined for not using a light at
night. They not only help you to see the road but
also allow other bikers and motorists to see
you.
Call 723-9633 for more information
