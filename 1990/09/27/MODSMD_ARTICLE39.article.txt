# ISADORA DUNCAN AWARD WINNERS
## 
### 
Major winners at the
Isadora Duncan Awards
Ceremony Tuesday night
Included Jim Sohm, who
received a Sustained
Achievement Award for 15
years of performances
with the San Francisco
Ballet; Stanford Dance
Division faculty member
Halifu Osumare, a Special
Sward for producing
"Black Choreographers
Moving Toward the 21st
Century"; and Gloria Untl,
a Sustained Achievement
Award for 20 years of ser
vice In the field of dance
education.
