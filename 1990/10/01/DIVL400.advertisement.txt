Kepler's Presents
Ira Sandperl
TUESDAY, OCTOBER 2, 8 PM
Ira Sandperl, Gandhian scholar and non-violent activist,
will give a talk on the life and work of Mahatma Gandhi.
Peter Steinhart
WEDNESDAY, OCTOBER 3, 8 PM
Peter Steinhart, author of Tracks in the Sky and a
columnist for Audubon, will discuss CALIFORNIA'S WILD
HERITAGE: THREATENED AND ENDANGERED ANIMALS
IN THE GOLDEN STATE. This handbook surveys some
100 endangered species in California and the concise
information essays are accompanied by outstanding
photographs.
Monday October Bth 12 noon-1 PM
Bill Walsh signing copies
of his new book Building a Champion
All events are free to the public.
Convenient underground parking lot off Santa Cruz Avenue.
