Join us in celebration of the
Stanford American Indian Organization's
20th Anniversary
1970 -1990
m
L
Tuesday, October 9,1990
Second Floor Lounge, Tresidder Union
5:00-7:00 PM
AMERICAN INDIAN ART IN TRANSITION
An exhibit from the permanent collection of the
Institute of American Indian Arts in Santa Fe, New Mexico
featuring a wide array of traditional and contemporary
fine art. Rick Hill, Museum Director of the Institute,
will be our featured speaker.
Wednesday, October 10,1990
133 Cubberley, School of Education
7:00-8:30 PM
A talk by William G, Demmert, Jr. (Tlingit/Sioux),
Visiting Professor at the School of Education, Co-Chair of
the U.S. Department of Education's Indian Nations at Risk
Task Force and former Commission of Education for the
State of Alaska, on the topic "INDIAN NATIONS AT RISK?
CONTEMPORARY ISSUES FACING AMERICAN INDIANS AND
ALASKA NATIVES."
*Co-prcscnted in cooperation with the Ho Fund, Tresidder Union, Xerox, and the Stanford Centennial Operating Committee
