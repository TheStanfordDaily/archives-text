STRATEGIC COMPENSATION ASSOCIATES
Los Angeles • New York • Chicago • Mexico City
MANAGEMENT CONSULTING
We advise top management and Boards of Directors on how to link
executive compensation with company pertormance. Our client base
includes a wide variety of Fortune 1000 companies. Due to the rapid
growth of our international consulting practice, we are in immediate need
of Research Associates for our Los Angeles office.
As a Research Associate, you will be an important member of our
consulting team and will be given a first-hand view of management
decision making at the highest levels.
Primary responsibilities of Research Associates include:
• Collection and analysis of industry and compensation information
• Application of computer and research skills
• Development of implications from analyses
• Interaction with project teams and client management
• Growth into consultant role
Ideal candidates will have an undergraduate degree in an analytic
discipline, a top academic achievement record, and strong interpersonal
skills.
We offer a stimulating work environment, an excellent compensation
package, and an opportunity for significant growth in a young,
entrepreneurial consulting firm.
We are currently interviewing 1990 graduates (June and December) for
immediate employment. If you wish to become an important member of
our consulting team, please send a cover letter, your resume and a
transcript to:

644 South Figueroa Street • Los Angeles, California 90017
Attention: Ms. Lisa Clark • (213) 488-8567
