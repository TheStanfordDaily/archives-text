SYSTEMS APPLICATIONS INTERNATIONAL
An internationally recognized environmental consulting firm located in Marin County,
California, will be at Stanford November 9th, interviewing candidates for entry level and
senior positions including:
• Policy Analyst
• Statistician
• Meteorologist
• Chemical Engineer
• Environmental Engineer
• Atmospheric Scientist
• Aerosol Scientist
Candidates who are near completion of 8.5., M.S., or Ph.D. programs in the following
areas are encouraged to request interviews:
• Chemical Engineering
• Mechanical Engineering
• Environmental Engineering
• Public Policy
• Chemistry
• Statistics
• Physics
• Atmospheric Sciences
Sign ups for interviews start Friday, October 19th. Please contact the Career Planning
and Placement Center.
