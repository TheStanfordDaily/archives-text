Do you ever take aspirin, Tylenol®,
Motrin®, or other over-the-counter
medications for your aches and
pains? If so, you may qualify for entry
into a clinical research study compar
ing two over-the-counter pain
medications. You will be required to
make two visits to the clinic in San
Carlos and complete a diary about
your pain (e.g. headache, muscle
ache, menstrual cramps, minor pain
of arthritis, etc.). You will receive free
physical exams and a $40 stipend for
your involvement.
For more information regarding this
study, please call Grant at:
(415) 364-4165.
