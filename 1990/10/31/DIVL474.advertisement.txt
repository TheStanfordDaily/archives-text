announces

JANET LEWIS
will read from her work
Monday, November 5, 8:00 p.m.
Kresge Auditorium
Free and open to the public:
for information - 723-2637
TEACH FOR AMERICA
information session
Thursday, November Ist
Holiday Inn, Palo Alto

7:00 p.m.
Teach For America is a national teacher
corps of talented, dedicated individuals
