# HIP HOT & HAPPENING
## 
### 
One Miserables year

The longest-running Broadway show in San Francisco theatre
history celebrates its one-year anniversary tonight, as the Curran
Theatre presents Les Miserables. The musical's run has been
extended through November 25. Performances are at 8 p.m. Hies,
through Sat., 2 p.m. Sat. and 3 p.m. Sun. Student tickets cost $16
and are available through Ticketron at 243-9001.

Ragtime in Roble

Get out your dancing shoes for Stanford's self-proclaimed
"newest tradition" — the Ragtime Ball — which will take place next
Saturday in the Roble Dance Studio. Learn the Hirkey TYot, the
Grizzly Bear, the Castle Walk, the Charleston and the Wicked
Tango, as you enjoy the musical stylings of the 12-piece Paul Price
Society Orchestra. Dance instruction begins at 8 p.m., with the reg
ular program beginning at 9 p.m. Tickets are being sold for $10
each in White Plaza from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m. Thurs. through Wed.
Proceeds will benefit the Stanford Dance Division Scholarship
Fund.
