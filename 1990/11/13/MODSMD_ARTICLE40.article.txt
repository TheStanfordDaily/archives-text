# For the record
## 
### 
An article in yesterday's Daily
about the Women's Needs
Assessment survey said a higher
percentage of women of color were
included in the survey than the
percentage of white women.
Actually, a greater percentage of
women of color were included
than are represented at Stanford.
An article in yesterday's Daily
about the Martin Luther King Jr.
Papers Project misidentified the
location and the eventual size of
the project. It will be a 17-volume
collection and is housed in Jordan
Quad.
