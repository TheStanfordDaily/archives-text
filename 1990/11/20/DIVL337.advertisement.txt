Memorial Church

"THE EXTRAVAGANCE
OF VIOLENCE"
An exploration of the violent sacrifice of
women in Holy Scripture as it illuminates
contemporary structures of patriarchy. Focus
will be on the Levite's concubine in the Book
of Judges.
"Misogyny belongs to every age, including our
own. Violence and vengeance are not just charac
teristics of a distant, pre-Christian past; they in
fect the community of the elect to this day. To take
to heart this ancient story is to confess its present
reality." — Phyllis Trible, Texts of Terror.
