WE SAY THERE IS ANOTHER WAY
Peaceful Alternatives in the Gulf
To the President and Congress of the United States:
We, the undersigned, oppose the prospect of U.S.-initiated armed conflict in the Middle East, which would cause
massive casualties on both sides, as well as profound social and economic disruption in the region.
We demand that you actively pursue a diplomatic settlement with Iraq.
We urge you to do everything in your power to prevent the outbreak of war, for the sake of all peoples involved.
Karen Abrams
