THE BOSTON CONSULTING GROUP
BCG is a premier management consulting firm specializing in corporate strategy.
We offer a unique developmental opportunity for a select group of
college graduates in our Associate Program.
Associates assume professional responsibilities as
fully integrated members of teams working
on client business problems.
BCG's two-year associate program provides unparalleled
training and experience in the range of business
consulting tasks including research, analysis,
field interviewing, and client presentations.
If you are a candidate who exemplifies superior academic performance, strong
quantitative aptitude, leadership traits, and excellent communication skills,
send a resume, transcript and board scores by January 15th to:
Teresa McLaughlin
The Boston Consulting Group
