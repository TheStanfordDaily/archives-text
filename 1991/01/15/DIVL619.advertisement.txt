The Stanford Alumni Association and
the Student Alumni Network (STAN) present
Senior Casino Night
'Echoes of the Jazz A.ye
All seniors and their guests are invited to an evening
of fun and games on Friday, February 1, 1991
featuring jazz by Potential. Tickets go on sale
'Thursday, January 17, 1991
at Bowman Alumni House.
Tickets must be purchased in advance.
i $16 per person
$13 Alumni Association members.
Transportation available from campus.
Senior I D. required to purchase tickets.
