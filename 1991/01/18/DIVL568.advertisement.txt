COMMITTEE ON RESEARCH
OPEN MEETING
January 22, 1991
The annual open meeting of the Committee
on Research will be held on Tuesday, Janu
ary 22, from 3:00 until 5:00 p.m., in the Oak
Room at Tresidder Union.
Updates will be presented on University in
direct cost rates and the various indirect cost
reviews currently underway. There will be
an opportunity for general discussion and
questions and answers on these issues.
This open meeting also provides an oppor
tunity for any interested member of the
University community to raise questions or
concerns about any of Stanford's Research
Policies or their implementation.
Tuesday, January 22, 3 P.M.
Oak Room, Tresidder Union
