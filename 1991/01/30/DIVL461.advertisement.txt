FREE
BRUNCH!
You and your friends are invited to a HOME
COOKED BRUNCH at the home of a local
Stanford alum! Alumni have volunteered to
host groups of 5-10 students to brunch in
their homes on February 24 and April 21.
Sign-up between January 28 and February
1 in the lobby at Bowman Alumni House.
Space is limited, so hurry. This event is for
undergrads only. There will be a separate
brunch for grads. For more information, call
Carolyn or Sean at 725-7826.
Sponsored by STAN and the
Stanford Alumni Association
