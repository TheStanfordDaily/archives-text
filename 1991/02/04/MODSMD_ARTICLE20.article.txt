# Letters Policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes letters from its
readers. Letters will be published at the
editor's discretion and are subject to edit
ing. All letters must include the author's
name, phone number and Stanford affili
ation, if any. Submissions with more than
five signatures must designate a repre
sentative.
Letters should be turned in on a Mac
intosh disk in Microsoft Word format
with a paper copy of the submission and
should be limited to 250 words. Disks
should have the owner's name written
clearly on the front and may be picked up
two business days after they have been
dropped off.
