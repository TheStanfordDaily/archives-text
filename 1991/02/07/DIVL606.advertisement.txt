Books Events
FEB. 11: William Trevor, Irish novelist and writer of
short stories, will read from his works at 8 p.m. in
Kresge Auditorium. Trevor's most recent work is
"Family Sins and Other Stories/'
FEB. 13: Journalists Martin Lee and Norman Solomon
will read from their new book, "Unreliable
Sources," at 8 p.m. at Printers' Inc. Bookstore on
El Camino Real, Menlo Park. The work is a look
at the corrupt state of the American mass media.
FEB. 15-17: The California International Antiquarian
Book Fair, featuring old and rare books, prints
and maps will be held in San Francisco at the
Crystal Court. Call Cynthia at 495-0100 for
more information.
