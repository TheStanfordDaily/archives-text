Stanford in Government

Present
A Panel Discussion:
Arab-Israeli Relations &
War in the Middle East
WHEN: Wednesday, Feb. 13 at 7 p.m.
WHERE: Education Bldg., Room 133

Arab-Israeli conflict and possible resolutions to the conflict.
Speakers:
Michcl Nabti: Former Professor of Middle Eastern Political
History at Stanford.
Geity Nashat: Senior Research Fellow, Hoover Institution.
Louise Stoll, PhD: National Middle East Committee of the
Anti-Defamation League.
Baruch Greenwald: Emissary from Israel.
Neal Keny-Guyer, Moderator: Director of Save the Children
for the Middle East, North Africa, and Europe.
