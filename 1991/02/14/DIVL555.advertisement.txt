ELECTRICAL ENGINEERS
SYSTEM ENGINEERS
SOFTWARE ENGINEERS
ORBITAL SCIENCES CORPORATION, the leader In the
commercialization of space, seeks electrical, systems
and software engineers for a variety of launch vehicle
and satellite programs in the Space Systems Division at
our Fairfax, Virginia headquarters. As an OSC employee,
you will work with the best in the industry in a progressive
environment.
Positions require a broad range of skills within each
discipline including design, analysis, test, and
subcontract management. All positions require a BS
degree or greater and excellent technical and
communications skills.
If you are among the best in these fields, we would
like to talk with you. OSC will conduct interviews at the
Stanford Park Hotel on Thursday, February 28, 1991.
For further information on OSC, please visit you
placement office or write to us at:
Director, Personnel and Administration
