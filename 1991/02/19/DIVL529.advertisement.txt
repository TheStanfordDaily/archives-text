Stanford University Engineering Students:

February 19th
Ford Engineering Information Session • Tuesday, February 19
Cypress Room, Tresidder Union • 6:00 p.m. - 8:00 p.m.

You're invited to attend an open information
session about your future and Ford Motor
Company.
Join us on Tuesday, February 19, to find out about
the many career opportunities available to you as a

global market. And, representatives from Ford
Motor Company will discuss the many rewards of
being a contributing member of the Ford
engineering team.

one with Ford representatives. When you join us at
the Information Session, please dress casually.
Refreshments will be served. We look forward to
meeting you!
