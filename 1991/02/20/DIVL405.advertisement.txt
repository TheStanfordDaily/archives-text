UNDERGRADUATE ADVISING CENTER
********
"Are You Interested in an
Individually Designed Major?"
Come to The Undergraduate Advising Center (UAC) and meet
with Hector Cuevas, Director of UAC to discuss your interests.
Keep in mind the following:
• Each student submitting a new proposal must have at least
three (3) full quarters' undergraduate work remaining at
Stanford.
• Each major shall consist of at least 60 units, at or above the
1(K) level.
• A maximum of 15 of these 6(1 units may be taken as pass/no
credit.
• A maximum of 5 of these 60 units may be taken in individual
study of directed reading.
• The proposed nu\jor must constitute a coherent program that
achieves a clear academic goal.
• The proposed miyor must he comparable in quality and
academic rigor to degrees obtained in other degree-granting
programs offered.
• The proposed major cannot duplicate or be achievable through
a major offered by another degree-granting program or
department.
• Students must present evidence that demonstrates their ability
to do independent work.
WINTER QUARTER DEADLINE:
Friday, March 1, 1991
Proposals are accepted once per quarter. Check with UAC for cxact dates
in Sweet Hall, Ist Floor or phone 723-1151 for more information.
