HEY SENIORS!
COULD YOU BE THE NEXT
Volunteer
Coordinator
at the Haas Center for Public Service?
The Volunteer Coordinator is responsible for the
promotion and support of service on the Stanford
campus. It is a one year, full time, paid position
open to recent Stanford graduates, including Class
of '91 graduating seniors.
Application Deadline: April 3,1991
Applications available at the Haas Center for Public Service.
For more information contact Victoria Deise at 725-2865
