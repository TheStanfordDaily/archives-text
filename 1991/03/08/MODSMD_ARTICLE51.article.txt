# '20/20' segment won't run until after hearings
## 
### John Wagner Senior staff writer 
"20/20's" segment on the indirect
cost controversy won't come into
view until after the March 13 hear
ing by the House Subcommittee on
Oversight and Investigations, a pro
ducer from the ABC news weekly
program said yesterday.
The earliest the piece would air is
Friday, March 22, said Bill Willson.
The segment includes interviews
with University President Donald
Kennedy, federal regulator Paul
Biddle and Rep. John Dingell, D-
Mich.

C-SPAN has not yet decided if it
will broadcast Wednesday's hear
ings.
A decision will not be made until
next Tuesday, according to a spokes
person for the cable station.
