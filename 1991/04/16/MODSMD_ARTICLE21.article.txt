# Letters Policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes letters from its
readers. Letters will be published at the
editor's discretion and are subject to edit
ing. All letters must include the author's
name, phone number and Stanford affili
ation, if any. Submissions with more than
five signatures must designate a repre
sentative.
Letters should be turned in on a Mac
intosh disk in Microsoft Word format
with a paper copy of the submission.
Disks should have the owner's name writ
ten clearly on the front and may be picked
up two business days after they have been
dropped off.
Submissions are published on a space
available basis at the discretion of the
Opinions editors. The Daily regrets that it
cannot print letters that publicize upcom
ing events.
Bring letters to The Daily's offices in
the Storke Publications Building, fax
them to (415) 725-1329 or mail them to:
Letters to the Editor, The Stanford Daily,
Storke Student Publications Building,
Stanford, CA 94305.
