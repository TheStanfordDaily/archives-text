UNDERGRADUATE ADVISING CENTER
"Are You Interested in an
Individually Designed Major?"
Come in to The Undergraduate Advising Center (UAC) and meet
with the Program Coordinator to diseuss your interests. Keep in
mind the following:
• Kach student submitting a new proposal must have at least
three (3) full quarters' undergraduate work remaining at
Stanford.
• Kach major shall consist of at least units, at or above the
l(H> level.
• A maximum of 15 of Chest 60 units may be taken as pass no
credit.
• A maximum of 5 of these 60 units nia> be taken in individual
study of directed reading.
• The proposed major must constitute a coherent program that
achieves a clear academic goal.
• The proposed nuyor must be comparable in quality and
academic rigor to degrees obtained in other degree-granting
programs offered.
• The proposed imyor cannot duplicate or be achievable through
a major offered by another degree-granting program or
department.
• Students must present evidence that demonstrates their ability
to do independent work.
SPRING QUARTER DEADLINE:
Friday, May 3, m\
Proposals arc accepted once per quarter. Cheek with UAC tor exact dates
in Sweet Hall, Ist Floor or phone 723-1151 tor more information
