# For the record
## 
### 
In Tuesday's Daily, a caption
accompanying a story on bisexual
ity misidentified Mike Almodova
as Steve Pitts.
In addition, a story on the Sex
ual Assault Task Force reforms
incorrectly stated the status of the
orientation program. It will only
go into effect after it is approved
by Sally Mahoney, acting vice
president of Student Resources.
An article Monday incorrectly
stated the conditions behind
Health Promotion Specialist
Louise Miller's call to Stuart
Reges. Miller said Ronald Buck
nam asked her to call Reges to
make sure he was aware of the
University's drug policy.
