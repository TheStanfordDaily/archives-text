# Baseball at Arizona
## 
### 
TONIGHT — 7 p.m. first pitch at
Sancet Field in Tucson. Satur
day at 7 p.m. and Sunday at 1
p.m.
RECORDS - Stanford (30-14, 13-
7 and second in the Six-Pac),
Arizona (23-28, 8-16 and sixth).
Stanford is ranked No. 7 by
Baseball America, No. 11 by Col
legiate Baseball/ESPN. Arizona
is unranked.
RADIO - KZSU (90.1 FM) will
broadcast all three games live
STARTERS - John Reid (5-3)
will start for Stanford tonight,
Jamie Sepeda (5-1) will go on
Saturday, and Willie Adams (6-
2) will pitch Sunday.
