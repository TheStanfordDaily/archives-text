Stanford University's Committee on Health and
Safety will hold a meeting open to all members
of the public
Thursday, May 2, from 7:00 to 9:00 P.M.
in the Cypress Room in Tresidder Memorial Union.
The purpose of the meeting is to report on the status of Stanford's health
and safety programs and to give members of the Stanford community
and neighboring communities an opportunity to present their concerns
directly to the committee.
Professor Robert Simoni, Chairman of the University Health and Safety
Committee, will report on the Committee's charge and its work to date.
Thomas F. Mcßride, Director of Environmental Health and Safety, will
report on the overall status of health and safety programs at Stanford.
Following those reports there will be an opportunity for questions or
comments from members of the public. The Committee welcomes oral
and written comments from members of the public. Comments may be
sent to University Health and Safety Committee, c/o ESF, Oak Road,
Stanford, CA 94305-8007.
