# Stanford Centennial includes Cinco de Mayo celebration
## 
### 
This celebration is brought to you
by the number five.
And the month of May.
And the Stanford Centennial Cele
bration.
The University's annual celebration
of Cinco de Mayo begins this weekend
with music, dance and theater perfor
mances sponsored by El Centro Chi
cano and the Centennial Operating
Committee.
Cinco de Mayo celebrates the 19th
century victory of the Mexican army
over the French at the Battle of Puebla,
and festivities on campus will celebrate
Mexican culture.
The celebration kicks off on Friday
with a concert at Burnham Pavilion
featuring Dr. Loco and his Rockin'
Jalapeno Band.
The band, known for high energy
performances, is comprised of Chi
cano musicians from East Los Angeles.
And the music, a blend of rock, blues,
salsa and cumbia, begins at 9 p.m. and
continues until 2 a.m.
