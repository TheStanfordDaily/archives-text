PHI BETA KAPPA
TEACHING PRIZE
Nominations are now being accepted for Stanford
teaching prize awarded exclusively by students.
The Phi Beta Kappa award for undergraduate in
struction is intended to recognize excellence in
teaching, and specifically effectiveness in eliciting
scholarly achievement by students in the liberal
arts and sciences. If you would like to recognize a
faculty member who makes a special effort to seek
out and encourage student scholarship, submit a
descriptive letter of recommendation on his or her
behalf to:
PBK Teaching Prize Committee
Building 1
Stanford, CA 94305-2070
Nominations will be accepted until
MAY 17
