The Hoagland Prize is awarded annually to an outstanding teacher of undergraduates.
The winner will receive a monetary prize as well as release from routine duties In order to pursue new
Ideas and directions In teaching, or to spend time on special projects.
Nominating Is easy. If you have had a professor you've especially appreciated, write a letter
describing the qualities that make her or him a fantastic teacher, and send It to the Dean of
Undergraduate Studies at Building One, Stanford, CA 94305-2070. Nominations are due JUNE 7th.
If you have questions, please call the Undergraduate Studies Program Assistant at 725-1572.
Only you can recognize the best professors this campus has to offer!!
