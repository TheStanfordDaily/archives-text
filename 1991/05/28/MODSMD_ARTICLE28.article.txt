# Graham wins at French Open
## 
### 
Junior Debbie Graham won her
first round match at the French
Open yesterday. The Fountain
Valley, Calif., native defeated Aus
tria's Beate Reinstadler in
straight sets 6-1, 7-5.
Former Stanford star Tami
Whitlinger also advanced by beat
ing Erika de Lone 6-1, 7-6 (9-7).
