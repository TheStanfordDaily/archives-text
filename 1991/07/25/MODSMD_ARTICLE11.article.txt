# A 						tighter system of control
## 
### 
Some of the specific accounting reforms Stanford plans to implement:
■ The University will develop a written code of ethics and conduct which sets
forth the standardsgoverning the conduct of all University employees.
■ The University will establish accounts to capture unallowable costs and pro
cedures for more effective front-end screening.
■ The University has requested a new external auditing team from Coopers &
Lybrand. The engagement partner will relocate his office from Boston to San
Francisco to oversee the Stanford audit.
■ The University has named a new chief financial auditor and a new director
of internal audit.
■ The University has shifted government liason repsonsibilities from the
Controller's Office to the director of internal uudit.
