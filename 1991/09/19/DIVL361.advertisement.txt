Student Health Insurance
Deadlines
September 26,1991:
This is the deadline
for students
to add or waive
the student health
insurance.
September 15,1991:
This is the date that new dependents to the plan need to pay their
premium to ensure a September 15th effective date.
(The policy effective date is September 15,1991 or the date the premium is paid, whichever is later.)
October 15,1991:
This is the deadline for continuing dependents on the plan to pay
their premium to ensure continuous coverage.
To add or waive the student health insurance, or to pay
dependent premiums, come to the Insurance Desk at Cowell
Student Health Center by the dates specified above. If you have
questions, please call Linda Moran, 723-2135.
