ATTENTION
PROFESSORS
The rules regarding production of Course Readers
have changed dramatically
• The law makes it clear that you and/or your department can be
held liable for copyright violations.
• Copy Central has a trained staff who can safely guide each
article through the copyright approval process. This same
department already handles copyright clearance for UC Berke
ley Course Readers for Copy Central.
• It is vital that we receive reader materials from you at least
4 weeks earlier than in the past.
What we need from vou
• At least 4 weeks lead time, and more if possible
• For each article:
a. The copyright holder/publisher
b. Article name
c. Article author
d. Page numbers
What we will do
• Contact each copyright holder (by fax if they allow) and request reprint permission
• Manage the entire process, follow up publishers
• Give credits in Course Reader, maintain records
• Track and pay publisher royalties
• Produce high quality customized Course Readers for you and your students
Doing it RIGHT...on time
For Information, please call
