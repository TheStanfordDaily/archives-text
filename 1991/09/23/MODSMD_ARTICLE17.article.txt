TOMORROW
Auditions: The Stanford Savoyards will
hold auditions for Gilbert & Sullivan's
"Patience, or, Bunthorne's Bride" from 7-10
p.m. In Braun Rehearsal Hall (Stanford
Campus, Music Bldg.). Principal roles for 3
sopranos, 1 mezzo, 1 contralto, 1 tenor, 4 bari
tones, plus choruses of 20 rapturous maidens
and 20 officers of the Dragoon Guards. Bring a
song prepared in English. An accompanist will
be provided. Orchestra, set & costume crews,
production staff are also needed. For more
information call Rita Taylor at 857-1345.
