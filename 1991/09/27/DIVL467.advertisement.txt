STANFORD UNITED FOR RAPE ELIMINATION
SURE Escort is a student-run organization dedicated to
providing the Stanford community with safe and reliable
escorts for those travelling alone at night on campus.
We provide free radio-dispatched golf cart escorts from
sundown to 1 a.m., seven days a week.
Interested in getting involved with SURE?
SURE Escort is now hiring student escorts to drive our golf
carts and staff the phones. In addition to escort duties,
escorts are also expected to perform 5-10 hours of
community service each quarter in the areas of campus
security/rape prevention. Our first meeting is on Tues
day, October 1st, at 7pm in the OS A office (2nd floor
Tressider). All returning escorts, as well as any interested
students should plan on attending. For more informa
tion call Jeff at 497-5803 or Chris at 497-0906.
