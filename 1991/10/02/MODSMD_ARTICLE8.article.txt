# Hearings on budget held this week
## 
### 
The University budget will be discussed this
week at the following meetings:
Today
Faculty Open Forum I
Annenberg Auditorium, 8-10 p.m.
To discuss recommendations on
undergraduate education and revenue
enhancement.
Tomorrow
Faculty/Staff Town Meeting
Memorial Auditorium, 1-3 p.m.

Informational meeting on budget planning
process for faculty and staff.
Student Open Forum
Cubberley Auditorium, 7-9 p.m.
Informational meeting on budget planning
process for students.
Faculty Open Forum II
Annenberg Auditorium, 8-10 p.m.
To discuss recommendations on research and
scholarship, graduate and professional education
and administrative services.
