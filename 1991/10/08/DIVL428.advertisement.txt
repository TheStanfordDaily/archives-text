KEPLER'S PRESENTS
Molly Ivins
Tuesday, October 8, 8 PM
Journalist Molly Ivins, contributor to the
Dallas Times-Herald, Mother Jones, The Nation,
The Progressive, and the MacNeil-Lehrer
News Hour, will discuss MOLLY IVINS CAN'T SAY
THAT, CAN SHE? Full of illuminating commentary,
hilarious anecdotes, and caustic wit, this
collection of pieces will show the reader
unfamiliar with her work why John Leonard of
CBS Sunday morning calls her "My favorite
newspaper columnist in the whole world."
Mary Clearman Blew
Wednesday, October 9, 8 PM
Mary Clearman Blew, author of the story
collections Lambing Out and Runaway, and one
of the editors of The Last Best Place: A Montana-
Anthology, will read from ALL BUT THE WALTZ:
ESSAYS ON A MONTANA FAMILY. "Subtle prose that
transports to a magical place, dissolving the line
between memory and the present. A superbly
realized vision." Publishers Weekly
