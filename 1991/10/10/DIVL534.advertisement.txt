F.N. Wolf & Co., Inc. is a full service investment

counter market, plus offer a wide range of
Investment vehicles. We do Tax Free Bonds, Gov
ernment Securities, Mutual Funds, Insurance, Real
Estate Partnerships, and Retirement Programs, but
our real specialty is growth oriented New issues and
NASDAQ listed stocks. Based on our experience
and performance records, we are also one of the
leading underwriters on Wall Street.
Our entire investment philosophy is geared toward
satisfying individual investor needs. However, we do
specialize in the selection and recommendation of
stocks in smaller-to-medium-sized companies that,
in our opinion, offer greater than average potential
for capital appreciation.
