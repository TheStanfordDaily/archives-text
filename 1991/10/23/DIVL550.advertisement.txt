Paul Alexander, author of Rough Magic Sylvia Plath, Rough Magic, by Paul
Alexander, to be published by Viking on
October 15, 1991, at $24.95.
Paul Alexander will discuss The Rough Magic: A Biography of Sylvia
Plath. "Choosing to write Plath's life without consent and probable
constraints of the estate, Alexander eschews quoting from Plath's
work; his is not a literary study. Yet the results are impressive: a
thorough, beautifully fashioned chronicle rich in new materials and
significant minutiae." Publishers Weekly
