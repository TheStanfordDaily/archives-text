The Rice University Publishing Program, July 13-August 7,
IW2, is designed to develop talent, skills and career opportuni
ties for persons interested in book and magazine publishing. I he
program is designed for students who will be entering their senior
year in IW2 and for college graduates. Although participants come

the country.
