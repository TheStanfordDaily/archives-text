# How to avoid HIV
## 
### 
•Anal or vaginal intercourse
with condoms
•Oral-penile contact that
uses a condom or ends
before ejaculation
•Oral-vaginal contact with a
dental dam (a piece of
stretched, thin rubber)
which prevents the mixing
of saliva and vaginal
secretions
Source: Herant Katchadourian, "Funda
mentals of Human Sexuality"
