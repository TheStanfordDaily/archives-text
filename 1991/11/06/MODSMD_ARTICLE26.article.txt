# Men's Basketball vs. High Five America
## 
### 
TONIGHT — 7:30 p.m. tip-off at Roscoe Maples Pavilion.
RECORDS — This is the exhibition opener, and the game does not count on
Stanford's record. High Five lost at USC Monday, 104-101, as the Trojans'
Harold Miner scored 44 points.
NOTES — High Five America is a team of former college players that
spreads information about the dangers of drug and alcohol abuse. Troy
Lewis (Purdue), Chris Morris (Houston) and Dexter Howard (Pepperdine)
lead the team. Alexander Gomelski, head coach of the USSR Olympic
Team from 1956-88, is an assistant coach.
