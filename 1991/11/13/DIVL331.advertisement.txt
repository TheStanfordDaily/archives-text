McKinsey &Company, Inc.
McKinsey & Company, Inc.,
a leading international management
consulting firm, cordially invites
members of the 1992 Stanford senior
class to learn more about our 2-year
business analyst program.
We will be having a presentation and
reception for interested students on
Thursday, November 14,1991
at the Oak East Lounge
of Tresidder Union
from 7 p.m. to 9 p.m.
We look forward to seeing you there.
