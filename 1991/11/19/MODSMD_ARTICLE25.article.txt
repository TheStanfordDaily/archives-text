# Wrestling
## 
### 
TONIGHT— vs. San Francisco State at 7:00 at Burnham Pavilion
NOTES — Stanford kicks off its 1991-1992 season with several returning starters as
well as a tough crop of top lightweight recruits. NCAA competitor Matt Topham leads
the team with senior classmate Mark Graham. Other returning starters include Lance
Johnson, Jay Jackson and David Sacks. In his 13th season at Stanford, head coach
Chris Horpel has another very formidable team this season. Admission tonight is free
with Stanford University Identification.
