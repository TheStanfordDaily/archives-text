Ground Rules:
1. The line will he formed at the base of the stage in White Plaza, and will wrap
around Dinkelspiel Auditorium. Please do not obstruct any paths. We also request
that only people buying tickets remain in line (this does not mean that your friends
and dates cannot visit you).
2. A line roster will be maintained and checked periodically by committee members.
Names will only be added to the end.
3. People may leave the line for a maximum of ten minutes. You must check out with
the committee member on duty. This time limit will be strictly enforced.
4. People in line must have a Stanford ID and a picture ID to remain in line and
purchase tickets. IDs will be used to verify names on the line roster. No ID, No
line, No roster, No tickets.
5. No tents and no fires. Please keep noise to a minimum. Please dispose of litter
properly.
6. People not abiding to these rules will not be allowed to purchase tickets.
7. 200 pairs of tickets will be sold each morning. Sales will begin at Bam.
8. Checks and exact cash only.
9. We reserve the right to modify or add to this list of rules any time prior to ticket
sales for any reason.
