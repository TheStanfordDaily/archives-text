STANFORD PRESIDENTIAL SEARCH
• OPEN MEETINGS •
Members of the Presidential Search Committee seek your views on
the selection of the ninth president of Stanford. Faculty, staff and
students are invited to attend one of the following open meetings:
FACULTY
Tuesday, JANUARY 14, 4:00 to 5:30 p.m., History Bldg., Room 203
STAFF
Monday, JANUARY 13, noon to 1:30 p.m., Tresidder Memorial Union
Oak Lounge, 2nd floor. All staff are encouraged to participate, and
subject to supervisory approval, may be released from work to attend
this meeting without suffering loss of pay.
STUDENTS
Monday, JANUARY 13, 3:30 to 5:00 p.m., Florence Moore Main Lounge
Wednesday, JANUARY 15, 7:00 to 8:30 p.m., Rains Buttery/Hacienda
Commons Room
Tuesday, JANUARY 21,6:30 to 8:00 p.m., Elliott Program Center Lounge,
Governors Corner
Monday, JANUARY 27,6:30 to 8:00 p.m., Okada House Lounge,
Wilbur Hall
Each meeting provides an opportunity for members of
the Stanford community to make suggestions and
recommendations for the presidential search.
