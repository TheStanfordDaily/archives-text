A Jewish Book of Months
Explores the Jewish calendar
drawing on knowledse and emotions concerning
the moon, the lunar calendar, female experience
and other cultural resources.
The 12-part mixed media/collage
will be on exhibit throughout
January In Tresldder Union
Join the Artist, Jenny Helbraun,
for a short talk and reception
on Thursday, January 16th
TODAY
from 4:00 - 5:30 p.m.
in the Tresidder Union Lounge
About the artist:
Jenny Helbraun was born and raised in a mostly secular,
politically-involved Jewish family in upstate New York. In 1982
she relocated to San Francisco where she continued her art
studies at SFSU and at the Mission Cultural Center's Mission
Grafica. She does mixed media graphics, using sllkscreen,
photography, painting and drawing.
