The Black Student Community
Service Opportunities Fair
Thursday, January 23, 1992
TVesidder, Oak West Lounge
11:30 am - 2:3opm
Stop by and talk to representatives from
service programs on and off campus. You
can also pick up valuable information on
volunteer opportunities, summer
internships and careers in public service.
Opportunities range from tutoring and
mentoring to working on public policy
issues. Remember, we must all lend a hand
to create the type of world we want to live
in. Let's do it together!!!
The Fair is open to all members of the
Stanford Community
For more information contact Angela Hilton, Community Service
Coordinator, Black Community Services Center, 723-1587.
