Self Defense for Women
Weekend
Workshops
Workshop will include ways to defend yourself with
your body, mind, and voice through physical self
defense technique training, assertiveness role plays,
and candid discussion of issues of sexual abuse and
violence. All workshops are open to women of all
ages and all degrees of physical fitness, and are
taught by women instructors. Workshop will be
offered twenty times during the winter quarter and
also will be offered during spring quarter.
Women's Self Defense Workshops (WSDW) is a joint effort of the
Office of Student Activities (OSA), the Associated Students of
Stanford University (ASSU), the Coalition Against Sexual Assault,
and the Health Promotion Program in Cowell Student Health Center.
