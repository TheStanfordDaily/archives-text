Applied Decision Analysis, Inc.
OR and Math/CS
INFORMATION
SESSION
Applied Decision Analysis, Inc. (ADA) wants to meet OR graduate
students and Math/CS undergraduate students who are interested in
quantitative analysis and management consulting. If your background
includes mathematics, statistics, computer science, industrial
engineering/operations research, physics or other quantitative sciences,
we would like to meet you.
ADA is a small, growing consulting company, located in Menlo Park,
that applies sophisticated analytical techniques to real-world problems.
ADA's strength is high quality work in areas such as strategic planning,
decision analysis, operations management, analysis of public policy,
forecasting markets for new products, R&D planning, and basic
research.
We would like to meet students who have:
An interest in solving important, complex problems,
in* Skills in a broad range of mathematical techniques,
hi* Communication skills to present analytical results in a clear,
concise manner, and
ii* A high level of enthusiasm for challenging work in an informal
atmosphere.
Also, please join us at
