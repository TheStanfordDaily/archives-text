SUMMER RA
POSITIONS
Jobs are available in the summer residences for
Stanford undergraduates working as Summer RAs.
Duties include serving as a combined resident and
program assistant for: Stanford students taking
courses and/or working at Stanford in the summer,
visiting students from other U.S. colleges and
universities, foreign students, and advanced high
school students. Positions are available primarily in
Kimball and Branner. The Summer RA job is a twenty
hour a week commitment; room and board is
provided.
Interested students should pick up an application
packet from the Summer Session Office, Nitery
Building, Old Union, beginning Thursday, February 6.
Applications must be submitted by FRIDAY,
FEBRUARY 21.
