SESSION
Applied Decision Analysis, Inc. (ADA) wants to meet EES graduate
students who are interested in quantitative analysis and management
consulting. If your background includes mathematics, statistics,
computer science, industrial engineering/operations research, physics
or other quantitative sciences, we would like to meet you.
ADA is a small, growing consulting company, located in Menlo Park,
that applies sophisticated analytical techniques to real-world problems.
ADA's strength is high quality work in areas such as strategic planning,
decision analysis, operations management, analysis of public policy,
forecasting markets for new products, R&D planning, and basic
research.
We would like to meet students who have:
ii* An interest in solving important, complex problems,
hi# Skills in a broad range of mathematical techniques,
n# Communication skills to present analytical results in a clear,
concise manner, and
in# A high level of enthusiasm for challenging work in an informal
atmosphere.
