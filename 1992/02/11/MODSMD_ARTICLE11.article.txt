# The 						Stanford Daily
##

The Stanford Daily (USPS 518-420) is an independent
student newspaper owned and published by The Stanford
Daily Publishing Corp., StorVb Publications Building, Stan
lord, CA 94305.
Letters, columns, cadoons and advertisements do not
necessarily reflect editorial opinion.
The Daily is published weekdays during the academic
year except Dead Week, when one Issue is published, finals
week and holidays.
Subscription rates: For California residents, $97.43 per
year or $37.89 per quarter. For residents in the rest of the In
the United States. Canada and Mexico $90.00 per year or
$35.00 per quarter Second class postage paid at Palo Alt-),
CA. Send check or money order in advance.
