# Women's Basketball vs. UCLA, USC
## 
### 
THIS WEEKEND — 7 p.m. tip-off tonight against the Bruins at Pauley Pavilion in Los Angeles.
The match against the Trojans is scheduled to begin at 2 p.m. Sunday. Both matches will be
broadcast live locally on campus radio station KZSU (90.1 FM).
RECORDS — Stanford (17-2, 7-2 and tied for first in the Pacific 10 Conference), UCLA (13-6,
6-3 and tied for third), USC (13-6, 6-3 and tied for third).
PREVIOUS MEETINGS — Stanford beat UCLA 95-92 Jan. 18., but the Cardinal was down at
halftime. Val Whiting led Stanford with 35 points and 11 rebounds, while the Bruins were led
by Natalie Williams' career-high 28 points and 15 rebounds. Stanford defeated USC 87-72
two days before, putting together a second-half run after 40% first-half shooting. Once again
Whiting scored 35 points for the Cardinal with 9 rebounds, while the Trojans' big woman, 6-
5 Lisa Leslie, scored 25 points but only pulled down 5 rebounds.
