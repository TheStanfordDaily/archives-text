# Men's Basketball vs. Washington
## 
### 
TONIGHT — 7:30 p.m. tip-off at Maples Pavilion, with live campus radio cov
erage by KZSU (90.1 FM).
RECORDS — Stanford (14-8, 6-6 and tied for fourth in the Pacific 10 Con
ference), UW (11-13, 4-9 and eighth in the Pac-10).
PREVIOUS MEETINGS — Stanford has won five in a row at Maples against
Washington. On Feb. 2 the Cardinal hit 10 of 10 free throws down the stretch
to win 64-58 in Seattle, as Marcus Lollie scored a career-high 17 points.
THE HUSKIES — The Huskies are built around 6-foot-10 center Rich Man
ning and are the polar opposite of USC in the quickness department. The
Dogs are hungry for an NIT berth.
