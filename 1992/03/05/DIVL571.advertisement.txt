Born in the valley of the Nile, famed performer Hamza El-Din
has traveled the world integrating music of different cultures
and traditions. At the end of a diversified North American tour
including concerts with the Kronos Quartet at Lincoln Center
and with the Grateful Dead at Oakland Coliseum, Hamza
stops at Stanford to share his music on the oud (lute) and on
percussion.
