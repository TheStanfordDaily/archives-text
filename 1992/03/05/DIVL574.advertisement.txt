Planning to be in New York City
this summer?
Barnard College offers summer housing at moderate rates to
those who plan to study or work in New York City.

street from Columbia University and accessible to all the
cultural resources of New York City.
The dorms offer a variety of living arrangements —
singles, doubles, or suites.
