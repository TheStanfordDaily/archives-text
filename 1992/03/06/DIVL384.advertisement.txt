COWELL STUDENT
HEALTH CENTER
STANFORD UNIVERSITY
VOLUNTEER HIV PEER
COUNSELORS NEEDED!
To provide anonymous HIV Antibody Testing, Cowell
Student Health Center is recruiting graduate and
undergraduate students to do the required pre and post
test counseling. If you are interested in being trained as
an HIV peer counselor, applications are available at the
Health Promotion Program Office, 2nd floor of Cowell.
If you would like more information, please call 723-0821.
