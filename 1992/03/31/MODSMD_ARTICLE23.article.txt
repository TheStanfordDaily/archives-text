student newspaper owned and published by The Stanford
Dally Publishing Corp., Storke Publications Building, Stan
ford. CA 04305.
Letters, columns, cartoons and advertisements do not
necessarily reflect editorial opinion.
The Daily Is published weekdays during the academic
year except Dead Week, when one Issue Is published, finals
week end holidays.
Subscription rates: For California residents. $97.43 per
year or $37 89 per quarter. For residents In the rest of the
United States, Canada and Mexico $90.00 per year or
$35.00 per quarter.Second class postage paid at Palo Alto.
CA. Send check or money order In advance.
