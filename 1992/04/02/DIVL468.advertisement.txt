IF YOU:

■ like to eat real Asian food
■ any or all of the above
THEN
Come to a reception & informational with the Asian Community
Immigration Clinic and the Stanford Asian Law Students Association.
•The Asian Community Immigration Clinic provides direct services to low-income Chinese,
Vietnamese & Korean communities. (Services can be expanded to other communities based on
volunteer interest). Volunteers arc trained to provide legal counseling on naturalization and
immigration procedures. Volunteers also participate in legal research and advocacy.
