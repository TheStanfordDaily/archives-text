Valuable Opportunity
The Stanford Alumni Association is now
accepting applications for the position of:
Stanford Graduate Student Representative
association to the Alumni Board of Directors
The Stanford Alumni Association is goverened by a board of 25 alumni members, one
graduate student, one undergraduate student, and two other ex-officio representatives.
The board meets three times a year arid sets the direction and policies of the Alumni
Association. The graduate student board representative will convey the needs and
concerns of the graduate student body to the Alumni Board of Directors, influencing the
relationship between the Stanford Alumni Association and the Stanford graduate
community. The two-year term begins at the next Board of Directors Meeting, May 8-9.
Please stop by Bowman Alumni House for an application, or call DJ Dull at 725-0683 for
more information.
Application deadline: April 27, 1992.
