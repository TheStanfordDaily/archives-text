# Sport Shorts
## 
### 
Basketball, men's: Three Cardinal players were
named to the 1991-92 Pacific 10 Conference Men's
Basketball All-Academic team. Juniors Peter Dukes
and Paul Garrett, along with sophomore Jason
Weaver received the honor on Tuesday. To be eligible,
a player must compile a grade-point-average of at least
3.0 (on a 4.0 scale) over his entire academic career.

Golf, men's: Results from yesterday's Far West
Intercollegiate Tournament in Santa Cruz will
appear in tomorrow's Daily. After three rounds, the
Stanford men were tied for third place with San
Jose State with a cumulative total of 585 strokes,
just two behind second-place Pacific, and five off
the pace of leader Fresno State.
