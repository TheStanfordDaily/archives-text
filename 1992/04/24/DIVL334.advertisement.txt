NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEALTH &
SAFETY MEETING
Stanford University's Committee on Health and Safety
ANNUAL PUBLIC MEETING — MONDAY APRIL 27th
from 7:30 to 8:30 p.m.
Terman Auditorium, Terman Engineering Building
Committee Chair Bill Brown will report on the Committee's Charge
and its work to date. Thomas F. Mcßride, Director of Environmental
Health and Safety, will report on the overall status of health and
safety programs at Stanford. Matthew Allen, Associate Director for
Environment Safety and Health, will report on programs at SLAC.
Following these reports, there will be an opportunity for questions
or comments from members of the public. The committee
welcomes oral and written comments. Written comments may be
sent to the University Committee on Health and Safety, c/o ESE
Oak Road, Stanford, CA 94305-8007.
