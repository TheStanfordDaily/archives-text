# Men make finals
## 
### 
Last night in Lincoln, Nebraska,
the No. 3 Stanford men's gymnas
tics team advanced to the finals of
the NCAA Tournament. The Car
dinal's team score of 287.20 was
tops in the eight-squad NCAA
semifinals field. Also qualifying
for the finals were host Nebraska,
winner of the West Regional, with
a 286.925 mark, and Ohio State,
winner of the East Regional, with
a score of 286.80.
The three-team finals will be
held today and tomorrow. Com
plete results will appear in Mon
day's Daily.
