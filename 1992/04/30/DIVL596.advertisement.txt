KEPLER'S PRESENTS
Slavenka Drakulic
discussing
How We Survived Communism and Even Laughed
Slavenka Drakulic is a respected journalist and
cultural commentator in Yugoslavia and a regular
contributor to The Nation and The New Republic
Tonight she will discuss Hoiv We Survived
Communism and Even Laughed (Norton). "Slavenka
Drakulic has been called the Simone de Beauvoir of
Eastern Europe, and this book Is a testament to why.
As a feminist free-speaker, political theorist and
activist, and just plain good writer, hers is that rare
voice to hearken to —and trust." — Robin Morgan
8:00 pm TONIGHT, APRIL 30,1992
