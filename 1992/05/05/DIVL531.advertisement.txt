GORBACHEV LOTTERY WINNERS
ALL STUDENTS, FACULTY AND STAFF
WHO ENTERED THE LOTTERY
ARE WINNERS!
If you filled out a registration card last week in Inner Quad and
had your Stanford identification card punched, you are entitled to
one ticket to the Mikhail Gorbachev lecture on Saturday, May 9 in
Frost Amphitheatre.
With Stanford identification, each winner may pick up one ticket
from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Wednesday and Thursday, May 6 and 7, at the
Tresidder Ticket Office. Unclaimed tickets will be given out, one-per
person-per-Stanford-ID, on a first-come, first-served basis beginning*
at 10 a.m. Friday, May 8, at the Tresidder Ticket Office. Unclaimed
tickets will be available only to Stanford individuals who have not
previously obtained a ticket.
