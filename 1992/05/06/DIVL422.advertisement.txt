The Institute for Research on Women & Gender
invites you to attend
The Deborah L. Rhode Annual Lecture
"Three Models of Social Change"
Speaker: Nannerl O. Keohane
President, Wellesley College
Nannerl O. Keohane is an eminent scholar, author, and political philosopher. She has written extensively in the fields of political
philosophy, feminism, and education. Keohane is the author of Philosophy and the State in France: The Renaissance to the
Enlightenment, and coeditor of Feminist Theory: A Critique of Ideology. A former faculty member in Stanford's Department of
Political Science and former visiting scholar at the Institute for Research on Women and Gender, Keohane became President of
Wellesley College in 1981.
Thursday evening, May 7th, 7:30 p.m. - Tresidder Union, Oak Lounge
