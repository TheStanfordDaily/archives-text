# Music
## What's Up 
### 

Spring Migration, the Dance Division's annual concert, will
take place Fridays and Saturdays, May 8-9 and 15-16, at 8:30 p.m.
in RobJe Studio. Student tickets are $5, available at the door.
You don't need to go farther than Dinkelspiel Auditorium to
hear great music this weekend. The Stanford Jazz Ensemble will
perform Saturday at 8 p.m., and the Stanford Symphonic Band will
give their annual Sousa-style concert Sunday at 2 p.m. These two
shows cost $4 for students. Tickets can be purchased through
Tresidder Ticket Office or at the door.
The Living Stage Theatre Company of Washington, D.C., pre
sents a special performance of "Lynched Hopes and Unsung
Songs," Saturday at 7:30 p.m. in East Palo Alto. Phone 323-8311
for tickets and information.
