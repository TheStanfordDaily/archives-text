# What's Up
## 
### 
Lively Arts brings those musical and comical "cowboys/' Riders
in the Sky, to Frost Amphitheater Sunday, May 17, at 2 p.m. Stu
dent tickets start at $14 at Tresidder Ticket Office. Or mosey down
to Student Rush — 15 minutes before showtime at the door —
when remainders are sold for half-price like so many stray cattle.
Senior Kevin Donlon will perform original music at the Knoll
Ballroom Friday, May 15, at 8:30 and 10:30 p.m. Admission is free.
Grease, this year's starry-eyed Flo Mo Musical, will be performed
under the stars Friday and Saturday, May 15 and 16, at 8 p.m. in
the Flo Mo courtyard. Admission is free.
The Anton Schwartz Jazz Quartet will play acoustic jazz at the
Coffee House tonight from 9 to 11. Admission is free, but coffee
and beer prices will send you screaming to the ATM.
The Stanford Chamber Orchestra spring conceit is Sunday,
May 17, at 2:30 p.m. in Dinkelspiel. Student tickets are $4 at
Tresidder Ticket Office — where else?
