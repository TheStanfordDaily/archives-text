Undergraduate Draw and
Graduate Lottery assignments
are now posted
Oil-campus students: The deadline to accept assignments and join the Waiting List is
Friday, June 5 at your current residence office.
Off-campus students: The deadline to accept assignments and join the Waiting List
Is Monday, June 15 at the Stanford Housing Center.
You must Join the Waiting List If you want to be considered for housing In future
y Assignment rounds.
A COURSE TIMED TO THE
SUMMER SCHOOL SCHEDULE!
The Program will offer a new course for the October 3 LSAT, starting
Thursday, June 25 and continuing through the week of August 14, meeting mostly
on TUesday evenings. The Program's regular courses for the October LSAT and
GMAT will start in early August.
All curricular preparation, instruction, and feedback are offered by a former
Stanford faculty member who is a Stanford J.D. and a practicing attorney who has
prepared members of the Stanford community since 1978 for the LSAT. Medians
for Stanford related persons in the past year are above the 90th percentile.
Enrollment in this condensed course will be limited to eight students, and the fee
is $550.
■ over 30 hours of instruction ■ a professional educator as your instructor
■ supervised homework ■ copious practice materials
