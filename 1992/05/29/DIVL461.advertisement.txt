The Hoagland Prize is awarded annually to an outstanding teacher of
undergraduates. The winner will receive a monetary prize as well as release from
routine duties in order to pursue new ideas and directions in leaching, or to spend
time on special projects.
Nominating is easy. If you have had a professor you've especially
appreciated, write a letter describing the qualities that make her or him a fantastic
teacher and send it to Anne Peck, Associate Dean and Director of Undergraduate
Studies at Building 310, Stanford, CA 94305. Nominations are due JUNE 12.
If you have any questions, please call Christy Lang at 725-1528.
Only you can rccognize the best professors Stanford has to offer!
