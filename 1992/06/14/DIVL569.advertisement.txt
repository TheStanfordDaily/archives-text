■ uncertainty ■ instability
■ divorce ■ the threat of illness
■ fear of losing a job
PRAYER STABILIZES
CHANGE
a talk by Dorothy Dipuo Maubane
a member of the Christian Science Board of Lectureship
Sponsored by Second Church of Christ, Scientist, Palo A/to.

Dorothy Dipuo Maubane was

professional Christian Science
practioner, working full-time in
the healing ministry.
