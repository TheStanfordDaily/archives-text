roe captured the Wimbledon
men's doubles title with his part
ner Michael .Stich. McEnroe and
Stich outlasted Card alum Jim
Grabb and Richey Reneberg in a
five-hour classic. The two-day
match, scored 5-7, 7-6, 3-6, 7-6,
19-17, was the longest Wimble
don doubles final in history.
