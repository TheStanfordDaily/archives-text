LSAT/GMAT
The Program's courses for the October 3
LSAT and October 17 GMAT start on August
5 (LSAT) and August 12 (GMAT) and meet
weekly up to the exam dates.
All curricular preparation, instruction, and feedback are
offered by a former Stanford faculty member who is a
Stanford J.D. and a practicing attorney who has prepared
members of the Stanford community since 1978 for these exams.
Medians for Stanford related persons in the past year are above the 90th
percentile in the LSAT and above the 95th percentile in the GMAT. Enrollment
limited to 10 students (LSAT) and 12 students (GMAT), and the fee is $550.
