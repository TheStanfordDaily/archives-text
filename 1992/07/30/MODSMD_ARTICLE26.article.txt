# Frosh hit the Farm
## 
### 
The Cardinal's highly touted class of frosh
football recruits starts practice today under
the watchful eye of Assistant Head Coach/Of
fensive Coordinator Terry Shea. Shea was
the head coach for San Jose State during the
past two seasons. The recruits will be running
through drills this afternoon at 4 p.m. and
Friday and Saturday at 10 a.m. and 4 p.m.
Stanford Head Coach Bill Walsh is currently
in Barcelona as a guest of NBC Sports, but he
is expected back on the Farm on Saturday.
