# Federalists sponsor Scalia speech
## 
### Howard Libit Editorial staff 
The Stanford Federalist Society has attracted anoth
er U.S. Supreme Court Justice to speak on campus.
Justice Antonin Scalia is scheduled to give a talk
entitled "Legislative History" at 2 p.m. tomorrow in
Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
Although the speech is intended primarily for
students at the law school, it is open to the public.

Scalia's speech is expected to last a little more
than 30 minutes, with a question-and-answer ses
sion to follow, according to Ted Fikre of the society.
Last November, the group brought Justice Antho
ny Kennedy to campus. Kennedy discussed the rela
tionship between the Supreme Court and Congress
before a packed audience in Kresge Auditorium.
Justice Sandra Day O'Connor spoke on campus in
Oct. 1988. Both O'Connor and Kennedy are alumni
of Stanford.
