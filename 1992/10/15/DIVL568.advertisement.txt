Self Defense
for Women
Weekend Workshops
Workshop will include ways to defend yourself with your body. mind, and voice through
physical self-defense technique training, assertiveness role plays, and candid discussion
of issues of sexual abuse and violence. All workshops are open to women of all ages and
all degrees of physical fitness, and are taught by women instructors. Workshop will be
offered eighteen times during fall quarter. Attend one workshop only.
Dates &
Times:
Place:
Cost:
Classes offered from 10:00 a- 1:30 am:
Saturdays: Oct 17. Oct 24. Oct 31. Nov 7. Nov 14. Nov 21
Sundays: Oct 18. Oct 25, Nov 1. Nov 8. Nov 15. Nov 22
Classes offered from 2:oopm-s:3opm:
Saturdays: Oct 24. Nov 7. Nov 21
Sundays: Oct 18. Nov 1, Nov 15
Stanford campus: Breer Library. 672 Lomita Court.
Take Campus Drive to Santa Teresa Street, turn right on Lomita Drive pass the
Knoll, fork rtght around the bend, the first building is Breer Library
$12 workshop fee for Stanford students, faculty, spouses, or partners.
$18 workshop fee for the general public.
Scholarships are available: if Interested contact Caroline Mitchell (725-8891).
Registration: Purchase a ticket In advance at the Tresidder Ticket Office.
The Ticket Office is located on the first floor of Stanford's Tresidder Union, next to
the Information booth. It ts open Monday through Friday. 10:00 a m to 5:00 p.m
and Saturday. 12:00 p.m. to 4.00 p.m. Call 725-ARTS for ticket Information
Enrollment: Maximum enrollment is 12 women: minimum enrollment is 6 women.
If the minimum enrollment for a workshop is not met. the workshop may be can
celled. Tickets would be refunded.
Questions: If you have other questions about the workshop, please call 725-8891.
