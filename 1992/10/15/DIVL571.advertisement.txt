Student Organization
Registration Deadlines
Attention Voluntary Student Organizations,
House Governmenets, Fraternities, and
Sororities'.
Your organization must be registered with the
Office of Student Activities by this Friday,
October 16, in order to be listed in this year's
Stanford Directory.
The final deadline for registration for all
organizations for the fall quarter is October 30.
For information or forms, contact the
Office of Student Activities, 2nd floor
Tresidder, 723-3542.
