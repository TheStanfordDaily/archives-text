SATURDAY — 12:30 p.m. at Washington.
RECORDS — Stanford is 6-2 overall, 3-1 in the Pac-10 and is ranked
No. 15 in the latest AP poll. Washington is 7-0 overall, 4-0 in the Pac
-10 and is ranked No. 2.
