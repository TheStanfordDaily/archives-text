# Football
## 
### 
SATURDAY — 3:30 p.m. at Stanford Stadium.
RECORDS — Stanford is 6-3 overall, 3-2 in the Pac-10
and is ranked No. 21 in the latest AP poll. USC is 5-1-
1 overall, 4-1 in the Pac-10 and is ranked No. 11.
TELEVISION AND RADIO -Prime Network will televise
the postponed game (originally scheduled at 12:30)
live to the western region. KZSU (90.1 FM) will also
broadcast the contest with the jocose Jamie Zani
novich on play-by-play and scintillating Scott Walker
on color. Barry Tompkins and Bob Murphy will call the
game for KFRC (610 AM).
GAINES HONORED -1992 Stanford graduate Chryste
Gaines will be honored in on-feld halftime ceremonies
Saturday. Gaines, a three-time Pac-10 Champion and
winner of last year's NCAA title in the 100 meters, will
be presented the prestigious Jumbo Elliott Award.
