HARVARD UNIVERSITY* S
JOHN F. KENNEDY
SCHOOL OF GOVERNMENT
is looking for future leaders in Public Affairs. We
offer a 2-year Master's program in Public Policy,
with concentrations offered in:
• Criminal Justice
• Energy & Environmental Policy
• Government & Business
• Health Policy
• Housing 8c Community Development
• Human Services, Labor & Education
• International Affairs & Security
• International Development
• International Trade & Finance
• Press & Politics
• Science & Technology
• Transportation
• Urban Economic Development
Interested? Then come meet with the Kennedy
School Representative who will be on your
campus on:
DATE: Monday, November 16,1992
TIME: 10:00 a.m. group session
LOCATION: Please contact the Student
Counseling/Career office for
this information.
