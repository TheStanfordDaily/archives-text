McKinsey & Company, Inc.
Invites
Juniors and Seniors
to a
Presentation and Reception
to learn more about our 2-year
Business Analyst Program
Includes discussion on how to approach case interviews
Wednesday, November 18,1992
at the Oak West Lounge of Tresidder Union
from 7:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.
We look forward to seeing you there!
McKinsey & Co. is an international strategic management consulting firm with 54 offices in 26 countries around the world.
We serve mostly Fortune and International 500 company top executives on issues of strategic importance. Our mission is
twofold: to help clients make substantial and lasting improvements in their performance, and to build a firm that is able to
attract, develop, excite, and retain exceptional people.
Interviews will be held on January 27, 28,29,1993.
Interested candidates should submit their resumes
along with a transcript and SAT/GMAT/LSAT
scores by January 12,1993 to the Career Planning
and Placement Center.
