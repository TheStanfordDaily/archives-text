# Feast Your Ears on Music
## 
### 
If you like Handel but don't want to sing
along, go sit back in Mem Chu and let the
Stanford Symphonic Chorus entertain you
with Handel's "Alexander's Feast" at 8 p.m.
Friday, Dec. 4. Cast as an oratorio, "Alexan
der's Feast" is actually an ode in celebration
of St. Cecilia, the patron saint of music and
supposed inventor of the pipe organ.
Tickets cost $7 ($4 for students), and
are available at the door.
— Michael Green
