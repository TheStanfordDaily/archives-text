# Selected recommendations from the Final Report of the Women's Needs Assessment Study Group

nity centers to work more
comprehensively and
effectively with women of
color and with the identifi
cation of concerns specific

and implementation of
ongoing self-help, skills
acquisition workshops
which focus on women's
assertiveness, self-esteem,
self-advocacy (specifically
in the university environ
ment), stress management
and general support.

dents to enhance sensitivi
