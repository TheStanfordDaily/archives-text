STANFORD HOUSING CENTER
JOB OPPORTUNITIES
Spring Quarter
■ Housing Assistants: Provide clerical support for the
Undergraduate Draw, Graduate Lottery, and Summer
Housing Allocation.
Spring & Summer Quarters
■ New Undergraduate Student Information Coordinator:
Makes freshman and transfer housing, freshman
roommate, CIV, and Freshman English assignments.
Spring, Summer & Long Term:
R Rental Office:
Assist students, faculty & staff with off-campus housing needs. 13
Interested students should complete an employment application at
the Stanford Housing Center, Old Union 110, by February 12th. 'JjJ
