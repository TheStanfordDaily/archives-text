# For the Record
## 
### 
In the Feb. 5 article on cut
backs in student services, the
Financial Aids Office hours were
misstated. Advisers' on-call hours
on Tuesdays and Wednesdays are
9:30-11 a.m. However, on Thurs
days and Fridays, the advisers'
hours are 1-4 p.m. The Financial
Aids Office information window
is open from 9-12 and 1-4 Mon
days through Fridays.
The Feb. 5 article on the SEAS
environmental handbook stated
that Shannon Snyder could not
explain why some single rooms
received two handbooks. Those
rooms received two copies by acci
dent, Snyder said. Distributors
were explicitly instructed to leave
only one book per person.
