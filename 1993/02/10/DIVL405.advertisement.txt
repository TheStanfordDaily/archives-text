LAWSON INADA is one of America >1 finest poets and educators. The
recipient of two National Endowment for the Arts fellowships, Lawson has
' spoken at everywhere from Harvard to UC Berkeley to The White House. A
popular educational consultant and an English Professor at Southern Oregon
State college, Lawson Was awarded an "Excellence in teaching Award" by
Oregon Board of Education. In the '70s, he led an Asian American literary
movement that paved the way for Amy Tan, Maxine Hong Kingston, &
David Henry Hwang.
