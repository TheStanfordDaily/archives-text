# Women's Basketball
## 
### 
TONIGHT — at California at 7:30 pm at Berkeley's Harmon Gym.
RECORDS — Stanford is 17-4 overall, 8-2 in the Pacific 10 Con
ference and is ranked No. 8. California (13-5, 5-4 in the Pac-10)
and is ranked No. 25.
RADIO — KZSU (90.1 FM) will broadcast the contests with Jamie
Zaninovich doing play-by-play and Scott Walker providing
color commentary. Also, don't miss KZSU's look at the history
of the Stanford-Cal women's hoops rivalry before the contest at
7 p.m. and "Overtime" with Noah Blake, Scott Walker and Scott
Reiss immediately following the game.
