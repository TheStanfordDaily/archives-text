# Women's Basketball at UCLA, Connecticut
## 
### 
TONIGHT AND SATURDAY — Tonight at 7:00 p.m. at UCLA, Saturday at 6:00 p.m. PST at Con
necticut.
RECORDS — Stanford is 17-5 overall, 8-3 in the Pac-10 and is ranked No. 10 in the AP poll. UCLA
is 10-10 overall, 5-6 in Pac-10 play.
TV—The Connecticut game will be nationally televised on CBS, with Tim Ryan handling play-by
play and Ann Meyers on color commentary.
RADIO—KZSU (90.1 FM) will broadcast the contests with Jamie Zaninovich doing play-by-play.
NOTES—Stanford has prevailed in its last nine meetings with the Bruins, winning 79-70 at home
earlier this year.. .Stanford and Connecticut's only matchup occurred in Dec. '88, with Stanford
winning 72-53.
