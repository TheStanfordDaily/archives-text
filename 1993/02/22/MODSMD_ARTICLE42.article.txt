# For the record
## 
### 
The March 2 deadline for Cal
Grant applications mentioned in
the Feb. 19 issue of the Daily
applies only to first-time appli
cants or previous applicants who
did not receive a grant for the
current academic year. The dead
line for students who currently
receive a Cal Grant and want to
apply for financial aid next year is
April 26.
In addition, the reauthoriza
tion of the Higher Education Act
does not include provisions allow
ing the government to make
direct loans to students, as stated
in the article.
