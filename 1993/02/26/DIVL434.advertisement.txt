Synapse Productions presents:
"The Meeting"
A dramatization of a hypothetical meeting of Martin
Luther King, Jr. and Malcolm X in a Harlem Motel. The
play explores the personalities and philosophies of the
two men who led and came to represent the African-
American community's struggle for justice and equality.
