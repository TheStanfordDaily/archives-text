## World premiere ballet 
### 
No, it's not a band of Hasidic Jews doing the
horah.
The Lar Lubovitch Dance Company is world
renowned troupe comprised of five male and five
female dancers. Their style links modern dance to
classical ballet.
March 6 through 8 they will perform their pre
mier works in Memorial Auditorium. The perfor
mance will include the World Premiere of "Wrong
Face" and "Fandango."
Ticket prices range from $13 to $22 and are avail
able through Tresidder Ticket Office.
