# Adv. 94 Page 22
## 
### 
Undergraduates!
Interested in pursuing
an academic research project
in Japan this summer?
Apply for the
Ai Isayama
Memorial Scholarship
Established by members and supporters of the Asia Pacific Research
Center, it is made available through the major grant program of
Undergraduate Research Opportunities (URO).
Deadlines: April 2nd for major grants in natural, physical, and
social sciences and engineering; April 23rd for major grants in
humanities and creative arts.
In addition to this special fund, URO offers many other grants to
students on campus or around the world. Small grant proposals
on any academic research topic are due April 23rd. Applications
and information are available at URO, 122 Sweet Hall or call
723-3828.
