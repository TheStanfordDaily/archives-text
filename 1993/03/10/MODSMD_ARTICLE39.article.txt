# Women's Tennis
## 
### 
OVER BREAK - Mar. 22 at 1:30 p.m. at Stanford Tennis
Stadium vs. UC-Santa Barbara, Mar. 24 at 3 p.m. at
Florida, and Mar. 25 at 11:30 a.m. at Tennessee.
RECORDS — Stanford is 14-0 overall, 5-0 in the Pac-10
and is ranked No. 1. Florida is 5-1 overall, and is ranked
No. 2. Tennessee is 5-5 overall, and ranked No. 15.
NOTES — Stanford defeated Florida on Feb. 28 to take
over the top spot in the nation, and the Gators will be
seeking revenge in Gainesville... The new Intercolle
giate Tennis Association rankings place Laxmi Poruri at
No. 2 nationally, followed by Heather Willens at No. 4.,
Kim Shasby at No. 11, Kristine Kurth at No. 30, Beth
Berris at No. 47 and Amy Chiminello at No. 72.
