# Margaret Thatcher visits Hoover
## 
### 

Photos by Rajiv Chandrasekaran — Daily


Former British Prime Minister
Margaret Thatcher views a dis
play of material documenting
Russian-American economic
relations at the Hoover Institu
tion on Monday. During her
tour of the recently opened
exhibit, she talked with Hoover
archivist Anne Van Capip
(above). Earlier that day,
Thatcher spoke to Hooyer
scholars and University offi
cials, including former Secre
tary of State George Shiiltz
(left) and University President
Gerhard Casper, during an off
the-record breakfast speech.
On a 12-day tour of the United
States, the former Tory-party
leader talked about world
crises, NATO and the Euro
pean Community.
