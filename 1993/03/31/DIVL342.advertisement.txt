Got something on your mind?
Write The Daily a letter!
The Daily welcomes letters from its readers. Letters will be
published at the editor's discretion and are subject to editing. All
letters must include the author's name, phone number and Stan
ford affiliation, if any. Submissions with more than five signa
tures must designate a representative.
Letters should be turned in on a Macintosh disk in
Microsoft Word format with a hard copy of the submis
sion. Drop them at The Daily, ID Mail #2240.
