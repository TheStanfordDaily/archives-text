THE DAILY
The Stanford Daily is looking for a motivated and reliable student to serve as Credit Manager for
spring quarter.
Job duties include
• Maintaining Accounts Receivable
• Collecting outstanding debts
• Approving new accounts
• Assigning credit limits
The Stanford Dally Is one of only five Independent and Incorporated University newspapers in the country. The Daily has won numerous
national and regional awards for |oumallstic excellence, and is well-known for the exceptional caliber of its employees At The Dally, the
Credit Manager plays an integral role in the sound financial management of The Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation. Interested individuals
should have strong quantitative and computer abilities, possess excellent Interpersonal skills, and be detail-oriented
A commitment of 20-25 hours per week is required.
The position offers great experience and pays competitively.
For more Information, contact
Rick Wolfgram
Business Manager
725-8232
Please call by April 5, 1993.

THE STANFORD DAILY invites
all interested candidates to apply for the position of BUSINESS MANAGER for the 1993-94 school year.
Responsibilities include:
• Bottom-line performance of The Stanford
Daily Publishing Corporation, with
revenues of over $1.0 million.
• Hiring and management of a 20-person
Business Department.
• Management of growing assets, revenues, and expenses.
The position runs from June 16, 1993 to June 15, 1994. Forty hours per week commitment over the summer
and academic year are required. General business management experience is preferred, but not required. All
interested candidates are encouraged to apply. For more information, contact:
Rick Wolfgram
THE STANFORD DAILY
Storke Publications Building
Stanford, CA 94305
Application Deadline: Friday, April 2. Interviews will be held the following week.Applications available at
The Daily office now.
