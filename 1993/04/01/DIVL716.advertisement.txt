Traveling this summer?
If you are planning a trip outside the
US, Canada, or Western Europe over
the summer, you should visit the
Cowell Travel Clinic.
Vaccinations are recommended for
travel to many countries. Yellow
Fever vaccine is required for travel to
some Central and South American
countries, as well as to central Africa.
Visits to the Travel Clinic should be
made 4-6 weeks in advance of
your trip.
Students, faculty, staff, alumni,
spouses, and friends of Stanford are
eligible for care in the Travel Clinic.
Call early for an appointment:
725-1378
