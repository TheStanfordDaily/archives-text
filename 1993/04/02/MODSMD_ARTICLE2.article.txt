# Canción del corazón
## 
### 

Burt Herman — Daily
Renowned Latin American singer and songwriter Gabino Palomares performed for a crowd at Casa
Zapata yesterday evening. Palomares is known as a pioneer of the popular music movement called
La Nueva Cancion, or the new song.
