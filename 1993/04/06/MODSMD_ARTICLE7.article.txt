# For the Record
## 
### 
A story in yesterday's
Daily reported that "The
Game" is an annual event. It
is actually a periodic event.
In addition, the article
implied the event is regularly
known as RAT RACE. RAT
RACE was the title of the most
recent episode of "The Game."

It is The Daily's policy to cor
rect, in a timely manner, all
significant errors that are
brought to the editors' atten
tion. If you believe that we
have made such an error,
please contact the appropriate
section editor, managing edi
tor or the editor in chief at
723-4632, or via mail.
