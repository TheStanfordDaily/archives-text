# Letters Policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes letters
from its readers. Letters will be
published at the editor's discre
tion and are subject to editing. All
letters must include the author's
name, phone number and Stan
ford affiliation, if any. Submis
sions with more than five signa
tures must designate a represen
tative.
Letters should be turned in on
a Macintosh disk with a paper
copy of the submission.
Submissions are published on a
space-available basis at the discre
tion of the Opinions editors.
