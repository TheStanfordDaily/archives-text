STANFORD
ALUMNI
ASSOCIATION
Wanted:
Good Ideas
The Stanford Alumni Association announces the
1993 Robert L. Pierce Award. This $1500 cash grant
will go to an innovative student project that serves
the Stanford community. Last year's winner was
Hip Hop Night. Brine us your best idea, and we
just might make your aream come true.
Applications available at Bowman Alumni House.
Due: April 30,1993
For more information call Cindy Pearson at 725-0693.
FRATERNITY RUSH
Fraternity Rush is April 12-26 and is open to all interested
students. Check out the open houses at the locations listed below,
meet interesting people, eat great food, and find out what the Greek
System has to offer you. Stop by the Office of Student Activities
anytime for a free Rush Book with more information and a description
of each fraternity.
The Ravenswood Teoois/luttering Program
Tennis Anyone?
©
CJ

Stanford Volunteers.
Minimum tennis skill required
