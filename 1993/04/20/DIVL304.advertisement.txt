HARRY S. TRUMAN
PUBLIC SERVICE SCHOLARSHIPS
Students who will be juniors in 1993-94 and are committed to careers in the
government service, public-interest organizations, or public-service non
profit organizations may apply to be nominated by Stanford University for
a Harry S. Truman Scholarship. Truman Scholarships cover tuition, fees,
books and room and board up to $3,000 for the senior year and $27,000 for
two or three years of graduate study Approximately 85 scholarships will be
awarded nationally
An informational meeting will be held Friday, April 23, at 4 p.m. in the
Forum Room, Meyer Library
If you are unable to attend the meeting, contact Elizabeth Rafferty,
Room 161G, Building 160.
