Undergraduate Advising Center
Are You Interested in an
Individually Designed Major?
Come in to The Undergraduate Advising Center (UAC) and meet with the
Program Coordinator to discuss your interests. The following rules apply:
• Each student submitting a new proposal must have at least three (3)
lull quarters' undergraduate work remaining at Stanford.
• Each major shall consist of at least 60 units, at or above the 100 level.
• A maximum of IS of these 60 units may be taken as pass/no credit.
• A maximum of S of these 60 units may be taken in individual study of
directed reading.
• The proposed major must constitute a coherent program that achieves
a clear academic goal.
• The proposed major must he comparable ui quality and academic
rigor to degrees obtained in other degree-granting programs offered.
• The proposed major cannot duplicate or be achievable through a
major offered by another degree-granting program or department
• Students must present evidence that demonstrates their ability to do
independent work.
WINTER QUARTER DEADLINE:
Wednesday, May 12, 1993

in Sweet Hull. Ist Floor, or phone 72J-2426 for more information.
