THOUSAND PIECES OF COLD
WITH KENJI YAMAMOTO
THURSDAY, MAY 6,1993,5:50 PM
THOUSAND PIECES OF COLD is a film
often compared to DANCES WITH WOLVES
as one of the great untold stories of America's
West. Filmmaker / Editor / Producer KENJI
YAMAMOTO captures an inspiring story of
the struggles of Lalu, a Chinese woman who
was sold into slavery by her father, learns to
confront racism, and finds self-reliance and
love in a harsh alien culture during the
Goldrush. This is a true storv based on
Ruthann Lum McCunn's 1981 novel. Join us
for a dinner viewing and informal talk with
Mr. Yamamoto.
