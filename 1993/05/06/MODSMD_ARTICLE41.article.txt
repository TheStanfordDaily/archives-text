# out'n about
## More samples 
### 
Remember the good time you had at
the Samples concert in Frost a while back?
Well, guess who you can sample again,
only this time along with several other
great artists like Shawn Colvin and Blues
Traveler?
These performers and The Samples
will play at the Laguna Seca Daze in Mon
terey Saturday, May 29. Phish, the Jeff
Healey Band and the Allman Brothers
also perform that day. And on Sunday,
10,000 Maniacs and Big Head Todd & the
Monsters join the line-up.
Music starts at 11 a.m. and tickets are
available at BASS. Call 974-6726 for more
information.
