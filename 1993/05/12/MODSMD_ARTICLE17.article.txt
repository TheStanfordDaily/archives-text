# Letters Policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes letters from
its readers. Letters will be are sub
ject to editing.
All letters must include the
author's name, phone number and
Stanford affiliation, if any. Submis
sions with more than five signatures
must designate a representative.
Letters should be turned in
Microsoft Word format with a paper
copy of the submission.
