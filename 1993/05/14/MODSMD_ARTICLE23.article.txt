# Men's Tennis NCAA Team Championships
## 
### 
TOMORROW — 12 noon (EDT) at Athens,
Ga. vs. winner of today's New Mexico-
Colorado match.
NCAA NOTES — If the fourth-seeded,
defending national champion Cardinal
wins Saturday, it will face the winner of the
Texas-Tennessee match on Sunday at
noon. Monday's semifinal at 2 p.m. would
probably feature a showdown between
Stanford and top-seeded USC. Stanford
has won 12 NCAA titles in 20 years, and five
of those in the last seven years. The team
finals will be held Tuesday, May 18. Individ
ual competition will begin the following day.
