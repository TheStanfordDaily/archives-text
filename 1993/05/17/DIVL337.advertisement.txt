ANNOUNCING INTERNSHIPS IN
SOFTWARE ENGINEERING AND
PRODUCT MARKETING
SUMMER INTERNSHIPS
Banner Blue is seeking prospective graduates with EE, CS, or CSE majors for an
internship in Software Engineering. We are also seeking prospective graduates
with majors in Business or Liberal Arts for an internship in Product Marketing.
We want students who are bright, motivated and interested in working
for a high technology company.
BANNER BLUE AT A GLANCE
Do you value traditional principles of hard work and
commitment? Banner Blue does. We reward these
principles with competitive salaries, high levels of
responsibility, promotions from within, and an
excellent benefits package. Banner Blue Software
develops and markets unique software products for
IBM and Macintosh personal computers. We design
and develop high-quality, leading-edge products that
bridge the gap between technology and society. Our
flagship product. Org Plus, was introduced In June
of 1985, and Is now considered a standard In the
business world.
CHALLENGING INTERNSHIP IN ENGINEERING
The Ideal candidate will have an excellent academic
record, an Interest In programming end-user
software, and familiarity with programming In C,
DOS and/or Windows experience Is an added plus.
UNIQUE INTERNSHIP IN MARKETING
The Ideal candidate will have an excellent academic
record and zero to two years of experience In the
field of marketing. Candidate must have excellent
written, communication, and analytical skills.
THE POSmON
You will be working In the Engineering Department
significantly contributing to the design of new
products and/or the upgrade of current products.
Past Interns have experienced national recognition
for their contributions. Tor example, a portion
of our product, Uncle Sam's Budget Balancer, was
coded by an Intern last summer. Reviews of that
product were written up In the Wall Street Journal,
Business Week, and the San Jose Mercury news.
CNN did a feature Interview with Banner Blue's
President giving the product national coverage.
This Is an excellent opportunity for ambitious
students who want 'real life" experience In the
software Industry.
THE POSITION
You will be working In the Product Marketing
Department, responsible for collecting market
feedback, conducting competitive analyses, and
geographically evaluating our sales and distribution
channels. Additionally, you will cooperate with
other members of the Product Marketing
Department on various marketing projects. This Is
an excellent opportunity for a well-rounded
"Marketing Thinker" who can develop a passion for
his/her product.
BEGINNING YOUR CAREER AT BANNER BLUE
At Banner Blue, you will experience all the
advantages of a small company with few of the
risks. We have been profitable every year of our
existence. Sound budgeting practices allow us to
self-finance Instead of borrowing money. We hire
skilled, motivated people, and we keep that talent
by training and promoting from within.
BENEFITS AND REWARDS
• Competitive Salaries
• Sr. Level Programmers as Mentors
• Unique "Real Life" Work Experience
• Friendly Office Environment
To apply for on-site Interviews send a resume and
cover letter to:
Banner Blue Software
Attn: Recruiting Coordinator
39500 Stevenson Place, Suite 204
Fremont, CA 94539
FAX 510-794-9152
BANNER BLUE WILL BE ON CAMPUS FOR JOB
CONNECTION DAY THURSDAY, MAY 20TH.
