# Rags to Riches
## 
### 
"The Prince & The Pauper," a new Broad
way-bound musical based on Mark Twain's
classic tale of mistaken identity receives its first
workshop production at The Nitery.
The musical is being produced by Stanford
alumna Simone Genatt, Class of '88. Led by
veteran-director Michael Montel, the diverse
cast of 17 includes 15 Stanford students, along
with two local actors who play Edward, Price of
Wales and pauper Tom Canty.
Performances are at 8 p.m. tomorrow and
Saturday and May 26 through 29, with a 2:30
p.m. matinee this Sunday.
