# Fade to black
## 
### 
This Monday, Stanford's new black film col
lective hosts "Cinema in Black," a film festival
displaying the works of Stanford students as
well as local filmmakers.
Attendees will not only watch these films, as
they will be given the opportunity to discuss
each work with its respective directors, produc
ers or actors. Also to be screened is the pilot for
"South Central," a television series dealing with
life in Los Angeles.
"Cinema in Black" will be held in Rm. 2 of
the History Corner from 2 p.m. to 5 p.m.
