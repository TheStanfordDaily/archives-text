# Got something on your mind?
## 
### 
The Weekly welcomes letters
from its readers. Letters will be
published at the editor's discre- |

tion and are subject to editing.

All letters must include the

author's name, phone
number and Stanford
affiliation, if any. Sub-

missions with more
than five signatures
must designate a
representative.

Letters should be turned in on a Macintosh disk in
Microsoft Word format with a hard copy of the submis
sion, or can be entered on computer through the Storke
server. Drop them at The Weekly ID Mail #2240.
