# Letters Policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes let
ters from its readers. Letters
will be published at the edi
tor's discretion and are sub
ject to editing.
All letters must include
the author's name, phone
number and Stanford affilia
tion, if any. Submissions
with more than five signa
tures must designate a rep
resentative.
Letters should be turned
in on a Macintosh disk in Mi
crosoft Word format with a
paper copy of the submis
sion.
Submissions are pub
lished on a space-available
basis at the discretion of the
Opinions editors.
