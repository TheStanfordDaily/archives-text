STANFORD RUGBY
Be a part of history
A tradition of excellence since 1896.
Stanford Rugby 1991-1992 Summer Tour
Throughout its illustrious 96 year history, Stanford University's Rugby
Football Club has won more national championships than any other collegiate
team. Join the International S.U.R.F. Club and tour the world. Open to all
Graduate and Undergraduate students. Season highlights include:
• A sixteen game schedule for all sides, accommodating all
skill levels
• Annual Tour — New Zealand, Daytona Beach, the Bahamas
(1987), Hawaii (1989,1991 & 1993), Australia (1990),. France, (1992),
and proposed tour of New Zealand/Australia for 1994
• The Twenty-fourth annual Stanford Tournament, the largest
preseason tournament in the country
