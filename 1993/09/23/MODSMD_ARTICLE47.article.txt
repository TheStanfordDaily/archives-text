# Tips for the clueless
## 
### 
Being a freshman is much
like being a gopher on a large,
landlocked version of the Love
Boat. Some simple tips to get
you through when those help
ful quotes from Approaching
Stanford fail you:
• Turning your sweat
shirts inside out effectively
doubles your wardrobe.
• Pass left, keep right.
• Punch at frat parties
isn't really made with 10 per
cent fruit juice.
• Long sweater, big ass.
• Baggy shorts, small
penis.
• When having sex while
your roommate is in the
room, try to include him or
her in the conversation.

• With a little ingenuity, a
friend at Kinko's, and a laser
printer, Flicks passes can be
made "wholesale."
• "Free Champagne
Brunch" at the KA house
should seem fishy.
• Calls to break up with
your high school sweetheart
are cheaper after 11.
• Make-up is reserved for
Halloween and Purim.
• Gilgamesh dies at the
end.
• When the weather is
right, a small "garden" out
side your window can help
pay your tuition.
• You're in a dorm: their
shampoo, your shampoo—
what's the difference?
