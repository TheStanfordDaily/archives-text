# out'n about
## Limpin' comedy 
### 
When a bunch of smooth
criminals decide to produce a
Broadway play, the results crush
the funny bone.
Actor Danny Aiello ("Do the
Right Thing," "Moonstruck")
takes the stage in the touring Tom
Dulack Broadway comedy "Break
ing Legs." The show runs through
Oct. 3 at the Curran Theatre in
San Francisco.
For information about show
times and ticket prices, call the
Golden Gate Theater box office
(also in The City) at 776-1999 or
Bass-TM-Tickets at 510-762-2277.
