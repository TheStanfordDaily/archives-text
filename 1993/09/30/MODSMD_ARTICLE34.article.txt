# Preview of a revue
## 
### 
Palo Alto Theatre Works season is opening Saturday, Oct. 2
with the West Coast premiere of the imaginative musical revue
"Sparks." Described as a passionate journey of the spirit told
through uplifting song, Sparks will continue through Oct. 30.
Tickets are $14 and available through the CAPA Box Office at
329-2623.
