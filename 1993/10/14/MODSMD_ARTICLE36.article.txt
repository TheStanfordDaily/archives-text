## He's got soul 
### 
Peter White, a contemporary
jazz guitarist, will play selections
from his latest album, "Prome
nade," at 8 p.m. Friday, Oct. 15
at the Mountain View Center for
the Performing Arts Main Stage.
White, who was influenced by
folk artists such as Joni Mitchell
and James Taylor, describes his
third effort as a more soulful
album than his previous albums.
Tickets for his show are $22
and are available at the Moun
tain View Center for the Per
forming Arts and at BASS out
lets. Call (408)-762-BASS.
