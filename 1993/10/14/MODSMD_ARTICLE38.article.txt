# String me along
## 
### 
An award-winning string quartet comes to Stanford on
Oct. 13 in the form of Eugene Drucker, Philip Setzer,
Lawrence Dutton and David Finckel.
The Grammy award-winning Emerson String Quartet
are acclaimed for their ability to perform difficult material
like Ives and Shostakovich. Tickets are $25 and $21 (stu
dents receive a $3 discount) and are available at the Tresid
der Ticket Office or by calling 497-ARTS.
