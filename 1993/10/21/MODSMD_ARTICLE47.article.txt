# Seattle sound
## 
### 
The Stanford String Quartet
performs a multi-media work from
multiple composers in Dinkelspiel
Auditorium Oct. 24 at 2:30 p.m.
"Circle of Faith — the Words of
Seattle" is based on the speech the
Suquamish leader, Chief Seattle,
delivered to the first governor of
Washington Territory in 1854. The
musicians play, accompanied by
the Eagle Child Singers performing
traditional Native American music
and a narrator reading aloud the
words of Seattle.
Tickets for the performance,
which is sponsored by The Lively
Arts at Stanford, are $18-20 (stu
dents receive a $3 discount) and are
available at the Tresidder Ticket
Office and at BASS Ticket Outlets.
