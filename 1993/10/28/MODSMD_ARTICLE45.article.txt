# Singing sensations
## 
### 
Thirty years ago the first a cappella group was founded
on the Farm. Today, the ever-popular Stanford Mendicants
are hosting a reunion of former crooners.
The Mendicants will celebrate with other Stanford a cap
pella groups in Dinkelspiel Auditorium at their 30th Anniver
sary Reunion Concert, Oct. 29 at 7:30 p.m. Tickets for the
concert are on sale now in Tresidder Union in White Plaza.
Prices are $5 for students and $10 for the general public.
