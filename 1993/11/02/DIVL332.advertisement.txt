# Adv. 17 Page 5
##

McKinsey & Company, Inc.
Invites
Juniors and Seniors
to a
Presentation and Reception
to learn more about our 2-year
Business Analyst Program
Tuesday, November 2, 1993
at the Oak West Lounge of Tresidder Union
from 7:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.
We look forward to seeing you there!
McKinsey & Company, Inc., is an international strategic management consulting firm with 58 offices in 28 countries around the
world. We serve mostly Fortune and International 500 company top executives on issues of strategic importance. Our mission is
twofold: to help clients make substantial and lasting improvements in their performance, and to build a firm that is able to attract,
develop, excite, and retain exceptional people.
Interviews will be held on January 26, 27, 28, 1994.
Interested candidates should submit their
resumes along with a transcript and
SAT/GMAT/LSAT scores by January 10, 1994,
to the Career Planning and Placement Center.
