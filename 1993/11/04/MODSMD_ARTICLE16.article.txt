# Letters Policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes letters from
its readers. Letters will be published
at the editor's discretion and are sub
ject to editing.
All letters must include the author's
name, phone number and Stanford
affiliation, if any.
Submissions with more than five sig
natures must designate a representative.
letters should be turned in on a Mac
intosh disk in Microsoft Word format
with a paper copy of the submission, or
E-mailed to opsCodaily.stanford.edu
Submissions are published on a
space-available basis at the discretion
of the Opinions editors.
Disks should have the owner's
name written clearly on the front and
may be picked up two business days
afler they have been dropped off.
