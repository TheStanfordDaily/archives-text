Luce Fellowship Deadline Extended!
The deadline for Applications for the Luce
Fellowship has been extended to November 19,
1993. The Luce Fellowship offers an experience
in Asia designed to sharpen perceptions of Asia,
and broaden understanding between the United
States and Asian Countries. Eligible applicants
are graduating seniors, recent alumni, graduate
or professional students, and junior faculty who
do not have extensive experience in Asia and
have no professed career interest in Asian
Affairs. If you have any questions or would like
an application, please contact:
Hena Borneo
Institute for International Studies
