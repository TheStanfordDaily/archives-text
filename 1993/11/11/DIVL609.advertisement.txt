The Institute for Research on Women and Gender
Presents
"California Women's Health Day"
Tresidder Union, Oak West and East Rooms
Friday, November 12, 1993
8:30 a.m.-2:00 p.m.
Speakers will include: Iris F. Litt, M.D., Kate O'Hanlan, M.D., and
Marjorie Hansen Shaevitz. At 12:30 p.m. there will be a screening of the
film, "AIDS and Women: The Greatest Gamble."
The purpose of this day is to examine the unmet health needs of women in
California and to promote awareness of these unmet needs across the state
through a variety of speakers, information booths, and activities.
Governor Pete Wilson has agreed to issue a proclamation through the
Institute to establish November 12th as California Women's Health Day.
Please join us for this special day! Free and open to the public
For more information, contact Pam Mosher, Project Coordinator, at (415) 723-1995
