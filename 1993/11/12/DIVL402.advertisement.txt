Masters, Ph.D. students and Post Docs in Engineering and
Economics who are seeking full-time Associate positions in

analytic and quantitative skills, strong communications

collaboratively in a team environment. Candidates should
also have the ability to speak, read, and write with complete
fluency in either Cantonese or Mandarin.

presentation and at the Career Planning and Placement Center.
