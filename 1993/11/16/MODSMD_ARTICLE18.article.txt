# Letters Policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes letters from
its readers. Letters will be pub
lished at the editor's discretion and
are subject to editing.
All letters must include the
author's name, phone number and
Stanford affiliation, if any. Sub
missions with more than five sig
natures must designate a repre
sentative.
Letters should be turned in on a
Macintosh disk in Microsoft Word for
mat with a paper copy of the submis
sion, or E-mail to ops(a>daily.stan
ford.edu.
