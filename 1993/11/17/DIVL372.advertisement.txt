EXCELLENT BUSINESS EXPERIENCE
The Stanford Daily is looking for
a motivated and reliable Credit Manager.
Job duties include:
• Maintaining Accounts Receivable
• Collecting outstanding debts
•Approving new accounts
• Assigning credit limits
•Managing circulation
Expected start date: January 3, 1994.
The Credit Manager plays an integral role in the sound financial
management of The Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation. The
Credit Manager will also be in charge of circulation of The Daily, and
will have managerial responsibility over 5 newspaper distributors.
Interested individuals should have strong quantitative abilities,
possess excellent interpersonal skills, and be detail-oriented.
Apple/Macintosh computer experience a must,
particularly Microsoft Excel and Claris FileMaker.
Accounting experience is strongly recommended.
A commitment of 25 hours per week is required, 40 hours preferred.
The position offers great experience and pays competitively.
A minimum 6 month commitment is expected.
Ideal for a senior graduating early or student spouse.
