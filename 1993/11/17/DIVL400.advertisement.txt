Associated Students of Stanford University
OPEN HOUSE
Thursday, November 18,1993
4:00 P.M. - 6:00 P.M.
Refreshments will be served.
Come meet the ASSU Financial and Administrative
Managers, the COP, the Senate, and the ASSU staff.
Find out about all that the ASSU brings Stanford:
Lecture Notes, SURE Escort, Fee Refunds, Speakers
Bureau, Concert Network, Legal Counseling,
Publications, Programs, Community Service,
Shirt Shak, and more!!!
