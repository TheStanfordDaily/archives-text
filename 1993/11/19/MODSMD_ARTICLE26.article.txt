# Football vs. California: The 96th Big Game
## 
### 
SATURDAY — 12:30 p.m. at Stanford Stadium.
RECORDS— Stanford (4-6, 2-5 Pacific 10
Conference); Cal (6-4, 3-4 Pac-10).
TELEVISION — Tape-delay Saturday night at
10:00 p.m. on Sports Channel.
RADIO - KZSU (90 1 FM) and KFRC (610 AM).
CARD NOTES — A win would give Stanford
three victories in its final four games ...
Injury update: Receiver Brian Manning
(pulled hamstring), outside linebacker Brian

Batson (right ankle fracture), and corner
back Vaughn Bryant (turf toe) are probable.
Outside linebacker John Sims (left knee
bruise) and outside linebacker Leon Vickers
(back) are questionable
BEAR BITS — Quarterback Dave Barr ranks
first in the Pac-10 in passing efficiency . . .
Cal still has bowl possibilities, depending on
the outcome of the Big Game and its Nov.
27 meeting with Hawaii.
