# High school hijinks
## 
### 
The Gunn High School Theatre Department hangs on to fall
for a while longer with its production of "The Cherry Orchard."
A play by Russian playwright Anton Chekhov, adapted by David
Mamet, "The Cherry Orchard" will be showing Dec. 2 through
Dec. 4 and Dec. 9 through Dec. 11 at 8 p.m. Tickets are $5 for the
night performances and S3 for the matinee, available at the
door. All performances take place at the Gunn High School Stu
dio Theatre, 780 Arastradero Rd., Palo Alto.


Courtesy of Gunn High School
