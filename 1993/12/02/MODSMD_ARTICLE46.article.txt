# Mixed-up months
## 
### 
Palo Alto Theatre Works has something different going on
this holiday season with its production of "Almost Septem
ber," a musical by David Schechter and Steven Lutvak. A tale
of childhood innocence and the inevitability of growing up,
"Almost September" runs from Dec.2 through Jan. 8 at the
Lucie Stern Theatre, 1305 Middlefield Rd., Palo Alto. Tickets
run from $12 to $24 and are available through theCAPA Box
Office at 329-2623.
