# A 						classic swing
## 
### 
The Stanford Symphony Orches
tra presents its fall concert on Sun
day, Dec. 5, with a program includ
ing Tchaikovsky's Symphony No. 4,
Benjamin Britten's Four Sea Inter
ludes from Peter Grimes and the
Trilogy by Wayne Peterson.
The fall concert is also the debut
of the Stanford Symphony Orches
tra's new conductor, Karla Lemon.
The symphony consists of 65 musi
cians from all parts of the Stanford
community, from freshmen to post
doctoral students.
The concert will take place on
Dec. 5 at 2:30 p.m. in Dinkelspiel
Auditorium. Tickets are $7 for gen
eral admission and $4 for students
and are available through the Tresid
der Ticket Office.
