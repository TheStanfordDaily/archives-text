# Women's Basketball vs. Tennessee
## 
### 
TONIGHT — 9 p.m. at Maples Pavilion.
RECORDS AND RANKINGS - Stanford (1-1, No 9 Associ
ated Press poll); Tennessee (2-0, No. 1 AP).
TIMELY TIDBITS — Stanford looks to extend a 34 game
home winning streak as Cardinal head coach Tara Van-
Derveer guns for her 350 th career victory. Stanford is
coming off a 59 48 victory over Utah in Salt Lake City,
while the Volunteers triumphed over Montana 82-66 in
their home opener last Sunday. Tennessee holds a 6-2

advantage in this series and won both encounters in
1992-93, but Stanford posted victories in the teams'
only two Maples meetings in 1989 and 1991.
RADIO - KZSU (90 1 FM) brings you the action if you are
not among the 7,500 people packed into Maples Marc
Oppenheimer calls the play-by-play and Scott Walker
provides color commentary
TELEVISION — ESPN carries the game live to a nation
wide audience.
