# Better off with the Blues
## 
### 
Blues legend Junior Wells, who collaborated with Buddy Guy
and the Rolling Stones, will open for Van Morrison in a series of
Bay Area performances. Morrison will perform at the Berkeley
Community Theater on Dec. 13 and 14 and at San Francisco's
Nob Hill Masonic Auditorium Dec. 17 through 19. These con
certs catch both Van and Junior coming off the successes of crit
ically acclaimed new albums. Tickets are now on sale at all BASS
ticket outlets.
