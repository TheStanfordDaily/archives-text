Fire damages chemistry building
A chemistry experiment gone awry caused a blaze that result
ed in more than $5,000 worth of damage in the Mudd Chemistry
Building the night of Dec. 10.
Four people who might have been exposed to corrosives had to
be decontaminated at Stanford Hospital, according to a report
from the Palo Alto Fire Department. Harmful vapors were emit
ted from an ether and acetone flask, the report said.
Structural damage is estimated at $5,000, and about $500 in
equipment and supplies was lost.
