# out 'n about
## Signing star 
### 
Assoc. Drama Prof. Anna De
veare Smith will let her fingers do
the talking during an appearance
Jan. 8 at 1 p.m. at the Fine Arts
Museum Store at Macy's Union
Square in San Francisco. Smith
will sign copies of her one-woman
play, "Fires in the Mirror: Crown
Heights, Brooklyn and Other
Identities." In order to witness the
play, one need only travel to the
Berkeley Repertory Theatre, 2025
Addison St., Berkeley, where
Smith will be performing through
Jan. 28. For more information,
call (510) 845-4700.


Courtesy of Anna Deveare Smith
