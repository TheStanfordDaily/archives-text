# Dancer-in-dorms
## 
### 
Patricia Lent, a member of the White Oak Dance Project direct
ed by Mikhail Baryshnikov, will teach five weeks of dance heritage
and Merce-Cunningham dance technique as part of the Artists-in -
Residence program. Lent is a 10-year veteran of the Merce Cun
ningham Dance Company who has taught workshops worldwide.
An opening potluck reception for the dancer will be held on Friday,
Jan. 7 at 6 p.m. in Roble Gym. Please bring a healthy snack to share.
For more information regarding the reception and Lent's classes,
call the Stanford Dance Division at 723-1234.
