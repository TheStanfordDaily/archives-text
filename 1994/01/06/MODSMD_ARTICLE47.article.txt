# Four plus five makes nine
## 
### 
The Kronos Quartet and five members of The Throat Singers
of Tuva will join their harmonious sounds in a performance in
Memorial Auditorium on Saturday, Jan. 8 at 8 p.m. Presented
by the Lively Arts at Stanford, the concert includes one set by the
Kronos Quartet, one by the Throat Singers of Tuva and one in
which both groups perform together. Tickets cost between $19
and $25 with a $3 discount for students and are available
through the Tresidder Ticket Office.
