# Shakespeare reduced
## 
### 
The Reduced Shakespeare Company will perform "The
Complete Works of William Shakespeare (Abridged)" this
Friday, Jan. 14 at 8 p.m. in Memorial Auditorium. Three ac
tors rapidly perform Shakespeare's 37 plays and 154 sonnets.
After performing half-hour versions of "Hamlet" in San
Francisco, the troupe decided to take their act to the stage.
Tickets run from $13 to $22, with a $3 discount for students,
and are available by phone at 725-ARTS.
