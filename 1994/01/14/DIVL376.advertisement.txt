The Hopkins Marine Station
announces the Spring Course
Biology 175H
"Research Problems
in Marine Biology"
Course work includes field and lab
studies, lectures, and individual projects.
There are no formal prerequisites and
SCUBA in not required.
Interested? Find out more at the Biology
Student Services Office, Gilbert 108, on
TUesday, 18 January or Thursday, 20
January. Come by anytime between noon
and 1:00 p.m. to talk with faculty
members and former students about this
unique research opportunity.
