# Modern memories
## 
### 
Modern dancer Molissa Fenley and the Abel Steinberg Winant
Trio will perform on Jan. 22 at 8 p.m. in Memorial Auditorium
in a show sponsored by The Lively Arts at Stanford. Fenley will
perform her own choreography in a number of works including
"Threshold." In addition, Stanford dance lecturer Janice Ross
will moderate a "Dance Partners" discussion with Fenley and the
Trio in Campbell Recital Hall, Braun Music Center, on Friday,
Jan. 21 at 7:30 p.m. For ticket information, call 725-ARTS.
