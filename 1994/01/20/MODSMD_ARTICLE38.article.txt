Black-and-white photographs of women field workers in
California's agricultural valleys will be on view beginning
Jan. 24 at the Institute for Research on Women and Gender,
located in Serra House. The photographs are the focus of
"Una Mirada al Campo: Images by Herlinda Cancino." Can
cino is a former affiliated scholar at the Institute. The exhib
it will remain at Stanford through March 22; Serra House is
open from 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.
