Applied Decision Analysis, Inc. (ADA) wants to meet EES
students who are interested in quantitative analysis and
management consulting. If your background includes
mathematics, statistics, computer science, industrial
engineering/operations research, physics or other quantitative
sciences, we would like to meet you.
ADA is a small, growing consulting company, located in Menlo
Park, that applies sophisticated analytical techniques to real-world
problems. ADA'S strength is high-quality work in areas such as
strategic planning, decision analysis, operations management,
analysis of public policy, forecasting markets for new products,
R&D planning, and basic research.
We would like to meet students who have:
O An interest in solving important, complex problems
O Skills in a broad range of mathematical techniques
O Communication skills to present analytical results in a clear, concise manner
O A high level of enthusiasm for challenging work in an informal atmosphere.
