GIBSON, DUNN fc) CRUTCHER
STANFORD INTERNSHIP PROGRAM
Gibson, Dunn & Crutcher, one of the nation's largest and most successful law
firms, offers this unique program for recent graduates of Stanford University who
are interested in the legal profession. Interns are involved in all facets of a case as
it prepares for trial:
• Legal research
• Evidence review and analysis
• Research for amicus briefs
• Document organization and maintenance
• Data collection
• Witness interviews
• Trial preparation and observation
• Settlement negotiations
• Pro bono work
• Continuing Legal Education
If you possess initiative; creativity; and research, writing and organization skills,
coupled with an interest in law, the Gibson, Dunn & Crutcher Stanford Internship
Program offers an ideal opportunity to experience the legal world firsthand. Please
direct inquiries and/or send a resume, transcript, and cover letter to either of our
Intern Recruitment Coordinators by February 21,1994.
