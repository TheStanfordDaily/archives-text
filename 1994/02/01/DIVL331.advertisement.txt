111 March. Seeks Public Policy Intern
The Washington, DC, office of AmFAR is seeking candidates for its summer 1994
internship program. Interns work closely with the AmFAR staff to develop and build
support for sound HIV/AIDS public policies. Each intern is responsible for an
independent research or organizing project. 77te internship lasts 12 weeks and offers a
$2,500 stipend.
Send resume, writing sample and references to:
Scott Sanders
AmFAR
1828 L Street, NW
Suite 802
Washington, DC 20036
Deadline: February 15,1994.
