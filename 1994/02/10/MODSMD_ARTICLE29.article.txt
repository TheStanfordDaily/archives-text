# Men's Basketball vs Arizona and Arizona State
## 
### 
TONIGHT — 7:00 p.m. vs. Arizona at Maples Pavilion.
SATURDAY — 7:00 p.m. vs. Arizona State at Maples Pavilion.
MAPLES NOTES — The first 1,000 Stanford students get in free with SUID.
But the Cardinal's last three games have sold out — get to the games early.
RECORDS/RANKINGS - Stanford (13-5 overall. 6-3 in the Pacific 10 Confer
ence, unranked); Arizona (17-4,6-3, No. 15 in the CNN/USA Today Coach
es' Poll); Arizona State (11-7. 6-3, unranked).
STANFORD STARTERS — Dion Cross (14.1 points per game, 2.7 rebounds
per game), Brevin Knight (12.4, 3.9), Brent Williams (14.8, 7.7), Andy Pop
pink (13.6, 7.6), Jim Morgan (5.7, 4.7).
SERIES NOTES — Last month, the Cardinal lost to both teams on the road,
89-72 to Arizona and 78-67 to Arizona State.. .Arizona has won 12 straight
over Stanford.. .Arizona leads their all-time series with the Cardinal, 30-15.
. .Arizona State holds a 21-14 all-time advantage.
RADIO — Angelo Puglisi and Scott Reiss call the shots for KZSU (90.1 FM),
while Bob Murphy and John Platz handle the game on KFRC (610 AM).
CARD NOTES — Brent Williams is scoring at a 17.1 points-per-game clip in
Pac-10 games.. .the Cardinal has not gone 6-3 in conference play since
the 1987-88 season.
