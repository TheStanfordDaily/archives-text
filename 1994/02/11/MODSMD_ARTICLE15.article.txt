# Today In Stanford history
## 
### 
On this date in 1969, the majority report of a nine-member student-faculty
committee declared that ROTC "is not compatible with the University," recom
mending that academic credit be denied for all ROTC courses.
"The incompatibility is inherent . . . and cannot be removed by various
changes which are from time to time proposed," the majority wrote in its report.
Complaints against ROTC included the fact that the program — which used
Stanford facilities and staff — was "clearly non-academic in nature."
