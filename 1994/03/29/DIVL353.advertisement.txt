THE DONALD AND ROBIN KENNEDY
JEWISH STUDIES
UNDERGRADUATE AWARD
The Program in Jewish Studies at Stanford University announces
the second annual Donald and Robin Kennedy Undergraduate Award.
The competition honors the best course paper written by an
undergraduate at Stanford on a Jewish theme during the 1993-94
academic year. One prize of $500 will be awarded.
Papers must be typed, double-spaced, and accompanied by a cover
letter with the name of the applicant. The applicant's name should
not appear on the paper itself. Please submit three (3) copies to
Hava Megiddo, The Program in Jewish Studies,
Building 60, Room 61-E, Stanford University, Stanford, CA., 94305.
Deadline for submissions of the essay is May 15, 1994.
