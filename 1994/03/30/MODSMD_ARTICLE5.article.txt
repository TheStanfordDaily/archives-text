# Dalai Lama lottery starts
## 
### 
Tickets for the Dalai Lama's
April 19 speech at Memorial Church
will be available beginning today on
a lottery basis to 1,100 Stanford fac
ulty, staff and students.
Those wishing to enter the lot
tery must complete a small applica
tion form at the Tresidder Union
ticket office. Applications are avail
able between the hours of 10 a.m.
and 5 p.m. through the end of this
week and next Monday and Tues
day and Saturday from 2 to 4 p.m.

Completed applications must be
returned to the ticket office by 5
p.m. on April 5. Names of the lottery
winners will be announced in the
April 13 Campus Report.
Winners may then pick up their
tickets at the ticket office between 7
a.m. and 6 p.m. on the 18th and
from 10 a.m. to noon on the 19th.
Unclaimed tickets will be made
available on a first-come, first-serve
basis at the ticket office from noon
until 4 p.m. on the 19th.
