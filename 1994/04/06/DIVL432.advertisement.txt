Reader's Guide to The DAILY
The Stanford Daily is an independent student newspaper that covers the news and events of Stanford University. It has a circulation of
13,500, and papers are distributed free in the Palo Alto and Menlo Park area as well as throughout the Stanford campus. Members of the
newsroom and advertising staffs at The Daily are full-time students.
