ANDRE DUBLJS
READING ON APRIL 11, 8:00 PM
Peninsula Orchid Society
Invites You To Attend Their Annual
Spring Orchid Show and Sale
"Orchids in the Village"
Admission is Free!
Thousands of flowering orchid plants from all over the world will be on
display and for sale. Cultural talks and potting demonstrations will be given
on both days. Bring your cameras for some fantastic photos.

April 9th and 10th, 1994
Show Hours:
Saturday: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM
Sunday: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
Location:
Town & Country Village
Palo Alto
(at the corner of Embarcadero and El Camino Real)
