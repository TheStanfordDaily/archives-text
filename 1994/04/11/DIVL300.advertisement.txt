Oracle Corporation, a world leader in software products, offers a position to an
enthusiastic individual with an outstanding academic records to assist with Senior Patent
Counsel in administering Oracle's patent program. We are seeking a recent graduate in
electrical engineering or computer science (0-3 years) who desires patent legal experience.
I he ideal candidate will be a self-starter who is an administrative organizer, possessing
excellent technical, writing and communication skills. Responsibilities range from
administration, monitoring and coordinating legal projects and development of patent
legal skills. Please submit a cover letter, a resume, and a copy of your transcript to:
Oracle Corporation
Legal Department
Attn: Katy Schnitz
Box 659507
500 Oracle Parkway
Redwood City, California 94065
No phone calls please. Oracle offers competitive salary and benefits.
Oracle is an equal opportunity employer.
