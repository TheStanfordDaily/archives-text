new Science Bug
If you're interested in writing a humorous science column published

editor at science@daily.stanford.edu or dropped off with the
receptionist at The Daily office in the Storke Publications Building
next to Terman Engineering Center. Call the science editor at
725-2100 if you have any questions about applying to be the Bug.
