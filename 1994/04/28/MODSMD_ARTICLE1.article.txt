# Dancin' the night away
## 
### 

Vincent Ho — Daily
Members of the Auxiliary Committee for Big Dance '94 tried their hand at the swing last night in the Schiff House lounge.
The swingers included (left to right) Rezal Rahman, Liza Suh, Afra Zomorodian and Janel Robinson. The dance for charity
will be held Friday, May 13.
