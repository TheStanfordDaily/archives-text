# Letters Policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes letters from its
readers. Letters will be published at
the editor's discretion and are subject
to editing. All letters must include the
author's name, phone number and
Stanford affiliation, if any. It is
advised that submissions be as con
cise as possible.
I .etters should be turned in on a Mac
intosh disk in Microsoft Word format
with a paper copy of the submission, or
e-mailed to ops (Wdaily.stanford.edu.
