PUBLIC SERVICE SCHOLARSHIPS
Students who will be juniors in 1994-95 and are committed to careers in the government service, public
interest organizations, or public service, non-profit organizations may apply to be nominated by Stanford
University for Harry S. Truman Scholarship. A Truman Scholarship covers tuition, fees, books, and room
and board, up to $3,000 for the senior year, and up to $27,000 for two or three years of graduate study
Approximately 85 scholarships will be awarded nationally.
