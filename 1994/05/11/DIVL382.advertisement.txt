LEAVING CAMPUS THIS QUARTER?
Will you be registered Autumn Quarter 1994/95?
If not ...
Remember to attend an EXIT INTERVIEW for your student loans:*
Monday May 16th 10:00 AM, 11:00 AM and 12:00 PM
Tuesday May 24th 10:00 AM, 11:00 AM and 12:00 PM
Thursday May 26th 12:00 PM, 1:00 PM and 2:00 PM
Friday May 27th 12:00 PM, 1:00 PM and 2:00 PM
Interviews will be held on the hour every hour.
Location will be Tresidder Oak East.
*This does not include Stafford (formerly GSL) loans..
You must contact the Bursar's Office at (415) 723-2183 to sign up. If you are stopping out please let our
office know so that documents may be prepared for your session.
The Stanford Daily
is looking for a motivated
and reliable student to serve as
Credit Manager.
Job duties include
• Maintaining Accounts Receivable
• Collecting outstanding debts
• Approving new accounts
• Assigning credit limits
•Managing circulation
Expected starting training dates: May 18 - May 25.
Full responsibilities start date: June 13, 1994.
THE STANFORD DAILY is one of only five independent and incorporated
University newspapers in the country. The Daily has won numerous
national and regional awards for general excellence, and is well
known for the exceptional caliber of its employees. At The Daily, the
Credit Manager plays an integral role in the sound financial
management of The Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation. The
Credit Manager will also be in charge of circulation of The Daily/ and
will have managerial responsibility over 5 newspaper distributors.
Interested individuals should have strong quantitative abilities, possess
excellent interpersonal skills, and be detail-oriented.
Apple/Macintosh computer experience a must, particularly
Microsoft Excel and Claris FileMaker.
Accounting experience is strongly recommended.
A commitment of 25 hours per week is required, 40 hours preferred.
The position offers great experience and pays competitively.
A minimum 6 month commitment is expected.
Ideal for those taking a year off or Student Spouse.
Applications available at The Daily Office.
For more information, contact
Buffy Chiang
Credit Manager
725-1282
or email
credit_manager@daily.stanford.edu.
Applications Due May 13, 1994.
