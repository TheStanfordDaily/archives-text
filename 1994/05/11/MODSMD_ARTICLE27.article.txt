# Sports short
## Field Hockey 
### 
Sheryl Johnson, Stan
ford's head field hockey
coach for the past nine years,
will be inducted into the
United States Field Hockey
Association Hall of Fame
Sunday, May 22.
Johnson joins 31 other
women currently in the Hall.
During her tenure at Stan
ford, she has guided the Car
dinal to five NorPac Confer
ence Championships and five
appearances in the NCAA
Playoffs. Her overall record
at Stanford is 71-59-11, 37-
14-9 in NorPac play.
A member of the U.S.
National team from 1979-
1991, Johnson played on the
Olympic Sports Festival
team from 1978-1990. She
was also a member of the
1987 Pan Am Games Team.
