Black Graduation Program
1994
Needs Your Assistance
Any Sophomores or Juniors willing to assist with the
preparations are encouraged to participate.
Extended Housing will be available for those
involved in Graduation Planning.
The 1994 Black Graduation Program will take place on Saturday June, 11
1994. If you are interested in helping, please meet at the Black House on
Thursday May, 19 at 4:3opm.
