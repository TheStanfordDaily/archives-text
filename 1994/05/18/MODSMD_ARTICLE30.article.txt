# For the record
## 
### 
In the Commencement
Countdown issue of The Daily,
May 13, the calendar misstated
that tickets are required for the
Commencement exercises in
Stanford Stadium. However,
tickets are not required.
In addition, an article in yes
terday's Daily incorrectly stated
that there were 21 John S.
Knight fellows selected for the
1994-95 school year. There were
18 selected, 12 U.S. and 6 inter
national. Jim Bettinger is deputy
director of the Knight fellows
program and Communication
Prof. Jim Risser is the director.
