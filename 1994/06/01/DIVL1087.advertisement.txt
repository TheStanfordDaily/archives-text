UPS wants to help
UPS wants to help you return home for the summer.
On June 6 - 14, UPS will have a shipping station set up to help you transport your
personal packages anywhere in the world.
■ 150 lb wt. limit
■ 130 inch width & girth
■ Computers/Stereo/electronic equipment packed in original container
The Center will be located at Wilbur Hall (hours 9-5 p.m.) and
Governor's Corner (hours of 9 -1, 2- 5 p.m.)
We will have representatives available to assist you with your questions, proper
packaging and supplies!
