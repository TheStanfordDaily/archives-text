IMPORTANT
NOTICE
FOR ALL STUDENTS RETURNING
TO STANFORD FOR 1994-95
ACADEMIC YEAR.
BEFORE YOU LEAVE CAMPUS
BE SURE TO REGISTER (COMMIT)
FOR THE 1994-95 ACADFMIC
YEAR USING THE AXESS SYSTEM
DON'T FORGET TO UPDATE
YOUR MAILING ADDRESS
ALL MAIL (SPRING GRADES,
AUTUMN BILLS, ETC.) WILL BE
SENT TO YOUR MAILING
ADDRESS, NOT YOUR
SECONDARY MAILING ADDRESS.
PLEASE CHECK FOR HOLDS,
THEY WILL BLOCK YOUR
REGISTRATION!
