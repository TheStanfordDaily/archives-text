Your Connection
to the Future is Here!
CUOnline is ready to deliver convenience
to your desktop right now. As a member of Stanford
Federal Credit Union, you now have FREE access to a
powerful, exciting remote banking system that puts you
in touch with your money anytime.
Link up by using the University's FOLIO System
(simply type "Select CUOnline") or access CU Online via
modem by calling (415) 969-0171. Log-on using your
Credit Union account number and FAS-TELL PIN.
If you're not a member of Stanford Federal Credit Union,
stop by one of our convenient Service Centers
and join today.
The future is here. Don't miss it.
