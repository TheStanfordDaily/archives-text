# Adv. 35 Page 10
##

WE WANT
SOPHOMORES
To be a part of
STAMFORD
REUNION
HOMECOMING
WEEKEND 94
(Friday - Sunday October 14-16)
Welcome Alumni
Celebrating a Reunion
Back on The Farm
The Alumni Association and the
Office of Development would like
warm, enthusiastic sophomores to
interact with alumni, introduce
classes without quizzes, answer
questions about life on the farm
nowadays, and be an exciting
part of a special event.
For more info, come to an interest meeting:
Wednesday
October 12, 7pm to B:3opm
at Bechtel International Center
or call 725-0693.
