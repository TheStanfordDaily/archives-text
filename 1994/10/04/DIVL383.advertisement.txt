In this writing for performance course taught by renowned Chicano playwright and poet,
Cherrie Moraga, students will develop original material to be performed by class members.
Through playwriting and acting exercises (e.g., dramatic monologue and scenework, docu
drama, agit-prop, teatropoesia and oral histories), students willlend their voices to the
creation of new Latino theatre for the 21st century.
No previous theatre experience required.
This course is part of the Committee on Black Performing Arts'
East Palo Alto Research to Performance Project and is co-sponsored by
the Irvine Multicultural Fund, CBPA, and the Department of Drama.
