For Undergraduates
INFO MEETING
TUESDAY, NOON, OCTOBER 11TH
AT THE HAAS CENTER FOR PUBLIC SERVICE, 3RD FLOOR
Any freshman, sophomore, junior, or senior may apply, regardless of major. No Asian language requirement.
