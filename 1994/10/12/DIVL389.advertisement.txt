The Care You Need, The Caring You Deserve
Last September, Diane Bradford gave birth to four boys - the first quadruplets ever bom to
Palo Alto Medical Foundation patients. The birth, not linked to fertility drugs, was a one in
700,000 shot. Bom at Stanford University Hospital, five-and-a-half-weeks premature, the
boys recently celebrated their first birthdays and are doing very well.
