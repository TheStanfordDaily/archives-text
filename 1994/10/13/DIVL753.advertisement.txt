What do the Supreme Court,
comedy, and overpopulation
have in common?
STANFORD REUNION HOMECOMING'94
Stanford students are invited to the following events:
Thursday, October 13, 8:30 p.m.-9:30 p.m.
Memorial Auditorium
A Funny Thing Happened on the Way to the Farm
Dr. John Bunzel, Hoover Institution Senior Research Fellow, will deliver his
famous stand-up comedy routine about American politics.
Friday, October 14, 9:00 a.m.-11:00 a.m.
Frost Amphitheater
Protecting Our Planet and People: The Politics of Sharing the Earth
Experts from across the U.S. will tackle the issues of overpopulation, crime,
disease, and environmental decline, at this roundtable forum. Be prepared for a
serious dose of reality that affects all of our futures.
Sunday, October 16, 11:30 a.m.-12:30 p.m.
Frost Amphitheater
Remarks by Supreme Court Justice Stephen Breyer
Stephen G. Breyer, '59, is the newest Associate Justice of the U.S. Supreme Court
and we are pleased and honored that he will serve as this year's keynote speaker.
No tickets are required for any of these events.
These events are sponsored by the Stanford Alumni Association and Stanford University.
