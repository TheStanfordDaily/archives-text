# Adv. 6 Page 2
## 
### 
When is the last time
you heard from a Supreme Court Justice?
Well, here's your chance!
STANFORD REUNION HOMECQMING '94
Don't miss this very special Stanford Homecoming event!
No need to register—no tickets required, all Stanford students are welcome!
Sunday, October 16,11:30 a.m.-12:30 p.m.
Frost Amphitheater
Keynote Remarks by Justice Stephen G. Breyer
We are pleased and honored to draw on a member of the Stanford family to serve
as this year's keynote speaker for Homecoming Weekend. Join us to welcome
Stephen G. Breyer, '59, the newest Associate Justice of the U.S. Supreme Court,
back to the Farm.
This event is sponsored by the Stanford Alumni Association and Stanford University.
