Instead o! asking, "What makes people sick?"
Dr. Pelletier asks the more compelling question, "What makes people
healthy?" While not discounting the value o! exercise or diet, Pelletier's
research shows how optimal health is far more dependent on positive,
purposeful life orientation than on regimented diets and aerobic
workouts.
Pelletier provides a variety of practical and effective techniques for
achieving a positive mind-body relationship leading to optimal health
and long life, including such strategies as problem confrontation,
behavior modification, and relaxation techniques.
Dr. Pelletier is a Clinical Associate Professor of Medicine and
Director of the Stanford Corporate Health Program, Department of
Medicine, Stanford University School of Medicine. He is also the
author of the bestselling Mind Am Healer, Mind Am Slnjer (1992).
