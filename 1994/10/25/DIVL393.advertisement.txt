Special research grants available:
Chappell-Lougee Scholarships
If you plan to major in the humani
ties or social sciences, you can apply for up
to $2500 for a research project. Pick a
topic, choose a faculty sponsor, and apply
by Friday, December 2,1994.
For further information, stop by
Undergraduate Research Opportunities,
122 Sweet Hall, or call 723-3828.
