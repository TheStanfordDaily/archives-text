# 1994 Big Game Pins
## 
### 
The Stanford Axe Committee
is once again producing a Big
Game commemorative pin.
The 1994 pin is the eighth in
a series of eleven that will
end with the 100 th Big Game
in 1997. The pin is priced at
$8 each (including tax,
postage, and handling) and
the Axe Committee is
accepting orders now. To
order, make checks payable
to the Stanford Axe
Committee, and send your
order to the Stanford Axe
Committee, P.O. Box 2031,
Stanford, CA 94309.
