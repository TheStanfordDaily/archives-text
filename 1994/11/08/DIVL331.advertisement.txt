Ron Rapoport
A Kind of Grace:
A Treasury of sportswriting by Women
(Zenobia Press, $14.95)
Ron Rapoport, a graduate of
Stanford, popular sports commentator,
and nationally syndicated columnist,
brings together seventy of America's
top women sportswriters in the first
anthology of its kind: A tribute to the
artistry of women athletes.
"I don V think being an athlete is
unfeminine. I think of it as a kind ofgrace. "-Jackie Joyner-Kersee
Mr. Rapoport will be speaking on how he
came up with the idea for the book, as well as
about the wide range of women's sportswrit
ing and the strength that women have brought
to this field. Ann Killian of the San Jose
Mercury News and Joan Ryan of the San
Francisco Chronicle, contributors to the book,
and Nancy Levin, editor of the Women } s Sports
a Bay Area newsletter devoted to
women's sports, will also be speaking.
This event will be held at the Bookstore on campus.
