Carl Djerassi
The dourbaki Gambit
(The University of Georgia Press, $19.95)
Professor of Chemistry and "Father of
the Pill" Carl Djerassi explores the culture
of collaborative science in his absorbing
new
novel
about
deception and revenge. Professor Djerassi
focuses on three issues: Scientists' passion
ate desire for recognition by their peers,
science's inherent collegiality, and the
graying of Western science. His other
books include the novel Cantor's Dilemma;
the autobiography The Pill, Pygmy Chimps,
and Degas' Horse*, and From the Lab into the
World: A Pill for People, Pets, and Bugs.
