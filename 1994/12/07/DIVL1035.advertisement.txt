BASEBALL COACHES NEEDED. The Palo Atto
Babe Ruth Baseball League needs managers to
coach 13-15 year old players. Come to the
Clearinghouse at the Haas Center for
applications. Applications are due December 15,
1994. Call 364-8100 for more information.
COMMITTED TO COMMUNITY SERVICE?? The
Black Community Services Center Is looking for
an African-American student, preferably a
freshman or sophomore, to be a community
services Intern at the Black House. This Is a paid
position with flexible scheduling for 8-10 hours
per week beginning Winter Quarter. The Intern
will work as a liaison between the BCSC and
other service centers on campus and should be
computer proficient with Word, PageMaker and
Excel programs.
INTERESTED IN HELPING OUT IN AN
ELEMENTARY SCHOOL COMPUTER LAB?
Hoover Elementary school is looking for
students who are interested in helping third
grade students in the computer lab. All that is
required is basic computer knowledge and a
willingness to work with young kids. For more
information, please contact Gina Miccio at 856-
1377.
VOLUNTEERS NEEOEDIII The Harvey Milk
Institute will be the first major adult education
program for the gay/lesbiari/bisexual
community. The Institute is presently In great need of
volunteers to help answer telephone inquiries, assist
with data entry, and help with general office
operations. Please call (415) 552-7200 If you are
Interested In volunteering.
LOOKING FOR AN INTERNSHIP IN WASHINGTON D.C.
FOR SPRING QUARTER? The Women's Legal Defense
Fund Is currently accepting applications for Its Spring
1995 Volunteer Internship Program. Available
positions are Communications Intern and Development
Intern. The application deadline Is December 9,1994.
Come to the Haas Center Clearinghouse for more
Information.
HAVE YOU STARTED THINKING ABOUT YOUR
SUMMER PLANS FOR 1995? The New Yorlc City
Government Scholars Program provides worV
experience in the Mayoral office or agency. Your work
may Involve policy analysis and review and you will
attend weekly seminars addressing Issues in NYC
government. The application deadline Is January 13,
1995. Come to the Haas Center Clearinghouse for
more information.
ARE YOU A SENIOR? LOOKING FOR WORK AFTER
GRADUATION? The New York City Urban Fellows
Program is for those individuals who are interested in
policy planning, long-term research, and day-to-day
problem solving at the office. Fellows also attend
weekly seminars led by leaders in government,
community organization, business and the media. The
application deadline Is January 20,1995. Come to
the Haas Center Clearinghouse for more
Information.
CORO FELLOWS PROGRAM. Interested in
working with key leaders In government business,
non-profit organizations and labor, exploring
current public policy Issues, or developing
leadership skills In a multicultural environment?
Applications are now being accepted for the Coro
Fellows Program for 1995-1996. Come to the
Haas Center Clearinghouse for more Information.
APPROACHING '95 SUMMER INTERNSHIP
DEADLINESI The J. Paul Getty Museum Yearlong
Graduate Internship deadline Is December 31, the
Chicago Botanic Garden application deadline Is
January 1, and the American Heart Association
and International Foundation for Education and
Self-Help both have deadlines on January 15.
Come to the Clearinghouse to get summer
Internship Information and to learn about
approaching deadlines. It's never too early to start
looking for a summer internship!
