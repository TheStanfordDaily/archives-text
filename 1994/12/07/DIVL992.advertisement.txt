Delilah's work explores Chicana/o spiritualism and the
Spanish/Indian encounter from a feminist mestiza
perspective. Her art has appeared in shows including
the 1990-93 Chicano Art: Resistance and Affirmation,
1965-1985; 1992-94 Chicano Codices: Encountering Art
of the Americas, 1992; and 1992 Quincentennial
Perspective: Artists Discover Columbus.
