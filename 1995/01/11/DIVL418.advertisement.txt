# Adv. 46 Page 11
## 
### 
We Are Looking For
People With
Poison Oak Rash
On Their Skin.
If you are between the ages of 18 and 65, you
may be eligible to participate in a
research study to develop new ways
to treat poison oak dermatitis.
All study-related procedures will be
provided free of charge, and you will receive
up to $200 for your time and travel.
For more information, please contact
either Karen or Christy, at
Psoriasis Research Institute,
(415) 326-1848.
