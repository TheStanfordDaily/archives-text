ON-CAMPUS RECRUITING PROGRAM
Winter Quarter • 1995
LIVE ORIENTATIONS**: To find out the "HOW TO'S" and the "WHAT IPS" regarding the
On-Campus Recruiting Program, you must attend one of the following five orientations:
• Wed, Jan. 11 3:3opm-spm Tresidder, Oak West
• Thurs, Jan. 12 12noon-l:3opm and 3-430pm Tresidder, Oak East
• Fri, Jan. 13 12noon-l :30pm and 3:30-spm Tresidder, Cypress
** All seating available on a first-come/first-served basis.
Jan. 30 - March 10 (6 weeks) — Winter Recruiting Interviews
Friday, Jan. 13 - Tuesday, 17 @ Bam — Bidding for the first week of winter interviews. Bidding is
required to gain access to interviews for full-time and summer positions. For more information,
attend one of the orientations listed above.
Tues, Jan. 17 @ 4pm — Employer Pre-Select (EPS) Document Drop-off Deadline for winter EPS
interviews. EPS interviews give employers the option to review in advance student resumes and
select whom they wish to interview. For more info, attend an orientation.
Thurs, Jan. 26 @ 12noon — EPS Results available; sign-ups begin.
CPPC, Recruiting Unit, Stanford University • A Division of Student Affairs
