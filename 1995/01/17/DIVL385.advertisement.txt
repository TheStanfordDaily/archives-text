Strategies for Standardized Tests in a Diverse World
Strategy *9: Study Real Questions
The LSAT, GMAT, and GRE have been made public since 1980. Yet
some candidates still buy books—and even lake courses—that feature
"simulated" tests questions.
Testing for the Public never makes up a phony question. If we made up
our own questions, we wouldn't show you how we did it! Our courses show
you how real lest questions are put together —so you can tear them apart.
