VENTURE CAPITAL
ASSOCIATE POSITION
Summit Partners, one of the nation's largest and fastest growing venture
capital firms, is seeking candidates for the position of Associate in our
Palo Alto and Boston offices.
We provide equity financing to growth-oriented companies in technology,
health care, and service-based industries. Associates assist in every phase of
the investment process, including financial analysis, company and industry
research, and due diligence.
Applicants should demonstrate an interest in investing and/or entrepreneur
ism, a strong academic and extracurricular record, and well-developed
leadership qualities.
Interested seniors and recent graduates should submit cover letter and
resume by January 27th to:
C. Jason Payne
Summit Partners
