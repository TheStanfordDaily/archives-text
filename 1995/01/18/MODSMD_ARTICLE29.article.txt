# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

Teressa Trusty — Daily file
Kate Starbird
While the United Nations may frown upon bombing and thefl, the Stan
ford women's basketball team was glad that Kate Starbird played terror
ist on the hardwood against Arizona last Saturday. The sophomore for
ward registered 17 points and three steals.
"Kate turned the game around when she made a steal and hit a cou
ple of threes," said coach Tara VanDerveer.


Mark Leibowitz — Daily file
David Johns
In a clash of No. 1 versus No. 2, the Stanford diving team played a pivotal
role in helping the Cardinal swimming and diving program score an upset
against Michigan last Friday at deGuerre Pool. Among the numerous con
tributing divers was platform specialist David Johns. The junior from
Springfield, Va captured the 1-meter dive with a score of 191.4. In addition,
he finished second in the 3-meter competition.
