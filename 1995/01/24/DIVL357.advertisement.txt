Strategies for Standardized Tests in a Diverse World
Strategy # 8: Find the Right Answer
Most test-takers think that multiple-choice tests mean eliminating wrong
answers. So do other coaching courses. Yet this takes time, lots of thought,
and leaves you with the one option you couldn't eliminate.
Testing for the Public prefers to find the right answer. We show you how
to look for it, how to recognize it, and how to justify it. You'll use your time
more efficiently and be more confident in the answers you pick.
