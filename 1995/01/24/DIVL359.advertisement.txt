# Adv. 16 Page 6
## 
### 
Today is the election for the Editor in Chief
of Volume 207. All staff members are invited
to attend a grill session at noon in the Daily
offices where you will have an opportunity
to ask both candidates questions. Lunch will
be provided. Voting, which must be done in
person, will be conducted between 1 p.m.
and 10 p.m. The election results will be
announced at 10:30 p.m. in the Daily offices.
Any questions? Call Elise at 5-2100.
