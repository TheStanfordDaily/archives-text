Applied Decision Analysis, Inc.
INFORMATION SESSION
Applied Decision Analysis, Inc. (ADA) wants to meet OR graduate students who are
interested in quantitative analysis and management consulting. If your background includes
mathematics, statistics, computer science, industrial engineering/operations research,
physics or other quantitative sciences, we would like to meet you.
ADA is a small, growing consulting company, located in Menlo Park, that applies
sophisticated analytical techniques to real-world problems. ADA's strength is high-quality
work in areas such as strategic planning, decision analysis, operations management,
analysis of public policy, forecasting markets for new products, R&D planning, and basic
research.
We would like to meet students who have:
An interest in solving important, complex problems
Skills in a broad range of mathematical techniques
mfr Communication skills to present analytical results in a clear, concise manner
wfr A high level of enthusiasm for challenging work in an informal atmosphere.
