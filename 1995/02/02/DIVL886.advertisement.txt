Wanted: Science editor
The Stanford Daily is looking for someone to edit
the Science page in Volume 207.
Tasks of the science editor include thinking up
articles on science for the newspaper, assigning them
to writers, editing their work and laying out your
page. The Science section is a full page every
Wednesday. The job runs from now until the end of
the school year. The salary is $900.
The main qualifications for the job are an interest in
science, the ability to explain scientific and technical
concepts to the layman and the ability to write well.
Past editorial experience is helpful but not necessary.
