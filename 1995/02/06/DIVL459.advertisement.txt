Lottery tickets available in White Plaza on Monday and Tuesday,
Feb. 6th and 7th, from 11 am - 2 pm.
Open Lottery Drawing held 6 pm Tuesday, Feb. 7th, location TBA
Ball Tickets purchased through Lottery : $65.00 per couple
1. You must have a student ID and a picture ID to acquire a lottery ticket. One lottery ticket per SUID number.
If more than one lottery ticket is acquired, all lottery tickets will be forfeit.
2. 125 pairs or Ball Tickets will be available through the lottery.
3. Lottery results will be posted Wednesday, 8 am, at Tressider, Roble Gym, and most dorms.
4. Ball Tickets may be purchased at the Drawing on Tuesday, or in White Plaza, Wednesday, 11-2.
5. Alternate lottery numbers will be selected if not all Ball Tickets are sold by Wednesday. These people
will be contacted and have the opportunity to buy tickets in White Plaza, Thursday , 11-2.
6. You must have you student ID and your lottery ticket to pur chase Ball Tickets.
7. General Ground Rules and Disclaimers below apply to tickets pur chased through the lottery.
Ground Rules and Disclaimers
1. The line will be formed at the base of the stage in White Plaza and will wrap around Dinkelspiel
Auditorium. Please do not obstruct any paths. We also request that only the people buying tickets
remain in line (friends and dates can visit).
2. A line roster w ill be maintained and checked periodically by committee members. Names will only be
added to the end of the list.
3. People may leave the line for a maximum of 10 minutes. You must check out with the committee
member on duty. The time limit will be strictly enforced.
4. People in line must have a Stanford ID and a picture ID to remain in line and purchase tickets. ID's will
be used to verify names on the line roster. No ID, No tickets!!
5. No tents and no fires! Please keep noise to a minimum. Please dispose of litter pr operly.
6. People not abiding to these rules will not be allowed to purchase tickets.
7.125 pairs of tickets plus those tickets not sold in the lottery will be sold Friday moming. Sales will
begin at 8 am.
8. Checks or exact cash only. NO REFUND PROVIDED.
9. Once the receipt is in your hands, VB committees are not responsible for it! You MUST have a receipt to claim
your VB tickets on March 2nd or 3rd. No receipt, No tickets!
10. On the night of the Ball, obviously intoxicated guests will be prevented from entering the Ball, and NO
refund trill be provided for these guests.
11. We reserve the right to modify or add to this list of rules any time prior to ticket sales for any reason.
Ball Ticket Sales in White Plaza at 8 am, Friday February 10th
price: $60.00 per couple
For General Info or Faculty Ticket Info, call 497-6979
