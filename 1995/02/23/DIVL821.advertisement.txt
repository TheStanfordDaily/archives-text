The Stan ford Daily
An Independent Newspaper
invites all interested candidates
to apply for the position of
BUSINESS MANAGER
FOR THE 1995-96 YEAR.
Responsibilities include:
■ Bottom-line performance of The Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation, a company with annual
revenues exceeding $1.0 million.
■ Hiring and management of 23 person Business Department.
■ Management of approximately $600,000 in assets.
The position runs from June 16,1995 to June 15,1996. Full time commitment over the summer and 30-40
hours per week commitment during the academic year required. General business experience is preferred,
but not required.
For more information, contact:
Brad Shong
The Stanford Daily
Store Publications Building
Stanford, CA 94305-2240
415-723-2555 Ext. 301
