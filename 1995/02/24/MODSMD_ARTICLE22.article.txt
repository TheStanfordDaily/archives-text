# Letters Policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes letters from its
readers. letters will be published at the
editor's discretion and are subject to
editing. All letters must include the
author's name, phone number and
Stanford affiliation, if any. Submis
sions with more than five signatures
must designate a representative.
Disks should have the owner's name
written clearly on the front and may be
picked up two business days after they
have been dropped off.
Letters should be turned in on a
Macintosh disk in Microsoft Word
format with a paper copy of the sub
mission, or they can be e-mailed to:
letters@daily.stanford.edu
