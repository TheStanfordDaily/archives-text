Patent Legal Assistant Position
Oracle Corporation, a world leader in software products, offers a position to an
enthusiastic individual with an outstanding academic record to assist Senior Patent counsel
in administering Oracle's patent program. We are seeking a recent graduate (0-3 years)
who desires legal experience.
The ideal candidate will be self-starter who is an administrative organizer, possessing
excellent technical, writing and communication skills. Responsibilities range from
administration, monitoring and coordinating the patent program, to collaborating on
challenging legal projects and developing of patent legal skills. Please submit a cover
letter, a resume, and a copy of your transcript to:
No phone calls please. Oracle offers competitive salary and benefits. Oracle is an equal opportunity employer.
