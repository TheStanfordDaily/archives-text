❖ The Office of Development at Stanford
University offers a full time, one year, paid
Development Internship beginning
June/July 1995.
❖ We offer an opportunity for accelerated
professional growth in the area of higher
education fundraising. The program addresses
the need to increase diversity, especially ethnic
minorities, among professionals in the field of
development.
❖ We are seeking candidates with outstanding
communication skills a demonstrated eagerness
to learn, a strong sense of teamwork, a self
starter with a can-do attitude and a
demonstrated ability to meet deadlines. The
successful candidate must possess a basic
understanding of the need for philanthropy and
an appreciation and commitment to higher
education. Computer experience required.
