Write to The Daily
The Daily welcomes letters from its readers. Letters will be pub
lished at the editor's discretion and are subject to editing. All let
ters must include the author's name, phone number and Stanford
affiliation, if any. Submissions with more than five signatures
must designate a representative. Disks should have the owner's
name written clearly on the front and may be picked up two
business days after they have been dropped off.
Letters should be turned in on a Macintosh disk in
Microsoft Word format with a paper copy of the submission,
or they can be e-mailed to: letters@daily.stanford.edu
