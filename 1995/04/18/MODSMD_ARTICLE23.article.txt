# Letters Policy
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes letters from its readers. Letters will be published at the editor's discretion and are subject to editing.
All letters must include the author's name, phone number and Stanford affiliation, if any. Submissions with more than five signatures must
designate a representative. Letters should be turned in on a Macintosh disk in Microsoft Word format with a paper copy of the submission. Disks
may do picked up two business days after they have been dropped off. Submissions can also be e-mailed to The Daily s electronic mail address,
opo@aauy.stanford.edu. Submissions are published on a space-available basis at the discretion of the Opinions editors.
