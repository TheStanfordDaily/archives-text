Daily is guilty of publishing The Most Horrible Slander

1 REALIZE that you are regular citi
zens, and you do not understand
about the arcane points which cause
arguments among religious fanatics.
And, I realize that you are not in
contact with Reality and don't know
what Reality is. You are just students.
It is a student publication.
However, I reviewed the article you
nicely published about me on May 20,
1992. As you just went along with
things, you did not realize that You
Published The Most Horrible Slander
about me.
Also, my Opponents are guilty of
that particular, and it contributes to
their persisting with negligent homi
cide. My Opponents were bad in casting
evil spells at me and that caused the
death of Jessica Williams and Professor
Albert Elsen. Because of that Particu
lar.
Specifically, "Being entranced by the
Theories of Metaphysics."

That is a Horribly bad thing. It is in
the Malicious Accusatory Version which
Hateful people say about me because it
is such a bad thing.
I am innocent of that. In May / June
of 1992 I thought not much protest was
needed. My Teaching is: People please
do not be entranced by the Theories of
Metaphysics. There is a Way to get near
to Zero in Bizarre Disasters, Senseless
Crimes for one region. On the side of
things, trying to be of influence, "Pre
tend Like only the material world is
real."
On the side of things that is diagno
sis of past trouble, realize that the hor
rible Metaphysical things may be
Cause. However, total boycott of using
those is the way.
If someone tells you that they know
the Thoughts of Another, say that they
probably got an error somehow, just
stray Thoughts, or delusions, hallucina
tions, whatever.

If you want to Know the Thoughts of
Another, you must use the Material
World for a means of conveying the
question, and Respect their Intent, of if
they want to answer or not. Words spo
ken aloud, or letters, or hiring private
detectives is the way to ask a question.
And, the answer must come through
the material world. This is my recom
mendation.
There is a Value of knowing that bad
things happen to good people Because
the Metaphysical Realm contains many
tangles and Injustices, and it does not
target as you may fantasize.
However, that is different from
"being entranced by the Theories of
Metaphysics."
My Opponents are entranced by the
Theories of Metaphysics for Use in try
ing to be of Influence. The Devil Satan
Recommends that, but claims to be
God. My Opponents are completely
deceived and think God whispers in

their ears. When actually the Devil
whispers in their ears, and urges the
couse upon them which leads to death
and conflict and grief, the Goals of the
Devil Satan.
My Opponents persist with Negligent
Homocide because they are following
that Devil who uses God's name in Vain
and Claims No Side Effects from
thought projection done to Promote
Christianity.
Also, the Article Published May 20,
1992 was in Error in Claiming my cru
sade was against "New Age Religions."
Actually, the Crusade is Against Having
the Tools of the Devil in Control of all
Churches.
Mindreading Causes Brain Damage
to small children. Mindreading is Uni
versally Practiced all across America.
The Angels Speak Against in. Reforms
needed. Quit having those Tools of the
Devil Being in Control of All Churches.
Joe Jonah Euclid
