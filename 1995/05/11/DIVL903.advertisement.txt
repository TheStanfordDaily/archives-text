LEAVING CAMPUS THIS QUARTER?
Will you be registered Autumn Quarter 1995/96?
If not...
Remember to attend an EXIT INTERVIEW for your student loans:*
Monday May 15th 10:00 AM, 11:00 AM and 12:00 PM
Tuesday May 16th 10:00 AM, 11:00 AM and 12:00 PM
Thursday May 18th 11:00 AM, 12:00 PM and 1:00 PM
Monday May 22nd 11:00 AM, 12:00 PM and 1:00 PM
Interviews will be held on the hour every hour.
Location will be Tresidder Oak East.
*This does not include Stafford (formerly GSL) loans..
You must contact the Bursar's Office at (415) 723-2183 to sign up. If you are stopping out please let our
office know so that documents may be prepared for your session.
