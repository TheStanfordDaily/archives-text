# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

Teressa Trusty — Daily file
Andrea Baxter
Sophomore Andrea Baxter posted a three-round total of 216
strokes to finish second individually at the NCAA Western Region
al Championships this past weekend. After the first day of the
tourney, Stanford languished in ninth place. Baxter was the only
team member who had a solid round. However, her teammates
soon followed her example and catapulted the Cardinal into third.


Teressa Trusty — Daily file
Paul Goldstein
Freshman Paul Goldstein had four wins in four chances at his No.
3 singles position during the men's tennis team's victorious journey
through the NCAA tournament. Goldstein lost only 16 games in those
matches, defeating Duke's Peter Ayers 6-1, 6-2, downing Mississippi
State's Laurent Miquelard 6-3,6-3, beating UCLA's Justin Gimelstob
6-0, 6-1 and outlasting Mississippi's Ali Hamadeh 6-1, 7-5.
