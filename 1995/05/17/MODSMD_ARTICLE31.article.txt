# Sports short
## 
### 
Department of Athletics
Scott Schuhmann, who came to Stanford in 1989 as an assistant football coach, was named Assis
tant Athletic Director by the Department of Athletics yesterday.
Schuhmann was the Cardinal's offensive line coach from 1989 to 1992. He was named Stanford's
Athletic Recruiting Coordinator in February 1993. He then went on to assume the duties of Athletic
Services Coordinator.
Among other responsibilities, Schuhmann will assist head football coach Tyrone Willingham in
the day-to-day operations of the Stanford football program.
