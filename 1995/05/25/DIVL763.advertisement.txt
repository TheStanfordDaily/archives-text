Great Opportuntity for Individuals
Who Enjoy Computers—No Experience Necessary
Storm Software (a start-up consumer software company) is currently looking for
individuals with an eagerness to earn some extra money by demonstrating two new
products, the Easy Photo Reader and Easy Photo software. Hours are up to you.
Recommended by the Wall Street Journal and PC Magazine, the Easy Photo Reader
brings personal snapshots into any PC with the click of a button. Easy Photo
software makes it quick and easy to add photographs to any document.
If you have basic knowledge of the operation of IBM-compatible
PCs/Windows, a strong motivation to educate others, and
would like to learn more about this program, please call
Pamela Spaan at (415) 691-6693.
