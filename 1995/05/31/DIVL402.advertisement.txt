You know the best...
Acknowledge them.
Nominate an outstanding professor for the
Hoagland Prize
for
Undergraduate Teaching
The Hoagland Prize is awarded annually to an outstanding teacher of undergraduates.
Nominating is easy. Write a letter describing the qualities that make her or him a
fantastic teacher and send it to Ellen Woods, Assistant Dean, Humanities and Sciences,
Building 1, Stanford, CA 94305 or woods@leland. Nominations are due JUNE 16th. If
you have any questions, please call Ellen Woods at 723-9378.
Help us recognize the best professors Stanford has to offer!
