"WIRED" PART-TIME JOB OPENING
ON CAMPUS 1995-96
with high growth computer-related company
Ricochet/Metricom, the wireless data communications technology leader is coming to the Stanford
Campus fall of 1995. Ricochet is a wireless modem and network that provides wireless access to the
University net and the "wired" world beyond. For company info see home page: www.metricom.com.
We have openings for talented people who can help us sell modems and subscriptions on campus.
These positions are part-time with a base pay plus commission. In addition to providing great work
experience, this is the chance to join a high growth company recognized for cutting-edge technology.
Wc will provide the training, direction, software, wireless modem and connection to the network. You
will need to provide your own laptop computer, strong selling skills and the desire to demonstrate and
support our radio modem on campus and among nearby off-campus students. A practical working
knowledge of networking and the Internet is desirable, however all applicants will be considered. Wc
are an equal opportunity employer.
