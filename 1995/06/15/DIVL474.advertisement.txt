File your registration commitment through Axess NOW to register for
next year. Those who commit late are charged $30-$5O late registration
fees and, until registered, won't receive Autumn ID cards or complete
University bills, can't move into Autumn University Housing, can't receive
some kinds of stipend/aid, can't request registration certification, etc. etc.
Don't cause yourself administrative
pain and late fees!

year 1995-96
