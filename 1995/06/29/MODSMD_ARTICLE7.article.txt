# Letters Policy
## 
### 
The Weekly welcomes letters from its readers. Letters will be pub
lished at the editor's discretion and are subject to editing.
All letters must include the author's name, phone number and
Stanford affiliation, if any. Submissions with more than five signa
tures must designate a representative.
Letters should be turned in on a Macintosh disk in Microsoft
Word format with a paper copy of the submission, or e-mailed to
letters@daily.stanford.edu
Submissions are published on a space-available basis at the dis
cretion of the Opinions editors.
