If You Like Rafting ...
Grand Canyon or Green River ...
You'll Love Stanford Flying Club!
The Grand Canyon and many other
adventures can be explored within just a few
hours, and at a surprisingly low cost with a
pilot's license from Stanford Flying Club.
Most members complete our Solo Pilot License
course with 10 to 20 hours of flying time.
Two-seat planes start at $39/ hr including fuel,
and you only pay for the actual flight time.
You can even take a lesson while flying to the
Grand Canyon.
Call for details, a free flying-time map, and a
free information folder. Or try a $75 test
flight. You'll love it!
