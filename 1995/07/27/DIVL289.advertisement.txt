# Adv. 14 Page 4
##

Got something on your mind?
The Weekly welcomes letters from its readers. Letters will be published at the editor's discretion
and are subject to editing. All letters must include the author's name, phone number and Stanford
affiliation, if any. Submissions with more than five signatures must designate a representative.
Letters should be turned in on a Macintosh disk in Microsoft Word format with a hard copy
of the submission, or emailed to letters@daily.stanford.edu. /
