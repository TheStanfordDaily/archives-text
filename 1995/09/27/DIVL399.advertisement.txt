DO YOU
HAVE WORK
IN YOUR
FINANCIAL
AID
PACKAGE?
INTERESTED
IN
COMMUNITY
SERVICE?
READ ON!
The Community Ser
vice Work-Study Pro
gram allows students
who qualify to be paid
for working in non
profit community
agencies. Participants
may be involved in
direct service or com
munity-sponsored re
search activities. If you
have work as part of
your financial aid
package you may be
eligible.
You do not have to
have Federal Work-
Study to qualify.
For more information,
stop by the Haas
Center for Public
Service Information
& Resource Center
(Across from the Bike
Shop.)
