LSAT PREPARATION FOR
DECEMBER 2 EXAM STARTS OCTOBER 10
I he Program's course to prepare students for the
December ISA I starts Tuesday, October 10 and
meets mostly Tuesday evenings (with a few weekend
classes) through November 28.
All curricular preparation, instruction, and feedback are
offered by a former Stanford faculty member who is a Stanford J.D.
and a practicing attorney who has prepared members of the Stanford
community since 1 for the LSAT. The median score for the
Program's Stanford-related students is over 164. Enrollment will be
limited to eight students, and the fee is $625.
• over 30 hours of instruction
• a professional educator as your instructor
• supervised homework
• copious practice materials
