# Write to The Daily
## 
### 
The Daily welcomes letters from its readers. Letters will be published at the editor's discretion and are subject to editing. All letters must include the author's name, phone number and Stanford affilia
tion, if any. Submissions with more than five signatures must designate a representative. Disks should have the owner's name written clearly on the front and may be picked up two business days after they
have been dropped off. letters should be turned in on a Macintosh disk in Microsoft Word format with a paper copy of the submission, or they can be e-mailed to: lettere@daily.Btanford.edu
