Senior midfielder Carmel Murphy and head coach lan Sawyers were named Women's Soccer Player of the Year and Coach of the Year of the
Pacific 10 Conference, respectively, when the All-Pac-10 team was announced Wednesday. Murphy, who finished the season with 11 goals and
seven assists while anchoring the Cardinal's offensive attack, was awarded the conference's top honor. Junior forward Erin Martin, who led the
team in scoring with 35 total points (14 goals, seven assists), was named to the second team. Junior defender Suzie Boots and freshman forward
Kelly Adamson also earned spots on the first team, while sophomores Elie Foster and Rita Hermiz garnered second-team honors.
