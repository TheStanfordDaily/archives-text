# Poll methodology
## 
### 
This Stanford Daily poll
is based on telephone inter
views with 374 Stanford
students selected randomly
from listings in the Stanford
Directory.
The poll was conducted
by paid interviewers
between 6 p.m. and 10 p.m.
on Nov. 19, 20 and 21.
The responding pool was
comprised of 181 under
graduate and 193 graduate
students.
Of the total 374 partici
pants, 225 were men and
149 were women.
The questions have a
margin of error of approxi
mately 5 percent at a 95 per
cent confidence level.
