# Sports short
## 
### 
Football
Following yesterday's announcement of Stanford head coach Tyrone Willingham as Pacific 10 Con
ference Coach of the Year, the conference disclosed its All-Pac-10 teams. Senior kicker Eric Abrams, the
Cardinal's all-time scoring leader, senior offensive tackle Jeff Buckey and sophomore return specialist
Damon Dunn were named to the first team.
Three other Cardinal players, all coming from the offensive side of the ball, were named to the All-
Pac-10 second team: senior quarterback Mark Butterfield, sophomore running back Anthony Bookman
and senior wide receiver Mark Harris.
