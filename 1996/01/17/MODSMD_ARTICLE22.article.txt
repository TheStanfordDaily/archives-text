# Don't try this at home
## 
### 

Sasha Mitelman Daily
Top: Demonstrating the versatility and potential energy lurking in a lowly cucum
ber, teaching assistant Jon Carter creates an electrical current from a single
kosher dill pickle for Electrical Engineering 101: "Introduction to Circuits.''
Despite the latent fueling and party possibilities of this new energy source,
Carter strongly urges that this procedure be confined to the laboratory, since the
current is strong enough to kill the unlucky experimenter. Right: And you thought
only light bulbs could illuminate the night.
