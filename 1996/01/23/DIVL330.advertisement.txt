Stanford Summer Jobs
Stanford Summer Residence Counselor (formerly Summer RA) positions
are available in Branner working with advanced high school students and
in Mo Mo and Mirrielees working with undergraduates (both visiting
undergrads and Stanford students).
Summer RCs assume a leadership role in the residences by:
Planning orientation events
Organizing educational, entertainment and sports activities
Fostering a comfortable and cooperative living environment
Providing academic counseling
Reinforcing behavioral guidelines
Compensation is room and board plus a stipend. Interested students may
pick up an application packet from the Stanford Summer Session Office,
Building 590 (the Nitery), Room 104.
Summer Quarter runs June 25 - August 17,1996.
Applications are due February 15.
