Environmental Equity Week
February 5 & 6, 1996
Discussing Issues of En vironmental Racism
Robinson House: Man.. Feb. 5 @ 6:30 pm
A panel of speakers will discuss community-based
environmental issues from various perspectives:
government, grass-roots, international, and legal.
Speakers will include:
Bradley Angel - Greenpeace
• Jose Padilla - California Rural Legal Assistance
• Jay Peterson - California Indian Legal Services
Mike Tomaszkiewicz - Citizens for a Better Env.
/
A dams House: Tues.. Feb. 6 @ 7:00 pm
Adams House will focus on the effects of
environmental racism against migrant farmworking
communities, touching on various issues such as
health care services for rural women, worker safety,
community education services and fair housing.
• Luke Cole - California Rural Legal Assistance
UNIVERSITY PUBLIC WORSHIP
AT
MEMORIAL CHURCH
Sunday, February 4 ♦ 10:00 am
"Help My Unbelief"
The Reverend Christina Miller
Church school for young people,
ages 5-13, and infant/child care are
available during the service.
