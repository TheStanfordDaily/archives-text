SCHOLARSHIP FOR
ACADEMIC DISTINCTION
PHI BETA KAPPA, Northern California Association,
offers scholarships of $1000 or more for academic
distinction to members of Phi Beta Kappa who will
pursue advanced study at a northern California
institution in the forthcoming academic year. Nominees
will be selected by the Stanford Phi Beta Kappa Chapter,
and final awards will be made in June by the
Association.
Pick up an application form in the Chemistry
Department Office, Mudd 121. Completed applications
are due February 21, 1996 and should be returned to
the Chemistry Office, c/o Prof. Robert Waymouth.
