Stanford University
Program in Jewish Studies
presents
The Annual Jewish Community Endowment Fund Lecture
"Ritual Murder" in the Modern Era:
The Damascus Affair of 1840
Professor Jonathan Frankel, Hebrew University of Jerusalem.
Author of Prophecy and Politics, and many other books on
Jewish history 7, Russian socialism, and politics.
Tuesday, February 13, 1996
8:00 p.m.
Tresidder Union, Oak West Lounge
Stanford University
A reception will follow
The lecture is free and open to the public. For more information, please
contact the Program in Jewish Studies office at 415-723-7589.
