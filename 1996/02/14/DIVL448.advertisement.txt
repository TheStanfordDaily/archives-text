GIBSON, DUNN CRUTCHER
STANFORD INTERNSHIP PROGRAM
Gibson, Dunn & Crutcher, one of the nation's largest and most successful
law firms, offers this unique program for recent graduates of Stanford
University who are interested in the legal profession. In addition to
participating in pro bono work and Continuing Legal Education, interns are
involved in all facets of a case as it prepares for trial:
• Legal research
• Evidence review and analysis
• Research for amicus briefs
• Document organization and maintenance
• Data collection
• Witness interviews
• Trial preparation and observation
• Settlement negotiations
If you possess initiative, creativity, research, writing and organization skills,
coupled with an interest in law, the Gibson, Dunn & Crutcher Stanford
Internship Program offers an ideal opportunity to experience the legal world
firsthand. Please direct inquiries and/or send a resume, transcript, and cover
letter to either of our Intern Recruitment Coordinators by February 23, 1996:
