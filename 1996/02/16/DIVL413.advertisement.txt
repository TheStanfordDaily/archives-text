The Community Development Fellowship
provides students with the experience and
support necessary to affect social change at the
community level. Fellows spend their summer
in internships with grassroots community
development organizations, followed by
educational programs during the academic
year. Applicants should have a strong
background in community work, and a
demonstrated commitment to social justice.
Open to sophomores, juniors, and co-terms
returning to campus in the fall. $2,000 stipend
