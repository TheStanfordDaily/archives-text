THE SECOND ANNUAL
BART J. McCORMICK
MEMORIAL GOLF TOURNAMENT
STANFORD GOLF COURSE
MONDAY, MARCH 4, 1996
SHOT GUN START 10:00 am - MEET AT CLUBHOUSE 9:15 am
The Stanford chapter of the Sigma Chi Fraternity established The Bart J. McCormick Memorial Golf
Tournament in memory of our brother, Bart J. McCormick. The annual tournament is meant to be an
enjoyable event for both Stanford and Palo Alto Communities.
Net proceeds from the tournament will benefit cancer research at the Lucile Salter Packard Children's
Hospital at Stanford. The entrance fee of $150 includes greens fees, golf cart, prizes and a reception to be
held at the Sigma Chi house afterwards. Your support is crucial to the success of this year's event as we are
looking to draw over 100 players. We hope to see you there 1
For further information, contact:
Tim PortAvood at 415-723-0070 or Gavin Turner at 415-497-4502.
