# Daily wins ACP awards
## 
### 
The Daily won top honors from
the Associated Collegiate Press yes
terday at the organizat ion's nation
al conference in San Francisco.
The ACP awarded The Daily
first place in the category of best
daily broadsheet newspaper from a
four-year university. There were
separate categories for smaller-for
mat and weekly newspapers.

In addition, The Daily won
second place for the best news
paper from a four-year school in
California. California State
University at Chico, which pro
duces a weekly broadsheet
newspaper, took first place.
College newspaper editors from
all across the United States and
Canada attended the conference.
