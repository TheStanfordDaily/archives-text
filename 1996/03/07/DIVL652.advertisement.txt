Community Meeting
To Discuss the New
Comparative Studies and Ethnicity Program
Monday, March 11,1996
7-9 pm
Building 200, Room 303 (History Corner)
The Planning Committee for Comparative Studies in Race and
Ethnicity (CSRE), a new IDF which silll offer an undergraduate
major/minor in Comparative Ethnic Studies, invites all members
of the Stanford community to come hear the plans for the new
program, and to provide input into its design.
WE WON'T JUST RAISE YOUR HOPES...
WE'LL RAISE YOUR SCORES.
• Highly trained instructors
• Four full-length practice tests
• Maximum class size of 15 students
• Free extra help
• Classes grouped by ability
• Satisfaction guaranteed
The Princeton Review
**Classes have moved to 2390 El Camino Real
in Palo Alto at El Camino and California St. **
The Princeton Review is not affiliated with Princeton University or ETS.
