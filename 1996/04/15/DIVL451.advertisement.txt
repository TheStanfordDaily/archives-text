Description of Position
Sophomore College is a set of programs for returning second-year students who live
together in a student residence and work under faculty supervision in a series of small-group
classes during early September, before the opening of Fall quarter. Each faculty member in the
Sophomore College will be assisted by two SAs, each of whom will serve as a peer advisor for
five students. SAs will also serve as academic advisors and residence staff for the Sophomore
College, and will report to the Sophomore College Coordinator. SAs will receive room,
board, and $600 for full-time work during the course of the program, and for training and
possible program planning at the end of spring quarter.
