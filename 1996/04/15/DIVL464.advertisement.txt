Informational meeting for
students with disabilities
applying for housing
An informational meeting will be held on Tuesday,
April 16 at 4 p.m. for students submitting housing
disability exemption requests for Summer 1996 or
for the 1996-97 academic year. Location: Mark
Taper Law Center (on Escondido Road between
("rothers and Crothers Memorial Halls). First-year
students and students who have not previously sub
mitted requests are strongly encouraged to attend.
Summer and 1996-97
Graduate Housing
booklets now available
Pick up a booklet at your local residence office
(if you live on campus) or at Housing Assign
ment Services, 110 Old Union (if you live off
campus). Submit your application in Axess by
Sunday, May-5.
