Senior Pub Crawl:
The Sequel
♦ In San Francisco at the Union Street Bars
♦ This Thursday, April 18
♦ Early buses leave Tresidder at 8:15 p.m.
+ Late buses leave Tresidder at 10:15 p.m.
♦ Tickets on sale in White Plaza at lunchtime
♦ Sponsored by Senior Class Presidents
RICHARD S. GOLDSMITH AWARD
$1,000 PRIZE
will be awarded for the best paper written by
a Stanford student on a topic relating to
DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Papers written for any purpose are eligible, including seminar and course
papers. The student paper may concern disputes of any sort, between
individuals, organizations or nations.
Papers may be submitted by students,
or by faculty members on behalf of students, to:
Andrew Ward
Associate Director, Stanford Center on Conflict and Negotiation
Room 6, Owen House
ALL PAPERS MUST BE RECEIVED BY FRIDAY, MAY 17, 1996
THE AWARD WILL BE MADE IN JUNE, 1996 ■ FOR MORE INFORMATION,
CALL 723-2574
This award is being coordinated by the Stanford Center on Conflict & Negotiation
