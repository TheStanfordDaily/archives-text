# Election begins
## 
### 
The ASSU spring general elec
tion will be held today and tomor-

row. Polling opened at midnight and will close
tomorrow at 11:59 p.m. Students must have a
leland account in order to vote on the Web. The
URL is http://election.stanford.edu.
In addition, paper ballots will be available
from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. both days at the Law
School. Voters must show a valid student ID.
Further questions about voting procedures
should be directed to Elections Commissioner
Ryan Medders at 723-2839.
