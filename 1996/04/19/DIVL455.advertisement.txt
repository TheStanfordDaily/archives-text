Ernesto Galarza Commemorative Leeture
"Preparing for the Challenges to Latinos
in the 21st Century"
Bert N. Corona
Mr. Corona has been a political activist and union organizer lor over
fifty years. He has worked on local, state, and national political
campaigns, including the "Viva Bobby Kennedy" campaign; orga
nized worker's and immigrant's rights movements, including the
AFL-CIO and the United Cannery, Agricultural Packing and Allied
Workers of America; and taught at prestigious universities across the
United States. He is currently the National Director ol LaHermandad
Nacional Mexicana (HNM), which he helped found in 1969. HNM
is the first national mutual-aid and pro-union organization of undocu
mented workers and other Spanish speaking immigrant workers in
the U.S.
For more information, please call 415-723-3914.
