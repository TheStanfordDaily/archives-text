The Stanford
Daily
An Independent Newspaper
Announces openings for membership on The Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation's
Board of Directors
The Daily is looking for three board members with the following qualifications:
• A registered student of Stanford University.
• A person having business knowledge or experience.
• A member of the Leland Stanford Junior University
faculty or staff.
The term of office for the student member is one year, ending June 15,1997. The term of
office for the business and faculty /staff members is two years, ending June 15, 1998.
None of the three board members may be members of The Stanford Daily staff during their
term of office.
An election for the new Board of Directors members will be held on May 16. Directors are
elected bv members of The Stanford Daily staff. Declarations of intent to run for one of
the positions must be submitted no later than 5 p.m. on May 9. A declaration of intent
should contain a one-half to one page letter stating which position the candidate is
interested in occupying and why the candidate is interested in running for that position.
The declaration should also contain a resume demonstrating that the candidate is qualified
to occupy the spot for which he or she is running
All declarations should be sent to:
Burt Herman, editor
The Stanford Daily
Storke Student Publications Building
Stanford, CA 94305-2240
Anyone with questions regarding the election should call Burt Herman, editor of
The Stanford Daily, at (415) 725-2100 x5Ol, or send email to eic@daily.stanford.edu
