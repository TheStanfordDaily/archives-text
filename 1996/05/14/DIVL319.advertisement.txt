Join the Stanford Jazz Orchestra for this evening of classic big band jazz
featuring drummer composer and arranger Louie Bellson. Mr. Bellson
has recorded nearly 200 albums and has performed with a host of jazz
greats. including Benny Goodman, Count Basie, Ella Fitzgerald,
Stan Getz, Duke Ellington, and countless others.
