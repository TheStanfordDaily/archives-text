Elaine Pagels
"Satan and All His Angels/'
(How Early Christians Came to Demonize Jews,
'Pagans' and Heretics)
Professor Pagels is well known for her studies of religion and
social dynamics in the ancient world: The Gnostic Gospels
(National Book Award), Adam, Eve, ami the Serpent, and (most
recently) The Origin of Satan. A former Mac Arthur Fellow and
currently the Harrington Spear Paine Professor of Religion at
Princeton, Elaine Pagels grew up in Palo Alto and majored in
Classics as an undergraduate at Stanford.
