# Adv. 8 Page 5
## 
### 
Telephone & S<i>=
Service
IN ON-CAMPUS STUDENT RESIDENCES
Leaving campus?
Moving to another room?
Don't forget to disconnect your service!
UNDERGRADUATE HOUSING RESIDENTS:
♦ Telephone and Btv service in all undergraduate residences
will be disconnected by 5:00 p.m. on June 1 7th.
♦ Students leaving campus early can have their service
disconnected prior to June 1 7th by contacting
Communication Services at 7-HELP.
♦ Students remaining in the same room or moving must
contact Communication Services at 7-HELP
to continue their service throughout the summer.
GRADUATE HOUSING RESIDENTS:
♦ Graduate housing residents who are
leaving campus permanently or just for the summer
must contact Communication Services at 7-HELP
to disconnect their telephone and stv service.
To disconnect telephone or $tv service,
or for further information, call
Communication Services at 7-HELP (7-4357).
