File your registration commitment through Axess NOW to register for next year.

Don't cause yourself administrative pain and late feesl
FILE YOUR REGISTRATION COMMITMENT NOW FOR AUTUMN 1996-97.

year 1996-97"
Respond as needed to the prompts to complete and save your
registratior commitment.

deadline. You must commit through Axess.
