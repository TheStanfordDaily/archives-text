CONSULTING
Stanford Consulting is a student organization that provides members
an opportunity to work in teams to solve real-life business problems.
We are looking for qualified, highly motivated undergraduate and graduate
students to contribute to the organization for the '96 -'97 academic year.
I Please attend our information session to learn more about the organization,
the people, the opportunities, and the application process.
