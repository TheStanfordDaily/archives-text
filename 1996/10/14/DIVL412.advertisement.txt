Prestige Stations, Inc. (PSI), a wholly-owned subsidiary of ARCO, is seeking highly motivated candidates tor entry into our
Management Development Program.
TRAINING
The Management Development Program (MDP) Junctions as an intensive, tast-track position designed to develop and prepare
candidates as multi-unit supervisors within a twelve- to eighteen-month period. As an MDP participant, you will be provided with
extensive retail marketing training and hands-on managenal expenence.
QUALIFICATIONS
Must be willing to relocate within Calitornia, Arizona, Washington, Nevada, or Oregon. Experience in the retail industry is desirable.
BENEFITS
Successtul candidates enjoy the following benefits:
• Comprehensive medical, dental and vision benefits. • Rapid, merit-based promotion.
• Educational assistance for graduate degrees. • Career progression opportunities within PSI and possibly ARCO
• Starting salary of $26,000-$28,000 + bonus potential • Additional benefits upon promotion to Supervisor-in-Training
(up to 40% of base). status.
