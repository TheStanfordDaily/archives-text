Indigenous People's Day 1996:
A Day of Remembrance and
Celebration of Native American
Perseverance on Turtle Island
When: October 14, 1996, From 10 p.m. till 12 a.m.
Where: White Plaza @ Stanford University
Who: Everyone is welcome
What: A time for the Bay Area Indian community
to speak, remember, laugh, pray and celebrate
being Indian in the Americas in 1996
Whv: Because we are still here
Eagle Nation Singers will provide the music
Open niike from 10 p.m to 12 a.m.
For more information please contact the Stanford Amctican Indian Program office
@ (415) 725-6944 or the Native American Cultural Center @ (415) 723-4078
Sponsored by the Stanford American Indian Organization and the ASSU
