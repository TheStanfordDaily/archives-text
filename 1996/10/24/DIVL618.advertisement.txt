WANT TO BE A
RESIDENT
ASSISTANT?
Leaving the campus for winter or winter & spring quarters?
Write this on your calendar:
Learn about the RA application process.
Information Meeting
on
Wednesday, October 30
4:00 - 5:00 pm
Cypress South Room
Tresidder Memorial Union
You can also come by and pick up application materials at the
Office of Residential Education, 306 Old Union after October 30.
The information session is for students interested in becoming
Resident Assistants in undergraduate houses for the 1997/98
academic year, but will be away from campus during winter or
both winter & spring quarters. Applications due Nov. 14.
Office of Residential Education, 306 Old Union, 725-2800
