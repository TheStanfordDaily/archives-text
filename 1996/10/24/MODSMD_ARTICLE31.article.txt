# THEATER
## What a Feelin'! 
### 
Courtesy of Best ol Broadway
Rodgers and Hammerstein's musical "Carousel," playing now through Nov. 10 at the Golden
Gate Theater (1 Taylor at Golden Gate and Market streets), will stun you the moment the or
chestra starts playing, effortlessly blending modern staging with a great book and score.
The story revolves around carousel barker Billy Bigelow's (Patrick Wilson) marriage to Julie Jor
dan (Sarah Uriarte), a gentle mill-worker At first, Julie seems to tame Billy's ways, but he ends
up failing her in every possible way.
Tickets cost $34-$62.50 and are available at the box office or at any BASS ticket center. Call
776-1999 for tickets.
