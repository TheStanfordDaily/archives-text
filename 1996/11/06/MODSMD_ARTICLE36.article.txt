# Report: Harrick to quit as UCLA coach
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES (AP) - Jim
Harrick, who in 1995 guided
UCLA to its first NCAA champi
onship in 20 years, will resign as
the Bruins' basketball coach
today, Fox Sports News reported.
According to the cable televi
sion network, a news conference
will be held this morning on the

UCLA campus.
The network said Harrick was
told to resign or he would be fired.
Harrick, 59, was cleared two
weeks ago by the school and Pacific
10 Conference of violations in the
sale of a car he owned to the older
sister of prized recruit Baron
Davis.
