When a parent dies,
Social Security
can help...
Family dreams dim; times can be
hard. Social Security can make a
difference.
These boys, whose mom worked
and paid Social Security taxes, will
receive benefits until they're 18.
Social Security's helping this family
stay together. And it's helping
3 million other kids pursue their
dreams.
To learn more about Social Security,
call 1-800-772-1213. Ask for the
booklet Basic Facts About Social
Security.
