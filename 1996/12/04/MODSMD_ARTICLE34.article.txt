# Prescription for a good night's sleep
## 
### 
00...
—*• Try to go to bed and wake up at roughly the same
time each day.
—*• Relax one to two hours before bedtime. (Meditation,
relaxation exercising or even reading that novel you've
been putting off for winter break may help you fall asleep.)
—*• Clear your sleeping area of books. tests or other
anxiety producing materials.
Don't...
—*• Party it up the night before. Alcohol
consumption can lead to sleep fragmentation.
Consume caffeinated beverages and fotxis (i.e.
chocolate) two to three hours before bedtime.
Exercise or snack two to three hours before
bedtime.
—*• Indulge in daytime naps.
