# CALENDAR
## Dance Five stud-muffins show off the prowess of their really big feet in 'Tap Dogs' 
### 

Courtesy o* Browne & Zukow Associates
Dein Perry (center, in black) and company tap their way out of working-class Australia
and find themselves in some of the hippest stages of North America, including San
Francisco's Orpheum Theater (1192 Market St.) through Dec. 15. Catch this reinven
tion of tap for the '90s before the troupe heads off to its New York engagement in the
spring. This dance-fest shows Tuesday through Friday at 8 p.m., Saturday at 5 p.m. and
9 o.m. and Sunday at 1 p.m. and 5 p.m. Tickets cost Sl5-S3B and are available at the
