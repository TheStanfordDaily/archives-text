# SPORTS SHORT
## 
### 
Men's Tennis
Sophomore Ryan Woltera
is the only remaining Cardi
nal player in the third day of
competition at the Milwaukee
Tennis Classic today. Yester
day, Wolters garnered a pair
of victories as he defeated
Matt Wright of Michigan 6-3,
6-4 in the third round and
Chris James of Purdue 6-3, 6-
3 in the fourth round.
Senior Grant Elliott won
his third-round match against
Cary Biorkman of Arizona
State 7-5, 6-1 but couldn't
hold on in the fourth round,
losing to Daniel Anderson of
Boston College 7-6, 6-0. Car
dinal junior Paul Goldstein
lost in the third round to
Gavin Sontag of Illinois 2-6,
6-4, 6-2.
The Milwaukee Tennis
Classic continues through
Saturday.
