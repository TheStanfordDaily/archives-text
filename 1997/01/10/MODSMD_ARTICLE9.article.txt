CORRECTIONS POLICY
It is the policy of The Stanford Daily to correct all significant
errors brought to the attention of the editors.
If you think there is a need for a correct.on regarding any arti
cle, editorial, column, headline, photo or graphic printed in The
Daily, please contact Editor in Chief Beth Berselli at (415) 725 2100,
ext. 501, or e-mail her at eic@daily.stanford.edu.
The Dailv will investigate all legitmate requests for corrections
and reserves the right to determine if a printed correction is neces
sary. All corrections will be printed in this space.
