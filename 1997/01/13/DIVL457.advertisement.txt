ONE DAY ONE UNIT* ONE GREAT COURSE!
BIOLOGICAL NUCLEAR CHEMICAL
MecHcal, Envlronmental, ancl Socletal Consequences
I
Saturday February 8,1997
8:30 - 5:00 Room Ml 14
In this epoch every educated person should have some knowledge of
the effects of nuclear war, the nuclear arms race, and biological and
chemical warfare.
This course is designed to meet that need.
The high-quality faculty are drawn from the fields of internal medicine,
surgery, psychiatry, physics, radiology, biochemistry, pediatrics,
international security, and weapons analysis.
