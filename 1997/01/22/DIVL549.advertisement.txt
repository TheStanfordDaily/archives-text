Original Winter One Acts
A collection of three student-written,
student-directed one-act plays: "Waiting for
'Waiting for Godot, Connection Down" and"The
Voluntary Inane." Performances are today and
tomorrow at 8 p.m., Friday at 7 p.m. and 10:30
p.m. and Saturday at 7 p.m. at the Nitery Theater
in Old Union. Tickets are $8 for students, $15 for
general admission; they are available at White
Plaza from 11 a.m. to noon and at the door.
