Stanford Summer Jobs
Summer Residence Staff positions are available in Branner working with
advanced high school students, and in Flo Mo and Mirrielees working with
undergraduates (both visiting undergrads and Stanford students).
Summer Residence Staff assume a leadership role in the residences by:
Planning orientation events
Organizing educational, entertainment, and sports activities
Fostering a comfortable and cooperative living environment
Providing academic counseling
Reinforcing behavior guidelines
Compensation is room and board. Branner staff also receive a stipend. Inter
ested students may pick up an application packet from the Stanford Summer
Session Office, Building 590 (the Nitery). For questions, call 723-3109.
Applications are due Friday, February 14.
