Viennese Ball Tickets will be sold in White Plaza at 8:00 AM. Thursday January 3a
General Tiaket Sale: $65 per couple
General Ticket Sale/Sleep Out Rules and Disclaimers
1 The line will be formed at the base of the stage in White Plaza and vvill wrap around Dinkelspicl Auditorium. Please
do not obstruct any paths. We also request that only people remain in line (friends may visit)
2 A line roster will be maintained and checked periodically by committee mambers. Names will only be added to the
end of the list.
3. People may leave the line for the maximum of 10 minutes. You must check out with the committee member on duty
The time limit will be strictly enforced.
4 People in line must have a Stanford ID to remain in line and purchase tickets. Ids will be used to verity names on the
line roster. No ID, No Tickets!!
5. No tents and no fires! Please keep noise to a minimum Please dispose of litter properly
6 People not abiding these rules will not be allowed to purchase tickets.
7 75 pairs of tickets plus those not sold in the lottery will he sold 1 hursday morning
Sales will begin at 8 AM
8 Checks or exact change only. NO REFUND PRONIPKD
9 Once the receipt is in your hands, we arc not responsible for it' You must have a stamped receipt to claim your
tickets on February 20th or 21st. No Receipt, No tickets!!!
10 On the night of the Ball, obviously intoxicated guests will be prevented from entering the Ball and NC
REFUND will he provided for these guests.
11 We reserve the right to modify or add to this list of rules any time for any reason
