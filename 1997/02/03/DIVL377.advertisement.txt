NOTICE OF NONDISCRIMINATORY
POLICY AS TO CHILDREN
The Children's Center of the Stanford Community
admits children of any race, color, national and
ethnic origin to all the rights, privileges, programs,
and activities generally accorded or made available
to children at the center. It does not discriminate
on the basis of race, color, national and ethnic
origin in administration of its educational policies,
scholarship and loan programs, and athletic and
other center administered programs.
