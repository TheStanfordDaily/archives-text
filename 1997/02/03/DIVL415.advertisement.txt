Part-time Coordinator
Another Way is a collaborative and grassroots program which raises money for
the homeless in the Palo Alto area. We are seeking a half-time Coordinator
(flexible hours) to provide a variety of administrative tasks, develop program
materials and maintain/promote the collection can project found in over 130
stores. Other activities include coordinating fundraising events, directing
community awareness activities, and recruiting volunteers. Salary range: $10.00-
$12.00/ hour. Send resumes to P.O. Box I 146, Palo Alto, CA 94302.
Fax: 324-1215 Phone: 329-2375. Deadline: February 28.
