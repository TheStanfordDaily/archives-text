American Foundation for AIDS Research
Seeks Public Policy Intern
The Washington, DC, office of the nation's largest AIDS foundation is seeking candidates
for its summer 1997 internship program. Interns work closely with the Am FAR staff to
develop and build support for sound HIV/AIDS public policies. Each intern is responsible for
an independent research or organizing project. The internship lasts 10 weeks and offers a
$2,500 stipend. More detailed information available at the Haas Center.
Send resume, writing sample, and two letters of recommendation to:
