The Amos Tuck School of Business Administration at Dartmouth College
The Tuck Business Bridge Program
June 16 - July 11,1997
Are you a senior starting on a business career after graduation? Are you a junior contemplat
ing such a track? Or if you're not sure...
For the First time, the Amos Tuck School of Business Administration at Dartmouth College
will open its campus to a select group of juniors and seniors from top liberal arts colleges for a
four-week residential summer program designed and taught by Tuck MBA faculty and corpo
rate partners.
The management program will show students the excitement of a management career and
help them develop practical, marketable, analytical business skills.
If you are interested in finding out more, please contact the Tuck School at Dartmouth by call
ing (603)646-2831. Applications are now being accepted.
Check out our WEB site at <http://www-Tuck.dartmouth.edu/>.
