Hitler and the Decisions for the Final Solution
A Lecture by Professor Christopher Browning
Department of History, Pacific Lutheran College; Research Fellow, U.S. Holocaust Memorial Museum.
Professor Browning is a distinguished Holocaust scholar and author of Ordinary Men, the Path to
Genocide, and Fateful Months: Essays on the Emergence of the Final Solution.
Tiiesday, March 4,1997 at 8:00pm
Kresge Auditorium, School of Law
Reception and book signing will follow lecture.
This lecture is free and open to the public. It is supported by a grant from the Jewish Community
Endowment Fund of the Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco, the Peninsula, Marin, and
Sonoma Counties.
For more information, please call 415-723-7589
