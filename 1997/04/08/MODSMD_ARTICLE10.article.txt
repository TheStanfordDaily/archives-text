CORRECTIONS POLICY
It is the policy of ThG Stanford Daily to correct all significant er
rors brought to the attention of the editors.

editorial, column, headline, photo or graphic printed in The Daily,
please contact Editor in Chief Elizabeth Goldman at (415) 725-2100
