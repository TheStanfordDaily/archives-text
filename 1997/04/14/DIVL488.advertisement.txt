THE DAILY'S SCHEDULE
FOR ASSU ELECTION
ENDORSEMENTS
Tomorrow The Daily will endorse one ticket for
ASSU president and vice president; it will also issue
endorsements on all 30 special-fee requests.
Wednesday and Thursday, the days of the election, The Daily
will print condensed versions of all its endorsements.
On those days, it will reserve an extended Letters space
in which to print responses to all its endorsements.
For complete text of The Daily's
ASSU election endorsements,
visit The Daily Online,
http://daily.stanford.org/assu
