# World & Nation
## Guess who's coming to the embassy 
### 

Routers
Oscar-winning Hollywood star Sidney Poitier speaks with aides in front of a Tokyo hotel yesterday
after he formally presented his credentials as the new Bahamian ambassador to Japan. Poitier,
who has no plans to live in Japan, will carry out his duties while continuing to make movies around
the world. He has appeared in 46 films during a 47-year career, including roles in "Guess Who's
Coming to Dinner," "In the Heat of the Night" and "Sneakers." He won best actor at the 1963 Acade
my Awards for his role in "Lilies of the Field."
