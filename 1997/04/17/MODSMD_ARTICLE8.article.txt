# Election continues
## 
### 
The ASSU Spring Quarter General Election
will be held today. Polling closes tonight at 11:59
p.m. Students must have a leland account in order
to vote on the World Wide Web. The URL is
http://election.stanford.edu.

In addition, paper ballots will be available from
10 a.m. to 4 p.m. both days at Tresidder Union from 10 a.m. to 4
p.m., the Ix'iw School from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m., the Business School
from 11 a.m. to 3 p.m. and at the Med Student Cafe from 11 a.m.
to 1 p.m. Voters must show a valid II).
Further questions about voting procedures should Ik; directed
to Elections Commissioner Ghazal Badiozamani at 723-2839.
