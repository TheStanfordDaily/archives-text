Applied Decision Analysis, Inc.
ATTENTION SENIORS!
Applied Decision Analysis, Inc. (ADA) wants to meet graduating
seniors who are interested in quantitative analysis and management
consulting. If your course work includes mathematics, statistics,
computer science, industrial engineering/operations research, physics or
other quantitative sciences, we would like to meet you.
ADA is a small, growing consulting company, located in Menlo Park, that
applies sophisticated analytical techniques to real-world problems.
ADA'S strength is high quality work in areas such as strategic planning,
decision analysis, operations management, analysis of public policy,
forecasting markets for new products, R&D planning, and basic research.
We would like to meet students who have:
An interest in solving important, complex problems,
■# Skills in a broad range of mathematical techniques,
Communication skills to present analytical results
in a clear, concise manner, and
A high level of enthusiasm for challenging work in
an informal atmosphere.
We have a limited number of positions available for MS and PhD graduates.
If you are interested, please send a cover letter,
resume, and transcripts to:
Gill Hartford
Applied Decision Analysis, Inc.
2710 Sand Hill Road, Menlo Park, CA 94025
