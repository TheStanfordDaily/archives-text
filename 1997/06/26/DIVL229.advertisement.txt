HEALTHY VOLUNTEERS NEEDED
FOR DIARRHEA STUDY
Compensation is $75 per day ($300) for completing the four day study.
Subjects will be admitted to the General Clinical Research Center at
Stanford University Hospital. They will drink one of several strains of E.
coli that may cause mild diarrhea; the chances of becoming ill are about
one in three. All expenses will be paid and volunteers will be under the
continuous care of expert staff and physicians. Volunteers will receive a
thorough medical evaluation. An additional $150 will be offered to
patients who undergo endoscopy. If interested call (415) 725-4762.
