HOW TO PLACE AN AD
BY PHONE BY FAX
Call 415-723-2555 Ext. 302 Call 415-725-IFAX
Monday thru Friday from 9AM-2PM Include Credit Card # and Exp Date
BY MAIL
Mail to; The Stanford Daily, Storke Bldg., Stanford, CA 94305, Attn. Classifieds En
close text, dates to run, your name & phone, payment (check or credit card #
with expiration date) - ID Mail Code #2240. Also email to above address
OFFICE VISIT
Come to the above address, Monday thru Friday 9AM - 2PM
RATES & DEADLINE
SUMMER RATE
$3.25/32 character line for 2 issues
Boxed/Dispay Rates Available
AD DEADLINE
Regular line ads ( new ads, changes, cancellations):
12 noon, one business day preceding publication
Classified Display ads ( new ads, changes, cancellations):
3 p.m. two business days preceding publication
DEADLINE & PAYMENT
PAYMENT
Classified ad must be paid in full at the time of placement None will
be billed, VISA and MasterCard are accepted, with a 2 line minimum
TERMS & POLICIES
No refunds are given for cancelled classified ads Check your ad the first day it
runs; The Stanford Daily will only assume errors the first day the ad runs
