# Art
## 
### 
Through Sept. 7
The Stanford Art Gallery presents
"Insula Canihalium: Paintings
and Works on Paper by Enrique
Chagoya." Assistant professor of
art Chagoya's twenty paintings
and works on paper deal with is
sues of race, religion and politics.
Gallery hours are 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.
Tuesday through Friday, and 1 to
5 p.m. Saturday and Sunday. Ad
mission is free.


Courtesy ol Disney
Not new but recommended: Disney's grand return to form, "Her
cules," playing at the Century 16 in Mountain View (1500 Shoreline).
