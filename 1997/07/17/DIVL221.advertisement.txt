If You Like Shakespeare ...
You'll Love Stanford Flying Club!
♦ Fly to the Ashland (Oregon) Shakespeare festival in
less than two hours in a private airplane lor less than

♦ Shakespeare and many other adventures in the

♦ Most Members complete our Solo Pilot License
