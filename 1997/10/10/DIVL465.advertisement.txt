SCHOLARSHIP OPPORTUNITIES IN MATHEMATICS,
THE NATURAL SCIENCES OR ENGINEERING FOR ELIGIBLE
SOPHOMORES & JUNIORS
• If you are a sophomore or junior interested in a career in
mathematics, the natural sciences, or engineering
• YOU may be eligible for the Barry M. Goldwater Scholarship.
• Awards cover up to a maximum of $7,500 per year for expenses
such as tuition, fees, books and room & board.
HOW TO APPLY
For application information pertaining to the Barry M. Goldwater
Scholarship, contact Samantha Meritt in the Humanities & Sciences
Dean's Office at 725-1572, or shamiran@leland. Completed applications
must be returned to Ms. Meritt no later than noon on December 1, 1997.
