ETHICS IN SOCIETY
PROGRAM
The Ethics in Society Program is an interdisciplinary
honors program which encourages students to reHect on
fundamental issues of moral and political philosophy.
Such issues include: the nature and implications of
treating people with equal dignity and respect; the scope
of liberty; tne legitimacy of government; and the
meaning of responsibility. The program poses these
issues, and others in the context of debates which have
arisen in our common public life.
Would you like to join the program?
Find out about writing a thesis, and connecting political
philosophy with public service.
Meet with faculty and students involved
in the program over pizza.
Where: Building 90 (Philosophy) Rm 92Q
When: 5:30 pm, Monday, October 20, 1997
