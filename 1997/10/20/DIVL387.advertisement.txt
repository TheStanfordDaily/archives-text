Special research grants
available:
Chappell-Lougee Scholarships
If you are planning to major in the
humanities or social sciences, you
can apply for up to $2500 for a research
project. Pick a topic, choose a faculty
sponsor, and apply by Friday, December
5, 1097.
For further information, stop by
Undergraduate Research Opportunities,
122 Sweet Hall, call 723-3828, or visit
http://www-uro.stanford.edu
