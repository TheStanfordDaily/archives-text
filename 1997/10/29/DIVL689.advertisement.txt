n XILINX®
College Students: Xilinx has great opportunities!
Xiliax supplies the world's most advanced programmable logic
solutions, producing industry-leading device architectures and world
class design software. We invented the FPGA in 1984, creating new
markets with our cutting-edge semiconductor and software
technology. We are continually hinng new college graduates in a
variety of technical positions: IC Design. Customer Applications
Support, Product/Test, Product Device, Product Marketing, Quality,
Reliability, Software, etc... We also have openings for Financial
Analysts, Accountants, Planner/Analysts, and MIS programmer/
analysts.
If you want to be part of a team that has it all - dynamic and friendly
culture, cutting-edge technology, the chance to contribute and
influence, stock options, stock purchase plan, excellent compensation
then join us! Check us out when we are on campus or email your
resume to lisa.yamamoto@xilinx.com or fax to (408) 559-4726 or
mail to: Xilinx, College Relations, HR, 2100 Logic Drive, San Jose,
CA 95124
Xilinx will be on campus!
