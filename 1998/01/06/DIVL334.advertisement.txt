Fact Set, a leading provider of online financial, market,
and economic information, will be recruiting on campus
for
Software Engineers
Consultants
More than 10,000 of the world's leading fund managers
and investment banks use Fact Set to research and
analyze company and stock market news and
information.
A growing company, Fact Set has a solid financial record
and offers a challenging work environment as well as the
opportunity to participate in the company's success.
At Fact Set, Software Engineers design the databases
and applications our clients use every day The
Consultants then take a thorough understanding of that
technology and combine it with their finance knowledge
to provide the highest degree of Fact Set utility to the
clients. Both Software Engineers and Consultants work
closely with all areas of the company to ensure that
Fact Set's technology is highly responsive to the changing
needs of the investment community.
Fact Set is headquartered in Greenwich, Connecticut, and
has offices in San Mateo, London, and Tokyo.
For more information, contact our recruiting coordinator,
Darlene Lacey, at 203.863.1568 or visit us on the web at
www factset com
Look for us February 3 at our information session in the
Tresidder Memorial Union at 6 30pm.
We'll return to Stanford for interviews on February 11
See your placement office for details.
Fact Set is an equal opportunity employer
