Stanford Bachelors
The Stanford Bachelors is a charitable organization made up of single Stanford Alumni.
Our organization puts on singles dance parties in the Bay Area to raise funds for
Stanford based charitable causes. To find out more about us check out our website at
HYPERLINK http://www.Stanfordßachelors.org www.Stanfordßachelors.org.
Scholarships Available
Up to 6 scholarships will be awarded in the next year, one $5,000 and five $1,000
scholarships are available. Eligibility requirements: 1) must be at least 21 years of age
and a Stanford student; 2) must meet high academic standards; 3) must be a member
of the Stanford bachelors and actively participate our charitable activities. Note that
undergraduate, graduate and medical students are eligible. For information write to:
Stanford Bachelors, attn Scholarship Chairman, PO. Box 2345, Stanford, CA 94309.
THE HUMANITIES AND SCIENCES DEAN'S OFFICE IS SEEKING APPLICATIONS FOR THE
Morris K. Udall Scholarship
and
Excellence in National Environmental Policy Program
Awards will be made on the basis of merit to two groups of students:
1) those who intend to pursue careers in environmental public policy.
2) to Native American and Alaska Native students who intend to pursue careers in health
care or tribal public policy.
Applicants must be sophomores or juniors in the current academic year, 1997-98, have an
average of at least a B or the equivalent, and be a U.S. citizen, U.S. national, or resident alien.
Winners may be awarded a maximum of $5,00<) covering expenses such as tuition, fees, books,
and room and board for one year.
HOW TO APPLY
Application information and complete rules of eligibility, are available from Jodi Berardino (jodib@leland)
in the Humanities and Sciences Dean's Office, Building One, Room 208.
Completed application materials are due in the Dean's Office by noon on Friday, February 20, 1998.
