Stanford Bachelors
The Stanford Bachelors is a charitable organization made up of single Stanford Alumni.
Our organization puts on singles dance parties in the Bay Area to raise funds for
Stanford based charitable causes. To find out more about us check out our website at
HYPERLINK http://www.Stanfordßachelors.org www.Stanfordßachelors.org.
Scholarships Available
Up to 6 scholarships will be awarded in the next year, one $5,000 and five $1,000
scholarships are available. Eligibility requirements: 1) must be at least 21 years of age
and a Stanford student; 2) must meet high academic standards; 3) must be a member
of the Stanford bachelors and actively participate our charitable activities. Note that
undergraduate, graduate and medical students are eligible. For information write to:
Stanford Bachelors, attn Scholarship Chairman, PO. Box 2345, Stanford, CA 94309.
