BUSINESS ANALYST POSITION
SC A ( onsulting is a management consulting firm which assists senior
management in creating and sustaining shareholder value. The firm has achieved
demonstrable success tor its clients by developing performance measurement
programs, compensation plans, and value-management programs that focus
executives on achieving their business objectives. Our client base includes
numerous l ortune 500, high growth, and high technology companies.
As a Business Analyst at SCA, you will:
• Analyze strategic, financial, and organizational data and develop
recommendations from these analyses
• Work directly with SCA partners and client executives
• Experience the challenge of high-level exposure and responsibility
inherent in the entrepreneurial small firm
• Gain insight into multiple industries
Qualities of the ideal candidate include:
• Fop academic and achievement records
• Strong analytic and quantitative skills
• Intellectual curiosity and sound judgment
• Exceptional interpersonal skills with the maturity and poise to interact
with senior management immediately
• Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
In addition to providing a challenging long-term career path, the Business Analyst
position oilers outstanding preparation tor business or other graduate schools.
