Undergraduate Students

In Boston, New York and San Francisco
Cowen & Company is seeking accomplished, motivated individuals for our two-year
Financial Analyst Program in our Boston, New York and San Francisco Investment
Banking Practices. Strong academic performance, superior analytical, computer and
communication skills and exceptional interpersonal skills are essential.
Cowen Financial Analysts work on a variety of challenging assignments. Typical
responsibilities include financial and strategic analyses such as structuring and
executing equity and debt offerings, and identifying, evaluating and implementing
mergers and acquisitions - always with the ongoing guidance and instruction from
senior Investment Banking professionals and extensive interaction with our clients.
