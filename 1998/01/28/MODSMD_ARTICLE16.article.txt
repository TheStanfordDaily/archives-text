# This week in Stanford history January 26, 1942
## 
### 
Fifty-six years ago this week the
Stanford men's basketball team
routed California 51-35, a win
that helped catapult Stanford men's
basketball to its only NCAA Champi
onship in the school's history. The leg
endary IIM2l l M2 team, coached by Everett
S. Dean, swept a four game series with

California, beat UCLA four times and
went on to beat Dartmouth in the final
rounds of the NCAA tournament held
in Kansas City, MO. The team finished
the season with a record of 28-4 and an
11-1 record in the PCC (South). After
expenses, Stanford's share of the
NCAA pot was $93.75.
