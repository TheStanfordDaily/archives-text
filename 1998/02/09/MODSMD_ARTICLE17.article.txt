# Ralph Reed
## 
### 
• Former executive director of the Christian Coalition, a group organized by Pat Robert
son to help infuse politics with religious values. The coalition claims a membership of 1.8
million people.
• President of Security Strategies, a campaign and marketing company for conservative
political candidates.
• outspoken conservative on issues such as abortion, pornography and gay rights.
• strong proponent of "family values," including fidelity and Christian faith.
