MUSIC AT STANFORD
CONCERTS PRESENTED BY THE MUSIC DEPARTMENT
EARLY MUSIC SINGERS
Fri., February 13, 8:00 pm • Memorial Church • Free
With special guests, The Whole Noyse. Monteverdi: late Venetian vespers
for St. Valentine. The third in the series of Historical Vespers Concerts.
ALEA 11: NEW MUSIC ENSEMBLE
Wed., Feb.' 18, 8:00 pm • Campbell Recital Hall • Free
Featuring Jonathan Harvey's Chant, with Keith Chapin, viola; and
Karlheinz Stockhausen's Mantra, with pianists Thomas Schultz and
Joan Nagano. Program hosted by Jonathan Harvey.
Tickets at Stanford Ticket Office, (650) 725-2787 or at the door.
