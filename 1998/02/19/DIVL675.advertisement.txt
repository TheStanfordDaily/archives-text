EXPECTING TO
GRADUATE
IN APRIL OR JUNE?
UNDERGRADUATE
The deadline to apply to graduate WINTER
QUARTER (April 2, 1998) or SPRING QUAR
TER Pune 14, 1998) is February 22, 1998.
The last day to notify the Undergraduate Degree
Progress Office that you do NOT want a Parent
Commencement Information Packet automatically
mailed to your Parent Mailing Address (as listed
in Axess) is MARCH 6,1998.
GRADUATE
Apply early to graduate for WINTER or SPRING
graduation by February 22, 1998 to insure the
printing of a diploma for June Commencement.
The deadline to apply to graduate for WINTER
QUARTER (April 2, 1998) is March 13, 1998
The deadline to apply to graduate for SPRING
QUARTER (June 14, 1998) is June 3, 1998.
Parent information will be available to graduate
students through their departments in late April.
File your application to graduate through Axess. From the Axess Main Menu,
select the Graduation function and then select "apply to graduate" option.
