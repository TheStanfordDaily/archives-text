MUSIC AT STANFORD
CONCERTS PRESENTED BY THE MUSIC DEPARTMENT
CHAMBER CHORALE
Fri., Feb. 20, 8 pm • Memorial Church • $8/$4 students
Stephen M. Sano, director. There is Sweet Music: folk and art music
from the British Isles and North America. Works by Elgar, Delius,
Stanford, Butter and others.
STANFORD WOODWIND QUINTET
Sat., Feb. 21, 8 pm • Dinkelspiel Auditorium • $8/$4 students
Featuring Mozart's Serenade in C Minor, K. 388. Also music by William
Grant Still, John Harbison, and Ruth Crawford Seeger.
Tickets at Stanford Ticket Office, (650) 725-2787 or at the door.
