A five-unit course on the theory and practice of teaching public speaking, ElOO trains students to serve as instructors
in the Technical Communications Program's public speaking courses. ElOO offers a hands-on introduction to designing
an effective course, devising tutorial techniques, and teaching delivery, organization, audience analysis, visual aids, and
style. Students deliver lectures, lead discussions, critique each other's speeches, and take part in fieldwork.
Pick up an application, arrange for an interview, and receive additional information from Dave Lougee in Terman 105,
the Technical Communications Program office (telephone: 723-2573; e-mail: dlougee^leland).
Fisher Investments, Inc.
Investment Management Training Program
Fisher Investments, Inc. is a nationally recognized investment management firm based in Woodside, CA
with more than $1.8 billion of institutional and private client assets under management. The firm is
seeking Spring 1998 graduates for positions in its 2-year Investment Management Training Program.
Qualified applicants will possess exceptional analytical, communications, and computer skills; a keen
interest in developing an understanding of the financial markets from the perspective of a buy-side firm; and a
strong desire to participate in the development of a fast-growing business. Responsibilities of the position
include rotations through the following departments: Research and Portfolio Management, Trading, Client
Services, and Sales and Marketing. Successful candidates will have the rare opportunity to gain a broad
exposure to various career opportunities in the investment management industry. Economics and Business
Administration are majors preferred. All majors are welcome.
