AUTHOR READING
MEET JON EISEMSON
author of
BIBLICAL REV ISI oris
and Other Verses
TUESDAY, MARCH 3, AT 4:00 PM
Jon Eisenson, Professor Emeritus, Hearing and Speech Science at
Stanford, will discuss his latest work, Biblical Revisions and Other
Verses. In writing Biblical Revisions, Professor Eisenson tried to Keep the
events as told in the Hebrew Bible, but with his own interpretation of the
events. Most stories have either a commentary and/or a midrash—an
explanation of the events related to his narration. Jon Eisenson is a past
president of the American Speech and Hearing Association and formerly
served as Distinguished Professor of Special Education, San francisco
State University. v 1
