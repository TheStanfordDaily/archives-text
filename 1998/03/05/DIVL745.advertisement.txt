MUSIC AT STANFORD
CONCERTS PRESENTED BY THE MUSIC DEPARTMENT
UNIVERSITY SINGERS
Friday, March 6, 8 pm • Memorial Church • $8/$4 students
Stephen M. Sano, director. Featuring Duruflt's Requiem, Op 9
SYMPHONY ORCHESTRA
Sat., March 7, 8 pm • Dinkelspiel Auditorium • $8/$4 stu.
J. Karla Lemon, director. Chen Yi, Duo Ye; Dvorak, Concerto in B Minor
for Cello and Orchestra, Op. 104, with Alison Hu, cello;
Stravinsky, The Firebird.
SYMPHONIC BAND
Sun., March 8, 2:30 pm • Dinkelspiel Auditorium • $8/$4 stu.
Giancarlo Aquilanti. conductor. Glazunov, Concerto for Saxophone
with soloist Matt Ashcraft; Elgar, Severn Suite; Vaughan Williams,
English Folk Song Suite.
BENJAMIN SIMON, VIOLA
Fri., Mar. 13, Bpm • Dinkelspiel Auditorium • $8/$4 stu.
An eclectic program featuring the viola m duet with various acoustic and
electronic instruments. With Music Department faculty members Stephen
Harrison, Chris Chafe, Frederick Weldy and others.
Tickets at Stanford Ticket Office, (650) 725-2787 or at the door.
