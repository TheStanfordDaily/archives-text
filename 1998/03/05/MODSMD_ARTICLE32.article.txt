March 7 - March 13:
M&M on SCBN
Movies and Music for Dead Week. Watch 24-
hr movies and enjoy all sorts of musical
breaks. Special Stanford performances:
Classical music with Alex Rayskin, swing

and jazz with the Stanford Jazz Orchestra,
hip hop and rap, Taiko and lots more.
Just watch! For a detailed schedule, check the
SCBN web page: www-leland.stanford.edu/
group/SCBN-TV/
You do not need to subscribe to cable to watch
SCBN. Just plug your TV in the cable hook-up and
enjoy the rule. Call 3-9069 to vote for the movie
you want to see, every Saturday night, at 10:30.
