11E PART OF AN EXCITING NEW
PIUMiIIAM AT STANFORD IN ORAL
COMMUNICATION!
If you have a way with the spoken word and a desire to
teach (and earn $9.00 an hour), take Teaching Oral
Communication, CTL 119. In addition to helping you to
refine your own speaking skills, this course will train you to
be a speech tutor in the Center for Teaching and Learning's
new oral communication program.
CTL 119, Teaching Oral Communication. Spr, 3 units,
TTh 2:15 - 4:05 PM Admission by consent of instructor
This course will be taught as a workshop, with readings and
exercises to expand your knowledge of public speech and
rhetoric, and with opportunities to make your own creative
contribution to communication at Stanford.
Interested students should contact Doree Allen at 725-4149
or doree.allen@leland.
Enrollment is limited, so don t delay!
