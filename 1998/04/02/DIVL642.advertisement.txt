Celebrate Founders' Day
Join us in commemorating the founding of Stanford University
and honoring Jane and Leland Stanford.
Sunday, April sth
FOUNDERS' DAI EVENTS:
10:00 a.m.
Mausoleum opens to the public (from 10 - 12 noon).
This is the only day of the year for members of the Stanford
Community to view the interior of the Mausoleum, the final
resting place for the Stanford family.
10:30 a.m.
Mausoleum Program, including the laying of the wreath
and a performance by Talisman (last year's National College
A Cappella Group Champions).
11:30 a.m.
Memorial Church Ceremony. featuring President Casper,
Dean Robert Gregg of Memorial Church, Marian Hammond,
a Junior in American Studies and N. Duru Ahanotu, an
Engineering-Economic Systems Ph.D. graduate student.
12:30 p.m.
Quad Celebration, featuring an old-fashioned, family-style
picnic lunch with barbeque chicken, mashed potatoes, cole
slaw, pie and ice cream. Sponsored by Polio's, Stanford Catering,
and Jamba Juice with music by the Stanford Jay./. Band.
Lite founding family
Come meet Jane & leland and see the Stanford 112 amily horse-drawn carriage!
ALL EVENTS ARE FREE AND OPEN
TO
STUDENTS, FACULTY, STAFF, ALUMNI & FRIENDS OF STANFORD

For more information, visit the Founders Day Website:
http://www-leland.stanford.cdu/--actionj/FD.html
or contact the Dean of Students at 723-2733
