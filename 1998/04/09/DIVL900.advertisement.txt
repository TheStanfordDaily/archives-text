### 
Dean Search Committee
to Hold Open Forum
Members of the Search Committee
for the Dean of the School of
Humanities and Sciences will host
an Open Forum on Thursday,
April 9, from 5:00-6:00 p.m. in
Room 307 of the History Depart
ment, Building 200. Any member
of the Stanford faculty, staff, or
student body is invited to attend the
Forum to discuss issues of concern
in the School as they relate to the
search for the new Dean.
