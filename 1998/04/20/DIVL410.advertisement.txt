Stanford Bookstore
Announces its Annual
Stanford Community
Informational Meeting
April 21, 1998, at 7 pm
Stanford Law School Rm. #80
In accordance with its bylaws, the Stanford
University Bookstore will hold its annual
informational meeting on Wednesday,
January 22, at 7 pm at the Law School,
Room #80. Stanford University Bookstore
President and CEO, Peggy Mendelson and
Board of Directors Chair, Professor David
Kennedy invite members of the Stanford
Community to attend. Members of the
Bookstore's Board of Directors and repre
sentatives of the Bookstore management
team will be available to answer questions
and discuss the concerns of the community.
http://bookstore.stanford.org
A nonprofit organization serving Stanford University since 1897
