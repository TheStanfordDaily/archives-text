Announcing openings as
SOPHOMORE ASSISTANTS (SAs)
for the 1998-99
Sophomore College
August 31 - September 20, 1998
Description of Position
Sophomore College is a set of programs for returning second-year students, who live together in a student residence and work under faculty
supervision in a series of small-group classes during early September, before the start of fall quarter. Each faculty member in the Sophomore College will
be assisted by two SAs, who will also serve as academic advisors and residence staff for the College, and who will report to the Sophomore College
Director. SAs will receive room, board, and $600 for full-time work during the course of the program, and for training at the end of spring quarter.

Applications
Application forms for the SA position are available at 124 Sweet Hall, and are due
there by 5 p.m. on Fri., May 1. Applications may also be submitted through the
Web at www.stanford.edu/group/sophomores/college/. You may apply to serve as
a SA with up to two professors (a separate application will be required for each one).
For further information call 3-4338 or e-mail hf.rgk@forsythe.
Sophomore College student applications will he available April 30.
