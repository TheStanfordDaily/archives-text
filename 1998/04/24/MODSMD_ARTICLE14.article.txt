# March of the Living
## 
### 

Reuters
Jews carry Israeli flags as they walk past the gate of the Auschwitz-Birkenau con
centration camp during the March of the Living in Auschwitz yesterday. More
than 7.000 Jews from 42 countries, including Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin
Netanyahu and Polish prime minister Jerzy Buzek, took a part in the march to
commemorate the victims of the Holocaust.
