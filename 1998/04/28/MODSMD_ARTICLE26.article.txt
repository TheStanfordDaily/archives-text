# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 
In an all-Stanford final at the Pacific 10 Conference Champi
onship in Ojai, Calif., senior Paul Goldstein defeated his regular
doubles partner, junior Ryan Wolters, 6-1, 6-2. Stanford tennis
players won three of the four titles up for contention at the tour
nament.

At New Maloney Field on Sunday, sophomore Hedy Born ;ind
the Cardinal women's lacrosse team steamrolled over Santa
Clara 19-4 in the Northern Division Regional and earned a trip to
next week's Final Four. Born led the Card by slashing through
the Bronco defense for six goals.
