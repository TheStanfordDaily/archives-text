ON THURSDAY, MAY 14, faculty, staff and
students are invited to learn more than they
ever wanted to know about "Interacting
with Industry: Technology Licensing and
Industrial Sponsorship," sponsored by Stan
ford's Office of Technology Licensing.
Stanford has a long history of close, mutually
beneficial ties with industry and this seminar is designed
to assist faculty, staff and students in understanding
Stanford's formal relations with industry, through
sponsored research and technology licensing.
The seminar will be held in the Oak Lounge of
Tresidder Memorial Union from 1:30 pm to 3:30 pm.
Please contact Sandra Bradford in the Office of
Technology Licensing at 725-9037 for more information.
Light refreshments will be provided.
