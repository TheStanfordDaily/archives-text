The Stanford Savoyards announce
AUDITIONS
for Gilbert & Sullivan's
me mikado
For Stanford students only:
Wed., Sept. 23 7 - 9:30 p.m., Braun Rehearsal Hall
Open to all:
Thurs., Sept. 24 7 - 9:30 p.m., Campbell Recital Hall
Sat., Sept. 26 10 a.m. - 12:30 p.m., Braun Rehearsal Hall
Bring a prepared song in English, with sheet music for our accompanist
Callbacks for leads: Sunday, Sept. 27, 1-4 p.m., Braun Rehearsal Hall
Performances: November 6-7, 13-15 in Dinkelspiel Auditorium
All students, faculty, staff, and friends of Stanford are welcome
both onstage and backstage!
In addition to the cast, we need production staff members, technical crew
(sets, lights, costumes, props), rehearsal pianists and orchestra musicians.
For more information or to volunteer, contact our producer:
Kristofer Jennings jennings@sUt.stanford.edu
home (and answering machine): (650) 566-9150
campus office: (650) 725-5988
