Hillel Foundation
at Stanford University
Welcomed all
students, faculty, staff, and community members to our
Rosb Hasbanab and Yom Kippur Services
September 20-22 and 29-30
Reform Services will be held in Memorial Auditorium
Rosb Hasbanab (first day only):
Sunday, Sept. 20: Early Service 6:30 P.M.
Late Service 9:00 P.M.
Monday, Sept. 21: Early Morning Service 8:30 A.M.
Late Morning Service 11:00 A.M.
Conservative Services will be held in Kresge Auditorium
Rosb Hasbanab (both days):
Sunday, Sept. 20: Evening Service 7:00 P.M.
Monday, Sept. 21: Morning Service 8:30 A.M.
Evening Service 7:00 P.M.
Tuesday, Sept. 22: Morning Service 8:30 A.M.
Services led by
Rabbi Yoel Kabn, Executive Director, Hillel at Stanford
Rabbi Patricia Karlin-Neuman, Associate Dean for Religious Life
Rabbi Amy Bardack, Conservative Service Leader
Those who wish to attend Orthodox services should call Hillel for information.
Please join your fellow student* for our pre-Rosb Hasbananb Sinner
Sunday, Sept.2o, 5 P.M.
Branner Dining Hall
Free to students (please bring your dining card if you have one)
RSVP is requested
