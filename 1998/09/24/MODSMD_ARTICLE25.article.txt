Music
Sept. 26
San Jose Symphony's Gala Concert will be a salute to George Gershwin on the
100 th anniversary of his birth. 8 p.m. @ San Jose Center for the Performing Arts.
For tickets and information call (408) 288-2828. $25-75.
Sept. 26
Palo Alto Chamber Orchestra performs its first concert of the season. 8 p.m. @
Gunn High School's Spangenberg Theatre, 780 Arastradero Rd. Information and
reservations: 856-3848. $8-10.
Sept. 28
Stanford Organ Series presents Roger Sherman, from St. Mark's Cathedral, on the
Fsik-Nanney organ (the big one!). 8 p.m. @ Memorial Church. Free to Stanford stu
dents with I.D.
Film:
Sept. 30
"Ponette," by Jacques Dalton. Presented by the French Cin6-Club of Palo Alto. 8
p.m. @ the Cultural Center Auditorium, 1313 Newell Rd. For more information call
325-3831. $6 general admission.
Theater
Sept 3-Oct. 4
"Fool Moon," American Conservatory Theater's Bay Area premiere of this work.
Geary Theater in San Francisco. Info: (415) 749-2250. Sept. 12-27

"The Desk Set," Palo Alto Players' production of William Marchant's 1950's period
spoof. Bpm Lucie Stern Theater. Info: (650) 329-0891. Tickets are $22-$24, stu
dents receive a $3 discount.
Museums
Ongoing
Sept 19-Jan. 3
"Holding Patterns: Selections from the Photography Collection of Arthur
Goodwin," an exhibition of 60 images by major photographers. SJ Museum of Art.
Hours: Tues. thru Sun., 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.; Thurs. 10 a.m. — Bpm, closed Mon. $4
students.
Tuesday through Thursday: "Body Works," a figurative art show featuring work by
over 30 local artists. 11 a.m. to 5 p.m.
Other
Sept. 25-27
"Critical Resistance: Beyond the Prison Industrial Complex," a conference on crim
inal justice. Speakers include Gloria Steinem and Maxine Waters. UC Berkeley.
Info: (510) 643-2094. Pay what you can.
Sept. 26
"Fall Fest" presented at the San Francisco Zoo. 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. @ Sloat
Boulevard and the Pacific Ocean. Information: (415) 753-7080. Free with paid Main
Zoo admission.
