Interested in TUtoring?
If you are a returning tutor or a new tutor interested in
tutoring during the 1998/99 academic year, please be
aware that tutoring is now handled by Lynn Freeman,
Tutor Coordinator, at the Undergraduate Advising
Center. Please stop by the UAC desk on the first floor
of Sweet Hall to fill out returning tutor or new tutor
applications. Or contact Lynn Freeman at
3-2426, email: lfreeman@leland.

the first floor of Sweet Hall or call 3-2426.
