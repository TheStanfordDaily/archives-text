J R. Morgan is inviting applications from Stanford University
graduate students and graduating seniors for full-time positions in Tokyo
for the following areas:
Investment Banking
Markets
Credit Research
Technology
Consulting Group
Financial

lytical and quantitative aptitude, strong interpersonal and communication
skills, ability to work as part of a team, and an interest in our global finan

October 10/ 1998 to "recruit_japan@jpmorgan.com" bye-mail
and are encouraged to meet our Tokyo representatives at the following
career fair:
Nikkei Boston Career Forum

at the Boston World Trade Center
