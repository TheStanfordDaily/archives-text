# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

Tomoeh Murakami — Daily file
Carly Smolak
Stanford's sophomore goalie, Smolak was named the Pacif
ic 10 Conference's Women's Soccer Player of the Week after
helping the Cardinal hold No. 2 Santa Clara scoreless in Sunday
afternoon's game with the Broncos. Recording 10 saves, five of
which came in overtime, Smolak gave the Cardinal a 0-0 tie.


Rebecca Roberts — Daily file
Jamie Clark
The Cardinal's senior defender, Clark scored Stanford's game
winning goal against USF last weekend. A midfielder and de
fender, he played a crucial role in shutting out the Dons with his
intense defensive play. Clark and teammates play in the Stan
ford Invitational this weekend.
