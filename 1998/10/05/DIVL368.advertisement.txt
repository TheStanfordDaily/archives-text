ASSU
BUREAU
Ambassador Richard Holbrooke, scheduled by the
ASSU Speakers Bureau to speak in Kresge
Auditorium tonight at 7:00 p.m., was forced to post
pone his lecture in order to meet with Yogoslavian
President Slobodan Milosevic about the situation in
Kosovo. The lecture will be rescheduled sometime
later this month. The ASSU Speakers Bureau asks
that all current ticket holders discard their tickets
and apologizes for any inconveniences this may
have caused. Once the event is rescheduled, the
time and date will be advertised across campus.
