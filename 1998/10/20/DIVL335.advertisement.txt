Stanford University
Program in Jewish Studies
presents
The Fifth Annual Lectures in Yiddish Studies
Professor Janet Hadda,
Department of Germanic Studies, UCLA
Professor Hadda is the author of Isaac Bashevis Singer: A Life,
Passionate Women, Passive Men: Suicide in Yiddish Literature,
and a biographical study of the poet, Yankev Glatshteyn
Isaac Bashevis Singer: Family as Fate and Fortune (in English)
Tuesday, October 20, 1998, 8:00 p.m.
Annenberg Auditorium, Cummings Art Building
Ashkenaz: Memory and Fantasy/Ashkenaz: Zikom un Fantazye (in Yiddish)
Wednesday, October 21, 1998, 5:00 p.m.
History Corner, Building 200, Room 2
Lectures are free and open to the public.
Sponsored by the annual Yiddish Studies Fund.
For more information, please call 650-723-7589
