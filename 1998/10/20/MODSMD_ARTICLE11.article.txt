# Tyson gets fight license back
## 
### 

Reuters
Boxer Mike Tyson pretends to bite the ear of his wife Monica Turner Tyson
before the start of his re-licensing hearing yr-sterday before the Nevada State
Athletic Commission in Las Vegas. The Commission voted four to one to allow
Tyson to fight again. Tyson had his license revoked for biting the ears of Evan
der Holyfield in a heavyweight title fight in1997.
