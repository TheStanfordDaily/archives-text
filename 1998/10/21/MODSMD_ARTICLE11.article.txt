# Pinochet under arrest in London
## 
### 

Reuters
Pinochet supporters yell slogans against the Spanish government during a
protest outside the residence of the Spanish ambassador in Santiago yesterday.
Pinochet was arrested in London Oct. 17 while on a visit to Great Britain for med
ical treatment and is being held under armed guard in London following an
appeal for his extradition to Spain to face charges of genocide and torture.
