# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

Kevin Scheirer — Daily file
Troy Walters
After missing part of the season due to a sprained ankle,
flanker Troy Walters has returned strong. Walters scored
one touchdown and grabbed 131 yards on 10 receptions,
Thursday at Arizona State, making highlight reels across the
nation with his spectacular catches.


Miler Lee — Daily file
Monique Leroux
Leading the Stanford field hockey team in goals and assists,
and thus the team leader in points, senior forward Monique
Leroux helped the Cardinal get back on track this weekend.
Stanford defeated Pacific, 5-1, as Leroux scored her 11th
goal of the season.
