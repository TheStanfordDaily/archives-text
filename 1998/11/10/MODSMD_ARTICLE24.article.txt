# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

Kwezi Leggett — Daily file
Kcll> Adainson
The senior came through in a big way for the Cardinal on Senior
Day, scoring two goals in the Cardinal's 4-0 victory against Ore
gon. She also added an assist in a 4-0 victory against Oregon State
on Sunday to help the Cardinal earn an at-large berth for the
NCAA tournament.


Miler Lee — Daily file
Lee Morrison
Morrison contributed two goals in last weekend's two games,
the final homestand of the season for the Cardinal. His goal
against Cal State-Fullerton on Sunday clinched a 1-1 double
overtime tie for Stanford. The freshman starter has made an im
mediate impact on the Farm with his strong defensive play.
