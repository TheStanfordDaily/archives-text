National Security Studies
Edmund A. Walsh School of Foreign Service
The Georgetown University National Security Studies
Program offers a Master of Arts degree in security stud
ies at one of the nation's most prestigious universities.
The program is ideal for students seeking careers in
the defense community, whether in the government, the
private sector, or the non-profit community. 97% of
graduates report job placements within three months of
graduation.
If you are interested in learning more about graduate
study in national security and international relations at
Georgetown, a recruiter will be on campus to answer
your questions. Come meet:
Patricia Clune
Program Administrator
Thursday, 19 November 1998
10:00 am to 12:00 noon
Building 60 (Main Quad), Room 6211
