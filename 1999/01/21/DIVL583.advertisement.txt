The Walt Disney Studios
seeks
Graduating Seniors
for
Financial Analyst
opportunities
The Walt Disney Studios is an integral part of a global corporation, synonymous
with family entertainment. Encompassing the creation, production, promotion, sales,
acquisition, and distribution of motion pictures, television programs, videocassette
releases, and music and soundtrack recordings domestically and internationally, the
Studio is dedicated to continuing the rich tradition established by Walt Disney seven
decades ago.
The Walt Disney Studios will be interviewing Stanford's graduating
seniors for the opportunity to be Financial Analysts directly involved
in financial forecasting and reporting for our operating units. Work
responsibility is diverse and includes preparing the annual operating and
five-year strategic planning processes, determining the projected profit or
loss of a new film release, and analyzing potential product acquisitions for
home video. In addition, analysts work on a variety of other special
projects related to their business unit.
Applicants should have strong analytical skills, a record of outstanding
academic achievement and be dedicated to working in the entertainment
industry.
For consideration, please fax or mail your cover letter, resume and
transcripts, postmarked by Friday, February 5, 1999.
