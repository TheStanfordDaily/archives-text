# World & Nation
## Sifting through the rubble 
### 

Reuters
Survivors of the earthquake look for belongings amid the rubble in the town of Armenia, Columbia
yesterday. More than 1,000 people were killed and more than 2,700 injured when a powerful earth
quake, measuring 6.0 on the Ritcher scale, ripped through Colombia's central coffee-growing
region Monday.
