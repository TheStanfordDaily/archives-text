Possession of CLAD or BCLAD is desirable.
• Special Education • Spanish • Music
• Elementary • Math • Physical Education
• English • Science • Health
The District also seeks highly motivated ALTERNATIVE
CERTIFICATION teacher candidates in the following areas:
• Special Education • Mathematics • Physical Education
• Elementary • English • Health
• Elementary Bilingual • Science • Music
• Spanish
Earn $31,926-$55,672 per year.
Bilingual Differential: up to $5,000 per year
