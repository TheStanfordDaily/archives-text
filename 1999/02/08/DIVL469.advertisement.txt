Fact Set, a leading provider of online financial, market, and
economic information, will be recruiting on campus for
Software Engineers
Consultants
More than 17,000 of the world's leading fund managers
and investment banks use Fact Set to research and analyze
company and stock market news and information.
A growing company, Fact Set has a solid financial record
and offers a challenging work environment as well as the
opportunity to participate in the company's success.
At Fact Set, Software Engineers design the databases and
applications our clients use every day. The Consultants
then take a thorough understanding of that technology and
combine it with their finance knowledge to provide the
highest degree of Fact Set utility to the clients. Both
Software Engineers and Consultants work closely with all
areas of the company to ensure that Fact Set's technology
is highly responsive to the changing needs of the
investment community.
Fact Set is headquartered in Greenwich, Connecticut, and
has offices in New York, San Mateo, London, Hong Kong,
Sydney, and Tokyo.
