# History of Investment Responsibility at Stanford
## 
### 
19705: Stanford Board of Trustees estab
lishes Commission on Investment Respon
sibility; first major university to adopt a pol
icy of this kind.
1980s: After careful examination, Stanford
divests of stocks held in 10 companies
doing business in South Africa.
Early 19905: CIR is renamed Advisory
Panel on Investment Responsibility.
Number of shareholder resolutions consid
ered by APIR continues to increase.
1997: Student proposal to establish a sep
arate socially responsible endowment fund
is rejected.
1998-99: Student research position creat
ed to support APIR efforts.
