Honors in Environmental Studies
Interested in creating an individual, dynamic and interdisciplinary thesis?
The Interschool Honors Program brings to together students from the
Schools of Humanities and Sciences, Engineering, and Earth Sciences to
participate in a small group seminar. If you want to graduate with an honors
certification in Environmental Science, Technology, and Policy, come to an
informational meeting (all class years welcome):
Wednesday, February 24, 1999
4:00 p.m.
Center for Environmental Science and Policy
Institute for International Studies
Goldman conference Room,
4th floor Encina East
Questions? Contact Roz Naylor, director of the Honors Program at 723-5697
or roz@leland
