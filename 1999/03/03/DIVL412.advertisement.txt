An Open Invitation
To All Members of the Stanford Community
To Make Recommendations for the
Gores, Dinkelspiel, and Cuthbertson Awards.
These awards are given annually at
University Corrunencement Ceremonies in June.
Students are encouraged to submit nominations.
For Excellence in Undergraduate or Graduate Teaching
The Walter J. Gores Awards recognize excellence of teaching at any level.
Awards are made to:
• a senior faculty member or senior lecturer
• a junior faculty member or member of the teaching staff
• a teaching assistant (undergraduate or graduate student)
For Distinctive Contributions to
Undergraduate Education
The Lloyd W. Dinkelspiel Awards recognize distinctive contributions to the
education of Stanford Undergraduates. Awards are made to:
• two graduating seniors
• a faculty or staff member with 10 or more years of service
• a faculty or staff member with less than 10 years service
For Exceptional Service to Stanford University
The Kenneth M. Cuthbertson Award recognizes contributions of all kinds to
Stanford University.
• ordinarily one award is made.
Submit a letter describing the nominee's accomplishments to the Subcommittee on University and Departmental
Honors, c/o Office of the Registrar. Old Union Room MC3005. Beginning with this year's awards, the winners
will be announced prior to the end of Spring Quarter; the award presentation will continue to be made at the
University Commencement Ceremonies.
Nominations received hy Monday April 5, 1999 will be considered fot this Spring's awards.
http://www.stanford.edu/dept/registrar/awards/indcx.html
