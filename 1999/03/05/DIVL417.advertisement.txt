PRESENTS:

Monday, March 8,1999
10:30 A.M. -11:30 A.M.
Room 330, Herbert Hoover Memorial Building,
The Hoover Institution
Dr. Allen Weinstein is President and founder of The Center for Democracy, a
non-profit foundation created in 1985 to promote and strengthen the democratic
process, based in Washington D. C. His books include The Haunted Wood:
Soviet Espionage in America • The Stalin Era (Random House, 1998) and
Perjury: The Hiss-Chambers Case (Knopf, 1978).
