The Donald and Robin Kennedy
Jewish Studies
Undergraduate Award
The Program in Jewish Studies at Stanford
University announces the annual Donald and
Robin Kennedy Undergraduate Award.
The competition honors the best essay written
by an undergraduate at Stanford on a Jewish
theme during the 1998-99 academic year.
One prize of $500 will be awarded. Essays must
be typed, double-spaced, and accompanied by a
cover letter with the name of the contestant.
The contestant's name should not appear on
the essay itself.
Please submit three (3) copies to
The Program in Jewish Studies
Building 240
Room 103
Stanford University
Stanford, California 94305.
Deadline for submission of the
essay is May 14, 1999.
