CRITICAL PERSPECTIVES
ON POPULAR CULTURE
Introduces critical perspectives on popular
culture, including Hollywood and Indian film,
Latin American and U.S. soap operas, popu
lar music, and video. Issues: The relationship
between film and nationhood, television sit
coms and racial identitiy, soap operas and
gender, and video and ethnicity.
