HObJ TO LURE A TECHIE
Act like you think his start-up company will actually make money.
Tell him your dad is Bill Gates.
Write him love letters in binary.
Decorate your bedroom like Sweet Hall.
Reassure him he's a sensitive hunk, not just a dork with poor social skills.
HOW TO LURE A FUZZY
Pretend to enjoy the haikus he wrote about his cocker spaniel's eating disorder.
Agree that his 60-unit major is really as hard as engineering because
writing is so much more challenging than problem sets.
Always say "Darling, you're so deep"
Deride his corporate sell-out friends who are getting jobs after graduation.
Assure him that really, you'll come visit him when he's
practicing poetic performance art in Tijuana.
