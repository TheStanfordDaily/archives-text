Institute for International Studies
O'Bie Shultz Fellowships in International Studies
Grants for graduate students to assist with costs associ
ated with advanced work in international studies.
Awards support modest travel and research expenses
during periods of dissertation field research conducted
abroad, or for supplemental cost-of-living stipends and
tuition for students in the final write-up phase of the
dissertation. Funds are not available to support costs
related to dependents. Field research grants can be used
in any country.
Applications due Monday, April 12, 1999
Applications & information available from the lIS web
site at: http://www-iis.stanford.edu
