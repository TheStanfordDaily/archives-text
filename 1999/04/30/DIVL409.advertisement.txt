The Stanford Daily
is seeking candidates for
positions on the
1999-2000 Board of Directors
1-year Term
Reserved for a
registered student
of Leland Stanford Jr. University.
•
2-year Term
Reserved for a person having
legal knowledge or experience.
•
2-year Term
Reserved for person having
journalistic knowledge or experience
•
Please submit a resume and cover letter to:
Jim Tankersley, Editor in Chief,
by 5 pm Monday, May 17.
Materials may be submitted to The Daily's office in the
Storke Publications Building, via fax at 725-1329, or via
e-mail to eic@daily.stanford.edu.
The Daily staff will vote on candidates on May 20.
Questions? Call 725-2100 ext. 501
▼
Storke Publications Building
Stanford, CA 94305-2240
