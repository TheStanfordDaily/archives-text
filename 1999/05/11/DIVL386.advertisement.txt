Stanford University
Program In Jewish Studies
presents:
The Annual Aaron-Roland Lecture in Jewish Studies
"Jews in Contemporary France:
Between State and Community"
A Lecture by
Pierre Birnbaum
Professor of Political Science, University of Paris-1. Professor Birnbaum is the author of
many books, including Anti-Semitism 'in France: A Political History from Leon Blum to
the Present and The Jews oj the Republic: A Political History oj State Jews oj France
Jrom Gambetla to Vichy
Tuesday, May 11,1999,6:00 p.m.
Tresidder Union, Oak West Lounge
Stanford University
This lecture is free and open to the public.
For more information, please contact the Program in Jewish Studies, 650-723-7589.
