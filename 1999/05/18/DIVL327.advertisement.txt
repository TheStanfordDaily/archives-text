9x9 Industries Literary
Magazine Reading
This reading celebrates and
promotes the first issue of 6,500,
the literary magazine of 9x9
Industries. To be published
twice-yearly, 6,500 already carries
the work of some of the hottest
young poets of the New York and
San Francisco scenes, as well
translations of new Russian and
German material. The first issue
Is edited by Brandon Downing.
9x9 Industries is a San Francisco
consortium of writers combining
Apollonian precision of
language with Dionysian
performance skills. 9x9 A
can be found at X
http://www.paraffin.com. /
