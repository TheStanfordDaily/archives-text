Survey Coordinator Needed RAND
Survey Research Group is seeking a
Survey Coordinator. Must have at least
2 years survey experience and
thorough knowledge of data
collection procedures, particularly
telephone surveys and field projects,
and be familiar with most aspects of
data collection including instrument

sampling plans, and managing day-to
day survey operations. Requires a
8.A./B.S. minimum and a Masters
degree is a plus. The ability to use
computerized data collection software
(especially CASES), spreadsheets, word
processing, and database man
agement software is also preferred.
