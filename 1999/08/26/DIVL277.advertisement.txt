Help
Wanted
The Daily is looking for a graphic designer with
experience in page design and layout. A single full
time positions or multiple part-time positions would
split time between typesetting editorial content and
designing advertisements for a daily newspaper.
The ideal candidate would have a strong working
knowledge of Quark XPress (3.32 and 4.0), Adobe
Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator, be comfortable
working in a relaxed college newspaper environ
ment, and have a desire to teach design skills to
student apprentices.
