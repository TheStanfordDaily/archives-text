Hillel Foundation
at Stanford University
Welcomes all
students, faculty, staff, and community members to our
Yom Kippur Services
September 19 and 20.
Reform Services will be held in Memorial Auditorium
Sunday, Sept. 19: Kol Nidrei Early Service: 6:30 P.M.
Kol Nidrei Late Service: 9:00 P.M.
Monday, Sept. 20: Early Service: 8:30 A.M.
Late Service: 9:00 P.M.
Minchah, Yizkor, Neilah: 5:00 P.M.
Conservative Services will be held in Kresge Auditorium
Sunday, Sept. 19: Kol Nidrei 6:30 P.M.
Monday, Sept. 20: Shaharit 8:30 A.M.
Minchat, Yizkor, Neilah 5:00 P.M.
Services led by
Reform: Rabbi Noa Kiutbner, Director of Education and Programming,
Hillel at Stanford
Rabbi Patricia Karlin-Neuman, Associate Dean for Religious Life
Conservative: Julie Roth, Rabbinical student at
New York Jewish Theological Seminary
Those who wish to attend Orthodox services should call Hillel for information.
Pica** join your fellow students for our Pre-Fast Yom Kippur dinner
Sunday, Sept. 19, 4:30 P.M.
Old Unum Clubhouse Ballroom
