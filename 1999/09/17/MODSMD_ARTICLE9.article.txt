# Gettin' juiced for the freshmen
## 
### 

Adeh DeSandies Daily
Residence staff at Okada House paint their front windows with the dorm's theme, "Tropokada." The
arrival of new students has inspired staff around campus to create other inventive themes as well,
such as "Larkin-n-Out," "Indiana Jones and the Temple of Donner" and "Theta Potata."
