Another Deloitte Consulting Difference
DELOITTE CONSULTING:
Flexibility.
The culture at Deloitte Consulting is different from what you'll find at other consulting firms. You'll find we work in a very collaborative,
collegial team environment where providing results for our clients is our driving mission. As a member of the Deloitte Consulting team, you
will work with premiere global clients solving complex business problems. You will work with bright people affecting change at the highest
levels of the client organization. We do not dictate solutions to clients... we arrive at answers by working things through... together.
So if you favor a team approach, make a smart move and contact us.
We're looking for top performers to participate in either our Systems Analyst or Business Analyst Program.
As a Systems Analyst at Deloitte Consulting, you will work with seasoned professionals on high-profile assignments, as well as take part in
technical and management consulting training courses offered around the country. Aftei training, you will hit the ground running - and take
on meaningful responsibilities and tackle significant business problems leveraging state-of-the-art technologies.
The Business Analyst Program is designed for individuals wishing to pursue an MBA from a top business school. As part of a client
engagement team, you'll develop both consulting and business competencies.
