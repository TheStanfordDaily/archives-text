Sometimes Even Army Ants
Need to Call in the Troops^
Call on Peer Tutoring at the UAC
Are you wondering who can help you get
started on that paper? Are you having trouble
understanding concepts? Is problem solving
getting you down? If so, take advantage of the
Undergraduate Advising Center's peer tutor
drop-in hours offered at several campus
locations. Tutors are ready to help with math,
chemistry, physics, biology, human biology,
economics, and writing.
Tutoring Begins October 3rd
Drop-in Hours: Sun-Thurs, 7pm- 11pm
Check out our website for locations and hours
uac-server.stanford.edu/tutor/
