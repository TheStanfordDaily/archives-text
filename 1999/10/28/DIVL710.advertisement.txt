Expecting a refund this year on
your federal income tax?
Use IRS e-file and get it back
in half the usual time. Even
faster if you specify Direct
Deposit to your bank account!
Click! IRS e-file is simple, fast
and secure.
It's so accurate, there's less
chance you'll get one of those
letters from the IRS.
Many tax professionals can
e-file for you. Or if you do your
return on your PC or Mac, why
not take the next step and file
electronically too.
Even if you owe more tax, you
can still e-file your return early
and wait until April 1 sth to pay.
Any questions? Visit our
Web site at www.irs.ustreas.gov
or see your tax professional.
