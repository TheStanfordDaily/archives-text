STANFORD UNIVERSITY
PROGRAM IN JEWISH STUDIES
presents:
The Eleventh Annual Elsie B. Upset Jewish Community Endowment Fund Lecture
"A Time For Every Purpose:
Yiddish Culture in America During the Holocaust"
by
Anita Norich
Professor of English, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor
Author of The Homeless Imagination in the Fiction of Israel Joshua Singer
Monday, December 6, 1999, 8:00 p.m.
Tresidder Union, Oak Lounge
Stanford University
Reception following.
This lecture is free and open to the public.
It Is supported by a grant from the Elsie B Llpset Fund at the Jewish Community Endowment Fund of the
Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco, the Peninsula, Marin, and Sonoma Counties.
For more information, please call 650-723-7589
