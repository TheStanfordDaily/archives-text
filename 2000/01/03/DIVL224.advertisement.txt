The Walt Disney Studios
The Walt Disney Studios will be
interviewing Stanford's graduating
seniors for the opportunity to be a
Financial Analyst directly involved
in financial forecasting and reporting
for ,our operating units. Work
responsibility is diverse and includes
preparing the annual operating and
five-year strategic plan, determining
the projected profit or loss of a new
film release, and analyzing potential
product acquisitions for home video.
In addition, analysts work on a
variety of other special projects
related to their business unit.
JO
iii i
Applicants should have strong
analytical skills, a record of
outstanding academic achievement
and be dedicated to working in the
entertainment industry.
For consideration, please fax or mail
you cover letter, resume and
transcripts, postmarked by Friday,
January 14, 2000.
