The Department of Microbiology and Immunology Course:
Topics in Microbiology—Ml 185
MWF at 10:00 a.m.
in M 110
This year, the first half of the course will deal with growth, energetics
and regulation to lay the foundation for in-depth coverage of two top
ics in the latter half: Molecular basis of antibiotic resistance and
Molecular bioremediation. These will involve student participation.
Dr. A. C. Matin
Professor of Microbiology & Immunology
