Circulation & Distribution
The Daily is usually delivered between 8 and 1 1 a.m. on weekdays during
the academic year except Dead Week (when one' issue is published on
Wednesday), finals week and holidays. To reguest distribution to your
location or to report a distribution problem, please contact the director of
reader services at (650) 723-2555 x 302.
Subscriptions
First Class Mail subscriptions to the Daily are available by the academic
guarter or the academic year. Subscription rates: For California residents,
$ 166.71 per academic year or $58 46 per guarter. For residents in the
rest of the United States, $ 154 per academic year or $54 per guarter.
Back Issues
Back issues from the past week are available in the lobby of the Storke Publica
tions Building Content from all issues is available at the newspaper's web site
Reprints
Permission to reprint an article or photograph in another publication may be
obtained by contacting the editor in chief at (650) 725-2100. Prints of Daily
photographs for personal use may be purchased from the Photography
Department by calling (650) 725-2100 x 513.
CORRECTIONS
It is the policy of The Stanford Daily to correct all significant errors brought to the
attention of the editors. If you think there is a need for a correction regarding any
article, please contact Editor in Chief Ritu Bhatnagar at (650) 725-2100 x5Ol
or e-mail her at eic@daily.stanford.edu The Daily will investigate all legitimate
reguests for corrections and reserves the right to determine if a printed correction
is necessary. All corrections will be printed in this space.

Circulation & Distribution
The Daily is usually delivered between 8 and 1 1 a.m. on weekdays during
the academic year except Dead Week (when one' issue is published on
Wednesday), finals week and holidays. To reguest distribution to your
location or to report a distribution problem, please contact the director of
reader services at (650) 723-2555 x 302.

First Class Mail subscriptions to the Daily are available by the academic
guarter or the academic year. Subscription rates: For California residents,
