Highlights of Jewish Culture Week 2000
Hillel Foundation at Stanford University
February 21-27, 2000
Art Spiegelman on Comix 101
"Spiegelman has become one of the New York's most sensational artists, in recent years drawing illustrations
for covers that are meant not just to be plainly understood but also to reach up and tattoo your eyeballs with
images once unimaginable in the magazine of moneyed taste. —Los Angeles Times
Saturday night 2/26 at 7pm in the Nathan (ummings Art Building
Admission: $10.00 Stanford Students - free
(co-sponsored by the Program in Jewish Studies and the Koret Foundation)
Merle Feld
Award winning playwright and poet reads from her work:
A Spiritual Life: A Jewish Feminist Journey
Thursday evening 2/24 at 7pm in the Women's Center (433 Santa Teresa, Ist floor)
Free Admission
"God" by Woody Allen
A comedy about Faith, Sex, Reality Theatre, the Marx Brothers, and other Cosmic Matters
Performances in Kresge Auditorium:
Wednesday 2/23 at Bpm, Thursday 2/24 at 9pm, Sunday 2/271pm
Admission: $10.00 Stanford Students $5.00
co-sponsored by Highlights and Hillel, produced by special arrangement with Samuel French, Inc.
For more information call 650-723-1602 or email, Hillel@leland.stanford.edu
