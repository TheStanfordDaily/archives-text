GIBSON, DUNNfe) CRUTCHER
STANFORD INTERNSHIP PROGRAM
Gibson, Dunn & Crutcher LLP one of the nation's largest and most successful law firms,
offers this unique program for recent graduates of Stanford University who are interested
in the legal profession. In addition to participating in pro hono work and Continuing
Legal Education, interns are involved in ail facets of a case as it prepares for trial:
• Legal Research • Data collection
• Evidence review and analysis • Witness interviews
• Research for amicus briefs • Trial preparation & observation
• Document organization & maintenance • Settlement negotiations
If you possess initiative, creativity, research, writing and organization skills, coupled
with an interest in law, Gibson, Dunn & Crutcher LLP Stanford Internship Program
offers an ideal opportunity to experience the legal world firsthand. Please direct
inquiries and/or send a resume, transcript, and cover letter to our Intern Recruitment
Coordinator by March 1, 2000:
James Ecklund
Gibson, Dunn & Crutcher LLP
Jamboree Center
4 Park Plaza
Irvine, CA 92714
(949) 451-3827
The cover letter should address the following: the candidate's interest in the legal
profession and unique aspects of the candidate's background applicable to this position.
See the CPPC Internship Database for more information
