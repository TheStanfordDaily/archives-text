# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 
Scoring a perfect 10 on the floor Monday night, se
nior Larissa Fontaine broke the Stanford record in
the all around competition with a total of 39.825.
Fontaine took all four individual events and led her
team to victory against BYU, as she also had done
just three nights earlier against Cal.

Senior David Moseley sparked the Cardinal's
victory over Cal this weekend with a career-high
28 points in just 20 minutes of play. Moseley is
averaging 1 2.1 points per game this season. The
win boosts the team to the No. 1 ranking in the
country.
