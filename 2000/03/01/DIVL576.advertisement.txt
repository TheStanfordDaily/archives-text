PUBLIC SERVICE
SCHOLARS PROGRAM
2000-2001
INFORMATION SESSION
Wednesday, March 1 st
Pizza Dinner, 6:30 to 7:30 pm
Donald Kennedy Conference Room
Haas Center for Public Service
What kind of knowledge is needed to
create a more just world?
Would you like to write and honors thesis that is
academically sound and useful to the community?
"The Public Service Scholars Program demonstrates how serious
scholarship and community service can be combined in ways that
contribute enormously to both the undergraduates in the program and the
community groups with whom they work." -Professor Leonard Ortolano,
Chair, Urban Studies
Information and applications are available on the Haas Center website:
www.haas.stanford.edu
Or visit the BIRC (Ist floor of the Haas Center) for more information!
APPLICATION DEADLINE: April 10th, 2000
