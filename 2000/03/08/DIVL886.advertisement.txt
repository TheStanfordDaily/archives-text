American Studies 202
New Politics of AIDS Activism:
Impact on Diverse Communities
This course aims to understand the politics of the activism behind the AIDS epidemic in
America. In particular, we will discuss how AIDS activists among different communities, includ
ing blacks, women, gays and lesbians, have responded to the crisis both locally and nationally.
