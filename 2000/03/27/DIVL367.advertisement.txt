The Donald and
Robin Kennedy
Jewish Studies
Undergraduate Award
The Program in Jewish Studies at
Stanford University announces the annual
Donald and Robin Kennedy Undergraduate Award.
The competition honors the best essay written by an
undergraduate at Stanford on a Jewish theme during
the 1999-00 academic year.
One prize of $500 will be awarded.
Essays must be typed, double-spaced, and
accompanied by a cover letter with the name
of the contestant. The contestant's name
should not appear on the essay itself.
Please submit three (3) copies to
The Program in Jewish Studies,
Building 240, room 103,
Stanford University, Stanford , CA 94305.
Deadline
for submission of the essay is
May 12, 2000.
