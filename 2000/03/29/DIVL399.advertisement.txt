Special Course Offerings in Latin American Studies
LAS 181/281
Political Systems of Argentina, Brazil, and Chile
This course will be taught by Argentine sociologist Torcuato Di Telia, professor of sociology at the
Universidad de Buenos Aires and the author of numerous books, including Historia de los partidos poli'ticos
en America Latina and the recently published Los partidos pohticos: teoria y analisis comparativo. Professor
Di Telia is currently the Tinker Visiting Professor at the Center for Latin American Studies.
