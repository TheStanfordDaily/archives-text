Specific duties include:
• Advise sophomore residents on selecting and declaring majors and academic
concerns.
• Schedule individual and small group advising meetings with Toyon residents.
• Work as part of a team led by the Head AA to create and implement academic
programming that focuses on the needs of sophomore students.
• Get resident feedback on academic topics they would like addressed through
programs.
• Act as liaisons with academic departments to facilitate department outreach to
sophomores in the residence.
• Act as liaison between students and faculty/staff advisors assigned to
the residence.
• Create a personal and advising presence in Toyon.
If you have questions, contact hector.cuevas@forsythe.
Toyon
Resident Sophomore Advisor
RSA Positions
If you will be a junior or senior next year, you are invited to apply
for one of the two Resident Sophomore Advisor (RSA) positions in
Toyon Hall, an all sophomore residence. Applications are available
at the UAC, first floor, Sweet Hall, and are due Monday, April 10.
An e-mail version is available from hector.cuevas@forsythe.
Applicants should have a passion for their studies and be prepared
to advise Sophomores about selecting and declaring majors and
other academic issues. RSA's will live in Toyon.
Compensation provided.
Rev. John Walsh, Chaplain
