SOPHOMORE ASSISTANTS (SAs)
for the 2000-2001
Sophomore College
September 4 - September 23, 2000
Position Description
Sophomore College is a set of programs for returning second-year students, who live together in a student residence and work under faculty supervision in a series of small
group classes during early September, before the start of fall quarter. Each faculty member in the Sophomore College will be assisted by two SAs, who will also serve as academic
advisors and residence staff for the College, and who will report to the Sophomore College Director. SAs will receive room, board, and $750 for full-time work during the course of the
program, and for training at the end of spring quarter.
