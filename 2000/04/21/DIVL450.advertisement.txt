How to submit a letter
Mail: Letters to the Editor
The Stanford Daily
Storke Publications Building
Stanford, Calif. 94305
E-mail: letters@daily.stanford.edu
Fax: (650) 725-1329
Letters must be signed and accompanied by a tele
phone number that can be used to verify the identity of
the author. Submissions may be edited and repub
lished in any format — in print or electronically.
