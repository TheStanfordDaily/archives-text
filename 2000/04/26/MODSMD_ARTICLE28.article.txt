# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

JING CAI/The Stanford Daily File
Dana Sorensen
In crucial games against No. 4 Fresno State and No.
9 California last week, freshman pitcher Dana
Sorensen did not allow any earned runs. Because of
this outstanding performance, Sorensen was hon
ored as Pacific 10 Conference Pitcher of the Week.


JOANNA HIAIT/The Stanford Daily File
Michael Stember
Senior Michael Stember clocked a time of 1:48.16
in the 800 meter competition this weekend to win
the race and provisionally aualify for the NCAAs.
Stember's time is the second best in the Pacific 10
Conference this season.
