STANFORD UNIVERSITY
PROGRAM IN JEWISH STUDIES
presents
The Annual Aaron-Roland Lecture in Jewish Studies
Matters Of Life And Death:
A Jewish Approach To Modern Medical Ethics
A. lecture by Elliot N. Dorff
Rector and Professor of* Philosophy, University of Judaism
Wednesday, May 17,2000, at 4:30 p.m.
Fairchild Auditorium, Fairchild Center
School of Medicine, Stanford University
This lecture is free and open to the public. It is supported by the Aaron-Roland Fund
for Jewish Studies, and co-sponsored by the Center for Biomedical Ethics and
the Department of Religious Studies at Stanford University.
For more information, please contact the Program in Jewish Studies, 650-723-7589.
