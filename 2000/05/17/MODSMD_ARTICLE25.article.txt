# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

JOANNA HIATT/The Stanford Daily File
JOY GOFF
After setting a new personal best, senior Joy Goff
placed second at the Pac-10 Championships in the
Heptathlon this weekend in Oregon. Goff had a
fantastic last day and finished with 5,1 33 points.


JOANNA HIATT/The Stanford Daily File
ALEX KIM
Junior Alex Kim, Stanford's No. 1 singles plaver,
led the Cardinal to victory in the first two rounds of
NCAAs this weekend. Kim didn't drop a set, de
feating his opponents 6-0, 6-2 and 6-3, 6-4.
