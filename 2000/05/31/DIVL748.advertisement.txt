An etymological public service announcement from your freinds at the Daily:
geek (gk) (n. Slang)
1 .An odd or ridiculous person.
2.A carnival performer whose show consists of bizarre acts, such as biting the head off a live chicken.
[Perhaps alteration of dialectal geek, fool, from Low German gek, from Middle Low German.]
From the American Heritage Dictionary of the English Language
Keep that in mind the next time you're surrounded by geeks in some Silicon Valley cube farm.
