Coming Soon!
Stanford Bookstore
Renovation
Renovation beings Monday, June 19, 2000
What to look for:
• New Mission Oak design for the Trade Book
and General Merchandise floors
• New high-tech Silicon Valley style for
the Supply and Textbook floors
• Expanded Tully's Cafe
The renovation will neither add nor consume additional space or square footage. |
The new design will make better use of the existing space and provide a pleasant, |
updated shopping experience. All essential services will be available with 1
the exception of Tully's Cafe and Photo Express.
* i
Stanford Bookstore apologizes in advance for any inconvenience the renovations may cause;
however the end result will be a welcome change and an added value to the Stanford community.
* •
The renovation will be completed in early September 2000 in time for the fall quarter rush.
