Dear Ayse,
We are so proud of you. Always follow your dreams.
You are our shining star.
Today,
you may not know all the world has to offer...
but we know that the world will be a better place
because of what you have to offer it.
Today,
you may not know which path you should f0110w...
but we know that you will listen to your heart,
for it will never lie.
Today,
You may question what you already have accomplished...
but we know you will never doubt what you can do.

As all of your tomorrows
open themselves to you,
never forget how proud we are
of you
and how much we love you...
yesterday, today, and always.
by Michael Coffey Tarbert
Love, anna & baba.
