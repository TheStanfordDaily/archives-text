# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

LEVON UTIDJIAN/The Stanford Daily File
Marcia Wallis
Sophomore forward Marcia Wallis scored three
goals and tallied six shots enroute to earning Bay
Area Soccer Classic All-Tournament honors this
weekend. The 1999 co-Pac-10 Freshman of the
year leads the team in scoring with eight goals.


SASHA SIDDHARTHA/The Stanford Daily File
Scott Leber
Senior midfielder Scott Leber has recorded a point
in each of Stanford's six games this season. Leber,
who until this year had only two career goals, leads
the team in scoring with seven goals. The men's
team returns to action tonight versus San Jose State.
