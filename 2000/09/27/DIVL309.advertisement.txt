Hillel at Stanford invites students, faculty and staff to join us for
Rosh Hashanah, the Jewish New Year, and Yom Kippur, the Day of Atonement
SERVICE INFORMATION
Free of charge with Stanford ID, reservations requested (see form below)
Erev Rosh Hashanah, Friday, September 29
Liberal Services: Memorial Auditorium at 7:00 PM Conservative Services: Kresge Auditorium at 7:00 PM
First Day Rosh Hashanah, Saturday, September 30
Liberal Services: Memorial Auditorium at 9:30 AM Conservative Services: Kresge Auditorium at 8:30 AM
Second Day Rosh Hashanah, Sunday, October 1
Conservative Services: Kresge Auditorium at 8:30 AM Tashlich: Old Union Courtyard following services
Kol Nidrei, Yom Kippur Eve, Sunday, October 8
Liberal Services: Memorial Auditorium at 6:30 PM Conservative Services: Dinkelspiel Auditorium at 6:30 PM
Yom Kippur Morning, Monday, October 9
Liberal Services: Memorial Auditorium at 9:30 AM Conservative Services: Dinkelspiel Auditorium at 8:30 AM
Yom Kippur Afternoon and Evening - Mincha, Yizkor, Neilah - Monday, October 9
Liberal Services: Memorial Auditorium at 4:00 PM Conservative Services: Dinkelspiel Auditorium at 4:00 PM
MEAL INFORMATION
Meal reservations required (see form below)
Erev Rosh Hashanah Dinner $7
Friday, September 29 5:00 PM in Ricker Dining Hall
Pre-Yom Kippur Meal $7
Sunday, October 8 4:30 PM in Tressidor Union, Oak Lounge
Fast-Breaking (free for Stanford students)
Monday, October 9 Following services in Old Union Clubhouse Ballroom
For more information contact Hillel at 650-723-1602 or Email to: hillel3@stanford.edu,
check our website at http://www.stanford.edu/group/hillel/
or come by our office in the Old Union Clubhouse.
