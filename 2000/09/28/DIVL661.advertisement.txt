Western Opera Theater
Die Fledermaus
Friday, October 6, 8:00 p.m.
Memorial Auditorium
A cheating wife, a jealous husband, tax evasion—a contemporary scandol? Not exactly Die
Fledermaus is Strauss' frothy soap operetta of 19th-century Vienna. Western Opera iheater,
Son Francisco Opera's national touring company, complete with full orchestra, concocts o mix
of sumptuous voices, fabulous sets, and o lively score. Overhead subtitles in English guaran
tee you won't miss any of the fun. The San Francisco Chronicle declares this is "an extraordi
narily bubbly and well-sung production of Die Fledermaus."

Salome

Memorial Auditorium
Post performance discussion (Friday only)

Friday, October 27, 8:00 p.m.

Dinkelspiel Auditorium
