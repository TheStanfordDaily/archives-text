R. B. WEBBER & COMPANY —
INCORPORATED
Needed: Consulting Engagement Managers
R. B. Webber & Company, a 50-person consulting firm in Palo Alto, is recruiting
consulting engagement managers. R. B. Webber provides strategic planning advice to
high-technology companies. Multiple openings are available for qualified case lead
ers, junior engagement managers, and senior engagement managers
Responsibilities include day-to-day management of client projects, interaction with
client teams and supervision of internal consulting teams.
Qualifications include two years or more as a research associate/analyst at a technol
ogy-focused strategy consulting firm or an MBA or technology degree, plus two years
or more of work experience in marketing, project management, or product manage
ment at a technology company.
Employment site is Palo Alto, California.
Please fax resume to:
