Fledermaus is Strauss' frothy soap operetta of 19th century Vienna. Western Opera Theater,
San Francisco Opera's national touring company, complete with full orchestra, concocts a mix
of sumptuous voices, fabulous sets, and a lively score. Overhead subtitles in English guaran
tee you won't miss any of the fun. The San Francisco Chronicle declares this is "an extraordi
narily bubbly and well sung production of Die Fledermaus "

Mirabai
Sunday October 29, 2:30 p.m.
Dinkelspiel Auditorium
Robert Mirabai, flutist, composer, and Amazon.com's New Age Artist of the Year for 1999,
fuses Native American music with the rock 'n' roll of his youth fora distinctively unique and
wondrous sound. Mirabai, o member of the Toos Pueblo in New Mexico, is joined by his
troupe of dancers and musicians. "The ancestral drumming and ritualistic chanting dominating
Mirabai create an intoxicating swirl as they intermingle with contemporary ideas and
sounds," soys the Village Voice.
