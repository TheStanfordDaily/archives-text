Special presentation for the Stanford campus community
Living in the Bonus Round
One man and a piano come together to offer a unique
educational experience about living with AIDS
Performed by: Steve Schalchlin
Wednesday, October 11, 2000
5:00 - 6:00 pm
Kncina llall - Bechtel Conference Room, Stanford University
This event is free and open to the public.
Sponsored by:
STANFORD UNIVERSITY MEDICAL CENTER
Stanford Center for Biomedical Ethics Stanford Medical Informatics
The Health Library at Stanford Paul & Peterson Foundation
This event is pail of the 10th Annual Jonathan J. King Lectureship. Main lecture: Tuesday, October 10, 2000, 5:00 - 6:00 pm,
Fairchild Auditorium, Medical School, Stanford University.
Performance is the same both days.
