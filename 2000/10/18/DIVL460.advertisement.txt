The Board of Trustees of
The Leland Stanford Junior University
encourages your attendance at the
Inauguration and Investiture of
John L. Hennessy
as Tenth President of the University
Friday, October 20, 2000 at 9:30 a.m.
in Laurence Frost Amphitheater
and to a Reception immediately following
in the Inner Quadrangle
Attendees must be seated by 9:25 a.m. Seating is limited and
tickets are required. TICKET DISTRIBUTION for students and
staff will be held on a first-come, first-served basis beginning on
Wednesday, October 11, 2000, at the Stanford Ticket Office at
Tresidder and at Stanford Visitor Information Services at
Memorial Auditorium. Hours of distribution are 10:00 a.m. to
5:00 p.m. Limit of one ticket per Stanford I.D. Questions? Call
723-2551.
