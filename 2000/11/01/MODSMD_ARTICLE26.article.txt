# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

KEITH ITO/The Stanford Daily F <■
Scott Leber
Last Sunday against Cal, Stanford's leading scorer
led the No. 1 Cardinal to a 3-0 win with a pair of
goals, one on a 12-yard laser and the other on a
header. Leber, a senior midfielder, was named
Pac-10 Player of the Week for his efforts.


JOANNA HIATT/The Stanford Daily File
Women's Cross Country
Behind a third-place finish by Lauren Fleshman
(above) and two other runners in the top six, the No.
2 Stanford women claimed the Pac-10 title at the
conference championships in Seattle last weekend,
topping Arizona State and Arizona.
