Announcing A Unique Opportunity for Summer 2()()1:
YEAR 2001
BEAGLE II
AWARDS
The office of the Vice Provost for Undergraduate
Education and Undergraduate Research Programs are
pleased to announce a call for proposals for Beagle II
Awards, 2001. In a spirit similar to Charles Darwin's 1831
scientific voyage aboard the 11. M.S. Beagle, the Beagle II
Awards provide research-minded undergraduates $3500 fora
voyage of scientific discovery anywhere in the world to explore a topic in which they have a passionate interest.
Applications are currently available in I ndergraduate Research Programs (URP), 4th floor Sweet Hall. Application
deadline: February 13, 2000 by 5:00 p.m. in the URP office.
INFORMATION SESSION
Tuesday, November 28, 2000 4:15-5:15
Building 360, Room 361J-K
Address questions to Professor William Durham, Faculty Coordinator for the Beagle 11, Building
360-362-L, or Susan Brubaker-Cole, Director, Undergraduate Research Programs, Sweet Hall
736-11 S3, susiehc@stanford.edu.
For more information: www.stanford.edu/group/beagle2
