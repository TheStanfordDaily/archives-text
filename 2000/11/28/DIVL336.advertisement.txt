Heitman Capital Management
invites members of the class of 2001
to submit resumes by January 8"'
fora position as a
Real Estate Investment Associate.
The firm is seeking qualified undergraduate or co-terminal students graduating this academic year
tor the position of Real Kstate Investment Associate in our Chicago office. Associates work in small
teams in the acquisitions group structuring and analysing various multi-million public and private
real estate investment opportunities throughout North America.
Students interested in the Associate position
should submit a resume through the CDC by January 8, 2000.
Interviews will be held on campus Monday, January 29,
with second-round interviews in Chicago
February 1-2.
Anyone with questions about the Associate position or Heitman should call
Skip Schwartz (class of '95) or Jasin Alfaro (class of '98) at 312-855-5700.
