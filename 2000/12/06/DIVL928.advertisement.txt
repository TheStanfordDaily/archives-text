Are You Thinking of
Field Research Abroad?
Bring your lunch on Thursdays at noon
and listen to faculty panels discuss such topics as
sources of funding, making contacts, interviewing,
archival research, household surveys and
aggregate data, participant observation and
ethnography, case studies, the ethics of
research, and human subjects protocol.
Designed for grads/undergrads
I from any department or school.
