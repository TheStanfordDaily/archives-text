Interested In Quantitative Analysis And
Management Consulting?
Applied Decision Analysis, LLC (ADA) is a growing consulting com
pany that applies mathematical techniques to management problems.
Our strength is high-quality work in areas such as strategy analysis,
market forecasting for new products, environmental policy analysis, risk
analysis, Real Option Valuation, decision analysis, operations manage
ment, and basic research. Our clients include Fortune 500 companies,
government agencies, and start-ups.
We would like to meet graduating seniors (BA/BS) who have:
A background in a quantitative field (e.g. math, engineering,
physics, operations research, statistics)
>- An interest in solving important, complex problems
>• Skills in a broad range of mathematical techniques
Communication skills to present analytical results in a clear,
concise manner
A high level of enthusiasm for challenging work in an informal
atmosphere.
If you have any questions, please call Bethanie lams at 650-926-9235
or visit our website at www.adainc.com!
