Become a columnist
for The Daily
Application deadline: Friday, Feb. 9, 5 p.m.
What you need to do:
• Submit two 500-700 word sample columns and
10 sample topics.
• Drop them off in a manila envelope at the Daily office
with your student ID number on every page.
• E-mail opinions@daily.stanford.edu with your
name and student ID.
What we are looking for:
• Dedicated writers with passion
• Columns that deal with issues affecting Stanford students and
Stanford life
If you have any questions please e-mail
opinions@daily.stanford.edu.
