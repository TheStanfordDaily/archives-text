Institute for International Studies
Littlefield International Graduate Fellowships
Five awards will be made in 2001-02 to outstanding
Stanford graduate students whose dissertations treat
some aspect of international political economy
Applicants must have completed all qualifying exams
and be engaged in dissertation research or write-up at
the time of application. The awards will provide TGR
fees and a living stipend for up to one year, with the
possibility of a second year renewal, subject to
satisfactory progress and reapplication.
Applications due Monday, March 5, 2001
Applications & information available from
the lIS home page at: http://iis.stanford.edu/fellows/
or call 725-3036
