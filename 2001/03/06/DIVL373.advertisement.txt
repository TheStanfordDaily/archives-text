Career Development Center - Cardinal Recruiting — Spring Interview Sign-Ups Start Monday, March 12 th
Many companies are coming for our last season of interviews,-this year. Don't miss it!
For eligibility and registration see the Cardinal Recruiting section of our web site:
http://www.stanford.edu/dept/CDC/page/recruiting/Recruiting.html
If you are already registered with Cardinal Recruiting there is no need to do so again. Just log-on and submit resumes to employers you are interested in, by the deadlines.
