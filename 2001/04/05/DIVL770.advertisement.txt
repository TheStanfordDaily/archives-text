Stanford Bookstore
KICKS Off Baseball Season with
The Summer that
Saved Baseball
By Dave Kaval and Brad Null
Thursday, April 5, 2001
5:00-8:00 p.m. at the Stanford Bookstore
ppf~ -
nut u t oiitf h*t#i
Following their 1998 graduation,
Stanford Alumni Brad Null and
Dave Kaval departed Palo Alto on
a quest to fulfill their childhood
dream-to see a baseball game in
all 30 US ballparks in 38 days.
The Summer that Saved Baseball
recounts this amazing tour,
incorporating a narrative of their
many humorous and memorable
experiences, as well as a guide
to the ballparks. It features
evaluations on food and fans and
top ten lists ranking everything
from the stadium to the beer.
For more information on The
Summer that Saved Baseball
go to www.baseballtour.com
DON'T MISS this fun
opportunity to talk to Dave and
Brad about their adventures and
have a copy of the book signed!
