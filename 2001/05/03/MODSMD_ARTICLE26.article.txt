# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

JOANNA HIATT/The Stanford Daily File
THE MEN'S FOUR-MILE RELAY TEAM
The team of sophomore Seth Hejny, freshman Don
ald Sage, junior Jonathon Riley and senior Michael
Stember raced to victory in a time of 16:15.70,
shattering the old school record of 16:23.16 in
1998 at the Penn Relays this weekend.


MAT I SAYIFS/The Stanford Daily File
JACKIE FRANK
Frank, a sophomore, had 10 saves in the Cardinal
8-5 win over UCLA, giving the team the 2001 MPSF
Women's Water Polo Championship. The goalie
collected 28 saves in three games for Stanford,
earning her tournament MVP honors.
