Cardinal Care ID Card
Identification cards will he mailed to you directly from
the insurance company. For those students whose only
listed address in the Stanford system was outside the
United Suites, your Cardinal Care II) card is available
for pick up at the Cowell Insurance Oil ice. ID cards
returned to Health Net by the Post Office have also been
forwarded to Cowell for pick-up.
Dependents
Dependents of Stanford students are eligible to
purchase the Stanford University Student
Dependent Plan. Enrollment inlormation is
available through the Insurance Office at Cowell.
Enrollment received by 10/15/00 will carry an effective
date of 9/1/00.
11l
===========-
To enroll student dependents in the Stanford Dependent
Insurance Plan, please come to the Insurance Olfice at
Cowell by the date specified above.
If you have questions, please call Lincfa Moran, 723-2135
