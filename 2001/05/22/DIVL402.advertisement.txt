The Election Selections Committee is now taking resumes for the '01-'O2 Elections Commissioner.
The Elections Commissioner runs and manages the ASSU elections process during the winter and
spring. To apply for the position, please leave your resume at the ASSU office on the second floor
of Tressider Union. All resumes need to be submitted by Tuesday, May 29. If you have any ques
tions, please contact Joe Narens at jnarens@stanford.edu.
MUTIM mimsillllt: Salary: $3,1100
Oversees the Elections Commission, which is responsible for the following:
•creating the elections handbook
•all operations details of the election
•all publicity of the commission
•creating advertisements, flyers, posters, and e-mails
related to Election activity
•creating, maintaining, and updating the Elections website
•creating the elections ballot
