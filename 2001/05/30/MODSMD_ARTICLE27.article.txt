# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

MATT SAYIES/The Stanford Daily File
Laura Granville
Sophomore Laura Granville captured her second
consecutive NCAA Women's Singles title last week
end with a 7-6, 6-3 victory over teammate junior
Lauren Kalvaria. After her win, Granville announced
her intention to turn professional this summer.


MATT SAYLES/The Stanford Daily
Ryan Garko
Sophomore Ryan Garko's two-RBI single in the top of
the eighth inning against Texas on Sundav helped
the Cardinal came back to win the regional. Garko,
in the midst of a 20-game hitting streak, was named
co-Most Outstanding Player of tne regional.
