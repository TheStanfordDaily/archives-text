Congratulations
Graduates!
You may be leaving campus, but you can take
Stanford Federal Credit Union wherever you go.
Whether you need a loan to buy a car, or
e-Services to simplify your financial life,
SFCU's loan services, savings and investment
programs, and world-wide ATM access will be
there for you.
Once a member, always a member at
Stanford Federal Credit Union.
