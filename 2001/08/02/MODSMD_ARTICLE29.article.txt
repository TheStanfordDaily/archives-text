Urticina piscivora, a fish eating anemone, is common in the waters from Alaska to
Southern California. This particular anemone was photographed at Monterey Bay
National Marine Sanctuary. Living in rocky reefs and ranging in size from 20 to 25
cm across, it feeds on invertebrates and small fish using its red, pink and yellow poi
sonous tentacles.
