3) Germany's Jana Kandarr fell Friday to Daven
port in straight sets, 6-3, 6-1; 4) After handling
Lilia Osterloh in straight sets Friday, Monica Seles
ran out of gas in the semifinals; 5) Venus Williams
was overmatched by Meghann Shaughnessy's
power in her quarterfinal match.
