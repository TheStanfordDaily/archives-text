# Picture of the Week
## 
### 

Courtesy of National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration
An Adelie penguin (Pygoscelis adeliae) rookery on the Antarctic Peninsula. Adelies stand
about 27 inches tall ana are jet black, except for tfieir abdomens and the undersides of their
flippers, which are white. Each eye is encircled by a thin white ring. Thousands of Adelies
gather here to mate and build nests for their eggs.
