# Winning Streak Hits Home
## 
### 
AAAII SAYIES/The Stanford Daily
(Clockwise from upper-left): The No. 4 Stan
ford women's volleyball team remained unde
feated in the Pac-10 with a victory in a five
game thriller against No. 7 Arizona on Sun
day. The win closed out a weekend sweep of
the Card's first three home matches. Here,
sophomore setter Anna Robinson sets the ball.
She recorded a career-high 52 assists on the
night. Freshman Ogonna Nnamani hits the ball
over the fingertips of an Arizona blocker. Ju
nior Ashley Ivy celebrates during the Cardinal's
close battle with the Wildcats.
