On Sunday, April 7, 2002, the Office of the President and
Provost will sponsor Community Day at Stanford, a university
wide open house for Stanford's neighbors. Community Day
planners are looking for student organizations to participate by
setting up an exhibit or running a program of interest to the
local community. Registered student groups that are able to
educate, entertain or involve Stanford's neighbors are encour
aged to apply.
To find out more, or to pick up an application, visit online at
http://www.stanford.edu/dept/news/neighbors/communityday
or call the Community Day hotline at 4-2933.
