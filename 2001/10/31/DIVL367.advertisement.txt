Morgan Stanley invites you
Morgan Stanley, a global leader in financial services, is looking for people with a
passion tor the financial markets. We provide institutional clients with sales and
trading services in virtually every type of financial instrument, including stocks,
bonds, derivatives, foreign exchange and commodities. As a sales person or trader
you'll face the challenge of assisting clients with their portfolio strategies, uncover
ing investment opportunities, or committing the Firm's own capital in an atmosphere
of constant change.
How far can a career in the financial markets take you? Morgan Stanley is the
place to find out. Whatever your aspirations, we can give you the tools and the
opportunities to achieve them.
