2001 Pa La
Pre-Law Conference
\
%
%
Students from all Bay Area schools are invited to attend and to talk
informally with admissions officers from approximately 60 law schools.
Wednesday, November 14
10:30 am - 2:00 pm
Stanford University
Oak Lounge, Tresidder
Sponsored by the Undergraduate Advising Center
A Division of the Office of the Vice Provost for Undergraduate Education
Breakout Sessions
The Sequoia Room
10:15 am and 11:15 am
DON'T Miss Breakout Sessions Running Concurrently With the
Conference. Meet with Law Representatives to Discuss Issues Like
Applying to Law School, Financial Aid, and the Future of Law Schools.
