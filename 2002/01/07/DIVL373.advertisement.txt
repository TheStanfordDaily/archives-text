Historical Perspectives on Public Service
in Higher Education
(Urban Studies 193)
Winter Quarter, Tuesday and Thursday, 3:15-
4:45PM
Location: Haas Center for Public Service
Join this research semmar, available to undergraduate and
graduate students, in examining the intersection of public
service and higher education in the U.S., with an emphasis on
urban areas such as Philadelphia, New York, San Francisco
and Chicago. Students will conduct original historical
research and share their work with the university community
at large through a continuing publication series sponsored by
the Haas Center for Public Service.
For information on other service-learning courses contact kkoth@stanford.edu.
