Teaching Opportunity
m
Offered winter quarter, Eloo—Teaching Public Speaking explores the
theory and practice of teaching public speaking and trains students to
serve as instructors in the Technical Communications Program's public
speaking courses.Eloooffers a hands-on introducion to designing an
effective course, devising tutorial techniques, and teaching delivery,
organization, audience analysis, visual aids, and style. Students deliver
lectures, lead discussions, critique each othe's speeches, and take part in
fieldwork.
If you have exceptional experience in debate or drama or public speaking
or teaching, take EICO, become a speech inatictor in Stanford's premier
public speaking course, and earn at leastsl2:ooan hour teaching students
from all disciplines.
Five units. Meets M, 7:30 - 10:00 p.m. and Th, 12:15 - 1:05.
Admission by consent of instructor. Places in this course ae limited, so
apply early.
Pick up an application, arrange for an interview, and receive additional
information from Dave Lougee in Terman 105 (telephone: 723-2573;
email: dlougee@stanford).
