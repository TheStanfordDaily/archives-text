You thought about dropping out of
school to start your own company, but
the market crashed. You looked at
those Wall Street jobs, but weren't sure
they were right for you. You'd like to
get an MBA,, but you're too young.
What can you do?
Be CEO. As the head of Stanford
Student Enterprises, you have an
unparalleled opportunity to learn
management skills, financial analysis,
and strategic decision-making. SSE is
the only place you can evaluate 80-
year-old businesses and analyze brand
new ventures on a daily basis.
SSE is the financial branch of the ASSU.
Our portfolio of businesses includes the
Stanford Directory, Flicks, the Stanford
Store, and the Stanford Design Group.
SSE also manages over $8 million in
assets, including the ASSU endowment.
Why wait? Be CEO.
The CEO position requires 20 hours per
week for training during spring quarter.
As CEO, you will work full-time during
the summer and the following
academic year. Compensation is
competitive.
All majors are welcome to apply.
Preference will be given to students
graduating by June 2002.
Applicants should have significant
independent leadership experience and
demonstrated skills in project
management, numerical analysis, and
team building.
Interested candidates should consult
the job posting on Jobtrak / Cardinal
Recruiting. First-round interviews will
be conducted on January 28th. To
interview for the position, you must
submit a resume and cover letter and
sign up (on Jobtrak) between January
14th and 24th.
Info Session: Monday, January 14th at 7PM in Cypress South
Open Signups begin on Jobtrak January 14th
