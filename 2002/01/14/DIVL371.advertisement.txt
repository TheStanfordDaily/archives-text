BASES Social Entrepreneur's
Challenge Kick Off Event!
Tuesday, January 15th, 2002 at 5:00 PM
Tresidder Union, Cypress Room
Free admission, food provided
Learn about BASES Social Entrepreneur's Challenge
business plan competition ($ 1 OK in prizes).
Build on seed ideas for both non-profit and for-profit business plans
Network with students from the Schools of Law, Business,
Engineering, Medicine, Education, and Humanities.
Workshops and feedback on social venture plans are provided in a low-risk
environment by entrepreneurs and foundation executives.
The deadline to enter the BASES E-Challenge and Social E-Challenge is
February 7th, 2002. More information is available at:
http://bases.stanford.edu/echallenge.html.
To subscribe to the e-mail list, send an e-mail to: majordomo@lists.stanford.edu.
In the text body, type: subscribe social-echallenge
