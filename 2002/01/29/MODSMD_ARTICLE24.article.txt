# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 
Yamasaki, a senior, was named the Pac-10
Women's Basketball Player of the Week, and right
fully so. She averaged 24.5 points and 3.5 rebounds
in home wins against Arizona and Arizona State.

With games like last weekend (team-leading 27
and 1 8 kills), junior Toppel has established himself
as one of the best all-around players in the nation.
He also had eight and five digs in the two wins.
