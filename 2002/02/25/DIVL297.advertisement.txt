+ HAAS CENTER STUDENT WEB MANAGER
POSITION AVAILABLE
Apprentice spring quarter, promotion in June! The Web Manager
works with the Haas ('-enter's External Relations/Development team
to keep information, text and graphic images current for the public
service-oriented web site. Qualified applicants will be additionally
trained inediting and developing HTML pages based on pre-existing
styles. Must commit to 8-10 hours. Summer, 2002 and during the
academic year, 2002-03. If interested, email resume to
efmiller@stanford.edu by Feb. 28.
+ BLACK LIBERATION MONTH PROGRAMS
Mon., Feb. 25, 7:30 p.m., Kresge Auditorium, Angela Y. Davis,
renowned activist and Professor of History of Consciousness at the
University of California, Santa Cruz, hosts a film screening and dis
cussion of the Prison Industrial Complex and the struggle against it.
Thurs., Feb. 28, 7 p.m., Bldg. 200, Room 2 (History Comer),
Stanford African Students Association Film Night: Maangamizi.
Sat., March 2, 5 p.m. - Dinner; 7 p.m. - Open Mic: AfRIKA
UNpluggcd!: Spoken word artists, singers, and musicians, focusing
on the African Diaspora. Mixed beats after-party. Co-Sponsored by
the Stanford African Students Association and Akwaaba Ghanaian
Students Association
«> TUTORS NEEDED AT ffiff AJ LASH!
This non-profit organization offers numerous services to people suf

and college students in basic algebra, writing, etc. Every day, 4-6
p.m., so scheduling is flexible. Contact Michelle at rniche@stan
ford.edu.
+ SUMMER INTERNSHIP/FELLOWSHIP OPPORTUNITIES
Everett Public Service Internship Program offers internships at
over 60 organizations in Washington, DC and New York. Apply
directly to the host organization. Stipend available. Contact Jon
McConnell at Haas Center for booklet listing organizations. For more
information visit: wu w.eivreltinternships.org.
Short-term or long-term Internships in Tanzania or
Thailand are available through the Global Service Corps:
schools. HIV awareness, sustainable agriculture, community health
centers. Academic credit and financial aid available. Email
scoffice@earlhisland.org with address and phone. Web: unvu.gtoh
alservicecorps. org.
