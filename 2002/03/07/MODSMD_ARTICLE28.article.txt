# Athletes of the Week
## 
###

ence Basketball Player of the Week. Borchardt
averaged 23.5 points, 13 rebounds and 2.5
blocks during the Cardinal's sweep of the Arizona
schools.

The sophomore swimmer set a new American record in the 100-yard breaststroke with a
