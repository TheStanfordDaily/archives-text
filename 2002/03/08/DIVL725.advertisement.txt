Spring Quarter Course:
El 00 —Teaching
Public Speaking
(5 units)
Offered spring quarter, ElOO —Teaching Public Speaking explores the
theory and practice of teaching public speaking and trains students to serve
as instructors in the Technical Communications Program's public speaking
courses. ElOO offers a hands-on introducion to designing an effective
course, devising tutorial techniques, and teaching delivery, organization,
audience analysis, visual aids, and style. Students deliver lectures, lead
discussions, critique each other's speeches, and take part in fieldwork.
If you have exceptional experience in debate or drama or public speaking
or teaching, take Eloo, become a speech instructor in Stanford's premier
public speaking course, and earn at least $12:00 an hour teaching students
from all disciplines.
Five units. Meets M, 7:30 - 10:00 p.m. and Th,12:15- 1:05.
Admission by consent of instructor. Places in this course are limited, so
apply early.
Pick up an application, arrange for an interview, and receive additional
information from Dave Lougee inTerman 105 (telephone: 723-2573;
email: dlougee@stanford).
