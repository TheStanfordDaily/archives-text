# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

MATT SAYLES/The Stanford Daily File
NICOLE POWELL
The sophomore forward / point guard was named
to the 2002 NCAA Division I Kodak All-America
Women's Basketball Team yesterday. Powell fin
ished the season averaging 16.6 points, 9.3 re
bounds and 6.3 assists per game.


MARIO SCUSSEL/The Stanford Daily File
MICHAEL BRUCE
The sophomore took second in the 200-yard breast
stroke with a career-best time of 1:54:81 at the
NCAA Championships. Bruce also teamed with
Randall Bal, Peter Marshall and Dan Westcott to help
Stanford capture the 400 medley relay.
