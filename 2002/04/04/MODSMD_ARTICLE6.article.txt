# Lesh tickets go on sale Friday
## 
### 
The Stanford Concert Network
confirmed yesterday that former
Grateful Dead bassist Phil
Lesh will perform at Frost Am
phitheater on Sunday, June 2.
Student tickets will go on
sale for $20 this Friday at

10:00 a.m. in White Plaza.
Tickets will go on sale to the
general public for $35 on Sun
day at 10:00 a.m. at the
Tresidder Box Office and on
line at www.tickets.com and
www.philatfrost.com.
