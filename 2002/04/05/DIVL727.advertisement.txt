Fun Research
$20 per Hour
A Popular Program on Campus Seeks Your Participation
Researchers at the Stanford Graduate School of Business are regularly
looking for participants in experiments and survey studies on various
intriguing topics such as information processing, group performance,
and advertising. These studies are fun to do, and they usually pay $20
per hour.
Here is how you can participate in these studies:
Join the Research Alert mailing list and weill send you email announce
ments about upcoming studies, and information on how to sign up for
them. There are many studies taking place each quarter, and you can
choose which ones to do according to your schedule and interests.
To join our announcement list, simply send an email to
majordomo@lists.stanford.edu
with the following message in the body of the email:
subscribe researchalert
Studies are taking place as we speako
Join NOW and Don't Miss Out!
You must be US citizen or Permanent Resident, 18 years or older.
Check our website www.gsb.stanford.edu/behavioralJab/ResearchAlert.html for more info.
