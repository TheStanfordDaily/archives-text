Don't let the title fool you - this book
is a very comprehensive and detailed
approach to understanding Buddhism.
It attempts to keep the beginning
Buddhist on track and show easy ways
to make Buddhism a part of your life.
In this book, Gary Gach explores who
the Buddha was and what he means
today, introduces the Four Noble Truths
and the Eightfold Path, and discusses
the major schools of Vipassana,
Zen and Pure Land, as well as the growth
and development of
Buddhism in America.
