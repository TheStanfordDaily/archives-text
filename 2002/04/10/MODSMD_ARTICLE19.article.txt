# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

JESSICA BOROWICK/The Stanford Daily File
JESSICA MENDOZA
Senior Jessica Mendoza was named as a finalist for
the USA Softball Player of the Year on Monday. The
centerfielder is a three-time first-team All-American
and leads the Cardinal in 12 offensive categories, in
cluding batting average (.436) and runs scored (45).


MARIO SCUSSEL/The Stanford Daily File
PETER MARSHALL
Peter Marshall is capping off the athletic week of a life
time. The sophomore brought home four medals (two
gold, one silver, one bronze) from the World Cham
pionships in Moscow. He is also a member of the
400-meter relay team that set a new world record.
