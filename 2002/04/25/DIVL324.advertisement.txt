Gretchen C. Daily a Bing Interdisciplinary
Scientist here at Stanford has teamed up
with Pulitzer Prize-winning journalist
Katherine Ellison to offer a captivating and
informative look at a new "new economy"
- a system that recognizes the worth of
natural systems and the potential profits
in protecting them. Through individual
profiles of charismatic trailblazers.
The New Economy of Nature describes
the dynamic balance of science, economics,
business, and politics that will be needed
to create successful models and lasting
institutions for conservation.
