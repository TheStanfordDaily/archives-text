# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 

MARIO SCUSSEL/The Stanford Daily File
GABRIELA LASTRA
Senior Gabriela Lastra captured the singles title at
the Pacific 10 Conference Championships in Reno,
Nev., on Sunday. Lastra upset seventh-ranked Jewel
Peterson of Southern California 6-3, 6-3, improving
her winning streak to 14 matches.


MATT SAYLES/The Stanford Daily File
JOHN HUDGINS
Sophomore John Hudgins pitched his first career
shutout on Sunday against Oregon State to lead the
Cardinal to a 13-0 victory. The 6-foot-2 righty held
the Beavers scoreless, allowing only four hits and
striking out five to improve his season record to 7-0.
