OTHER INFORMATION
Circulation & Distribution
The Daily is usually delivered between 8 and 1 1 a.m. on weekdays during
the academic year except Dead Week (when one issue is published on
Wednesday), finals week and holidays. To request distribution to your lo
cation or to report a distribution problem, please contact the director of
reader services at (650) 723-2555 x 302.
Subscriptions
First Class Mail subscriptions to the Daily are available by the academic
quarter or the academic year. Subscription rates: For California resi
dents, $166.71 per academic year or $58.46 per quarter. For resi
dents in the rest of the United States, $ 154 per academic year or
$54 per quarter.
Back Issues
Back issues from the past week are available in the lobby of Storke Publi
cations Building. Content from recent issues is available at the newspaper's
Web site.
Reprints
Permission to reprint an article or photograph may be obtained by contact
ing the editor in chief at (650) 725-2100 x5Ol. Prints of Daily pno
tographs for personal use may be purchased from the Photography Depart
ment through The Daily's WeD site or by calling (650) 725-2100 x 513.
CORRECTIONS

the attention of the editors. If you think there is a need fora correction regard

2100x501 ore-maileic@daily.stanford.edu The Daily will investigate all le
gitimate requests for corrections and reserves the right to determine if a printed
correction is necessary.

OTHER INFORMATION
Circulation & Distribution
The Daily is usually delivered between 8 and 1 1 a.m. on weekdays during
the academic year except Dead Week (when one issue is published on
Wednesday), finals week and holidays. To request distribution to your lo
cation or to report a distribution problem, please contact the director of
reader services at (650) 723-2555 x 302.
Subscriptions
First Class Mail subscriptions to the Daily are available by the academic
quarter or the academic year. Subscription rates: For California resi
dents, $166.71 per academic year or $58.46 per quarter. For resi
dents in the rest of the United States, $ 154 per academic year or
$54 per quarter.
Back Issues
Back issues from the past week are available in the lobby of Storke Publi
cations Building. Content from recent issues is available at the newspaper's
Web site.
Reprints
Permission to reprint an article or photograph may be obtained by contact
ing the editor in chief at (650) 725-2100 x5Ol. Prints of Daily pno
tographs for personal use may be purchased from the Photography Depart
ment through The Daily's WeD site or by calling (650) 725-2100 x 513.
CORRECTIONS
It is the policy of The Stanford Daily to correct all significant errors brought to
the attention of the editors. If you think there is a need fora correction regard
ing any article, please contact Editor in Chief Mark Boucher at (650) 725-
2100x501 ore-maileic@daily.stanford.edu The Daily will investigate all le
gitimate requests for corrections and reserves the right to determine if a printed
correction is necessary.
