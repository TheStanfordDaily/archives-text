# Bank of the West Classic
## Best in tennis get hot out west 
### 

MARIO SCUSSEL/The Stanford Daily
Having failed to win a singles match for the previous two months, No. 56 Anna Kournikova surprised tournament fans
with victories over No. 8 Anna Smashnova and Indonesia's Wynne Prakusya before falling to Venus Williams.


LEVON UTIDJIAN/The Stanford Daily
Despite a valiant effort, No. 55 Conchita Martinez and partner Janette Husarova fell in the dou
bles final to the top-seeded duo Lisa Raymond and Rennae Stubbs 6-1,6-1.



MARK SCHRUMPF/The Stanford Daily
No. 2 Venus Williams clinched her second Bank of the West title on Sunday,
trouncing Kim Clijsters. The victory bodes well as Venus tries to reclaim the
No. 1 ranking she lost to her sister Serena this summer at Wimbledon.

LEVON UTIDJIAN/The Stanford Daily
No. 6 Jelena Dokic advanced to the quarterfinals with victories over Amy Frazier and doubles
finalist Conchita Martinez. Dokic then fell to No. 3 Lindsay Davenport 6-2, 6-2.
