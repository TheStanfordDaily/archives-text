Stanford's Academic Requirements
2:00 p.m. - 2:50 p.m., Building 420, Room 40
All of your life people have told you to be well rounded,
without explaining how. Stanford's commitment to a
broad liberal arts education is manifested in our Gener
al Education Requirements (GERs). There are four areas
of GERs and different combinations for fulfilling each
one. Some classes don't count toward the GERs, some
count for one and other classes count for two! And you
can't graduate until you fulfill them all. Need help sort
ing this all out? This session is for you. (Repeated at 4:00
p.m.)
