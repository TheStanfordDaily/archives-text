Offered autumn quarter, ElOO —Teaching Public Speaking explores
the theory and practice of teaching public speaking and trains students
to serve as instructors in the Technical Communications Program's public
speaking courses. ElOO offers a hands-on introducion to designing an
effective course, devising tutorial techniques, and teaching deliver)',
organization, audience analysis, visual aids, and style. Students deliver
lectures, lead discussions, critique each other's speeches, and take part in
fieldwork.
If you have exceptional experience in debate or drama or public speak
ing or teaching, take ElOO, become a speech instructor in Stanford's
premier public speaking course, and earn at least $12:00 an hour teach
ing students from all disciplines.
Five units. Meets M, 7:30 - 10:00 p.m. andTh,12:15- 1:05.
Admission by consent of instructor. Places in this course are limited, so
apply early.
Pick up an application, arrange for an interview, and receive additional
information from Dave Lougee in Terman 105 (telephone: 723-2573;
email: dlougee@stanford.edu).
