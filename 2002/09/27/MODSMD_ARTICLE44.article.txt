# Cardinal football
## On The Move

All photos by MATT SAYLES/The Stanford Daily
Stanford put on quite a second-half display versus San Jose State on Sept. 14, winning 63-26. Clockwise from
top left: Freshman running back J.R. Lemon takes the handoff from quarterback Kyle Matter, sophomore corner
back Stanley Wilson fights for the ball with Spartans wide receiver Charles Pauley, flanker Ryan Wells stiff-arms
Spartans kicker Nick Gilliam on a kickoff return, and head coach Buddy Teevens reacts to his first win as Stan
ford's head coach.
