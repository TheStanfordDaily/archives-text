Asthma?
Certain types of personal writing (like journaling) have been shown scientifically to improve
lung function in people with asthma. In this Stanford/ VA study, we are trying to figure out what
types of personal writing are most helpful and for whom.
Who is eligible:
Adults with Asthma
Must not have Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease or Post-traumatic Stress Disorder.
Study Duration:
About 3 hours spread over a three month period.
Some of the study activities can be done at home.
