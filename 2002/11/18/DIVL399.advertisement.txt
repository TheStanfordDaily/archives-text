Monday, Nov. 18, 2002, 7 p.m.
"Poincare's Maps"
Tuesday, Nov. 19, 2002, 5 p.m.
"Empires of Time: Einstein's Clocks"
Seminars - Stanford Humanities Center
Tuesday, Nov. 19, 2002, noon
"Sin and the Scientific Self'
Friday, Nov. 22, 2002, 10 a.m.
"Images of Subjectivity"

Program in History & Philosophy of Science, Lane History Corner, Building 200, Rixmii 33.
