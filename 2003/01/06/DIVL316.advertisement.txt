CAREER DEVELOPMENT CENTER - CARDINAL RECRUITING
WINTER INTERVIEW SIGN-UPS START ED DECEMBER 2nd
The first DEADLINE for interviews is TODAV January Gth. Don't miss it!!
For eligibility and registration see the Cardinal Recruiting section of our web site:
http://www.stanford.edu/dept/CDC/page/recruiting/Recruiting.html
If you are already registered with Cardinal Recruiting there is no need to do so again.
Just log -on and submit resumes to employers you are interested in, by the deadlines.
