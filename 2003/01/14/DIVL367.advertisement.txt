Rosencranz is a Consulting Professor in Human Biology, teaching several courses in U.S. and global envi
ronmental policy. His two most recent books are Environmental Law and Policy in India, 2nd ed. (2001);
and Climate Change Policy (2002). He is the founder and former president of Pacific Environment, an
international environmental NGO.
Rosencranz has had a variety of roles at Stanford, including law student, graduate student, student body pres
ident, trustee, resident fellow and consulting professor. His two sons are recent Stanford graduates.
What Matters to Me and Why encourages reflection within the Stanford community on matters of personal values,
beliefs and motivations in order to better understand the lives and inspirations of those who shape the University.
