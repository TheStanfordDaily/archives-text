development centers serving the Office of the Secretary of

qualified individuals with a doctoral or master's degree in the

• Mathematics
• Chemistry

• Economics/Microeconomics
• Engineering Sciences (Aerospace, Computer,
Chemical, Electrical/Electronics, Mechanical,
Materials)
• Computer Science
