Taking an Antidepressant?
Experiencing Sexual Side Effects?
Certain antidepressants can cause sexual side effects such as:
Decreased Libido
Diminished physical arousal
Difficulty achieving orgasm
If your antidepressant is working for your depression, but is causing sexual side effects,
you may be eligible fora treatment study at Stanford University. This medication study
will investigate the effectiveness of a new treatment for sexual side effects caused by
antidepressant. If you are a female, 18 to 50 years old, currently taking an antidepres
sant, and bothered by sexual side effect(s), you may be eligible for the treatment study
at no cost.
Due to the nature of the study, you will be asked personal/sensitive questions.
