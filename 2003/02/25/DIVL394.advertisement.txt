What Matters to Me and Wfoj
Joan Roughgarden
Professor in the Departments of Biological Sciences and Geophysics
Wednesday, February 26
12:00-1:00 P.M.
Memorial Church
Joan Roughgarden is a Professor in the Departments of Biological Sciences and
Geophysics and received her Ph.D. from Harvard University. Professor Roughgarden's
research focuses on the relationship between evolutionary biology and ecology, and
involves a combination of theoretical ecology and field studies and has done work on the
relationship between evolution and gender.
Discussion and lecture series encourages reflection within the Stanford community on matters of personal values,
beliefs, arid motivations in order to better understand the lives and inspirations of those who shape the University.
Open to all. For more information, contact Rabbi Patricia Karlin-Neumann at rabbipkn@Stanford.EDU .
WMMW is supported by the Deans for Religious Life arid ASSU
Please see our Weh site hrtp://religiouslife.stanford.edu for more information.
