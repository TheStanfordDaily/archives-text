Come hear legendary serial networking entrepreneur
Wu-Fu Chen
speak at the final DFJ Entrepreneurial Thought
Leaders Session of the Quarter!
"Starting A Company In The New
Global Economy "
Wednesday, March 5, 4:30-5:30 PM
Terman Auditorium
*Free & open to all*
Recognized as one of the most influential people in the hi-tech industry,
Wu-Fu Chen has helped to launch several successful networking companies
in the last 15 years, including Cascade Communications (sold to Ascend
for $2.6 billion), Arris Networks (bought by Cascade for $145 million),
Ardent Communications (sold to Cisco for $232 million), and Shasta
Networks (sold to Nortel for $340 million). He guided Cascade through its
IPO in 1994 and reached capitalization of nearly $10 billion. Most recently,
he served as the VP of technology at Cisco Systems leading the company's
multi-service access concentrator product development, and was a member
of Cisco's Service Providers Voice Strategy and Technologies panel.
