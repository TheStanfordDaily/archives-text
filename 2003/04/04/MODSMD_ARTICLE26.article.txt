# Internship Opportunity
## 
### 
Sports Illustrated is seeking outgoing, highly motivated students
to participate in an elite internship opportunity, our SI Campus Rep
program. Serving as an on campus consultant for SI, campus reps will
act as an ambassador for the SI franchise through continuous dialogue
with the sales development, consumer marketing, edit, events, promo
tions and sports marketing groups. While this is an unpaid internship,
students will receive free merchandise, products, and a chance to win
an all-expense paid trip to Sports Illustrated's annual Swimsuit and
Sportsman of the Year parties in New York city, along with many other
cool incentives.
All interested students should SEND ALL RESUMES TO
sicampusreps@timeinc.com ASAP.
