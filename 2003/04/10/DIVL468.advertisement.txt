The Stanford Alumni Association is now accepting nominations for the
J. E. WALLACE STERLING
AWARD
Since 1979, the award has been presented annually at the Senior
Class Day Luncheon. The award recognizes a Senior whose
undergraduate activities demonstrate the strong potential for
continued service to the University and the Alumni Association.
Service might include involvement in athletics, clubs, drama and
musical productions, fundraising, journalism, public service, religious
organizations, residential education, social and political organizations,
student government, or University committees.
Deadline: MONDAY, APRIL 14, 2003
Applications: www.stanfordalumni.org/students
Submit to: Online or in person to the Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni
Center (MC: 5017) c/o SAA Student Programs
Contact: 736-2480 or studentprograms@stanfordalumni.org
