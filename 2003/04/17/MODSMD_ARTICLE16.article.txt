# Sports
## Athletes of the Week 
### 

ANDREA COX/The Stanford Daily
Stanford slugger
By extending his career-high hitting streak to 22 games, junior outfield
er Carlos Quentin raised his batting average to .415. He also leads the
Cardinal in slugging percentage and has stolen eight bases.


DARREN BECHTEL/The Stanford Daily
Sharpshooter
Kelsey Twist propelled the No. 1 o women's lacrosse team to victory on
Sunday over No. 15 Notre Dame with four goals, including the game
winner in the second overtime. With the win, the team improved to 10-2.
