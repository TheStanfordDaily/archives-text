PowerPoint and Pedagogy
Multimedia slide shows can be an effective
supplement to your presentations. However,
PowerPoint can harm as well as help your presentation.
This hands-on workshop covers strategies
for integrating slides into your presentation,
as well as the basic techniques of creating slides
using the PowerPoint application.

Meyer Library, Rm 280
