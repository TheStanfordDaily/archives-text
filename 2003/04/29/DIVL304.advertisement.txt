We're looking for:
Graduates and Undergraduates
interested in positively influencing the academic &
social transition of First-Year Stanford Students
During the 2003-2004 academic year
Expanded Advising and Peer &
Academic Support are comprised of:
PASs
(Undergraduate Peer Academic Advisors)
PAE Mentors
(Partners for Academic Excellence)
FARM PAAs
(Freshman Advising, Resources & Mentoring)
PSI-FT PAAs
(Public Services Initiatives in the First Year)
LOBT-CASA
Mentors (Lesbian Gay Bisexual Transgender -
Community Academic Support & Advising)
in co-sponsorship with the following communities:
Black Community Services Center, Stanford Athletics, Transfer Students,
El Centro Chicano, Native American Cultural Center,
LGBT Community Resource Center, Haas Center for Public Service
Stop by the UAC or one of the community centers listed above to pick up an application,
OR
Visit us online at: http://www.stanford.edu/dept/undergrad/uac/eap/liome.html
Applications Due to the UAC (Sweet Hall, First Floor)
or on-line by Friday, May 9 at 5 PM
Questions? Contact Duane Voigt: duane.voigt@stanford.edu
