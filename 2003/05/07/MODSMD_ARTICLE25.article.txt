# Athletes of the Week
## 
### 
The senior catcher earned his first ever Pac-10 Player of the Week honor
with his performance last weekend at USC. In Stanford's three-game
sweep, Garko went 10-for-13 with three homers and 12 RBI, including his
first career grand slam. He was also named co-national hitter of the week.

In her first Big Meet, the true freshman lea Stanford's scoring with 15
points. She finished first in the 100 meters, second in the 200 meters and
was a member of the winning 400- and 1,600-meter relay teams. The
women beat California 111-51.
