CLASS OF 2003:
GER's You Didn't Know About!
Presented by the Stanford Alumni Association
Cardinal Class Core
Nominations due Friday, May 16
The Stanford Alumni Association seeks
nominations for seniors who are likely to
commit a portion of their energy and pas
sion to Stanford through the Cardinal Class
Core program. The program aims to create
a nucleus of Class of '03 alumni who will
become key volunteers and leaders for the
class and for Stanford. Cardinal Class
Core members will dedicate some of their
enthusiasm to Stanford and help foster a
thriving network amongst all of their
Stanford classmates. All seniors, faculty
and staff are invited to submit a nomina
tion. An induction ceremony will be held
during Commencement weekend to which
all inductees, their families and nomina
tors will be invited.
The nomination form is available at:
www.stanfordalumni.org/students
Senior Send-Off
Thursday, May 29
4:00 p.m. - 5:30 p.m.
Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center
Senior Send-Off is your first official
Pre-CommefiCement event! It's a senior
tradition. You'll find things here to help
you through the last few weeks as
students and the next weeks, years,
decades as alumni!
■ Lifetime alumni email, Moving boxes
for SAA members, Frosh dorm
reunions, Food, Resume help, The
Stanford Career Network, and much
more!
- Win: A pizza party in your new city,
Free membership to the Stanford
Alumni Association, A VIP table at
Senior Dinner on the Quad, and other
great stuff!
Get everything you need, see everyone you
haven't seen, and eat everything in sight.
Don't miss out!
The event is free to all seniors.
Senior Dinner
on the Quad
Wednesday, June 11
7:00 p.m. - 12:00 midnight
Main Quad
Sandstone arches and swaying palm trees.
Dinner by candlelight. A senior video that
takes you down memory lane. Dancing
under the stars. It's an evening unlike any
other you've had at Stanford. Celebrate the
end of finals and your senior year with
hundreds of your classmates.
The event is free to all graduating
seniors with valid SUID.
An e-mail invitation will be sent
directly to all seniors in late May.
Deadline to RSVP is June 8.
Class Day Luncheon
Saturday, June 14
11:30 am - 1:00 pm
Ford Plaza Lawn
Join us fora celebration that features
a "last class lecture" from Professor
of Law and former University President
Gerhard Casper, the presentation of the
Sterling Award, the presentation of Senior
Gift, and a farewell address by the Senior
Class Presidents. It is an inspirational
program that honors all graduating Seniors.
All seniors and their guests are invited.
$13 per guest.
Free to all seniors with
SAA membership.
All guests must have
a ticket to enter.
Deadline is Friday, May 30.
The Night Before Party
Saturday, June 15
8:30 - 12:00 midnight
Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center
The countdown to graduation is on! The
Night Before Party - a Stanford tradition -
features hors d'oeuvres, beverages and
dancing in the Alumni Center. It's a perfect
place and time to gather family and friends
of all ages for an evening of celebration
before seniors become alumni. The party
is for all Class of 2003 graduates and their
guests. Bring your dancing shoes, cam
eras, and brace yourself fora memorable
evening!
$10 per senior.
$10 per guest.
All guests must have a ticket to enter.
Deadline is Friday, May 30.

In Good Company: Connecting Stanford's Past and Present
The Stanford Alumni Association (SAA) provides students with networking forums, celebratory and social events, and special awards
that help students meet the needs of college life as well as smooth their transition into alumni hood. The SAA fosters classmate and
student/alumni connections and develops programs that leverage a community of 200,000 students, alumni, and faculty worldwide.
Questions? Call: (650)736-2480, visit: www.stanfordalumni.org/students, or e-mail: studentprograms@stanfordalumni.org
