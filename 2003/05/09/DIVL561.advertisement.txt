Tiled School, Here I Come!
Give It Once, It's a Nice Gift.
Give It Eveiy Year and
It's a Nice Education.
U.S. Savings Bonds are gifts with a future. And when you give them
every year, they can make a real dent in someone's college bills. With eight
denominations to choose from, there's a size for every wallet. And whether
you choose the popular Series EE Bonds or the inflation-protected I Bonds,
your gift will grow safely until that special person needs it.
Savings Bonds can be purchased through most local banks, where you
work, or automatically through the new Savings Bonds Easy Saver M Plan at
www.easysaver.gov.
Call 1-800-4 US BOND for recorded rate information, or write to:
Savings Bonds Pocket Guide,
Parkersburg, WV 26106-1328. Uk
