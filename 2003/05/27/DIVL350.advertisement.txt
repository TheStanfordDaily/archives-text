Meet the candidates for YOUR next Dean of Students
MiCTIil
Wednesday, May 28 Friday, May 30
Refreshments will be served
Stanford is conducting a search for a new Dean of Students, the individual with a significant role to play in student life on
campus. The Dean of Students oversees the vast majority of Stanford s extracurricular student life, including student clubs,
Greek organizations, and the expansive activities and services of the community centers. The Dean of Students helps estab
lish and enforce student conduct policies and works closely with the ASSU.
If you are concerned about who will be the next dean, then you should plan to come to the discussions, meet each of the
finalists, ask questions, and give your input to the student members of the search committee.
