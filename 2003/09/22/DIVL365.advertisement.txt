CTL115/215. Voice Workshop
Innovative focus on breath, voice production, expansion of vocal
range, stamina, and clarity of articulation. Workshop is geared
toward public speaking, lectures, and job talks.
Thomas Freeland, Ph.D.

CTL 111/211. The Art of Effective Speaking
Practice effective oral communication. Develop skills
in framing and articulating ideas through speech. Speak
extemporaneously, prepare and deliver multimedia presentations,
formulate persuasive arguments, refine critical clarity of thought.
Enhance general facility and confidence in oral self-expression.
Thomas Freeland, Ph.D.
Tuesdays & Thursdays, 2:15-4:05, Meyer Forum Room
CTL 201. Science Course Design
Apply research on science learning to the design of effective
course materials in your discipline. Topics include syllabus design,
course content, format decisions, assessment planning, grading,
and strategies for teaching improvement.
Robyn Wright Dunbar, Ph.D.
Thursdays, 3:15-5:05, Building 320, Room 221
