The Committee on Black Performing Arts at Stanford is one of the few university programs in the
country devoted to the investigation, study and presentation of Black artistic culture and its role

the world. The CBPA is affiliated with the Drama Dept. and is directed by Prof. Harry J. Elam, Jr.
