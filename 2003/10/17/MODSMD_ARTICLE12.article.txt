# 14 arrested on campus last week, say police
## 
### MICHAEL MILLER STAFF WRITER 
This report from the Stanford Uni
versity Police log covers all events from
Oct. 7 to Oct. 14 that were reported to
the police department. This is a summa
ry of last week's crimes on campus.
Oct. 7
At 11 p.m., a suspicious person is re
ported in the bushes at the Alpha Sig
House.
Oct. 8
A driver is arrested at 9:35 a.m. for
driving with a suspended license.
A DVD player is reported stolen
from a room in Bldg. 300.
Oct. 9
At 1:22 p.m., two students are ob
served leaving the Bookstore without
paying for their books. They are cited
and released.

alcohol poisoning at the Hammarskjold
House.
At 3:50 a.m., a student is arrested on

charges of driving under the influence on
Alvarado Row.

license and no insurance is arrested.
At 1:20 p.m., a student is cited for
shoplifting at the Bookstore.

out of parking structure 5.
At 11 p.m., a student is arrested on

charges of driving under the influence

colliding with a bus stop and blue emer

At 1:15 a.m., a man is arrested on the
charge of driving under the influence
and released on $2,500 bail.

from Freshman / Sophomore College

minors.
At 2:35 p.m., six juveniles are arrested
for skateboarding near Building 380.

At 10:28 a.m., a driver not wearing a
