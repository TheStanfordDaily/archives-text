### 
The Morris K. Udall
Foundation Scholarship
Scholarship opportunities for college sophomores or juniors in
the current academic year and in fields related to the environ
ment or Native American/Native Alaskan health care or tribal
public policy.
If you are a sophomore or junior in fields related to the environment or Native
American/Native Alaskan health care or tribal policy you may be eligible to
apply for the Morris K. Udall Foundation Scholarship.
Each scholar receives up to $5,000, or the cost of tuition, fees, books, and room
and board, whichever is less. Scholarship recipients are eligible for one year of
support per application year.
HOW TO APPLY
Pick up application materials at the Undergraduate Research Programs offices
on the 4th Floor of Sweet Hall. For further information, contact us at 724-
7562; urp@vpue.stanford.edu; or http://urp.stanford.edu/
Application Deadline: Thursday, January 8, 2004 by 5:00 pm
