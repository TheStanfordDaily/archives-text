Google is Hiring
We're seeking intelligent minds and exceptional
individuals. (In other words, we're looking for you.)
Google is looking for outstanding candidates from the Stanford Class of
2004 for Associate Product Marketing Manager positions*.
In this position, you will help launch and market innovative products,
implement customer communications strategies, develop collateral, and
define market research studies.
Please submit your resume to Cardinal Recruiting from October 25 - 30,
2003 for interview consideration.
For more information on this position, please go to: www.google.com/jobs
*Google will be recruiting for product management and engineering jobs during the winter.
