Listening for What's After Stanford
Note:
This event is geared for Stanford women who are graduating in the near future.
It may only be fall quarter, but there is already a lot to think about for seniors
and other students preparing for post-Stanford transitions. Facilitated by Senior
Associate Dean for Religious Life Rabbi Patricia Karlin-Neumann, Heartbeats
Away will be a progressive series of three conversations for women.
It will provide a comfortable space for you to think more deeply about yourself,
your experience at Stanford, your mode of making decisions, and ultimately, how
to listen for what might be your next step after Stanford. Rather than addressing
particular career goals, these discussions will draw on emotional and spiritual
resources for considering what's next.
Sundays, November 9, 16, 23.
5:00 - 7:00 p.m.
Common Room on the third floor of
Old Union Clubhouse
Vegetarian dinner will be provided;
please let us know if you have any further dietary restrictions.
RSVP each time to heidi.williams@stanford.edu by Friday 4 p.m.
"The place God calls you to is the place where your
deep gladness and the world's deep hunger meet."
- Frederick Buechner
