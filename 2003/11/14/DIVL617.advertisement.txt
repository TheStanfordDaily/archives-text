SCHOLARSHIP AWARD
THE 2004
HAAS-KOSHLAND
Established by the Jewish Community Endowment Fond of the Jewish Community
Federation of San Francisco, the Peninsula, Marin and Sonoma Counties,
the award provides a year of study and personal development
in Israel beginning summer or fall, 2004.
TO APPLY: Submit an application form and a brief statement outlining how your
interests would be uniquely enhanced by a year's residence in Israel.
Contact John Pearson at the Overseas Resource Center, for further information.
gPSI JEWISH APPLICATION DEADLINE:
COMMUNITY January 5, 2004
The winner will be notified before March 1, 2004.
This annual award was inspired by the enthusiastic commitment of Walter A. Haas, Sr., and Daniel R Koshland. Sr.,
to student life at many colleges and universities, and a lifelong dedication to the spirit of Israel.
