Google is coming
to campus
Want to learn more about the people, technology,
and vision behind your favorite search engine?
Now's your chance. Susan Wojcicki, Google veteran, will give an overview
of who we are and why we love what we do. Students interested in
product marketing positions at Google are encouraged to attend.
Wednesday, November 19, 5:30 - 7:00 p.m.
The Faculty Club, Gold Lounge
Interested applicants should bring a resume to the talk. For more information on
these positions, please go to: www.google.com/jobs
