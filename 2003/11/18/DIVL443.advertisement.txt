Interested in a Career in Science?

Biological Sciences announces the new
Pre-Grad Program
for students interested in biological research
and possible careers in science.
A new two-course series will be offered:
Biosci 54 - Genes, Genomes and Proteins:
Introduction to Advanced Independent
Research Laboratory (winter)
Biosci 55 - Advanced Independent
Research Laboratory (spring)
Taught by Profs. Tint Stearns & Martha Cyert
Students will carry out independent research in
a state-of-the-art lab, integrating computational,
genetic, genomic and microscopic analysis.
Application required.
Preference given to sophomores.
Apply Now!
Deadline: 5 PM Monday Nov. 24
http://www.stanford.edu/group/pregrad/
Funded by the Howard Hughes
