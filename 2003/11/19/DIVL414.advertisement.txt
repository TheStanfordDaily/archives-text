trend" (Nov. 13) incorrectly stated that Stanford's School of Medicine saw
its applicant pool decrease this year. Medical school applications to Stan
ford have been down in recent years, following a national trend. But, ac
cording to Dean of the Medical School Philip Pizzo, the number of appli
cants this year will likely increase.

the name of Israeli Minister of Education Limor Livnat was misspelled.
