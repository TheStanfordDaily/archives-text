Tuesday, December 9, 2003 - Thursday, December 11, 2003
11 am -4 pm • Stanford Bookstore Art Alcove
• Bring in your PC or Apple computer and receive a voucher for the fair market value
of your equipment to use toward the purchase of the latest technology.
• Come learn about the benefits of Mac OS X and why it's time to upgrade now!
• Special Educational Discounts will be available on selected Apple laptops and
desktops
• Now you can get into the newest Apple technology for as little as $l9 a month
Personal purchases only. Equipment owned by Stanford University is ineligible for this program.
Vouchers may not be used with P-Card or Purchase Order purchases.
Monitors excluded from this program. Computers over 3 years old may not be eligible for trade in program. Trade in vouchers good thru January 14, 2004
and may only be used toward the purchase of a new computer. Voucher is good for purchase of any Apple or PC equipment.
Before you drop off your computer for the trade in, be sure that You have backed up your critical data.
