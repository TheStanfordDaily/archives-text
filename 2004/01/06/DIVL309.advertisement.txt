The Stanford Library
has a New Web Site
The Stanford University Libraries and
Academic Information Resources (SULAIR)
Web site has a completely new look.
In addition to an updated look and feel,
the redesigned Web site boasts a more
user-centered information design and
improved ADA compliance.
New features of the site
include:
• Improved, quick access to many useful
research resources,
• Easy access to information about
SULAIR 1 s services,
• A How To section with links to
instruction and policies, and
• A Did you know? feature highlighting
new and exciting library resources.
Check out the new Stanford University Libraries and Academic
Information Resources site at:
http://library.stanford.edu/
