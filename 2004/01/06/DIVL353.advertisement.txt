Join Stanford students, faculty and staff for an exciting new learning opportunity!
CEES7SI - SUSTAINABLE HABITS: INTERACTIONS
BETWEEN STUDENTS AND SYSTEMS AT STANFORD
Lectures and discussions on the complete cycles of resource systems that provide
us food, water, heat and electricity, buildings and infrastructure, waste disposal
and recycling service, and transportation. Emphasis is on the interconnections between
resource systems, and the externalities inflicted on ecological, social and economic
environments by the systems currently in place.
