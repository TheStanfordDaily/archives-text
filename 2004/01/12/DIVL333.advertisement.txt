##

Official Notice:
There will be a meeting of the members of The Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation on
Wednesday, January 14,2004, for the purpose of electing the President of the Corporation
and the Editor in Chief of The Stanford Daily. If a runoff election is required, there will
also be a meeting of the members of the Corporation on Thursday, January 15,2004. Both
meetings shall take place at the offices of the Corporation in Suite 101 of the Storke Publi
cations Building. The meetings will be in session from 10:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. both days.
