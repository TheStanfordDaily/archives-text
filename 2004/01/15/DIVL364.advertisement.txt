Group Discussions
Thursday, January 15
The Round Room, behind and adjacent to Memorial Church
5:30 - 7:00 p.m.
The Road Less Traveled:
A Conversation about Sexuality and Spirituality in the Black Community
Stanford undergrad and grad students only please. Jan Barker-Alexander, Asst. Dean of Students and
Director, Black Community Services Center, and Lisa Moore, Asst. Director, Women's Community
Center, will host the discussion.
Dinner will be served.
All backgrounds welcome.
Contact joanne.sanders@stanford.edu for details/questions. RSVP to heidiw@stanford.edu.
Sponsored by Stanford's Office for Religious Life, http://religiouslife.stanford.edu
The Deans for Religious Life are committed to welcoming students of all genders and sexual
orientations, all religious and non-religious traditions, and all cultural backgrounds.
