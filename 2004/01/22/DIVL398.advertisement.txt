The Program in Ethics in Society
Invites Applications for
The Development of New Courses With an Ethical Focus
for the 2004-2005 Academic Year
We will offer up to $7,500 per course to
assist with development, research and technologi
cal support. We will also provide a workshop to
assist faculty in formulating the ethical dimension
of the course.
Applicants should submit a course description
(no more than 5 pages) including a budget.
Applications must be received by March 5, 2004.
