Multifaith Martin Luther King, Jr.

Veteran Stage Actor Aldo Billingslea will preach from the pulpit of Memorial
Church in character as The Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Billingslea is an
Assistant Professor of Acting and Performance Studies at Santa Clara
University. He has appeared in productions by August Wilson, Tennesse
Williams and William Shakespeare. He teaches acting as well as lecture
courses in Multicultural Theatre, American Theatre from the Black Perspective
and Literature and Performance.
