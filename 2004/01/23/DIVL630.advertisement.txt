Thanks to a special alumni gift
The Department of Anthropological Sciences
is pleased to offer
The Beagle II Awards
■ Awards of up to $4000 will be made to the best two or three proposals for a
summer voyage of scientific discovery to any appropriate location
in the world.
■ Open to Stanford undergraduates of all majors and all years
Deadline : Applications due by 5:00 PM, Tuesday, February 24, 2004
Awards will be announced Monday, March 8, 2004
Information Forum
Tuesday, January 27, 4pm
Building 360, Room 361K
Faculty advisors and 2003 recipients will describe
the Beagle II and its application process in detail
Highlight of the event will be presentations
by each of last year s Beagle II Award winners
Don't miss it!
For Information on the Beagle II Awards check the website:
www.Stanford .edu/aroup/beaqle2
Faculty Coordinators for Beaale II:
Prof. William Durham,
eb.whd@stanford .edu
Prof. David DeGusta,
deausta@stanford .edu
For help with questions contact:
Mary Cahill,
Student Services Coordinator,
Building 360, Room 361F
736-0734,
mcahill@stanford .edu
