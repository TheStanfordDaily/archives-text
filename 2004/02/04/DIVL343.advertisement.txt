Interested in Being 4 Row Manager?
(This information applies to all Row Houses & EASI House)
Interested in joining the management team of a Row House?
Here's what you have to do:
• Go to the Row Manager Application Website:
http://selection.stanford.edu
This website contains the on-line application form.
This form contains:
■ Application Due Dates/Where to Turn In Your Application
• Who Is Eligible to Apply
- Required Training Dates-Who to Contact for Questions and Interview Processes
• Other Important Information
• Attend ONE Mandatory Manager Informational Session
Your attendance is required for you to be considered for any Row Manager position.
Please Note: Though the application contains all requirements in the application the
following are important to note.
• You must be of Junior or Senior Status for the 2004-2005
academic year (some grad/co-term spots available in certain
houses) - see application.
• You must hove remaining years of guaranteed undergrad housing.
• If you are applying fora Row RCC position you must fill out a Row Student Manager
General application and a Row CC application.
• You must attend one Mandatory Information Session whether you are applying fora
positon in a Greek or Non-Greek house.
