February 9-14
- Wear red.
- Come tie a ribbon to our remembrance
fence ui White Plaza all week to honor
and support anyone touched by sexual
assault or violence.
7 p.m. COHO Spoken Word Night! Bring
your friends and hang out fora cool night
of poetry, spoken word and memory.
10:30 p.m.: Meet in White Plaza to do the
chalk campaign.
12:00 p.m.: Rape panel discussion at the
WCC with Sally Baird and Sarah Javis,
director of Rape Trauma Services.

tape. You may never have another chance
to do anything like this .. .
8 p.m.: Come see "The Vagina Monologues"
in Dink. Also come to hang art and sell
chocolate and buttons. We'll need lots of
woman power to help it all get ui action.
