Stanford Bookstore
Author Event
Betty Shabazz:
A Remarkable Story of Survival and
Faith Before and After Malcolm X
by Russell Rickford
Friday, February 13,2004 • 12:00 Noon
Stanford Bookstore Art Alcove
Russell Rickford, Ph.D.
Candidate, History,
Columbia University,
New York, is former
correspondent for The
Philadelphia Inquirer
and author of Betty
Shabaaz: Her Life with
Malcolm X and Fight to
Preserve his Legacy and
co-author of the
American Book Award
winning Spoken Soul:
The Story of Black
English. He received his
B. A. with Honors, Magna
Cum Laude, from
Howard University,
where he also served as
two years as president of
the Alpha Phi Alpha
Fraternity, the nation's
oldest black fraternity
with members such as
Martin Luther King, Jr.
and David Dinkins.
*Co-sponsored with the
African/American
Studies at Stanford.
