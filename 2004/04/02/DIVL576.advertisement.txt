7 University Public Worship
At
MEMORIAL CHURCH
10:00 a.m.
Sunday, April 4
"Palm Sunday"
The Rev. Joanne Sanders
University Organist Robert Huw Morgan and the Memorial Church
Choir, under the direction of Gregory Wait, will perform a specially
composed choir and organ piece written for the re-dedication of the
100 year old church. A student liturgical dance team will also be a
part of the service.
All are welcome.
Child care provided, pre-school age and under.
http://religiouslife.stanford.edu
