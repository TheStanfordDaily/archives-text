The Office for Religious Life presents:
What Matters to Me and Whtj
Rosa Gonzalez
Director, Diversity and Access Office
Wednesday, April 14
12:00-1:00 P.M.
Side Chapel in Memorial Church.
Please feel free to bring your lunch.
Rosa Gonzalez, Director of the Diversity &. Access Office and Resident Fellow at
Yost, the Spanish language residence, has been at Stanford for nine years. Rosa
received her B.A. in History from the University of Santa Clara in 1980 and her
J.D. from Hastings College of the Law in 1993. Immediately after graduating
from the University of Santa Clara, Rosa spent a year as a Jesuit Volunteer in East
Los Angeles teaching 2nd grade.
What Matters to Me and Why is a discussion and lecture series that encourages reflection within
the Stanford community on matters of personal values, beliefs, and motivations in order to better
understand the lives and inspirations of those who shape the University. All are welcome.
For info., call the Office for Religious Life at 723-1762 or visit our website.
http://religiouslife.stanford.edu/events/wmmw/index.html
