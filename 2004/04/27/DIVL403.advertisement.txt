For Graduate Students and Visiting Scholars
EFS 688: Intensive English and Academic Orientation for Foreign Graduate
Students (6 units). An intensive course for students beginning graduate study in the fall.
July 2-August 13,2004. Application deadline May 20.
EFS 688V: Intensive English for Visiting Scholars (5 units). An intensive course for
visiting scholars and researchers. July 2-August 13,2004. Application deadline May 20.
Courses for continuing graduate students: Discussion, Oral Presentation, Speaking &
Teaching in English, Pronunciation & Intonation, Advanced Pronunciation, Academic
Writing, and Advanced Graduate Writing (3 units each). June 22-August 13,2004.
Application deadline June 21.
Continuing students are also welcome in special I-unit EFS 689 sections in
Vocabulary and Idiom, Living and Working with Americans, Learning English on Your
Own, and American Humor.
For information on all EFS courses, see our website at
wwn.stanford.edu/eroup/efs or email tafowlerfa stanford.edu
