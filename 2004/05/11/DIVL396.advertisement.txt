General Motors invites students
from Stanford University to the
Road to Hydrogen
Tech Tour on May 12 at The Presidio.
Participants* will have an
excellent opportunity to
ride and drive
close to a dozen GM advanced
technology vehicles - including
the revolutionary Hy-wire, the world's
first driveable fuel cell propulsion
vehicle with drive-by-wire technology.
WHEN: Wednesday, May 12,2004
8:30 a.m.- 4:30 p.m.
Students will not be excused from class to attend
GM executives and
technology experts will
be on hand to present
information on hydrogen
fuel cells, hybrids and
improvements to the
internal combustion
engine, as well as
discuss the future
of transportation.
* Everyone is welcome to attend.
Drivers must be 21 years of age
and possess a valid driver's license
WHERE: Presidio National Park, San Francisco
GM Technology Trailer
Located at Main Post Parking Lot
GM Tech Tour/Stanford Shuttle Schedule
Departing times from Stanford at the Oval:
7:30 a.m.
9:30 a.m.
11:30 a.m.
1:30 p.m.
Departing times from the Presidio:
10:30 a.m.
12:30 p.m.
2:30 p.m.
4:30 p.m.
WHO: GM executives, company engineers
and subject matter experts
For more information or to reserve a space, please contact
Tom Hajkus at tom.hajkus@hassmsl.com or call 734-377-6554.
