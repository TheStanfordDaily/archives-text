# LOOKING BACK...
## The
						Year in Photos 
### 
(Top right) Two students steal a kiss in front of Memorial Church in a
rare serene moment during Full Moon on the Quad on Oct. 9. The fes
tivities didn't end well for all students, however, as several suffering
from alcohol poisoning had to be taken to the hospital.
(Top left) Debris outside Maples Pavilion marked the beginning of a
reported $30 million renovation project that will displace men and
women's basketball and women's volleyball until Jan. 2005.
(Bottom right) Students hold a candlelight vigil to close Stanford's
second annual Books not Bombs demonstration on March 4.
(Middle left) Josh Childress speaks at a press conference after the
men's basketball team fell to Alabama 70-67 on March 20, ending the
team's run in the NCAA tournament.
(Bottom left) A fire sparked in the shrubs outside Crothers Memori
al Hall at 12:30 p.m. April 17 drawing students from nearby dorms,
three fire trucks and an ambulance to the scene.
