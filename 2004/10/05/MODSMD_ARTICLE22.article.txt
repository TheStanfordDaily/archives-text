Opinions submission policy:
All Stanford community members
are encouraged to submit materials
representative of their views on cur
rent issues or events. Such submis
sions can either be e-mailed to
letters@daily.stanford.edu, faxed to
(650) 725-1329 or delivered in person
to The Daily's office, which is located
in the Storke Publications Building.
Submissions should be in the form of a
letter to the editor or a guest column.
The Daily reserves the right to make re
visions to any materials submitted, so
long as the author's intent is preserved.
