Daniel Pearl Music Day
Honoring Stanford alumnus and reporter
Pearl, 2004 Chamber Music LIVE! features
works by Bach, Berger, Bruch, and Weir, with

Anna Coburn, soprano; Jennifer Lane, mezzo

Vision
Three women of three different faiths, who
are living the reality of the Israeli-Palestinian
conflict, will share their experiences with the
painful conflict and ongoing violence and their
hopes fora just peace. Building 420 Room 041,7
p.m.
Video Presentation: Muhammad, Legacy of a
Prophet

the lives of more than 1.2 billion people around

Muhammad, as he is chronicled in historical
records. Graduate Community Center, 750 Es
condido Rd (Next to Mirrielees),7:3o p.m.

Pavilion Next to Hoover Tower, 11 a.m.

Renaissance Painting

ing a painting to a known artist. Cantor Arts Cen

um Way.

Bing Wing, Green Library, Stanford University.

Finding Sellaio: Conserving and Attributing a
Renaissance Painting
This exhibition focuses on the conservation of
a Renaissance painting entitled "The Virgin,
Child, and Saint John." The exhibition shows
how the conservation process assists in attribut
ing a painting to a known artist. Cantor Arts Cen
ter, 328 Lomita Drive, off Palm Driver at Muse
um Way.
