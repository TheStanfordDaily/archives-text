EUROPEAN FORUM
A NEW BEGINNING?
WHAT THE UNITED STATES CAN DO
WITH EUROPE NOW
Speaker:
Timothy Garton Ash
Timothy Carton Ash, an internationally acclaimed contemporary historian, is a Professor
of European Studies at Oxford University and a Senior Fellow at the Hoover Institution.
His latest book "Free World: America, Europe and the Surprising Future of the West"
ivas published in the US on November 9, the 15th anniversary of
the fall of the Berlin Wall
Tuesday, November 16, 2004
4:30 - 6:00 PM
Oak Lounge, Tresidder Memorial Union
Stanford University .
Free and open to the public
For further information, please visit our website at http://siis.stanford.edu
