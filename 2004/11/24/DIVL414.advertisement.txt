# Adv. 1 Page 1
## 
### 
The Daily has completed
its daily publishing cycle
for autumn quarter. We
will publish three more
issues this year. On Mon
day, Nov. 29, The Daily
will print "2004: The year
in news," a compilation
of the best news stories
from 2004. On Tuesday,
Nov. 30, The Daily will
print "2004: The year in
sports," a wrap-up of
sports from 2004. On
Thursday, Dec. 2, The
Daily will print its last
issue of the quarter.
