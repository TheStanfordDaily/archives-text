### 
The Morris K. IJdall
Foundation Scholarship
Scholarship opportunities for college sophomores or juniors in
the current academic year and in fields related to environmental
science or policy, or Native American/Native Alaskan
health care or tribal public policy.
Information Meeting
Tuesday, November 30, 2004
4:30 pm
History Corner 200-305
If you are a sophomore or junior in fields related to the environ
ment or Native American/Native Alaskan health care or tribal
policy you may be eligible to apply for the Morris K. Udall
Foundation Scholarship.
Each scholar receives up to $5,000, or the cost of tuition,
fees, hooks, and room and board, whichever is less.
Scholarship recipients are eligible for one year of support per
application year.
HOW TO APPLY
Pick up application materials at the Undergraduate Research
Programs offices on the 4th Floor of Sweet Hall. For further infor
mation, contact us at 724-7562; urp@vpue.stanford.edu; or
http://urp.stanford.edu/
Application Deadline:
Friday, January 14, 2005 by 5:00 pm
