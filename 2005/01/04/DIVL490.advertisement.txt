# Adv. 10 Page 4
## 
### 
Attention Sophomores and juniors:
INTERESTED IN INTERDISCIPLINARY STUDIES?
consider joining...
THE HUMANITIES HONORS PROGRAM
• An honors program open to students in any major
• An opportunity for students who are minors
• in one of the humanities departments to write senior
w- theses in their minor fields
• An option for those who wish to devise their own
interdisciplinary majors in humanities
New members of the Program should enroll in
HUMNTIES 100 "Honors Core Colloquium"
TOPIC: *HAMLET*
Tuesdays OR Thursdays at noon - 3 units
Mb restricted to members of the Humanities Honors Program
The interdisciplinary colloquium provides a rare opportunity to
focus on the text of a single play in order to explore the seemingly
infinite array of interpretive possibilities.
