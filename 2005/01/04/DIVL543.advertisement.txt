Reconstituting Iraq:
Making the Interim Constitution
Speaker:
Feisal Istrabadi
Feisal Istrabadi is the Deputy Permanent Representative of Iraq to the United Nations,
and one of Iraq's most important constitutional thinkers. He was one of the principal
legal drafters of Iraq's interim constitution (adopted on March 8 of 2004) and the lead
author of its Bill of Fundamental Rights.
Wednesday, January 5, 2005
4:30 - 6:00 PM
Oksenberg Conference Room, 3rd Floor
Encina Hall, Stanford University
corner of Serra and Galvez
For further information, please visit our website at http://siis.stanford.edu
