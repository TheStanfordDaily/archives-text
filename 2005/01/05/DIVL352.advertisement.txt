Reconstituting Iraq:
Making the Interim Constitution
Speaker:
Feisal Istrabadi

and one of Iraq's most important constitutional thinkers. He was one of the principal
legal drafters of Iraq's interim constitution (adopted on March 8 of 2004) and the lead
author of its Bill of Fundamental Rights.
