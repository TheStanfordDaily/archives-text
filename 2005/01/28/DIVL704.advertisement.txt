Stanford
Law School
The Program in Law, Economics & Business invites you to attend
The Presidential Election of 2004:
What happened and what does the future hold?
A presentation by Cameron F. Kerry
Cameron F. Kerry, brother of and advisor to Democratic Presidential candidate
John Kerry, will analyze the aftermath of the 2004 election, discuss its
implications for the political process, and describe the challenges facing the
Democratic Party. Mr. Kerry is a member of the law firm of Mintz Levin, where
he specializes in litigation involving regulated industries, superfund claims, and
toxic torts.
Monday, January 31, 2005
12:30 p.m. in Room 190
