Interested in Being
4 Row Manager?
(Jhis information applies lo all How Houses & EAST House)
Interested in joining the management team of a Row House?
Here's what you have to do:
• Go to the Row Manager Application Website (will be available 2/3/05):
http://selection.stanford.edu
This website contains the on-line application form.
This form contains:
■ Application Due Dates/Where to Turn In Your Application
■ Who Is Eligible to Apply
• Required Training Dates-Who to Contact for Questions and Interview
Processes
• Other Important Information
• Attend ONE Mandatory Manager Informational Session
Your attendance is required for you to be considered for any Row Manager
position.
Please Note: Though the application contains all requirements in the applica
tion the following are important to note.
• You must be of Junior or Senior Status for ifie 2005-2006
academic year (some grad/co-term spots available in certain
houses) - see application.
• You must have remaining years of guaranteed undergrad housing.
• If you are applying fora Row RCC position you must fill out a Row Student
Manager General application and a Row CC application.
• If you are applying fora Row CC position, you must go through a qualifying
interview with Res Comp AND be interviewed at the house level.
• You must attend one Madatory Information Session whether you are applying
fora positon in a Greek or Non-Greek house.
