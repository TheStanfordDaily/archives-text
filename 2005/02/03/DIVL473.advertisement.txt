Keck Graduate Institute (KGI), working with world-class advisors from science,
industry, and higher education, focuses exclusively on educational programs and
research leading to beneficial applications for emerging discoveries in the life
sciences, and on education leaders for bioscience industries. KGI is a member of
the Claremont Colleges consortium in southern California. All students receive
generous Fellowships with no research or teaching requirements.
