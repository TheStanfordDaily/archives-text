### 
Strategic Growth Associate
The D. E. Shaw group, a highly successful investment
and technology development firm headquartered in
New York, is seeking an individual with an extraordinary
record of professional or academic achievement to
manage recruiting activities at its new Silicon Valley office.
The Strategic Growth Associate will be responsible for
executing strategies to identify and recruit world-class
talent in a variety of fields and will work closely with
senior management to attract successful applicants. This
is a dynamic, high-visibility position in an informal yet
intense start-up environment, offering the opportunity to
grow with the office's new business lines. Candidates must
have outstanding interpersonal, writing, and presentation
skills, as well as a B.A. or B.S. degree; prior recruiting
experience is not required. Send resume and cover letter
along with any available standardized test scores to
J. Myers at StanDaily-JMyers@career.deshaw.com.
Members of the D. E. Shaw group do not discriminate in
employment matters on the basis of race, color, religion, gender,
national origin, age, military service eligibility, veteran status, sexual
orientation, marital status, disability, or any other protected class.
