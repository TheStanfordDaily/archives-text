STUDENT
RESEARCH INTERN
SAN MATEO
US Investigations Services has intern
ships for students who can perform
extensive internal computer data
entry and some field work, as well as
travel to various locations for local
police and court checks. These are
part-time paid opportunities that
progress to a full time work schedule
during the summer.
The starting rate is $12.62 per hour.
Interns will work from our San Mateo
Office. Visit our website at
www.usis.com for details and to apply.
