Economics Department
Town Hall Meeting
Plans for Economics Curriculum and Courses
Please attend the Economics Department Town Hall Meeting.
*
Economics Building, Room 140
Tuesday, March 29th, 5:30 p.m.
Current Majors, Future Majors,
and other students interested in Economics
courses are all invited to attend.
SENIORS:
By spring quarter of your senior year, we hope there's no mystery as to why
you were admitted to Stanford. But if you are still unsure...
Come find out how admission decisions are made and learn how you can
become involved with our undergraduate admission programs and events
after graduation.
TOMORROW:
Wednesday, March 30th
7:00 pm - 9:00 pm
Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center, Lane Lyons Room
Snacks and drinks will be provided.
Please RSVP to Jo Ann Will at jwill@stanford.edu today!
(This event is for seniors only. Everyone else can keep guessing.)
