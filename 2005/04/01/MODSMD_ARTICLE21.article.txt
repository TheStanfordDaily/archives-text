# Thefts; campus intruders; arrests for alcohol, drugs
## 
### 
By CAITUN BORGER
This report covers a selection of
crimes from March 15 to March 30
as recorded in the Stanford Police
Department Public Bulletin.
Tuesday, March 15
At 4 a.m. an unknown male en
tered a woman's room in Rains
complex and left after she yelled
for him to get out.
At 4:10 a.m. an undergraduate
male was booked for being drunk
in public.
Wednesday, March 16
A vehicle's passenger side tires
were slashed in Panama parking lot.
Friday, March 18
A person broke into an under
graduate's vehicle at Wilbur Hall
and stole the CD stereo.
At 12:30 p.m. a stolen golf cart was
found behind Pine Hall.
At 4:40 a.m., a person was cited

and released for possession of
marijuana on Quarry Rd.
Saturday, March 19
A purse containing a wallet,
cell phone and other property
was stolen from a vehicle some
time during the night.
At 1:20 a.m. a man was booked
into the Santa Clara County Main
Jail for trespassing, resisting arrest
and disobeying court order in
Building 60.
At 4 p.m. an unknown person
entered an office in Terman Engi
neering Building through an open
window and went through a per
son's belongings.
Sunday, March 20
It was reported that CDs and
other personal property were
stolen from a vehicle in Laganita
Court on the night of March 18.
Monday, March 21
At 3:00 a.m. an unknown per

son entered a dark room in the
Kappa Alpha Theta House and
stole a laptop computer.
Tuesday, Match 22
At 8:00 a.m. an unknown
person pried open a window in
Bollard Dining Society cafete
ria, but nothing was reported
stolen.
At 8:30 a.m. an unknown per
son entered a room at Terman En
gineering Center and rifled
through property, but nothing was
reported stolen.
Friday, March 25
At 1:35 p.m. one person was
booked into juvenile hall and an
other was cited and released for
stealing wallets.
Saturday, March 26
A person reported their car
stolen, but then found it in a differ
ent parking lot with no apparent
damage.

Sunday, March 27
An unknown person broke into
The Daily news office sometime
on Saturday night and stole six
computer monitors from several
rooms.
Monday, March 28
At 3:50 p.m. a law school stu
dent's electric scooter was
stolen from the Law School
vicinity.
Tuesday, Match 29
A person's backpack contain
ing a media player and other items
was stolen from the athlete area
during a track meet sometime be
tween 4 and 6 p.m.
Wednesday, March 30
At 12:55 a.m. a suspicious
male was found knocking on the
door of a women's restroom in
Potter House. He said he was the
janitor.
