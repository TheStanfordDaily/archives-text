# Thefts in parking lots; dorm intrusions
## 
### CAITLIN BORGER 
This report covers a selection of
crimes from March 31 to April 5 as
recorded in the Stanford Police
Department Public Bulletin.
Thursday, March 31
An unknown person smashed a
vehicle window and stole the car
stereo in the Stern Hall parking
lot.
At 3 a.m. a man in a motor ve
hicle was found with an open con
tainer of alcohol.
At 12:30 p.m. a fire started on
the third floor of Mirrielees from a
pot of oil that was left unattended.
It was reported that an un
known person had stolen cables
from the construction site at the
Arrillaga Family Sports Center.
Friday, April 1
Sometime during the night an
unknown person entered a private
home located on the campus, went

through some cupboards and ran
sacked two cars. Only a key ring
was reported stolen.
The in-dash stereo was stolen
from a vehicle parked in the Quar

An unknown person entered a
room in the Junipero dorm in
Wilbur Hall and attempted to steal
a laptop.
At 6:30 p.m. a graduate stu
dent's backpack and wallet were
taken from a bench between
buildings 320 and 360. The back
pack was found, but the wallet is
still missing.
Saturday, April 2
At 1:10 a.m. an undergraduate

Jail for being drunk in public at the
Kappa Sigma house.
At 1:20 a.m. another under
graduate was booked into San
Jose Main Jail for being drunk in
public on Lomita Drive.
Cash was stolen from the safe at
Jamba Juice sometime during the

night.
At 10 a.m. a person threat

Oval.
At 10:30 a.m. the windows in
two different vehicles were
smashed in the Museum Way
parking lot. but nothing was stolen
in either case.

At 11 a.m. an unknown person

Tuesday, April 5
A car stereo was stolen from an
