Opinions submission policy:
Readers are encouraged to submit
columns or letters to the editor by e
mail to letters@daily.stanford.edu; by
fax to (650) 725-1329; or in person to
The Daily's office in the Storke Publi
cations Building. The Daily reserves
the right to edit any submission for style,
content or length.
