Did you know you can direct your University
Bill to a specific address (such as your parent
or third party sponsor)? To add or update your
billing address, log on to http://axess.stanford.edu,
navigate to the Personal Info tab and select the
Addresses function.
If you have a question about your ITSS bill, submit
a HelpSU request at http://helpsu.stanford.edu.
Under Request Category select "Student Services."
Under Request Type select "Student ITSS Services
billing."
