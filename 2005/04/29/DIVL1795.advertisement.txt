ENGLISH PROGRAMS
Local International Community & Visitors
July 15-August 12, 2005: Application deadline May 20
EFS 683: California CLASSIC
(4 units) MTThF 10-noon and 1-3 p.m.
California CLASSIC (Culture and
Language at Stanford, Summer Intensive
Course) includes classes which focus on
effective communication, writing,
listening comprehension, literature and
discussions of California and American
culture. Also included are a number of
field trips and other social activities.
NOTE: Enrollment is limited to adults
18 years of age or older.
Incoming Graduate Students & Visiting Scholars
July 1-August 12, 2005: Application deadline May 20
EFS 688: Intensive English and Academic Orientation for Foreign Graduate
Students (6 units). An intensive course for students beginning graduate study in the fall.
EFS 688V: Intensive English for Visiting Scholars (5 units). An intensive course for
visiting scholars and researchers.
Current Stanford Graduate Students
June 21-August 12,2005: Application deadline June 20
Courses offered in the summer for continuing Stanford students are as follows:
Listening & Communication, Oral Presentation, Speaking & Teaching in English,
Pronunciation & Intonation, Academic Writing, and Advanced Graduate Writing (3 units
each).
Continuing students are also welcome in special EFS 689 sections in Vocabulary
and Idiom, Living and Working with Americans, Learning English on Your Own, and
American Humor.
For information on all EFS courses, see our website at
