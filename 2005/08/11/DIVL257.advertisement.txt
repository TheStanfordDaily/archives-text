DEADLINE & PAYMENT
AO DEADLINE
Regular line ads (new ads, changes, cancellations):
3:00 p.m. one business day preceding publication
Classified Display ads (new ads, changes, cancellations):
3:00 p.m. two business days Drecedinq publication
PAYMENT
Classified ads must be paid in full at the time of placement.
None will be billed. Visa and MasterCard are accepted, with a
two line minimum. Ad runs five consecutive issues for each
week ordered.
TERMS Si POLICIES
No refunds are given for canceled classified ads. Check your
ad the first day it runs; The Stanford Daily will only correct errors
reported on the first day
