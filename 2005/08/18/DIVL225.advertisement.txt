Circulation & Distribution
The Weekly is usually delivered between 8 and 11 a.m. on Thursdays. To
request distribution to your locotion or to report a distribution problem,
please contact the business manager at (650) 723-2555 * 301

Back issues from the past week are available in the lobby of the Storke
Publications Building. Content from all issues is available at the newspaper:
Web site.
Reprints
Permission to reprint an article or photograph may be obtained by contact
ing the editor in chief at (650) 725-2)00 x5Ol. Prints of Daily photograph:

CORRECTIONS
It is the policy of The Stanford Daily Publishing Corp. to correct all significan
errors brought to the attention of the editors. If you think there is a need for
a correction regarding any article, please contact Summer Editor Camille
Ricketts at (650) 725-2100 x5Ol or e-mail her at eic@daily.stanford.edu
The Daily will investigate all legitimate requests for corrections and reserve;
the right to determine if a printed correction is necessary. All corrections wil
be printed in this space.
