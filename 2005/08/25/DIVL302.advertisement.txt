On Orientation Day at Stanford University, over 30,000 new students and parents will arrive in Palo Alto, Los
Altos, and Menlo Park looking for food, entertainment, and a place to stay. Do you want their business?
Introduce yourself to them in The Stanford Daily's Orientation 2005 Special Issue. Printed at the onset of
Orientation, The Stanford Daily's Orientation Special Issue is the best way to get your business noticed by arriving
Freshmen and parents. Don't miss out on this opportunity to make a great first impression on students and parents
this year!
• The Orientation Issue is the first and most widely read paper of the year.
• Circulation of 15,000 and a readership of 30,000 students, parents, faculty and other visitors.
• The Orientation Issue has four sections, including bonus "Welcome Back" sections:
Campus Life: A survival guide for life on the Farm. Information on where to buy a bike, get a bite to eat,
find a bank, and all the other essentials for freshmen during their first week on campus.
Dining Guide: A special advertising insert that will run 3 times over the year- Orientation Issue, Big Game
Issue, and Parents' Weekend. Your bar, restaurant, or cafe will be listed on the map with the purchase of a
minimum 10-inch ad.
