VOLUNTEERS NEEDED
Do you want to help raise awareness about lung health and lung disease? We are looking for volunteers to act as American Lung
Association representatives at health fairs in the Peninsula. While experiencing the fun setting of a community fair, you will be dis
tributing educational material on lungs, as well as information on the American Lung Association's programs and services. This is

Stanford Introductory Seminars
Welcomes You to the 2005-2006
Academic Year at Stanford University
Stanford Introductory Seminars (SIS) give freshmen, sophomores, and first-year
transfer students the chance to work in small-group settings with some of
Stanfords most esteemed faculty from students' very first days on campus. The
courses are designed to create an environment that fosters long-term mentoring
relationships between students and faculty. Become an active participant in the
processes of learning and critical inquiry!
Go to http://introsems.stanford.edu/ to submit an application. Applications are
required and a student may submit up to three per quarter.
