# SCHEDULE

"Grad Night" every Thursday.
"Grad Nights," open to anyone 21
and over, will feature special food
and drink offers and entertainment
such as DJs, radio stations and live
bands, Thomason said. The first of
these events will take place on
Sept. 22 starting at 9 p.m.
Other events include a trivia
night starting at 7 p.m. on Oct. 11,
and a karaoke night starting at 9
p.m. on Oct. 21, Thomason told The
Daily.
Along with the GSC and other
graduate student organizations,
The 750 also planned a welcome
night to encourage all kinds of
graduate students to socialize. The
event will be held on Sept. 30 at the
Graduate Community Center from
9 p.m. to 1 a.m.
