University Public Worship
Ecumenical worship featuring choir and organ music, with speakers and preachers from diverse religious
traditions. Holy Eucharist offered twice each month; sacrament of Baptism once per quarter during the
academic year. Multi-faith services generally once each month. See our website for upcoming preachers
and services, as well as past sermons.
