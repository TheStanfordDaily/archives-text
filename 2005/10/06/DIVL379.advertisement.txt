Add this NEW One-Unit Fall Quarter Course Before 10/16 Deadline
Confronting Katrina- Race, Class, and
Disaster in American Society (CSRE 5-1R)
This one unit (P/NC), one-time only course is open to undergraduate and graduate students who will
participate in a four part series taught by various faculty affiliated with CCSRE.
Panel Presentation Schedule:
October 10th, 7:00-9:00 PM (Braun Hall, Bldg 320, Rm 105)
"Foundations of Neglect"

"Media, Culture, and the Politics of Representation: Viewing a Racialized Disaster"
November 7th 7:00-9:00 PM (Braun Hall, Bldg 320, Rm 105)
"Organizations as the Solution and the Problem"
November 28th 7:00-9:00 PM (Braun Hall, Bldg 320, Rm 105)
"Lessons from Katrina"
