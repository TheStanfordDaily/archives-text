November 6, 2005
sth Annual
Race Against PH Run/Walk
Sunday, November 6 at 9 a.m.
Stanford Football Stadium
Galvez St. & Nelson Rd., Stanford
Pulmonary Hypertension (PH) is a debilitating disease which
affects the heart and lungs of children and adults. Currently
there exists no cure for PH. The Vera Moulton Wall Center
for Pulmonary Vascular Disease at Stanford is pleased to
sponsor this event to raise awareness and funds for the fight
against PH. Proceeds benefit the Ewing Family Fund for PH
Research and the Pulmonary Hypertension Association.
For more information or to register,
please call 1-800-640-WALL (9255) or
e-mail wallcenter@stanford.edu
