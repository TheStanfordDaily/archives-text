# Dalai Lama visits Stanford
## Community welcomes the 14th Dalai Lama to the Stanford campus 
### BARRETT W. SHERIDAN SENIOR STAFF WRITER 
It was perhaps not as coveted as a guest
spot on "The O.C" or an acceptance to a top
law school, but a ticket to see the Dalai
Lama speak last Friday was certainly a hot
commodity.
Tenzin Gyatso, the 14th Dalai Lama,
spiritual leader of the Tibetan people and
winner of the 1989 Nobel Peace Prize, spent
a total of four hours discussing his views on
meditation, non-violent resistance and
other topics on Friday.
The 70-year-old leader delighted
crowds with his mischievous wit. After
lofty introductions from Rev. Scotty
McLennan, dean for Religious Life, and
University President John Hennessy, he
