STANFORD
BOOKSTORE
Stanford Students...
DID YOU KNOW?
• Beginning today, November 10,
Thursdays are Student Discount Days.
Enjoy a 20% discount on all clothing.
• Students receive a 7% discount on
textbooks every day.
• Stanford Bookstore Cafe now serves
Campbell's soup and students receive a
50% discount.
• Wireless internet connection available
throughout the store.
• Students receive education discounts
in the Stanford Bookstore Computer
Department every day.
• National, local and Stanford authors
give readings in the Alcove.
• You're always welcome at the bookstore!
