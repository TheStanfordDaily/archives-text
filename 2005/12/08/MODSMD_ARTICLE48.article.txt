Chair Tom Lee, a fourth-year electrical engineering

Old Union Renovation
The historic building, constructed from 1909 to
1922, is being renovated into a more traditional student
union which will house a new multi-faith center as well
as the offices of the ASSU.
"The Old Union project is currently completing the
early phases of design and is estimated to complete

construction in late 2006," said Jack Cleary, director of
project management at Stanford.
