Academic Year 2006-2007 • Professional School Students Eligible
FLAS Fellowships provide tuition and stipend to graduate students for the
study of certain foreign languages. Only U.S. citizens or permanent
residents are eligible. FLAS fellowships at Stanford are available as below.
Center for African Studies:
Deadline January 31. Beginning, intermediate or advanced levels of sub-Saharan
language in combination with area studies and international aspects of profes
sional studies. Arabic instruction eligible. View details and download
application at http://www.stanford.edu/dept/AFR. Phone 723-0295.
Center for East Asian Studies:
Deadline January 17 for advanced Chinese, Japanese, and Korean language
in combination with area studies/international aspects of professional studies.
January 24 for summer language fellowships. View details and download
application at http://ceas.stanford.edu/funding/gradf.php. Phone 723-3362.
Center for Russian, East European & Eurasian Studies:
Deadline January 13. Advanced Russian language in combination with interna
tional aspects of professional studies. Other languages of Eastern Europe and
Eurasia may also be eligible. Applications available at http://CREEES.stanford.
