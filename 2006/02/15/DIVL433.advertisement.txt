Dwight H. Perkins is the Harold Hitchings Burbank Professor of Political Economy at Harvard University, and the Director of" the Harvard

governments of Korea. China. Malaysia, Vietnam. Indonesia, and Papua New Guinea. Professor Perkins received his B.A. from Cornell
