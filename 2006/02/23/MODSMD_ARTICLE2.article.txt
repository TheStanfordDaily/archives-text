# Brooke Smith
## 
### 

ALVIN CHOW/The Stanford Daily
Junior center Brooke Smith is the Cardinal's second-leading scorer with 16.8
points per game, and leads with 7.3 rebounds. Along with the rest of the Cardi
nal, though, she has faced problems with aggression. Smith hopes she and the
team can remedy this for a strong push into the post-season.
