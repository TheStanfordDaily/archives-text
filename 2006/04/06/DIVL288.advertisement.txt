Taube Center for Jewish Studies
announces
The Donald and Robin Kennedy
Jewish Studies Undergraduate Award
The Taube Center for Jewish Studies at Stanford University announces the annual Donald and
Robin Kennedy Undergraduate Award. The competition honors the best essay written by an
undergraduate at Stanford on a Jewish theme during the 2005 - 2006 academic year.
One prize of $500 will be awarded*
Essays must be typed, double-spaced, and accompanied by a cover letter with the name of the
contestant. The contestant's name should not appear on the essay itself.
Please submit four (4) copies to:
Taube Center for Jewish Studies, Building 240, Room 203, Stanford
University, Stanford, CA 94305
Deadline for submission for the essay is May 5, 2006
