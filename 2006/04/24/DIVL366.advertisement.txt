jWnat Matters to Me
and Why
Find out what matters to:
Sekou Sundiata- Poet and Artist in Residence, Stanford Lively Arts
Wednesday, April 26
Abbas Milani- Research Fellow and Co-Director of the Iran
Democracy Project at the Hoover Institution; Director of the
Iranian Studies Program; and Visiting Professor,
Department of Political Science
Wednesday, May 3
Herb Fong- Manager, Grounds Services Department, and Solid
Waste and Recycling Programs
Wednesday, May 10
Janice Ross- Associate Professor of Drama and Dance
Wednesday, May 17
12:00 noon-1:00 P.M.
Side Chapel, Memorial Church.
Please feel free to bring your lunch.
What Matters to Me and Why is a discussion and lecture series that
encourages reflection within the Stanford community on matters of
personal values, beliefs, and motivations. Come discover what
inspires and really matters to those who shape the university!
Free and open to all!
For information, call the Office for Religious Life at 72.3-1762, visit our
website at http://religiouslife.stanford.edu,
or visit http://events.stanford.edu.
