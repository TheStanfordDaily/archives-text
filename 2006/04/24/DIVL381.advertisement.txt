Sir John Boardman is a leading world expert on Greek art and is the Lincoln Professor of
Classical Archaeology and Art (emeritus) at Oxford University. He is the author of more than

outside Greece in antiquity and later eras. He is a Fellow of the British Academy and was
made a Knight of the British Empire in 1989.
