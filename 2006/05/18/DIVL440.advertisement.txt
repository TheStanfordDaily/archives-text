The Intel Foundation Congratulates
Gael Close and
Jeremy Rowlette
2006-07 Intel Foundation Ph.D.
Fellowship Award Recipients
Gael Close and Jeremy Rowlette were awarded Ph.D. fellowship grants from the Intel Foundation
for the 2006-07 academic year. Close's fellowship research will focus on Nanotechnology in
Semiconductor Technology and Manufacturing. Rowlette's fellowship research will focus on
Device Physics in Semiconductor Technology and Manufacturing.
This year, the Intel Foundation Ph.D. Fellowship Program awarded 40 fellowships totaling more
than $ 1.8 million to outstanding Ph.D. candidates pursuing leading-edge research at 18 U.S.
universities. Gael Close and Jeremy Rowlette were selected from more than 210 applicants.
To learn more about Intel's commitment to higher education, visit:
www.intel.com/education/highered
