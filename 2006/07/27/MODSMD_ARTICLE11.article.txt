# POLICE BULLETIN
## 
### 
By CHRISTIAN TORRES
NEWS EDITOR
This report covers a selection of
crimes from July 17 to July 24 as
recorded in the Stanford Police
Department Public Bulletin.
Monday, July 17
At 10:45 p.m., four minors were
caught in possession of alcohol in the
Toyon Groves.
Tuesday, July 18
Overnight, $50,000 of damage was
done to the Band Shak on Galvez
Street.
Wednesday, July 19
At 7:45 a.m., a victim reported $60

being stolen from her wallet in her
room at the Alpha Sig house.
Thursday, July 20
An unknown driver struck the
control arm of the gate to the Center
for Educational Research on Galvez
Mall, causing damage.
Friday, July 21
In the early morning, a trespasser
was found with stolen property in the
Herrin Labs.
Sunday, July 23
At 10:15 p.m., a driver was found
under the influence, with a suspended
license, at Serra Street and Campus
Drive.
