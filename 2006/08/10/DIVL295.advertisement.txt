AD DEADLINE
Regular line ads (new ads, changes, cancellations):
3:00 p.m. one business day preceding publication
Classified Display ads (new ads, changes, cancellations):
3:00 p.m. two business days preceding publication
PAYMENT
Classified ads must be paid in full at the time of placement. None will be billed. Visa
and MasterCard are accepted, with a two line minimum. Ad runs five consecutive
issues for each week ordered.
TERMS & POLICIES
No refunds are given for canceled classified ads. Check your ad the first clay it runs;
'ITie Stanford Daily will only correct errors reported on the first day.
