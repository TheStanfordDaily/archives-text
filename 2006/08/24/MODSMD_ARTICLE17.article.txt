# POLICE BULLETIN
## 
### GEORGIA WELLS 
This report covers a selection of crimes
from Aug. 15 to Aug. 21 as recorded in
the Stanford Police Department Public
Bulletin.
Tuesday, Aug. 15
Sometime during the night, an
unknown suspect broke the rear win
dow of a vehicle parked at 680 Serra St.
and stole a bicycle from inside the vehi
cle.
Wednesday, Aug. 16
At 12:05 a.m., a man was found dri
ving without a license on Galvez Street
at El Camino Real. He was cited and
released, and his car was towed.
Thursday, Aug. 17
At 7:00 a.m., an unknown suspect
took an unattended laptop from the
Florence Moore Hall Paloma lounge.
Friday, Aug. 18
At 4:30 p.m., an unknown suspect
took cash from an unattended wallet at
the Graduate School of Business on 500
Memorial Way.
Saturday, Aug. 19
At 8:00 p.m., two juveniles were
cited and released for petty theft on
Arboretum Road at Palm Drive.
Sunday, Aug. 20
A man was found driving under the
influence on Arboretum Road at Palm
Drive. He was cited and released.
