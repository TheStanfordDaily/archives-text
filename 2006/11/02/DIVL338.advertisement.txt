RACE AGAINST PH
6th Annual Race Against PH
5K Run/Walk
Sunday, November 5, 2006 at 9 a.m.
Stanford Football Stadium/Pac 10 Plaza
Galvez St. & Nelson Rd., Stanford
Pulmonary Hypertension (PH) is a debilitating disease
which affects the heart and lungs of children and
adults. Currently there exists no cure for PH. The Vera
Moulton Wall Center for Pulmonary Vascular Disease
at Stanford is pleased to sponsor this event to raise
awareness and funds for the fight against PI I. Proceeds
benefit the Ewing Family Fund for PH Research and
the Pulmonary I Ivpertension Association.
For more information or to register,
please visit www.raceagainstph.org
