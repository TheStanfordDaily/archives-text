The Siemens Competition in Math, Science and Technology is the nation's premiere math and science research competition
for high school students. The Siemens Foundation provides nearly $2 million in college scholarships and awards each
year for talented high school students in the United States. By supporting outstanding students today, and recognizing the
teachers and schools that inspire their excellence, the Foundation helps nurture tomorrow's scientists and engineers.
