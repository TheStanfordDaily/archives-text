# Inside The Game:
## Pitch And Catch 
### 
Stanford put the ballgame
away on its first posses
sion of the fourth quarter. On
Ist and 15 from the Cardinal
26-yard line, T.C. Ostrander
(13) fired a dart to Richard
Sherman (9) behind the line
of scrimmage. Following a
great block on the corner,
Sherman sped downfield,
dodged a tackier and went
all the way to the Washing
ton end zone to make the
score 17-3. With six catches
for 177 yards on the day,
Sherman accounted for over
60 percent of Stanford's total
offense.
