# SPORTS BRIEFS
## Sherman scores Pac-10 honors 
### CHRISTIAN TORRES DESK EDITOR 
Along with a win, Stanford loot
hall has scored another season first
with freshman wide receiver
Richard Sherman earning U.S. Bank
Pac-10 Football Player of the Week
honors.
Awarded for the offensive side,
Sherman caught a career-high
matching six passes toward a career
high 177 yards last Saturday against
Washington. Sherman also pulled off
a 74-yard touchdown reception in
the Cardinal's longest play of the
season, leading to its 20-3 win over
the Huskies.
On the defensive side, Arizona's
Antoine Cason was the Pac-10 Play
er of the Week for the second
straight time, while Arizona State's
Jesse Ainsworth was the winner
among special teams.
