January 19 - Clarence Braddock (Bio Medical Ethics)
Bldg 60, Rm 61F
The Ethical Significance of Healthcare Disparities
With growing awareness of the extent of healthcare disparities in the US, attention has shifted to
understanding causes and their significance. Although disparities are important from a quality-of
care standpoint, they also have ethical significance. We will review what we know about disparities
and will examine the potential causes through the lens of ethical and professional responsibilities.
