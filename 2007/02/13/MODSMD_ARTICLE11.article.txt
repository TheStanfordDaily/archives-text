# Day In The Life
## 
### 
Feb. 12,1974:
Beverly Hills investment banker B.
Gerald Cantor has donated 88 pieces of
Rodin sculpture, the greater part of his
private collection, to the University's art
department.
Canot's donation will "give the Bay
Area the greatest number oof Rodin
works anywhere outside Paris," said Art
History Prof. Albert Elsen, an interna
tionally known Rodin scholar. "This is
the kind of collection that will attract
visitors from all over the world."
