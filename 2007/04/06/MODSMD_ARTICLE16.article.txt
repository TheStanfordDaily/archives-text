# 2007-08 Knight Fellows named
## 
### THE DAILY NEWS STAFF 
Eight foreign journalists were awarded John S.
Knight Fellowships last week.They will be joined at
the University for the 2007-2008 academic year by
12 fellows from the United States, who will be se
lected next month.
Next year's fellows have experience in print.dig
ital. radio and online journalism. One is a reporter
from Zimbabwe, who currently lives in exile in
London. The University has also selected its first
ever Knight Fellows from Albania and the Domini
can Republic.
The fellowships were named after American
journalist John S. Knight after the Knight Founda
tion donated $4 million to Stanford in 1984.
