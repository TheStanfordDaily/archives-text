FORUM ON CONTEMPORARY EUROPE
The Iberian Studies Program at Stanford's Forum on
Contemporary Europe presents
Joan Laporta
President, Football Club Barcelona
"More than a Club
in the World"
May 7, 2007
4:30 p.m. to 6:00 p.m.
Kissick Auditorium
Arrillaga Family Sports Center
Stanford University
Free and open to the public
Cosponsored by the Office of the Provost
Department of Athletics, and the Freeman Spogli Institute for
International Studies
There will be a reception immediately after the lecture. For more
information, please visit http://fce.stanford.edu/events/4795/
