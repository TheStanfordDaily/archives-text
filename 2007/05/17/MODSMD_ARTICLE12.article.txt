# AWARDS & HONORS
## Cadet to White House ROTC senior will meet president, take oath 
### THE DAILY NEWS STAFF 
Diana Clough "07 will travel to the
White House today to take her Re
serve Officers' Training Corps
(ROTC) Oath of Office from Presi
dent George W. Bush. Clough
will take the oath with 50 other
ROTC cadets who plan to enter the
Arm\, Air Force or Navy.

The occasion will mark the first
time the president has conducted the
swearing-in ceremony for ROTC
cadets, according to Lt. Col. Shari
Corbett, the military science depart
ment chair at Santa Clara University.
Because Stanford prohibits ROTC
chapters from being based at the Uni
versity, students who opt to participate
in the program must train elsewhere.
Air Force cadets train at San Jose
State University. Navy midshipmen
drill at UC-Berkeley and Army cadets
attend courses at Santa Clara.
Today's ceremony will occur at
2:20 p.m. Eastern Standard Time.
