# Deans, ASSU meeting invites student input
## 
### 
The Dean of Students Office
(DOS) and the ASSU will be holding
Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportuni
ties, and Threats of Failure (SWOT)
sessions today and Thursday solicit
ing student input to reform its vari
ous branches.
Tonight's session will tackle issues
surrounding the Office of Student

Activities (OSA), Old Union, and
Tresidder Union, while tomorrow's
session will cover the Office of Judi
cial Affairs and the Organizational
Conduct Board. Both sessions will he
held in the Career Development
Center (CDC) Conference Room
from 7:30-9 PM. All undergraduate
and graduate students are encour
aged to attend.
