Grab your cap and gown and join fellow
classmates at 8:45 am, inside Memorial Church
to get ready for . . .
Baccalaureate 2.007!
Saturday, June 16
9:30 am
Quad in front of Memorial Church
This year's student-led Multi-faith Baccalaureate features
guest speaker, the Rt. Rev. William Swing, Retired
Episcopal Bishop of the Diocese of California (1980-
2006), as well as readings from a variety of religious
traditions reflecting on this important life passage. The
winner of our Baccalaureate Student Speaker
Contest, Nikki Pareno Serapio, will add poignant
insights about his spiritual journey while a student at
Stanford, and Talisman, Taiko and the Oxford Street Brass
will punctuate the morning with rousing celebratory music.
For more information:
http://commencement.stanford.edu/baccalaureate
