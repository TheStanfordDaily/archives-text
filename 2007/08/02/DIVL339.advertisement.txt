New York Times
Student Essay Contest
Is the college experience less critical to the notion than it was a generation ago? The Times is looking for stu
dent essays addressing that question. The winner will be published in the Sept. 30 special College issue of the
newspaper's magazine. Five runner-up essays also will be published online. The deadline is Aug. 6. For
details, visit http://nytimes.com/essay.
