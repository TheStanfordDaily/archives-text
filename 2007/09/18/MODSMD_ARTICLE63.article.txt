# Quattro
##

2050 University Avenue, East Palo Alto, CA 94303
Tel: (650) 566-1200

is the Italian word for "four", and is in line with the archi
tedural design of the restaurant as a large light airy room with glass
on three sides. The room is lit dramatically at night and illuminates
the front of the hotel entrance. Quattro features Northern Italian sea
sonal and light style cuisine with an Italian twist at breakfast and lunch.
The dinner menu features contemporary Italian cuisine by our Italian
born Executive Chef Alessandro Cartumini, a native of Lago Maggiore
in the Piedmont region of Northern Italy. Chef Alessandro has fea

Attentive, unobtrusive service compliments the menu. An extensive
wine list features California wines with many boutique offering as well
as an extensive Italian selection. ■
