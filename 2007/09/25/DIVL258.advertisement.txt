Teaching Opportunity
Offered autumn quarter, lIOO— Teaching Public Speaking explores
the theory and practice of teaching public speaking and trains
students to serve as instructors in the Technical Communications
Program's public speaking courses. TKX) offers a hands-on
introduction to designing an effective course, devising tutorial
techniques, and teaching delivery, organization, audience analysis,
visual aids, and style. Students deliver lectures, lead discussions,
critique each other's speeches, and take part in fieldwoi k.
If you have exceptional experience in debate or drama or public
speaking or teaching, take I KM), become a speech instructor in
Stanford s premier public speaking course, and earn at least $IJ:50
an hour reaching students from all disciplines.
Five units. Meets VI, 7:30-10:00 p.m. and Th, 12:1 5-1:05.
Admission by consent of instructor. Arrange for an interview, and
receive additional information from Dave Lougee in I'erman 105
(723-2573 or ).
