# Day In The Life
## 
### 
November 7,1963: Voting Starts Next
Week for Annual Red Hot Prof Contest

The fourth annual Red I lot Prof Contest will jingle
its way through a three-day run at the Engineering
Corner next Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday.
Stanford students will have one of their few oppor
tunities of the year to display the esteem in which they
hold their favorite faculty and staff members, includ
ing deans and R A.'s.
Voting will be done with coins, twenty-five cents
being required to nominate a candidate and each cent
thereafter counting as an affirmative vote. The win
ning prof will have the privilege ot leading a yell of his
own creation at the Washington State game Novem
ber 16.
Students may also vote negatively at three cents
per vote, with a special award, yet to be announced,
going to the receiver of the greatest number of unfa
vorable ballots.
A tote-board will be posted at the Engineering
Corner, with the running total for each candidate vis
ible at all times. All funds will go to the benefit of the
World University Service.
