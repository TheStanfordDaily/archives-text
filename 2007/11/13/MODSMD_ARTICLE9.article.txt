## Stanford edges WSU, wins Fitness Challenge By THE DAILY NEWS STAFF 
### 
Stanford came from behind to
beat Washington State in the Pac-10
Fitness Challenge on Friday. As the
weeklong event came to a close,
Stanford had logged 55,335.63
weighted miles, while second-place
finisher Washington State tallied
44,737.44 weighted miles. As of
Thursday, more than 2,300 Stanford
participants had registered online,
and the number of online partici
pants was close to 3,000 by the time
the Challenge ended on Friday. Ac
cording to Senior Associate Director
of Athletics Eric Stein, the event
marked the highest-ever degree of
participation in a single campus
recreation event at Stanford.
