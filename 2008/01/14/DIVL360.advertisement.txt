Be a writing tutor!
Hume Writing Center undergraduate Peer Writing Tutors are chosen
on the strength of a written application, a faculty recommendation, and
an interview. They take a spring quarter training course on how to
tutor writing and then are paid to tutor four to six hours a week in the
Center and at many other locations across the campus. Some choose
to become Resident Writing Tutors and join a dorm staff.
The deadline to apply is February I, 5:00 p.m.
