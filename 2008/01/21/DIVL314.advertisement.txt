Investment Banking Summer Analyst Position
Gleacher Partners seeks self-motivated college seniors with strong analytical abilities,
well-developed oral and written communication skills and a preference for an
entrepreneurial environment. Potential Gleacher Partners analysts should possess
superior academic qualifications.
The summer analyst position at Gleacher Partners is a ten-week commitment. Given the
si/.e of the Firm, analysts are expected to play an expanded role on each transaction. In
addition to financial analyses, Gleacher analysts assume a broad range of other
responsibilities integral to the completion of buy-side and sell-side mergers and
acquisitions, restructurings and other strategic advisory assignments. Former Gleacher
analysts have continued on to top private equity firms, hedge funds and business schools.
About the Firm
Gleacher Partners was established in 1990 by Eric J. Gleacher, former head of
Worldwide M&A at Morgan Stanley and founder of the M&A Division at Lehman
Brothers. The Firm is well-known for providing quality strategic advice to major
international companies on complex M&A assignments.
Since inception, the Firm has advised on over $200 billion of M&A transactions.
Recently, the Firm advised AT&T on its $22 billion sale to SBC Communications, BAE
Systems on its 54.2 billion acquisition of United Defense Industries and Gold Kist on its
$1.2 billion hostile offer defense.
