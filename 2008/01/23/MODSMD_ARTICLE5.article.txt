# REMEMBERING ROE V. WADE
## 
### 

MAE RYAN/The Stanford Daily
Stanford Students for Life set up a pro-life demonstration in White Plaza yesterday to recognize the thirty-fifth anniversary of Roe v. Wade, the landmark Supreme Court case that expanded abortion
rights in the United States.
