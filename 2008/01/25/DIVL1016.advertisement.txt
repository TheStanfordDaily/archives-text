Patent Agent / Technical Specialist Program at Fenwick & West LLP
Fenwick & West LLP is currently seeking candidates with degrees in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Physics, or
Computer Science to participate in our patent agent and technical specialist program. Our patent agents and technical specialists
interact directly with inventors from leading companies in the Silicon Valley and across the United States while also interacting
with the Patent Office to protect inventions in the computer/engineering technology and life sciences industries. They may also
assist with litigation and corporate projects that require technical assistance.
Candidates should have solid academic credentials and/or prior work experience in addition to strong written and verbal
communication skills. Candidates need not have prior patent law experience and are not required to be registered to practice
before the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office.
