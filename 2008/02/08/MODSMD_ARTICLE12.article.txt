# Police Blotter
## 
### SHELLEY GAO 
This report covers a selection
of crimes from Jan. 29 to Feb. 3 as
recorded in the Stanford Police
Department Public Bulletin.
SUNDAY, FEB. 3
■ Several subjects became
involved in verbal dispute at
Okada in Wilbur Hall at 2:18
a.m.
■ Between 10 and 11:30 p.m. at
Xanadu, an unknown suspect
broke a window on a victim's
parked car and stole a GPS
device.
■ Between 3 and 11:35 p.m. at
Casa Italiana, two instances of
an unknown suspect breaking
the window of a victim's
parked car, taking a GPS unit,
an iPod and items of clothing
occurred.
■ Between 11 p.m. and 2:30 a.m.
at Mayfield Avenue and Haus
Mitteleuropa, three instances

of an unknown suspect break
ing the window of the victim's
parked car and stealing a GPS
device occurred.
SATURDAY, FEB. 2
■ At Xanadu, between 12:50 and
12:52 a.m., battery was commit
ted when a victim was struck in
the face several times by an
unknown suspect. The victim
was treated at the scene by
PAFD personnel and trans
ported to the Stanford emer
gency room for further treat
ment.
TUESDAY, JAN. 29
■ Forgery' was committed when
an unknown suspect created
counterfeit Stanford University
checks and attempted to make
purchases with them at the Old
Union surge trailer.
