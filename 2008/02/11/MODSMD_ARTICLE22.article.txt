# Moerner recieves Wolf Prize in Chemistry
## 
### DAILY NEWS STAFF 
Chemistry Prof. William E.
Moerner will receive the 2008 Wolf
Prize in Chemistry for technologies
that enable researchers to measure
how individual molecules interact.
Moerner will share the $100,000
prize with Allen J. Bard of the
University of Texas-Austin for their
work in single molecule optical
imaging and electrochemistry.
Israeli President Shimon Peres
will present the Wolf Prize on May
25 in a ceremony at the Knesset in
Jerusalem. Ihe Wolf Foundation is
an Israel-based nonprofit organiza
tion that awards scholarships and
grants to students and scientists in
Israel, as well as offering prizes to
exceptional scientists and artists
worldwide.
