# NEWS BRIEFS
## Earth Sciences creates new department 
### THE DAILY NEWS STAFF 
The School of Earth Sciences has
created a new department, the
Department of Environmental
Earth System Science.
The core of the academic classes
in the new department will come
from the interdisciplinary and cur
rently interdepartmental Earth
Systems major. The major's trans
formation into a department —
which will be housed in the Jerry

Yang and Akiko Yamazaki
Environment and Energy building
— should by finished next year.
Students in the department will
study the chemical, biological and
physical processes of the natural
world. The department is also con
sidering offering master's and doc
toral degrees.
