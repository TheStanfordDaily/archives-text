DINNER SERIES
" What is a Major?"
A Humanities Major Panel
On Wednesday evening, February 27th 5:30-7:oopm
Faculty Club, Gold Lounge
All undergraduates are invited.
R.S. V.P. to Doraine Couillard by February 22st
dorainec(a)Manion or phone 724-0061
Department chairs and directors from Drama, English, History
American Studies and Comparative Studies in Ethnicity
present over a delicious dinner the question of what constitutes a major:
What does it mean to do focused work in a particular subject area?
Flow can this concentration possibly lead to an honors thesis? What type
of research will this require?
Better understand what concentrated work in a particular
subject entails and what it means to select a major area of study.
This evening is a glimpse into the methodologies and philosophies of each
panelist's discipline. Join in the discussion of what focusing in
this discipline might mean for you!
Sponsored by VPUE, UAR and Drama, English, History,
American Studies and Comparative Studies in Ethnicity
