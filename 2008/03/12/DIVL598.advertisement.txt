Frustrated with health care in America?
Why not learn more about the system ...
Topics covered in this course will include competing health care
reform proposals at the state and local levels. Focus is on California,
including proposals for expanding coverage for children, a single payer
system, employer and individual mandates. Recent proposals in other
states, including Massachusetts, Maine, and Vermont covered.
Attention to local reform efforts, including those in San Francisco.
