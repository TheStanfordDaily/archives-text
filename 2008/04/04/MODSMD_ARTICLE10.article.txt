# Police Blotter
## 
### JOANNA XU SENIOR STAFF WRITER 
This report covers a selection of crimes from Mar.
5 to Apr. 1 as recorded in the Stanford Police
Department Public Bulletin.
WEDNESDAY, MAR. 5
■ At 11:30 a.m. on east campus, a vehicle collided
into another vehicle, causing damage. The driver fled
the scene without providing contact information.
MONDAY, MAR. 10
■ At 2:40 a.m.. an unknown suspect climbed a fence
and balcony, entered the victim's unlocked sliding
glass door and removed five bottles of beer from
the refrigerator. The bottles were found aligned
along the outside of the balcony ridge. The suspect
fled on foot.
■ Between 11:30 p.m. and 1:00 a.m. at the Sigma
Alpha Epsilon house, the victim entered his locked
dorm room and discovered an unknown suspect
inside. The suspect jumped from the window and
ran from the scene. No property was taken. The
victim did not chase the subject.
■ Between 2:00 and 9:15 a.m. at Studio 5 on Olmsted
Road, a suspect unlawfully attempted to commit a
violent injury to a victim with a knife.
TUESDAY, MAR. 18
■ At 4:50 a.m., a previously reported missing person
was located by deputies in good health. He was
removed from the missing person system.
■ At 5:45 a.m.. the arrestee was contacted inside Meyer
Library asleep. He had an outstanding arrest warrant
and was cited and released on the warrant.

MONDAY, MAR. 24
■ Between 4 p.m. on Mar. 21 and 1:50 a.m. on Mar.

24, a reporting party observed a subject drive a
golf cart off the road and run away. The cart
was impounded and subsequently reported as
stolen.
■ At 2:42 a.m., a suspect was seen damaging a bath
room window at Wilbur Hall, as well as a bicycle
belonging to a student and a vacuum cleaner
belonging to student housing.
■ At 4:10 p.m. at Tresidder Memorial Union, a
reporting party stated that he was moved by a
supervisor at a department meeting.
■ An unknown suspect made several cash transfers
from a victim's bank account at Stanford Federal
Credit Union.
THURSDAY, MAR. 27
■ Between 5 p.m. and 6 p.m. in the Nixon School
parking lot. an unknown suspect broke into multi
ple cars and removed cameras, wallets, purses, cell
phones and other personal items.
MONDAY, MAR. 31
■At 7:40 p.m. outside Kimball Hall, a victim
observed a person exiting his dorm room carrying
his laptop computer. A struggle ensued when the
victim and several other students attempted to
keep the arrestee from fleeing. The arrestee was
taken into custody.
■ At 4:15 p.m. in the general quad, a pedestrian was
struck by a passenger car. The passenger was treated
at the scene and transported to Stanford ER.
TUESDAY, APR. 1
■In front of the Kappa Sigma House, an
unknown suspect or suspects took a Stanford
University roadside directional sign from
Campus Drive.
