# NOT-SO-PERILOUS PLUNGE
## 
### 

MASARU OKA/The Stanford Daily
Students often take advantage of bright, sunny weather and Lake
Lagunita's considerable depth by drifting out onto the surface of the water
in small boats to relax before the quarter begins to pick up steam.
