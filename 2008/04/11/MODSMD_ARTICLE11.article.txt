# Police Blotter
## 
### SHELLEY GAO STAFF WRITER 
This report covers a selection of crimes from Mar.
27 to Apr. 5 as recorded in the Stanford Police
Department Public Bulletin.
THURSDAY, MAR. 27
■ Between 5 p.m. Wednesday, Mar. 26 and 9 a.m.
Thursday, Mar. 27. institutional burglary occurred.
An unknown suspect took three keys belonging to
the Hoover Institute from a locked drawer.
SUNDAY, MAR. 30
■ Between 9 a.m. Sunday, Mar. 23 and 10 a.m.
Sunday, Mar. 30. an unknown suspect or suspects
entered a victim's apartment in the Rains Complex
without apparent use of force. An i Phone was
taken.
■ At 1:15 a.m.. a victim reported an unknowasuspect
prowling around two buildings in Escondido
Village and peeping into a window.
MONDAY, MAR. 31
■ An unknown suspect or suspects stole a fiberglass
ladder from the basement of the Beckman Center
between 7:30 and 10:30 a.m.
■ For ten minutes starting at 11 a.m.. a disturbance
occurred at Sweet Hall. The arrestee verbally
threatened his estranged spouse. He was arrested

for making criminal threats and booked into the
main jail in San Jose.
TUESDAY, APR. 1
■ Between 10:30 and 11:30 a.m., an unknown suspect
took a sweatshirt containing personal items from
the gym tloor while the victim was playing basket
ball at the Arrillaga Recreation Center.
THURSDAY, APR 3
■ At 4:53 p.m., a case of suspicious circulation of email
occurred at Galvez Modular. The reporting party
received a disturbing email. No threats were made.
FRIDAY, APR. 4
■At Galvez Field between 11 a.m. and 4:45 p.m., an
unknown suspect broke the rear window to the
victim's vehicle. No property was taken.
SATURDAY, APR. 5
■At the Hillel-Ziff Center, an unknown suspect
removed an unattended purse at 11:59 a.m.
■ Between 11 p.m. Friday, Apr. 4 and 1 a.m. Saturday,
Apr. 5, an unknown suspect or suspects stole the
victim's personal belongings at Theta Delta Chi.
■ At Arroyo, the victim's rear wheel was stolen from
his bike between 6 p.m. Friday. Apr. 4 and 1 a.m.
Saturday, Apr. 5.
