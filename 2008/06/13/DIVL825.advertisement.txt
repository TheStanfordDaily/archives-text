Multi-faith Baccalaureate Celebration
Saturday, June 14, 9:30 - 10:30 am
Main Quadrangle, in front of Memorial Church
No tickets required!
You came. You learned. You're graduating! Graduating seniors, grad and
professional school students: Grab your cap and gown, and bring your family and
friends to this student-led commemoration - the official kick-off to commencement
weekend and a time to reflect on your years at Stanford.
The celebration includes guest speaker The Rev. Professor Peter J. Gomes,
Plummer Professor of Christian Morals and Pusey Minister in The Memorial Church at
Harvard University; graduating senior Barry Fischer; the music of
Talisman, Taiko and the Menlo Brass Quintet; and readings from a variety of
religious traditions reflecting on this important life passage.
For more information: http://commencement.stanford.edu/baccalaureate
