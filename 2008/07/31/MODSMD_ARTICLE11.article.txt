# Police Blotter
## By ERIC MESSENGER 
### 
This report covers a
selection of crimes reported
from July 23 to July 28 as
recorded in the Stanford
Police Department Public
Bulletin.
A series of bike thefts were
reported during this time.
JULY 27
■ At Roth Way and Campus
Drive between 9:30 and 11
p.m., an unknown suspect
broke into a car via the pas
senger-side window and
stole a GPS system.
JULY 26
■At Campus Drive and
Mayfield at 3:30 p.m., a
vehicle-on-vehicle-on-vehi
cle collision occurred, but
there were no injuries.
JULY 25
■ At 3:05 p.m., an unknown
patron at Green Library
made verbal threats to
physically harm another
person and violated library
etiquette by using their out
side voice.
■ Outside of the Packard
Electrical Engineering
building's shipping and
receiving area, a golf cart
was stolen at 5:10 a.m.
JULY 24
■ At 4:05 p.m. a vehicle-on
vehicle collision occurred
on Santa Teresa Street, with
no injuries.
■ At 2:17 p.m. at Junipero
Serra and Santa Maria, a
bicycle-versus-vehicle colli
sion occurred, causing an
injury.

■ Between 6 p.m. on July 23
and 8 a.m. on July 24, a
failed burglary attempt
occurred at the Gates
Computer Science building,
where unknown suspects
attempted to pry open the
door to the student study
laboratory.
■ In a crime reported on July
24, an unknown suspect
entered an unlocked vehicle
at 5 Comstock Circle and
stole a digital camera,
between 11 p.m. on July 5
and noon on July 6.
JULY 23
■ Between 10:30 and 11 p.m.,
an unknown and presum
ably bored suspect at
Coronado Way and
Salvatierra ignited several
yard waste debris piles.
■ At 9:45 p.m., a bicycle col
lided with a fixed object at
Santa Teresa Street west of
Duena, resulting in injury
and defeat.
■ Between 4 and 6:30 p.m. at
the Lokey Chemical
Biology building, an
unknown suspect stole a
laptop from a victim's desk.
■At 11:10 a.m. at the
Center for Integrated
Systems, a juvenile was
cited and released for rid
ing a skateboard in a
restricted area, as the
juvenile had been admon
ished earlier in the day for
the same activity. A par
ent was notified.

senger@stanford. edit.
