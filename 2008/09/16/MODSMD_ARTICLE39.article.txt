# Scott's Seafood
## 
### 
#1 Town & Country, Palo Alto, CA 94301
Tel: (650) 323-1555
http://scottsseafoodpa.com/
Serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner M-F. Serving brunch and
dinner Sat-Sun.

I or over twenty years Scott's Seafood
has been a Palo Alto tradition. Our
simple and elegant decor with warm
wood and white linen provide an invit
ing counterpoint to our exquisite
menu. We feature the freshest
seafood from local and distant waters
as well as pasta, chicken dishes, and
USDA Prime Dry-Aged Steaks. Relax
with a drink from our full bar or let our
professional wait staff help you
choose the perfect wine to comple
ment your meal. For a romantic
evening, formal business luncheon or
a casual dinner with friends, come to
Scott's Seafood and let our experience
work for you. We feature live music
every Thursday and Friday night from
6:00 - 9:00 p.m. Come join us for
Happy Hour seven days a week from
4:00 - 7:00 p.m., featuring 1/2 off
appetizers and special drinks. ■
