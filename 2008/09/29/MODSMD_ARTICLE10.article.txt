# PRESIDENTIAL DEBATES
## 
### 

MASARU OKA/The Stanford Daily
Students watch the first presidential debates between Republican presidential nominee John McCain (R-Ariz.) and Democratic nominee Barack Obama
(D-lll.) at the Axe and Palm on Friday night. The Axe and Palm neared maximum capacity as students from all years crowded in to watch the debates.
