# Under my umbrella
## 
### 

PHUONG NGUYEN/The Stanford Daily
A sea of umbrellas line the landscape of newly renovated White Plaza, which was swamped with eager recruiters and students brandishing resumes. Students donned business suits as they
sought to secure jobs and summer internships. This year's career fair was marked with a sense of apprehension about job prospects due to the financial crisis on Wall Street.
