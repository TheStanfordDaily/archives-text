# Stanford team wins AT&T mobile challenge
## 
### THE DAILY NEWS STAFF 
A team of Stanford under
graduates was declared the win
ner of the AT&T 2008 Big
Mobile on Campus Challenge for
mobile application development.
Kavyon Bevkpour '10.
Aaron Wasserman '10. Ben
Cunningham '10 and Felipe
Pimentel '10 will share a
$10,000 scholarship as a prize
for their submission.
"iStanford." a mobile applica
tion that allows students to add
and drop courses, plan sched
ules around their major or
minor, access their University
bill and search campus directo
ries. iStanford is now provided
for free through Apple's App
Store.
