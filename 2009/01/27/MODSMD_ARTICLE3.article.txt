# A 						LITTLE SOMETHING RE-FRAICHE-ING
## 
### 

AGUSTIN RAMIREZ/The Stanford Daily
A new frozen yogurt shop called Fraiche opened in Tresidder Student Union.The sweet shop, which has a partner store on Emerson Street in Palo Alto,
greeted long lines of students on its first day. Fraiche will be open until 11 p.m. on Fridays and Saturdays, and until 10:30 p.m. otherwise.
