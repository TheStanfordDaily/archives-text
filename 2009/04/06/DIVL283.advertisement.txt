Stanford University School of Education
Cubberley Lecture Series
Featuring In conversation with
Bob Wise Larry Cuban
President, Alliance for Excellent Education Professor Emeritus of Education
Governor of West Virginia, 2001-2005
Monday, April 6, 2009 from 6:00 to 7:00 p.m.
Cubberley Auditorium, School of Education
485 Lasuen Mall - welcome reception begins at 5:00 p.m.
In his latest book Raising the Grade: How High School Reform Can Save
our Youth and Our Nation, Bob Wise, former governor of West Virginia
and current president of the Alliance for Excellent Education, describes
the growing crisis of our nation's public high schools, which are failing to
graduate 1.2 million kids every year. At a time when American students
are falling far behind other countries in secondary math and science
achievement, few federal resources are aimed at improving secondary
education to address the expanding achievement gap. Join Governor Wise,
in conversation with Professor Emeritus Larry Cuban, as he lays out his plan
for revitalizing U.S. high schools.
Free and open to the public.
For more information, please
