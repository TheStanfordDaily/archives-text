# Condoleezza Rice dines with students at Roble; protests outside
## 
### 

TOP SAMMY ABUSRUR/The
Stanford Doily Roble residents
selected through a lottery
won the opportunity to dine
with Rice. At the dinner, Rice
shared stories from her time
as Secretary of State. Earlier
this year, she dined with
students at FroSoCo.


LEFT AGUSTIN RAMIREZ/The
Stanford Daily Students
protested outside of Roble
while Rice dined with
students, brandishing signs
that read "no war criminals!"
and "prosecute torturers."
