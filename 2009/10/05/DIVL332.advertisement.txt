The Gustavus and Louise Pfeiffer
Visiting Professor Lectures
Children's Health in Latin America
Jose Ignacio Santos, M.D.
Professor in the Department of Experimental Medicine,
School of Medicine, National Autonomous University of Mexico
and Stanford Medical School Alumnus
>
H1N1: The Mexican Experience
Pediatric Grand Rounds
Lucile Packard Children's Hospital Auditorium
Friday, October 9, 2009
Lecture at 8 a.m.
Children's Health In Latin America:
The Major Challenges
The Pfeiffer Visiting Professor Lectureship
Stanford School of Medicine
Room M-106
Friday, October 9, 2009
Lecture at 5:30 p.m.
Open to the Stanford Community
Dr. Jose Ignacio Santos, Professor in the Department of Experimental Medicine, School of Medicine at the National
Autonomous University of Mexico, will be visiting the Stanford School of Medicine as the Pfeiffer Visiting Scholar. He
is the former General Director of Hospital Infantil de Mexico Federico Gomez and was Director of Mexico's National
Infant and Adolescent Health Program and Immunization Program. He is also the past president of the Mexican and
Pan-American Infectious Diseases Societies and is a fellow of the Infectious Disease Society of America (IDSA).
Sponsored by
The Gustavus and Louise Pfeiffer Research Foundation, The Latino Medical Student Association, the Center of
Excellence in Diversity in Medical Education and the Stanford University School of Medicine
Center of Excellence in
Diversity in Medical Education
For more information,
please visit http://coe.stanford.edu
