# SIMITIAN TO SPEAK TONIGHT IN P.A.
## 
### DAILY NEWS STAFF 
State Sen. Joe Simitian (D-Palo
Alto) is set to appear today in a spe
cial joint meeting with the Palo Alto
City Council.
City staff said on their Web site
that Simitian and council members
will discuss the outlook on high
speed rail funding, as well as the
state budget and water policy.
The meeting is at 6 p.m. at the
council chambers at 250 Hamilton
Ave.
