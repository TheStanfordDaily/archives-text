# Freshman Andy Brown out for season after tearing ACL again
## 
### 
After losing four key contributors
to graduation, the Stanford men's
basketball team has suffered another
loss.
Freshman forward Andy Brown
tore the anterior cruciate ligament
(ACL) in his left knee on Saturday
and will miss the entire 2009-10 sea
son.
Brown suffered the same injury
last year, ending his senior season
early.
Hailing from Yorba Linda, Calif.,
Brown led Mater Dei High School to
the state championship in his sopho
more and junior seasons. Brown was
a two-time all-league player in high
school and he was on the all-state
team in his junior season.
The Cardinal, minus Brown, will
begin the regular season at San Diego
on Nov. 13.
