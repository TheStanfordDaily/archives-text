ART + INVENTION SPEAKER SERIES: STEVE REICH AND BERYL KOROT
FREE EVENT | THU, JAN 7, 7:30 PM, PIGOTT THEATER
Aurora Forum director Mark Gonnerman explores the collaborative artistic relationship of the
husband and wife team of Pulitzer Prize-winning composer Steve Reich and pioneering video
installation artist Beryl Korot.
We gratefully acknowledge the support of Helen and Peter Bing, Elizabeth and Kirk Radke, and the Stanford
Institute for Creativity and the Arts (SiCa), underwriters of reduced-price tickets for Stanford Students.
TICKETS: livelyarts.stanford.edu | 650-725-ARTS
STANFORD TICKET OFFICE: Tresidder Memorial Union, Ground Floor
