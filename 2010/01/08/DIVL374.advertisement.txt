GRADUATE SCHOLARSHIP OPPORTUNITY
The Northern California Association of Phi Beta Kappa Honor Society invites Stanford
students currently pursuing graduate studies or professional degrees to apply for the
annual graduate scholarship.
00 Applicants must have been elected to Phi Beta Kappa by a chapter anywhere in
the United States and must be currently enrolled graduate or professional
students.
°° Application materials include a statement of objectives and accomplishments,
undergraduate and graduate transcripts, application form, and three letters of
recommendation. Applicants apply to the Stanford Phi Beta Kappa Chapter
which will select up to six (6) to forward to the Northern California competition.
00 Application due date: 5:00 p.m. - Friday, February 19, 2010.
Please submit to:
Jayne Patterson
Undergraduate Advising and Research
590 Escondido Mall, Ist1 st Floor, Sweet Hall
Stanford University
Stanford, CA 94305-2010
Tel: 650 -726-8853
Fax: 650-725-1436
For application form and guidelines, please contact Jayne Patterson (jayne@stanford.edu)
