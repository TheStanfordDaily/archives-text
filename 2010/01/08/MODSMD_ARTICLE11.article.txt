# CLAP ON, CLAP OFF
## 
### 

ALEX YU /Staff Photographer
Members of the group So Percussion provide a handful of entertainment for members of the Stanford community as they lead a lunchtime "Clap-Along."
The group will be performing the work of Steve Reich, 2009 Pulitzer Prize winner in music, on Saturday at 8 p.m. in Dinkelspiel Auditorium.
