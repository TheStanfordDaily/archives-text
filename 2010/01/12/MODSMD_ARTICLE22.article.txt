# SPORTS BRIEFS
## Ogwumike wins third Pac-10 Player of the Week award 
### Nate Adams 
After a phenomenal rookie season
in which she helped drive the Cardi
nal women's basketball to its ninth
consecutive regular season confer
ence title, Stanford guard Nnemkadi
Ogwumike was named the Pac-10
Player of the Week after a strong
roadtrip to USC and UCLA. She has
now earned the award three times this
season.
Ogwumike, a member of the 2008-09
Pac-10 All-Freshman Team, averaged

21.0 points and 10.5 rebounds on the
weekend. She was a combined 14-for-20
from the floor and 14-for-15 on free
throws and currently leads the confer
ence in shooting accuracy with 64.3 %.
"My biggest goal was to become
more of a threat to my team," Ogwu
mike said last week. "I wanted to work
on my outside shot and get more of an
aggressive mentality."
Thanks in large part to Ogwu
mike's efforts in Los Angeles, the Car
dinal (13-1, 3-0 Pac-10) is currently
tied with Oregon for the Pac-10's top
spot. Stanford will return to Maples
Pavilion to play Washington State this
Thursday at 7 p.m., then Washington
on Saturday at 2 p.m.
