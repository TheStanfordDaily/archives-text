# NEWS BRIEFS
## Final Study List Deadline today at 5 p.m. 
### DAILY NEWS STAFF 
Today marks the final day that students may
add or drop classes for winter quarter. Students
must finalize their study lists by 5 p.m. today,
which the administration declared the Final
Study List Deadline.
Today is also the deadline for students to
adjust units on variable unit courses.
Students may continue to withdraw from
classes until Feb. 26. The Change of Grading
Basis Deadline is on the same date.
