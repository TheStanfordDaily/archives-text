# POLICE BLOTTER
## 
### JANE LEPHAM SENIOR STAFF WRITER 
This report covers a selection of incidents from
Feb. 3 to Feb. 8 as recorded in the Stanford Police
Department Public Bulletin.
Police received reports of bike thefts during this
period from the post office, the Gates Computer
Science building, Stern Hall, Meyer Library, Toyon
Hall and Memorial Auditorium. A number of medical
emergencies also occurred. (They were by and large
not related to the bike thefts).
WEDNESDAY, FEB. 3
■ Between 12 a.m. on Feb. 1 and 12 a.m. on Feb. 3, an
unknown suspect or suspects made unauthorized
entry into Munger Bldg. 1 and stole chairs and a
painting.
■ Between 8:45 p.m. on Feb. 2 and 7 p.m. on Feb. 3,
outside of Lagunita Court, an unknown suspect
broke the passenger's side front window and stole
an iPod and a charger from the vehicle.
■ Between 5:40 p.m. and 5:50 p.m., an unknown sus
pect stole two iPhones belonging to the victims, who
had been playing basketball at the Arrillaga
Recreation Center.
■ At 9:25 p.m., an injury vehicle vs. bike collision
occurred on the corner of Palm Drive and Museum
Way. CHP arrived on scene and conducted an inves
tigation.
THURSDAY, FEB. 4
■ The reporting party said the suspect had been mak
ing harassing phone calls to her residence for the
past month. The victim feared for her and her fami
ly's safety.
■ The reporting party said that on Feb. 2, an unknown
suspect(s) stole the victim's iPhone and credit card
from his vehicle on the corner of Campus Drive and
Galvez Street.
■ Between 11 p.m. on Feb. 2 and 12:15 p.m. on Feb. 4,
an unknown suspect(s) smashed the window of the
victim's vehicle in the Lagunita Court parking lot
and stole a GPS system.
■ At 12 a.m., a non-injury hit-and-run collision
occurred on the corner of Lomita Court and
Museum Way.
■ At 1:30 p.m. at Ayershire Farm Lane on Campus
Drive, the individual's vehicle was towed for having
seven outstanding parking citations. The prior six
citations had only been satisfactory.
■ At 8:10 p.m., an injury bike vs. fixed object collision
occurred on the corner of Searsville Road and
Governor's Lane.
FRIDAY, FEB. 5
■ Between 1 p.m. on Feb. 4 and 7:45 a.m. on Feb. 5, an
unknown suspect(s) smashed the driver's side win

dow of the victim's vehicle, which had been parked
at Governor's Corner.
■ Between 1 a.m. on Feb. 1 and 2 a.m. on Feb. 5, an
unknown suspect stole the victim's purse from a
common area in the Kappa Sigma house and
dumped it nearby. Most of the victim's property was
recovered.
■ At 1 p.m. at the Music Knoll, a vehicle with expired
registration tabs was towed and stored.
SATURDAY, FEB. 6
■ At 12:25 a.m. at 675 Lomita Loop, a juvenile was
cited and released for being a minor in possession of
alcohol.
■ At 12:55 a.m. and again at 1:15 a.m. on Lomita Drive
and Campus Drive, two individuals were cited and
released for being minors in possession of alcohol.
SUNDAY, FEB. 7
■At 12:40 a.m., two individuals were cited and
released for creating a public nuisance by urinating
in public outside of the Phi Sig house.
■ At 1:45 a.m. outside of Sigma Alpha Epsilon, the
arrestee was transported to the main jail in San Jose
and booked for public intoxication and possession
of a controlled substance.
■At 3:05 a.m. on the corner of Campus Drive and
Arguello Way, the arrestee was transported to the
main jail in San Jose and booked for public intoxi
cation.
■ At 2:15 p.m. on the corner of Stanford Avenue and
Raimundo Avenue, the individual was cited and
released for driving unlicensed.
■ Between 10:30 p.m. on Feb. 6 and 1:45 a.m. on this
day, an unknown suspect(s) broke the exterior rear
view mirror on the victim's parked vehicle on 1047
Campus Dr.
■ Between 11:15 p.m. on Feb. 6 and 12:30 a.m. on this
day, an unknown suspect(s) entered the victim's
dorm room at Chi Theta Chi through a window and
stole a laptop computer.
MONDAY, FEB. 8
■ Between 2:20 p.m. on Feb. 5 and 2:10 p.m. on Feb. 8,
an unknown suspect broke the driver's side mirror
on the victim's vehicle, which had been parked at
680 Serra St.
■At 8:15 a.m., a vehicle vs. pedestrian collision
occurred at the Lower Emergency Room parking
lot on Quarry Road. Of all the places to get hit by a
car...
■ At 8 a.m., a non-injury vehicle vs. vehicle collision
occurred at the Facilities Operations Courtyard at
Bonair Siding.
