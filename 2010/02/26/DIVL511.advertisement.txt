Hey Parents!
Come Check Out What the
Libraries Have to Offer
What: Tours of Green Library, the
main campus library. The Cecil H.Green
Library houses one-third of Stanford's
8.3 million volume research collection,
focusing on the social sciences and
humanities.
WherG! Beginning at the entrance to
the Bing Wing of Green Library, facing
the Main Quad.
When: Friday, February 26 -11:00 a.m. to 12 noon
Saturday, February 27 - 9:00 to 10:00 a.m.
Saturday, February 27 -1:30 to 2:30 p.m.
Why: Stanford's libraries offer all kinds of information resources
and services, from rare books to IT support in the residences. Join
Stanford librarians for a brief introduction to these key academic
support agencies.
Stanford University
