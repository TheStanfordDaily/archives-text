A live pipe bomb caused the Palo
Alto Police Department to become
the scene of an evacuation on
Thursday evening, according to the
San Jose Mercury News.
A man brought what officials
believed to be a plastic pipe bomb
inside a shopping bag to the PAPD
office on Forest Avenue for disposal
at about 5:30 p.m. Thursday. After
telling the man to leave the bomb
just outside the lobby door, police
evacuated employees from both the
department and the downtown
library. Pedestrian and vehicle traffic
were also stopped on neighboring
streets.
The Santa Clara Bomb Squad det
onated the bomb at 6:30 p.m., and
traffic resumed normal flow.
It is unknown where the man
found the bomb. He is currently
being questioned and is not consid
ered a suspect.
