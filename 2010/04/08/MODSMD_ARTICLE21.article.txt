# Women's basketball rally
## 
### 

MASARU OKA/Staff Photographer
Redshirt senior guard Rosalyn Gold-Onwude speaks at Stanford women's basketball's welcome home rally. Gold-Onwude and senior center Jayne Appel are the two graduating seniors of the team. Head
coach Tara VanDerveer also spoke to the crowd of Stanford students and supporters on Wednesday, the day after the No. 2 Cardinal fell in the national championship game to No. 1 Connecticut 63-57. It is
the second consecutive season the Huskies have eliminated Stanford.
