What Matters to Me and Why
Find out what matters to
Jack Rakove
W.R. Coe Professor of History
and American Studies and
Professor of Political Science
Wednesday, April 28, Noon-1:00 pm
The CIRCLE, Common Room
(Old Union, 3rd Floor)
What Matters to Me and Why is a discussion
and lecture series that encourages reflection
within the Stanford community on matters of
personal values, beliefs, and motivations.
Bring you lunch and discover what inspires
those who shape the university!
Sponsored by the Office for Religious Life
http://religiouslife.stanford.edu
