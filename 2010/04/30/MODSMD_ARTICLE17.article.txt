# POLICE BLOTTER
## 
### JANE LEPHAM SENIOR STAFF WRITER 
This report covers a selection of incidents
from April 22 to April 28 as recorded in the Stan
ford Department of Public Safety bulletin.
A series of bike thefts and medical emergen
cies occurred during this period. Bike thefts oc
curred at Trancos, Bldg. 80, Wilbur Hall, Escondi
do-IV Highrise and Ujamaa.
THURSDAY, APRIL 22
■ At 11:43 a.m., an unknown suspect wrote graf
fiti on the southwest exterior wall of the Braun
Music Center.
FRIDAY, APRIL 23
■ Between 8 p.m. on April 22 and 6 a.m. on April
23, an unknown suspect spray-painted graffiti
on the door to KZSU and on a Memorial Audi
torium sign.
■ Between 2 a.m. and 2:05 a.m., an unknown sus
pect stole a victim's unattended backpack from
the front lobby of Old Union. The victim was
securing the building at the time of the theft.
■ At 6 a.m. at Meyer Library, an arrestee was
transported to the main jail and booked for
being in possession of stolen property.
■ At 7 a.m. at Stanford Avenue and Junipero
Serra, the Dept. of Public Safety assisted in re
sponding to a non-injury, vehicle-vs.-vehicle
collision.
■ At 7:30 a.m. on the footpath between Herrin
Labs and the Oval, an unknown suspect wrote
words and phrases on a trash can, light pole, pic
nic table, utilities corner and on the paved foot
path.
■ At 8 a.m. at 1604 Mariani Dr. in Sunnyvale, a re
porting party requested assistance from the
Dept. of Public Safety in obtaining informa
tion on a University phone number that was
used in conjunction with a criminal investiga
tion.
■ Between 2:30 p.m. and 3:30 p.m. at Hoover
Tower, someone noticed his wallet was missing
after he had participated in a tour and believed

it had been "lifted" off him. Everyone deals
with budget cuts differently.
■ Between 6:10 p.m. and 6:15 p.m., a small fire
accidentally started inside a kitchenette locat
ed on the first floor of Storey.
SATURDAY, APRIL 24
■At 12:45 a.m. on the corner of Santa Teresa
Street and Duena Street, an arrestee was cited
and released on an outstanding warrant out of
San Jose State University.
■ At 12:55 a.m., an unknown suspect got onto a
victim's bike and rode off while the victim was
taking pictures in the Main Quad.
■ At 1:05 a.m. on the corner of Campus Drive
and Escondido Road, an arrestee was trans
ported to the main jail and booked for public
intoxication.
■ Between 9 p.m. on April 23 and 3:13 a.m. on
April 24, an unknown suspect damaged three
windows of the Asian American Activities
Center.
■ At 4:37 a.m. on the corner of Palm Drive and
Campus Drive, an arrestee was transported to
the San Jose main jail and booked for driving
under the influence.
■ Between 11:50 a.m. and 11:55 a.m., an un
known suspect stole a victim's wallet from in
side the administration building of Lagunita
Court.
■ Between 8:15 p.m. and 8:45 p.m., an unknown
suspect entered a victim's unlocked dormitory
room in Wilbur Hall and stole personal prop
erty, including a laptop and a Play Station con
sole.
■ Between 10 p.m. and 10:15 p.m. at Soto, two
unknown suspects stole a victim's child bike
trailer from his locked bike.
■At 10:25 p.m. at Parking Structure 5 on Oak
Road, two people were cited and released for
being in possession of less than one ounce of
marijuana.
SUNDAY, APRIL 25
■ At 12 a.m. at Parking Lot 31 at Roble Hall, a
victim encountered two suspects inside his ve

hide. The suspects stole personal property, in
cluding cash. One suspect possibly had a hand
gun in his pocket.
■ Between 12:40 a.m. and 1 a.m. at 664 Lomita
Ct., an unknown suspect entered a victim's
dorm room and stole a laptop.
■ At 4:30 a.m. on the corner of Campus Drive
and Bonair Siding, an arrestee was transported
to the San Jose main jail and booked for driv
ing under the influence.
■At 4:50 a.m. at Stern Hall, an arrestee was
transported to the main jail and booked for
public intoxication.
■ At 8:50 a.m. at Lagunita Court, an unknown
suspect peeped into a women's shower stall
while a woman was taking a shower.
■ Between 1 p.m. and 9 p.m., an unknown sus
pect stole money from a victim's unattended
backpack at Stern Hall.
■ A lost wallet was found and turned into the
Dept. of Public Safety. The wallet was booked
as found property.
MONDAY, APRIL 26
■ Between 12 a.m. on April 23 and 12 a.m. on
April 26, an unknown suspect vandalized the
interior wall of an elevator car by spray-paint
ing it with yellow paint.
TUESDAY, APRIL 27
■ Between 8:30 a.m. and 10:30 a.m., an unknown
suspect vandalized the walls of the men's and
women's first-floor restrooms at Bldg. 160 by
using a black and silver marker.
■At 2:10 p.m. on the corner of Campus Drive
East and Escondido Road, an arrestee was
cited and released for driving unlicensed and
for being in possession of marijuana while
driving.
WEDNESDAY, APRIL 28
■ At 11:15 a.m. on the corner of Via Ortega and
Via Pueblo, a person was cited and released
for driving unlicensed.
