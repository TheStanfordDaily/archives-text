# Hot water outage affects east campus
## 
### DAILY NEWS STAFF 
Several residences were affected
by a problem with steam production
that curtailed the production of hot
water on Tuesday afternoon.
Wilbur, Wilbur Dining Hall, Mir
rielees and Branner were among the
residences to see issues.
The stream production issue af
fected heat in rooms and hot water in
sinks and showers. Hot water re
maining in the pipes, as well as nor
mal cold water, was still available.
Residents were first notified of
the problem shortly after 1:30 p.m. In
the late afternoon, residents received
a message that the problem had been
resolved, and by approximately 5
p.m. normal service had returned.
