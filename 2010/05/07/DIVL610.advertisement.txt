Have you experienced extreme mood swings, such as feeling on top of
the world to a deep depression? Are you between the ages of 18-65?
Do you feel anxious?
STANFORD
SCHOOL OF MEDICINE
Stanford University Medical Center
VA sIPAHCS
Veterans P" Palo Alto Health Care System
The Bipolar Disorder research program at the VA Palo Alto with the Stanford School of
Medicine may be able to help. Eligible participants will receive no cost medical
evaluations and treatment, plus financial compensation.
This study is open to the general community.
Bipolar disorder is a real illness and medical treatment can help.
Call (650) 849-0161 or visit BipolarDisorderßesearch.org for more
information.
