# A 						Show of F.A.I.T.H.
## 
### 

JIN ZHU /Staff Photographer
Stanford F.A.I.T.H. held an open-air concert called "Biting Back Against Malaria" in the Old Union courtyard on Tuesday night to raise awareness
for malaria prevention. The event featured Indian food and student musicians from five diverse religious traditions; it was co-sponsored by Muslim,
Buddhist, Episcopal and Christian student organizations, the Stanford African Students Association and Initiative Against Malaria.
