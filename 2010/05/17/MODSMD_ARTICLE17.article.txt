# Jane Sings for Stanford's Birthday
## 
### 

MASARU OKA/Staff Photographer
Students celebrated Leland Stanford Junior's 142 nd Birthday Party in White Plaza Friday afternoon. A performer named Jane sang songs while students
played games. Stanford was born in 1868 and died in 1884 of typhoid fever, and the Stanfords founded the University to memorialize their son.
