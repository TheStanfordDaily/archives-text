## President Hennessy to advise two Irish universities 
### DAILY NEWS STAFF 
University President John Hen
nessy has signed on as an "interna
tional advisor" to a partnership be
tween two Irish universities.

Trinity College Dublin and Univer
sity College Dublin announced last
week that Hennessy will serve as an
advisor to their Innovation Alliance.

"Strategic alliances between high
er education institutions at both [the]
national and international level are
vital to developing the research and
knowledge transfer ecosystem which
must be at the heart of a smart econo
my," said Brian Cowen, the head of
the Irish government, about the bur
geoning partnership.
