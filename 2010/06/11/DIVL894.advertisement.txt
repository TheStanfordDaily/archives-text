Continue your World Wide Coverage
with
Summer Cardinal Care
Whether you are staying on
campus, traveling the U.S. or the
World, stay covered with Cardinal
Care during the Summer Quarter.
All students currently on the Plan, in
cluding those who will graduate, are
eligible to remain on the plan through
the Academic year (8/31/10).
If you decide to remain on the plan, Car
dinal Care will continue to cover you
worldwide at either Tier II or Tier 111.
To enroll, come into the Insurance Office
at Vaden Health Center before 6/20/10.
Questions? Please call the Insurance Office
Staff at (650) 723-2135. Office hours are
Monday through Friday; 8 am to 5 pm.
