# POLICE BLOTTER
## 
### AARON BRODER DAILY INTERN 
This report covers a selection of incidents from
July 6 to July 12, as recorded in the Stanford De
partment of Public Safety bulletin.
A series of bike thefts and medical emergencies
occurred during this period. Bike thefts occurred at
the Abrams Midrise, the Rains Complex, the Sloan
Math Center, the Hoskins Highrise, Studio 2, the
Governor's Corner Guest Suite and Angel Court.
TUESDAY, JULY 6
■ Between 12 a.m. and 9:50 a.m., an unknown sus
pect stole the victim's registration tab from his ve
hicle at Maples Pavilion.
■ Between 12 a.m. and 2:30 p.m., an unknown sus
pect broke one of the windows in the victim's ve
hicle at Parking Structure 6 and stole stereo
equipment.
WEDNESDAY, JULY 7
■ At 8:40 a.m., a golf cart that had previously been
reported stolen was recovered on Galvez Mall
though there's still no word on how the unknown
suspect did on the back nine.
■ At 2:15 p.m., a non-injury, vehicle-vs.-vehicle col
lision occurred on the corner of Lomita Drive and
Roth Way.
■ On July 7, the victim reported that she had been
receiving strange e-mails, text messages and
Facebook messages from another individual
since May 9.
■ An individual reported that between July 4 and
July 7, an unknown suspect defrauded him of his
Apple iPhone, which he had listed for sale on
Craigslist. The victim sent the iPhone to the sus
pect, but received no payment.
THURSDAY, JULY 8
■ A victim reported that he had left his backpack
unattended in the Packard Building between 6:30
p.m. on July 5 and 7:30 a.m. on July 6, and found
it missing upon returning to the building.
FRIDAY, JULY 9
■ The victim reported that some time between June
1 and July 8, an unknown suspect entered their
residence on Governor's Avenue and stole per
sonal property.
SATURDAY, JULY 10
■ At 4:10 p.m., the reporting party found a wallet
containing currency on the Main Quad near
Memorial Church and turned it in, while Jesus
smiled.
SUNDAY, JULY 12
■ A victim reported that between July 9 and July
12, an unknown suspect stripped and stole the us
able parts of their bike at the Quillen Highrise.
■ At 3 p.m., an individual was cited and released for
misusing a disabled person's placard at Parking
Structure 2.
