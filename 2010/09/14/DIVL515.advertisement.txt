# Adv. 15 Page 7
##

Welcome to Stanford!
Explore the diverse spiritual opportunities available to Stanford students.
Events are hosted by the Office for Religious Life and Religious Life Orientation
Committee. All religious and non-religious backgrounds are welcome!
FROSH £• FAITH: AN OPEN HOUSE OF RELIGIOUS TRADITIONS
Tuesday, September 14th ♦ 1:45 - 3:45 pm
CIRCLE Common Room - Old Union, 3rd Floor
Meet the Office for Religious Life staff and representatives of SAR (Stanford
Associated Religions) from many different traditions in the Center for Inter-Religious
Community, Learning and Experiences (CIRCLE).
MULTIFAITH PANEL
Thursday, September 16th ♦ 10:00- 11:00 am
CIRCLE Common Room - Old Union, 3rd Floor
Ask a panel of multifaith students questions about religious life and community on campus.
Hobbee's coffee cake - a Stanford tradition - and other goodies will be served!
LABYRINTH
Friday, September 17th ♦ 8:00 am -1:00 pm
Memorial Church
Enter the labyrinth and discover that walking in circles can actually put direction back in
your life. Join us at Memorial Church to liberate your mind and sprit!
UNIVERSITY PUBLIC WORSHIP (UPW)
Sunday, September 19th ♦ 10:00 - 11:00 am
Memorial Church
Christian ecumenical service, a Stanford tradition since 1903, featuring Dean Scotty
McLennan preaching and music by University Organist, Dr. Robert Huw Morgan.
LOVER, PROPHET, MYSTIC, SAGE: ICE CREAM SOCIAL
Sunday, September 19th ♦ 12:00 - 2:00 pm
Old Union Courtyard
Create your own delicious ice cream sundae and meet new people! Take a quiz
to determine your "spiritual type" and take home a cool souvenir button.
MULTIFAITH CELEBRATION OF SPIRITUAL LIFE AT STANFORD
Sunday, September 26th ♦ 10:00 - 11:00 am
Memorial Church
Experience an inspiring gathering as we celebrate the many spiritual and religious traditions
that are part of the Stanford community. Featuring reflections by students, extraordinary
drumming by Stanford Taiko, stellar acapella ensemble Talisman, and the exceptional
music of the Memorial Church Choir and University Organist, Dr. Robert Huw Morgan.
Office for Religious life ♦ http://religiouslife.stanford.edu
