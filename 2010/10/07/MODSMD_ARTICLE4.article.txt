Fields Medal Winner and UCLA math professor Terence Tao speaks to a crowd at Cubberley Auditorium on
Wednesday. The talk, entitled "The Cosmic Distance Ladder," centered on methods of measurement that
scientists use to determine the distance from the Earth to the sun and moon, as well as other cosmic distances.
