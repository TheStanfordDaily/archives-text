Evening Labyrinth
As twilight deepens into evening, experience the
serenity of walking the labyrinth in the candle-lit
ambiance of Memorial Church. It may be just the
path you need to follow after a day of work or
classes. Trained labyrinth facilitator available to
assist and enhance your labyrinth experience.
All are welcome.
Memorial Church
Tuesday, October 12th
5:30 - 7:00 pm
Office for Religious Life | http://religiouslife.stanford.edu
