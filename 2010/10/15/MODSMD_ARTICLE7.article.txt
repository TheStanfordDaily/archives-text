GROUP NAME:

Stanford
Archery
CATEGORY:
Club
Sport
FOUNDED:
2002

FACTOIDS:
Shooting a score of 10-10-10 is
called a Pornstar. 9-9-9 is a
German Virgin.
PURPOSE:
"Nobody on the team did
archery prior to Stanford. We
start out as average people
with a curiosity in archery and
go from learning how to use a
bow to representing Stanford
at regional and national colle
giate competitions. We are
consistently a top-10 team na
tionwide. All you need is that
little curiosity. Well take care of
the rest." Chanh Nguyen,
president
