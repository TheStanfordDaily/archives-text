PEARL
INTERNSHIP
The Daniel Pearl Memorial
Journalism Internship is awarded
to an outstanding Stanford Univer
sity student journalist, commemo
rating the work of Stanford gradu
ate Daniel Pearl.
The 2011 internship will be in a
Wall Street Journal foreign bureau.
The Journal will pay the internship
salary and the Daniel Pearl
Memorial Fund will provide a
$6,000 stipend for travel, housing,
and other incidentals.
Candidates will be selected on the
basis of journalism qualifications
and commitment to the ideals of
Daniel Pearl: explaining different
cultures, writing about ordinary
people rather than the powerful,
and focusing on the dignity of
individuals.
Applicants must write an essay of
about 500 words on how their work
and career goals put those
principles into practice.
To apply, please send a cover
lettter, resume, a dozen of your
best bylined clips and your essay to:
