# Tibetan Monks Visit Campus
## 
### 
The monks from Deprung Loseling Phukhang
Monastery spent several days on campus. They
prepared dinner at Profs. Anne and Russell Fer
nald's home (bottom left) and constructed a sand
mandala in the Alumni Center, which will depart
with the monks (top, lower right).
