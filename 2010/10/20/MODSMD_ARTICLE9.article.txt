GROUP NAME
Stanford in Government (SIG)
CATEGORY:
Public Service

FOUNDED:
1963
FACTOIDS:

Hosts student lunches and
meetings with policymakers, holds
campus-wide Big Speaker events,
does local public service work
and offers more than 30 paid
local, D.C. and international sum
mer fellowships to Stanford under
graduates.

PURPOSE

One of the oldest and largest
student organizations at Stanford,
SIG aims to promote an interest in
public service among Stanford stu
dents. SIG brings people involved
in all kinds of public service to cam
pus and has them engage with stu
dents, while at the same time giving
students an opportunity to engage
in public service themselves in the
community and through summer
fellowships.
