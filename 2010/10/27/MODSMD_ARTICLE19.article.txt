# Chasing the NorPac Title
## 
### 

SIMON WARBY/The Stanford Daily
Stephanie Byrne (10) and the Stanford field hockey squad posted a dominant 6-0 win over Pacific on Sunday. With the victory, the Card kept alive its hopes of sharing the
NorPac regular season title with cross-Bay rival California. The two teams will meet this Friday night for the regular-season finale in Berkeley.
