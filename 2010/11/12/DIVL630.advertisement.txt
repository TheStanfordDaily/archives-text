Your thoughts on ROTC sought
The Ad Hoc Committee on ROTC is seeking input from members of the
campus community about whether and in what form Stanford should
expand its relationship with the Reserve Officer Training Corps
programs. Faculty, staff, and students are invited to submit letters to
the committee by mail:
c/o Ingrid Deiwiks
Ell 5 Encina Hall
Freeman Spogli Institute for International Studies
Stanford University
Stanford, CA 94305-6055
or by email to: rotc_2olo@stanford.edu
Further information on the issues on which the committee seeks input
as well as background materials on the topic are available on the ROTC
Committee website at http://facultysenate.stanford.edu.
