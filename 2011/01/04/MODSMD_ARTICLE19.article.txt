# Sweet Success
##

Stanford beat Virginia Tech 40-12 in the Orange
Bowl on Monday in Miami Gardens, Fla. The team
capped its historic season with a 12-1 record, the
best in the Stanford's history. Photos by Michael Liu
and Simon Warby.
