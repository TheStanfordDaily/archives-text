MAP SUSTAINABLE ENERGY FELLOWSHIP
BROWN BAG LUNCH SERIES
Past MAP Sustainable Energy Fellows will discuss their Fellowship experiences over lunch.
Join us for insights, discussion, pizza, and drinks as you consider applying for the
2011 Fellowships. The Fellows will discuss their life-changing experiences with some
exceptional NGOs.
Lunch will be held in the Y2E2 Room 299,12:00-1:00PM
Wednesday —Jan 12,2011
MAP Fellowships:
Border Green Energy Team Green Empowerment
Natural Resources Defense Council Rocky Mountain Institute
Union of Concerned Scientists United Nations Foundation
US Green Building Council World Resources Institute
Worldwatch Institute
Sponsored by MAP and School of Earth Sciences —Earth Systems Program
