# Small Town, Big Drug Problems
## 
### 

JENNY CHEN/The Stanford Daily
Paul Costello and Nick Reding, author of the best-seller "Methland: The Death and Life of an American Small Town,"
discuss the growing methamphetamine epidemic in small-town America. The talk, held at the Li Ka Shing Center at the
Medical School on Wednesday, is part of the ongoing Health Policy Forum Series. Read more at stanforddaily.com.
