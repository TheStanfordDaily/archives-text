# Lacrosse's Leslie Foard named MPSF Player of the Week
## 
### 
Senior midfielder Leslie Foard

was named Mountain Pacific
Sports Federation Player of the
Week after contributing two goals
and an assist in the No. 13 Cardi
nal's 13-12 upset over No. 10 Notre
Dame.
This is the first time Foard has
won the award in her career, and
she is the first Stanford player to
win it since Claire Hubbard on
April 26 last year.
