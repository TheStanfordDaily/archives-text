Major Changes to
Marguerite Shuttle Routes
Be ready on Tuesday, March 1
Major service changes to Lines A, B, C, VA, Medical Center
Loop, Palm Drive Express, and Midnight Express.
Time and/or stop changes to Shopping Express, SLAC, Line S,
Research Park, and Medical Center to 1050 Arastradero.
Additional "limited" service is offered during peak periods on
many of the lines.
For details, including maps,
schedules, route descriptions,
and rider guides, visit
transportation.stanford.edu.
Route Change Presentations and Q&A Sessions
All are welcome to attend.
• Feb. 22 - Alumni Center 11:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.
• Feb. 23 - Green Library 11:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.
• Feb. 24 - Li Ka Shing Center 11:30 a.m. to 12:45 p.m.
• Feb. 25 - Lucile Packard Children's Hospital 10 a.m. to 12 p.m.
• Feb. 28 - Palo Alto Transit Center (Q&A only)
7:30 to 9 a.m. and 4:30 to 6 p.m.
• Feb. 28 - Lucile Packard Children's Hospital 2 p.m. to 4 p.m.
Comments or questions?
