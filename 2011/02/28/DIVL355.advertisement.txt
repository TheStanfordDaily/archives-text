Major changes to Marguerite Shuttle routes
Be ready on Tuesday, March 1
Major service changes to Lines A, B, C, VA, Medical Center Loop, Palm Drive Express,
and Midnight Express.
Time and/or stop changes to Shopping Express, SLAC, Line S, Research Park, and
Medical Center to 1050 Arastradero.
For details, including maps, schedules, route descriptions,
and rider guides, visit transportation.stanford.edu.
Route Change Presentations and Q&A Sessions
All are welcome to attend.
• Feb. 28 - Palo Alto Transit Center (Q&A only)
7:30 to 9 a.m. and 4:30 to 6 p.m.
• Feb. 28 - Lucile Packard Children's Hospital
Freidenrich Auditorium, 2 p.m. to 4 p.m.
Comments or questions?
