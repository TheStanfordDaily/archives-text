# Helmet Hookup
## 
### 

MEHMET INONU/Staff Photographer
Helmet Hookup, held in White Plaza on Friday, sought to increase use of bike helmets on campus and awareness
of bike safety issues. Free drinks at Coupa Cafe were offered to helmet-wearing riders, among other inducements.
