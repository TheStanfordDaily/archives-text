# Color war at Holi festival
## 
### 

MEHMET INONU/The Stanford Daily
The Sand Hill fields hosted a celebration of the Indian festival of Holi on Sunday afternoon. Participants,
who came both from campus and around the Bay Area, pelted each other with colored powder and dye.
