# CO-GYMNAST OF THE YEAR
## 
### 

Stanford Daily File Photo
Stanford senior Tim Gentry was named the MPSF Co-Gymnast of the Year after leading the Cardinal to the
MPSF title overthe weekend. Gentry is a four-time Ail-American and the current national leader in the still rings
after winning the individual MPSF title in the event. He will share his award with Oklahoma's Steven Legendre.
