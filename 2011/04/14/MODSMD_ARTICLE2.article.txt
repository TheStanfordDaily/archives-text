# Sikh Turban Day
## 
### 

JIN ZHU/The Stanford Daily
The Satrang Sikh Student Association organized Turban Day in White Plaza
on Wednesday. The event aimed to foster understanding of the distinctive
features of the Sikh identity, one of which is the turban. Above, Angad Singh
'14 ties a vibrant red turban for event participant Nick Cariello '13.
