# Sifa in concert
## 
### 

CELESTE NOCHE/The Stanford Daily
Rwandan singer Enric Sifa performed at a concert Monday night at the Black Community Services Center as the second part of a three-event series
on human trafficking. The event was sponsored by Vox Clara, the Women's Coalition, the Women's Community Center, Stanford Concert Network,
Stanford Film Society, Crothers Global Citizenship and the Program in Feminist Studies, among several other campus groups.
