# Wandering from Communism
## 
### 

KATIE TOPPER/The Stanford Daily
Mykola Riabchuk, a senior research fellow at the Ukranian Center for Cultural
Studies in Kyiv, Ukraine spoke at the Arrillaga Alumni Center yesterday.
He argued that Ukranian society shows a strong civic potential and demand
for rule of law, and that a leader in that grain has a good chance to succeed.
