STUDENT HOUSING
Have you applied for housing
for 2011-12 yet?
Undergraduate Housing Draw applications are due Tuesday, May 3, 6:00 p.m.
Graduate Housing Lottery applications are due Thursday, May 5, 6:00 p.m.
Summer Housing Lottery applications are due Thursday, May 5, 6:00 p.m.
Have questions? We have answers!
Two Draw information tables will be held this week from 11:30 a.m. - 1:15 p.m.:
Monday, April 25 at Wilbur Dining & Wednesday, April 27 at Lakeside Dining
Housing Assignments will hold extended office hours for your convenience:
Monday, May 2, 8:00 a.m. - 8:00 p.m. & Tuesday, May 3, 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.
Apply via the R&DE Application
in Axess: http://axess.stanford.edu
Do not delay - apply today!
*
Student Housing strongly suggests that you submit your application before the
due date. The portal does have a limit to the number of applications which
can be processed at one time. Get your application in before Admit Weekend
so you have time to enjoy the ProFros!
Information is available online:
http://draw.stanford.edu http://studenthousing.stanford.edu
Student Housing is a division of Residential & Dining Enterprises
