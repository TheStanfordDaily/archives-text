Fellowship Opportunity
for Students
Rathbun Fellowship for Religious Encounter is an opportunity
to engage in meangingful dialogue with your peers.
Students from a variety of religions and spiritual
traditions are encouraged to apply to participate in this exciting
program spanning the 2011 -2012 academic year.
Retails and the application are available at
http://religiouslife.stanford.edu by clicking on
the Fellowship link in the Students section or dropping by the
Round Room at the rear of Memorial Church.
Applications due: Monday, May 9th by 5:00 pm
