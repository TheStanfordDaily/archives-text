STRING QUARTET
W/ ANDRES DIAZ CELLO
SUN / MAY 1 / 2:30 PM
DINKELSPIEL AUDITORIUM
SLSQ reprises its lauded interpretation
of Osvaldo Golijov's Yiddishbbuk. The
program also features Schubert's last
instrumental work, the String Quintet
in C, D. 956 (with guest cellist Andres
Diaz), and Beethoven's early String
Trio in E-flat Major, 0p.3. Note: Violinst
Mark Fewer replaces Scott St. John for
this performance.

THE GURS ZYKLUS
SAT / MAY 14 / 8 PM
MEMORIAL AUDITORIUM
Uniting an ensemble of singers with
the awe-inspiring musical inventions
of Mac Arthur Fellow THmpin, The
Gur. s Zykhis ("Gurs Cycle") explores
the story of the French internment
camp of Gurs in a genre-defying
program that is innovative, immersive,
and profoundly moving.
