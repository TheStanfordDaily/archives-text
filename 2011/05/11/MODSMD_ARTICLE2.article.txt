# Israel Independence Day on the Farm
## 
### 

LAN GARCIA-DOTY/The Stanford Daily
Students congregated in White Plaza yesterday afternoon to celebrate Israel Independence Day. The event, put on by
the Stanford Israel Alliance, offered falafel, volleyball and music to students hoping to enjoy the 63rd birthday of Israel.
