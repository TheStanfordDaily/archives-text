# Bring It On
## 
### 

LUIS AGUILAR/The Stanford Daily
Students seeking fun and free t-shirts gathered at Old Union yesterday for the second annual Battle of the Classes.
The event, put on in concert with Spring Faire, featured activities like four square, a dunk tank and eating contests.
