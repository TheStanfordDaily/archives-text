# Students share opinions at SUES town hall
## 
### 

LAN GARCIA-DOTY/The Stanford Daily
Students shared their opinions on undergraduate education, especially freshman requirements and general education requirements (GERs),
with members of the Study of Undergraduate Education at Stanford (SUES) at a town hall held last night in the Toyon Lounge.
