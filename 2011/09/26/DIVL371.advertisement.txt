The Fannie and John Hertz Foundation is pleased to announce the
Fall 2011 Graduate Fellows
Mr. Loh, Mr. Dean, Mr. Miller, and Mr. Shulaker are 4 of 15 exceptionally
talented and creative young innovators to receive the
Hertz Graduate Fellowship Award of up to $250,000
in the Applied Physical Sciences or Engineering.
The Hertz Foundation would like to extend congratulations to
Stanford University
for attracting these Hertz Fellows to the Stanford graduate program.
