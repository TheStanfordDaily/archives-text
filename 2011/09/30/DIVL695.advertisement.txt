Stanford Flu Vaccine Study
Seeking healthy volunteers!
18-30 years old
Would you like to participate in a research study of the
immune responses to the seasonal flu vaccine?
You will receive 2011-2012 licensed flu vaccine and
$3O compensation for each of 3-4 clinic visits.
To be eligible:
You must not have received a flu vaccination last year
; For more information contact:
J Stanford-LPCH Vaccine Program
650-498-7284
Email us at: Vaccines_Program@stanford.edu
Or visit our website at: vaccines.stanford.edu/clinical trials.html
