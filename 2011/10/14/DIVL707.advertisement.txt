VALI NASR
Vali Nasr is Professor of International Politics at the Fletcher
School of Law and Diplomacy of Tufts University, Senior Advi
sor at Kissinger Associates, Senior Fellow in Foreign Policy at
the Brookings Institution, and a columnist at Bloomberg View.
Nasr served as Senior Advisor to U.S. Special Representative
for Afghanistan and Pakistan Ambassador Richard Holbrooke
and previously served as Senior Fellow at the Council on
Foreign Relations and Senior Fellow at the Kennedy School of
Government, Harvard University.
4:30 PM
October 18, 2011
Encina Hall
Bechtel Conference Center
Stanford University
Free and open to the public
