# Corrections
## 
### 
In "Union Underground reopens after last spring's trial run" (Oct. 12),
The Daily incorrectly reported that Union Underground is open from 12
p.m. through 2 a.m., every week from Friday to Sunday. In fact, Union Un
derground is open from 12 p.m. to 2 p.m.

In "Disney probes women and war" (Oct. 13),The Daily incorrectly re
ported that Abigail Disney's documentary focuses on women in Cambo
dia. In fact, Disney's documentary focuses on women in Colombia.
