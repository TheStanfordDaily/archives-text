# Imprisonment in Iran
## 
### 

LUIS AGUILAR/The Stanford Daily
Shahla Talebi, professor of religious studies at Arizona State University, spoke
Monday evening about her experience as a political prisoner in Iran from 1977
to 1979 and from 1983 to 1992. She told stories and read excerpts of her book,
titled, "Ghosts of Revolution: Rekindled Memories of Imprisonment in Iran."
Please see the story on page 4
