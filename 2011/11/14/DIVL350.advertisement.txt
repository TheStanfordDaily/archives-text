David Vann is the author of Legend of a
Suicide, Caribou Island, A Mile Down,
and Last Day On Earth. His work has
been translated into 16 languages, won a
dozen prizes, including the Prix Medicis
in France and Premi Llibreter in Spain,
been on 42 Best Books of the Year lists
worldwide, selected by The New Yorker
Book Club and The Times Book Club, and
serialized on North German radio and
the BBC. A current Guggenheim Fellow
and former Wallace Stegner Fellow, John
L'Heureux Fellow, and NEA Fellow, he's a
professor at the University of San Francis
co and has written for various magazines
and newspapers in the US and UK. www.
