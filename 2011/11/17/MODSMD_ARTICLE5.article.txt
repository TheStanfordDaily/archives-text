# Gaieties takes the stage
## 
### 

SHADI BUSHRA/The Stanford Daily
Gaieties opened Wednesday night at Memorial Auditorium. Brendon Martin '13 directed the production titled, "Leland Stanford Must Die,"which
marked Gaieties' 100th anniversary. The play will show Thursday and Friday nights at 8:00 p.m., concluding the festivities of Big Game week.
