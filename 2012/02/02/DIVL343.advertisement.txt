Not for Pr#fit:
Why Democracy Needs the Humanities
Martha Nussbaum
Philosophy, University of Chicago
February 2, 2012
7:30 pm - 9:00 pm
Cubberley Auditorium / Stanford
Nussbaum argues that education's
current shortsighted focus on profitable
skills has damaged our ability to deal
with complex global problems. Instead,
she states, we must reconnect educa
tion to the humanities to give students
the capacity to be true democratic
citizens of their countries and the world.
