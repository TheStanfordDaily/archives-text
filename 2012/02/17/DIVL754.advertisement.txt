Stanford Continuing Studies presents:
Mozart's Birthday Celebration
Join us for a musical party in celebration of the 256 th birthday
of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. This delightful evening will be
hosted by the San Francisco Chamber Orchestra's (SFCO)
musical director, Benjamin Simon, and will feature the SFCO's
All-Stars: Robin Sharp and Debra Fong, violins; Sharon Wei
and Ben Simon, violas; and Robert Howard, cello. Enjoy two
glorious Mozart viola quintets, a very early one: String Quintet
No. 1 in B-flat Major (K. 174), and one of his mature masterpieces:
String Quintet No. 5 in D Major (K. 593).
