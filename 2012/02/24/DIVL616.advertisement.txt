Have to Offer
What: Tours of Green and Meyer Libraries.
The Cecil H. Green Library houses close to one-third of
Stanford's 9 million volume research collection, focusing on
the social sciences and humanities. Meyer Library is home
to Academic Computing Services, supporting technology
for students and faculty.
Where: Meet at the Bing Wing entrance of Green Library,
facing the Main Quad.
When: Friday, February 24 -11:00 a.m. to 12:15 p.m.
Saturday, February 25 - 9:00 to 10:15 a.m.
Saturday, February 25 -1:30 to 2:45 p.m.
Why: Stanford's libraries offer all types of information
resources and services, from rare books to IT support in the
residences. Join Stanford librarians for a brief introduction
to these key academic support agencies.
Stanford University
