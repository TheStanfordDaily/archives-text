Stanford's Information Security Office Memo #l3
Most computer malware comes from
malicious downloads.
Just as you wouldn't let a stranger in
your room or house, vet your
downloads carefully.
If you don't know their origin, or if you
don't have absolute confidence in
their contents, "just say no".
http://securecomputing.stanford.edu
