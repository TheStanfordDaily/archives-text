# Purim partying
## 
### 

ALISA ROYER/The Stanford Daily
Stanford's Jewish Student Association hosted a Purim celebration Wednesday night at the Bechtel International Center. Students
marked the holiday with live music, a costume contest, drinks and carnival games. Readings were held earlier in the day.
