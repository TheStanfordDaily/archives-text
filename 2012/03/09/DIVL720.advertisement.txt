Stanford's Information Security Office Memo #l5
Unsolicited email can be real trouble.
Legitimate financial institutions do not
solicit personal or financial info via email.
No legitimate organization spontaneously
sends requests for private information
via email.
If you didn't request the email, delete it.
http://securecomputing.stanford.edu
