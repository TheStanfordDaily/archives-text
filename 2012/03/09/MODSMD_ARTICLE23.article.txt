This report covers a selection
of incidents from Feb. 27 through
March 7 as recorded in the Stan
ford Department of Public Safety
bulletin.

■ Personal electronic devices and
a wallet were stolen at the Arril
laga Center for Sports & Recre
ation between 3 p.m. and 4 p.m.

stolen from an unlocked vehicle
sometime between 9:15 a.m. on

puter from the Arrillaga Center
for Sports & Recreation be
tween 7:30 p.m. and 8 p.m.

age shelf in the Arrillaga Center
for Sports & Recreation be
tween 8:15 p.m. and 8:40 p.m.

Arrillaga Center for Sports &

and 3 p.m.

from the racks outside of Ar
royo between 6:10 p.m. the pre
vious night and 9:50 a.m.

was stolen between 11 a.m. and
7:15 p.m.

leased for being public nui
sances on Levine Field at 1:30
a.m.

Pearce Mitchell Complex be
tween 2:30 a.m. and 3 a.m.

outside of Meyer Library at
9:15 a.m. on a warrant out of
San Francisco.

was stolen between 9 a.m. and
noon.

was stolen between noon and
1:30 p.m.
■ A male was arrested and trans
ported to the San Jose Main Jail
for domestic violence. The inci
dent occurred in the Escondido
Village High-rise between 8
p.m. and 11:05 p.m.

■ A female was transported to
the San Jose Main Jail and

toxicated and resisting arrest
