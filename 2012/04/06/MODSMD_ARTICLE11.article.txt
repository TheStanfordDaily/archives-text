# POLICE BLOTTER
## 
### ALICE PHILLIPS DESK EDITOR 
This report covers a selection of
incidents from March 26 through
April 4 as recorded in the Stan
ford Department of Public Safety
bulletin.
MONDAY, MARCH 26
■ A 'C' Permit was stolen from a
car parked in Lot 17 between 9
a.m. and 6 p.m.
TUESDAY, MARCH 27
■ Sometime between 5 p.m. on
March 23 and 9 a.m. on March
27, somebody stole a bike near
the Escondido II Highrise.
WEDNESDAY, MARCH 28
■ A bike was stolen near Faisan in
Florence Moore Hall between 5
p.m. the previous night and 5:30
p.m. on the 28th.
THURSDAY, MARCH 29
■ Somebody stole a tire off a bike
parked near the Escondido IV
Highrise between 6 p.m. the
previous night and 8 a.m. on
March 29.
FRIDAY, MARCH 30
■ Somebody was transported to
the San Jose Main Jail and
booked on a warrant from the
Los Angeles Sheriffs office at
12:19 a.m. near the intersection
of Palm Drive and Roth Way.
SATURDAY, MARCH 31
■ Sometime between 10 a.m. on
March 23 and 11:30 a.m. on
March 31 a bike was stolen
from a rack on Nelson Road.
■ At approximately 11:50 p.m.,
somebody was cited for being a

minor in possession of alcohol
near the intersection of Stan
ford Ave. and Yale St.
SUNDAY, APRIL 1
■ A male was cited and released
for driving on a suspended li
cense on Cabrillo Court at 6:55
p.m.
■ A bike was stolen from near
Anderson sometime between
10 a.m. on March 24 and 8 p.m.
on April 1.
MONDAY, APRIL 2
■ A U-locked bike was stolen
from a rack outside Twain East
in Stern Hall between 6 p.m. the
previous night and 8:50 a.m. on
April 2.
■ Somebody was arrested at
12:35 p.m. for being in posses
sion of a stolen bike near the in
tersection of Nelson Road and
Sam McDonald Mall.
■ A bike was stolen from outside
of Griffin between 6 a.m. on
March 29 and 3 p.m. on April 2.
TUESDAY, APRIL 3
■ Somebody stole a bike from out
side of Twain North in Stern Hall
sometime between 10 a.m. on
March 20 and 7 p.m. on April 3.
WEDNESDAY, APRIL 4
■ Wheel covers were stolen off a
vehicle parked in Parking
Structure 2 between 7:25 a.m.
and 5 p.m.
■ A wallet was stolen from the
Arrillaga Center for Sports &
Recreation between 9 a.m. and
9:40 a.m. Just enough time for a
morning run.
■ Somebody stole a bike from
outside Y2E2 between 10 a.m.
and 6 p.m.
