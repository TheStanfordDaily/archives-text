# POLICE BLOTTER
## 
### ALICE PHILLIPS DESK EDITOR 
This report covers a selection of incidents from
April 10 through April 15 as recorded in the Stan
ford Department of Public Safety bulletin.
TUESDAY, APRIL 10
■ Two bikes were stolen between 12 p.m. and 3
p.m. near the intersection of Costanzo Street
and Campus Drive.
WEDNESDAY, APRIL 11
■ A bike was stolen between 1:30 a.m. and 7 a.m.
near Otero.
■ A bike was stolen outside Adams House be
tween 9 p.m. the previous night and 10 a.m.
■ A bike parked outside Yost House was stolen
between 9 p.m. the previous night and 10 a.m.
■ A bike was stolen near the History Corner be
tween 10 a.m. and 10:55 a.m.
■ Sometime between 8 p.m. on April 5 and 12 p.m.
on April 11, a bike was stolen outside Escondi
do Studio 1.
THURSDAY, APRIL 12
■ A cell phone was stolen from the Geology Cor
ner between 11 a.m. and 11:02 a.m.
FRIDAY, APRIL 13
■ Between 4 p.m. the previous night and 7:50 a.m.,

a bike was stolen outside Arroyo.
■ A bike was stolen outside Building 250 between
1:45 p.m. and 2:15 p.m.
SATURDAY, APRIL 14
■ A male was cited and released for being a
public nuisance near the intersection of May
field Avenue and Santa Ynez Street at 12:40
a.m.
■ Someone was transported to the San Jose
Main Jail and booked for being publicly in
toxicated near the intersection of Mayfield
Avenue and Santa Ynez Street at 1 a.m.
■ A bike was stolen from outside the Center for
Educational Research between 10 a.m. and
12:15 p.m.
■ A camera was stolen between 12:55 p.m. and
1 p.m. from the center wing of Encina Hall.
■ A male was transported to the San Jose Main
Jail and booked on a warrant from the San
Jose State University Police Department at 3
p.m. in Braun Music Center.
SUNDAY, APRIL 15
■ A male was cited and released for driving un
licensed at 1:35 a.m. near the intersection of
Palm Drive and Campus Drive.
■ A male was cited and released for being a
minor in possession of alcohol at 3:55 a.m. in
Mirlo.
