This report covers a selection of
incidents from April 17 through
April 20 as recorded in the Stan
ford Department of Public Safety
bulletin.
TUESDAY, APRIL 17
■ A female was cited and released
for petty theft at the Stanford
Bookstore at 2:55 p.m.
■ Somebody was cited and re
leased on two warrants out of
San Francisco at 4:15 p.m. at
Montag Hall.
■ A U-locked bike was stolen from
outside the Ford Center between
3 p.m. and 4:50 p.m.
■ A male was cited and released
for driving on a suspended li
cense at 6:15 p.m. near the inter
section of Campus Drive and
Lane A.
■ A cable-locked bike was stolen
from a rack on Avery Mall be
tween 6:30 a.m. and 7 p.m.
WEDNESDAY, APRIL 18
■ A U-locked bike was stolen from
outside the Wilbur Hall adminis
tration building between 8 p.m.
the previous night and 10 a.m.
■ A cable-locked bike was stolen

from a rack behind Nora Suppes
Hall between 10:45 a.m. and 1:15
p.m.
■ A cable-locked bike was stolen
from a rack outside Hewlett
Teaching Center between 1 p.m.
and 2:05 p.m.
THURSDAY, APRIL 19
■ A male was transported to the
San Jose Main Jail and booked
for driving under the influence at
3:45 a.m. near the intersection of
Bowdoin Street and Pine Hill
Court.
■ A U-locked bike was stolen from
a rack near 535 Lasuen Mall be
tween 10 a.m. and 10:45 a.m.
■ A laptop was stolen from Phi
Kappa Psi between 3:45 p.m. and
4:45 p.m.
■An unlocked bike was stolen
from a rack outside the History
Corner between 4:30 p.m. and
6:30 p.m.
■ A male was cited and released
for providing a false ID to a
peace officer at Theta Delta Chi
at 11:35 p.m.

■ A female was transported to the
San Jose Main Jail and booked
for being publicly intoxicated
near Roble Hall at 12:10 a.m.
