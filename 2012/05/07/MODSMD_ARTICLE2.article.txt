MADELINE SIDES/The Stanford Daily
The Taste of Palo Alto, held Sunday from noon to 4 p.m. in White Plaza,
hosted local eateries such as Buca di Beppo, Coconuts, Siam Orchid, The
Counter and The Prolific Oven. The event also featured performances.
