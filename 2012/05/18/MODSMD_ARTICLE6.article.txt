This report covers a selection
of incidents from May 11 through
May 15 as recorded in the Stan
ford Department of Public Safety
bulletin.
FRIDAY, MAY 11
■ A bike was stolen from outside
the Thornton Center in the Ter
man Annex between 11 a.m.
and 11:50 a.m.

for driving on a suspended li
cense near the intersection of
Lomita Mall and Santa Teresa
Street at 7:40 p.m.

leased for being minors in pos
session of alcohol and for pro
viding false identification to
peace officers on Lane W at
11:35 p.m.

the San Jose Main Jail and
booked for driving under the
influence near the intersection
of Mayfield Avenue and Santa
Ynez Street at 12:09 a.m.

San Jose Main Jail and booked
for being publicly intoxicated
near the intersection of Cam
pus Drive and Costanza Street
at 1:10 a.m.

for driving on a suspended li
cense near the intersection of

Campus Drive and Cowell
Lane at 1:40 a.m.

dido IV high rise between 10:30
p.m. the previous night and

■ A golf cart was stolen from out
side of Kimball Hall between
11:50 p.m. the previous night
and 11:30 a.m.

■ A male was cited and released
for being a minor in possession
of alcohol near 675 Lomita Dr.
