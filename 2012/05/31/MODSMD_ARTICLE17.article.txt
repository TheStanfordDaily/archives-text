# Women's golf coach Caroline O'Connor resigns
## 
### 
Caroline O'Connor has stepped down
from the post of Stanford women's golf head
coach after 17 years at the job. O'Connor led
the Cardinal to 15 appearances at the NCAA
Championships in her 17 seasons, including
the program's best-ever finish, second place
in 2000.

A former professional golf instructor, O'-
Connor came to the Farm and instantly tied a
school best by leading Stanford to a fourth
place NCAA finish in the 1996-1997 season.
She went on to help the Cardinal win a Pac
-10 title in 1999 at the Stanford Golf Course
and finish in the top five nationally three
times.
