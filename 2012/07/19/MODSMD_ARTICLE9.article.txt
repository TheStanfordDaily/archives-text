# POLICE BLOTTER
## 
### KAREN FENG 
This report covers a selection of
incidents from July 9 through July
15 as recorded in the Stanford De
partment of Public Safety bulletin.
SUNDAY, JULY 9
■ A fraud theft occurred at 42
Olmsted Road sometime be
tween July 3 at 12 p.m. and July
9 at 9 a.m.
■ A cable-locked bike was stolen
from the bike rack at Lorry Lokey
between 3 p.m. on July 3 and 10
a.m. on July 9.
■ A wall was vandalized at
Dinkelspiel Auditorium at 11
a.m.
MONDAY, JULY 10
■ A male reported that he lost his
wallet sometime after getting on
the bus from Berkeley to Stan
ford University at Roble Hall
sometime between 8 p.m. the
night before and 12:01 a.m. on
July 10.
■ A non-injury vehicle-vehicle
collision occurred near the in
tersection of Lasuen Street and
Campus Drive at 12:50 p.m.

TUESDAY, JULY 11
■ A wallet was stolen from a purse
located inside a conference room
at the Landau Economics Build
ing between 11:45 a.m. and 12
p.m. One of the credit cards was
used in Palo Alto.
WEDNESDAY, JULY 12
■ Someone used a stolen credit card
to purchase rims and tires over the
Internet at 137 Running Farm
Lane between 1 p.m. on July 10
and 12:28 p.m. on July 12.
■ A male was cited and released for
driving unlicensed near the inter
section of Campus Drive and
Cowell Lane at 8:15 p.m. The ve
hicle was not towed.
THURSDAY, JULY 13
■ A male was cited and released
for driving unlicensed near the
intersection of Page Mill and
Page Mill West at 3 p.m.
■ Two books left at the Stanford
Post Office were stolen between
2:45 p.m. and 3:15 p.m.
■ A male was transported to the
San Jose Main Jail and booked
for public intoxication at the

Center for Nanoscale Science
and Engineering between 2 p.m.
and 3:45 p.m.
■ A cable-locked bike was stolen
from Sunken Diamond between
9 a.m. and 4 p.m.
FRIDAY, JULY 14
■An unlocked bike was stolen
from Manzanita Field between
6:30 p.m. the night before and
2:30 a.m. on July 14.
■An unlocked bike was stolen
from outside Crothers Hall be
tween 10 p.m. the night before
and 12 p.m. on July 14.
■ A fire started when a cardboard
box was accidentally placed on a
lit stove at 4:30 p.m. in Studio 4,
51 Dudley Lane. The sprinkler
system caused water damage in
12 rooms.
SATURDAY, JULY 15
■ Someone was cited and released
for driving unlicensed at 11:45
a.m. near the intersection of
Mayfield Avenue and Lagunita
Drive.
