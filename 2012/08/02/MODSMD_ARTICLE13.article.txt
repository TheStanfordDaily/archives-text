dents from July 23 through July 29 as
recorded in the Stanford Department of
Public Safety bulletin.
MONDAY, JULY 23
■ Cash was stolen at EAST House be
tween 9 a.m. on July 21 and noon on
July 23.

the bike racks near the Arrillaga
Alumni Center between 2:30 p.m. on
July 20 and 2:30 p.m. on July 23.
■ A bag containing two passports was
stolen from the trunk of a vehicle near
the Sport Complex between 6 p.m.
and 7:30 p.m.
TUESDAY, JULY 24
■ A bike was stolen near the Arrillaga
Family Sports Center between 5
p.m. the night before and 9 a.m. on
July 24.
■ A bike was stolen near Tresidder
Union between 10:30 a.m. and 11 a.m.
■ The window of a vehicle near the An
gell Field south bleachers was
smashed and a laptop was stolen be
tween noon and 11:55 p.m.
■ Somebody was injured in a bike-bike
collision near the Jerry Yang and
Akiko Yamazaki Environment and
Energy Building at 6:10 p.m.
WEDNESDAY, JULY 25
■ Somebody was injured when a bike
collided with a fixed object near the
Hewlett Teaching Center at 3:55 p.m.
THURSDAY, JULY 26
■ A male was cited and released for dri
ving unlicensed near the intersection
of Campus Drive and Roth Way at
8:55 a.m.
■No one was injured when a vehicle
collided with a fixed object near
Tresidder Union at 10:15 a.m.

■ A bike was stolen from Skilling Audi
torium between 11 a.m and 11:50 a.m.

■ A bike was stolen near the Lucas Cen
ter for Imaging between 10 a.m. and
noon.
■ A male was cited and released for dri
ving unlicensed near the intersection
of Nelson Road and Galvez Street at
10:30 p.m.
FRIDAY, JULY 27
■An iPhone was stolen at Murray
House between 10 a.m. and 10:15 a.m.
■ A GPS was stolen at Parking Struc
ture 1 between 8 a.m. and 2 p.m.
■ An accidental vegetation fire was re
ported near the intersection of Campus
Drive and Galvez Street at 5:30 p.m.
■ Money was stolen from the kitchen of
Florence Moore Hall between 4:30
and 8 p.m.
■ A female was transported to the San
Jose Main Jail and booked for domes
tic battery at Escondido II Highrise
between 8:15 p.m. and 8:40 p.m.
SATURDAY, JULY 28
■ No incidents were reported.
SUNDAY, JULY 29
■ A male was transported to the San
Jose Main Jail and booked for public
intoxication near the intersection of
Stanford Avenue and Bowdoin Street
at 2:08 a.m.
■ Somebody smashed the window of a
vehicle and stole a computer and pass
port near the intersection of Galvez
Street and Nelson Road between 1
p.m. and 6:30 p.m.

sessing marijuana near the intersec
tion of Electioneer Road and Campus
Drive at 11:40 p.m.
