# Freshman Burgess named Pac-12 Freshman and Defensive Player of the Week
## 
### 
The women's volleyball team swept
California and Utah to open Pac-12
play last weekend, and freshman out
side hitter Jordan Burgess was reward
ed for her efforts in the victories with
the first two conference awards of her
young career.
The Fort Myers, Fla., native racked
up a match-high 16 kills against Cal, as
well as 13 digs and five blocks. She was
equally impressive on the road against
the Utes, notching her fifth double-dou
ble of the year with team highs of 16
kills and 17 digs.
The No. 7 Cardinal is back on the
road this weekend, traveling south to
take on Arizona and Arizona State.
