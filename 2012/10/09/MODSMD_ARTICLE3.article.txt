# Candlelight vigil for Indigenous People's Day
## 
### 

MADELINE SIDES/The Stanford Daily
The annual Indigenous People's Candlelight Vigil was observed in White Plaza on Monday night, featuring speeches and
performances. The vigil falls on Columbus Day every year.
