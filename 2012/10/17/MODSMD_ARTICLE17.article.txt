## Football: Oct. 27 kickoff against Washington State set for 3:15 p.m. 
### â€“ Tom Taylor 
The Pac-12 Conference announced
on Monday that Stanford football
team's late October contest against
Washington State will be played at
3:15 p.m. at Stanford Stadium.
The No. 20 Cardinal (4-2,2-1 Pac
-12) will first face California in the
Big Game this Saturday, where it is
favored to retain the Axe for the
third straight year.
After inconsistent performances so
far this season, though, both games
will be crucial tests.

The game will be broadcast live
on both the Pac-12 Networks and
Stanford's KZSU radio.
