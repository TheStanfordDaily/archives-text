# Men's waterpolo: Bret Bonanni named Player of the Week
## 
### 
With a pair of hat tricks against
No. 2 UC Santa Barbara and No. 8
Pepperdine last weekend, freshman
driver Bret Bonanni helped the No. 5
Cardinal to back-to-back wins and
secured MPSF/KAP 7 Player of the
Week honors.
Bonanni is now second on the
team with 26 goals in 11 games and
ranks tenth in the Mountain Pacific
Sports Federation with 2.36 goals per
game.

Next up for Stanford is a road trip
to face California in the Big Splash
this Saturday morning ahead of the

Big Game, followed by games
against Bucknell and Air Force in the
Rodeo Tournament in Santa Clara
on Sunday.
