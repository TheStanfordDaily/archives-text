The Fannie and John Hertz Foundation
is pleased to announce the
Fall 2012 Graduate Fellows
Ms. Datta, Mr. Lawrence, and Mr. Steinhardt
are 3 of 15 exceptionally talented and creative

in the Applied Physical, Biological and
Engineering Sciences.
The Hertz Foundation would like to extend
congratulations to Stanford University for attracting
these Hertz Fellows to the Stanford graduate program.
