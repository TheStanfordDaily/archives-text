# POLICE BLOTTER
## 
### ALICE PHILLIPS DESK EDITOR 
This report covers a selection of incidents
from Oct. 11 through Oct. 16 as recorded in the
Stanford Department of Public Safety bulletin.
THURSDAY, OCT. 11
■ A bike was stolen from outside of Cedro
between 9 p.m. on Oct. 10 and 6:45 a.m. on
Oct. 11.
■ A duffel bag was stolen from a vehicle parked
in the Stock Farm lot between 10 a.m. and 6
p.m.
FRIDAY, OCT. 12
■ A female was cited and released for being a
minor in possession of alcohol near the inter
section of Mayfield Avenue and Campus
Drive at 11:50 p.m.
SATURDAY, OCT. 13
■ A male was transported to the San Jose Main
Jail and booked for being publicly intoxicated
near the intersection of Mayfield Avenue and
Campus Drive at 1:35 a.m.
■ A male was transported to the San Jose Main

Jail and booked for being publicly intoxicated
near the intersection of Running Farm Lane
and Escondido Road at 5:35 a.m.
■ A bike was stolen from outside the Munger
Graduate Residences, Building 4, sometime
between 10 a.m. on Oct. 8 and 4 p.m. on Oct.
13.
■ A bike was stolen from outside of Larkin
North between 11:30 p.m. on Oct. 12 and 8
p.m. on Oct. 13.
■ A male was cited and released for driving
unlicensed at 11:25 p.m. near the intersection
of Serra Street and Campus Drive.
SUNDAY, OCT 14
■ A bike was stolen from outside of Casa
Zapata between 9 p.m. on Oct. 13 and 6 p.m.
on Oct. 14.
TUESDAY, OCT 16
■ A female was transported to the San Jose
Main Jail and booked for domestic violence
at 12:45 a.m. An emergency protected order
was granted.

Contact Alice Phillips at alicephillips@stan
ford.edu.
