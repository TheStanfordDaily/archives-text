# Graduate community holds forum to discuss ARP
## 
### 

ALISA ROYER/The Stanford Daily
The Office of Community Standards and the ASSU held the first of two town halls Tuesday to discuss the Alternative Review
Process (ARP), a judicial procedure for cases involving sexual assault. The second discussion will be held tonight at 8:30 p.m.
