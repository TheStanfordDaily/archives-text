# All the Duke's men
## 
### 

ROGER CHEN/The Stanford Daily
The Duke's Men of Yale University, an a capella group founded in 1952, performed at the CoHo on Wednesday evening.
The group performs both old favorites and modern renditions. Among their repertoire was a cover of Rihanna's "Umbrella."
