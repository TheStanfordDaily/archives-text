# Debating the death penalty
## 
### 

MADELINE SIDES/The Stanford Daily
The Stanford Debate Society led a discussion Monday night about Prop. 34, a California ballot initiative that seeks
to replace the death penalty with life without parole. Students were allowed to ask questions during the debate.
