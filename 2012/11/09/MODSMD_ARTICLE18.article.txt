# SPORTS BRIEFS
## Football's Anderson named to Academic All-District Team 
### 
Stanford starting defensive end
Henry Anderson was selected to
the Capital One Academic All-
District 8 Team on Thursday by the
College Sports Information
Directors of America (CoSIDA).
The junior political science major
has a 3.43 grade-point average, and
has recorded 3.5 sacks fourth
best amongst Cardinal defenders
and 30 tackles this season.
