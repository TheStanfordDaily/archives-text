Matthew Jay Roach '79's HBO film "Game
Change" won a Golden Globe award for best
miniseries or TV movie last night.
The film is based on the 2008 U.S. presiden
tial election campaign, adapted from a book by
the same name. It was screened on campus at
Cemex Auditorium on Feb. 28,2012.
Roach has also directed "Austin Powers,"
"Meet the Parents" and, most recently, "The
Campaign," a comedy starring Will Ferrell and
Zach Galifianakis.
