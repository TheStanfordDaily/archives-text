# Acoustic Broccoli Forest
## 
### 
MADELINE SIDES/The Stanford Daily
Enchanted Broccoli Forest hosted an Acoustic Jukebox event Monday
night. Ben Mckenzie '15, above, performed on guitar and piano.
Andrea Slobodien '13 and Jake Wachtel '09 also performed at the
event, part of a series at EBF. The Student Organizing Committee for
the Arts (SOCA) is sponsoring the 2012-13 Acoustic Jukebox series.
