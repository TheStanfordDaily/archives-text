Public Policy 135
Regional Politics and
Decision Making in Silicon Valley
Wednesdays 3:15pm-5:05pm
Dynamics of regional leadership and decision making in Silicon Valley,
a complex region composed of 40 cities and four counties without any
overarching framework for governance, formal and informal institutions
shaping outcomes in the region. Case studies include transportation,
workforce development. housing and land use, and climate change.
