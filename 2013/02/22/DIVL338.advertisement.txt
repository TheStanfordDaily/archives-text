Welcome, Parents!
Come See Wkat the Libraries Have to Offer
What: Tours of Green and Meyer Libraries.
The Cecil H. Green Library houses close to one-third of Stanford's
9 million volume research collection, focusing on the social sciences
and humanities. Meyer Library is home to Academic Computing
Services, supporting technology for students and faculty.

Main Quad. Tours of Meyer Library immediately follow most of the
Green Library tours.

11:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
Saturday, February 23 10:30 to 11:30 a.m.
(This tour does not include Meyer Library; Meyer is closed until 1 p.m.)
Saturday, February 23
2:30 to 3:30 p.m.
Why: Stanford's libraries offer all types of information resources and
services, from rare books to IT support in the residences. Please join
Stanford librarians for a brief introduction to these key academic
support agencies.
