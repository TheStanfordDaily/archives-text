Stanford Continuing Studies Presents
An Evening with Felix Mendelssohn
Join San Francisco Chamber Orchestra (SFCO) music director
Benjamin Simon, tenor Brian Thorsett, and an allstar team
of SFCO musicians for an evening of songs and string
quartets by the early Romantic master Felix Mendelssohn.
Featured selections include Mendelssohn's fabulous first
quartet, Op. 12 in E flat major and a brilliant arrangement of
several songs for tenor and string quartet by Albert Riemann.
Benjamin Simon is a former member of the Stanford String
Quartet. He has been teaching Continuing Studies music
courses since 1996, and has also organized popular
celebrations of Bach, Beethoven, Mozart, Schubert, Bartok,
Bernstein, Schumann, and Brahms.
Saturday, March 2 • 7:30 pm
Annenberg Auditorium, Cummings Art Building
Stanford University • Free and open to the public
For more info: continuingstudies.stanford.edu
