"Alas," said the mouse, "the world is growing smaller every day.
At the begming it was so big that I was afraid[ I kept running and
running, and I was glad when at last I saw walls far away to the
right and left, but these long Malls have narrowed so quickly that I
am in the last chamber already, and there in the corner stands the
trap that I must run into." "You only need to change your direc
tion, " said the cat, and ate it up.
—Kakfa, "A Little Fable"
