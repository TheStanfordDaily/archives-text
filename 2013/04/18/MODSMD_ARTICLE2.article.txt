# Students rally for justice in Venezuelan elections
## 
### 

AVI BAGLA/The Stanford Daily
Students gathered in White Plaza on April 17 to protest alleged electoral crimes in Venezuela's recent presidential
election. Many protestors dressed in yellow, red and blue to represent the Venezuelan flag.
