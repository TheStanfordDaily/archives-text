# Sustainability Festival
## 
### 

SAM GIRVIN/The Stanford Daily
Sustainable Stanford hosted its annual Celebrating Sustainability Festival on Earth
Day, April 22, at the Science and Engineering Quad courtyard with food, live music
and activities geared to educate guests on Stanford's green initiatives.
