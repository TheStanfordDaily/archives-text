# University breaks ground on new hospital
## 
### 

AVI BAGLA/The Stanford Daily
Mariann Byerwalter, chair of the Stanford Hospital & Clinics Board of Directors, Amir Dan Rubin, president
and CEO of Stanford Hospital & Clinics, University President John Hennessy, Lloyd Minor, dean of the School
of Medicine, and Palo Alto Mayor Greg Scharff broke ground on the new Stanford Hospital on Wednesday.
