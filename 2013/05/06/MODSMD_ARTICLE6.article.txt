# Human chess comes to Stanford
## 
### 

IAN GARCIA-DOTY/The Stanford Daily
The Stanford Chess Club hosted a human chess match on May 5 at 4 p.m. in White Plaza. Students signed up to either play
as individual chess pieces or to direct the game, which was held in an effort to publicize the Chess Club.
