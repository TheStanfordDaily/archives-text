# Norwegian Crown Prince comes to campus
## 
### 

SAM GIRVIN/The Stanford Daily
President John Hennessy shares a laugh with Haakon, Crown Prince of Norway, and his wife, Mette-Marit, Crown
Princess of Norway, while conducting a tour of Stanford's campus on May 8. The Prince and Princess were visiting the
Farm as part of a Norwegian technology and business delegation to San Francisco and Silicon Valley.
