# Powwow displays Native American culture
## 
### 

IAN GARCIA-DOTY/The Stanford Daily
The Stanford American Indian Organization sponsored the 42nd annual Stanford
Powwow, which ran from May 10-12. The event featured social dances, live
music, an American Indian art market and the 17th annual Fun Run.
