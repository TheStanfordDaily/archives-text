# Israeli peace activist speaks at Bechtel
## 
### 

SEAN CHRISTOFFERSON/The Stanford Daily
Miko Peled, Israeli peace activist and author of "The General's Son: Journey of an Israeli in Palestine," spoke to students at the Bechtel
International Center on Tuesday. The event, in which Peled discussed peace in the Middle East and equal rights for Israeli and
Palestinian citizens, was co-sponsored by the Muslim Student Awareness Network and Students for Palestinian Equal Rights.
