Stanford Continuing Studies and the Ho Center for Buddhist Studies at Stanford present
Buddhist Compassion Workshop with
Norman Fischer and Spring Washam
♦
Join us for a Saturday afternoon workshop co-sponsored by the Ho Center for
Buddhist Studies at Stanford, Everyday Zen Foundation, Spirit Rock Meditation Center,
and East Bay Meditation Center. The workshop will combine guided meditation and talks
by two of the Bay Area's most distinguished Zen practitioners.
(Participants should feel free to bring a yoga mat).
Training in Compassion: Zen Teachings on the Practice of Lojong, talk given
by Norman Fischer, Founder and Spiritual Director, Everyday Zen Foundation;
Former Abbot, San Francisco Zen Center.
Wisdom and Compassion: The Wings of Awakening, talk given by
Spring Washam, Teacher, Spirit Rock Meditation Center; Co-founder and
Core Teacher, East Bay Meditation Center.
Saturday, May 18
1:00 - 4:00 pm
