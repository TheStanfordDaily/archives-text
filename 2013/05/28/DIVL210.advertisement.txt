V Losses
An Interfaith Grief Memorial
Thursday, May 30, 4:00 pm
CIRCLE Sanctuary
Old Union, 3rd floor
The Stanford community is invited to take part in a service
of hope and healing for those grieving losses recent and
past. Light candles to honor those who have died; take in
soothing music and sacred readings about loss and hope.
All losses, whether recent or in the past, will be honored.
Refreshments will be served after the service.
Stanford I Office for Religious Life
