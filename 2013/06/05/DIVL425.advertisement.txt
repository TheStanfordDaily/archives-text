Senior Tie Gift For Coach Dawkins
After a tough year for the
Cardfnaf men's basketball
team, graduating seniors Wilfys
DeVoll and Max Wernecke
have donated a set of three ties
to help Coach Dawkins
stylishly inspire and lead the
Cardinal to an NCAA berth
next season.
