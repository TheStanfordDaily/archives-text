## Women's soccer begins road trip with 1-0 win at Washington 
### — Joseph Beyda

6-3-0 Pac-12) held off Washington (7-7-4,3-2-
4) in Seattle 1-0 on Thursday night, benefit
ing from junior Lo'eau Laßonta's fourth goal
of the season and six saves by goalie Jane

Laßonta's 44th-minute tally was all the
Cardinal needed for its third 1-0 win of
2013. The Cardinal now travels to Washing

as it looks to improve its seeding in the up
