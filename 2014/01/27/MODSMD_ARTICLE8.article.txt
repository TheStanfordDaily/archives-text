# KSA hosts Korean traditional mask dance workshop
## 
### 

SAM GIRVIN/The Stanford Daily
The Korean Student Association hosted a workshop on Talchum, a centuries-old Korean traditional mask dance,
on Saturday at Arrillaga dining hall. Attendees had the opportunity to create their own masks as well.
