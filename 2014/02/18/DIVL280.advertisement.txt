Honoring Lives, V
Remembering Losses
The Stanford community is invited to this
interfaith memorial service intended to
provide hope and healing for those grieving
losses, recent and past. Refreshments will be
served after the service. All are welcome.
Thursday, February 20, 4:00 pm
CIRCLE Sanctuary (Old Union, 3rd floor)
Sponsored by the Office for Religious Life, Counseling and Psychological
Services, Residential Education and the Faculty and Staff Help Center.
