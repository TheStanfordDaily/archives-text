Welcome, Parents!
Co me See Wkat the Libraries Have to Offer
What: Tours of Green and Meyer Libraries.
The Cecil H. Green Library houses close to one-third of
Stanford's 9-million-volume research collection, focusing on
the social sciences and humanities. Meyer Library is home
to Academic Computing Services, supporting technology for
students and faculty.
Where: Meet at the Bing Wing entrance of Green Library, facing
the Main Quad. Tours of Meyer Library immediately follow most
of the Green Library tours.
When: Friday, February 21 11:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
Saturday, February 22 10:30 to 11:30 a.m.
(This tour does not include Meyer Library; Meyer is closed until 1 p.m.)
Saturday, February 22 2:30 to 3:30 p.m.
Why: Stanford's libraries offer all types of information resources
and services, from rare books to IT support in the residences.
Please join Stanford librarians for a brief introduction to these
key academic support agencies.
