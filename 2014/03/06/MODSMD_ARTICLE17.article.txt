portedly raped by a male

cant force during the at

power cord.

the Environment and En
ergy Building (Y2E2).

■ A reportedly U-locked
bike was stolen from the
bike parking area near
Building 160 between
10:00 a.m. and 11:00 a.m.

ed and transported to the
San Jose Main Jail for
rape, domestic violence
and making criminal
threats between 11:00

p.m. and 11:45 p.m.
■ Between Feb. 28 and Mar.
3, an unknown suspect or
suspects stole property at

ergy Building (Y2E2).

3, an unknown suspect or
suspects vandalized a win
dow in the Landau Eco
nomics Building by pulling
the window frame and
causing the hinges that
open and close the window
to bend.
TUESDAY, MAR. 4
■An unknown suspect or
suspects stole a watch
from a vehicle between
8:25 a.m. and 10:15 a.m. at
the 600 Block of Nelson
Road. There were no signs
of forced entry into the ve
hicle.
■ An individual was arrest
ed and transported to the

Avenue between 3:50 p.m.
and 4:00 p.m.

suspects vandalized the el
evator leading up to the

making sliding hand
prints.
