# POLICE BLOTTER
## 
### CALEB SMITH CONTRIBUTING WRITER 
This report covers a selection of incidents
from Mar. 24 to Apr. 1 as recorded in the
Stanford Department of Public Safety bul
letin.
MONDAY, MAR. 24
■ In a "road rage" incident involving two bi
cyclists, a male cyclist struck a female cy
clist, lost his balance and fell. The victim
was uninjured. The suspect fled the scene
around 8:29 a.m. at Campus Dr. and Elec
tioneer Rd.
TUESDAY, MAR. 25

■ A cable-locked bike was stolen from a bike
rack between 1 p.m. and 3:30 p.m. behind
74 Barnes Ct.
■ A reportedly U-locked bike was stolen
from outside the front porch of a residence
in Escondido Village 111, Building 104.
■ A bike reportedly U-locked to itself was
stolen from outside the front patio of a
residence in Escondido Village 111, Build
ing 104.
WEDNESDAY, MAR. 26
■ An individual was cited and released at
11:05 a.m. at Stanford Ave. and Junipero
Serra Blvd. for driving unlicensed.
■ An individual was arrested and
transported to the San Jose
Main Jail at 2:15 PM at 2600 El
Camino Real at Page Mill Road
for possession of concentrated
cannabis (hash oil) and for be
ing in possession of a medica
tion without a prescription.
■ Plants were stolen from a walk
way at the Organic Chemistry
Building.
■ Bike wheels were stolen from a
locked bike at the Beckman
Center for Molecular and Ge
netic Medicine.
FRIDAY, MAR. 28
■ An individual was arrested and
transported to the San Jose Main
Jail for domestic violence battery
between 8:50 p.m. and 9:05 p.m.
at Studio 1. A firearm was report
edly used in the attack.
■ An unlocked bike was stolen
from Escondido Village 11,
Building 92. The bike was re
portedly stolen from the front

door patio area of the victim's
apartment.
SATURDAY, MAR. 29
■ An individual was cited and re
leased at 11:15 a. m. at the 600
block of Junipero Serra Boule
vard for driving unlicensed.
■An iPad was stolen between
2:00 p.m. and 5:00 p.m. at 295
Galvez St. at Campus Dr. The
iPad was stolen when an un
known suspect or suspects en
tered the victim's trunk by pry
ing the lock.
■ An individual was cited and re
leased at 4:10 p. m. at the 700
block of Junipero Serra Boule
vard for driving on a suspended
license.
TUESDAY, APR. 1
■ Three individuals were arrested
and transported to the San Jose
Main Jail at 4 a.m. at the 300
block of Olmsted Rd. for posses
sion of stolen property and pos
session of a controlled sub
stance. A stolen vehicle was re
covered.
