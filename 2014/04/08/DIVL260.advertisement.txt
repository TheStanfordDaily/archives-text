Stanford Continuing Studies presents
BiLi the Mime and His Solo Show
Join us for an evening of entertainment, illusion,
and fun with BiLi the Mime and his solo show. A
professional mime of more than thirty years, BiLi
studied mime with a disciple of Marcel Marceau
and Etienne Decroux. This show will highlight
traditional mime techniques—with a twist of poetry
and humor—and a handful of skits will encourage
audience participation. The topics chosen will depict
episodes of everyday life and are suitable for anyone
above eight years old. Bring the entire family and
discover the art of storytelling without words!
Thursday, April 10
7:30 pm
Cubberley Auditorium
School of Education
Stanford University
Free and open to public
For more info: continuingstudies.stanford.edu
