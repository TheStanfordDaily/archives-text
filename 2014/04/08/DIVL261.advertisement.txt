WHAT MATTERS
TO ME AND WHY
with
Jennifer Summit
Professor of English
I 4
{ "
Wednesday, April 9, 2014
12:00-1:00 pm, The Sanctuary
Center for Inter-Religious Community, Learning and Experiences
(Old Union, 3rd floor)
Jennifer Summit (Professor of English) researches and teaches topics
related to reading and literacy in the Middle Ages and Renaissance and
today. She is the author of Lost Property: The Woman Writer and English
Literary History, 1380-1589 and Memory's Library: Medieval Books in
Early Modern England.
The purpose of What Matters to Me and Why is to encourage reflection
within the Stanford community on matters of personal values, beliefs,
and motivations in order to better understand the lives and inspirations
of those who shape the University. All are welcome.
