# Stanford Splash! attracts youth to campus
## 
### 

KRISTEN STIPANOV/The Stanford Daily
This weekend, the Stanford Educational Studies Program hosted their biannual event, Splash!, a two-day event taking place in and
around the Main Quad. This spring, Splash! drew 2,822 middle and high school students to campus to attend a range of Stanford-student
taught classes. In total, 371 Stanford students participated, teaching 331 classes, which ranged from "Intro to Magic Tricks"
to "The Chemistry of Cooking" to "The Inner Life of a Cell: How Our Cells Use DNAto Give Us Everything We Need."
