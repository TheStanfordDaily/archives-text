# Start.Home solar house moves to Jasper Ridge Biological Preserve
## 
### 

ZETONG LI/The Stanford Daily
Following the Solar Decathlon competition, the student-engineered Start.Home solar home was transported to Jasper Ridge Biological
Preserve. The new home now serves as a home for Brooke Fabricant, the resident ranger patrolling the Jasper Ridge trails, and his family.
