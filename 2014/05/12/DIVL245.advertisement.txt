Jeff Chang is Executive Director of the Institute for Diversity in the
Arts + Committee on Black Performing Arts at Stanford University.
He is continuing his long career specializing in culture, politics,
music and the arts.
The purpose of What Matters to Me and Why is to encourage
reflection within the Stanford community on matters of personal
values, beliefs, and motivations in order to better understand the
lives and inspirations of those who shape the University. All are
welcome. ~_ r ~
