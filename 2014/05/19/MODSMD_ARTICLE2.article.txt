# Lantern Festival 2014
## 
### 

KRISTEN STIPANOV/The Stanford Daily
On Sunday night, the Undergraduate Chinese American Association
held its annual Lantern Festival in front of Toyon Hall. The
celebration included free food and riddles for attendees.
