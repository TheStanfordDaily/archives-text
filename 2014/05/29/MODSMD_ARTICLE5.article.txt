# Friends of Music Jazz Faculty Showcase
## 
### 

AVI BAGLA/The Stanford Daily
On Wednesday night at Campbell Recital Hall, the Friends of Music at Stanford offered a free showcase that featured members of
Stanford's jazz faculty. Among the performers were Rick Vandivier on guitar, Charlie McCarthy on saxophone and their friends.
